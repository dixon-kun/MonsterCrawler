        InCadence Strategic Solutions is a top technology company that provides cutting edge solutions, deep functional domain subject matter expertise, operational intelligence and high-end engineering services to our government clients.
        InCadence is now seeking a
         Cognitive Data Scientist
        to work on our client site in Tampa, FL.
        The Cognitive Data Scientist position is responsible for defining, developing and building cognitive solutions for intelligence operational needs, such as Natural Language Processing (NLP), Linguistics, Advanced Semantic Design, Information Extraction, Information Retrieval, Probabilistic Decision Making, image recognition, deep machine Learning, cognitive science and analytics.&#160; This position will require a TS/SCI security clearance. &#160;They will serve as a developer and Subject Matter Expert (SME) in one or several of the above mentioned domains and will help establish a competency in those cognitive areas for applied research organization.&#160; The data scientist will work closely with various functional teams and specialists to incorporate cognitive and advanced analytics models and algorithms in into a fast paced data analytic environment and serve as cognitive specialist, developing support for client facing activities and special projects. &#160;&#160;They will be responsible for modeling and algorithm development and optimization, working with software engineers to produce and implement models and algorithms to support advanced cognitive development tasks and projects. Knowledge of analytical tools and emerging analytic technologies to assess and implement for the appropriate use and desired outcomes. Such as IBM Watson or other cognitive tools
         Required Qualifications:
          Degree in Computer Science, Applied Statistics, Mathematics, Engineering, Physics or other quantitative discipline with specialization and experience in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, Cognitive Science or other related areas; with a minimum of five years of work experience.
          Strong mathematical background with strong knowledge in at least one of the following fields: Cognitive Science, Advanced Semantic Design, Information Extraction, Information Retrieval, Probabilistic Decision Making, image recognition, deep machine learning.
          Excellent verbal and written communication skills with the ability to work with diverse teams in a highly fluid environment and brief senior executives.
