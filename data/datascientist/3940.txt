          Responsibilities
           This position will be within the Data Science/Data Analytics Center of Excellence (DAC) team. You&#8217;ll be responsible for creating enhanced visualizations of complex analytics in support of strategic business efforts and will leverage cutting edge research and discovery to guide the Data Science Team in new areas of opportunity with a focus on supporting BI and Visualization.
           You will utilize advanced knowledge, especially in the areas of Marketing and Finance, including expert level Visualization experience, Data Science capabilities, including research, analytics. You will use strong interpersonal and cross department relation building abilities and presentation skills. You will help expand the exploratory, innovation, and research capabilities of the team in support of the DAC.
