          Presale Partner, Data Science &amp; Analytics-169374
            Description
          Analytics and data unleash the potential of great companies.
          Are you passionate about making companies smarter? Teradata provides end-to-end solutions and services in big data, data science, analytics and data warehousing enabling companies to become an analytics-driven business. We are looking for people who share our vision to join our team of associates around the globe.
          As the Partner, Data Scientist &amp; Analytics, you will&#160;be responsible for ensuring that the Analytics organization supports Teradata account teams to design, scope, sell and deliver high quality analytical solutions to our clients. The role will be responsible for managing the identification, creation, and maintenance of new data science offerings that Teradata can take to market across commercial and open source platforms and toolsets, delivered through on-premises, cloud, and as-a-service based offerings.
          Key Areas of Responsibility
          &#8226; Influence the sales of all data science consulting efforts in the Americas
          &#8226; Ensure the practice members builds relationships with clients by understanding their business challenges and formulates business solutions using analytics
          &#8226; Focused on driving Analytics/DS consulting and solution revenue for the practice.
          &#8226; Motivate, lead, and manage a team of data science consultants to help the ABC organization meet revenue goals and deliver high quality and high value solutions
          &#8226; Understands and promotes the business value associated with data warehouse solutions, big data, business intelligence, and data science.
          &#8226; Work with delivery leads to ensure that the data science team members deliver high value projects that are delivered accurately, on time, and to high standard expectations
          &#8226; Review complicated analyses to ensure the results identified will be relevant to solve client business problems for a successful problem resolution and business outcome
          &#8226; Provide thought leadership, knowledge transfer, technical and industry expertise to internal Teradata teams, strategic partners and clients
          &#8226; Understanding the various analytic techniques and technologies that can be applied to solving complex business problems
          &#8226; Foster an atmosphere of collaboration and innovation so that the organization can continue to develop new offerings that target the latest industry needs
          &#8226; Creates an atmosphere where client proposals are aggressively positioned and pursued following client meetings, which will be qualified in advance
            Qualifications
           &#8226; Ten years of relevant experience using data analytics and data science with demonstrable successes using quantitative approaches
           &#8226; Bachelor&#8217;s Degree or equivalent work experience
           &#8226; Ability to work cross-functionally in matrix organization where internal relationship building and credibility is critical
           &#8226; Ability to apply critical thinking to develop and solve business challenges.
           &#8226; Highly skilled in organizational politics, value proposition development, solution selling, focusing on the customer, sales ability / persuasiveness, and business needs analysis.
           &#8226; Substantial data analysis experience utilizing and coding in standard tools such as R, SAS, SPSS, Teradata Warehouse Miner and very strong SQL skills including complex query structures
           &#8226; In depth knowledge of standard algorithms such as linear regression, logistic regression, clustering, decision trees, and affinity analysis
           &#8226; Hands on experience with reporting and visualization tools such as Tableau, Qlik, Cognos, others
           &#8226; Knowledge of, and ideally use of, a wide range of common industry platforms including Teradata, Hadoop, Spark, and Teradata Aster
           &#8226; Exceptional consulting skills in communicating, establishing of strategic direction, influencing others, negotiating skills, operational decision making, problem solving, facilitating and managing meetings, and questioning and probing.
           &#8226; Strong written and verbal communication skills
           Preferred Skills:
           &#8226; Master&#8217;s Degree
           &#8226; Prior people management experience
           This position is working in a virtual office environment, including home office &amp; customer site. A combination of independent work and team collaboration will be required. This position will have travel up to 75%.
           Our total compensation approach includes a competitive base salary, 401(k), strong work/family programs, and medical, dental and disability coverage.
