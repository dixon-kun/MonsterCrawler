         Data Scientist/Intelligence Analyst, Senior
         Key Role:
         Work as a data scientist building rapid analytic methodologies and tools in support of solving highly technical analytic intelligence problems. Work in a fast&#8211;paced environment with tight deadlines and high levels of uncertainty. Work with analysts and intelligence data sets closely to find new and innovative means of generating analytic findings. Work with developers to automate and streamline the methodologies they create into deployable and scalable solutions. Develop data visualizations from various data sources and tool suites with an emphasis on simplicity and interoperability. Perform gap analysis and act as a key advisor for a new comprehensive data strategy for clients with an emphasis on providing analytic intelligence solutions.
         Basic Qualifications:
         -3+ years of experience in working with analysts closely or as an analyst
         -Experience with quantitative and qualitative research
         -Experience with Microsoft Excel and Access and R Studio, SAS, or JMP
         -Experience with prediction, forecasting, or anticipatory analytics and research
         -Knowledge of complex and multi&#8211;source quantitative analysis
         -Knowledge of one or more scripting language, including Python or R
         -Active TS/SCI clearance
         -BA or BS degree
         Additional Qualifications:
         -1+ years of experience with working in the intelligence community
         -1+ years of experience with one or more of the following communities: US law enforcement, military intelligence, or DoD
         -Experience with software development, i2 Analyst's Notebook, SQL, and Regular Expressions
         -Experience in working with NoSQL databases, including MongoDB and Accumulo
         -Possession of excellent oral and written communication skills
         -Possession of excellent analytic and problem solving skills
         -MA or MS degree
         Clearance:
         Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information; TS/SCI clearance is required.
