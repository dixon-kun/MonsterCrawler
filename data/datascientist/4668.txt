          At Wunderman, we are creatively driven and data inspired&#8212; it&#8217;s what sets us apart from other agencies. We are seeking savvy Project Managers who can direct our digital campaigns and lead the team&#8212;from start to finish. We need talent that can take something new&#8212;think project, client or agency opportunity&#8212;and translate it into a structured organized plan. This position is held by leaders who are experts at running complex projects that integrate multiple digital channels.
           Understands the strategy and ideas for a project to facilitate the organization of those concepts into a clear framework throughout the project life cycle
           Responsible for creating and managing scope, timelines, and tasks for concurrent projects of multiple clients
           Creates clear and detailed project timelines including project stakeholder roles defined
           Ensures requirements are synchronized within capabilities for given resources
           Leads effective client calls and/or internal meetings complete with agendas and documented next steps/key decisions
           Keeps project team members aligned on optimization efforts and approved budgets
           Manages deliverables of approved scope of work and ensures change orders are completed for unapproved work
           Understand, facilitate, and manage project dependencies and next steps; identify gaps and conflicts
           Responsible for ensuring communication and alignment for both internal and external teams throughout the life of the project
           Consults with management about status, risks or opportunities
           3 year minimum experience in digital marketing project management
           Strong ability to handle objections and quickly adapt to a new solution or path
           Understand people, groups and how to navigate organizations to get stuff done
           Proven time management, organization, and presentation skills
           Experience leading cross-functional teams
           Ability to translate and communicate data into actionable items
           Agency and/or consulting experience a plus
           PMP certification preferred
