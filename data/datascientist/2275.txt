        Senior Data Scientist &#8211; Commercial Business
        About Ten-X:
        Ten-X is changing real estate as we know it. Already the world's largest online real estate marketplace, we have facilitated $37B in transactions since 2007 and we're just getting started. The company's vision is to transform the way real estate is bought and sold by providing a more efficient and transparent process, using technology and best-in-class marketing to maximize the value of real estate assets in the sales process.
        The Role:
        Data and our ability to leverage it is seen and championed as a key competitive advantage from our CEO on down. We are looking for a top tier data scientist work with our data science team on building out proprietary tools and models around our customer and asset data (both internal and external sets)
        * Perform a variety of data and BI related tasks including:
        * Experience with ETL in both a SQL and Hadoop environment
        * Java Map Reduce Jobs in Hadoop
        * Algorithm development and implementation, in particular around fuzzy matching and NLP
        * Designing, developing, optimizing major high-volume big data, analytics &amp; business intelligence systems
        * Developing, evolving current &amp; new data-related scripting, automation and other processes
        Skills
        * Strong domain knowledge in the latest analytics technologies
        * Good technical skills in this area including:
        * Ability to understand business problems and translate them into technical requirements
        * Strong oral and written communication skills and be able to communicate complex technical knowledge in layman's terms
        * Undergraduate degree (and ideally a Masters) in Computer Science from a top tier University
        * Hadoop and all the related stack (Pig, Hive, HBase, etc.)
        * SQL skills and SQL Databases
        * Strong knowledge of all traditional Data Warehouse-related components (Sourcing, ETL, Data Modeling, Infrastructure, BI, Reporting) and multiple tools to support those components
        * Algorithm development, fuzzy matching, NLP
        Bonus points for experience and/or passion in:
        * Screen Scraping
        * CRM
        * Clickstream data
        * Third Party Data Sets
        * Commercial Real Estate
        * LI-MH1
