          About the Job
            Are you a Data Engineer with etl, tableau, redshift, and star schema experience? Do you want to be a part of an extremely well funded start-up? If so, read on...
            Based in midtown Manhattan, we are on a mission is to build data solutions to serve a broad set of digital properties. We have 65 million unique visitors every month. You'll spend your time helping us learn how to move, manipulate, and extract value from our data across distributed systems at an ever-increasing scale. You'll work closely with Data Scientists to help uncover and build data features that power machine learning models and learn hard-won techniques for avoiding trouble from our experienced team. You'll also contribute to designing and discovering new best-practices in our rapidly expanding field.
            Top Reasons to Work with Us
            - Great pay ($100k - $120k DOE)
            - Well funded and fast growing company
            - Full benefits
            - Great company culture
            - Unlimited room for growth
            - PTO
            - Excellent Glass Door rating
            What You Will Be Doing
            - Lead efforts to create/maintain system engineering
            - Data warehouse schemas
            - Redshift data Warehouse/ ETL
            - Tableau administration
            - Data quality
            What You Need for this Position
            2+ years professional data engineering experience
            - Experience with Tableau, ETL, Star Schema, and Redshift
            - Backend experience
            - Hadoop is a plus
            So, if you are a Data Engineer with experience, please apply today!
