          The Integrated Producer will have a strong conceptual understanding of the latest marketing platforms and technologies.            The Integrated Producer is the leader/coordinator for the client and coordinates&#160;with strategists, designers, creative, tech, studio, account and executive management in determining project scope, estimating, planning, executing and monitoring assigned projects and deliverables
