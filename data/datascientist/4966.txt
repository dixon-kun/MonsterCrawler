           The Vice President of Marketing Analytics will lead a team responsible for analysis and optimization of marketing activities across MGM Resorts International. The Vice President will build and lead a team of experienced marketing analysts and data scientists to drive insights and recommendations across MGM&#8217;s acquisition and retention marketing platforms which include digital media, loyalty, and direct marketing. The Vice President will also be responsible for gaining a greater understanding of our customer&#8217;s journey to ensure marketing efforts optimize EBITDA performance by driving the right offers to the right customers.
            Oversee a team that provides marketing strategy insights and recommendations to optimize the acquisition and retention of customers.
            Partner with casino and direct marketing departments to analyze and inform player reinvestment strategies and return on reinvestment.
            Collaborate with the loyalty marketing department to understand trends in membership and behaviors, including identifying effectiveness of program to various market segments.
            Develop methodology to measure and improve the effectiveness of media spend across multiple touchpoints, brands and business units through the implementation and governance around attribution.
            Work with eCommerce team to identify trends and insights impacting website conversion.
            Collaborate with Digital Marketing and Business Intelligence to establish and prioritize data collection needs and consumption methods required for big data analytics across customer databases, cloud solutions, etc.
            Build rapport with business area leads to understand their business problems and analytical needs
            Communicate and review findings with business partners and senior leadership.
            Demonstrate and foster an environment that promotes a high performing team and is focused on our values of leadership, mutual respect, engagement, self-led skills development and can-do attitude.
            Define and set standards with EA and across business areas
            Facilitate learning and skills development opportunities across the EA team
            Network externally to seek innovation
            Strategically identify other synergies and opportunities within the organization, including inter-department collaborations, outside of current team scope where opportunity for high-impact exists
            Perform other job related duties as requested.
            Required:
            Bachelor&#8217;s Degree in Marketing, Applied Mathematics, Industrial Engineering or related field.
            At least 12 years of experience in Marketing Analytics with expertise in digital marketing, loyalty programs and direct marketing.
            Strong leadership skills - ability to foster a high performing, engaged team of professionals focused on driving value for the department and MGM as a whole.
            Knowledge of data structures, systems and BI tools related to marketing data management.
            Strategic view and ability to execute on quality data enabling EA and business goals.
            Able to use influence to ensure involvement and alignment.
            Excellent customer service skills.
            Able to lead and mentor a team.
            Have interpersonal skills to deal effectively with all business contacts.
            Professional appearance and demeanor.
            Work varied shifts, including weekends and holidays.
            Able to effectively communicate in English, in both written and oral forms.
            Preferred:
            Master&#8217;s degree in Marketing, Applied Mathematics, Industrial Engineering or related field.
            Previous experience working in a similar resort setting.
