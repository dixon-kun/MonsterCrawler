         Overview:
            The Data Visualization Architect role is to design the most effective way for the customer to understand their large, complex sets of data through visual representation.
            The design must visually present the information in a way that effectively answers the customers questions and thus drives efficient fact based decision making.
            The role includes working closely with the Customer, the ETL Engineer and the Data Scientist to implement a solution via &#160;Business Intelligence software and systems, including integration with databases and data marts. This involves gathering requirements, designing, documenting, and rolling out BI solutions to end users. This also includes end user training and support.
            The Data Visualization Architect will also assist in ensuring high levels of BI availability through support functions and in-depth testing.
         Responsibilities:
             Analyze &#8211; Work with stakeholders to gather requirements/processes in order to document organization needs and opportunities.
             Design &#8211; Create design documentation mocking up expected end user experience for dashboards, reports, and ad-hoc consumption of business intelligence solutions.
             Development &#8211; Work with BI and ETL developers to explain, suggest, and assist with development of components required.
             Test &#8211; Write and execute basic unit tests for business intelligence objects.
             Train &#8211; Work with end users to get user acceptance testing and training on new and existing components.
         Qualifications:
             2-3 years experience working with real data.
             Experience creating Tableau dashboards using Tableau Desktop.
             Read, write and speak fluent English; fluent in host country language required.
             BS or BA Degree.&#160; Statistics, Operations Research, Computer Science, Engineering, Economics, or other quantitative discipline strongly preferred.
            Preferred Qualifications:
             Experience with Tableau Desktop and Server
             Experience with in-memory solutions
             Experience with Oracle, and/or MS SQL
             Familiar with designing and developing dashboards and reports for mobile consumption.
             Familiar with web design and development concepts.
             Familiar with ETL (Extract/Transform/Load) activities.
             Experience with a tier-one business intelligence suite and the ability to learn new tools.
             Understanding of relational database structures, theories, principles, and practices.
             Expert computer skills and knowledge of standard programs including, but not limited to: Microsoft Office applications and Business Intelligence software tools and systems.
             Ability to analyze user requirements and design business intelligence solutions including reports, dashboards, and ad-hoc consumption.
             To qualify, applicants must be legally authorized to work in the United States, and should not require, now or in the future, sponsorship for employment visa status.
             PRA is an EEO/AA employer and is committed to providing opportunities to minorities, women, veterans and individuals with disabilities.
