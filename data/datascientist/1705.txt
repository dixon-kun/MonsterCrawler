        BitVoyant &#8211; Data Scientist
BitVoyant produces business and network intelligence, enabling companies to intimately understand their interactions and risks in real-time and at Internet scale.  We&#8217;re growing our team to create, enrich, and mine this intelligence for insights that matter to our customers.
As a Data Scientist (Big Data), you will apply cutting-edge statistical and mathematical methods to the collection, correlation and analysis of large structured and unstructured data sets from a broad, ever-evolving set of sources. The successful applicant will apply industry knowledge, contextual understanding, and innovative insights into the development of novel analytical techniques and tools in response to urgent, complex questions.  The ideal candidate will:
Responsibilities:
&#183;       Provide valuable insights into new data sets quickly
&#183;       Mine large amounts of data for insights with both automated and ad-hoc queries
&#183;       Communicate complex findings and ideas in plain language that is friendly to business and operational audiences
&#183;       Problem solve &#8211; turn requirements into solutions
&#183;       Collaborate with team members with varying technical and non-technical backgrounds towards a shared goal
&#183;       Rapidly research and iterate on analysis methods by finding elegant, reliable and understandable techniques to build analytics that matter
&#183;       Create technical assessments and develop custom approaches in response to time-critical urgent customer needs
&#183;       Employ sophisticated analytics programs, machine learning, and statistical methods to prepare data for use in predictive and prescriptive             modeling
&#183;       Examine new and more convincing methods for data reporting, visualization &amp; presentation
&#183;       Develop new approaches to apply large-scale computing technology to solve customer problems
&#183;       Be involved in all stages of analytic development from idea formation through prototyping, automation, and productizing
&#183;       Maintain up-to-date knowledge of technology standards, industry trends, emerging techniques, and analytic best practices
&#183;       Keep informed of evolution and impact of adversary tactics and strategies
&#183;       Ensure analytical issues are quickly resolved and help implement strategies and solutions to reduce the likelihood of reoccurrence
