        The Manager, Luxury &amp; Truck Vehicle Valuation Operations is responsible for leading the effort to determine the valuation of all used, new, and Cost to Own values across the Truck &amp; Luxury vehicle segments. This includes leading a group of highly motivated Analysts and Senior Analysts that are responsible for vehicle valuations of these segments at KBB. In order to successfully fulfill these responsibilities, the Manager will lead the team in analyzing large datasets and determine appropriate action as it relates to valuation adjustments. In addition, the Manager, Luxury &amp; Truck Vehicle Valuation Operations will be responsible for ad hoc projects working with internal and external clients and partners.
        Key Responsibilities and Essential Functions:
        * Lead a team of highly motivated Valuation Analysts for the Truck &amp; Luxury segments.
        * Collaborate with both internal and external clients to educate the market on KBB values
        * Lead, Develop, and mentor Analysts and Senior Analysts on Truck &amp; Luxury Vehicle Valuations
        * Supervise/Manage/Direct the selection, training, development, and work assignments of personnel
        * Stay current on the automotive industry as a whole, e.g. manufacturer pricing in the new and used car market.
        * Performs all other related duties as assigned
        * Ad hoc reporting projects
        * Analyze weekly bias reports to determine areas of focus for week
        Required
        * Bachelor's Degree in Business, Communications or other related field
        * 4 to 6 years related experience and/or training; or equivalent combination of education and experience
        * Prior automotive experience preferred
        * Strong mathematical/quantitative knowledge to engage with Statisticians and Data Scientists
        * Ability to define problems, collect data, establish facts, and draw valid conclusions.
        Preferred
        * Master's Degree in Business, Communications or equivalent combination of education and experience
        * Ability to query large datasets in SQL and other software programs
        * Automotive industry experience
