        Job Description
          The Senior Data Scientist is responsible for creating and maintaining data infrastructure for gathering multiple sources of data into a single data hub for the generation of research insights. Directs the company's measurement of its customer experience with all benefits and services. Takes a lead role in ensuring the integrity and structure of data created by new technologies like CRM. Carries out data analysis that informs strategic decision making by senior management. Presents analysis to various constituencies (senior management and client stakeholders).
