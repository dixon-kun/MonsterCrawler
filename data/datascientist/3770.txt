        Senior Product Manager, Measurement - Data Cloud
         Preferred Qualifications
           Position Title: &#160;Product Manager, Measurement
         Oracle Data Cloud Measurement, DLX ROI
         We&#8217;re looking for a super smart, highly motivated product manager to own and drive requirements across our next generation advertising measurement product portfolio. Since acquiring Datalogix, the Oracle Data Cloud delivers Measurement products to a wide variety of customers in the Automotive, Consumer Packaged Goods, and Retail sectors. Effective marketers realize that good data-driven marketing requires the ability to close the loop and understand the how their marketing efforts actually drove sales and how to improve those efforts. The ODC Measurement solution leads the market with a suite of innovative measurement products that allow our customers to fully understand the impact to their business of their addressable media.
           Department Description:
         Measurement is all about giving marketers the power to understand how well their ad budget is being spent and how to spend it better the next time. We do this by connecting actual offline purchase behavior to the households exposed to digital ads and accurately measuring the lift in spend an ad drives. This is a new and innovative space and that energy pervades the work we do every day. If you like the idea of matching wits and building solutions with the best and brightest in social media and advertising&#8212;Facebook, Google, P&amp;G or Chrysler&#8212;this is the right place for you. We are grounded in the needs of our end customers and make decisions that will help make the product more valuable to them.
           Job Responsibilities:
         As a Product Manager you will engage with a very smart, cross-disciplinary team to conceive, design and implement the ODC measurement strategy from day one, covering all bases from product workflows, new product features, methodologies and new deliverables. You will ensure our needs are being met across our tech, analytics and publisher partners, with best-in-class results being generated for our internal sales teams and Measurement customers. You are responsible for defining the priorities and optimizing your roadmap for the biggest wins. You must be responsive, flexible and able to succeed in an open collaborative peer environment. Fundamentally, you will be responsible for ensuring that new ODC measurement products mirror our leadership in addressable media measurement.
         As a member in the product development division, you will define product roadmap and set development priorities. You will gather and analyze information to define product specifications and review design specifications.&#160;&#160;It&#8217;s your responsibility to communicate the product strategy and functionality to both internal and external teams. And, of course, you ensure successful product releases based on corporate priorities.
