             Lead Data Scientist within our Human Resources department.&#160; Please see the description below and let me know if this is something you think you could fill?
             We are looking for a dynamic and results-driven Lead Data Scientist with 7+ years of experience to join the HR Business Intelligence &amp; Advanced Analytics team.&#160; This individual will be
             responsible for providing a strong data modeling capability to the Human Resources Department.

Through application of advanced analytics and visualization, this person will identify insights and aid in the development of compelling story-telling to make strategic recommendations to senior HR management.
