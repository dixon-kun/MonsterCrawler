        The Data Integration Strategy Manager is responsible for coming up with the Data and Integration strategies for IHG's revenue management (RMS), central reservations (CRS), and property based systems (PBS), products and tools. The Manager will develop functional requirements related to Data and Integration delivery &amp; harmonization across those three product categories. She or he will provide inputs into the overall product roadmaps as they related to Data. He or She is responsible for engaging with various stakeholders &#8211; internal and external as well as different business functions (Sales, Marketing, Distribution, Loyalty, Brands along with Global and Regional commercial and operational teams) to build out and socialize Data needs, standards, roadmap and strategy to integrate them from the product perspective. He or She is also responsible for engaging with IHG Global Technology to ensure that the functional requirements are delivered to plan in a cost effective manner. He or she will represent the Global Revenue and Property Based systems team within the broader IHG Data &amp; Analytics governance model to ensure alignment with global priorities.
        Essential Duties and Responsibilities &#8211;
        * Direct overall strategy Data and Integration needs behind PBS, CRS, RMS, Data Warehousing from a hotel revenue &amp; guest service delivery perspective.Direct data platform lifecycle from planning to tactical activity.
        * Develop and maintain relationships with external data vendors like Amadeus, PMS Vendors, TravelClick, RateGain etc.
        * Participate in the strategy development around engaging with external vendors regarding data.
        * Act as an advisor to subordinate managers or staff to help meet established schedules and/or resolve technical or operational problems.
        Education &#8211;
        Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or an equivalent combination of education and experience.
        Experience &#8211;
        7 years progressive work related experience with demonstrated proficiency in multiple disciplines/technologies/processes related to the position as well as 3 to 5 years managing teams and/or significant complex projects. 8 or more years' experience in the area of implementing Data analysis and Integration based solutions within the travel/Retail/hospitality industry.
        Technical Skills and Knowledge &#8211;
        * Demonstrated knowledge and proficiency of Structured Query Language (SQL), relational database management systems (RDBMS) SAS, R, C/C , Java, Hadoop and modern n-tier designs.
        * Demonstrate project management experience in organizing, planning and executing large-scale technically complex projects from conception through implementation.
        * Demonstrate experience in leading and developing people.
        * Demonstrated knowledge in Data Discovery, Data Analysis, and Providing Data Insights (Data Scientist)
        * Demonstrates effective verbal and written communications skills for the purpose of providing information for clients, vendors, and staff as well as communicating technical information to non-technical audiences.
        * Ability to understand the data from a business perspective and build data enabled decision support systems
