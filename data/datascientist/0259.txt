          About the Company
          PlaceIQ is a powerful, location-based audience and insights platform that organizes a wide variety of consumer activity data around a precise location base map at massive scale. PlaceIQ uses its detailed understanding of location and consumer activity to reach a targeted audience, and also to derive powerful insights about consumer behavior to inform market and business strategies for national brands. The company is headquartered in New York City&#160;and has offices in Palo Alto,&#160;Chicago, and&#160;Detroit.
          The Role:
         Having already assembled an exceptionally skilled, diverse and passionate team of engineers and data scientists, we are looking for experienced, full-stack engineers to join our world-class team building the PlaceIQ Location Analytics Platform. Our technology teams thrive on huge challenges and can readily and continuously deliver production code in a fast-paced, agile environment.&#160; If you have a passion for imagining and building technology solutions that will make an immediate impact in the rapidly growing field of location analytics, we want to talk to you!
          Minimum Requirements:
          BS/MS/PhD in Computer Science or related quantitative field
          3-5 years of proven development experience with Java, Scala or C++
          Familiarity with high-scale design and implementation
          Experience with tools in the Hadoop ecosystem
          Preferred Qualifications:
          Strong background in applied mathematics or statistics
          Extensive experience with Scalding, Spark, HBase or Cassandra
