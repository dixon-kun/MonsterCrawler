        Job Description
          As a Senior Data Scientist, you will work as a member of an Analytics Project Team to generate high-value business insights on transformational, analytical projects. In addition, you will:
          &#8226;Work with multiple large, complex, diverse data sets and perform appropriate analytical methodology to provide insights and decision modeling for the specified projects.
          &#8226;Create and implement algorithms which manage the data to enable it to be analyzed in an efficient manner and to enable repeatability.
          &#8226;Create and develop new data and analytic applications through research, modeling and advanced analytics.
          &#8226;Lead the identification of the required data sources.
          &#8226;Lead the working relationship with the relevant business area. Lead the communication of derived insights in a manner easily understandable by business partners, especially through appropriate visualization techniques.
          &#8226;Proactively stay up-to-date with the latest appropriate analytical techniques and recommend these where needed, especially from academia.
          &#8226;Explain implementation and usage in business terms. Work closely with the Data Infrastructure teams to ensure data is modeled efficiently and in such a way to facilitate the required analytics.
          &#8226;Proactively apply analytical techniques to prospect for business insights and find patterns in data which could be valuable for the business.
          &#8226;Act as technical specialist for IT Domain management for all matters concerning analytical techniques.
          &#8226;Play a lead role in assessing third party analytical offerings.
          &#8226;Play a lead role in advancing the business transformation of the Enterprise through analytics, for example through discussions with the Analytics Leadership Team (ALT).
        Qualifications
          The skills and attributes you possess include:
          &#8226;5  years of experience applying statistical analyses and analytical methodologies on advanced analytics projects, with the ability to demonstrate the development of algorithms and predictive models to solve business issues.
          &#8226;2  years of experience working on cross-functional/divisional projects.
          &#8226;Experience with current analytical platforms and be able to utilize these fully independently.
          &#8226;Highly proficient command of the programming languages SQL, SAS, R, Python, Spark
          &#8226;Theoretical knowledge and practical experience in statistical and analytical techniques together with the development of predictive models and machine learning algorithms.
          &#8226;Working knowledge of Heavy Machinery product lines and the types of data available for each
          &#8226;Knowledge of the manufacturing processes as well as Marketing and Customer Support processes
          &#8226;Ability to communicate complex analytical insights in a manner which is clearly understandable by the business partners.
          &#8226;Experience with and ability to integrate unstructured data sets.
          &#8226;Experience with Machine Learning techniques
          &#8226;Experience with natural language processing
          &#8226; Experience in Big data solutions, such as Hadoop, AWS
          Education:
          - Ideally, you will have a Master&#8217;s Degree in a relevant study area such as mathematics, statistics or advanced analytics
