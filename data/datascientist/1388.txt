             Organizational Overview
           At DST Financial Services we believe that in order to better service our clients it&#8217;s through our use of data. In our Australia, United Kingdom, and United States offices we provide services to mutual funds, brokerage firms, retirement plans, wealth management, and other financial services. Everyday our global offices work together to master the complexity of the financial industry to transform the most challenging tasks into opportunity and competitive advantage.
             Role Overview
           We are currently hiring a Machine Learning Scientist to join our Applied Analytics division, product development team.&#160; A key focus for the Applied Analytics division has been the creation of a data repository, freeing over 6 petabytes of data from DST&#8217;s traditional mainframe environment and housing it within a big data stack. This approach enables the development of industry focused products, industry research and client specific solutions within a scalable environment decreasing time to market. Supporting the new platform is a robust data governance and data management methodology, incorporating best practices in anonymization and entity level management.
           Our product development team focuses on combining benchmarking, segmentation, predictive models and recommender systems to create scalable, repeatable products that allow clients to leverage our analytic capabilities.
             Responsibilities
           Machine Learning Scientists contribute directly to the features and capabilities deployed in our applications. They are responsible for advanced project delivery, execution, and support. They work closely with cross-functional business units, software engineering, and data scientists to simulate and test econometric/probabilistic relationships across the big data stack. They also work with product and marketing teams to understand client goals and turn research into products.
