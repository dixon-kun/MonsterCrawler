        Senior Data Scientist
        The internet is experiencing a transformation &#8211; ad blindness and blocking are forcing media companies and advertisers to rethink the way they connect with consumers. This evolution is changing the way we consume content and how we interact with brands. Nativo is enabling this shift to advertising that is mindful of the user experience, and is focusing on user attention and engagement - not just clicks and traffic. Our technology is digital advertising 2.0, and we pride ourselves on being thought leaders in the space as well as technology leaders utilizing ad technology that is not available anywhere else.
        The Nativo platform is used everyday by thousands of publications across billions of pageviews, by some of the largest and most important media companies in the world, who power their native programs and engage consumers with content rather than interruptive ads. Our main focus is to make elegant software that is both powerful and enjoyable to use.
        The Data Scientist will be part of a team responsible for designing and improving of our big data &amp; data-science infrastructure. The team is also responsible for providing various advanced optimization technology &amp; targeting solutions for large-scale problems in other areas of our system. We process over a billion events daily and the amount of data we process is growing rapidly. We believe in being smart, innovative, pragmatic, polished, and efficient and are looking for people that fit those values. This role reports to our Director of Data Science in Engineering.
        What you'll do:
        * Develop various predictive models to help solve various big data problems
        * Adapt &amp; improve various parts of the Nativo's big data infrastructure/stack to handle the above models
        * Work with various team members to help improve areas which deal with high volume low latency problems, using various statistical/mathematical models
        * Research on the latest &amp; greatest data&#8211;science techniques such as Deep Learning technology, XGBoost etc in order to improve our data &#8211; science engines.
        What you'll need:
        * Good background and experience with statistical and other advanced math problem solving
        * Proven experience writing advanced algorithms on top of Big Data infrastructure
        * Knowledge in a variety of non-SQL big data open source products such as Spark, EMR/Hadoop etc.
        * Programming experience, ideally in Python, Scala, Java or R, but we are open to other experience if you're willing to learn the languages we use
        * Good understanding of Matrix Factorization, Gradient Boosting, Random Forest, Batch/Stochastic Gradient Descent, Neural Network, SVM etc.
        * Good understanding of evaluation metrics such as F1 scores, AUC, ROC, confusion matrix, logloss etc.
        * Knowledge of machine learning libraries and such as Vowpal Wabbit, H2O, Tensorflow, Theano, Keras or xgboost and ability to apply machine learning at scale.
        * Knowledge of natural language processing
        * Ability to operate in an agile, entrepreneurial start-up environment
        * Excellent teamwork skills with an ability to reach out and use team strengths to get the work done with minimal supervision
        * Good communication skills
        * You must be motivated, driven, and passionate about programming and technology
        What we offer:
        At Nativo, you make an immediate impact. Nativo is made up of smart, talented and driven people looking for other potential team members with the same attitude of innovation and excellence. We offer incredible opportunities to learn and work on projects that transform digital advertising. We offer a competitive compensation and benefits package that includes stock options, health coverage, employer-matched 401k, cutting edge work, and the opportunity to join a rapidly growing startup with a proven product
