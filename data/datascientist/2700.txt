         Job Description
          Recorded Future is organizing the web for analysis. We help people defend against cyber attacks by gathering and organizing information from the open web, deep web, and dark web. Our harvesting pipeline reads over 700,000 web sources, and our real-time multilingual natural language processing technology takes that content from raw text to alerts and visualizations in minutes. The data science team takes ownership of this unique dataset. We implement crucial harvesting capabilities, manipulate our data structures to obtain solutions and insights, and explore new use cases and capabilities for the data.
          You write code that loves big data. You&#8217;re prepared to respond quickly in a fast-moving data universe, developing custom tools and techniques for interacting with the data, collaborating with our engineers on our analytical signals and infrastructure, and rapidly iterating prototypes for custom data-driven applications.
