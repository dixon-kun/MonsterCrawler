         Job Description
          Job Description:
          Lead the development and execution of software architecture and supporting software modules that lead to a big car data analytics product ready for deployment into series production systems, accompanied by the needed tools to support data collection and analysis. The ideal candidate will have expertise in big data systems as well as ADAS systems, cloud based processing and telematics. The individual should also be comfortable in a small team &#8220;start-up&#8221; environment where diversity of skills is a strong benefit.
           Job Responsibilities
           :
           &#8226; This person will be responsible for Big Car Data Architecture and Software development. Specific tasks include:
           &#8226; Architect and develop big data analytics software. Effort includes collecting car data and architecting a big data lake cloud architecture to derive business intelligence for the automotive industry
           &#8226; Develop software modules to support integration into multiple possible client systems, emphasizing portability and low impact on embedded resources
           &#8226; Introduce an event based language that helps collects detailed data in an efficient manner
           &#8226; Define, architect and implement a streaming cloud architecture that can scale to millions of sources and terabytes of data collected every day
           &#8226; Define, architect and implement indexing and reporting mechanisms to detect outliers, system failures and rare network effects
           &#8226; Lead and coordinate technical activities across global locations both within and external.
        Qualifications
          Basic Qualifications:
          &#8226; Bachelor&#8217;s Degree or higher in Computer Science, Mathematics or similar
          &#8226; More than 10 years professional experience with Java and relational databases; must have used Hadoop, Spark, Mesos or similar
          &#8226; At least 5 years of big data analysis professional experience; must have used Python, R, Pig, Hive or similar
          &#8226; Experience with streaming protocols such as Kafka, Flume, MQTT or similar
          &#8226; Experience with developing business intelligence reports (Tableau, Qlic or similar)
          &#8226; Experience with video, radar, lidar capture and compression techniques
          &#8226; Ability to work effectively within a matrix environment
          Preferred Qualifications &amp; Desired Competencies:
          &#8226; Experience in car data is highly desirable
          &#8226; Working knowledge of deep learning algorithms and models
          &#8226; Experience in vehicle system design
          &#8226; Working knowledge of CNN, machine learning to interface with data scientists effectively
          &#8226; Working knowledge of ASPICE compliant processes
          &#8226; Experience in developing embedded software in a vehicle environment
          &#8226; Understanding of Implementation of functional safety elements as required to support product safety goals
        Additional Information
         All your information will be kept confidential according to EEO guidelines.
