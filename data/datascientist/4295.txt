         This position will help enable and grow the Customer's nascent content analysis effort. We are looking for an individual with technical and data science expertise who can assist the Customer in building a program to analyze and make sense of rich unstructured text datasets in response to new requirements.
         Bachelor's Degree in a related technical discipline, or the equivalent combination of education, professional training, or work experience.
         Desired Skills:
          &#8226; Demonstrated programming experience with Java and Python.
          &#8226; Demonstrated experience working with COTS and/or customer-developed Natural Language Processing (NLP) and text analytics/content analysis tools/algorithms.
          &#8226; Demonstrated experience with one or more of the following: XML, JSON, or related data formats.
          &#8226; Demonstrated experience with principles of distributed computing and/or distributed databases and hands on SQL/NOSQL experience with schema design and query development (e.g. Spark, Hadoop, Hive, Pig, etc).
          &#8226; Demonstrated experience with principles of distributed computing and/or distributed databases, and hands-on SQL/NOSQL experience with schema design and query development (e.g. Hadoop, Hive, Pig, HBase, and other columnar databases).
          &#8226; Knowledge of data manipulation, ETL, data storage (Hadoop/HDFS), documentation (Git).
          &#8226; Familiarity with C, R, HTML, JavaScript, and/or related languages.
          &#8226; Familiarity with machine learning.
          &#8226; Familiarity with LSI and LDA.
          &#8226; Familiarity with statistical and sentiment analysis.
          &#8226; Familiarity with Customer's security and accreditation processes.
          &#8226; Familiarity with Customer's enterprise data layer, unstructured formats, and contents.
         8-10 years of related experience.
