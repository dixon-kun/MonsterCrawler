        Ever wanted to work with the latest Microsoft devices and services technologies and partner with the world's top developers? &amp;nbsp Our partners are building amazing applications and we are looking for a Data Scientist with strong analytical and developer skills to join the DX Technical Team with the objective to drive adoption and development of industry leading applications and services across devices by transforming data from our customers/partners into deep insights. &amp;nbsp
        The most successful candidate will have years of experience analyzing data from a wide range of datasets and across a breadth of technology platforms. They are driven by the desire to solve problems for partners whether by leveraging sophisticated custom machine learning models or straightforward data analysis. They enjoy rapid and changing projects and have strong analytical skills, technical aptitude and a collaborative work style. We are looking for people who see challenges as opportunities and people who can look at complex problems and are able to provide actionable insights and informed decisions. &amp;nbsp As part of the role, the candidate will need to work closely with other engineering groups, provide partner feedback, and influence the roadmap for future product releases. The candidate will also engage with partners to chart their applications' architectural direction and develop the resulting solutions. Based on their deep expertise developing this software, the candidate will present at top industry events. &amp;nbsp
        This candidate will be responsible for: &amp;nbsp &amp;nbsp
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Apply machine learning and predictive modeling techniques across the business.
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Developing commercially available solutions using OSS, Windows, Azure, Bing, and Office technologies.
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Technical subject matter expertise in OSS and Microsoft technologies to build code and other technical content to support developer events, architecture design sessions, and resolve issues. &amp;nbsp
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Providing subject matter expertise and leadership to Microsoft teams worldwide.
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Working closely with Microsoft teams to influence the future product roadmap.
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Developing and driving new ways of thinking across groups within the division to improve quality, engineering productivity, and responsiveness to feedback and changing priorities.
        Requirements: &amp;nbsp &amp;nbsp
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Expertise in machine learning with a broad understanding of supervised and unsupervised learning methods.
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Strong algorithmic background.
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Deep knowledge of applied statistics, data-mining strategies and ability to prototype solutions quickly.
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Excellent analytical and problem solving skills, with hands-on experience applying it to big data and the ability to translate insights to executive level audiences.
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp A deep understanding of cloud computing technologies, and emerging trends.
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Great communication and presentation skills &amp;nbsp Broad functional knowledge base to solve complex customer problems.
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp A sense of commitment to end-to-end product or service quality, completeness and the resulting user experience for the life of products and services.
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Strong knowledge of application development practices and a track record of working closely with large development team.
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp 8+ years of machine learning, business intelligence and quantitative analysis experience.
