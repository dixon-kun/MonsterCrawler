          Cloudera is looking for an software engineer to join a team focused on accelerating data science productivity on Cloudera&#8217;s modern platform for machine learning and advanced analytics. &#160;You will be responsible for helping design, build, scale, and deliver a world-class experience for data scientists and data engineers tackling some of the world&#8217;s biggest problems.
          Over the past few years, Cloudera has experienced tremendous growth, making us the leading contributor to the Hadoop ecosystem and a leading provider of enterprise solutions for machine learning and advanced analytics at scale. The purpose of this team is to accelerate Cloudera&#8217;s next stage of growth by enabling data scientists and data engineers to do more with data from exploration to production.
          Responsibilities
           Designing and building application features to accelerate&#160;data science productivity.
           Writing and contributing to data science libraries and frameworks.
           Evaluating new tools and technologies for incorporation into our platform.
           Working with the open source community to improve data science tools.
           Working with software engineers and data scientists across the company to build an integrated experience for machine learning and analytics.
          Minimum Requirements
           At least four years of software engineering experience.
           Fluency in two or more of the following: Python, R, Scala, Java, Go, or JavaScript.
           Experience with applied data science and data science tools.
           Experience with modern web applications (e.g. AngularJS, React).
           Strong sense of ownership and craftsmanship.
           Strong written and verbal communication skills and collaborative mindset.
           Experience with Spark and Hadoop a strong plus.
           Experience with containerization (e.g. Docker) a plus.
           A track record of contributing to open source data science tools a plus.
