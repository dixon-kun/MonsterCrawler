        What a Healthcare Data Scientist is at Fuse
        We are looking for people with analytical background, coding abilities, data science experience and (preferably) health-care related experience to drive analytics and statistical relevance for our products and solutions at Fuse by Cardinal Health
        Requirements:
        MS or PhD in a quantitative discipline: Computer Science, Statistics, Applied Mathematics, Operations Research, Engineering.
        Ability to thrive and demonstrate constant applied learning in highly complex, interdisciplinary, and dynamic work environment
        Expert in analyzing large complex, multi-dimensional data sets with a variety of tools
        Experience working with large SQL data models.
        Quantitative background and degree(s) in majors such as Computer Science, Engineering, Science, Mathematics or similar.
        Willingness to engage in necessary data wrangling.
        Will use technologies from Machine Learning. Background in Machine Learning and strong interest in further developing expertise.
        Strong programming experience in R, Python, Java or equivalent
        Experience with Unix.
        Preferred:
        Working knowledge of health care systems and healthcare terminology.
