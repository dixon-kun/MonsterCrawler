        Trivia Writer &amp; Research Resident
        BuzzFeed is looking for a writer with a substantial background in research to produce trivia-oriented posts. The role would involve taking assignments to build posts in particular subject areas &#8211; history, science, religion, sports, pop culture &#8211; but would also allow the writer to pitch their own ideas and pursue specific personal interests. The ideal candidate would have an unquenchable thirst for knowledge and excellent research skills, plus a strong interest in entertaining an audience.
        Responsibilities:
        * Research and produce fact posts and trivia quizzes on a wide range of topics
        * Assist other writers by suggesting efficient research methods
        Requirements:
        * Extensive experience in research and/or library science
        * A good sense of what makes for an interesting/challenging/fun trivia quiz
        * A wide range of interests and an ability to adapt writing style for different audiences
        * A positive, curious, playful disposition
        Interested candidates should include the following when applying:
        * A few short paragraphs explaining why you'd be a good fit for this particular position. Please share your background in research and interest in trivia.
        * A resume.
        * Links to published work.
        * Links to your social media accounts such as a Twitter, Tumblr, Pinterest, or personal blog.
        About BuzzFeed
        BuzzFeed is the leading independent digital media and tech company delivering news and entertainment content to a global audience. We have offices in 19 cities around the world and more than 1500 employees including reporters, video producers, data scientists, engineers, brand strategists, and more. We fuse hard work and fun, through a culture of experimentation, teamwork, equality, and humble confidence. As an employee, you'll enjoy perks like office events, snacks, career development courses, and no work on your birthday! But most importantly, you'll work with inspiring colleagues to build tools and create content that helps connect people all over the world.
