        About Us
We are a growing company with a solid customer base, excellent compensation and benefits, and a collaborative yet flexible work environment.  If you enjoy challenging projects, using new technologies to deliver innovative business solutions, and you're interested in working for an entrepreneurial company, we may have a home for you.
PTP is looking for a Data Scientist to join our Customer Experience practice.  At PTP, we value aptitude and creativity as well as experience. We are a diverse organization and are looking for bright, passionate and committed professionals who strive to be the best at what they do.
Responsibilities
   &#8226; Use machine learning techniques to create scalable solutions for business problems
   &#8226; Analyze and extract key insights from rich stores of customer data
   &#8226; Implement highly innovative models for predictive learning for applications like recommendations and targeting
   &#8226; Establish scalable, efficient, automated processes for large scale data analyses, model development, model validation and model implementation
   &#8226; Research and implement novel machine learning algorithms for new business problems
Requirements
   &#8226; PhD. in Computer Science with a focus on machine learning
   &#8226; Track record of having developed novel machine learning algorithms, SIGKDD/ICML/NIPS
   &#8226; Hands-on experience in predictive modeling and analysis over large volumes of data
   &#8226; Strong problem solving ability
   &#8226; Strong programming skills in C/C++  or Java
   &#8226; Experience with R/SAS/SPSS
   &#8226; Experience with SQL and MPP databases
   &#8226; Experience with large scale distributed programming  paradigms &#8211; experience with the Hadoop stack(HDFS/MR/Pig/Hive)
   &#8226; Familiarity with AWS and other cloud-based technologies that can be used with large mapReduce efforts
   &#8226; Experience in free-text analytics / natural language processing (NLP)
   &#8226; Excellent written and verbal communication and skills
Compensation is DOE and is extremely competitive.
