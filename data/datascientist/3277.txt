                 Description:

                   Responsibilities:
                   &#149; Apply various statistical and machine learning techniques, to develop segmentations, predictive models, experimental designs, and decision analyses
                    &#149; Gather, manipulate, and analyze large data sets from multiple sources
                    &#149; Develop algorithms to optimize product offering, pricing, and distribution channel
                    &#149; Transform data into impactful business insights, strategy recommendations and implementation plans to improve key performance metrics for the business
                    &#149; Evaluate model output, formulate and test hypotheses, weigh alternatives, and determine appropriate responses
                    &#149; Apply deep, creative, rigorous thinking to solve broad business problems
                    &#149; Collaborate closely with various functional groups such as Product, Marketing, and Technology to successfully deliver results
                    &#149; Effectively communicate findings and solutions to a variety of audiences
                    *** The preferred locations are posted but we will consider anyone located within the Wells Fargo footprint
                     Required Qualifications:
                      &#149; 8+ years of experience in one or a combination of the following: reporting, analytics, or modeling or a Masters' degree or higher in a quantitative field such as applied math, statistics, engineering, physics, accounting, finance, economics, econometrics, computer sciences, or business/social and behavioral sciences with a quantitative emphasis and 5+ years of experience in one or a combination of the following: reporting, analytics, or modeling
                      &#149; 5 + years of experience building predictive models
                      &#149; 5 + years of experience using quantitative machine learning techniques

                        &#149; Extensive knowledge and understanding of research and analysis
                        &#149; Strong analytical skills with high attention to detail and accuracy
                        &#149; Excellent verbal, written, and interpersonal communication skills
                        &#149; Ability to think creatively to find innovative solutions to complex analytical questions
                        &#149; Ability to work in a fast paced deadline driven environment
