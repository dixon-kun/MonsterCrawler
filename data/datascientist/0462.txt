            Fulltime Position
           Our Client is looking for &#160;Big Data Analytics Architect to join their Software Development organization. The ideal candidate has significant experience in building scalable data platforms that enable business intelligence, analytics and data products. As a development lead for the team, this candidate must possess strong, hands-on technical expertise in big data technologies with an ability to work with team members in an agile development environment.
            Responsibilities:
             Design and develop a distributed data processing platform using Big Data technologies.

             Experience with commercial ETL platforms like Informatica, SSIS, Talend, etc.
             Experience working with, processing and managing large data sets (multi TB scale).
             Experience with test driven development and automated testing frameworks.
             Capable of delivering on multiple competing priorities with little supervision.
             Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
             Bachelor's Degree in computer science or equivalent experience.
            Nice To Have:
             Experience working with AWS technologies like EC2, S3, EMR, Redshift, etc.
