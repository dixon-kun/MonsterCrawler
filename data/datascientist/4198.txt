         BAE Systems is seeking qualified technical interns for our summer 2017 session. At BAE Systems, our interns spend their time collaborating with each other and our leadership to lead and create innovative, mission focused solutions to everyday challenges. All of this will lead to an engaging experience preparing you for the future. You will have the opportunity to apply your classroom experience in a challenging and fast paced business environment, and perform on meaningful work throughout the summer, while also supporting the development and growth of new skills in your chosen area of study.
