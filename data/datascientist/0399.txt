          Machine Learning / Predictive Modeling / Statistics
          AIG is seeking world-class dynamic Data Scientists to lead AIG&#8217;s anti-fraud efforts. You would lead a team of Data Scientists within AIG&#8217;s Anti-Fraud &amp; Investigations unit while working directly with the business to help identify and investigate fraud in AIG&#8217;s multi-billion dollar insurance businesses.
          More broadly, our data scientists drive transformational change through evidence-based decision making at the company and in the industries that AIG operates.
          &#8226; Are you a curious high performer?
          &#8226; Do you want to work on a team that values harmonious collaboration?
          &#8226; Is your desire to learn and reinvent?

          Position Summary:
          Our ideal candidate should possess the following:
          &#8226; Very strong background in one or more computational areas (Computer Science, Statistics, Economics, Physics, Computational Linguistics)
          &#8226; Proven facility with multiple modeling techniques
          &#8226; Exceptional programming skills in one or more platforms
          &#8226; The drive to deliver on commitments and an openness to new ideas
          &#8226; Master Degree (Preferably a PhD)
          &#8226; 10+ years of relevant experience (will be flexed for exceptional candidates)
          Position Requirements:
          Responsibilities include but not limited to:
          &#8226; Understanding complex business challenges, designing scientific solutions, manipulating large data sets, using cutting edge machine learning or statistical modeling techniques and synthesizing insights
          &#8226; Building scalable solutions that create great business impact
          &#8226; Strong and effective communication (both written and verbal) with colleagues and business leaders
          &#8226; Continuously advancing your skills and those of others
