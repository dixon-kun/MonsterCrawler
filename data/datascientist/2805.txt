             POSITION SUMMARY
              Aetna&#8217;s Consumer Health Services Clinical Analytics team creates cutting edge analytic tools to drive better and faster decision making within the organization. We are currently looking for an exceptional Data Scientist to join us in developing products to improve health outcomes.&#160;This position will use predictive and behavioral analytics, and analytically derived insights to develop and test solutions that improve consumer engagement, health outcomes and lower health care costs.

              This position is a unique opportunity for someone passionate about applying innovations in data science and machine learning to improve health care services and outcomes.&#160; The team uses an industry leading scalable data platform and techniques in creating solutions to challenging business problems.You would be part of a new and expanding team, with plenty of opportunities to interact with project stakeholders and business partners at all levels of the organization.&#160;This is a fast-paced, collaborative and iterative environment requiring quick learning, agility and flexibility.
