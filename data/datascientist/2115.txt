          About the Job
            If you are a Data Engineer with experience, please read on!
            A million people a year die in car collisions around the world. That number should be zero. You can help us create a new insurance company that uses the latest technology and data science methods to save lives by preventing car collisions before they happen. The field is rich with data and we will be pushing the boundaries of what is possible with data. We are a San Francisco startup in the financial district that is well funded. We are temporarily in stealth mode.
            What You Will Be Doing
            - Develop fast data infrastructure leveraging machine learning to personalize experiences for our customers
            - Work with data scientists to carry out site-wide analytics and build A/B test experiments for hypothesis testing
            What You Need for this Position
            - Practical experience with AWS
            - Expert in Python or Scala
            - Expert in Spark
            - Expert in Kafka (or Kinesis)
            - Experience in building RESTful services and complex systems at scale
            - Proven hands-on experience with distributed databases
            - Strong understanding of Continuous Integration and Continuous Delivery
            - Deep experience with automated testing
            - Experience building and scaling modern architectures (such as lambda architecture).
            - Strong machine learning and statistics experience a plus
            What's In It for You
            - Top market salary
            - Your own office overlooking downtown SF
            - Healthy, catered meals daily
            - Compensation Review every 6 months to reset salary
            - Initial funding $35 Million (funded by $100B/yr Fortune 500 company)
            - Make a difference and save lives!
            So, if you are a Data Engineer with experience, please apply today!
