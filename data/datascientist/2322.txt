          About the Job
            Our client is looking for a
             Sr. Splunk Consultant
            for a permanent opportunity in Sacramento, CA.
            Job Description
            This role requires the ability to design, architect and implement Splunk solutions in support of cybersecurity analysts and data scientists. The focus of this role is primarily on engineering, maintaining and monitoring a large Splunk environment deployed on systems running on Linux and Windows platforms. A key aspect of this job will be to help build and direct the Splunk technical capability. The successful candidate will have experience in architecting, implementing, and using Splunk solutions and applications to integrate data feeds and create content in a distributed computing environment. The candidate must have solid Splunk experience and be able to work collaboratively with diverse end users.
            Responsibilities/Duties:
            &#183; Architect, implement, and use Splunk solution in a highly available, redundant, distributed computing environment.
            &#183; Collaborate with Splunk Business Partner and technical consultants to provide support for system issues, or introduce new solutions.
            &#183; Help manage the strategy of the Splunk Business Unit within the company.
            &#183; Perform Splunk forwarder deployment, configuration and troubleshooting across a variety of platforms.
            &#183; Deploy new Splunk instances, including clustered deployments.
            &#183; Monitor Splunk internal logs to identify and resolve potential performance issues.
            &#183; Tune the Splunk infrastructure and lead capacity planning and continuity of operations tasks.
            &#183; Troubleshoot technical issues to determine root cause and resolve or implement workaround as necessary.
            &#183; Reproduce customer issues, file bug reports and escalate cases to Splunk support as necessary.
            &#183; Maintain Splunk systems internal documentation, including SOP's and design documents.
            Required Skills:
            &#183; 14+ years' IT experience in a technical position.
            &#183; 4+ years' experience with Splunk.
            &#183; Hands-on experience installing, configuring and administering Splunk.
            &#183; Proficiency with Linux and Windows platforms.
            &#183; Familiar with various distributed compute environments including client-server, VMs, AWS, GCP.
            &#183; Experience with one or more programming/scripting languages (e.g., Perl, Python, Java).
            &#183; Basic VMWare knowledge.
            &#183; Familiarity with network based storage technologies.
            &#183; Understanding of the function, operations and management of network infrastructure components (switches, routers, firewalls, load balancers, etc.).
            &#183; Knowledge and experience in proposal development and preparation is desirable.
            Education/Certification Requirements:
            &#183; Certified Splunk Administrator/Architect preferred
            &#183; Bachelor's Degree in related technical discipline/Master's Degree preferred
            Qualifications
            Splunk, Windows / Unix / Linux, scripting / programming language.
