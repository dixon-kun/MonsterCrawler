          Companies understand all about their sales by store, region, product, department and season. In today&#8217;s environment, this is not enough. An understanding of their customers is essential. At Wunderman,&#160;we help companies understand their customer&#8217;s shopping patterns &#8211; How often do they shop? What channels do they use?            ?
          Wunderman puts its expertise to work to answer these important questions for or customers.
           Use customers&#8217; raw data and analytical tools such as SAS, SQL, Excel and PowerPoint to help them better understand their customer base
