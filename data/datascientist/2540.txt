        The Senior Data Scientist will be responsible for devising strategies to track and analyze data around online video and user engagement. The data will be used to help inform the core Engagement, Content, and Platform teams on developing compelling strategies across all verticals within the channel. The Senior Data Scientist will be required to:
        * Develop strategy and methodology for data analysis for AJ+
        * Implement tools for successful tracking
        * Provide comprehensive tracking reports of video consumption of content
        * Work with Management, Development, and Editorial teams to provide insight into user behavior around video consumption on various platforms
        * Develop strategies for testing of new video content on various platforms to track user behavior and engagement
        Bachelor's degree in statistics or analytics, 5 years' experience in data science within an online media environment, and experience in data analysis around social media discovery and interaction around online content.
        The desired candidate will have excellent statistical and mathematical knowledge, ability to build data tools, visualize data reports, and an understanding of social media ecosystems.
        Al Jazeera English is an equal opportunity employer.
