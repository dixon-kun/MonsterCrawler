         POSITION SUMMARY
         This position provides analytical consulting services within the EP Strategic Services Division. The overall goal is to provide data mining, statistical analysis, and predictive modeling to improve decision-making process.
         ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS
         &#8226; Adapt statistical methods in order to solve specific problems in many fields, such as economics and engineering.
         &#8226; Analyze and interpret statistical data in order to identify significant differences in relationships among sources of information.
         &#8226; Evaluate the statistical methods and procedures used to obtain data in order to ensure validity, applicability, efficiency, and accuracy.
         &#8226; Use techniques such as probability and inference to find new or improved methods of obtaining and evaluating numerical data.
         &#8226; Identify relationships and trends in data, as well as any factors that could affect the results of research.
         &#8226; Process large amounts of data for statistical modeling and graphic analysis, using SAS.
         &#8226; Report results of statistical analyses, including information in the form of graphs, charts, and tables.
         &#8226; Develop an understanding of fields to which statistical methods are to be applied in order to determine whether methods and results are appropriate.
         &#8226; Prepare data for processing by organizing information, checking for any inaccuracies, and adjusting and weighting the raw data.
         &#8226; Perform quantitative and qualitative analyses to support strategic projects and initiatives.
         &#8226; Non-Essential/Marginal Functions:
         &#8226; SAS experience desired
         &#8226; Oil and Gas Industry experience desired
         &#8226; Formulating queries of relational databases using SQL
         PREREQUISITES
         &#8226; Bachelor&#8217;s Degree in Engineering, Computer Science, Statistics, or Applied Mathematics with minimum 4 years of related experience.
         &#8226; Master's degree preferred.
         &#8226; Strong ability in theoretical modeling, statistical modeling or data manipulation.
         &#8226; Exceptionally good in problem analyzing, solving and troubleshooting skills, with strong detail orientation.
         &#8226; Highly proficient in the use of all applications in the Microsoft Office Suite.
         &#8226; Proven leadership skills, demonstrated in both academic and business environments.
         &#8226; Excellent verbal and written presentation skills, with the ability to communicate clearly and persuasively.
         &#8226; High degree of initiative and results orientation.
         &#8226; Open attitude towards and ability to learn and utilize new technologies and standards.
         &#8226; Excellent interpersonal skills in areas such as teamwork and communications.
         &#8226; Excellent analytical and technical skills.
         &#8226; Ability to quickly comprehend the functions and capabilities of new technologies.
