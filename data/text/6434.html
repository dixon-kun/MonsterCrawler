Data Scientist Senior - Customer Analytics

Job Number

:143792

Our CustomerRelationship ManagementAnalytics (CRM)Team

Delivering strategically impactful products, ourCRM Data Science team is focused on helping to improve our customer experience. The solutions and techniques driven by our team help us to understand customer behavior and improvehow we engage with our customers and improve product offerings.

Your Role on the Team
As a member of the Customer Experience Advanced Analytics team, you will drive insights and process improvements that affect millions of our customers. By using advanced analytical, machine learning, and data transformation techniques, you will pinpoint the best actions to take throughout a customer’s lifecycle to earn loyalty, improve retention, and drive additional sales. You will work with unstructured data such as call transcripts or documents sent in by customers as well as event sequences to develop new ways of understanding the customer experience and improving our ability to create customer journey.

Your Day-to-Day
•Lead projects and communicate results to business partners, ensuring recommendations are understood and incorporated in products and processes
•Use large-scale data mining and machine learning techniques to discover actionable patterns in data that optimize or innovate and solve business problems
•Perform rigorous model evaluation, designs hypothesis tests, oversees test execution and result evaluation
•Applies knowledge of computer science to run algorithms more efficiently, deploy solutions and find new ways of managing and analyzing the data

Must-Have Qualifications
•Bachelor's degree with quantitative focus in Econometrics, Statistics, Operations Research, Computer Science or related field (e.g., Mathematics)
•Two years of relevant experience in Statistical/Quantitative Modeling and/or Machine Learning tools (R, Python, etc.) and in using various database tools (Hadoop, SQL) processing large volumes of structured and unstructured data
oIn lieu of a bachelor's degree, we'll also accept four years of relevant experience in these areas
•Two years of experience with mathematical model creation and/or evaluation, hypothesis testing and experimental design

What Else We Look For
•Demonstrated skills in data mining, machine learning, programming, and dynamic optimization
•Ability to query databases and apply advanced data manipulation techniques across multiple sources
•Solid project management skills and the ability to handle multiple projects simultaneously while meeting tight and potentially changing deadlines in a high-paced and dynamic environment
•Problem solving and structuring skills
•Ability to mine large datasets and surface actionable insights
•Attention to detail and ability to simplify complex ideas
•Solid collaboration and communication (written and verbal) skills
•A passion for empirical research and answering hard questions with data
•Proficiency in at least one higher level programming language - Python preferred, Linux/Unix
•Thought leadership experience, such as applying data driven models with a tangible impact on business
•Ability to understand and articulate the "big picture"
•Ability to perform in an ambiguous environment and to work with multiple stakeholders from various business units
•Ability to identify and learn applicable new techniques independently as needed

Our Company and Benefits
As the fourth largest auto insurance company in the nation, Progressive's data analysts and scientists have access to the most telematics data recorded. With freedom and encouragement to implement your own ideas, you’ll be solving big data problems that impact billions of dollars of business. You’ll also benefit from:
•Annual gainshare bonus of up to 30% of your salary; Progressive rewards each of us with an annual bonus based on company performance
•401k which includes dollar-for-dollar company match of up to 6%
•Dedication to work/life balance which includes flexible work arrangements and tools to support your lifestyle
•Dynamic company culture that encourages engagement, supports Employee Resource Groups, values your input and embraces a relaxed atmosphere
•Onsite gym and wellness programs with discounts & rewards
•Healthcare onsite and standard benefits (medical, dental, vision)
•Relocation assistance to Northeast Ohio available; learn more about all Cleveland has to offer including. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=56p2ENKC1Bw
