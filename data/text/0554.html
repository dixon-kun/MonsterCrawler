About Publicis Health
Publicis Health is the premier global health and wellness communications network. We are one of the four solutions hubs of Publicis Groupe, the third-largest advertising and media company in the world, giving us unparalleled global reach.

We manage top-tier agencies specializing in promoting innovative solutions in advertising, medical education, clinical trial program development, sales and marketing, digital, market access, and medical and scientific affairs. Our nearly 6,500 employees work at 15 agency brands across 60 offices located in 10 countries.

We are a connected solutions hub, equipped for continual business transformation in today’s world. We integrate services across our global offices and brands to deliver on our clients’ needs. Each of our agencies brings a unique approach to business, which makes us even stronger together. We understand the rapidly changing healthcare environment and leverage it to maximize the potential of our clients’ brands.

Position Overview
In a constantly changing world, our goal is to create life-changing dialogue around health and wellness. We are looking for a VP, Account Director to join our Razorfish Health team. We are looking for a dynamic leader who will not only lead our internal teams to success, but lead our clients to new innovations. You will manage the client relationship and partner with senior leadership on defining and executing a high-level client strategy. The VP, Account Director will be responsible for, but not limited to:
• Responsible for being the lead strategic marketing consultant to the client and is charged with the day-to-day management of the business of the account, including team collaboration, team harmony, big-picture strategic thinking, and financial performance.
• Develops specific, measurable client business objectives and frequently reports on progress
• Views strategic goals in a broader business context
• Establishes priorities for key agency projects
• Initiates long-term projects for teams that will contribute to greater agency revenue
• Support new business initiatives
• In partnership with Project Management, take ownership of the financials for clients
• including forecasts, budgets and investments
• Work with Resource Management to align business with the appropriate staff
• Deliver strategic client presentations and recommendations
• Develop team members by coaching, training and identifying areas for growth
• Partners with clients and Discipline Leads to build active brands
Qualifications
• Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, or related degree
• 12 - 15 years’ progressive experience within the health and wellness space
• Experience running multichannel and/or multi-agency teams
• HCP Launch experience is desired
• 5+ years of leadership experience, managing teams
• Strategic thinking. Collaborator
• Effective presentation skills and strong client facing skills
• Up to 50% of travel to the client is required
