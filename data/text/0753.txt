         Our Industry: Where we play
        We sit at the intersection of technology and Life Sciences industries. Humbly, we&#8217;ve been told that we are the leader in application software for the clinical development process, tackling real-world problems and making a real difference in the lives of patients everywhere. With the current transformation in the life sciences industry, we are at the forefront of providing an advanced clinical development platform for our clients to further improve the accuracy, timeliness and ease of the data collection process. That means better treatments can reach waiting patients sooner. We are publicly traded (MDSO) with over 800 customers, customer retention rates above 99%, and the experience of supporting more than 12,000 clinical trials. We are still led by Co-founders, Tarek Sherif and Glen de Vries, and have global operations in US, Europe and Asia.
         The Position:
        The Vice President, Product leads two distinct, but related functions:
        1. Product Management for Medidata&#8217;s Platform program and related solutions, which include iMedidata, Cloud Administration, eLearning and numerous platform-related services shared by our products;
        2. Product Management for Medidata&#8217;s reporting and analytics solutions, which include product-specific reporting solutions, cross-platform reporting solutions and landing pages/dashboards, and the &#8220;productization&#8221; of data science-driven solutions.
         The Responsibilities:
        As a leader of Product, you will:
        &#9679; Manage, build and develop product teams that support the two functional areas described above in NYC. These teams consist of product managers, project managers, business analysts and UX/UI staff members.
        &#9679; Define, own and communicate a product roadmap that delivers products and features that drive revenue growth through strong positioning and differentiation.
        &#9679; Champion ongoing competitive, market, and user research to ensure product decisions are based on sound use of both qualitative and quantitative data.
        &#9679; Collaborate with customers to evaluate and define new platform and analytics capabilities and solutions.
        &#9679; Collaborate with key functional stakeholders (including sales, marketing, services, support, engineering, and finance) to establish product plans, pricing frameworks, business cases and go-to-market plans for platform solutions and new reporting solutions.
        &#9679; Work with architecture and engineering to define an innovative product analytics platform that will reveal powerful usage insights and drive strategic decision-making for customers and the industry.
        &#9679; Support sales and other customer-facing teams in high-value meetings with prospects and customers.
        &#9679; Cultivate a deep understanding of the Clinical Trials space, including engagement with all segments of the customer base.
