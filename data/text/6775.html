Director, Predictive Analytics at Analytic Recruiting, Inc.

Newark, NJ

Report this job

About the Job

Director, Predictive Analytics and Modeling with proficiency in statistical modeling, machine learning working with structured and unstructured data sets using SAS, Python or R sought by global New Jersey-based financial services firm. This is a high level individual contributor role that will partner with business stakeholders to provide data-driven business insights. Strong communication and the ability to present results to a non-technical audience is a MUST. Responsibilities include:

  • Develop predictive models, including (but not limited to) purchase/retention models, customer segmentation
  • Execute data mining and machine learning solutions to uncover business trends and new opportunities for using analytics
  • Integrate and analyze multiple disparate structured and unstructured sources including speech Analytics, social media listening, digital footprints, customer behavior, financial information, proprietary market research, syndicated data, etc.
  • Present analysis results in a non-statistical manner with appropriate conclusions and actionable insights

Requirements

  • Master’s degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Operations Research, Data Science, or other analytics discipline
  • 5+ years’ experience with statistical modeling/machine learning in a business environment.  Financial services industry experience is a plus
  • Experience with unstructured data is desired
  • Proficiency with SAS, SAS Enterprise Miner, Python or R
  • PowerPoint and Excel is a must. Data visualization experience desired
  • Strong communication skills. Ability to present results and identify key insights from the data

Keywords: predictive modeling; segmentation; machine learning; SAS; statistical modeling; analytics; data mining; financial services

Refer to Job #22612 – and send MS Word attached resume to Howard Fishman, howard@analyticrecruiting.com
