        Program Manager &#8211; Data
        Imagine working on one of the most important issues surrounding digital advertising today; using massive online and offline data sets and predictive analytics to create digital consumer audiences to drive superior marketing results. This is a tremendous opportunity to shape the future of digital advertising and the Oracle Data Cloud (ODC) is at the forefront of creating new types of targeting capabilities for marketers.
        As program manager, we are looking for you to effectively translate product strategy into tangible capabilities for our clients and partners. You will engage with an experienced, cross-disciplinary staff to build solutions for fulfilling ODC audiences to leading ad tech companies. Your work will further establish ODC audiences as a crucial component to the digital advertising ecosystem and expand the breadth and depth of data usage for all marketing use-cases.
        Core Program Manager Responsibilities
        You are responsible for driving clarity across stakeholders (Product Managers, Engineering Leads, Data Science, and Operations). Key responsibilities of the job include:
        Requirements Definition
        * Work across teams to ensure functional specifications are complete
        * Groom user stories for a given feature based on the requirements provided by product management
        * Write clear and detailed user stories for specific technical needs not addressed by product management and map to business requirements
        Release Management
        * Responsible for accurate reporting of status on planned/in progress initiatives
        * Collaborate with implementation teams to size all features and user stories
        * Manage the user story queue for product features and slot stories into development iterations
        * Assess alternatives to resolve roadblocks and formulate plans for resolution
        Manage Complexity
        * Initiate, manage, and implement cross-team efforts with major implications to the business
        * Work on complex issues where analysis of situations or data requires and in-depth evaluation
        * Determine new methods and procedures for solving complex issues
        Product Delivery
        * Define user acceptance criteria for all facets of a product or capability
        * Establish on-going tracking and monitoring of product deployments
        * Identify issues that will keep the product from delivering on time and/or with the desired requirements, and communicate to leadership
        Other Required Skills and Qualifications
        Detail Oriented: From creating specific and clear requirements specs, to understanding the trade-offs that come with each engineering decision, to identifying and managing intricate dependencies, you are well organized and ensure that all details are covered in a given release.
        High Standards: There are critical moments when someone will say "good enough" and your job is to ask if we can do better. You ensure that we build high quality products for our customers, with great attention to the scalability of our processes and products.
        Fierce Reducer: Overly complicated solutions add complexity and are difficult to maintain. You reduce what product wants to the bare essence of what needs to be built to satisfy the customer need. You invent new processes and technologies when what we have won't cut it.
        Deep Domain Knowledge: You have a deep understanding of the technologies we use and those that we don't. There is no task that is too small for you, because you have a deep well of knowledge upon which to draw and help find solutions to our problems.
        Concise: We do exactly what is needed, and nothing more. Conservation of resources is important. You are boiling down the requirements to what is needed and keep scope creep at bay. From executives to junior developers, internal and across partnerships, you'll ensure everyone maintains a consistent vision and that progress, issues and needs are well understood.
        Build Trust and Accountability: To be effective, you need the alignment of engineering, product, analytics and operations. It's your job to ensure work is designed and executed in the right way, but you need to be open minded and a good listener. You have strong convictions, but are savvy enough to know when to bend. You must be willing to take ownership of your mistakes. Holding everyone accountable to business commitments is one of your core values.
        Technology/Process: Experience with Agile Software development principles and familiarity with associated planning tools such as Rally, JIRA or similar packages. Programming background or experience in mining/analyzing data (via SQL, Excel, etc) a plus.
        Work Experience/Education: 6-8 years professional experience with 4 years of program management experience in internet-related software applications, big data management, and advertising technologies; BA/BS in a quantitative discipline; MBA from leading university desirable.
