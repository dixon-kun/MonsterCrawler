        Business Intelligence Sr. Advisor- Round Rock, Texas
        Dell Technologies is a world-class organization with global workforce across the globe. We are focused on driving business transformation, superior customer satisfaction, and revenue growth through innovation and cutting-edge technology that leverages Big Data platforms, cloud, analytics, mobile, and social solutions.
        As a Senior Hadoop data engineer helping with Business intelligence and data science projects, you will be responsible for design and development of new capabilities on Hadoop data platform to solve many business problems.
        Responsibilities
        * Design and develop data ingestion, aggregation, integration and advanced analytics in Hadoop
        * Develop architecture and design patterns to process and store high volume data sets
        * Build continuous integration and test driven development environment
        * Research and deploy new tools, technologies, frameworks on big data platform
        * Work with Hadoop system engineers to optimize application performance
        Requirements
        * Undergraduate degree and 8-10 years relevant experience OR graduate degree and 6-8 years relevant experience.
        * At least 4 years of experience working with Hadoop data platform architecture and development with HBase/Hive/MapReduce/Impala/Sqoop is required while knowledge on Spark, Kafka, Pig, Flume is also preferred.
        * Experience orchestrating complex data flows is required
        * Experience working with SQL, XML, JSON and UNIX are required
        * Ability to communicate technical ideas and concepts to less technical audiences, innovate new approaches to complex problems and work effectively with global teams.
        * General operational expertise such as good troubleshooting skills, understanding of system's capacity, bottlenecks, basics of memory, CPU, OS, storage, and networks preferred
        * Familiarity with open source configuration management and deployment tools such as Puppet or Chef and Linux scripting.
