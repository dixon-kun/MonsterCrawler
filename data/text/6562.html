Scrum Master

Preferred Qualifications

Senior Program Manager/Scrum Master – SoftwareDeveloper Services


The Oracle Data Cloud (ODC) Software DeveloperServices team is looking for a program manager/scrum master. The requirementsfor the position are for you to effectively help translate delivery automation productstrategy into tangible capabilities for our internal development community.  You will engagewith an experienced, cross-disciplinary staff to build delivery automation solutionsfor our Engineering, Analytics and Operations teams.  Your work will further establish anultra-modern delivery automation platform to move value to market at everincreasing speeds. The platform is an advanced, modern platform to movesoftware from code commit to production with the push of a button.


This role entails more than justmanaging schedules, Gantt charts, facilitating status meetings, and tracking criticalmilestones.  You will also be expected totake a lead role in partnering with other Program Managers in our development operationsand product development groups to help clearly define the features built for ourengineering, data science and operations teams. We are looking for candidates that are inspired by both the definitionof the work in ways that are effective for delivery teams, and the execution ofthe work across teams, using Agile/Lean best practices.


Core Program Manager Responsibilities



Youare responsible for driving clarity across stakeholders (Product Managers,Engineering Leads, Data Science, and Operations).  Key responsibilities of the job include:



ScrumMaster Responsibilities


· Coordinate all Agile Ceremoniesincluding Iteration Planning, Standups, retrospectives, Demos, Backlog Grooming,and Release Planning


· Mentor and coach teams onAgile/Scrum practices and self-organization


· Remove impediments to teamdeliverables and product goals


· Assess Scrum Maturity of team andbring the team to higher levels of maturity at a sustainable pace.


· Facilitate the building of atrusting and cooperative environment


· Facilitate discussion,decision-making, and conflict resolution


· Drivegrooming of user stories for a given feature with the product owner


· Provide leadership and support toteam and lead by example


· Manage project risks, dependenciesand issues


· Effectively communicate projectexpectations to team members and stakeholders in a timely and clear fashion


· Helpwrite clear and detailed user stories for specific technical needs notaddressed by product management and map to business requirements



ReleaseManagement


· Responsiblefor accurate reporting of status on planned/in progress initiatives


· Collaboratewith implementation teams to size all features and user stories


· Managethe user story queue for product features and slot stories into developmentiterations


· Assessalternatives to resolve roadblocks and formulate plans for resolution



ManageComplexity


· Initiate,manage, and implement cross-team efforts with major implications to thebusiness


· Workon complex issues where analysis of situations or data requires and in-depthevaluation


· Determinenew methods and procedures for solving complex issues



ProductDelivery


· Helpdefine user acceptance criteria for all facets of a product or capability


· Establishon-going tracking and monitoring of product deployments


· Identifyissues that will keep the product from delivering on time and/or with thedesired requirements, and communicate to leadership





Other Required Skillsand Qualifications




Detail Oriented :  From creating specific and clear requirementsspecs, to understanding the trade-offs that come with each engineeringdecision, to identifying and managing intricate dependencies, you are wellorganized and ensure that all details are covered in a given release.



High Standards :  There are critical moments when someone willsay “good enough” and your job is to ask if we can do better.  You ensure that we build high qualityproducts for our customers, with great attention to the scalability of ourprocesses and products.



Fierce Reducer :  Overly complicated solutions add complexityand introduce risk.  You help reduce whatthe product owner wants to the bare essence of what needs to be built tosatisfy the customer need. You invent new processes and technologies when whatwe have won’t cut it.



Concise :  We do exactly what is needed, and nothingmore.  Conservation of resources isimportant.  You help in boiling down therequirements to what is needed and keep scope creep at bay.  From executives to junior developers, internaland across partnerships, you’ll ensure everyone maintains a consistent visionand that progress, issues and needs are well understood.



Build Trust and Accountability :  To be effective, you need the alignment ofengineering, product, analytics and operations.  It’s your job to ensure work is designed andexecuted in the right way, but you need to be open minded and a good listener.  You have strong convictions, but are savvy enoughto know when to bend. You must be willing to take ownership of your mistakes.  Holding everyone accountable to businesscommitments is one of your core values.



Technology/Process :  Experience with AgileSoftware/Lean product development principles plus familiarity with associatedplanning tools such as Rally, JIRA or similar systems.  Programming backgroundor experience in mining/analyzing data (via SQL, Excel, etc) a plus.



WorkExperience/Education : 6-8+ years professional experience with 4+ years of program managementexperience in internet-related software applications, big data management, andadvertising technologies; BA/BS in a quantitative discipline; MBA from leadinguniversity desirable.



Certifications: Scrum Masteror other relevant certification. Scaled Agile experience nice to have but notrequired.

Detailed Description and Job Requirements
Manage the development and implementation process of a specific company product.

Manage the development and implementation process of a specific company product involving departmental or cross-functional teams focused on the delivery of new or existing products. Plan and direct schedules and monitor budget/spending. Monitor the project from initiation through delivery. Organize the interdepartmental activities ensuring completion of the project/product on schedule and within budget constraints. Assign and monitor work of systems analysis and program staff, providing technical support and direction.

Work is non-routine and very complex, involving the application of advanced technical/business skills in area of specialization. Leading contributor individually and as a team member, providing direction and mentoring to others. BS or MS degree or equivalent experience relevant to functional area. Seven years of project management, product design or related experience preferred.
