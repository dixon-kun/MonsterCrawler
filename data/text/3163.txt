         Product Manager, Intel AI Saffron
          Job Description
         Directs a comprehensive product strategy from product conception and definition through end of life. Designs new products that customers will love. Writes compelling specs that engineers will want to work on. Serves as key interface between sales, marketing, and engineering. Makes software teams more efficient, more effective, and more fun. Validates design and/or technology choices using empirical methodologies, when possible. Evangelist for user-focused design. Responsible for success of a software product through all phases of its lifecycle.
          Qualifications
         - Bachelor's degree required, Masters preferred Computer Science, Applied Mathematics, Statistics, or Computational Linguistics a plus- 8 years of software experience- 5 years of product management experience- Working knowledge of concepts in machine learning, data science, and/or artificial intelligence- Demonstrated ability to deliver and stay focused in a fast-paced environment.- Deep experience with product management practices- Passionate about new product development- Ability to cheerfully perform under pressure, prioritizing activities and managing multiple tracks simultaneously- Able to forecast the approximate benefit of a project, measure benefit once projects are launched, and factor learnings into their future prioritization and forecasts- Ability to appreciate great design and be able to distinguish it from good design- Can understand the technical complexity of the features they put on the backlog, without any costing input from devs- Experience shipping software using agile methods and tools- Ability to influence management, negotiate win-win solutions and work effectively in a highly-matrixed environment.- Excellent verbal / written communication and analytical skills and the ability to work well within a diverse, geographically distributed, multi-cultural environment.- Strong problem solving abilities - the ability to ramp up quickly on a concept or problem, recommend possible solutions and communicate solutions to business and technical audiences.- Ability to travel to other Intel international and within USA and customer sites when required.
          Inside this Business Group
         Intel Labs, the New Business Initiatives group (NBI), the Perceptual Computing Group and the New Devices Group (NDG).Additionally, New Devices Group and the New Business Initiatives group were combined into the New Business Group (NBG) which nests into NTG.
