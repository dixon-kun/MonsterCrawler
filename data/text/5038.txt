        Position Description
        McKesson's Undergraduate Intern program provides you with an opportunity to experience the healthcare industry first-hand. By applying your education to a real-life working environment, you will receive a solid foundation of experience as a McKesson intern. Through project-based work across key business initiatives, as well as hands-on experience, you will be exposed to many aspects of the business and draw upon your varied skill set. Further, by working in tandem with others, you will build a network of support to help you develop professionally. This is a hands-on opportunity to gain practical experience in a dynamic and growing industry!
        Undergraduate Intern Program Details
        * Paid Internship
        * Full-time work for 10 weeks
        * Position will reside in Alpharetta, GA
        * No company sponsored housing, relocation, student visa or Green Card assistance available
        * Each intern is assigned a project-focused opportunity in a specific business unit and works directly with an Intern Coordinator and Sponsor. This is a hands-on opportunity to gain practical experience in a dynamic and growing industry!
        * The internship program also provides networking, access to executives, structured performance &amp; coaching and fun, local activities!
        ROLE SPECIFIC OPPORTUNITY:
        Here's the opportunity to make a difference, have fun, and get things done!
        Work with a crack team of data &amp; analytics ninjas! You will be a member of a recruited cohort responsible for addressing a singular challenge.

        * Solid grounding in Statistics fundamentals; exposure to Machine Learning &amp; AI is a plus
        * Data Analysis &amp; Visualization (Example tools: Tableau, Alteryx, Toad)
        * Proficient in Programming (Example Languages: R, Python, Julia, Scala)
