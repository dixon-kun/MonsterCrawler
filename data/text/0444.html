Job Title: Director of Product Management, Financial Crime and Compliance.

Organization Name: Oracle FSGBU, Oracle Financial Services Analytical Applications

Department Description

Oracle's Financial Services Analytical Applications group is responsible for the development and marketing of financial industry specific marketing, profitability, risk management, fraud and regulatory compliance analytics applications. Our software is in over 70% of Global Systemically Important Financial Institutions (GSIFS). Our market leading financial crime and compliance software suite is used by over one hundred leading financial institutions globally and underpins Anti-Money Laundering, Operational Risk and Enterprise Fraud systems at some of the largest financial institutions in the world.

Our software is powered by advanced analytics (R, Apache Spark, Oracle Exadata), and our group employs data scientists, financial engineers, software developers and product managers, all of whom are focused exclusively on developing advanced analytical applications for the Financial Services Industry.

We are looking for an individual with broad domain expertise and extensive experience in Financial crime and compliance analytics to lead a team of product managers who are responsible for a broad product portfolio covering compliance, case management and advanced analytics. We wish to transform how the industry does business, leveraging the newest technologies, the latest advances in data science and the brightest minds. Join us and help realize our vision.

Brief Posting Description

We have a world-class team of product managers who are responsible for entire product life cycle of products that power our Financial Crime and Compliance (FCCM) applications. You will lead a team of product managers and be part of the leadership team that sets the direction for Oracle's Financial Services Analytics Group. You are expected to work in close collaboration with Data Science, Engineering, Marketing, Sales and Pre-sales leadership in the development and implementation of product strategy and roadmap for AML and other compliance applications. This is an opportunity to be impactful in a meaningful way, towards the building of next generation platform for our FCCM product suite.

Detailed Description

This role requires broad knowledge of the current state of customer expectations, market and competition in Financial crime and compliance. Prior experience in senior leadership role as a product manager within a leading software vendor, as a consultant to the industry or as a compliance department leader in a global bank is required. Knowledge of trends in market, and awareness of the current pain points in the industry is essential to success in this role , as is prior experience in building software products in AML, Fraud, and/or Cyber crime applications.

Experience in managing a globally disperse team of product managers as well as experience in engaging with senior management and stakeholders at our customers is also required.

The person in this role will be responsible for building and managing the lifecycle of various products that are used in our Compliance applications. You will mentor other product managers and you will work closely with our development managers, software engineers and data scientists in developing requirements and specifications for the software.

Job Requirements

* Broad business domain expertise in Financial Crime, specifically in the areas AML, Fraud, FATCA, KYC/CDD/EDD, Case Management, Operational Risk, Advanced Analytics.

* Strong leadership skills proven through experience of managing a sizeable team that is disperse.

* Intimate knowledge of product life cycle from inception, design, construction, release management, to maturity.

* Experience in customer facing roles – engaging senior leadership at customers and prospects.

Qualifications

* 10 years of experience in product management or related roles in financial crime

* MBA and/or Engineering degree with relevant Financial crime and Compliance industry experience

* Excellent written and oral communication skills
