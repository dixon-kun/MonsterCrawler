The Opportunity:

The ideal candidate for this position is a high-potential individual who possesses strong technical accounting skills and is an effective, motivating manager. He/she will have served in positions of increasing responsibility throughout his/her career, and be comfortable with forming and maintaining relationships with stakeholders at every level of an organization.  The Accounting Manager will be responsible for coordinating and managing the general accounting functions required to provide timely and accurate financial data.  The position is based in Waltham, MA and reports to the Corporate Controller.

Education, Experience and Skills:

  • Bachelor’s degree (Accounting major preferred), CPA required
  • Experience in both public accounting and corporate finance settings
  • Extensive knowledge of accounting principles and standards
  • Advanced proficiency in accounting systems and other key business systems. Netsuite experience preferred.
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy
  • Ability to multi-task, delegate and manage a team in a fast-paced environment
  • Self-motivation to work with minimal supervision
  • Willingness to expand beyond typical accounting tasks as needed
  • Commitment to ethics and integrity

Key Responsibilities:

  • Monthly close and consolidation
  • Annual audit, bank audits
  • Maintaining Options records and Cap Table
  • Bank reporting (BBC, financials)
  • Providing information for tax returns
  • Managing accounting staff
