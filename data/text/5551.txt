           Job Summary:
          This position will work on multiple projects associated with data management for the Department of Anesthesiology. The position will solicit data needs from our clinical faculty and identify deficits in data availability. This will include but is not limited to ensuring the correct data is being extracted and placed in the data warehouse. This position will be responsible for supporting our Patient Safety and Quality Improvement Team by pulling and completing initial analysis of data needed for various safety and quality initiatives.
           Description of Job Responsibilities:
           Operates as the chief analytical resource to the Department of Anesthesiology for perioperative clinical quality and related financial outcomes.
           Utilizes Business Objects, Epic Reporting Workbench, Clarity, and other tools to mine clinical outcome and financial data.
           Analyzes data and summarizes findings
           Collaborates with UNC HCS ISD, Practice Quality and Innovation, Operational
           Efficiency, Clinical Quality Improvement, Enterprise Analytics and Data Sciences (EADS), NC TraCs, and others to improve data collection methods, data governance, and informing development teams of stakeholder requirements. Participates on development teams through these areas where beneficial to the objectives of Anesthesiology.
           Works with UNC HCS ISD to develop Epic Anesthesiology Quality Metric Dashboards
           Communicates effectively and develops effective working relationships with a variety of stakeholders
           Prioritizes duties according to department goals
           Operates with a high level of confidentiality and professionalism
           Designs and implements data models, dashboards and reports to monitor quality improvement and other key metrics.
           Serves as a key member of the Department of Anesthesiology Patient Safety and Quality Improvement Team.
           Participates in System-wide quality improvement initiatives.
           Works with Epic and Epic users across the country to determine the best way to access the data the department needs to facilitate patient safety and quality improvement.
           Completes other projects as assigned
