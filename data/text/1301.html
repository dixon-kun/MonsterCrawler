POSITION OVERVIEW

General Mills is seeking a Global Consumer Insights Data Scientist .  This position is part of the Global Consumer Insights (GCI) Big Data team and will focus on providing insights around our Marketing Activities.  This individual will need to work cross functionally with Central Marketing, IT and with many others in GCI.  They will be charged with identifying new data sources and developing new processes and insights from our existing data, with the goal of improving our marketing media.

As part of the Big Data team, you will find opportunities to grow your Big Data skills which include working with Hadoop, machine learning and your analytical story telling skills.  A typical workflow for our area includes taking a business question, developing an analysis plan to answer that question, executing that plan and developing a compelling story for our end business user so they take action on the insight. A successful candidate will have the right blend of programming, math, business acumen and storytelling.

MAIN RESPONSIBILITIES

In this role you will:

  • Work with Data Stewards to build and improve marketing mix data processes in order to identify new ways to track our media and build deeper measurement capabilities.
  • Work with end marketers to develop data products they can use to make marketing decisions
  • Partner with other teams to evaluate new data sources:
    • Quickly identify if the new data source is useful to answer currently identified business questions
    • Identify other uses for the data
    • Provide final recommendation on whether to bring new data sources into our data ecosystem
  • Ability to work in cross-functional teams to operationalize insights:
    • Work with Engineering to enable the required data flows
    • Operationalize analytics (transforms, models, analyses)
    • Work with Data Visualization teams to turn a one-time analysis into an ongoing insights product
    • Work with end users to ensure the operationalized insight meets their needs

MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS

  • Bachelor's Degree in quantitative field, advanced degree preferred
  • 5 years of experience with marketing data analysis
  • Deep experience working with relational databases (Oracle, SQL) and Hadoop, comfortable in data manipulation on large data sets.
  • Experience using clustering, regression, time series and discrimination and classification methods/techniques
  • Experience with statistical programming languages (like R, SAS, SPSS) and visualization tools (like Spotfire or Tableau) are desired
  • Proficient with R, Python and SQL scripting languages

The General Mills Big Data team works in a fast-paced environment where our success is fueled by the talents of our team. What makes someone well-suited for this role and drives success?

  • Analytical experience : Highly proficient in working with digital analytics (data and tool-sets), relational databases and business analytics tools
  • Can interpret “signals”: Pattern recognition is a key skill in analyzing complex datasets. An ability to identify “signals through the noise” is key
  • Curiosity : Demonstrates creative thinking while looking across consumer behaviors to identify opportunities and new insights
  • Agility : Adaptable to changing team needs and requests for information and intelligence
  • Attention to detail : High-level of comfort developing standardized reporting processes to deliver performance metrics to multiple teams
