JOB SUMMARY: Digital / E-Commerce Analyst will work with our Analytics and Insights, Sales and Marketing teams to understand the effectiveness of our digital marketing strategies in driving site traffic, customer engagement and conversion with key e-tail accounts. This includes defining and implementing measurement strategies, and publishing meaningful KPI dashboards and monthly performance reports with metrics and actionable insights to deliver our business objectives. DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES: Responsibilities •Digital/ e-commerce analyst measures the success of our online presence with retail and commercial transactional sites. •Role will analyze and report changes in online sales to a team of product & marketing managers so they are well-informed when making business decisions. •Drive innovative approaches to promotional tactics, including partnering with customers to execute in a multi-channel environment where appropriate. •Partner with digital content specialist to ensure product data and item content is optimized for onsite search and discoverability, relevant, engaging, accurate and in compliance with e-tailer guidelines. •Leveraging available data and analytical tools, analyze and report all key performance indicators to optimize online merchandising efforts, develop promotional strategies, and improve overall conversion for The Master Lock Company's branded products. •Report KPI's for multiple product lines and customers within The Master Lock Company's e-commerce business •Beyond sales, this role may assist in business analytics, tracking the company's presence on various partner sites, general web analytics, search engine ranking, advertising campaign results and e-commerce branding across a website. •Provide support as needed for account service needs including item setup/maintenance and sales forecasting. •Provide input for presentation materials for sales managers. •This role is responsible for gathering data that includes sales, rankings, customer reviews and the standing of a company's online competition. •The business analyst may also work with software developers to create a customized online transaction system that gives detailed information on how a customer found the website. Knowing how a customer came to a retail site is highly valuable information for those making advertising decisions. Skills •An e-commerce business analyst must be highly organized, technically-minded and an excellent communicator as they transform complicated charts of online data into valuable information for The Master Lock Company. •They are expected to present clearly to a variety of people from executive leadership team members and marketing/product managers to customers. •It is necessary that they keep up with the latest web analytics software and search engine rules so as to provide the most up-to-date and complete information. Requirements

QUALIFICATIONS: •A bachelor's degree or higher in business management, mathematics, data sciences, actuarial sciences, marketing or online marketing. •2-4 years' experience in e-commerce merchandising, digital/interactive marketing, and Search Engine Optimization (SEO) •Proficiency with basic computer software is necessary in this job, knowledge of Microsoft Office, PowerPoint, and Excel. Experience with Google Analytics, SEO, SEM, are required.•Durable products experience a plus •Ability to work independently and in a team environment. •Wiliness to travel domestically •Ability to establish and maintain project timelines, meet deadlines. •Ability to manage competing priorities and timeframes.
