Position Summary
ADP seeks to hire a Lead QA Engineer with expertise in Manual Testing. Additional experience with Automated Testing is desired. Participates in all stages of the software development life cycle (SDLC) to ensure technical completeness and accuracy. Monitors product development progress throughout the software development life cycle (SDLC). Implements and executes quality activities per the standardized SDLC. Develops test plans, specifications, cases and scripts. Presents documentation for reviews to ensure technical completeness and accuracy. Supports automated script development and execution with QA Automation.

*  Analyzes results to ensure quality standards and the software meets/exceeds documented business requirements.
