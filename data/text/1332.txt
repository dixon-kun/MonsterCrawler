        This role is for a Scrum Master that will oversee the delivery of application development teams enhancing the capabilities of TransUnion's leading-edge, multi-petabyte platforms.
        TransUnion's Development organization is the delivery backbone supporting both internal and external facing applications and solutions that support a myriad of capabilities. Our user community is a diverse group of data scientists, data wranglers, business analysts, product strategists, and market analysts, as well as a broad base of TransUnion's expansive and diverse customer base.
        How you'll Contribute:
        * Manage release and sprint planning processes and the execution of all the ceremonies associated with those processes
        * Participate in long term road map planning, manage quarterly capitalization processes and change management processes
        * Understand the general principals of Agile planning as outlined in the Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) and be well versed in SCRUM and Kanban practices
        * Work with technology teams to ensure that infrastructure requirements and technical dependencies are well understood and meet project objectives
        * Prepare weekly release and sprint reports by collecting, analyzing and summarizing information and trends.
        * Assist in the recruitment and selection of project personnel
        * Participate in the coordination of activities of internal project personnel, business departmental teams, technology resources and external vendors as they relate to release plans for applications
        * Establish and execute project communication plans for all IT activities
        * Initiate, review, and communicate modifications made to release plans
        * Monitor and track road map and release milestones, issues and risks
        * Schedule and facilitate meetings
        * Manage all project documentation and assist the product owners with backlog grooming
        * Confer with project personnel to identify and resolve problems
        * Monitor the performance of project team members
        * Ensure that release deliverables are submitted and that quality standards are consistently met
        * Deliver clear and concise stakeholder communication to include presentations to all levels of the organization
        What You'll Bring:
        * A minimum of four years of relevant experience managing projects and/or scrum teams
        * A minimum of four years of relevant IT experience
        * Familiarity working with Agile project delivery tools, such as JIRA and/or Agile Central/Rally (preferred)
        * Experience in the big data space a plus
        * Strong written and verbal communication skills
        * Expertise in office productivity tools, such as Excel and PowerPoint
        * A bachelor's degree from an accredited institution of higher learning
