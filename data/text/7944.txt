        Why Is This Role Critical?
        The Director, Actuarial Services will be a key Leader providing actuarial expertise by overseeing all risk based contracts through naviHealth's Medicare Advantage and Bundled Payment lines of business. This role presents a unique opportunity for an Actuary to join the healthcare industry driving a Value Based Care model, by seeing the views of the payor as well as supporting operational decisions based on your analysis and risk evaluation. The Director will be instrumental in facilitating upfront conversations setting contract structure with external customers and partners, as well as seeing that the contract requirements are carried out by our operations and finance teams.
        What you will be accountable for&#8230;
        * Provide analysis on whether internal and external projects, solutions or investments are likely to lower healthcare costs and to what degree
        * Participate with Business Development, Medical Integrity, Clinical, etc. Teams on initiatives to ensure appropriate and relevant healthcare cost and health economic factors are captured
        * Using mathematical modelling techniques and statistical concepts to determine probability and assess risks to price Medicare services
        * Responsible for development, oversight and reporting of Medicare cost impact models which include economic and statistical analyses
        * Responsible for incorporating metrics and benchmarks (both internal and external) in analyses to proactively identify and evaluate both leading and trailing indicators of Medicare costs that may affect economic and Medicare cost impact models
        * Partnering with Sales Team on product pricing, financial modeling and business/contract review of all business development activity.
        * Revenue forecasting overview
        * Cross functional projects with data science, clinical outcomes, and analytics.
        What you will need to be successful&#8230;
        * ASA or near ASA
        * 5+ years of experience in the healthcare industry
        * Experience with health insurance products, including: Compliance and Filing, Rating, Product development, Underwriting and Business Development
        * Advanced knowledge of medical claims data
        * Well-organized with the ability to handle several projects/clients simultaneously
        * Well-developed oral and written communication skills
        * Risk adjustment and predictive modeling experience a plus
