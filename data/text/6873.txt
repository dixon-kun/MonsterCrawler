        Our Data Scientist:
        The candidate should be highly analytical, detail-oriented and extraordinarily passionate about building data driven solutions to business problems.         The Job:
        As a Data Scientist you will work in a collaborative team environment that encourages you to perform at your best, and challenges you to engineer elegant algorithmic solutions for complex business problems. You will architect, design, and develop robust and scalable machine-learning-based, server-side software for applications that handle billions of transactions each day in an ultra-low latency setting. You will be an accomplished well-rounded developer, with a mastery of object-oriented design with appropriate use of best practices, design patterns, and frameworks and a thorough understanding of the implication of software designs and implementation choices on application performance and maintainability.
        Your Role:
        * Design, develop and maintain high-throughput, ultra-low latency industry-leading products, leveraging technologies such as Spring, Hibernate, MySQL, Coherence, Solr , MongoDB, RabbitMQ, etc .

        * Collaborate with other developers on best practices, code reviews, internal tools, and process improvements.

        * Guide, coach, and mentor team members as required, assisting in the team's skills development during software development projects.

        * Advanced degree in Computer Science with significant exposure to machine learning, data mining and distributed systems.
        * Strong analytical and problem solving skills, good understanding of different data structures, algorithms and their usage in solving business problems
        * Fundamental knowledge of parallel processing, cloud computing and map reduce programming paradigm.
        * A solid understanding of database technologies, logical and physical data structuring and modeling

        * Excellent verbal and written communication skills.

        * Experience using big data technologies such as Hadoop, Spark, Impala, Hive, etc.
