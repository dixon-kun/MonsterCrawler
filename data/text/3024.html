Junior Test Engineer at BESC

Ashburn, VA 20147

Report this job

About the Job

BESC has an immediate opening for a Test Engineer to join our team in the development and delivery of data mining and analysis applications for our customers in commercial and intelligence-related industries. As a member of the BESC team you will be exposed to a wide range of interesting data science applications, and be involved in the end-to-end definition, development, and delivery of systems that solve our customer’s most challenging problems.


At BESC we believe everyone’s contribution is important. We encourage a collaborative work environment where all members of the team contribute to the successful delivery of a system. As a Test Engineer you will work closely with development and requirements engineers to define, implement and maintain system test cases that will be used for functional testing to validate software being delivered to our customers. You will be involved in these phases of the testing process:

  • Working with team members to learn new technologies and techniques.
  • Writing test cases that describe system functionality.
  • Testing the system during development activities.
  • Reporting defects (bugs) related to the software under development.
  • Providing feedback to requirements and development engineers.
  • Writing system documentation.


Required:

  • One year software testing experience (manual or automated) using a formal methodology.
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills.
  • Strong/excellent computer skills.
  • Ability to work collaboratively with minimal direction. Self-starter.
  • Desire to contribute and improve themselves in a fast-moving program.
  • Detail-oriented. Highly organized.
  • Excellent writing and oral communications.
  • Minimum 2 yr diploma in a STEM curriculum.
  • US Citizenship Required.


Preferred (Not Required):

  • Prior experience working in an Agile development environment.
  • Experience with database testing (SQL, MS Access, etc.).
  • Requirements definition and refinement.
  • HTML/JavaScript fundamentals.
  • Programming experience (Java, Python).
  • Experience with automated testing tools.


BESC benefits include:

  • Excellent Compensation.
  • Flexible Work Schedule.
  • Training Provided.
  • Insurance 100% paid - Health, Dental, Life.
  • Competitive Vacation Package.
  • Retirement - SEP IRA Account.

Company Overview:

BESC is a rapidly growing company based in Ashburn, Virginia. We specialize in the design and development of innovative research, analysis and reporting systems. We sell by demonstrating high value capability to our customers and effectively fulfilling our commitments. We support clients in a variety of industries, including commercial business, government, defense, intelligence, and law enforcement. Our company is organized to maximize employee income while providing best value solutions to our customers. Our people are critical to our success and we invest in them by covering medical benefits and providing paid leave, training and excellent compensation. See our website www.4dvresearch.com for more information.
