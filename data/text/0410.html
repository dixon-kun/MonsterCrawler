Position Summary

Known for its scientific and operational excellence, Regeneron is a leading science-based biopharmaceutical company that discovers, invents, develops, manufactures, and commercializes medicines for the treatment of serious medical conditions. Regeneron commercializes medicines for eye diseases, high LDL-cholesterol, and a rare inflammatory condition and has product candidates in development in other areas of high unmet medical need, including oncology, rheumatoid arthritis, asthma, atopic dermatitis, pain, and infectious diseases.

Summary: Performs tasks related to the scheduling and planning of manufacturing operations, coordination of non-manufacturing activities such as maintenance and training involving manufacturing resources, and capacity model building.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:
• Generation and maintenance of daily manufacturing operations and resource schedules.
• Assist in the coordination of scheduling activities with both manufacturing and non-manufacturing groups.
• Development of effective shift and training schedules.
• Analyze and determine training needs related to personnel and production schedules.
• Perform data collection and analysis related to process durations, operator efficiencies, and characterizing scheduling delays.
• Create data models using spreadsheets, code, or other relevant methods.
• Assist in capacity planning decisions.
• Assist in improving communication between different shifts, process areas, and levels of the organization.


Education and Experience:
• BS/BA in Science, Engineer or related field required. MS in similar field preferred.
• Relevant biotech manufacturing experience is a plus.
• Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate information with attention to detail and accuracy.
• Background in data analytics or relevant data science field is preferred.
• Can work both independently as an original thinker and in a team setting.
• Strong communication skills and the ability to present complex data is preferred.
• Level commensurate with experience.

This is an opportunity to join our select team that is already leading the way in the Pharmaceutical/Biotech industry. Apply today and learn more about Regeneron’s unwavering commitment to combining good science & good business.
