          Brief Summary
         The Data Scientist (Operations Engineering) will be responsible for production modeling, financial analysis, module vendor comparison, comparative analysis, and informing PV system design of SoCore&#8217;s nationwide solar PV portfolio. &#160;This person will be in direct contact with the design, procurement and field operations teams to inform system design, vendor selection and production monitoring.&#160; The ideal candidate will have a strong foundation in engineering and experience with computer programming and data analysis within the energy space.
          Key Responsibilities
          Perform production estimates for solar PV projects using production estimation software such as PVSYST, PVWATTS, SAM, and Helioscope
          Prepare reports and comparisons between system configurations, module vendors, and multiple variables simultaneously for project optimization
          Remain up-to-date on new technologies, trends, and industry standards for production modeling to ensure installations are compliant with industry best practices and prudent assumptions within the PV energy market
          Monitors historical production of existing project installations to verify performance in accordance with financial expectations and address any discrepancies in the PVSYST models compared to actual system production over time
          Reports production and O&amp;M activity across departments
          Troubleshoots system issues and helps investigate root cause of frequent failures
          Performs system acceptance testing at the completion of project

          Takes initiative and ownership of projects
          Solves problems
          Communicates effectively (written &amp; verbal)

          Database Administration experience
          Experience with scripting languages including SQL,Python and JAVA
          Solid understanding of power generation plants, utility systems, and photovoltaic systems
          Experience with production estimating software tools such as PVSYST, PVWATTS, SAM, Helioscope
          Direct experience working with Field Service Engineers
          5-7 years of experience in a similar position, ideally in the renewable energy industry and in utility environment
