DATA ANALYST

Data is rarely perfect. We are looking for a Data Analyst who has gained experience with traditional data pipelines and is now looking for a new data challenge. You may use tools you have never used before. You will experience very challenging data and you will apply best practices, combined with thinking outside the box, to create solutions.

There will be routine work, tough puzzles, and you must truly love data. However, you will join a dynamic team that sees change as an opportunity to learn and grow. You will work in a cross functional group that spans Client Strategy, Data Management, Analytical Insights, and Product Solutions. You will use a keen eye for detail, an understanding of the client's industry and business practice, and common sense to solve data puzzles and problems.

What you'll be doing:

* Working with a variety of data related to traditional marketing and digital marketing, as well as supporting data (economy, weather, etc.)

* Using existing processes implemented in SAS to process incoming files and produce analytical data sets

* Producing reports and summaries

* Adhering to best practices, standards, and discipline

* Working in an agile environment where there is pressure for delivery and pressure for quality of output

* Partnering with statisticians and marketing strategists (account managers) on problem solving and troubleshooting data related issues

* Documenting your technical implementation

* Maintaining a knowledge base that is specific to your clients' projects

* Working with multifunctional teams responsible to deliver to a specific client and experiencing a variety of client problems

* Gaining exposure to mind-boggling problems and challenges that come with applicable statistics and models that are related to your data

* Integrating quality assurance into processes and helping to bring traditional best practices into this environment

* Mentoring team members

Skills, Talents, Experience you will need:

* To be a "tinkerer". We rarely have a solution that is obvious. You have to try different things

* To be ready to ask questions and learn from fellow team members and other departments regardless of current experience

* Experience as a Data Engineer or with Data Warehousing or Business Intelligence

* To have worked on R-based or Python-based projects where you acquired data from files or scraped data from the internet, integrated it and used it for a purpose such as reporting, analysis, regression etc.

* Experience in end-to-end ETL implementation for business operations, finance, or marketing

* Interest in working with imperfect data

* Bachelors in a hard science; Masters is a plus

* To be well versed in SQL

* Experience using PostgreSQL, Oracle, Hive, Base SAS with an extensive library of Macros, R, and Excel is desired

* 2-5 years of experience

In this position, you may not work with tools that you have worked with in the past. We are challenging the industry; our main tools are technology based and we utilize a variety of different tools and techniques – including the cutting edge. Our team is quick, adaptable, is ready to ask questions and is interested in learning about and improving the space they are in.

Why work with us?

Because you're smart, entrepreneurial and are ready to be challenged every day to break new ground in next-gen marketing cloud technology that empowers boardroom through to machine-to-machine decisions. You are willing to deliver excellence & innovation, fight for customer success, work collaboratively, and manage complexity.

Our customers use the MarketShare DecisionCloud to create an analytics control center that simplifies and scales the decision-making process. As a leader in our field, we are helping to influence tens of billions of dollars in marketing spend. We deliver a full spectrum of marketing decision support apps from best-in-class strategic optimization through to detailed digital attribution thus enabling rapid course corrections and recommendations to drive ROI and connect marketing to revenue.

About Us

MarketShare is a software and analytics company that uses data science and technology solutions to bring order to complexity and help our clients make better, faster, data-driven decisions – with confidence. MarketShare Culture -- What's important to us, and ideally to you, include:

* Comfort with ambiguity: We embrace the complexity of our clients' environments and believe that there are seldom-easy answers.

* Embracing the high-wire act: We're inventing the space where marketing meets math meets technology. This is a new category, and we have to innovate as we go.

* Intelligently curious: We have never met a problem that we didn't want to solve with solutions that are practical, innovative, and value-rich.

* Innovating Products: We are developing avant-garde SaaS solutions and cloud based products to make the analytics real and actionable

* Being a results-driven team player that is all about collaboration, growth, and excellence.

With today's explosion of data and consumer touch points, complexity keeps growing. Our team, our technology and our insights are the single place to turn to get the best possible recommendations for driving performance from both offline and online marketing investments. We align decision making from the most strategic to the most tactical. Our clients range across sectors including automotive, retail, insurance, financial, travel & leisure, technology, consumer electronics, entertainment, pharmaceuticals and others.

MarketShare has broad experience working with a fast-growing roster of major global brands.

Our solutions help marketing executives answer complicated questions such as:

* What is the effectiveness of our marketing investments in generating sales, and where should our next dollar be spent?

* How do TV, radio and print media drive online behaviors and resulting sales, and how do we balance our online-offline mix?

* Within online channels, how can we continually optimize our digital marketing across placements, publisher sites, keywords, etc.?

* How can I adjust my pricing to quickly changing market environments?

* How can I use proprietary data to see how my company compares against peers and generate recommendations to optimize media plan effectiveness.

MarketShare team has grown worldwide across Los Angeles, New York, San Francisco, London, Tokyo, Hyderabad, and Bangalore.
