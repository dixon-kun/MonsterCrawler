        Senior Operations Analyst
        Larson-Juhl

        Our Company
        Larson-Juhl US LLC, a subsidiary of Berkshire Hathaway; designs, manufactures, and distributes framing products in the United States and Canada. As the world's leading manufacturer and distributor of custom framing products we are a company committed to our Values:
        Customer always comes first.
        Fair and honest in all dealings.
        Respect for the individual.
        Excellence in products and service.
        Rewards tie to performance.
        Leadership by example.
        Our Opportunity
        We are seeking a proactive, independent thinker with strong communication and interpersonal skills to join our financial team. This position will report to the Director of Business Intelligence and work closely with organizational department heads and Executives across the organization. We are looking for somebody who is not just interested in creating tools for the organization to use but enjoys diving into challenges, is impact oriented and thrives on finding ways to &#8220;move the needle&#8221;.
        Primary Duties &amp; Responsibilities
        Conduct insightful, ad hoc analyses to investigate operational issues and/or researching issues they&#8217;ve found that are limiting the organization
        Assist with the development of dashboards and performance scorecards
        Determine new methods for delivering analytical based reporting to our Leaders
        Perform daily, weekly and monthly reviews and analyses of current processes using operational metrics and reports
        Review a variety of areas including operations, purchasing, inventory, distribution and facilities
        Actively engage and work closely with colleagues to understand how Larson Juhl operates and where improvements can be made to support the company's business model
        Understand and communicate the financial and operational impact of any changes
        Suggest changes to senior management using analytics to support your recommendations and actively participate in the implementation of approved changes
        Create informative, actionable and repeatable reporting that highlights relevant business trends and opportunities for improvement
        Create financial models of &#8220;what if&#8221; scenarios to help future business planning decisions
        Sources data from multiple platforms to create integrated views that can be used to drive decision making
        Leverages understanding of SQL and databases to manipulate data to drive insights
        Skills &amp; Attributes
        Bachelor&#8217;s degree in a business-related field, ideally with an emphasis in finance, economics, statistics, mathematics, accounting or data science
        At least five years of business-related experience is preferred with an emphasis in Operations and/or Logistics, preferably involving data analysis and financial modeling
        Solid technical knowledge and experience working with Excel, Word and PowerPoint - Database programming experience is a plus
        Strong critical thinking skills and the ability to gather, analyze and organize data using a logical and systematic process
        Ability to ascertain relevant and irrelevant data when conducting analytical work, visualize and articulate problems and concepts in a systematic manner, recognize patterns and issues in sets of data, and identify obstacles impeding project progression
        Ability to present ideas in a business-friendly language and tailor communications, formal or informal, to the audience
        Excellent interpersonal, verbal and writing skills are required along with strong attention to detail
        Excellent organizational skills and ability to manage and execute tasks
