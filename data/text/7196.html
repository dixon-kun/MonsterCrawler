Qualifications

Background/Training: Entrepreneurship, client service, expertise, technology and excellence are at the heart of HA&D, therefore we need talented and ambitious people who:

* Excellent and creative problem solver,have a high level of entrepreneurship and enjoy working in a young team managed as a start-up

* Are curious, willing to push themselves, and want to learn

* Results-oriented, positive, can-do attitude in face of challenging operating environment; At ease with ambiguity

* Have a proven record of leadership in a work setting and/or through extracurricular activities; able to act autonomously, bring structure and organization to work

* Have a working knowledge of data and analytics technologies (ideally the Hadoop ecosystem) and of SaaS technical issues (e.g., configuration, integration, etc.)

* Have experience in building data and analytics products and a demonstrated aptitude for analytics

* Able to work effectively with people at all levels in an organization

* Have experience in agile methodologies.

* 5 - 7 years of professional experience in product management roles, including meaningful experience in

* Strategic product management, product marketing

* Product design & development

* Technical product management, functional design

Who You'll Work With

You'll work on the within our Healthcare Analytics & Delivery team based in New York

The Healthcare Analytics team uses healthcare data (payer / provider / 3rd party data) and analytics to answer some of the most pressing questions our healthcare clients have today such as "How do we manage cost of care while improving quality?", "How do we remain competitive in the evolving healthcare landscape?", "Who are the most vulnerable patients and what interventions can we design to better care for them?".

The Healthcare Analytics team is part of New Ventures, which is a recently formed part of McKinsey focused on fostering innovations driven by analytics, design thinking, mobile, social and other technologies and integrating them into our client work. It is a foundation for—and expands our investment in—our entrepreneurial culture.

As one of the fastest-growing parts of our firm, New Ventures has more than 1,000 dedicated professionals (including more than 800 analysts and data scientists) and we're hiring more mathematicians, data scientists, designers, software engineers, product managers, client development managers and general managers.

What You'll Do

You will work with the McKinsey Healthcare Analytics & Delivery domain teams to identify how our experience and knowledge can be converted into competitively advantaged software products.

You'll work with our product development staff to build and execute product roadmaps. Together with the Tech Leads, you'll architect and oversee deployment of new products, and make existing products evolve to answer client's needs.

You'll have the opportunity to leverage your expertise in product strategy, product architecture, configuration & integration strategies, and long-term customer success. You will employ an affirmative, can-do approach to evaluating and pursuing new ideas, while also bringing judgment to ensure that the solutions always exceeds expectations from clients and McKinsey consultants.
