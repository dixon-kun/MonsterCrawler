         Job Description
        HomeAway is hiring Economists to report to our Head Economist (Justin Rao). This role will be applying cutting edge econometric and ML techniques to solve core business problems on one of the largest two-sided marketplaces in the world. The position requires both building economic models and frameworks, and estimating the core parameters of the model, working in tandem with data scientists and business partners. Areas of work include market design, demand estimation, behavioral economics, promotion optimization, optimal pricing and advertising effectiveness. There are opportunities to work with world class economists that we engage with outside of the company.
