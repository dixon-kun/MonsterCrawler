1)    Data Architect/Scientist/Engineer Lead - Honeywell | Jobs.com
        The primary responsibilities of a Data Architect are to assist projects in the design of data related structures and provide technical guidance to project teams about processes related to the life cycle of data from creation through destruction; and metadata related to these processes.
        A Data Architect performs the following specific duties and responsibilities:
        * Leads project requirements reviews and initial analysis of available data
        * Leads or reviews the design of application data structures using data modeling tools and insures data designs follow enterprise and data domain standards and processes
        * Gives direction to and participates with project teams in the implementation of processes which result in a high degree of data quality and integrity; and assists in the resolution of data quality issues
        * Insures data is properly classified for security purposes and insures data security designs meet enterprise standards
        * Coordinates construction of the database and database objects with the database administrators
        * Participates in the design and implementation of processing related to data archiving, retention and purging; data backups; and data recovery
        Data Architects may occasionally be required to be available during non-business hours and weekends to assist with off-hour implementations and problem resolution.
        All Data Architects are responsible for maintaining a high level of competency and knowledge of database, data warehouse, metadata, and data security concepts; and practical application of those concepts as they relate to providing cost effective business solutions.
        Basic Qualifications:
        * Bachelor's degree in Information Systems, Computer Science, Engineering; in lieu of a degree, 6 years of relevant work experience
        * 5 years of development experience in Birst, HANA, Business Intelligence, SAP, Oracle, MS SQL, etc
        Additional Qualifications:
        * Preference given to individuals with Greenbelt and DFSS certifications
        * Demonstrated experience designing and/or developing applications using relational databases and a knowledge of SQL
        * Basic project management skills
        * Birst development experience
        * HANA modeling experience
        * Business intelligence
        * SAP functional or technical knowledge
        * Formal data modeling or data architecture
        * Data warehouse design, development, or operations
        * Oracle, MS SQL Server, or other technical certifications
        * Self-motivated, self-starter, and an ability to effectively train and mentor others
