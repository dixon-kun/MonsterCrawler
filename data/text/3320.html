Job Summary

Job Description

As a member of the Sales Development team, you’ll leverage insights provided by the larger field marketing organization to contact prospective clients for a specific FICO solution or group of related solutions across an identified territory.  Specific responsibilities include:

  • Engaging in outbound calling to prospective clients to establish relationships, explore the prospect’s technology needs and brief the customer on FICO’s solution capabilities.
  • Collaborating with assigned Pre-Sales and Solution Sales experts to further develop and close identified sales opportunities.
  • Utilizing Salesforce tool to ensure best practice BANT processes are followed during client conversations leading to effective lead tracking.
  • Leveraging digital and inside sales prospecting tools to increase connect rates, timely and relevant messaging and enhance your overall sales network.

Requirements

  • 1-2 years of experience selling technology solutions with experience doing so within the banking and insurance industries a plus.
  • Demonstrated success in high volume customer prospecting activities to establish and build effective relationships.
  • Proven ability to quickly learn complex business and technical environments in order to uncover client objectives, internal issues and solution sale opportunities.
  • Experience using Salesforce and/or other technology tools to drive and manage prospecting activities a plus.
  • Excellent verbal and written communications skills.
  • Self-motivated with strong collaboration and teamwork skills aimed at achieving both individual and shared goals.
  • Bachelor’s degree required, preferably in a business, software engineering, data science or a related technical field of study.

Our Rewards Structure

  • Competitive base salary
  • Compensation Mix: 70-80% Base Salary; 20-30% Targeted Incentive
  • Incentive Drivers:  Sales Accepted Leads (SALs), MBOs
  • Development-focused culture supporting career advancement opportunities
  • Comprehensive health and welfare benefits program
  • 401(k) with 5% net match, immediate vesting
  • Flexible work environment with competitive paid-time-off policies

Why Make a Move to FICO?

At FICO, you can advance your career with a leading organization in one of the fastest-growing fields in technology today – Big Data analytics.   We have a 50+ year history of excellence in providing analytics and decision management solutions.  Here are a few examples of our impact globally:

  • Credit Scoring —150+ billion FICO Scores have been sold to date, making it the most used credit score in the world.
  • Fraud Detection and Security—2.5+ billion credit cards globally are protected by FICO fraud systems.
  • Lending —3/4 of US mortgages are approved using the FICO Score.

FICO’s proven science leverages the latest technology to drive consumer behavior insights and optimize business processes. We help many of the world’s leading banks, insurers, retailers, telecommunications providers and other firms reach a new level of success.

Our success is founded on really talented people – just like you – who enjoy innovative and high-performance environments with the opportunity to grow.  Join the FICO Family and help change the way business makes decisions!
