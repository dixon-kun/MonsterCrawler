Software Guidance & Assistance, Inc., (SGA), is searching for a Business Intelligence Architect for a full-time opportunity assignment with one of our premier clients in St. Petersburg, FL

Responsibilities :
  • The Business Intelligence Architect will serve as the technical lead owning the integration of measurements and the presentation of metrics across many business units.
  • As the primary technical expert, a BI Architect leads the team to design and build high quality analytical systems primarily within the Microsoft BI suite and other BI tools.
  • This individual will serve as a mentor and coach to the entire BI team and business stakeholders who are engaging in self-service business intelligence.
  • Assist management with the setting of priorities, defining resources needs, and estimating work efforts.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, with strong skills in facilitation and negotiation, as this role will regularly interact with management and business stakeholders across the organization.
  • Work closely with business and IT stakeholders in a collaborative, team environment to envision and implement modern BI platform architectures that are of extremely high quality.
  • Define data architectures for OLAP systems and large scale enterprise DW solutions.
  • Perform research and development with new technologies to ensure the most effective analytics for the business users.
  • Design complex reports and dashboards with the Power BI suite. Expert in SQL.
  • Full lifecycle development surrounding large scale data warehousing and business intelligence solutions.
  • Focus on information/data quality by establishing data standards including common vocabulary, naming conventions, and ETL or ELT rules.
  • Develop and implement a strategy for increasing adoption of BI practices across the company to help build a data-driven culture at all levels.
  • Evangelize BI capabilities among many business units and determine strategies for continuous improvement of the overall BI platform.

Required Skills :
  • 10 years data warehouse and business intelligence experience with common industry tools and methodologies ? data warehouse, ETL, data mart, etc.
  • 7 years of working experience with the Microsoft SQL Server platform.
  • Proven success in enterprise architecture, business information modeling, and system design.
  • Ability to provide guidance to junior level resources and working with offshore resources.
  • Experience in an agile development environment.
  • Extensive knowledge of business intelligence best practices, processes, tools, standards, and methodologies.
  • Experience with business intelligence for reporting, trend analysis, root/cause analysis, correlation analysis.
  • Expertise in at least 2 major BI toolsets (e.g. Tableau, Cognos, Power BI, MicroStrategy, Qlikview).
Preferred Skills :
  • Experience in the finance/insurance sector.
  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in related field or equivalent experience.
  • Data science knowledge.
  • Project management skills.
  • Knowledge in master data management.
  • Experience with offshore development model.
  • Knowledge or skills in additional technologies, such as SaaS and cloud solutions.
  • Experience with business process management tools, workflow solutions, collaboration solutions.
