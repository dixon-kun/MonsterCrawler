With a growing portfolio of top-tier clients, Wunderman specializes in designing consumer-centric strategies focused on combining creativity with data to build valuable relationships between consumers and brands.

We are seeking an Associate Project Manager to deliver on one of the largest clients in the New York agency.  This is an exciting opportunity to deliver powerful solutions across the full spectrum of data, strategy, technology and marketing services, in order to help our client maximize the opportunities provided by our integrated offering.


  • You will have previous experience in the management and delivery of online advertising campaigns, including social media
  • You will have a strong client facing experience and have experience in delivering projects throughout the full project management life-cycle and managing client expectations throughout the project.
  • You will be scoping, budgeting and managing the client's expectations. You will also be responsible for the financial monitoring of projects, ensuring that projects are scoped accurately and are profitable, making sure that all projects are delivered on time and on budget and to the highest quality
  • Involved at all client project briefing to collect as much information as needed / possible to support discovery
  • Coordinate the discovery process with account and interactive development teams to collect critical client requirements
  • Collaborate with various departments within the organization to collect project requirements, including project milestones and budgets
  • Notify account teams early of potential budget overages or shortfalls; ensure overages or additional budgets or investments are approved as required

  • Bachelors Degree
  • 1-2 years’ experience as an Interactive Project Manager or Producer with some knowledge and experience across multi-channel media (digital, print, social) and ability to apply sound project management philosophy, tools and techniques to deliver marketing and advertising solutions
  • Ad agency experience is a plus
  • Experience managing social media projects is a plus
  • Strong problem solver with sound financial acumen is a strict requirement
  • MS Project is a plus, high level of computer competency and associated software (Microsoft Suite: Outlook, Excel, Word, PowerPoint, and Adobe Acrobat Pro) is a plus
  • Ability to establish and maintain effective relationships to gain trust and respect is a strict requirement

Wunderman is Creatively Driven. Data Inspired . A leading global digital agency, Wunderman combines creativity and data into work that inspires people to take action and delivers results for brands. Wunderman Health is a division of Wunderman.   In 2015, industry analysts named Wunderman a leader in marketing database operations as well as a strong performer in customer engagement strategy. Headquartered in New York, the agency brings together 7,000 creatives, data scientists, strategists and technologists in 175 offices in 60 markets. Wunderman is a WPP company (NASDAQ: WPPGY). For more information, please visit www.wunderman.com and follow us @Wunderman.
