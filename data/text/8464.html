Job Description As an Ecommerce Analytics Manager, you will lead efforts to create outstanding digital marketing and ecommerce analytic capabilities at Pizza Hut US. This will include shaping performance monitoring with senior leaders, generating ideas for improved customer experiences, assisting with digital marketing assessments and collaborating on next generation projects with digital media, data science, and marketing.

Whether the interaction is "Hey Alexa," the press of a Pie Top, or a mouse click, your expertise will help our product managers understand customer experience gaps, prioritize opportunities, and track improvements. Upstream to our ecommerce experience, you will help digital marketers optimize across placement channels. You will work with senior leaders in the company to help drive understanding of, and improvement in, our business results. If you have great digital ecommerce analytics expertise, a high Digital IQ, exceptional organizational skills, and great communication abilities, then apply with us as an Ecommerce Analytics Manager.

* Shape KPI's and metrics used in daily, weekly, and monthly management of ecommerce performance and digital marketing performance and share insights with digital and product stakeholders

* Provide insights into the performance of digital marketing and campaigns including areas of opportunity, assistance with ROAS, and in-funnel performance of campaign audiences

* Identify, assess, and present opportunities to improve the customer journey from marketing-driven acquisition through to conversion using recurring reporting and ad hoc analyses

* Support key projects with data-driven insights to enable localization, personalization, and optimization that affect click paths and product selection

* Assist with recommending, reviewing, and management of digital analytics tools and vendors

* Consult and potentially lead web analytics methodologies such as experimentation and testing, competitive analysis, surveys, and market research

* Work with the Business Intelligence team to import selected digital analytics data back to a central data store for integration with other data sources

* Escalate of usability and performance issues as required; maintaining ownership of issues through resolution

Minimum Requirements EDUCATION:

* Degree in Computer Science, Economics, Engineering, Mathematics, or related analytical field

* MBA and/or advanced degree a plus

EXPERIENCE (and other qualifications):

* 5+ years' experience working with Google Analytics and associated digital analytics ecosystem (e.g., Google Tag Manager, Google AdWords integration, Google Analytics IQ)

* 2+ years' experience working with Google Analytics Enhanced Ecommerce

* Preferred experience working with analytics integrations for a single-page application

* Experience using project / task / bug management tool such as Rally / JIRA / Basecamp

* Experience creating analytics for both website and mobile apps particularly measuring campaigns' abilities to impact upper, mid and lower funnel metrics

* Experience creating and interpreting dashboards for weekly business management, educating peers and co-workers on efficient use of Google analytics

* Experience measuring and communicating effectiveness and improvements to digital marketing campaigns
