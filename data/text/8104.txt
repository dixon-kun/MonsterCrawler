        Implementation Consultant, Professional Services
        Duties:
        * Provides business focused as well as specific product expertise for client assignments
        * Guarantees the effective implementation of products with respect to the client requirements
        * Represents PROS in an effective and professional manner in customer-facing engagements
        Responsibilities
        Providing product expertise
        * Analyze and document customer business &amp; technical requirements
        * Documents the clients requirements and translates these into implementation specifications
        * Configure PROS solution and/or guide clients in their own configuration
        * Constructing solutions in supplementary technologies to PROS solution e.g. database scripting, report writing, javascripting
        * Participate in and/or lead test planning, document test plans, and test conditions; perform solution testing, document test results, and assist with defect resolution
        * Participate in and/or guide clients through production launch
        * Be available and approachable as application/training/support resource during implementation and training
        * Identify and present Professional Services opportunities to appropriate PROS team members
        * Augment the PROS library of best practices by sharing lessons learned, case studies, and other artifacts learned from your implementation experience or shared with you by others at PROS
        * Contribute to peer network through which to provide and receive solution and skills best practices
        * Utilize expert knowledge of pricing software solutions and price management solutions to maximize customer value
        * Contribute to 100% customer satisfaction by providing timely and functional solutions
        * Work alongside multiple disciplines within PROS, such as business development, product management, and development.
        * Leads training sessions for clients and/or partners
        Acting as a project team member
        * Attends customer project meetings
        * Consistently communicates and tracks progress on his/her assigned tasks
        * Contributes to the update of internal project scorecards
        * Reports progress to the client and the assigned PROS project lead
        * Involved in the creation of sales proposals for scope changes, upgrades, etc.
        Wider responsibilities
        * Represents the voice of the customer through motivating enhancement requests
        * Contributes to best practices and to the knowledge base (training material, design templates, implementation packages, etc.)
        * Consistently develops and extends his/her product expertise
        * Acts as an internal reference for co-workers
        Profile and Skills:
        * General or computer engineering degree
        * Vocational qualifications in computing
        * Demonstrable experience within software development, ideally Java
        * Proven record of configuring packaged CRM software, ERP software, E-commerce portals or similar
        * Proficiency in business process analysis and the usage of associated methods and tools
        * Experience in leading the deployment of solutions based on package software products
        * Industry-specific in one of the following areas &#8211; manufacturing, telecommunications, insurance, distribution &#8211; would be a plus
        Technical skills
        * Strong experience configuring solutions on large-scale pricing, SCM, CRM, CPQ or ERP implementations
        * Good knowledge of SQL and relational databases (Oracle, SQL Server)
        * Good knowledge of a scripting language such as VBA, PHP or Javascript or similar
        * Exposure to distributed 3-tier architecture, ideally J2EE
        Language skills
        * Excellent business and day to day usage of English is vital (written and spoken)
        * French/German language skills would be a plus
        Professional qualities
        * Excellent inter-personal and communication skills

        * Experience with project management tools
