This is an opportunity to join a team that is working to revolutionize healthcare. As an early member of the team you will play a significant role in leading that charge. You can be one of the first experts to have ever tackled these problems. The novel work you perform will have a meaningful impact on worldwide medical practices. You will have the opportunity to partner with world-leading clinicians on a regular basis and lead the creation of new diagnostic models.
In this role, you will:
  • Provide guidance to data collection teams on test protocols including design of experiments, sample size, and statistical distributions.
  • Design and implement networks to provide automation of clinical tasks using one or more of medical images, electronic medical records, waveforms and clinical reports.
  • Design and implement scene labeling techniques for medical images
  • Design and implement image preprocessing and alignment algorithms
  • Hyper parameter optimization
  • Demonstrate algorithms meet accuracy requirements on general subject population through statistical analyses and error estimation
  • Write technical reports summarizing development, analysis, training and validation of the algorithms
  • Provide design input specifications, requirements, and guidance to software engineers for algorithm implementation for product development
  • Collaborate with team members and external experts
  • Provide presentations to the management and cross-functional teams
  • Stay up-to-date in the field of machine . Apply new technologies in development of products
