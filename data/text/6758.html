POSITION SUMMARY:

ADP Small Business Services (SBS) Insurance Services is expanding its global product & technology team and is looking to hire a software architect to work on exciting new initiatives that employ the latest technologies. You will be responsible for designing new client self-service, B2B commercial insurance broker solutions while working hands on with the different agile teams implementing them. You will partner with internal development teams, product managers, innovation lab teams and external partners to create new capabilities and leverage existing frameworks. SBS provides solutions that power and help nearly 500,000 small businesses achieve their goals. We have a passion to provide the very best services & products to our clients in a way that delights them and enables them to focus on what matters to them, their employees and their customers.

We strive for every interaction to be driven by our CORE values: Insightful Expertise, Integrity is Everything, Service Excellence, Inspiring Innovation, Each Person Counts, Results-Driven, & Social Responsibility.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

* Design technical architectures for complex solutions

* Evangelize technical best practices, incorporate emerging solutions and employ scalable designs that can be easily adopted

* Drive delivery of software solutions through hands-on prototyping and immersion across multiple lean/scrum teams

* Provide technical and thought leadership, and mentor developers during code review sessions

* Take pride in your communications and collaborate to innovate and empower team members

* Adapt and learn quickly, identify opportunities for improvement and act as a business owner to prioritize and take action

QUALIFICATIONS REQUIRED:

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering or related field, and years of relevant work experience

* Minimum of 10 years of experience writing great software and architecting client-facing solutions

* Demonstrated mastery or working knowledge leading solutions that have employed: Java/J2EE, JavaScript (incl. AngularJS and ReactJS), JQuery, Spring, HTML5, Hibernate/JPA, WebSphere/Liberty, Oracle DB and PL/SQL, incl. stored procedures

* Passion for development operations including experience implementing and/or evangelizing continuous integration/build and automated testing framework principles using proven or emerging tools (e.g. Selenium, JUnit/TestNg, Cucumber, Mocha, Jenkins, Maven or equivalents)

* API design experience to integrate two isolated systems with independent domains considering data privacy

* Experience with SOA and micro services architecture, employing design patterns for scalability - including a RESTful web service tier in client-facing solutions

* Experience with distributed version control tools (Git/GitHub/Bitbucket) and source control governance on a global team

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:

Preference may be given to candidates who have the following:

* Working knowledge of Agile (scrum) SDLC management, including experience as a scrum master or contributing architect leveraging Jira/Rally

* Experience incorporating event-based APIs, messaging and middleware technology (e.g. IBM MQ/IIB, MuleSoft) in software solutions

* Operational experience with Splunk, Introscope, SiteScope, performance testing and caching technologies

* Familiarity with NoSQL database (preferably MongoDB or Cloudera Hadoop) and NodeJS best practices

* Exposure to small business software development in human resources, insurance or financial services domains
