POSITION SUMMARY:

ADP's Global Enterprise Technology and Solutions organization is seeking a forwarding thinking team player who will contribute to the architecture, design, implementation and ongoing automation and engineering of tools to support our distributed database deployment as part of the Database and Tools team. The Software Database Engineer functions as a key resource between database administrators, software engineers and the operations teams. In this role you will leverage your programming as well as database expertise to develop and engineer solutions that take manual processes and automate them for improved efficiencies. As databases are central to what we do, automating for a high volume, 24/7 production environment will require unique approaches, low risk implementations and forward thinking.

The ideal candidate must be creative, self-motivated, innovative, detail oriented, and eager to work with the latest technologies along with a strong desire to maintain and help improve ADP's applications and supporting services.

Responsibilities (including but not limited to):

Partner closely with database administrators, software engineers, and operations teams to architect and engineer solutions to manual processes

Achieve and maintain a holistic understanding of ADP applications, technologies, and services

Drive continuous improvement of automation through the Database and Tools organization to ensure optimal operations, performance, and stability of services

Collaborate with key stakeholders (Service Managers, Project Managers, Database Administrators and Operations) to gather and develop requirements

Promote use of innovative new technology and best practices for a large scale enterprise

Qualifications:

* 5+ years of IT/Internet industry experience in a high volume, 24/7 production environment

* Ability to lead and influence in a technical lead role

* Experience in one of the following database technologies preferred (Oracle, SQL Server, MySQL, Mongodb, Cassandra, Gemfire, Cloudera Hadoop)

* Application design experience

* Hands on experience in one or more languages (Python, JAVA, C/C++ etc)

* Must work well with cross functional teams

* Ability to work well under pressure & complete project deliverables within deadline and with minimum supervision

* Excellent team player

* Strong communication skills both verbal & written

* Experience with automation and monitoring technologies as related to integration with Database technologies

* Experience with cloud technologies / methodologies strongly desired

Education

* B.S. in Computer Science / Engineering from reputed US university preferred (or related field or equivalent experience; Graduate degree preferred)
