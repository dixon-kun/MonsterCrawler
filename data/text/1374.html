Test Automation Engineer

CCC provides the technology, data and insight needed to help our customers make informed, accurate auto collision claims and repair management decisions. As the industry leader, CCC works with more than 350 insurance carriers – including the largest carriers in the country – and more than 21,000 repair facilities, processing the majority of automotive claims in the U.S. each year. Our software solutions improve our customers' ability to efficiently manage the claims and repair process by improving communication between property/casualty insurance carriers, automotive collision repairers, parts suppliers, rental car companies, and vehicle owners, among others. Our decision-support tools draw from insights contained in our extensive data warehouse, which includes aggregated claims and repair information from millions of claims-related transactions processed through CCC over the last 30 years. CCC leads the industry in technology innovation, including the latest mobile technology and apps, and continues to make strategic investments in areas that will help existing and future customers get consumers back in their cars quickly following an accident.

You can find out more about CCC Information Services Inc. by visiting the company's web site at www.cccis.com

Team & Position Overview

The Python Test Engineer in Data Science is responsible for defining the automated software testing approach and ensuring its successful implementation for the Data Science team. The role involves identifying the appropriate testing tools and methodologies, implementing the tests, and providing guidance on the corresponding resources and requirements for the defined approach.

Job Duties

* Engineer and implement automated software tests for predictive analytics models to ensure accuracy and consistency of predictions

* Debugs complex software modules, working from written and/or verbal descriptions of problems

* Develops and executes unit tests, end-to end customer driven test cases, automated regression testing

* Collaborate with others to define and continuously improve the QA processes and standards for the team

* Work closely with team to understand how product changes affect the maintenance of test cases and the QA testing environments

* Participate in review sessions of business requirements and contribute to design discussions

* Identify and resolve issues/defects within testing environments in a timely manner and continuously develop functional and/or regression tests to ensure the code is functioning as designed

* Manage defect tracking and resolution processes so that a consistent, documented process is used and followed and maintain a well-organized records of test results

* Qualify and quantify new, improved testing models and produce documentation and thorough communication during

Qualifications

* MUST have strong skills in Python.

* Consistent focus on solving problems and delivering thoughtful analysis

* Ability to test individual components or perform end-to-end system tests

* Experience working in a team-oriented, collaborative environment

* Organized and detail-oriented; able to effectively perform multiple/concurrent tasks within deadlines in a dynamic environment

* Ability to translate requirements and/or designs into test cases

* Ability to troubleshoot issues and narrow down possible causes before assigning issues to developers

* Ability to identify and research additional testing tools

* Ideally 2 plus years of experience with Python programming languages, including experience designing, testing, and extending production applications

* Experience with one or more Python testing tools such as; tox, nose, pytest, unittest (2), doctest, mock is a plus

* Experience with predictive modeling is a plus

* Experience with other languages is a plus: C/C++, Perl, bash.

Education

The ideal candidate has either a Bachelor's Degree in engineering or mathematics with a scientific/operational research specialization and 2-3 years of experience in testing design or a Master's Degree and 1-3 years of experience in testing design.
