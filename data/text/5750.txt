        Our Opportunity:
        Chewy is looking for a bright, analytical, and enthusiastic Merchandising Operations Analyst to join our Merchandising Operations Team based in Dania Beach, FL. Rockstars will leverage business acumen and technical skills to drive business efficiency and strategy. Keys to success also include strong accountability, a passion for innovation, and hustle.
        What you'll do:
        * Conduct research through detailed analysis of historical data in order to identify opportunities.
        * Monitor business trends, analyze data, and develop forecasts for our eCommerce retail platform.
        * Develop tools to assist in the opportunity identification process.
        * Analyze catalog and competitor assortment and use findings to contribute to overall retail structure as well as future assortment planning and competitive strategy.
        * Develop tools to create efficiencies such as quickly identifying optimal source offerings for each item in the catalog.
        What you'll need:
        * Minimum of 3-5 years working in a data analysis-intensive environment.
        * Excel guru able to write and understand VBA.
        * Heavy experience working in Tableau or similar data visualization platform.
        * Strong experience writing and optimizing SQL queries.
        * BA/BS in Statistics, Finance, Business, Computer Engineering, or similar field. Less importance will be placed on degree if candidate demonstrates applicable experience.
        * Some experience in web programming (HTML/Javascript frameworks) or similar

        * Demonstrated excellent business judgment with strong written and oral communication skills.
        * Demonstrated ability to manage multiple projects - prioritization, planning and task delegation.
        * Track record of taking ownership and driving results in an environment where tactical and strategic activities are expected to be driven in parallel.
        * Demonstrated ability to work in a team environment.
