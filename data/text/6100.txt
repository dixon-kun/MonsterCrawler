        Hulu's Reporting, Analytics, and Data Science Technical Program Management team is seeking a Senior Technical Program Manager who will be an exceptional addition to our growing team. As a Senior Technical Program Manager at Hulu, you will lead complex and cross team programs and contribute to establishing the vision and technology in conjunction with our Data Quality Engineers and business stakeholder. This vision will drive the Quality and Governance of Hulu's Big Data Environment enabling the business to transform data into value. The ideal candidate has a strong focus on understanding business expectations and requirements, sets a high bar for him/herself, likes to solve problems, is tenacious when developing and instituting new processes and influencing the adoption across technology and business. This person should be intellectually curious and an innovator. If you are someone who thrives in a team environment and gains personal satisfaction when accomplishing challenging projects while partnering with teams across Hulu, then this is a great role for you.

        * Introduce new concepts across the organization.

        * Self-starter, able to determine a challenge and provide and action a solution.
