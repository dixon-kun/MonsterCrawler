Purpose:

The Data Analyst Senior II, Data Analytics & Fraud Forensics reports to the Manager, Data Analytics & Fraud Forensics. The Senior II's primary responsibility is to execute high quality data analytics projects in support of Internal Audit, Compliance, and Fraud Team engagements, in a timely manner and in accordance with professional standards and occasionally overseeing more junior staff. Accountable for departmental data analytics and fraud investigation tools, the Data Analytics & Fraud Forensics team is responsible for leveraging organizational data for analysis, monitoring, investigation, and planning activities. The Senior II is accountable for producing workpapers and deliverables (such as reports and data visualizations) that are professionally written for an executive audience and that reflect an understanding of company objectives and capabilities, control requirements, and industry best practices. The position is responsible for maintaining connections to, and proficiency in, established organizational data repositories to facilitate data analytics work. The Senior II is also responsible for leading various computer forensic and investigative activities in support of Fraud Team investigations and coordination with technical resources in the Information Security and Information Services departments. The Senior II is also responsible for assisting the manager with training the staff in data analytics tools and techniques. Senior II is able to evidence leadership capabilities to facilitate change in a constructive and collaborative manner. This position will be expected to lead complete projects including meetings with departmental and external management.

Responsibilities:

* Department Goals: Successfully manages many competing demands and is able to manage complete projects on time and within budgeted hours.Enhance the culture through the execution of analytics in support of Internal Audits, Compliance Projects, and Fraud Team investigations.Increase departmental capabilities for full population testing (and reduce reliance on sampling) in audit work to enhance insight into the severity and frequency of identified audit exceptions.Meets departmental goals for productivity and is proactive in searching out additional tasks and projects during downtimes.

* Leadership:Participates in and occasionally leads departmental and organizational special projects. Possesses strong public speaking / presentation skills and is comfortable leading opening and closing meetings, including explaining issues to executives and handling constructive disagreements to resolution. Builds and maintains collaborative relationships with key subject matter experts and leaders outside of the department who are central to furthering Data Analytics initiatives (i.e. data warehouse teams, information security, etc.).Provides feedback to auditors on their performance and is able to train auditors on appropriate tools and techniques of data analytics and fraud forensics. Proactively fosters an understanding of, and monitors changes in, the Data Analytics and Healthcare industries. Assists department Managers and Directors in the evaluation of applicants for open positions.

* Monitors project work for timeliness and adherence to departmental quality standards and validates evidence is organized to fully support conclusions and findings.Identifies root cause of complex issues and proposes valuable and effective solutions, with knowledge of the UPMC organization. Reviews all workpapers as appropriate and finalizes deliverable drafts for review by Discipline or Department Management, ensuring professional writing and value-added industry perspective.

* Risks & Industry:Identifies and contributes analytic approaches to improve risk assessment, auditing planning, root cause identification, and perform targeted monitoring.Able to conduct a walk-through to understand key business processes and their risks, the data generated from the business process, and how the appropriate use of that data within analytics. Demonstrates a strong understanding of business processes and is able to create or supervise a process flow to review with the client.Contributes to ongoing departmental goal of entity-wide risk identification and logging for the creation of Audit and Compliance Plans.Possesses advanced understanding of IT, data analysis, and healthcare terminology/concepts and industry risks. Participates in industry and / or discipline professional organizations. Leverages their professional network of contacts to stay current with industry developments.

* Technology:Possesses strong knowledge of analytical techniques, computer and mobile forensics, data querying and manipulation technologies, business intelligence and data visualization tools.Works with the Manager to distribute technical and procedural knowledge across the team and identify relevant training where needed.Able to research and analyze new and complex business, regulatory, clinical, IT, and operational processes to identify key data analysis opportunities and risks, and explain them to non-technical personnel. Stays current with new and emerging technologies in data analysis and changes in the Organization's IT systems. Identifies opportunities for advanced data analytics, focused monitoring, and/or data visualization.Assists with developing data extractions which allow for a one pull multiple use model so as to limit the impact to backend systems. Possesses strong knowledge of internal and external control expectations, frameworks, and regulations.Possesses strong analysis and software skills with, data mining, and data visualization, and workpaper technology.Serves as subject matter expert in the daily execution of data analytics and fraud technologies and techniques.

* Workpapers & Reports:Utilizes and initiates the revision of Data Analytics templates and guidance documents. Also assists with communication/training of these standards throughout the department, under the direction of the Manager.Conducts continuous auditing tests and analyzes complex datasets for a variety of patterns including fraud, control breakdown and regulatory non-compliance, succinctly conveying the findings to management. Validate source data integrity and evaluate results and variances from expectations. Develop and utilize good coding practices when scripting analytics with ACL (or other tools) to allow for repeatability, QA testing, and peer reviews. Script analytics in accordance with departmental standards.Participates in departmental initiatives to improve the use of data visualization in audit, compliance, and fraud team reports. Capable of developing and approving strong testing steps for complex statistical analytics opportunities.

* Bachelors degree and 6 years related work experience (or 4 years and preferred certification or Masters degree), including 3 years of experience in the areas of Data/Business Analytics, Data Warehousing, Business Intelligence, Information Technology/Information Security/IT Auditing.

* Professional Certification and/or Masters degree are preferred.

* Degree Preferences: Data Science, Business Analytics, Health Information Technology, Information Technology/Systems, Information Security, Computer Forensics, Computer Science.

* Experience Preferences: Data Analytics, Business Intelligence, IT Auditing, Information Security, Computer Forensics, Information Technology, Health Care, Database Management.

* Certification Preferences: CISA, CISSP, CFE, CIA, CPA, CHC, CCEP, Six Sigma, ACDA.
