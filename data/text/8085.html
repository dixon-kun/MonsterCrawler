PROS: The revenue and profit realization company. We deliver the perfect blend of simplicity and data science to help customers generate meaningful revenue growth, expand and sustain profitability and modernize business processes. Realize your Potential with PROS.

The ADR will support the Executive Account Manager by nurturing and managing their early stage funnel development and the development of key influencer relationships. The ADR will profile and document territory target accounts and opportunistic accounts, including key influencers and partners. S/he will be responsible for identifying business opportunities and setting up appointments with P&L owners and other key stakeholders and influencers in these accounts. They will also work on inbound enquiries and together with Marketing and the Executive Account Manager mobilise, nurture and develop leads to a point where they are ready for active sales involvement.

Primary Responsibilities:

* Provides ongoing territory account development and nurturing various regions across APAC with focus on Australia and New Zealand

* Engages Automation Qualified Leads (AQLs), contacts in Target Accounts, partners, and inbound enquiries

* Generates demand and nurtures potential interested accounts through a clearly defined qualification process

* Supports the territory development needs of the PROS Executive Account Manager responsible for APAC

* Conducts detailed target account & local partner screening and profiling

* Appointment setting with key influencers and P&L owners (Sr. Manager/Director level or higher)

* Supports territory trade shows/events and other Marketing initiatives

Skills and Experience:

* Some industry experience working in similar role for an enterprise technology corporation promoting and/or selling business critical applications, SaaS experience a plus

* Native in English, and business fluent in 1 other major APAC language (desirable)

* Experience of Enterprise B2B phone sales and lead nurturing through various online means

* Experience of selling business-critical enterprise software applications

* Experience in working with a partner network

* Demonstrable experience of effective telephone communication skills to board-level executives

* Good listening and documentation skills

* Ability to plan work and communicate with Sales leaders, consulting teams plus Marketing groups effectively

* Good working knowledge of Microsoft Office and Salesforce.com

* Outgoing personality

* Team player

KPIs:

* Mutually agreed number of target accounts profiled and qualified in/out per month

* Mutually agreed number of outbound activities (calls, emails, etc.)

Mutually agreed number of Meetings set and Sales validated opportunities over an agreed timeframe

About PROS

PROS Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: PRO) is a revenue and profit realization company that helps B2B and B2C customers consistently realize their potential through the perfect blend of simplicity and data science. PROS offers solutions to accelerate sales, formulate winning pricing strategies and align product, demand and availability. PROS customers experience meaningful revenue growth, sustained profitability and modernized business processes because of PROS revenue and profit realization solutions. To learn more, visit pros.com.
