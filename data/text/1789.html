Job Summary:

The Manager, Customer Success Onboarding and Adoption is a senior leadership role within our Customer Success organization.  The successful candidate will serve as a member of the Customer Success Executive Committee and oversee all aspects of early-stage Adoption and ongoing Program Management within the Customer Success organization.  This position will manage both other managers and senior level Individual Contributors and help develop Blackbaud’s strategy for ensuring all clients are succeeding with our solutions.  Importantly, this role is heavily accountable for making sure newly configured/installed clients experience consistent, sustainable adoption of all product features within the first 90 days of using our products, with heavy emphasis on our most strategic products, including RE NXT, FE NXT and Luminate.

Key Responsibilities:

Leads a team of Customer Success Adoption Specialists who work with new clients to make sure they are activating and actively using all key features of Blackbaud products within the first 90 days of product use, across all Blackbaud products (i.e. RE NXT, FE NXT, OLX, eTAP, LUM, etc..


Directly manages the leader of the Luminate Go! Program, which is a team of Adoption specialists helping our largest On-Line Marketing clients experience early success with that platform. Heavily metric’d leadership role, critical to the broader transformation we’re driving to increase our focus on making sure clients are successful with the products they buy from Blackbaud.

Manages a team of Data Science (Analyst) professionals assessing our installed base of 25,000 customers, identifying trends, guiding CSM’s on actions and alerts, proactively determining risk within our customer base, and providing strategic guidance to the Customer Success Executive Committee

Has responsibility for all Success Messaging, including the developing of our ongoing Messaging strategy, ensuring consistency in delivery of programs/messages to our base that help them be more successful with our solutions. Also manages the internal/associate communications across the COO’s broader organization, i.e. weekly Customer Success Spotlights, etc

This position is both a Manager of Manager’s and a Manager of senior level Individual Contributors as indicated above. This team is expected to grow significantly over the next two years and requires a senior level leader to develop the overall Customer Success Adoption and Programs strategy to propel that growth.

Education & Experience Requirements:

5+ years previous management experience
3+ years experience leading various aspects of Customer Success organizations
Strong Executive Presentation skills as this role will report out key metrics to Blackbaud ELT and GMBU’s EC monthly
Demonstrated familiarity with early stage product adoption and strategies to drive high levels of usage increases
Ability to work well in a matrixed environment where ability to influence others not immediately in your control is important
Knowledge of the Philanthrophic industry and associated use cases for Blackbaud Solutions
