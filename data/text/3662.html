This is a great opportunity to join a fast growing business and fast growing team, and make an immediate and lasting impact. As a Data Analyst, Fraud and Risk at Upwork, you will be responsible for making Upwork the world’s safest and most trusted online workplace.  You’ll play a critical role working with Product and Operations teams to tackle a range of Fraud and Risk problems.

Your Responsibilities:

  • Manage loss and chargebacks through a combination of rules and models. Detect and prevent fraud through extensive data collection and analysis. Leverage predictive analytics and modeling to identify opportunities to solve the wide range of Fraud & Risk problems
  • Leverage tools like R, Python to drive efficient analytics reporting, extract and manipulate large set of data, and work with Data Science team in building detection models
  • Design and generate reports/dashboards to track risk management performance, policy enforcement/impact and fraud review effectiveness/efficiency. Conduct complex data analysis to identify opportunities for optimizing resource and minimizing loss
  • Work closely with the cross functional teams (Operations, Product) to translate requirements into technical specifications, and to quantify the risk exposure as well as the downstream impact of product / strategy improvements
  • Develop, enhance, and maintain in-house datasets, platforms and tools to optimize operations

What it takes to catch our eye:

  • Master's degree in quantitative field, or Bachelor's degree with 1~3 years of related working experience, or Bachelor's degree with 3~5 years of related working experience
  • Strong SQL and Excel and PowerPoint skills
  • Experience / knowledge in at least one scripting language (Python, PHP, etc.)
  • Solid understanding of fundamental concepts in statistics and data science
  • Passion for Upwork and a strong motivation to solve challenging risk problems to protect company revenue

How to really knock our socks off:

  • Expertise in SQL and experience in handling large scale structured/unstructured data
  • Demonstrated experience in analysis and presentation of results in a clear and effective manner
  • Familiarity with at least one scripting language such as Unix Shell Scripting, Python, etc.
  • Predictive analytics experience a plus
  • Strong interest or previous experience in working in risk & fraud domain
  • Ability to work independently as part of a small  team
  • Desire to work in a fast-paced environment and manage priorities across multiple simultaneous projects
