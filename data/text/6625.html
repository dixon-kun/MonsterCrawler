Position Title: Sr. Solution Developer, Senior Analyst

Location: Denver, CO.

Our Story

Merkle is a global data-driven, technology-enabled performance marketing agency and the largest independent agency in the US for CRM, digital, and search. For more than 28 years, Fortune 1000 companies and leading nonprofit organizations have partnered with us to build and maximize the value of their customer portoflios. We work with brands like Dell, T-Mobile, Samsung, Geico, Regions, Kimberly-Clark, AARP, Lilly, Sanofi, NBC Universal, DIRECTV, American Cancer Society, Susan G. Komen, and many others to build and execute customer centric business strategies.

Role Purpose

The Solution Manager, Digital Messaging is a unique hybrid of business and technical consultant with the ability to operate as a data scientist, digital tech lead, and business analyst. As the owner of email solutions for multiple clients, this person is responsible for the successful onboarding and overall technological direction for those clients and works as a liaison between the account management and operations team to design and deliver high quality, scalable technical solutions. Part of this role involves serving as a business intelligence lead and architecting dashboards and reports using Tableau, and converting legacy Excel reports to Tableau. The successful candidate will be able to display thought leadership, technical prowess, and the ability to manage cross-division, cross-functional teams from project inception to completion.

Key Responsibilities

* Demonstrate technical expertise while scoping and driving the creation of digital solutions, email campaigns, email reporting, and data integration.

* Serve as the email team's reporting liaison and "voice of reason" toward other Merkle teams and clients, developing dashboards and reports that people want to use because they eschew clutter and display the right information in the right way.

* Gather business, marketing, and technical requirements in order to craft a comprehensive and scalable solution.

* Ensure alignment of business requirements with Merkle standards and policies, and with industry best practices.

* Architect data feeds, platform configuration, and components needed to run complex automated email campaigns.

* Proactively identify and resolve problems including client data feed issues.

* Stay abreast of industry technology innovations, new business practices, and third-party technology solutions.

* Help us continuously improve by identifying, creating, and enhancing internal systems and processes based on recurring client requests or evolving industry standards.

* Act as a consultant and technical expert on one of the "marketing clouds" (Salesforce ExactTarget, Oracle Responsys/Eloqua, or Adobe Campaign) to serve clients' needs.

Key Skills and Experience

* Bachelor's degree in a technical field, or work experience that demonstrates comparable abilities.

* 3+ years experience in information technology or technical marketing, with at least 2 years in a client-facing position.

* Tableau (or a comparable BI package) experience coupled with a strong desire to further our entire team's competency around the visual display of marketing information.

* Experience in a technical leadership role designing, building, and executing complex automated email campaigns in a leading ESP, such as Salesforce ExactTarget, Oracle Responsys or Eloqua, Adobe Campaign, Epsilon, Acxiom, Yesmail, etc.

* Excellent interpersonal skills, with the ability to conceptualize and communicate highly detailed technical concepts to technical and non-technical audiences.

* Ability to take initiative as well as lead, motivate, and oversee teams to produce results.

* Solid understanding of:

* Business intelligence in relation to digital marketing and email reporting

* Relational database concepts, with exposure to SQL

* Data management, segmentation, and ETL best practices

* Solution design and architecture

* Scripting and query development

* Web analytics
