The Opportunity
Would you like to be a part of a company that is disrupting an industry?  Do you strive to make a difference and are committed to your work?  We are looking for a Data Scientist to join our team to work with our revolutionary mobile platform and drive new business!

The Work
As a Data Scientist, you will design and implement the next generation of our data platform.  You will play a key role in developing real-time machine learning for risk, fraud and marketing purposes.  You will be involved all areas from the design, development, and tuning of data to setting and documenting policies and procedures to ensure our success.

The Skills Required
  • Proficiency in Python, Scala, R, with related machine learning packages.
  • Background in data mining, machine learning, statistical analysis, and modeling, with experience deploying models in a production environment.
  • Strong SQL abilities and experience with massive relational database systems.
  • Energetic, efficient multitasker able to work well with teams on tight deadlines.
  • MS or equivalent in Computer Science, Statistics, Applied Mathematics, or related field with 3+ years of experience.
