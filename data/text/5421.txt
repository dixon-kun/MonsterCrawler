**Company description**
At Red Hat, we connect an innovative community of customers, partners, and contributors to deliver an open source stack of trusted, high-performing solutions. We offer cloud, Linux, middleware, storage, and virtualization technologies, together with award-winning global customer support, consulting, and implementation services. Red Hat is a rapidly growing company supporting more than 90% of Fortune 500 companies.
**Job summary**
The Red Hat Global Finance team is seeking an experienced Principal Data Engineer to join us in Raleigh, NC. In this role, you will oversee all information refinement,  reporting governance, and maintenance with the goal to provide integrated information assets pertaining to revenue, bookings, and expense across Red Hat. You'll integrate these disparate data sets across geographic, partner, and customer dimensions while providing thought leadership to cross-functional stakeholders. We will need you to be comfortable as a self-starter, and capable of formulating a vision that others will adopt.  We'll need you to have excellent analytical skills and be committed to contributing to Red Hat's operational success as we work to ensure our practices are accurate and we are supporting our business by enabling data -driven decisions across the company.
**Primary job responsibilities**
+ Act as the key representative for defining and implementing the data strategy, with the ability to work on cross-functional and geographically dispersed teams as a trusted consultant and leader
+ Understand strategic business goals and anticipate needs in order to communicate prioritized requirements to the team
+ Enforce and assist with all information governance initiatives, including, but not limited to security and compliance; ensure audit compliance
+ Query data stores and uncover patterns to detect gaps in data flow
+ Reverse engineer ETL applications via code reviews in case of insufficient documentation
+ Profile data at regular intervals to uncover weakness in data and protect the data integrity
+ Review data transformation rules and recommend changes as appropriate to the data model
+ Assist analytical users and data scientists in extracting accurate data by understanding user's intent and the context in which data will be used
+ Develop reporting standards, branding, and formatting
+ Maintain enterprise metadata repository to ensure data elements have concise, consistent, and unambiguous definitions
+ Maintain and govern the quality of critical business measures
+ Manage reports dashboards, statistical models, and lifecycle management
+ Communicate ideas clearly, both verbally and in writing, to executive staff, business sponsors, business analysts and technical resources in concise language
+ Evaluate and propose how best to leverage the Salesforce.com technology and ecosystem to meet evolving business requirements and information needs both within and across all departments
+ Work effectively with all stakeholders such as regional operations team, India offshore team, IT etc. to identify and prioritize specific requirements and dashboard reports and data management processes
+ Work closely with India offshore team to leverage their capacity and speciality to drive initiatives and handle daily task
+ Develop an understanding of Red Hat workflows and policies
+ Participate in training and change management activities
**Required skills**
+ Bachelor's degree, an equivalent degree at an accredited university, or equivalent experience
+ 7+ years of industry experience in data management
+ Expert level competency for data analysis, data modeling, and database processes and tools
+ Experience using analytics and BI tools such as Business Objects, Qlikview, Python, R, etc.
+ Experience using Python Packages such as numpy, pandas for data manipulation
+ Ability to understand and transform data to meet customers' needs and business context in a customer-friendly, quality-first manner
+ Expert analytical, problem-solving, reporting, and quantitative skills, with the ability to work with large data sets while maintaining a high attention to detail
+ Experience working with Salesforce.com data structures and reporting
+ Skilled with using advanced SQL, including knowledge of sub queries, aggregate functions, etc.
+ Experience working with PostgreSQL or Redshift database is a strong plus
+ Experience reconciling and refining data across different systems (Salesforce.com, financial and commissions systems, Oracle, etc.)
+ Experience working with data integration into Google Sheets or Google Apps Script is a plus
+ Excellent persuasive communication, presentation, and relationship-building skills, with the ability to work under time-sensitive deadlines
+ Ready to consistently work extended hours during periods of high demand (may include weekends).
+ Willingness to occasionally travel internationally
