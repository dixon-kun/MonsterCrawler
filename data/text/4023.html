We are searching for a Data Analyst to join our Data Center Global team in Sunnyvale, CA. This is a full-time Mon-Fri, long-termneed for our team. You would be working closely with TechnicalProgram Managers and other Data Analysts to build on business reportingneeds. This is a great fit for someone who has the ability and passion to lead data reporting projects utilizing projectmanagement and business operations skills. We need sound dataanalysis skills and someone who is passionate about data in anengineering space.

Your main responsibilities:

  • Extracting, organizing, and analyzing data center capacity deliveryperformance data using tools and functions such as SQL, excel, andGoogle spreadsheets.
  • Auditing and maintaining data center physical layouts and capacitydelivery performance reports
  • Resolving issues involving capacity delivery metrics derived for datacenter project data
  • Follow-up with internal teams to get datacorrected
  • Resolving minor issues regarding risk identification and managementin relation to delivery of data center capacity with guidance from otherteam members
  • Manage multiple projects and effectively communicate status updateswith Project Managers
  • Detail and explain methodology used in analyses as requested byProject Managers
  • Generate various visual charts and reports pertaining to data center capacity


Required Skills
  • B.A./B.S. or Masters degree with emphasis on coursework of aquantitative nature (e.g., Statistics, Computer Science, Engineering,Mathematics, Data Sciences).
  • 5+ years of experience in an operational role performing dataanalytics
  • 5+ years of experience with articulating operations questions, pullingdata from datasets (SQL) and using statistics to arrive at an answer
  • Deep interest and aptitude in data, metrics, analysis and trends andapplied knowledge of measurement, statistics and program evaluation
  • Experience in scripting with SQL, extracting large sets of data, anddesign of ETL (Extract, Transform, and Load) flows.


Required Experience

Data presentation in such tools such as Tableau
