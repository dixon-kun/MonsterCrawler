          Description
         The Advanced Analytics team is seeking a data scientist for supporting our Smart + Connected Workspaces initiative and Steelcase Enterprise Data Lake. The individual will work with the data lake architect, fellow data scientists, and various business partners in developing solutions. This includes the design and development of the lake using cutting edge technologies for Big Data such as Hadoop &amp; Spark, R, SAS, Python, and Perl.
         The Advanced Analytics team works closely with the business on multiple entrepreneurial initiatives. This is a fast paced environment bringing new opportunities to market involving IoT, Big Data, and various tech based products.
