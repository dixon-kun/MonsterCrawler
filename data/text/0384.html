Immediate Job Opportunity

Position at Corporate Technology This is a unique opportunity to join Hearst Technology in the role of UC Director, as a specialist who will establish an enterprise class communications platform. The right candidate will use their leadership skills and experience to efficiently raise the bar of voice, video and collaboration systems used throughout our many businesses. Reporting to the Vice President of Network Services, the UC Director will ensure that there is strategic alignment between our technology and business needs, while maintaining scalable, day-to-day operational duties. Responsibilities:   • Drive transformational initiatives to modernize Hearst’s voice and video communication platforms to meet the demands of a modern workforce.   • Continuously leverage opportunities for operational efficiencies through a thorough assessment of existing assets, capabilities and needs   • Foster a culture of that seeks to learn about the latest available technology solutions and about customer needs throughout the UC team   • Manage the cost of services and support for Hearst’s voice and video communication platforms   • Develop KPI’s and appropriate Metrics to provide strong visibility into service availability, quality, and customer satisfaction   • Provide team leadership and vision to align engineering skills to communication technology transformation across the Hearst landscape.   • Oversee Incident and service request management for communication platforms, ensuring appropriate SLA’s are upheld accordingly Qualifications:   • 8+ years managing globally diverse Voice over IP Systems   • 8+ years managing telecommunications carriers and vendors   • Able to create and maintain an operational budget for all responsible systems   • Experience with Cisco Call Manager, Avaya, and other systems   • Experience with video conferencing systems   • Strong awareness of ITIL, common IT controls and frameworks   • Proven ability to stay abreast of technological changes in the field of VoIP, Video Conferencing and Collaboration tools   • S. in computer science or equivalent technical degree.   • Excellent communication and customer service skills (verbal and\or written) About Hearst Hearst is one of the nation’s largest diversified media, information and services companies with more than 360 businesses. Its major interests include ownership in cable television networks such as A&E, HISTORY, Lifetime and ESPN; majority ownership of global ratings agency Fitch Group; Hearst Health, a group of medical information and services businesses; 30 television stations such as WCVB-TV in Boston and KCRA-TV in Sacramento, Calif., which reach a combined 19 percent of U.S. viewers; newspapers such as the Houston Chronicle, San Francisco Chronicle and Albany Times Union; more than 300 magazines around the world including Cosmopolitan, ELLE, Harper’s BAZAAR and Car and Driver; digital services businesses such as iCrossing and KUBRA; and investments in emerging digital and video companies such as Complex, BuzzFeed, VICE and AwesomenessTV. Follow us on Twitter @HearstCorp and subscribe to Hearstlink. About Hearst Technology Hearst Technology, Inc. works to develop platforms and solutions to power the next phase of Hearst’s digital growth, technology product development, and to solidify it as a leader in the industry. Hearst Technology continues to push the boundaries of how technology can propel the company forward. Lead by Hearst CTO Phil Wiser, the Hearst Technology team ideates daily on groundbreaking ways to expand the connection between Hearst’s 300-plus companies and the growing audiences and clients they serve. Hearst Technology’s team is leading the digital media industry with the following initiatives: Hearst Audience Platform - Building a holistic audience approach across all of Hearst's digital properties and audience of 220M monthly unique visitors; Enterprise Technology - Implementing high-performing, cloud-based IT solutions; Video - Getting to 1B annual video views; Native & Emerging Technology Team - Accelerating high-growth, multi-screen digital media offerings; Hearst Data Warehouse - Leveraging Hearst's Big Data brain; and Hearst Data Science – developing new product offerings in the area of Artificial Intelligence. Follow us on Twitter @HearstTech.
