DRINKS, a stealth-mode VC-backed startup, is a top-five US Direct to Consumer (DTC) marketing technology company that manages wine brands. The Company is led by an entrepreneurial team of marketing technology, data science, DTC, and M&A experts with several successful exits. DRINKS operates in a compelling market: a $33B industry virtually untouched by the Internet, though that is now changing quickly.

To help us build the next generation of online and mobile wine stores that will be used by tens of millions of people, we’re on a relentless hunt for a superb Operations Manager who shares our passion for building cutting-edge experiences to transform how people buy wine.

Responsibilities:

  • Oversee  daily operation of designated projects, including suggestions for  improvement
  • Improve the  existing procedures for outgoing and incoming resources and track  inventory for the warehousing department
  • Participates  in and coordinates the design and implementation of new processes
  • Improve  operational management systems, processes, and best practices that enable  the company to improve operational efficiency

·       Coordinate internal resources and third parties/vendors

·       Plan, commence, execute, and supervise assigned projects

·       Identify and raise issues and risks and recommend actions to resolve

·       Oversee the supervision of one or more specified operating and/or service activities within specified business units

·       Maintain work process flows by coordinating information and requirements with related operational departments; participating with and providing resources to business improvement teams

·       Keep management informed by preparing reports, making presentations, interpreting information, and making recommendations

·       Drive initiatives in the management team and organizationally that contribute to long-term operational excellence

·       Examine financial data/statements and use them to improve profitability

·       Contribute to short and long-term organizational planning and strategy

·       Direct and coordinate activities of businesses or departments concerned with the production, pricing, sales, and/or distribution of products

·       Ensuring that all workings are manufactured in a correct, cost effective and timely manner in alignment with specifications and quality requirements

Qualifications:

·       Bachelor's degree (BA or BS) from four-year college or university

·       2+ years of experience in operations management or project management position

·       2+ years of experience with a DtC and/or B2C company preferred

·       Knowledge of budget preparation, cost estimating, monitoring, and fiscal management principles

·       Background and work experience in finance

·       Excellent computer skills and proficient in excel, word, outlook, and access

·       Excellent communication skills both verbal and written

·       Excels at operating in a fast pace, community environment

·       Ability to look at situations from several points of view

·       Ability to effectively communicate with all levels of the organization

·       Highly self-motivated

·       Ability to adapt to a quickly changing environment

Benefits:

Competitive benefits package including a flexible vacation plan, generous healthcare plan, dental insurance, vision insurance, life insurance, short and long term disability insurance, and covered garage parking. Plus, we cater lunch weekly, host frequent wine giveaways and tastings, and provide leading-edge technology tools.
