The primary objective of the Client Information Strategy team at PIMCO is to partner with and enable our client-facing professionals to align our energies and resources to relationships and activities which present the greatest opportunity for growth and success. To achieve this objective we plan and execute what we call the “client information value chain”. This information value chain encompasses information acquisition, data quality management processes, data modeling, application user experience, reporting, business intelligence, and the generation of insights from advanced analytics. To deliver on this mission we are building a team with skills spanning asset management domain expertise; technical skills for querying, coding and data preparation; and applied mathematical skills. Everyone on the team is also expected to have basic business analyst and project management skills, as well as strong communication skills. We recognize that this combination of skills is rare; so we are particularly enthusiastic to meet highly motivated candidates with a partial skills match and a hunger to learn. To this end, our team has developed a strong learning and development program and is committed to supporting position-relevant ongoing education.Responsibilities We are seeking a Senior Data Science Practitioner who will drive business results by enabling quantitative decision-making for client-facing business leaders. The role’s success will in part be measured on the ability to improve campaign effectiveness through data analysis, segmentation, clustering, and predictive techniques. The role also entails analysis and visualization of campaign results with statistical techniques and advanced visualizations to describe a story from the data.In order to achieve these outcomes, responsibilities include:Management and execution of substantial analytics projects, both as a practitioner and coordinating the input of other team members.Develop our understanding of client behavior by researching and exploiting new datasets.Develop strong relationships to understand business goals and requirements to derive insights from data.Measure and articulate the effectiveness of sales and marketing campaigns.Develop prototypes to affirm business requirements and expectations.Troubleshoot and assess impact of erroneous data, suggest solutions to correct the root cause.A strong contribution to the development of our analytics function through:Selection and integration of the analytics tools and technologies used by the Client Information Strategy team.Evaluation of vendors, products and services.Creation of analytical processes and practices that create and record intellectual capital without bureaucracy.Development of the team’s talent by mentoring junior team members as they build data science skills.

Position Requirements

Requirements5+ years of experience of quantitative problem solving. A subject matter expert in predictive techniques; including the selection, configuration, optimization and comparison of algorithms. Familiar and versant in the code libraries used for predictive and machine learning in Python and R.While this role is unlikely to involve developing new analytical methods; success will depend upon lateral thinking, and an an ability to research, apply and adapt techniques into our business domain.Able to operate efficiently and effectively: without reliance on others to prepare data and without reliance on any particular commercial analytical software suite, tool or language.Proactively curious, self-sufficient professional able to convey complex ideas and datasets to actionable uses for business analytics. Able to change direction quickly based on findings.Experience in structured and unstructured data, their associated technologies and the scripting skills required to interact with them in SQL, R, and Python.At ease installing software and solving the common problems associated with installing, integrating and operating open source software.Experience and understanding of distributed processing environments for analytics, and able to design scripts and solve problems related to the distributed nature of processing.PhD or MS degree in applied mathematics, statistics, data science, or similar quantitative field of study.Attention to detail and quality with an ability to keep larger goals in mind.Ability to work well in a team environment.
