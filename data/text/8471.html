We are looking for a highly motivated individual, passionate about technology to join the GE Oil & Gas Digital team as Senior Staff Analytics Advocate. In this role, you will drive the adoption and usage of industrial analytics across the O&G industry working closely with engineers, data scientists and software developers. You will be an ambassador representing the analytics team with both internal GE teams and external customers to help evangelize our analytics products.

As Senior Staff Analytics Advocate, you will:

  • Lead analytics framework development, working closely with
    analytics researchers, data scientists, mechanical, electrical, petroleum and
    chemical engineers
  • Work with Product Managers and technical staff in customer
    engagements to understand user needs and define requirements for the analytics
    development and productization
  • Develop technical specifications, implementation plan and
    productization plan for developing and validating analytics
  • Work with analytics development, product strategy &
    product management teams to conceptualize new analytics products and features.
  • Build connections with stakeholders in the GE Oil & Gas
    business and represent O&G Digital with customers and industry
    associations.
  • Provide technical support to sales and marketing teams for
    evangelizing industrial analytics products.
  • Serve as a thought leader externally and internally,
    providing guidance on best practices, industry trends, and future potential for
    industrial analytics.
  • Be able to influence from both a strategic and technical
    standpoint across the function and business. Ability to influence at the peer
    and senior leadership level is critical.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Ph.D. in a “STEM” major (Science, Technology, Engineering,
    Mathematics) with industrial/research work experience of 7 years or more
  • Strong interpersonal skills
  • Experience coding in one or more languages (e.g. Python,
    Matlab, C, C++, R, or Java)
  • Experience incorporating engineering analytics or advanced
    data-driven analytics into software solutions
  • Exceptional written, verbal communication, and presentation
    skills
  • Detail oriented and demonstrated ability to develop
    technical requirements based upon proactive analysis of customer needs
  • Demonstrated ability to work in highly collaborative,
    cross-functional environments
  • Work experience in a customer facing technical role
