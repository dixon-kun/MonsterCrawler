About the Job

Consultants - Data Analytics & Predictive Modeling

Reperio, Inc., a Boston area Executive Search Firm, has been engaged by one of its clients to recruit multiple Vice President, Associate (Consultant) and Analyst level individuals with deep analytic, big data, business intelligence, data mining and predictive modeling/analytics related experience. Strong preference for candidates with ETL related tools experience (e.g., Datastage, Informatica, DTS, SAS, SQL, etc.). Hadoop experience strongly preferred.  Our client is an established, global management consulting firm building a national practice around advanced data analytics and delivering deeply quantitative solutions to some of the largest companies in the world. Immediate opportunities exist in Boston, New York City and London.  Travel required.

Ideal candidates should have a professional services / client-facing background, combined with hands-on expertise in the area of big data / data analytics. Prior work experience at large consulting firms delivering data analytic solutions (e.g., Accenture, Deloitte, Opera Solutions, Bain, McKinsey, Booz Allen, Monitor, A.T. Kearney, Charles River Associates, Compass Lexecon, Pricewaterhouse Coopers (PWC), KPMG, Boston Consulting Group (BCG), Ernst & Young, Sapient, CSC Consulting, IBM etc.), or boutique consulting firms specializing in this area, is highly desirable.

Candidates should have 2+ yearsof quantitative experience combined with a technical degree (Computer Science, Mathematics, Economics, Physics, Statistics, etc.) from a top-tier academic institution, with a preference for advanced degrees (PhD, MS, etc.). Recent PhD graduates with strong data analysis skills will also be considered. Our client tends to hire extremely smart, accomplished, quick learners with high energy and a demonstrated ability to get results.

To be considered, please forward a Word version of your resume in confidence. Only qualified candidates will be contacted.
