        The Challenge
        Qualtrics is an industry leading, hyper growth SaaS company backed by three of the top venture capital firms in the world, Sequoia Capital, Accel Partners and Insight Venture Partners. In February of 2016, we announced our future direction of a single platform that captures customer, market, and employee insights in one place. We've seen tremendous momentum across our businesses with the launch of our ground-breaking new platform that differentiates us from any other provider in the market.
        We're tying together the power of market data, employee insights, customer experience, data science, action management, and advanced analytics to elevate our message and story. Applications that help companies drive transformation to engage customers, empower employees, optimize operations and transform product.
        The challenge we face is creating a completely new category and a differentiated position in the minds of our prospects and customers that drives irresistible desire for Qualtrics. Join the team and help us transform the business applications market! The Qualtrics Marketing team is looking for a passionate product marketer leader to drive our GTM, messaging, and vision for our platform solutions. The ideal candidate has 8-15 years of SaaS Marketing experience and has an MBA from a top tier university. You'll develop compelling, differentiated value propositions, use innovate story-telling to bring the message to life for our field, partners and customers. You'll also act as a liaison to the demand generation, business planning, product management, R&amp;D and field readiness teams to ensure effective awareness and enablement.
        The Expectation for Success:
        You'll know you're doing a great job when you exceed your quarterly goals by knowing your customers and market so intimately that you define and communicate a clear, differentiated, defensible, and monetizable market position. You'll be customer-obsessed, strategic, detail-oriented, and highly creative driving 6 core missions:
        * Know our customers and markets better than anyone else in the company and understand how they make decisions
        * Own messaging, product positioning, product launches, and market intelligence
        * Create sales enablement material and adoption that will drive successful selling for their respective products
        * Create industry defining thought leadership strategy
        * Bootstrap, experiment and develop highly creative demand generation strategies that dramatically increases pipeline and awareness
        * Continually innovate our GTM strategy and be accountable for market success
