        Position Description
        The Role: As a Sr. Scientist/Developer, you will create new and improve existing econometrical models that allow extracting market insights from rich variety of data sources valuable to our clients in the automotive industry. You will also work on the models implementation using high level programming languages (C# and similar). The development environment/tools ranges from R, SAS, SQL, Excel, etc. to writing own software applications in C#. All members of our team perform all range of activities from math model creation and early prototyping to implementation of production version and further maintenance and reevaluation of modeling parameters as new data comes in.
        Responsibilities:
        * Development of new math-models and implementing them using innovative analytical tools for decision support in the areas of incentive planning, marketing, and product positioning in the automobile industry and other fields that rely on demand modeling and profit optimization (Discrete Choice Modeling, Random Utility, Nested and Random Coefficient Logit, Clustering, Non-linear Regression, etc.).
        * Maintenance and enhancement of math-model for Incentive Planning suite of applications and other modeling applications in production (these systems won the 2006 Marketing Science Practice Prize and were a finalist of the 2007 Edelman Award). Analysis of large data-sets using standard (SQL, R) and proprietary tools, as part of new product development ideation.
        * Development/maintenance of computational or data processing programs using various high-level programming languages (C#, R, etc.), including econometric modeling packages.
        Skills &amp; Qualifications:
        * At least 6 years of quantitative analysis and modeling involving scientific computing.
        * Strong math/analytical abilities and attention to details.
        * Solid knowledge of general programming languages, at least one of: C#, Java, C++.
        * High proficiency in utilizing complex SQL queries for data analysis.
        * Testing/prototyping math-models using analytical tools such as R, SAS, etc.
        * Good verbal and writing communication skills.
        * M.S. (required), Ph.D. (preferred) in Mathematics, Physics, Economics, Operations Research, Computer Science, Engineering or other field with strong emphasis on math-modeling and computer technology.
        Career Opportunity:
        * Tremendous opportunity to learn new mathematical methodologies, computer technologies, as well as share knowledge among bright like-minded individuals.
        * Taking charge and leading projects (upon demonstration of pattern for successful delivery of assigned tasks under minimal supervision).
        The Team / Business: A small team of quantitative scientists/developers in a dynamic, goal oriented, yet flexible and friendly environment, with a good work/life balance and high productivity of our team.
