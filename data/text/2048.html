Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services (CMS) provides health insurance to more than one third of all Americans. Managing programs and projects at this skill is a daunting task. Noblis provides program and project management support to CMS, which includes strategic and technical advise.


To enhance decision-making, Noblis is working with CMS to investigate the role of graph analytics. Towards that end, we are seeking an individual to:

* Transfer data between CMS and Noblis,
* Ensure the security, privacy, and availability of the data,
* Transform flat files and other tabular data into RDF data,
* Develop use cases for the data,
* Conduct analyses of the RDF data,
* Communicating results to CMS technical staff and executive leadership
* Provide technical direction to a team of junior analysts.


Required:

  • Masters degree in Computer Science, Health Informatics, or a related field + 3 years experience OR Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Health Informatics, or a related field + 5 years experience
  • Experience with data analytics / data science
  • Familiarity with RDF and Semantic Web technology
  • Comfort with distilling complicated analyses into an accessible format for non-technical audiences (e.g., white papers, presentations, documents)
  • Experience with Linux and a scripting language (e.g., Perl or Python

Desired:

  • Experience with SPARQL
  • Experience with cloud architectures
  • Experience working with CMS or Commercial healthcare data (provider, beneficiary, claims)
