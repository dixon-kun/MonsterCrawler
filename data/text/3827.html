What You'll Do
  • Get your hands dirty as you help us continue to scale our analytics infrastructure. We use homegrown, open source, and 3rd party solutions

  • Maintain a strong understanding of our evolving data warehouse
  • Conduct ad-hoc analyses to drive strategic decision-making throughout the company
  • Identify and drive efficiency opportunities and empower others
What We Look For
  • Passion and drive to change banking, payments and online marketing for the better
  • BA/BS degree in a quantitative discipline or equivalent experience
  • At least 2-3 years of experience in an engineering/analytical role focused on coding visualization and data infrastructure
