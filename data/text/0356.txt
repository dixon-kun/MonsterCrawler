          Consulting Director, Business Solutions (Metro NYC)-171651
            Description
          The Consulting Director &#8211; Business Solutions (CD-B) for New York/New Jersey leads the consulting activities for targeted accounts/customers in the Territory. The primary focus for this leader is selling solutions that leverage data to achieve business-based outcomes for specific industries. Services for the targeted customers will focus on business buyers for analytic and business consulting services as well as analytic applications and solutions. Secondarily, these services will help to extend the customer&#8217;s leverage of Teradata&#8217;s technology solution components including; Integrated Data Warehouse, Data Warehouse Appliances, Aster, Hadoop, and many Analytical and Business Intelligence applications. Consulting Services are an integral part of Teradata&#8217;s go-to-market strategy, and the Director leads the business development and sales process for projects with significant scale and business outcome. He or she develops and executes the sales plan and positions service offers for specific customer accounts. He or she is responsible for building senior level client relationships within the business, managing the overall sales process, identifying business problems, scoping, pricing, and contracting of services, and collaborating with others to extend our capabilities. This position will report to the Senior Consulting Director within NY/NJ and New England territories.
          Key Responsibilities:

          &#8226; Understands client business problems and develops POVs which articulate problems along with Teradata solutions
          &#8226; Builds relationships in new parts of the client business organization to generate demand for solutions in new areas
          &#8226; Manages the overall selling process for business-oriented consulting services
          &#8226; Identifies opportunities which would require other specialized expertise and coordinates with internal resources to bring experts in as needed
          &#8226; Regularly meets with account team lead to discuss account strategy and future sales opportunities
          &#8226; Coaches and develops Project Managers and Consultants
          &#8226; Manages process of scoping, planning, costing, and pricing new projects
          &#8226; Develops Statements of Work for new projects; defines goals and objectives, client requirements, resources needed, budget, risks, and deliverables
          &#8226; Serves on consulting engagements as a thought partner and subject matter expert for the consulting delivery team and as a key relationship map for project sponsors
          &#8226; Engages with other account teams and consulting leadership as needed to use expertise to help sell solutions to other clients
          &#8226; Maintains projections for pipeline of potential future engagements
