        This role will focus on the in integration of traffic engineering practices into the Roadway Sensors products and services portfolio. This person will interface with public-agency customers, sales professionals, product design engineers, field and test engineers, and data scientists.They will build and maintain relationships with key traffic engineering &amp; ITS clients. Position will require work in the field for evaluations, testing and assessment and will involve some travel.
