Hi. We’re TiVo. At our core, we’re innovators who continuously seek to fuel the ultimate entertainment experience. We touch  the lives of binge-watching, music-loving, entertainment fanatics every day by inventing and delivering beautiful user experiences, and enable the world’s leading media and entertainment providers to  nurture more meaningful relationships with their audiences.

We work hard, celebrate success and challenge everyone in our organization to make an impact.  If you are as passionate as we are about the intersection of technology and entertainment, join us today.


We are looking for individuals who are passionate about working with large amounts of data and who are excited to use Big Data and predictive analytics to revolutionize the nearly $200bn/year TV advertising and promo businesses. We want exceptional individuals who are excited to be part of a small, hardworking team building a new product and business from inception. The Product Support Analyst position within the TiVo Analytics group is responsible for delivering top notch, customer support for all TiVo Analytics products between the hours of 1PM and 9PM . Our business and development cycles are fast-paced, challenging and always shifting to respond to the dynamic marketplace as well as our customers’ needs. We’re looking for a highly motivated individual who can easily adapt to changes in priorities and will provide a great end-user support experience to our customers.

SDET / Product Support Analyst (2nd shift)

The Role:

  • Work as part of the Software Development and Analytics Support Team whose focus is development and support of TiVo’s next generation analytics and delivery platforms.
  • Understand the overall technical architecture and how each feature is implemented.
  • Create test plans and automation strategies that scale.
  • Actively participate in cross-functional teams with a focus on test, system level automation and E2E monitoring and validation.
  • Be a vocal proponent for quality in every phase of the development process.
  • Interface directly with customers to provide guidance, provide status updates, and resolve reported issues.
  • Partner with Technical Account Management, Operations, Data Science, and Engineering for resolution of complex problems.
  • Contribute to knowledge base articles.
  • Must be able to work the hours of 1:00 - 9:00pm EST.

Basic Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related technical field
  • 2 years of Software QA/testing experience
  • Exceptional experience in designing SQA test plans and test cases according to requirements
  • Exceptional skills in testing multi-tiered, distributed solutions.
  • Strong background in automation using Java or other object-oriented programming language
  • Strong experience in Perl, Python, Shell or other scripting languages
  • Strong SQL experience
  • Strong interpersonal, project management and organizational skills with ability to track multiple test executions simultaneously and synthesize results
  • Excellent communication, analytical and problem solving skills

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience testing multi-tiered big data solutions (Services, MapReduce, AMQP, HBase, HDFS, Flume) with high loads
  • Experience using open source tools such as Nagios for system monitoring.
  • Comfortable developing and testing in Linux environments
