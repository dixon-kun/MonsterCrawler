About the Job

The Samsung Strategy and Innovation Center is looking for a talented full stack software developer to help design, build and launch the next wave of amazing Samsung Digital Health products and services. Be part of a stellar cross-functional team that is working on creating the “Next Big Thing” within Samsung. As part of the engineering team, you’ll lead the effort in building a highly scalable cloud service platform that will enable a range of future connected devices. Samsung’s Digital Health Initiative provides an exciting opportunity for the brightest minds in the technology world to come together to develop the products that will, for the first time, put individuals in the driver’s seat in understanding their own health and wellness. At a time when healthcare spending is at record levels and when the number of people over the age of 60 worldwide is expected to exceed more than 1.2 billion by 2025, digital health is an incredibly important area for innovation. We believe this initiative is an essential first step and we invite you to join us in creating the technologies of the future that will help make people’s lives healthier This Digital Health Initiative is part of Samsung’s Strategy and Innovation Center, a global organization within Samsung focused on identifying and nurturing new technologies in health and wellness, cloud infrastructure, Internet of Things, human-computer interface, and other related areas. Headquartered in Silicon Valley, with offices in Korea, Israel and the U.K., SSIC’s mission is to develop and accelerate groundbreaking technologies through open innovation working in collaboration with entrepreneurs and strategic partners to enhance people’s lives and help make the world a better place. Responsibilities: • Develop web based UI and back end to facilitate data ingestion, storage, annotation and visualization. • Design and develop client side APIs and libraries to interface with server, cloud storage and compute platform. • Work with data science team and transform the requirements into automation software pipeline. • Evaluate and integrate third party tools. • Support, maintain, and help document software functionality. • Focus on the critical evaluation and selection of the software components of the infrastructure. Qualifications: • Bachelor’s degree in CS/EE/CE at the minimum. • 3+ years relevant as full stack developer. • Excellent knowledge of JavaScript and Python. • Hands on experience in modern server side programming such as NodeJS. • UI and UX design using single page application JavaScript frameworks such as AngularJS or React. • Familiarity with visualization frameworks and software such as d3.js and Tableau. • REST based web service design and development. • Experience working with NoSQL databases such as HBase and MongoDB. • Familiarity with AWS as well as high volume data processing pipelines such as Kafka, HDFS and MapReduce is a plus.
