        Altamira brings a commercial mindset to solving the most complex national security problems by delivering mission application development, multi-intelligence analysis, and data science technologies and solutions to the defense, intelligence, and homeland security communities. Altamira's culture of innovation and excellence, and mid-market-size, positions us as the premier next generation leader bringing technology solutions to mission.
        Altamira is an Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action employer. All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, disability, or protected veteran status.
        Altamira is seeking experienced SAR Analysts that will research, develop, and evaluate new Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) capabilities. The SAR Analysts will be part of a dynamic team developing advanced, comprehensive GEOINT products for NASIC government customers. Procure, process, exploit, and disseminate SAR data using various types of Electronic Light Tables (ELT) and software. Provide peer reviews, assessments, and recommendations of other SAR intelligence reports while adhering to the standard operating procedures of the client. Responsible for communicating and working closely with various organizations within the Intelligence Community (IC). The successful candidate will be able to understand the NASIC SAR production workflow/capabilities while identifying improvement opportunities of the Intelligence Cycle for the government client. This position is located in Dayton, OH.
        Required Skills:
        Three to 10 years' experience exploiting airborne and national SAR collection platforms. Imagery analysis experience in the IC at the national level. Knowledge of softcopy exploitation functionalities. Attended either the Geospatial Intelligence Training Program or equivalent. Demonstrated good oral and written communication skills.
        Basic Qualifications:
        * Experience with RemoteView, SOCET GXP, ESRI ArcGIS Suite, IEC, NES, Google Earth, and common office software to include PowerPoint presentations, spreadsheets, and word processing.
        * Familiar with radar processing and exploitation of both, literal and non-literal radar discipline, including SAR, LiDAR, OTH, or LOS
        * Understanding of radar fundamentals and SAR phenomenology and its interpretation
        * AA or BS degree
        * Knowledge and experience applying Full-Spectrum GEOINT capabilities
        * Performs high-level/expert imagery analysis, intelligence procedures, processes, techniques, and methodologies used to develop complex military imagery systems solutions to requirement
        * Expert working knowledge of the discovery, analysis and exploitation of geospatially oriented data and the management of that data within a variety of compliant applications, software, and databases.
        * Ability and experience exploiting real-time data and disseminating information in a dynamic, high-stress, fast-paced 24/7 365 environment.
        * Ability to manage time and resources
        * Active TS/SCI clearance required
        Preferred Skills:
        * 5+ years of experience with processing and exploiting radar data for intelligence purposes at the national or operational military command
        * BS degree in EE, Remote Sensing, Geospatial Science, Physics, Mathematics, Engineering Science, or a related field; MA or MS degree a plus
        * Experience with programming or scripting languages including MATLAB, C++, IDL, ArcObjects, or Visual Basic
