As Netflix continues to grow and expand, we are looking for a leader for our marketing planning & analysis team in the Americas. North and Latin America is actually comprised of many markets that differ significantly in terms of macroeconomy, technology infrastructure, entertainment landscape, and Netflix's time in market. Marketing leadership relies heavily on the Marketing Planning & Analysis team to shape strategy and inform important business decisions with actionable data-driven insights.

In this role, you will need to be visionary leader who will build, manage, and mentor a highly analytical, strategic problem solving team to partner with Marketing and other cross functional partners to grow Netflix's business throughout the large Americas markets, such as US, Canada, Mexico, and Brazil, as well as a highly varied set of other Latin American markets. As a leader who will not only partner with colleagues in the Los Angeles office but also colleagues in other US and regional offices, you must be adept at bridging cross-functional and cross cultural points of view to advance our thinking and solutions in marketing.

Netflix needs a self-starter, who is able to work effectively, autonomously and drive strategy and effective decisioning with the regional Marketing team and cross-functionally with Consumer Insights, Data Science & Engineering, Science & Algorithms, Payments, etc. You must cultivate a strong partnership with the US/Canada Marketing VP and the LATAM Marketing VP, plus their Marketing Directors. You must thrive in a fast-paced environment, possess a high level of intellectual curiosity, focus on generating results as an individual and a team leader.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

You personally must be an inspiring leader that will hire, manage, and mentor a high performing team. You must be an objective trusted advisor to the North and Latin America marketing executives and other cross-functional leaders in the North and Latin American teams - blending data driven insights and sound judgement to help challenge and solve marketing problems. Because the markets in North and Latin America comprise many different cultures, competitive landscapes, and growth drivers â€“ you need to understand the complexity and lead the definition of actionable strategy without losing sight of big picture priorities. While your scope is Americas, you must demonstrate the ability to think globally.

You will be responsible building and leading the Marketing Planning and Analysis function for the Americas. You and your team will:

* Partner with North and Latin American marketing executives to develop and drive the regional marketing growth strategy

* Act as a strategic thought partner to regional marketers across strategic and operational challenges

* Create strategic value-added analyses around growth opportunities, marketing campaigns, media optimization, and core business metrics and drive better decisioning and results

* Facilitate marketing planning and the allocation of the marketing budget, and manage in-quarter optimization and adjustments

* Develop and execute testing for marketing, leading to actionable insights and better business results

* Monitor subscriber acquisition and retention; analyze key predictors, identify trends and present actionable insights to the Marketing team

* Develop insights to support our continued expansion in North and Latin America

* Conduct market research, track and compile industry trends and competitive analysis to inform marketing strategies

QUALIFICATIONS:

A successful candidate will have a strong track record of leadership and demonstrated success as a strategist, analyst, and/or data driven marketer.

* 10-15 years of experience in highly analytical environments - top management consulting firm, investment bank, corporate strategy, or digital marketer. Of the 10-15 years of professional experience, at least 5 years of media and/or entertainment specific experience in highly analytical roles

* Proven track record and enthusiasm for building, managing, mentoring, and inspiring teams and influencing peer organizations

* Strong communicator with the ability to moderate based on audience and demonstrate an ability to partner with a range of cross cultural and cross functional partners

* History of self starting and defining what success means in different professional environments and achieving that success

* Experience working in multiple North and Latin American markets

* Top-tier MBA (or equivalent)
