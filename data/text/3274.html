Description
The Data Delivery Lead will be responsible for executing data projects and enhancements driven by our Commercial Lines Actuarial and Research business groups. This will include expanding our strategic assets (data warehouses, analytical datasets, etc.) as well as ensuring that these data assets are meeting the needs of our business partners.  This position will be part of the Data Asset Management team within the Enterprise Data Office (EDO) and the ideal candidate is a highly motivated, results oriented individual with strong data acumen.
The Data Delivery Lead will be a trusted partner and advisor for the business and will help build the right solutions that allows them to leverage the data for effective decision making. This leader will have 5 to 6 direct reports (employees), in addition to overseeing vendor partner team members who work on their portfolio.  They will look at ways to improve speed of invest delivery and reduce cost leveraging a strong data delivery lifecycle with continuous improvement mindset.
Responsibilities:
  • Manage delivery of data projects and enhancements driven by Actuarial and Research business groups
  • Build data marts, analytical data sets to support key business processes
  • Enhance existing data assets like the warehouse with new sources of data and integrate them within the current business model
  • Work with other delivery leads to tightly manage dependencies between the changes
  • Drive operational planning sessions for the business to build out the delivery plan
  • Partner with the business and the information architect to prioritize the work appropriately
  • Build trusted partnership with business stakeholders and drive business enablement leveraging data
  • Be a thought leader and change agent who can drive a step change in the data space by improving speed of delivery and reduce cost while simplifying the data environment
  • Rewrite or enhance processes to simplify the delivery of data
  • Drive agile mindset across business and data teams
  • Work closely with business sponsors and stakeholders to drive execution of data strategies and solutions, and ensure portfolio projects are successfully executed and delivered on time, on budget, with high quality and enables the business
  • Manage a self-sufficient team of project managers, analysts, engineers and data modelers that will drive execution
  • Build talent within the team through coaching as well as acquiring and growing talent
  • Instill inspiration in project team by effectively communicating the big picture and clear plan to execute, build commitment and empower others, communicate with clarity, courage & timeliness
  • Tightly manage the project and team financials and come up with cost conscious options for the business

  • Bachelor’s degree or Master’s degree in MIS, Computer Science, Project Management, or related field or equivalent work experience.
  • 10+ years of data warehouse experience.  In depth knowledge of data management strategies, principles and practices.
  • 5+ years of managing/executing large and complex data projects.  Desired experience in Agile methodology.
  • Experience in managing large scale data assets and managing a team of 5 employees
  • Preferred experience supporting Actuarial/Research business groups
  • Excellent written, verbal communication and presentation skills with ability to effectively communicate with senior management.  Knowledge of effective communication strategies and how to impact and influence others
  • Proven track record as a results-oriented technology leader that collaborates well with business leaders and manages outcomes holistically and accurately
  • Proven ability to simplify technology concepts to business stakeholders and help them adopt new technologies/processes
  • Demonstrated ability to apply strategic and decisive problem solving skills in ambiguous situations.
  • Strong analytical, critical thinking and problem solving skills
  • P&C Insurance and Big Data experience preferred.
Behaviors at the Hartford
  • Deliver Outcomes – Demonstrate a bias for speed and execution that serves our shareholders and customers.
  • Operate as a Team Player – Work together to drive solutions for the good of The Hartford.
  • Build Strong Partnerships – Demonstrate integrity and build trust with others.
  • Strive for Excellence – Motivate yourself and others to achieve high standards and continuously improve.
