          GSO Data Science Intern I
          Major Responsibilities:
          Teradata Global Support Organization is seeking Data Science interns to join our Analytics team dedicated on automating customer support and transforming customer support from reactive to proactive by leveraging data and analytics. As a member of our team, the candidate will work in collaboration with other members to:
          &#8226; Manage remote connectivity via Server Management to get messages from Customer systems to Teradata Backend applications so customer system events can be monitored, actions can be recommended based on event rules and taken in order to track, investigate and resolve issues as rapidly as possible, for example, new incidents can be created with diagnostics information attached for support engineers to investigate.
          &#8226; Automate monitoring of the messaging traffic from Customer systems, detection of abnormal traffic and timely notification with proactive recommendations in order to resolve issues immediately, prevent potential issues from happening, protect infrastructure&#8217;s ability to process messages and create incidents, and project backend infrastructure capacity requirements to meet SLAs.
          &#8226; Create and update event rules by collaborating with Product Engineers, Field Services Engineers, and Support Engineers in order to minimize unnecessary false positives and to cover new scenarios where incidents shall be automatically generated.
          &#8226; Research, design, prototype, test and document novel algorithms for event signature identification, representation, persistence, retrieval and matching, in order to achieve automated, near real time decision making on data and problems.
          On a daily basis, you will work with massive datasets, which includes data integration, data cleansing, exploratory analysis, predictive modeling, and rapid prototyping. You will present findings to teammates and guide the transition of new algorithms into operational code, which will be productized and used by support engineers and customers for resolving real world problems.
