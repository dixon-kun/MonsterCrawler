        The SME-level Data Scientist will lead a team of data scientists in utilizing a broad range of programmatic and quantitative methods to find patterns and relationships in large data sets utilizing complex mathematical, statistical or other data-driven problem solving analysis to identify significant intelligence issues or trends in business operations to senior internal and external customers. &#160;They are expected to work independently under broad guidance from the government client.
         Duties and Responsibilities:
        Under government direction, lead and manage all aspects data science program in substantive and administrative matters.
        Mentor other data scientists to support employee and product development
        Under government direction, oversee data science programs in support of internal and external customers, U.S. interests, and policy objectives
        Provide accurate, timely, highly complex and sophisticated data analysis in support of key agency, U.S. national security, and foreign policy objectives, Agency business operations, and to shape Intelligence Community (IC) analysis and priorities.
        Conceive, prepare, and communicate a wide range of strategic, highly complex graphics, computational models/tools, or written/oral assessments to internal and external peers and customers (e.g., policymakers, IC components).
        Manage projects and associated resources
         Knowledge, Skills, Abilities:
        Thorough knowledge of individual, organizational, technological, or transnational issues of national security concern
        Thorough knowledge of Agency business operations and associated processes
        Thorough knowledge appropriate analytic methods and methodological tools in one or more of the following areas:
        Applied mathematics (e.g. probability and statistics, formal modeling, &#160; &#160; &#160; &#160; &#160; &#160; &#160; &#160;computational social sciences)
        Computer programming (e.g. programming languages, math/statistics &#160; &#160; &#160; &#160; &#160; &#160; &#160;packages, computer science, machine learning, scientific computing)
        Visualization (e.g. &#160;GIS/geospatial analysis, telemetry analysis)
        Thorough knowledge of research designs
        Thorough knowledge of collection methods, capabilities, and tasking processes
        Thorough knowledge of project management concepts and principles
        Working knowledge of resource management principles and applicants
        Intellectual curiosity; creativity and innovation to go beyond current tools to deliver the best solutions to complex problems.
        Exceptional analytical and critical thinking skills
        Ability to leverage multiple data management tools to organize relevant information and make decisions
        Ability to develop comprehensive software applications, as needed
        Ability to translate complex, technical findings into an easily understood narrative in graphical, verbal, or written form
        Ability to effectively express complex, multi-disciplinary ideas and insights
        Ability to identify and/or develop business opportunities
        Ability to multitask and change focus quickly as demands change
