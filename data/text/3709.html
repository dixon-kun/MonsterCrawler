As a Data Science Engagement Leader you will be responsible for shaping customer opportunities and facilitating the delivery of analytic services and solutions for one or more channels to market, in alignment with the Predix Data Science roadmap. You will work on highly complex projects that require in-depth knowledge of data science technologies and practices in an industrial context.
  • Use technology, industry expertise, competitive intelligence, and a deep knowledge of customer preferences to conceive, evaluate, design & deliver superior product/service offerings that exceed customer expectations.
  • Drive continuous improvement and new ways of thinking, across groups within the division, to improve quality, Data Science productivity and responsiveness based on feedback and changing priorities.
  • Uncover opportunities for collaboration across groups in the division, ensure that cross-team commitments are set, and achieve scale in their personal work efforts by enabling the work of others.
  • Serve as a subject matter expert in processes and methodologies with ability to adapt and improvise in various situations.
  • Use expertise to navigate through ambiguity and prioritize conflicting asks.

    • Basic Qualifications:

  • MBA or other Master’s Degree in “STEM” (Science, Technology, Engineering, or Math).
  • 5-8 years of experience in Product Management, P&L responsibility, or Strategic Consulting
  • GE Leadership Program Graduates will get credit towards relevant work experience, commensurate to the program they have completed

    Eligibility Requirements:

  • Legal authorization to work in the U.S. is required. We will not sponsor individuals for employment visas, now or in the future, for this job
  • Must be willing to travel 15%
  • Must be willing to work out of an office located in San Ramon, CA
    • GE will only employ those who are legally authorized to work in the United States for this opening. Any offer of employment is conditioned upon the successful completion of a background investigation and drug screen.

    Technical Expertise:

  • Effectively manages GE Digital risks while satisfying customer requests and demands
  • Uses outcomes-based methodologies to identify analytics-enabling customer value propositions, and to define iterative engagements that rapidly reduce risk, expose value, and test hypotheses with the customer.
  • Effectively drives customer engagement and execution methodologies by integrating practices from traditional engineering design and analysis methods, heuristics, and data-driven methods to maximize customer outcomes.
  • Expert at customer engagement, outcomes-based selling, objection handling, and establishing credibility around the adoption of new and innovative methods (e.g., lean agile project management, data science) in conservative industries.

    Business Acumen:

  • Leverages knowledge about competitors, trends, and changing regulations across the broad environment to advance the product portfolio
  • Understands global vs. regional requirements and works with regional teams to ensure total compliance
  • Assesses the impact of market dynamics, competitor & customer actions, and identifies appropriate action plans from the research
  • Applies knowledge of sales cycle, including direct and indirect sales, channels, business development goals, channels and alliances, and the product's services model

    Leadership:

  • Articulates the story, uses two way communication and influences outcomes and ongoing results
  • Proactively identifies and removes project obstacles or barriers on behalf of the team
  • Drives stakeholder understanding and acceptance of analytic solutions in their business
  • Envisions, compares and contrasts multiple potential medium range operational futures

    Personal Attributes:

  • Defines objectives, problem statements, and ambiguities before generating and evaluating solutions
  • Makes appropriate decisions and takes appropriate actions in situations where all existing information may not be possible to obtain
  • Maintains excitement for a process and drives to new directions of meeting the goal in the face of unfavorable odds and setbacks
  • Looks to connect ideas and solutions from a broad array of sources, such as benchmarking reports, brainstorming with others etc.
