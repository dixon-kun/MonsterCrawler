UTC Climate, Controls & Security is a leading provider of heating, ventilating, air conditioning and refrigeration systems, building controls and automation, and fire and security systems leading to safer, smarter, sustainable and high-performance buildings. UTC Climate, Controls & Security is a unit of United Technologies Corp., a leading provider to the aerospace and building systems industries worldwide.

At EcoEnergy, we provide intelligent and sustainable solutions for enterprise-wide energy operations and efficiency management. We have built analytical models and correlation logics to deliver energy efficient solutions to you. We work to reduce the carbon footprint, energy wastage, and recover your energy losses.

We are looking for someone who has a good understanding of the enterprise solution sales, consultative selling in Energy Services and Energy management sectors. A proven record of driving sales growth, experience building a successful relationships with senior management, a desire to use innovative and creative approaches to identifying and solving customer problems. It is an exciting opportunity to roll up your sleeves and help grow an established name in the market that still offers a high-energy, start-up feel.

In this Role, you will be responsible for complete sales process from prospecting, reach out, negotiating and closing large multi-year contracts with National accounts (Retail, Hospitality, Banks).

* Prospect and close deals with fortune 500 companies based on the organization's focus list

* Engage senior management on customer side to understand and accordingly present the solutions

* Work with engineering and presales teams to customize the solutions and engage customer in consultative manner

* Work with facilities, Energy, Procurement and Legal teams [ in customer organization] during negotiation and contracting process

* Provide weekly sales reports and detailed account plans

* Work with internal functions to strategize account pursuits and solution development for specific customers

Qualifications 5+ years of Sales and Account Management experience in Energy Management / Energy Services

Experience in engaging senior management

Self driven and willing to learn and talk technology, data science and analytics

50% time in travel

Some late evening / early morning work is expected (primarily team calls)

Education Graduate Degree (MBA is desirable but not a requirement)
