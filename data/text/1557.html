Accuen is looking for talented Trading Supervisor candidates with experience in digital media and/or search marketing, excellent account servicing skills, strong analytical and data enabled problem solving abilities, is comfortable taking risks and has an entrepreneurial spirit that will allow them to be successful in a dynamic culture.

Role & Responsibilities:

The Trading group is the core of our organization, responsible for monitoring campaign performance and assisting the Client Solutions team in managing client relationships. They must possess excellent active listening skills to understand client needs and translate those needs into campaign objectives. Once a campaign is underway, Trading Supervisors evaluate performance on a regular basis to ensure client goals are met or exceeded.

Trading Supervisors report to the VP of Trading & Inventory.

Responsibilities include:

Account Management

* Direct a team that manages client campaigns including: strategy development, project management, report delivery, and client communication.

* Identify organic areas of growth on existing accounts and lead efforts to capitalize on those opportunities

* Develop processes, documents, checklists, etc. that ensure efficient procedures and continual improvement for both new and existing clients.

* Work closely with agency account teams and brand clients to develop best-in-class solutions

* Act as a resource for, and collaborate with, the Client Solutions teams through the sale/up sell process

* Provide strategic insight to develop long term execution plans from client’s stated business objectives and implement those plans

* Communicating with the management team on account and group status in a professional manner befitting a senior level team member

Organizational Management

* Display organizational and leadership capabilities to track progress, execution, and consistency of programs

* Manage a team of Sr. Analysts and Analysts and facilitate each team member’s development

* Train team members and others on trading best practices

Performance Management

* Master trading-related technologies, including DSPs, DMP, 3rd party ad servers and manual exchanges

* Work closely with our partners (technology, media, and data) to oversee the launch and execution of campaigns

* Review campaign performance and ensure the team develops optimization recommendations and make certain campaign recommendations are implemented (either directly or through partners)

* Consult with the Data Sciences team to understand current performance and find opportunities to deliver better performance for clients

* Work with the Product team to enhance the capabilities of trading-related technologies

Required Experience and Education:

* Working understanding of Paid Search and Display (Banner Advertising)

* Bachelors degree in: business, economics, statistics, mathematics, physics, engineering, or advertising. Master’s degree a plus

* Experience working with Microsoft Office Suite

* Advanced Excel/spreadsheet capabilities, Macro/VBA experience a plus

* Knowledge of paid search campaign tracking software a plus

* 4-8 years of work experience, with recent managerial experience in search or online advertising

* Previous experience in media/DR management, search or marketing on a managerial level

* 4+ years of solid client service/account experience with proven track record of managerial roles

Required Skills:

* Proactive, self-starter, capable of setting goals and reporting progress against them

* Exceptional ability to use data to solve problems and understand campaign performance; analytical thinker and processor

* Strong leadership & communication skills

* Team player

* Strong organizational navigation – able to manage and prioritize multiple projects and work well with a matrixed organization

* Be confident in analyzing and acting on marketing data

* Ability to train and inspire people and foster a strong, cohesive working team by giving proper, thorough, and timely feedback

* Data analysis in Excel, including Pivot Tables & basic statistics. Experience with SPSS, SAS, Cognos, Business Objects or other BI & statistical toolsets a plus.

* Advanced understanding and experience with Ad Trafficking (DoubleClick specific a plus)

* Strong business acumen and account management ability– demonstrated ability to be forward-thinking and innovative while understanding client needs and the limitations of our business processes and technologies
