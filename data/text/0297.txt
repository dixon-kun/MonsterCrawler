        The Opportunity:
        The Data Science and Analytics Team is unlocking the vast amount and huge potential of Shutterstock data to evolve the company's key metrics that generate actionable insight to empower data-driven decisions. We provide stakeholders, such as Marketing, Sales, and Product, timely and informative data analysis, drive business growth through customer insight and advanced analytics, enable stakeholders to make informed decisions based on insight and analytics, and turn insight into action.
        As Director of Business Analytics, you will lead data-driven efforts and initiatives focused on monetization, growth, and optimization of our customers' experiences. Reporting to VP, Data Science and Analytics, you will focus on solving complex business problems and communicating solutions through storytelling. You will also lead a passionate team of data analysts in operational, financial, and strategic analysis of new and existing initiatives and projects. You will partner with the Finance department on P&amp;L impact and forecasting analysis as well as the Business Intelligence team to strengthen and enhance our data quality and reporting capability.
        A successful Director of Business Analysis will challenge traditional thinking and drive business through data in new ways that ultimately benefit our customers.
        Responsibilities
        * Solve complex business problems; communicate complex solutions through storytelling
        * Perform business and P&amp;L impact analysis to identify business growth gaps and levels
        * Direct and guide a team of data analysts in operational, financial and strategic analysis projects
        * Partner with Finance and BI teams on the delivery of accurate and timely reporting that are critical to Marketing, Sales, and Product.
        * Work closely with Sales &amp; Marketing and Product leadership to drive the development and execution of new data-driven marketplace offerings
        * Work very closely with Data Governance team to drive our initiatives towards a streamlined data environment for effective business analysis
        * Champion efforts across the company to collect, manage, and use internal and external data to drive better decision making as well as to identify and pursue data-driven growth and monetization opportunities.
        Requirements and Experience
        * Minimum 7+ years of experience managing teams and successfully delivering analytic solutions, ideally in an eCommerce environment.
        * Strong analytical background with business acumen, and heavy experience in business and analytical consulting.
        * Strong experience in solving complex business problems through data and analysis.
        * Proficient and hands-on SQL skills for data analysis.
        * Robust understanding of online measurement and analytics, with the ability to provide meaningful and actionable insights and analysis. Digital attribution experience strongly preferred.
        * Expert level communication skills with a track record in relating technical issues to both technical and non-technical audiences.
        * Extensive experience attracting, managing, coaching, and retaining high-performing team.
        About Shutterstock:
        Shutterstock, Inc. (NYSE: SSTK), is a leading global provider of high-quality licensed photographs, vectors, illustrations, videos and music to businesses, marketing agencies and media organizations around the world.Working with its growing community of over 100,000 contributors, Shutterstock adds hundreds of thousands of images each week, and currently, has more than 80 million images and nearly 4 million video clips available.
        Headquartered in New York City, with offices in Amsterdam, Berlin, Chicago, Dallas, Denver, London, Los Angeles, Montreal, Paris, San Francisco, and Silicon Valley, Shutterstock has customers in more than 150 countries. The company also owns Bigstock, a value-oriented stock media agency; Offset, a high-end image collection; PremiumBeat a curated royalty-free music library; Rex Features, a premier source of editorial images for the world's media; and WebDAM, a cloud-based digital asset management service for businesses.
        For more information, please visit www.shutterstock.com and follow Shutterstock on Twitter and on Facebook.
