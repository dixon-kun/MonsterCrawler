Responsibilities

This position will be within the Data Science/Data Analytics Center of Excellence (DAC) team. You’ll be responsible for creating enhanced visualizations of complex analytics in support of strategic business efforts and will leverage cutting edge research and discovery to guide the Data Science Team in new areas of opportunity with a focus on supporting BI and Visualization.

You will utilize advanced knowledge, especially in the areas of Marketing and Finance, including expert level Visualization experience, Data Science capabilities, including research, analytics. You will use strong interpersonal and cross department relation building abilities and presentation skills. You will help expand the exploratory, innovation, and research capabilities of the team in support of the DAC.

You will create visualizations and analytics for senior leadership which will enable them to make quick evaluations of strategic projects. You will work directly with the business to develop new strategies for reducing churn, increasing gross ads, and increasing overall profitability. Your responsibilities will include not only strategy but development and implementing of Visualization solutions using the latest visualization tools and methodologies.

Qualifications

Must have:

•BS Degree in Statistics, Math, Economics, Engineering, Business Analytics, Data Science, IT, or a related field or equivalent work experience.

•Work experience in IT, compliance or operations and working with data within wireless

•Extensive experience with SQL, Reporting, Analytics, Data Modeling, Data Preparation, Data Mining, Data Visualization and working with Complex Data sets

•Strong working knowledge of Tableau

•Working knowledge of Teradata, Unix

•Advanced knowledge of Microsoft Excel, Word, and PowerPoint

•Experience in presenting detailed analysis to non-technical staff

Ideally will also have:

•Tableau Certification

•Statistical software:  SAS, R, and/or Python

•Experience with Hadoop

•Familiarity and experience with creating analytical data sets

•Working knowledge of machine learning, natural language processing, and statistical analysis methods

•Problem solving skills to develop quick yet sound solution to resolve complex issues

•Teamwork & collaboration skills to work across organizations and lead cross-functional teams

•Communication & stakeholder management skills
