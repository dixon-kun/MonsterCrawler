Health Data Scientist

Cary, NC

The Carolinas Center for Medical Excellence seeks a Health Data Scientist to assist with research and study design, methodology and statistical analysis in support of health care quality improvement and research activities. This role serves as consultant to managers and directors regarding study design and consults on and leads heath care quality improvement initiatives. Duties include reviewing and validating health plan performance measures and performance improvement projects, analyzing clinical and non-clinical data, and survey methodology evaluation and analytics.

This position requires a strong background in health research methodology and data analysis. Position is contingent upon contract award.

Applicants should meet the following requirements:

  • Master’s degree in public health, statistics, epidemiology or related field with 3 – 5 years of experience; biostatistician preferred
  • Working knowledge of HEDIS technical specification highly preferred
  • Knowledge of SAS or comparable analytic software
  • Experience validating research and QI projects, surveys, and performance measures
  • Familiarity with CMS protocols related to External Quality Review
  • Experience reviewing SQL code highly preferred
  • Ability to write analytic plans and conduct program evaluations
  • Ability to assess pros and cons of research methods
  • Ability to implement complex analytic techniques
  • Ability to conduct data analysis and generate analytic reports, charts, and tables with SAS or similar analytic software
