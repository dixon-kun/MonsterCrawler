Clearlink’s summer internship program blends real-world experience with an extensive overview of the marketing industry. Knowledgeable mentors will provide guidance as you gain professional hands-on experience across a variety of digital marketing channels. The summer internship program is 14 weeks in length and offers a unique perspective into a leading provider of content and conversation services supporting top name brands like Dish, CenturyLink, Verizon, AT&T and more. Compensation is competitive.

We are looking for students to work in Data Science in each of the following areas:

  • Information Management
    • MySQL, Business Intelligence cube management, query optimization, stored procedure migration into alternate solutions, AWS Redshift analytical database optimization, +.
  • Digital Analytics
    • Historical evaluation of A/B testing results, market research for competitive analysis with SimilarWeb.  Work will include exposure to Google Analytics, SessionCam, Mouseflow, and Google Tag Manager technologies.

Attributes:

  • Data driven mindset with the ability to manipulate complex sets of information and draw conclusions
  • Innate sense of intellectual curiosity that drives you to be constantly learning
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to work well in a fast-paced team environment

Education:

  • Graduate-level students in a quantitative or technical discipline preferred, undergrads will be considered
  • Ability to work 30-40 hours per week from May 16 - July 29, 2017
