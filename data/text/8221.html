Finance Manager -Performance Analytics - Manufacturing or Industrial Engineering Background a Plus at Z&A Recruiting

San Antonio, TX

Report this job

About the Job

Growing San Antonio based Company with a phenomenal reputation of being people centric is adding to the team due to growth. This great Company is known for giving back to the community. The Company is also well known for its track record of internal promotion and career development. There is the room and opportunity to fast track to manager. Offers great benefits and several weeks of vacation! Culture is open of team work and collaboration with great work life balance.

The Senior Manager of Analytics is responsible for leading a team who provides key support in understanding the drivers of business performance across a broad set of functional areas, including marketing, sales, and commercial operations. This role will act as an internal consultant to our business partners and contribute to the identification of issues and opportunities to enhance the customer experience and improve profitability.

Be a lead part in maximizing performance – Look at output, efficiency, cost, waste.

Measure performance and do more than that

Great opportunity to delve into the story that unfolds – what is going well, what is not, why, and how do we change it.

Responsibilities:

• Mentor and develop a great financial analyst team

• Provide detailed, accurate sales reporting and analysis to the executive leadership team.

• Lead analytical projects that drive optimization of business decisions to ensure we are maximizing effectiveness of sales and marketing resources to improve the performance of the Americas sales team.

• Provide historical sales data to Business Leaders to aid in planning and goal setting.

• Partner with IT and other stakeholders to promote operational efficiencies and the strategic vision of the department while aligning the company goals and objectives towards revenue generation.

• Obtain and maintain technical expertise of available analytics and reporting tools for self and staff. Develop and recommend development of reports to support the business.

• Use business acumen and technical knowledge to develop and deliver Business Intelligence reports for internal customers.

Skills and Experiences:

• Capable of managing multiple tasks and be willing to reorganize work to accommodate projects with very short deadlines

• Strong communication and presentation skills

• Well versed in using MS Power Point to communicate to Sr Management



Qualifications:

• Bachelor’s in Statistics, Mathematics, Computer Science, engineering or similar degree

• At least 3 years in a business leadership role directly managing others to achieve functional business goals

• At least 5 years of sales analysis, data science, financial modeling, business analytics, business intelligence, or related work experience

• Experience implementing analytical, data-driven improvements

• Master’s a plus

• Experience with big data discovery tools

• Familiarity with Hadoop or Tableau  a plus

• Familiarity with enterprise BI solutions (OBIEE, Cognos, Microstrategy, or similar)
