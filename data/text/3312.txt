        You will work as a forecasting model manager in the Pratt &amp; Whitney Engine Services organization as part of the Development Analytics group. The Development Analytics group provides forecasting models and other analytical tools to support the sales and maintenance activities of the Commercial Engines &amp; Aftermarket business, encompassing a significant portion of Pratt &amp; Whitney's $14 billion in total revenues in 2015.
        We will teach you how to use and develop models in our state-of-the-art Monte Carlo simulation system running on a high-performance computing platform. It would be helpful, but not required, if you already possess a basic familiarity with statistical modeling. In addition to model development, you will be involved in the many of Development Analytic's studies and projects to further our understanding of the commercial aftermarket business. These studies often require working across PW organizations (Engineering, Programs, Finance, Sales). Most importantly, we're looking for your ability and willingness to learn, perform and drive projects to completion.
