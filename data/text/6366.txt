        SUMMARY OF POSITION
        Medpace, a rapidly growing global full service Contract Research Organization headquartered in Cincinnati, OH, with operations in more than 40 countries. Following a recent successful sale to a new private equity partner Medpace is focused on further organic growth and adding dynamic leaders to the team for the future success of the organization. As part of this strategic initiative we are looking for a Global Head, Biostatistics, to be based in Cincinnati, OH.
        In this new position you will report to the Vice-President of Biostatistics and Data Sciences and will be a key contributor to the overall success and future growth of Medpace. The Global Head of Biostatistics will be responsible for the leadership and strategic direction of the Global Biostatistics and Statistical analysis teams; ensuring operational excellence and optimal services to our clients.
        Qualifications
        QUALIFICATIONS/REQUIREMENTS
        * PHD in Biostatistics/statistics with at least 10 years of relevant experience in a Biostats leadership position within a CRO;
        * Previous experience and proven ability to lead and coordinate departmental activities related to process building/improvement and integration of new technology;
        * Ability to maintain effective communication with current and prospective clients as well as Medpace senior management and manage multiple critical issues
        WHY MEDPACE?
        The Medpace culture is one of expertise and excellence. We encourage our employees to utilize their talents and experience to take an assertive approach with research, problem-solving and continuous process improvement. Medpace offers competitive salaries and excellent benefits, including bonus eligibility.
