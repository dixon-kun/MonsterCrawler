        Job Summary
        Managing the enterprise manufacturing business intelligence team. Responsible for analysis, selection and modification of enterprise systems and application software in the EMI area. Leading the product team that builds and/or deploys cutting edge analytic and reporting platforms to provide actionable manufacturing data to the hands of the users. Provide overall leadership to the effort required to protect the company's data, tools and information systems.
        Key Skills Required:
        Ability to manage a global team of business analysts, data scientists and developers across different aspects of business intelligence within the manufacturing area. Covering items such as descriptive, diagnostic, predictive and prescriptive analytics
        Previous experience of an enterprise business intelligence environment such as Microsoft BI - such as SSRS, MSAS, SQL Server and PowerBI
        Solid understanding of data science practices such as Model Creation, Model Testing and Model Training with understanding of different types of models such as Time Series, Regression, Clustering, Decision Tree, etc
        Experience of Machine Learning platform such as Azure ML and R an advantage
        Previous experience of creating and enhancing enterprise wide reporting portals with self service capability, preferably in the manufacturing environment
        Big Data knowledge of platform such as Hadoop, Map Reduce, Spark, MongoDB essential.
        Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities
        * Define, develop and implement a department strategy, which contributes to the business strategic direction.
        * Develop and communicate an understanding of the business strategy as it pertains to other IT Departments.
        * Understand and communicate business strategy and develop application &amp; technical infrastructure to support this strategy.
        * Deliver IT services in line with business requirements to internal customers, standardize platforms
        * Financial Management/ Cost Center Management; forecasting of costs and management of IT financials for your IT cost center including variance analysis. Management of Assets, Capital Costs, Prepare ROI related documentation and Business Cases. Prepare and Analyze financial data and results. Create action plans as needed.
        * - Drive awareness of change management activity for high category services ensuring that the business impact of change activity is understood and risks to business processing highlighted.
        * Selects, develops, and evaluates personnel to ensure the efficient operation of the IT function.
        Education &amp; Experience Requirements
        * Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or Management Information Technology.
        * Plus 6 to 8 years' experience in a relevant Jabil IT position OR equivalent external work experience, including at least 5 years' experience in a supervisory or management role within an Information Technology department.
        * ITIL Foundation Certification Preferred.
        * Lean Six Sigma Certification Preferred.
        * Or an equivalent combination of education, training or experience.
