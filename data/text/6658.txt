        Company Overview:
        Charter Communications is America's fastest growing TV, internet and voice company. We're committed to integrating the highest quality service with superior entertainment and communications products. Charter is at the intersection of technology and entertainment, facilitating essential communications that connect 24 million residential and business customers in 41 states. Our commitment to serving customers and exceeding their expectations is the bedrock of Charter's business strategy and it's the philosophy that guides our 90,000 employees.
        Job Summary:
        Data engineering, data science and operations skills combined to increase efficiency of a high-volume, high-availability suite of applications.
        Apply data engineering skills to implement and maintain the operational toolset to drive efficiency in the support of Charter's OSS suite. These skills will be used to extract data from relational and NoSQL databases, as well as subscription to real-time data feeds such as Apache Kafka, to drive operational alerting, metrics gathering and operational reporting.
        As an Operational Analytics &amp; Data Engineer within Charter's Advanced Platform Operations (APO) group, you will research and design data gathering methods for a variety of systems and data sources. You will also employ your technical skills to both operationalize the data extraction and assist with broader team efficiency goals, such as ongoing configuration management and automation of the ecosystem.
        How will you make an impact?
        This is a new group within an existing team that will succeed on our ability to assist the larger team in achieving leaps of efficiency. Individual success will be best enabled through a customer-focused approach applying a broad technical skillset to extract relevant data and implement tools in support of the larger team.
