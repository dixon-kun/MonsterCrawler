Who We Are

Our mission is to invent the future of farming... We're transforming the oldest and most important industry in the world from "gut instinct" to data science. Our platform already exposes critical operational data insights to 1 out of every 3 farms in the U.S. and our users absolutely love us.

Who You Are

You're efficient and thorough; when something lands on your plate, your colleagues are amazed by how rapidly you can work with others to turn around quality execution. Your broad experience and deep understanding of business and financial operations have instilled you with a sense of confidence equally matched by your attentiveness to others' needs and perspectives. You're well-liked and respected among your colleagues; you have a knack for being able to cut through to the core of any issue and build consensus around a common goal. You are a great listener; you respect and consider others' opinions but once you've come to a conclusion, you are decisive in your action. You've mastered the science and art of sifting through quantitative and qualitative information to make well informed, strategic decisions and can effectively communicate the "why" behind your decisions so people walk away feeling confident and informed.

About the Role

As our VP of Operations & Finance, your job will be to work across the entire business, streamlining day-to-day operations to support our rapid growth while adding to our bottom line. You handle the things that don't squarely fit on anyone's plate but can't be ignored or done incorrectly. In that sense, you are an invaluable partner to the CEO and the entire business. You're both tactical and strategic; you focus on planning and goal-setting but also know how to lead the organization to execute on those goals. You are a key partner to the leadership team and help us look ahead to the challenges we will need to overcome as we grow. You spin up new initiatives and teams, figure out how to make them a success, and hand them over to someone more permanent so you can move on to the next challenge. Your success will be measured by your ability to help us scale the business without feeling the burden of ever-increasing organizational complexity.

Tasks You'll be Juggling

* Collaborating with Leadership Team to implement and execute strategic vision; developing short and long-term goals to measure success

* Building a Finance and Operations team from the ground up and establishing processes that allow us to manage the business strategically

* Operating as a trusted business partner to CEO, enabling him to be more leveraged and focus on high-level organizational priorities such as culture, hiring, etc.

* Implementing measurement of and reporting on key business and operational metrics

* Jumping in and help make sure that things get done correctly and on time wherever necessary

* Managing critical one-off or infrequent tasks (annual filings, select a billing platform, terms of service updates, diligence requests, etc)

* Overseeing financial management of the company including; setting budgets, monitoring revenue and expenses and making adjustments as necessary

* Directing the company's accounting and financial reporting policies in accordance with accounting standards and SaaS-based finance best practices

* Generating and distribute financials for the board and investors

* Streamlining business operations and reducing day-to-day complexity for rest of staff, making it easier for every employee to plug into FarmLogs even as we continue to grow

Your Creds

Required

* Business or Finance degree required; Master's in Business Administration preferred

* M&A and FP&A background, with previous experience building or scaling finance/accounting department

* Experience with SaaS business models

* Senior level in-house operations, finance, or strategic management experience gained in a high-growth organization

* Proven track record of success facilitating progressive organizational change and development within a rapidly scaling organization

* Strong mentoring and coaching experience to a team with varying levels of experience

* Results-oriented, strategic and creative problem-solver

* Advanced financial modeling and forecasting ability, comfort and experience with BI and analytics packages/tools

* Exceptional written, verbal, interpersonal, and presentation skills with the ability to effectively interface at all levels of the organization, including the Board

* Ability to operate as an effective tactical as well as strategic thinker

* Comfortable in ambiguous contexts; able to make decisions without perfect information

Nice to have

* Previous experience running a business

* Agriculture background or connection to farming
