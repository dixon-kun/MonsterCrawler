        The Aggregation Operations Specialist will be responsible for coordinating the implementation and ongoing maintenance of AEP Energys municipal aggregation programs. These responsibilities include requesting and acquiring needed data from utilities, coordinating data processing with internal and external stakeholders, coordinating marketing materials and legal approvals with internal stakeholders and assisting communities and brokers throughout the governance process.
        Key Duties &amp; Responsibilities:
        Ongoing Activities
        * Monitor PUC websites and other news outlets for current and relevant information related to municipal aggregations.
        * Foster positive relations with community leaders and brokers to build positive relationships and build a robust lead funnel.
        * Support/backup the Director, as needed, at community/social functions.
        * Complete post-sale win/loss analysis.
        Pre-Sale Functions
        * Review RFPs and Plan of Operation and Governance documents sent from brokers and Community Partners.
        * Create deal summary sheets for upcoming new/renewal municipal aggregations, including refreshes.
        * Conduct deal review sessions with internal stakeholders and draw consensus around timelines necessary to achieve agreed upon deliverables.
        * Obtain needed account and usage data from utilities.
        * Arrange for the utility files to be sent over to the Customer Data Sciences team.
        * Interact from time to time with internal pricing team as it relates to deal inputs and changes.
        Post-Sale Execution
        * Create and formalize a process for municipal aggregations that is comprehensive, anticipates unique needs and leads to flawless execution.
        * Serve as the internal point-person for closed municipal aggregation deals.
        * Involve internal stakeholders about key elements of the deal and lead the process as referenced above.
        * Coordinate with Marketing, Legal, Operations, and other stakeholders to see the aggregation process through to full execution, ensuring Director is appropriately informed.
        * Communicate with brokers and community leaders about the deal status throughout the deal execution phase.
