POSITION DESCRIPTION

The Analytics Principal – Advanced Analytics will be a key member of the Advanced Analytics Center of Excellence (AACOE) in the Global IT Business Transformation organization and work closely with the AACOE staff in formulating/executing departmental plans for insights and analytics capability development. As an Analytics Principal, you will be counted upon to partner closely with AACOE staff as well as other IT, BU and/or Functional peers and stakeholders across the enterprise to identify/extract/ingest diverse data from diverse data environments and repositories to enable the execution of analytics projects in support of business needs.

Additionally you will support the development and execution of standard work processes and metrics for the AACOE to drive efficiency and performance. An ideal candidate will have documented experience in the following:

POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES

Develop and/or enhance analytics platforms to effectively store and manage diverse internal and external data so as to enable predictive/advanced analytics

Partner with BI and other clients/stakeholders as necessary to effectively identify, migrate/ingest, and process pertinent data from diverse databases (SAP, HANA, Oracle, SQL Server, etc.) into advanced analytics data platforms (eg: Hadoop)

Implement proof of concepts on Hadoop stack and different big data analytics tools

Engage with prospective internal clients and stakeholders to understand data/analytics needs and translate into platform implications and requirements

Collaborate effectively with AACOE staff and client team members to define and scope out analytics project plan.

Work with other project members to ensure meeting of project goals.

Participate in business development activities related to furthering the AACOE mission and strategy.

Ensure governance, compliance, privacy, security and regulatory processes are followed.

Contribute to a team-oriented, positive, engaging and challenging 'global' work environment to ensure high productivity, employee engagement, and optimal performance.

IN ORDER TO BE CONSIDERED FOR THIS POSITION, THE FOLLOWING BASIC QUALIFICATIONS MUST BE EVIDENT ON YOUR RESUME

BASIC QUALIFICATIONS

EDUCATION REQUIREMENTS:

GED/High School Graduate

YEARS OF EXPERIENCE:

15+ years of IT experience with a GED/High School Graduate

11+ years of IT experience with Associate Degree

7+ years of IT experience with a Bachelor's Degree

5+ years of IT experience with a Master's Degree

Specialized Knowledge or Skills Required

Experience designing and building data platforms and architectures

DESIRED/PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:

Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Sciences, Mathematics, Statistics, Operations Research, or other data/analytics intensive field.

Experience as a BI, Analyst, Software Engineer, or decision sciences role

7 years in a role with growing responsibility in developing data architectures

Experience with Software Engineering – SDLC knowledge (e.g. Agile, waterfall, or other models) to be able to programmatically generate, acquire, and propose new ways to make digital data accessible/available for analysis.

Solid understanding of foundational concepts (e.g., File systems, data formats, data modeling, and ETL) and architecture frameworks (e.g. Webservices, APIs, and Hadoop) and tools (IDEs, Version control tools, etc.), to navigate and implement enterprise information architecture services.

Hands-on experience with architecting/implementing large scale data solutions, such as --- Hadoop, Teradata, Microsoft Analytics Platform System (APS), IBM Netezza, etc.

Demonstrated experience of strong SQL Skills with RDBMS's such as Oracle, SQL Server.

Well-versed in open source platforms (Hadoop, Linux, Apache)

Proficient in the following programming languages: Java, JavaScript; C#; Python; Scala.

Technical and functional understanding of business intelligence solutions (e.g. data acquisition, enriching, integrating, interrogating and visualizing).

Demonstrated skill in problem definition, scoping and problem solving.

Strong results orientation (driving to deadlines, financial targets, project goals, etc.).

Strong collaboration, communication and organizational skills.

Software architecture development specifically pertaining to predictive/advanced analytics on large unstructured datasets

Demonstrated expertise programming in at least one of the following languages: Java; JavaScript; C#; PHP; Python

Experience in designing and deploying distributed data processing frameworks: NoSQL databases; Hadoop; High-performance computing clusters.

Proficiency in Data Science and Analytics programming & software tools: Python, R, SAS, SPSS; Spotfire; Tableau; Lumira etc.

Strong team player who likes to take responsibility and drive projects to completion
