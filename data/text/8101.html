The Role:

This Director, Assistant General Counsel position will be responsible for assisting on a broad range of consumer regulatory matters related to Capital One's Home Loans business as a member of Capital One's Financial Services Legal team. The position will serve as a legal advisor for Capital One's Home Loans business on all matters relating to residential mortgage, home equity and consumer lending issues. This includes consumer protection regulations, operations, originations, sales, product development, servicing, loss mitigation, and fair lending issues, among others. The Director, Assistant General Counsel will serve with legal professionals on the Financial Services team providing legal advice to the Home Loans Sales, Operations, Servicing and Loss Mitigation business lines. This position will regularly advise Capital One's Home Loans lines of business on creation of new products, services, fees, compliance with consumer regulations, state and federal laws and regulations including but not limited to TILA, ECOA, TRID, RESPA, HMDA, FCRA, FDCPA, SCRA, Dodd Frank Act and related matters and will collaborate regularly with other stakeholders within Capital One.

Responsibilities:

* Serve as legal subject matter expert and counselor, providing advice to Capital One's Home Loans business.

* Provide strategic and proactive leadership on legal and regulatory issues affecting the Home Loans business.

* Ensure timely identification, mitigation, resolution and reporting/escalation of existing and emerging legal issues and risks.

* Support marketing and advertising activities related to mortgage and home equity lending.

* Review internal policies and procedures and advise Capital One's Home Loans business on best practices, policies and procedures.

* Provide advice and guidance to the Home Loans business on applicable federal and state laws, rules and regulations relevant to consumer lending and servicing.

* Support new business initiatives, services, and other innovations in the originations, servicing, and loss mitigation functions.

* Build and maintain effective working relationships with internal stakeholder groups.

A Successful Candidate will have:

* Financial services industry knowledge and expertise, including experience with consumer regulatory requirements relating to residential mortgage and home equity businesses.

* A thorough knowledge of residential mortgage consumer protection laws, including TILA, ECOA, TRID, RESPA, HMDA, FCRA, FDCPA, SCRA, Dodd Frank Act and accompanying regulations.

* Complete understanding of the regulatory framework applicable to a bank originating residential mortgage and home equity loans subject to oversight by the OCC and CFPB.

* General familiarity with GSE and FHA requirements

* Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with minimal supervision.

* Ability to manage large volume of work, including complex legal issues that may arise suddenly.

* Ability to thrive in an environment primarily defined by close collaboration with the Home Loans business and legal colleagues to solve complex legal issues and to develop guidance and advice.

* Ability and willingness to work as a team player, with comfort interacting with associates across departments and job levels, both inside and outside the legal department.

Basic Qualifications:

* Juris Doctorate (J.D.) and a member to a state bar

* At least 7 years of law firm, in-house legal department or government experience in an attorney role

* At least 5 years of experience working with consumer regulatory matters in an attorney role that focus on residential mortgage and home equity regulations

Preferred Qualifications:

* 8+ years of law firm, in-house legal department or government experience in an attorney role

* 8+ years of experience working with consumer regulatory matters in an attorney role, with a focus on residential mortgage and home equity regulations
