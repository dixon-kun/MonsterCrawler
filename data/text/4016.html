The Data Scientist Intern will be part of a team that performs statistical analysis, predictive modeling, algorithm and new product development. These activities are conducted in support of furthering the development of the company’s products and technology.


The Data Scientist Intern’s duties will include, but not be limited to:

  • A research project tailored to their strengths, spanning the domains of machine learning, search, consensus algorithms, graph analytics, process automation etc.
  • Assist the team with the analysis, design, development, testing, and documentation of predictive models, tools, visualizations and algorithms.
  • Building statistical models: after participating in the model design, the intern will work with an experienced modeler in the preparation of data, development of candidate models, evaluation and selection of final models.
  • Completing written documentation and reports of results. This will take the form of business reports, internal technology summaries and statistical analysis.
  • Delivery and presentation of accurate and actionable business and technical analyses to support sales, customer retention, and technology improvement efforts.
  • Developing and improving tools for investigative and analytics workflows.
  • Managing and improving complex computational systems for processing and scoring data.
  • Other duties as assigned.

  • Prefer candidates working towards Master’s degree or PhD in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or a related technical field. Consideration will be given to exceptional Bachelor level candidates as well.
  • Strong coding and scripting skills are required. Working knowledge of Python and/or UNIX scripting tools is preferred, but deep knowledge of any language is valued.
  • Prior statistical modeling and/or machine learning experience is desirable.
  • Experience with large data sets, data mining, natural language processing, deep learning and distributed algorithms is highly desirable.
  • Must have exceptional communication skills, both written and oral, in order to explain technical concepts and analysis implications clearly to a wide audience and to translate business objectives into actionable analyses.
  • Must be able to understand and creatively solve problems that span statistics, technology, and business.

This brief summary is not an all-inclusive description of job duties. Other job duties and responsibilities may also be assigned by the incumbent’s supervisor at any time based upon Company need.
