THE OPPORTUNITY:

Coordinates Customer Insights data management and governance activities across affected customer-facing business areas, Technology, and Marketing. Leads the analysis and documentation of the Metadata, Data Lineage and Data Quality for all data elements under the Enterprise Platform for Insights on Customers (EPIC) data warehouse.

POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES:

Serve as a Data Steward for Marketing in Data Governance efforts to ensure Bank data (EPIC and otherwise) is defined and monitored as per the six principles of M&T's Enterprise Data Management Policy and that they are adhered to in the future state

Partner with internal Data Analysts/Data Scientists to integrate and implement Business Intelligence & Advanced Analytics tools with the data necessary to meet their needs. Effectively manage internal relationships with stakeholders to gain acceptance Bankwide.

Coordinate data additions or retirements to the EPIC data environment through Data Governance policies and procedures and ensure its ongoing maintenance and health

Provide ongoing technical/architectural/data guidance to Marketing/Office of Customer Advocate for any and all tactical/strategic programs and campaigns, both for Sales and Service purposes

Adhere to applicable compliance/operational risk controls in accordance with Company or regulatory standards and policies.

Promote an environment that supports diversity and reflects the M&T Bank brand.

Maintain M&T internal control standards, including timely implementation of internal and external audit points together with any issues raised by external regulators as applicable.

Complete other related duties as assigned.

NATURE AND SCOPE:

The Customer Insights team consults with and delivers customer outreach and analysis across the M&T and Wilmington Trust brands. This role supports these efforts to:

Accelerate identification and acquisition of primary bank and wealth relationships and cross-sell opportunities by leveraging our investments in newly centralized customer data via the Enterprise Platform for Insights on Customers (EPIC)

Maximize the value of each sales and service opportunity by creating and leveraging insight into the full range of customer interactions across branches/offices, phone, mail, e-mail, text message, websites, mobile apps and more

Reduce and minimize the need for the costly, duplicative customer data identification, aggregation and movement activities taking place across business areas

MANAGERIAL/SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITY:

None

MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS REQUIRED:

Bachelor's degree in an applicable field or in lieu of a degree 4 years equivalent in experience

3 years' experience with analysis and data governance – specifically in aggregating data from disparate sources (internal systems, external feeds, the web, etc.) – ideally with customer information

High energy, with the ability to energize others and excite them about data and its importance for the future

Proven success in simplifying and communicating complex data issues/opportunities to unfamiliar audiences such that they can understand and take action

Proven ability to form relationships throughout an organization and work collaboratively to solve data challenges

IDEAL QUALIFICATIONS PREFERRED:

Marketing experience within Financial Services

Masters of Business Administration (MBA) or other applicable Master's degree

Strong skills in facilitation, documentation, organization, and communication

Results oriented; ability to work independently under tight deadlines

Financial/Banking industry experience preferred
