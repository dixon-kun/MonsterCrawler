Job Description :

General Overview:

The consulting workforce leverages combined people, process, and technology competencies to support large-scale, complex customer engagements; collaborates with Product, Infrastructure, and Security teams to configure solutions and deliver value to customers; acts as subject matter expert and trusted advisor in both practice and customer spaces; and works closely with engagement and practice leadership team to define strategic and tactical direction.

The Associate Principal is responsible for driving customer value through client facing advisory and delivery services in support of HM Health Solutions product offerings. Accountabilities include, but are not limited to, leading major workstreams and projects, supporting pre-sales and sales activities, owning deliverables, building key customer relationships, building and sharing institutional knowledge, and developing junior professionals.

This role will collaborate with consulting services, product teams and other internal and external customers / stakeholders to craft vision and scope for all analytics engagements and is accountable for driving the timely, effective and efficient delivery of those engagements. This role will also work closely with business development teams on growth opportunities. You will be responsible for formulating the long term strategy around our analytics, information management, data integration and warehousing, data governance and Business Intelligence functions along with successfully delivering on the engagements for all our internal and external customers in this space. This role will also be responsible for mentoring the teams regarding various standard and emerging healthcare disciplines, enterprise analytics and business intelligence including operational reporting.

Essential Job Functions:

  • Contribute to the delivery and execution of complex, large scale, multi-year (high cost, high complexity and/or high risk) client enterprise transformation programs by: gathering required data through direct observation and/or inquiry and synthesizing data into meaningful observation; analyzing, identifying and problem solving from information sets (issues could be business or systems issues) drawing conclusions from information sets based on analysis, creating hypotheses and presenting recommendations to team; developing client specific requirements, standard operating procedures, functional roles - responsibilities, process flow and user guides.
  • Build, share, and maintain expertise in both practice area and client environment, regularly interface with Product, Infrastructure, and Security teams to deliver on customer commitments.
  • Contribute to operational readiness, organizational change management, solution capability, packaging, and estimating, planning and delivery management.
  • Design and own deliverable artifacts to deliver client value and meet contractual obligations.
  • Lead major engagement workstreams and projects to meet milestones on time, and to deliver with high quality and within agreed to budgets.
  • Contribute to pre-sales and sales activities.
  • Perform personnel management responsibilities including, but not limited to involvement in hiring and termination decisions; coaching, mentoring, and professional development of practitioners; practitioner rewards and recognition; on-going performance management; and evaluation of staff productivity.
  • Plan, organize, staff, direct and control the day-to-day operations of the department; develop and implement policies and programs as necessary; may have budgetary responsibility and authority.
  • Other duties as assigned or requested.

Minimum Required Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's Degree Business Administration, Management Information Science, Health Administration, or relevant field
  • 8+ years' consulting/and or industry experience
  • Ability to travel up to 80% of the time

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master's Degree Business Administration, Management Information Science, Health Administration, or relevant field
  • 5+ years' healthcare/insurance industry experience
  • 5+ years' technology platform delivery experience
  • 5+ years' program/project management experience
  • Experience engaging, influencing, and advising senior executive leaders
  • Experience working in complex matrixed environments.
  • Project Management Professional, Information Technology Infrastructure Library
  • 9+ years of experience in analytics, information management, data integration, data science and/or business intelligence platforms including at least 4 years' experience as a leader (ex: Sr. Director, Principal, Associate Principal) in this space responsible for delivering upon customer commitments
  • Ability to partner with engineering and business teams to create and lead the delivery of innovative and practical roadmaps, communicate with customers, understand the complexities and yet be able to explain them to non-technical resources in a simple manner
  • Proven, hands on experience working with very large datasets and healthcare information models with deep technical expertise across analytics and informatics technologies like SQL, Hadoop ecosystem, SAS, Teradata, R, Tableau, Cognos and Python data packages (or) Proven, hands on experience working with very large healthcare data integration platforms and initiatives with deep technical expertise covering multiple tools across ETL, data validation, information modeling, data mining, data integration disciplines
  • Ability to understand and successfully navigate the complexities of working with data at scale (volume, variety, velocity and veracity), along with the clear ability to mentor and grow others w.r.t those skills
  • Strong understanding and experience in creating, validating, productionizing advanced healthcare business analytical modules, customer and consumer analytics, data integration, data mining, predictive modeling, statistical methods and attribution models along with exploratory, experimental analyses?
