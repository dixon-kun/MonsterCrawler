        Responsibilities and Expectations:
        * Credit Product: Optimize credit product terms and credit strategy to improve the customer fit and profitability of our credit product offerings
        * Credit Risk Analysis: Develop improvements in credit strategy by relating historical patterns to future consumer credit behavior
        * Test-and-Learn: Validate credit hypotheses in a test-and-learn environment using rigorous methodologies, monitoring, and analysis
        * Financial modeling: Build financial and NPV models for different customer segments to utilize in credit decisioning and portfolio monitoring
        * Accountability &amp; Execution: Execute on credit product strategy; Implement multiple initiatives or deployments concurrently; "Devil is in the details" attitude toward execution
        * Analytical Powerhouse: Proven track record of decision making and problem solving based on analytics; strong quantitative orientation complemented by conceptual thinking and customer-orientation
        * Cross-functionality: Consider problems from all angles, including those outside your immediate expertise by bringing in subject matter experts as necessary
        * Executive Communication Skills: Impeccable written and oral communication skills, and the ability to make data-driven recommendations to management team
        * Results-Oriented: Intensely focus on achieving short term and long term goals; able to attain measurable results in an uncertain and fluid environment
        Required Qualifications:
        * Bachelor's Degree in Quantitative field such as Math/Statistics, Finance, Engineering, or Economics
        * 1-3 years unsecured consumer/small business loan or credit line management analytics, particularly within deep subprime segments
        * Strong analytical and technical skills (SQL, Excel), with experience working with very large datasets
        * Some experience with big data platforms or programming (Python, Machine Learning, Hadoop, R, SAS)
        * Strong verbal written and communication skills, strong attention to detail and organizational skills
        * Ability to work autonomously under tight timelines
        * Ability to work in a dynamic environment with the ability to adapt to change and re-prioritize
