Description

You Must Have:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Predictive Analytics, Applied Mathematics, Applied Statistics, Computer Science, Actuarial Science, Business Analytics, Business Intelligence & Analytics, Economics, Marketing Analytics, Mathematics or related discipline.
  • 7+ years of experience in Data Science or related role
  • 5-8 years of demonstrable experience in building predictive analytics and condition monitoring systems in industrial domain.
  • 5 + years of hands-on experience with either Python, R, Tableau, Smart Signal, or similar tools.


We Value:

  • Master’s or Ph.D. Degree in the above disciplines. MBA with Technical Undergrad, Operations Research, Quantitative Finance, or equivalent.
  • Mechanical engineering background with problem solving, ability to draw clear connections between data modeling activities and machine / component failure.
  • Experience working with Start Ups and driving end customer engagements on analytics.
  • Experience responding to Analytics RFPs & participating in POCs with end customers
  • Experience working with remote and global teams in a matrix organization
  • Experience in Condition Monitoring, Failure Prediction or similar analytics applications in Aerospace industry.
  • Familiarity with aircraft systems and data sets.
  • Customer focus and process discipline with excellent collaboration & program management skill.
  • Results driven with a positive can do attitude
  • Travel up to 10%


Location:


  • Phoenix, AZ.

Relocation: Available.
