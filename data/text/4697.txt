        Location: Schlumberger Doll Research (SDR), Cambridge, MA, USA
        Fundamental and applied research projects are carried out in Schlumberger Doll Research (SDR) to develop new technologies for application in the oilfield. The Mathematics and Modeling Department is investigating the development and application of state of the art data science to multi-physics measurement data pertaining to complex problems in the development of oilfields. Scientists leverage domain expertise, extensive data bases and cloud computing infrastructure available locally and throughout Schlumberger.
        Job Description &amp; Responsibilities
        The role of the Research Engineer or Research Scientist is to work in a team of scientists and domain experts to develop and implement new applications of physics-informed data-driven interpretation frameworks. Successful candidates have strong background in domains such as machine learning, signal processing, probability and statistics, cognitive computing, Bayesian frameworks for reasoning and inference. They are excited about working on data science applications in domain-rich problems. They will help elicit information from databases and experts across a wide range of sub-domains. They are committed, talented, and ambitious and have an enthusiasm for science and technology coupled with an ability to work within a team of scientists and engineers. They have strong oral and written communication skills.
        Qualifications
        * MS or PhD degree in electrical engineering, computer science, artificial intelligence, physics, or related fields with thesis work related to the subject domain.
        * Software development skills and familiarity with technology platforms/tools in cognitive computing (e.g. TensorFlow, Theano, Torch, etc.) are preferred.
        Schlumberger is the world's leading supplier of technology, integrated project management and information solutions to customers working in the oil and gas industry worldwide. Employing more than 105,000 people representing over 140 nationalities and working in approximately 85 countries, Schlumberger provides the industry's widest range of products and services from exploration through production. Schlumberger has always invested significant time and money on R&amp;E as a long-term strategy to support and grow its technology leadership. Schlumberger invests more each year in R&amp;E than all other oilfield services companies combined.
