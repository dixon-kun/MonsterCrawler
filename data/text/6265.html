Senior Project Manager - Data Science at Penske Truck Leasing Co., L.P.

Beachwood, OH 44122

Report this job

About the Job




Description





Position Summary:


As a Senior Project Manager, this role will help implement Penske's Big Data Strategy and drive the next generation Supply Chain & Logistics Analytics. Manages and coordinates projects that have organization-wide implications and impact, including international initiatives. The projects at this level are large and complex, often involving more than one product line. The Senior Project Manager works on 3 - 5 projects concurrently. Provides analysis and develops approach and scope on each project. Such efforts require cross-function involvement with customers, vendors and suppliers. Responsible for the formalization of comprehensive project plans, including clarifying timelines, milestones, goals and ownership. The Senior Project Manager focuses on creating the shape of the projects, removing barriers and leading the assigned team to successfully completing the project.




Major Responsibilities:


-Leads and assists the development and implementation of Enterprise Big Data Strategy and framework at Penske Logistics


-Develops and leads the creation of innovative models, tools and algorithms that enable Penske and its customers make data driven decisions and gain deep actionable insight from our rich and rapidly growing data across all product offerings.


-Works with cross functional teams - including business, IT, Finance, etc. - to proactively identify and prioritize project opportunities.


- Develops business case document that not only explores various solution options but clearly articulates recommendations and justifications to gain leadership approval.


-Develops project implementation strategy and schedule


-Manages the clients' expectations regarding our team's satisfactory performance, project execution and deliverables











Qualifications





-6 - 9 years related experience with 2 - 3 years' experience in leading projects and at least 3 years' experience designing and building machine learning application using both structured and unstructured datasets.


-Demonstrated experience with two or more machine learning techniques including logistic regression, decision trees, random forests and clustering.


-Practical experience programming using Python, R or other high level scripting languages is required. Knowledge and experience with SQL is required.-Masters degree in Engineering, Operations Research,,,, Statistics, Applied Math or related quantitative field preferred, Bachelors required


-Good understanding of the logistics, transportation and warehousing industry and passionate about data and solving big industry wide challenges


-Willingness to travel as necessary, work the required schedule, work at the specific location required, complete Penske employment application, submit to a background investigation (to include past employment, education, and criminal history) and drug screening are required.





Physical Requirements:


-The physical and mental demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an associate to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.


-The associate will be required to: read; communicate verbally and/or in written form; remember and analyze certain information; and remember and understand certain instructions or guidelines.



-While performing the duties of this job, the associate may be required to stand, walk, and sit. The associate is frequently required to use hands to touch, handle, and feel, and to reach with hands and arms. The associate must be able to occasionally lift and/or move up to 25lbs/12kg.



-Specific vision abilities required by this job include close vision, distance vision, peripheral vision, depth perception and the ability to adjust focus.





Penske is an Equal Opportunity Employer, including individuals with disabilities and protected veterans.
