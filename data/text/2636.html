MDA Information Systems LLC is looking for a UI/UX Designer at our Charlottesville, VA location.  We have an immediate opening for a UI/UX Designer supporting our Rapid Prototype team. We’re looking for someone that is passionate about UI/UX design and implementation. We need an expert who can work hand-in-hand with a multi-disciplinary team to bring creative concepts to life. You will work with members of the Rapid Prototype development team, composed of scientists and engineers, and our valued clients to build mockups and functional prototypes.

The Company : MDA Information Systems LLC is a major supplier of weather information and geospatial intelligence. The firm serves a diverse client base including commercial and governmental organizations in agriculture, energy, defense, and civilian government.

MDA is an industry leader in earth observation remote sensing technology and image processing.  We build satellite ground systems that enable users to acquire and process imagery from the most sophisticated Earth-observing satellites in the world:  much of the satellite imagery that you see on Google Maps was generated by our software!

To be qualified you must be a U.S. Citizen and eligible for TS/SCI. Active TS/SCI; with CI polygraph is preferred.


Essential Functions

  • Collaborate with other dev team members & customers to define and implement innovative solutions for application aesthetic and experience.
  • Make innovative and engaging design and user-experience decisions.
  • Conceptualize original ideas that adhere to simplicity and user friendliness in solving complex design challenges.
  • Create wireframes, storyboards, user flows, and process flows to communicate ideas for stakeholder discussions.
  • Conduct user research and user testing.
  • Consistently implement new technologies, trends, and best practices to keep designs fresh.

Required Qualifications/Software:

  • Undergraduate degree in Design, Computer Science, Web Development, or comparable discipline
  • Portfolio demonstrating breadth of front-end development skills
  • Expert knowledge of HTML and CSS
  • Proficient in JavaScript and related frameworks
  • Extensive use of APIs and understanding of HTTP and REST architecture
  • Experience with version control (e.g. Github, Stash)
  • 4-6 years of overall development experience

Desired Qualifications:

  • Graduate degree in Design, Computer Science, Web Development, or comparable discipline
  • Experience with AWS
  • Experience developing applications for data science
  • 6-10 years of overall development experience
  • Creative background, a passion for unique data visualizations
  • Experience with React, Angular, and/or Polymer
  • Experience prototyping both browser-based and native mobile applications
  • Experience with JavaScript mapping frameworks (e.g. Leaflet, OpenLayers, Google Maps)
