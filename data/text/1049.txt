The Center for Marketing Science Innovation is a new team within Facebook focused on understanding consumer's response to media for applications in advertising research and product development. In service of this goal the Neuroscientist will work with vendors and internal Facebook partners to analyze data outputs from consumer neuroscience research and translate findings into meaningful results for advertisers. We seek an expert in neuroscience and data science with experience influencing the advertising industry and analyzing complex data sets. This team is instrumental in informing Facebook&#8217;s overall product strategy, advertising strategies recommended to Facebook clients, and new measurement platforms used across the advertising industry. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in the media industry, research methods and statistics. Experience with implicit, biometric, and neuroscience research methods strongly preferred. This position is full-time and based in New York, NY.&#13;
