Description

Data Science Service Area – Big Data Lead

Who is CSRA? The name represents the coming together – figuratively and literally – of two outstanding companies: the North American Public Sector business of CSC and SRA. It's indicative of our common values and cultural elements, including our collective dedication to supporting government clients in their mission. The CSRA name is not an acronym. It is not an abbreviation for a longer name; rather it represents the joining forces of two industry leaders.

We are seeking candidates to make a difference by providing meaningful data science solutions to optimally manage nation's information. We are seeking candidates with subject matter expertise in Big Data disciplines to support our Data Science Service Area in our corporate office in Falls Church, VA.

The role of a Big Data Lead includes:

* Provides domain expertise in big data distributed processing frameworks, Hadoop MapReduce and Yarn, Apache Spark, Open-Source and Commercial distributions (Cloudera, Hortonworks, MapR) and Cloud-based services (AWS EMR, Azure HD Insight)

* Provides domain expertise of open-source and commercial NoSQL databases (e.g. MongoDB, Cassandra, Accumulo) that implement document, key-value, columnar, graph, and search databases

* Provides domain expertise of hybrid data architectures that combine big data with enterprise data warehouses, data lakes, data hubs, and streaming data

* Drive market demand for data science solutions by providing concise messages tailored for CSRA customers and their desired outcomes

* Help define our go to market strategy for Modern data management, data warehousing, big data, and advanced analytics offerings

* Collaborates closely with our leveraged service center to incubate, design and deliver leveraged data science offerings

* Help activate, energize and engage a community of data management engineers and practitioners to build a strong sense of community, to provide career mobility, achieve meaningful corporate reach back and to design training and certification pathways

A Big Data Lead is familiar with Big Data market trends, helps to define new markets and ensures that CSRA is in a position to be the best choice for meeting our customers' data science needs through collaboration with customers, partners, and internal stake holders to understand the requirements and connect them with CSRA capabilities and offerings.

Additional duties:

* Reports to and supports the Data Science Service Area Director

* Identifies market trends in technology for data science solutions with a focus on Big Data

* Helps set CSRA strategy for the data science portfolio

* Works with vendors to identify new potential modern data management offerings

* Works with system architects to builds proof of concepts and demonstrations

* Helps incubates offerings

* Works with industry in tailoring offering strategies specific for them and work with appropriate government relations to shape agency procurement

* Shape procurement by presentations to clients and through speaking engagements

* Determines which alliances to pursue and events for CSRA to participate

* Actively works with partners to shape procurements and future technologies

* Identifies market for the data science portfolio along with industry

* Identifies offerings to place on GWACs like GSA Schedule 70.

Qualifications

Basic Qualifications

* Bachelor's degree or equ1ivalent combination of education and experience

* Bachelor's degree in business administration, computer science, information technology or related field preferred

* Twelve or more years of business solutions experience

* Experience working with company products and services, business processes and re-engineering

* Experience working with business environment, business industry and competitor products and services

* Experience working with appropriate programming languages, operating systems, product-line hardware and software

* Experience working with office productivity tools; delivery assurance and/or industry standards on deliverables

* Experience working in a global organization of size, scope and complexity

Other Qualifications

* Requires expertise and experience implementing three or more of the following technology areas: database design, data warehousing, master data management, big data, NoSQL, data science, data analytics, machine learning, streaming data.

* Deep knowledge of data formats and structures, data storage, retrieval, transport, optimization, and serialization

* Demonstrated experience developing engineering solutions for both structured and unstructured data.

* Experience working with large datasets including data integration, data migration, analysis and visualization

* Strong communication skills to communicate with customers, support personnel and management

* Strong skills at managing risks during product launches

* Strong time management skills

* Strong creative thinking, problem solving, planning, time-management and organization skills to balance and prioritize work

* Strong human relations skills to select, develop, mentor, discipline and reward employees

* Strong ability to manage multiple programs, handle multiple assignments simultaneously, and switch between assignments quickly

* Strong ability to create and maintain formal and informal networks

* Strong ability to lead and work in a team environment

* Strong ability to manage by influence

* Willingness to travel
