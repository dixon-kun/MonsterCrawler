About Citi


Citi, the leading global bank, has approximately 200 million customer accounts and does business in more than 160 countries and jurisdictions. Citi provides consumers, corporations, governments and institutions with a broad range of financial products and services, including consumer banking and credit, corporate and investment banking, securities brokerage, transaction services, and wealth management. Our core activities are safeguarding assets, lending money, making payments and accessing the capital markets on behalf of our clients.



Citi’s Mission and Value Proposition explains what we do and Citi Leadership Standards explain how we do it. Our mission is to serve as a trusted partner to our clients by responsibly providing financial services that enable growth and economic progress. We strive to earn and maintain our clients’ and the public’s trust by constantly adhering to the highest ethical standards and making a positive impact on the communities we serve. Our Leadership Standards is a common set of skills and expected behaviors that illustrate how our employees should work every day to be successful and strengthens our ability to execute against our strategic priorities.



Diversity is a key business imperative and a source of strength at Citi. We serve clients from every walk of life, every background and every origin. Our goal is to have our workforce reflect this same diversity at all levels. Citi has made it a priority to foster a culture where the best people want to work, where individuals are promoted based on merit, where we value and demand respect for others and where opportunities to develop to are widely available to all.



Global Head of FX Strategy, Content and Analysis



The Global Head of FX Strategy, Content and Analysis will be charged with the mandate to lead the design, development and implementation of a fully integrated and market leading FX content offering.



This role will lead CitiFX’s team covering Strategy, Flows, Technical Analysis, FX Wire and several other delivery channels and content products within CitiFX.   By fully integrating and aligning this confederation of high calibre teams together under a single leadership structure we are looking for a senior leader who will be both capable and highly motivated to offer and grow a world-class product suite.  Furthermore, delivering industry-leading content is essential to both enhancing our client value proposition while also providing our trading teams with the leading edge markets insights to effectively interpret markets to successfully manage risks.



Given the rapidly changing FX industry, Citi's content teams also play a critical role in ensuring we remain at the forefront of industry developments, covering global FX markets through an increasing use of proprietary data and analytics.  Growing this analytical endeavour will be an important objective for this team.



This role will report to the Head of Foreign Exchange and be part of the Foreign Exchange trading management team.



Job Overview

  • Leading Citi's FX strategy team via thought leadership and regular written content on Foreign Exchange markets.
  • Manage a global FX content team across all generation functions and offerings, developing talent and ensuring a positive, team-oriented approach.
  • Closely collaborate with Macro, Economics and other Citi Research teams to ensure consistency and leverage insights across every part of Citi.
  • Developing analytical insights thorough analysis of proprietary data sets on foreign exchange and other relevant markets and growing Citi’s leadership in data science driven content.
  • Regular interaction with Citi sales and trading teams and helping those teams analyse market development and generate thoughtful trade ideas.
  • Regular client visits and deep interaction and connectivity with Citi's platinum and priority clients.
  • Representing Citi FX in both Citi and industry conferences, events and forums.
  • Develop new content product and distribution strategies in partnership with Citi Velocity and FX sales teams.

New York, NY or London, UK


Qualifications
  • Proven experience (15+ years) in FX strategy or similar roles
  • Exceptional written and verbal communications skills
  • Demonstrated leadership ability and prior experience in leading research/strategy teams
  • Top tier presentation skills and experience presenting to large, diverse audience
  • Industry knowledge and significant degree of existing client connectivity
  • Ability to work with and partner across diverse businesses/products/geographies and manage complex and competing interests
  • Global focus with willingness to travel frequently to connect with Citi clients
  • Innovation minded and passion for finding new areas to leverage and deliver value-added content and analysis.
  • Strong skills in data science and quantitative analysis
  • Bachelor’s degree required (finance, mathematics, engineering, or a related field)
