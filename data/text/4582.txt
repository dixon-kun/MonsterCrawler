             Job Description
            Biogen is looking for a Senior Data Scientist to join our Data Science team in our Digital Health Technology and Data Science organization. As a member of the Data Science team, you will be tasked with weaving structured and unstructured data together from across Biogen to help answer some of our biggest questions.
