At eHealth, we are passionate about solving our nation's toughest data problems and we are seeking a data scientist to help us unlock the value within our data.  This person will be expected to assess the data opportunities within our company and then help to build the required technologies to capture those opportunities.  The Data Scientist will be part of a new and growing team that is positioned to have a valuable impact on the entire company; eHealth will invest in your career development.

There are some important things we need you to have to be successful in this role...

  • You have a sense of excitement to dig through our data and liberate its untapped value.
  • Working experience with complex data analysis and computational statistics.
  • Knowledge of advanced data modeling, machine learning algorithms, and probability theory.
  • Comfort working with diverse data systems such as SQL, Mongo, MySQL, AWS and/or Hadoop.
  • Knowledge of various ETL techniques and frameworks.
  • Extensive work with a blend of the following methods: statistical analysis, forecasting, means clustering, linear regression, cross validation,principal component analysis, support vector machines, decision trees, algorithm-building, or other similar analyses.
  • Ability to perform analysis for omni-channel customer analysis including cross-channel attribution, cross channel optimization, audience building and segmentation, conversion and engagement.
  • Solid programming background in Java, Python, R or related languages/environments.
  • Experience deploying elastic search.
  • Can dream big and execute.
  • Ethical leadership when handling data.

And if these are also part of your work and experience, that makes us extra happy...
  • Ability to create beautiful data visualizations using Kibana, Grafana, or similar tools.
  • Experience working with call center operations.
  • Product familiarity with Adobe Analytics, Cisco systems, Google Analytics, Oracle SQL, Qlikview, Tableau, or Wherescape.
  • Recent experience deploying master data management (MDM) or similar BI platforms.
  • Experience working in highly regulated industries like Healthcare.

Here are some example data problems we're trying to answer with better data...
  • What are the attributes of our best customers?
  • What insights can be gained by deploying natural language processing technologies through our call centers?
  • How is the health insurance market going to change in specific markets and what should we do about it?
  • How can our company evolve to use data more effectively in the months and years to come?

The Company
An industry innovator over the past decade, eHealth, Inc. is a technology company that is transforming the way health insurance is bought and sold in the United States.

Today, eHealthInsurance.com has enrolled over 5 million people in health insurance coverage and is the leading online marketplace for individually and family health insurance products in the nation. Licensed to market and sell health insurance in all 50 states and the District of Columbia, eHealthInsurance was responsible for the nation's first Internet-based sale of a health insurance policy, and we have partnerships with more than 180 health insurance companies, and offer more than 10,000 health insurance products online.  Every day we provide thousands of visitors with information about a broad array of health insurance-related topics and plans, including a selection of price and benefit options, complemented by a full-service Customer Care Center of highly trained customer service representatives.

eHealth is a publicly-traded company (Nasdaq:EHTH) with headquarters in Mountain View, CA, but this position will be based in San Francisco.  Our San Francisco office is directly across from AT&T ballpark.
