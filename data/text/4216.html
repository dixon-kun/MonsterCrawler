Position Title - Senior Accountant

Position Description/Responsibilities:

* Month End Close – Preparation of journal entries, review of general ledger activity and preparation of account reconciliations

* Review AP, corporate credit cards, and expense reports (Concur) for proper coding

* Bank reconciliations – prepares monthly bank to book reconciliation for all cash accounts

* Fixed assets – maintains fixed asset subledger; tracks and records in-transit fixed assets and capital in process; understands capitalization rules, runs fixed asset reports, records depreciation, and prepares fixed asset roll-forward. Responsible for periodic physical fixed asset count

* Prepaid assets – maintains prepaid asset reconciliation; records prepaid assets and related amortization; ensures proper classification between LT and ST

* Liabilities – responsible for ensuring accrued liabilities are recorded properly; works with outside vendors to track costs; and liability account reconciliation

* Assists with consolidation and intercompany reconciliation process

* Coordinate with subsidiaries in APAC and EMEA and assist with reviewing of international management reports

* Assist in preparation of the month over flux analysis for management review

* Own the publisher payout process. Run reports from internal databases to compile a monthly list of payments to our publishers, and coordinate with business owners for approvals and entering the payments into our payment processor (Tipalti).

* Partner with Engineering Team and Account Managers to reconcile and resolve issues related to publisher payout process

* Miscellaneous – assists with preparing and gathering information for external audits, assists with ad hoc projects

Core Competencies include:

* In depth experience with ERP, specifically Netsuite (preferred)

* Excellent skills with Microsoft Suite: Word, Excel

* Strong analytical and communication skills

* Details and thorough

* Ability to organize, set priorities, and work independently

* Multi-currency experience a plus

Requirements:

* 4+ years of relevant experience

* College degree with emphasis in Accounting

* Has or working towards CPA a plus

* Big 4 experience a plus
