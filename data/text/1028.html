Description

:

We are looking for an experienced and intellectually curious Data analyst to help drive our Data science agenda as part of our new our new global technology and Innovation hub based in Budapest. Reporting to the Team Head, you will help us do smart and elegant things through data – whether it’s helping with our data strategy, embedding new marketing technology or developing new APIs and algorithms to support Digital Products.

All of this will help deliver on the team’s goal which is to transform our delivery of digital products, marketing and sales strategies across EMEA so that our interactions with our clients are frictionless, precision-targeted, and always on.

Responsibilities:

•Support marketing campaign improvements through the creation of segmented and modelled data and support of campaign teams in selection creation

•Support marketing campaign improvements through the delivery of campaign evaluation and campaign reporting

•Accelerate the data science agenda by defining precision-targeting requirements, evolving our scoring models

•Support the sales and marketing strategy and planning through the creation of client insight and reporting e.g. Commercial dashboards

•Act as the custodian of our data amp; compliance obligations across EMEA

•Be able to work with new data sources for rapid insight and support the subsequent automation of new data feeds and the growth of the analytics platform

•Support the automation of data processes to enhance execution

Key Technical:

•Pig/Hive for creation of analytical data files (desired)

•SQL (must)

•Python/R for data analysis and modelling (desired)

•Tableau/Qlikview/PowerBI for delivery of management information and reporting

Requirements:

•Educated to degree level in a numerate discipline.

•Intellectual curiosity and a passion for data and emerging technology is a must.

•Highly quantitative, with a keen eye for teasing out insights from volumes of data.

•Hands-on understanding of technology - i.e. automation/coding of data calculations, defining requirements for APIs and algorithms, running A/B testing evaluations.

•Experience using analytical and database tools such as SQL (must have), R (desirable), Hadoop (desirable)

•Strong Excel skills with experience in Lookups, Pivot Tables, Macros, etc.

•Experience using data visualization tools, in particular Tableau/PowerBi/Qlikview

•Ability to clearly and concisely present information to internal stakeholders in different parts of the organisation across all levels

•Strong team player and ability to multi-task
