Crestron Electronics has an exciting opportunity for a Big Data Scientist . This is a great time to join a dynamic team in a fast growing organization.

We are seeking a polished, proactive, and professional Big Data Scientist to join the Data and Analysis group. This individual will be responsible for analyzing the big data our new and existing products collect.  Whether the data is structured or unstructured, this person must analyze this data and find patterns to formulate questions that can be answered.

"Our company will provide relocation"


  • Work with stakeholders to identify business problems
  • Work with engineers on reviewing the data collected by new and existing products
  • Strong experience working with datasets of different sizes and shapes
  • Ability to run algorithms on large size data effectively and efficiently
  • Analyze data to find trends and patterns to draw insights and conclusions
  • Helped build an AI strategy for new and existing products

  • Programming experience in R is required
  • Strong knowledge of Hadoop is required
  • Experience with Azure or AWS machine learning tools is a plus (Apache Mahout, Spark MLib)

Must be able to work in the US without sponsorship

Please note only applicants who meet the requirements listed in this ad will be considered

At Crestron, we build the technology that integrates technology. Our automation and control solutions for buildings and homes let people control entire environments with the push of a button, integrating systems such as A/V, lighting, shading, IT, security, BMS, and HVAC to provide greater comfort, convenience, and security. All of our products are designed and built to work together as a complete system, enabling you to monitor, manage, and control everything from one platform.
