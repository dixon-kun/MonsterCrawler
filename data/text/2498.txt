        Job Description
         Do you like working with big data and using it to drive decisions and the direction of products that impact the lives of people every day?
         As a Data Scientist dedicated to decision support and strategy at Pluralsight, you will have the opportunity to see a direct link between your work and business critical questions, insights, and outcomes.
         You will be part of an established team of top-notch, collaborative data scientists working on breadth of challenges across the company and you will be working with a cutting-edge data platform. For senior members of the team, your responsibilities will also include providing leadership and mentoring to teammates and championing data-driven decision making throughout the organization
          What You Will Do
          Interact with key internal business stakeholders in our Marketing, Product, Engineering, Sales, Finance, Corporate Development / Strategy departments to understand operational and strategic questions and determine the appropriate technical approach
          Develop innovative analytical solutions to business problems using statistical, mathematical, machine learning, or other quantitative methodology
          Analyze large and complex datasets, interpret trends and patterns, and distill findings into actionable insights
          Effectively communicate results, findings, and insights from complex analyses to any audience, particularly to company leaders
          Collect, process and cleanse data from a wide variety of sources. Transform and convert unstructured data set into structured data for analysis
          Develop and implement predictive models, machine learning algorithms, and/or optimization models
          Distill the assumptions, general approach, and output of solutions based on mathematical algorithms or models to business partners and company leaders, eliminating &#8220;black box&#8221; distrust
          For more senior team members, provide leadership and mentoring to teammates on best practices, methodology, and business problem solving.  Serve as lead on projects and for specific departments of the business, and oversee work of junior data scientists
         Qualifications
          Strong foundational knowledge of statistics and machine learning theories and techniques, particularly stochastic modeling, time series, operations research, and/or econometrics
          Excellent critical thinking, problem solving, and analytical skills
          Strong capability to manipulate and analyze complex, high-volume data from a variety of sources
          Mastery of one or more scientific programming languages (e.g. R, Python)
          Fluency in big data platforms (Spark, Hive, Impala, Kafka, etc.) and relational databases (e.g. PostgreSQL, SQL Server)
          Demonstrated experience delivering effective data visualization solutions (e.g. Shiny apps, D3, Tableau)
          Excellent communication skills and the ability to serve as an &#8220;analytic translator&#8221;
          Ability to problem solve independently and prioritize work based on anticipated business value
          Experience in solving operational or strategic business problems; amount of work experience can vary with seniority of the applicant, but hands-on experience in a business (not
            only
          academic) setting is critical for success in this role
          M.S. or Ph.D in Applied Math, Operations Research, Computer Science, Engineering, Computational Finance, Economics or other relevant quantitative discipline
