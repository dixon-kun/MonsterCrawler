Full stack developer to help build out our web-based analytics and operations platform to manage Core Tech IT infrastructure and assets. As a full stack developer you will have the opportunity to build new applications from scratch, and influence system design.
  • Capture system level requirements by brainstorming with Sr. Architects, Data Scientists, Businesses & Product Managers, Sr. Engineers
  • Identify the scope of the work
  • Help lead agile software development and manage the core inputs and outputs in each cycle.
  • Engage in technical discussions; participate in technical designs and present technical ideas through white boarding. Work closely with your peers and engage in a fast paced development team
  • Execute in a fast pace delivery mode and focus in delivering tasks to meet the product release goal
  • Maintain code quality through best practices, unit testing and code quality automation
  • Drive code deployment establishing best practices and updates/revisions of software
  • Seek and provide feedback on design and development
  • Demonstrate the ability to make informed technology choices after due diligence and impact assessment
  • Understand whole product, its modules and the interrelationship between them while being an expert in the assigned component or module
  • Help in design and implement interfaces and information exchange between systems
  • Possess advanced domain knowledge and show great customer focus. Expert in processes / methodologies and skilled in build, release & deployment processes
  • Articulate the need for scalability and understand the importance of improving quality through testing.

    • Basic Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or in “STEM” Majors (Science, Technology, Engineering and Math)
  • A minimum of 2 years of professional experience in Software Development/Data ETL/Cloud Infrastructure OR Master’s degree with 1 years of experience in Software Development

    Eligibility Requirements

  • Any offer of employment is conditioned upon the successful completion of a background investigation and drug screen
  • Be an expert in assessing application performance and optimizing/improving it through design and best coding practices
  • Be an expert in core data structures as well as algorithms and has the ability to implement them using language of choice
  • Be responsible for providing technical leadership and defining, developing, and evolving software in a fast paced and agile development environment using the latest software development technologies and infrastructure
    • GE will only employ those who are legally authorized to work in the United States for this opening. Any offer of employment is conditioned upon the successful completion of a background investigation and drug screen.
  • Interest in Big Data technologies
  • Experience with cloud development/deployment, specifically AWS (S3, EMR, Lamda, etc)
  • Strong knowledge of Object Oriented Analysis and Design, Software Design Patterns and Java coding principles
  • Hands-on experience with streaming Big Data services (Kinesis)
  • Hands-on experience with messaging queues (RabbitMQ, Kafka, etc..)
  • Hands-on experience in web services (REST, SOAP, WSDL etc.), SQL Database such as Oracle MySQL, PostgreSQL
  • Hands-on experience with Python application development
  • Platform engineering and full stack development experience is a plus
  • Experience with open source in-memory compute solutions: ATLAS (Scala)
  • Interest in data science/analytics
  • Experience with Big Data / Hadoop / NoSQL Database (Cassandra etc.) / Data Warehousing is a big plus
  • Strong problem solving abilities and capable of articulating specific technical topics or assignments
  • Experience in building scalable and highly available distributed systems
