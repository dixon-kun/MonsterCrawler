Key Responsibilities:

The Analytic Developer will support the Signals Analysis customer. The data scientist will have developer experience or a hybrid developer/data analyst. Must have experience in design, development, and troubleshooting of analytics. Various mission areas need assistance in determining the value of their products and workflow. The analytic developer will forward deploy to work with mission subject matter experts and create analytics that will feed CRYSTALEYES, a data-driven tool that enriches and visualizes outcomes.

An initial mission area has been identifies and tasks will be for developing and evolving analytics for situational awareness, trending, and change detection. The work will involve identification of source data, combination methods, and visualization needs.

The analytic developer will work with CRYSTALEYES back end developers in getting the data to CRYSTALEYES and/or incorporating code into its codebase.
