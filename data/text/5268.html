Market Place Manager/Coordinator

Manage the day to day responsibilities for all marketplaces.
Manage inventory on Amazon, eBay and other online marketplaces.
Create, manage, and optimize listings.
Coordinate marketing for all online marketplaces, including promotional opportunities, pricing optimization, and content creation (guides, banners, HTML templates, and email).
Research and identify new marketplaces, including logistical needs and pricing.
Maintain detailed understanding of the mechanics of all backend tools, processes and systems.
Monitor, analyze and report on marketplace performance metrics, sales, competitive listings, and pricing.
Review customer feedback and marketplace health, updating processes as needed.
Proactively identify product, service, and marketplace issues and resolve them accordingly.
Work with internal and external resources (ex. Amazon, EBay, Wal-Mart, Sears, Alibaba, etc.) to ensure products are accurately described and priced.
Increase listing visibility and traffic through marketplace tools and promotions.
Uncover and execute new initiatives within each marketplace that will improve the customer experience and increase overall business.
Build relationships with key players on the Amazon account team.
Track weekly marketplace performance metrics and implement strategy to improve them.
Lead projects and objectives that improve operations within each marketplace.
Manage and maintain strategies on Amazon FBA, Amazon Drop Ship, Amazon Retail/Seller Central/Vendor Central, Seller Fulfilled and Seller Fulfilled Prime to meet growth objectives as well as other Market Places platforms.

Working hours: M-F 8:00-4:30

Skills:
Prefer a Degree in Business, Marketing, Data sciences, or Related fields, and/or several years of recent on the Job Position/experience in the online marketplaces.
Experience in e-commerce, online marketing, Social Media or equivalent.
Knowledge of online marketplaces (Amazon, eBay, Wal-Mart, Sears, Alibaba, etc.), experience buying and selling (large scale) on each marketplace is preferred.
Excellent interpersonal skills with the ability to work efficiently and effectively within a collaborative, cross-functional environment.
Exceptional attention to detail, the ability to plan, and to manage multiple tasks and projects in parallel.
Ability to create, analyze, and comprehend marketplace data and sales analytics for use in forecasting, production, and communication with others.
Experience with product listings, data feeds and web analytics is strongly preferred.
Ability to work well under pressure in a, fast-paced team environment while meeting critical deadlines.
Proficient with Microsoft Office (Excel, Access, PowerPoint, etc.)
