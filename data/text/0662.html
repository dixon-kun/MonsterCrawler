NEW YORK CITY GRAPHIC DESIGNER FOR FINANCIAL SERVICES/TECHNOLOGY FIRM
New York-based technology company in the world of finance. Our client brings together the world's top technologists, data scientists and analytical minds to help the world invest and is experiencing explosive growth.
Outstanding Opportunity:
Graphic Designer:
We are looking for a talented Graphic Designer to become a core contributor of our growing Creative Team. The candidate will drive the development of company's brand and own a major portion of the brands creative operation needs.
The ideal candidate is a design self-starter with an exceptional ability to inspire others through his/her creative vision, strategy and polished graphical design skills. This is an exciting opening for someone who enjoys working in a team-oriented and collaborative environment.
Responsibilities

: Responsible for the development, implementation and production of designs and design elements as instructed
: Execute design work and production assets that meet defined quality standards
: Effectively manage content and brand assets in an organized and accessible manner
: Help develop and integrate design workflow processes
Skills & Experience
: Bachelor of Arts/Fine Arts or Bachelor of Science degree in Graphic Design/Visual Arts & Communication
: 2 plus years relevant experience in graphic design
: Must have interest in financial services industry
: Full proficiency of Adobe Creative Suite (InDesign, Photoshop, and Illustrator)
: Experience with editorial design and annual report design
: Experience with infographic design, print design and corporate brochure design
: Eye for pixel-perfect detail
: Strong creative vision, communication skills and problem solving abilities
: Deep appreciation for simple solutions to complex problems
: A team player that enjoys working collaboratively
: Must have interest in Financial Services/Technology area envirinment
Please send resume to TomC@bachrachgroup for consideration and please indicate salary.
