        Senior Director, Data Analytics
        At Capital One, data is at the center of everything we do. When we were a tiny startup we disrupted the credit card industry by individually personalizing every credit card offer using statistical modeling and the relational database, cutting edge technology in 1988! Fast-forward a few years, and that little innovation and our passion for data has skyrocketed us to a Fortune 200 company and a leader in the world of data-driven decision-making.
        Twenty-five years after Capital One was started it's still led by its founder. Be ready to join a community of the smartest people you've ever met, who put the customer first, and want to use their data skills to make a difference.
        As a Senior Director, Data Analytics within Enterprise Model Risk, Analytics, and Data (EMRAD), you will shape and lead a new team in the validation of business impacting analytical tools. You will enhance your strategic technical and analytical skills, while also working closely with business leaders across Capital One to influence business strategy. With a network of hundreds of Data Analysts, Quants, and Data Scientists, challenging projects with an eye on the bottom line and a focus on work/life balance, we've create a dynamic environment with plenty of room for you to learn, grow, and realize your full potential.
        What you'll do:
        This dynamic position will initially help shape the strategy and structure for a new validation team. This function will have a significant impact on Risk Management, and has the potential to shape validation procedures across Capital One.
        In addition to establishing the framework for this team, you will lead the team in critical validation efforts, such as testing and reviewing analytical tools, maintaining a centralized system of record, and oversight of inventory refreshes and reviews. The individual in this position will also be a key member of the Enterprise Model Risk, Analytics, and Data (EMRAD) senior leadership team, driving the team's agenda. There will be plenty of opportunities for you to exercise your strategic and analytical capabilities.
        Given the substantial impact this position will have on Capital One, we are looking for someone who can passionately lead a team of Analysts and masterfully communicate and influence across the organization, all while pushing the envelope on our technologies.
        On a given day you will be:
        * Creating and refining a new Enterprise-wide analytical tools validation framework
        * Design and lead analytic validation projects
        * Understand business processes associated with analytical tools across Capital One, and the nature of tools usage within those processes
        * Leading the development of projects, setting and managing expectations, and delivering results through self and/or others;
        * Guiding the benchmarking of analytical tools methodologies and performance
        * Defining roles and responsibilities
        * Directing the annual inventory update
        * Overseeing validation at tool initiation
        * Ensuring timely and accurate reporting of all EUCs in scope
        * Ensuring adherence to the validation requirements
        * Communicating validation work to senior management and stakeholders
        * Leveraging education, colleagues and training opportunities to develop solutions to business problems
        * Lead and build the validation group, through contributions to hiring, training, and developing the team
        An ideal candidate will possess strong problem solving and conceptual thinking abilities in addition to communication, interpersonal and leadership skills. Prior experience driving tradeoffs within a business will be beneficial in this role. This position will be in a fast-paced and entrepreneurial environment where you will be handling multiple concurrent projects while working independently and in teams.
        Basic Qualifications:
        * Bachelor's Degree
        * At least 5 years of experience with professional data analysis
        * At least 5 years of experience managing people
        * At least 3 years of experience with leading edge data technology and modeling
        * At least 3 years of experience with data management or model risk management
        Preferred Qualifications:
        * Master's Degree or Doctorate in a Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics discipline
        * 10+ years of experience with professional data analysis
        * 5+ years of experience in financial services industry
        * 5+ years of experience in developing statistical or econometric models, or 5+ years of experience in validating statistical or econometric models
        * 2+ years of experience with data governance
        * 2+ years of experience with predictive analytics
