        What You'll Do
        As a Course Operations Coordinator, you'll work closely with team members to lead the day-to-day operations of our digital courses. You will be primarily responsible for ensuring smooth McKinsey Academy course runs, community management, and course administration.
        You'll oversee all operational aspects of digital courses, including: course set up: (course configuration, development of course calendar), participant onboarding (systems enrollment, group configuration, designing/hosting online kickoff sessions), and course operations (TA management, group work grading).
        In addition, you'll handle all communications with course participants (regular course announcements, individual 1:1 requests, and customer support) and monitor and analyze quantitative and qualitative data from multiple sources on the participant experience, provide recommendations for improvement based on this analysis
        You'll manage and define best practices for communications between the program managers, course operations, client services, and product / technology teams. You'll be responsible for conducting 1:1 feedback calls to assess participant's engagement with course content, and identifying opportunities to improve course and user experience design.
        You will also define, document, and implement best practices process/tool improvements for course operations team, and with other stakeholders (PMs, client services, product/engineering).
