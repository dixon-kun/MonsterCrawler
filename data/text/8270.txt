        Job Description
        Job Summary:
        Manage medium to large or multiple small to medium sized analytics projects. Responsible for project delivery including defining the business case, developing schedules, facilitating execution, and reporting status. Supports analysis activities as needed.
        Job Duties:
        * Delivery
        * Actively manage and monitor activities for all analytics projects.
        * Execute project scoping and initiation documentation including; demand/backlog management and project prioritization.
        * Produce high quality and timely project documents, status reports, QA reviews and presentations.
        * Develop and document project objectives, work plans, team members/structures, progress, strategies, scope and budgets.
        * Hold team members accountable for:
        * Delivery of work items on time
        * Reflecting status of work in defined management tool
        * Build strong partnership with IT PMO for delivery of IT related work.
        * Perform project post mortem/retrospective and document lessons learned.
        * Analysis Support
        * Conduct analysis in support of project delivery and produce documentation (engagement letters, requirements, etc.).
        * Conduct analysis/investigation on various subjects in support of overall team delivery.
        * Team Operations
        * Promote, encourage, and drive adoption of team processes, procedures, and delivery methods.
        * Continually identify ways to improve team efficiency in delivering work.
        * Mentor less experienced project managers / analysts.
        Qualifications:
        * Bachelor's degree required
        * Must have 7 years related experience along with a minimum of 3 years leading projects; prefer experience with delivering analytics/data science projects.
        * Working experience/certifications in Agile and/or Lean delivery frameworks required.
        * Strong oral and written communication skills are required; must be able to communicate effectively at all levels in the organization and with personnel located at LCO and in field locations.
        * Strong problem-solving skills required.
        * Must be proficient with Microsoft Office and Visio.
        * Must be able to work with little oversight.
        * Exposure to the Oil and Gas (exploration and production) industry preferred.
