Qualifications

* An advanced degree, preferably an MBA, Master's degree or PhD in a quantitative discipline (statistics, applied mathematics, econometrics, economics, operations research, or marketing science)

* 6+ years of business experience using analytics to drive business/marketing results specifically in the retail industry

* Previous consulting experience (ideally at a tier-one consulting); and/or deep experience in other client service oriented roles

* Demonstrated ability to develop and apply information-driven analytic approaches to solve critical business questions in a practical manner with tangible results

* Extensive experience managing analytic resources (statistical, data, econometric), both on-shore and off-shore, to create meaningful and measurable impact

* Experience leading cross-functional teams, and in-depth of knowledge/defined involvement in helping frame, design and execute solutions for challenging business situations

* A team-orientation and collaborative approach; outstanding listening skills and the ability to manage through influence

* Strong analytical and data management skills; knowledge of analytical packages such as SAS and/or R

* Excellent problem solving and communication skills

Who You'll Work With

You'll work with the New Ventures Marketing Team in Jersey City, NJ.

McKinsey New Ventures fosters innovation driven by analytics, design thinking, mobile and social by developing new products/services and integrating them into our client work. It is helping to shift our model toward asset-based consulting and is a foundation for –and expands our investment in –our entrepreneurial culture. Through innovative software as a service solutions, strategic acquisitions, and a vibrant ecosystem of alliances, we are redefining what it means to work with McKinsey.

As one of the fastest-growing parts of our firm, New Ventures has more than 1,500 dedicated professionals (including more than 800 analysts and data scientists) and we're hiring more mathematicians, data scientists, designers, software engineers, product managers, client development managers and general managers.

The McKinsey Marketing Navigator is a new software solution developed with the client's Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) in mind. It is focused on optimizing marketing investment from strategic to tactical levels, always with the decision maker in mind. Marketing Navigator is a rapidly growing solution in constant evolution. Analytics is the basis behind the decisions in the Marketing Navigator solution and we combine best in class econometrics and heuristics techniques to derive insights, as well as supporting clients with specific analyses when relevant.

What You'll Do

You will work with Industry Experts on complex client engagements from initiation through delivery.

As a Marketing Analytics & Effectiveness Specialist you will work with client representatives and McKinsey consultants in joint problem-solving sessions and facilitate the definition of project goals, tasks, and resource requirements. In this role you'll recommend process improvements regarding how projects are scoped, built, tested and deployed.

Additionally, you'll accompany clients in their use Marketing Navigator to sustainably drive high impact. This involves engaging with client sponsors on a regular basis to discuss how to drive user adoption, assist with questions they might have regarding the Solution usage or applicability and the building of client capabilities.
