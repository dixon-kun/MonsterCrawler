About Citi


Citi, the leading global bank, has approximately 200 million customer accounts and does business in more than 160 countries and jurisdictions. Citi provides consumers, corporations, governments and institutions with a broad range of financial products and services, including consumer banking and credit, corporate and investment banking, securities brokerage, transaction services, and wealth management. Our core activities are safeguarding assets, lending money, making payments and accessing the capital markets on behalf of our clients.



Citi’s Mission and Value Proposition explains what we do and Citi Leadership Standards explain how we do it. Our mission is to serve as a trusted partner to our clients by responsibly providing financial services that enable growth and economic progress. We strive to earn and maintain our clients’ and the public’s trust by constantly adhering to the highest ethical standards and making a positive impact on the communities we serve. Our Leadership Standards is a common set of skills and expected behaviors that illustrate how our employees should work every day to be successful and strengthens our ability to execute against our strategic priorities.



Diversity is a key business imperative and a source of strength at Citi. We serve clients from every walk of life, every background and every origin. Our goal is to have our workforce reflect this same diversity at all levels. Citi has made it a priority to foster a culture where the best people want to work, where individuals are promoted based on merit, where we value and demand respect for others and where opportunities to develop are widely available to all.



Position Objective:


This is a product management position responsible for delivering major business initiatives within TTS Enterprise Services, Analytics, Big Data & Innovation Commercialization Group.  The successful candidate will work with the Product, Compliance, Risk, Finance, Operations, Technology and front office teams to drive forward advanced analytics and data commercialization initiatives for TTS.



In addition, this role will cover a diverse range of Advanced Analytics & Big Data disciplines, facilitating best practice adoption & business benefit realization across Payments, Receivables, Trade & Commercial Cards businesses.



Key Responsibilities:

  • Direction and oversight of Data commercialization and Advanced Analytics projects
  • Delivery of  Advanced Analytics projects into mainstream production / BAU environments and document benefits
  • Communicate the value of Advanced Analytics approaches to the wider TTS Audience
  • Work with the Data Commercialization Board to prioritize Advanced Analytics projects
  • Guide Innovation lab and D10x Growth Projects in the execution of Advanced Analytics / Data Commercialisation
  • Assist in the preparation of projects for Growth Board / Data Commercialization reviews (fail fast)
  • Engage with TTS data and run speculative machine learning proof of concepts
  • Provide oversight of all program work streams in line with the overall program strategy of holistic technology and operations improvements.
  • Manage project schedule, deliverables and milestones across multiple work streams and regions.
  • Facilitate effective communication between the many key stakeholder groups to enable efficient execution of program goals.
  • Deliver regular status reporting to management and key stakeholders through a comprehensive program governance structure, supporting a continual technology & operations delivery schedule.
  • Evaluate and communicate any possible risks to the completion of the project tasks
  • Organize, lead and facilitate project-related meetings, including the creation of agendas and preparation of materials.
  • Escalate issues and manage prioritization as needed.
  • Proactively manage changes in the project scope, identify potential problems and devise contingency plans.
  • Identify improvement opportunities, process risks, gaps or any help needed and communicate with the business program owner.
  • Facilitate use of Program Management best practices and governance structure across all work streams.

Development Value:


TTS is a data rich organization and it is only just starting to transform into a data driven / advanced analytics organization.  This gives huge opportunity for an ambitious candidate to make an impact on a global scale in a top class company.  TTS is investing in its Big Data framework and there is already a well-developed Citi wide Enterprise Analytics Platform on which a successful candidate can deliver results.  The candidate will be intimately involved with the TTS Innovation Labs and D10x Transformational Growth programs which are taking non traditional  / fail fast approaches to the development of new technologies.  The key will be delivering innovative ideas into a production / BAU environment where they deliver value for TTS.



As a Product Manager in the highly visible Enterprise Services team, the candidate will also further develop their knowledge on Cash Management, Data Management & Innovation and leading industry tools and practices such as Big Data, Business Intelligence and Data Quality processing.  They will have Senior Management visibility, increasing responsibility and as such, will require good presentation and high level communication skills.



Broad exposure to other Citi business lines, as well as Global Functions and the Citi Architecture and technology groups will provide a significant knowledge expansion across multiple facts of the Banking industry.
