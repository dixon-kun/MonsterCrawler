Who is Georgia-Pacific? Watch to find out!

Georgia-Pacific Consumer Business aims to further strengthen the Brand Building organization through the addition of experienced top talent to lead Brand Analytics (aka Marketing Analytics, Business Analytics, Advanced Analytics), based in Atlanta, Ga.
The Consumer Business within Georgia-Pacific focuses on building iconic American brands with consumers, shoppers and retail customers. Our brands include Dixie®, Vanity Fair, Mardi Gras, Brawny®, Sparkle®, Angel Soft, Quilted Northern and Stainmaster. On top, we are the largest provider of Customer Brands (aka Private Labels) in paper products.
The Brand Analytics capabilities are essential to our Consumer Business as they will enable us to provide frequent, rapid, holistic, data-driven and actionable insights and recommendations in the areas of brand building, ecommerce and innovation, by applying integrated descriptive, predictive and prescriptive analytics, so that we can perform better experiments and/or make better decisions and, faster and with a higher level of confidence.
The Brand Analytics team is part of our broader brand building capabilities, called Brand Acceleration, which includes consumer knowledge, design, packaging, linear-, digital- and social media, content, shopper marketing, promotions, in-house design and digital agencies, in-house research services and the Brand Building Leaders for each of our 5 business units.
We are seeking a Leader, Brand Analytics, who will lead the team and support all brands, eCommerce and innovation and supports, influences and challenges all businesses. This role will be an integral member of the Brand Acceleration leadership team and will report to the Chief Marketing Officer. This role includes 4-6 direct reports.
A Day In The Life Typically Includes:
  • Initiate, lead and oversee the planning, development and activation of brand analytic programs across all brands, customer brands ecommerce and innovation. This includes the application of integrated, descriptive, predictive and prescriptive analytics.
  • Provide frequent, rapid, holistic, data-driven and actionable insights, recommendations to support business decisions and experiments.
  • Outline measurement-, analytics- and tracking plans for our (customer) brand building, ecommerce and innovation efforts, including the data needed, analysis processes, tools, (new) quantitative models, reporting and visualization.
  • Continuously provide analytics that support the continuous optimization of our targeting, marketing, ecommerce and initiative executions, incl. audience segmentation, linear-, digital- and social media, search, coupons, shopper marketing & media, website optimization etc.
  • Closely collaborate with IT and Business Services on data, enterprise solutions, tools and processes and partner with other advanced analytical capabilities in the areas of pricing & merchandising, sales and supply chain.
  • Identify, hire and maintain key external partners to continuously improve our approaches and/or experiment with new ones.
  • Lead the Brand Analytics team, including the supervision of direct reports and hiring, training, retaining and progressing talent.

Want to learn more about Georgia-Pacific?


We're part of your everyday life. From the walls of your home to the Dixie® plates on your table, the boxes at your local grocery store or the Quilted Northern® in your bathroom, there's a good chance a Georgia-Pacific product is nearby. We need people like you: People who can help us make life's simple necessities better.


Georgia-Pacific and its subsidiaries are among the world's leading manufacturers and marketers of tissue, paper-based packaging, office papers, cellulose, specialty fibers, non-woven fabrics, building products and related chemicals. Our familiar consumer brands include: Quilted Northern®, Angel Soft®, Brawny®, enMotion®, Sparkle®, Mardi Gras®, and Vanity Fair®, as well as the Dixie® brand of disposable cups, plates and cutlery. Its Harmon subsidiary is among the world's largest recyclers of paper, metal and plastics. The company employs approximately 35,000 people directly.
