        Assessment Specialist - Stamford CT and New York NY
        About Point72
        Point72 Asset Management is a global private investment asset management company managing the assets of its founder, Steven A. Cohen, and employees. The Firm focuses primarily on discretionary long/short equity investing and makes significant quantitative and macro investments. It is headquartered in Stamford, Connecticut, maintains offices in Tokyo, Hong Kong, Singapore, London, Boston, and New York.
        If you want to join a world-class investment management organization that operates in a fast-paced, dynamic environment and rewards talent with recognition and increased responsibility, then this is the right opportunity for you.
        About the Data Sourcing &amp; Strategy Team
        Data Sourcing and Strategy is a group within the Market Intelligence department that provides investment teams with access to market and other third party data in a compliant, effective manner.
        Job Description
        DSS is responsible for managing the data evaluation process for new, differentiated and alternative data (non-market data) sources. In this role, you will be required to create business use cases and determine applicability for a portfolio of new data sources. This includes working closely with DSS management, Market Intelligence, Point72's investment professionals and Point72 Aperio, the in-house data science team.
        Career Advantages
        * Knowledge and exposure to the fast pasted and competitive hedge fund world in which our firm practices
        * Managing projects from conceptualization to productization, gathering feedback from our portfolio managers and building products designed to improve their investment processes
        * Domain expertise in using differentiated big data to generate alpha, across a dozen industries and sectors
        * Building a network of external business relationships with companies ranging from start-ups to Fortune 500s that have differentiated data sources, and liaising with internal teams to deliver the most value adding data product
        Desirable Candidates
        * Have a strong understanding of Point72 and industry specific key performance indicators and how to apply KPIs to the fundamental investment research process
        * Experience working with disparate data sources, preferably in the investment research space
        * Project management and/or product development experience
        * Ability to quickly assess the value of new data sources and data sourcing opportunities across multiple sectors and industries
        * Experience utilizing vendor and customer relationship management tools such as Salesforce, task management tools such as Asana and Business Intelligence tools such as Tableau
        * Knowledge of and experience with databases, ETL frameworks and business intelligence tools a plus
        * Basic excel required, VBA and Macros a plus
        * SQL and Python, R, or another scripting language strongly recommended
        * ETL experience including usage of Hadoop, Spark, etc. a plus
        * Ability to simultaneously manage multiple projects at different stages with internal and external stakeholders
        * Working knowledge of statistics and experience using statistical packages for analyzing datasets, such as R and Python, a plus
        * Strong written and oral communication skills
        * 3-5+ years of experience in finance, adtech, marketing tech or a company that utilized 3rd party data to inform business decisions
        * Experience working with data science teams
