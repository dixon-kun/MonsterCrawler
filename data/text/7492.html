Job Description

The Intelligence Community Information Technology Enterprise Management (EMT) will provide an IC-wide service desk with a single point of contact for IC ITE service consumers to report incidents and receive support.

IC EMT will provide an IC Watch with near real-time health status, and situational awareness via dashboards with live status feeds from service providers.

Provided along with this document is the IC EMT supporting documents which illustrates the government's view of how IC EMT services shall be delivered and the interactions those services have with IC agencies and other IC ITE Service Providers.

Responsibilities:

  • Serve as a data analytics or data scientist subject matter expert in collecting, analyzing, and interpreting qualitative and quantitative data from multiple sources for the purposes of documenting results and analyzing findings to provide service improvement recommendations in support of the Enterprise Management Team (EMT) program.
  • Assist with data ingestion, data warehouse architecture, tuning, the development of Structured Query Language (SQL) queries, and performing data conversion in support of automated data ingestion and analysis.
  • Create custom analytic and data mining algorithms to help extract knowledge and meaning from vast stores of data.
  • Refine a data processing pipeline focused on structured, unstructured, and semi-structured data refinement.
  • Support quick turn and rapid implementations and larger scale and longer duration analytic capability implementations.
  • Use various data business intelligence tools, including TIBCO Spotfire, Tableau, and Microsoft Power BI to assist with data visualization and analysis in support of stakeholders.
  • The ideal candidate will successfully operate in a fast-paced work environment, Multi-task, and be comfortable with truncated delivery deadlines and open-ended questions.
Qualifications

Qualifications:

  • TS/SCI Clearance is required and must be current, preferably DIA (will need to take a CI poly)
  • BA or BS degree in Computer Science
  • Security Certification or ability to obtain within 30 days of hire
  • 3 years of experience with data analytics, data visualization, and reporting using various business intelligence tools, including TIBCO Spotfire, Tableau, Microsoft Power BI, or similar
  • Experience with complex data processing pipelines, including Extract Transform Load (ETL)
  • Experience data mining and analyzing unstructured and semi-structured data sets
  • Experience with data warehouse architectures
  • Experience with Microsoft SQL server data tools, Microsoft Business Intelligence Developer Studio, or similar
  • Experience with troubleshooting and developing using PowerShell
  • Experience with MariaDB, MySQL, or Microsoft SQL Server Analysis Services (SSAS) and Integration Services (SSIS) for automating data ingestions from EXCEL, SQL, CSV file, Web-service, and AWS data sources
  • Knowledge of data ingest and database architecture, including the design and composition of database tables, columns, data types, and database layouts, such as data warehouse concepts, design and data integration workflows, and NoSQL database architecture
