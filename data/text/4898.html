About the Job

Senior Python Developer


Atlanta, GA





eHire’s client, a technology firm passionate about solving real problems using their proprietary software platform that delivers actionable predictions to their customers, has engaged eHire in a search for a Senior Software Engineer, with strong Python and SQL knowledge.  A background or academic focus in predictive data analytics and machine learning would be preferred.





The Impact


The selected individual will be a contributor to the development of machine learning, data science and analytics related applications, making our clients’ customers industrial products work much more efficiently





The Challenge


This selected individual will challenged with improving on current code, generating new code, helping with the design & development.





The Opportunity


The selected candidate will not only have the opportunity work on some of the leading solutions in this space, but also be mentored by a stellar group of technologists.  As an early career developer, this position will give you a breadth and depth of knowledge in a variety of technologies that will stay with you throughout your career!








Responsibilities include:

  • Build quick-turnaround proof-of-concept applications
  • Build longer-cycle, well-tested releases for the predictive analytics platform
  • Design, build, and test server-side Python components for a predictive analytics reporting platform
  • Design, build, and test SQL/NoSQL queries to populate front end data visualization components and server-side processes.





Requirements
  • BA/BS in Engineering or Computer Science or a related technical field.
  • The field of study is less important than having a passion for what you work on and learn!
  • 5+ years of development experience (or an impressive code repository) using Python, Git
  • Prior use of Advanced SQL experience
  • PostgreSQL experience
  • Comfortable interacting with business and technical resources
  • Experience working in an AGILE environment
  • Problem solver, self-starter, and strong communicator
