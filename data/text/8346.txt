        Company Overview
        Flowserve is the recognized world leader in supplying pumps, valves, seals, automation, and services to the power, oil, gas, chemical, and other industries. With more than 18,000 employees in more than 55 countries, we combine our global reach with a local presence.
        Position Description
        Join a team whose services are in high-demand. Flowserve is seeking the best and brightest to join its Commercial Operations &#8211; Systems &amp; Analytics team. This team, working in concert with the information technology department, is responsible for delivering systems and business intelligence solutions for the quotation, sales and customer service business processes.
        The Senior Business Analyst will be responsible for supporting ETL, data warehouse, reporting and dashboard solutions. The analyst will be responsible for defining system requirements, designing the user experience, conducting testing and implementing solutions throughout the global organization. This role will serve as a liaison between the business and information technology departments; requiring constant communication with data analysts/scientists, sales, commercial operations, product management and operations leaders.
        Position Requirements
        * Bachelor's degree in Business, Computer Science, Information Technology, Data Science, Engineering or related discipline.
        * 5+ years of experience in a corporate environment with a minimum of 2 years in a role specific to business intelligence, customer relationship management (CRM) or configure/price/quote (CPQ).
        * Excellent interpersonal, written/verbal communication and leadership skills with the ability to quickly build credibility, influence change and make recommendations to all levels.
        * Strong analytic and problem solving skills with attention to detail.
        * Strong organizational and project management skills with an ability to multi-task, recognize interdependencies, prioritize and execute on assigned deliverables.
        * Demonstrated eagerness to learn complexities of our business and become subject matter expert for areas of focus.
        * Strong facilitation skills with emphasis on coordinating and cultivating strong relationships with business partners; team player with the ability to solve issues that require coordination across functional areas.
        * Advanced knowledge of Microsoft Office products (Excel, Word, PowerPoint, Visio).
        * Availability and willingness to occasionally work "off-hours" to support global associates in different time zones.
        * Advanced database querying capability (SQL) and understanding of data warehouse design.
        Preferred Qualifications
        * Master's degree in Business, Computer Science, Information Technology or Data Science.
        * Experience with Microsoft SSAS, Microsoft SSRS, SAP HANA and/or SAP Business Objects technologies.
        * Subject matter expertise in regards to account planning, territory management and/or master data management system design.
        * Demonstrated ability to balance desire for standardization versus accommodating business process complexity and variation.
        Preferences
        * Continuously enhance system and analytic capabilities to drive sales growth, increased efficiency and forecasting accuracy; maintain a rolling action item list (RAIL) for in-process tasks and prioritized "wish list" items.
        * Develop understanding of business processes in order to best merge data from disparate sources in to standardized data marts, leveraging master data systems when possible.
        * Respond to user inquires or reported problems in a timely manner; conduct root cause analysis to find solutions for prevalent process or data flaws.
        * Develop detailed and logical requirements that can be passed on to the information technology department and/or third-party contractors for execution.
        * Conduct user acceptance testing.
        * Develop and deliver training presentations and materials to ensure adoption of new tools and system capabilities.
