RMM Analyst Job Posting (Associate Analyst)

Key Roles and Responsibilities

  • Execute multiple survey research projects under the supervision of senior project staff
  • Contribute to the creation of study training materials, methods reports, IRB submissions, and other study documentation
  • Test data collection instruments
  • Coordinate, prepare and conduct detailed training session and reference documents under supervision of senior project staff
  • Coordinate project tasks between practice groups and operations
  • Create custom reports based on project needs
  • Review survey data results and ensure quality control of client deliverables
  • Test new survey research and operations methodologies and approaches, and work with the team to develop best practices based on current research for the use of new and existing methodologies

Knowledge and Skills:

  • Basic knowledge and understanding of survey methodology and data collection execution
  • Experience in MS Word, Excel and PowerPoint
  • Detail orientated and able to multi-task
  • Quantitative and logical thinking skills
  • Statistical software knowledge (e.g. SPSS or SAS) a plus
  • Coursework in data analytics, data mining, data science, etc. a plus
  • Experience with in-person data collection a plus

Experience required:

  • Bachelor degree in social sciences or related field, Masters a plus
  • 1 - 4 years of tenure/ survey research industry experience or strong academic/internship experience in survey research or equivalent combination of education and work experience
