        Responsibilities:
        * Drive transformational initiatives to modernize Hearst's voice and video communication platforms to meet the demands of a modern workforce.
        * Continuously leverage opportunities for operational efficiencies through a thorough assessment of existing assets, capabilities and needs

        * Manage the cost of services and support for Hearst's voice and video communication platforms
        * Develop KPI's and appropriate Metrics to provide strong visibility into service availability, quality, and customer satisfaction
        * Provide team leadership and vision to align engineering skills to communication technology transformation across the Hearst landscape.
        * Oversee Incident and service request management for communication platforms, ensuring appropriate SLA's are upheld accordingly
        Qualifications:
        * 8+ years managing globally diverse Voice over IP Systems
        * 8+ years managing telecommunications carriers and vendors
        * Able to create and maintain an operational budget for all responsible systems
        * Experience with Cisco Call Manager, Avaya, and other systems
        * Experience with video conferencing systems
        * Strong awareness of ITIL, common IT controls and frameworks
        * Proven ability to stay abreast of technological changes in the field of VoIP, Video Conferencing and Collaboration tools
        * S. in computer science or equivalent technical degree.
