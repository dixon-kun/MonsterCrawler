Job Description

The Marketing Analytics and Consumer Insights Director is responsible for enhancing GVWB’s insight for business leadership to more effectively measure the efficacy of our marketing initiatives and develop a disciplined learning agenda by implementing test/control experiments throughout all markets and businesses. These insights will be critical so that we successfully position ourselves by leveraging key data, market intelligence, and trends by market segment and product. The objective is to improve GVWB’s MetLife’s competitive positioning and guide our teams in terms of addressing market needs, consumer, customer, broker and partner focus, strategic alignment, and anticipated market opportunity.

In addition, this role is responsible for analyzing the performance of our current GVWB marketing campaigns and provide comprehensive and actionable reporting on current results. This leader will be responsible for partnering with the Enrollment organization to deliver post-enrollment reporting as well as for the National and Regional markets to measure the efficacy of our marketing programs.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop learning agenda for P&C and non-P&C campaigns which include designing and implanting test and control experiments in sponsored and broad market. Partner with digital marketing team to design coordinated tests.
  • Leverage AV Segmentation and attribution modeling with data science and digital marketing team
  • Product/program trend analysis and account-specific results including the Accident & Health trend reports, Life Insurance/Enrollsmart campaign performance and FEDVIP results.
  • Liaison with corporate Marketing Science to leverage consumer insights and industry trends to activate consumer decision journey process and understand competitive environment.
  • Partner with finance to develop marketing allowables measurement to provide guidance on the efficacy of current marketing programs to inform future efforts and spend.
  • Brand health tracking measurement in partnership with corporate marketing team
  • Leadership of five employees to ensure teams have a disciplined process to deliver results
  • Work with Finance team to create budget and forecasting analysis on research and business reporting budget.
  • Provide executive management reports detailing the cost benefit of additional reporting and analysis.
  • Liaison with internal business partners/data owners (UW, Finance, etc.) to ensure ongoing reporting of relevant business metrics to senior leadership.
  • Manage win/loss analysis across segments in partnership with market leadership teams to help develop competitive strategies for their individual market segments.


Required Qualifications

  • Undergraduate degree required
  • Strong record of success performing analysis and reporting
  • Experience in creating, executing and measuring test and control marketing experimentation
  • 5-10 years of experience within a given function (e.g., Marketing, Finance, Analytics)


Preferred Qualifications

  • MBA or other advanced degree preferred
  • Accomplished change management leader, able to operate effectively in a highly matrix environment.
  • Experience leveraging consumer insights and working with market research results to implement in the business
