The Manhattan College School of Business seeks applicants for two tenure track positions in Business Analytics. One of the positions can be filled as early as Spring 2017, the other is for the Fall 2017 term. Rank will be commensurate with qualifications.

The candidate should possess an earned Ph.D. in an area closely related to business analytics (e.g., data science, business statistics, management science, and business intelligence) by the start of the appointment. We are also interested in candidates that have a strong computing background and whose doctoral training is in a related area such as computational finance, supply chain analytics, marketing modeling and applied econometrics. Candidates must provide evidence of teaching effectiveness, research accomplishments, and/or relevant work experience.

Responsibilities include teaching undergraduate and graduate courses in various areas of business analytics and business statistics, engaging in scholarly activities, engaging with the business community and participating in campus and off-campus activities.  The successful applicant will collaborate with the Business School faculty in developing interdisciplinary curriculum and research initiatives to help advance the school’s mission of excellence in teaching and scholarship.

Applicants should submit in electronic form a cover letter and one PDF file containing the following documents:

-       a CV

-       evidence of teaching effectiveness,

-       a sample of current research, and/or relevant work experience

-       three letters of reference.

All materials should be submitted to Dr. Marc Waldman, Chair of the Search Committee at buan.cis@manhattan.edu . Application review will begin immediately and continue until the position is filled.
