        Data Governance Business Analyst
        Crystal City, VA
        Celerity, a business acceleration consultancy, is looking for a Data Governance Business Analyst. We are actively seeking Data Analysis professionals who are creative, collaborative, resilient, and continuously challenge themselves to do better.
        About our Talent Solutions Team
        Team Celerity is a proud hub of technologists, creatives, and business consultants dedicated to helping clients win and thrive in the digital world. Grounded in teamwork, creative courage, and dynamic flexibility, Team Celerity values an entrepreneurial spirit, the desire to grow professionally, and the passion to gain new opportunities in a high-growth environment. What's holding you back?
        Summary:
        This position is focused on the definition, harmonization, and governance of enterprise data and enterprise reports to deliver maximum efficiency and business value while simultaneously reducing risks related to inconsistent and incorrect data. The person in this role will be a subject matter expert on the business use(s) and source(s) of data. The Data Governance Business Analyst will be responsible for representing the Data Governance team in coordinating the acquisition of data onto the Enterprise Data Services platform and the understanding and usage of that data from the Enterprise Data Services platform. This position will coordinate and collaborate with other internal departments and software vendors, from the identification of business process impact / improvement, requirements gathering, testing, and rollout. This position will monitor the quality of data sourced onto the Enterprise Data Services platform and will work with other internal departments in resolving data quality issues and ensuring proper enterprise data integration, data quality, and integrity. The Data Governance Business Analyst will provide support for the successful implementation of Enterprise Data Services platform functionality, including data dictionary, data warehouse; data mart; and reporting and business intelligence implementations. Additionally, the incumbent will be engaged in ongoing continuous process improvement through internal engagement and interaction.
        Responsibilities:
        * Work with other internal departments in defining, documenting, and communicating business functional requirements, data source requirements, data mappings, and data integration and quality rules for the Enterprise Data Services platform.
        * Provide internal Enterprise Data consumers support via subject matter expertise, training, and consultation.
        * Maintain and govern the completeness and quality of the enterprise business data glossary, including business term names, definitions, and rationalized reference data.
        * Assist the Data Governance Lead in gathering and documenting requirements for business glossary software tool and data governance request and incident tool enhancements; and managing user defined fields and work flow definitions in both tools.
        * Acquire in-depth knowledge of enterprise data, including its purpose and uses. This includes data profiling against potential enterprise sources of data to understand the data's content, completeness, quality, and meaning.
        * Assist the Data Governance Lead in the monitoring, tracking, and reporting of compliance with the enterprise data policy and standards, including the setting of key performance and risk indicator targets and the progress towards achieving those targets.
        * Assist the Data Governance Lead in the enhancement of enterprise data policies, standards, and processes.
        * Works with process and system owners to research and resolve the root cause(s) of enterprise data quality issues.
        * Assist in and / or lead the creation, enhancement, and usage of enterprise reports and data views.
        Qualifications:
        * Bachelor's Degree in the field of business, computer science, decision support, data science, or comparable field of study is required
        * Minimum 7 years of related work experience
        * Excellent communication skills (verbal and written)
        * Excellent organizational skills
        * Excellent interpersonal and collaboration skills and ability to create rapport with different stakeholders
        * Superior analytical skills
        * Self-motivated and self-directed individual
        * Demonstrated willingness to work as part of a team
        * Detail oriented and able to prioritize and work efficiently on multiple work threads
        * 3 or more years working in the banking or financial services industry
        * 3 or more years working on data warehouse, data mart, business intelligence, and / or comparable initiatives in an analyst role gathering data usage and sourcing requirements and translating those requirements into data integration, data design, and report functional design specifications
        * 2 or more years with significant participation in developing logical data models, glossaries, and / or data dictionaries
        * Comfort with accessing, querying, analyzing, and presenting data at a detail and summary level
        * Demonstrated willingness to learn business concepts, processes, and data at an in-depth level
        * Demonstrated hands-on SQL skills (both writing and reverse engineering) using relational databases
        * Demonstrated data manipulation and analysis skills using end-user tools such as Excel, Excel pivot tables, and / or MS Access (or comparable)
        * Experience with IBM InfoSphere Information Server Information Governance Catalog and Information Analyzer software, or similar product(s), is a plus
        * Experience constructing reports and data views using one or more market-leading reporting or business intelligence tools, is a plus
