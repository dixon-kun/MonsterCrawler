Associate Business Analyst at Revco Industries, Inc.

Santa Fe Springs, CA 90670

About the Job

J ob S ummary

Revco Industries, Inc. is seeking an A ssociate Business Analyst in all aspects of analyzing operations, marketing, sales, and business development activities and effectiveness. The position is also responsible for managing our customer database and directly involved in tracking and analyzing Revco’s key customer program. The successful candidate will work on multiple projects at any given time, producing analytical work and support for our operations, sales, marketing, and business development areas.

Duties

  • Plan and execute data collection for market, sales activity, customer activity, etc.
  • Develop custom reports with data visualizations
  • Analyze data and generate useful business reports
  • Determine, implement, and evaluate business metrics to meet ongoing organization needs
  • Monitor competitors, market conditions, and production development
  • Monitor ongoing development efforts and provide regular progress reporting of current activities to business team
  • Identify user needs from user requests and develop strategies by which to meet those needs
  • Develop a research and analysis strategy
  • Work with IT team on methods and tools for data organization
  • Other duties as assigned

Qualifications

  • Strong quantitative, analytical, communication, writing and interpersonal skills
  • Outstanding organizational skills and attention to detail
  • Bachelor's degree in a quantitative field such as Business, Finance, Economics, Statistics, Data Science or Mathematics
  • Proficient with spreadsheets (Excel) and business intelligence tools
  • 3 + years in a business data analyst role
  • Ability to collaborate effectively and work as part of a team
