SAS, the leader in analytics technology, is prepared to offer an opportunity of a lifetime to the right person who can assist in forging our very own deep learning framework.  This is an opportunity to create and implement deep learning algorithms for recurrent neural networks that will be extensively used in every SAS product and service to recognize speech and power the next generations of SAS software.

In this role, you will work with a renowned group of Research and Development statisticians utilizing your expertise and knowledge to do what has never been done before at SAS.  We’re not looking for a data scientist to simply create algorithms but rather a creative and passionate research scientist who wants to touch most every aspect of life by developing deep learning algorithms that can change the world.

About SAS:
SAS believes in the whole employee experience. Meaningful work. Empowerment to make a difference that changes people’s lives. Dynamic work environments that foster innovation. And an award-winning culture that makes it all possible. We believe great ideas can come from anywhere. Whether you're a university recruit, or an experienced professional ready for the next big challenge, SAS brings perks, passion, and the potential to grow. No limits.

The nature of this role will have you focused on researching and developing deep learning algorithms that focus on text, audios and language models. We are seeking to hire someone who can handle such a high profile position and will be scrutinizing all applicants closely.

Some of the responsibilities include the following:

  • Develop SAS Deep Learning (DL) toolkit on the state-of-art SAS Viya platform
  • Implement various SAS DL architectures and algorithms on SMP, MPP, and GPUs for natural language processing, speech recognitions, and text analytics.
  • Discuss, propose, and implement new ideas and applications within the SAS DL team
  • Prototype the speech recognition system

Competencies and Qualifications:

Essential

  • Master’s degree or Ph.D. in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, or Physics (Machine Learning or Deep Learning is a plus)
  • Strong experience with audio signal processing and speech recognitions
  • Proven programming skills such as C, C++, or Java

Preferred

  • Strong experience with DL frameworks such as Caffe, Theano, Torch, CNTK, MXNet, or TensorFlow
  • Experience with GPU computing (CUDA, OpenCL) and HPC (MPI, OpenMP)
  • Experience with open source speech recognition packages such as Kaldi,  Sphinx, and HTK
  • Strong mathematical skills in machine learning and deep learning
  • Strong ability for teamwork and motivation for collaborations and good communication and organizational skills
