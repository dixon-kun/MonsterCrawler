The Configuration Analyst, Team Lead, provides direction to the Health and Welfare Configuration Analyst on assigned clients, including ongoing system integrity and stability, and system configuration changes. Provides hands-on advice and direction to CAs on complex issues. Functions as a product and technical expert for team members, clients, sales, and development. Conducts periodic system audits to ensure that system configuration is consistent with client requirements. Transitions new clients from Implementation to Operations. Ensures that operational and procedural standards for CAs are identified and that compliance monitoring occurs for assigned clients. Interfaces with internal and external clients to resolve escalated issues and answer product and process questions. Makes complex configuration changes to the application and resolves escalated issues for multiple custom client databases. This position will play an active role in the interviewing, training and mentoring of new CA associates and providing feedback to management to ensure proper training, performance management and project/client assignment recommendations for new associate(s) as well as other CA associates on the team. Additional responsibilities include; researching new features and functionality and sharing this knowledge with the CAs through training, documentation and conference calls. Will also be required to act as project lead for resolution of global issues, application upgrades or other business unit initiatives. Participates in client meetings and/or conference calls as needed.

RESPONSIBILITIES:
* Creates, documents, maintains and monitors operational and procedural standards for CAs, including SAS 70 and Sarbanes Oxley requirements.
* Provides direction to the Health and Welfare Configuration Analysts on assigned clients, including ongoing system integrity and stability, and system configuration changes.
* Provides hands-on advice and direction to CAs on most complex issues.
* Also, provides operational support for escalated clients.
* Utilizes standardized processes, tools, and deliverables.
* Process may be adapted to fit the specific requirements of the client, resulting in the delivery of high-quality services.
* Applies consistent tools and testing standards to all assigned tasks in order to consistently produce error free results in a timely manner.
* Directs CAs to identify and resolve escalated client and production issues by conducting root cause analysis of the issue as necessary and resolving the issue or working with the appropriate resource to resolve.
* May perform training for CA new hires and for Open Enrollment.
* May also assist with the development of the training curriculum beyond new hire.
* Coaches and mentors other CAs.
* Prepares work plans for owned tasks.
* Prioritizes assigned work to ensure completion in a timely manner.
* Manages self to the completion of those tasks and informs others when problems occur or when commitments cannot be met as planned.
* Responds and reacts to a variety of critical issues simultaneously.
* Provides technical direction and support to CAs to write data base queries and reposts using standard tools. (R-R Report Writer; SQL Crystal Reports) and also writes queries and reports as appropriate.
* Maintains/updates functional documentation to support client processing.
* Demonstrates highest level of expertise of Health - Welfare system applications.
* Develops most complex solutions and recommendations to satisfy even the most complex client needs through the use of the application; understands impact on scope and timeliness.
* Uses effective communication and facilitation skills to advise and influence clients.
* Prepares/reviews written communications such as status reports, project/internal correspondence, and functional specifications.
* Reviews all Change Controls and sizes accordingly providing information back to the applicable Service Delivery Manager.
* Manages time efficiently and effectively in order to help meet goals and objectives.
* Participates in global projects that require CA involvement.
* Partners with Management and other ADP product areas on global issues.
* Assists management by providing input and feedback for new hire interviews, workload balancing and performance reviews.
* May perform CA role for clients in transition from Implementation and for escalated/overflow clients from the team.
* Learns new system functionality and facilitates sharing with CAs.
* Share key learning experiences with team members to help them avoid known pitfalls.
* Organizes and conducts functional meetings for all CAs (across all client teams).
* Participate in process improvement and client/associate satisfaction improvement initiative.
* Exude service orientation in daily, weekly, monthly, and ad hoc tasks.
* Keep current in benefit administration industry knowledge
* Other duties as assigned 

  

QUALIFICATIONS REQUIRED:
* Bachelor's Degree in a related field or equivalent in education and experience.
* Experience in benefits administration and/or application configuration REQUIRED.
* Communication and leadership training or equivalent.
* Technical training or equivalent in related technologies and/or applications.
* Proven high level of expertise in the role of a Configuration Analyst demonstrating knowledge of system, processes and procedures and delivering quality work products.
* A combination of two to four years of experience in a relevant industry, a HRIS practitioner and/or experience with complex application support.
