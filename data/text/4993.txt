            Do you live and breathe data? Are you familiar with hybrid IT environments? Do you know the difference between &#8216;schema at read&#8217; and &#8216;schema at write&#8217;?               Your position
             As a Data Architect you will be part of the Data Science team within Global Services. The team strives to improve the Vanderlande service offering and delivery by bringing the organisation up to the next level of fact-based decision making. For example by correlating data generated by our Material Handling Solutions and Maintenance history data to predict when components need to be replaced and to improve our product design as well. As Data Architect, you will safeguard the data integrity of the Data Science platform. You will manage the data interfaces with other information systems and the platform architecture. Hereto, you will be the sparring partner for colleague IT architects from our ICT department and Software architects from Softwarehouse. Your main activities will be:
             Manage the data science platform
               Review current data architecture and professionalize the Data Science platform
               Set and safeguard data architecture principles for a scalable hybrid Data Science platform that enables the implementation of applications; Promote architecture principles, guidelines and procedures within the organisation
               Safeguard the integration of additional Data Science application
               Set up a DTAP environment, deployment solution and perform admin activities
             Manage interfaces
               Define, develop, optimize and maintain standardized interfaces of the Data Science platform with other Vanderlande IT systems and external data sources
               Document interfaces and interface requirements
             Knowledge sharing and advice to application engineers/users
               Define and consult in development of generic (cross-domain) apps/knowledge objects within the Data Science platform (e.g. availability calculation)
               Define and consult in development of cross-domain and multi-site/customer reporting (e.g. &#160;to enable benchmarking)
              Your Department
             Global Services accounts more than 20% of the total sales at Vanderlande and is growing steadily. The department of Service Technology which is a part of the Global Services organization manages the life cycle of our products and the customer&#8217;s installed base. Service Technology also investigates and applies enabling technologies like advanced data analytics to improve our service offerings.
