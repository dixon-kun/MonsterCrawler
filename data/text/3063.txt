POSITION SUMMARY 
The Infrastructure &amp; Operations Group (I&amp;O) is an organization that designs, implements, and supports Infrastructure and Platform solutions in support of business need. I&amp;O established a PLAN, BUILD, RUN Operating Model based on the ITIL framework. Associated with the Operating Model are industry wide roles such as Architect, Service Manager, Project Manager, Design Engineer, Implementation Engineer, IT Operations Control, Tech &amp; App Management, Service Desk, and Client Product Support. 
I&amp;O Technical areas include: Solutions/Product Engineering, Data Network, Voice, Distributed Systems, Mainframe, Midrange, Middleware, Web, Database, End User Computing, Service Technology, Disaster Recovery, or Data Center Site Management. 
I&amp;O associates are expected to become familiar with any processes, tools or technologies in the areas they are working in. 
RESPONSIBILITIES: 
*  Configuration, installation, troubleshooting, analysis and maintenance of all systems and software applications. Includes desktops, laptops, printers, servers, virtual machines, routers, CDROM technologies, Security systems and all other associated systems.
