Data is important to us. Its what we do. We process terabytes of data every day, and transform that data into information that powers decisions for millions of home buyers, renters, dreamers, and real estate professionals. Data is the key asset and we want to extract increasing value from it to help our business lines make critical decisions and to build data products that will delight and inform our visitors.

We seek a very seasoned Data Scientist with leadership experience to join our data science team and develop it to its full potential. As a senior member of the data science team, you will be responsible for the ideation, research, predictive modeling, and machine learning algorithms for data products to delight and inform visitors to realtor.com and its mobile apps. You will also serve as a mentor for the junior data scientists on the team and provide oversight on their individual projects.

Responsibilities:

  • Perform exploratory analysis on Moves wealth of data including consumer web and mobile behavior and North Americas most comprehensive listing and properties data set.
  • Effectively partner with product management and the data product team on building new data driven features for personalization, targeting, recommendations, and market insights for a great consumer experience
  • Drive A/B, multivariate tests and design of experiments to facilitate testing of new product and design features, with focus on improving engagement, retention, and conversion.
  • Select, apply, and tune a diverse set of tools to coherently solve challenging business goals
  • Create automated learning systems that gracefully scale to increasing complexity and expectation
  • Develop predictive, explanatory models and machine learning algorithms
  • Generate descriptive visualizations and presentations to communicate insights
  • Mentor a team of data scientists on data exploration, machine learning and developing data based products
  • Work with a sense of ownership and urgency, advocate for experimentation based, agile culture.

Requirements:

  • MS or PhD in statistics, mathematics, computer science, quantitative analysis, economics or related field is required.
  • 7+ years of relevant experience in data science, data analytics, or applied statistics,
  • 5+ years experience with machine learning, data mining, statistical modeling tools and underlying algorithms
  • 5+ years experience in R, Perl, Python, spark, or other languages and frameworks appropriate for large scale analysis of numerical and textual data
  • Working experience with relational databases and SQL
  • Experience with experiment design and A/B and multivariate tests
  • Experience and proven track record developing data products for internet industry
  • Strong creative thinking and problem solving skills
  • Excellent oral and written communication and presentation skills
