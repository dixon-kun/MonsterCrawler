ABOUT THIS POSITION

PURPOSE OF POSITION:

Ever been outside when the air is just the right temp and you can't feel it at all? It's not hot, not cold, just kind of perfect? That's sort of what it's like to win a spot in the area's most exclusive Digital Marketing Internship program. The Digital Marketing Intern experience at Infinity provides opportunities to learn from and work alongside some of the best digital pros anywhere. During your 90-day dream job, you'll be up to your eyebrows in practical experience and earn certifications from the likes of Google and Bing. Plus, since life's not 100% about being practical, we give you nights and weekends off. Heck, we'll even pay you too. Infinity offers digital marketing internships in the following areas: Search Engine Marketing, Search Optimization, Content and Social Media Marketing, Interactive Graphic Design, User Experience Design, Marketing Automation, as well as Data Science and Analytics. Hurry though, application deadlines for summer positions are approaching fast.

SPECIFIC ACTIVITIES:

* 1. Completes research and reporting assignments such as competitive analysis, keyword research, and A/B test summaries.

* 2. May generate or edit creative depending on role.

3. Learns and utilizes digital marketing platforms.

4. Other duties as assigned.

* 5. Regular predictable attendance.

WORKING RELATIONSHIPS:

INTERNAL: Marketing , Sales and Marketing Technology

EXTERNAL: Advertisement agencies, PR firms, field marketers, and direct distribution.

SUPERVISION EXERCISED: None

SPECIALIZED KNOWLEDGE AND/OR SKILL REQUIREMENTS:

1. Must be a high school graduate and currently be enrolled in an accredited university pursuing a Bachelor's degree. Degrees in Math, Computer Science, Engineering, Marketing, Journalism, Communications, English, or Spanish preferred.

2. Must have basic computer skills including operating Microsoft Office or similar software.

3. Familiar with social media.

4. Excellent listening and communication skills.

SPECIAL DEMANDS OR WORKING CONDITIONS:

* Primarily a stationary job that requires typing and computer use.

* May be required to use a telephone or verbally communicate up to one third of time on the job.
