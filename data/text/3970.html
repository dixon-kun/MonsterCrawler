A9.com, an Amazon company, creates powerful, customer-focused search and advertising solutions and technologies . Our Advertising Technology team builds the technology infrastructure and ad serving systems to manage billions of advertising queries every day. The result is better quality advertising for publishers and more relevant ads for customers. Our infrastructure supports millions of Internet users and handles billions of queries per day, all delivered in milliseconds. Our data platform processes massive data sets to develop business intelligence and analytics that are critical for the efficiency and profitability of our advertising business.

As a Graduate Data Science Intern in Advertising Technology, you will have the ability to perform hands-on analysis and modeling with huge datasets to improve traffic monetization and the advertising customer experience. You will have the opportunity to work on one of the following teams:

Advertising Sciences: The Advertising Science Team works at the heart of Amazon’s world-class ad exchange in which billions of ad impressions are bought and sold every day. You will have the opportunity to be at the forefront of research in programmatic ad buying and learn about large scale science in areas such as predictive analytics, computational advertising, and information retrieval.

Forecasting Platform: Our forecasting platform is used across Amazon by a variety of advertising programs including Display, Video, Mobile and Kindle.  This team builds end-to-end solutions including data pipelines, large-scale index data structures, time series models, bidding landscape analysis and large-scale data visualizations.  Our systems are based on a mix of AWS and third-party technologies, using those that make the best sense in a given scenario.

Sponsored Links: Sponsored links (SL) is an online advertising program that displays ads in response to a person's search query or based on the content of a web page a person is viewing on Amazon.com. These ads give users more choice and constitute an important source of revenue for Amazon. This team performs hands-on data analysis and predictive modeling with huge data sets. Their efforts are aimed at developing insights that increase traffic monetization without compromising customer experience and merchandise sales.

At A9, you’ll experience the benefits of working in a dynamic, entrepreneurial environment, while leveraging the resources of Amazon.com (AMZN), one of the world's leading internet companies. We provide a highly customer-centric, team-oriented environment in our offices located in Palo Alto, California.

Basic Qualifications:

  • Pursuing a master’s degree focused on machine learning or related field
  • A minimum of 2 years of experience with scripting languages (e.g., Python)
  • A minimum of 1 year of experience with a statistical modeling tool (e.g., R)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Pursuing a PhD focused on machine learning or related field
  • Demonstrated interest in the advertising technology industry
  • Experience applying machine learning techniques in an industry setting
  • Experience with SQL and an RDBMS (e.g., Oracle) or Data Warehouse
  • Excellent communication skills and strong work ethic
  • Prior internship experience where you have demonstrated an ability to work in teams, problem solve and communicate effectively regarding technical projects
