Additional Job Details The Entrepreneurship Database Program (EDP) at Emory University is seeking to hire a Data Program Manager.
The Entrepreneurship Database Program collects and analyzes data from thousands of early-stage entrepreneurs that apply to more than 100 accelerator programs around the world to both produce sector reports and support academic research. With our partners, we work to improve the efficacy of accelerator programs to stimulate more positive entrepreneurial outcomes in socially-oriented sectors and in developing regions around the world. Learn more at www.entrepreneurdata.com.
This program is housed within Social Enterprise @ Goizueta (Emory’s Goizueta Business School) and is a proud partner of the Global Accelerator Learning Initiative (GALI) - a collaboration between the Aspen Network of Development Entrepreneurs (ANDE) and Emory University.

The program explores – and answers - key questions about enterprise acceleration including: Do acceleration programs contribute to revenue growth? Do they help companies attract investment? Do they work as well for developing-world impact entrepreneurs as they do for developed-world tech entrepreneurs?

Job Description:
The Data Program Manager will report to the Program Director with a focus on managing and maintaining data flows in / out of the database along with leveraging data analytics to produce insights. The Data Program Manager also works collaboratively with the Global Accelerator Partner Manager Team to coordinate program surveys and reports.

Primary Responsibilities:
• Manage and update EDP survey tools and data processes
• Ensure that application and follow-up data are collected and integrated into master database
• Work with Program Director to ensure maximum data quality and integrity
• Generate reports for participating accelerator partners in a timely manner and work closely with Partners Management Team to distribute
• Manage (annual) releases of aggregated application and follow-up data files
• Work with Program Director to refine tracking and forecasting systems and processes
• Leverage data analytics analysis to produce insights for various program stakeholders
• Provide (limited) support to academic and industry researchers who want to use the data
• Support Program Director to prepare requests and reports to EDP funders and supporters
• Lead/assist with special projects including designing new report features
• Serve as liaison with Emory / Goizueta Business School IT team to coordinate maintenance, updates, customizations
