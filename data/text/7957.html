Description
JOB SUMMARY
HealthTrust is a group purchasing and supply chain organization supporting more than 1,600 not-for-profit and for-profit acute care facilities, as well as ambulatory surgery centers, physician practices, and alternate care sites. HealthTrust Technology Innovation (TI) is a division of HCA Information Technology & Services that is responsible for designing, developing, and deploying innovative products to HealthTrust customer and internal business units. We are looking for intelligent, dynamic, motivated team members who have a passion for quickly delivering value to customers.
The Manager of Modeling and Analysis Services operates within the Business Intelligence and Data Services program, which delivers analytical products, data integration services, and advanced consulting services for the company. Within that mandate this position is responsible for leading data analysis efforts to explore the viability of new business intelligence use cases, profile data quality issues, and support information architecture initiatives.

GENERAL RESPONSIBILITIES
• Execute, under the guidance of the Director of Data Integration Engineering, highly professional data analysis and PoC exercises to enable development teams
• Lead establishment of data analysis and profiling standards
• Lead the development and evolution of the Enterprise Data Model and Canonical Message Model in collaboration with Architecture
• Collaborate with Architecture team in the coordination and implementation of Information Architecture initiatives
• Oversee proof-of-technology and tool evaluation efforts to ensure that the organization continues to responsibly innovate and improve technology capabilities
• Guide adoption of new computational methods and models
• Participate in research and development activities into centralized, Enterprise scale, advanced analytics and data science algorithms
• Create and execute a holistic staffing and training model within scope of responsibilities
• Ensure implementation of corporate data governance initiatives and establish internal standards to ensure consistency
• Educate internal TI teams in the utilization of Data Services and Big Data capabilities
• Adhere to, and participate in the development of, standards and best practices for the implementation and utilization of data assets
• Partner with business stakeholders to establish detailed plans for achieving tactical business objectives and create strong communication practices to establish trusted relationships
• Participate in IT management duties such as: budget activities, process standards (development, improvement, and governance), program and project management, enterprise application portfolio management, vendor management, strategic sourcing, workforce planning, information security, and strategic planning
• Participates in the creation and execution of the Business Intelligence strategy

MANAGERIAL QUALIFICATIONS
• Minimum of 5 years of experience leading teams and projects
• Excellent problem solving skills
• Desire for achieving excellence in customer satisfaction, process efficiency, product quality, and reliability.
• Excellent oral and written interpersonal communication skills
• Thrive on solving technical challenges with elegant sustainable solutions
• Committed to continuous improvement and innovation in productivity and reliability of software and systems

TECHNICAL QUALIFICATIONS
• Minimum of 10 years of experience in Information Technology roles
• Minimum of 5 years of experience with Business Intelligence
• Minimum of 3 years of experience working in data architecture related roles
• Detailed understanding of logical and physical data modeling
• Strong knowledge of SQL development practices
• Strong understanding of systems and applications architecture processes, design patterns, and standards.
• Experience with agile development methodologies and strong knowledge of all phases of system development life cycle
• Strong understanding of and experience in systems integration.
• Knowledge of Supply Chain and Healthcare, disciplines and standards is a strong plus.
