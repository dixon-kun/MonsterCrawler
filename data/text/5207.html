The Job

The Pindrop engineering team solves tough problems and invents new ways to battle fraud using big data and audio science in the cloud. Our engineers are creating innovative products to solve global problems, and they are looking for an experienced Ruby on Rails Engineer to join them on a contract basis to help refactor an existing Rails application.

Primary Skills:

  • Strong experience with Ruby on Rails
  • Solid background with SQL
  • Working knowledge of modern JavaScript (ES6) and HTML5
  • Experience building / optimizing client-side-heavy web applications with advanced client-side technologies such as React and Redux a plus

Responsibilities:

  • Take the lead and be the "go to" Engineer for all things Rails
  • Work with a growing team to maintain and upgrade a production web application
  • Refactor existing systems and architect new solutions
  • Help develop and introduce React into our current systems (you don’t need to be an expert but we will encourage your input on its implementation)
  • Have the ability to learn other parts of the system since you will be a part of a large enterprise appliance

Who We Look For:

  • Highly competent engineers who are passionate about working with new technologies and sharing new discoveries
  • Someone who is eager to learn and share gained knowledge
  • Engineers who enjoy a highly collaborative environment and are eager to present new ideas and solutions to their peers
  • Those that won't hesitate to ask for help when needed. We're all learning something new at Pindrop

Perks at Pindrop

We hire good people, and take care of them. Pindrop believes in fostering an environment where employees don’t just come to work hard, but play hard. We host regular game nights, team challenges, and offsite events (not to mention the endless snacks and catered meals) to keep everyone involved in our company culture. None of these strike you? We appreciate new ideas any and everywhere. Let us know what interests you and we can incorporate it into our Tea Time twice a month. Grab a beer and discover something new.

Keep learning. Pindrop offers regular Lunch & Learns and Tech Talks that promote an environment for people to grow outside of their job title. Want to learn to code with minimal experience? Come to our Intro to Python Tech Talk. Interested in March Madness? See how data science can help you beat the bracket at our Data Science in the Real World Lunch & Learn. In addition to these events, we promote a continued education budget for each of our employees to grow and improve in tandem with the company.

Our employees are creative, excited, and above all a team. Pindrop is one of the fastest growing security startups in the country, but we make sure to hire only the best of the best. Better yet, every employee is passionate about the company and our mission. (Can you blame us?) When you put that passion in a collaborative setting it unites everyone as a team.

Fighting criminals is in the job description. No matter which facet of Pindrop you want to work for, we are all working towards the same goal-- making sure the bad guys don’t win.

Our Bio

Founded in 2011  |  Raised $122 million

Venture backed by Andreessen Horowitz, Google Capital, IVP, Citi Ventures, Felicis Ventures, & GV

Pindrop is the pioneer in voice fraud prevention and authentication. We provide enterprise solutions to reduce fraud losses and authentication expense for some of the largest call centers in the world. Pindrop’s patented Phoneprinting™ technology can identify, locate and authenticate phone devices uniquely just from the call audio thereby detecting fraudulent calls as well as verifying legitimate callers. We have been selected by the world’s largest banks, insurers, brokerages and retailers, detecting over 80% of fraud even for attackers never seen before. Pindrop’s solutions are allowing customers to reduce call time and improve their customers’ experience even while reducing fraud losses.
