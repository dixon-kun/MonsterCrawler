Senior System Engineer at AT&T

Alpharetta, GA

Report this job

About the Job

Overall Purpose: Responsible for translating business needs into technical solutions, and defining solutions to problems through reasoned application of information technology.
Key Roles and Responsibilities: Designs, develops, documents and analyzes overall architecture of systems, including hardware and software. Determines integrated hardware and software architecture solutions that meet performance, usability, scalability, reliability, and security needs.

Coordinates design and integration of total system including subsystems. Researches and recommends technology to improve the current systems. Job Contribution: Seasoned technical professional. Contributes through proven technical expertise. Has significant dept/functional impact. Responsible for Data throughout its lifecycle from ingestion through to production solutions. Understands the BIDW ecosystem and develops strategy for where data resides and how it is accessed. Works closely with Data Engineers to develop requirements for derived and business ready data sets. Guides Data Engineers and Data Scientists to the correct data sets / systems of record to deliver solutions to the business. Works closely with QDM and Data Quality specialists to implement the right levels of governance and quality metrics
KNOWLEDGE Subject Matter Expert (SME) within own discipline/specialty area:

·Basic knowledge of other disciplines/specialty areas

·Deep technical knowledge

·Applies in depth knowledge of discipline/specialty area standards/processes

·Integrates industry experience and deep professional/technical knowledge

·Technical leader and recognized SME expert on select ATT technologies/systems/procedures

·Architect role that involves 3 areas: BIDW Project Assignments, Project Pre-Assessment and Project Consultations

·BIDW DS Project assignments provide architectural/design guidance on data integration points, storage requirements and access tools. DS will be reviewer/approver of design documents

·DS Pre-Assessment evaluations will require request review of initial project documentation provided by Enterprise Architect to identify suggested application impacts to DW, DataLake and other BIDW software Apps

·DS Consultation provides program/project managers, technical architects and business clients guidance on proposed high level solutions based on high level discussions and requirements
Other Skills:
• Elicits data information requirements using interviews, document analysis, requirements workshops, business process analysis, and use cases
• Translates business discussions into baseline data and software tools integration requirements
• Assists in the development of project baselines related to LOE and helps to monitor and control project scope, schedule, and quality related to LOE
• Reports project performance, issues and risks to management and appropriate stakeholders
• Demonstrates high level knowledge and functions related to BIDW applications
• Assist in project LOE analysis by providing system and functional oversight with written descriptions of high level analysis
• Incorporates project lesson learned into future analysis
·     Analysis/problem solving solves unique problems through evaluative judgment/precedent. Independently applies sophisticated analysis in evaluating issues. Develops new concepts/methods/techniques for cross functional initiatives. Recognizes/pursues alternative methods.
·    Independence guided by department goals/objectives. Exercises latitude in determining objectives/approaches to projects. Leads multiple small to medium sized projects and technical breadth.
·      Contribution to ATT Technology Key contributor on complex projects/initiatives. Impacts current and future business opportunities through application of specialized technical/ industry knowledge. Develops methods/techniques based on strategic project objectives.
·    Communication Mentors and provides technical guidance and explains advanced concepts to others in work area.  May mentor others in department.  Coordinates across multiple departments. Promotes active listening and open communication. Provides leadership/guidance to others.



Qualifications:
•Bachelor's Degree (quantitative field preferred)
•5+ years experience working in a professional environment in an analytical role
•Proven ability to think critically and use sound logic and reasoning to arrive at conclusions
•Curiosity and eagerness to learn and implement new tools and techniques to advance the organization's data strategy
•Ability to understand business needs from internal stakeholders and translate these needs into technical requirements for development teams
•Confidence in asking questions to refine stakeholders' and support team members' approach to their work and requests, including inquiries that demonstrate solid systematic thinking about business and technology cause and effect.
•Proven ability to maintain attention to detail
•Excellent customer service, interpersonal, and communication skills
•Proven ability to be involved in data projects end to end
•Fluency in Microsoft Excel and Access functions
•Experience building effective internal and external relationships
•Preferred: Experience with big data storage and analysis tools related to RDBMS, Hadoop, Business Objects, and Microstrategy
•Preferred: Experience building queries with SQL to  join large, disparate data sets
•Must be a self-starter, with strong planning and organizational skills and the ability to work with minimal direction
•Ability to work in a fast• paced, constantly• evolving environment and efficiently manage a challenging workload.
