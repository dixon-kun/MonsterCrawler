Company Overview:
The ADT Corporation (NYSE: ADT) is a leading provider of electronic security, interactive home and business automation and monitoring services for residences and small businesses in the United States and Canada. ADT's broad and pioneering set of products and services, including ADT Pulse interactive home and business solutions, and home health services, meet a range of customer needs for today's active and increasingly mobile lifestyles. Headquartered in Boca Raton, FL, ADT helps provide peace of mind to more than six million customers, and it employs about 17,000 people at 200 locations. More information is available atwww.adt.com.
Position Highlights:
At ADT, data drives all of our decision-making. As part of the Residential Business Finance team, you will use data to inform business and product decisions. Using your technical skills, business acumen and creativity, you will build tools to automate reporting and generate insight that will allow internal clients to quickly and accurately see how our key business products and processes are performing.  Previous experience working with business partners to frame operational problems, analyze information and design and develop reporting and metrics that properly evaluate outcomes.  Previous experience with Oracle and Microsoft databases with emphasis on providing business intelligence and data driven insight through reporting, will have equipped you well for this role. You’ll be involved in projects from inception to delivery, ensuring that reporting delivers high-quality and relevant data to intelligently grow our business. You will also have extensive knowledge of key operational systems, and experience working with a range of other business intelligence tools and platforms.
Responsibilities & Qualifications:
  1. Design and develop extract, transform, load (ETL) packages from our source systems that are performant and scalable
  2. Design, develop and implement visualizations for our business teams following  effective design principles  to support ongoing business analysis and operations
  3. Analyze complex data systems and document data elements, data flow, relationships and dependencies to contribute to logical and physical data models.
  4. Support Residential business teams in development of advanced analytic solutions that leverage the data warehouse for information.
  5. Support Residential business and will help analyze and report out on early attrition performance indicators (at the local market) level to help increase profitability and enforce company policy
  6. Identify KPIs and develop automated solutions (reports/ dashboards) to monitor progress
  7. Collect and compile statistical customer quality data
  8. Analyze data to identify areas for improvement in customer quality
  9. Develop, recommend and monitor corrective and preventive actions
  10. Identify training needs and organize training interventions to meet customer quality standards

Education/Certification:

  • Bachelors Degree (MIS, Computer Science, Mathematics or Data Science) or equivalent experience; MBA preferred.
Experience:
  • 3+ years of data mining, data compilation, data extraction, and data analytics using Microsoft Access, Microsoft or Oracle SQL (data mining & extract), and report generating experience
  • Experience working with many different tools in a typical BI stack including several different database technologies, several ETL Tools, and various visualization technologies.
  • Advanced expertise in SQL Server, SSIS/DTS, TOAD Data Point, Tableau or other visualization tools
The above statements are intended to describe the general nature and level of work being performed by individuals assigned to this job. They are not intended to be an exhaustive list of responsibilities, duties and skills.
