        Position Overview
        Freddie Mac's Single-Family Data Governance &amp; Management (SF DG&amp;M) organization in the SF Division is currently seeking an Shared Information Support - Senior who will work as a Data Engineer in its Business Intelligence, Analytics &amp; Modeling Services team. The SF DG&amp;M team is responsible for making data available to manage and support the SF business. This data is utilized by teams that create analytics and models. The Data Engineer will provide data engineering (ingestion) and data enablement support the provision of analytic ready data in a cross-functional team environment. By actively participating in strategic discussions, this person will provide use case support primarily during Data engineering for analytical purposes, work with appropriate IT Parties to identify data security and develop use case solution architecture.
        Ideal candidates would have working knowledge of Hadoop ecosystem, relational data stores, Data Integration techniques, XML, Python, Spark, SAS, R, emerging Big Data tools and technologies, Visualization tools in Big Data.
        Responsibilities include:
        Data Engineering:
        * Work with stakeholders and Big Data Center of Excellence (CoE) counterparts to understand and deliver on business requirements for the enablement of data
        * Work closely with the core technology team and business to prepare data for use case execution
        * Work with large, complex data systems to aggregate, organize, and prepare data for use in business analytics, including structuring unstructured/semi-structured data
        * Understanding of data policies, including privacy and security standards, as well as, mitigation techniques (e.g. masking, de-identifying, security)
        * Perform data scrubbing and cleansing specific to model and tool specifications
        * Continuously assess and enhance the industrialized data feeds
        * Ingest and enable data in an agile environment to support Reporting and Analytics
        Data Best Practices and Guidance:
        * Coordinate with IT to build and maintain infrastructure for solution for execution of use cases
        * Owns one or more processes, reports, procedures or products, and may also be considered analytical or procedural "experts" representing a unit or team on cross-function process or project deliverables.
        Qualifications
        * Able to work efficiently under Unix/Linux environment
        * Proficiency in using SQL and NoSQL databases
        * Experience working with Big Data Computing Platform (using PySpark) and Big Data Manipulation Tools (Hive, and Pig).
        * Experience with modern analytics programming languages and statistical modeling
        * Experience working with Data Scientists
        * Experience designing and developing data sourcing routines utilizing typical data quality functions involving standardization, transformation, rationalization, linking and matching
        * Knowledge of data, master data and metadata related standards, processes and technology
        * Experience working with multi-Terabyte data sets (structured, semi/unstructured data)
        * College Degree or equivalent experience
        Preferred Skills
        * Experience in the Mortgage industry, Freddie Mac, and information management needs are
        * Demonstrated flexibility and adaptability to changes
        * Demonstrated ability to manage multiple priorities and deadlines
        * Ability to work independently or as part of a team
        * Working knowledge of Unified Mortgage Data Programs (UMDP) and the new data sets associated with the underlying initiatives.
        * Advanced studies/degree
