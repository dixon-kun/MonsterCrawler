The Director, Marketing Intelligence will join the MTV Connected Content group focused on two primary tasks:

  • Owning our paid marketing and media discipline and advancing our segmentation and targeting capabilities
  • Advancing both quantitative and qualitative insights driven community profiling across platforms, including community size, behaviors and influencer identification

From tweets to News articles to GIFs to video, the successful candidate is expected to continually enhance our ability to identify, reach and engage target audiences with premium content distributed across dozens of platform touch points, including owned-and-operated websites and apps, brand social network channels and partner websites. This person will have access to state of the art vendors and tools; will operate cross-functionally with our Growth Insights, Data Science, Product and Editorial teams; will directly support two Directors of Audience Development and report to the SVP Audience Growth and Engagement.

Responsiblities:

  • Direct and evolve holistic social media and marketing planning and buying strategy on behalf of MTV Connected Content group and MTV News
  • Create and manage targeted marketing campaigns across Facebook, YouTube, Outbrain, Taboola, Twitter and new/emerging marketing channels to drive:
    • UV to mtv.com
    • Streams to our short form video across platforms
    • Community growth around our franchises and MTV News beat social channels
    • Engagement KPIs like time spent, return visit %, shares and social actions
  • Work with internal/historical data, cookies, CRM lists and our social platform partners to optimize campaigns, and develop our custom audience and retargeting lists, and scale them across platforms
  • Work with Growth Insights team to provide regular reports and post mortems on campaigns, including implementation of necessary tracking systems
  • Support editorial team content creation process with insights into audience behaviors, driving audience-led content and new audience prospecting
  • Coordinate tightly with Consumer Marketing team to align on all network priorities, while coordinating performance marketing and awareness marketing efforts
  • Support our Partnerships Manager in using tools to find outreach targets, both on the fly and in a way that develops community relationships over time

  • BA or BS from accredited four-year university. Degree in media, marketing or communications a plus
  • 4-5 years experience in media planning and buying, or equivalent experience
  • Expert knowledge of Facebook Ads platform, Google Ad Words and YouTube marketing, Twitter Ads platform and buying across content recommendation services including Outbrain and Taboola
  • Experience in data-driven full funnel marketing optimization and analysis
  • Experience driving data-informed targeting and segmentation marketing
  • Experience in newsletter management, e-commerce and broader digital audience development tactics a major plus

  • Passion for news, pop culture and media, ability to pivot strategies and tactics quickly in a fast paced news media environment, and strong collaboration and interpersonal skills are a must – this is a highly collaborative and team-oriented role
