Title: Analytics Development Lead

Location: Morris Plains, New Jersey

Are you passionate about taking analytic modeling results and creating a production solution that gets used? Do you enjoy working with a team that takes raw data and creates value with that data? Do you love making new technology work efficiently for your company? If your answer is yes, Honeywell has an intriguing opportunity for you as an analytics developer.

You will be responsible for working closely with data scientists to write the applications that integrate analytic modeling results into operational platforms, including Hadoop and NoSQL architectures. You will also work with solution architects to design effective systems that seamlessly integrate models to production environments.

You will be a member of a team that delivers contemporary analytics solutions for all Honeywell business groups and functions. You will be expected to actively participate in defining and governing our analytics delivery strategy for Honeywell, as well as begin a strong leader in defining the architecture for analytics driven projects as we build out capabilities. You will provide technical leadership and guidance to other developers to ensure successful analytics implementation. You will be part of Honeywell's Data Analytics Solution organization.

Honeywell International is a $40 billion diversified Fortune 100 leader with more than 131,000 employees in 100 countries around the world. We invent and manufacture technologies that address some of the world's toughest challenges linked to global macro trends like energy efficiency, clean energy generation, safety and security, globalization and customer productivity. We are building a world that's safer and more secure, more comfortable and energy efficient, more innovative and productive. For more information, please visit Honeywell.com or follow us @HoneywellNow.

Specific Responsibilities:

* Implementing advanced analytics/modeling projects, including data ingestion, data management (scripts, modeling), data extraction, and execution of predictive models.

* Working with and educate our data science and analytics teams to understand production implications of the solutions they are developing.

* Performing lead developer functions on Big Data projects in cloud storage management activities, data ingestion, data management (scripts, modeling), data extraction and visualization and support for execution of predictive modeling activities.

* Managing production analytic environments, and work with the IT organization to develop support and maintenance strategies for those environments.

Basic Qualifications:

* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Applied Mathematics or related field

* 5 years experience in implementing software solutions (e.g. Hadoop, SAS, Oracle, Siebel, or similar software)

Preferred Qualifications:

* 5 years' experience in analytics development

* 2 years' hands-on experience in design and implementing big-data-processing on Hadoop platform especially Serde (serialization/deserialization) plug-ins with HIVE

* 2 years' experience with versioning, change control, problem management

* 2 years' experience with all aspects of software engineering and the development lifecycle

* 2 years' in UNIX architecture, systems administration and UNIX tools

* 2 years' experience developing with SQL and RDBMS(s)

* Experience working with remote and global teams

* Strong customer focus and process discipline

* Results driven with a positive can do attitude

* Excellent collaboration and negotiation skills

* Highly team oriented

* Ability to work collaboratively with analytic design staff through the design and build of new business processes and systems

* In depth understanding of best-in-class analytics configuration and development processes

* Proven experience ingesting, managing and visualizing high volume telemetry data, such as connected device data or manufacturing / shop floor data into a distributed computing platform

* Proven experience using open source and/or commercial solutions deliver standardized analytic applications, including development tools and automation/scripting

* Curious, critical thinker that likes pushing boundaries
