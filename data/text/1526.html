Must Have:
1. 2-4 years of IT Administration within the Business Intelligence space
2. 2+ specifically administering SAP Business Objects (with 100+ users)
a. Must be comfortable having full ownership over the platform
b. Comfortable with Web Intelligence (Webi) and Crystal Reports
3. SQL skills and comfortable w/ SQL Server installations, configurations and maintenance
4. Must be very comfortable interacting with and supporting end users
Plus:
1. Experience with SSRS (SQL Server Reporting Services)
2. Experience in the Financial Services Industry
3. Desire to learn and explore new areas such as Data Analysis, BI Development, Data Science, etc.
D2D: This person will be joining the Business Intelligence team and will be responsible for administering the Business Intelligence reporting systems for all business units through support of the reporting platform and generating reports. They will support BI systems by working with end users to identify, collect and synthesize data and ensure that data is consistently and accurately delivered to meet business needs. This is a very upbeat, energetic, and passionate team and they are looking for someone who will bring that same energy. They must be a self-starter with the ability to work on projects independently.
