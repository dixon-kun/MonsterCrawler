Lanyon believes that when people come together amazing things happen, relationships are built and business gets done. This is why we have created the industry's leading cloud-based software for managing corporate meetings, events and travel programs.

From a one-to-one sales meeting, employee training or a large flagship customer conference, Lanyon's unmatched software and the data it provides helps thousands of organizations and hotels around the world to better engage their customers, reduce costs and grow revenue.

The proof of the results that we deliver is demonstrated by the customers that choose us, including over 80% of the Fortune 100, more than 10,000 small and medium businesses and over 100,000 hotels.

We are looking for a Data Analyst that will help us discover the information hidden in vast amounts of data, and help us make smarter decisions to deliver even better products. The Data Analyst's primary focus will be in applying data mining techniques, doing statistical analysis, and building high quality prediction systems integrated with our products. The ideal candidate will be able to link and mash up distinctive data sets from various sources to discover new insights. This often requires connecting different types of data sets in different forms as well as being able to work with potentially incomplete data sources and cleaning data sets to be able to use them.

"What's in it for you?"

* Intellectual stimulation from a dynamic business and talented colleagues

* Rewards & Recognition for top performance

* Accelerated career advancement opportunities based on performance

Additional Information

* Lanyon is currently not offering sponsorship opportunities, including transfers

* No agencies

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

* Conceptual modelling: to be able to share and articulate modelling;

* Statistical analysis: to understand and work around possible limitations in models;

* Predictive modelling: using technology to enable us to predict future outcomes;

* Hypothesis testing: being able to develop hypothesis and test them with careful experiments. Selecting features, building and optimizing classifiers using machine learning techniques

* Data mining using state-of-the-art methods

* Extending company's data with third party sources of information when needed

* Enhancing data collection procedures to include information that is relevant for building analytic systems

* Processing, cleansing, and verifying the integrity of data used for analysis

* Doing ad-hoc analysis and presenting results in a clear manner

* Creating automated anomaly detection systems and constant tracking of its performance

Skills/Requirements

Job Requirements

* Bachelor Degree in related field required (ex. Statistics, Applied Mathematics, Econometrics); Masters degree in related field preferred

* 3+ years of experience delivering data science outcomes where you've solved complex analytical problems using quantitative approaches with your unique blend of analytical, mathematical and technical skills

* Proficiency in using query languages such as SQL, Hive, Pig to perform statistical analysis

* Experience applying advanced statistics skills such as distributions, statistical testing, regression, etc.

* Experience advising senior management in clear language about the implications of their work for the organization

* Experience creating examples, prototypes, demonstrations to help management better understand the work

* Proven experience analyzing large, complex, multi-dimensional datasets with a variety of tools like SPSS, SAS or Cognos

* Some experience with SQL, Python, Java and C/C++ preferred
