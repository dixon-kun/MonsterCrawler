Optum Data Management is the enterprise data management services organization representing integrated Optum-wide data, technology, and delivery services. In addition, Optum Data Management consults both internally for UHG and externally for our constituent markets. As a key leader you will have experience working with a variety of clients on their technology needs, be able to assess gaps and advise on solutions that provide significant ROI, and recognize the opportunity to build new capabilities into the existing ODM products. We are looking for someone who is passionate about asking and answering questions through the use of large structured and unstructured data. It is critical to have exceptional communication skills and the ability to make complex concepts simple and easy to understand.&#160;&#160;&#160;

Client Engagement and Account Management (75% Time Allocation):

Drive strong working relationships and customer satisfaction with existing clients by becoming a trusted client partner and strategic advisor from a data management and technology perspective 
Increase value delivered to existing clients and stakeholders by driving and enabling modern technologies and innovative delivery models for account growth 
Act as client advocate by partnering with delivery teams to ensure product and service programs / projects are providing business value as expected within the existing account(s)

New Business Development (25%): 
Drive new customer growth and opportunities where Optum may provide business value of modern data management and technology services 
Support business development activities that support Optum growth directives, including new product development in support of internal and commercial markets 
Work collaboratively with business customers and stakeholders while representing data management and technology strategic perspective based on client needs 
Ensure the business development strategy aligns to practice objectives and client strategy 
Leverage existing client relationships to drive new client growth directives
