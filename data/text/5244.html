General Position Summary

We’re looking for a data scientist to work across all areas of our business. You will enjoy working with one of the richest healthcare data sets in the world, cutting edge technology, and the ability to see your insights drive the direction of the organization. The perfect candidate will have a background in a quantitative or technical field, will have experience working with large data sets, and will be adept at working directly with functional leaders to gather requirements, present results, and give recommendations. You are scrappy, focused on results, a self-starter, and have demonstrated success in using analytics to drive the understanding, growth, and success of a business or functional area.

MedExpress Core Responsibilities

  • Has a contagious and positive work ethic, inspires others, and models the behaviors of Genuine, Caring, Friendly.
  • Demonstrates effective verbal and written communication that is clear, well-organized, and demonstrates an understanding of audience needs.
  • Through genuine and positive communication, makes each customer feel informed, understood, and special.
  • An effective team player who contributes valuable ideas and feedback and can be counted on to meet commitments.
  • Is able to keep up in the MedExpress environment by facing tasks and challenges with energy and passion.
  • Pursues activities with focus and drive, defines work in terms of success, and can be counted on to complete goals.

Major Responsibilities

  • Apply your expertise in quantitative analysis and data mining to the presentation of data to see beyond the numbers and understand underlying trends in the data.
  • Partner with functional leaders to solve problems and identify trends and opportunities.
  • Inform, influence, support, and execute our strategic decisions.
  • Acquire and blend data from a variety of sources for use in analysis.
  • Build scalable solutions to perform scheduled analytical processes and models.
  • Design and evaluate pilot studies.
  • Propose and adhere to a roadmap.
  • The Data Scientist, Analytics role has work across the following three areas:
    • Analysis and Presentation
      • Evaluating and defining metrics
      • Building and analyzing dashboards and reports
      • Building key data sets to empower operational and exploratory analysis
      • Monitoring key metrics, understanding root causes of changes in metrics
    • Exploratory data analysis
      • Proposing what to build in the next roadmap
      • Understanding patient behaviors and long-term trends
      • Identifying new levers to help move key metrics
      • Building models and forecasting
      • Designing and evaluating experiments
    • Predictive analytics

Building models to predict future outcomes and behaviors

Working Relationships

  • Multiple interactions weekly with all levels of leadership across departments and business units.
  • Tight relationship with the Information Technology organization.
