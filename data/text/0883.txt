Personalization and Discovery - Spotify | Jobs.com
        We are looking for a passionate and seasoned product leader to join Spotify's User Engagement group. Our goal is to make every listening session more engaging through personalization and discovery; in this role, you'd help multiple product teams find better ways to combine our understanding of the world of music with our 100M+ users' unique tastes and preferences, helping music fans listen for longer, find new artists they love, and come back to Spotify more often.
        What you'll do
        * Work closely as part of a tight-knit team with your tech and design counterparts in the User Engagement group
        * Lead the unified vision and roadmaps for Spotify's flagship personalized experiences &#8211; including Discover Weekly, Daily Mix, and Release Radar &#8211; as well as explore new opportunities and personalization user needs
        * Drive experimentation on&#8212;and improvements to&#8212;our recommendation/ML models and programming infrastructure, with a clear vision of how to measure quality as well as assess the potential impact of new capabilities
        * Incorporate advances in research into product opportunities, and help guide research direction based on product roadmap
        * Be the voice of the user at all times, and work hand-in-hand with our user research, data science, and editorial teams to continually identify, understand, and address underserved user populations and needs
        * Manage, inspire, and grow our group's existing product organization and squads
        * Work from our NYC office (with frequent travel) to serve teams that span NYC, Boston, and Stockholm
        Who you are
        * You have substantial demonstrated experience managing and developing well-loved software products. You've had many experiences defining product roadmaps, building collaboratively with design and engineering peers, and shipping at scale.
        * You have experience managing Product Managers and a portfolio of multiple products and teams
        * You are a compelling storyteller with experience communicating concepts and ideas to engineers and non-engineers alike
        * You have good instincts but match that with deep analytical thinking to focus on the things that matter most, especially with expertise in large amounts of data
        * You have a proven ability to influence decision-making at all levels within an organisation, and in a variety of contexts
        * You have a strong gut based on experience, but take a critical mind even to your own ideas and are ready to prove them wrong with data
        We are proud to foster a workplace free from discrimination. We strongly believe that diversity of experience, perspectives, and background will lead to a better environment for our employees and a better product for our users and our creators. This is something we value deeply and we encourage everyone to come be a part of changing the way the world listens to music.
