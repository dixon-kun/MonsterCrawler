Anyone who has redeemed a coupon, filled a prescription or returned a product, has touched Inmar. We apply technology and data science to improve outcomes for consumers and those who serve them. As a trusted intermediary for over 35 years, we have unmatched access to billions of consumer and business transactions in real time. Our analytics, platforms and services enable engagement with shoppers and patients, and optimize results.


POSITION SUMMARY

The client marketing project manager provides marketing support to more than 40 sales personnel including sales leadership, ensure they can present best-in-class materials for their clients. He or she leads project teams across functions that create client-specific sales content in a high volume, fast-paced environment. He or she understands a wide variety of projects and contributes to the strategic planning with the sales person in a partnered approach. The successful candidate will be agile, passionate, driven and calm under pressure. He or she will take initiative independently while also able to get along in a team environment. He or she will have an ability to lead or manage up and in all situations, drive on-time execution.

PRIMARY JOB RESPONSIBILITIES include (with other duties as assigned):

  • Manage presentations, proposals, RFPs and other sales deliverables
    • Proactively manage project content and components
    • Facilitate cross-departmental/all level project teams and timelines
    • Understand Inmar products from a sales content perspective to better assist with collateral
    • Recommend content that demonstrates sales value and message to create project drafts
    • Deliver projects that meet branding and messaging standards
    • Review/edit projects for written flow and detailed grammar, etc.
    • Understand assigned accounts on a broad level in support of the CVM/BDM
    • Maintain reasonable accessibility due to deadline or event
    • Maintain project-related administration (e.g., project time log, folder organization and archiving)
    • Act as backup for teammates
  • Manage other team initiatives and improvement projects
    • Support client on-site visits as needed in the Innovation Lab
    • Support long-term client marketing projects to improve close rates (e.g., case studies, competitive intelligence, etc.)
    • Work with marketing directors and managers to update content and streamline process
    • Support competitive intelligence, market research department initiatives
  • Assist manager with team training and development program
  • Other duties as assigned.


Minimum Requirements:

  • Intermediate to advanced knowledge of Microsoft Outlook, Excel, Word and PowerPoint
  • Bachelor’s degree (communications, marketing, business, preferred) or equivalent experience
  • Four of more years of relevant experience (project management, sales support, marketing preferred)
