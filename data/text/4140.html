This position serves as an important resource for data- based insights related to customers. Responsible for querying data, and providing meaningful displays of findings to support customer facing areas such as Residential Programs, etc., in order to aid in their understanding of our diverse customer population, customer preferences and their engagement with our services/programs. It includes utilizing, querying and data manipulation techniques to acquire data from various siloed databases for various purposes, such as supporting Program Staff with customers, key performance reporting, identifying opportunities, among others. This position provides information relevant to customer targeting and campaign effectiveness, as well as other relevant customer metrics, and collaborates with Program Staff and other Departments in order to align data presentment and other outcomes with client data needs.Utilize advanced data manipulation techniques while extracting, querying, transforming and loading data as needed in support of various reporting and analysis needs. Perform data analysis of information gathered, and develop recommendations/actions to be taken based on the applicable information. Analyze data/information and creates data visualization, reporting and presentation of insights developed through the analysis. Act as a point of contact regarding business problems as they relate to Residential Program analytics. Handle client expectations related to schedule/timeline. Work effectively and collaborate with other team members and across the organization. Maintain current knowledge and awareness of technical developments in the field of data analytics. Performs other duties as assigned.

External Qualifications:

Requires a bachelor's degree (or equivalent experience), preferably in data science, data analytics, or social science and 4 years of professional experience related to analytical activities. Strong analytical thinking, analysis, and problem solving skills and ability to communicate abstract ideas clearlyKnowledge of and ability to manage data acquisition from multiple sources, and analysis.Business acumen, technical knowledge and knowledge in applications (ie SQL) and technologies.Ability to manage multiple work streams independently.Good relationship management skills.Ability to identify and draw on analytical tools and techniques to develop creative approaches and insights to client issues.
