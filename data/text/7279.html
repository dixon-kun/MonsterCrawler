Senior Data Scientist at HCL Technologies

Redmond, WA 98052

Report this job

About the Job

Responsibilities:

· Interprets problems and provide solutions to business problems using data analysis, data mining, optimization tools, and machine learning techniques and statistics

· Contributing to recommendations on predictive / analytical modeling products, services, protocols, and standards in support of product development efforts

· Contributing to predictive / analytical modeling standards, ETL, reporting, and data analysis methodologies

· Leading a team of data scientists ( PhDs ) to build algorithmic models for IVHM, HAR, M2M Analytics, Service Intelligence, and Product Intelligence

· Mentor Data Science Associates ( Experienced, but no / limited exposure in Analytics / non PhDs)

· Create External Visibility (Publications, and International Conferences ) and filing Patents

Required Capabilities (Generic):

· Experience in solving client's analytics problems and effectively communicating results and methodologies

· Ability to prototype statistical analysis and modeling algorithms and apply these algorithms for data driven solutions to problems in new domains.

· Proven ability to rationalize disparate data sources and the ability to intuit the large picture within a dataset

· Software development skills in one or more high level languages (Java /C/C++/C#), one or more scripting languages (Python/Perl/Shell), and common ML tools (Weka, R, RapidMiner, KNIME, scikit, SAS, SPSS, ModelBuilder etc.)

· Knowledge of machine learning and data mining techniques in one or more areas of statistical modeling methods

o Recommender systems, collaborative filtering techniques, propensity modeling

o Natural Language Processing, LDA, Topic Modeling, search relevance

o Logistic Regression, Linear Models Family (Poisson models, Survival models, Hierarchical models, Naïve-Bayesian estimators), Conjoint Analysis, Spatial models, Time-series models

o Neural Networks, Genetic Algorithms, SVM, KNN, Deep Learning

· Experience designing algorithms on Hadoop ecosystem or Knowledge and experience working with distributed computing systems is a plus

Required Capabilities (Specific Domain) : Position 1 – Semiconductor / Manufacturing Analytics

· Prior experience in developing analytical or statistical models for Wafer test pattern analysis, Yield control and asset optimization, statistical design of experiments and data analysis of semiconductor test data, Regression Failure Analysis, predictive maintenance or reliability engineering

Required Capabilities (Specific Domain) : Position 2 – Telecom / Network Analytics

· Prior experience in developing analytical models for network planning and provisioning, cell site optimization, RAN Analytics, Network Congestion control, Dynamic network control or Preemptive Customer Care

Qualifications :

· PhD degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Statistics, Bio statistics,  Physics, Mathematics, Operations Research or equivalent technical field, min 6+ years post Ph.D


Can work from either Redmond, WA or Sunnyvale, CA


Please apply to skolli@hcl.com

About this company

HCL Technologies Ltd.

At the dawn of the new millennium, a unique information technology company was born: HCL Technologies. As narrated in the video below, HCL Technologies has demonstrated remarkable growth through the recent economic downturn, emerging as one of only eight 21st century listed technology companies in the world to cross $1bn in Net Profit, $6bn in Revenue and $15bn in Market Capitalization.

This was possible because we believe that real value lies in the relationship created between customer and provider, which is built on three deeply rooted value systems:

  • We put Employees First , empowering them to believe in everyday ideas that create immense value for our customers, making HCL Technologies the world's largest Ideapreneurship™ .
  • Our fixation on Value Centricity is dedicated to delivering more value for our clients, putting their interests ahead of ours and delivering value that goes beyond the contract.
  • Our belief in Trust, Transparency and Flexibility drives us to step into our customers' business reality, guaranteeing outcomes with great enthusiasm.

While we pride our eye to detail in constructing robust contracts with clients, we realize that only a Relationship Beyond the Contract can capture what will change tomorrow.

The management's role at HCL Technologies is to empower employees to believe in the power of ideas - a culture we call Ideapreneurship . Helping clients from all spheres of work through innovative technology solutions, Ideapreneurs are transforming global businesses - one relationship at a time.

Over the years, we have seen these values manifest in the form of platforms such as the Value Portal , where front line employees have generated and implemented ideas that have unlocked close to $600mn of unforeseen business value for our customers. Or MAD JAM, a celebration of employee-seeded ideas that are nurtured by leaders and harvested into successful business offerings.

We believe that our deeply ingrained value system has helped us win multiple awards , establishing us as a leading IT provider among our Indian peers. These awards include the ICSI Corporate Governance Award, Best Governed Company from the Asia Center for Corporate Governance and the CNBC Outstanding Company of the Year Award.
