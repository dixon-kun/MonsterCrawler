We're looking for our next MVP—a Product Marketing Manager – Services Marketing rock star to lead the development and growth of our portfolio of strategic and outcome-oriented consulting, support and managed services. The ideal candidate is someone with an entrepreneurial spirit, who thrives on taking ideas from the whiteboard to the market with excitement and passion.  We strongly believe that Blackbaud is uniquely positioned to deliver strategic consulting services that help our customers achieve key business outcomes such as increased fundraising, expanded supporter files and improved e-marketing and social engagement performance.

We’re looking for the right leader to partner with our Customers and our Services, Sales and Product Marketing teams to take this vision to our sector, and to grow this segment of our business over the next three years. If you eat, breathe, sleep product or services marketing and have a passion for collaboration and winning, we’d like to hear from you!


Major Areas of Accountability:

Drive our “Go to Market” performance

  • Develop a keen understanding of our customers, their business needs, industry trends and best practices
  • Quickly develop a thorough understanding of Blackbaud’s fundraising and online products and related services
  • Size and define market segments, understand the competitive dynamics of targeting, positioning, pricing, packaging and differentiation for assigned solutions within the Success Services portfolio.
  • Collaborate with Customers, Sales and Services resources to define Success Service offerings
  • Simplify and manage to a concise, relevant, and agile Success Services portfolio, including guiding recommended investments, determining new service entry timing and opportunity, and accurate market approach and positioning.
  • Own and drive successfully launching new services, as well as refine and retool our on-going marketing strategies and execution for existing services.
  • Continually assesses and refine service offerings to ensure customer value, marketability and profitability
  • Develop and execute go-to-market plans to support our sales and revenue objectives

Empower internal stakeholders; influence external nonprofit decision makers and thought leaders

  • Effectively engage Marketing, Sales, Product Management, and Services; liaise across various organizational levels to ensure the overall success of the complete Success Services portfolio in the market.
  • Ensure that the Sales organization is effectively equipped and collaboratively educated on our strategy to enable full achievement of revenue goals.
  • Leverage your reputation as a ‘market expert’ to partner with senior leadership and guide the short and long-term agenda for our Success Services portfolio.
  • Arm internal stakeholders with: value/differentiation presentations, training, demonstrations, call scripts, case studies, web content, white papers, ROI modeling.
  • Maintain internal customer reference/sales collateral website (source.blackbaud.com)
  • Collaborate with Data Science to develop industry benchmarks and performance KPI’s that can be used to define service offerings, to inform ROI stories, identify target prospects and generate sales leads

Crisp execution of projects and analysis, and amazing cross team, cross location collaboration

  • Clarify and articulate Blackbaud’s position as a leading provider of Success Services to our market
  • Optimize benefits statements, proof points, and conduct and deliver differentiation analysis.  Continuously measure, manage and appropriately adjust marketing program effectiveness:  lead generation performance; lead conversion ratios; sales cycle conversions.
  • Collaborate and drive key demand generation activities in collaboration with Marketing Programs/Channel Marketing team:  content strategy, campaigns, tradeshows, seminars/webinars, and other lead generation activities across the marketing organization.
  • Collaborate with Corporate Marketing to develop or boost market awareness and sales effectiveness through media relations, social networking, advertising, product and segment collateral.
  • Present weekly/monthly performance analyses to Blackbaud Executive Team and other stakeholders

Key Performance Indicators (KPI’s)

  • Leads ($, #)
  • Services bookings ($)
  • Services Units (#)
  • Services Attachment Rate (%)
  • Customer ROI

Experience

  • 4+ years of technology market experience.  Prior experience marketing service offerings is highly desired.
  • Software-as-a-Service or “Cloud” technology experience a plus
  • Experience and a track record of driving growth and results for a specific product/service line
  • BS/BA required, MBA also advantageous
  • Strong problem solving, analytical and mental
