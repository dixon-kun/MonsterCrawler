          Global Head of FX Strategy, Content and Analysis
         The Global Head of FX Strategy, Content and Analysis will be charged with the mandate to lead the design, development and implementation of a fully integrated and market leading FX content offering.
         This role will lead CitiFX&#8217;s team covering Strategy, Flows, Technical Analysis, FX Wire and several other delivery channels and content products within CitiFX. &#160; By fully integrating and aligning this confederation of high calibre teams together under a single leadership structure we are looking for a senior leader who will be both capable and highly motivated to offer and grow a world-class product suite.&#160; Furthermore, delivering industry-leading content is essential to both enhancing our client value proposition while also providing our trading teams with the leading edge markets insights&#160;to effectively interpret markets to successfully manage risks.
         Given the rapidly changing FX industry, Citi's content teams also play a critical role in ensuring we remain at the forefront of industry developments, covering global FX markets through an increasing use of proprietary data and analytics.&#160; Growing this analytical endeavour will be an important objective for this team.
         This role will report to the Head of Foreign Exchange and be part of the Foreign Exchange trading management team.
