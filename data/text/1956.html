Northrop Grumman Mission Systems Sector is looking for top industry talent to support the Sector Data Analytics Homeroom on the Mission Assurance Data Analytics, Tools, and Metrics team. The position will be located in Baltimore, MD. Work location flexible, full-time telecommuting option available.

Data Scientist 4 will support a wide range of advanced analytics initiatives and focus on predictive analytics execution of internal business data sources. A successful candidate will collect and analyze data; facilitate data triage, extraction, transformation, and loading; perform statistical and qualitative analyses; define technical strategy; present approach and results; and interface with customers.

Additional responsibilities are as follows:

* Develops data analytics systems to support NGMS objectives.

* Utilizes machine learning algorithms, which may include belief networks, Hidden Markov models, Artificial Immune Systems, Gaussian Mixture Models, Decision Tress, graph theory, Information Geometry, Genetic Algorithms, and basic statistical analysis.

* Tests algorithms on large and small data sets, including textual, numeric, temporal, and symbolic data.

* Employs relevant analytics COTS products.

* Programs in Java, JavaScript, and/or R.

* Collaborates with stakeholders to develop solutions to problems and potential problems.

* Interfaces with customers and consumers to develop system requirements.
