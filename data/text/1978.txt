        The Advanced Analytics - Senior Analyst will use in-depth knowledge in quantitative database marketing and an understanding of business processes to develop technical solutions for business issues. Serves as a resource within area of expertise and provide guidance to more junior team members; may lead small teams on a project basis.

        * Predictive statistical models
        * Customer profiling
        * Segmentation Analysis
        * Survey Design and Analysis
        * Data Analysis and Mining
        * External Data Enhancement

        * Assist in presenting ideas and findings to future and existing clients
