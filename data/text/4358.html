Description

Santander US is transforming how we serve and interact with our customers and clients.  That transformation is heavily dependent on innovative analytics.  Analytics & Decision Sciences (ADS) is a new function that is focused on leveraging all of our data assets to solve business challenges, optimize profitability, grow customer relationships and improve customer experience.    As we build leading analytic capabilities, we are looking for a diverse team of thought leaders who are passionate about utilizing analytics to make improvements for our customers, employees and businesses.


Responsibilities :

  • Utilize analytics to identify new growth opportunities, including business case creation and partnering with functional partners and business leaders on implementation
  • Translate organizational goals and objectives into analytic opportunities
  • Be the domain expert for segmentation, modeling and targeting to support business unit objectives
  • Utilize innovative analytic methods to develop unique customer and product segmentation, client target models and profitability analysis that optimize business investment and resource decisions
  • Support strategic and ad hoc analysis to quantify “what”, “how” and “why” customer behavior shifts are occurring
  • Lead the development of new customer response and propensity models to enhance targeting and marketing campaign effectiveness
  • Interface regularly with senior stakeholders and communicate analytic results to non-technical partners



Qualifications

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s Degree required in quantitative areas (Statistics, Mathematics, Operations Research, Economics, or other related fields).  Advanced degree is preferred
  • Minimum of 3-5 years’ experience in predictive analysis and statistical modeling in the financial services industry.  7+ years of total experience required
  • Working knowledge of structured programming languages including SAS, SQL, Unix and/or R; exposure to unstructured data platforms including Hadoop and/or Cloudera preferred
  • Independent thinker that can stretch the boundaries of traditional analytic problem solving
  • Strong track record of analytics innovation, data science and new solution development
  • Experience working with large volumes of data
  • Strong project management and partnership skills.  Must be able to communicate analytic results to non-technical partners
  • Solid consumer and SME P&L knowledge required
