Description

Even if you’re not familiar with our name, you’ve almost certainly heard of our billion-dollar brands like Cadbury, Milka, Prince and Oreo. Developing the world’s favorite snacks, we’re passionate about creating delicious moments of joy for people all around the world. Working at Mondelēz International is all about the power of big - and small.

Why big? Because we’re the world’s leading maker of Chocolate, Biscuits, Candy and Gum. We’re ranked #1 in chocolate, biscuits and candy, and #2 in gum. What’s more, we employ over 100,000 people worldwide and market products in around 165 countries.

Why small? We go out of our way to hear everyone’s point of view, and develop local roots wherever we go. It’s this combination of big and small that drives everything we do.

For more information, please visit: http://www.mondelezinternational.com/ and www.facebook.com/mondelezinternational.

Strategy is where you’ll help us develop plans to shape an exciting future for Mondelēz International. Working with our excellent team of strategy professionals, you’ll diagnose key business growth drivers, set strategic direction for the future, prioritize our growth initiatives, and identify opportunities to improve efficiencies.
The Data Scientist is responsible utilizing technologies and methodologies to evaluate current processes and systems to determine areas of improvement that will enhances our sales capabilities and reduce inefficiencies. This role is ideal for candidates who love spending their day with numbers, data mining & analysis, reports, drawing insights from numbers and sharing out findings and recommendations from their analysis. This position will be responsible for determining the most appropriate resource tools and software providers to advance our organizations mobile technology capabilities. They will provide end to end leadership and collaborate with key stake holders and business partners to design, develop and innovate, effective and efficient processes within our DSD organization.
Main Responsibilities
Pull Alteryx data and then leverage Tableau to evaluate and provide statistical analysis reports evaluating the efficiency of current processes, procedures, equipment and associates. Establish data model and framework that captures internal and external structured data sources to enable predictive modeling and insights. Drive operational excellence in the Sales organization through identification of opportunity areas that create efficiency, remove obstacles, or create improved processes and approaches to drive increased revenue growth and productivity. They will provide end to end leadership and collaborate with key stake holders and business partners to design, develop and innovate, effective and efficient processes within our DSD organization.  50%
Anticipate and solve strategic business problems with broad impact by applying leading-edge theories and techniques to investigate problems, detect patterns and recommend solutions. Lead and participate in the identification, prioritization, implementation, and control of cost reduction and process improvement initiatives using lean processes. Present learnings and insights as a basis for strategic decision making. 25%
Evaluate vendor and supplier proposals for new system/hardware capability purchases to ensure increased efficiency and evaluate the contribution to cost saving measures. Utilize Lean Manufacturing/Six Sigma principals in developing continuous improvement activities/initiatives. 15%
Preform ad hoc requests as needed including being the Alteryx power user for Strategy and Deployment team. 10%
Qualifications

1) General Sales Experience, retail operations planning a plus (5 yr/min)

2) Experience leading and implementing manufacturing process improvement activities (3 yr/min)

3) Industrial engineering technology, application and project management & data analysis (2 yr/min)

4) Bachelor Degree in Finance/Business/Marketing/Engineering or related Manufacturing field required.

5) Lean/Six Sigma training/certification a plus, efficient in Microsoft Office/Tableau
