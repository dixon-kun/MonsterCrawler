        Position Summary:
        The Global Insights Data Analyst (GIDA), Data Science is responsible for leveraging structured &amp; unstructured consumer data along with other data sources to drive insights and actionable business recommendations. The scope of these recommendations will span both tactical and strategic goals of the Marketing organization. The GIDA will work closely with multiple cross-functional business partners to develop hypotheses and work with multiple data sources to bring structure and integration to the data. This will lead to robust analysis by the GIDA which will deliver visual presentations that turn complex data into a clear story allowing business partners to drive action/decisions quickly and effectively. This position will be instrumental in developing insights that are part of a 360 consumer view and enable real-time data-driven marketing and optimization. The GIDA will also be responsible for building and improving data science capabilities within GI, guiding the function on how to best leverage this skill and build it out for further impact across Sales and Product Development in the future. Position reports to Associate Director, Data Science.
        Key Responsibilities:
        *  Build/Code/Analyze Appropriate Data in order to Drive to Actionable and Clear Business Recommendations
        *  Build complex data sets from multiple data sources
        *  Build learning systems to analyze and filter continuous data flows and offline data analysis.
        *  Conduct advanced statistical analysis to determine trends and significant data relationships.
        *  Identify opportunities for data to opportunistically inform marketing decisions &amp; optimization
        *  Leverage data science tools and techniques to analyze large data sets and develop custom models/algorithms to uncover trends, patterns and insights in the data
        *  Drive integration of data with traditional insights as appropriate to develop more holistic and complete recommendations
        *  Leverage data visualization tools &amp; techniques to deliver insights that drive business recommendations, innovative business solutions and enable real-time marketing and optimization
        *  Contribute to Progressing the GI Function in Developing Appropriate Data Science Capabilities to Enable 3D Objectives
        *  Monitor data science trends, identify implications and proactively build into Clorox capabilities and educate cross-functional business partners
        *  Evaluate existing data science tools/techniques and aid in the development of best practices.
        *  Develop process and approaches to improve the effectiveness and visibility of data science
        *  Identify how data-driven insights and approaches can transform traditional insights tools/ processes and inform/improve our insights processes.
        *  Partner with IT and Analytics to develop best in class processes and techniques to leverage data science across all 3Ds
