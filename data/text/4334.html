Description

Escalation Engineering group is in need of proven leader in raising customer satisfaction. The Engineering manager will lead the team to deliver high quality fixes and solutions to DataXu customers with rapid turn around time. This hands-on leader should have the technical abilities and customer relations experience to work with the Customer Success, Support and the Sales organization to prioritize requests on both a technical and customer-relations level. Further, the manager should continuously evolve the process and facilitate the bridging between engineering and Customer Success for a rapidly growing organization with increasingly demanding and rapidly expanding customer base.

On the Job.....

Lead a top-tier team of Java, and frontend developers and help troubleshoot complex problems seen in the product

Work with Customer Success, Support, and Operations team management to prioritize incoming requests

Manage resourcing to dedicate to customer issues

Improve the defect resolution process for minimized latency and increased customer satisfaction

Ensure communication flows and hand-offs are done well cross-organizationally and resolution tickets are updated continuously

Work proactively with rest of engineering to ensure for maintainability, diagnosability, and serviceability of future releases

About You....

7+ years of experience in a combination of Engineering and Sustaining/Support leadership roles

Experience in enterprise software, including issues with threading, network and disk IO, data transformations, data integrity, systems management, systems administration, monitoring

Understanding of database systems, analytics, reporting software

Understanding of Web application software, including html, CSS, Javascript, Ruby on Rails, Flash, REST, and associated issues

Proven track record to define process for effective escalation and customer satisfaction management

Proficiency in Linux platforms, including scripting on respective platforms

Systems Administration experience a plus

Bachelors Degree or higher in Computer Science or equivalent experience
