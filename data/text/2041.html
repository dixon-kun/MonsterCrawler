Raytheon Applied Signal Technology is a diversified intelligence and defense company that develops systems to provide integrated sensor and signal processing products in support of intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance.

In this position you will work on complex technical problems and provide solutions. Application of advanced technical principles, theories and concepts will be necessary while contributing to the development of new principles and concepts and ensuring proper setup, configuration, and functionality of systems being deployed.

The candidate will work with other staff in our various customer offices for the protection of our country. The successful candidate will be able to generate briefings and present material clearly to team members, customers, and Raytheon Applied Signal Technology management as necessary.

Required Skills

* A minimum of 2 years computer science experience with high-level language software programming

* Within the last 3 years, a minimum of 2 years experience with designing, coding, testing, and debugging software to run under either Microsoft Windows (XP or later) or UNIX (Solaris 8 or later, Redhat LINUX) Operating System

* Within the last 3 years, a minimum of 2 years experience in at least one (1) of the specializations
