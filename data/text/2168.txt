        Arizona Care Network, LLC (ACN) is a clinically integrated(CI) physician network and Accountable Care Organization (ACO) that is acollaboration sponsored by Dignity Health and Abrazo Health. The Director of Analytics is an employee ofMedProVidex (MPV), a wholly-owned subsidiary of Dignity Health, providingsupport to ACN.
        The Director of Analytics assists the ACO leadership andprovider network in meeting the goals of the ACO including but not limited toanalysis of population health metrics, network clinical, financial,utilization, and patient satisfaction in support of the optimization of ACO'sperformance relative to value-based contracts. The Director will provide handson data science and analytics functions for ACO leadership, day-to-dayoversight of ACO data team members and collaborate with ACO leadership and providernetwork to achieve organizational goals.
        PRINCIPLES DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
        * Directs and leads ACN's data and analyticsprogram/activities.
        * Collaborate closely with ACO senior leaders and networkstakeholders to meet the strategic and operational analytics and reportingneeds of organization.
        * Coordinates and is responsible for data integrity within theACN Enterprise Data Warehouse (EDW).
        * Develops and implements ACN business rules for applicationto the EDW curated data prior to exporting such to ACN analytic &amp; reportingvendors.
        * Oversee and develop data quality and audit processes toensure the integrity of the analytics and reporting within ACN vendorsolutions.
        * Collaborates and support the Chief Information Officer withinformation exchange across stakeholders.
        * Collaborates and supports the Chief Medical Officer in theanalytic, reporting, and strategic planning needs in supports of the ACO'sPopulation Health Management Programs, including population risk-segmentation,risk-stratification, care gap identification, performance benchmarkcalculation, and provider performance reporting.
        * Collaborates with the ACO Information Technology (IT) teamregarding EMR data capture optimization, data file transfers, HealthInformation Exchange (HIE), portals and more to identify and implement bestpractices in data capture, mapping, and governance in support of ACO clinicaland financial insights.
        * Performs gain-sharing and risk-sharing calculations inaccordance with payer contracts and criteria established by ACO seniorleadership and analyzes trends and costs of care by provider in the network.
        * Helps develop and revise gain-sharing and risk-sharingmethodologies based on operational needs and available data.
        * Performs ad-hoc analysis in support of the businessintelligence needs of the ACO
        * Performs other duties as assigned.
        POSITION QUALIFICATIONS
        A) EXPERIENCE: Minimum seven (7) years of experience in thehealth plan or hospital industry with extensive experience in informationsystems, data warehousing, analytic system implementations, consulting andstrategic planning.
        B) EDUCATIONAND TRAINING: Master's degree in HealthEconomics, Health Administration or a related field or experience commensuratewith position.
        C) SPECIALIZEDKNOWLEDGE AND SKILLS: Demonstratedskills in the areas of accounting, supervision, management, written and verbalcommunication, judgment, problem-solving, presentation and public relations.
        Minimum of seven years of experience with SAS EnterpriseMiner and Text Analytics, SAS/STAT, Enterprise Guide, Visual Analytics,Business Rues Manager, Model Manager, SQL and SAS coding. Previous experiencewith analyzing large datasets and developing data-driven statistical models isrequired.
        Previous experience in Accountable Care, specificallyMedicare Shared Savings program or Next Generation ACO analytics preferred.
        D) OTHERREQUIREMENTS: This position requires adynamic and highly motivated person with the ability to inspire and leadpeople, as well as communicate effectively with Physicians.
