        Title: Senior Data Wrangler - 18929
        Your Responsibilities
        As a Senior Data Wrangler, you will work within an Analytics Team within the Enterprise Analytics Accelerator (EAA) to generate business insights on transformational high-value analytics projects together with a Data Catalyst and Data Scientist. Using analytical programming languages, create automated processes for data gathering, cleansing &amp; improve efficiency and accuracy of data collection, entry &amp; verification. In addition, you will:
        * Lead the collaboration with the business and IT domains to design and integrate the data required for Data Scientists to generate insights &amp; provide data access to Data Scientists for modeling or predictive analysis.
        * Lead and execute activities to support all aspects bringing new data sources into the EAA.
        * Collaborate extensively with IT domains to design data ingestion, data models and automated operational metrics for consistent high quality data loading to EAA.
        * Locate and acquire data for designated projects within the Enterprise Analytics Accelerator (EAA).
        * Develop strategies, standards and best practices in the areas of data wrangling and data integration and lead adoption throughout EAA and adjacent IT domains.
        * Validate data quality in conjunction with respective data stewards by developing reports and tools to monitor and visualize data quality.
        * May perform initial analyses on the data. Independently organizes and cleans the acquired data to remove quality issues.
        * Lead the effort in closing systemic data quality gaps by working with business and across IT domains to identify process or data management changes, integration opportunities with other data groups, and/or data training needs within the business.
        * Explore new ways of organizing data specifically for EAA projects.
        * Focus on developing sustainable data usage for EAA Business Partners including access, costs, and organization processes.
        * Suggest new data storage systems Works across other IT domains to ensure the architecture of the data meets the needs of the EAA analytics teams and aligns with overall IT data management strategies
        * Participate in creating analytics models as required by the EAA Data Scientists to ensure the data is valid, accurate, and applicable for approved EAA projects.
        * Support data governance activities which could include acting as a data steward or custodian of certain data elements
        What Skills You Need
        * 3 + years of experience of working with SQL, Python, R, PYSPARK and other analytics tools.
        * 3 + years of experience in data wrangling and working with large and complex databases, especially in the area of data integration
        * Knowledge of Hadoop/Data Lake/Data Cloud ecosystems.
        * Knowledge of SAS &amp; modeling or predictive analysis.
        * Good communication skills across a technical environment and with business users.
        What Makes You Stand Out
        Other areas of experience or interest that will help us place you on the right team include:
        * Knowledge of Java, Spark, Julia, Unix, Scala, map reduce, hive, Lisp (natural language processing), and node.red.
        * 2 + years of experience managing large databases.
        * A Master's degree in programming or similar field or equivalent experience.
        Education:
        Ideally, you will have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Economics, or equivalent quantitative field.
