Job Description:

TradeLink LLC is well-recognized as a successful proprietary trading firm and investment adviser hedge fund program. The firm is headquartered in the heart of Chicago with an investment centers in London and Warsaw. Our trading strategy and quantitative research teams have multi-disciplinary academic and professional backgrounds. The quantitative researchers develop, both independently and in collaboration, trading strategies in their full cycle of evolution: from idea generation to validation and implementation.

This full-time position is for a Masters degree or Ph.D. (preferred) level in Computer Science, Data Science, Math or Physical Sciences. We are seeking an exceptional quantitative researcher for several of our trading strategy teams. The candidate we hire will be an analytical thinker and a creative problem solver with extremely strong quantitative and computational proficiency.

A quantitative researcher is responsible for market research and algorithmic model development based on sound economic, statistical and computational principles. The researchers formulate, test and deploy highly complex forecasting algorithms and trading strategies.

Experience and Skills:
  • Masters or Ph.D. in Computer Science, Data Science, Math or Physical Sciences
  • Excellent programming and modeling skills
  • Proven quantitative and computational aptitude
  • High proficiency in Python and/or C++ language skills
  • Analytical, mathematical and statistical proficiency, including research experience
  • Rigorous attention to detail, data accuracy and quality of output
  • Solid and creative problem solving abilities

Experience in the financial markets is helpful, but not necessary.

Interest, curiosity and some reasonable level of obsession in the operation, history and evolution of financial markets is mandatory.

EOE
