DESCRIPTION / OPPORTUNITY

The real estate market is undergoing asignificant and exciting transformation – driven by data and technology. Thus, strategyassociate plays a vital role in CBRE’s mission. The Strategy Associate will bea critical part of the strategy team reporting to the Vice President. Thisperson will combine business acumen with analytical techniques to understandthe commercial real estate ecosystem and how CBRE serves its client base.Furthermore, this person will assess the competitive landscape (both at CBREand externally) and identify opportunities for creating new value and forbetter serving our client base. The ideal candidate must be willing to work ina highly collaborative environment and manage multiple competing priorities andassignments.

JOB RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Through number of means (e.g., interviews,research, pilots, etc.), articulate strategic opportunities in better servingclients
  • Scan for technology trends; assess implications toCBRE and what we should do about it
  • Analyze data around real estate markets and theoperations of real estate assets across multiple data sources; provideleadership with powerful facts and analyses that outline solutions and drivechange
  • Develop plans and programs for changeimplementation in the organization
  • Drive process re-engineering efforts alongsiderepresentatives from other business functions
  • Drive select special projects end-to-end – fromoutlining the type of solution through implementation, interpretation ofresults and internal capability handoffs

DESIRED QUALIFICATIONS

  • A minimum of a Bachelor’s degree in business administration, economics,statistics, data science or engineering; Masters or MBA preferred
  • A minimum of 5 years of professional experienceinvolving data driven strategy, managing complex assignments and working withincross-functional teams; strategy consulting experience a plus
  • Passion for understanding business problems andtrying to address them by leveraging data
  • Interested in technology and the implications itcreates in the market
  • A solid team player and an independent thinkerthat is both curious and analytical
  • Strong verbal, written and presentation skills
  • Mastery of commonly used business productivitysoftware (e.g., Tableau, PowerPoint, Excel, Office, etc.)
  • Experience with SQL server, MS Access and similar preferred
