Job Summary

Virgin Pulse seeks an experienced Data Scientist to support the organization’s efforts to maintain an innovative leadership position in the employee engagement SaaS industry. This role accesses datasets from various sources, conducts advanced analysis, and presents the findings of each analytic and reporting project. The incumbent will be able to interpret the findings and clearly communicate results and recommendations to internal and external customers. We are looking for someone who is self-directed and thrives working in a fast-paced, collaborative environment, in which expectations are high both for the quality and speed of work.

Job Duties
  • Consult to and collaborate with analytics and software product team members to ensure appropriate data is collected and analyzed and that results are provided in an insightful and actionable format
  • Collaborate with product staff on iteration of the results of the analysis, translation to product improvements
  • Write SQL programs to access, clean, and transform required data prior to analysis and reporting
  • Liaise with product on analytic planning aligned to development, release schedules
  • Plan and manage data analytic and reporting process to manage projects efficiently
  • Conduct ad hoc analysis as required using varied analytical tools and techniques
  • Achieve annual Key Performance Indicator objectives, which can include report volumes and scope, internal and external client satisfaction, introducing new areas of data and analysis, and influencing company product and process decisions
Required Education, Training, Knowledge and Experience
  • A bachelor’s degree in statistics, computer science, economics, or related field; Master’s degree is a plus
  • A minimum of four years of work experience in a similar position
  • Experience with SAS, SQL and data visualization tools and techniques
  • Experience with producing and delivering results using varied media (i.e., multiple MS office formats, dashboards/visualization tools, and potentially other formats)
  • Experience in SAAS product industry preferred
  • Experience in health management/health engagement industry preferred
  • Experience with scientific methods and experimental design preferred
Core Skills, Competencies, and Characteristics
  • Creative/critical thinking, data storytelling, and consultative skills
  • Strong analytical skills, with an emphasis on quantitative analysis, descriptive and inferential statistics; deep/machine learning or AI experience a plus
  • Expertise in SAS, or the ability to learn SAS through prior experience with other tools such as R, SPSS or similar statistical software·
  • Strong consulting, communication and presentation skills SQL and database programming skills, experience with MS SQL Server, RedShift, Postgres, and Cassandra/NOSQL databases preferred
  • Experience working with large-scale datasets and multiple projects simultaneously
  • Creative energy, self-starter, works equally well independently and collaboratively
