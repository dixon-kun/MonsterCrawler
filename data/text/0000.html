Director IT Data Analytics Function/Purpose: The Director IT Data Analytics is required to lead the advanced analytics team responsible for managing all non-Business Intelligence analytics functions at Hasbro including Social Reporting, eCommerce, Consumer Insights, Digital Marketing, and other advanced analytics and data science capabilities. This person will serve as the interface between the IT analytics team and those business partners who require analytics support to make data-driven decisions by identifying and documenting analytics capability and business requirements. This individual will also lead a team whose responsibilities include data science, architectural design of the underlying technology stack (data, compute/tools, & presentation layers) and delivering a solution that will allow our business partners to use the capability in a test and learn manner in the hopes of making more successful, timely, data-driven decisions. Primary Duties and Responsibilities:   • Serve as the analytics interface to the global business partners, identifying all data sources and KPTs in need of tracking to measure success as well as those functions required to create analytics insights (i.e. A/B testing)   • Working with the Enterprise Architecture and Advanced Analytics teams to estabilsh a technology platform roadmap for Hasbro   • Lead a team to increase and enhance the use of advanced modeling and analytic methods and procude results that fully leverage availble data and analytics.   • Implement "test and learn" analytic methododgies to ensure Hasbro improves upon our ability to implement a variety of methodologies which improve conversation rates and increase sales   • Analyze outcomes from the sales team engagements to continue to understand drivers and learnings to increase sales.   • Craft and maintain budget necessary to fulfill ongoing projects and support future analtyics projects   • Manage vendor relationships with key analtyics vendors and partners Qualifications:   • MBA or advanced degree in quantitative descipline preferred   • BS equivalent required   • 10+ years professional experience, preferably in data management, analytics platforms, and business interaction.   • Advanced MS Excel is a must.   • Knowledge of latest advanced analytics data structures/data lake, compute layer tools, and visualization tools   • Must be comfortable working in a cross-functional role   • Strong analytical and strategic thinking skills required   • Self-starter, fast learner, adaptive to fast-paced working environment,   • Strong communication skills, both written and verbal, with the ability and confidence to succinctly convey complex information to senior management   • Proven experience as an integral member of a high performing work team Hasbro (NASDAQ : HAS) is a global play and entertainment company committed to Creating the World's Best Play Experiences. From toys and games to television, movies, digital gaming and consumer products, Hasbro offers a variety of ways for audiences to experience its iconic brands, including NERF, MY LITTLE PONY, TRANSFORMERS, PLAY-DOH, MONOPOLY, LITTLEST PET SHOP and MAGIC: THE GATHERING, as well as premier partner brands. The Company's Hasbro Studios and its film label, Allspark Pictures, are building its brands globally through great storytelling and content on all screens. Through its commitment to corporate social responsibility and philanthropy, Hasbro is helping to make the world a better place for children and their families. Learn more at www.hasbro.com , and follow us on Twitter ( @Hasbro & @HasbroNews ) and Instagram ( @Hasbro ).
