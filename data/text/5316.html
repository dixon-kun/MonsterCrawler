Medecision is looking for a Master Data Architect , a passionate power contributor who will provide effective collaboration, facilitation and coaching skills supporting the development of our applications. The individual will be responsible for data integration for our current solution offerings, as well as contribute key data architecture and persistence design input into the Technology Strategy and Roadmap for future solutions. On a daily basis this person will work with development teams to provide guidance on projects associated with new solution introduction and/or solution enhancements. When required this role will research and recommend new technologies, techniques and patterns along with providing sample programming/standards and best practices for their usage.

Reports to: VP, Enterprise Architecture

Location(s): Wayne, PA; Dallas, TX; Remote


YOU’RE GOOD AT:

  • Designing complex enterprise data solutions with comprehensive understanding of both Operational and Analytical Data Architecture, Security, Performance, Scalability, and Reliability.
  • Performing a key management and thought leadership role in the areas of advanced data techniques, including data modeling, relational and non-relational DB design and implementation, data access, data integration, data visualization, text mining, data discovery, statistical methods.
  • Profiling source data and meeting regularly with IT partners to develop complete source to target data mappings
  • Developing requirements, performing data collection, cleansing, transformation, and loading to populate facts and dimensions for data lake
  • Interacting with application development, enterprise architecture, business intelligence, data science, technology services, and vendors on a regular basis
  • Contributing to predictive / analytical modeling architectures, modeling standards, reporting, and data analysis methodologies.
  • Working with staff and customers to understand the business requirements and business processes, design data warehouse (“DW”) schema and define extract-translate-load (“ETL”) and/or extract-load-translate (“ELT”) processes for DW
  • Familiar with ingestion and integration with 3rd party data souces

YOU HAVE:

  • Bachelor's degree in engineering, computer science or related field; Master's degree preferred
  • Minimum of 8-12 years' experience in diverse data environments. Extensive Database and Data Analysis technology skills and experience. Requires substantial experience with Oracle and the Hadoop ecosystem of Big Data technologies. Experience with MongoDB and other NoSQL technologies is a plus
  • Experience in using statistical analytics and predictive modeling tools and collaborating with and supporting analysts and data scientists. Experience with R is a plus.
  • Knowledge of with Talend or other ETL/ELT/CDC tools
  • Expert level of understanding of Data Modeling, Data Integration, Data Warehousing, Database Design. Familiarity with Universal Data Models and data modeling patterns is a plus.
  • Extensive experience in addressing both operational and near real time needs as well as in addressing more traditional longer-latency data needs
  • Extensive experience with Master Data Management, Data Quality Management and Metadata Management tools and practices
  • Experience in building and maintaining data mapping and data lineage documentation
  • Expert level of understanding of SQL, PL/SQL, and API's of assigned modules
  • Solid understanding of conditional logic
  • Excellent written, verbal, and diagrammatic communication skills
