        The role of a Big Data Lead includes:
        * Provides domain expertise in big data distributed processing frameworks, Hadoop MapReduce and Yarn, Apache Spark, Open-Source and Commercial distributions (Cloudera, Hortonworks, MapR) and Cloud-based services (AWS EMR, Azure HD Insight)

        * Help activate, energize and engage a community of data management engineers and practitioners to build a strong sense of community, to provide career mobility, achieve meaningful corporate reach back and to design training and certification pathways
        A Big Data Lead is familiar with Big Data market trends, helps to define new markets and ensures that CSRA is in a position to be the best choice for meeting our customers' data science needs through collaboration with customers, partners, and internal stake holders to understand the requirements and connect them with CSRA capabilities and offerings.
