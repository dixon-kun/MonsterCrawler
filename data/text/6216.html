Business Intelligence Analyst at TDK Technologies, LLC

Saint Louis, MO 63141

Report this job

About the Job

Summary
The Data Science team develops, maintains and supports the various systems for providing and delivering data and analysis required to plan and manage the business operations of Drury Hotels. This information typically takes the form of regularly scheduled and ad-hoc reporting; as well as specific analytic projects as required. The Data Science team is also responsible for working with key stakeholders and consumers of these reports and analysis to insure consistent and accurate interpretation of the information.
The Business Intelligence Analyst will identify, evaluate, design and implement statistical analyses of historical operations, market, third party, and other sources of data. A primary focus will be evaluating and supporting rate management strategies pertaining to the revenue management functions of the company. The Business Intelligence Analyst will use various tools to help develop improved business practices based on predictive analytics, efficient algorithms and improved data quality; and will develop and support the end-to-end process for implementation and ongoing support of these practices. The Business Intelligence Analyst will also work on a variety of projects across other (multiple) departments to improve system and process decisions.
Essential Duties and Responsibilities (including but not limited to)
 Supporting hotel and corporate operations with data analytics and analytical tools
 Evaluate and inform strategic decisions
 Optimize revenue through analytics around rate strategy and demand modeling
 Implement processes and tools that scale to support strategies to support the entire operation
 Communicate technical scopes to end users and take business needs from concept to end product
 Interface with other Data Science Analytics team members to develop reporting and operational tools
 Developing key performance metrics
 Communicate analysis results and methodology through presentations

Qualifications
To perform this job successfully, an individual must have a strong grasp of data analytics, including statistical methods and modelling, at both theoretical and technical levels. Experience with SQL Server to generate and evaluate very large datasets is highly desired. Experience with analytic packages such as R/SAS/SSAS or similar tools are required.
Experience

This role requires at least 3 years of data analysis experience, with a high preference on experience with SQL Server.
Education
Bachelor’s or Masters degree in computer science, statistics, mathematics, or related technical field.
Language Skills
Ability to read, write and speak English clearly. Ability to write business correspondence clearly and professionally.
Presentation Skills
Ability to present findings and beliefs clearly and compellingly in verbal and in written form. Working knowledge of MS Office to communicate results is required, with an emphasis on using MS PowerPoint presentations as primary means of presenting findings.
Reasoning Ability
The Business Intelligence Analyst must have a strong aptitude for analytics and leveraging findings to drive business an possess excellent critical thinking skills.,
Computer Skills
Experience with the following programs is required:
 R / SAS / SSAS or similar analytics software
 SQL Server Management Studio (SSMS)
 Advanced Excel (Pivot Tables, Lookups, Macros)
 PowerPoint

Additional consideration will be given to experience with the following:
 Web development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
 SQL Server Integration Services (SSIS)
 .NET Framework
 C#
 MongoDB

Design
Analytical tools and results must be designed to effectively communicate information to end users.
Project Management

Analytical
Ability to synthesize complex or diverse information, use intuition and experience to improve work flow processes and procedures.
