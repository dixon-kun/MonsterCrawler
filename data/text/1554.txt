        The Rev+ team is looking for a talented and passionate Technical Product Manager (TPM) to lead delivery for its product development teams based in Chicago. The team is responsible for Expedia's Rev+ revenue management application within Expedia Partner Central, our platform that supports our global hotel partners. On this team, you will utilize your critical thinking and technical skills to help directly drive Expedia's success.
        Your responsibility will be aligning business and technical components to ensure delivery of Rev+ features. You will work directly with a talented team of product owners, developers, testers, and data scientists who are responsible for building the products and architecture to communicate this information across the Expedia landscape. The ideal candidate will be passionate about technology, travel, hotels, revenue management, and will have strong technical skills to understand and review the team's roadmap and implementations. You will guide implementations, and take end-to-end ownership of systems in coordination with your peers in the Engineering and Product groups.
        Functions and Responsibilities:
        * Take ownership and advocate Agile methodologies while driving new feature development through every stage of the product lifecycle
        * Establish processes, tools, and metrics for product development
        * Drive planning and facilitate roadmap alignment with the product owner and key partners
        * Work with business partners to balance business and technical objectives that are conveyed as clear, stable priorities for the team
        * Turn high-level project objectives into a comprehensive set of user stories by leading intensive, multi-discipline, collaboration sessions
        * Manage delivery of a high-quality product that is stable in production and satisfies scalability, reliability, and performance goals by working with engineers, data scientists and testers
        * Understand and actively advance best practices within a strongly Agile environment such as Continuous Delivery &amp; Test automation
        * Actively contribute to our team's culture of embracing change, delivering quality products, and having fun together
        Requirements / Qualifications:
        * Familiarity with frontend and backend development and an understanding of typical cloud architecture
        * Experience moving business requirements into clearly defined user stories
        * High degree of competency with project management including: project planning, issue and risk identification and management, tracking dependencies and milestones, and communication of overall project status across a global organization
        * Proven ability to work with cross functional teams to drive the implementation of new features as well as resolve operational issues
        * Experience with the entire SDLC and shipping of software using Agile methodologies &#8211; Engineering experience strongly preferred
        * Self-starter, accustomed to working in a changing environment, who enjoys and takes pride in technical solution delivery
        * Ability to creatively solve challenging business and technology problems that impact our users
        * Excellent verbal and written communication skills including presentation and listening skills with the ability to present complex technical information clearly to varying audiences
        * Technical degree with overall 2+ years of IT experience within the web development arena &amp; 1+ recent years in a technical program or product management role
        Education:
        * Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Physical Sciences, Engineering, Information Technology, or a related field
