        Crop Modeler
        Who We Are
        Our mission is to invent the future of farming. We're transforming the oldest and most important industry in the world from "gut instinct" to data science. Our platform already exposes critical operational data insights to 1 out of every 3 farms in the U.S. and our users absolutely love us.
        Who You Are
        You love crop science and want to have an impact on the world. You have a strong background in crop modeling techniques for row crops, but can think out of the box to apply that knowledge to real fields. The thought of your work scaling to thousands of farms motivates you. You want to see your ideas put into action in a way that can transform the agricultural industry for years to come. You are passionate about making a difference for farmers and the worldwide issue of food security. You are constantly looking for ways to improve yourself and your work and you are not afraid of taking risks and approaching things differently. You love data and statistics. You appreciate and understand the startup culture and come from a farming background.
        About the Role
        As the first dedicated Crop Modeler on our Research team, you will own the development of a flexible crop model. Using broad datasets like weather, satellite imagery, and soil data, you will help provide our growers with insight into the variability in their fields. You will integrate the model into our existing tools, translate product vision into specific model features, and teach the rest of the company how it works.
        Tasks You'll Be Juggling
        * Apply crop models at subfield and regional scale, integrating data science and modeling techniques
        * Build models based on remotely sensed data and soil surveys, with limited ground truthing data
        * Improve crop model predictions via data assimilation and optimization techniques
        * Develop methods for incorporating individual simulation models into a larger, multi-model predictive analytics framework
        * Understand and interpret crop model performance through rigorous sensitivity and uncertainty analysis
        * Refine existing crop models at the process-level (e.g. crop growth and stress, nutrient, soil hydrology) by implementing new algorithms
        * Work as a member of our research team to develop agricultural decision support, crop monitoring and land management tools
        * Serve as the in-house expert on our crop model, sharing your expertise with multiple teams across the company as needed
        Your Creds
        Required:
        * Agricultural background
        * PhD, Masters or equivalent in statistics or modelling
        * Experience with soil surveys, soil sampling, and field scale nutrient modelling on row crops
        * Familiarity with modeling at sub-field scale, but also across regions of the country
        * Background in geostatistics or remote sensing
        * Experience in computational statistics and building crop models from scratch
        * Programming experience with software such as Python
        * Excellent organizational, verbal and written communications skills
        Nice To Have:
        * Working experience or knowledge of practices in the corn belt or wheat belt
        * GIS experience
