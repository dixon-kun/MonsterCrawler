Job Title: Data Scientist

Location: Springfield, VA

Job Description:

ALEX - Alternative Experts is seeking various junior to mid-level Data Scientists to provide statistical and mathematical support in analyzing and interpreting a wide range of data.  Specific duties include but are not limited to:

  • Leveraging a range of data sources including web traffic, requests for information, surveys, and production databases
  • Providing insights that will be used to inform strategy and decisions
  • Working with teams examining website design, customer interactions, and future products and services
  • Working with stakeholders to develop business questions and identify the data necessary to analyze to answer them
  • Collaborating across a diverse team of statisticians and data analysts
  • Utilizing industry standard tools to visualize and communicate complex information and analysis to senior executives
  • Using data mining for structured and unstructured information to join and explore relationships in disparate data sets
  • Applying analytics to derive insights from a combination of unstructured, semi-structured, and structured data sets
  • Manipulating data between applications using appropriate software (Apache Spark, Elastic Search, Python, Kibana…)
  • Applying business analytics and customer segmentation techniques into corporate functions (marketing analytics, consumer analytics, operations analysis, human capital analytics, or other relevant fields)

Experience Required :

  • 1-6 years of experience as a Data Scientist
  • Familiarity with data mining, and ability to develop and modify databases
  • Experience using statistical software such as SPSS, SAS, or MatLab
  • Experience working with Microsoft Windows operating systems
  • Experience using Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Outlook, Excel, and Access)
  • Experience with software packages used for advanced statistical analysis of operational data
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Good interpersonal skills with ability to work cooperatively and effectively as a team member

Mid-Level Requirements :

  • Ability to develop mathematical algorithms and applications in response to complex requirements
  • Ability to identify system enhancements and new capabilities, concept demonstrators, and capability business cases
  • Ability to provide training as new systems and capabilities move into operational use
  • Ability to take leadership roles within technical teams or consortiums
  • Background working with tools for data visualization
  • Strength in collaboration and teamwork within, between, and across organizational boundaries

Education Required :

  • Bachelor’s Degree or 4 years of additional experience
