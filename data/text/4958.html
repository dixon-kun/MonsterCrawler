Description

Newell Brands is a leading global consumer goods company with a strong portfolio of well-known brands, including Paper Mate, Sharpie, EXPO, Parker, Elmers, Calphalon, Rubbermaid, Graco, Baby Jogger, Aprica, Goody, Irwin, Lenox, Rubbermaid Commercial Products, Coleman, First Alert, FoodSaver, Jostens, K2, NUK, Oster, Rawlings, Sunbeam and Yankee Candle. Driven by a sharp focus on the consumer, leading investment in innovation and brands, and a performance-driven culture, Newell Brands helps consumers achieve more where they live, learn, work and play. Newell Brands and its subsidiaries are Equal Opportunity Employers and comply with applicable employment laws. EOE/M/F/Vet/Disabled are encouraged to apply.



Serves as the consumer’s advocate. Accountable for mining consumer and campaign data and deriving insights that identify opportunities for CRM activity across all brands. CRM Analytics Managers work with the CRM team, brand teams and cross-functional partners to align strategic objectives and provide actionable insights that enable data-informed decision-making for campaign development/refinement, segmentation and performance analysis – sometimes with the assistance of direct reports.

Job Responsibilities:

CRM Strategy & Execution
• Able to gain insight into target brand consumers and global positioning in order to recommend CRM initiatives that align to these.
• Understands consumer journeys and how to leverage data to create dialogues and nurture relationships over time.
• Understands how to analyze and derive insights from expressed and implied brand preferences, lifestyle interests, and communication preferences – aggregate, cluster and individual subscriber levels.
• Understands business rules to define contact strategies and prioritization of communications across multiple brands.
• Experienced deriving insights from data sets across channels (email, social, web, mobile, text, etc) and comfortable recommending how to activate insights.
• Understands the platform database structure (enterprise, business units, data extensions, suppressions, etc).
• Understands universal control groups – business rules and how to measure differential performance over time.
• Understands engagement and success metrics across channels – tracks trends and recommends campaign/model refinements.
• Understands tagging and tracking campaign activity and attribution across channels.
• Experienced identifying data-driven, statistically-significant opportunities for campaign development.
• Defines a process for maintaining active data collection points, retiring fields that are no longer actionable, and refreshing actionable opportunities on a regular schedule.
• Defines a test and learn agenda across all brands – tracks performance and recommendations following each test.
• Demonstrated experience with data science models and algorithms, especially applied to CRM/Consumer data.
• Experienced with business intelligence and data mining tools (ex: Qlik, Tableau, SAS, R, Power BI, etc.).
• Experienced building presentations for leadership that pulls a concise and compelling story out of the data to articulate the key analysis of a strategy, project or test.
• Offers creative solutions to project challenges and manages issue resolution.

Activation
• Understands the brand planning process to help brands integrate CRM initiatives within their integrated marketing plan and programming.
• Works with the CRM team to package data-informed recommendations to brand teams.
• Lives daily in the dashboards to assess campaign performance, capture lessons learned, recommend campaign pivots, and identify opportunities for developing new campaigns.
• Maintains audit of all data collection points, how the data is actionable, when statistical significant volumes have been reached, what initiative(s) were activated, and how the initiative(s) performed.
• Assesses, recommends, implements and tracks test and learn opportunities.
• Delivers campaign reports within a week of deployment with key learnings and recommendations outlined.
• Packages key insights and dashboard summaries for executive review and consumption on a regular schedule.
• Participates in briefing agencies on objectives. Works with agencies, contractors, supplier partners and brand teams to monitor execution of specific tactics.

Insights
• Applies understanding of the consumer and the brand to both short and long-term decisions. Links independent insight and observations to form a holistic understanding of the consumer. Translates marketplace insights into winning CRM strategies and tactics for the brand.
• Identifies gaps in organizational understanding and educates others on CRM analytics best practices.
• Informs marketing metrics, key brand, and quality measures to track and understand the underlying drivers and interaction of each metric.
• Completes CRM ROI analysis on projects and recommends changes to strategy as needed.
• Develops deep knowledge across multiple divisions and brands.

People Development
• Sets clear objectives and prioritizes initiatives for self and direct reports, if applicable. Assigns clear projects and tasks to direct reports and coaches and develops their skills. Performs semi-annual performance reviews for team members.
• Collaborates with the CRM team, brand, cross-functional and agency partners to share CRM analytics best practices.




Qualfications

·   Four-year college degree, MBA preferred


·   4-6 years of equivalent marketing experience


·   15% travel required


·   Has worked on a business with global or international exposure.  Demonstrates an understanding of differences between countries and cultures.


·   Experience

  • Translating marketing objectives into CRM strategies and plans.  Proven success in executing a comprehensive CRM marketing plan that is integrated across multiple consumer touch points.
  • Understands and has led CRM initiatives from either the agency or the client side.
  • Is knowledgeable about CRM trends and innovations and understands how to implement them to meet a specific objective.
  • Demonstrated success working in a matrix environment.
  • Strong communication skills, written and oral.
  • Proven ability to work on and lead a cross-functional team.

·   Fluent in English.
