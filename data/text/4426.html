Aberdeen Group ( www.aberdeenservices.com ) is a is a recognized, established and trusted brand and leading provider of marketing data products and services to the technology and telecommunications industries in North America, Europe and Latin America. Clients use our research data, analytics and content to drive a variety of sales and marketing programs including telemarketing, direct mail, e-Mail marketing, market segmentation, territory planning, and more. Our clients succeed with our information because it shortens their sales cycles and improves the effectiveness of their marketing campaigns. We have the only integrated data science and content solution of its kind. And we have the people, the expertise and the success stories to back it up. In this world, there are leaders and followers, and our global Aberdeen Group team are definitely leaders.

About the Opportunity:

Aberdeen Group is currently searching for aspiring market/industry analysts, content developers and/or technology specialists to join our Research team as a Market Research Associate.  This is a unique opportunity that will enable individuals to expand their understanding of the business landscape, hone their market analysis skills, and expand their ability to communicate emerging industry intelligence to the business community. The Market Research associate will learn how to develop user driven research emerging in critical areas of the business world such as: analytics and business intelligence, information technology, supply chain management, financial management and GRC, and, human capital management. To see a full list of our business practices, go to: http://www.aberdeen.com/_aberdeen/-/-/-/-/search.aspx This position is a springboard opportunity for a career in market research and content creation and is poised for growth within Aberdeen Group.


Your role as Research Associate will include:

  • Track and monitor emerging trends and technologies related to a specific research group
  • Conduct market research on technology trends and business processes (surveys, first person interviews)
  • Analyze research findings and draw inferences and conclusions in response to the research query
  • Write cohesive research that illustrates the learnings, and build content that educates the community
  • Present your findings (by phone and in-person) with technology end-users, software vendors and the marketplace
  • Build a vendor/market landscape to help guide you research editorial plans
  • Manage and improve research data collection and analysis processes
  • Conduct statistical analysis from data collected

  • Highly motivated and energetic
  • 3-5 years of practical experience
  • Conversant in Excel and comfortable with statistical analysis
  • Bachelor's degree is required; Master’s degree/MBA strongly preferred
  • Excellent writing and verbal communication skills
  • Strong critical thinking, problem solving, and analytical skills.
  • Experience in an extremely fast paced work environment preferably in research, consulting or marketing/strategy
  • An interest in complex research and analysis and an eye for creating interesting stories illustrated by data
  • Strong interest in emerging technology solutions and an eagerness to learn more
  • Very proficient in MS Office (especially Word and Excel);
  • Ability to use online and other secondary research materials quickly and effectively.
  • Ability to work independently; self-motivated
  • Desire to be responsible for own success, entrepreneurial spirit is very welcome
