Why Is This Role Critical?

One size does not fit all.  Marketing that hits its target spot-on is not only the most efficient and effective, but the most admired.  To consistently deliver messages that resonate and drive action, one needs to be an expert on the audience and their buying behavior.  Who are they? What tools do they need to do their jobs better? What media do they consume? What topics keep them up at night?  A segment leader is ultimately responsible for their audience.  He/she needs to understand the target better than anyone else—based on market research, industry analysis, assessment of past campaigns, competitive offerings and product/service capability—and transfer that knowledge to the rest of the organization.  A tailored marketing approach is new to naviHealth—and an opportunity to raise the bar and achieve never-before-seen results.

What you will be accountable for….

This role will report to the Director, Segment Marketing and work closely with Sales, Account Management, Data Sciences and Product to understand solutions being delivered to the Health Plan/Payor market and serve as an evangelist for those solutions.  Given the long buying timeline and high value of these potential contracts, communications plans created will include establishing the right combination of high-touch, relationship-building initiatives and solutions messaging.   He or she also works with Awareness, Thought Leadership, PR, and Business Development teams to monitor success/failure of those acquisition strategies in the market.

  • Master deep understanding of target market—Health Plans and Payor prospects.  Be the expert for industry trends, business drivers and competitive forces.
  • Set up appropriate market research and competitive analysis tools (internally & externally, including shadow rides with Sales/AM teams).
  • Identify hyper-focused, micro-segmentation opportunities and tactics.
  • Build out tailored go-to-market plans and sales support materials, including compelling content angles.
  • Attend and manage key industry events, trade shows and speaking opportunities.
  • Partner with Sales & Product to fully understand internal needs & gaps.
  • Maintain understanding of how effective positioning and campaigns are in the market including win/loss analysis. Optimize initiatives based on learnings.

What you will need to be successful in this role… ​ ​

  • BS required
  • Well-organized, results-oriented self-starter
  • Creative & not afraid to take risks, try new things
  • Comfortable with fast-paced environment
  • Background understanding of sophisticated target marketing/high-touch B2B experience
  • Manage multiple go-to-market initiatives concurrently while maintaining on-going knowledge of current market offerings
  • Ability to segment and align messaging
  • Desire to establish relationships with cross-functional teams, internally & externally
  • Good communicator with the ability to communicate and present to executives, customers, prospects, and other team members.  Very detail-oriented.

Travel Requirements:  50% (national)

  • In-field time to better understand our various customer/prospect segments.
  • Presence at various events/trade shows/speaking opportunities.
  • Time spent at Newton, MA office with rest of Marketing team.
