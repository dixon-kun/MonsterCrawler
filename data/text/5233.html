OVERVIEW:

We’re looking for a Content Marketing Manager to join our rapidly growing marketing team.

The Content Marketing Manager is an out-of-the-box thinker with a background in content development and distribution. This individual will work as part of the marketing team to conceptualize and create content to fuse new value into all stages of the customer journey. The role requires both a creative and sales-focused mind, and offers the opportunity to lead major marketing and content initiatives from conception to promotion.

As a Content Marketing Manager, you will be exposed to all facets of the business and be encouraged to learn and develop skills both within and beyond your role. You’ll be working in one of the most collaborative offices in Center City Philly, developing and working with machine learning technology, and surrounded by passionate, high-performing individuals.

QUALIFICATIONS:

The Content Marketing Manager candidate is an intelligent, motivated person who thinks differently. The candidate should have:

  • 4+ years experience in content marketing with a strategic approach to content creation and content distribution across relevant channels
  • Portfolio illustrating varying content marketing campaigns for different audiences (preferably B2B)
  • Knowledge of the inbound and/or account-based marketing methodology, and how to develop content that aligns to it
  • Fluency in the retention marketing methodology, and how to align content to it
  • Exceptional writing, editing, and proofreading skills
  • Knowledge of how to spark and fuel ongoing engagement around content
  • Keen ability for finding the story and the news
  • Strong understanding of how copy and design coexist, and the ability to craft copy that can be visualized well
  • Strategic eye for SEO and crafting content for both readers and search engines
  • Impeccable attention to detail and strong follow-through; you’re o rganized, self-motivated, and able to balance priorities in a fast-paced environment
  • High energy to learn and grow from a group of marketing and creative pros, while also providing guidance and recommendations back to the team
  • Hands-on experience with WordPress preferred
  • Knowledge of AP Style a plus
  • Experience in retail, e-commerce, advertising, and technology welcomed
  • BA/BS in journalism, communications, marketing, or English; or relevant experience in similar fields

RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Act as a primary developer of marketing content for Sidecar, supporting sales enablement, product communications, customer retention, and thought leadership goals with creative, mixed-media assets
  • Develop and execute successful content distribution plans using owned, earned, and paid media channels to drive downloads and sign-ups
  • Identify campaign themes and build integrated content bundles to generate and nurture leads, educate prospects, engage customers, promote Sidecar’s technology, convey competitive differentiation, and increase brand awareness
  • Conceptualize and create content for all stages of the customer journey, working across teams to produce assets such as e-books, blog posts, emails, one-pagers, infographics, sales collateral, etc.
  • Collaborate with the creative team to provide ideas and input on design, layout, and imagery to help bring content to life
  • Play a central role in managing and executing blog content
  • Contribute to the innovativeness of the marketing team, devising new and successful ways to use content marketing to help meet organizational goals

WHO WE ARE:

Sidecar built a technology from the ground up that’s dedicated to one mission: Tackle the challenges retailers face in data-driven e-commerce channels. B ased on advanced machine learning algorithms and deep data science expertise, our technology digests and analyzes massive, real-time datasets, allowing it to spot trends and continually improve campaigns for Google Shopping, Facebook dynamic ads, and Bing Shopping.

These channels are changing every day—and have become too complex and competitive to manage manually or with outdated technologies. They require automated, sophisticated, and ever-evolving technology to stay ahead. That’s what Sidecar is all about. We’re proud to work with retailers of all sizes and verticals in the U.S. and abroad, including Under Armour, Crate & Barrel, and Boscov’s, to name a few.

As a company, we’re a group of energetic people who work hard to make a lasting impact in the rapidly evolving world of e-commerce marketing. And our work is paying off—we are quickly becoming a tech leader and the go-to partner for e-commerce marketing pros.


Our open-floor work environment and Slack -enabled team encourages collaboration, creativity, and growth as individuals and as a company. Many of us come from e-commerce, statistical, or start-up backgrounds, but we certainly welcome talent with outside perspective and experience. We believe in regular company social events inspire friendships and strengthen our bond.
