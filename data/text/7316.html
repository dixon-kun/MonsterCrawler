Business Analyst

Key Role:
Work closely with clients to identify user business requirements, lead support of in-house corporate systems business requirements, as required, interpret business needs and translate them into system requirements, and write business specifications and forward them to technical staff for system development. Act as the conduit between a user group and an IT development team. Work to be viewed as a consultant with capability knowledge who excels at client delivery, having developed expertise in a specific area. Take a leadership role in generating intellectual capital to help the firm grow its business. Develop relationships with clients and leverage knowledge and trusted advice, including interacting directly with senior-level clients. Develop competency in project and financial management and be involved in managing teams and formulating business strategy. Guide the progress of projects while mentoring junior staff in both firm and technical competencies.

Basic Qualifications:
-3+ years of experience with requirements gathering and documentation
-Experience with intelligence analysis
-Ability to quickly learn new systems and functional pieces
-TS/SCI clearance

Additional Qualifications:
-Experience with data science, business intelligence, knowledge management, devops, and continuous integration preferred
-Experience with the universal de-identification platform (UDiP) and natural processing language (NLP) preferred
-Experience in researching, raising questions, and working with technical SMEs and vendors to seek best practices and solid technical decisions
-Experience with Microsoft Office
-Experience with conducting end user training
-Knowledge of a variety of Web-based systems and portals and the overall systems development lifecycle (SDLC), including the federal sector
-Knowledge of the information flows and process architecture necessary for implementing solutions
-Ability to quickly comprehend and gain technical awareness for the end-to-end solution and quickly ramp-up on tools and technologies
-Possession of excellent oral and written communication skills, including interpersonal expertise and flexibility
-Possession of excellent analytical and solution-oriented thinking skills
-BA or BS degree in a related field

Clearance:
Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information; TS/SCI clearance is required.
