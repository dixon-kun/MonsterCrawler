        Analytic Solutions New Business Leader
        Location: Morris Plains, NJ,
        Do you passionately pursue the stories hidden in a large repository of data, understand the power of analytical models and the power of insight? Do you relish working with customers to identify their unsatisfied or unmet business needs and working through all available data and analytical models to search for answers? Do you enjoy telling a meaningful story with data driven insights? If your answer is yes, Honeywell has an intriguing opportunity for you an analytics solutions product manager.
        You will be responsible for working within Honeywell to identify opportunities for new growth and efficiency based on data analysis. The focus will be on the Performance Materials and Technologies business unit. This product role will work closely with experts in the business unit, data scientists and solution architects to solve client unmet needs through data modeling and delivering insights by integrating results into operational platforms or creating new insight solutions.
        You will be a member of a team that delivers contemporary analytics solutions for all Honeywell business groups and functions. You will be expected to actively participate in defining and governing our analytics strategy for Honeywell, as well as begin a strong leader in executing data science driven projects as we build out capabilities and our architecture. You will report to the Data Science Leader in Honeywell's Business Intelligence and Analytics organization.
        Honeywell International is a $40 billion diversified Fortune 100 leader with more than 131,000 employees in 100 countries around the world. We invent and manufacture technologies that address some of the world's toughest challenges linked to global macro trends like energy efficiency, clean energy generation, safety and security, globalization and customer productivity. We are building a world that's safer and more secure, more comfortable and energy efficient, more innovative and productive. For more information, please visit Honeywell.com or follow us @HoneywellNow.

Drive Honeywell's engagement with research groups at top-tier universities (e.g. Carnegie Mellon University), government (e.g. National Renewable Energy Laboratory) as well as industry players both large (e.g. industrial consortia) and small (e.g. startups and incubators).

        * Work with data scientists to provide data modeling, mining, pattern analysis, data visualization and machine learning solutions to address customer needs
        * Work with solution architects to create detail requirements and the development and assurance teams to deliver on the client delivery solution.
        * Communicate project output in terms of customer value, business objectives, and product opportunity
