JOB SUMMARY

The Sr. Business Intelligence Analyst will be responsible for developing, enhancing, and overseeing all functions related to Risk management analytics & card brand risk programs. This role will lend assistance to management in creating operating mechanisms that aid the Risk team in making critical loss prevention decisions with respect to specific merchant level risk, as well as overall portfolio risk. The optimal candidate should possess the ability to influence organizational change based on comprehensive findings from complex data sets.

DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

* Develops processes that support continuous monitoring of merchant portfolio to identify gaps in un-known exposure

* Conducts policy driven processes that expose portfolio level merchant risk and identify required evaluations

* Conducts periodic financial reviews on specific subsets of populations within the merchant portfolio

* Completes ad-hoc exposure evaluations from Risk staff when required

* Maintains a constant state of awareness with respect to economic conditions and develops predictive tools that aid in identifying areas that require increased focus

* Assists in developing effective loss prevention and mitigation tools that leverage historical experiences, economic conditions, and social media platforms

* Critically evaluates post mortem reviews and suggests attribute changes required to mitigate future organizational loss

* Collaborates with internal business analysts and outside vendors to implement system/process improvements

* Evaluates card brand specific reporting which emphasizes at risk program merchants

* Collaborates with merchants and/or sales offices on card band violations to identify root cause and draft effective remediation plans

* Drafts required responses to Visa & MasterCard that outline findings, metrics, and action plans that will lead to successful remediation outcomes.

* Monitors progress against communicated remediation plan and ensures merchant is compliant with card brand risk programs

* Updates internal & board reported metrics in an effort to capture all production activities within the Risk Operations team

* Prepares and presents data findings to Sr. Management and/or Risk staff

* Provide guidance & support to Risk staff by leveraging industry specific experience

* Assists in developing & distributing training material when required

* Perform other duties as assigned

SKILLS REQUIREMENTS

* Previous experience in the capacity of a risk management function for acquiring, issuing, and/or card brand risk compliance preferred

* Highly proficient with all Office products, with an emphasis on advanced Excel skills

* Experience in SQL, Oracle, Tableau, Business Objects, Qlik, and/or other business analytics software is required

* Previous exposure to evaluating personal & commercial credit profiles is required

* Prior history of evaluating financial strength of small/medium/large businesses is preferred

* Extensive knowledge of processing platforms such as TSYS, FDR preferred

* Experience incorporating aspects of social media into risk scoring and evaluation processes is preferred

* Deep understanding of risk assessment procedures with excellent judgment and decision making skills is required

* Ability to effectively articulate complex data in simplified form to executive team is required

* General understanding of BSA/AML requirements is preferred

* Regular and predictable attendance is required

* Maintains an adaptive mindset which accepts and acts upon constructive feedback

* Professional and effective written and oral communication skills with external partners, customers, management, and fellow employees

* Passion for recognizing irregularities in large data patterns and developing actionable tasks aimed at preventing loss

* Highly organized with advanced analytical skills

* Ability to effectivity organize and drive to completion competing priorities by the allotted deadlines

* Expectation of integrity & confidentiality in all interactions

EDUCATIONAL REQUIREMENTS

* High School Diploma or equivalent required

* Bachelor's Degree or equivalent from an accredited college with emphasis in business, finance, statistics, or similar fields related to data science; or 4 years' experience in a related field, or a combination of work experience and education required

* ETA CPP credential preferred

WORKING CONDITIONS

* Must be flexible and willing to work outside of standard business hours, including some holidays

* Moderate noise / office atmosphere

* Occasional weekend (on-call) and evening work, if required

* Fast paced ever-changing atmosphere

We offer highly competitive salary, benefits and more. If you are interested and meet these requirements we would love to hear from you!
