NEXON M is a mobile game publisher based in Emeryville. Founded in 2013, NEXON M focuses on free-to-play games for mobile and counts some of the world’s best developers as its partners, including Big Huge Games, maker of the top-grossing game DomiNations; Shiver Entertainment; and Particle City. NEXON M’s focus on free-to-play games for mobile can be traced to our roots as a subsidiary of NEXON Corp. As Korea’s largest game company, NEXON is known for having created the first free-to-play games in the early 2000s and has multiple games which recently passed their 10-year anniversaries of live operation. The Data Analyst is responsible for compiling raw game data into usable views and reports for both game developers and stakeholders within the company, as well as generating novel insights on game performance, user acquisition, and user experience. JOB RESPONSIBILITIES • Create and maintain scripts and code to continually process raw data for multiple products into aggregated views for technical end users. • Prepare data visualizations, reports, and high-level summaries for producers, product managers, executives, external developers, and other non-technical end users based on both raw data and aggregate results. • Test and validate instrumentation, aggregations, and automated reports — identify and resolve any gaps or errors. • Identify the weakest areas of game performance and user experience, and work with producers and product managers to create data-driven solutions. • Create novel analytics tools to meet evolving needs within the company. • Familiarize external developers with existing analytics infrastructure, and assist external developers with analytics instrumentation. JOB QUALIFICATIONS Work Experience • 3+ years of analytical experience with RDMS data sources, queries, and report generation • 2+ years of mobile experience, including multiple title launches • Product management experience on a live title Education, Professional Training, Technical Training or Certification • Bachelor’s degree in related field or equivalent experience Knowledge/Skills • Proficient with PostgreSQL, Excel, R, Tableau, or equivalent data analysis and data visualization tools • Functional knowledge of basic statistical methods • Excellent time and project management skills • Very detail oriented • Strong interpersonal and communication skills • Good understanding of mobile gaming mechanics • Working understanding of mobile gaming market, including user acquisition • Able to manage shifting priorities in a fast paced environment • Experience with cloud-based computing (especially Amazon Web Services), procedural programming (especially PHP or Python), EMR frameworks such as Spark or Hadoop, and data science and machine learning methods such as k-means clustering, random forest, decision tress, etc. are all a plus This is a full-time position with NEXON M (nexonm.com). Our office is located in Emeryville—the Emery-Go-Round stops right outside. A casual, friendly work environment, comprehensive benefits package, competitive salary, and a great opportunity for career growth and development are all part of what makes Nexon M a great place to work.
