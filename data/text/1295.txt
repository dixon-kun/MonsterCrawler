        Metro Transit is seeking to fill two (2) full time positions for a full time Data Scientist. The position may be filled at any one of the following three (3) levels outlined below. This position will be located in Minneapolis, Minnesota.
        Senior Data Scientist (Full Salary Range: $66,747.00 to $94,702.00 Annual) &#8211; As a Senior Data Scientist work assignments are more complex. Advanced and lead professional research and data science work is expected. This level of work is distinguished from the other two junior levels.
        Data Scientist (Full Salary Range: $61,734.00 to $87,589.00 Annual) - At this level you will be expected to perform professional level of research and data science work.
        Associate Data Scientist (Full Salary Range: $57,117.00 to $81,016.00 Annual) &#8211; As an aspiring data scientist this is an entry level position with directed professional research and data science work.
        In any and all of the levels you will apply advanced data manipulation and analytical methods, including advanced machine learning and statistical models, in support of data-driven decision making efforts in all Metro Transit functional areas. Additionally, you will support effective use and communication of work by designing and developing highly visual, interactive and accessible reports and tools for agency staff.
        To find out more about the Metropolitan Council and our great benefits, please click here:
        http://metrocouncil.org/About-Us/The-Council-Who-We-Are.aspx.
        Metro Transit is a division of the Metropolitan Council. It is the largest provider of transit service in the seven-county metropolitan area and one of the country's largest transit systems. The agency operates bus and rail service essential to the region's mobility, and also provides resources for carpooling, van pooling and bike commuting. Metro Transit helps people get to where they're going, reduces regional traffic congestion and promotes a cleaner environment.
        Examples of Duties:
        1. Perform full performance statistical and machine learning modeling and analysis to guide Metro Transit in all functional areas:
        * Design and implement machine learning algorithms (e.g. random forest and other tree-based methods, support vector machines, etc.) along with traditional statistical models (e.g. GLMs)
        * Apply time-series methods (e.g. ARIMA, exponential smoothing, etc.) in support of ridership forecasting and modeling
        * Use clustering methods (e.g. k-means, hierarchical, etc.) in support of survey analysis for market segmentation, customer satisfaction analysis, etc.
        * Use logistic regression, survival methods, etc. in support of predictive maintenance planning
        2. Perform lead and advanced data analysis research
        * Review scholarly articles and technical papers in academic and industry databases/journals
        * Coordinate transit research projects with University researchers
        * Write literature reviews and papers for internal and external publication
        3. Perform lead and advanced development of interactive data visualization tools
        * Use R Shiny, D3, etc. to develop interactive data visualization and modeling tools in support of analysis of on-time performance, travel time, ridership, etc. to support service planning and delivery, customer information, etc.
        * Meet with internal and external stakeholders to develop and define requirements. Iteratively develop tools.
        4. Perform full performance design and evaluate systems for data capture:
        * Collect, clean, and prepare data for analysis
        * Create and maintain databases and spreadsheets
        * Organize data and results of analytics projects
        * Design, implement and utilize advanced data management systems and cluster computing environments to facilitate complex models on very large data sets.
        5. Provide limited work direction to Associate Data Scientists and interns.
        * Evaluate the work of Associate Data Scientists, Data Scientists, and interns.
        * Provide limited mentoring, coaching, and guidance to Associate Data Scientists, Data Scientists, and interns.
