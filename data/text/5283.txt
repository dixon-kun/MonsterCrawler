         Purpose:
        The Data Analyst Senior II, Data Analytics &amp; Fraud Forensics reports to the Manager, Data Analytics &amp; Fraud Forensics. The Senior II's primary responsibility is to execute high quality data analytics projects in support of Internal Audit, Compliance, and Fraud Team engagements, in a timely manner and in accordance with professional standards and occasionally overseeing more junior staff. Accountable for departmental data analytics and fraud investigation tools, the Data Analytics &amp; Fraud Forensics team is responsible for leveraging organizational data for analysis, monitoring, investigation, and planning activities. The Senior II is accountable for producing workpapers and deliverables (such as reports and data visualizations) that are professionally written for an executive audience and that reflect an understanding of company objectives and capabilities, control requirements, and industry best practices. The position is responsible for maintaining connections to, and proficiency in, established organizational data repositories to facilitate data analytics work. The Senior II is also responsible for leading various computer forensic and investigative activities in support of Fraud Team investigations and coordination with technical resources in the Information Security and Information Services departments.  The Senior II is also responsible for assisting the manager with training the staff in data analytics tools and techniques. Senior II is able to evidence leadership capabilities to facilitate change in a constructive and collaborative manner. This position will be expected to lead complete projects including meetings with departmental and external management.
