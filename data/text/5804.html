Description
LIFE. POWERED BY YOU.
Are you looking to make a difference in your career? We’re working on smarter grids, cleaner energy and tools to help people manage energy more efficiently.
Position Overview:
This position will be in the Human Resources division within Southern California Edison. This position will report to the Principal Manager, Strategy & Workforce Insights. The HR Workforce Analytics & Insights Senior Manager will manage a team of 5 and is responsible for the development and delivery of strategies, processes and capabilities for HR business intelligence, workforce analytics, human capital business insights and workforce planning to the enterprise. This leader will drive the workforce planning, budget planning, data analytics/insight strategies aligned with HR organizational goals, vision and people strategy.
The Senior HR Workforce Analytics & Insights Manager will engage with HR and business leaders to understand business and people strategies, leverage enterprise data investments and translate these needs into the mission & strategy of their team. He/she will lead a team of analytic professionals to deliver services on diagnostic analytics to address business identified talent issues; proactive analytics to independently identify workforce & talent opportunities for the business; predictive analytics to impact attraction, development & retention trends; and prescriptive analytics to build an ability to understand what is happening in our workforce, why it’s happening, what actions will improve or maintain it and the best approaches to achieve desired results.
Qualifications
Minimum Qualifications:
  • Must have a minimum of 5 years of experience supervising or managing an organization, unit or team.
  • Must have a minimum of 7 years of work experience in roles requiring quantitative analysis, reporting and data-driven decision making with a successful track record of effectively solving business challenges.
Desired Qualifications:
  • Bachelors in Business, Finance, Statistics, Human Resources, or related field or an equivalent combination of education, training, and experience.
  • MBA or Master’s Degree, Analytics, Data Science, or related field preferred.
  • SPHR Certificate, Project Management Certificate preferred.
  • Management of the enterprise Employee Engagement survey and Exit Survey functions.
  • Management of the Workforce Planning discipline across SCE, including driving company-wide workforce planning efforts such as workforce forecasting, providing modeling and decision support, and partnering with stakeholder groups (i.e., Staffing, Strategic Business Partners, IT, Operations Support, Controllers) to identify impacts of workforce planning efforts;
  • Manage external vendor relationships with engagement, analytics/workforce planning, HR effectiveness survey, and benchmarking vendors.
  • Demonstrated ability to build and strengthen data analytics capabilities.
  • Experience developing dashboards, monitoring workforce trends, and consulting with HR business partners and leaders to maintain a high quality workforce.
  • Demonstrated experience gathering, analyzing, interpreting, summarizing results, and presenting to business leaders to inform their decisions.
  • Proven business acumen, project management skills, leadership competencies, and a strong data analytic orientation.
  • Experience delivering data and insights to business leaders on focus areas such as attraction, engagement, development, retention, workforce planning and trends.
  • Ability to establish trust, credibility, and build strong partnerships, motivate others and self to drive results and achieve high standards for excellence and quality.
  • Demonstrated ability to provide practical, solution-driven outcomes and human resource processes that are perceived as quickly adding value to the business
  • Demonstrated demand management ability to manage multiple priorities and set effective systems in place to track and report on projects and work.
  • Demonstrated ability to integrate work across relevant areas, manage risks, develop and execute business plans, manage information, and provide exceptional service.
  • Demonstrated ability to stay current with relevant technology, innovation, and industry best practices.
  • Demonstrated experience working with HR systems, reporting & analytics processes, systems & tools.
  • Demonstrated knowledge of SAP Human Capital Management (HCM) modules and processes.
  • Demonstrated ability to follow safety protocols and safe work practices.
  • Demonstrated experience using Microsoft Office Suite (i.e., Word, Excel, and PowerPoint).
  • Must demonstrate strong ethics, influence and negotiation, leadership, interpersonal skills, communication, and the ability to engage in continuous learning.
