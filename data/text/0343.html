As part of Viacom’s Data Strategy team, you’ll dive deep into how we use data (big and small), machine learning and data science to power our research, advertising and content. Our fans consume media across a growing number of traditional and emerging platforms - as such, Viacom’s data ecosystem is now in the critical path for the current and future business. If you have an entrepreneurial spirit, know how to deliver, are innovative, and long for the opportunity to build technical solutions to challenging problems then we want you as a Technical Product Manager for Viacom’s Data Strategy team. Responsibilities include:

  • Product manage / own major parts of Viacom’s new data platforms
  • Become expert in our business metrics and KPIs. Steward insights and use of data
  • Become expert in the capabilities and limitations of our data platform, the data we manage, and the semantics of the data itself
  • Take ownership of our data ingest, management and visualization tools; drive their development and use them to effectively deliver meaningful business intelligence
  • Define and manage product roadmaps for key internal development teams and vendors
  • Work with cross functional teams to find and implement new views, products and partnerships
  • Write requirement documentation, design and deploy data dashboards that summarize and communicate opportunities across the business

  • 3-5 years of agile / lean product management experience
  • Ability to navigate a complex stakeholder matrix and manage multiple priorities
  • Familiarity with media (nielsen, adobe) and social (facebook, instagram, youtube) centric datasets
  • Experience with data aggregation and business intelligence toolsets like DOMO and Tableau
  • Familiarity with modern modeling, machine learning, NLP, regression and classification techniques
  • Deep knowledge of relational & non-relational databases and SQL

  • Outstanding interpersonal skills to build relationships with key technical and non-technical stakeholder
