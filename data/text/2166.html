Senior Manager, Sales Analytics

Location: Phoenix, AZ

As the Senior Manager of Sales Analytics, you would be responsible for delivering actionable insights to enable growth for Honeywell's Sales & Marketing teams. You would be working closely with the sales leadership & execution team to identify opportunities and develop business driven analyst roadmaps to enable their strategy. You will work with mix of open source and in-house developed tools but looking for you to help us define the best-of-breed tool set.

Key Responsibilities Include

* Work closely with the Sales & Marketing teams to provide strategic leadership in analytics applications. Collaboratively identify, prioritize & execute on opportunities for analytics to deliver business value.

* Transform data from various sources (sales, social, customer support, services, contracts etc.) into actionable insights for the sales team that drive business growth.

* Analyzing large data sets using open source tools such as R, Python, build models and derive actionable insights

* Speaking at conferences, writing and contributing to scientific papers, and encouraging others to do the same.

Experiences and Characteristics we are looking for include:

* Self-starter with the drive to unlock the true power of big, noisy, messy, real-world data

* You have extensive demonstrable experience in delivering business value through data analytics applications in sales & marketing domain.

* Experience with the development of machine learning algorithms and predictive models

* Experience in strategy, ability to engage with the business, provide thought leadership to identify opportunities with high ROI.

YOU MUST HAVE:

* Bachelor's degree in Predictive Analytics, Applied Mathematics, Applied Statistics, Computer Science, Actuarial Science, Business Analytics, Business Intelligence & Analytics, Economics, Marketing Analytics, Mathematics or related discipline

* Minimum 5 years of experience in Data Science or related role

WE VALUE:

* MBA with Technical Undergrad, Operations Research, Quantitative Finance, or equivalent.

* Master's or Ph.D. Degree in the above disciplines.

* Experience in Sales, Operations Planning, Finance, Pricing or Supply Chain analytics.

* Experience with SQL, Python, R, Minitab, Tableau, or similar tools.

* Business acumen, with ability to draw clear connections between data modeling activities and business challenges/opportunities
