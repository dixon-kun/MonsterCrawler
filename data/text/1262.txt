        Job Description
        Business Analytics Manager - Wayzata, MN-00PZ9
        Position Summary
        The Business Analytics Manager will manage, oversee, and act as the primary subject matter expert for the Retail Banking Business Analytics team. S/he will lead the business requirements gathering and solution proposals by partnering with Retail Banking executives and functional leaders; is accountable for providing leadership and direction to ensure the appropriate and consistent collection of data, documentation of information, and presentation of findings to drive product performance and operational support efficiencies. S/he will provide mentoring and guidance to staff.
        Major Areas of Accountability
        * Develop and implement BI tools and transfer knowledge. Drive creation and maintenance of BI analysis and reporting from project inception, technical design, development and testing
        * Collaborate on the development of multi-touch marketing strategies across targeted segments, assuring optimized marketing investment and performance across all channels, working closely with marketing, CRM, digital and social media teams
        * Build predictive models to help assess segment performance, and provide actionable insights for marketing and product teams; work with the data science team to productionalize those models
        * Collaborate with cross-functional teams to drive the adoption of data science decision-making
        * Spot trends in data, help fine-tune business strategies, marketing programs, and customer support efforts
        * Partner with Finance team to drive profitability analysis, extract actionable insights and make recommendations to business partners
        * Perform data extraction/data mining and associated statistical analysis
        * Prepare and present the strategic rationale for new business initiatives
        * Manages all activities of the department. Includes setting priorities, monitoring performance, evaluating and developing procedures and motivating staff to achieve an efficient, economical work environment.
        * Oversees personnel functions of supervisory or senior staff within the department, including staffing, promotional changes, training, performance evaluations, disciplinary action and other issues that arise.
        * Performs other job duties as required by manager/supervisor.
        Qualifications
        Minimum Qualifications
        Required:
        * Bachelor's Degree in Business, Accounting, Finance, or equivalent experience
        * Eight to ten years related work experience or six to eight years experience with advanced degree or professional certification (CFM, CMA, or CPA)
        * Advanced end user computer skills
        * Excellent verbal and written communication skills
        * Proven problem solving ability
        * Strong leadership and managerial experience
        * Proven experience in building complex financial models
        * Proven experience and skill in developing effective reports for senior management.
        * Strong ability to read and interpret technical research and reports and apply information to specific business situations
        * Advanced organization skills
        * Ability to work under deadlines
        Preferred:
        * Professional certification (CFM, CMA or CPA) or advanced degree
        * Financial Services
        Supervisory Responsibilities:
        * Manage a team of professional staff
