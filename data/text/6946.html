Position Summary

ISO Solutions, a unit of Verisk Analytics, is a leading provider of information and analytics to the insurance industry. We're seeking an Analytics Product Integration Manager to:

* Plan integration of Analytics Suites including modeled data and statistical data to develop techniques to add credibility to both complete market modeling and detailed statistical analysis.

* Partner across analytics teams to develop strategies to effectively combine information to optimize customer satisfaction. Help to develop joint roadmaps across Products. Understand product capabilities across multiple offerings and provide expertise in honing solutions to customer requirements

* Develop strategies for Peer Benchmarking both by client overall DWP and individual premiums (Small Commercial, Middle Market, Large Accounts)

* Lead development of mapping solution for NAICS/SIC to ISO Class Codes

* Partner with team to reduce latency of data.

Requirements

* Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Data Science or related discipline required

* 5 + years' experience with strong a record of building great internal or external customer relationships that focus upon gaining actionable insights and forming collaborative partnerships with the goal of innovation, product improvement or ease of use.

* Strong computer background which includes desktop applications such as MS Excel, MS Word, MS PPT, Knowledge of SQL and data manipulation required. Experience with one or more of the following is required SAS, SPSS, Tableau, Microstrategy

* Knowledge of economic & census data and P&C Insurance a significant plus.

* Experience in Benchmarking and Peer Group Analysis strongly preferred

* Experience with data analysis and working with combining multiple data sources, including third-party and publicly available data (e.g. Census data)

* Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau, Qlik, Spotfire strongly preferred

* Ability to work independently

* Will sponsor Actuarial Exam candidates

We offer an excellent compensation package. Our benefits package is competitive and includes full healthcare options, a 401(k) plan, and generous paid time off program.
