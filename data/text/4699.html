On the Job

  • Serves as subject matter resource in the field of security, developing and maintaining a strong understanding of the field and its related technologies.
  • Proactively develops and maintains knowledge of DataXu platform and related components, and makes recommendations to improve the reliability, performance, and security of these systems as appropriate.
  • Deep involvement in development, enforcement and maintenance of DataXu security controls, procedures, and standards.
  • Active participation in audits.
  • Participate in level of service standards and operating procedures for overall security system availability and individual system components
  • Utilizes various enterprise-class security solutions for vulnerability management, intrusion detection, security information and event management to protect DataXu & its customers’ data. Has the ability to quickly learn and leverage these solutions.
  • Perform security risk assessments to analyze DataXu systems and processes, identifying risks from both a technical and business perspective, and recommending mitigation strategies to address those risks to an acceptable level.
  • Collaborates with multiple technology and business groups.

About You .....

  • Bachelor’s degree or equivalent experience in a related technical field.
  • 4+  years of related Information Security related experience.
  • Demonstrate technical infrastructure knowledge, playing a vital role in design of production, staging, QA and development infrastructures running in a 24×7 environment
  • Experience in multiple large projects definition, selection, and implementation of security tools, technologies, and processes
  • Experience in global datacenter infrastructure and AWS
  • Working knowledge of Unix/Linux, Databases, Networking, Windows Domains, Active Directory, a plus.
  • Strong analytical, writing, and problem solving skills.
  • Strong interpersonal, communication, and presentation skills.
  • Ability to multi-task and shift priorities as circumstances warrant.
  • CISSP preferred.
