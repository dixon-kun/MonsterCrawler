        Exponent is a leading engineering and scientific consulting firm. Our multidisciplinary team of scientists, engineers, physicians, and regulatory consultants brings together more than 90 different disciplines to solve complicated problems facing corporations, insurers, government entities, associations and individuals.         Exponent's Biomechanics Practice is currently hiring for Managers/Senior Managers in our Chicago and LA offices.
        Responsibilities for this position include:
        * Managing and executing biomechanics projects related to human injury analysis
        * Attracting new clients, developing new business, leading projects, conducting research, using analytical tools, and synthesizing technical biomechanical issues involving kinematics, dynamics, mechanisms of injury, and human tolerance
        * Ability to develop additional expertise and gain additional industry exposure through our unique technical consulting services
        * Publishing papers in technical journals, and present findings at conferences
        * Recruiting and developing the careers of support staff
        * Actively participating in our Biomechanics Practice and assisting in marketing the practices technical capabilities to clients and the scientific community
        Qualifications for this position include:
        * Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering or a related engineering field
        * At least 7 years of professional experience conducting technical analyses concerning the causation of physical injuries resulting from incidents involving motor vehicles, industrial equipment, and/or consumer products
        * Must have developed client relationships and be a recognized expert in his/her field with a strong publication history
        * Outstanding verbal and written communication skills
        * Ability to manage a group of highly motivated professionals as well as attract projects to support full-time staff
