Job Description:

3M is seeking a Computer Vision Engineer for the Oral Care Solutions Division located in Maplewood, MN. At 3M, you can apply your talent in bold ways that matter. Here, you go.

Job Summary:

3M Oral Care is seeking a Lead 3D Computer Vision Engineer to develop 3D reconstruction algorithms and 3D data manipulation software.  This position will primarily include the design and development of medical imaging software and systems for 3D model creation and manipulation.  As a Technical Leader of an R&D product development team, you will be responsible for the technical strategy and architecture of newly emerging 3D software platforms and technical leadership working with technical business analysts and product managers to meet technical and business requirements.

Primary Responsibilities include but are not limited to the following:

  • Defining technical strategy and platform architecture
  • Leading and developing team members in the area of 3D data reconstruction and manipulation
  • Participating in computer vision and machine learning algorithms and applications development
  • Ensure quality of software developed meets or exceeds expectations
  • Work closely with designers and production staff to help define software and hardware requirements

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelors of Science Degree or higher in a science or engineering discipline from an accredited university
  • Experience developing computer vision algorithms and applications

Preferred Qualifications:

  • MS or PhD in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or Applied Mathematics from an accredited university.
  • Experience commercializing medical device software systems in imaging and diagnostics
  • Expertise in computer vision sub-disciplines such as:  image analysis (object recognition and feature detection), 3D computer vision (acquisition / 3D sensing, computational imaging, segmentation, registration), machine learning (feature extraction, classification, clustering), embedded and real-time computer vision, video understanding (action recognition, scene summarization), analysis and reduction of high-dimensional data.
  • Experience with 3D sensor data (ToF, Stereo, Structured Light, Lidars)
  • Experience in dealing with real world data in one or more of the following areas: computer vision, machine learning, data science, probabilistic inference, and/or computational statistics.
  • Experience with 3D visualization algorithms and computational approaches, e.g. meshing of point clouds, marching cubes algorithm variants, 3D geometry and texture rendering and shading.
  • Experience with regulatory processes, quality systems and FDA requirements.
  • Programming languages:  MATLAB, C, C++, Python, C#, .NET
  • Demonstrated scientific excellence as evidenced by publications and presentations

Location : 3M Center in Maplewood, MN

Travel: Minimal travel anticipated for this role

Relocation Benefits: Are not authorized for this position
