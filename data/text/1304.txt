        The Associate Director of Business Intelligence, Operations Analysis, leads a team of business analysts and consultants who serve as trusted advisors to Payment Integrity leadership. Your team is responsible for delivering accurate savings forecasts, daily/weekly/monthly operations performance analysis and insights into cycle times, bottlenecks, efficiency and yield, as well as supporting various initiatives to grow and optimize Payment Integrity revenues. Your team analyzes and interprets data and trends, creating visualizations to enable faster and more informed business decision making. You have a deep knowledge of the business ecosystem, processes and trends, along with the ability to create process workflows to define standard analytical approaches and problem solving techniques. You work across the Business Intelligence organization, matrix partner organizations such as Enterprise Reporting &amp; Analysis, and technology partners to give feedback to the long term data strategy as well as ensure analytical requests are solutioned efficiently and in accordance with agreed operating models.
        Primary Responsibilities:
        * Serve as overall owner of the Operations Analysis intake model supervising the intake, prioritization and delivery process for requests
        * Serve as primary point of contact for operations on analytical priorities, and lead governance process to approve and sequence all incoming requests
        * Liaise with Client Management on requirements, and oversee high priority client requests to guide solutions and ensure delivery per commitments
        * Ensure highest quality delivery through the implementation of on-time delivery, turn around time and quality metrics and controls
        * Build and maintain high customer satisfaction levels by incorporating feedback on completed projects and ad-hoc solutions into recurring processes and standards
        * Implement and maintain listening mechanisms for capturing voice of customer and measuring NPS
        * Define strategy and supervise execution of comprehensive operational performance monitoring (drivers of operational KPIs and savings) across OPI value streams
        * Support troubleshooting of issues that affect savings performance for FWAE operations
        * Lead the definition and refinement of an agreed operating model for operations analysis across business units
        Required Qualifications:
        * Bachelor's degree, preferably in information systems, data management, data science
        * 2 years of leading data management or data science organizations
        * 2 years of organizational leadership experience
        * 7 years of information technology / business analysis experience

        * Demonstrated leadership and hands-on experience, strong leader and motivator with the ability to mentor talent within the organization; able to provide work direction; able to attract and retain top talent

        * Expert at strategic conceptualization both at a local level and enterprise level
        * Proven capability to influence and lead

        * Strong verbal and communication skills with all levels in an organization
