            Senior Data Scientist
            Major Responsibilities:
           Teradata's GSO Analytics team&#160;is seeking Data Scientists to join our team. As a member of our team, the candidate will work in collaboration with other members to research, design, prototype, test and document novel algorithms and predictive analytics for automated, near real time decision making on data and problems. On a daily basis, you will work with massive datasets, which includes data integration, data cleansing, exploratory analysis, predictive modeling, and rapid prototyping. You will present findings to teammates and guide the transition of new algorithms into operational code.
            Qualifications:
            Master&#8217;s or PhD degree in a related discipline (Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, Data Science, or Operations Research)
            10+ years of related experience
            Prior work and/or research demonstrates experience in the following areas
              Statistics: exploratory analysis, model development and theory
              Data mining: discovery of patterns &amp; signals in data or anomaly detection
              Developing large scale data analytics solutions based on machine learning such as decision trees, probabilistic, neural networks, clustering, case based reasoning, etc.
              Drawing conclusions from data and generating actionable information
            Extensive knowledge of at least one scientific or statistical programming language such as Matlab, R or Python (must include SciPy, NumPy, Pandas)
            Experience working with SQL and databases
            Experience with NoSQL data environments and tools such as Hadoop and/or Spark
            Experience with at least one general purpose, high level programming language such as C/C++, Java or Python
            Excellent verbal and written communication skills
            Ability to collaborate on an interdisciplinary team members to solve problems
            Self-starter with a positive attitude, intellectual curiosity and a passion for analytics and solving real world problems
           Preferences:
            Experience with either Teradata or Aster database technology
            Experience with at least one of the following: SQL, Tableau, SAS, Spark
            Developing novel analytical approaches and applying them to automatically process data (structured and unstructured) and make decisions in medical diagnosis, enterprise operations, customer support, etc.
            Demonstrable skills in creative-problem-solving of complex and advanced technical subject matter
