Job Purpose:

The Data Scientist utilizes data and analytical ability to find and interpret rich data sources; have domain expertise and operationalize and measure performance; manage large amounts of data despite hardware, software, and bandwidth constraints; merge data sources; ensure consistency of datasets; create visualizations to aid in understanding data; build mathematical models using the data; and present and communicate the data insights/findings.

The role focuses on exploratory analysis and rapid iteration, and to produce and present results with dashboards.
