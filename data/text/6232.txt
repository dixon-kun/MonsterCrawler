        Internship Description
        The Advanced Analytics Intern will perform data analyses to derive insight, patterns and correlations from Hallmark's data and other external data to derive insights that can inform revenue growth opportunities, identify cost avoidance opportunities, and shape strategy. Analysis will include applying advanced statistical, machine learning, and advanced data mining techniques. The analyses will need to be translated into a form for easy consumption by decision makers leveraging a variety of visual approaches.
