We are seeking talented Associates and Senior Associates to join our team that will be building next generation risk/reward models and developing analytics capabilities that will apply niche quantitative techniques, operate with real time banking technologies, use transaction information for tens of millions of customers, and leverage alternative data sources.More specifically your main duties will include:Actively participate in all stages of model development from data collection, model building, model validation, testing and calibrationAnalyze a wide range of inputs, including quantitative data, use cases, market research, and personal experience to identify new opportunities to improve pattern detectionMine large multi-disciplinary data sets including financial information, bureau records,  structured sources (e.g., transactional data), unstructured data (e.g., communication meta-data and referential information) to gain deep business knowledge and insights of embedded relationships and customer behaviorsConvert massive amounts of data on customer activity and usage behaviors into attributes that are leverageable in statistical analysisDevelop next generation models, scorecards, customer segmentation schemes, expert rules and other types of decision analytics to detect risks and improve returns by leveraging advanced statistical techniques and expert-based methodologiesCreate technical documents, test cases and participate in the implementation of models in live operating environments within the organizationTrain line and operating personnel in model usage and problem detectionAssist internal businesses in the ongoing management and monitoring  of their scorecards and related strategiesCommunicate technical subject matter clearly and concisely to individuals from various backgroundsWe are seeking a motivated individual with:PhD/Post Doc in any field with advanced quantitative focus or Masters or Bachelor’s degree with high distinction in modeling oriented discipline including but not limited to Statistics, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Econometrics, Engineering, etc.Background and experience in small business or consumer risk or marketing, especially scoring and forecasting modelsStrong understanding of predictive / analytical modeling techniques, theories, principles, and practices.Intensive experience in key econometric, psychometric and statistical techniques (predictive modeling, logistic regression, survival analysis, panel data models, data mining methods, and other advanced techniques)Strong hands-on knowledge of data mining / predictive modeling tools such as R, SAS, SPSS, etc.Strong familiarity and experience with data preparation and processing - such as assessment of data quality, new variable creation, variable selection, etc. Specific experience in converting transactional data into attributesAbility to conduct research into predictive / analytical modeling issues, practices, and products as required.
