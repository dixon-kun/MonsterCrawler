        Director of Algorithms &#8211; Pricing and Profitability
        Wayfair is looking for an experienced Data Scientist or Product Manager who can lead other data scientists in developing and managing algorithms whose success will be measured in quantifiable business outcomes. In this newly created role, this leader would leverage his/her wealth of experience in unlocking value out of multi-terabyte datasets, natural business intuition/acumen, and ability to work across teams.
        The Pricing &amp; Profitability Team monitors, manages, and maximizes short and long term profitability for each of our business lines globally. It does so by leveraging big data, cutting-edge modeling techniques, nuanced experimentation, and insightful data analysis.
        This is an ideal opportunity for someone motivated to make a measurable impact to the bottom line. The Director would join Wayfair's Leadership team, leading a team of top notch data scientists and working cross-functionally with key stakeholders within the company.
        Job Responsibilities
        * Identify opportunities for algorithmic solutions that can act as a business lever for managing profitability
        * Partner with business leaders in defining the objectives of each algorithm
        * Lead workflows of data scientists and engineers responsible for designing and executing algorithmic solutions
        * Provide technical guidance to a team of data scientists
        * Monitor and ensure ongoing reliability of algorithms and systems by leveraging other engineering operations teams
        Required Qualifications
        * B.A. / M.S. in Computer Science, Machine Learning, Engineering, Statistics, Economics, Operations Research
        * Development and ownership of advanced algorithms
        * Strong exposure to programming
        Preferred Qualifications
        * PhD. in an area of heavy quantitative focus such as Computer Science, Machine Learning, Engineering, Statistics, Economics, Operations Research. Demonstrated ability in database design or in implementing large scale optimization or predictive models
        * Familiarity/strong interest in statistical modeling
        * Experience in leading data scientist teams
