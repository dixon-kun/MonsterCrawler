CDK Global Stability meets Agility. CDK is one of the largest technology providers to the automotive industry, with more than 26,000 dealer sites in over 100 countries and nearly 9,000 employees worldwide. From data scientists to sales and operations experts, we're hiring to support your growth and ours.

The most important thing to us about you is that you have a passion for working on cool stuff. And can work well with cool people. We love the energy shown in your projects (and those side projects you do, 'just for you'). And we love that you can get in a room with amazing product managers and learn and teach and contribute and grow. Our Agile, collaborative approach is important to everyone here.

So, if you have product management skills and interests, and can enjoy joining a team that is building on our market leading, Fortune 1000 product mix, we want to hear from you.

For our MBA Product Management Intern role the right candidate should be passionate about product development, self-motivated and have strong product management experience and previous business responsibility for managing analytics and insight platforms. The person in this role will be expected to contribute new ideas and strategies to keep the program on the cutting edge in this rapidly evolving medium.

* Research automotive manufacturer and dealership needs for products

* Coordinate with external-facing product managers and product marketing

* Candidates enrolled in their 1st year of a MBA program or a related master's degree with an anticipated graduation date between 2017-2018

* Exceptionally strong written and verbal communications with a strong aptitude for succinctly presenting thoughts and ideas to senior internal/external stakeholders

* Demonstrated record of turning ideas into realities

* Proven ability to simplify the inherently complex and organize and plan solutions necessary to iteratively address market needs

* Team player who can collaborate and build consensus at all levels within the organization

Location: 2525 SW 1st Ave. Suite 400, Portland, OR 97201
