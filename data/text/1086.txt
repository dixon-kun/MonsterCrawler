        Fraud Risk
        The role of the Fraud Risk team is to assess the risks we face and ensure strategies are put in place to mitigate these risks to protect our business, clients and customers without compromising the accessibility of our products and services. We can never fully stop fraud, but we can deter criminal elements by making the bank a more resilient place to fraud attempts, which in turn gives our customers greater confidence to entrust their business with us. Please visit our website here for more information.
        Overall purpose of role: To understand and mitigate fraud trends &#8211; particularly on the e-commerce side - causing losses to the Barclaycard US business
        o Monitor fraud losses on a daily basis and take corrective action as required
        o Work with technical, data management and customer servicing teams to identify key issues causing fraud losses and provide solutions
        Key Accountabilities and Skills required:
        o Perform analysis of current and historic data (e.g. authorizations, write off data) to keep Fraud systems and processes at the cutting edge of fraud detection
        o Identification of data driven fraud trends and development of broad strategies to address the trend
        o Conduct defect reviews of high fraud losses cases to identify any gaps in Fraud strategy, case management, or make workflow adjustments
        o Provide support, including analysis, to various Strategic Fraud initiatives outlined by the business as and when necessary
        o Support partnership requests for deep dive analysis into individual portfolios for strategy optimization
        Stakeholder management and leadership.
        o Organizational awareness &#8211; Ability to see the 'whole picture' and recognize the impact and opportunities of activities across the organization.
        o Team Player - Ability to work with and in cross-functional and virtual teams.

        Decision making and problem solving.
        o Analytical ability &#8211; Must have a clear understanding and experience in performing data analysis using a standard statistical package such as SAS/SQL
        o Ability to solve advanced problems and deal with a variety of options in complex situations
        o Innovation &#8211; Create innovative solutions to satisfy business requirements with analysis where needed
        o Risk and Control: All Barclays colleagues have to ensure that all activities and duties are carried out in full compliance with regulatory requirements, Enterprise Wide Risk Management Framework and internal Barclays Policies and Policy Standards
        Basic Qualifications:
        o 2 years of credit card experience required, preferably in risk management function.
        o Bachelor's degree in a quantitative discipline like Statistics, Mathematics, Operations Research etc.
        o 1 year of SAS/SQL Experience
        Preferred Qualifications:
        o Master's degree preferred
        o Strong analytical skills, technical skills in SAS/SQL and statistical skills with proven ability to process vast amounts of data into meaningful information.
