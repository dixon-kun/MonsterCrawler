        Overview of the Position
        Across the entire insurance industry, Commercial Auto is currently one of the most profit-challenged lines. The Hartford is determined to beat the competition and we're improving our profitability using continuous innovation and a deep understanding of the business. The line has support and visibility at the senior management levels, making it a high-profile opportunity.
        The position will be part of a team responsible for understanding the performance of the Commercial Auto class plans. Our auto product is changing quickly and we need to understand the effectiveness of our changes to position ourselves for continuous improvement. The candidate is expected to work closely with multiple business segments to drive auto profitability and influence good business decisions.
        Responsibilities:
        Specifically, the individual in this role will:
        * Work closely with business partners in Middle Market and Small Commercial on a variety of dynamic projects required to answer specific business needs. Projects often require working with Commercial Auto indication and rate implementation units. Present findings and recommendations to partners to guide future decisions.
        * Partner with the Data Science team to refresh the predictive model underlying the new auto class plan, with the ultimate goal of refreshing on a semi-annual basis. Propose changes to be implemented based on potential gains, dislocation estimates and the strategic direction for Middle Market and Small Commercial
        * Participate in the Commercial Auto AOC analysis, which projects future loss ratios and highlights risks and future profit opportunities. An example of this work is gaining a deep understanding of our current net rate and projecting this into the future.
        * Continue enhancing the way we use our new rating engine (ARE) to understand our Auto Class plans and use these results to influence good business decisions. This includes working closely with the Data Science ARE team to interpret results and making recommendations for future enhancements.
        * Become a subject matter expert regarding available Auto data and participate on upcoming data projects.
        Experience &amp; Skills
        * 1-2 years of experience in P&amp;C insurance pricing, auto experience is a plus.
        * Working knowledge of SAS and Excel.
        * Predictive Modeling and Emblem experience would be helpful, but not required.
        * Strong analytical and critical thinking skills, with thorough attention to detail. Ability to own projects and anticipate obstacles and customer needs.
        * Proactive individual, who challenges existing processes, can influence others and can drive a process to a conclusion while meeting critical deadlines.
        * Strong written and verbal communication skills to convey technical results to the business and influence decisions.
        * Team player with strong collaboration skills and ability to build trusting, effective partnerships.

        * Build Strong Partnerships &#8211; Demonstrate integrity and build trust with others.
