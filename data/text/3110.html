Syntelligent Analytic Solutions, LLC , a SDVOSB and HUBZone Small Business Concern, is a professional services and consulting firm catering to Government and private sector customers.  We provide highly qualified professionals who work in partnership with our clients to provide unique, creative and innovative professional, management and technical services and solutions in the areas of: Program Management & Support, Analytic & Intelligence Operations, Information Technology, Security Operations and Business Consulting.

Syntelligent is seeking a Mid-level Intelligence Production Specialist/Report Writer to support DoD contract work in Reston, VA.

  • The candidate tasks and collects exploited data for all of DIA’s Space Counter-Space (SCS) branch for reporting from all sections.
  • Production specialists are responsible for developing collection-based and theme-based technical intelligence products on current and emerging issues concerning space situational awareness (SSA)
  • Production Specialist conducts quality control of all reports prior to dissemination and ingestion in the Library of National Intelligence and should be able to create queries using multiple research tools.

Principal Accountabilities:

  • Independently author and submit high-level documents for publication to senior DIA leadership on a consistent basis.
  • Collating, analyzing and synthesizing information in order to transform it into a coherent and intelligence based report; IIRs.
  • Collating, analyzing and synthesizing the required information in order to transform it into a monthly summary of activities report.
  • Works closely with engineers, physicists and technical intelligence professionals to identify relevant information responsible for responding to national intelligence gaps for inclusion within the reports.
  • Sorting through large volumes of data, identifying the most important items, and addressing or issuing timely reports containing appropriate data fields.
  • Review, edit, and disseminate Technical Intelligence Notes as required.
  • Developing and codifying business practices in Standard Operating Procedures (SOP).
  • Collaborate with Data Scientist on formats and coding for adding metadata to reporting.
  • Upload reports to appropriate analytical tools within the Intelligence Community (IC).
  • Maintain and track requests for collection.
  • Gathering, maintaining, and reporting metrics.

Physical Requirements:

  • Normal office environment with controlled temperature and lighting conditions.

Experience:

  • Experience in the IC or related community.
  • Demonstrated very strong oral and written communications skills.
  • Proven experience and capability using MS Office Suite with emphasis on MS Word, MS Excel, and MS PowerPoint.

Preferred:

  • Knowledge of DIA organization and structure
  • Knowledge of National MASINT Requirements System (NMRS)
  • Knowledge of TCPED Cycle

Education:

  • Bachelor’s Degree (Two years of related work experience may be substituted for one year of education)

Clearance:

  • Active TS/SCI with CI Poly
