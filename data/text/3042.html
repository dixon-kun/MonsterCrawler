Position Description

This position is responsible for mining Union's Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system and other data sources to uncover and analyze key strategic information and develop strategic recommendations that will support the Retail, Commercial, Fee-Based Businesses and Marketing groups in the development of effective strategies designed to increase market share and maximize the profitability of customer accounts and relationships.

This teammate will direct the strategic development, management, and enhancements of the CRM system as a primary "Super User" but will not be responsible for the daily or ongoing operational maintenance of the CRM database.

This position will direct and manage Marketing Business Analyst(s).

Position Accountabilities

* Define Strategic Information Needed – Work directly with Retail, Commercial, Fee-Based Businesses and Product/Marketing managers to define the strategic information needed to support the development of product and business strategies.

* Development / Analysis of Strategic Reports - Design ongoing and ad hoc reports and dashboards that present, organize, and summarize key strategic information in a manner that allows users to quickly and easily review and evaluate the strategic information required to make prudent business decisions. In other words, the reports should "tell a clear, concise and relevant story" for the user that supports the ability to make business decisions or develop product/business strategies. Develop an analysis of reports and data related to the business decision and make specific recommendations.

In addition, develop prospect lists to support targeted marketing strategies and special reports to track campaign results.

* Strategic Management of Union's CRM System – as a "Super User", provide strategic direction to support the ongoing selection, development and enhancement of Union's CRM system to assure the system provides the key strategic information needed by key users to support product/business strategies and decisions.

* Manage Marketing Business Analyst(s) – direct, manage and develop marketing teammates who are responsible for extracting and analyzing key strategic information from Union's systems and databases and developing, maintaining and analyzing a wide variety of management reports.

* Deploy quality assurance techniques to ensure data and report integrity.

* Develop and maintain appropriate policies, procedures, data dictionary and other documentation as needed.

* Develop efficient, scalable report generation processes.

Organizational Relationship

This position reports to the Marketing Product Director.

RequirementsEducation & Experience

* Bachelor's degree in data science or related field is required; Master's degree a plus

* 10+ years of experience in a position with primary responsibilities for developing and using databases and CRM systems to extract and analyze large volumes of data and develop recommendations to support business strategies or decisions; preferably in the banking, financial services, marketing or a related field

* Experience managing people desired

Knowledge & Skills

* Proficient in using CRM and multiple internal and external databases to extract large volumes of strategic data, develop effective management reports, and develop and present strategic recommendations to key users and senior management

* Advanced skills using Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Access, SQL and other appropriate tools and deep experience in using these programs to manipulate CRMs and other information databases to prepare user-friendly management reports including supporting graphics

* Knowledge of external data sources of information which can be used to supplement the bank's customer data

* Ability to effectively manage and partner with third party vendors and data providers

* Business acumen required to proactively work with business partners to design and develop reporting requirements

* Ability to:

* Interpret user needs and requirements and help the user define the strategic information needed to support business

decisions or the development of product/business strategies

* Employ sophisticated analytics programs, machine learning and statistical methods to prepare data for use in predictive and

prescriptive modeling

* Analyze data from a variety of angles to determine hidden weaknesses, trends and/or opportunities and develop effective

reports that highlight the information or trends that are the most relevant to the users or the business decision

* Develop data-driven solutions to the most pressing challenges

* Recommend cost-effective changes to existing procedures and strategies

* Review management reports to assure data is error-free virtually all of the time

* Excellent communication and writing skills including ability to present analytical concepts, results and conclusions in an easily understandable and logical manner

* Strong interpersonal skills with experience in working with, and presenting to, senior & executive level managers

* Ability to take direction and work independently as well as in a team environment

* Possess confidence and ability to effectively lead project teams

* Ability to manage people both directly as a supervisor and indirectly in project teams

* Skilled at multi-tasking and managing multiple projects with a high level of attention to detail

* Flexible, able to adapt to change
