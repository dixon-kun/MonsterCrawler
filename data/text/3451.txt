        A9.com, an Amazon company, creates powerful, customer-focused search and advertising solutions and technologies. The Search Analytics team is part of Amazon Search, powering the largest shopping search engine in the world. Whenever a customer visits an Amazon site worldwide and types in a query or browses through product categories, our services go to work. We design, develop, and deploy high performance, fault-tolerant distributed search systems used by millions of Amazon customers every day.
        The Senior Manager for Search Analytics will lead the Search Analytics team and evangelize data based decision making across the Amazon Search organization. The team owns and manages the richest and largest behavioral product search dataset in the world and runs offline relevance measurements. Our data and systems provide business insight for Amazon's retail businesses, behavioral data for machine learning algorithms, analysis of A/B experiments, and definition and tracking of key search goals. We drive strategic business driven analysis both within search and more broadly across Amazon, we develop tools for tactical decisions made by thousands of employees across A9 and Amazon, and we develop systems to process massive quantities of user data from Amazon Search and external websites.
        At A9, you'll experience the benefits of working in a dynamic, entrepreneurial environment, while leveraging the resources of Amazon.com (AMZN), one of the world's leading internet companies. We provide a highly customer-centric, team-oriented environment in our offices located in Palo Alto, California.
        Responsibilities:
        * Develop roadmap and vision around data based decision making in Amazon Search
        * Lead a multiple functional teams of data scientists, data analysts, software engineers, and program managers including senior staff members and managers responsible for providing data pipelines, novel data storage and information retrieval services.
        * Grow the cross functional team through networking, and active recruiting.
        * Provide active leadership in the measurement of Search, including creation and oversight of key business measures
        * Oversee strategic analysis and present key findings to executives
        * Work with business stakeholders to understand key developments in Amazon's business and enable new forms of analysis
        * Guide innovative technical system development to enable self-service analysis across Amazon

        * At least 7 years of progressive experience managing high performance teams of data scientists, software developers or data analysts
