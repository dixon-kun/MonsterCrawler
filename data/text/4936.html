JOB SUMMARY:

The Sales Operations Specialist supports the functions essential to sales productivity and client success. They will be responsible for sales support and operational activities, including analytics, dashboards & reporting, CRM, and other sales enablement tools including but not limited to our sales acceleration platform, call center solution, and our lead distribution platform.

MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS AND REQUIREMENTS:

* Bachelor's degree desired, or 1+ year of advanced knowledge of MINDBODY Software and Business Partners

* Advanced Salesforce knowledge

* Ability to assess perspective's subscriber business requirements (systems, processes, etc.) and recommend proper product solutions

* Advanced Excel knowledge is a plus

PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

* Ensures that Salesforce CRM is used in the most efficient and effective manner, providing troubleshooting support and proactive training to the wider sales team.

* Will master and ensure proper execution of sales enablement tools.

* Support the Sales Operations Manager and Data Science to develop relevant dashboards and analytics, proactively providing insight into the performance of the Sales Team and identifying areas for focus and prioritization, while recommending revisions to existing reports, and assisting in the development of new reporting tools as needed.

* Partner with Learning and Development on sales training related to tools or process.

* Will serve as a technical resource to the sales team on demos and presentations.

* Will re-route, upload, and assign leads to sales reps and managers.

MINIMUM ATTRIBUTES REQUIRED TO PERFORM THE ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS OF THE POSITION:

* Is seen as a team player and is cooperative.

* Extremely organized â€“can orchestrate multiple activities at once to accomplish a goal.

* Excellent time management skills; uses time effectively and efficiently.

* Ability, on a consistent basis, to maintain attention and concentration for extended periods of time.

* Ability, on a consistent basis, to function in a high stress environment, under substantial pressure.

* Ability to plan and execute and lead complex projects.

* Ability to learn quickly when facing new problems; relentless and versatile leader that is open to change.

WORK ENVIRONMENT AND PHYSICAL DEMANDS:

* Dexterity of hands and fingers to operate a computer keyboard.

* This position is mostly stationary and will be required to remain stationary for extended periods of time.

* Specific vision abilities required by this position include close vision, color vision, and the ability to adjust focus.

* The noise level in the work environment is usually moderately quiet.

SCOPE OF SUPERVISION/AUTHORITY:

Duties are performed under limited supervision. Will receive training and guidance when working on new processes and procedures. Most decisions will be routine and do not require supervisor assistance. No direct management responsibility.
