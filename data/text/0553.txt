          Position Description:
          Rater and Modeling group in AIG Commercial Underwriting is seeking a data scientist to help us design, structure, execute and implement predictive models. We are a team of high performers with diverse backgrounds&#8212;our roster includes actuaries, mathematicians, computer scientists, economists, operation researchers, and more. We all share a passion for data and a desire to make an impact through implementable analytics. We are looking for creative and curious talents to join us.
          Responsibility:
          &#61607; Extract, clean and conduct descriptive analysis on large scaled internal/external (structured and unstructured) datasets
          &#61607; Participate in predictive modeling project using actuarial methods, statistical technique and machine learning algorithms in the area of commercial underwriting and pricing
          &#61607; Work with SMEs of other areas such as product support, rate filing department, line of business actuaries, and IT group to develop rater and deploy predictive models
          &#61607; Communicate insights from quantitative analyses to technical and non-technical audiences.
          Position Requirements:
            BA/BS degree from a quantitative area (Math, economics, finance, computer science, operation research, insurance and risk management, etc), advanced degree is preferred
            Thorough, hands-on understanding of advanced modeling methods such as GAMs, GBMs, GLMs, GLMMs, GMMs, decision trees, regularization techniques, SVMs, and random forest etc.
            Advanced SQL skills and excellent programming skills in at least one of the quantitative analysis software packages/languages such as VBA, R, SAS and Python
            Demonstrated ability to effectively interpret data and modeling results, distill actionable insights and present them to stakeholders
            CAS designation (Exams, ACAS, FCAS) is a plus, but not requirement
            Knowledge of an additional general purpose programming language such as C++ or Java.
            Excellent communication skills both in written and verbal
            Organized, self-motivated, results-oriented and resourceful
