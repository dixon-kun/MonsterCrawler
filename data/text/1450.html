Our Team:

The Strategic Intelligence and Analytics team focuses on qualitative and quantitative analysis related to economic, client and competitive ecosystems in which CME Group operates. Through data science, generating insight, developing tools, and report automation, the team creates a competitive advantage to drive better and faster decision making by the firm’s senior leaders. We serve the broader units to identify and promote significant market and client opportunities.

Responsibilities:

* Collaborates with business leaders to refine project goals and help solve real business challenges.

* Works in team environment collaborating with business and technology.

* Designs and implements creative approaches to gain insight.

* Analyzes historical trends using statistics and provide recommendations.

* Assists with analytics to help identify right targets.

Minimum Qualifications:

* Currently pursuing a BS or MS degree at a University with an expected graduation date between December 2017 and December 2018.

* Majoring in Computer Science, Information Systems, Management Information System, Statistics, or Economics.

* Working knowledge of SQL.

Preferred Qualifications:

* Analytical skills and the ability to decipher complex business problems.

* Working knowledge of any one BI tool (Business Objects, QlikView, Microsoft BI etc.).

* Data visualization skills using Tableau or any visualization/BI Tools.

* Understanding of the commodity and financial derivative markets is preferred.

Job location: Chicago, IL
