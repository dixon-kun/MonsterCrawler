We are seeking a talented and enthusiastic Senior Consultant to help accelerate IMS's Predictive Analytics offering in the US. You will join this high profile team to work on ground-breaking problems in health outcomes across disease areas including Ophthalmology, Oncology, Neurology, chronic diseases such as diabetes, and a variety of rare conditions.

The Predictive Analytics team work hand-in-hand with statisticians, epidemiologists and disease area experts across the wider global RWES team, leveraging a vast variety of anonymous patient-level information from sources such as electronic health records. We provide solutions based on IMS's unrivalled access to 500+ million anonymized patient records as well as through bespoke, customized partnerships with providers and payers.

The Role:

You will play an important part in developing IMS's Predictive Analytics offering in the US across a range of challenging analytical areas relating to patient health. You will work closely with both the Global Predictive Analytics team and the US RWES team to accelerate Predictive Analytics offerings and revenue in the US. The role will encompass a mix of delivery and business development responsibilities including:

I) Delivery: Contributing to the design and execution of Predictive Analytics client and R&D projects, ensuring projects are analytically rigorous and delivered on time, within budget and to a high standard; developing and mentoring project managers without specialist analytics knowledge to facilitate the scale-out of Predictive Analytics offerings.

II) Business development: Working closely with US RWES Principals and sales teams to grow revenue from Predictive Analytics projects through support in identifying opportunities, writing proposals and pitching to clients.

Ideally, you will have a strong statistical/analytics/data science background (preferably involving predictive analytics), a pro-active, problem-solving attitude, extensive business or commercial experience in healthcare (preferably in the pharmaceutical sector), outstanding written and verbal communication skills and demonstrable project management skills. Above all, you will have a passion to marry advanced analytics and real-world patient data to drive insight and value in healthcare. This is an unrivalled opportunity to play a pivotal part in a key growth area in healthcare and IMS Health.

IMS Health's Real-World Evidence Solutions (RWES) teams work with global clients to leverage value from complex, clinically rich patient-level healthcare datasets, through advanced analytic methods. Our approach is unparalleled in the industry, leading to breakthrough insights on diseases, market dynamics, therapy area changes, outcomes research and other scientific, clinical and commercial issues.

The RWE Healthcare Analytics Center of Excellence (CoE) was formed to address the Big Data challenge for healthcare. It develops novel analytic approaches specifically designed for large data sets, building on techniques developed across industries. Its core competencies encompass technical and data science leadership, high performance infrastructure, real-time systems, new standards in UX and data visualization and novel analytical methods and algorithms.

Skills & Experience

Candidates interested in joining the Global Predictive Analytics team as a Senior Consultant will have:

* A strong analytical background in data science, advanced statistical methods or machine learning.

* Extensive experience in healthcare / life sciences or the pharmaceutical industry.

* Experience in healthcare / life sciences with focus on strategic business cases for analytics services or products.

* Strong capability in managing analytics projects and juggling priorities so that deadlines are met while retaining consistently high quality outcomes.

* Experience of working in sizable/multi-disciplined teams

* Experience of developing credible relationships with senior level managers and executives in the pharmaceutical/healthcare industry.

* Knowledge of key issues and current developments in the pharmaceutical and healthcare industries.

* Demonstrable analytical, interpretative and problem-solving skills.

* Inquisitiveness, attention to detail, ability to assess the situation and question norms.

* Excellent presentation, communication and client influencing skills.

* Well-developed written and verbal communication skills including presentations, chairing meetings, workshop facilitation, business and analytics report writing.

* Prior experience with software packages such as R, Python, Matlab and SAS is desirable.

* Knowledge of consulting methodologies, tools and techniques would be desirable.

Qualifications & Other Requirements

In addition to the skills and experience above, candidates interested in joining as a Senior Consultant will have:

* BA/BSc from a top university, preferably in a relevant and / or numerate subject, e.g. computer science, economics, life sciences, chemistry, engineering, mathematics, statistics etc.

* A postgraduate qualification in life sciences or computer science discipline is desirable although not essential.

* Extensive experience in healthcare consulting / experience in life science sector industry.

* Excellent conversational and business English (written and oral).

* A solid track record of extra-curricular achievement.
