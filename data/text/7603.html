Lead stand-up of new targeting cell for a technology focused customer attempting leading edge operations. Oversee team of five targeting analysts, providing strategic guidance and direction. Identify and acquire access to key data sources and targeting tools to initiate targeting effort. Establish relationships with Intel and collection partners to facilitate analysis and collection tasking. Develop targeting strategy to prioritize Intel needs based on customer program requirements and create future collection plan. Identify operational opportunities based on targeting analysis. Brief customer on targeting strategy, opportunities, risks, and analysis.



Typical Duties and Responsibilities:
  • All-Source Analysis experience required
  • BA/BS degree with 15+ years of related IC experience in targeting, intelligence analysis, operations, collection management, data science or technology development (or 12+ years with MA/MS).
  • 5+ years of specific experience with the DoD or the IC as a technical targeting analyst
  • Experience developing or using targeting / analysis tools is required
  • Ability to collaborate and communicate effectively with team members and mission partners with minimum supervision is required
  • TS/SCI with Polygraph is required


Required Qualifications:
  • Experience with hard target sets
  • Knowledge of aerospace industry and technology.
  • Knowledge of Open Source research
  • Foreign language expertise.
