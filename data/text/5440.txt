Primary responsibilities will include; developing ETL&#8217;s using a variety of scripting languages and ETL tools to ingest data into Hadoop; working collaboratively with customers and team members supporting large business initiatives; working closely with other scientists to build a better understanding on how Data Science integrates with the Hadoop platform.
