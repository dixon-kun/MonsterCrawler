Company Description

Verizon Digital Media Services brings together world-class technology to prepare, deliver and display content — so users can watch and enjoy on their terms. The purpose of our platform is simple. It delivers next-generation digital experiences for today’s demanding users, whether it be their favorite TV shows, catching up with friends via social media or shopping for the latest gadgets. Job Description

We are looking for graduate internship candidates to help innovate cutting-edge technology and solve next generation problems. Selected interns will work on advanced research, applied algorithms, and networking technologies for the next generation Internet. The internship will focus on the areas of web-cache optimization and study of Internet traffic patterns and behaviors. This would include developing and evaluating new and existing techniques.

Qualifications

  • Must currently be enrolled in coursework at the PhD level
  • Pursuing a PhD in CS, Statistics, Electrical Engineering, Applied Mathematics (Especially systems/networking) or related programs
  • Great skills in statistics, data analysis, and algorithms
  • Desired programming skill sets in C/C, Python, R
  • Experience in Networking (HTTP, DNS, TCP/IP, transport layer optimization, etc.) and systems
