To protect patient safety and drive supply chain integrity, countries around the world, including the U.S., Brazil, China, and the E.U., have accelerated adoption of pharmaceutical product serialization and traceability regulations. With regulations expected to cover more than 75% of the global medicine supply by 2018 .

For Pharmaceutical Supply Chain organizations, inability to meet serialization and traceability regulations across all markets where they sell product creates severe negative business outcomes including:

  • Severe financial penalties from regulators for non-compliance
  • Inability to ship product into global markets resulting in lost revenue/market share
  • Damage to brand reputation from ongoing threats that compromise product quality & safety, such as counterfeiting, diversion, theft, and importation of unapproved or substandard drugs.

Compliance with pharmaceutical serialization mandates is critical to business continuity for pharma supply chain companies.

Axway provides market-leading software and services that empower our customers to meet these requirements and achieve compliance with serialization and traceability regulations.

Axway is seeking a seasoned professional with the acumen of both enterprise architect and business analyst expertise. Reporting to the Product Line Manager, the primary goal for this position is to guide the creation, market performance and life cycle of the Track & Trace product offerings including enabling software, services, solution expertise and partnerships. The candidate leads cross-functional business teams to drive product roadmaps, assumes accountability for successful market adoption and life cycle management and conveys the solution vision to internal and external stakeholders. The Track & Trace Product Manager also collaborates closely with product owners, marketing, sales, services and support. This leadership role is responsible for managing the “whole product” offering definition, articulating the “what” and “why” for each offering and negotiating the contents with the team. The primary focus is delivering differentiated economic value to customers and improving Axway’s overall ability to win in the market.


  • Leading cross-functional business teams to drive product roadmaps. The Product Manager defines and prioritizes the product roadmap to deliver compelling value propositions in the market. This role is responsible for effective communication, verbally, visually, and in writing, with cross-functional business teams to determine the optimal scope of product offerings, including the enabling software, services, solution expertise and partnerships. The Product Manager relies on and empowers Product Owner(s), Services Portfolio leads, Service Line Directors, Product/Solution Marketers, Presales and Operations to determine specific product features, services packages, business processes, go to market programs, launch plans and sales methodologies for each product offering.
  • Assuming accountability for successful market adoption and life cycle management. The Product Manager drives the key performance indicators (KPI) for their product offering, including pipeline, revenue, market share and profits. The qualified Product Manager develops intimate relationships with customers to regularly capture buyer and user persona input into the product offering definition through product advisory councils, focus groups, industry conferences, customer meetings and other means. He or she proactively identifies areas of opportunity and improvement in all technical and operational aspects of the product offering, initiates action plans to address those areas, contributes hands-on to executing the plans and follows through on the results to ensure business cases for the product offering are met.
  • Conveying the solution vision to internal and external stakeholders. The Product Manager invests significant time externally articulating the vision of their product offering to customers, partners, analysts, industry bodies and other influencers. The ideal Product Manager collaborates closely with Marketing and Presales to help them to deliver sales enablement in the form of competitive intelligence, tools, training, and roadmap presentations.

  • Safeguard Axway and Axway customer data through compliance with Axway policies and the appropriate use of Axway equipment and facilities.


  • An imaginative and decisive business technologist who possesses deep domain knowledge of customer problems, market trends and competitor capabilities. Orientation is towards the definition of solutions spanning one or more business cases using strong visual, verbal and written communication.
  • Champions “whole product” thinking, ensuring sufficient consideration of non-functional requirements and implications for how product is promoted, sold, delivered, and monetized.
  • Has built credibility and a reputation for guiding cross-functional teams to execute on quality deliverables, i.e. product requirements document (PRD), product roadmaps, services statements of work (SOW), pricing models, sales program plans, marketing plans, sales presentations, etc.
  • Lives and breathes the voice of the customer, capable of translating market trends, requirements and competitive dynamics into comprehensive product offerings, fostering healthy debate and ambitious thinking within a team environment to arrive at a suitable solution.
  • Collaborates without boundaries to ensure transparency into timelines, quality of delivery, exposure to risk, barriers to execution, business viability, and remediation plans.
  • Strong aptitude for hands-on creation of compelling visual and persuasive written content for internal and external consumption.
  • Takes an inclusive and listen-first approach to appropriately influence release contents, schedules, and technical decisions, forming a highly interactive tight-knit relationship built on trust.
  • Continues learning and adapting to change by being receptive to coaching and addressing gaps by proactively developing desired proficiencies. Seeks mentoring and feedback.

Required Education / Experience:

  • Bachelor degree or comparable academic training in User-Centered Design, Computer Science, Engineering, Data Science, Management Information Systems, Computer Information Systems, or closely related discipline.
  • Master of Science or comparable post-graduate academic training, a plus.
  • 5+ years of progressive leadership owning product management and/or product marketing in the Healthcare/Pharma space, with a reputation of delighting customers, meeting tight deadlines, and fostering cross-functional collaborative working relationships.
  • Experience in the creation of more than one product from inception to release with meaningful results measured in terms of customers, revenue and/or market share.
  • Background with direct responsibility as a product owner, business analyst, presales consultant, or technical support engineer, a plus.
  • Familiar with CFR21 Part 11 and GxP and other standards.
  • Knowledgeable on serialization for pharmaceuticals and related compliance regulations like USDSCA, EU FMD and others is a pre.
  • Track record in Healthcare / Pharma industry oriented solutions in a role as product owner, product manager or business analyst.
