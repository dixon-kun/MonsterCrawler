        Background and Responsibilities
        The role will be a key member of the FMG Advanced Data Analytics group in New York. The position will be an individual interested in the field of big-data infrastructure, machine learning algorithms, and data visualization solutions. In addition to having passion in these fields, the candidate should be comfortable with multiple programming languages (Scala, Java, Python, R &#8230;) and multiple technologies (Hadoop, Spark, Tensorflow, RDBMS&#8230;) to support the modeling and infrastructure requirements of the diverse nature and demands of the team. In addition, the candidate will be supporting more senior members of the team to create tools and solutions that can help our clients with their investment decisions.
