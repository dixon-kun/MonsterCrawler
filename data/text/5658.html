ADP is looking to hire a Lead Developer- OCR & Automation Solutions

We are looking for an experience OCR, RPA developer who possesses a passion for pushing OCR and RPA technology and will work with ADP's talented team to build the next generation of Intelligent Process Automation that can work across various applications and devices.

Job Responsibilities:

* Work in a team environment to improve process performance

* Design and build advanced & intelligent OCR engine that can work across platforms

* Collaborate with cross- functional teams to define and design new features

* Complete unit-test code for usability, reliability and overall performance

* Work on fixes to improve overall system performance

* Support evaluation of tools to implement new technologies

* Identify and communicate technical infrastructure requirements

* Able to communicate with vendors on software related issues, suggest improvements

* Able to create test procedures and scenarios pre UAT.

* Able to communicate and support the project team throughout the rollout phases (Assessment, requirement creation, testing and operations deployment)

* Provide superior level of experience and knowledge of RPA solution to support Project Team throughout the delivery lifecycle

* Provide architectural control over solution designs to ensure consistency and continuity across business RPA's Roadmap

Desired Skills, Experience and Qualifications

* Bachelor's degree in science and technology

* 3+ years of OCR development experience Captiva software preferred

* Experience in optical form scanning, optical recognition for conversation to data base, extracting data via OCR and transforming it

* Work experience in Java Development, Java solutions design

* Experience supporting excel macros, Visual Basic scripts or other configuration/ scripting type, REST Web Services, APIs

* Experience in Agile SDLC

* Good communication and written skills with the ability to produce clear and concise documentation

* Knowledge of RPA software (BluePrism preferred)

* Highly motivated and dedicated to delivering results within tight timeframes

* Strong relationship builder with people within and across the organization
