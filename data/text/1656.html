Description

GGP has an immediate need for a Financial Analyst at our Corporate Office in Chicago, IL.

GGP has been in the shopping center business for more than 50 years blending innovation, tradition and reputation to create some of the country’s top shopping centers. As employees we are collaborative, creative, self-starters who aim to positively impact the shopping experience for our customer every day. A career with GGP is your chance to embark on a rewarding journey into the retail real estate industry!

Position Summary:

Responsible for accurate and timely financial analysis as a member of the Finance department that directly supports the CFO and other executives to make business decisions, the analyst reports to the Director of Finance.

Responsibilities include the following:

  • Facilitate the annual budgeting and monthly forecasting process for revenue and expenses, including a detailed review of the assumptions and calculations
  • Provide feedback on financial health of assets in retail real estate portfolio, and provide guidance and suggestions on how to improve NOI
  • Prepareadvanced analysis for retail real estate portfolio (Using Excel, Argus, and POWER BI)
  • Reviewand understand shopping center financial operations, including impact of tenant sales, occupancy costs and health ratios on property NOI
  • Other duties as assigned
Requirements

Qualifications include the following:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in STEM field, Finance or Accounting (other fields considered with proven analytic capabilities)
  • 1 – 5 years analytic experience
  • Individualmust be able to handle a range of issues and multiple projects simultaneously
  • Individualmust be a self-starter driven by intellectual curiosity
  • Strong Excel skills required; Data Science/Data Visualization skills big plus
  • Organizational skills, attention to detail, and strong communications skills
  • JD Edwards, ARGUS, Salesforce, Power BI, and HFM skills a plus
  • Experience reading retail leases and understanding of tenant recoveries a plus
