        About the Opportunity
        As a Sales Solutions Architect at Pitney Bowes, you will provide business and technical pre-sales support to the America's Services and Sales teams for solution sales opportunities. You thrive on solving clients' business challenges and generating cutting edge software solutions positively impacting revenue objectives. You see opportunities where others see challenges.
        Build. Deploy. Bridge. Grow.
        In this role, you'll join our CIM/LI Solutions Sales team in our Digital Commerce Global Sales division reporting into the Global Solutions Director. This position may work remotely from a home office. You will be responsible for:
        * Partnering with sales representatives to lead and/or facilitate conversations with clients and prospective clients, to effectively and clearly communicate methods to solve business problems.
        * Providing direct input / recommendations on software bundle recommendations and associated pricing dependent upon high-level architecture recommendations.
        * Participating in all stages of opportunities to support the sales objectives.
        * Partnering with the Solutions Management, Product Management, and Partners on the ongoing enhancements of capabilities in support of sales opportunities.
        * Providing the necessary insight and direction to our Global Services teams on the development of services proposals and engagements that support sales opportunities.
        * Driving sales activities, responding to RFPs and client requests.
        Qualifications
        * 5-7 years of experience with Master Data Management / Single View of Customer solutions with multiple vendors products in more than one industry/client
        * 3-5 years experience in selling software and/or services
        * Ability to effectively challenge and present alternatives to traditional Master Data Management practices / solutions while maintaining a constant eye on the building of strong relationships and becoming a trusted advisor
        * Technical experience with project implementations; preferable to have come from either a services or implementation back ground
        * Ability to work within a matrix team structure to create partnerships in solving for customer problems
        * Willingness and ability to travel up to 60% including overnight travel
        * Bachelor's or Masters in Information Management, Data Science, Analytics or a related discipline preferred.
        The Team
        Our Software team powers global commerce, processing billions of transactions every year. Our Software technology and solutions help businesses operate faster and more efficiently. How? By identifying trusted data and insights to create targeted, effective messaging, by providing accurate geographic data to make smarter business decisions, and by delivering relevant and engaging interactions across the customer lifecycle to drive increased satisfaction.
