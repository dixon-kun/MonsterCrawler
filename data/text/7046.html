Job Descriptionand Responsibilities:

The Business AnalyticsProject Manager (PM) is a well-rounded individual with technical, business, andproject management skills. This PM generally works on implementingcomplex and large data projects, leveraging prior experiences on how tointegrate multiple systems and data sets. This individual should be able to link andcombine distinctive data sets to discover new insights, connecting differenttypes of data sets in different forms and finding ways to use them. This PM should be able to create examples,prototypes, demonstrations to help management better understand the work.

Ideal candidatefor this role is someone with data analysis, data engineering as well asbusiness skills to ask the right questions and find the right answers. Must beable to work in a fast-paced multi-disciplinary environment where new data flowsin rapidly. This role also requires someonewith stronger curiosity and patience to dive deep into the material to uncovernew insights on a trial-and-error approach, but at the same time a broadmindset to ask the right business questions.

Strong written andverbal communication skills are a must, because he/she is expected to advise seniormanagement in clear and concise language about the findings and implications forthe organization.

Required Skillsand Experience:

* Bachelor's degreein Computer Science, Management Information Systems, Data Science, Statistics or similar field. Masters ispreferred.

* 3-5 years ofexperience building and managing complex analytics products/solutions.

* 4 years'experience within data technology or statistical modelling functions offinancial services industry.

* Experience inusing and managing forensic technologies, including data analysis tools. Proficientin one of the following: R, SAS, Python, QlikView or similar tools.

* Understanding ofHadoop, Hive and/or R.

* Familiarity withdisciplines such as: Natural Language Processing, Machine learning, Conceptualmodelling, Statistical analysis, Predictive modelling and hypothesis testing.

* Projects withevidence of creative and critical thinking a must. Demonstrated ability to work independentlyand within a team in a fast changing environment with changing priorities andchanging time constraints.

* Communicationskills: ability to interpret businessrequests as well as communicate findings in a user-friendly manner; ability towrite clear, concise reports and presentations; ability to verbally communicateor present effectively to management.

* Strong organizationaland documentation skills.

* Understanding of insuranceunderwriting concepts is strongly preferred, with ability to apply businesslogic to design and implement data mining techniques on large data sets.

* Understanding ofData Mining is preferred.

* An understandingof risk management methodology and factors.
