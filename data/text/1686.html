The Sr. Analyst is an integral part of the analytics team that analyzes detailed customer data to develop powerful insights that drive the effectiveness/ROI of marketing strategies. The Sr. Analyst will utilize innovative analytic and statistic based approaches to optimize marketing efforts and further support business decisions in a fast-paced, data-focused retail environment. This position will also be responsible for presenting analyses to multiple internal groups, with varying levels of technical expertise.

PRINCIPAL DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES

* Work with internal partners to assess business needs and transform them into meaningful observations by selecting optimal analytic technique(s) to pull the appropriate data elements.

* Summarize and present analyses/models to audiences ranging from e-commerce and store teams to senior management.

* Deep familiarity of SAS (and/or R) or SQL, ability to pull data using SAS or SQL, and other technologies from the customer database and other sources â€“ data which includes campaign response, transaction data, and customer profile information.

* Design, program, generate and automate concise KPI reports and/or dashboards that track marketing effectiveness and ROI.

* Establish methodologies to ensure data integrity including data reconciliation, data integration and data audits.

* Analyze Ulta customer behavior including behavioral customer profiling, life cycle analysis, customer segmentation and market basket analysis.

* Evaluate the effectiveness of marketing tactics, strategies and techniques.

* Experience building predictive models and performing descriptive analytics using statistical methods such as clustering and segmentation techniques, logistic regression, and propensity modeling to support the marketing efforts and business needs of the CRM/Loyalty and Merchant teams (as examples).

Requirements

* 5+ years of experience developing business strategies resulting from quantitative marketing analytics/data mining and presenting those results to various levels of leadership.

* Experience in retail or CPG industry a plus.

* BA/BS degree Business Analytics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Data Science, Business or other degree with analytic experience. Advanced degree preferred.

* Proven command of SAS and/or R, SQL, relational databases and MS Excel/PowerPoint.

* Experience with data visualization tools (i.e. Tableau) a plus.

* Capability to execute the complete modeling process from data extraction/manipulation through model development and presentation.

* Excellent interpersonal, verbal, and written communication skills - must be able to communicate complex ideas in both technical and user-friendly language.

* Capable of working in a dynamic environment, handling multiple projects, meeting deadlines, able to prioritize appropriately and respond to issues quickly and creatively with an open and positive attitude.

* Excellent analytical, mathematical and creative problem-solving skills.

* Energetic and self-motivated - must be extremely organized and able to make proactive recommendations.

SPECIAL POSITION REQUIREMENTS

* SQL, SAS or R, MS Excel/PowerPoint

* Ability to build predictive models

WORKING CONDITIONS

* Dynamic, fast-paced office environment
