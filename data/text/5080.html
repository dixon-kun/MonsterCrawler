Director of Software Engineering - Telephony

The Job

Our engineering team solves tough problems and invents new ways to battle fraud using big data and audio science in the cloud.  We are looking for a Director of Software Engineering on the Engineering team to help define, design, lead and manage the creation of our bleeding edge products.  We have technically challenging problems to be solved and must demonstrate thought leadership in the security space, so we need a leader who can execute our strategy on a daily basis by inspiring and growing a high caliber software engineering team.

Responsibilities

  • Develop, mentor and coach a diverse team to continue to deliver high-quality, innovative products in a fast paced, entrepreneurial environment
  • Lead by example, care for your team and establish credibility with the quality of your and your team's technical execution
  • Continuous improvement in process and implementing controls to increase efficiency and productivity of engineering practices
  • Implement and measure engineering best practices and metrics
  • Improve the team’s technical ability, utilization, and overall efficiency
  • Collaborate closely with other organizations to foster an environment of teamwork throughout Pindrop
  • Responsible for evolving the architecture of the solution and developing a comprehensive technical implementation plan.

Education and Experience

  • BS in CS or EE. Masters in an engineering discipline preferred
  • 5+ years as a technical manager or director of software engineering in the Telecom domain
  • Linux experience a must
  • Preferred self-starter ideally with some startup experience with a commercial software company
  • Experience engineering distributed cloud-based solution
  • Telecom domain knowledge (SIP, VOIP, IP, SS7 etc…)
  • Python and C++ experience preferred
  • Software architecture and patterns, including Software as a Service and API development
  • Strong collaboration skills with disciplines like Test Engineering, Product Management, and Customer Success
  • Building out engineering systems including project management, source control, and quality
  • Proven track record of successful and timely product delivery, and of driving technical innovation
  • Experience developing and presenting information to executive-level audiences
  • Scrum/Agile methodologies knowledge helpful
  • Experience with Cloud-based solutions preferred
  • Ability to deal with ambiguity in a fast-paced dynamic environment
  • Managerial skills and ability to grow people to their true potential
  • Strong communication skills, both inward and outward

Perks at Pindrop

We hire good people and take care of them . Pindrop believes in fostering an environment where employees don’t just come to work hard, but play hard. We host regular game nights, team challenges, and offsite events (not to mention the snacks and catered meals) to keep everyone involved in our company culture. We appreciate new ideas any and everywhere.

Keep learning. Pindrop offers regular Lunch & Learns and Tech Talks that promote an environment for people to grow outside of their job title. Want to learn to code with minimal experience? Come to our Intro to Python Tech Talk. Interested in March Madness? See how data science can help you beat the bracket at our Data Science in the Real World Lunch & Learn. In addition to these events, we promote a continued education budget for each of our employees to grow and improve in tandem with the company.

Our employees are creative, excited, and above all a team. Pindrop is one of the fastest growing security startups in the country, but we make sure to hire only the best of the best. Better yet, every employee is passionate about the company and our mission. (Can you blame us?) When you put that passion in a collaborative setting it unites everyone as a team.

Fighting criminals is in the job description. No matter which facet of Pindrop you want to work for, we are all working towards the same goal-- making sure the bad guys don’t win.

Our Bio

Founded in 2011  |  Raised $122 million

Venture backed by Andreessen Horowitz, Google Capital, IVP, Citi Ventures, Felicis Ventures, & GV

Pindrop is the pioneer in voice fraud prevention and authentication. We provide enterprise solutions to reduce fraud losses and authentication expense for some of the largest call centers in the world. Pindrop’s patented Phoneprinting™ technology can identify, locate and authenticate phone devices uniquely just from the call audio thereby detecting fraudulent calls as well as verifying legitimate callers. We have been selected by the world’s largest banks, insurers, brokerages, and retailers, detecting over 80% of fraud even for attackers never seen before. Pindrop’s solutions are allowing customers to reduce call time and improve their customers’ experience even while reducing fraud losses.
