Inmar Overview:

Anyone who has redeemed a coupon, filled a prescription or returned a product, has touched Inmar. We apply technology and data science to improve outcomes for consumers and those who serve them. As a trusted intermediary for over 35 years, we have unmatched access to billions of consumer and business transactions in real time. Our analytics, platforms and services enable engagement with shoppers and patients, and optimize results.


Position Summary:

The Business Development Executive at Inmar has a hunter’s instinct. Drawing from extensive success in previous selling roles, this seasoned, top-performing pro knows how to target prospects, cultivate relationships, close deals and aggressively drive revenue. The Business Development Executive is adept at continuously learning new concepts, services and products, and conveying them in a compelling way to their targets.

The Business Development Executive s ells Digital Promotion solutions for manufacturers to existing and potential clients; fields in-coming sales calls; maintains sales database and generates analysis reports; maintains sales resource library; and performs product presentations.  Manage a fast self-paced work day while completing revenue generating tasks as well as prioritizing daily responsibilities.  Must meet deadlines and assigned quotas that are set by the manager. The incumbent ensures the company's business relationships with targeted accounts are effectively cultivated; makes presentations and recommendations to potential Clients’ management representatives; negotiates pricing and legal agreements; and prepares detailed correspondence.

Inmar’s client list is a “Who’s Who” of the largest and best-known – as well as the most innovative emerging –  brands, retailers and health systems. This position will target prospects and sell technology and data analytics solutions within Inmar’s Promotion Network – including digital and paper coupon settlement, digital and paper rebates, analytics, mobile, trade promotions and promotion consulting .

Major Responsibilities/Essential Functions:

  • Performs outbound cold calls to generate leads/opportunities.
  • Receives/responds to/re-directs sales call-ins.
  • Maintains sales database and generates special reporting.
  • Performs product/solutions presentations.
  • Develop senior level strategic relationships.
  • Leverage Inmar’s market position to drive strategic solutions in a complex sales environment.
  • Sell software/consultative solutions in a big box retail environment or CPG manufacturer environment.
  • Create/develop innovative networking skills to drive pipeline development at the decision making level.
  • Create and deliver value based solution pricing.
  • Performs other duties as assigned.

Minimum Requirements:

  • A Bachelor’s degree is strongly preferred.
  • Digital/Media experience required.
  • Generally requires 5-10 or more years of demonstrated successful experience in solutions sales or successful demonstration of the other requirements indicated below.
  • Broad experience in team sales and/or team solution development.
  • Experience in team sales and/or team solution development.
  • Experience selling business process outsourcing deals involving executive level decision making across Operations, Finance / Accounting, Logistics, IT, etc.
  • Successful experience chasing, cultivating and closing relationship based deals.
  • Experience in selling a non-commoditized, non-required products from a leading market position.
  • Successful in understanding strategy, positioning and building relationships.
  • Experience in developing and subsequently winning solutions which are highly competitive.
  • Team player who has strengths to cultivate internal relationships as well as external.
  • Understanding of a balance sheet, income statement and inventory “ins” and “outs’ as they impact a financial statement.
  • Ability to travel up 60% of the time.
  • Ability to work very autonomously and manage presentations and strategy associated with sales as well as internal communications garnering support and getting appropriate buy-in with little to no assistance from their Sales Leader.
