ADP is hiring a Senior Product Manager to work on solutions supporting the MNC product portfolio including Global Payroll, HCM Products, Time, Cloud HCM Integration and Analytics. In this position, you'll be responsible for combining business and product strategy to ensure the development and execution of the product is in line with the product road map and vision that the senior product owner sets forth. RESPONSIBILITIES: 
*  Support the senior product owner's strategy by articulating the vision and mission of the product roadmap, including implementation and prioritization 
*  As product owner, manage execution for a specific product from product definition and planning through production, release, and end of life 
*  Develop user stories and requirements while evaluating their potential and practicality by analyzing market and customer needs 
*  Serve as the central resource with design, process, test, quality and marketing as the product(s) move to production and distribution 
*  Act as user champion when coordinating product development with business stakeholder and align all development activity with business goals and priorities
