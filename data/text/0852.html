Software Engineering Manager (Ruby)

Averity - New York, NY 10016

Posted: 3/22/2017

We need a Software Engineering Manager to manage a team of 4 Ruby Engineers in house in NYC..

We build a predictive analytics tool used by some of the largest brands in publishing across the web. Our core API is built with Ruby on Rails. We have a team of 4 solid Ruby Developers in house in NYC, a team of 6 in Portugal and another person working remotely in San Diego. You will be responsible for the team in NYC.

You can expect to still be hands on about 50% of the time; however, the majority of the time will be spent at an architect and manager level role. This will include performance management, code reviews and general problem solving. We need someone that knows how to take on problems and execute on a solution. The important attribute you’ll be using is good judgment on why you are taking the angle you take.

Our technology stack doesn’t include an RDBMS, but we need someone that knows databases well.

What is our technology stack?

  • Languages: Ruby/Rails, Ember.JS, Python, Go

  • Databases: Cassandra, Postgres, Redis, Solr, Vertica

  • Other: Spark, NSQ, Titan, Chef, Kibana

Who are we?

Our software that tracks content in real-time across the social web. We collect and analyze over 3 billion points of data each day for content creation, organic sharing and paid distribution. Our products are used by massive content producers like publishers, agencies and independent brands. We’ve hired a crazy smart data science team which has crunched the numbers, and built algorithms to identify trending content as it takes off.

We track over 40 Million articles across 7000 sites and use that data to understand exactly how any single article will trend. Being a big data company means that we store every share, click and page view for all your content. Want to know which author wrote the most tweeted articles in the politics section last week? We do that and a whole lot more.

What else makes us different?

You'll be in a total transparent environment! We have a Town Hall meeting every Tuesday filling in the company on where things are going, how we are doing financially and what directions we are trending towards. On Thursdays, you can join in our lunch and learn sessions with the team. Everyone gets a chance to share and teach our team about a cool skill you want to share.

Finding the right fit is far more important to us than the salary we’re offering. We’re assertive in offering the right packages to keep our employees incredibly happy. We have a huge new office space with plenty of room to move around including stand-up desks.

If this interest you, feel free to reach out to Chris Allaire from Averity directly.
