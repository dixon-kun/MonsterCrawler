We are currently seeking talented Business Information Architect for our San Antonio, TX facility.

Incumbents are responsible for the development and implementation of the information architecture to support the measuring and monitoring of enterprise business needs. Collaborates with the architectural communities and other information management activities. Influences and drives awareness of information strategy and architecture to the enterprise. Maintains the design around the full set of Enterprise information capabilities that support existing and strategic business capabilities.

Job Requirements

* Leads the performance of regular assessments of the health and maturity of information capabilities for a given domain.

* Applies expert knowledge to identify new information capabilities needed to support business capabilities of a given business domain. Maintains the information capability repository with these identified needs

* Leads efforts in defining the mission, goals, critical success factors, principles, policies and Statements of Direction for information strategy and information architecture. Accountable for achieving business objectives and results.

* Partners with the Business Information Architects and other technical and business architects to enable the development of the USAA Integrated Information Capability Roadmap. Provides guidance to cross-functional team members to ensure goal achievement and team success.

* Partners with key stakeholders to build the business cases required to secure approval and funding for the Data and Analytical efforts needed to support the Information Capability Roadmap. Advises and makes recommendations to senior management

* Leads the development and maintenance of a conceptual model for information capabilities for a given domain. Understands end to end change impact by managing linkages from information capabilities to technical assets (operational + analytical).

* Leads the development and maintenance of an integrated logical data model to understand data and its inter-dependencies regardless of its usage pattern. Provides consultation and guidance regarding the usage of the enterprise integrated logical model for use in development. Sets the direction regarding the appropriate data sources to be used in solution development and delivery.

Minimum Requirements

* Bachelor's Degree in Information Architecture, Information Management, Information and Data Science, Information Studies or a related technical field. Four years work experience in information management or related experience can be substituted in lieu of a degree in addition to the minimum years of work experience required OR A Master's Degree in Information Architecture, Information Management, Information and Data Science, Information Studies or a related technical field OR A PhD in Information Architecture, Information Management, Information and Data Science, Information Studies or a related technical field.

* If Bachelor's degree, 8+ years experience in information and/or data architecture to include strategy, planning, and delivery oversight, 3+ years of direct and/or indirect management experience OR If Master's degree, 6+ years experience in information and/or data architecture to include strategy, planning, and delivery oversight, 3+ years of direct and/or indirect management experience OR If PhD, 5+ years experience in information and/or data architecture to include strategy, planning, and delivery oversight, 3+ years of direct and/or indirect management experience

* Qualifications may warrant placement in a different job level.*

When you apply for this position, you will be required to answer some initial questions. This will take approximately 5 minutes. Once you begin the questions you will not be able to finish them at a later time and you will not be able to change your responses.

Preferred

* 8+ years experience in information and/or data architecture to include strategy, planning, and delivery oversight.

* Expertise in analyzing business requirements and translating those into data and information specifications.

* Expertise in building information target states, information blue prints, understanding information gaps, and building information strategies and roadmaps.

Knowledge/Skills/Attributes

* Expert in the practices of information architecture and information strategy

* Proven ability to translate business capability into information capability

* Proven ability to think creatively and to work well both as part of a team and as an individual contributor

* Ability to understand how information capabilities and domains enable business strategy

* Proven ability to flex communication to technical and non-technical audiences, at different levels (senior management to IT developers)

* Proven ability to articulate complex business and information/data concepts for general consumption

* Expert in developing information roadmaps, and align information needs to business capabilities and business processes

* Proven ability to influence project teams and senior decision makers on the direction of information strategy/information architecture

* Proven ability to anticipate opportunities and potential solutions, and leads team through the problem resolution process.

* Proven ability to develop deep insights that consistently influence and drive action with the most senior Business Executives by using factbased,

holistic and forward-looking perspectives.

* Proven ability to lead a team of analyst; train and review analysts work; assist the manager in assigning tasks and managing workflow.
