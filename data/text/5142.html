Sr. Manager – Digital Platform Activation
We are currently seeking a Sr. Manager – Digital Platform Activation for the team in Atlanta, GA. This position will act as the business owner for owned digital platforms and the Georgia-Pacific consumer database (internally called Consumer Data Driven Marketing or CDDM). This role will lead strategy, activation and measurement of these consumer data-driven marketing efforts across brands with support from the internal capability teams and external agency partners. This position reports to the Director of Media and Marketing Technology.

A Day In The Life Typically Includes:

  • Act as a change agent for Consumer Data-Driven marketing efforts, crafting vision through execution of program.

  • Act as the business owner for digital platforms such as Sitecore, Silverpop and consumer database.
  • Lead brand strategic planning and experience design for use of owned channels, understand overall business implications, and represent marketing needs for all requirements, insights and recommendations.
  • Develop frameworks and operating principles for brand owned channels (websites, email, etc), taking into consideration business requirements and technical architecture.
  • Lead data acquisition strategy to grow known and unknown 1 st party data, as supported by media and consumer promotions teams.
  • Support data activation management and governance, in partnership with internal governance and IT teams, to ensure the right data is being used the right way at the right price.
  • Provide strategic support to brand teams in how to leverage full Consumer Data Driven Marketing (CDDM) capabilities in brand activation across platforms.
  • Leverage technology, data science and analytics to identify areas for improvements and drive implementation of these strategies and innovations into actionable marketing campaign
  • Collaborate with internal partners and external agencies to activate and measure plans.
  • Provide knowledge share on CDDM activations.
  • Develop long term CDDM experimentation plan that delivers on objectives.
  • Own management of how PII is collected, maintained and analyzed.
  • Manager of email marketing manager and external agency support.

What You Will Need:


Required Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in marketing, digital communications, IT or equivalent.
  • 7+ years progressive digital channel marketing (customer acquisition, lifecycle, brand, retention)
  • 5+ years of email marketing experience
  • Experience working with cross functional teams
  • Proficiency in marketing automation technology and analytics tools
  • Experience in IT architecture design, database management systems and systems integration in multi-platform environments.

What May Put You Ahead?


Preferred Qualifications:

  • CPG experience
  • Retail or shopper marketing experience
  • Marketing analytics knowledge
  • Silverpop ESP
  • Sitecore CMS
  • Other: Google Analytics, DMP, SQL and Tableau

Knowledge - Skills-Abilities:

  • Courage to conduct all affairs with integrity and strive for full compliance with laws and regulations.
  • Demonstrated ability to create short- and long-term value for consumers, shoppers, customers and company
  • Ability to operate like a principled entrepreneur, who takes initiative, applies economic thinking and good judgment, operates with a sense of urgency and takes responsibility.
  • Develop relationships with internal customers (brands, shopper marketing, eCommerce) and capabilities to understand their value, contributions and profitably anticipate their needs.
    • Ability to apply subject matter expertise of digital and data-driven marketing to manage projects from concept to completion.
    • Strong background in analyzing data, A/B testing and segmentation.
  • Act with humility and respect.
  • Possesses strong written and verbal communication, project management and presentation skills.

We're part of your everyday life. From the walls of your home to the Dixie® plates on your table, the boxes at your local grocery store or the Quilted Northern® in your bathroom, there's a good chance a Georgia-Pacific product is nearby. We need people like you: People who can help us make life's simple necessities better.
Georgia-Pacific and its subsidiaries are among the world's leading manufacturers and marketers of tissue, paper-based packaging, office papers, cellulose, specialty fibers, non-woven fabrics, building products and related chemicals. Our familiar consumer brands include: Quilted Northern®, Angel Soft®, Brawny®, enMotion®, Sparkle®, Mardi Gras®, and Vanity Fair®, as well as the Dixie® brand of disposable cups, plates and cutlery. Its Harmon subsidiary is among the world's largest recyclers of paper, metal and plastics. The company employs approximately 35,000 people directly.
