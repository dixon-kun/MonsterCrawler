        Optoro is a fast-growing tech company that is revolutionizing the retail industry. Every year, more than 15% of retail goods are returned or simply never sell. This creates tons of unnecessary waste and costs retailers billions. We provide a technology solution that keeps these items out of landfills and connects them to their best use, increasing recovery value, giving consumers great deals, and reducing environmental waste.
        Backed by some of the top investors in the country, including Kleiner Perkins and Revolution Growth, Optoro is powered by its collaborative, unconventional, and resourceful employees. We are looking for individuals with similar creativity and energy to help build a lasting company focused on the triple bottom line.
        The Senior Manager, Product Content &amp; Pricing, reporting to the VP of Data, is responsible for Optoro's Product Catalog functions, which include customer-facing content, product (inventory) pricing, automation, and vendor management.
        The Senior Manager plays a cross-functional role coordinating across the business to understand requirements, set expectations and priorities with other departments, and ensure execution within the team.
        The Senior Manager is responsible for the accuracy, speed, cost, and coverage of product content &amp; pricing for all inventory. The Senior Manager provides guidance and support to on- and off-site teams responsible for content, pricing, and automation. The Senior Manager is responsible for monitoring team and individual key performance indicators, including marketplace coverage.
        Responsibilities
        * Oversee the Product Catalog teams responsible for content, pricing, automation, and vendor management.
        * Drive automated content &amp; pricing solutions through Product Management, Technology, and Data Science teams
        * Develop innovative processes to ensure the teams are operating at peak efficiency
        * Monitor and report on KPIs: speed, accuracy, cost, and coverage
        * Interface with Business Operations, Client Success, and eCommerce departments to establish and monitor client expectations as they relate to Product Catalog
        * Assist direct reports with establishing weekly priorities based on incoming inventory requirements
        * Monitor marketplace coverage to take advantage of all remarketing opportunities
        * Ensure the Product Catalog teams are accountable for their performance, including but not limited to quality, volume, and time/attendance
        * Hold regular one-on-one meetings with direct reports to assist in their professional development and communicate performance stats
        * Conduct quarterly performance check-ins with direct reports
        * Act as the subject matter expert for Product Catalog and the related requirements for marketplace remarketing
        * Other duties as may be assigned from time to time by your manager
        Qualifications
        * A Bachelor's degree or equivalent work experience
        * 5+ years leading teams of exempt &amp; non-exempt staff
        * Previous eCommerce, retail, or catalog / merchandising experience
        * Ability to communicate / coordinate across departments
        * Ability to identify, prioritize, and communicate workloads to the team
        * Intrinsic motivation to improve processes, elevate the team
        * Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
        * Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
        * Excellent verbal and written communication skills
        * Ability to write basic SQL preferred
        * Experience with analytics and reporting software preferred
