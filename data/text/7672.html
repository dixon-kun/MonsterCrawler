eCommerce Product Manager

Capital One's Digital Commerce team is solving big problems. We were the first company to develop and offer mass customization and personalization of credit card products, and we have been innovating relentlessly ever since. Today through our Digital team, we are a high-tech company, a scientific laboratory, and a nationally recognized brand all in one that reaches fifty+ millions of consumers. We are a passionate and entrepreneurial team that embraces bold ideas, fosters collaboration and delivers for product leaders to build out the next generation of digital experiences and lead the company through the next stage of growth led by technological innovations. You will collaborate with Designers, Developers and others across the business to invent features and experiences that make Capital One even more engaging for both dealers and consumers.

Wielding right and left brains in equal proportion, digital product managers at Capital One are committed to delivering great product experiences for our customers. If you have the drive for building amazing digital products that impact the lives of millions daily, we want you!

We're looking for passionate, energetic, self-driven technologists to work as Product Managers in our eCommerce product group. To fit in, you have to be jazzed about delivering amazingly simple experiences that make life better for our customers. You will be able to apply your deep knowledge of web and eCommerce to deliver products that deliver breakthrough experiences.
