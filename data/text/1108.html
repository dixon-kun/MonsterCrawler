About the Job


Title: Data Scientist
Location: Minneapolis/St. Paul area
Employment type: Contract. Local canddiates only.

Duties and Responsibilities:
Extract, cleanse, and combine data from multiple sources and systems
clustering, correlation, linear/logistic regression, time series modeling, and others
Effectively communicate technical analyses and results to business management
Perform exploratory and targeted analyses, with a wide variety of statistical methods, including k-means
Develop and continually improve multi-source data architecture and analytics procedures, systems, workflows

Required Skills:
Four-year degree in mathematics, computer science, business or equivalent technical degree, Masters degree preferred
5+ years of experience and increasing responsibility in advanced statistical analysis and predictive modeling
Experience with a majority of the following tools: SQL, R, Python, Tableau, Alteryx, Amazon Web Services Suite

Please reach out to taylor.mccormick@experis.com if you are interested
