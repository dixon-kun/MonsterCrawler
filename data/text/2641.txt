        About the Agency
        This position will be involved in the Big Data Analytics and Machine Intelligence capability team of the Comprehensive Digital Transformation initiative supporting Langley's strategic goal of infusing transformational technologies and tools for enhancing NASA's Technical Excellence, innovation and mission success. This position is responsible for researching, planning, designing and leading the implementation of innovative and cutting-edge big data, machine learning and data analytics solutions for NASA specific challenges. Works in partnership with research, science, and engineering customers contributing directly to the NASA mission success.
        This position is included within a specialty that involves the application of computer science, machine learning, statistics, mathematics, numerical analysis, and data analysis techniques for the solution of aerospace, aeronautical, space science and/or earth science problems.
        Position may be filled at either the GS-11, GS-12 or GS-13 level. The full performance level for this position is GS-13. If selected at the lower levels, the employee may be non-competitively promoted to the next grade when qualified and recommended by management. Identification of promotion potential in this position does not constitute a commitment or an obligation on the part of management to promote the employee.
        This position has a Selective Placement Factor (screen-out) which will also be used to determine minimum qualifications for this position. Applicants not possessing this Selective Placement Factor will be found ineligible for the position. The Selective Placement Factor is programming experience and expertise in at least two of the following: Python, MATLAB, R, Tensor Flow, Theano, Caffe, OpenDL, or Torch. Those without this experience specified in their resume will be found not qualified for this position.
        To receive consideration, you must submit a resume and answer NASA-specific questions. The NASA questions appear after you submit your resume and are transferred to the NASA web site. If you successfully apply, USAJOBS will show your application status as 'Received'. If your status is 'Application Status Not Available', you have not successfully applied. Do not rely on a USAJOBS email to confirm successful application. Only an email from NASA confirms a successful application.
        Duties
        At the full performance level, the incumbent leads design, development and implementation of innovative big data and data mining solutions to help NASA Langley scientists, researchers and engineers perform their jobs better by helping them analyze and mine scientific information, and computational and experimental data. The key tasks involved are, development, application and integration of data reduction, analysis and visualization software, and development and implementation of methods for capturing and converting aerospace data into forms and formats that can be analyzed and reported. The main goal is to analyze and make sense of complex scientific and engineering structured and unstructured data for patterns, correlations, and insights not possible otherwise and using visualization techniques to translate results into easily consumable form. Leads the development and application of big data and analytical tools and platforms such as Python, R, MATLAB, Weka, JAVA, Hadoop, MapReduce, IWatson Analytics technologies, SPSS, SAS and others in developing a big data analytics/mining capability with innovative machine learning and statistical algorithms for NASA Langley. Integrates technical, user, and operational requirements for helping to design solutions for aerospace information and data discovery challenges. Translates customer requirements into working prototypes and systems on an agile, iterative basis. Adapts methodologies and technologies as needed for problem resolution.
        Keeps abreast with emerging technologies and developments in the area of big data, data mining and machine learning in all domains and recommends possibilities and applicability of these techniques to NASA problems. Keeps up-to-date with the various Universities, Industry and government sponsored research in these areas and actively participates in these initiatives to help NASA take advantage of the new technologies and tools resulting from these initiatives. Recommends and provides input regarding how NASA can use big data and machine intelligence techniques in anticipation of future NASA missions. Plans and conducts experimental studies involving several engineering disciplines that result in new design guidelines, and have a profound effect on program or mission capability and operating procedures. Documents and reports study results to management and contractors. Serves as an information resource to researchers within the laboratory regarding big data mining technologies and their use.
        Accomplishes varied and complex project management duties in conjunction with leading project teams and making decisions affecting areas of complex responsibility. Works with team members to ensure the development and implementation of big data analytics software and digital technologies are on time and within budget. Serves as a technical specialist and a key source of information for assigned areas of responsibility. Maintains liaison with scientists and engineers who are experts in related fields. Leads studies and assessments to evaluate the effectiveness and efficiency of new technologies involving big data analytics and machine learning and current systems capabilities and gaps, and recommends improvements to management.
