Job Description

We are looking for a Product Manager to help lead efforts to create the next generation of intelligent platforms that leverage machine learning, DNA Science, and Big Data to unlock new value for our customers . In this role, you will work closely with a cross-functional team of product, engineering, data science, and DNA science leaders to build platfroms for a range of products that help a global audience learn more about themselves through discovering who thy are, where they’ve come from, and how they’re connected to the world around them. These platforms leverage Ancestry’s unparalleled Big Data assets to unlock the facts, stories, and relationships that make people unique in each of our member’s trees. This position requires a product manager with proven skills that demonstrate technical understanding of Big Data and Machine Learning, experience in working with, and an ability to drive and deliver quality solutions.

As a Product team, we’re passionate about our users and about building products that delight and engage. Our Product Managers lead through understanding areas of product opportunity, creating a vision, validating that vision through rapid prototyping, measurement, and research, then partnering across the team to bring your vision to market.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Drive continual product improvements in services, with a focus on creating services that securely expose systems and data about member’s ancestors
  • Identify opportunities for improving platforms with improved performance, security, and privacy
  • Author product stories, tasks and acceptance criteria for scrum teams; own the backlog
  • Partner with other product managers and engineers to understand the platform service needs and identify the appropriate deliverables and SLAs for those services.
  • Work with cross-platform teams across the organization to make sure their needs are understood and considered as part of your product roadmap
  • Track, analyze, and report on key metrics
  • Work with Data Science and DNA Science teams to incorporate intelligence into our platforms and provide data assets to them for analysis
Qualifications

  • Minimum of 1-2 years of interactive consumer focused, online web product management experience, i.e. gaming, education technology, finance or similar products in the consumer space
  • Bachelor's degree, ideally computer science, mathematics, or similar discipline. Alternatively, equivalent experience in software development is also acceptable
  • Smart, creative, driven and able to deliver results quickly in autonomous environment
  • Self-starter, highly organized, and able to work well with individuals at all levels and across functions
  • Driven to succeed by the company’s core mission to help people discover, preserve and share their family history
  • Strong organizational and product management skills
  • Effective communications skills (written and oral), presentation techniques, meeting mechanics, and team building skills
  • Excellent quantitative and analytical skills
  • Willing and able to travel occasionally
  • Background in and understanding of data science and machine learning with some applied experience
  • Passionate about family history a plus



Additional Information

We’re a cutting-edge tech company with a very human mission—to help every person discover, preserve, and share the story of what led to them. Combining the rich information in family trees and historical records with the genetic details revealed in DNA, we create unique experiences that give people a new understanding of their lives, because connecting all the pieces of our family story can give us the deepest sense of who we are.
