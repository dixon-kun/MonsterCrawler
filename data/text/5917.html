SUMMARY

Hulu’s Product Management team is seeking a Senior Manager, Testing and Optimization who will be an exceptional addition to our Subscriber Growth Product Management team. As a Senior Manager of Testing and Optimization at Hulu, you will lead a team that proactively seeks, identifies, and evaluates innovative opportunities to acquire users and grow our subscriber base, relying on data to drive your decision-making. The right person for this role is either a growth marketer with a strong product sense or a product manager with a bent for optimization. If you are someone who is ambitious and incredibly analytical with a proven track record of delivering growth, then this is a great role for you.

WHAT YOU’LL DO

  • Identify and develop requirements for tools needed to enable testing and optimization teams to execute and deliver results
  • Manage, coach, set goals and grow high performing team members leveraging their strengths
  • Use your deep knowledge of testing and optimization to build, design, A/B and MVT test, implement and analyze improvements to sign-up funnels and acquisition flows across all Hulu digital experiences, with an emphasis on mobile and living room platforms and marketing channels
  • Work extremely closely with the senior managers of Product, Engineering, Data Science, FPandA, and Metrics teams to project optimizations and analyze results, generate insights, and build future strategies and product features
  • Work with the UX team to ideate on and launch innovative customer experiences and features
  • Partner with other stakeholder departments to create data-driven customer acquisition strategies leveraging customer experiences and value propositions such as video content, multiple device support, and new product features
  • Collaborate closely with the User Acquisition Marketing team to identify and tune off-channel marketing efforts to tie to on-channel acquisition experiences
  • Partner with the Subscriber Growth Product team to develop a prioritized roadmap and backlog focused on advancing Hulu’s subscriber growth objectives
  • Generate business and product requirements to increase value to customers and partner teams

WHAT TO BRING

  • 8+ years of technical product management or equivalent experience
  • 5+ years of experience in people management and team leadership
  • 5+ years collaborative work with marketing teams on acquisition and reacquisition related efforts
  • Bachelor’s degree in a technical discipline or equivalent technical experience
  • Extraordinary collaboration capabilities and willingness to accept and incorporate feedback
  • Be a team player with character, high humility and integrity, helping build a healthy team environment
  • Proven experience with conversion rate optimization, setting up and utilizing A/B tests using Optimizely or similar tools, and optimizing funnels across multiple device categories
  • Ability to interact and communicate effectively with a diverse set of technical and non-technical stakeholders
  • Exceptional written, verbal, interpersonal, and presentation communication skills

NICE-TO-HAVES

  • Demonstrated experience across multiple device categories within a subscription business model
  • Passion for television, Streaming Video On Demand (SVOD), or Over the Top (OTT) industry
