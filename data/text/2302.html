Who We Are

Our mission is to invent the future of farming. We're transforming the oldest and most important industry in the world from "gut instinct" to data science. Our platform already exposes critical operational data insights to 1 out of every 3 farms in the U.S. and our users absolutely love us.

Who You Are

You got into recruiting because you loved people and learning new things. You're comfortable talking to just about anyone and you love connecting people with their dream opportunity. You're incredibly hard-working, organized, and drive for results in both yourself and in your team. You enjoy leading and managing others because of what the work gives back to you. You're excited about the opportunity of building - of coming into an environment that doesn't yet have all the kinks worked out. You're incredibly flexible and adaptable; you actually find comfort in ambiguous situations because it allows you the opportunity to dig in and problem-solve in a more creative way. Most of all, you love growing your team and building a high-performing recruiting function.

About the Role

We're looking to build a data-driven recruiting machine and as our recruiting manager, you'll be responsible for taking our recruiting function to the next level. You'll be developing and overseeing our recruiting and recruitment marketing programs, referral programs and community presence from the ground up.

Tasks You'll Be Juggling

* Provide day-to-day management and career development for recruiting team

* Prioritize recruiting workload; allocate recruiter and manager time to appropriate positions

* Work with leadership to develop proactive hiring plans in accordance with business needs

* Provide oversight to interview and recruiting process to ensure roles, responsibilities, and performance objectives are clearly defined

* Recruit for leadership positions

* Further define, document and advocate for FarmLogs' recruiting and interviewing philosophy

* Develop and document processes for other managers and teams to interact with recruiting

* Support hiring & recruiting strategy at the position level

* Implement organizational recruiting process and advocate for recruiting team and philosophies

* Lead diversity recruiting strategy initiatives and assign responsibilities to recruiting team

* Provide outward communication to rest of organization regarding recruiting process and strategy

* Be a champion for a great candidate experience, recruiting, and interview process with managers and employees across the organization

* Track and report on recruiting metrics for the organization

* Develop strategy for recruiting events, recruitment marketing & evaluate ROI

Your Creds

Required

* 2+ years of people management experience over a recruiting team

* Demonstrated ability to lead and manage a strategic recruiting function

* Demonstrated ability to build a well-refined, metrics-driven recruiting process

* Some knowledge of HR process

* Prior in-house technical recruiting experience

* Flexible and adaptable approach to recruiting; you know when process is important and when it needs to be adjusted

* Ability to work effectively with different types of individuals

* Strong written and verbal communication skills

Nice to Have

* Interest or background in agriculture

* Previous startup experience
