         Newell Brands is a leading global consumer goods company with a strong portfolio of well-known brands, including Paper Mate, Sharpie, EXPO, Parker, Elmers, Calphalon, Rubbermaid, Graco, Baby Jogger, Aprica, Goody, Irwin, Lenox, Rubbermaid Commercial Products, Coleman, First Alert, FoodSaver, Jostens, K2, NUK, Oster, Rawlings, Sunbeam and Yankee Candle. Newell Brands and its subsidiaries are Equal Opportunity Employers and comply with applicable employment laws. EOE/M/F/Vet/Disabled are encouraged to apply.
         Serves as the consumer&#8217;s advocate. Mines consumer and campaign data and derives insights that identify opportunities for CRM activity across all brands. CRM Analysts work with the CRM Analytics Manager and CRM team to provide actionable insights that enable data-informed decision-making for campaign development/refinement, segmentation and performance analysis.
         Job Responsibilities:
         CRM Strategy &amp; Execution
         &#8226; Able to gain insight into target brand consumers and global positioning in order to recommend CRM initiatives that align to these.
         &#8226; Understands consumer journeys and how to leverage data to create dialogues and nurture relationships over time.
         &#8226; Understands how to analyze and derive insights from expressed and implied brand preferences, lifestyle interests, and communication preferences &#8211; aggregate, cluster and individual subscriber levels.
         &#8226; Understands business rules to define contact strategies and prioritization of communications across multiple brands.
         &#8226; Experienced deriving insights from data sets across channels (email, social, web, mobile, text, etc).
         &#8226; Understands the platform database structure (enterprise, business units, data extensions, suppressions, etc).
         &#8226; Understands universal control groups &#8211; business rules and how to measure differential performance over time.
         &#8226; Understands engagement and success metrics across channels.
         &#8226; Understands tagging and tracking campaign activity and attribution across channels.
         &#8226; Experienced identifying data-driven, statistically-significant opportunities for campaign development.
         &#8226; Maintains active data collection points, retires fields that are no longer actionable, and refreshes actionable opportunities on a regular schedule.
         &#8226; Demonstrated experience with data science models and algorithms, especially applied to CRM/Consumer data.
         &#8226; Experienced with business intelligence and data mining tools (ex: Qlik, Tableau, SAS, R, Power BI, etc.).
         &#8226; Experienced with pulling a concise and compelling story out of the data to articulate the key analysis of a strategy, project or test.
         Activation
         &#8226; Works with the CRM team to package data-informed recommendations to brand teams.
         &#8226; Lives daily in the dashboards to assess campaign performance, capture lessons learned, recommend campaign pivots, and identify opportunities for developing new campaigns.
         &#8226; Audits all data collection points and documents how the data is actionable, when statistical significant volumes have been reached, what initiative(s) were activated, and how the initiative(s) performed.
         &#8226; Implements and tracks test and learn opportunities.
         &#8226; Supports delivery of campaign reports within a week of deployment with key learnings and recommendations outlined.
         &#8226; Supports Analytics Manager with packaging key insights and dashboard summaries for executive review and consumption on a regular schedule.
         Insights
         &#8226; Applies understanding of the consumer and the brand to both short and long-term decisions. Links independent insight and observations to form a holistic understanding of the consumer. Translates marketplace insights into winning CRM strategies and tactics for the brand.
         &#8226; Informs marketing metrics, key brand, and quality measures to track and understand the underlying drivers and interaction of each metric.
         &#8226; Supports Analytics Manager with completing CRM ROI analysis on projects as needed.
         &#8226; Develops division knowledge across multiple brands.
