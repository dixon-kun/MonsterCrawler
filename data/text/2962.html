The Threat Intelligence Analyst will serve as a member of the Information Security and Compliance organization and will directly contribute to establishing and maintaining a broad and deep view of emerging cyber threats and risks to the organization. Through innovation, attention to detail, and a constant drive to enhance capabilities to prepare, detect, and respond to security challenges, the Threat Intelligence Analyst will drive meaningful and actionable information to protect Hilton Worldwide's global hotel operations and assets.

What will I be doing?

The Threat Intelligence Analyst will serve as a member of the Cyber Threat Protection team focused on curating and delivering meaningful and actionable cyber threat intelligence for consumption throughout the Hilton Worldwide organization. The Team Member will be primarily responsible for analyzing and managing information related to the organization's cyber security posture on a continuous basis.

Other key priorities are listed below:

  • Responsible for reviewing and analyzing open-source and subscription based cyber threat intelligence and applying it to protect Hilton Worldwide's assets and reputation

  • Lead and oversee efforts to assimilate derived and direct intelligence into practical and applicable information through qualitative and quantitative analysis

  • Will be a key contributor to reports, charts, presentations, and briefings to update company leadership on the changing cyber threat landscape and impact to Hilton Worldwide’s security posture

  • The research and analysis performed by this position will be leveraged to update security event monitoring and sharpen defences across the global information security program

  • Help to continually manage and support the global physical and digital footprints for the organization

  • Strong analytical and critical thinking skills

  • Able to apply qualitative and quantitative approaches to work to reduce large, complex problems to refined intelligence artifacts

  • Must be a self-starter and able to work independently as well as within a team setting

  • Strong oral and written communication skills are essential

  • Strong statistical, data sciences, analytical, big data, or math skills are desired

  • Ability to speak multiple language is a plus

What are we looking for?

Since being founded in 1919, Hilton Worldwide has been a leader in the hospitality industry. Today, Hilton Worldwide remains a beacon of innovation, quality, and success. This continued leadership is the result of our Team Members staying true to our Vision, Mission, and Values. To fulfill this role successfully, you must possess the following minimum qualifications and experience:

  • High School Degree/GED

  • A minimum of three (3) years of IT Security experience

  • Experience identifying, obtaining, and managing large data sets of disparate intelligence

  • Experience applying intelligence gathering constructs to technical and non-technical data sets

  • Experience performing quantitative analysis of intelligence data to support prioritization of resources

  • Experience qualitatively and quantitatively assessing security posture based on applied intelligence information

  • Ability to identify and utilize analytical skills and/or tools to quickly process and refine information into actionable intelligence

  • Ability to use Microsoft Excel and other tools for statistical analysis purposes

  • Ability to create and update graphs, charts and visual aids to convey information

  • Ability to travel as required, less than 10%

It would be advantageous in this position for you to demonstrate the following capabilities and distinctions:

  • BA/BS Bachelor’s Degree or MA/MS Master’s Degree

  • A minimum of five (5) years of experience in the Intelligence industry

  • Experience with scripting languages for monitoring of intelligence sources (Perl, Python, Java, Ruby, API integration)

  • Advanced certifications including: CISSP, CISM, CEH, CWSP, GCWN, CSA

  • Experience in Incident Response and/or Forensics investigations is a plus

  • Data analytics and statistical analysis experience
