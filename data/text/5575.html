P O S ITI O N SUMMARY

T h e Marketing Research and Proposal Manager (Government) assists sales personnel including sales leadership. He/she is responsible for developing, coordinating, and preparing responses to RFIs, RFPs, and custom proposals within the government channel. He/she leads project teams that create sales content, sales deliverables, increase sales efficiencies and drive sales results. The ideal candidate will be capable of juggling a wide variety of sales and team projects and based on experience, will contribute to the strategic planning process with the sales person or manager in a collaborative manner.

PRIMARY JOB RESPONSIBILITIES include (with other duties as assigned):

M anag e RFIs, RFPs and proposals within the government channel

  • Responsible for supporting the coordination and content response development of public sector requests for information (RFIs), requests for proposals (RFPs), and other public sector procurement documents and unsolicited proposals for SaaS/PaaS solutions.
  • Participate in the analysis of RFI-RFP opportunities as a team and Go/No Go decision process.
  • Develop internal response plan and schedule for all phases of the development process in response to RFPs and RFIs.
  • Manage/track all aspects of content preparation, revision, and submission; collaborate and coordinate inputs from sales, solution engineering, security, and legal teams; coordinate and communicate with responding partners where required; and coordinate internal content reviews with executive management and legal.
  • Structure, write, edit, and format technical response content, tailoring the content around the RFI-RFP customer requirements, in a creative, compelling, and compliant manner.
  • Participate in the development of written questions to seek clarification from RFI-RFP procurement officials throughout the procurement process, and remain current on all amendments and modifications.
  • Review and understand customer requirements. Conceptualize and write compelling RFP response content using proposal boilerplate library and from scratch as required.
  • Participate in developing and writing proposal win themes and executive summary sections.
  • Identify customer use cases and customer references to be considered. Customize company overview and customer use case references to reflect the specific customer criteria and requirements.
  • Participate in quality assurance/compliance review to ensure proposal response adheres to customer requirements.
  • Work on multiple RFI-RFPs, balancing milestones and due dates of each.

  • (A Plus) skills with Federal and/or State and Local compliance matrix software (i.e. Visible Thread)
  • Strong word processing skills with proficiency in the Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Visio, Power Point).

  • Self-motivated; possess the ability to work in a fast paced environment with changing priorities and deadlines.
