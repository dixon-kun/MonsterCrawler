        What part will you play?
        * The Research Analyst for TCM/FilmStruck is a unique opportunity to work with a brand with media, digital, social, event and direct-to-consumer touchpoints
        * This position will work closely with the Director of Research to understand and strengthen the relationship that existing fans have with the TCM brand while identifying new audiences and brand extensions and provide insights to help inform the strategy and product roadmap for FilmStruck
        * This position is based in Atlanta, GA
        What will you be doing?
        * Perform trend and data analysis of linear audience metrics (Nielsen Arianna) and digital usage metrics (Adobe Analytics/Nielsen, Rentrak/comScore, Accenture) along with internal analytics for evaluation of programs, products, franchises and the competitive landscape
        * Work with clients to gather digital measurement requirements and partner with technical support to ensure objectives are met and troubleshoot data issues with Rentrak/comScore tagging and Adobe variables
        * Apply an array of statistical methods ranging from traditional to new developments in the Big Data space and make recommendations based on data findings from behavioral data and where possible, tying in survey data
        * Investigate various models for predicting subscriber uptake and turnover for various TCM brand extensions and evaluate the need for new content based on behavioral data and subscriber turnover patterns
        * Conduct multi-variate tests to improve landing page performance
        * Participate in executing in-house and vendor-led primary research studies (qualitative and quantitative) and analyzing data from both primary and secondary research sources
        * Prepare basic communications that turn research findings into actionable knowledge; may present results to management, clients and/or decision-makers
        * Receive work in the form of short-term assignments that often require the application of independent judgment
        What do we need from you?
        * A genuine interest and knowledge of the media and digital industry landscape
        * 1+ years of media research experience, including experience with linear and digital
        * At least a Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Psychology, Business or related field
        * Experience with quantitative analysis of market research data and media math a plus
        * Strong analytic skills, including ability to tell a story about data using multiple data sources
        * Prior experience with research tools that extract data from primary and syndicated sources, such as Adobe Analytics, Rentrak/comScore, Nielsen, Sysomos/other social media measurement tools, MRI, Audience Watch, Star, etc. and the ability to master new software
        * Proficiency in Excel, PowerPoint, and MS Word applications is required
        * Tableau knowledge desired
        * Data science experience desired with basic knowledge of and skills in tools/software such as R, Python, etc.
        * Background in using advanced statistics and models and quantifying significance of data variances
        * Experience with survival models and attrition analysis highly desirable
        * Ability to collaborate, meet deadlines, and produce accurate, detailed results while balancing multiple projects
        * Ability to effectively resolve basic problems and roadblocks as they occur
        * Excellent written and verbal communication skills
