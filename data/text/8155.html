The Business Intelligence Analyst role is described as part analyst, part artist. A data scientist is somebody who is inquisitive, who can stare at data and spot trends, really wants to learn and bring change to an organization. Whereas a traditional data analyst may look only at data from a single source – a CRM system, for example – a Business Intelligence Analyst will most likely explore and examine data from multiple disparate sources. The Business Intelligence Analyst will sift through all incoming data with the goal of discovering a previously hidden insight, which in turn can provide a competitive advantage or address a pressing business problem. A Business Intelligence Analyst does not simply collect and report on data, but also looks at it from many angles, determines what it means, then recommends ways to apply the data.

Responsibilities

  • Analyzes, statistically models and performs data mining on massive amounts of transaction-based data
  • Builds metrics and business cases for key platforms and products in the high speed data, phone and video space
  • Develop new analytic methods for massive data sets to learn about our network and our customers, understand products, and improve services and operations.

  • Communicate data nuances and insights to all levels of the company in a clear and concise manner.
  • Utilize the user group forum to share best practices around analytical findings based on the analysis conducted.
  • Manage detailed project plans used to drive successful project completion. Including gathering detailed business requirements, process requirements, establishing definitions and gaining approval

  • Identify solutions to enhance the existing Business Intelligence infrastructure processes and technology.

  • Create predictive models for business leaders use and create reactionary plans.
  • Leverage expert knowledge of Business Intelligence capabilities and deep familiarity with business trends identifying opportunities and threats.
  • Work with cross functional teams to ensure alignment
