Booz Allen Hamilton has been at the forefront of strategy and technology for more than 100 years Today, the firm provides management and technology consulting and engineering services to leading Fortune 500 corporations, governments, and not-for-profits across the globe. Booz Allen partners with public and private sector clients to solve their most difficult challenges through a combination of consulting, analytics, mission operations, technology, systems delivery, cybersecurity, engineering and innovation expertise.

Solution Architect

Key Role:

Design and develop system architectures and define key capabilities and performance requirements. Define design and technology maturity constraints of the system in accordance with client specifications. Develop thorough definitions of system external interfaces, define system implementation approaches and operational concepts, ensure requirements are met, and evaluate performance with the client. Apply advanced technical principles, theories, and concepts. Work on unusually complex technical problems and provide solutions which are highly innovative and ingenious. Develop advanced technological ideas and guide their development into a final product. Take a leadership role in generating intellectual capital to help the firm grow its business an act as an advisor to management and clients on advanced technical research studies and applications.

Basic Qualifications:

-8+ years of experience with designing, implementing, securing, and maintaining software applications

-Experience with DoD or IC

-Experience in data science

-TS/SCI clearance

-BA or BS degree

Additional Qualifications:

-Experience with data modeling and development with Oracle and SQL Server databases, business intelligence, knowledge management, DevOps, continuous integration, Linux, Docker, OpenShift or Ansible, and AWS a plus

-Experience with Database–as–a–Service (DaaS) solution development, Universal De–identification Platform (UDiP), and Natural Language Processing (NLP) preferred

-Experience with researching, raising questions, and working with technical SMEs and vendors to seek best practices and solid technical decisions

-Knowledge of a variety of Web–based systems and portals and overall systems development lifecycle (SDLC), including in the federal sector

-Ability to quickly comprehend and gain technical awareness for the end–to–end solution and quickly ramp–up on tools and technologies

-Ability to demonstrate a strong client communication orientation

-Possession of excellent oral and written communication skills

-Possession of excellent consulting skills

Clearance:

Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information; TS/SCI clearance is required.

Integrating a full range of consulting capabilities, Booz Allen is the one firm that helps clients solve their toughest problems. by their side to help them achieve their missions.  Booz Allen is committed to delivering results that endure.
