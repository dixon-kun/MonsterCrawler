        PRINCIPAL DATA SCIENTIST
        Primary Responsibilities: Concurrent Technologies Corporation (CTC) is seeking a full time Principal Data Scientist to support the United States Postal Service (USPS) Office of Inspector General (OIG).
        The selected applicant will be responsible for:
        * Discovering and interpreting data through multiple sources and techniques.
        * Working with large amounts of data with various hardware software and analytical tools
        * Combining data from different sources
        * Understanding, testing and validating data; creating analytical and visual outputs to display results; building mathematical and statistical models using the data; presenting data findings through a variety of media or communication to end users who may or may not have subject matter expertise or understanding.
        Basic Qualifications:
        * The qualified applicant must possess Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or similar field with 10 or more years of experience or a Master of Science degree in Computer Science or similar field with 5 or more years of experience in data analytics, data mining, data visualization or data management.
        * Experience with SAS Analytics, SAS Visual Analytics, Microsoft SQL Server Reporting Services (SSRS), Microsoft SQL Server Analysis Services (SSAS), Microsoft SQL Server Integration Services (SSIS), MicroStrategy Analytics, Oracle, Crystal Reports, Cognos, Informatica, or similar technologies.
        * Background with USPS Data Analytics and Data Management processes and technologies preferred.
        * Excellent communication skills are a must.
        * Experience in Commercial Real Estate Valuation
        * Experience Valuing Postal Properties
        1. Using CoStar commercial real estate comp data
        2. Valuing properties in CoStar markets
        Clearance: The candidate must be capable of obtaining a USPS Postal Sensitive Clearance or equivalent for this position.
