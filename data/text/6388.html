Description

Do you love to build solutions to big, challenging problems? Do you embrace the challenge of using unstructured and structured data to deliver actionable insight for a leading healthcare organization?

As the Director of Data Engineering, you’ll be responsible for conceptualizing and implementing a corporate-wide program that helps Medical Mutual of Ohio speed the development of insight from high quality, timely, and well-structured and governed data sources.

You’ll create and architect innovative solutions to scale our ability to learn from data.

Lead a team of software developers, DBA’s and data modelers to:

·        Design and launch scalable, reliable and efficient processes to move, transform and report on large amounts of healthcare data from a diverse set of production systems to a data warehouse infrastructure

·        Create a well-crafted multi-layer warehouse that can be used by Data Scientists and Business Analysts at all levels of the organization

·        Develop visible data quality measures to ensure high data quality

·        Develop visible metrics and dashboards based on business needs

·        Implement a data governance structure that ensures joint ownership at the “local level” for data

·        Leverage automated tests to validate code and ensure new releases are done with high quality

·        Debug software and deliver timely fixes

·        Troubleshoot complex technical issues until resolution

·        Produce accurate, unambiguous technical design specifications to the appropriate detail

·        Identify and propose alternative technology in order to create scalable implementations and achieve results


Contribute to the team:

·        Act as the technical subject matter expert

·        Translate functional requests into a robust and flexible data model

·        Contribute to sprint planning and road mapping

·        Take ownership of what the team builds after the release

·        Advise and mentor more junior team members to maximize overall productivity and effectiveness of the team

Work collaboratively across the organization:

·        Collaborate with members of Sales, Health Informatics, Provider, Finance, IT and UX teams to design flexible solutions

·        Collaborate with members of Compliance, Legal and IT security to ensure solutions meet appropriate regulatory requirements

·        Cultivates strong business relationships with business stakeholders

·        Coordinate efforts within own team/ immediate team members

Qualifications

·        Bachelor’s Degree required

·        Significant software engineering skills and computer science experience

·        Experience in an Agile environment preferred

·        Knowledge of modern programming languages such as: C#, R, Shiny, and javascript/jQuery

·        Knowledge of Unix/Linux, Big Data (Hadoop), MS SQL, DB2, NOSQL, and various other technologies

·        Experience with object oriented programing, relational database technologies, distributed computing tech (Hadoop, spark), RESTful API, WebUI (HTML 5) and Modern JS frameworks
