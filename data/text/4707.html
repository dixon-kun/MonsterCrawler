Job Description

You will be joining Huawei at an architect level with the ability to influence and drive ICT computing research in the areas of data science, cognitive computing, parallel and distributed computing, heterogeneous computing, high-performance computing, within the Central Research Institute (CRI). You will be comfortable to join in a varied team of researchers with a keen interest in innovating the most advanced artificial intelligence, computing and data science technologies. This is a new and exciting technical role, and opportunity to work with elite counterparts in the industry or academic community, promoting a healthy ecosystem. Your ability to communicate at all levels experts within the company will be a valuable asset as you traverse across the company to build trusted relationships that will help you meet your objectives and maintain excellence. This is an extremely important; high demanding; and visible role within the company.

Requirements

* Sound technical background in Cognitive Computing, Machine Learning and related areas. Good Understanding of IT industry trends and potential future directions of data science. Expertise in Deep Q&A, Deep Learning, NLP Processing is a plus.

* A proven technology experience in Cognitive Computing, machine learning technology with a deep knowledge & understanding of technologies from core industry players (Google, Baidu, IBM and alike).

* Familiar with research landscape in academic communities and the influential conference in data science field, such as AAAI, NIPS, ICML, IJCRA, IROS, CPVR, KDD, etc.

* Familiar with key technologies of the most impressive startups relevant to future Cognitive Computing.

* Overall knowledge of open source organizations, key players thereof and their typical components relevant to data science, such as TensorFlow, Torch, Theano, Spark, Hadoop, etc.

* Excellent English communication skills (written and verbal).
