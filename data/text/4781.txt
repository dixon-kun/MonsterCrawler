        Who You'll Work With
        You'll work with our Wave team in San Francisco, Chicago, or Waltham. Wave is part of McKinsey Solutions and McKinsey's New Ventures.
        Wave equips clients to successfully manage improvement programs and transformations. Focused on business impact, Wave allows clients to track the impact of individual initiatives and understand how they affect longer term goals. Its mix of an intuitive interface and McKinsey business expertise givens clients a simple and insightful picture of what can otherwise be a complex process by allowing them to track the progress and performance of initiatives against business goals, budget, and time frames
        New Ventures fosters innovation driven by analytics, design thinking, mobile and social by developing new products/services and integrating them into our client work.         What You'll Do
        You will be responsible for developing and delivering approaches and activities to generate advisory and service sales and to drive adoption and satisfaction rates across our client base.
        You'll have a particular focus on the early deployment, training and adoption phases. You'll also operate as the lead point of contact for best practices in deployment and adoption of our solution, and you'll partner with McKinsey's client development, operations and client relationship teams to deploy the solution, deliver ongoing adoption activities, provide ad-hoc advisory services.
        Additionally, you'll gather internal and external expertise to advise on key structural configuration choices and governance in function of program type and subject matter, you'll guide internal teams and clients to ensure fit-for-purpose and high-quality configuration, and you'll support the development and delivery of training and advisory modules to foster client adoption and usage throughout their user lifecycle.
        You'll have the opportunity to develop a trusted advisor relationship with internal and external stakeholders and in coordination with the Marketing function, you'll develop and deliver in-person or remote post-sales activities such as workshops, forums, a newsletter, events, etc.
        In addition to the specific responsibility for post-sales activities, you will support Wave team activities by contributing to best practices development regarding Wave for Program Management and Impact Tracking. You'll assist in understanding client business needs and responding to RFP in pre-sales, you'll maintain knowledge of industry trends and competitor offerings , and you'll contribute ideas for new product and service development, marketing and communication initiatives and operational improvement.
