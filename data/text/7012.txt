        The Cybersecurity organization&#8217;s objective is to ensure that JPMC is able to effectively detect, prevent, and respond to cyber threats against our technology infrastructure\.  The scope of Cybersecurity comprises detection and monitoring of threats and vulnerabilities, managing security incidents, and evolving our preventive infrastructure to keep ahead of the threat\.         The Role&#8230;
        We are seeking an Excel/VBA or Perl or Python or Powershell coder to design and implement process automation using RAD or Agile techniques\. This automation will integrate with existing code bases, front end usable prototypes, and existing solutions to demonstrate and/or achieve efficient and effective operation of security functions\.
        Continual enhancement of the confidentiality, integrity and availability of data and systems through a robust information security agenda is a key strategic objective of JP Morgan Chase and of the Corporate and Investment Banking \(CIB\) division\.  An array of information security initiatives are planned to include risk controls framework, enhancement to Information and Cyber Security, to Identity &amp; Access Management \(IAM\)\.  These initiatives require an individual to work within one of the highest growth areas within JP Morgan and build the Chief Information Security Office function\.

        + Formulate and document data sourcing and make recommendations for quality or process improvement
        + Transform complex multilayer/multitech processes into simple automated/repeatable process
        + Perform complex data analysis received from numerous systems
        + Identification of data specific issues and work with key technology partners in resolving them
