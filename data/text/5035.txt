          Managing Consulting Director, Southeast Named Accounts-172125
            Description
          The Managing Consulting Director (MCD) leads the data and analytics consulting activities for customers and targeted accounts aligned to Named Accounts (large existing customers) in the Southeast sales territory. Within the assigned territory, the MCD is responsible for:
          &#8226; PS orders, revenue, and profit;
          &#8226; Engagement planning, staffing, quality, and execution; and
          &#8226; Client satisfaction and relationship management.
          As a player/coach, the MCD is also expected to be the consulting executive for specific, assigned customers within the territory.
          The MCD has a dual reporting relationship with the primary linkage to the Southeast Consulting VP and secondarily to a member of the Southeast sales leadership team. The MCD is directly involved in account planning for consulting services in the assigned territory and is responsible for developing and managing revenue forecasts for the territory. The MCD takes a leadership role in securing and defining new engagements and negotiating with customers and contractors, including services related contracts (Managed Services and Consulting Services).
          The MCD plans, organizes and coordinates the long-range and day-to-day activities of the business managing multiple, complex engagements, large project teams and internal support resources. This includes oversight accountability for a team of professional services Consultants that may include PS Partners and Principal Consultants, Solution Architects, Project Managers, and Off-shore resources, as well as Contractor, Partner, COE, and Client resources.
          Within the consulting organization, the MCD has full operational responsibility for planning, finances/budget, end results and setting policies within an assigned territory as well as for the development of forecasts for resource deployment, business process integration, change management and results tracking. The MCD develops and executes the practice&#8217;s business plan and the practice&#8217;s capabilities in terms of demand creation, service delivery and resource management within assigned geography. At this level the MCD typically has responsibility for the management of revenue and gross margin dollars at the one or more PS Practice or Area level and may contribute to revenue with a personal billable target.
          The MCD has responsibility for the recruitment, development, and retention of PS associates, and drives and manages the PS knowledge management strategy and initiative, including revenue generation through IP offerings. The MCD provides advice and counsel to PS leadership on quality, process, profitability strategy, and resource requirements.
          Supports data and analytic service offerings across a wide set of Industries including Retail, Travel, Transportation, Financial Services, Manufacturing, and Consumer Goods. Ability to work within complex organizations typical in Fortune 500 companies.
