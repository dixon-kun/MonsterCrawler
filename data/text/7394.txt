            Job Title:
           Consulting Director, Business Solutions - Government Services

           The Consulting Director, Business Solutions (CD) for the Center for Medicare &amp; Medicaid Services (CMS) Account leads the consulting activities for targeted accounts/customers in the Baltimore area and other locations. Primary focus is selling solutions that leverage data to achieve business-based outcomes for specific industries. Services for the targeted customers will extend their Data and Analytic capabilities while leveraging Teradata&#8217;s technology solution components including; Integrated Data Warehouse, Data Warehouse Appliances, Aster, Hadoop, and many Analytical and Business Intelligence applications. Professional Services (PS) are an integral part of our go-to-market strategy, and the CD leads the business development and sales process for projects with significant scale and business outcome. He or she develops and executes the sales plan and positions service offers for specific customer accounts. He or she is responsible for building senior level client relationships, managing the overall sales process, identifying business problems, scoping, pricing, and contracting of services, and collaborating with partner companies to extend our capabilities. This position will report to the Managing Consulting Director or Senior Consulting Director.
           Key Responsibilities:
           &#8226; Creates demand for consulting services in new areas of client organization through business development efforts, focused on business outcomes
           &#8226; Leads and coordinates overall selling process for consulting services, including projects of significant scale, bringing in other specialists as needed
           &#8226; Brings deep industry and/or capability expertise to the account team in order to sell Teradata solutions
           &#8226; Understands client business problems and develops POVs which articulate problems along with Teradata solutions
           &#8226; Builds relationships in new parts of the client organization to generate demand for solutions in new areas
           &#8226; Manages the overall selling process for consulting services
           &#8226; Identifies opportunities which would require other specialized expertise and coordinates with internal resources to bring experts in as needed
           &#8226; Regularly meets with account team lead to discuss account strategy and future sales opportunities
           &#8226; Coaches and develops Project Managers and Consultants
           &#8226; Manages process of scoping, planning, costing, and pricing new projects
           &#8226; Develops Statements of Work for new projects; defines goals and objectives, client requirements, resources needed, budget, risks, and deliverables
           &#8226; Serves on consulting engagements as a thought partner and subject matter expert for the consulting delivery team and as a key relationship map for project sponsors
           &#8226; Engages with other account teams and Client Partners as needed to use expertise to help sell solutions to other clients
           &#8226; Maintains projections for pipeline of potential future engagements
           &#8226; Responsible for billing, teaming agreements, and other partnering documents
           Skills &amp; Attributes:
           &#8226; Robust history of success in consultative selling, particularly in services
