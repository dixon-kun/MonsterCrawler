        Visual Designer
        PROS: BIG data solutions for optimizing ..... Sales, Pricing, Quoting, Rebates, Revenue Management. Software for companies in the manufacturing, distribution, services and travel industries is hiring!
        PROS is seeking an experienced visual designer to work on designing equally functional and beautiful user interfaces for PROS products. This role will drive product innovation by developing a wide range of creative ideas and designs, with a focus on engagement, branding and usability. Candidates must have strong visual and interaction design skills and the ability to help shape product strategy.
        This positions reports to the Visual Design Manager on the User Experience Team.
        Responsibilities:
        * Design new and existing PROS products and features.
        * Help drive and refine the design language and experience of PROS applications. Collaborate cross-functionally with product management, engineering, user research, and other teams.
        * Participate in design reviews and other company-wide design efforts.
        * Adapt design strategies to take advantage of user research, analytics, and client feedback.
        * Contribute to and maintain the PROS application style guide.
        Requirements:
        * Experience designing and building products for the web and mobile
        * Strong visual design skills (including layout, typography, iconography, color theory and use of imagery)
        * Experience working in an agile environment within UX and product teams
        * Excellent communication skills and attention to detail
        * Ability to juggle multiple projects on tight deadlines without compromising quality
        * The following experience is desirable but not required for this role: HTML and CSS coding, Flash, presentation design, email and landing page design
        Note: Portfolio submission is required for consideration for this role. (PDF or PPT Format)
