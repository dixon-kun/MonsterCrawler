Job Description

Job Title : Senior Data Scientist/ Lead / Architect

Location : San Jose, CA

Duration : 6 Months

Job Description:

The candidate should be well known in the ML area and should have a good track record in ML competitions.

We need someone with a strong quantitative background. An advanced degree in a quantitative field is nice, but not required – an analytical mindset and fluency with mathematical and statistical methods are required.

Demonstrable qualifications that you can conduct statistical analysis, explore data, and correctly apply machine learning to solve hard problems.

• Excellent coding and scripting skills (e.g.: Python, R, Java, Scala, Shell scripting, Perl, C, Ruby, etc.)

• Excellent and thorough knowledge of SQL skills required

• Cloud platform programming experience (e.g. Microsoft Azure or Amazon Web Services)

• Experience using Microsoft Azure Cortana Intelligence Suite and/or Azure Machine Language is a plus

• Solid understanding of general business fundamentals: marketing, accounting, finance, and management practices

• Strong knowledge of distributed computing, data warehouse, data mining, business analytics and software development

• Deep knowledge of a range of analytic methodologies: statistical inference, Bayesian methods, supervised and unsupervised machine learning methods, dimensionality reduction, anomaly detection, recommendation systems, non-linear optimization, natural language processing, graph analysis, etc.
