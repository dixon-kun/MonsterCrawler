        Responsibilities:
        The Senior Data Analyst position is responsible leading, maintaining and developing analytics using established technology resources. The Senior Analyst must be capable of working with and providing instruction to more junior Analyst working within the department. Additionally, the Senior Analyst must be capable of working with business leaders as a subject matter expert in discussing, testing, and implementing functional requirements. The ability to analyze large data sets is key to include the ability to carefully review outputs to ensure quality. Good communication and facilitation skills required with an ability to work across multiple work groups to reach consensus.
        Responsible for managing and analyzing large, real-time and historical data sets, data mining, statistical analysis, and data visualization. Models created, formed and managed will provide the insights necessary to improve overall business performance. This role requires a motivated problem solver capable of working with diverse systems and teams.
        Summary of key responsibilities:
        + Advanced knowledge in the field of analytics, data science or informatics
        + Capable of formulating complex models
        + Thorough understanding of data structures &#8211; both relational and non-relational
        + Established investigatory skill in crafting new models
        + Strong knowledge of the systems necessary to run complex analytics
        + Capable of recommending the best visualization method given end user need
        Additional responsibilities:
        + Define, prepare, analyze and deploy business models for various business functions. This will require gathering information from a variety of source systems and applications. Data volume being analyzed is substantial, so the refined skill to manipulate data and detect errors/outliers is essential.
        + Knowledge of varied systems used in the development and deployment of business analytics: Business Intelligence (SQL, Oracle, Cognos, etc.); CRMs (Salesforce.com, Siebel); Statistical software (SAS, R, etc.); Visualization (Tibco-Spotfire, Tableau).
        + Programming skill in some of the following: R, SQL, Java, Python, etc. Experience with open-source technologies ( R) preferred.
        + Expertise and use of a variety of statistical methods: Factor Analysis, Cluster Analysis, Survival Analysis, Logistic &amp; Linear Regression, Hypothesis Testing, Segmentation, etc.
        Qualifications:
        + Master&#8217;s Degree or equivalent work experience. This roles requires previous experience in developing advanced business models, and in guiding team in the completion of complex projects.
        + Assist in the development of new resources, tools and analytics to drive business results. Work in collaboration with varied work groups to measure key performance indicators and benchmarks. Guide more junior Analyst in the completion of assigned projects
        + Strong verbal and written communication skills. Must be able to communicate effectively and confidently with users, team members and management. Flexibility and willingness to take on varied tasks is essential.
        + This role assists in evaluating and reporting business performance. Based on the findings, the data is then used to define appropriate actions to take in measuring team performance.
