The Wayfair Display team is our internal ad trading desk. We run ads on websites around the Internet to acquire and reengage customers for our Wayfair, Joss & Main, and AllModern brands. Our campaigns drive significant revenue for the business while boosting brand awareness in the process. We leverage cutting edge technologies and tools including real-time bidding and programmatic buying. We also work directly with leading publishers to run homepage takeovers and sponsorship campaigns. Internally, we regularly collaborate with Wayfair’s product management, creative design, and data science teams to ensure the best user experience for our customers.

As the Associate Director on the Display team, you’ll lead a team of analysts running our display acquisition campaigns. You will expand existing campaigns, design new measurement approaches to unlock additional value for Wayfair, and define the display playbook for each of our brands. This is an exciting role for someone with an entrepreneurial spirit and ability to drive proj ects internally and externally.

  • Manage and o ptimize a multi-million dollar budget to meet ROI goals.

  • Manage the overall execution, performance, and effectiveness of digital marketing strategies
  • Relentlessly drive growth through innovative strategies and meticulous execution to drive step change improvements in performance.
  • C ultivate and strengthen relationships with external partners and vendors, negotiating favorable media and technology deals for Wayfair.
  • Institute rigorous test-and-learn methodology to achieve desired results.
  • Collaborate with creative, data science, and engineering teams to improve the experience of customers acquired through display ads.
  • Leverage Excel and SQL to build reports and conduct analyses.
  • Develop talent within the organization, mentoring/developing small team of analysts.

About Wayfair:

Wayfair Inc. offers an extensive selection of home furnishings and décor across all styles and price points. The Wayfair family of brands includes:

  • Wayfair.com, an online destination for all things home
  • Joss & Main, where beautiful furniture and finds meet irresistible savings
  • AllModern, a go-to online source for modern design
  • DwellStudio, a design house for fashion-forward modern furnishings
  • Birch Lane, a collection of classic furnishings and timeless home décor

Wayfair generated $2.25 billion in annual sales in 2015 and $786.9 million in net revenues for second quarter 2016. Wayfair employed 5,398 people as of June 30, 2016 and is headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts with operations throughout North America and Europe. The company has previously been named one of America’s Most Promising Companies by Forbes Magazine and selected by the Boston Business Journal and Glassdoor.com as a Top Company to Work For and Top Company for Well Balanced Life.
