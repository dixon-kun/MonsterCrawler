Job Title: Data Services Manager
Location: Raleigh, NC (100% onsite)
Duration: 6 month Contract to Hire Position

Description & Responsibilities
End client is a Large Financial Institution

Expert SAS Programmer and SAS DI Studio/SAS Data Management to plan, connect, design, schedule, and deploy enterprise data for decision makers throughout the organization. Candidate will need to develop, monitor and maintain ETL processes, departmental data policies and procedures, and the data warehouse design. The candidate will provide leadership, strategic direction and oversight for key enterprise data initiatives and ensure end-to-end technical delivery of Enterprise Data Management of large and complex projects. This is not an entry level position. This is a leadership position at the management level

Department: Enterprise Information Management
Reports To: Director of Business Intelligence

Summary :
Plans, connects, designs, schedules, and deploys enterprise data for decision makers throughout the organization. Develops, monitors, and maintains ETL processes, departmental data policies and procedures, and the data warehouse design. Provides leadership, strategic direction, and oversight for The Bank's key enterprise data initiatives and ensures end to end technical delivery of Enterprise Data Management of large and complex projects.

The following statements are intended to describe the general nature and level of work performed; they are not intended to be an exhaustive or exclusive list of the required responsibilities, duties and skills; Management retains the discretion to add to or change the duties and requirements of this position at any time, with or without notice, as business needs dictate.

Essential Duties & Responsibilities :
1. Plans, creates, coordinates, and deploys the Enterprise Date Warehouse.
2. Consolidates and optimizes current data warehouse infrastructure.
3. Creates and documents best practices for data loading and extraction.
4. Designs and implements ETL procedures for intake of data from both internal and outside sources; as well as ensure data is verified and accurate.
5. Designs and implements ETL processes and data architecture to ensure proper functioning of analytics, as well as client's or third-party's reporting environments and dashboards.
6. Collaborates with business and technology stakeholders in ensuring data warehouse architecture development and utilization.
7. Facilitates requirements gathering using expert listening skills to meet the immediate and future needs of business customers.
8. Monitors and performs database performance analysis; designs and extends data marts, meta data, and data models.
9. Supports the development and validation required through the lifecycle of the Data Warehouse and Business Intelligence systems; maintains user connectivity, and provide adequate security for the Enterprise data warehouse.
10. Interacts with senior leadership concerning the alignment of enterprise data capabilities with the long-term business goals.
11. Maintains effective communication between Enterprise Information Management, IT staff, and information stakeholders throughout the organization.
12. Complete, pass and maintain an up-to-date status for all positional and company required compliance and regulatory courses by assigned due date(s).
13. Participate in special projects and assignments and performs other duties as assigned

To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.
Qualifications :
  • Bachelor's Degree in Business, Information Sciences, Quantitative Social Sciences, or other quantitative field with 4-6 years of experience in large data environments and broad experience in data architecture, ETL, data quality, data warehouse, data governance or an equivalent combination of education and experience.
  • Graduate degree in a quantitative or data sciences field, or an MBA are preferred, but not required.
  • Experience in Financial Services is preferred, ideally interspersed with experience in other industries in similar roles.
  • Strong foundational knowledge of data warehousing concepts.
  • Programming Experience: SQL or SAS programming
  • Expert experience with SAS DI Studio or SAS Data Management
  • Experience access data from MS Sql Server and DB2 on AS400. Knowledge of establishing and working with ODBC connections to these databases is required.
  • Experience with AS400 environment is a benefit.
  • Experience loading data in MS SQL Server is required.
  • Proficient knowledge of Microsoft Windows operating system & Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and Outlook.
  • Ability to read and listen, understand and communicate information and ideas in a clear and understandable manner, both orally and in writing with senior executives, business unit management and staff, state and federal regulators and officials and significant business partners.
  • Strong ability to read, analyze and interpret financial reports and legal documents; performs complex calculations; effectively researches, analyzes and evaluates information to make decisions, solve problems and achieve goals; strong project management skills desired.
  • Certificates & Licenses: None

Supervisory Responsibilities : Exercises the usual authority of a manager concerning hiring, staffing, training, performance appraisals, promotions, salary recommendations and terminations. Provides leadership by example at all times, through support and endorsement of bank policy.

Control Related Responsibilities : Become knowledgeable regarding the department's internal controls as outlined in the policies and procedures and comply with all control requirements.

Compliance : Ability to comprehend and adhere to Federal and State banking regulations, including compliance with the Bank's Bank Secrecy Act/Anti Money Laundering (BSA/AML) program. Follows all Company policies and procedures, and successfully participates in regulatory and Company training requirements via multiple and variable delivery methods.

Safety, Security and Confidentiality : This position requires keeping complete confidentiality of all customer transactions and information, and paying close attention to security within and around the bank's premises at all times.
