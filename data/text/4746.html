GEI is a leading consulting engineering firm ranked #94 in the ENR top 500 (2016). We serve Government, Energy, Industry and Institutional clients, and have completed project engagements throughout the United States, Canada, and more than 20 other countries. Founded in 1970, our privately held, employee-owned company consists of over 700 employees located in 37 offices across the U.S.A.

GEI’s technical experts are involved in dozens of specialty services emanating from our traditional core practices which include geotechnical, environmental, infrastructure, water resources, ecology, and transportation. GEI “seller-doers” are considered some of the leading subject matter experts (SMEs) in their respective fields. GEI is in the early stages of a digital business transformation, and is seeking an exceptional leader for our data science team.

In 2014, GEI began leveraging our expertise in the design, implementation, and maintenance of client-owned and hosted data management systems to develop a cloud-based data analytics subscription service that provides advanced data storage and retrieval, automated analytics, document hosting, reporting and GIS capabilities to our clients. With management of information resources more complex than ever, GEI is deeply committed to enterprise-level, cloud-based resource management systems that promote management and ease of information sharing. GEI has now reached the point where we’re adding a new addition to our leadership team to oversee our business information systems and data science efforts.

Essential Responsibilities & Duties:

•Lead GEI’s commercial data science team of cloud-based data technology experts as well as data entry specialists, GIS specialists, and data validation experts.
•Strive to make GEI a thought and business leader in the practice.
•Coordinate data science needs with client account and project managers as well as Branch Managers, Practice Area Leaders and management throughout GEI.
•Analyze scientific, engineering and business requirements to develop technical network solutions and their framework.
•Monitor and predict emerging trends within the practice and their potential impacts on client, staffing, training, and business needs.
•Stay abreast of how technology infrastructures are currently impacting and driving competitors.
•Develop test plans, implementation plans, and project timelines for various projects
•Must be able to efficiently reach an understanding of complex tasks while using this understanding to produce project artifacts that satisfy project objectives.
•Defines, prioritizes, communicates, and fosters shared understanding of project objectives and scope.
•Coordinate the development of standard operating procedures (SOPs), technical training programs, and QA/QC procedures for staff and work product.
•Supervise and train all staff necessary to complete assignments.
•Participate in recruiting efforts and integration of new staff.
•Coordinate with national and regional management to identify technical expertise and staffing levels needed to support practice growth.
•Know the skill sets of staff within the practice and company-wide and work towards developing them aligned with GEI and the practice’s vision and goals
•Coordinate and oversee the professional development of staff within the practice, including personally mentoring and serving as a development resource to the team.
•Provides input in staff performance evaluations; mentors and coaches staff.
•Coordinate active participation in professional, industry, and policy groups, and encourage professional publications by staff within office.
•Manage technology vendors and subcontractors.
•Work with marketing and others to help develop GEI data science “branding”.
•Collaborates with stakeholders to elicit, prioritize, validate, and document testable, developer-ready business requirements.
•Collaborates with stakeholders to identify, evaluate, and manage changes to project scope and requirements.
•Collaborates with technical team members to ensure the solution design satisfies project objectives and business requirements.
•Assists the business in determining if and how new systems or system enhancements may improve business processes and capabilities.
•Serves as a critical resource and liaison between subject matter experts and technologists throughout the project lifecycle.
•Experience in system integration testing.
•Able to identify, document and communicate any software problems and issues with the team and the vendor/developer.
•Other duties as assigned.

Minimum Qualifications:

•2-4 year’s minimum experience in a position overseeing a similar scope of work.
•Bachelor's Degree, or equivalent experience, from an accredited college or university required.
•Project management experience preferred.
•Database experience required.
•Ability to effectively supervise and review other’s work for a nationwide team.
•Able to work effectively in GEI’s partnership model, including a team environment, building rapport and relationships.
•Ability to plan and meet budget, develop project plans and meet deadlines.
•Able to act as a change agent to respectfully challenge the norm.
•Insatiably inquisitive self-starter with attention to detail and stakeholder needs.
•Proficient in synthesizing information and conveying it in a way that fosters understanding.
•Able to critically analyze and solve problems of a complex nature.
•Able to work on multiple projects of moderate complexity simultaneously.
•Proficient in organization and time management skills.
•Proficient in the use of web-based data science applications.
•Familiar with relational database concepts, and client-server concepts.
•Experience with integration of enterprise level cloud-based security, access portals, ArcGIS, SQL Server, SSRS, EQuIS or similar enterprise-level database systems is required.
•Familiarity with leading edge Big Data concepts, tools and processes preferred.
•Familiarity with engineering and/or environmental projects and data preferred.

GEI is open to filling this position at any of ouroffices nationwide, but is primarily targeting our Boston or Denver locations.
