        The Compliance Testing Data Mining Group ("DMG") is at the forefront of using analytics to identify and address regulatory and compliance risks globally. The group focuses on the use of modern analytical techniques and systems and controls testing to manage key risks and control points within the compliance risk environment. The team is globally structured, with offices in major global theatres.
        The Compliance Data Mining Officer will be responsible for developing and delivering analytics that support AML Pre-Closure Testing. The position will develop and execute analytical procedures drawn from audit testing, statistics and mathematics, and data science to validate and mitigate project risk in AML Topics such as Transaction Monitoring, Optimization and Segmentation, and Know Your Customer. The candidate will work with project owners, GAML PMO, business and others on a daily basis to inform of findings, execute analytical testing plans, and produce artifacts and documenting supporting Compliance Testing results.
        The ideal candidate will have experience in audit\testing or similar discipline, and comfortable in using technology and data to empirically substantiate and support conclusions and results. Additional experience in financial services and AML or compliance risk is a plus, and the candidate should be fluent in AML terminology and standards. The position reports to the AML Data Mining PCT Project Leads within Compliance Testing.
        The candidate should demonstrate strong verbal and written communication skills and be comfortable collaborating with business, compliance officers, and testers. Experience working in small, fast-paced teams is required. The candidate will frequently be involved in matters that require complex judgments and solutions based on sophisticated analytics and complex regulatory topics. The candidate must be able to recommend appropriate interventions when needed and propose creative and pragmatic solutions for risk and control problems.
        Key Responsibilities include:
        * Apply and use audit\testing methodologies and analytical techniques to perform controls and systems testing.
        * Co-lead and execute testing reviews over enterprise wide AML systems and regulatory programs.
        * Drive the innovative use of analytics through direct participation in all phases of Pre Closure reviews (planning, fieldwork, and reporting).
        * Coordinate with business, compliance officers, and technology from the identification of the testing target through the delivery of the analytic.
        * Translate requirements into models/analytics using various data analysis and visualization tools.
        * Work with team members to distill analytic results into impactful findings that translate into regulatory/compliance risks for senior management.
        * Effectively perform quality control over the work of others.
        * 5 years in Testing or Assurance/Audit required.
        * 3 years of database or data analysis skills in the Financial Services or related industry.
        * Bachelor's Degree required in any of the following: Finance, Information Systems, Computer Science, Engineering, Accounting, Finance, and Economics.
        * Excellent analytical ability to understand business processes and related risks and controls, and develop innovative audit analytics based upon compliance testing needs.
        * Proven ability to quickly assimilate and organize large amounts of new information and produce professional products (using Microsoft tools).
        * Proven ability to identify possible exceptions based on key risk indicators.
        * Proven ability to understand and document results, including the use of trend analysis.
        * Experience in basic query languages such as using SQL, T-SQL, or PL/SQL (SQL Server or Oracle).
        * Excellent verbal skills, both written and oral.
