Decisive Analytics Corporation is looking for an OPIR Machine Learning Scientist to join our team in Dayton, OH or Boulder, Colorado.


  • The OPIR machine learning scientist will perform research on projects involving the exploitation of data from Overhead Persistent Infrared (OPIR) sensors including the development of new data algorithms, and the transition of emerging capabilities to end user. Machine learning techniques will be used with remotely sensed data to produce real time intelligence products.
  • Work location could be at NASIC with travel to Decisive Analytics facilities and the OPIR Battlespace Awareness Center (OBAC) in Colorado or work location could be at the OBAC with travel to Decisive Analytics facilities and NASIC, depending on individual geographical preferences.

Required Skills :

  • Proficient with JAVA, Matlab & C/C++
  • Active DoD TS/SCI clearance required
  • Ability to work independently or in a team environment
  • Strong communication skills
  • Experience working with information at different levels of classification
  • Knowledge and experience with Modeling & Simulation (M&S) environments
