Technical Project Manager (Boston)

DataXu’s vision is to make marketing better using data science. The world’s top brands and agencies partner with DataXu to better understand and engage their customers across all available media formats, devices, channels, and buying modes.  Our advertising solution, ranked #1 in the industry by Forrester Research in 2015, provides marketers with unparalleled Media Activation, Marketing Analytics and Data Management capabilities.

https://www.dataxu.com/programmatic-marketing/

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5lBeQxdSEAs

The Technical Project Manager helps DataXu’s Engineering team to deliver our most complex, cross-functional, critical software development projects to market. The ideal candidate will be well organized, experienced in agile project management, technically skilled, and genuinely interested in understanding the business goals and technology aspects of their projects (i.e., not satisfied with simply tracking tasks on a checklist or project plan).

On the Job

  • Partner with multiple engineering teams to provide agile project management support to key product development and core engineering projects on a day-to-day basis:

    • Collaborate with product owners to clearly understand and document business needs, set priorities for sprint planning, and review deliverables at sprint completion

    • Collaborate with engineering leadership to secure engineering resources, manage priorities and dependencies across teams, and resolve issues in a timely manner

    • Proactively identify, track, and communicate dependencies, risks, and issues affecting projects and collaborate on finding a resolution

    • Report to stakeholders on project status and estimated delivery schedule on a regular basis

    • Ensure that projects follow a lightweight methodology and meet quality standards before being released to end users

  • Oversee, track, and report status on a broader program of quarterly engineering initiatives on a weekly basis

  • Participate in and help to drive the quarterly and yearly roadmap planning processes

  • Collaborate on defining and growing the Project Management organization within DataXu

About You .....

  • Have a Bachelor’s Degree or commensurate work experience

  • Have 5+ years of experience working on software projects, ideally in SaaS and/or ad tech solutions

  • Experienced in managing projects using Agile methodologies; ScrumMaster certification a plus

  • Experienced with project management tools such as Jira, Microsoft Project, Smartsheet, or similar

  • Possess both business and technical acumen

  • Have an interest in understanding the “what” and “how” aspects of your projects and willingness to be a hands-on team member beyond tracking tasks and reporting status

  • Eager to understand both the business needs behind a project and the technical solutions developed to satisfy those needs

  • Willing to work in a fast-paced environment and handle multiple projects simultaneously
