Responsibilities

As a Senior Data Scientist, you’ll create analytical models to predict customer behavior and business events. You will work on predictive modeling in support of solving business problems including acquiring new customers, selling new products, and reducing churn. You’ll then present the results to various business partners such as marketing, finance, and our customer relationship management group. You’ll have the opportunity to work on interesting and unique large data sets.

In this role, you’ll work on (and be responsible for the delivery of) multiple medium to large-size projects that have direct impact on the business’s bottom line. You will develop resolutions to complex problems creatively and make decisions that impact projects & staff members. You will use a combination of the typical Data Scientist math and analytical skills, with research, advanced business, communication, and presentation skills.

Qualifications

Must have:

  • Bachelor’s degree or six or more years of work experience.
  • Experience with software packages SAS, SPSS, R, Python.
  • Experience building predictive models and algorithms.
  • Experience with SQL.
  • Experience creating and presenting Executive level presentations.

Ideally, you will also have:

  • Bachelor’s/ Master’s Degree in Statistics, Math, Economics, Engineering, Computer Science, Business Analytics, Data Science or a related field.
  • PhD in related area.
  • Recent hands on experience building predictive models and algorithms in support of solving business problems.
  • Experience with Teradata or Hadoop.
  • Teamwork & collaboration skills to work across organizations and lead cross-functional teams.
  • Communication & stakeholder management skills.

Not to boast, but a little bit about us

Verizon powers America’s fastest and most reliable network. We’re also leading the way in cloud and security solutions, Internet of Things and video entertainment. Technology moves fast and so do we.
