         Schwan's Company is looking fora
          Business Intelligence Analyst
         to join our team in Bloomington, MN. The BI Analyst partners with various functional areas, such as HR, Finance, Trade, and Field Sales, to understand business issues and data challenges. This is a great opportunity to work in an Agile environment, within a small, self directed team. Schwan&#8217;s BI group has done extensive clean up of data and reporting and now they are in the &#8220;rebuilding phase&#8221;. In this rebuilding phase, the goal is to improve core data reporting methodologies and systems, as well as provide end-user support for deployed reporting solutions.Come be a part of the team that will help articulate the business challenges, and ensure solutions are delivered that resolve them! Opportunity to work with Data Visualization, Mobile BI and have partnership with the Data Science team.
         Responsibilities include:
           Facilitates the design of proposed business intelligencesolutions, including reports, dashboards, analytical and ad-hoc reportingenvironments.
           Builds a relationship throughout the Business Unit and IT todevelop an understanding of their reporting needs and gaps.
           Assists the business in identifying required changes to existingreports and analytics, and frames the business issues and data challenges forthe respective business area.
           Identifies strengths and weaknesses in reporting design plans andsuggests areas for improvement.
           Documents, reviews, and edits requirements, specifications,business processes, and recommendations related to a proposed solution.
           Assists in the development of Reporting and Analytics roadmaps forbusiness areas.
           Translates business requirements into technical requirements and communicatesneeded changes to development teams.
           Drives adoption of SAP BI best practices (including BW and HANA)to ensure the tool is organized and efficient.
           Develops materials and ensures that the systems for reporting havebeen appropriately rolled out to enable full utilization of BusinessIntelligence tools by the Business Unit.
           Leads projects when required following established projectmanagement processes.
           Develops test scripts and plans and leads testing efforts.
           Tracks business trends and maintains a high level understanding ofavailable technologies to seek innovative solutions for key reportingrequirements.
           Drives adoption of Business Objects reporting tools as perestablished guidelines.
           Facilitates BI Center of Excellence across the enterprise toensure BI and Data Governance policies and procedures are adhered to and beingleveraged by Business Units for their projects.
