Senior Manager:  Automation through Data Analytics

If you're passionate about e-commerce and creating scalable processes that will drive our business and elevate customer, supplier, and employee experiences, we are looking for you to join our Product Catalog team.

Senior Managers on our team must be able to transform data into insights and strategy to improve the throughput and quality of products in the Wayfair online catalog. They each lead a medium sized team of analysts and managers to help them improve upon the way we bring products to market.  They work cross functionally with Executive Level Leadership, making appropriate project related tradeoffs across brands, and measuring success of the team’s initiatives.

This Specific Leader Will:

  • Learn the intricacies of Wayfair’s merchandising processes to become a subject matter expert.
  • Drive an agenda of automation and quality improvement leveraging the expertise of our Data Science team
  • Validate the results and quantifying the value add of the improvements made.

Example Projects Include:

  • Mining the supplier provided “blob” of romance copy into structured data tags that can power storefront filters
  • Using image recognition to identify potential duplicate products and likely option variants (eg a new color of an existing product)
  • Using image recognition to tag attributes of an image (eg is it a ¾ angle shot, is it in a holiday setting, is the lamp on or off)
  • Mining product data to determine the appropriate class of product

In all these cases this leader will be handling the project management and validation work, while the Data Science team will be developing the algorithms.

Job Requirements:

  • 5+ years management experience in ecommerce or a relevant operations field
  • MBA from top tier school or equivalent experience
  • Proven track record of taking ownership and driving results
  • High comfort level with technology-driven environments
  • Experience managing a large team in eCommerce or relevant operations field
  • Process-oriented with strong analytical and communication skills
  • Ability to think strategically and tactically within a dynamic, fast-paced organization
  • Extremely collaborative and effective in a cross-functional environment
  • Evidence of “scrappy startup execution” and how to ‘get it done’ attitude no matter what when time is tight and stakes are high
  • Ability to manage through ambiguity, stress, adversity and conflict to establish focus and deliver against business goals
  • Able to quickly learn necessary technical tools. Experience with SQL a plus.

About Wayfair

Wayfair Inc. offers an extensive selection of home furnishings and décor across all styles and price points. The Wayfair family of sites includes:

  • Wayfair, an online destination for all things home
  • Joss & Main, where beautiful furniture and finds meet irresistible savings
  • AllModern, your home for affordable modern design
  • DwellStudio, a design house with a decidedly modern vibe
  • Birch Lane, a collection of classic furnishings and timeless home décor

Wayfair generated $3.4 billion in net revenue for full year 2016. Headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts with operations throughout North America and Europe, the company employs more than 5,600 people.
