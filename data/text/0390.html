**Chase Card Services Associate of Account Management New York, Columbus 170020989**



**Description**



Job Summary:



We are looking for a highly\-motivated Account Manager to help scale our growing media management team\. This person will be responsible for ensuring that the agency is meeting the client’s needs and working with all the key functions that go into creating the agency’s advertising output\. They are constantly solving problems, managing internal and external communications, and driving projects to completion\.



Responsibilities:




* Effectively communicate and coordinate efforts between internal cross\-functional teams including analytics, data science, reporting and legal




* Participate within the Media Management team to develop and refine media best practices and processes to implement agency wide




* Manage multiple agency teams and ensure they are following JPMorgan Chase\. procedure




* Assist with tracking media efforts across all lines of business to develop of a comprehensive view of media




* Support collaboration across creative, planning and buying teams to create holistic marketing strategies



**Qualifications**



**Experience:**




* 3 years’ experience in digital planning or account management




* Experience owning and managing multiple projects in a dynamic environment




* Organized, with strong attention to detail and time management skills




* Be an effective communicator, both verbally and in writing




* Must be detail\-oriented and have the ability to remain composed and professional under demanding circumstances\.




* Experience with Google DoubleClick products a plus
