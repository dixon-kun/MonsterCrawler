POSITION SUMMARY:

Under minimal supervision of the Sr. Director, Sales, the Project Manager, Government plans, organizes, and manages all phases of large complex projects within the government channel. He/she has primary accountability for developing effective project plans, managing cross functional teams, and delivering on the project objectives, schedule, and budget. This is accomplished by applying the knowledge of project management tools and processes towards project activities, combined with the skills and abilities to guide a diverse team to achieve a common goal. He/she may directly supervise other team members assigned to government projects and proposals.

PRIMARY JOB RESPONSIBILITIES will include, but are not limited to, the following:

Effectively applies knowledge of project management processes and tools leads to large complex projects, including project planning, project direction, team formation and development, project tracking and control, and project deliverables by:

  • Determining resource requirements, selecting team members, and coordinating support functions.
  • Defining project tasks, formulating plans for expected performance, and assigning tasks to project team members.
  • Tracking, monitoring, and reviewing project team performance against objectives.
  • Ensuring the proper evaluation and recognition of project team performance.
  • Auditing the status of commitments, plans, schedules, work scope and finances on a periodic basis.

Ensures satisfaction and quality of deliverables by:

  • Working with management to forecast resource demands and task completion.
  • Directing the formulation of the management plan for the project to meet contract requirements using the best available tools.
  • Managing project changes, communicating/justifying changes to appropriate management, and managing the related execution of prescribed project charge control procedures.

Serves as primary contact between all constituents throughout the project by:

  • Fully understanding the goals and objectives of each project and the business need(s) driving those objectives.
  • Communicating expectations/objectives clearly and continuously to team members and stakeholders.
  • Articulating and representing issues and obstacles faced by the project team.
  • Managing development of technical resolutions.
  • Developing communication plans and managing resource plans.
  • Reporting project progress to appropriate management.

Provide consulting and business analysis in developing solutions and solving problems.

Leads, coaches, and motivates project team members on a proactive basis.

Value, understand, and integrate diverse points of view to explore innovative solutions while at the same time keeping the team on track, focused, and on plan.

Monitor the team’s adherence to documented security, confidentiality, and privacy guidelines.


MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS:

  • A minimum 5 years of hands-on experience in project management
  • A Bachelor's degree in computer science, business , engineering or related area
  • Project Management certification is preferred
  • Ability to effectively manage all aspects of a project, including planning, monitoring, control, team formation, project tracking, and project deliverables
  • Ability to develop and maintain effective communication and resource plans
  • Demonstrated ability to lead and manage teams of people; demonstrated success in leading cross-functional and inter-disciplinary teams towards common objectives.
  • Objectively review team members and communicate results to appropriate management
  • High degree of proficiency in composing professional business correspondence and presentation materials
  • Arrive at valid conclusions, recommendations, and plans of action
  • Advanced problem-solving and root cause analysis skills
  • Demonstrated leadership ability to guide project teams in achieving objectives and balancing constraints.
  • Detail oriented with a capability of achieving results
  • Advanced negotiation and conflict resolution skills
