Role Overview

Cortland Partners is looking to leverage its position as the fastest growing company within multifamily investment over the past five years to continue to become one of the biggest and most technologically advanced in its space.

The HRIS Technology Analyst will work with an agile team, building out an HRIS analytics and data science ecosystem to facilitate continued company growth.


The Role You'll Play

  • Facilitate HR/Talent data governance initiatives
  • Guide new cloud data architecture around HRIS requirements
  • Leverage ETL tools to integrate HR software platforms and market data
  • Manage cross-departmental HR strategy execution and reporting automation
  • Develop Business Intelligence and Data Science solutions using modern platforms and best practices

Your Building Blocks of Success

  • Excellent working with HR platforms such as ADP/UltiPro, iCims, and Cornerstone
  • Expertise with technologies such as data warehouses, SSIS, R, Python, Git, and VBA
  • Experience with ETL tools, Business Intelligence platforms, API Integration, Data Science and machine learning

Required Knowledge and Experience

  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Mathematics, Statistics or related field
  • GPA 3.5 or higher

Company Overview

At Cortland, we create, reimagine, and manage apartment communities for over 100,000 residents nationwide. Headquartered in Atlanta, GA, we have communities in ten states and regional offices in Charlotte, Dallas, Denver, Houston, and Orlando. From product design and procurement to general contracting and property management, we do it all – to make sure our communities are the perfect setting for living life to its fullest.

Our success is fueled by our belief in a better life – where hospitality is always a given, each detail is worth a second thought, and every open door is a new opportunity to go beyond expectations. We come to work every day to create possibilities for people – possibilities that translate into superior living spaces and experiences designed to inspire our residents, associates, and investors to live a better life focused on what matters most to them.
