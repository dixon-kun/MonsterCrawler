        Jabil Data Scientist - Quality
        Job Summary
        The Data Scientist is an experienced statistical/operations research analyst in various analytical disciplines, such as linear and constraint programming, modeling, simulation, time series analysis, text analytics, multivariate analysis, and other various predictive analytics techniques. This role will have experience querying and analyzing large data sets using traditional structured data base tools and 'big data' tools for unstructured data to generate actionable insights and solutions.
        ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
        * The main responsibilities of the role are investigation of real-time global manufacturing data as captured in diverse business sectors through the use of predictive analytics data mining tools.
        * In addition, the advanced quality team also conducts deep analysis of data on a broad range of strategic and tactical initiatives from production line data requirements and targets.
        * A strong passion for empirical research and answering hard questions with data; the demonstrated ability to conceptualize, promote and implement new ideas for the business.
        * Expert knowledge of various platforms; IBM, Microsoft Azure, and R required and candidates with strong knowledge of GE and SAS is preferable.
        * Strong working knowledge of data mining algorithms--decision trees, probability networks, structural equation modeling or association rules, clustering, time-series, composite distance, linear and logistic regression,-- as applied to production line, and customer or suppliers stream data.
        * Familiarity with database structures and data warehousing principles including a strong knowledge of SQL with the ability to ETL both structured and unstructured data from various sources is required, as well Hadoop, and enterprise networking.
        * Develop and implement data mining solutions to fit business problem, which may include applying algorithms from a standard tool or custom algorithm development.
        * Work closely with data warehouse architects and software developers to generate seamless business intelligence solutions for end users.
        * Support production data mining solutions.
        * Perform ad hoc statistical and data mining analyses
        KNOWLEDGE REQUIREMENTS
        * We are looking for scientist comfortable manipulating and analyzing complex, high-volume, high-dimensionality data from varying sources and then promoting the output of those analyses to the site manufacturing staff, managers, and executives of the business.
        * Experience working with large data sets within distributed computing tools is a plus (Hadoop, Hive, etc.)
        * Creativity to go beyond current tools to deliver best solution to the problem of predictive analytics
        * Strong programming skills, especially shell scripting in PHP, Python, Perl, Unix or Java
        * Ability to communicate complex quantitative analysis in a clear, precise, and actionable manner with excellent interpersonal, written, and verbal communication skills especially with Power-point.
        * Strong data visualization skills, with Tableau experience preferred.
        * Ability and comfort working independently and making key decisions on projects
        EDUCATION &amp; EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS
        * Ph.D. in Computer Science with a focus on Data Mining/Machine Learning. Statistics, Physics, Operations Research or related field are also acceptable
        * M.S. in related field with 8+ years experience in applying data mining techniques to real business problems.
