Jacobs is looking for a Senior Solutions Architect to join our Jacobs Connected Enterprise (JCE) Center of Excellence (CoE) team to enhance our next generation technology capabilities and grow our business.  This role – Requirement IHO00001Q – reports to the Director, CoE.  The most successful candidate in this “cornerstone” role will have a “complex systems mindset,” the ability to develop a comprehensive strategy for the technical execution of a solution, and the ability to communicate that strategy clearly and concisely.  We are looking for someone to make an immediate and positive impact and deliver value to our organization by displaying behavior consistent with Jacobs’ values, building and maintaining strong relations with our customers – both internal and external, and representing Jacobs through participation in key industry events and communication forums.  This position can be worked from the National Capital Region, Tullahoma, TN or Nashua, NH (in order of preference). The MOST successful candidate for this opportunity MUST be able to:

· Lead the technical architecture and development of our newly established Jacobs Connected Enterprise (JCE) Center of Excellence (CoE), a solutions development environment which will integrate existing, geographically distributed technology lab infrastructure and provide a flexible and scalable Cloud-based, Software Defined Networking (SDN) controlled environment within which to design, develop, prototype, demonstrate and deploy solutions for Jacobs’ customers across all lines of business

· Be highly conversant / technically proficient in Networking and SDN technologies – two critical components of this position – as well as with Cloud platforms – AWS, Azure, etc. – and complex IoT/IIoT environments.

· Be the Subject Matter Expert and key advisor to CoE leadership on all CoE labs inter-connectivity, IoT/IIoT and related technology matters

· Lead the technical architecture and development lifecycle for Internet of Things (IoT) and Industrial IoT-enabled solutions that support the delivery of better business outcomes for Jacobs’ customers

· Lead the development of roadmaps for future capability and solution development based on input from – and close coordination with – Jacobs’ internal business area and functional leads, key customers, partners, and third party industry and technology sources

· Support the development of Managed Services offerings around developed capabilities and solutions / Solutions as a Service

· Consult with IT team members and customers on best practices and optimized application development and deployment models within cloud-hosted (public cloud, private cloud, and hybrid cloud environments) and SDN-managed environments.

The requirements of the Senior Solutions Architect opportunity are appended below:

· BS degree in Engineering, Information Technology, Computer Science or related field from an accredited college or university and fourteen (14) years relevant/specialized experience in IT operations and/or deployment experience in an enterprise environment, or an MS degree and twelve (12) years of progressive and relevant experience, or a Ph.D. degree and seven (7) years of progressive and relevant experience.  Experience should include five (5) to seven (7) years in Data Center / Cloud/ Enterprise Network Design

· Must have demonstrated understanding and capabilities with successfully deploying cloud technologies, SDN/network architecture, IoT/IIoT (sensor technology and integration), data science/analytics to develop and deploy analytics applications, business continuity design requirements, network security, compute virtualization.

Prior experience supporting the following is a significant PLUS:

· Large, complex workload requiring coordination of multiple resources, and multi-tasking in a fast-paced environment

· Evaluating challenging problems and developing creative solutions

· Working with senior levels of customer engagement, both Technical and Management

· Public and/or Private Cloud environment, Big Data, and IoT/IIoT protocol / strategy

· Mentoring or training team members and sharing expertise within and across contract, organization boundaries and with customers

· Several enabling IoT/IIoT technology areas, to include embedded systems, network topologies, transport protocols, cloud computing, stream processing, data management, data analytics, security, identity management, industrial control systems, and application development (i.e programming and scripting languages such as Java, J2EE, Python, Perl, etc.)

· Complex, enterprise-level, systems integration environments that involve multiple IoT/IIoT technology areas as described above

· Working with IoT/IIoT platforms such as Thingworx, Predix, etc.

If you believe that you are a STRONG candidate for this position and would be interested in discussing the opportunity further, I would encourage you to apply to the requirement on our Jacobs Careers site at: http://www.jacobs.com/careers .  Applying to the requirement allows us to track your application and resume, work efficiently with our hiring managers throughout the evaluation process and – most importantly – remain OFCCP compliant.  The application process should take just 5 to 10 minutes. If you are not interested in this opportunity but know of someone in your network that may be, please don’t hesitate to send them our way!
