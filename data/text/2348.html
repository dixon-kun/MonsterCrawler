Research Analyst / Project Administrator

This position requires a highly analytical, detail-oriented and quality-driven person with a high level of intellectual curiosity. As a Research Analyst / Financial Administrator, you play a role in supporting the delivery of strategic insights to our clients and to senior management through the execution of research and consulting projects. Your work includes a variety or studies requiring sharp time management skills and a keen attention to detail. The ideal candidate is a business-minded individual who is interested in and intrigued by integrating data and research into actionable recommendations for top tier clients and executives. This is an excellent opportunity for those with a strong academic background in math, science, or business. A dynamic super high achiever with a liberal arts degree? You will fit too!

This role will be split approximately 60% Project Administrationwork, and 40% Research Analyst work. However, workload by role will vary week to week.

Key Areas of Responsibility

  • Making projects happen: Coordinating and managing survey research projects on time and on budgets, working closely with our buddies in other departments. Assisting and ultimately soloing in project management.
  • Getting into the client’s business: Developing an understanding of our clients’ business issues and the industries in which they operate. Bringing it every day.
  • Show off your skills: As you progress and develop, you will get to analyze and interpret results; help prepare client reports and presentations. Set the stage for your promotion: Create and manage the necessary documentation of project requirements to ensure safety to the business and allow you to manage others doing those tasks while you go on to learn something else.

Qualifications

  • You are awesome.
  • You love, love, love playing with data. You aspire to be called a “data scientist”.
  • Within you is the potential to tell killer stories in presentation, visually and in writing.
  • You have a strong interest in the product quality and customer satisfaction.
  • Strong working proficiency with MS Office (specifically MS PowerPoint, Word and Excel).
  • Completed Bachelor’s Degree level of education or higher, preferably in a related major such as marketing, science, social science, business or mathematics with an overall GPA of 3.0 or higher.
  • Beyond this, extra points for experience in working with quantitative analysis packages, especially SPSS.
