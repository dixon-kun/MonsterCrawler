        Data Science Manager, Analytics
        Tumblr is seeking a manager well-versed in statistical analysis and algorithm design, ready to be let loose on Tumblr's many terabytes of data.
        What You'll Do:
        * Experiment with big data, big systems, and big challenges with an extraordinarily small and talented team
        * Partner with Product and Engineering teams to solve problems and identify trends and opportunities for user growth
        * Empower each team to make data-driven decisions independently
        * Build key data set and user behavior models to empower exploratory analysis and influence product/business decisions
        * Work with the Director of of Data Science, Analytics to set goals and key metrics
        * Influence product decisions through data analysis while spreading best analytics practice to product teams
        * Create an environment in which your team members are continuously challenged and exposed to new opportunities.
        What We're Looking For:
        * BA/BS degree in quantitative field strongly preferred.
        * 5+ years of experience working with very large datasets of varied levels of structure.
        * Experience managing a team of Product Analysts
        * Must have Python experience
        * Knowledge of SQL skills against large datasets.
        * Proven quantitative skills, ability to think analytically, and strong attention to details.
        * Experience vetting product ideas by forecasting business impact
        * Experience using multiple models and methods for measuring, analyzing, and reporting on user interaction data.
        * Extensive experience in solving analytical problems using quantitative approaches
        * Experience extracting data and deriving insights to facilitate product development
        * Experience with user growth and retention in an Internet-based company a plus
        * An effective manager and communicator who can articulate a clear direction for the team, set expectations for performance, and foster a positive working environment
        * Smarts, humility, and equal willingness to learn and teach.
        * Ability to work with APIs in Python a plus (e.g. Google Analytics, Tumblr).
        Tools We Use:
        * SQL, HQL, Python

        * Google Sheets, Excel
