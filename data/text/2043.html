Process Technology Scientist

Apply

Piper Clinical Solutions is seeking a motivated Process Technology Scientist in King of Prussia, PA to support the technology and facilities transfer of a new pharmaceutical manufacturing development.

Responsibilities for the Process Technology Scientist:

  • Technology transfer of pharmaceutical manufacturing pro cesses from development groups to operations
  • Monitoring technical operations of the transfer through communication with outside vendors
  • Assist in technology transfer to all involved external partners, including contract manufacturers and other parties
  • Coordination of activities between internal and external manufacturing operational groups
  • R e present the Process Technology D epartment at operational meetings and present results during meetings
  • Troubleshoot production problems f or the technical success of multi-million dollar production projects
  • Responsible for ensuring accuracy of manufacturing operatio nal data and supporting information is adequate to support regulatory filings
  • Monitor processes in ups tream or downstream operations
  • Collects information and maintain campaign summary database of process knowledge
  • Responsible for creation of batch documentation
  • Plan , assess , and support introduction of technologies vital to large-scale GMP production

Requirements for the Process Technology Scientist:

  • 2+ years of pharmaceutical manufacturing process development and implementation experience
  • 2+ years of experience in a GMP environment with regulatory documentation management
  • Experience in project management and delivery, including ability to prioritize and forward plan courses of action
  • Experience with Lean Sigma and Root Cause Analysis methodologies
  • Experience with SAP software a plus
  • M.S. in chemical engineering, biochemical sciences, or related engineering/scientific field or B.S. in listed disciplines with a minimum of 4 years of relevant experience

Compensation for the Process Technology Scientist:

  • Salary up to $ 100 ,000 based on meeting of requirements
  • Benefits: Health, Dental, & Vision Insurance and 401K contributions

Please send all qualified resumes to: idusci@pipercompanies.com
