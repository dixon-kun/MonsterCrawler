        Digital Data Scientist
        Job Responsibilities
        Your specific duties as a Digital Data Scientist will include:
        * The Digital Analytics Lead will be responsible for planning and executing reporting and analytics, delivering key insights and provide ongoing testing and optimization support.
        * The Digital Analytics Lead will help the team move from the 'what' of reporting to the 'why' of performance through the combination of deeper analysis, A/B testing &amp; the utilization of experience optimization tools.
        * Manage the analysis of site performance including traffic patterns, navigation and conversion paths, marketing campaigns and site enhancements and proactively seek and define opportunities to improve conversion, user experience, marketing effectiveness and ROI
        * Work with analytics vendors, development teams, agencies and internal analytics teams to ensure that all tagging and reporting is completed as per best practices and that feedback is provided back across the teams on improving standards and execution
        * Manage online testing program, including: (a) development of test recommendations; (b) design and implementation of testing plans; (b) analysis and interpretation of results; (c) communication of results and future recommendations to testing committee
        * Manage online voice of the customer program, including: (a) development of customer survey recommendations; (b) cross brand analysis, interpretation of results and communication to stakeholders (c) recommendations for enhancements to the voice of the customer program
        * Develop managerial dashboards, leveraging online and offline data to enhance understanding of the digital consumer, navigation across digital properties, and marketing effectiveness.
        * Make recommendations on how to apply these learnings across the business.
        * Build strong data-driven working relationships with internal teammates across key departments to link online and offline data to better understand overall customer behavior throughout the purchase process.
        * Guide key stakeholders in the appropriate implementation and validation of new tagging requirements and fixes, and ensure existing tracking remains intact
        * Proactively monitor the integrity and accuracy of analytics and 3rd party data used for analysis and work with the technical teams to resolve any issues
        * Proactively identify opportunities to evolve our measurement, reporting and testing capabilities and develop business case for executive approval
