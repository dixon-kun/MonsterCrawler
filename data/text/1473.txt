**Job Description:**
We are building a team that is changing the perceptions of one of the most recognized brands in the world.  This new team will be comprised of creative, innovative and disciplined storytellers that are committed to telling smart, succinct and sustained stories on behalf of McDonald&#8217;s.  McDonald&#8217;s is changing and is committed to doing the right thing, committed to using its size and scale for good and committed to consistently delivering feel good moments for all of its customers and employees.
If you are looking to join an organization as a communicator that acts as a change agent, that influences the future of a leadership brand and helps shapes new processes and modern public relations plans, then this position is for you. As the Supervisor of Brand Trust Communications for McDonald's Corporate Communications team, you will help create and change the perceptions of one of the most recognized brands in the world.
Reporting to one of the three Managers of Brand Trust, the Supervisor will be responsible for developing and managing communications programs and content to support the brand reputation stories that drive perceptual change that consists of either Food Quality, Food Responsibility, People/Employer, Restaurant Experience, Digital Innovation or Community Impact stories.  The Supervisor will work closely with their Manager and the Brand Trust team leader to create the strategic framework, story ledes, and approach all planning with 360 degrees of storytelling plans that integrate relevant stories across multiple audiences.  The supervisor will need strong storytelling and writing skills, the ability to mine data and apply learnings for results-driven program recommendations and outputs, as well as a firm understanding of digital and social media platforms, and an understanding of targeting key audiences.
In this role, the Supervisor will provide communications counsel and support to internal business partners, apply data-driven learnings to develop resources and materials that enhance national and regional brand building programs and drive awareness to enhance overall brand image and advocacy.
