The Safety Statistics Group (SSG) at AbbVie is a leader and driver of the Safety and Statistics Excellence Program (SSEP) and Benefit-Risk (B/R) assessment. This group is an important strategic and tactical part of Data and Statistical Sciences (DSS) that works as part of a collaborative, cross functional partnership in drug safety and benefit-risk assessment.

The SSG focuses specifically on partnering with Pharmacovigilance and Patient Safety (PPS) in evaluation, interpretation, and reporting of safety data analyses. The work involves all stages of development and life cycle management in the AbbVie portfolio, with a focus on purpose-oriented, proactive, aggregate/integrated safety and benefit risk analyses planning, execution, and interpretation.

AbbVie is seeking an experienced statistician with strategic skills to serve in a leadership role in our growing Safety Statistics team. The Director, Safety Statistics will be responsible for statistical support for safety assessments, including people management responsibility within SSG for one or more of AbbVie's primary therapy areas, and developing one or more best practices in safety or benefit-risk technical expertise (eg, meta-analysis, Bayesian methods), tool development (e.g., JReview), or processes.

Key Responsibilities:

* Responsible for compliance with applicable corporate and divisional policies and procedures

* Responsible for project and resource management within SSG for projects in Therapeutic Area(s) of responsibility

* As a leader in SSG, ensure the identification and implementation of best practice processes and safety analytic and graphic methods in collaboration with SSG colleagues across AbbVie therapeutic and functional areas

* Responsible oversight of timely development of Product Safety Statistical Analysis Plans (including Benefit Risk planning), Integrated Summary of Safety Statistical Analysis Plans, and his/her team's deliverables; Ensure alignment of the PSSAP with the PSP, CSR and ISS SAPs, and timely and quality delivery of ISS, DSUR, PSUR/PBRER, IB, and RMP deliverables

* Work with our partners in PPS and in Pipeline Statistics, Statistical Programming, and Data Sciences to strategically plan and ensure delivery of PSSAP and ISS SAP outputs according to agreed-upon timelines

* Active participant in Benefit-Risk value tree creation/discussion and responsible for the appropriate BR analyses (Benefit Risk Product Assessment and relevant submission documents)

* Provide specifications on how the data should be integrated across studies in accordance with the PSSAP provided to statistical programming and ensure appropriate analyses and interpretation for PBRER/DSUR/IB/RMP/Regulatory Requests, signal evaluations and Safety Summary for NDA/BLA/Other Regulatory Activities

* Understand and support the use of safety graphics for ongoing safety review and appropriate inclusion in safety documents as part of regulatory submissions

* Represents SSG in strategic initiatives that address processes relating to interpreting, monitoring, assessing, and reporting Safety Data

* Represent SSG/DSS on initiatives driven by external professional organizations such as ASA, PhUSE, DIA, etc.

Qualifications:

* MS/PhD in Statistics, Biostatistics or related field. Minimum 10 years of experience in pharmaceutical development and applied statistics/statistical consulting

* ·Experience with assessment of product safety is highly desired

* Advanced knowledge of statistical methodology and global regulatory requirements; expertise in drug discovery or development

* Must have demonstrated exemplary experience in managing and completing multiple projects or equivalent experience

* Substantive interactions with regulatory authorities preferred

* 3 years of experience in leading a diverse group of statistical scientists, and oversight of safety-oriented projects

* Demonstrated highly effective written and verbal communication skills and demonstrated ability to navigate, negotiate and influence benefit-risk and safety monitoring/integration

* Awareness of key challenges in the rapidly evolving drug safety and benefit-risk environment

* Proven ability to understand and implement relevant benefit-risk and/or safety monitoring methods and visualizations

Key Leadership Competencies:

* Builds strong relationships with peers and cross functionality with partners outside of team to enable higher performance

* Learns fast, grasps the 'essence' and can change course quickly where indicated

* Raises the bar and is never satisfied with the status quo

* ·Creates a learning environment, open to suggestions and experimentation for improvement

* Embraces the ideas of others, nurtures and manages innovation to reality
