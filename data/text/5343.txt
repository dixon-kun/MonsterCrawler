        Technical Manager
        Why Our Technical Managers Matter
        Our Technical Managers are the influencers and leaders across multiple agile delivery teams. They are key participants in the delivery organization management team, overseeing the work of mobile application software developers and software quality assurance engineers across the business. Our Technical Managers take ownership of the Technical Design and User Stories supplied by UX and Architecture teams at the beginning of the project, and become the recognized expert of required feature functionality for the project. They work closely with Project Managers to maintain realistic task schedules for which all technical resources are prepared and available to meet delivery deadlines. Technical Managers should be able to put their fingers on the keyboard and write code, help troubleshoot complex production issues, and assist in the technical design of the systems and software.
        Technical Managers at Mobiquity are dedicated to our clients, and harness the best of their hands on experience with leadership and model behaviors. Our Technical Managers possess the unique ability to inspire, motivate and rally their teams toward the desired goal. The Technical Manager's passion and drive to create truly innovative mobile solutions helps Mobiquity remain a top competitor in an ever-changing mobile industry, and encourages their teams to continue creating enterprise scale and quality solutions.
        The Skills that Matter
        * 10+ years of experience developing full-stack enterprise software
        * Ability to context switch and work in an open-space environment on-site at a customer facility
        * Ability to troubleshoot large, enterprise systems hosted in AWS
        Role: Technical Manager: as part of an agile team, responsible to:
        * analyze new requirements,
        * design and implement technical solutions
        * execute code reviews
        * coach and mentor colleagues on development team
        * research and test new solutions, including authoring sample code, to solve customer technical obstacles
        * interact with client managers, developers, testers and operations teams
        Technical Skills
        * 10+ years of overall full-stack software development experience
        * 3+ years of experience in agile development teams
        * 2+ years in leading technical teams
        * Strong knowledge and experience with Java / JEE
        * Experience in cloud development technologies (AWS)
        * Ability to implement continuous integration, source control and automated testing
        * Bachelor of Science in Computer Science or related field
        Leadership skills
        * Ability to lead and work with a team of 6-10 members
        * Must be able to work with the Scrum Master in mapping the agile lifecycle, outlining the sprints &amp; overall roadmap and schedule, and set / review the overall quality of deliverables
        * Must be passionate about coding and able to mentor the local and remote team members as part of execution
        * Experience in handling or managing the customer interactions and deliverables
        * Excellent communication, collaboration, problem solving and facilitation skills.
        * Excellent writing and presentation skills.
