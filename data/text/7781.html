The Group: The Products group is the home of manufacturing and innovation at Morningstar. The individuals in this group transform our high-quality data, independent research, and technological expertise into well-designed products that delight our clients. The group’s product management, product development, and service teams collaborate with our Solutions group to help them provide clients with relevant products and bundled solutions The Role: You are a dynamic individual that can manage and lead the product strategy and direction for Morningstar’s portfolio of business intelligence and data analytics offerings. This is a new service/group offering and strong, continued investment is expected in coming years as this product portfolio represents a high growth area to meet market needs. You will have the opportunity to define the new product such that it leverages advanced analytics to address business and regulatory concerns. You have a good understanding of enterprise data analytics techniques, machine learning, competing software applications, and experience in developing analytics solutions. Most importantly, you are an inquisitive self-starter and willing to learn about new techniques and technology. As a Product Manager you will drive the success of our projects with high profile customers. Analytics is an emerging space in the financial service vertical and you will work with customers to identify opportunities that leverage portfolio data generated from internal systems as well as investors “held away” investments. The product will bring new insight and provides actionable value to these customers that mitigates risk and improves performance. You must be able to work with technical and non-technical customers. With these customers, you will define new analytical modules to enhance our platform. You will also be hands on with with a multi-disciplinary data science and engineering teams to develop products for international market needs. Responsibilities: Engage with customers to understand their problems Drive innovation! Build products from existing ideas, and help to develop new ideas based on your industry experience, your contact with prospects and your consumption of secondary research sources. Possess a unique blend of business and technical savvy; a big-picture vision, and the drive to make that vision a reality. Enjoy spending time in the market to understand customer and end user problems, and find innovative solutions that can translate to the broader market. Research, define and support the development of new products with analytic solutions to improve customer operations Create proposals and statements of work Ability to drive proof of concepts with very crisp success criteria Understand and communicate the basic technology and development to customers Function as the Product Owner in an Agile development environment Explore new technologies and new ways of looking at data to solve business needs Minimum Qualifications: Three plus years experience as a product manager/product owner for enterprise data analytics products Experience in defining and delivering data analytics solutions Experience working in the financial services A technical background to understand and communicate data science and machine learning concepts The ability to interface with customers and translate vague needs to crisp deliverables upon which the customer can take action Must be capable of identifying insight derived from analysis that fits the customers ability to take action Experience with SaaS and cloud based software solutions Ability to work with across teams in an agile environment Problem solver, self starter, and strong communicator Ability to travel approximately 25% Preferred Qualifications: Experience developing and deploying enterprise SaaS software Experience developing analytic solutions for large enterprise operations High-level knowledge of Cloud Services such as AWS, Azure or Predix Familiarity with Hadoop and big data storage and movement (Greenplum/HAWQ, Hive, HBase, Kafka) Knowledge of analytic technologies like Spark MLlib, H20.ai, Big Insights, R, MADlib or other statistical tools Education: Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Operations Research, or other engineering discipline or relevant work experience Morningstar is an equal opportunity employer. 001_MstarInc Morningstar Inc.
