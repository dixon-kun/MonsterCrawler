Dewberry is seeking IT Data Engineer preferably with experience in geospatial analytics. This position will support a wide array of projects at national, regional, and local scales. Candidates will have strong competencies and demonstrated experience using statistical, numerical and computer application techniques to conduct analyses. Builds systems and infrastructures that collect and process data according to company needs and goals. Builds, evolves and scales out infrastructure to ingest, process and extract meaning out data.


Designs, develops and implements statistical models to carry out various novel aspects of classification and information extraction from data. Leverages existing data infrastructure to fulfill all data-related requests, perform necessary data housekeeping, data cleansing, normalization, hashing, and implementation of required data model changes. Analyzes data to spot anomalies, trends and correlate similar data sets.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Support large-scale data collection, processing, cleaning, and verifying the data used for analytics
  • Data mining using statistical methods
  • Preparing dashboards for visualizing analytics results
  • Conducting data analyses and packaging results tailored for both technical and non-technical audience.
  • Coordinating with the internal and external stakeholders to collect requirements, inform analyses, and develop presentations of results.

Must have experience that demonstrates a strong background in the application of GIS and skills to execute efficient solutions. The candidate should have strong organizational skills, as well as the ability to multi-task and work independently and as a team player.

  • Bachelor’s with 3+ years’ experience with Data Science, Computer science, operation research or any quantitatively related fields.
  • Experience with numerical and statistical packages like pandas, Numpy, SciPy, matplotlib, and R.
  • Experience with data visualization tools like Tableau, Microsoft Power BI, D3.js, and Visual.ly
  • Experience in writing complex database queries using SQL
  • Preferred experience in distributed computing frameworks likes MapReduce, Spark, Hive, Impala
  • Preferred knowledge of machine learning packages likes Scikit-Learn , TensorFlow
  • Preferred experience or knowledge of GIS tools like ArcGIS 10x, Leaflet, PostGIS, Fiona
  • Innovation, enthusiasm, and creativity - ability to develop new ideas and to chart a path
  • Ability to effectively manage multiple assignments concurrently
  • Communicate complex information using visual tools and reporting to the technical and non-technical audience.
