Product Analyst(

Job Number:

FNG0006099)
Americas-US-CA-Los Angeles

Description

Works with President, Digital Product Development & Management and other DCG executives to analyze existing and competitive digital projects, to track and report on product team efforts, to help manage product team budgets, and to assist with ad-hoc requests/presentations.  Primary goal is to assist the product team with management of all aspects of FOX’s digital product development.

Responsibilities:

  • Perform ad-hoc and structured analysis on Fox Networks’ digital products, competing digital products and broad market product trends.
  • Advise management on risks and product opportunities.
  • Create written reports and presentations of findings
  • Create business cases with leadership team to guide future expectations and identify potential product opportunities
  • Strategic business cases completed for leadership
  • Assist President and other DCG executives with presentations and deliverables
  • Create decks, models, project plans, etc.
  • Assist with FOX DCG budgeting process: develop and maintain monthly/quarterly/yearly budgets, track expenses, assist with on-boarding new vendors for payment and manage invoicing and payment process
  • Complete budget, expense and invoice management
  • Assist President with end to end business operations tracking: product timeline tracking, feature tracking, new hire screening, etc.
  • Complete business operations support


Qualifications

  • Education / Experience: Business, Finance or Computer Science degree from a top tier university.  Experience with the entertainment business a must (either through internship or coursework)
  • Language / Communication: Must have superior verbal and written communications skills, particularly, the ability to develop cogent arguments and present them concisely to a range of stakeholders, especially executives
  • Mathematical / Reasoning Skills: Data modeling and analysis is a requirement.  Must have the ability to combine a broad range of factors into models
  • Statistical analysis and data science capabilities are huge positives but not required
  • Strong problem solving skills such as prioritization, critical thinking and hypothesis development
  • Ability to think pragmatically, not just theoretically, and drive execution of concepts
  • Computer Skills: Fluency in Microsoft Office applications, especially Excel, Powerpoint, and the Google equivalents is required
  • Nice to Haves
    • Computer programming experience
    • Knowledge of computer programming and app development as well as digital product management
    • Experience with BI tools such as Tableau, Microstrategy, C3 and Amazon Quicksite is a huge positive
    • Experience with linear and digital television reporting sources including Nielsen, Rentrak, Freewheel, and the Adobe Marketing Cloud (Analytics, Audience Manager, Campaign, etc.)
    • Knowledge of product management tools (Gantt Charts, etc.)
  • Other Skills & Abilities Required:
    • Must be a voracious user of multiple TV Everywhere digital video services
    • Must have an interest in digital product development.  This is not a stepping stone to the content creation side of the business!!!
    • Ability to combine strong quantitative skills with excellent interpersonal skills
    • Ability to craft concise recommendations based on thorough business and market analysis
    • Ability to communicate complex ideas and data sets visually in an effective way
    • Familiarity with working in or with large matrixed teams
    • Outstanding multi­tasking and teamwork capabilities
  • Physical Demands: Regularly required use of hands and fingers to write, manipulate telephone and computer keyboard.  Regularly required to hear, talk and use close vision.
  • Typical tools, equipment and materials utilized: Computer, iPad, iPhones, Connected Devices (e.g. AppleTV, etc.), telephone, and printer.
  • Work Environment: Primarily works in a quiet professional office environment.

Division Description:

The Fox Digital Consumer Group is a strategic business unit within Fox Networks Group (FNG). The group’s focus is the development of TV and long-form video-centric products that bring Fox’s content to more consumers. This includes the continued innovation of Fox’s existing TV Everywhere products including FXNOW, FOX NOW, Nat Geo TV and Fox Sports Go, as well as the creation of brand-new consumer propositions.
