            Description
             Position Overview
             Join a dynamic and growing team whose focus is business impact and value added. This position is responsible for driving revenue decisions, customer valuation/segmentation and revenue optimization. The right individual will understand and apply best practices from the perspectives of effective modeling/data handling, airline metrics, building business cases, and improving processes and procedures. Opportunities to present data-driven recommendations and results of revenue initiatives to internal stakeholders and senior leadership. Involvement in corporate initiatives, process improvements, forecasting/modeling, and special projects across multiple lines of business. Ability to track relevant details, identify and explain assumptions and impactful factors, and understand big picture are a must.
              Key Responsibilities:
              Evaluate business impact for pricing and inventory decisions, including standard revenue management analyses and cross-Commercial considerations
              Analyze demand, seasonality, customer booking valuation, and other inputs into revenue management system and other commercial processes
              Continuously improve accuracy of data, reporting, and forecasting quality and processes
              Perform adjustments to processes, programs, analyses, systems, and reports to maximize commercial impact; make and implement recommendations based on findings
              Develop simplified rules and automatable algorithms for implementation of commercial and programmatic suggestions
              Create ad-hoc and automated reporting to provide insight into revenue results and performance tracking for internal customers
              Design and produce commercial decision making tools to support planning and revenue management teams
              Participate in special projects or ad-hoc analyses in the commercial area as assigned
            Requirements
              Minimum Requirements:
              Proven mastery of analytical tools and thinking including outcome-focused data-driven projects such as retrospective and predictive modeling, statistical and probabilistic evaluations, pattern and exception detection, or financial/economic and sensitivity analyses
              Experience with a logic-based programming language including direct querying or creation of large datasets (1K-10M); data cleaning and manipulation; data sorting, filtering, joining, and merging
              Strong presentation and communication skills, particularly related to the visual display of quantitative information
              Ability to effectively synthesize and draw conclusions from complex data
              College degree in a quantitative discipline or equivalent years of experience in a quantitative capacity
              Preferred Qualifications:
              3-5 years of work experience in an analytical role
              Graduate degree in statistics, data science, actuarial science, operations research, computer science, applied mathematics, or similar quantitative discipline
