Title Data Analyst / Research Analyst

Description Role: Data Analyst / Research Analyst

Responsibilities:
• Provide expert services to public sector client within Department of Homeland Security as a subject matter specialist for research, data analysis, modeling and advanced analytics
• Provide range of services, as required, to include working collaboratively with clients for identification of data sources, data extraction from enterprise systems and data warehouses, creation and operation of data and analytical models and methodologies, and development of finished reports, briefing memos, analyses, and recommendations based upon results
• Develop and execute complex technical tasks and apply analytical problem solving methodologies
• Support ad-hoc analysis requests as well as scheduled requests
• Perform interviews with clients and stakeholders to identify and scope issues

Required Skills and Education:
• U.S. Citizen (only)
• Bachelors Degree in data sciences, data analytics or similar discipline
• At least 7 years of relevant professional experience
• Extensive experience using software and tools such as SPSS, SAS, Stata, R, SQL, Microsoft Excel and Microsoft Access
• Experience performing data analysis, advanced analytics, data modeling, and/or data science
• Experience in planning, evaluating, directing and coordinating research and development projects


Preferred Skills and Experience:
• Project Management Professional (PMP) Certification preferred
• Experience supporting IT, Human Capital or cybersecurity modernization efforts
• Experience supporting and/or demonstrated knowledge of homeland security and/or national security missions

Company overview:
Capgemini, one of the world's foremost providers of consulting, technology, and outsourcing services, enables its clients to transform and perform through technologies.

Capgemini Government Solutions, LLC (Capgemini GS) is a subsidiary of Capgemini focused on providing high quality Capgemini services to the U.S. Federal Government.

Capgemini GS provides equal employment opportunities (EEO) to all employees and applicants for employment. In addition to federal law requirements, Capgemini GS complies with applicable state and local laws governing nondiscrimination in employment.
