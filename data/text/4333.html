Senior Manager, Sales Engineers

DataXu’s vision is to make marketing better using data science. The world’s top brands and agencies partner with DataXu to better understand and engage their customers across all available media formats, devices, channels, and buying modes.  Our advertising solution, ranked #1 in the industry by Forrester Research in 2015, provides marketers with unparalleled Media Activation, Marketing Analytics and Data Management capabilities.

We are looking for a senior person to lead our customer-facing technical teams, focusing on revenue growth, management of a great team, and strategic thinking with our sales leaders.

On the Job

  • The Senior Manager of Sales Engineers, or Solutions Architects, is a critical member of the sales management team that will have a team that acts as the technical face of DataXu to our customers. Solutions Architects have the unique opportunity to apply their technical knowledge and industry expertise to help customers solve their business and technical challenges.  The role is integral to DataXu’s success, and would be managing a team that demonstrates DataXu’s platform, creates and develops custom solutions, explains the return on investment (ROI), and ensures customer success after the sale.  This  role presents unique and new experiences every day and requires someone who is creative, energetic, motivated by challenges and willing to work hard.

About You…

  • Experience managing global teams of people that are customer facing and technical.
  • An intimate understanding of the advertising technology ecosystem, including the prominent companies in the space and the technology that drives them.
  • 5+ years solutions engineering, solution architecture, or management consulting experience selling software platforms to Marketing departments.
  • Industry experience in Media, CPG, Financial Services, Retail
  • Foundational knowledge of web technologies, relational databases, and past programming experience.
  • Strong presentation and public speaking skills.
  • Advanced technical degree.

Bonus Points…

  • Direct experience with Demand Side Platforms (DSP) and Data Management Platforms (DMP)
  • Experience selling advertising technology platforms in the past.

Day-to-Day at the ‘Xu…

Free weekly lunches, happy hours and costume competitions? Yeah, we’ve got all that. We’re totally obsessed with our customers, our product, our people and innovation—all of which tie right in with our corporate values. We Collaborate across all teams and global offices. Our customers Trust us because we put transparency and their needs first. Innovation is part of everything that we do. Excellence is our standard mode of operation. And of course , Customer Obsession . We wouldn’t be here without our customers! So, interested in working here yet? Visit DataXu.com to learn more!
