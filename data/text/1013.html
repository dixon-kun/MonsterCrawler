About the Role

Our rapidly expanding marketing team is looking for a Marketing Media Analyst to be a core part of our team to analytically support how we spend, optimize, and manage across all our marketing channels to hit objectives. How are our campaigns performing? How can we improve our acquisition funnel effectiveness? What customer insights can we glean to better understand behavior? You will be a key partner in driving significant marketing spend decisions, performance reporting, and new opportunities for both branding and customer acquisition initiatives.

By joining SoFi, you're joining a new kind of finance company based around speed, transparency, and alignment with our members' interests. Our goal is to be the center of our members' financial lives. We created student loan refinancing, addressing the biggest financial challenge this new generation has through a new approach to lending. We expanded into other types of loans, and then into insurance and wealth management with similarly inventive products. As the company has grown, we've been able to help more people with these tools.

SoFi has achieved significant growth, with big plans ahead. In just the last year, we've more than doubled our member base (260,000 strong), doubled loan volume ($8 billion in 2016), and grown our team from under 200 people to more than 900. We're preparing to go global, with expansion to Australia and Canada planned this year. And we're well capitalized to power all this growth, having raised $1.9 billion in equity backing. But we'll only be able to continue this growth with great talent, and that includes you.

Responsibilities

* Design, analyze, and interpret the results of different marketing channels and campaigns and make optimization recommendations to the internal media buying team

* Develop metrics and frameworks for measuring campaign, site, conversion effectiveness from all media channels

* Support annual and quarterly media budgeting and buying process; Track and report campaign spend against targets.

* Make recommendations to online and offline media partners based upon data/results; Benchmark results to external data.

* Create and deliver weekly media delivery and performance reports from 3rd party systems and directly from external media partners; Reconcile any discrepancies with internal systems for billing.

* Request and analyze campaign data from external media partners for any third party reporting we can't get through GA or DCM

* Track and optimize live campaigns and build final campaign analysis upon conclusion

* Partner with Business Intelligence and Data Science teams to provide feedback on marketing attribution models, incorporate internal data into marketing analyses, and build dashboards for ongoing measurement and insights

* Provide ad hoc analytical support across all parts of the marketing organization

Qualifications

* Bachelor's Degree

* 3+ years experience media and marketing analytics to inform marketing budgeting and planning decisions from media delivery to conversion

* Strong knowledge of media performance measurement, insights and optimization across all media types (digital, TV, outdoor, mail, etc)

* Strong analytics, problem-solving, strategic thinking, and data capabilities

* Marketing analytics experience at a consumer, tech, or financial brand

* Familiarity with Google Analytics, DoubleClick Campaign Manager, and Tableau

Benefits

* Catered lunches, a fully stocked kitchen, and subsidized gym membership.

* Competitive salary packages and bonuses.

* A flexible vacation policy allows you to truly relax and reboot.

* Comprehensive health, vision, dental, and life insurance as well as disability benefits.

* 100% of health, vision, and dental premiums paid by SoFi for employees and their dependents.

* 401(k) and education on retirement planning.

* Tuition reimbursement on approved programs, up to $5,250 a year.

* Monthly contribution to help you pay off your student loans.
