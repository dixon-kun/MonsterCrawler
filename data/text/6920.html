Data Scientist will build innovative and integrative analytical solutions for quantitative analysis of multidimensional datasets towards development of predictive/ translatable biomarkers that will impact high priority therapeutic and business areas. The candidate will be responsible for developing solutions using machine learning, data mining, image processing and signal processing techniques working closely with various business clients, data stewards and other IT colleagues to turn data into critical information and knowledge that can be used to make sound decisions towards drug development.

Data Scientist will research, design, implement and validate cutting-edge algorithms to analyze diverse sources of data to achieve targeted outcomes. Data Scientist will participate in all phases of projects, including ideation, data discovery and preparation, data analytics and/or data mining, and finally assessing options and making recommendations.
