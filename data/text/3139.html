Job Description

Are you looking for a career in software? Start off on the right foot with an internship in Intel's Software and Services Group! Intel's Software and Services Group has opportunities for internships in a large variety of software areas. You will work on real projects that are crucial to Intel's software and services business. We are looking for interns to work in a variety of areas including Machine Learning, Open Source, Operating Systems, Data Science and more.

Qualifications

You must possess the below minimum qualifications to be initially considered for this position. Experience listed below would be obtained through a combination of your school work/ classes/ research and/or relevant previous job and/ or internship experiences.

Minimum Qualifications:

Must be pursing a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering or a related degree

Must have at least 6 months experience coding

Must have the unrestricted right to work in the US without requiring sponsorship
