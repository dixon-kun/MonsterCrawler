Analyst, Research Operations & Technology

Latinum was built on the idea that the cultural economy—the people, goods, and services that are inspired by or driven by culture in all its forms—will transform and define the world. We formed in 2009 focused on the U.S. Hispanic economy and through the years we have added over 100 Fortune 1000 client-members, and expanded our scope to address cultural marketing in all its forms—Mexican, Caribbean, millennial, Gen-X, African-American, Asian, hipster. If it's cultural, we're on it. We're here to disrupt the marketing space. We are innovators and builders, here to create and reinvent, and through the force of our ideas, transform industries—nothing less.

In 2012, we created Voz Latinum, a first-of-its-kind shared cost insights community. As a part of the Consumer Research and Insights team, the Research Operations and Technology Analyst will manage our survey process from end-to-end, manage panel operation and serve as a product-engineering interface within our Products and Technology team

Key Responsibilities

* Panel Operation

* Recruiting of new panelists

* Warnings to and purging of satisficers and gamers

* Manage incentives for participation

* Manage relationships with sample vendors and our survey platform provider

* Survey Fielding and Programming

* Program surveys and work with analysts to ensure feasibility (logic, flow, ultimate impact on analysis)

* Manage sample feasibility and acquisition

* Manage fielding of surveys

* Data and Tools

* Ensure clean survey data by updating the gamer table

* Produce crosstabs and other data outputs for ultimate analysis; where features are not available in current tools, work with Product lead to write and prioritize user stories for inclusion in our products

* Export data to our to our data warehouse (working with Engineering); write and prioritize stories to make this process faster and more automated

* Work with panel technology provider to rely increasingly on APIs vs. manual data steps

* Champion scaling and automation of research operations by gathering user stories from Research analysts and working with Product lead to prioritize and deploy

* Champion reproducibility across functions (version control, full raw data-to-results continuity)

Desired Skills and Experience

* Natural curiosity and desire to understand how things work (via debugging)

* A perfectionist mindset (someone who isn't happy until they are sure that something is right)

* Systems thinking (e.g. ability to visualize survey flow and scripts, seeing downstream implications of filtering data and weighting data, etc.)

* Basic computer programming experience, in particular Python, R, Selenium, and/or JavaScript, with a hunger to learn more

* Basic database understanding, including ability to write simple SQL queries

* A software development mindset to problem solving (put in the time now to prevent pain later)

* An understanding or hunger to learn data science techniques and skills

Other Qualifications

* Fluency in Spanish preferred but not required

* Experience using a version control system such as GitHub

* Experience with or understanding of Agile development environments

* Must reside in or be willing to relocate to Washington, DC area
