The Project Controls Systems Manager at the Massachusetts Port Authority serves as a system manager for capital project applications. Responsibilities to include systems administration and training, assistance with system integrations and overall data integrity. The Capital Systems Manager will also be responsible for engaging in functional enhancements of such applications, and typical project control tasks for various projects. This position will support the Authority's Capital Program totaling approximately $450 Million in annual capital investments.

Essential Tasks of the Job:Systems Administration / Implementation

Application Administrator for capital applications and oversight of the implementation of new project management systems and initiatives

Technical System Integrations

Partner with internal business teams to define business requirements and associated system requirements. Work with the Capital Program team to develop, build and deploy technical solutions

Oversight of System Integrations

Maintain and monitor all Capital Program systems integrations to ensure compliance with requirements, processes and work flows. Assess and suggest process improvements

Training of Systems

Train various staff on utilization of various systems and applications

Data Management

Business analytics â€“ collect, analyze, validate, and clean data. Design reports to be presented to senior management to inform decision making and strategic planning. Document repository development and management. Archive obsolete data according to MA state requirements

Project Controls

Assist Project Controls Manager to ensure projects are adhering to defined project control processes (estimating, budgeting, forecasting, scheduling, communication, analysis and reporting) for the entire life of the project

SECONDARY JOB TASKS:

Provide technical expertise and support to project managers on selected projects.

Performs other duties as assigned.

Job Requirements and Qualifications:EDUCATION: Bachelor's Degree in Engineering/Computer Science or equivalent professional experience required.

EXPERIENCE: 2-5 years of relevant experience.

UNIQUE EXPERTISE/CERTIFICATION/REGISTRATIONS:

Familiarity with Project Management Information Systems Preferred: (Primavera Contract Manager Cost control software, PMWeb)

Familiarity with Primavera P6 scheduling software

Familiarity with Financial Systems (PeopleSoft, Hyperion)

Familiarity with basic Project Controls Principles

Familiarity with administering websites and sharepoint sites

Proficiency with relational databases

Familiarity with data warehouses and data science concepts

Proficiency with Microsoft Office products

Current and valid driver's license

Ability to pass a Massport background check and obtain a Massport Security Badge

Ability to pass airfield driving test and obtain Aerodrome License

Ability to pass Massport pre-placement physical, controlled substance testing and background security check
