SUMMARY

Hulu's Reporting, Analytics, and Data Science Technical Program Management team is seeking a Senior Technical Program Manager who will be an exceptional addition to our growing team. As a Senior Technical Program Manager at Hulu, you will lead complex and cross team programs and contribute to establishing the vision and technology in conjunction with our Data Quality Engineers and business stakeholder. This vision will drive the Quality and Governance of Hulu's Big Data Environment enabling the business to transform data into value. The ideal candidate has a strong focus on understanding business expectations and requirements, sets a high bar for him/herself, likes to solve problems, is tenacious when developing and instituting new processes and influencing the adoption across technology and business. This person should be intellectually curious and an innovator. If you are someone who thrives in a team environment and gains personal satisfaction when accomplishing challenging projects while partnering with teams across Hulu, then this is a great role for you.

WHAT YOU'LL DO

* Own program scope and definition, develop milestones and launch schedule, and drive program execution.

* Partner with both business and engineering teams to quickly and efficiently develop standards, best practices and influence policy in this new RAD team.

* Manage vendors, proof of concepts and the integration of new data governance tools into our current environment.

* Stand up new processes and evangelize the new data governance and data dictionary protocols.

* Introduce new concepts across the organization.

* Manage requirements, relationships, dependencies, risks, timelines and deliverables.

* Manage impediments, and drive consensus in engineering teams and between business and technologies.

* Manage multiple projects with shifting and competing priorities.

* Capture business and product requirements and translate them into actionable roadmap for the engineering teams.

* Lead Sprint / release retrospective to identify lesson leant and best practice for continuous improvement.

* Influence the business in driving this new initiative to completion.

* Work to bring order across multiple data sources,

* Self-starter, able to determine a challenge and provide and action a solution.

* Critical thinker whom holistically sees the big picture and works to manage to a broader vision.

WHAT TO BRING

* 4+ years of experience in Data Analytics, preferred experience program managing Big Data Environment Challenges (Cloudera, Hortonworks, Hadoop/Spark)

* Experience with Organizational Transformation Initiatives

* Previous experience leading an implementation of a Master Data Management System, Data Dictionary or Data Governance model across a medium to large organization

* 2+ years of experience in Agile software development

* Self-starter, able to determine a challenge, then provide an action and a solution

* Critical thinker whom holistically sees the big picture and works to manage to a broader vision.

* Communicator with analytical skills and business sense

* Ability to manage concurrent projects and interact with a diverse set of technical and non-technical stakeholders.

* Ability to work effectively with people distributed across geographical locations.

* Strong passion and motivation for advertising and/or big data / data analysis products and technologies.

* Good interpersonal skill and effective written and oral communication skills in English.

NICE-TO-HAVES

* Bachelor's degree in business, computer science, or other related field.

* Formal training in project management; certification a plus

* Formal training in Agile/scrum; certification a plus

* Insight into current Quality Engineer tools and technology offerings (Trillium, SAS, Informatica. Talend, Paxata)

* Preferred experience program managing Big Data Environment Challenges (Cloudera, Hortonworks, Hadoop/Spark)

* LI-CW1
