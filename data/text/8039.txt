Data Engineer Intern (Summer 2017) - Rocket Software | Jobs.com
         Rocket Software is looking for a passionate Data Engineer to help us build and launch a cognitive solution. You will work side-by-side with the advanced analytics team and the lead data engineer to acquire, prepare and publish datasets that are needed for the machine learning models being worked on by the advanced analytics team. You will be involved in a team that is solving some of the hardest problems for our Fortune 500 customers.
         Join our team and take on this unique opportunity to reinvent database administration as we know it. If you are a developer who is passionate about data science and analytics, we want to talk to you!
          Our Intern Program is open to candidates who are currently attending College/University and are working towards completion of a degree.
         What you will do?
          Implement high-performance data acquisition, and shaping pipelines in support of the advanced analytics team.
          Work closely with other cross functional teams to understand characteristics and create new data feed pipelines.
          Work closely with the advanced analytics team to understand the machine learning models being built and the data acquisition and shaping needs for such models.
        Required Skills
         What you will bring?
          You find tedious work boring and would rather automate it
          You thrive in a fast-paced environment, are curious, industrious, and see yourself as a partner with the end-user with the shared goal of moving the end-user more efficient.
          You can argue your viewpoints vigorously, listen to all sides of a debate and adapt to new perspectives that may emerge from the discussion.
          You have a high tolerance for ambiguity and fast-changing contexts.
          You are motivated to explore new technologies and learn, and can do so without taking formal education.
          You love freedom and given the context, capable of self-direction.
         Why Join Us?
         The internship experience will provide an insider view of how a fast moving, innovative, and entrepreneurial company operates through an exciting and challenging work experience. Our interns will operate as a team to design and build, from the ground up, brand new and innovative applications related to statistical analysis, and supervised machine learning.
