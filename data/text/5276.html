Responsibilities:

The Performance Management Business Intelligence Analyst is a key member of the team responsible for producing and delivering the enterprise performance reporting and analytics to the Senior Leadership Team and other key stakeholders, within the required time-line. This includes:

  • Gathering and compiling data from various functional areas across the enterprise
  • Developing and maintaining metrics repository.
  • Report and dashboard creation and updating
  • Analyzing the information and building insights into root cause and impact.
  • Providing analysis on the information with a business mindset of “why”, “how” and “what”
  • Performing quality reviews on information (data/ report/ dashboard/ scorecard) built.
  • The Business Intelligence Analyst will be responsible to improve the reporting process through automation and building of visual analytics.
  • Provide support for other functional areas related to ad-hoc requests, specific projects and initiatives.
  • Commits to satisfying internal and external customers.
  • Works in a team environment to accomplish objectives.

Education/Experience:

  • 3 to 5 years' of experience, perferably in business, analytics or operations.
  • 3 years of experience with Business Intelligence tools (Qlikview/ Business Objects/ Tableau or any open source reporting/ analytics tool).
  • 1 year of experience with developing worksheets, dashboards and stories in Tableau.
  • 3 years of experience in Macro building, automation using Visual Basic / Access Database/ Excel.
  • Bachelor's Degree required in Math, statistics or data science.
  • Ability to perform data and business analysis and translate analysis into operations and business impact.
  • Able to meet deadlines and work within timeline constraints.
  • Strong process-management skills.
