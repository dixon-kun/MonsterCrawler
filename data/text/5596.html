The Senior Compliance Officer role will support the Head of AML Future State Strategy in creating and driving strategic opportunities across functions within Global AML Monitoring and Investigation. The role will work proactively in using research and architectural skills to spot opportunities in the solution, business process and data strategies to optimize the effectiveness of the overall AML monitoring and investigation program around the globe.

* Oversee and certify project and operation works in conformance with strategic directions.

* Accountability for significant business results.

* Develop and document current and future state solution/functional strategies and roadmap around AML suspicious activity detection channels use and design in consideration of source data type and volume, platform architecture, detection logic, target suspicious activity type and emerging risk/trends.

* Evaluate, identify and refine detection channel strategy according to latest trends, emerging risk and banking technology, such as IP address, mobility banking, block chain, etc.

* Design and oversee implementation of end-to-end integrated AML detection channel solutions.

* Key contributors to initiatives that drive the detection channel improvement inline with overall monitoring and investigation strategy.

* Communicate decisions, plans, and goals on AML detection channel strategies.

* Gain organizational commitment for strategic initiatives.

* Maintain in-depth knowledge of industry best practices, technologies, architectures and emerging trends

* 10 years' relevant solution and architecture design and implementation experience in data flow, business analytics, data processing and process controls.

* Data analytic, transformation and processing technology and language skills, including SQL, Oracle, Teradata, ETL, ER Diagram, Big Data, etc..

* 5 years of experience in MS offices tools, such as Excel, PowerPoint, Word and Visio

* Subject Matter Expertise in specific subject areas related to AML, e.g. customer/account data, transaction monitoring sanction watch lists, AML alerts/Case/SAR management

* 2 years experience in coordinating and reconciliation of project work, timeline, scope and resource management.

* Requires complex judgment and solutions based analytical thought.

* Bachelor's Degree required and a higher degree in mathematics, statistic or data science will be a plus
