Bachelor's degree in software engineering or a related technical field is required (or equivalent experience), plus a minimum of 8 years of relevant experience; or Master's degree plus a minimum of 6 years of relevant experience. Agile experience preferred.

  • Critical: Hybrid Developer/Data Analyst

Knowledge:

  • Critical: Nifi and Scala programming
  • Preferred: Accumulo, Spring Boot

CLEARANCE REQUIREMENTS: Department of Defense Top Secret with Polygraph security clearance is required at time of hire. Applicants selected will be subject to a U.S. Government security investigation and must meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information. Due to the nature of work performed within our facilities, U.S. citizenship is required.


General Dynamics Mission Systems has an immediate opening for an Analytic Developer.  This position provides an opportunity to further advance the cutting-edge technology that supports some of our nation’s core defense/intelligence services and systems.  General Dynamics Mission Systems employees work closely with esteemed customers to develop solutions that allow them to carry out high-stakes national security missions.

DUTIES AND TASKS: Looking for a data scientist with developer experience or a hybrid developer/data analyst.  Must have experience in design, development, and troubleshooting of analytics.  Various mission areas need assistance in determining the value of their products and workflow.  The analytic developer will forward deploy to work with mission subject matter experts and create analytics that will feed a follow-on tool that enriches and visualizes outcomes.

  • Tasking will include developing and evolving analytics for situational awareness, trending, and change detection.  Work will involve identification of source data, combination methods, and visualization needs.
  • Analytic Developer will work with follow-on tool back end developers.

  • A seasoned, experienced professional having a wide range of professional experience and possessing a strong understanding of software engineering concepts, principles, and theories and expert at applying technical standards and methodologies to resolve a wide range of issues in creative ways

  • Strong knowledge of the software development process as well as software engineering concepts, principles, and theories
  • Strong knowledge of software tools supporting Integrated Development Environments (IDE), version control, defect reporting and scripting
  • Detailed knowledge of other related engineering and support disciplines
  • Strong ability to excel at the development of new theories and methods

  • Strong ability to be creative, innovative and responsible, and able to master advanced decision-making skills

  • Shows initiative on assignments, and professionally executes projects as a technical lead with little or no direction, frequently provides direction and leadership to technical teams and determines methods and procedures
  • Strong ability to handle significant responsibility, leadership and accountability while executing very complex assignments
  • Strong ability to be a team player who thrives in collaborative environments and revels in team success, meeting and exceeding personal and team commitments
  • Advocate and champion at interpreting, defining and applying company policies and procedures to resolve very complex issues
