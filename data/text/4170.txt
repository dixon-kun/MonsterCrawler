        The Role: Lead Data Scientist
        Students interact with Chegg site through a number of products and platforms. From these interactions, Chegg learns a lot about the behavior and preferences of users. The Staff/Lead Data Scientist will use analysis, modeling, and data science to quantify user preferences and behavior and help our product team provide a great experience to our students.
        A key part of this role is the ability to quantify and clearly communicate what our data us telling us about our users. The right person will quickly grasp new and different datasets, and quickly drive meaningful insights from them. This person will work regularly with every part of Chegg, balance multiple efforts, and leverage other analytics team members and their skills to deliver insights. The ideal candidate is excited about joining our team's mission to help students save time, save money and get smarter.
        Responsibilities:
        * Perform analysis on Chegg engagement data. Analyze who our students are and what drives them to engage.
        * Develop and define key product and business insights to help guide decision makers and executives.
        * Engage with product and business leaders to drive the future strategy for the company &#8211; grounded in data science.
        * Develop presentations to communicate broadly across the product groups and business units.
        * Generate dashboards, tools and templates to monitor user engagement.
        Requirements:
        * Master's degree in a quantitative field (Sciences, Engineering, Economics, etc.)
        * 5+ years of previous analytics experience is required, preferably in user experience and engagement analytics
        * Fluent in SQL and familiarity with Excel for data manipulation; comfortable using various data access tools.
        * Strong experience with languages such as: Python and/or R.
        * Experience working with big data platforms such as Hadoop and Hive, a plus.
        * Extensive analytical toolset including an advanced understanding of statistics (time series analysis, cluster analysis, multivariate analysis).
        * Experience working with data from multiple sources of varying quality.
        * Experience creating dashboard and key metrics. Familiarity with BI platforms such as Tableau a plus.
        * Self-motivated, independent, organized and proactive, highly responsive, flexible and adaptable when working across multiple teams.
        * Passionate about producing high-quality analytics deliverables and communicating results to a broad audience.
        * Strong communication skills.
