Internship Program Description

At The Timken Company, you will have the opportunity to grow, optimize your performance and unlock your ambitions through positions in many areas of the company. While working independently or in a team environment, you will develop leadership and critical thinking skills to help you launch your career. Our internship program allows students to gain real job experience and receive on-the-job training that focuses on our technologies and methodologies.

At Timken, we place a great value on our interns and our programs are structured to maximize your hands-on experience as we develop a lasting relationship with you.

Potential Internship Areas

Internship opportunities are available in Corporate Marketing.

The assignment of this position will primarily be focused on predictive analytics on the merchandising team. The responsibilities will also include creating a pricing model for our global aftermarket warehouses. This position will be exposed to Timken's marketing, eCommerce, and analytics teams.
