General Description

In today's fast evolving technology world, one aspect remains common – reliance on data to drive the next wave of innovation. Strategic Analytics is Samsung's Center-of-Excellence for driving the adoption of data driven decision making and product development across the company. The team's core focus is developing best-in-class solutions that provide Samsung's marketing and service organizations with a 360 degree view of the Samsung customers. Strategic Analytics is powering a paradigm shift at Samsung and the global industry. We are looking for highly technical team members who are passionate about data, have the rigor needed to solve billion dollar problems, and possess an innate entrepreneurial spirit to explore the uncharted. Strategic Analytics combines the engineering backbone of a best-in-class Big Data Platform with the analytic expertise of advanced mining and predictive modeling. If you want to work among the very best talent in the industry, working on the most innovative products in the world, Samsung is the place to be.We are seeking an outstanding analytic leader experienced in designing, developing, and distributing data insights and solutions to Samsung's marketing and service organizations that have a measurable impact on the business. This person will work within and across teams to help identify viable opportunities to maximize the use of customer analytics in daily operations. This is a leadership role accountable for driving the data strategy, business requirements, design, development, deployment, and ongoing enhancements of the US Customer Profile in support of the SEA business lines. This person will be responsible for managing the complex integration of business needs and partnerships in the execution of the required solutions. The jobholder will need to have the leadership skills to drive to closure on key functional and non-functional requirements while managing the relationships with new and existing business partners, and the other teams within SEA. He or she will craft and manage descriptive analyses and predictive models, profile delivery standards, and organizational reporting strategies as well as being able to understand how to integrate different data sources within the organization.

Necessary Skills / Attributes

* Visionary leader with experience defining advanced analytical solutions that drive explicit, meaningful impact to the customer and lines of business• Leading and managing teams of highly technical resources in the mining, analysis, and deployment or data related to the Customer Profile in support of personalization and customer relationship marketing programs using traditional and Big Data platforms• Technical knowledge around big data and analytic hardware, software and solutions, such as Master Data Management, Hadoop, Cloudera, Tableau, etc.• Deep experience with customer target marketing solutions and data enhancement solutions • Partner with other teams in SEA to assure delivery of value from data assets• Proven ability to influence, communicate and negotiate with all levels of management across functional and business unit boundaries (i.e., Managers through Senior Leadership Team members);• Demonstrated experience organizing and motivating teams of individuals from multiple departments outside of his/her control • Ability to lead the design and development of the Customer Profile and advanced analytics therein• Ability to lead business requirement consolidation and implementation across diverse business functions• Experience in telecommunications or consumer electronics industry• Support the establishment of data and analytics standards in the creation of new solutions• Support the platform and data direction for the establishment of the informational platform for SEA• Program/project management skills• Excellent oral & written communication and presentation skills, with ability to communicate confidently and customize based on level of use and need to build strong relationships across the organization.Education: Advanced Degree in Technology related field
