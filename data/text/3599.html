BNY Mellon Innovation Center Silicon Valley (ICSV) is currently seeking a Senior Data Scientist to join our rapidly growing team. The ICSV is a startup within an enterprise, focused on applied research that can be quickly brought to market as production services and new financial services products. BNY Mellon is the world's leading provider of financial services technology, and current business operations provide a wealth of fascinating business opportunities and requirements, as well as terabytes of the world's most interesting data science challenges. Help us revolutionize global financial services!

This is an outstanding opportunity to be a part of a very small and talented team, giving you the ability to help pioneer the usage of consumer and advertising data science techniques, on data that is vastly more valuable and complicated.

As a Sr Data Scientist, you will take lead on exploring and creating business value from data science operations in new business data domains, working with our data model architects, machine learning architects, storage architects, product managers, and line of business owners, You will formulate strategies for Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and follow-on data science processes and architectures, including statistical analysis, machine learning modelling etc. You will help ensure understanding of the business objectives in the data science, and will help invent new ways to analyze, visualize, and derive value from enterprise and financial services data.

Qualifications

* LI-SH1

Key Qualifications

* PhD in Statistics

* 5+ years commercial machine learning experience

* A strong understanding of machine learning theory.

* Mid-level programming experience. Python, Java or C++ experience is highly desired.

* A principled approach to solving algorithmic problems with a focus on what will make users happy.

* A pragmatic approach to rapidly evaluating new algorithmic ideas.

* A very high attention to detail and ability to thoroughly think through problems.

* Excellent written and oral communication skills on both technical

and non-technical topics.

* Strong understanding of R or python ML packages and statistics.

* Proven ability to mine a new data area and derive business value
