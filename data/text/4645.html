Tech Recruiter at Randstad

Boston, MA 02215

Report this job

About the Job

TO APPLY: Email Resume to: nick.flanscha@randstadusa.com

Job Description:
Looking for an impactful Tech Recruiter who can really dig into searches and find people with Machine Learning and Data Science experience. Must have an understanding of the HR process and business side with ability to push back on managers when necessary, but also be flexible in finding ways to attract talent."
Core Job Duties:
- Provide direct Tech Recruitment support, working with HR and leadership
o Phone screen candidates, active sourcing/develop pipelines
o Conduct full cycle recruitment
- Develop and maintain new employee onboarding program and resources
- Develop and maintain policies and procedures, in conjunction with HR
- Support leadership in development of culture code/conduct
- Support development and revision of job descriptions
- Support leadership and HR with development of performance review process and documentation
- Develop training criteria for summer interns
- Maintain and update candidate tracker tool
o Connect with interviewers to capture feedback, confirm final outcomes
o Input and update: interview notes, interview and candidacy status, sources, outcomes, etc.
- Maintain and update interview materials
- Schedule and coordinate interviews
- Onboard new employees
- Function as liaison for visa application documentation
- Maintain updated organization chart

Required Experience:
--3+ years of Tech Recruiting and HR experience
o-full life cycle Tech Recruitment experience
o-Proficiency with Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS), specifically Taleo or similar
o-Performance appraisal processes
o-Development of HR infrastructure
o-Visa processing
o-Understanding of the HR process and business side

Shift Required: First shift

Pay Rate: $55-$60/hr

**All Job Offers contingent on applicant passing a Criminal Background Check and Drug Screen**
