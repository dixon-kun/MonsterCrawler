ORGANIZATION:

The Customer Experience Group is a newly created division tasked with driving MassMutual's transition to a customer-centric, digitally enabled company under a single, unified brand and set of customer experiences.

The Customer Experience Group will be oriented around five core functions or activities. Data Science and Business Analytics, Customer-Facing Digital Tools and Interfaces, Full Stack Application Development, Brand, Research/Design and Partnerships.

POSITION SUMMARY:

This is an opportunity to join MassMutual, an organization that is committed to be the best company in the industry based on superior financial strength, high dividends and delivery of a high quality customer experience. It's about our customers – and keeping them at the center of all that we do. Seeing ourselves as our customers do: as MassMutual – One company, One culture, One brand all working toward a shared Purpose…. To help people secure their future and protect the ones they love.

MassMutual is looking for a seasoned content marketer to develop and manage a content strategy based on MassMutual's critical brand and business objectives. This strategist will build and manage a holistic content calendar that integrates key topics and themes to support the newly launched brand, social, media and internal business partner needs. Most important is the understanding on how to manage ecosystem of paid, earned, and owned content programs.

This person is a strategic expert who has the analytical abilities necessary to gather key business and user insights, and apply their strategic planning skills to create a long-term vision for the content that's generated, earned and owned.

REPORTS TO: Head of Brand Activation and Engagement

OVERALL RESPONSIBILITY:

* Create data driven content strategies to understand how strategy is performing against business goals and competition. Build testing and traffic plans based on understanding of industry tools and partners for syndication and content promotion

* Owns all project management around the content calendar to keep projects on track and within budget.

* Build measurable plan to track, test and optimize new and existing types of content targeted around MassMutual key target audiences.

* Build a content lifecycle vision that builds key themes from prospect to customer to not only attract new customers but retain them with relevant, engaging content discussion. Identify content that attracts, engages and converts.

* Solid understanding of both organic and paid social programs (SEO and SEM) as well as the technical understanding of how to buy and optimize.

* Build and manage detailed integrated content calendar that supports key themes of the brand and business units. Manage distribution and production of content outlined within the plan and coordinate closely with Social team to align integrated content plan. Work closely with internal writers and editorial support for content generation that aligns with overall content calendar.

* Manage and review content calendar on a quarterly basis to update along themes and optimize for content measurement results

* Work closely with MassMutual Analytics team to manage dashboard of continuous analysis of content performance. Manage continuous content analysis on how strategy is performing to optimize, identify new trends, and pivot real-time with core topics, themes and content types

* Work closely with MassMutual.com content team and content strategist to understand how audience consumes content and how we can support and create new assets in line with the sites content strategy.

* Work closely with external advertising and media agencies to support their plan to communicate MassMutual's brand's story, and collaborate on how content will be presented or distributed in various formats.

* Track and monitor competitors and industry best practices to adjust content strategy.

* Oversee content requirements and create content strategy deliverables across an ongoing project life cycle. This can include conducting content audits as well as gap analyses.

* Create and maintaining editorial calendars, style guides, taxonomies, and support/consult with digital team on metadata and url frameworks. Maintain budget.

* Assist in project managing content resources: editors, freelancers, technical/other content type's providers.

CANDIDATE QUALIFICATIONS:

* Bachelor degree required; Master's degree preferred.

* 7+ years of experience with an Advertising agency or corporate marketing/communications department managing media and integrated communication planning.

* Experience in web analytic tools, digital advertising, or marketing desired.

* Strong understanding of the relationship across earned, owned and paid content programs. Background in SEO and SEM a must.

* High-level thinker, standout idea generator, and problem solver with excellent communication and organizational skills.

* Strong project management and performance management skills. Ability to manage complicated content calendar and lifecycle content programs. Very detail oriented.

* Must be a self-starter, entrepreneurial, resourceful and comfortable working in a fast-paced team environment where self-sufficiency is essential. Ability to work closely with teams in remote locations - MA and NY.

* Outstanding communication skills: Must be collaborative good partner with agencies and internal business units.

* Excellent budget management and understanding of different industry tools and content partners.

* Strong collaborative skills and the ability to build relationships across diverse stakeholder and constituent groups.

* Expert at juggling multiple priorities through strong project-management skills, leadership and collaboration.

* Authorized to work in the US without sponsorship.
