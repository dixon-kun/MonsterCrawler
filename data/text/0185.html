Position Overview

Technology

Our technology teams are responsible for the Bank's entire information technology ("IT") infrastructure, and the development, implementation, and protection of the software required to support all of the Bank's businesses. We move over EUR 1.6 trillion across the Bank's platforms, support thousands of trading desks, and enable millions of banking transactions, share trades and emails every day.

With award-winning mobile banking apps and trading systems, our technology platforms help Deutsche Bank deliver high quality products to clients. Naturally, we make sure that the phones work, emails are delivered and PCs run - but we also develop collaboration platforms and workspaces that help our people share their knowledge, expertise and passion for our business.

Developments in technology are changing the way we communicate, work and think. Join us here, and you'll constantly be looking ahead. We'll look to you to capitalize on new technological trends that can reshape our business and deliver value for our clients, whilst still meeting the demands of customers, regulators and the markets.

Role Responsibilities:

Support development and engineering with planning of the right data ingestion strategy

* Support implementing of controls defined by Information Security Policy, Standards, Procedures and Guidelines derived from it.

* Maintaining an inventory/catalogue of data assets for which they have responsibility and which are agreed with the respective Business Managers and Information Asset Owners. Manage Software Configuration Management for Splunk

* Support the coordinate Data Integration and hosting on a large scale

* Discover synergies with other data source owners

* "Translate" Cyber-Security requirements for analytics into Querries/Rules/Machine Learning heuristics

Prior Experience:

* 4+ years of experience as a professional software engineer

* 2+ years of experience as a data scientist with an demonstrated experience on information security or an advanced degree in information security

* Proven ability to synchronize and cooperate with data librarians of other departments,

* Great working knowledge of cyber security challenges, analytics and big data concepts (feeds, catalogues, on-boarding processes, access management)

* Experience with web application security and common exploits

* Strong Extensive experience with Big Data Solutions and either relational or non-relational databases

* Experience with development methodologies, including SDLC, Agile and DevOps

* Experience in Enterprise environments

* Awareness of latest security issues and technologies, including testing tools and techniques

* Experience with working internationally distributed teams

* Well versed in communication with Management as well as Subject Matter Experts

Education:

* Significant experience with Cyber Security SIEM tools and processes

* Awareness of latest security issues and technologies, including testing tools and techniques

* Expert in Security Information Management

* Solid and proven experience with Network Security Technologies

* Proven qualification for Detection of Insider Threat

* Certificated Machine Learning Scientist / Data Miner

* CISSP certification (preferred)
