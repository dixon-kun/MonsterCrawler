        Technical Product Analyst
        You are a relationship builder with a relentless focus on collaboration in cross-functional teams and can successfully engage with diverse personalities. You have strong root-cause business analysis and problem-solving skills, ideally within the context of software product development, management or QA are also helpful.
        What you'll be doing: (these will vary with role seniority)
        * Identifying product requirements to support client's marketing efforts
        * Leading software deployment efforts for the internal team composed of account managers and engineers
        * Consulting throughout the project to boost client software adoption
        * Advocating for clients' needs throughout all stages of a deployment
        * Deploying software to clients in a quality, timely manner
        * Ensuring that all stakeholders are kept fully informed of project status and progress
        * Developing metrics to identify and track business trends, determine root-causes, and develop recommendations to improve products and processes
        Skills, Talents, Experience you will need:
        * 5+ years supporting client success in an analytic, marketing, or software industry (Adtech a strong plus)
        * or related combination of client facing and analytic experience
        * Ability to understand technical issues and communicate effectively with technical teams
        * Experience in media planning/buying, trafficking, reporting, and analytics
        * Campaign execution and measurement experience across multiple digital channels
        * Demonstrated success in client facing roles
        * Experience in media planning and measurement for offline channels such as TV, radio and print is a big plus
        * Ability to evaluate technology solutions, including build / buy / partner options
        * Bachelor's Degree (MBA a plus)

        Because you're smart, entrepreneurial and are ready to be challenged every day to break new ground in next-gen marketing cloud technology that empowers boardroom through to machine-to-machine decisions. You are willing to deliver excellence &amp; innovation, fight for customer success, work collaboratively, and manage complexity.
        Our customers use the MarketShare DecisionCloud to create an analytics control center that simplifies and scales the decision-making process.
