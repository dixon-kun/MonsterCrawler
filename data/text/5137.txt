        The Entrepreneurship Database Program (EDP) at Emory University is seeking to hire a Data Program Manager. The Entrepreneurship Database Program collects and analyzes data from thousands of early-stage entrepreneurs that apply to more than 100 accelerator programs around the world to both produce sector reports and support academic research. With our partners, we work to improve the efficacy of accelerator programs to stimulate more positive entrepreneurial outcomes in socially-oriented sectors and in developing regions around the world. Learn more at www.entrepreneurdata.com. This program is housed within Social Enterprise @ Goizueta (Emory&#8217;s Goizueta Business School) and is a proud partner of the Global Accelerator Learning Initiative (GALI) - a collaboration between the Aspen Network of Development Entrepreneurs (ANDE) and Emory University. The program explores &#8211; and answers - key questions about enterprise acceleration including: Do acceleration programs contribute to revenue growth? Do they help companies attract investment? Do they work as well for developing-world impact entrepreneurs as they do for developed-world tech entrepreneurs?
