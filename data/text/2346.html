Description

This is a unique opportunity to join Enterprise GSM & Data Analytics team. The team was formed late in 2016 to lead both, analytics strategy and project-based solutions, focusing on the companyâ€™s most critical business areas. The focus of the group is to create dashboards that capture and visualize BorgWarner data to improve decision making to solve business problems, drive savings, and improve efficiency. This is a senior role that will provide insight into leading analytic practices and methods, design and lead iterative development and learning cycles, and ultimately produce new and creative analytic solutions to be leveraged by multiple Business Units. The person in this role will be responsible for data architecture planning and for designing and building effective data models from multiple data sources. Sr.BI Data Architect will work with a wide variety of users throughout all Business Units to understand the problems that business is looking to solve. This role will collaborate with Data Analytics Governance board to evolve and enforce policies and standards regarding business intelligence initiatives.


Responsibilities

Work with cross-functional teams to discover and develop actionable, high-impact data analytics need and opportunity statements in a variety of core business areas.

  • Develop industry leading solutions including:
  • Determining analytic approaches to be used, defining the appropriate methodologies
  • Ensuring integrity of the analytical solutions in terms of the underlying data models and assumptions
  • Assessing the current analytic models and architectures, reviewing for gaps against business needs and industry best practices
  • Identifying detailed data needs, sources, and structure to support solution development
  • Designing and building effective data models from multiple data sources
  • Lead POCs to create breakthrough solutions, performing exploratory and targeted data analyses.
  • Utilize Set Analysis when required for specific use cases.
  • Design and build intuitive user interface designs in analytics/ data visualization tool.
  • Work with business users and IT to validate business and technical requirements.
  • Develop knowledge transfer documentation for long-term support.
  • Establish sound timelines and deadlines on projects chosen for implementation.
  • Coordinate with other team members but take accountability for overall delivery of Data Analytics projects.
  • Support applications in production.
  • Deliver regular reports to functional managers on project status.
  • Manage issue resolution and escalation within both IT and functional areas.

The successful candidate should demonstrate the following qualities:

  • A flexible analytic approach that allows for producing results at varying levels of precision.
  • Strong listening and communications skills, with ability to clearly and concisely explain complex problems and technologies to non-expert and executive audiences.
  • Evidence of being able to transfer solutions to business stakeholders for successful adoption and value realization.
  • Highly collaborative work style, with ability to provide leadership to a cross-functional team

Qualifications

Skills Requirements:

  • Strong business acumen
  • Effectively communicating analyses, recommendations, status, and results to business management and executives in business terms
  • Ability to create structured problem statements and conceptualize potential solutions from rough specifications and information
  • Ability to propose analytic strategies and solutions that challenge and expand the thinking of the working team
  • Knowledge of predictive modeling concepts and disciplines
  • Knowledge of industry-leading practices
  • Familiarity with analyzing source system data and data flows
  • Familiarity with manipulating high-volume, high-dimensionality data from varying sources to highlight patterns, anomalies, relationships and trends
  • Understanding of relational databases
  • SQL and Data Modeling expertize
  • ETL skills (extract, transform, load), knowledge of Alteryx highly desirable
  • Experience working with Data Analytics and Visualization Tools (like Qlik Sense, QlikView, Tableau)
  • Ability to translate business needs into technical specifications
  • Critical thinking and ability to drive conclusions based on data findings
  • Education Requirements
  • Bachelor' s degree required, Masterâ€™s degree preferred in computer science, data science
  • Ten (10) years of related experience in BI, data management required or an equivalent combination of relevant education and/or experience
