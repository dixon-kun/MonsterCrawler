           JOB SUMMARY:
          The Global Services Group has an opening for a Data Manager to provide analytical support to a government customer in Northern Virginia. The successful candidate will have a background in data management, data science, data mining, or data analysts as well as experience in one or more of the interdisciplinary fields associated with extraction of knowledge or insight from data in various forms.
            PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES:
            Organize and process data in preparation for analysis
            Support efforts in developing solutions to extract data (take data from various sources), normalize data (deal with imperfections to include missing values and inconsistent formatting), analyze data (develop means to recognize correlations) and visualize data (effective ways for communicating information)
            Assist efforts to identify and document analytic standards, best practices and methodology used by the center
            Support efforts to develop and implement a broad-spectrum training program that improves and sustains the center&#8217;s core analytical proficiencies
            Develop automated approaches that save analysts time and effort
            Assist customer leadership with tracking prioritization of support requirements, cataloging assignment and completion of analytic tasks, and archiving productivity
            Assist in the discovery of capabilities and datasets of benefit to the center
            Provide expertise on IT requirements for the integration and implementation of new analytic capabilities and/or datasets
         Qualifications:
           BASIC QUALIFICATIONS:
           Bachelor&#8217;s degree and 8+ years of experience
           5 years of experience in analysis or organization and processing of data in preparation for analysis
           2+ years of experience with LE, IC, or DoD communities
           Knowledge of IT hardware and software requirements necessary to run analytics
           Experience in analytical methodology and platforms
           Experience with knowledge management
