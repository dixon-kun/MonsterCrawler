         Job Purpose:
        Strengthen the Institute processes for major enterprise investments by facilitating and coordinating gating and decision processes. Establish and promote an enterprise level portfolio management approach with a focus on specific investment types (e.g. technology investments). Oversee and support project execution through continual coordination of program/portfolio plans, data analysis, reporting and education.
         Major Responsibilities:
          Manage enterprise level programs and portfolios through the design and facilitation of Institute wide governance processes.
          Guide and support project and program managers in the initiation, planning, execution, and closing of projects within defined portfolios.
          Advise and mentor organizational leaders on the creation and launch of portfolio management functions across the Institute.
          Provide portfolio management oversight to include creation and tracking of metrics, project status, prioritization and ranking, and roadmaps.
          Facilitate the development of business cases for potential projects. Work with the proposing unit leader, the Institute budget office and procurement to perform total cost of ownership analysis data analysis, cost benefit analysis, and financial modeling.
          Design, develop and maintain tools and processes for portfolio management and enterprise level tracking. Partner with business services to measure adherence to established governance processes.
          Assist with the creation and delivery of materials related to project/program management education and mentoring.
          Perform other related duties as assigned.
