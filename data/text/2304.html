As the Sr Data & Information Architect within the Finance Data Lake Ops team, you will engage with GE Business customers and Data Science teams on enhancements and user support related to the Data Lake analytics and reporting platforms including OBIEE, Tableau, and other visualization applications running against Hadoop/HDFS and Greenplum mpp databases.
The candidate will function as level 2 SME on OBIEE. Tableau and other analytics tools as well as the Finance Data Lake standard data model which includes Payables, GL, Receivables, Fixed Assets, Order Management etc.

  • Engage with GE Businesses to gather ongoing enhancement requirements within the Finance Data Lake platform, resolve data issues and provide solutions for reporting and Data-as-a-Service initiatives.
  • Provide level 2 support for analytics users in OBIEE, Tableau, and other visualization and analytics tools
  • Responsible for providing Subject Matter Expertise on Finance Data Lake standard data model for subject area that include AP, AR, GL, Cash, Orders, Assets etc.
  • Maintain and enhance finance data lake metadata
  • Responsible for administration and performance tuning of analytics and platforms
  • Responsible for level 2 information security administration and troubleshooting
  • Responsible for enhancement and modification of PL/SQL scripts, towards production support and maintenance as required, based on application criticality.
  • Leads the UAT testing process and ensures all customer requirements are met as per specifications
  • Ability to query data both in source and destination and anticipate corrective measures.
  • Strong interpersonal skills, with ability to professionally interact with a diverse blend of personalities to reach resolution and maintain strong relationships.
  • Develops migration and implementation plans, and may support projects based on these activities.
  • Review application performance and proactively monitors performance.
  • Ensures that plans for assigned applications integrate effectively with other aspects of the technical infrastructure.
  • Manage scope, budget and schedule, delivery of a successful product, closure and documentation, if assigned as the project manager.

    • Basic Qualifications

  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, MIS, Finance, Industrial Engineering, or related field.
  • 1+ years OBIEE and Tableau power user experience, or equivalent
  • Minimum of 5 years of experience utilizing strong functional & technical knowledge of iProcurement, Purchasing, Accounts Payables, General Ledger or Cash Management, Human Resource modules.
  • Minimum 5 years of database experience with developing and debugging PL/SQL.

    Eligibility Requirements

  • Legal authorization to work in the U.S. is required. We will not sponsor individuals for employment visas, now or in the future, for this job.
    • GE will only employ those who are legally authorized to work in the United States for this opening. Any offer of employment is conditioned upon the successful completion of a background investigation and drug screen.
  • Prior experience developing with reporting tools like Tableau, OBIEE etc.
  • Experience developing analytics against Oracle ERP data model and/or SAP ERP data model
  • Prior exposure to Data Warehousing, Analytics, Business Intelligence, or Big Data technologies.
  • Prior experience with Hive/HDFS/Hadoop, Presto, Spark, Jupyter notebook, Python, or other Big data query tools.
  • Strong problem solving skills, communicates in a clear and succinct manner.
  • Certified in ITIL or Six Sigma.
  • Work effectively with project managers and client stakeholders to understand and refine project requirements and deadlines.
  • ITLP graduate.
