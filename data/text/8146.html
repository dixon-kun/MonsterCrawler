The Director of Total Rewards provides administrative leadership by developing and executing strategic compensation, benefits and technology initiatives that support the organization's mission, vision and values. This position is integral to the strategic positioning of the organization as an employer of choice in the industry and the region through the careful crafting of extrinsic and intrinsic rewards that work to promote value, equity, motivation, engagement and wellness among the organization's staff. This individual will have the authority and accountability for planning, directing and coordinating all total rewards programs for the organization.

PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES

* Develop/design and maintain compensation and benefit plans that are aligned with the organization's objectives, while ensuring the achievement of consistent and market-driven levels of compensation.

* Lead the development and communication of compensation and benefit programs while coordinating with local HR support teams as required.

* Manage vendors and coordinate with third parties as pertains to the implementation of local benefit/compensation initiatives ensuring local legal compliance, market competitiveness and alignment with the overall Total Rewards strategy.

* Ensure support systems are in place to collect and monitor information required for sound HR metrics designed to align with organizational goals and objectives.

* Be the go-to person on matters of executive compensation and incentive issues.

* Keep the appropriate stakeholders informed and aware of the current status of key programs ensuring proper communications are available on any new initiatives.

* Drive cost management as pertains to compensation and benefit plans ensuring proper benchmarking and market knowledge.

* Oversee HR systems and processes including current and future HRIM installations, partnering with stakeholders to identify opportunities to simplify and standardize HR.

* Lead the development and communication of goals and objectives to align with the broader organizational objectives.

* Direct strategic and functional activities by developing project initiative proposals, organizing functional project teams, developing budgets, and reviewing team performance to achieve project deliverables and milestones.

* Provide leadership for total rewards products, trends, and methodologies.

* Ensure compliance with all governmental regulations related to compensation and benefits programs.

* Work with financial resources to develop cost estimates, forecasts, and expenditures for budget planning, tracking, and changes to rewards mix.

* Develop competitive best practices for total rewards design that are cost effective and consistent with labor market trends as well as organizational objectives.

* Oversee and direct the research, analysis, design, implementation, administration, and communication of compensation and benefits activities and services, including management of staff, vendors, consultants, and brokers.

* Participate as an active member of the HR Leadership team in development of human capital strategies and tactical approaches to deliver high impact solutions to all employees and internal customers.

Skills

* Ten plus years of hands-on compensation, benefits and HRIM management experience.

* Expertise in the design, implementation and management of compensation and benefit programs in healthcare organizations and how they relate to the overall Total Rewards equation.

* Excellent presentation, communication, and relationship-building skills and the ability to adapt messaging to varied audiences.

* Demonstrated ability to manage simultaneously several complex projects on both a tactical and strategic level.

* Ability to design and develop cash compensation plans (including base salary and incentives).

* Understanding of executive compensation strategies and their implementation.

* Worked with or understands the use of HR metrics and related measurement systems.

* Possess a solid foundation in current HR data, service delivery, and support systems.

* Results-oriented individual, willing to take initiative and confident in presenting ideas and leading groups.

* Customer-focused style to management, leadership, and employees. Desire to provide a high level of responsiveness and customer attention and service.

* Ability to demonstrate exceptional skills in all forms of communication.

* Experience working in a large and complex not-for-profit organization an asset.

* This is a strategic position. Candidates must have experience driving change. Must also be a participative thinker, fun, partner with HR Leadership team.

Education

* An undergraduate degree in Business with a focus on Human Resources, Business or Accounting or equivalent.

* PHR, SPHR, CCP or CEBS (or CBP) preferred.

Required Experience

* "Ten years of progressive compensation and benefit design and management experience (three to five years at a management level) in the Healthcare industry.
