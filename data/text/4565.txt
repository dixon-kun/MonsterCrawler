          Job Summary
         The Portfolio Operations team provides the analytics, infrastructure, and processes that facilitate the success of the product portfolio.           Essential responsibilities
          Play a strategic role in driving product growth by creating the actionable analyses that impact adoption, usability, engagement, retention, cross-sell and upsell. Locate the trends and business drivers.
          Support the growth of our product pipeline by suggesting and creating the analyses that measure the value of early-stage product efforts
