          Overview
          Comrise, a global consulting firm, is seeking a Jr. Data Scientist. The candidate that is hired will have the opportunity to work with terabytes and petabytes of data and unique open-source technologies to help address big data challenges for our clients across the globe. &#160;In addition, he/she&#160;will become an integral part of a cross-disciplinary, international team and be working on highly visible projects that improve performance and grow our clients' product suite.
          There is a three-week training period in ECL, a declarative, data-centric programming language used to process big data across the&#160;HPCC Systems' platform. Following the successful completion of training, hired candidates will be assigned to a client project.
         Candidates interested in applying to this position must&#160;be willing to travel and/or relocate as the location of the project is dependent upon client location. Sponsorship is available.
           Responsibilities
           Mining and analyzing client data to identify critical business insights
           Proactively developing new metrics and studies to quantify the value of different aspects of content
           Design multi-factor experiments and validate&#160;hypotheses.
           Translating analytic insights into concrete, actionable recommendations for business service or product improvement.
           Design and implement scalable and repeatable solutions, and establish&#160;scalable, efficient,&#160;automated processes for large scale data analyses
           Work closely with the software engineering team to drive new feature creation
           Qualifications
           Basic
           Graduate degree in Data Science, Computer Science/Engineering, Statistics, Mathematics, Operations Research, or other quantitative fields.
           Strong critical thinking and attention to detail
           Strong interpersonal and communication skills.
           Must be able to explain technical concepts and analysis implications clearly to a wide audience, and be able to translate business objectives into actionable analyses
           Desirable
           Proficiency at querying relational databases (SQL) and ability to pull data from various sources
           Experience with scripting language, such as PERL, Java, Python, etc.
           Experience with analytic tools such as R, SAS, SPSS, etc.
           Experience with open-source technologies such as No-SQL, Cassandra, Hadoop, Pig, Hive, HPCC Systems, etc.
           Experience with Machine Learning (classification and regression)
