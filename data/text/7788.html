Responsibilities:

Noblis is helping FAA manage large and complex telecommunications and information management programs that use state of the art technologies to deliver enterprise infrastructure services essential to the safe and efficient operation of the largest, safest, and most complex aviation system in the world.  We are seeking  smart, passionate, and motivated people to join our proven team in a challenging and rewarding work environment where you will be actively engaged at the intersection of technology and operations enhancing our nation’s aviation system.  If you want to be involved in acquiring, implementing, and managing the communications and information solutions to achieve our nation’s aviation vision, this is the job for you!

Responsibilities

  • Deliver technical subject matter expertise and business leadership to existing client work to cultivate innovation and evolve the R&D portfolio for the Transportation & Telecommunications (T&T) Mission Area
  • Provide technical leadership, planning,  and guidance on internal R&D programs and contribute to business development efforts focused on the technology transformation of Noblis’ transportation, telecommunications, and earth observations capabilities
  • Provide strategic business leadership working collaboratively with the business development team and account managers, serving as point of primary accountability for leading the T&T R&D Portfolio and bringing capabilities to the client market
  • Ensure clarity of mission and accountability for critical R&D program objectives; assure alignment of R&D objectives with T&T mission area and corporate goals
  • Work collaboratively with T&T principal research investigators and account teams to lead Noblis' T&T research and development capabilities and contribute to business development efforts to address capability gap areas, as well as align to the mission area vision and broader corporate strategic plan
  • Own and/or participate in strategic R&D portfolio planning and technical reviews from initial ideation to go to market strategy; secure yearly capture of investment dollars
  • Successfully communicate schedule updates, project risks and associated mitigation plans to Mission Area VP and other stakeholders
  • Responsible for maintaining a high degree of understanding of the shift indicators and drivers that will influence the T&T technology landscape within the federal civilian market.
  • Serve as trusted technical advisor to Mission Area VP, Account Leaders, and Noblis Centers of Excellence

Qualifications:

Qualified candidates will possess a technical degree in computer science, network engineering, information technology, systems engineering, or a related field and have at least 10 years’ experience in research, development, and integration for cutting-edge technology applications such as: advanced networking and telecommunications, data analytics, cloud computing, cybersecurity, machine learning, and cross-platform software development.

Additionally, candidates shall possess:

-  7+ experience in the research, development, and integration of technologies in the public sector with strong cross-platform telecommunications, systems engineering, and software experience; advanced SDN/NFV networking, IOT, and/or intelligent transportation experience preferred

-  Direct experience formulating and executing advanced research and development programs

-  Strong understanding of federal government sector, industry partners, and market dynamics

-  Ability to make new business presentations and reach closure.

-  Proven track record working across the government proposing, developing, and operating in complex environments that include both public agencies and the private sector.

-  Extensive experience in all aspects of customer relationship management.

-  Strong leadership qualities and excellent communication and interpersonal skills.


Highly desirable skills/experience:

-  Intelligent Transportation Systems; Data Science; Network Virtualization; API Development; Advanced Algorithm Development; Machine Learning; Cybersecurity

-  PhD preferred

Overview:

Noblis and our wholly owned subsidiaries, Noblis ESI and Noblis NSP, are solving difficult problems that help our government and our country. We bring the best of scientific thought, management, and engineering expertise with a reputation for independence and objectivity. We support a wide range of government and industry clients in the areas of national security, intelligence, transportation, healthcare, environmental sustainability, and enterprise engineering. Learn more at noblis.org/about.

Why work at a Noblis company?

Our employees find greater meaning in their work and balance the other things in life that matter to them. Our people are our greatest asset. They are exceptionally skilled, knowledgeable, team-oriented, and mission-driven individuals who want to do work that matters and benefits the public.

Noblis has won numerous workplace awards. Noblis maintains a drug-free workplace and is an equal opportunity employer. All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race.
