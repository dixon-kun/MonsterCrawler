Production DataScientist

Location based data and services are at the center ofimmense growth and innovation. Automobiles and mobile applications aregenerating petabytes of data on a daily basis that contain information aboutour world. HERE leverages data from mobile and automotive use cases and usesits cloud-based predictive analytics platform to generate highly accurate andprecise map content and applications for location based services.

HERE's Data Science Group is looking for Production DataScientist. You will be continuously measuring the performance of HERE's numerousmachine learning models, use algorithms to learn their behavior so abnormalchanges in them can be detected, and troubleshoot and correct models with thedata science team.

Our work environment features short release cycles,experienced and talented employees, and a fast-paced work environment. You should be someone who takes pride in your work, honors your commitments,and enjoys working with teammates to find creative solutions to thornyproblems. If you want to work on the cutting edge of predictive analyticsand Big Data this job is for you!

Typical Responsibilities

* Continuously measure machine learning models'performance

* Use algorithms to learn the models' behavior soabnormal changes in them can be detected

* Set up Alerts and troubleshoot/fix existingmodels

* Create new Machine Learning models

* Track and Analyze Data Inputs and Outputs

* Tests and validate machine learning models

* Conduct scalable data research on and off thecloud

* Performs data visualization and analysis

* Discovers trends in the data and presents themto other scientists and business managers

* Design, modify and build new data processes

* Act as an expert and evangelist in areas of datamining, machine learning, statistics, and predictive analysis and modeling.

RequiredQualifications

* BSc or MS in disciplines such asStatistics/Mathematics, Computer Science, Econometrics, Operations Research,with an emphasis on one or more of the following: computationalstatistics/science, data mining, machine learning, and optimization.

* 2 years relevant experience

* Knowledge of data mining and analytic methodssuch as regression, classifiers, clustering, association rules, decision trees,Bayesian network analysis, etc.

* Proficiency with a statistical analysis packageand associated scripting language such as Python, R, Matlab, SAS, etc.

* Programming experience with SQL, shell script,Python, etc.

* Good knowledge of and some experience with toolssuch as Pig, Hive, etc., for working with big data in Hadoop for dataextraction and prep for analysis.

* Possesses strong exploratory analysis skills

* Demonstrates excellent verbal and writtencommunication skills as well as the ability to bridge the gap between datascience and business management

* Has the ability to think and research creatively

* Desired Qualifications

* Experience with web service such as; AWS,Redshift, S3, Spark

* Ability to connect data using Web API or RESTAPI

* Familiarity with data visualization tools, suchas Tableau

* Strong background in geographic data or locationintelligence
