DTN / The Progressive Farmer, part of Schneider Electric, is seeking a Product Manager – Agronomy Specialist with production agriculture expertise to design innovative new digital products that help producers make smarter decisions from either Burnsville, MN, or Omaha, NE. We are looking for an experienced problem-solver to leverage DTN/PF's existing and developing agronomic data streams into actionable insights for growers across diverse geographic markets. The ideal candidate should be dissatisfied with the status quo, naturally curious and driven to find more effective ways to help farmers prosper and maximize their returns.

You will join a team that is passionate about user experience and serves as the voice of the customer inside the broader business; facing the reality of maximizing business value from our products and acting as the primary liaisons between business and technology departments. Leveraging 5,000 proprietary weather stations, 50 degreed meteorologists and our award winning predictive weather intelligence, the PM Agronomy Specialist will leverage his or her own knowledge and expertise to efficiently solve production agriculture problems with unique and innovative digital designs and solutions.

A successful candidate will utilize their agricultural background alongside market research to define, communicate, and address producer trends, wants, needs, and concerns. You will engage with business partners and facilitate collaboration among product management, designers, data scientists, content specialists, and product delivery teams to create and realize new product concepts. This role requires a broad knowledge of row crop production practices, innovative vision, and a drive to realize that vision. You should have command of best practices and emerging trends in production agriculture, the ability to balance big picture thinking and appropriate attention to detail, and a passion for delivering innovation to maximize producer profitability.

As a Product Manager with agronomic expertise, you will have the opportunity to leverage your unique insight and expertise to solve real grower problems through the activities listed below:

What you will do

* Identify market problems - Engage with growers via interviews and surveys to understand pain points and identify opportunities for new product capabilities and requirements

* Assess competitive landscape – Become the expert on relevant competitors, their solutions, and how they are attracting buyers.

* Assess existing competencies – Identify opportunities to leverage in-house weather expertise, proprietary weather sensor network, product portfolio, and industry relationships.

* Select attractive market opportunities - Define target market, document usage scenarios and requirements, and assess strategic and financial fit

* Align with broader strategy - Work with product management team members to evaluate and integrate attractive solutions/offers into broader portfolio strategy and big picture roadmaps.

* Create support material - Create and present written and oral communication materials that effectively summarize findings and support fact-based recommendations

* Collaborate to deliver capabilities – Work with technical product manager, development teams, and user experience designers to deliver solutions that are useful, usable and desirable.

* Coordinate product launch – Plan the launch of new products and releases and manage the cross-functional implementation of the plan

* Generate buyer intelligence – Be the expert on your buyers, how they buy, their buying criteria, and purchase processes.

* Support marketing & sales efforts – Establish effective market positioning, identify lead generation opportunities, provide subject matter expertise for marketing and sales collateral, and develop sales tools and training

* Provide ongoing agronomy expertise – Build crop production knowledge base within product management team as well as broader organization to add agronomic insight into DTN/PF's product portfolio.

* Analyze customer data - Draw insights from customer data and research on ways to evolve our product offerings to better meet customer needs and increase customer engagement. Provide a connection between development and end users so that feedback, innovation and bugs are prioritized, understood and addressed

* Monitor industry evolution- Actively gather and communicate market conditions and trends, customer needs and values, and product gaps in order to support new or updated product offerings

What you will need to succeed

* 5 years of experience and passion in any one or more of the following areas: Agriculture, In-field/On-farm grower, Product Management, Product Marketing, Business Analysis, or Business Process Improvement

* Bachelor's or Advanced degree in Agronomy, Crop Science, Soils, Biotech or related field

* Proven knowledge of standard concepts, practices, and procedures within the agriculture industry in general and crop production in specific.

* Results-driven orientation, attention to detail and follow-through, and ability to work effectively within a team and at all levels within the organization and with partners

* Strong interpersonal and communication (written and oral) skills

* Self-starter with excellent problem-solving, organizational and analytical skills, with the ability to evolve product strategy based on research, data and industry trends

* Professional Agronomy certification – Certified Crop Advisor, Certified Professional Agronomist, or qualifications to become certified a plus

* MBA degree a plus

* An understanding of the Systems Development Life-Cycle (SDLC) is a plus

* Knowledge of software applications, database management systems, mobile apps, and other business related technologies is a plus
