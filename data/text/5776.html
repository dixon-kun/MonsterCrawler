Associate Manager, Operations Intelligence

Louisville, KY

1441 Gardiner Ln


Description:

Purpose of the job: This role will be responsible for utilizing data to gain actionable insights to help the KFC system improve operational effectiveness. This role will be responsible for discovering insights, correlations, and identifying areas of opportunity through the use of data mining, modeling, and visualization techniques with robust data sets. A primary area of focus will be developing and implementing robust operational intelligence reports that provide insights leading to improvements in operations. As a part of the Operations Intelligence team this role will be responsible for building models and accessing data through a variety of applications, data sets, and programs. Additionally, there will be direct influence over key vendors within our guest metrics. Key strengths include the ability to identify trends and actionable insights within data as well as the ability to multi-task and handle ad-hoc analysis. Support the Operations Intelligence team within the Restaurant Support Center in support of the following: o Develop and implement operational intelligence reports that provide key insights amongst large and varied data sets that drive sales, transactions, and margin improvement o Identify operational opportunities through analysis and lead the effort to structure a resulting business strategy o Manage relationship between the field leadership and the ops intel team o Directly support the COO with analysis that will be presented at the leadership team level o Support KFC on the journey to implementation of a robust Business Intelligence platform and solution o Ability to recognize trends in data and present data in a meaningful way that drives action o Identify and lead operations intelligence initiatives forward using a defined Project Management roadmap o Influence outside vendors within our voice of the customer program (SMG, NetBase, Steritech, and MarketForce) to manage and partner on analysis and data mining to drive future strategy. o Statistical analysis ability, understanding of correlations, ANOVA, DOE, regression modeling, etc.

Requirements:

• Bachelor’s degree in Data Science, Business Analytics, Mathematics, Statistics, or related field • Master’s degree in Data Science, Computer Science, Mathematics, or Statistics preferred • 5-7 years of work experience in data analytics or related field • Ability to prioritize projects, multi-task and deliver on deadlines utilizing strong project management skills • Strong communication skills • Demonstrated track record of creative problem solving • Strong analytic skills and attention to detail • Strong PowerPoint skills • Advanced Excel skills (VBA and Pivot tables) • Relevant advanced degrees will be considered in lieu of experience
