AVP, Data Science Innovation Team Lead

Commercial Lines

Charlotte, NC

Position Description

Overview of the Position

This role will lead a 4-5 member team in the pursuit of new insights to better understand the risk attributes and behavioral characteristics of prospects and customers for The Hartford's commercial products and services. Partnering internally with a very diverse group of colleagues, this team will explore the ever-expanding universe of third-party data, combined with the vast proprietary data sets of The Hartford, to develop new approaches for risk evaluation through improved exposure development, granular customer segmentation and the analysis of customer attributes that provide insight on the drivers of financial and physical losses.

This leader will be accountable for developing and managing internal partnerships at The Hartford necessary to successfully fulfill the Applied Research mission. The primary partners include our business units in Commercial Lines and Group Benefits, the Enterprise Data Office, the Office of the Chief Underwriter and our Innovation Team. Each of these groups has a significant role in defining opportunities, providing resourcing and support, and evaluating learning's.

The AVP will also have an externally facing role. S/he will engage with many firms that collect, curate and distribute data for use in a wide range of industries, including insurance. Additionally, this leader will be instrumental in the evaluation of non-traditional sources of data for use in our insurance processes. These may include aggregators of governmental data, social media firms, or non-insurance firms with unrelated business models.

The Applied Research Team will provide the data science expertise in a multi-disciplinary effort to expand our risk evaluation capabilities. Learnings will be shared with numerous internal groups to advance their work in pricing, underwriting, and process efficiency, among others.

The key attributes for this leader include:

* Deep data science expertise and experience

* Strong business acumen, demonstrated through strategic agility, a comprehensive view of how a business operates, and an ability to see the consequences of various actions, both intended and unintended

* Leadership and collaboration, particularly the ability to:

* Work across various teams without direct reporting relationships to create a highly efficient and effective applied research capability

* Engage with senior leaders, making a sound business case

* Engage externally with existing and potential business partners to develop valuable and unique business opportunities for The Hartford

* Deliver outcomes

* Connect the work of Applied Research to our business strategy and ensure the team's work is relevant to our future

* Managerial skill

* Excellent communication skills, especially the ability to share technical information with business customers

* Intense curiosity and a continuous learner

Responsibilities:

Key areas of responsibility:

* Provide business and strategic leadership for the development of a fast and agile data and analytic exploration and value evaluation capability

* Work with the EDO to design, develop and maintain a data and analytic laboratory that will support rapid data exploration and analytic prototyping

* Provide business and strategic leadership to the data science innovation team

* Provide support for a continually evolving set of unconstrained loss models – one for each line of business

* Data and Analytic Technology Innovation – partner with the EDO to:

* Explore and evaluate new analytic methodologies (e.g. GBM)

* Explore and evaluate new software (e.g. Alteryx)

* Explore and evaluate new data tools (e.g. Hadoop)

* Implementation of new data and analytic techniques – partner with the EDO to:

* Identify business cases that will generate real business value

* Plan and execute the needed infrastructure changes to support it

* Design and build the required data access

* Define the appropriate, repeatable process

* Train the modelers and their customer s on the technique and process

* Ad hoc consulting on complex or challenging projects

Experience & Skills

* 10-15 years of experience in P&C insurance or related industry with deep expertise in data, analytics and the technology that supports it; Minimum 5 years of demonstrated experience leading technical staff in a project-based, matrixed environment.

* PhD or Master's degree highly desirable

* Advanced subject matter expertise in data modeling and analytics with a focus on Statistics, Operations Research, Stochastic Modeling and Machine Learning

* Strong experience in structured data and unstructured data exploration, analysis and visualization

* Strong experience in unstructured data exploration and analysis with an emphasis in one or more of the following areas: Big Data Analytics, Text Mining, Process Mining, Geospatial.

* Strong leadership, negotiation and influencing skills

* Strong strategic thinking and exceptional analytical capability

* Strong verbal/written communication skills; Must be able to communicate effectively at all levels across P&C

* Results-oriented. Demonstrates a sense of urgency and ownership of business issues.

* Customer focused. Builds effective relationships with internal (actuarial, underwriting, operations and information technology) and external customers.

* Innovative. Seeks new and creative ways to approach analytical exercises and development projects.

* Requires sophisticated business acumen in order to successfully translate complex technical topics into business solutions and strategies; Knowledge of The Hartford's formal and informal structures, business processes, and data sources is essential

Behaviors at the Hartford

* Deliver Outcomes – Demonstrate a bias for speed and execution that serves our shareholders and customers.

* Operate as a Team Player – Work together to drive solutions for the good of The Hartford.

* Build Strong Partnerships – Demonstrate integrity and build trust with others.

* Strive for Excellence – Motivate yourself and others to achieve high standards and continuously improve.
