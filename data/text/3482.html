Designs, develops and programs methods, processes, and systems to consolidate and analyze unstructured, diverse “big data” sources to generate actionable insights and solutions for client services and product enhancement.

Interacts with product and service teams to identify questions and issues for data analysis and experiments. Develops and codes software programs, algorithms and automated processes to cleanse, integrate and evaluate large datasets from multiple disparate sources. Identifies meaningful insights from large data and metadata sources; interprets and communicates insights and findings from analysis and experiments to product, service, and business managers.

Acknowledged authority within the Corporation. Acts as a leader of large-scale company initiatives. Viewed by peers as a leader and top contributor and by line management as a key business partner. 10 plus years experience. BA/BS degree preferred.

You must be passionate about using

data to answer complex questions. You will work cross-functionally to define

meaningful success metrics for the marketing operations, use statistical

analysis, data and text mining techniques to better understand customer

behaviors and optimize business processes.

Responsibilities:

Proactive, in-depth analysis and modeling to uncover hidden

opportunities; identifying inconsistencies in complex data structures. Process

and prepare structured, semi structured and unstructured data for developing predictive/descriptive

models.

Create ad hoc and standard reports to monitor key performance

indicators of marketing operations and systems.

Collaborate with Developer, DBA, Business Analyst and List

Management teams on design and implementation of analytics environment and

create data driven processes to support the Modern Marketing initiative.

Work in close collaboration with the Marketing Automation and

Analytics team to develop and implement high business impact analytical

initiatives.

Analyze big data (campaign responses, clickstream, demographic,

etc.) in an effort to drive and optimize automated and digital marketing,

trigger campaigns, audience management and user experience.

Work within the Global Marketing Shared Services group to enable

cross-functional digital marketing campaigns.

Work with our customer database, third party data and other

internal data sources to:

o

Aggregate and analyze customer data

o

Model Lead and Opportunity data; including

forecasting

o

Analyze Contact and Account data for use in:

§

Propensity Modeling

§

Look-a-like Modeling

§

Behavioral analytics

§

Web & Social analytics

§

Campaign analytics

o

Content Consumption Analytics and Modeling

o

Data Mining

o

Data Integration

Skills

& qualifications:

BS/MS in Computer Science, Statistics, Math, Engineering, Computational

Linguistics or other quantitative discipline or equivalent experience.

Proven ability to deal and thrive with multiple imperfect, mixed,

varied, and inconsistent data sources; expert knowledge in one or more of the

following languages/tools/technologies to wrangle and understand data: Oracle

10g-11g-12c, MySQL, PostgreSQL, Hadoop, Map/Reduce, SQL, PL/SQL, R, Java,

RDF/OWL, RapidMiner, KXEN, Weka, Rattle, SPSS Clementine, Oracle Data Miner,

SAS Enterprise Miner. Exadata, Greenplum, Netezza or Asterdata experience is a

plus.

Experience in one or more of the following: regression,

classification, machine learning, decision trees, Bayesian methods, SVM,

boosting/ensemble methods, graph theories, natural language processing (NLP),

text mining, clustering techniques.

Process/technical documentation skills, ability to develop clean,

well-structured and documented code.

Aptitude for clean and effective visualization of discovered

insights.

Data analytics oriented MOOC (Coursera, edX, Udacity, Khan

Academy, etc.) certification, real world experience, B2B Marketing experience,

familiarity with main concepts, data elements and metrics of marketing, web,

social analytics is a plus.

Strong communication skills, fluency in English, both spoken and

written, are required.

Willingness to work flexible hours in collaborating with a global

team.
