        Aperio Data Scientist &#8211; Modeling - New York
        About Aperio:
        Aperio is Point 72's big data research platform. We conduct compliant, data driven research to provide actionable insights to Point72's Long/Short equity investing businesses. Our group analyzes billions of data points from various public sources to identify patterns and produce useful discoveries that result in more informed investment decisions. We are looking for undergrads and advanced degree holders (MS or PhD) in finance, computer science, mathematics, statistics, or other quantitative disciplines to join our team.
        When you join our team, you will have the opportunity to hone the following skills:
        * Statistical modeling and data mining on both structured and unstructured data to extract insights in a timely manner
        * Collaboratively manage all aspects of the research process including methodology selection, data collection and quality, modeling and analysis, and performance monitoring
        * Effectively deliver research findings to investment teams, portfolio managers and other internal clients
        * Work within a team to help drive innovation on metrics and modeling strategies
        * Teach as well as learn
        To succeed in this role you must&#8230;
        * Hold an advanced degree in a quantitative discipline
        * Have 3+ years of industrial working experience in a role that requires advanced statistical analysis
        * Have a proven track record of working with large structured and unstructured data sets to extract timely, meaningful and impactful insights
        * Have 3+ years of hands-on working experience with varied and complex data sets
        * Have extensive knowledge in machine learning, statistical models and data mining tools
        * Have strong programming skills in the following languages: SQL/NoSQL, Python (preferred), R, Scala, Spark
        * Be able to think creatively and independently
        * Be able to traverse big picture as well as finer details
