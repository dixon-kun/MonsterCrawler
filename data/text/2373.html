The Research Architect is responsible for the research, prototyping and development of new technologies to innovate and improve the capability of various functions at TD Ameritrade.  This individual will work closely with our business and technology teams (including security, engineering, application development and operations) to identify opportunities where transformational technologies can help produce better business outcomes.  This position has the ability to work in a variety of technology domains, and has experience in multiple development languages and platforms.  They are expected to work independently, as well as in teams, and can mentor consultants and junior developers on their projects.  The goals of the advance technology research and strategy will be to identify, prototype, educate and gain adoption for technologies that help improve TD Ameritrade.

Responsibilities:

  • Research vendor products and new technology concepts to improve capabilities of infrastructure, analytics and application development products for TD Ameritrade
  • Work with venture capital firms, universities, within TD Ameritrade, and tap into advanced technology forums to develop new ideas to pursue at TD Ameritrade
  • Develop prototypes of selected technologies leveraging partners, consultants, students, or Associates to demonstrate capabilities of new concepts within TD Ameritrade environment
  • Present pros and cons of prototypes to TD Ameritrade senior management to demonstrate capabilities, value proposition, and alignment to strategy
  • Determine and prioritize which prototypes to pursue based on impact to TD Ameritrade
  • Propose business case and value proposition to Senior leaders  to facilitate “go/no go decision” for new technology introduction into TD Ameritrade
  • Partner with Senior business leaders: application development, engineering, security, planning, and operations to launch new solution into production
  • Review proposed technical solutions and coordinate with business partners to launch solution into production
  • Review technical design and specifications to provide initial plans for new product
  • Work with product and services team to define new service offering
  • Oversee the development, planning and execution of firm-wide hackathons, innovation contests, and other innovation related events
  • Be a spokesperson and facilitator for firm-wide participation on innovation projects and events
  • Participate in the development of innovation management processes, evaluation of innovation management tools, and architecture alignment
  • Provide development opportunities for team members in technical research projects

Requirements:

  • Strong experience  with data science and statistics – mastery of statistical concepts, pattern recognition, and ability to glean insights from complex imperfect data
  • Four year college degree in Technology/Engineering Related/Data Science or Decision Science
  • 8+ years’ experience working with data, databases and the process of preparing data for analysis including the analysis itself
  • Strong experience with multiple programming languages, infrastructure, automation, and security
  • Ability to code in R, and Python for stats a plus
  • Familiarity with ETL (Extract Transform Load) a plus
  • Experience working in an agile, iterative manner to foster innovation
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills to work across multiple groups within organization
  • Ability to tailor communications to the appropriate level from intern level to the CEO
  • Knowledge of common software design patterns
  • 10+ years’ experience with Object Oriented Analysis and Design
  • 10 years’ experience with distributed systems development
  • 5+ years’ experience with real time, high volume transaction systems preferred
  • Experience working with highly concurrent systems preferred
  • Full knowledge and experience of multi-threading and concurrency
  • Experience working with networking programming and sockets preferred
  • Financial services experience preferred but not necessary
  • Experience with Unix/Linux, Windows
  • Demonstrated experience working with core business logic within applications
  • Keen awareness of current technology trends and emerging standards
  • Independent person able to handle all phases of research, build and present technology concepts
  • Military education or experience may be considered in lieu of civilian requirements listed
