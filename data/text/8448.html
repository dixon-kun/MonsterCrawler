Global Director of Insights (109075)

We are EA

#WeAreEA and we exist to inspire the world to play. EA values creativity, pioneering, passion, determination, learning, and teamwork. We mean things like acting with curiosity, speaking up with original ideas, and committing to each other as one team.

We’re looking for all the best kinds of people to make great experiences for our players. The best people want a job that inspires them, while giving them room to enjoy their lives. And we want to give them that. We celebrate diversity and inclusion by creating a place where you can come to work and be yourself.

And we want you to join us. We’re hiring a Global Director of Insights.

The Global Director of Insights will spend much of the time leading analysis and interpretation of research data using qualitative and quantitative methods, compiling and analyzing operational reporting and delivering meaningful insights and strategic recommendations. In addition to designing and executing research and analytical plans, the ideal candidate will be responsible for generating actionable insights and recommendations from various data sources.

The Global Director will work with multiple teams who utilize research data from across the organization, such as the Leadership, Operations, Partner Engagement, Employee Engagement and Programs Teams. The individual will routinely meet with them to understand their insights needs and execute best-in-class research to address them. This individual will attend various weekly meetings with various departments to provide updates on various projects, including but not limited to operational trends, player preferences, attitudinal and behavioral studies, channel performance and competitive activity. This position reports to the Head of CE’s Strategy, Programs and Insights team.

This role is part of EA’s Customer Experience organization. The Customer Experience organization strives to make EA known for taking care of its players. We’re players taking care of players and people talking to people – whenever, wherever, and however they need us.

The global gaming audience has never been bigger, and no other company can deliver EA’s portfolio of amazing games and services, across multiple platforms, to players across the world. It is an exciting era in games and an exciting time at Electronic Arts – particularly in our Worldwide Customer Experience (WWCE) organization where we are on a mission to become a company known for taking care of players.

So how does the Global Director of Insights make great experiences for players? They :

  • Establish a vision and an implementation plan for a robust research and analytics function.
  • Develop operational processes and procedures to deliver reporting, analytics, data exploration and insights to the organization.
  • Build the organization’s competence for collecting and transforming data to insights and create understanding on how to best utilize these resources.
  • Establish and maintain data strategy and data governance in partnership with IT, Global Analytics and Insights and other business units.
  • Design, develop, deploy, maintain, enhance, and socialize across the business enterprise data and analytic solutions that include both retrospective and real-time platforms using both quantitative and qualitative methods.
  • Lead strategy development of business intelligence including data warehouse and enterprise integration. Define data required for business intelligence, customer relationship management, data warehousing, meta-data repositories, and operational data stores. Serve as a key partner with the business leaders in defining data and analytics needs to drive key strategies
  • Demonstrate strong communication and presentation skills including the ability to articulate and facilitate discussions effectively across disciplines in an audience appropriate manner.
  • Provide leadership on multiple dimensions including strategic planning, system education, staff development, staff growth, business development, team leadership and collaborative leadership.
  • Design tools that unleash the accessibility to data that enables business staff to self-serve a majority of data reporting needs. Design and maintain core data models for key business area.
  • Ensure data-driven insights are generated on a regular basis to influence decision-making and strategic planning.
  • Maintain awareness of new analytical tools, trends and approaches and bring them into the organization as appropriate.

Candidates should be:

  • Intellectually curious: Has a naturally curious mind and a desire to frame and solve business problems; actively seeks out best-in-class capabilities.
  • Critical thinker: Strong quantitative and qualitative research and analytics skills; ability to go beyond findings to insights and actionable recommendations.
  • Collaborative: Works well in a collaborative team environment between analysts, internal teams, external partners & clients.
  • Self-starter: A self-motivated individual who can take limited direction and produce tangible results; proactively develops out-of-the-box solutions with the available tools and resources.
  • Technology forward: Has an interest in technology and knows the relevant platforms and models; user or at least strong understanding of emerging technology trends.
  • Communication: Clear and persuasive oral and written communication skills; an active listener and concise presenter.
  • Positive attitude: Can-do attitude; finds the bright side of even stressful situations and is willing to learn from them.

Educational Background or Professional Designation Required:

  • Master’s required within the social sciences
  • 10+ years of related experience: management skills, in-depth knowledge of primary research techniques, including problem definition, overall study design, sampling, data collection, data entry, statistical analysis, report writing and presentation
  • Experience in business application of advanced analytics and data modelling tools
  • Knowledge of emerging and established database structure/design, data science tools and technology and their applicability
  • Strategic acumen coupled with tactical excellence
  • Ability to build and motivate a team
  • Preference given for candidates with knowledge of and/or experience in the customer experience and/or interactive entertainment industries
