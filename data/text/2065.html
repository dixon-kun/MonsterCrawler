NETE providesstrategic management consulting, business systems development and integration, Web design and technology solutions, and managed outsourcing services to commercial companies and federal government agencies.

NETE supports the major federal agency that funds biomedical research. We seek a highly motivated, flexible, organized, and detail oriented Mid-level Data Architect to join our accomplished team and play a key role in a new exciting project. What you do at NETE will have a significant impact on the biomedical research community. If you want to learn, grow, and contribute to exciting projects then this is the job for you.

The ideal candidate would be a Data Architect with expertise in Open Source technologies as well as knowledge and experience in research data, scientific publications, and other documents. Good communication and interpersonal skills are essential. Qualified candidates will have a strong academic background in computer science and ideally data science/statistics.

Responsibilities:
-Work closely with a scientific lead, and data engineers to design, and implement an evolving vision for a novel data warehouse solution centered on linkages between funding data, research artifacts, scientific outputs such as publications and patents, and scientific impact. This data warehouse is based in the Azure cloud and uses custom Python code for ETL, PostgreSQL, and Apache Spark.
-Apply and enforce best practices for extracting, transforming and integrating new data sources and developing workflows for curation of these data.
