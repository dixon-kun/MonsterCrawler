        Quest Objectives
        The data engineering team has constructed more pylons and is now seeking an intern who is eager to level up his or her capabilities as a member of our team: to challenge him- or herself with cutting edge technology in the big data space; to build out his or her professional foundation with the fundamentals of data warehousing, data pipeline design, and systems architecting; and to contribute to the data engineers' win rate in Heroes of the Storm and Overwatch scrimmages against the analysts teams.
