QA/Product Support Analyst
Apply

Work as part of the Software Development and Analytics Support Team whose focus is development and support of next generation analytics and delivery platforms. You will actively participate in cross-functional teams with a focus on test, system level automation, product support, E2E monitoring and validation.

* Understand the overall technical architecture and how each feature is implemented.
* Create test plans and automation strategies that scale.
* Be a vocal proponent for quality in every phase of the development process.
* Interface directly with customers to provide guidance, provide status updates, and resolve reported issues.
* Partner with Technical Account Management, Operations, Data Science, and Engineering for resolution of complex problems.
* Contribute to knowledge base articles.
* Must be able to work the hours of 1:00 - 9:00pm EST.


Basic Qualifications


* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related technical field
* 2+ years of Software QA/testing experience
* Exceptional experience in designing SQA test plans and test cases according to requirements
* Exceptional skills in testing multi-tiered, distributed solutions.
* Strong background in automation using Java or other object-oriented programming language
* Strong experience in Perl, Python, Shell or other scripting languages
* Strong SQL experience
* Strong interpersonal, project management and organizational skills with ability to track multiple test executions simultaneously and synthesize results
* Excellent communication, analytical and problem solving skills


Preferred Qualifications


* Experience testing multi-tiered big data solutions (Services, MapReduce, AMQP, HBase, HDFS, Flume) with high loads
* Experience using open source tools such as Nagios for system monitoring.
* Comfortable developing and testing in Linux environments
