The Marketing & Digital Division is leading Caterpillar and our industry with the utilization of data analytics, innovation, digital channels and techniques to drive profit, create more efficient revenue growth, unlock new revenue streams and provide more durable competitive advantages for the business through unexpectedly great experiences for our customers.

Behind every customer success is the team at Caterpillar who is taking the bits and bytes of machine and market data and translating them into useable actionable information for our customers.

Apply now to join our team and help build what matters!

Caterpillar uses quantitative methods such as business simulations, data mining, and advanced statistical techniques to solve problems. The Machine Data Analyst contributes to this mission by leveraging his or her quantitative analysis, data management, modeling and/or data visualization skills as an individual contributor to project teams tasked with solving business problems.

Job Duties/Responsibilities may include, but are not limited to:

* Support business unit partners solving analytics problems using telematics data

* Develop new predictive and prescriptive analytics software and processes

* Validate the statistical and analytics models developed

* Assist with deployment of developed models and analytical capabilities

* Collaborate with research, product development and analytics groups

* Train and mentor student employees and oversee the execution of associated activities

Basic Qualifications:

* MS degree in an engineering, statistics or other data science related discipline

* At least 1 year of industry experience required processing time series data, developing statistical summaries, developing analytical models and reporting/visualizing results

* Previous industry experience with data mining languages / packages such as Python, R or SAS

* Previous industry experience in general programing languages such as C, C , or Python

* Previous industry experience with data visualization packages such as Tableau, Power BI or similar packages

* Previous industry experience with database systems such as SQL, Oracle or similar relational database

* Previous industry experience using revision control software such as Git or similar software

Top Candidates Will Also Have:

* Exposure to machine and telematics time series data

* Exposure to hardware and software aspects associated with machine data collection systems

* Exposure and/or experience in the construction / earth moving / mining industry

* Ability to effectively prioritize assignments tasks to deliver results on time

* Individual must have strong technical communication skills

* Individual must be self-motivated to gather background information, manage and complete challenging tasks on time
