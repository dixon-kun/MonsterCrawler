Role -  Project Manager with Pharmaceutical/Life science experience in Analytics or Data Science projects.
Location: Danbury, CT
Type of Hire - Contract to hire role

Pharmaceutical/Life science experience is mandatory
Why does this job exist:

Due to high volume of project initiatives in client organization, it is very important for the customer to drive all the initiatives and meet the organization goal. This requires a senior Project manager/Business Consultant, who can understanding on the business, proactive communication and ability to lead and drive projects. The individual need to be self-driven, proactive, understand and articulate the initiatives and translate them into appropriate projects.
How does the role add value: The individual will be responsible for the overall direction, coordination, implementation, execution and completion of projects driven by client’s Analytics and Insights team, ensuring the right expectations of business team are met, capture the project requirements, documentation of business rules and driving the project to closure.
What are the minimum prior qualifications to be an applicant for this role:
  • At least 3 years of experience in pharmaceuticals commercial business (pharma sales analytics or marketing/brand analytics) with overall experience of >8 years
  • Understand Pharmaceutical data: Retail, Non-Retail sales, Consumer, claims, Payer and Rep activity data.
  • Should have worked in client facing role for at least 2 years.
  • Adequate knowledge of project management (PMP certification is desirable).
  • Excellent Written and Verbal Communication Skills
  • Should have worked on any of the following technologies in the past: SAS, Spotfire, MS SQL, Oracle DB, QlikView, Tableau
  • Must have developed full scale project plans
  • Proficient in MS Office (Visio, Projects, Excel, Power Point, Access)
  • Good knowledge on SDLC process
