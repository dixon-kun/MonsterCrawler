Overview

Comrise, a global consulting firm, is seeking a Jr. Data Scientist. The candidate that is hired will have the opportunity to work with terabytes and petabytes of data and unique open-source technologies to help address big data challenges for our clients across the globe.  In addition, he/she will become an integral part of a cross-disciplinary, international team and be working on highly visible projects that improve performance and grow our clients' product suite.

There is a three-week training period in ECL, a declarative, data-centric programming language used to process big data across the HPCC Systems' platform. Following the successful completion of training, hired candidates will be assigned to a client project.

Candidates interested in applying to this position must be willing to travel and/or relocate as the location of the project is dependent upon client location. Sponsorship is available.

Responsibilities

  • Mining and analyzing client data to identify critical business insights
  • Proactively developing new metrics and studies to quantify the value of different aspects of content
  • Design multi-factor experiments and validate hypotheses.
  • Translating analytic insights into concrete, actionable recommendations for business service or product improvement.
  • Design and implement scalable and repeatable solutions, and establish scalable, efficient, automated processes for large scale data analyses
  • Work closely with the software engineering team to drive new feature creation

Qualifications


Basic

  • Graduate degree in Data Science, Computer Science/Engineering, Statistics, Mathematics, Operations Research, or other quantitative fields.
  • Strong critical thinking and attention to detail
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills.
  • Must be able to explain technical concepts and analysis implications clearly to a wide audience, and be able to translate business objectives into actionable analyses

Desirable

  • Proficiency at querying relational databases (SQL) and ability to pull data from various sources
  • Experience with scripting language, such as PERL, Java, Python, etc.
  • Experience with analytic tools such as R, SAS, SPSS, etc.
  • Experience with open-source technologies such as No-SQL, Cassandra, Hadoop, Pig, Hive, HPCC Systems, etc.
  • Experience with Machine Learning (classification and regression)

About Comrise

Established in 1984, Comrise is a global consulting firm with headquarters in the U.S. and China. Our teams specialize in Managed IT, Big Data, and Workforce Solutions – Staff Augmentation, Recruiting, RPO, and Payrolling. With nearly 30 years of experience, Comrise provides local talent and resources on a global scale.
