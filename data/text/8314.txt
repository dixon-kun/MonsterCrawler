         Responsibilities
         The role will be responsible for working directly with customers to understand and analyze their problem, working with a Solutions Architect to define the optimal solution, managing the solution through development and implementation, and maintaining a relationship with the customer by providing updates and walkthroughs as needed.
         Throughout the solution development process, the Sr. Solutions Analyst will need to work with multiple internal teams (engineering, data science, etc&#8230;) to ensure that the project deliverables are understood, and are completed on time, while meeting/exceeding expectations of the customer. This requires elements of both leadership and project management.
         This is a role that inherently deals with ambiguity, and the Sr. Solutions Analyst must be comfortable working with a standard framework, but willing to proactively learn and help move projects forward even when dealing with uncertainty.
         The Sr. Solutions Analyst is expected to own the project relationship with one of our clients with minimal supervision. This encompasses managing multiple projects simultaneously for that customer (up to 3 at the same time), while working with them to articulate and capture how these projects could be taken further, how they could connect where possible, and how to move them into production level deployments.
