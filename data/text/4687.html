Company Overview
Youth Villages has been a national leader in the implementation of research-based treatment philosophies in the field of children's mental and behavioral health. Our commitment to helping troubled children and their families find success spans 30 years and includes a comprehensive array of programs and services.
If you are looking for a positive career move where you are meeting the challenges of life and striving to make a positive difference, then Youth Villages is the place for you. We are looking for people with a strong sense of purpose and focus to continually build confidence in yourself and our organization.


Position Summary

This position will support the New England leadership team by providing key data analyses and recommendations that will help meet the goal of transforming the state system in order to radically improve outcomes for children in crisis by building strong families and increasing permanency for all youth. The Analyst will also assist the New England team in the transition from using multiple non-primary data sources and processes to effectively utilizing the Business Intelligence solution (aka SkYView) for operational and clinical decision-making.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities

  • Assist MA/NH leadership in charting the strategic course by providing analysis of critical aspects of the external environment:
  • Conducts data analysis/research to support strategic planning, business development, and government relations in MA and NH, including research on state trends, funding streams, competitive landscape and customer needs
  • Conduct data analysis/research on service needs/gaps, other providers, competitive models/programs and referral sources in existing and new areas to inform YV’s ability to both achieve scale and be an innovative state partner
  • Research the market and our competitors (e.g. analysis of state funding streams and budgets, youth served by state, analysis of competitor UFR's, financial status, structure, referral sources, market share, etc.)
  • Perform deep dives of MA/NH organizational data to identify trends, needs, opportunities.
  • Make strategic recommendations based on analysis of the available data.

Business Intelligence system support:

  • Work with state and program leadership and the corporate Business Intelligence team to determine appropriate data sources and measures for SkYView
  • Verify accuracy of data transformation processes that occur between the source data and the SkYView Identify gaps in information available to state leadership, as well as potential sources of data to fill those gaps
  • Work with the corporate Business Intelligence team to provide training for state and program leadership in effective use of SkYView
  • Monitor use of SkYView through regular attendance at leadership/clinical meetings; gather information from meetings on potential improvements that could be made to the SkYView

Flexibility
