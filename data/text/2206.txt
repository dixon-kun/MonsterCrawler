          Overall Purpose
         This position works with clients and internal groups to document and manage the development of new products in the credit card and consumer lending segments. This position is the primary contact for the Technology and Operations groups for business requirements, project meetings, and product functionality.
          Essential Functions
         This position works with internal groups (including other product teams) to manage the testing and acquisition of new data sources and to specify and manage the parameters and process related to data quality. This position is the primary contact for the Data Sciences, Technology and Operations groups for business requirements, project meetings, and capabilities required. The position will specify third party data needs and will drive the sourcing of potential providers. In addition, the successful candidate will work with the Data Quality team to specify and support the data quality process and the establishment of data anomaly parameters.
          Works with Technology to create business requirements to support product development initiatives in the credit card and consumer lending segments.
          Drives the product roadmap including establishing release milestones and launch plans.
          Insures that functional requirements support the business requirements.
          Works with the Delivery group to insure that maintenance and operational needs are reflected in the roadmap and related requirements.
          Responsible for sales readiness including working with marketing and sales to insure sales collateral, tools and training are current.
          Assists Sales in customer acquisition and retention including value study support.
          Responsible for insuring that the product provides the anticipated value and establishes key performance indicators to track product value.
          Works with Operations to define and track operational metrics.
          Serves as the escalation point for Technology and Operations.
          Engages with customers, potential customers and business partners to gain customer and industry needs insight utilized in the product development process.
          This position may or may not have direct reports.
