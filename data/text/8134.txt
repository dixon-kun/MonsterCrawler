           The Configuration Analyst
          is responsible for creating and maintaining system processing for clients, vendors and other ADP product areas. Interfaces with internal and external clients to resolve escalated issues and answer product and process questions. Develops and implements complex configuration solutions to the application and resolves daily processing issues for multiple custom client databases. Troubleshoots complex issues, develops solutions and recommendations. Utilizes data analysis techniques including, but not limited to writing queries and reports.
           Responsibilities:
           Provides operational support for clients after the ADP product has gone into production with the client.
           Develops and determines best methods to accomplish successful delivery of solutions. Applies tools and testing methods to processes in order to consistently produce error free results in a timely manner.
           Identifies and resolves escalated client and production issues by conducting root cause analysis of the issue and resolving the issue or working with the appropriate resource to resolve. Recommend process improvement and client/associate satisfaction improvement initiatives.
           Develops and manages work plans for specified client base. Manages self to the completion of those tasks and informs others when problems occur or when commitments cannot be met as planned.
           Responds and reacts to a variety of critical issues simultaneously.
           Writes data base queries and reports as appropriate. Creates, updates and maintains functional documentation to support client processing.
           Demonstrates thorough knowledge of applicable platforms/applications. Develops solutions and recommendations to client needs through the use of the application; understands impact on scope and timelines. Identifies and reports potential scope changes and opportunities to appropriate management.
           Uses effective communication and facilitation skills to advise and influence clients at the user and line management levels. Prepare written communications such as status reports, project/internal correspondence, and functional specifications.
