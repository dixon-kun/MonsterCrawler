        Senior Research Analyst &#8211; New York
        About iSpot:
        iSpot.tv is a fast-growing venture backed start-up with rapidly increasing revenues. iSpot.tv is the leader in real-time TV ad data and analytics. The attention and conversion analytics company measures TV ad activity at scale and directly from 10+ million smart TV screens. The proprietary platform measures ad impressions in a digital-like manner across linear, OTT, VOD and DVR environments and across all operators and zip codes. iSpot's attention analytics measure viewer retention and tune-out while ads are playing on the screen. iSpot conversion analytics set the industry standard for TV attribution by directly connecting TV ad impressions with web, app and other 1st party data. The company's dashboards, APIs and analytics are utilized by leading brands in every major industry, as well as by TV networks and agencies.
        This is a rare opportunity to get equity in the early stages of a company with significant traction and work with an experienced team.
        About You:
        You are an insights and analytics guru with customer-facing experience translating data and analysis into business insights and recommendations. You are excited about the power of data-driven decision-making and look forward to opportunities to develop and showcase insights to customers.
        You are comfortable collaborating with various stakeholders (customers and internal teams), understanding complex data challenges, and developing recommendations. You smile in the face of ambiguity, welcome the opportunity to get hands-on with the data, manage multiple workstreams, and look for opportunities to create win-win situations for all parties involved.
        Your responsibilities will include the following:
        * Work with the iSpot Customer Success team to develop detailed analyses, reports, presentations and marketing/sales materials
        * Respond to day-to-day research requests from Customer Success/Marketing and Research/Sales Management team members
        * Develop and disseminate analyses highlighting trends in iSpot TV attention and conversion analytics data
        * Participate in crafting compelling research stories for tent-pole events such as the Oscars, World Series, and Super Bowl
        * Proactively examine and analyze iSpot metrics and data in order to develop client-facing stories regarding TV viewing habits (e.g. viewer segmentation, creative effectiveness etc.)
        * Explore and evaluate potential new metrics &amp; insights that will enhance the value of iSpot's offerings to advertisers &amp; networks
        Besides being highly motivated and valuing the significant equity opportunity, we are looking for someone who has:
        * 5+ years of customer-facing experience in the areas of network ad sales, marketing analytics, marketing data science, business intelligence, and data visualization at an agency, research, TV, or digital media services firm (responsibilities included national TV ratings analyses and sales positioning)
        * A minimum with 4-5 years of hands-on analytics (media, media sales, customer, digital, big data) experience
        * A deep understanding or and experience in Network TV and/or digital media sales including the relationship between bought, earned and owned media
        * Experience developing analytical solutions and insights which can include qualitative and quantitative data from multiple sources
        * Excellent computer skills, particularly PowerPoint and Excel (formatting, creating charts/graphs, pivot tables and formulas)
        * Hands-on experience with Data Analysis (e.g. MySQL, MS Excel, SQL Server) and Business Intelligence/Data Visualization Tools (e.g. Tableau, Qlikview, PowerBI, Business Objects) preferred
        * Ability to understand and develop approaches to introduce advanced analytics techniques (e.g. statistics, probabilities, predictive modeling, MMM) into business decision-making processes
        * Strong communication skills and the ability to examine, synthesize, and present marketing and customer data to various stakeholders (customers and partners)
        * Comfort and ability to engage with and articulate analytical findings and insights to senior management and C-Level executives
        * Willingness and comfort with travel to customer sites
        * Bachelor's degree (B.A., B.S.) from an accredited college or university in Marketing, Mathematics, Statistics, Communications, Media or related field
