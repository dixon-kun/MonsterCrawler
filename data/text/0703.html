Drive transformative growth at OnDeck by evolving our Term Loan and Line of Credit products. Use data and generative research to build, launch and iterate on how we can meet small business owners' financing needs.

At OnDeck, we improve the world's economic landscape by changing the way small businesses access capital. We do it in an environment where we celebrate collaboration, but aren't afraid of healthy competition; where we're serious about our work but don't take ourselves too seriously; and where we are small enough to be nimble but strong enough to make a big impact.

As a Senior Product Manager, at OnDeck, you will:

* Use OnDeck's trove of data and your generative research with small business owners to develop hypotheses and business cases about how to evolve OnDeck's Term Loan and Line of Credit Products, value proposition and processes.

* Partner with Technology, Data Science, Marketing, Finance, User Research, Sales, and more to build and execute on tests that ground your hypotheses.

* Measure, iterate on and broadly launch the strongest opportunities that you test to drive material acquisitions growth for OnDeck.

Necessary qualifications for success:

* Strong business acumen demonstrated by experience making data and profitability-driven decisions.

* Comfort accessing, transforming, and analyzing data to uncover growth opportunities.

* User-centric mindset and drive to deliver the best possible solutions for your customers.

* Experience partnering and building strong relationships with Technology and Data Science to test, build, launch and grow transformative products.

* 5+ years of work experience, including Product Management experience.

* Exceptional communication skills across stakeholders, technologists, data scientists, and senior management.

About OnDeck:

We're all about teamwork, passion and making an impact. Oh and having fun – Whether it's community service events, book club meetings, team get-togethers or weekly social hours in the office.

OnDeck is the leader in online small business lending. Since 2007, the company has powered Main Street's growth through advanced lending technology and a constant dedication to customer service. To date, the company has deployed over $5 billion to more than 50,000 customers in 700 different industries across the United States, Canada and Australia.
