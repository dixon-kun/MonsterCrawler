DNV GL's - Oil & Gas Unit is seeking an Engineer. The Engineer will work with the risk management team developing and implementing risk assessment and management programs for Oil and Gas customers. This poisition will be located in our Dublin, Ohio office.

Tasks May Include

  • Assist in analyzing multiple sets of pipeline data using Excel or specialized software packages
  • Complete technical pipeline engineering assessments (e.g. defect assessments, change in operating conditions, change in stress conditions)
  • Assist in the development of risk assessment algorithms
  • Assist in facilitated hazard identification studies
  • Work closely with clients to ensure projects are run to their satisfaction and requirements

What We're Seeking

  • B.S., M.S., or Ph.D. in related engineering field
  • Minimum of 1 year of pipeline risk experience
  • Programming experience in VBA, Python, SQL, or other programming language
  • Working knowledge of reliability and statistics
  • Experience with relational databases
  • Excel lent computational and communication skills (both written and spoken)
  • Commitment to deliver detailed and quality work with minimum oversight
  • Willing to travel an estimated 25% of the year
  • Demonstrated proven ability to develop creative solutions to complex problems
  • Strong sense of responsibility and ownership for projects and tasks
  • We conduct pre-employment background checks and drug screenings

Preferred Skills

  • Knowledge of process safety management fundamentals
  • Previous risk assessment development & implementation experience
  • Database architecture experience
  • Data Science / Machine Learning experience

Driven by our purpose of safeguarding life, property and the environment, DNV GL enables organisations to advance the safety and sustainability of their business. We provide classification and technical assurance along with software and independent expert advisory services to the maritime, oil & gas and energy industries. We also provide certification services to customers across a wide range of industries.

Combining leading technical and operational expertise, risk methodology and in-depth industry knowledge, DNV GL empowers our customers’ decisions and actions with trust and confidence. We continuously invest in research and collaborative innovation to provide customers and society with operational and technological foresight.

Originated in 1864, DNV GL operates globally in more than 100 countries with our 15,000 professionals dedicated to helping our customers make the world safer, smarter and greener.

DNV GL is the leading technical advisor to the global oil and gas industry. We enable safe, reliable and enhanced performance in projects and operations.

We provide consistent, integrated services within technical and marine assurance and advisory, risk management advisory and offshore classification. Together with our partners, we drive the industry forwards by developing best practices and standards.

Our people combine industry expertise, multi-disciplinary skills and innovation to solve complex challenges for our customers.
