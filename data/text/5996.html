Job Summary:

The Retail Delivery team is seeking creative, energetic, and innovative professionals who may be interested in making a change into a role where they would really have an impact on the strategic direction of Union Bank. Retail Delivery is responsible for creating bottoms-up strategy for the retail bank. Daily activities would include industry research, data analysis, strategic roadmap development, and project inception. They will work closely with technology, business, and finance partners. The team is highly relied upon heavily by executive leadership to transform strategies into actionable initiatives for the bank. This position will play a key part in building out the analytical and research capabilities within Retail Delivery. The Data Scientist role is responsible to evolve the organization to use key insights and data to drive internal decision making efforts that will transform Union Bank into a business that truly differentiates and competes on smart, market-driven analytics, and campaign management.

Major Responsibilities:

Source Data:

* Identifies and integrates data sourced internal and external to the bank and plugging in accordingly.

* Builds strong business domain knowledge and develops necessary relationships with data owners.

* Correlates internal and external data points and rapidly structures big, unstructured data for relevant use.

Analyzes Data:

* Analyzes both quantitative and qualitative information using state-of-the-art data processing tools and/or analytical methodologies.

* Finds patterns streaming from different sources infers from those patterns, customer behavior and writes statistical models to pinpoint behavioral triggers.

* Analyzes structured data to identify business opportunities using statistical modeling, segmentation strategies, optimization algorithms, and data mining analyses (regression, predictive).

Draws Conclusions:

* Translates findings into business value.

* Presents Findings to drive strategic decisions.

* Summarizes well supported recommendations that are relevant, timely, and actionable.

* Use advanced data visualization techniques to provide a concise and compelling summary of analysis findings in reports and presentations to executive management.

* Provide continual consultation with senior and executive management to identify strategic direction for continual improvement relating to emerging data trends and issues.

Key leadership behaviors should include:

* Drive toward creative innovation in response to complex problems.

* Customer experience design focused, Broad (and bottoms-up) understanding of ancillary business scope.

* Emphasis on driving towards efficiency.

* Strong partnership, communication, and knowledge sharing skills.

* Challenging the status queue.

* Resourcefulness.

* Spirit of test and learn Requires.

* Fluency in SQL and prior programming experience (preferably Python and/or C ).

* Strong understanding of statistics and experience using statistical software (R, SPSS, SAS etc.).

* Strong working knowledge of Data Mining and Machine Learning techniques.

* Ability to communicate results to management teams.

* Previous experience in database marketing.

* Experience working with large data sets and distributed computing (Hive/Hadoop).

* Experience using third party data and APIs.

* 3-10 years of work experience, preferably within retail banking.

* Experience in consulting, corporate planning, or retail strategy at a leading firm, especially within a financial services context.

* Demonstrated problem solving, quantitative, analytical, and thought leadership skills.

* Ability to distill insights into a compelling deliverable to inform the strategic direction and roadmap.

* Track record of leadership and impact, covering both dedicated and cross-functional resources.

* Strong interpersonal and communication skills, and ability to effectively interact with senior executives.

* Ability to work independently and function as part of multiple concurrent teams.

* Strong attention to detail.

Education:

* Bachelor's Degree in Engineering, Math, Statistics, Finance, or Computer Science Preferred.

* Master's degree or higher in Engineering, Math, Statistics, Finance, Computer Science or other technical field.
