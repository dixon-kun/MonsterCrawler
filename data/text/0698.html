At Wunderman Health , we are inspired by the connected nature of people, data, and health content working in harmony to create innovative and profitable experiences. And our greatest reward is the growing list of global brands that turn to us time and time again for results they can count on.

Content has never been so plentiful, and consumer attention has never been in such short supply. But we believe that brands can still inspire people through imaginative experiences. Experiences to identify with, experiences to embrace, experiences to take with them. And that's what we help brands do every day.

We are Wunderman. We believe in a better future. And we’re looking for a Director of Content Strategy to help us build it.

About the role:

The Director of Content Strategy will lead our Consumer Health Content area and has substantial experience leading and developing consumer content, will be responsible for lead the development of client’s content strategy through identifying category opportunities and consumer insights to lead a team to develop and deploy multiple forms of content across multiple brands.

The Director of Content Strategy will lead content strategy development (e.g., strategy framework development and application), support the creative development process (e.g., creative brief development and engagement factors), and ensure that the implementation of the program is consistent with best practices (including ensuring the right level of support is being provided for analytics and SEO optimization before and during the program) for Wunderman World Health consumer health clients.


  • Understand the clients’ business and content needs, category drivers, trends and competitive dynamics
  • Conduct analysis of content marketing solution options, develop and recommend a strategic content marketing approach that meet clients’ business objectives
  • Lead client sessions to facilitate content needs and opportunities
  • Apply consumer insights to designing consumer content and engagement experiences
  • Collaborate with creative & technical teams to develop engaging and profitable content concepts
  • Conduct reviews of competitor and comparator content ecosystems
  • Identify and prioritize content requirements and deliverables
  • Author various deliverables suitable for the communication of the content strategy, brand team toolkits, and performance measurement of content ecosystem
  • Lead presentation of findings and recommendations, and facilitate implementation
  • Provide resource effort estimates and identify assumptions and risks for project tasks
  • Develop Wunderman World Health intellectual property and process by contributing frameworks and tools
  • Mentor and support the career development of more junior level content strategy resources


  • A professional with 5-10 years of experience in digital content marketing (both “always on” social presence, campaigns, and websites)
  • Preferred experience in content and media publishing
  • Preferred experience in package goods
  • Demonstrated understanding of online business and content models and strategies, and practical experience publishing content across multiple platforms as well as paid, owned and earned
  • Experience leading content and editorial strategy on website development, mobile applications and social media campaigns.
  • Highly professional, persuasive, and strong client interaction skills, especially with senior clients
  • Exceptional verbal and written communication skills
  • Drive for exceptional performance
  • A detail, solution, and delivery orientation
