The Adelphic platform, part of the Viant Advertising Cloud™, is a real-time mobile DSP that connects brands to consumers in order to maximize digital advertising investments. Our culture is a unique combination of creativity, ambition, smarts, and a keen flexibility to adapt to a rapidly changing marketplace. We are a community of innovators and problem solvers who readily accept challenges and crave a work environment that is guided by a lively exchange of ideas. At Adelphic, we look for the best and brightest talent in the marketplace, who can learn and grow with us to change the advertising landscape.

About the Role:

Adelphic seeks a VP of Engineering to join our Waltham, MA office. Our Engineering team works on the development of the Adelphic platform in an agile environment. Our platform uses advanced machine learning techniques and big data processing, and is deployed in a distributed, multi-region, cloud-based infrastructure.

Responsibilities:

  • Lead the exceptional Engineering team at Adelphic, including software developers; database architects, engineers, and administrators; and data scientists and analysts.
  • Establish and deliver clear and aligned vision, roadmap, role clarity, and standards for the organization.
  • Promote a software development culture of quality, collaboration, rapid delivery that is results-oriented.
  • Collaborating cross-functionally with organizational leadership in PM, Business Development, Marketing, Sales, Legal, and Finance to execute product vision.
  • Develop technical leaders in the Engineering team to promote sound architecture and design, technology choices, and development processes.
  • Improve performance, scalability, availability, and cost efficiency of the Adelphic platform.
  • Identify opportunities to drive both business growth and efficiency from an engineering standpoint.

Qualifications:

  • Proven ability to recruit, develop and grow the best engineering talent to create a self-organizing, empowered and motivated team.
  • Successful experience building distributed systems with high-throughput, low-latency, and high-availability requirements.
  • Successful experience with agile development and continuous integration processes.
  • Data science and big data experience a plus.
  • Experience with DevOps processes and cloud-based infrastructures a plus.
  • Ad tech industry experience a plus.

About Viant:

Viant Technology is a leading global people-based advertising technology company, enabling marketers to better plan, execute, and measure their digital media investments through our cloud-based platform.  Built on a foundation of people instead of cookies, the Viant Advertising Cloud™ provides marketers with access to over 1 billion registered users globally, one of the largest registered user databases in the world, and infuses accuracy, transparency, reach and accountability into cross device advertising.

In 2016, Viant became a subsidiary of Time Inc. (NYSE:TIME), one of the world’s leading media companies with over 100 influential brands including People, Sports Illustrated, Fortune and Time.  In January 2017, Adelphic was acquired by Viant to form the industry’s first people-based DSP, combining Viant’s first-party database and Adelphic’s programmatic platform. For more information, please visit www.viantinc.com and www.adelphic.com.
