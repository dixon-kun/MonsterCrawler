          Big Data Engineer - Intern-164819
            About the Opportunity
           As a data engineer, you have the keys to the kingdom &#8211; Pitney Bowes&#8217;s data from across the company made available to you through our data lake along with all the technology and computing power of the Amazon cloud. You will work with our team both in the US and offshore to implement data workflows, ensure data quality, and leverage new technologies to improve speed and efficiency. This position plays a critical role in ensuring our production systems remain operational and supporting the development of new data driven products.
            About Us
           We&#8217;re the big data team based out of Danbury, CT. Our main function is to develop and maintain the Pitney Bowes data lake, and to use its data and technologies to deliver value to teams across the company. We are a team of developers, architects, data engineers, security experts, data scientists, and business analysts who see the potential in data driven products and solutions.
            About You
           You are someone who is:
             High-Tech:
            Our team is constantly evolving its tech stack and evaluating new products on the marketplace. Although we do not expect you to be familiar with all of them, a strong technology background is important for being able to get up to speed quickly. If you enjoy coding and solving challenges, this will be a good opportunity for you.
             Curious:
            This position offers a lot of opportunity to explore new technologies and methods of data management. With the Amazon cloud offering 65+ services available at the click of a button, you can explore a new one every day of the internship and still not get through them all!
             Data-Driven:
            The data we have in our data lake is the life blood of our team; it enables us to do what we do every day. We are looking for someone with an interest in data management and who can bring innovative ideas to how we maintain our data platform.
             Organized:
            With our datasets coming from dozens of systems and many more teams from across the globe, it&#8217;s very important that we keep the datasets organized and that we understand how they link together. If you are able to understand complex interactions between production data systems, you will have a view that very few people in the company get, and in a great position to drive significant change!
            Qualifications
             Pursuing (or completion of) a degree in a technical discipline e.g. Computer Science, Security, Software Development, etc.
             Extensive coding experience (Python, Java, C++, SQL)
             Understanding of open source systems, data models, ETL workflows, and job scheduling
             Experience with big data technologies (Hadoop) preferred
             Experience with could systems preferred
             Able to thrive when working as a part of a small, collaborative team
            The Team
           Our global Innovation team is dedicated to using the best-in-class tools, processes and modern architectures to create great experiences for our clients. In a rapidly changing world, we have a clear technical vision for our future that includes SaaS, APIs, Big Data, Advanced Analytics, Mobile and the Internet of Things. We are also focused on creating great client experiences, utilizing a Design Thinking platform and approach.
