Location: 1 ADP Blvd. Roseland, NJ 07068

Core Skills: C/C++, Pro-C, Boost, AIX, TCL, Schell Scripting, Unit Testing, Rest Web Services, Java

RESPONSIBILITIES:

* Competent in all phases of the development project life cycle.

* Acts as an expert source of technical or functional information when it comes to C/C++, Boost, and Pro-C technology stack and for the suite of custom applications/modules that she/he is responsible for

* Works independently to manage current and new technologies, perform strategic upgrades, and make recommendations to technical management.

* Able to research, recommend, introduce new complex technologies consistent with mission of business unit.

* Define, document & implement technology standards.

* Acts as consultant in providing direction for assigned Applications.

* Working as part of the team to engineer, re-engineer, design, develop, implement, and test high volume Internet-enabled document processing and archiving application software

* Designs and develops high quality reusable code and working applications in accordance with specifications or based on the re-engineering of the legacy modules.

* Adheres to engineering standards, UI design standards and templates, various development methodologies -- either Agile/Scrum, Kanban, or Waterfall - as chosen by the enterprise Architect

* Leverage and reverse engineer existing software, implements new features that both abide to directions set by architects, and cost-effective in using the legacy code

* Documents design of applications to assist in training and maintenance

* Delivers code under demanding deadlines throughout the project lifecycle

* Performs unit and systems testing of applications/solutions. Resolves all defects/issues reported. Troubleshoots and supports solutions on an ongoing basis

* Working closely within the guidelines and practices established by ADP's security team & ARB to ensure the proper implementation and business practices.

* Meet all committed deliverable deadlines throughout project lifecycle

* Utilizes an efficient, clear, and concise communication style to regularly communicate ongoing status, milestones, project risks and successes with team leaders and managers as well as others within the development team

* Work with teammates to expand personal technical expertise and pursue cross-training opportunities

* Maintain technical skills and enhance skillset thru periodic training and research.

QUALIFICATIONS:

* Strong communication skills.

* Strong experience with C, C++, Boost, Pro C, OOAD and multi-tier software architecture

* 4+ years hands on experience developing and debugging applications in a Linux or AIX Unix environments using IBM VACPP, or GNU C++, or similar

* Experience with Oracle DB (preferably Oracle 11g), SQL, and PL/SQL are a must

* Experience with Tcl, Java, PDF-related APIs, or Application clustering will be counted as definite advantages

* RESTful services, application messaging middleware such as IBM MQ, noSQL, and JDBC is a plus

* Working knowledge of either Eclipse IDE or NetBeans is preferable.

* Understands the core of multi-threaded application development and experience in programming multi-threaded applications in Java and C/C++

* Familiarity with application servers (Tomcat) is a plus.

* Hands-on technical expertise in IBM AIX or Linux OS, CVS or VSS and ANT or Maven is required.

* Requires a thorough understanding of software development life cycle processes and methodologies.

* Understanding of basic security approaches (e.g. firewalls, ssh, data encryption and compression) across multiple sites is a definite plus

* Exposure to developing web based tools to configure sites/users/applications is a big plus

* Exposure to integration of internal corporate and vendor supplied application software

* Knowledge of web based self-service, HR, payroll, and benefit applications is a plus
