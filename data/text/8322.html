Sally Beauty Holdings (NYSE: SBH) is the world’s largest wholesale and retail distributor of beauty supplies.  Our world headquarters is conveniently located in the Dallas / Fort Worth Metroplex, just 5 minutes north of Lake Lewisville off of I35E.

We are currently looking for a Database Marketing Analyst to join our Marketing team!  This is a strategic and operational role responsible for leading customer strategy projects, developing all marketing analytics, and leading a combined team of analysts and data scientists.  Key responsibilities include:

  • Customer targeting and segmentation methodologies including scoring, propensity modeling, cohort analysis, and lifecycling.
  • Overseeing development of new marketing data sciences projects and model development. Manage weekly and monthly key performance measurement and reporting analytics
  • Leverage big data analytics tools like Alteryx to develop key customer behavior insights; partner with CRM Manager to develop strategies to operationalize.
  • Making continuous improvement recommendations with the data gathered as to what marketing changes need to be made in order to drive sales and EBITDA growth.

  • Design and manage complex data management and customer modeling strategy for direct and digital marketing programs.  Develop and implement segmentation and targeting schema. Partner closely with CRM Manager to design, implement, and analyze new marketing programs and tactics. Lead development, support, and communication of marketing analytics to leadership enabling agile marketing practices throughout retention and acquisition channels.
  • Leverage new analytics and visualization tools like tableau and Alteryx server to build and share marketing KPIs with the organization.  Manage, prioritize, and lead marketing data sciences roadmap including business case development and leadership sell-in.
  • Management of team and extended partners including Experian, Buxton, Cognizant, and Credera.
  • Skillset refinement, continuing education, and project R&D.


  • Bachelor’s Degree in marketing analytics, management science, or related field required
  • 5+ years of business experience, including 2 years managing junior level staff/agencies
  • Minimum 3 years in direct and digital marketing experience
  • Working CRM platform and campaign management tool experience
  • Must be proficient in SQL and database applications including Microsoft Access and SQL Studio
  • Excellent working knowledge of relational databases
  • Strong presentation skills with experience communicating with executive-level audiences
  • Ability to synthesize complex data from a variety of sources into a cohesive, clear, and concise body of work
  • Experience in multi-channel retail industry a plus

We offer a competitive salary, outstanding benefits package that includes medical, dental, vision, life Insurance, paid vacation and sick days, paid holidays, merchandise discounts, tuition reimbursement, profit sharing, and 401(k) with company match.

Qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to their race, color, religion, national origin, sex or disability.
