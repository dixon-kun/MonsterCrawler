The Job

Our engineering team solves tough problems and invents new ways to battle fraud using big data and audio science in the cloud.  Our engineers are creating bleeding edge products to solve global problems and they are looking for new team members to join them as they continue to build innovative voice and audio security. The future interface for all devices.  The DevOps Engineer will provide operational support for the strongest engineering organization in audio security.

Skills and Requirements

  • This position "owns" one of our platforms in terms of support, standardization, automation, extension, security patches, etc.
  • You have the ability to understand a hardware/software platform from the ground up, become a subject matter expert on it, and have the ability to troubleshoot, configure, and repair the platform
  • You have demonstrated the ability to make relevant, specific, and high value recommendations to the design or implementation of a platform.  This includes categories such as automation, features, security, manageability, etc.
  • Startup experience is a requirement.  The candidate must possess the ability to "wear many different hats", juggle multiple priorities, and thrive in an environment where you don't have a strictly defined job definition
  • On-call and night and weekend work are occasionally required

Tech Experience

  • Extensive Ubuntu linux administration experience.
  • Expertise with Chef/Puppet configuration management, and extensive automation responsibilities
  • Knowledge of some or all of our toolset:  Zabbix, Jenkins, vagrant, nginx, VMWare, Active Directory, iptables, tinc, PfSense
  • Experience with networking concepts within firewall, switch, and router configuration including: VLANs, DNS, VPNs, ACLs, etc.
  • Demonstrated experience identifying security vulnerabilities with the associated ability to remediate them in a timely manner
  • MySQL expertise as a DBA with the ability to write moderately complex queries
  • Scripting expertise, preferably Python
  • Demonstrated history working within a distributed architecture

Our Bio

Founded in 2011  |  Raised $122 million

Venture backed by Andreessen Horowitz, Google Capital, IVP, Citi Ventures, Felicis Ventures, & GV

Pindrop is the pioneer in voice fraud prevention and authentication. We provide enterprise solutions to reduce fraud losses and authentication expense for some of the largest call centers in the world. Pindrop’s patented Phoneprinting™ technology can identify, locate and authenticate phone devices uniquely just from the call audio thereby detecting fraudulent calls as well as verifying legitimate callers. We have been selected by the world’s largest banks, insurers, brokerages and retailers, detecting over 80% of fraud even for attackers never seen before. Pindrop’s solutions are allowing customers to reduce call time and improve their customers’ experience even while reducing fraud losses.
