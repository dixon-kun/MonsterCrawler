Join a new targeting cell for a technology focused customer attempting leading edge operations. Duties will include but are not limited to:

  • Analyze Intel sources to provide tailored targeting data to assist customer in operational and acquisition planning.
  • Leverage analytical tools to identify relevant data and collection needs. Identify operational opportunities based on targeting analysis.
  • Brief customer on targeting strategy, opportunities, risks, and analysis


Typical Duties and Responsibilities:
  • All-Source Analysis experience is required
  • BA/BS degree with 10+ years of related IC experience in targeting, Intelligence analysis, operations, collection management, data science or technology development (or 7+ years with MA/MS).
  • 5+ years of specific experience with the DoD or the IC as an Intel analyst or targeter.
  • Experience developing or using targeting / analysis tools is required
  • Ability to collaborate and communicate effectively with team members and mission partners with minimum supervision is required
  • TS/SCI with Polygraph is required


Required Qualifications:
  • Experience with hard target sets
  • Knowledge of aerospace industry and technology.
  • Knowledge of Open Source research
  • Foreign language expertise
