DRINKS, a stealth-mode VC-backed startup, is a top-five US Direct to Consumer (DTC) marketing technology company that manages wine brands. The Company is led by an entrepreneurial team of marketing technology, data science, DTC, and M&A experts with several successful exits. DRINKS operates in a compelling market: a $33B industry virtually untouched by the Internet, though that is now changing quickly.

To help us build the next generation of online and mobile wine stores that will be used by tens of millions of people, we’re on a relentless hunt for a superb Senior Web Designer who shares our passion for leveraging cutting-edge UX and visual design to transform how people buy wine.

Key skills and experience:

·       A senior level web designer with strong and successful technical lead experience

·       Creative design skills with a strong portfolio of creative work would be an advantage

·       Proven experience of performing technical aspects of a front-end build

·       Highly proficient in building websites using HTML, CSS, and jQuery

·       Proven experience of building responsive websites (please provide examples)

·       Experience working in a web design team alongside creative designers and front-end

·       developers

·       Able to meet tight deadlines and remain calm under pressure

·       Desire and willingness to improve knowledge of new emerging technologies

·       Developing cross-browser and cross-platform compatible solutions

·       In depth knowledge of current coding techniques and best practices

·       Excellent communication skills and attention to detail

·       A good understanding of applied website information architecture

·       Good communication skills

·       A flexible attitude with proven experience of working in a small team

·       Bonus: a high level of SEO experience, specifically onsite optimization, but also a full and broad

knowledge of effective SEO strategies and techniques

·       Bonus: Knowledge of writing extended CSS coding languages such as LESS/CSS or SASS

The ideal Web Designer has 6+ years of web design experience with a solid understanding of visual design, typography, layout/composition, color theory, interactive and responsive design principles.

Complete benefit packaging including a flexible (unlimited) vacation plan, generous healthcare plan, dental insurance, vision insurance, life insurance, short and long term disability insurance, and covered garage parking. Plus, we cater lunch weekly, host frequent wine giveaways and tastings, and provide your choice of leading-edge technology tools.

To be considered, please send your resume and portfolio to: mike.reisman@drinks.com
