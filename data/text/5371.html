Data Science/Engineering Consultant at Genzeon Corporation

Plymouth Meeting, PA

Report this job

About the Job

Exciting Opportunity with Genzeon Corporation, We are currently looking for Data Science/Engineering Consultant for the location. Plymouth Meeting , PA . This is a fulltime / Perm position with one of our Healthcare Client. Please find the job description of the same below.

Job Description:

2 Open Positions:

Required Skills:

  • Senior role requires skills in design, writing and implementation of ETL processes that convert data-sources into an industry-standard-based data-repository known as OMOP (Observational Medical Outcomes Partnership). Will provide guidance and mentoring to data developers on the team. Must ensure all coding standards are enforced by performing code reviews and participating in the Standards Committee.
  • Mid-Level role requires skills in maintaining and supporting all OMOP (Observational Medical Outcomes Partnership) conversion refreshes in Production. Will work with the development team to ensure refreshes are done on-time and according to schedule.  Will ensure all backlogs are accurate and reflected in the right priority.

Other Required Skills:

  • Big data/Hadoop knowledge
  • Must be capable of learning and understanding OMOP (Observational Medical Outcomes Partnership) Data quickly and efficiently
  • Amazon Redshift experience
  • Must gather requirements/business rules needed to ETL the source into OMOP CDM.
  • Needs to participate in scrum agile meetings – sprint planning, retro, backlog grooming, daily stand-ups, sprint demo
  • Strong SQL working-knowledge
  • Working knowledge of ETL process and databases
  • Scrum Agile experience

Nice to Have:

  • Existing knowledge of OMOP (Observational Medical Outcomes Partnership) or Medical and Pharmaceutical patient-level data experience is a plus
  • Rabbit in a Hat and White Rabbit a plus
  • Working knowledge of Linux Shell programming
  • Experience with version control systems such as Subversion and Git
  • Experience with Jenkins
  • Programing with Java, Scala
  • Microsoft VBA knowledge
  • Experience using Jira and Confluence

Bachelor’s Degree or higher
