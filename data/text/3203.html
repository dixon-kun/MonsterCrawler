Title:Advanced Analytics- Data Scientist
Location : Danbury, CT
Duration: 6+mths


About the Role
  • Lead and manage complex consulting statistical projects related to sales, marketing and payer for a large US based pharmaceutical client
  • Delivery management of analytics projects by leading a team of data scientists based in US as well as India
  • Transform client business problems into technical problems, translate business requirements and unstructured business issues into data analytic problems
  • Work with data scientists to identify data needs, sources, and structure to support client issues
  • Track and communicate project risks, proactively broaden and deepen client relationships by managing client relations with senior-level clients
  • Establish and successfully execute multiple engagements in parallel

Requisite Skills
  • Experience in managing statistical analytics account in pharmaceutical domain with hands on experience in SAS or R or any other statistical analysis platform
  • Demonstrated project management skills as well as the ability to efficiently manage multiple projects in parallel
  • Client consulting skills including ability to build relationships with clients, potentially with C-level executives
  • Strong communication skills with the ability to explain technical data analysis results to business people and business processes, challenges and issues to technical people
  • Experience on Data Analytic & Consulting on Life Science domain. Expert level understanding on Pharmaceuticals & Life Sciences area specially Payers, Pharmacy Benefit Management, Sales and Marketing Space. Deep knowledge in statistical concepts and statistical solutions
  • Deep knowledge in statistical concepts and statistical solutions e.g. Channel Afinity Models, MMX, Segmentations, DTC/Non DTC Promotions, ROI, HEOR, Sales Force Effectiveness, Payer Analytics, Real World Evidence etc.
  • Deep Experience with Pharma commercial and non-commercial datasets, business rules and key performance indicator definitions. Worked in multiple therapy areas and also in biological and specialty therapy areas
  • Working with senior stakeholders ideating on their analytics and defining actionable analytics solutions synchronized with their strategies, managing delivery with a cross functional team.
  • Aware about recent developments in pharma space, emerging data and analytics solutions
