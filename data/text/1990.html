Algorithm/Data Fusion Developer

Key Role:

Focus on the application of the latest scientific research and development to client Cyber challenges, work as the research engineer of a technical team, and support the development of advanced algorithms for the situational assessment, classification, information fusion, and automated exploitation of heterogeneous information gathered from the Cyber domain. Serve as a network or software engineer on a data science delivery team leveraging excellent analytic expertise. Work with other team members to develop machine learning, data mining, statistical, and graph

-based algorithms designed to analyze massive data sets. Partner with Cloud technologists to ensure data solutions are transformed and properly implemented. Guide team members in considering client environments and unique challenges during data production to ensure the custom data solution has a smooth transition from concept to prototype to implementation.

Basic Qualifications:

-5+ years of experience with one or more of the following: data mining, informatics, data science, programming, computational algorithms, information retrieval, including organizing and structuring data, statistical analysis, machine learning, including design and implementation, artificial intelligence, software engineering, or systems design and analysis, including Python, R, or SQL

-Experience with MATLAB

-Experience with applying existing and creating new data association methods, confirming known correlations between different data streams, and discovering new unknown correlations between those data streams

-Experience with analyzing both structured and unstructured data streams and enveloping models for situational assessment and data fusion

-Knowledge of object

-oriented programming (OOP) and C++ or Java

-Ability to document the results of research activities and write technical reports, conference papers, and journal articles

-Ability to apply machine learning to client problems by reviewing existing algorithms and assessing their applicability to solutions

-Ability to obtain a security clearance

-BS degree

Additional Qualifications:

-Experience with demonstrating successful technical leadership

-Experience with both structured and unstructured data analysis and processing

-Ability to independently build algorithms, mathematical models, or similar applications

-Ability to rapidly learn new domain requirements and develop practical solutions to address emerging challenges

-Knowledge of radar and IR data and multi hypothesis trackers, data fusion, and graph theory

-MS degree in EE, Computer Engineering, Physics, Mathematics, or a related field

Clearance:

Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information.
