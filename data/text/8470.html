Imagine a company that values your entrepreneurial spirit, gives you the ability to drive your own success, and allows you the freedom to explore creative solutions. Revionics – voted one of the Top Work Places in 2015 by the Austin American-Statesman – is looking to add new talent to our team.  Immerse yourself in a company culture that prizes individuality, self-motivation, and creative thinking.

The Customer Success Manager, Americas partners with our clients and technical teams in North & Latin America

Who You Are:

  • Develop a trusted advisory relationship by demonstrating knowledge of the retail industry, Revionics products and services, and understanding of customer’s short and long-term global objectives.
  • Work closely with Sales and Professional Services to establish deployment objectives, success criteria, and customer success plans.
  • Monitor performance; identify issues or trends that threaten customer success, and work cross-functionally to drive solutions on customers’ behalf.
  • Identify opportunities for footprint growth within the current customer base and coordinate appropriate resources to engage.
  • Proactively work with customers to optimize product utilization and offer guidance on how they can leverage Revionics products and services to drive key performance metrics and support stated goals.
  • Provide management with an assessment of the health of your customer portfolio, identify customer reference candidates for sales and marketing efforts, and serve as the voice of the customer to inform the product and customer-facing business processes.
  • Create periodic customer success reviews providing historical context of the Revionics products they currently have in place, an accurate snapshot of the current program structure and success criteria, and performance measurement based on the criteria in conjunction with the customer’s goals and needs.
  • Identify and assess renewal risks for subscription contracts and facilitate internal communication / collaboration on action plan to minimize risk
  • Proactively communicate with customers on escalated issues providing the strategy for resolution and timely progress updates. Ensure that we are meeting or exceeding the customer needs and escalate the issues internally to drive resolution.

What's in your tool box:

  • Bachelor's Degree in Business or other relevant field or equivalent professional experience
  • Minimum 5 years’ experience working with senior executives in retail or related industries
  • Minimum of 5 years’ experience supporting the Americas region to include Latin America
  • Minimum of 5 year’s experience in a customer success or account development role supporting enterprise software applications
  • Ability to communicate to a variety of levels within a retail environment from user to c-level
  • Experience collaborating and managing by influence with non-reporting groups/individuals
  • Excellent work ethic and leadership
  • Fluent in English and Spanish; conversation and writing
  • Willingness and ability to travel up to 50%

Preferred Candidate Attributes:

  • Experience with price optimization as a user and/or implementation resource or other forecast based retail software package
  • Strong analytical background in regards to key business metrics for the retail space
  • Experience across several retail verticals including grocery, apparel, hard goods and sporting goods

​

Who We Are:

Revionics is a leading profit optimization software company, enabling performance-driven retailers to leverage predictive analytics and demand-based science in building shopper-centric, responsive merchandising strategies that create a sustainable competitive advantage.

Our proven SaaS-based pricing , promotion , markdown and macro space solutions offer the speed, intuition, transparency and scale that retailers need to achieve a responsive merchandising model using predictive analytics in an era when business strategy intersects with data science. Learn why 62,000+ retail sites around the globe optimize with undeniable results with Revionics. Please visit www.revionics.com
