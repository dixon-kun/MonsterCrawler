This product role is part of the Data Platforms team in the Technology organization in ESPN.

        The right candidate will be able to articulate a platform and product vision and be capable of blending the needs from different technical &amp; business groups into a well formed technical product &amp; platform road map. The right candidate must be a self-motivated, team player who can contribute to the overall business objectives of the organization. This person must be able to work closely with other internal and external product and technology experts.
