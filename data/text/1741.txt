        The Sr. Analyst is an integral part of the marketing analytics team that influences business decisions focused on several of Ulta Beauty's strategic brands. The Sr. Analyst will take the lead on delivering data-driven customer insights, including the development and delivery of in-depth brand analyses and presentations to aid in decisions pertaining to customer behavior, profiles and customer-centric marketing strategies.
        PRINCIPAL JOB DUTIES &amp; RESPONSIBILITIES (*Essential Functions)
        1. Develop a strong partnership and collaboration with Ulta Beauty merchants and brand partners, performing as a product team member who consults with the brands on how to attain their business goals by leveraging customer data and insights.
        2. Analyze Ulta Beauty customer behavioral data, demographics, segmentation, and market basket data and transform it into meaningful insights for the benefit of the strategic brands
        3. Utilize existing brand dashboard templates to present analysis and actionable insights to both Ulta Beauty merchant and strategic brand audiences across various levels (up through C-level leadership) within their organizations.
        4. Extract member data, including customer profile info, campaign response, and transaction data, from existing Ulta Beauty systems, using tools such as SAS and/or complex SQL queries.
        5. Continually look for ways to improve analytics processes, deliverables, and brand presentations.
        6. Ensure data integrity by leveraging proven methodologies, including data reconciliation, data integration and data audits.
        Requirements
        * 5+ years of experience developing business strategies resulting from quantitative marketing analytics/data mining and presenting those results to the client/business partner.
        * Experience in retail or CPG industry a plus.
        * BA/BS degree in Business Analytics, Mathematics, Computer Science, Data Science, Business or other degree with analytic experience.
        * Proven command of SAS orSQL, and MS Excel/PowerPoint. Experience with SAP BW and/or other BI tools a plus. Ability to learn quickly and apply new tools &amp; techniques when needed.
        * Excellent interpersonal, verbal, and written communication skills - must be able to communicate complex ideas in both technical and user-friendly language.
        * Excels in building partnerships and providing customer service to the brand partners.
        * Capable of working in a dynamic environment, handling multiple projects, meeting deadlines, prioritizing appropriately and responding to issues quickly and creatively with an open and positive attitude.
        * Excellent analytical, mathematical and creative problem-solving skills.
        * Energetic and self-motivated - must be extremely organized and detail oriented with an ability to make proactive recommendations.
        PREFERRED OR SPECIAL POSITION REQUIREMENTS
        * SQL, MS Excel/PowerPoint.
        * Ability to communicate to a variety of levels and technical/non-technical audiences.
        WORKING CONDITIONS
        * Dynamic, fast-paced office environment and remote stakeholders.
        * Very limited, but occasional travel to brand locations
