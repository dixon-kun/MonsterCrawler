The incumbent will compliment the technology scouting, pre-platform, platform and KRS teams within FEI and work seamlessly with them to raise the level of the output to ensure impact for the FEI/S&amp;T teams across the OC business. The incumbent will be unique individual with a skill set not commonly found within an S&amp;T community and will act as a catalyst in transforming the "way things are done and presented" within OC.
