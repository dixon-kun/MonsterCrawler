        PRIMARY PURPOSE OF POSITION
        This job is responsible for data modeling and automation of existing business process, workflow to streamline system and operation.
        PRIMARY DUTIES AND ACCOUNTABILITIES
        1. Closely work business partners in the Risk Organization to gain a deep understanding of processes and systems to extract knowledge or insights from data in various forms to achieve efficiency of business operations.
        2. Use system or database programming to improve and/or develop automation algorithms for structured and unstructured data processing.
        3. Build self-checking and validation mechanism to ensure accuracy of the work product.
        4. Conduct user tests to reconcile and explain differences.
        POSITION SPECIFICATIONS
        Minimum
        * Minimum 2 years related experience
        * Advanced programming knowledge in SQL, VBA, C#, C ,MATLAB, Python.
        * Ability to ask the right (business) questions.
        * Ability to work in a fast-paced multi-disciplinary environment.
        * Ability to be both highly engaged while working in team and self-motivated when working independently.
        * Possess a strong sense of urgency and high work ethic.
        * Strong written and verbal communication.
        Preferred
        * Master's degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Applied Mathematics, Statistics or related discipline.
        * Experience for business automation and customer service is preferred.
        POSITION SCOPE
        * Reports to the Sr. Manager of Risk Analytics, within the Risk Analytics and International Group, Commercial Risk.
        * Coordinates closely with all Risk functions and the front desk quant group
        * Develop and maintain about 20 Risk models and multiple risk systems
        * Assist in developing risk reporting
        * Analyze and interpret risk metrics, as well as explain changes
