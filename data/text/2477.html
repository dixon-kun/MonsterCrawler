Job Description

We are looking for a Technical Product Manager to help drive the delivery of content systems that publish collection data across Ancestry. These systems take the images and indexes from around the world and make them discoverable on Ancestry. More than 20 billion records have been added to the site over the past 20 years with an average of two million records continuing to be added the site each day.

In this role, you will work with content operations, engineers and computer scientists, and content partners to make it easier for people to discover their family history through Ancestry’s world-class technology. This includes the integration of systems from partners to exchange images and indexes via the cloud with secure APIs and the efficient publishing of these collections on the site. Success in this role means delivering solutions that allow Ancestry to publish meaningful content more rapidly in a way that allows customers to discover meaningful records more quickly.

This position requires a product manager with proven skills that demonstrate technical understanding, experience in working with multiple teams – internally and with partners externally, and an ability to drive and deliver quality solutions across those teams. Strong project management and communication skills are needed.

Key Responsibilities :

  • Author product stories, tasks and acceptance criteria for scrum teams; own the backlog
  • Work with external partners to identify requirements to interact with the content systems and to securely exchange information with those partners – including defining and then delivering APIs with the content systems engineers for those partners.
  • Partner with other product managers and engineers to understand the content systems needs and identify the appropriate deliverables and SLAs for those services in Ancestry.
  • Manage complex projects that require both internal and external deliverables and the ability to communicate status to partners and leadership to understand key metrics and project state.
Qualifications

  • Minimum of 3-5 years of interactive consumer focused, online web product management experience, i.e. gaming, education technology, finance or similar products in the consumer space
  • Bachelor's degree, ideally computer science, mathematics, or similar discipline. Alternatively, equivalent experience in software development is also acceptable
  • Smart, creative, driven and able to deliver results quickly in autonomous environment
  • Self-starter, highly organized, and able to work well with individuals at all levels and across functions
  • Driven to succeed by the company’s core mission to help people discover, preserve and share their family history
  • Strong organizational and product management skills
  • Excellent project management skills that demonstrate the ability to manage complex projects
  • Effective communications skills (written and oral), presentation techniques, meeting mechanics, and team building skills
  • Excellent quantitative and analytical skills
  • Willing and able to travel occasionally
  • Background in and understanding of data science and machine learning with some applied experience
  • Passionate about family history a plus


Additional Information

We’re a cutting-edge tech company with a very human mission—to help every person discover, preserve, and share the story of what led to them. Combining the rich information in family trees and historical records with the genetic details revealed in DNA, we create unique experiences that give people a new understanding of their lives, because connecting all the pieces of our family story can give us the deepest sense of who we are.
