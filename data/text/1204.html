Pharmacy Informaticist - Minneapolis, MN at UnitedHealth Group

Minnetonka, MN

Report this job

About the Job

A career at UnitedHealth Group is anything but ordinary. We push ourselves and each other to find smarter solutions. The result is a culture of performance that is driving the healthcare industry forward. Instead of seeing a handful of patients each day, your work may affect millions for years to come. Join us and start doing your life’s best work.(sm)
You will support pharmacy content in market - leading analytics. You will collaborate with data science and information technology teams to strategize future product solutions. You will contribute to complex analysis. You will support consulting projects that require pharmacy expertise and make fact - based, data - driven decisions. You will be an integral part of a talented and dedicated development team. Are you ready for some new challenges?
Primary Responsibilities:
  • Provide pharmaceutical expertise in the development and implementation of licensed solutions and projects related to health care cost, risk and quality - of - care
  • Work with methodologists and medical directors to facilitate the incorporation of clinical data sources into analytical solutions
  • Consult with internal and external customers or project teams in the use of the drug hierarchy and drug or lab test code sets
  • Develop process or content improvements to currently - supported analytic solutions
  • Provide pharmacy expertise to projects led by data science and machine - based learning teams
  • Oversee and participate in analytic product testing and validation activities
  • Co-author and disseminate research in white papers, presentations, and peer-reviewed publications.
  • Create and apply drug coding hierarchical methods and mapping for decision support solutions

Required Qualification:
  • Hold a PharmD or BS Pharmacy degree from an accredited pharmacy school in the United States
  • Possess an active, unrestricted pharmacist license
  • Have 1+ years’ experience as a practicing pharmacist in the United States
  • Have 1+ years working directly with clinical and / or pharmacy claims data
  • Demonstrated experience using Excel
  • Must live in a commutable distance (50 miles or 50 minutes) of Minneapolis, MN or be willing to relocate without assistance
  • Possess a certification through the Board of Pharmacy Specialties
Preferred Qualification:
  • Experience working within a managed care environment from either a payer or a provider perspective
  • Experience using medical, drug and lab test taxonomies
  • Possess recent knowledge and / or experience in quality measures (e.g., STARS, HEDIS, State Measures)
  • Demonstrated strong organization skills (e.g., prioritize and manage multiple tasks, meet deadlines, complete complex long - term projects)
  • Experience directly collaborating within a multi-disciplinary healthcare team
  • Demonstrated experience using Access or SQL
Careers with Optum. Here's the idea. We built an entire organization around one giant objective; make health care work better for everyone. So when it comes to how we use the world?s large accumulation of health-related information, or guide health and lifestyle choices or manage pharmacy benefits for millions, our first goal is to leap beyond the status quo and uncover new ways to serve. For you, that means working on high performance teams against sophisticated challenges that matter. Optum, incredible ideas in one incredible company and a singular opportunity to do your life's best work.(sm)
