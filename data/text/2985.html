The University of Virginia is transforming the way human resources (HR) services are delivered.  Creating a high‐performing, efficient and integrated HR service model to advance the University's Mission is the focus of Ufirst, a strategic design initiative.

The Data Scientist is a member of the Ufirst Business Services project team who supports the data quality and enhancement. This role is responsible for extracting, transforming, and uploading all data between legacy systems, Informatica and the future Workday system. This role reports to the Business Services Lead, partners with Ufirst HR counterparts in order to meet UVA’s data requirements. This resource will work closely with technical representatives from across the University as well as key resources assigned to the project (Other Modules Analysts, data team, integrations team etc.)

If you have a passion for excellence, drive to enhance the HR experience and want to be a part of creating a legacy at UVA, apply to join the Ufirst team.


  • Support workstream/project planning activities and manage the day-to-day execution of the Data Quality and enhancement
    • Provide technical support throughout the implementation design, architect, test and deploy activities across all modules of Workday
    • Drive progress, report status and escalate risks, issues and required decisions to the Technical Lead
    • Work closely with other business services and technology support team members to manage implementation dependencies
    • Work with system advisors to understand and adapt leading practices to the needs of the University
  • Provide data support throughout the different stages of the project:
    • Selecting features, building and optimizing classifiers using machine learning techniques
    • Data mining using state-of-the-art methods
    • Extending company’s data with third party sources of information, when needed
    • Enhancing data collection procedures to include information that is relevant for building analytic systems
    • Processing, cleansing, and verifying the integrity of data used for analysis
    • Doing ad-hoc analysis and presenting results in a clear manner
    • Creating automated anomaly detection systems and constant tracking of its performance
    • Ability to query databases and perform statistical analysis
    • Developing or programming databases
    • Being able to create examples, prototypes, demonstrations to help management better understand the work
    • Having a good understanding of design and architecture principles
    • Being able to work autonomously
    • Ability to assess data, and then consolidate data into meaningful reports/presentations
    • Ability to develop data entry criteria and standards to be utilize across the University

  • Degree in Information Systems Technology or relevant HR SaaS implementation experience
  • Higher Education or Medical Center HRIS Implementation experience
  • PHR and/or SPHR preferred

Relevant Experience :

  • Working knowledge of software development life cycle activities (i.e., requirements, analysis, design, construction, testing, and deployment)
  • Higher education and/or Medical Center HRIS implementation experience preferred
  • Experience with iterative or agile software development methodologies
  • Experience in transforming business requirements into complex system configurations
  • Experience conducting data conversion and data quality for complex organizations and migrating and transforming data from multiple systems
  • Demonstrated ability to work in a team environment that requires quick turnaround and quality output
  • Strong MS Office skills required in Outlook, Word, Excel, Visio and PowerPoint (Advanced MS Excel Skills/formulas to be used to transform data)
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Being able to work in a fast-paced multidisciplinary environment as in a competitive landscape new data keeps flowing in rapidly and the world is constantly changing

Key Competencies :

  • Leadership: leading, motivating and enabling a diverse team
  • Comfortable working in VUCA (volatility, uncertainty, complexity and ambiguity) environments
  • Consensus and coalition building
  • Entrepreneurial spirit
  • Continuous improvement
  • Problem-solving capabilities
  • Attention to detail with innate need to investigate complex issues and thoroughly test solutions
  • Ability to facilitate, present and negotiate at an advanced level
  • Prioritizing, multi-tasking and responding with a sense of urgency to immediate needs
  • Ability to challenge the status quo and embrace constant change
  • Advanced critical thinking/analytical, logic, and problem solving skills
