Our client, a leader in the Pharmaceutical Industry has an immediate need for an experienced IT Project Manager to join their organization on a long term contract basis in the Chicago, Illinois area.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Candidate would be partnering with MA Data Repositories Director to manage portfolio of projects and operations.
  • Candidate must have a technical /business leader and project management background.
  • Candidate must have at least 10 years of experience managing and deploying systems within an M&D environment.
  • Data Science skills are also desired.
  • Preferred PMP and MBA with an MS in a scientific background.
  • Candidate must have excellent communication and a track-record of effective collaboration.
  • Candidate should have a history of delivering on projects and commitments without managerial oversight to demonstrate self-sufficiency.
  • The candidate will need to be hands-on and also be able to manage and lead projects / operations to support Director, MA Data Repositories.
  • Candidate should be familiar with validation, 21 CFR Part 11.

The candidate will support the Director of Medical Affairs Data Repositories in the following areas:

  • Operations Management - Includes demand management (operations tracking, issues/enhancements management)
  • Project Management
  • Resource management

Experience in the following technologies/knowledge areas:

  • SharePoint - creating/managing sites, forms and overseeing governance
  • IMPACT / CTMS technologies
  • Data Quality management
  • Anaplan
  • Planisware
  • Tableau
  • Informatica Rev
  • R
  • Pentaho
  • DW solutions (from data profiling/modeling to model architecture)
  • MS Office products (Excel in particular, Visio, VBA)
