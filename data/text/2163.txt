        Role and Responsibilities - Data Scientist ( remote base candidates welcome)
        In collaboration with the Analytics department, PARC, and other Xerox or non-Xerox data scientists, develop, enhance, and maintain statistical models that generate new business insights or deliver predictive services to Midas clients.
        * Understand complex computational and statistical techniques used to develop novel approaches for data analyses.
        * Expert with a statistical analysis tool such as R, Octave, MATLAB, KNIME, Julia, Python, and SAS; design machine learning models for data discovery and predictive insights.
        * Collaborate and communicate with software developers in the translation of research model to production code.
        * Translate and effectively communicate complex concepts to a broad audience.
        * Partner with the stakeholders to understand the business drivers, immediate and long-term opportunities, and identify solutions.
        * Collaborate and communicate with data integration teams when data quality issues are discovered.
        * Maintains appropriate knowledge of clinical and financial systems used across the healthcare continuum.
        Qualifications and Education Requirements
        Masters or PhD degree in Statistics or related field.
        Required Skills
        * 5 years demonstrated ability to employ analytic software tools, statistical methodologies, and Business Intelligence to large data sets residing within relational or object oriented databases
        * Advanced SQL skills
        * Strong written and verbal communication skills
        * Strong Programming skills and experienced with object oriented languages
        Preferred Skills
        * Ability to educate and mentor Analytics team members
        * Experience with PMML
        * Experience with NLP
        * Experience with scripting language
        * Experience with large transactional data sets and high volume data
        * Experience with temporal data
        * Experience with prescriptive analysis
        * Concrete experience with the following methods:
        Classification-(Decision Trees, Logistic Regression, Neural Net, SVM, Random Forest)
        Regression-(linear, Nonlinear, Boosted Regression Trees, Hierarchical)
        Clustering-(K-means, Fuzzy C-means, Hierarchical Clustering, Mixture Modeling)
        Time-Series- (AR, ARMA, GARCH)
