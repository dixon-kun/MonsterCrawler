About the Job

Job Description


If you are an experienced Data Scientists Analysts looking for a position with a leading company, Bartech can help! We are a leading staffing firm and our clients include some of the nation’s biggest companies. Our client has a need for a Data Scientists Analysts. This is a contract position; however it is not uncommon for assignments to transition into permanent positions with our client companies. If you have the background we are looking for, and you are interested in an opportunity to get your foot in the door with leading company, we want to talk with you!





Data Scientists Analysts


Job Responsibilities


Your specific duties as a Data Scientists Analysts will include:

  • This role offers a broad spectrum of technical challenges and requires hands on expertise in core Big data technologies.
  • You will support Data operations and Analytics teams leverage Analytic Infrastructure capabilities and /Big data technologies, and work closely with IT teams on engineering/support of tools.
  • Provide Analytic infrastructure / Big data technologies related support to Data operations and Analytic teams
  • Grow online technical knowledge platform, identify best practices and develop guidelines for optimum usage of tools
  • Manage projects to roll out and support tools to GDIA team members








    Data Scientists Analysts





    Job Requirements


    As a Data Scientists Analysts you must represent our company well by being responsible, punctual and motivated to go above and beyond the call of duty. You must also be highly detail-oriented and organized with excellent analytic and problem-solving abilities. It is also important that you display excellent verbal and written communication and interpersonal skills.



  • Bachelors Degree in Computer Science, Engineering or Information Systems
  • 3+ years of hands on experience with Big data and technologies including HDFS, Mapreduce, Pig, Hive, Sqoop, Flume, Oozie and NOSQL
  • SPARK HDFS Mapreduce Pig Hive Sqoop Flume Oozie NOSQL Basic Project Management
  • Strong experience in SPARK with more than two years of hands on experience developing data engineering solutions
  • Experience managing teams/projects; good communication/collaboration skills
  • Preferred
  • Masters degree in Computer Science, Engineering or Information Systems
  • Hands on experience developing Analytic solutions using R and Alteryx
  • R Alteryx
