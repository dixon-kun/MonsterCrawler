Job Description

Are you passionate about conceptualizing, nurturing, developing and proving new ideas and developing them into new solutions? Would you like to help create the next major innovation in and around the payment landscape pioneering and leveraging the most advanced devices, hardware and software in the world, anything and everything in and around world's largest payment ecosystem?

Are you looking to join a start-up or an incubator so that you could convert your ideas into real products? Come join us.

Visa will provide the tools, the environment, the eco-system and the support you need. You will find an informal, innovation friendly environment at 1 Market St in the middle of San Francisco downtown, overlooking some sweeping views of the Bay and Bay Bridge.

Interested? Read on and apply!

Job Description

  • Lead architecture and design discussions with internal and external stakeholders and mentor junior developers

  • Apply data mining and machine learning for inference, modeling, anomaly detection, classification, smoothing, and forecasting

  • Use Map-Reduce frameworks such as Pig, statistical software such as R or Statistica, and scripting languages like Python

  • Write complex SQL queries for data extraction and modeling

  • Co-create solutions with large banking, retail and technology partners which would change the way payments are done

Qualifications

  • Experience with Big-Data Cluster-Computation: Hadoop/Hive/Pig and related technologies

  • Experience with SQL and noSQL based technologies

  • Experience building and consuming REST APIs
  • Familiarity with Statistical analysis and modeling using R, MATLAB, Statistica, and Weka

  • Strong algorithmis, data structures, and computer science fundamentals

  • Thorough understanding and experience using SVM, HMM, k-means, kNN, Artificial Neural Networks (Deep Learning), and statistical smoothing techniques for regression

  • 10 years of software development experience with minimum 3 years in data mining and machine learning

  • Education: Masters's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Machine Learning, Mathematics, or Operations Research


Desired Skills

  • Education: PhD degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Machine Learning, Mathematics, or Operations Research

  • Experience dealing with large amounts of data for internet scale production applications

  • Experience with payments industry


Additional Information

Visa will consider for employment qualified applicants with criminal histories in a manner consistent with the requirements of Article 49 of the San Francisco Police Code.

All your information will be kept confidential according to EEO guidelines.
