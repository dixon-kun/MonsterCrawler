           Position Summary:
           The Product Manager, Digital Conversations will primarily be responsible for developing and managing the life cycle of products and tools including product strategy, requirements gathering, cost/benefits analysis, scoping, development, roll-out, reporting, optimization &amp; continuous innovation. Specific product focus areas may include:
           &#8226; Digital engagement applications
           &#8226; Sales and customer service conversations
           &#8226; AI backed bot models assisting customers, &amp; customer service teams

He/she will keep abreast of evolving industry tools and technologies that enhance the website, inside sales &amp; field sales experience to better serve Staples customers. Given the highly strategic and cross-channel nature of this role, the successful individual must possess a unique combination of structured strategic thinking, passion, and curiosity for technology, as well as strong leadership and collaboration skills.
