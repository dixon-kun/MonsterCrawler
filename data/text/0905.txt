        Job Details
        We are seeking an exceptional Data Visualization Engineer to construct complex, insightful, and inspiring visualizations. These visualizations will tell stories of fraud, risk, consumer preferences, and investment performances as well as inform customers on the benefits of MassMutual's insurance products.
        The candidate should be able to produce visualizations that are optimized to communicate the underlying data and enable users to drive insight. They should be familiar with established data visualization techniques and best practices. Also, the candidate should be creative in their approach to solving problems, with limited information, by using a variety of tools and techniques.
        A quality candidate should be able to design database schemas, query and aggregate data, as well as consume and process XML and JSON API endpoints. Additionally, the candidate should be able to handle building, open source, visualization libraries, written in R, Python, and JavaScript as well as construct web based applications to support data scientists and business analysts. Furthermore, the candidate will need to contribute to the construction of an automated dashboard pipeline.
        Finally, the candidate must have excellent communication skills and have an awareness of visual design, user experience design, and agile development in order to collaborate within a distributed, cross functional team of designers, engineers, and product managers.
        This is an opportunity to join a diverse and talented team of individuals and to work on high profile visualizations and dashboards that will have an impact on how MassMutual presents, consumes, interprets, and acts on visual information.
