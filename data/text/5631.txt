        Responsibilities include, but are not limited to, the following:
        * Plans, develops, and coordinates in-house training programs for all GT employees and internal stakeholders
        (contractors, support staff, business partners).
        * Obtains information needed to prepare in-house training programs; prepares training materials; develops course
        content; determines methodology; and coordinates the development of training aids.
        * Conducts training sessions and develops criteria for evaluating effectiveness of training activities. May utilize trainers
        with technical expertise. Continuously revises lesson plans to meet new training requirements and to keep technical
        information up to date.
        * Coordinates learning and talent development with GT Corporate and other GT Business learning leads
        * Provide guidance and direction on domestic and international messaging for suitability and GT compliance.
        * Identify, develop and complete export related training and communications requirements for program areas
        * Participate in development and implementation of company and BU policy, procedures, guidelines and instructions
