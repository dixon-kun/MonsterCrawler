        CIB\-Product Manager\-VP

        GLAS Client Data Product Manager
        J\.P\. Morgan Treasury Services \(TS\) is one of the world's largest providers of treasury management services\.  J\.P\. Morgan's TS business is a full\-service provider of innovative cash management, trade, Liquidity, commercial card, and escrow services \- specifically developed to meet the challenges treasury professionals face today\.  More than 135,000 corporations, financial institutions, governments and municipalities in over 180 countries and territories entrust their business to J\.P\. Morgan\.
        Objective:  Increasingly J\.P\. Morgan and TS Global Account Services and Liquidity are delivering new and innovative global solutions\.  In order to achieve the full value of these solutions it is critical to provide best in class visibility and management tools to clients that represent the latest technologies and innovations\. To ensure these solutions interact with the client in a consistent and comprehensive manner the GLAS client data visibility is managed centrally across products and regions\.
        This VP level position will be accountable for the design, execution, management and governance of the GLAS client touch\-points\. This includes the existing client online portal redesign and more strategically the next generation of client data visibility and interaction\.The position is part of the global GLAS Data and Client Product Management team and will work closely with the other global and regional product managers, ACCESS channel delivery, technology, operations, PMCS, data science groups and other product teams\.
        Responsibilities Include:
        + Set the business strategy for the GLAS client data interactions based on client needs, competitive, industry and market trends\.
        + Maintain a portal SME role across products and regions as a strong advocate for the client and their experience\.  Ensure all new product developments thoroughly consider client interaction and data needs\.
        + Drive the liquidity portal rewrite efforts including wireframe design, facilitation of client feedback sessions, oversight of technology development, management of pilots, and communication with internal partners on both pilot and large scale migration rollout\.
        + Work across regions to support global products and matrix effectively with partners\.
        + Coordinate, schedule and manage the end to end delivery of client specific developments; concept, market validation, build, pilot, commercialization and launch\.
        + Understand the holistic view of all developments within the Liquidity business to ensure all efforts synergize as a client focused program of work\.
        + Embrace an end\-to\-end focus on controls as a part of business strategy and appropriately raise any risk/control issues\.  Understand regulatory changes and execute changes as required to comply\.
        + Support 'Ready to Launch' plan to include communications for locally and globally lead initiatives\.
        + Collaborate on a marketing plan to showcase client data initiatives for internal and external awareness\.
        **Qualifications**
        Skills and Qualifications:
        + 5  years in product development, roadmap development and the Agile delivery process, preferably with experience across multiple geographies and matrix organizations in the financial services segment\.
        + Working understanding of client needs as they relate to treasury functions, including cash and investments is preferred\.
        + Background in user experience design, data visualization and client facing applications\.
        + Ability to analyze the market and develop programs to effectively position products internally and externally\.
        + Proficiency in project management, including project planning and budget management\.
        + Strong financial, analytical and strategic skill set\.
        + Be equally comfortable presenting business value to a client as you are an enhancement to a technology team\.
        + Strong verbal and written communication skills with ability to influence across global team and a variety of partners\.
        + Demonstrated self\-motivation and self\-discipline to work effectively in a high\-paced environment\.
        + Undergraduate or graduate degree preferred\.
