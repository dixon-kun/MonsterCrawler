Description
Advance your career at Liberty Mutual Insurance - A Fortune 100 Company!
Are you looking for a position where you work in a start-up like role within a Fortune 100 company and can directly influence and impact the direction of an entire industry? Our Product Technology Solutions – Connected Partnerships group within US Consumer Markets Product Management is seeking a Senior Analyst of Product Research to join their growing and highly visible group that is preparing for both the digital and connectivity disruption that is coming to the insurance industry.
This Connected Partnerships team is seeking an individual with strong traditional and digital analytics ability to help drive and optimize our telematics app development and our connected vehicle programs. This role will help us achieve our mission of providing superior capabilities, capturing new customers, acquiring new and unique Internet of Things data sets through new connected insurance channels, powering a best in class digital customer experience and make a positive difference in our organization. These actions will help provide Liberty Mutual with a sustainable competitive advantage in the next phase of connected insurance.
As a Senior Product Analyst, you will be an integral part of our team that is tasked with solving hard problems and creating great connected partnership products. You will be heavily involved with an emerging field of telematics insurance - Point of Sale Telematics – including programs such as:
  • Mobile telematics apps: Liberty Mutual's Try Before You Buy app called HighwayHero
  • In vehicle connected insurance programs with auto manufacturers: General Motors and Subaru
The role requires close collaboration with insurance product, digital product, design, and engineering teams and use data to uncover valuable insights, deliver reports, and recommendations. You will define and report on key metrics and analyze user behavior and trends. Your insights will be essential to prioritizing product features and influencing the overall product and CX/UX roadmap.
You …
  • Are highly analytical, know BI tools like SAS, Adobe Analytics, Google Analytics, Mixpanel, understand SQL, are familiar with big data systems like Hadoop and enjoy telling data-driven stories.
  • You enjoy emerging tech, know that Java is a coding language, not free coffee, and the “cloud” is not a strange concept to you. TechCrunch and CNET.com are daily reads.
  • Are comfortable interacting with Fortune 100 partners, startups, technology vendors and service providers.
  • Understand the impact of highly satisfied customers and work to turn every one into a net promoter of the company. You're willing to roll up your sleeves to identify issues and find solutions to customer issues early, and do it with passion.


Job Responsibilities

  • Conduct in-depth analyses of digital telematics sales funnel performance and app statistics across a variety of strategic online and mobile projects, and present recommendations.
  • Analyze the efficiency of customer actions and the results of optimization and testing efforts. Identify opportunities to improve customer experience and drive business results.
  • Empower product teams with data and insights to enable timely decisions and answer high priority user and business questions. Leverage insights to build business cases for additional features.
  • Conducts market sizing exercises for new auto, mobile, and telecom partners to inform long term feasibility of programs and ensure appropriate strategic direction.
  • Create and maintain insightful automated dashboards and data visualizations to track key business metrics and help deepen our understanding of user behavior.
  • Be the analytics owner for A/B testing experiments, to test new product ideas and convert the results into actionable recommendations.

If you possess exceptional teamwork and the communication skills necessary to collaborate with team members in product, marketing, sales, claims and customer experience we would love to speak with you.
The qualified individual can work in Boston or Seattle.
Qualifications:
  • Bachelors or Master’s degree (with an analytical discipline like Data Science, Economics, Mathematics, Statistics, Marketing Analytics or Business Administration).
  • 3-7+ years of relevant and progressively more responsible experience within a financial services, customer and technology focused organization.
  • Strong analytical, collaboration, organization, and presentation skills
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and work on multiple priorities required.
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills are required, including PowerPoint.
  • Ability to utilize statistical software packages (e.g., SAS).
