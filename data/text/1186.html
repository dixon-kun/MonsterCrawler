Data Services Manager from CTG

Eden Prairie, MN 55344

Report this job

About the Job

CTG is currently reviewing resumes for a Data Services Manager for a full-time a permanent position with our client located in Eden Prairie, MN.  You will lead a team of talented and dedicated Data Engineers focusing on traditional SQL, NoSQL, and Big Data infrastructure.  The team partners closely with their application development, data science, and big data teams to cohesively design and deliver modern data architectures.  If you are the ideal candidate, you will have experience designing and supporting large-scale, highly available, and distributed database systems.  Relocation assistance is available as well as a competitive salary and comprehensive benefit package.

Our client is one of the world’s top transportation companies, working with nearly every Fortune 500 company in America and most of Minnesota’s best-known brands.  They are building brand new, data-intensive software products that are focused on delivery sophisticated tools for their business, customers and carriers. Big data, machine learning, predictive analytics and cognitive computing are areas we are heavily investing in. They are well on our way to an established data science practice; they now need to formalize their delivery of these capabilities into their products.

Location : Eden Prairie, MN
Position Type : Direct hire

Job Responsibilities

  • Provide technical leadership to the Data Services team
  • Partner with internal customers to understand, provide input, and support their data services needs
  • Help guide and evaluate data services technology and tool choices for the enterprise
  • Stay ahead of technology demand through industry awareness, technology experiments, and research and development activities
  • Partner with enterprise architecture, data science, and big data communities to create and communicate strategic roadmaps and engineering designs
  • Contribute architectural inputs and participate in infrastructure design to meet the changing requirements around scaling, reliability, performance, and management
  • Take ownership and accountability for the successful completion of data services projects
  • Ensure the data services platforms and architectures are maintained and enhanced to meet the evolving demands of the business and IT
  • Rapidly respond, resolve, drive to root cause, and implement preventative measures as incidents occur
  • Engage with employees to understand their career interest and help drive their performance
  • Maintain relationships and effectively interface with IT leaders, direct reports, business and technology customers, and vendors

Required Qualifications Are:

  • Strong passion, interest, and experience with big data, machine learning, and building out modern infrastructure environments
  • Experience managing large scale structured data base environments (MS SQL, Oracle)
  • Technically competent, hands-on capable, with the ability to quickly learn and understand enterprise data architectures
  • Infrastructure support and operations experience with conscientious customer service
  • Proven ability to mentor and develop talent and drive for results
  • Desire to collaborate and work in a team environment
  • Commitment to continuous learning and open source systems
  • Strong analytical and interpersonal skills with good written and verbal communication

Preferred Qualifications Include:

  • Microsoft SQL experience
  • Search service experience (e.g. Solr, Elastic Search, Hive, HBase)
  • Experience operating Hadoop clusters, preferably in scaling clusters
  • Distributed computing with ETL experience
  • Working knowledge of Linux operating systems
  • Lambda architecture + restful service deployment frameworks

Preferred Education:

  • BS or MS in Computer Science/Engineering or equivalent experience.

Our client offers a competitive compensation package and excellent benefits including medical, dental, and vision insurance, prescription drug coverage, paid holidays and vacation, disability insurance, life insurance, 401K with company match, profit sharing, Employee Stock Purchase Plan, and the opportunity to prosper in a growing Fortune 500 company.
