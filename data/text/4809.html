Summary:

GEI Consultants, Inc. (GEI) is seeking an entry level Geotechnical Engineer with a background in civil and geotechnical engineering design. Under supervision of senior staff, this individual will provide support to ongoing field and engineering design efforts. Field activities will include coordination and documentation of geotechnical and environmental soil borings; installation of wells and geotechnical instrumentation; collection of groundwater, sediment, and soil samples; and oversight and documentation during construction. Projects include those related to dams and hydropower facilities, solid waste landfills, shallow and deep foundations and earth retention systems.

Essential Responsibilities & Duties:

•Support the development of decision making documents such as Feasibility Studies and cost estimates, design and procurement documents such as engineering drawings and specification packages, and tools for contractor bid evaluation and oversight.
•Support site investigations and site assessments including soil borings and testing, environmental sampling, and technical oversight of subcontractors.
•Collect geotechnical/environmental data at sites.
•Support data management and geotechnical analysis, technical report development, and assist in preparation of technical presentations and proposals to clients and regulators.
•This position has a field component, so travel is required. Candidates should be prepared to work in inclement conditions.

Minimum Qualifications:

•B.S. in Civil, Geotechnical or Geological Engineering; M.S. is a plus.
•E.I.T. is a plus
•1-4 years of previous professional experience in civil/environmental/geotechnical engineering and consulting; knowledge and familiarity with engineering design processes.
•Excellent technical report writing and communication skills.
•Experience with spatial analysis and visualization software such as CAD, Civil 3D, and GIS.
•Experience with geotechnical analysis software, such as GeoStudio (SEEP/W, SLOPE/W or SIGMA/W). Experience with advanced finite element or finite difference, nonlinear 2D or 3D models such as FLAC or Plaxis is a plus.
•Willingness and ability to travel as needed.
•Willingness and ability to perform field tasks and oversight
•40 hr. HAZWOPER is a plus
•Valid U.S. driver’s license.
