        Basic Qualifications
        Bachelor&#8217;s degree from an accredited college in a related discipline or equivalent experience/combined education, with 4 years of professional experience.
        Clearance requirement: Active Top Secret Level Clearance with SCI Eligibility, Access and Investigation must appear in JPAS and be within scope (5 &#8211; Years).
        Conceive and prepare thorough, well articulated tactical or strategic data science products (e.g. technical articles, visualizations, graphics, intelligence reports) that summarize the methods and results of data science solutions. Design a range of research projects, data collection and methodologies. Identify, retrieve, manipulate, relate and/or exploit multiple structured data sets from various sources. Identify problems to which data science can be applied and initiate appropriate solutions. Identify and appropriately evaluate a wide range of existing methods, models and algorithms in familiar domains for a variety of mission driven problems; recognizing the capabilities and limitations of methods. Demonstrated good oral and written communication skills.
        Job Description
        The Data Scientist Journeyman will analyze actual and predictable interacting operational activities of given target sets and client requests to obtain an outcome through the application of logic and scientific or economic disciplines and techniques.  Uses modeling and measuring techniques; utilizes mathematics, statistical methods, engineering methods, operational mathematics techniques (linear programming, game theory, probability theory, symbolic language, etc.), and other principles and laws of scientific and economic disciplines.  Use standardized methods, models and algorithms to address intelligence problems of limited scale as directed.  Provide informal documentation for methods and algorithms used in data science solutions.  Must be able to communicate the produced results effectively to the customer.
