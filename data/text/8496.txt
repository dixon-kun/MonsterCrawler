        Senior Recruiter
        Job Description
        Position Summary
        As a Senior Recruiter, you will be responsible for delivering all aspects of the company's recruiting success through the development of hiring plans and both the implementation of traditional sourcing strategies as well as new and creative recruiting ideas. Ultimately, we're looking for that special someone who will play a crucial role in helping to ensure we continue to hire rockstars with the best possible skillset and DNA match to the PROS culture.
        What you'll be doing
        * Develop and execute strategic recruitment plans in line with hiring schedule and overall growth
        * Work with hiring managers to properly understand personnel needs and workforce planning strategies, solidify the objectives of open positions, and develop recruiting plans
        * Help create job descriptions, role profiles, and compensation packages based on the position and company requirements and shape and manage the overall application and interview process
        * Conduct prescreening interviews of candidates to ensure position requirements are met and develop a pool of qualified and diverse candidates in advance for all roles as required
        * Conduct regular follow-up with hiring managers to ensure the effectiveness of recruiting plans and activities
        * Research and recommend the latest and greatest sources for active and passive candidate recruiting
        * Leverage and build networks to find and connect with passive candidates, communicate our vision and why we believe they are critical to its success
        * Network through industry contacts, association memberships, peers, employees, etc. in search of new talent
        * Assist in establishing PROS as a recognizable employer of choice, both internally and externally.
        * Communicate with managers and employees regularly to ensure good rapport, gauge morale, and help source new candidate leads
        * Manage relationships with third party recruiters as required
        * Coordinate with Recruiting Coordinator on all pertinent recruiting documentation including applicant and interview data, offer letters, and new employee orientation packages
        * Assist cross functionally with HR administration as required
        What we're looking for
        * 5+ years experience in full lifecycle recruiting. Software sector or technology sector preferred
        * BS / BA in Human Resources, Communications, Marketing, or related discipline preferred
        * Expert level of knowledge in all full lifecycle recruiting components including sourcing, qualifying, networking, assessing, legal, job analysis, salary trends, relationship management, and due diligence
        * Must be able to manage competing demands, accept and work with constructive feedback, while being extremely adaptable and flexible
        * Excellent project management, writing, and oral communication skills are required
        * Fun personality but with a demonstrated ability to command the attention and respect of senior level executives
        * Demonstrated ability to recruit for a broad/deep range of positions
        * Entrepreneurial spirit, must be a self-starter with solid judgment abilities and skills.
