        At Monsanto, we're focused on finding innovative solutions for the issues we face in agriculture. In our IT Products &amp; Engineering group, we leverage cutting edge technology, data, analytics and software to transform agriculture with digital products that accelerate the development &amp; delivery of revolutionary agricultural solutions and enable rich engagement with our farmer customers. You'll find a team-focused environment that thrives on innovation and encourages you to do extraordinary things. This is where your ideas could push the limits of agricultural technology and maybe even change the world. We're working toward producing more, conserving more, and improving lives.
        Monsanto is seeking a Product Manager to design innovative new digital products that enable our mission to unlock new yield potential on the farm. These product offerings are diverse and span from mobile &amp; web apps, to data &amp; analytics services, to smart devices using Internet of Things technologies.
        Product Managers work closely with software engineers to guide products from conception to launch. The role is expected to bridge the technical and business worlds as products are designed that enable our key business objectives through collaboration with business strategy, data science and product delivery teams.
        Responsibilities include:
        * Set product capability priorities, ranging from functional, to technical, to user experience.
        * Define a product vision and strategy.
        * Develop strategies and plans for product delivery.
        * Collaborate with agile development teams for sprint planning &amp; product design.
        * Collaborate with UX designers to create user experiences that are useful, usable and desirable.
        * Collaborate with world-class engineers to deliver state-of-the-art digital capability.
        * Interact with business users to understand needs and business leads to understand strategy.
