ReliaSource is seeking a Systems Architects who will be involved in the development and implementation of a client's system with capabilities that include extensive data management services on a shared cloud computing environment. The project will support the current and future implementation of software development and analytic business process transformations where system architecture includes data ingestion, transformation, and summarizations via data science methods to provide the substantive information to underlie the development of the system.
