        PROS: The revenue and profit realization company. We deliver the perfect blend of simplicity and data science to help customers generate meaningful revenue growth, expand and sustain profitability and modernize business processes. Realize your Potential with PROS.
        The DevOps Architect works directly on solutions to improve product deployments and operations in the PROS cloud. They are responsible for delivery of tooling, best practices and automation for product delivery. The DevOps Architect is a CI/CD expert, spanning the implementation and operation best practices for a product. They work within a community of Solution Architects, Architects and engineering teams to define and contribute to technology best practices.
        DevOps Architects may work collaboratively with other Architects to help with developing strategic initiatives across many PROS organizations as subject matter experts on software delivery.
        Primary Responsibilities:
        * Demonstrate understanding of cross-functional/cross-domain requirements and impact
        * Maintain knowledge of current and emerging technologies/products/trends related to delivery
        * Ensure alignment of solutions with business and IT goals/objectives
        * Facilitate/lead/participate in identification of solution options and features
        * Communicate solution options to business owners/stakeholders (e.g. architecture reviews)
        * Incorporate software and system architecture (e.g. infrastructure, network) into conceptual designs
        * Ensure alignment with enterprise technology standards (e.g. solution patterns, application frameworks, technology roadmaps, capital plans)
        * Maintain awareness of current delivery technology assets and the capability of each
        * Deploy solutions and assist the team as an SME for the delivery tools
        * Participate in PLM efforts for the products supported
        * Participate in an active AgileDevOps team and help lead our DevOps movement (e.g. on call rotation, support, center of practice, standups, postmortems)
        Required Qualifications:
        * Agile DevOps experience
        * 8+ years of experience in Solution Delivery and architecture
        * 7+ years experience with Windows and Linux Operating Solutions
        * 2+ years of full-lifecycle application support experience (solution, POC, deployment,
        * Deep understanding of industry patterns for application architecture, integration and configuration management
        Preferred Qualifications:
        * Git (contributions on Github)

        * Scripted environments experience
        * Experience with CHEF, Puppet and ARM templates
