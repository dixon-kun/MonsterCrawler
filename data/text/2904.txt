        Altamira has an upcoming need for a Collection Requirements Manager (CRM) . The CRM's role:
        * Provides collection requirements management support to CITP analysts at the NGIC.
        * Coordinates with the NGIC collection requirements managers to synchronize CITP requirements with the NGIC.
        * Use the Combined Information Data Network Exchange (CIDNE) Human Domain Workstation (HDWS), Community On-line Intelligence System for End Users and Managers (COLISEUM), HUMINT On-Line Tasking and Reporting (HOTR) and other automated requirements management systems to process incoming RFIs, submit RFIs to external agencies and monitor RFI status.
        * Assist NGIC-based analysts in completing evaluation reports, Source Directed Requirements (SDR), and other collection requirements and as necessary, the CRM will assist deployed analysts in processing collection requirements
        * Maintains liaison with Afghanistan theater detainee operations to ensure CITP receives Knowledgeability Briefs (KB) and has the opportunity to develop and submit SDRs.
        Role Requirements:
        * This position requires individuals to be government trained intelligence collection managers with a minimum of 5 years of direct experience as a collection manager.
        * Experienced with data processing systems associated with collection management, which include CIDNE, HOTR, COLISEUM and other SIPRNET and JWICS-based systems.
        * Be proficient at using basic computer applications and intelligence related automation to support analytical efforts and product development; possess strong writing and editing skills; be capable of working with analysts to gather collection requirements; and able to coordinate collection requirements both within the NGIC and with outside IC agencies.
        * Knowledgeable of the Intelligence Cycle, Collection Management Cycle, Targeting Cycle and intelligence analytic techniques including trend and pattern analysis, geospatial analysis, link analysis and network or social network analysis.
        * This position requires a Bachelor's Degree (may substitute 4 years of experience for education requirement).
        * This position requires current TS/SCI clearance.
