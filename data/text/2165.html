The Integrated Marketing Solutions (IMS) Global Acquisitions Analytics and Data Science team is responsible for globalizing and scaling acquisitions functions that have global synergies and identifying and prioritizing investment in high impact areas. The analyst will be a part of the Acquisitions Capabilities and Data Management Team and will report to a Manager within the team.

The Analyst will support the development and execution of strategic projects supporting the globalization of acquisitions capabilities across US and International markets.

Key responsibilities include:

* Develop business solutions for key acquisitions capabilities, including personalization of acquisitions efforts across the enterprise

* Develop a comprehensive understanding of end-to-end acquisitions processes, capabilities, systems and stakeholders in order to identify and close existing gaps

* Ensure execution of the personalization road map, including development of project plans to achieve the desired results within the defined scope

* Analyze and recommend requirements for the acquisitions capabilities strategy that identify and address process, policy and system gaps

* Collaborate with risk, technologies and marketing partners, leveraging SME's to inform solutions that integrate and globalize capabilities as appropriate

The successful applicant will need to demonstrate an ability to take ownership of the responsibilities of the role, the capacity to lead complex projects and the relationship management skills needed to build and cultivate long-term relationships with diverse stakeholders.

Qualifications

* Strong skills and experience managing complex projects with a wide array of stakeholders

* Relationship management skills with the ability to strengthen business partnerships across business units and functional areas

* Ability to think strategically and openly share innovative insights and ideas

* Excellent written and oral communication skills with proven success influencing business decisions and driving successful results

* Strong track record of delivering results against established goals and road maps

* Ability to multi-task, operate effectively under changing conditions and rigorously prioritize initiatives based on business objectives

* Technical background is highly preferred

* Experience with American Express digital marketing processes and systems is a plus

Depending on factors such as business unit requirements, the nature of the position, cost and applicable laws, American Express may provide visa sponsorship for certain positions.
