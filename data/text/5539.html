Strategic Marketing Analyst, Biofuel – North America, NC

We are looking for an innovative and highly motivated team player to join us as a Strategic Marketing Analyst in our Biofuel industry. In this role, you will be responsible for driving analysis for key projects and supporting strategy development for new growth opportunities within Biofuels. You will be part of a small team working closely together on the strategic processes and analysis. In collaboration with Global Marketing and Business Development, you will investigate industry trends and support development of actionable plans to adapt to changing market dynamics.

In this position you need to:

* Have a relevant undergraduate degree which could be in Data Science/Analytics, Business Administration, Finance, Economics, Statistics, Engineering or science related. Advanced degree/certification in Data Science/Analytics is a plus.

* Have a minimum of 2 years business/financial analysis, strategy and/or marketing (preferably B2B) experience.

* Have a strong skill set with analysis tools for modeling and financial forecasting and planning, with excellent proficiency in excel. Competency in Tableau visual analytics program is a plus.

* Have an excellent analytical skillset with ability to translate conclusions into clear business targets.

* Have strong problem-solving skills and curiosity for exploring new topics in detail.

About Novozymes

Novozymes is the world leader in biological solutions. Together with customers, partners and the global community, we improve industrial performance while preserving the planet's resources and helping build better lives. As the world's largest provider of enzyme and microbial technologies, our bio innovation enables higher agricultural yields, low-temperature washing, energy-efficient production, renewable fuel and many other benefits that we rely on today and in the future.
