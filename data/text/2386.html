Process Control Engineer (Mid-Level) from CTG

Ecorse, MI 48229

Report this job

About the Job

CTG has two immediate openings for mid-level Process Control Engineers to work on site at our client’s Great Lakes Works operation in Ecorse, MI.  This is a six month contract-to-hire opportunity!

Our client is an integrated steel producer with major production operations in North America and Central Europe and an annual raw steelmaking capability of 22 million net tons. The company manufactures a wide range of value-added steel sheet and tubular products for the automotive, appliance, container, industrial machinery, construction, and oil and gas industries.

JOB DESCRIPTION:

The successful candidate will join our client’s Process Control and Information Technology organization and become an integral part of a highly skilled, innovative team.  You will play a significant role in providing critical services to our client’s production operations and be responsible for the design, development, testing and support of process control automation systems.

RESPONSIBILITIES:
  • Support the critical steel making operations by ensuring that process control systems are highly available and error resistant, resulting in zero delay minutes in Steel Rolling and Finishing operations
  • Identify, participate in and/or lead high impact process control projects that result in improvements to safety, quality, production yield and/or financial benefits
  • Collaborate closely with Corporate Research & Technology, Engineering and Plant Operations leadership to define process control strategy and develop solutions
  • Partner with plant operations and global peer leaders to develop, support and champion the application of process and process control best practices
  • Work closely with operations teams to understand requirements and identify improvement opportunities; translate into project or minor enhancement specifications, complete with work estimates and forecasted benefits
  • Develop and maintain mathematical models, reporting, screen design and web page development
  • Support production servers, applications, SCADA and server network communications

REQUIRED SKILLS:
  • Three (3)+  years experience in operating systems, software design, programming concepts, Microsoft Office, and C, Java OR C#

PREFERRED (NICE TO HAVE) SKILLS:
  • Experience with any of the following is a huge plus!
  • Process control systems with a mix of Windows, Linux and OpenVMS operating systems; Oracle and MS SQL Server databases and OSI Soft’s PI data historian
  • HMIs, graphical user interfaces
  • Knowledge and solid understanding of SCADA systems
  • Computer hardware, electronic peripherals and networks
  • Microsoft Office, IBM Cognos skills
  • Predictive Analytics
  • Process controls (PLC’s)
  • Modeling
  • Smart Mfg. (SW Thingsworks)
  • Data Science
