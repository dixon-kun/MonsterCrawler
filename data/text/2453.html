Job Description:

3M is seeking a Data Acquisition Manager for the Health Information Systems Division located in Murray, UT or Remote.

Primary Responsibilities include but are not limited to the following:

Customer Data Acquisition Sales Specialist -

  • Works closely with the Data Informatics team and the sales organization to communicate what data is required for a successful sale and implementation of the product
  • Ability to speak with all levels of an organization including CIO, CMIO and IT

Internal Data Informatics Data Specialist –

  • Work closely with the internal technical, clinical research and data science team to ensure all data requirements are known, documented and communicated throughout the organization
  • Work with the sales and business development organizations to ensure all requirements are clear and known

3rd Party Data Acquisition –

  • Work with 3M Strategic Business Development team to identify, negotiate and purchase required data as identified

Define Technical Options –

  • Expert in defining the best approach to secure, transfer and interface the data to the correct platform
  • Alignment within the organization leveraging existing methods for securing data and also working with 3rd party companies to ensure we have interface capabilities with each client vendor

Documenting Requirements -

  • Work with our technical and documentation teams to ensure all sales, marketing, specifications, client care documentation is accurate and available with all current information

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree or higher in Computer Information Systems, IT or Technical from an accredited university
  • Minimum of three (3) years of experience working in a hospital or payer organization in IT with an emphasis in interfaces
  • Minimum of two (2) years of experience with at least three (3) healthcare systems vendors (EMR, billing, department, analytics)
  • Minimum of two (2) years of combined experience working with database architecture and/or data management

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master's degree in Healthcare IT or IT from an accredited university
  • Key characteristics include strategic thinker, creative, assertive, collaborative, action-oriented, clear writing and messaging ability, fast learner, strong business acumen, outstanding influencing skills and excellent presentation skills
  • Broad knowledge of healthcare, revenue cycle, Health Information Management and/or healthcare IT industry and trends
  • Demonstrated ability to communicate data and IT specifications across a broad set of platforms
  • Passionate, team player that energizes and leads others
  • High integrity and work ethic are expected and highly valued
  • Experience as a Project lead dealing with payers and providers
  • Ability to negotiate and influence people

Location: Murray, Utah
