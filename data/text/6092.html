Sr DevOps Engineer-49999

Description

The Senior Development Operations Engineer will work collaboratively with the Continuous Integration and Development teams to boost the overall organizational performance. You will be looked to for advancing our overall DevOps technical strategy.
You will help streamline and automate our operations and processes through build and test automation. Partner to build and maintain tools for deployment, systems monitoring and operational support; troubleshoot/resolve issues in our automated development, test and production environments.
You will provide support in all areas of Development Operations and Software Configuration Management including configuration item identification, application build management, develop scripts for automation, develop and maintain branching strategies, provide configuration status accounting, and ensure configuration management procedures are followed.
You will be looked to for researching, recommending and developing innovative and progressive development operations solutions and providing justification for your proposed solutions. You will be expected to mentor other team members.
You must be team oriented and possess a continuous improvement mindset that contributes to the overall success of our development operations vision.
RESPONSIBILITIES
  • Develop scripts and applications to automate system deployment, monitoring, scaling and infrastructure
  • Build or enhance tools to improve development team productivity
  • Build or enhance governance tools to ensure clean change management of code base and infrastructure
  • Maintain and evolve the continuous Integration environment
  • Interact with Development, QA and Operations teams
  • Provide production support for applications and infrastructure.
  • Help drive strategic improvements in systems, processes, and practices
  • Work with various teams to define and drive our DevOps and monitoring strategy
  • Stay current on advancements in infrastructure landscape and influence adoption across enterprise
  • Support existing IT staff in growth of their DevOps skills and build a culture of automation

Qualifications

QUALIFICATIONS
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, or related discipline, 5 to 10 years of related experience, or equivalent combination of education and experience.
  • A minimum of 5 years of DevOps experience.
  • Proven success in managing architectures that satisfy security, scalability, high availability, and network management/monitoring requirements.
  • Strong understanding of current technology approaches, techniques and tools used for emerging trends, including data science and analytics, SOA, and cloud “-as-a-service” offerings.
  • Must demonstrate proficiency in architecting large scale solutions using infrastructure automation techniques on “on premise” and hybrid cloud services: Microsoft Azure, AWS, etc.
  • Must demonstrate proficiency in monitoring strategies and tools.
  • Experience with TFS Release Management, Octopus Deploy is required.
  • Strong scripting skills with one or more of these is preferred: PowerShell, PowerShell DSC, Perl, Python.
  • Experience with TFS migrations is preferred.
  • Experience with other continuous integration/deployment automation tools and various container technologies is preferred.
  • Experience with relational databases and the design of highly available, distributed database applications is preferred.
  • Experience with Agile/Scrum development methodologies is preferred.
  • Experience with automated testing tools is preferred.
  • Outstanding oral, written, presentation and listening communication skills are required.
  • Expertise in a broad set of technologies and tools: C#, .NET, Java EE, EJB, MSMQ, JMS, IIS, Python, Octopus, Jenkins, Puppet, Chef, GO, Docker, etc will be a plus.
  • Up to 30% of domestic travel may be required.
PROFESSIONAL SKILLS
  • Ability to think and operate as a team player
  • Proven track record of project delivery results.
  • Extremely strong interpersonal and communication skills (written and verbal) with the ability to assess and clearly communicate risks and dependencies.
  • Ability to act as a consultant and provide recommendations to all project teams.
  • Ability to operate as independently or as part of collaborative effort
  • Demonstrate strong project leadership and ability to develop strong working relationships.
  • The candidate must be able to learn quickly, work in a fast-paced, team-driven environment and have the ability to communicate well with technical and non-technical personnel.
  • Willing to work after hours and weekends (as needed) in order to oversee deployment, troubleshoot if needed.
  • Must be able to multi-task, manage details and keep sight of overall objectives.
