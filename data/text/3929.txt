        RESEARCH HEALTH SCIENCE SPECIALIST
        Palo Alto, CA
        Duties:
        This position serves as a Research Health Science Specialist in the Program Evaluation Resource Center (PERC) within the VA Palo Alto Health Care System in Palo Alto, CA. Duties include, but may not be limited to the following:
        * Develops or contributes to data products (dashboards, databases, data tables, and files) which can be shared and accessed by a variety of customers.
        * Creates and maintains systems and databases designed to ensure that projects comply with relevant policies.
        * Completes projects independently that are both methodologically sound and meet requested timeframes.
        * Plans the acquisition of data resources to meet project needs, for example, by interacting with database administrators for different data systems.
        * Uses big data to understand areas including mental health and substance use disorder treatment, health system functioning, and staffing.
        * Performs Extract, Transform, Load (ETL) activities with a deep understanding of relational databases.
        * Trains data analysts on best practices for data analysis, database management, and data security and privacy issues.
        * Mentors and provides technical and scientific supervision to a wide range of personnel to advance their work with big data.
        * Documents existing processes and events that occur during query design and development to ensure operational continuity.
        * Generates clear and thorough responses to ad hoc queries to ensure stakeholders receive results in their requested timeframe.
