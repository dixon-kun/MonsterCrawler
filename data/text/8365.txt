        The Compliance Testing Strategic Data Solutions ("SDS") team is at the forefront of addressing global regulatory and compliance risks across Citi's Institutional Clients Group ("ICG") Compliance Testing program. The team focuses on providing forensic data mining and system and data assurance capabilities through the development of analytics, data visualizations, automating test scripts and continuous monitoring functions using an Integrated Operating Model. SDS performs a balance of recurring and ad-hoc testing to manage both the defined regulatory risks along with emerging risks within the industry and across each business sector. SDS partners closely with Global ICG Compliance Testing and Compliance Advisory to investigate findings and target areas for testing.
        The Senior Compliance SDS Officer will be responsible for defining and delivering the SDS ICG analytic program through planning, scoping, designing and delivering data analytics that support the Global ICG testing of securities laws and regulations as they relate to Citi's policies. As part of Compliance Testing, the candidate will liaise with business teams, compliance officers, and technology in order to apply various regulations, policies and processes across the supporting data infrastructure landscape. The role will require someone who is comfortable taking initiative and operating in a results-driven and fast-paced team environment.
        The ideal candidate will have deep business and product specific knowledge in the financial services industry as well as a strong technical background. The candidate should demonstrate strong verbal and written communication skills and be comfortable leading others. Experience managing and leading small teams is a must. The candidate will also need to perform quality control over the work of others, and be capable of communicating complex technical or financial concepts to stakeholders. Experience documenting for litigation, audit, or regulatory exams is a plus.
        Key responsibilities include:
        * Understand regulatory risk and how it applies to each business or product area.
        * Define the analytic targets that align with the SDS program objectives
        * Coordinate with business, compliance officers and technology from the identification of the testing target through delivery of the analytic.
        * Translate the testing requirements into models/analytics using various data analytic and visualization tools.
        * Translate the results from the analytics into impact on regulatory risks for senior management.
        * Effectively manage program delivery risks such as resource management, knowledge management, data access and quality control
