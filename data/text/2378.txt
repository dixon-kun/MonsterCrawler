        Job Description
        Data Analytics Scientist
        Position Overview/Description:
        Ford Motor Company's Global Data Insight &amp; Analytics (GDIA) organization is looking for an analytics scientist that has a deep understanding of data analysis and modeling. You will have the opportunity to work with some of the brightest global subject matter experts that are transforming the automobile industry as a member of the Advanced Operational Analytics team. In this role, you will lead initiatives to leverage unique data streams using predictive analytics and machine learning to quantify the customer value of vehicle technologies. You will also support the development of optimization models that provide decision support to other Ford organizations, such as Product Development, Strategy, and Sustainability.
        Responsibilities
        You will be responsible for providing the data analytics support for departmental tasks, including assembly of data for building models and performing predictive/prescriptive analytics. More specifically, you will:
        * Ingest large data sets from a variety of sources (Hadoop, SQL, Teradata, Oracle, and 3rd party sources in the public domain)
        * Identify key data elements to extract/decode/analyze
        * Lead the development of predictive analytics and machine learning algorithms using business critical datasets
        * Use data analytics to gain insight into customer usage patterns and environmental conditions to quantifying expected customer benefits, such as fuel economy improvements, cost reductions, durability improvements, and additional non-vehicle benefits
        * Work collaboratively with various business groups and regions to disseminate critical information and develop strategic opportunities
        Job Requirements:
        Basic Qualifications:
        * Master's Degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Engineering, Management Information Systems, Business, or related technical field
        * 2+ years of academic or business experience in the following:
        * A data analytics role including running queries, compiling data, and manipulating data from separate sources to form unified sets for analysis
        * Predictive analytics including machine learning and experience writing algorithms in Python, Scala, or other technical languages
        * Big Data technologies in the Hadoop ecosystem (HDFS, MapReduce, Hive, Spark, Hue, Zeppelin, etc.), and command line experience with loading and manipulating files within HDFS
        Preferred Qualifications:
        * PhD in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Engineering, Management Information Systems, or related technical field
        * Knowledge of data transfer and ingestion technologies including Talend and HVR
        * Demonstrated experience building visualizations using Tableau/Qlikview/Alteryx
        * Strong oral, written, and interpersonal communication skills
        * Proficiency in Word, Excel and PowerPoint
        * Ability to write complex NoSQL queries needed to identify &amp; analyze data
        * Experience with SPSS, AMPL, WEKA, Matlab, R, SAS, or similar software
        * Experience working with global teams
        * Comfortable working in an environment where problems are not always well-defined, and interested in learning new tools and techniques
