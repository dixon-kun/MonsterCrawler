Title Data Warehousing Lead ETL Architect
City Columbus
State OH
Description

Design and implement data warehouse ETL architecture and data models.

ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:

1. Re-architect existing marts and define ETL architecture for new marts, by resolving architectural conflicts and ensuring consistency and alignment with industry best practices.

2. Demonstrate expertise in network architecture, ETL architecture, and data modeling.

3. Provide thought and technical leadership, guidance, and cross-training on highly complex applications to a team of ETL developers and data modelers.

4. Work with Data Science, Research, IT, and business units to assess and incorporate demographic, firmographic, geographic, weather, traffic, model, and other data sources for analytic benefit.

5. Potentially introduce and manage unstructured, “Big Data” such as telematic, internet-of-things (IOT), drone, wearables, sensor, and social media, in a distributed ecosystem like Hadoop.

6. Act independently under general supervision using established automated software tools.

7. Design, supervise, plan, and coordinate highly complex projects.

8. Consider equipment capacity, limitations, and operating time while completing assignments.

9. Interpret requirements (written or oral) into technical specifications for projects across a broad knowledge base.

10. Have full technical knowledge of all phases of applications systems analysis and programming, including but not limited to designing, coding, testing, debugging, documenting, installing, etc.

11. Have quality assurance responsibilities and very limited after hours on-call support.

12. Monitor, plan, document, conduct, and evaluate assignment status with project teams and management.

13. Evaluate progress and results to recommend changes in procedures.

14. May assist with the review and evaluation of personnel performance.

15. Interact and communicate effectively with all business contacts, including group presentations and meeting facilitation.

16. Responsible for project completion and user satisfaction on highly complex projects.

17. Update the project management software weekly to reflect assigned task status and effort.

18. Update the problem management and tracking system to reflect problem status.

19. Update the Performance Objectives/Evaluation document.

20. Make suggestions and track internal and external EODB.

There may be functions (essential or marginal) of this position not listed in this description.

Requirements

1. Bachelor’s degree in Information Systems or related field, or equivalent education and experience required.

2. 6+ years of related work experience in similar or directly related functions, preferably in insurance or financial services.

3. Expert, demonstrated knowledge of relational database models such as Oracle, DB2, SQL Server, Teradata, and Netezza; ETL architecture; and data modeling. SSIS programming experience a plus.

4. Experience managing unstructured data in distributed ecosystems like Hadoop.

5. Ability to work independently under general supervision with considerable latitude for un-reviewed actions or decisions.

6. Must have an in-depth knowledge of a group’s applications programming system / platform and a general knowledge of all groups’ applications programming systems. Competent to work at the highest technical level of all phases of applications systems analysis and programming activities. SSIS expertise preferred.

7. Must possess excellent interpersonal and communications skills, excellent problem solving skills, and the self-motivation to improve skills and abilities as generally acquired through six years of experience.

8. Must demonstrate leadership, professionalism, and preferably an understanding of the insurance business.
