TOP LEVEL SECURITY CLEARANCE REQUIRED

TheClientseeks an energetic and creative data scientist. The office is a new element in the Sponsor, responsible for driving the transformation of the Sponsors learning experience. We improve job performance, learning outcomes, and decision making by introducing tools, methods, and analytics for effective teaching and learning. Our staff ensures that ideas, methods and tools that improve the business of learning are assessed, socialized, and aligned with innovation and technology strategies across the Learning Enterprise. The Analytics team will stand up a Sponsor-wide analytics capability to tell the organizations story. This team will identify and develop actionable insights through problem definition, application of statistical models, and analysis against existing and future data. They will collect and convey information about learning -- to improve the learning function at an Enterprise level. This team will provide learning evaluation and analytics using innovative research methodologies and tools to enable stakeholder decisions, increase the quality and effectiveness of LE products and services, establish relationships between the LE and mission execution, and implement data collection and data visualization tools, and produce internal/external reporting. In pursuit of this mission, the office is seeking one full-time contractor who will work in the Sponsors space leveraging data analytics and performing metrics development in support of the mission.

MANDATORY SKILLS: 1. Demonstrated on-the-job experience exhibiting problem solving skills and understanding not only how to display data but make sure the right data is displayed that is targeted at answering the questions.
2. Demonstrated on-the-job experience getting data or proposing ways in which to measure or collect the data in the future.
3. Demonstrated on-the-job experience working with and manipulating the data to answer the question at hand or develop metrics that are relevant.
4. Demonstrated on-the-job experience building dashboards (and/or placemats) and supporting thinking around the substance and content of those placemats.
5. Demonstrated on-the-job experience performing longitudinal studies and cohort analysis and building the datasets to enable these studies.
6. Master Degree or higher in a quantitative discipline such as statistics, economics, mathematics, computer science, psychometrics, econometrics.
7. Formal education and/or work experience in the field of psychology, sociology, and human resources performing data analytics and metrics.
8. Demonstrated on-the-job experience with Data Analytics skills in Oracle SQL Developer, SAS, Tableau, Python, R/R-Studio, basic javascript and familiarity with HighCharts or d3.js, experience with Qualtrics and working with JSON and .csv file formats.
9. Demonstrated on-the-job experience solving HR-related data problems, successfully and skillfully conveying process and outcomes to senior stakeholders in both written and oral formats.

OPTIONAL SKILLS: 1. Demonstrated on-the-job experience with the Sponsors data systems. Such as where data resides on the Sponsors systems as it relates to all things HR and Learning.
2. Demonstrated on-the-job experience extracting key data from the Sponsors systems via the use of various programming languages (e.g., SQL).
3. Demonstrated on-the-job experience briefing results and associated methodologies in a manner that is easy for non-technical audience to understand.
4. Demonstrated on-the-job experience standing-up an analytics team in theClientspace. Candidate should address how they have in the past stood-up an analytics function in the Sponsors spaces and delivered analytic products and me
