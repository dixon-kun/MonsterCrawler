TITLE: Data Architect

DEPARTMENT: Product

LOCATION: New York City

OVERVIEW:

The successful candidate will be responsible for shaping our vision and leading internal research and development efforts to design advanced data solutions and systems. This individual needs to be a thought leader with an understanding of technology, applications, industry best practices, and architectural solutions to drive initiatives that meet the needs of our data product (Instock) and ongoing objectives.

This individual should have extensive knowledge in data science, architecture domains, software engineering, high performance/cloud computing environments, "Big Data" programming languages and methodologies, along with SQL databases (including MySQL).

This position specifically requires extensive expertise and experience in database architecture development, best practices, processes, and methodologies.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

* Gather technical requirements, listen to business problems, ask probing questions, and develop the architecture model to meet the product/business needs

* Analyzes needs and requirements of existing and proposed systems, and develops technical, structural, and infrastructure specifications.

* Designs, models, documents, and guides the logical and conceptual relationship of data and database changes for the product.

* Build prototypes and Proofs-of-concept to illustrate the possible or validate as and when needed before feeding information to development teams

* Work with development to implement architected solutions

* Provide technical input to the product and technology roadmaps

* Responsible for Architecture and Design of data infrastructure to support analytical and management applications, including data transport, security, analysis, reporting, and life-cycle management, working closely with DevOps and Software Engineering.

REQUIRED BACKGROUND AND QUALIFICATIONS:

* M.S. degree or higher in Computer Science, Math, or related technical area preferred.

* At least 3 years hands-on experience with major big data platforms and languages such as Hadoop, MapReduce, Scala, BigQuery, RedShift, Kinetica, in addition to traditional databases like MySQL, Postgres, or MSSQL

* Experience with open source software platforms and languages like Java, Python, PHP, Ruby and/or R.

* Demonstrated ability to handle multiple projects, changing priorities and occasional urgent matters while managing time efficiently and communicating to business and other stakeholders accordingly.

* Demonstrated track record in architecting and implementing data solutions for any industry (Marketing, eCommerce or Retail/Fashion industry would be a plus)

* Extensive experience with Data Warehouses and ETL processes.

* A team player that furthers WGSN's culture and values

* Excellent analytical, communication (both verbal and written), interpersonal, and organizational skills.

* Must be able to communicate effectively and clearly present technical approaches and findings.

* Optional but preferred - Experience with Elastic Search and/or Solr/SolrCloud
