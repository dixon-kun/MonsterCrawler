About Freebird: Freebird empowers travelers to instantly and seamlessly get back on their way when life throws a wrench into their travel plans. Combining technology, data science, and a fresh take on travel UX, we’re tackling the age-old problem of flight delays and cancellations. With Freebird, travelers have the ability to rebook their disrupted flight in less than 30 seconds -- at no additional cost. Our product immediately notifies customers of travel issues on their itineraries, helps them find the best alternative flight/route in real time, and gives them the ability to instantly book a new ticket -- for free -- in just 3 taps. We launched in August 2015 and raised $3.5MM in seed funding from General Catalyst, Accomplice, and Slow Ventures in November 2015. About the role: Consulting, banking, and PE roles don't always map 1-to-1 to startup roles. Luckily, we've created a business associate (generalist) role to help you transition into working at a startup, and give you an opportunity to figure out exactly what startup role you want to fill long-term -- whether it’s finance, business development, project management, or another position. For starters, you'll work closely with our CEO, CTO, and VP of Business Development on a wide range of projects, which will include establishing metrics for our product, guiding growth strategy, developing pricing strategy for partners, integrating risk analytics into business reporting, creating materials for our Board/investors, and building financial models to track and forecast our complex business. If you are passionate about making an impact and want to move out of consulting/banking/PE to work at an early stage startup (10-15 employees) -- this job is for you. Ask yourself: • Do I thrive when thrown into the deep end? • Am I seeking a position where I can make a measureable impact on day one? • Am I sick of slaving away on projects multiple-steps-removed from the results? We are looking for energetic and friendly candidates who are excited to face new challenges, tackle new projects, and own the deliverables of everything we throw your way. We hire candidates who love to learn everyday and know how to have fun, too. At Freebird, you will be the one who makes projects happen -- and the one who sees them through to fruition. We are seeking candidates with: • A top-tier undergraduate education • 2-4 years of experience in consulting, banking, or PE • Bonus points: A deep interest in the travel industry (because hey, we’re trying to make the travel experience better for everyone!) Interested? Send us your resume and a cover letter telling us why you want to join the team!
