        Reports to the Chief Operating Officer and will be responsible for providing enterprise level vision and strategy for all data management activities, including determination of organization's current data quality and maturity level, establishing enterprise standards, coordination of data assets across the organization, defining and governing the next generation of enterprise-level data, and analytics strategy.         A Bachelor's Degree in Information Sciences, Quantitative Social Sciences or other quantitative filed is required. An advanced degree in quantitative/data sciences or MBA is preferred. A minimum of 12 to 15 years of experience in information management and/or analysis, with a minimum of 5 years in a leadership capacity supervising others. Preferably in a Health plan or HealthCare environment. Must have developed and managed an Enterprise Data Strategy and roadmaps that reflect enterprise strategic priorities, marketplace needs, value added vendor partnerships and alliance opportunities.
