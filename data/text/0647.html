Staff Accountant, Technology

Job description

The Staff Accountant role is an exciting opportunity for an applicant with a strong foundation in financial accounting and management reporting to move into an influential position where they will gain exposure to, and work in, an operational and financial role serving the Hearst Corporation's Technology Division.

Reporting to the Director of Financial Operations, this role is central to the division's financial governance and provides a platform in which to partner with the technology department heads and the broader Hearst businesses. The Staff Accountant will support day-to-day and month-end accounting activities that complement and enhance the business strategy, goals and objectives of Hearst Technology. Duties will include;

* Perform various monthly account reconciliations. Research and interact with other teams within Hearst Technology to assess the appropriateness of account transactions. Record adjustments as necessary

* Prepare monthly standard journal entries and supporting schedules â€“ particular areas of focus: reclasses, prepaids, accruals, assessments, intercompany transactions and other general ledger accounting

* Prepare and take ownership of schedules as needed to support financial reporting

* Maintaining detail Capital Budget and Technology Spend records to support management decisions

* Prepare the monthly Statement of Cash Flows (FP-1)

* Prepare data for Apptio Consumption model

* Perform duties in compliance with GAAP, company and department policies and procedures, internal controls and Sarbanes-Oxley requirements

* Provide superior internal customer service to Hearst Business Units

* Prepare state business personal property tax reporting forms

* Participate in and support special projects

Desired Skills and Experience

* Bachelor's Degree in Accounting or Finance

* 1-3 years of relevant work experience in general ledger accounting; affinity towards technology or prior experience working for a tech company a big plus

* Highly proficient in Microsoft Excel

* Team player with strong interpersonal, verbal and written communication and customer service skills required

* Experience and demonstrated success serving as an effective business partner in a client service oriented environment dealing with a wide range of business units/departments.

* Able to anticipate risks, make adjustments and inform appropriately.

* Excellent personal time management to coordinate several projects simultaneously.

* Fast-learner and ability to grasp new concepts quickly

* Working knowledge of Apptio, Cognos, Oracle ERP, and Hyperion, a plus.

* Working knowledge of IT Finance and/or TBM, a plus

* CPA certification, desired but not required
