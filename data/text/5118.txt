        Socrata is a company with a purpose. We believe that data &#8211; when it's easy to find and use and when it's presented with context &#8211; can illuminate, inform and inspire. Our industry-leading solutions are enhanced by a proven customer success methodology, delivered by professional digital government change agents. When we succeed at helping governments transform from mostly analog to fully digital institutions &#8211; becoming data-driven &#8211; the people who stand to benefit from streamlined access to government data include both external stakeholders and internal government employees.
