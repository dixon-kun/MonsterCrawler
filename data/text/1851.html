Description:

Are you excited about being a part of big data projects? Have you considered working at Google or Facebook but realized that you’d rather solve important problems in national security than sell ads? Do you want the chance to help build out a large, complex data processing architecture for IC customers who are willing to lean forward on the adoption of new technology? Do you know the difference between virtual networking, container networking, and data center networking ? Do you know what DARPA is, and would you be excited about the opportunity to transition cutting edge R&D technology into operational environments? Might you even want to work on DARPA programs yourself at some point in the future? Are you looking to work with colleagues who share your deep technical expertise , passion, dedication, and intellectual curiosity?


If you answer yes to the above, the Advanced Solutions Group (ASG) at Leidos currently has an opening for Senior Network Engineer to architect and build a scalable, flexible and fault tolerant network supporting a distributed data processing and data science platform (DOMEX Data Discovery platform – D3P) that will transform operations for a major IC activity at Bethesda, MD office and customer sites in the National Capital Region. More broadly, you will advance the state of the art in research and advanced applications to support ongoing and future programs in in the areas of big data, analytics, software analysis, and other related disciplines, by prototyping, developing, maintaining, and debugging platforms with an emphasis on networking to enable advanced analytics on big data and/or datasets of interest.


Roles and Responsibilities:

- Works closely with senior investigators, program managers, developers, data analysts, and researchers in small teams to architect, design, and build the D3P.

- Will be responsible for set-up, care, and feeding of production and experimental network configurations and prototypes.

- Capable of taking direction at a high level and translating that into functioning platforms and configurations in support of small and large-scale analytics production capabilities/prototypes.

- Produces and analyzes results, reports progress, contributes to publications, and disseminates findings and presentations.


Qualifications:

To be considered for this position, you must minimally meet the knowledge, skills, and abilities listed below

- Candidate must possess an active TS/SCI clearance with the ability to obtain and maintain a Polygraph

- Bachelor’s degree in an information technology or related technical/science field with 5+ years of experience in a network engineering/system administration, including aspects of Dev/Ops, with overall experience in IT-related field (computer science, systems engineering, electrical engineering, etc.) demonstrating multiple years of progressive technical work and outcomes.

- Track record of active learning and creative problem solving

- Ability to analyze and assess computer/network architecture requirements and determine optimum, cost-effective solutions


Preferred Qualifications:

Candidates with these desired skills will be given preferential consideration

- Experience with configuring and deploying Cisco series LAN/WAN switches routers, firewalls, optical networks, and Intrusion Detection devices

- Experience with Data Center switching (Ethernet and Fibrechannel), network topology design and routing protocols (OSPF, EIGRP, BGP, etc…) in highly available production environments

- Solid network and systems troubleshooting experience with network protocols (UDP, TCP/IP, DNS, HTTP) across OSI Layer, network security configurations (DMZs, firewalls, VPNs, IDS, VLANs), and network encryption including packet/payload inspection (tcpdump/wireshark, etc…)

- Familiarity with provisioning and deploying virtual and physical networks

- Experience with Cisco ASA and Sourcefire appliance configuration, deployment and maintenance

- Hybrid Network Engineering skills including some code or script development, configuration differences between production versus R&D network configurations, and experience in Linux operating systems and bash/perl/python scripting

- Experience with unit and system provisioning and configuration management (example SaltStack), as well as monitoring systems such as Nagios or Ganglia

- Proficient in one or more software development languages pertinent to Cisco switching (Cisco IOS, NX-OS), network operations, or system administration at the LAN, VLAN, and WAN levels of deployment

- Experience utilizing virtualization and Hadoop (or similar distributed file systems) in development and deployment environments

- Knowledge of computer security systems applications, procedures, and techniques to meet qualification of DoD 8570.01M CAT II IAT (Required), Cisco Certified Network Associate CCNA (Desired) and Security + (Required)

- Experience using git, svn, JIRA, or other version control technologies

- Experience with VMWare vSphere / vCenter including hypervisor networking configuration and maintenance

- Experience with OpenStack especially with networking configuration

- Experience with Redhat Enterprise Linux (RHEL) and troubleshooting/optimizing link aggregation/bonding, pxebooting, and kickstarting

- Experience with Microsoft Windows Server 7/X/Server 2012/Active Directory and Network Access Protection (DHCP)

- Experience with Amazon Web Services (AWS/C2S) and specifically VPC Networking

- Experience with Docker and cluster networking overlays such as Docker Swarm and Kubernetes

- Experience in one or more scripting languages (Python, bash and Powershell preferred)

- Assistance in the development of a concept of operations plans (CONOPS) to guide the tactical steps required to provide business continuance and disaster recovery

- Familiarity with hardware platforms, e.g., CPUs, GPUs, FPGAs, etc.

- Can configure, troubleshoot, and support high-performance networking of large capacity storage arrays of various types and vendors as well as high performance clustered file systems such as CephFS, GlusterFS, HDFS, etc. and the transport protocols to support those systems (generally Infiniband).

- Experience with information system security accreditation is strongly desired; D3P is in the process of ATO re-authorization and accreditation for unclass networks requiring a willingness to help with security accreditation issues and documentation.

- Previous experience supporting research in Data Analytics or big data

- Experience with Software Defined Network (SDN) and specifically Cisco’s Application Centric Infrastructure (ACI), Cisco Nexus 1000V, Cisco Application Virtual Switch (AVS)

- Certification in CCNA

- Comfort with both Agile and traditional SDLC

- A “self-starter” engineer with an aptitude for problem solving and a strong sense of accountability


ASG/SPO
