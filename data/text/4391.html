Reporting to the Consumer and Digital Analytics Manager this is an essential role within the team needed to redefine consumer segmentation through advanced analytics and machine learning that will be used to improve user experiences, personalized services and targeted marketing efforts. As a data analyst, you will partner with the team to enhance our understanding of consumer behaviors via data, delivering actionable insights and strategic recommendations.

  • Design and develop analysis systems to extract meaning from large scale consumer data sets
  • Synthesize behavioral based consumer profiles and insights across all consumer touch points and evangelize how to leverage such insights to create compelling business opportunities
  • Work in cross-functional atmosphere with stakeholders from marketing and sales to help drive business goals related to customer acquisition and retention
  • Identify analytic opportunities and approaches to support sustainable market growth and sales
  • Complete Ad-hoc data requests to support key stakeholders
  • Formulate success metrics, socializing them and creating dashboards/reports to monitor them
  • Convey concise and actionable stories through data to key stakeholders
  • Work with others to develop, refine and scale data management procedures, systems and workflows; Drive the collection of new data and refinement of existing data sources
  • Prepare data for machine learning algorithms
  • Collaborate with other data analytic leaders to share and bring best practices to New Balance

  • Strong analytical skills with ability to translate data analysis into business recommendations.
  • Organized and detail-oriented.
  • Exceptional communication skills.
  • Ability to influence cross-functional teams in a matrixed organization.
  • Excellent judgment, strategic thinker, enthusiastic and entrepreneurial.
  • Proven ability to thrive in a fast paced environment.
  • Uses sound judgment to identify issues and escalates when appropriate.
  • Self-starter; Works effectively with little supervision.

Education

  • BA or MS in relevant quantitative field (Mathematics/Statistics, Computer Science, Engineering)

Experience

  • 5+ years relevant professional experience;
  • Extensive experience solving analytical problems using quantitative approaches; translating business objectives into actionable analyses.
  • Comfort manipulating and analyzing complex, high-volume, high-dimensionality data from varying sources
  • A strong passion for empirical research and for answering hard questions with data
  • Knowledge of predictive analytics; experience preferred
  • Ability to communicate complex quantitative analysis in a clear, precise and actionable manner
  • Extensive experience with data visualization software (Tableau or Spotfire)
  • Extensive experience with relational databases and SQL
  • Advanced proficiency with Microsoft Excel
  • Knowledge of an analysis tool such as R, Matlab or SAS a plus
  • Experience with Microsoft Cortana Machine Learning a plus
