        The Compliance Testing Strategic Data Solutions ("SDS") team is at the forefront of addressing potential regulatory and compliance risks globally across Citi's Institutional Clients Group ("ICG") Compliance Testing program. The team focuses on providing forensic data mining and system and data assurance capabilities through the development of analytics, data visualizations, automating test scripts, and continuous monitoring functions using an Integrated Operating Model.
        The Compliance SDS Officer will be responsible for planning, scoping, developing, documenting and delivering analytics that support the Global ICG testing of securities laws and regulations as they relate to Citi's policies. The candidate will work with business teams, compliance officers, and technology support staff to understand various regulations and policies, business processes, and the data and systems to investigate, explore, and assess controls intended to mitigate risk to the organization.
        The ideal candidate will have a depth of experience with large scale numerical and textual data analysis using languages such as SQL, Python, R, and SAS, familiarity with Hadoop and related tools such as Hive, Hue, MapReduce, Impala, and Spark, and skill using data visualization tools such as Qlikview, Microstrategy, and D3.js. The candidate should have business and product specific knowledge in the financial services industry. The position will require someone who is comfortable taking initiative and developing innovative strategies to solve complex problems. The candidate should demonstrate strong verbal and written communication skills and be comfortable leading others. Experience working on and leading small, fast-paced project teams is preferred.
        Key responsibilities include:
        * Design and implement data analytics in support of a robust Compliance Testing program
        * Coordinate with business teams, compliance officers, and technology support staff from project inception to the delivery of an analytic
        * Articulate technical concepts to influence broad audiences and peers
        * Translate testing requirements into solutions using various data analytic and visualization tools
        * Work with team members to translate the results from analytics into impactful insights into regulatory compliance risks for senior management
        * Perform quality reviews on the work of others
