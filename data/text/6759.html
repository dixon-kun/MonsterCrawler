About Citi
Citi, the leading global bank, has approximately 200 million customer accounts and does business in more than 160 countries and jurisdictions. Citi provides consumers, corporations, governments and institutions with a broad range of financial products and services, including consumer banking and credit, corporate and investment banking, securities brokerage, transaction services, and wealth management. Our core activities are safeguarding assets, lending money, making payments and accessing the capital markets on behalf of our clients.
Citi’s Mission and Value Proposition explains what we do and Citi Leadership Standards explain how we do it. Our mission is to serve as a trusted partner to our clients by responsibly providing financial services that enable growth and economic progress. We strive to earn and maintain our clients’ and the public’s trust by constantly adhering to the highest ethical standards and making a positive impact on the communities we serve. Our Leadership Standards is a common set of skills and expected behaviors that illustrate how our employees should work every day to be successful and strengthens our ability to execute against our strategic priorities.
Diversity is a key business imperative and a source of strength at Citi. We serve clients from every walk of life, every background and every origin. Our goal is to have our workforce reflect this same diversity at all levels. Citi has made it a priority to foster a culture where the best people want to work, where individuals are promoted based on merit, where we value and demand respect for others and where opportunities to develop are widely available to all.
Citi's Compliance Communication team performs oversight for email, IM and voice communications for the North America region.  The department's primary responsibility is to conduct surveillance to reasonably detect that Citi's employees are in compliance with the applicable regulations and market conduct best practices.  The team is made up of an experienced Markets staff familiar with Trading and Sales practices.
The individual will work closely with Citi’s Data Scientists and will provide critical input used to development Citi’s future state machine learning surveillance system.   The role requires project planning, developing reports to demonstrate progress and coordination on projects within the Global program.  The candidate will be need to be able to develop new lexicon based policies/scenarios, written procedures and have constant engagement with the supported front office businesses, Compliance colleagues, Legal, and others to resolve issues that may arise and must be able to collaborate/communicate with the team members globally.

Qualifications
  • 5+ years of Markets Front office (Fixed income or Equities products) or Product Compliance experience with an understanding of e-comm/voice surveillance is preferred, 7+ years of relevant experience required
  • The candidate must have an understanding of a large Global Investment Bank
  • Exceptionally strong attention to detail, organizational skills, and follow-through
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, including tact and diplomacy
  • Sound judgment, professionalism, integrity
  • Some project management skills
  • Ability to analyze processes to identify opportunities for improvement, and to analyze and compile data from multiple sources to produce effective reports
  • Proficiency in MS Office applications (Excel, Word, PowerPoint)
  • Bachelor’s degree preferred or relevant, equivalent experience.
