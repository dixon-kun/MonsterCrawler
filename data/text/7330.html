Data Scientist with Ph.D. at Omni212

Redmond, WA 98052

Report this job

About the Job

We are looking for a bright and creative data scientist to help in maximizing the trustworthiness and utility of our data. The team is building services that help fuse the disparate signals of data coming from many channels into more useful forms and providing the systems that ensure reliability of data as it moves through the system.

Position Requirements:
  • Data categorization models. Build services that help categorize expense data based on usage and underlying attributes coming from a variety of systems.
  • Data quality anomaly detection. Creating systems that help quickly make anomalous patterns in our data pipelines known to teams throughout the company.
  • Improvements to data cleansing processes. Providing requirements and techniques into systems that help cleanse data being used in key data pipelines.

In Order To Accomplish This You Will Need To:
  • Dig into the data coming from many different systems and technologies.
  • Work with people throughout the company to understand how data is used and where we can best improve it.
  • Build the necessary data features needed to support modeling.
  • Use your data science background to identify and improve data elements.
  • Translate your work into requirements and samples that can be incorporated into engineering processes.

What do you need to bring?
  • Bachelor’s Degree or above. preferred Ph.D. in Data Science, Statistics, Computer Science, Math, or similar quantitative field.
  • 5+ years of professional experience with machine learning, statistical analysis, predictive modeling, and time series analysis.
  • Strong data discovery and analysis skills.
  • Experience with big data using technologies like Hadoop, Pig, Hive, and Spark.
  • Previous hands-on exposure to statistical software packages like R, SciPy, Matlab, SPSS, SAS).
  • Experience with a variety of relevant data manipulation languages (Python, Java, SQL)
  • Comfort with working in a Linux environment.
  • A genuine desire to organize data and make it accessible with the technical skills to bring your ideas to reality
  • An enjoyment in working with teammates to produce quality outcomes.
  • To launch your career with an organization whose products have a real positive impact on peoples lives, apply today and see where your journey takes you.
