The Marketing Data Scientist will lead the analysis, mining and interpreting of large databases to help the company gain market share, retain customers and "play offense" more strategically and effectively. This role provides strategic insight into the business and will present/communicate analytical results to multiple senior business leaders and stakeholders.

This role uses data and advanced reporting and analytics tools to enable internal customers to make the very best strategic decisions, maximize quality and identity efficiencies. This role will create models and reports leveraging SAS to extract and analyze data from numerous disparate sales, marketing, financial and operational systems and databases.

Responsibilities:

* Lead the analysis and mining of large databases to deliver business insights

* Interpret market, prospect, customer, financial and operational data to draw correlations and confirm/recommend and/or disprove various business hypothesis and strategies

* Make recommendations regarding market, product, programs and projects for future business opportunities to key internal stakeholders and senior business leaders

* Perform data analysis; analyze and track key marketing, sales, customer and financial metrics to foster process improvement and efficiencies

* Work closely with senior business leaders to define business requirements and leverage reporting tools to develop and deploy reporting solutions

* Progressively combine data science skills and knowledge with industry/business knowledge to derive and deliver innovative solutions to business partners

* Perform complex statistical analysis, prepare summary of findings, causes and present recommendations; work requires ability to develop solutions to complex problems, strong independent judgment and creativity

* Develop standard and ad hoc reports using a variety of internal systems. Integrate data from multiple sources into unified reports generating meaningful data

* Collaborate closely with the Marketing Operations team and the Ceridian Finance Data Analytics & Reporting team

* Leverage marketing data warehouse and other company databases (e.g., customer support, financial, operational)

* Define the direction and approach for new processes that have a significant impact of business results

Qualifications:

* Bachelors Degree in quantitative disciplines such as Statistics, Mathematics, Operations Research, Physics and Computer Science. MBA candidates preferred but not required

* Strong analysis ability with demonstrated knowledge of software and spreadsheet usage; ability to synthesize large amounts of data and translate into meaningful information; use analysis to drive change

* 5+ years of SAS programming experience with emphasis on report creation and/or predictive modeling

* Demonstrated proficiency in statistical analysis; including decision tree and regression analysis

* Expert proficiency in developing original statistical and econometric models to support B2B marketing programs

* Must be adept at translating the meaning and significance of the insights

* Must be detail oriented with strong analytical skills

* Expert level competency with scientific data analysis methods, statistics, data modeling, anomaly and outlier detections

* Ability to handle multiple assignments promptly and effectively

* Demonstrated ability to work calmly in a fast-paced team environment while effectively managing multiple projects

* Strong level of personal organization with excellent oral/written and interpersonal communication skills

* Strong knowledge of Marketing innovation and market trends

* Deep understanding of consumer behavior analysis, customer segmentation, predictive modeling and a working knowledge of digital and traditional media campaigns
