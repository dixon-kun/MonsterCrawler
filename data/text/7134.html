About Seneca Resources:
Seneca Resources is client driven provider of strategic Information Technology consulting services and Workforce Solutions to government and industry.  Seneca Resources is a leading IT services provider with offices in Reston, Virginia, Alabama and Columbia, Maryland that service clients throughout the United States.  The key to our success lies within our strong corporate culture which drives our business.  We challenge our staff through engaging work, and we reward our staff through competitive compensation, extensive professional training, and excellent opportunities for career advancement.  In turn, we look for only the best and brightest to join our team.

Position Title: Data Scientist
Location: Arlington, VA
Work Status: U.S Citizen eligible for a Public Trust.
Position Status: Full Time

Position Description:
  • Seeking a Data Scientist to support our work with an independent agency of a government client in Arlington, Virginia.
  • You will play a key role within a small data science team.  We're looking for hands on experience developing solutions for complex data science problems using Python, R, Hadoop, and Greenplum (or other Massively Parallel Processing solutions).

Required skills
  • Hands on experience developing solutions for complex data science problems using Python and R.
  • Experience with Hadoop, Greenplum, and/or other Massively Parallel Processing (MPP) solutions.
  • Can do attitude.  Hands on, eager and willing to learn, and do what it takes to be successful.

Desired skills
  • ETL, PL/SQL and/or data visualization tools experience is highly valued.
  • Financial institution (FDIC, OCC Treasury, Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac) and experience with banking and mortgages would be beneficial.
  • Multiple years of IT/data science experience.
  • B.S. Degree or higher in computer science, statistics, information systems, mathematics, or other data science/big data related specialization; value added to M.S. degree or higher holder

Clearance
  • Public Trust background investigation required to obtain agency badge.
  • Must be a US citizen.
