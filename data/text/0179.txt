        Summary Statement
        The primary responsibility of the AARP Experience (AARPx) VoC Insights Business Analyst: is to analyze Voice of Customer (VoC) data to uncover insightful findings and transform them into actionable insights for the organization. It will use a text analytic platform to build models, conduct categorizations, and sentiment analysis to analyze VoC data from multiple feedback sources, including email, voice call transcriptions, social media and surveys to discover opportunities for customer experience improvements.
        The position will report to the Director, AARPx Listening Programs and will be charged with:
        Insight Reporting -- The position will utilize the Clarabridge text analytics platform to conduct text, sentiment, and root cause analyses to answer business questions and produce stories that reveal key insights into consumer experience. It will also provide internal business partners with insights reports and dashboards that guide effective decision making.
        Data Management -- The position will provide text data oversight, including the movement of unstructured data between AARP/Clarabridge/Partners and manage the implementation of new VoC feedback sources. The position will also be responsible for ensuring the integrity and accuracy of text data in the tool through regular sentiment tuning, categorization audits and model maintenance.
        Tool Management --The position will manage the day-to-day relationship with Clarabridge concerning feedback sources and tool dependability.
        Responsibilities
        * Build and maintain VoC data categorization models
        * Perform basic and advanced sentiment tuning
        * Conduct categorization and sentiment audit analysis
        * Conduct VoC data analysis and interpretation
        * Ensure VoC data integrity and accuracy
        * Develop insights reports and dashboards
        * Incorporate advanced text analytics into customer insights and properly package business insights and findings for consumption
        * Succinctly communicate the findings and insights with proper verification and validation techniques
        * Use best practices around categorization, sentiment and insights principles
        * Implement and manage pipeline of new channels and data feeds
        * Identify early warning signals and opportunistic approaches from insights gathered
        * Manage internal groups and vendors to troubleshoot and provide issue resolution on text analytics data.
        Requirements
        * Bachelor's degree in Statistics, Analytics, Economics, Data Science or comparable
        * 2+ years of experience with text analytics and analyzing Voice of the Customer (VoC) findings to identify actions that improve customer experience
        * 2+ years of text analytic experience; experience with predictive modeling
        * 2+ years of experience with Business Intelligence and/or Data Mining Tools (MicroStrategy, SAS, or SPSS)
        * 2+ years of experience with the Clarabridge Text Analytics Platform or similar product
        * Ability to understand technical aspects of products and aptitude for working with IT professionals to integrate various data sets and platforms
        * Ability to manage multiple tasks simultaneously within a continually changing environment
        * Excellent problem solving skills and ability to navigate challenging situations
        * Ability to identify and tell a story using data
        * Strong written, analytical and verbal communication skills
        * Advanced proficiency with MS Excel and PowerPoint
        * Customer experience and loyalty background a plus
        Preferred requirement for this position: Text Analytics Platform certification
