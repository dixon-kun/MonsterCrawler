Provide technical support to Engineering Processes projects on a variety of related tasks. Participate as a part of a team to collect, compile, and report status on performance, associated with engineering. Develop and monitor tracking matrix for performance associated with projects and associated tasks. Produce tracking charts and reports for management review on a weekly basis.

Reports to the Manager of Engineering Operations. Perform assessments to defined processes. Develop and update scheduled activities and compare to baseline, perform and track performance. Track staffing needs as defined by managers.


The successful candidate will typically have an associate’s degree or higher and two years of data management, cost accounting, or book keeping experience. Needs to have a strong understanding of Microsoft Suite packages, especially Microsoft Excel including data manipulation and graphic representation. Good technical writing and presentation chart development skills would be an asset. Position will be based in Arlington, TX, and occasional travel to Red Oak, TX.

Preferred Qualifications:
• Data mining

• Data Analysis
