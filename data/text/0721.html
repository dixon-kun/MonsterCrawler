Overall Summary
Razorfish Health is seeking an Account Coordinator who executes flawlessly, prioritizes attention to detail, and exhibits professional and motivating behavior. The Account Coordinator will be responsible for executing the administrative tasks needed for not only the account team, but for the agency client as well. This role is designed to introduce the Account Coordinator to agency life and logistics. Other daily responsibilities are as follows but not limited to:
Project / Account Administration
• Schedules internal meetings for the team and in some cases schedules client facing meetings.
• Partners with delivery on project set up and oracle code set up tasks.
• Manages the process of submitting Med Legal Review through a defined process including submission to an off-site team.
• Takes notes during kick off meetings, internal and client status meetings and any other project related meetings as defined by the Project Management or Account Team disseminates notes & follows up as appropriate.
• May be responsible for coordination of information share between other agencies and third party vendors with oversight from client engagement.

Internal Client Service
• Collaborates with team to support client deliverable deadlines, working on assigned projects as needed
• Owns/prioritizes and complete team or client projects on time, updating relevant team members as necessary
• Understands the internal goals of the team, and the goals of the client

Accounting
• Performs all task work related to accounting when/where necessary.
• On a monthly basis this would include: retrieving data which will be used for billing purposes and checking for accuracy, helping to address any outstanding issues
• Organization of campaign documents, accounting documents, internal auditor for all

Qualifications
• Bachelor’s degree in Business or Marketing
• Intermediate Outlook User:- Understands how to schedule and accept meetings, manage folders, and accept and complete tasks as they are assigned
• Basic understanding of Power Point/Word
• Exceptional written and verbal communication skills
• High attention to detail
• Strong time-management and organizational skills
• Strong client-service orientation
• Flexible to evolving responsibilities and last minute changes
