        Description
        Provides strategic,analytical and technical support for the pricing activities of the business. Responsiblefor developing and directing sales strategies on all projects where sell pricerequires a deviation from allowed multipliers. Analyzes market price and workswith sales to optimize project margins. Develops sellingprice analytical reports and enforcement of sales department pricerelated policies.
        Responsibilities
        * Analyze the competition andhistorical pricing information to determine the best price points for thebusiness
        * Mine data, analyze andinterpret results using standard statistical tools and techniques. Provide insight on market analytics, pricingand cost
        * Identify, scope and executestrategic price improvement projects
        * Work with productdevelopment to gather and analyze data, and make recommendations that can beimplemented into revenue-generating products
        * Extract, transform, andload data from various databases, formats, and data sources. Use exploratory data analysis techniques toidentify meaningful relationships, patterns, or trends from complex data sets
        * Communicate businessfindings through effective data visualizations
        * Provide analyses of costdata and make recommendations to management regarding price. Analyze andpresent alternative pricing scenarios with varying risk trade-offs
        * Provide management withreports specifying and comparing factors affecting prices and profitability ofproducts. Regularly inform and consult with top management regarding negotiatingstrategies for large or new business proposals
        * Interface with US salespeopleand foreign buyers on bill of material reconfigurations need to meet profitrequirements
        * Represent the salesorganization interest with business unit leaders
        * Serve as the centralpricing approval authority for Liebert products. Manage and administer pricing sales policies
        * Coordinate thedetermination of minimum gross margin (GM) requirements for all Liebert sourcedproducts sold world wide
        * Develop analytical tools/models to quickly andaccurately determine GM. Trainevaluators and business leaders in the use of those tools
        * Develop standards and workinstructions for those performing GM calculations that ensure accuracy andconsistency
        * Develop feedback mechanisms to communicateGM to business leadership
        * Monitor and manage GM anddiscount multipliers for projects processed by the Strategic Pricing group
        * Provide guidance forRegional Sales Managers and field salespeople on pricing
        * Ensure compliance withfederal, state and other related pricing laws, regulations and guidelines tominimize legal and financial exposure
        * Use business acumen andtechnical knowledge to develop and deliver Business Intelligence solutions forinternal customers
        Additional Information
        * Standard office workenvironment
        * Occasional travel may berequired
        * Knowledge of Liebertproducts
        * Must have a proven abilityto create and modify Excel and Visio based documents.
        * Must be willing tochallenge discount request submitted by Representative and non US based marketunits and distributors.
        * Must be willing to reorganize work to accommodate projects with very short deadlines
        Requirements
        * BS/BA in Business, Engineering orrelated degree
        * At least 5 years of pricing analysis,data science, financial modeling, business analytics, business intelligence, orrelated work experience within a B2B environment
