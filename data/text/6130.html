Who are we?

Netflix is the world's leading internet streaming service with over 93 million members in 190 countries. Our members enjoy more than 125 million hours of streamed video per day, using over 1000 different types of devices, generating more than a third of downstream Internet traffic in North America. The Content Data Engineering and Analytics team is based in the entertainment capital of the world, focused on providing our colleagues rich data, tools, and insights so that we can make decisions on what series and films will bring the most joy to our subscribers. We are part of a larger team that supports Netflix's data-driven culture across marketing, streaming, product, and finance functions. As Netflix deepens its global reach and develops more and more amazing Originals content, the scope and impact of our team's work in the content space is exploding.
