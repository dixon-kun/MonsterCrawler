At Rolls-Royce, we create integrated power solutions and innovative technologies that keep our customers one step ahead of an ever-changing world. To do this, we need skillful and innovative people like you who want to help shape the future of global power.



Join our Digital Team and you'll be right at the heart of that mission, providing new perspectives on our ever-growing pool of data to help us solve complex problems. By creating apps, cloud solutions, data lakes and codes, you'll be able to extract data better and faster to help us create the technologies of the future.

As the pace of technological advancement continues to quicken, the way we are operating and serving our customers is developing further. Huge increase in the volume and sources of data presents massive opportunities to reveal new insights that have previously remained hidden, creating new avenues to explore for our customers.



Our aim is to create a series of highly collaborative Digital teams across the globe, each ready to share knowledge and embrace change to improve the way we work. Everything we do comes back to our vision of transforming our Digital future, where our engineering expertise, global reach and deep industry knowledge come together to develop outstanding customer relationships and solutions.

As a Solution Architect, you will have the opportunity to work in the central architecture capability supporting customer value streams to deliver directly to internal and external customers. Reporting into the Senior Architect, you will be responsible for providing the solution and software architectures including the governance of implementation and testing for the next generation of our cloud based services. In this respectable role, you will be fully supported by peers and seniors in solution and enterprise architecture functions and will become a go to point within the value streams team for technical leadership, making a noticeable and recognized contribution to the quality of our new services.

Accountabilities



This role is about completely immersing yourself in complex data, without ever losing sight of the end solution. You'll need to be able to see the problem-solving possibilities that data brings so that you can not only work on global change, but also drive it forward. You'll also need the ability to interpret complex business requirements into desktop and mobile experiences.

* Support software engineering and cloud activities within the value stream.

* In conjunction with other architects, ensure consistent application of software and IT design patterns, architecture principles and frameworks across teams.

* Work with the architecture and value stream teams to continuously develop and content mange the architecture and technical governance frameworks.

* Working according to DevOps to help build the continuous delivery capability of the value stream across software engineering and IT operations roles.

* Create solutions and support and guide design activities from others within the value stream.

* Consider security, performance, cost and other business (non-functional) requirements as part of all solutions.

* Contribute to evaluations, reviews and continuous improvement of software engineering and IT methods, tools and standards.

* Document all work in accordance with agreed standards.

* Provide guidance and assistance to colleagues in design, creation, testing and documentation.

* Apply practical, hands-on skills to both software engineering and cloud deployments to support proof of concept architectures, technology investigations and to demonstrate design and implementation good practice to IT and software engineers.

* Carry out process improvement assignments as part of a team, applying knowledge, skills and expertise.

* Follow the rules of Scrum/Agile and meet their daily and sprint objectives.

* Work to remove impediments, facilitate continuous improvement and coordinates solution implementation and delivery with the rest of their team.

* Work directly with internal and external customers to create and integrate architectural solutions.

* Work directly with suppliers and external agencies to enhance our technical capability.

* Support marketing and sales activities from an architecture perspective.

Qualifications

We're looking for talented professionals, with proven experience in a Software Engineering role. You'll also need experience of working in an agile environment with an understanding of how to architect highly scalable distributed systems using traditional and cloud-based software. Everything you do will come back to understanding complex data, so you'll need to be able to work with and source all types of data (structured, unstructured) from various sources for Ingestion onto Analytics. On top of this, you'll ideally be a degree level candidate in a Mathematics, Scientific, Computing, Engineering discipline or equivalent.

Basic Qualifications

* Bachelor Degree in Mathematics, Scientific, Computing or Engineering or similar discipline and 8 years of experience in mixed software engineering and IT projects.

* OR Masters Degree in Mathematics, Scientific, Computing or Engineering or similar discipline and 6 years of experience in mixed software engineering and IT projects.

* Awareness of architecture frameworks such as TOGAF.

* Familiarity with modelling tools and languages such as UML, SySML, BPMN.

* Hands-on experience with Microsoft Azure cloud services and technologies, preferably related to Azure AD, HDInsight, Data Factory, App Services, Storage and Power BI.

* Hands-on experience with SQL and Microsoft SQL Server, in particular Azure SQL and Azure SQL Data Warehouse.

* Exposure to application lifecycle tools such as VSTS (Visual Studio Team Services) or TFS (Team Foundation Server) and Git.

* Must be a US citizen

Preferred Qualifications

* Experience of working in an analytical engineering/data science environment would be desirable.

* Experience of participation in agile ceremonies such as sprint planning would be useful as would experience with techniques such as affinity sizing.
