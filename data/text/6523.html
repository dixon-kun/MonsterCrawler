Job Description
Senior Full Stack Web Developer
Join a cutting edge data science and statistical services team helping to build and promote the expansion of advanced analytic solutions. This Senior Full Stack Web Developer position includes the responsibilities of partnering with business, engineering and IT teams to quickly understand and improve business processes through web applications. This role will be located in Longmont, CO or Bloomington, MN

Typical work includes
  • Designing and developing full-stack applications across multiple platforms using modern industry-adopted languages and frameworks
  • Developing interfaces with industry adopted technologies as well as relational and non-relational database technologies
  • Taking direction from a principal project manager who manages project details
  • Collaborating and co-developing with Application UI/UX Designer
  • Meeting and collaborating with business users on requirements, objectives, measures, solutions and insights
  • Assisting in creating and presenting status reports to senior executives
All projects will require an innovative thought process that includes developing early prototypes for iterative review with customers to arrive at a final optimal and robust web application.
