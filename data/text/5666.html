Division Description:

Power Generation Services (PS)

Siemens is the leading service partner for ensuring high reliability and optimal performance of rotating power equipment within the utility, oil & gas, and industrial processing industries worldwide – as well as for wind turbines. Siemens’ extensive national network of service technicians is able to quickly and comprehensively offer expert service to maximize the lifecycle of power generation equipment, helping to ensure reliability and prevent downtime.

For more information, please visit: http://www.siemens.com/businesses/us/en/power-generation-services.htm



Job Description:

The digital organization in the Power Generation Services and Power and Gas businesses (PS & PG) is seeking exceptional engineers to develop products and solve complex problems as a part of the digital product innovation team. This position provides an exciting opportunity to work in a dynamic, startup like environment, where we use bleeding-edge technology to solve pressing business problems in PS and PG. The  position is a hybrid between full-stack developer, data scientist and digital technology evangelist. The right candidate will prototype cutting edge solutions that can be readily moved to the engineering team. The position reports to the head of digital product innovation.

Responsibilities

  • Assessment and evaluation of new digital technologies
  • Build prototype solutions that can be readily moved to engineering
  • Ability to drive a team through tough technical challenges
  • Support of digital engineering activities

Required Knowledge/Skills, Education, and Experience

  • Bachelor’s degree in a quantitative discipline or similar
  • 10 years in technology development
  • Technical skills (should have expertise in at least four of these skill areas)

·         Full-stack web development

·         Able to implement solutions on both the server and client-side

·         Experience designing APIs

·         Modern javascript framework (Ember, angular, etc.)

·         Familiar with build tools (gulp, grunt, etc.)

·         Dev-ops and infrastructure management

·         Knowledge of provisioning systems (salt, puppet, etc.)

·         Deployment to cloud platform service (AWS, Azure)

·         Computer science

·          Experience with low-level (C, C) and high-level languages (python, Scala)

·         Comfortable with different programming paradigms (procedural, functional, object-oriented)

·         Backend

·         Experience with SQL and NoSQL databases ( and key-value stores)

·         Knowledge of database clustering

·         Data visualization

·         Experience with frameworks/libraries (openGL/webGL, d3, etc.)

·         Ability to target different environments (destkop, web, VR)

·         Data Science

·         Experience in modern machine learning methods. Knowledge of various clustering and classification methods, order reduction methods, Neural nets, etc

·          Experience with industrial digital technologies is a big plus

·         Experience dealing with sensor data from machines

·         Experience with CT data

·         Lidar or laser sensing data etc.

  • Demonstrated detail-oriented and innovative thinking mindset
