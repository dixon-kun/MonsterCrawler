The Associate Data Scientist is part of a team that develops and refines data-driven models that power ID Analytics’ products and services, improves data technology infrastructure, and communicates ongoing research and analysis to stakeholders. The Associate Data Scientist provides technical and analytical support to internal and external organizations.  The Associate Data Scientist explores new data sources and regularly works with out-of-memory data sets.

This position requires the ability to design solution paths that achieve the needs of the customer. Depending on the assignment, frequent client interaction may be required to complete deliverables.


  • Processing large data sets and providing quality checks and analysis following established best practices with limited guidance.
  • Managing and improving complex computational systems for processing and scoring data.
  • Conducting data experiments to verify assumptions and hypotheses.
  • Running existing algorithms.
  • Performing analytical and technical investigations in support of customer and production related issues; making initial recommendations for resolution.
  • Scoring and analysis of customer data and presentation of the results.
  • Developing and improving tools for investigative and analytics workflows.
  • Working with technical and business personnel to ensure successful outcomes for customers.
  • Other duties as assigned.

  • Master’s degree in a relevant technical discipline (Math, Engineering, Computer Science, Statistics or similar field), or Bachelor’s degree in such a field with 2-4 years of relevant job experience.
  • At least one year of experience with mainstream programming languages such as Java, Python, and Scala.
  • Strong experience in data extraction, data manipulation, and data analysis.
  • Strong mathematical and statistics skills.
  • Experience with Unix/Linux system architecture and command line tools.
  • Experience with scripting languages.
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills (both written and oral) including the ability to communicate complex technical or statistical concepts to a non-technical audience.
  • Curiosity to pursue self-directed data analysis paths.

Preferred Skills

  • Experience with Python, AWK, and SQL.
  • Experience with data systems such as Hadoop, Hive, and MySQL.
  • Experience with statistical or scientific computing packages.
  • Experience in collaborative projects using software development best practices such as version control and testing.
  • Experience with data visualization technologies such as d3 and Tableau.

This brief summary is not an all-inclusive description of job duties. Other job duties and responsibilities may also be assigned by the incumbent’s supervisor at any time based upon Company need.
