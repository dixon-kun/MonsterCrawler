          Job summary
         Solutionreach is seeking a Data Scientist to help influence the direction of products and drive decisions that impact products, features, business outcomes, and end user experience. As a data scientist at Solutionreach you will have the opportunity to play a major role in strategy and decision support through data insights. You will have the unique opportunity to work with cutting edge healthcare IT technologies and big data tools and help establish the culture and leadership for what data science means for the company and healthcare in general
          Duties and responsibilities
          Propose innovative analytic insights that      translate into better product decisions
          Create innovative solutions and insights to      business problems using mathematical, quantitative, statistical, and      machine learning methodologies
          Compile and analyze large datasets of      structured and unstructured data to uncover necessary business insights that      can be trended, interpreted, and ultimately made actionable
          Effectively translate and communicate data      insights for key stakeholders
          Aggregate and compile data from various data      sources
          Assist in creating and defining predictive      algorithms for healthcare insights
          Qualifications
         Qualifications include:
          Graduate degree in Applied Mathematics,      Computer Science, Engineering, Statistics, or other quantitative      discipline (MS or Ph.D desired)
          Mastery level in big data platforms and      relationship databases
          Mastery level in one or more programming      language interfacing with REST services
          Ability to quickly analyze and manipulate      large data sets
          Mastery level in data visualization quickly      turn data sets into digestible visualizations
          Mastery of statistical theories and techniques
          Experience with NLP, regression,      classification, and experimental design
           Position is located in Lehi Utah, no remote work options available.
