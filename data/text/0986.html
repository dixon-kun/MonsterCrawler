General Description

Samsung has developed a proprietary ad platform that leverages unique first party TV data to help brands connect to their audience as they explore content across desktop, mobile, tablets and our SMART TVs. The Samsung Ad Platform delivers high-quality audience targeting powered by three key components: first-party audience data at scale, world-class data science, and brand-safe cross-device ad inventory. Reporting directly to the Vice President of US Sales, the Regional Sales Director (RSD) will be a key member of the US management team and will be responsible for hiring and scaling a team, generating new business and contributing to the go to market strategy for all products and solutions within your region. The individual should have a passion for TV driven data and have a pulse for new multi-channel advertising technologies. This Sales leader must be able to think strategically, anticipate how buying decisions are made, uncover the business needs of key clients and have a proven ability to help the team close deals. Additionally, this executive will be the consummate team-player and have the ability to work collaboratively across the sales organization within a fluid and fast paced environment. Samsung is looking for both a sales leader and a sales operator with a clear execution-oriented ability to visualize, formulate strategy, and actualize results. The RSD will be a highly visible leader both inside and outside of the organization and representing our almost 80 year world class global brand. The Regional Sales Director position is based in New York with domestic US travel as needed and periodic visits to headquarters in Korea. If you're interested in joining a rapidly growing team working to build a unique, world-class advertising sales organization with a relentless focus on design and customer experience, you've come to the right place.
