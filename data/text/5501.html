SUMMARY: Plans, connects, designs, schedules, and deploys enterprise data for decision makers throughout the organization. Develops, monitors, and maintains ETL processes, departmental data policies and procedures, and the data warehouse design. Provides leadership, strategic direction, subject matter expertise and oversight for Capital Bank's key enterprise data initiatives and ensures end to end technical delivery of Enterprise Data Management of large and complex projects.

The following statements are intended to describe the general nature and level of work performed; they are not intended to be an exhaustive or exclusive list of the required responsibilities, duties and skills; Management retains the discretion to add to or change the duties and requirements of this position at any time, with or without notice, as business needs dictate.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

1. Leads strategic initiatives concerning the build, maintenance, and optimization of the Enterprise Data Warehouse.

2. Plans, creates, coordinates, and deploys the Enterprise Date Warehouse.

3. Consolidates and optimizes current data warehouse infrastructure.

4. Creates and documents best practices for data loading and extraction.

5. Designs and implements ETL procedures for intake of data from both internal and outside sources; as well as ensure data is verified and accurate.

6. Designs and implements ETL processes and data architecture to ensure proper functioning of analytics, as well as client's or third-party's reporting environments and dashboards.

7. Serves as a subject matter expert when collaborating with business and technology stakeholders to ensure data warehouse architecture development and utilization.

8. Facilitates requirements gathering using expert listening skills to meet the immediate and future needs of business customers.

9. Monitors and performs database performance analysis; designs and extends data marts, meta data, and data models.

10. Supports the development and validation required through the lifecycle of the Data Warehouse and Business Intelligence systems; maintains user connectivity, and provide adequate security for the Enterprise data warehouse.

11. Interacts with senior leadership concerning the alignment of enterprise data capabilities with the long-term business goals.

12. Maintains effective communication between Enterprise Information Management, IT staff, and information stakeholders throughout the organization.

13. Complete, pass and maintain an up-to-date status for all positional and company required compliance and regulatory courses by assigned due date(s).

14. Participate in special projects and assignments and performs other duties as assigned.

The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.

QUALIFICATIONS:

* Bachelor's Degree in Business, Information Sciences, Quantitative Social Sciences, or other quantitative field with 7-10 years of experience in large data environments and broad experience in data architecture, ETL, data quality, data warehouse, data governance or an equivalent combination of education and experience.

* Graduate degree in a quantitative or data sciences field, or an MBA are preferred, but not required.

* Experience in Financial Services is preferred, ideally interspersed with experience in other industries in similar roles.

* Strong foundational knowledge of data warehousing concepts.

* Programming Experience: SQL or SAS programming

* Proficient knowledge of Microsoft Windows operating system & Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and Outlook.

* Ability to read and listen, understand and communicate information and ideas in a clear and understandable manner, both orally and in writing with senior executives, business unit management and staff, state and federal regulators and officials and significant business partners.

* Strong ability to read, analyze and interpret financial reports and legal documents; performs complex calculations; effectively researches, analyzes and evaluates information to make decisions, solve problems and achieve goals; strong project management skills desired.

* Certificates & Licenses: None.

SUPERVISORY RESPONSIBILITIES: Exercises the usual authority of a manager concerning hiring, staffing, training, performance appraisals, promotions, salary recommendations and terminations. Provides leadership by example at all times, through support and endorsement of bank policy.

CONTROL RELATED RESPONSIBILITIES: Become knowledgeable regarding the department's internal controls as outlined in the policies and procedures and comply with all control requirements.

COMPLIANCE: Ability to comprehend and adhere to Federal and State banking regulations, including compliance with the Bank's Bank Secrecy Act/Anti Money Laundering (BSA/AML) program. Follows all Company policies and procedures, and successfully participates in regulatory and Company training requirements via multiple and variable delivery methods.

SAFETY, SECURITY and CONFIDENTIALITY: This position requires keeping complete confidentiality of all customer transactions and information, and paying close attention to security within and around the bank's premises at all times.

The physical demands, travel and work environment described here are representative of those that must be met by a teammate to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.

PHYSICAL DEMANDS: While performing the duties of this job, the teammate is regularly required to use hands to finger, handle or feel; reach with hands and arms; and talk or hear. The teammate frequently is required to sit. This position regularly uses a computer (keyboard/mouse/screen) and telephone. The teammate is occasionally required to stand; walk; climb or balance; and stoop, kneel, crouch, or crawl. The teammate must occasionally lift and/or move up to 25 pounds. Specific vision abilities required by this job include close vision, and ability to adjust focus.

TRAVEL: Travel throughout the assigned geographic area and trips outside the area with overnight stays may be required and is expected to be approximately 10-15% of employees work time.

WORK ENVIRONMENT: The work is performed in a normal office environment. The noise level in the work environment is usually low to moderate.
