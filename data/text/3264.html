Qualifications

* Experience with data platform development and data ingestion (e.g., using AWS)

* Exposure to a broad range of advanced analytical tools and approaches (e.g., spark beyond)

* Experience with UI/UX software product development including data visualization, customer workflow mapping, and SaaS deployment

* Undergraduate degree with an excellent academic record; MBA or other graduate degree a plus

* 7+ years of experience, with a minimum of 3 years of leadership experience within a consulting organization or software/solutions company

* Proven success in entrepreneurial environment

* Ability to manage multiple ongoing projects in a dynamic, complex and fast paced environment

* Ability to influence and mobilize colleagues without direct reporting relationships

* Strong oral and written communication skills to articulate business impact from software and expertise

* Ability to navigate and thrive within a complex matrix organization

* Persistent and execution-oriented

Who You'll Work With

You'll work with our Banking Analytics team in New York, or an alternative East Coast city, along with a diverse set of banking and non-banking institutions, including universal banks, securities firms, national banks, exchanges, and information providers. Banking Solutions is part of McKinsey Solutions and McKinsey's New Ventures.

Our work encompasses overall strategies for the wholesale business and for specific products, client segments and geographies; organizational issues; performance improvement; and mergers and alliances. We also undertake work on client issues in information technology, risk management, branding and customer relationship management.

McKinsey helps a broad range of banking and consumer finance firms navigate a dynamically changing environment, in which regulatory constraints, consumer deleveraging and evolving channel dynamics present significant obstacles to profitability. Our approach to supporting clients is customized and collaborative, and combines deep industry expertise and experience with proprietary quantitative analytics and research. We help banks build the capabilities they need to succeed in the long term.

New Ventures is a recently formed part of McKinsey focused on fostering innovations driven by analytics, design thinking, mobile, social and other technologies, and integrating them into our client work. It is a foundation for—and expands our investment in—our entrepreneurial culture. A primary objective is to offer practical solutions and tools that help people at all levels of an organization generate insights they need to address both strategic and day-to-day decisions. These assets complement McKinsey's traditional client service and are delivered in many ways, including intensive deep dives, subscriptions to software tools, integrated workshops, hands-on training programs and project-based engagements. The design of the teams and operating models enables a true "startup" culture inside the nearly 100-year-old McKinsey, with all the benefits of both small-team agility and broad-scale experience.

What You'll Do

You will be responsible for developing strategic and operational plans to build a distinctive asset or deliver an expertise.

You will provide strategic input to the development of the product roadmap and work with tech accelerator and engineering group to drive the execution. You will be responsible for building demand for a portion of the Solution internally and awareness of the Solution externally (e.g. industry events, presentations, workshops). Responsibilities include managing all or a portion of the P&L, contributing as needed to client development and delivery activities and overseeing execution against a strategic plan. Additional activities will include idea generation for application of advanced analytics and "big data" to financial services, new product development working with our data science teams and developers to productize key business use cases in banking, and client development and relationship management. You'll also have exposure to banking practice leaders across the firm.

McKinsey's Banking Practice Analytics Team is a dynamic and quickly growing platform within the firm serving some of the top banks in the world. Our unique value proposition lies in an end-to-end approach for our clients ranging from identifying sources of value from big data all the way through to the adoption phase.

We help our clients achieve transformational change by developing data-driven insights specific to the Banking sector. Our work covers a wide variety of strategic and tactical engagements.
