        Azure Technical Evangelist
        Cloud Computing is the next step toward ubiquitous and pervasive computing and Microsoft Azure is Microsoft's world-class cloud computing platform. It can be used to build, host, and scale a variety of cloud-based applications and workloads and is an open platform enabling hosting of Windows and Linux virtual machines while also supporting modern programming languages such as .NET, Java, Node.js, and PHP. Join the US Developer Evangelism &amp; Experience (DX) technical evangelism team as an Azure Technical Evangelist and be front and center in the transformation to cloud-based architectures while at the same time playing an influential role in transforming Microsoft to a mobile first, cloud first company.
        As an Azure Technical Evangelist (TE), your primary responsibility will be to ensure our ISV partners are successful with Azure and to help them move customer workloads to Azure. The TE is a technical partner-facing role accountable for the end-to-end cloud technical architecture and solution deployment experience. In this role, you will own technical partner engagement leading Envisioning Sessions, Architecture Design Sessions (ADS), implementation projects and Proofs of Concept (POC).
        The ideal candidate will have experience in customer- and partner-facing roles and have demonstrated success leading technical and economic value discussions with senior executives to drive key decisions and implementation. Candidates need to possess the ability to impact and influence customers and partners with a high degree of autonomy, energy, flexibility and the drive to create real and measurable business results as evidenced by deploying qualified Cloud ISV partner solutions in Microsoft Azure.
        The Azure Technical Evangelist is responsible for:
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Serving as a trusted advisor to top ISV partners
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Executing rapid technical feasibility studies for moving identified workloads to Azure
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Building technical and security architectures in Azure for identified workloads
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Selecting a migration approach to move workloads to Azure or architecting a greenfield development or production platform for new applications
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Executing Architecture Design Sessions (ADS) or Proofs of Concept (POC) Workshops
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Driving onboarding plan in conjunction with integration partners
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Documenting and sharing technical best practices and insights with Microsoft product groups and the USDX Technical Evangelist community
        * &amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp&amp;nbsp Ensuring partner plan execution and Azure consumption targets are met
