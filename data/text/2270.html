Job Description
Data Analytics Scientist

Position Overview/Description:

Ford Motor Company's Global Data Insight & Analytics (GDIA) organization is looking for an analytics scientistthat has a deep understanding of data analysis and modeling. You will have the opportunity to work with some ofthe brightest global subject matter experts that are transforming the automobile industry as a member of the Advanced Operational Analytics team. In this role, you will lead initiatives to leverage unique data streams using predictive analytics and machine learning to quantify the customer value of vehicle technologies. You will also support the development of optimization models that provide decision support to other Ford organizations, such as Product Development, Strategy, and Sustainability.

Responsibilities


You will be responsible for providing the data analytics support for departmental tasks, including assembly of data for building models and performing predictive/prescriptive analytics. More specifically, you will:
  • Ingest large data sets from a variety of sources (Hadoop, SQL, Teradata, Oracle, and 3 rd party sources in the public domain)
  • Identify key data elements to extract/decode/analyze
  • Lead the development of predictive analytics and machine learning algorithms using business critical datasets
  • Use data analytics to gain insight into customer usage patterns and environmental conditions to quantifying expected customer benefits, such as fuel economy improvements, cost reductions, durability improvements, and additional non-vehicle benefits
  • Work collaboratively with various business groups and regionsto disseminate critical information and develop strategic opportunities
