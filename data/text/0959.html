JOB TITLE:      DIRECTOR, BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT OPERATORS
LOCATION:      NEW YORK, CHICAGO, OR RESTON VA
REPORTS TO:   VP BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT

JOB FUNCTION:

comScore Inc. is a recognized innovator that's changing the way companies develop and evaluate their marketing tactics and strategies across all platforms. This is an exciting and rewarding opportunity for a business development professional with strong understanding of marketing and marketing research/analytics within the online & TV advertising media industries. In this role, you'll be at the forefront of maximizing comScore's relationships with key MVPD & Telecom partners and clients. The qualified professional will have an innate ability to forge and nurture senior strategic relationships across our client and prospect organizations. The role will encompass a broad range of functions including but not limited to strategy, sales, relationship development, data and analytic utilization, market research and development, ad technical/ business process improvements. The Director develops the strategy and associated tactics for identifying and then personally closing new and recurring business opportunities for a select set of clients


ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:

• The Director will support the company’s goals through a range of sales and account management activities.
• Forge strong client relationships with key decision makers to solidify comScore as a true consultative partner for business and marketing insights.
• Develop account strategies to grow business within our MVPD and Telecom clients, with a particular focus in online/ television & OTT advertising, marketing research, and programming.
• As your clients day-to-day contact, develop a strong understanding of the clients business and demonstrate how comScores services can assist them to achieve their business goalsPrepare written documentation as required including client presentations, trainings, and contracts.
• Identify, track and manage key performance and operational metrics for MVPD based solutions that help guide comScore teams for optimal service level performance.
• Ensure project quality and delivery, working with product management and other internal teams.
• Become an expert in the comScore multiplatform products, understanding not just the methodologies, but also the applications of the products to specific client / operator use cases.
• Work with core comScore product, research, and data science teams to escalate and resolve issues related to products, research or reporting.
• Prioritize new solutions and align operator product roadmap expectations.
• Work both autonomously and within a team setting.
• Regularly update Salesforce with prospect and customer information.
• Travel as needed to support the operators on-site needs across key locations.
• Internally travel to comScore key offices to promote learning, collaboration, and camaraderie.
• Attend key trade and industry events to support comScore and operators.

QUALIFICATIONS:

• Minimum of bachelor's degree and 8 years professional sales management experience.
• A highly aggressive and analytical professional with a proven track record of success in achieving revenue plans, and cultivating client relationships to close new client sales.
• Specific experience managing or selling audience measurement, advertising effectiveness measurement services and/or custom research solutions to MVPDs, media companies, advertisers and advertising agencies
• Experience and established senior contacts and relationships within the media/advertising industry required
• Understanding of market research (quantitative for TV and digital) and other related industry knowledge and how information can be applied to help clients achieve their goals
• A high intellect individual who combines proven sales ability with analytical excellence, superior communication skills (presentation and written) and an ability to forge strong client relationships
• Technical background and aptitude a plus
• Highly organized but flexible with the ability to work in a fast-paced environment under tight deadlines with demanding quality requirements
• Strong listening skillsExperienced with MS Office, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Salesforce, and the ability to learn and utilize software sales tools specific to comScore

comScore, Inc. (OTC: SCOR) is a leading cross-platform measurement company that precisely measures audiences, brands and consumer behavior everywhere.
comScore completed its merger with Rentrak Corporation in January 2016, to create the new model for a dynamic, cross-platform world. Built on precision and innovation, our unmatched data footprint combines proprietary digital, TV and movie intelligence with vast demographic details to quantify consumers’ multiscreen behavior at massive scale. This approach helps media companies monetize their complete audiences and allows marketers to reach these audiences more effectively. With more than 3,200 clients and global footprint in more than 75 countries, comScore is delivering the future of measurement. For more information on comScore, please visit comscore.com.
