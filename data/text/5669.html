Job Description

Ashley Furniture Industries, Inc. is the largest manufacturer of furniture in the world. Established in 1945, Ashley offers one of the industry’s broadest product assortments to retail partners in 123 countries.  We’re a privately-held, family-owned business that offers great stability, a record of unparalleled success, and a company culture that’s steeped in continuous improvement.  Due to our ambitious growth plans, we’re always on the lookout for dynamic professionals to join our team.

Our Digital Marketing Team works in a state-of-the-art office facility located in the heart of the historic Ybor City surrounded by unique culture, architecture, and dining just minutes from the Crosstown, I4, and I275.

We are seeking a Director - Engagement Marketing who can engage existing and potential customers at the right time with the right message.  This extraordinary technical marketer will help improve and manage automated lifecycle marketing programs such as email focused on driving omni retail growth through intelligent segmentation, creative optimization, experimentation, and data science.  The incumbent will manage both our internal Engagement Marketing Team, as well as supporting vendor relationships.

The Director - Engagement Marketing is accountable for driving revenue and achieving ROI and contribution targets, as well as managing the overall health of our Engagement Marketing Program.

Overall, the Director - Engagement Marketing contributes to the Ashley high performance culture by modeling our core values and overseeing engagement marketing activities that keep the Ashley brand as the #1 home furnishings market leader in style, selection, quality and value.

Primary Job Functions

·         Develops and implements a clear engagement marketing program vision and strategy, including segmentation, personalization, and customer lifecycle, that achieves multichannel retail objectives

·         Works cross-functionally with our CRM, data science, creative, development, and external vendor teams to build and expand our automated email and engagement marketing campaigns

·         Lead and develop the testing, experiment and measurement/attribution methodology and structure by which we develop best in class engagement campaigns to run across multiple channels

·         Help optimize our multi-channel communication strategy addressing our complete customer lifecycle to drive continuous improvement

·         Apply email and marketing communication best practices and executional excellence to ensure brand communication integrity

·         Manages third party ESP and marketing communication vendor relationships including daily, weekly, monthly communication and ensuring all agreed to professional services are being provided.

·         Demonstrates the company’s core and growth values in performance of all job functions.

Job Requirements

Job Qualifications

Education:

• BA/BS in Business Administration, Marketing, Technology or related degree.

• Equivalent combination of education and experience may be considered.

Experience:

• 5+ years experience managing direct response, email marketing, or broader engagement campaigns, including strategy, implementation and analytics/reporting, managing a database of 500,000 or greater contacts.

• One or more years’ experience in a B2B or B2C marketing technology function in a high volume eCommerce environment required, preferably with a top 100 multi-channel retailer; furniture retail a plus.

• Experience working with best-in-breed Email Service Providers (ESPs); with expertise in using relevant ESP tools.

• Experience with API integrations with ESPs and MAPs to enable triggered messages, dynamic content and marketing automation.

• Experience with rapid A/B testing and experimentation to identify highest performing customer segments, offers, campaigns verses controls, etc.

Knowledge, Skills:

• Knowledge of best practices for email creative/templates, segmentation, dynamic content, testing methodologies, deliverability, list growth, etc.

• Knowledge of HTML and ability to learn technical systems and in-house tools quickly.

• Knowledge of CAN SPAM regulations.

• Strong project management, organizational and critical thinking skills.

• Strong MS Office skills, especially in Excel.

• Strong skills in verbal and written communication.
