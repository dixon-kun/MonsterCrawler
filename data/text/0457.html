Director of Data Science 29.12.2016 NYC New York

Dailymotion is one of the world's leading video-sharing platforms. It attracts over 300 million unique visitors and 3 billion videos views worldwide per month by offering the best content from users, independent content creators and premium partners.

As part of its growing activities, Dailymotion is looking to enforce its programmatic and monetization product by building its own video ad stack to deliver new monetization solution for its own ecosystem around online, mobile and TV, and provide innovative marketing solutions for advertisers.

Job description

As the Director of Data Science, you will:

Lead, hire, manage and mentor a global team of Data Scientists.

Working with fast-paced initiatives, overseeing projects from idea conception and strategy development to deployment, production, and operations.

Review, direct, guide the analytical work of entire team

Processing, cleansing, and verifying the integrity of the data used for analysis

Selecting and engineering features to build and optimize prediction and classification systems

Data mining using state-of-the-art methods

Enhancing data collection procedures to include information that is relevant for building analytic systems

Job Requirements

A Masters or PhD in computer science, statistics or a related field (Mathematics, Physics…)

Understanding of online advertising technologies and ecosystem (Ad-Tech Background)

Predictive modelling experience

Management experience

Excellent understanding and experience of machine learning techniques (eg k-NN, Naive Bayes, SVM, Decision Forests, Neural Networks, etc.)

Experience with common data science toolkits, such as R, NLP, Weka, NumPy, MatLab.

Proficiency in at least one high level programming language like Java, Scala, or Python

Excellence in at least one of these is highly desirable

Great business sense and communication skills

Proficiency in using query languages (SQL, Hive, Pig…) and in MySQL or NoSQL databases (MongoDB, Cassandra, HBase)

Consultative nature, with a proven track record of using data to provide actionable business results

Data-oriented personality

Information about the job

Location: New York City (US)

Type of contract: full and permanent
