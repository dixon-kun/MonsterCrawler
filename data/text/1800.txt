             Job Title
            Data Scientist
             Position Summary
              Responsible for modeling complex health care inquiries, discovering insights and identifying opportunities through the use of statistical, algorithmic, data-mining and visualization techniques.
              Integrates and analyzes large, varied datasets and communicates results of large-scale analytics.
             Required Education
              Master's of science degree in biostatistics, epidemiology, health economics, computer science or a related field,
               OR
              A doctorate in epidemiology, statistics or biostatistics.
             Required Work Experience
              Five years of relevant quantitative and qualitative research and analytics experience.
              Ph.D. training will be counted as three years of experience.
             Required Skills and Abilities
              Experience with supervised (statistical) and unsupervised (machine learning) modeling.
              Advanced SQL skills (e.g., proficiency in windowing function) and ability to experience querying large, complex data sets, including multiple joins.
              Experience with data management in relational and non-relational (NoSQL) data sources.
              Experience working on Natural Language Processing (NLP) in health care.
              Strong programming skills relevant to data-science and big data frameworks, such as R, Python, SAS (preferred R) and data visualization tools such as Qlik or Tableau.
              Expert knowledge of statistical techniques, including statistical analysis, quantitative analytics, forecasting/predictive analytics and multivariate testing within the health care industry.
              Solid foundation in analytics, with proven history of data-oriented project completion.
              Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
              Strong written, oral and presentation, communications, negotiation and interpersonal skills, including the ability to communicate technical approaches to all levels of staff, management and leadership.
              Proven ability leading multidisciplinary teams to achieve successful product integrations within a matrix management environment.
              Demonstrates and promotes a workplace culture of innovation, high performance, accountability, commitment, respect and collaboration.
              Demonstrated ability to prioritize, manage time, multi-task and work with minimal supervision.
              Results-focused, proactive and detail-oriented.
              Strong knowledge of multiple platform capabilities and relational database concepts.
              Expert computer skills, including PowerPoint presentations, database management, SAS programming, software and hardware configuration.
