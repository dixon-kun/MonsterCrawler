Today, there were over 500 million Tweets, over 85% of U.S. Consumers used their mobile device while watching TV, and within the next four years, women will control $28 Trillion in annual consumer spending. At Nielsen, you will help analyze and understand what all this means to our consumers, and how it truly influences and drives consumer behavior in what people watch and what people buy. Nielsen is the science behind what's next for our clients.

To explore What People Watch, Nielsen measures digital video viewing, such as the latest television shows streamed through a laptop or viral YouTube clips viewed on smartphones. In order to determine the audience demographics behind YouTube content, Nielsen partners closely with YouTube by combining their immense but incomplete big data with small, high quality data from Nielsen's desktop, smartphone, and tablet panels. With these two rich data sources, there is plenty of analysis to be done. As an intern, you will get the chance to explore both datasets, brainstorm on the latest and most innovative research methodologies (eg. statistical distributions or machine learning algorithms), design a model, and recommend whether it is appropriate to implement your model into final reporting to share with clients.

Responsibilities

* Research the latest industry and academic methods and determine their applicability to Nielsen's methodology

* Design an experiment and code/run your analysis in Python or R

* Summarize and present your research findings to both technical and nontechnical audiences

Selection Criteria

* Undergraduate/ Masters Student majoring in Statistics, Data Science, Math, or related fields

* Anticipated graduation in 2018

* Statistical coding experience with R, Python preferred

* Strong communication skills key

* Candidate is detail-oriented, curious, and passionate about data quality and statistical methods

* Availability full time June - August 2017 in New York City

* Permanent work authorization in the U.S.

About Nielsen

Nielsen is a global performance management company that provides a comprehensive understanding of what consumers Watch and Buy. Nielsen's Watch segment provides media and advertising clients with Total Audience measurement services across all devices where content — video, audio and text — is consumed. The Buy segment offers consumer packaged goods manufacturers and retailers the industry's only global view of retail performance measurement. By integrating information from its Watch and Buy segments and other data sources, Nielsen provides its clients with both world-class measurement as well as analytics that help improve performance.

Nielsen, an S&P 500 company (NYSE: NLSN), has operations in over 100 countries that cover more than 90% of the world's population.
