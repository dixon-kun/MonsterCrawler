            General Summary/Overview
           Partners Healthcare Systems&#8217; (PHS) Division of Research IS &amp; Computing, Enterprise Research Infrastructure and Services (ERIS http://rc.partners.org), in collaboration with the BWH Department of Cardiology and the One Brave Idea, is immediately seeking a Data/Database Engineer. This position works within multi-disciplinary teams that build data processing pipelines and Hadoop/Spark-based analytics jobs. Using your knowledge of distributed computing systems and sys/ops development, you will specialize in data tools and data methods to facilitate biomedical research and personalized medicine. Centered at the interface between the ERIS&#8217; Scientific Computing and Big-Data Analytics service and our scientific communities, the role includes both implementation and consultation aspects.
           We are seeking someone with the proven ability to help teams and projects successfully accomplish their technology and data objectives. Key characteristics are an eagerness to learn and apply new technologies, versatility and breadth in technical skills, and communication and customer service skills. Candidates are not expected to be deeply knowledgeable in all areas, but must demonstrate the ability and desire to learn and to support a large and diverse environment in its use of technology. Ideal candidates thrive on variety and innovation in their daily work, on interaction with customers who are world-renowned leaders in their scientific field, and on working with a wide range of technologies in a decentralized non-standard environment (academic).
           ERIS enables and supports the highly successful and innovative research programs of the largest teaching hospitals in the nation--Massachusetts General (MGH), Brigham and Women&#8217;s (BWH), McLean and Spaulding Rehabilitation Hospitals--with their more than 3200 grant-sponsored programs in the biomedical sciences, from basic to clinical and applied research.
