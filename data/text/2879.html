Job Summary

In this exciting and challenging role, you will be responsible for developing BI and Analytic solutions ranging from basic reports to analytic-driven applications. The ideal candidate has strong technical skills but is also analytical, client focused and detail oriented. He/she takes pride in the product being delivered and is able to create a consumer quality user experience, blending a strong sense of aesthetics with best practices in UI/UX. This role will utilize a variety of technologies including but not limited to Cognos 10+, MS Reporting Services, MS Analysis Services and Tableau. The candidate must be able to build and sustain strong partnerships with business users, internal engagement managers and data architects.

Primary Activities and Responsibilities

  • Create BI & Analytic solutions that access various data sources including data warehouses, data marts, cubes, and relational databases.
  • Design, develop and maintain BI content including reports, dashboards, widgets and portal pages.
  • Design, develop and maintain Meta Data Models (Cognos Framework Manager, and others).
  • Design and develop OLAP cubes using Microsoft SQL Server Analysis Services
  • Design and implement security models used by BI Content
  • Perform end to end performance tuning.
  • Work closely with business users to translate high level concepts to actionable BI and Analytic Solutions.
  • Work closely with business users to perform research and analysis, document and refine business and technical requirements.
  • Perform data analysis to identify potential issues (i.e. data quality) or opportunities (i.e. trends) throughout the solution delivery life cycle.
  • Assist with application administration, internal user support and training as needed.

Minimum Qualifications

  • Bachelors degree
  • 3-5 years developing BI and Analytic content
  • 3-5 years working with Oracle DBMS and MS SQL Server in a Data Warehouse environment

Knowledge and Skills

  • Superior analysis and problem solving skills; does not give up easily when confronted with roadblocks/challenges
  • Strong research skills; does not readily accept 'known' technical limitations, looks for work around and alternative approaches
  • Thrives under sometimes nebulous circumstances; is able to spot and capitalize on opportunities
  • Experience developing business and technical specifications as well as technical and architectural documentation.
  • Working Knowledge of data models commonly used for BI solutions (data marts, star/snowflake schemas).
  • Must be able to design, analyze, develop, code, test, debug and performance tune BI content.
  • Must be able to communicate effectively (written & oral) with team members and business partners in an onshore + offshore environment.
  • Experience with OLAP development required
  • Superior query building skills using SQL and MDX against Oracle and SQL Server databases
  • Knowledge of Agile development methodologies
  • Ability to balance the design, development, and deployment of new solutions while supporting and enhancing existing enterprise solutions
  • Some knowledge of application programming languages (JAVA,.NET, etc) a plus.
  • Knowledge of and/or ability to learn Data Science tools (SPSS, R, etc.) and techniques a plus.

Education: Bachelors
