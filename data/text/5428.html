2017 Summer MetLife Tech University (MTU): Intern Associate

MetLife Global Technology and Operations (GTO) is seeking innovative, enthusiastic, technically-oriented individuals to participate in its paid, 10-week MetLife Tech University (MTU) Internship Program.

MetLife GTO Overview:

MetLife is a global leader in the insurance industry serving customers in nearly 50 countries. MetLife's Global Technology & Operations (GTO) division is the foundation for MetLife's global business strategy. GTO is comprised of a diverse team of engineers, developers, business analysts, and project managers committed to delivering innovative solutions and infrastructure to help enable MetLife to deliver a world-class customer experience. We have thousands of employees around the world, collaborating as one team to serve our close to 100 million customers. By leveraging our scale, simplifying operations, and leveraging cutting-edge technology, we're earning the right to be the customer's first choice. For more information on MetLife GTO, visit www.metlife.com/careers.

MTU Internship Program Overview:

* 10 week paid internship

* Available for rising juniors and seniors with an expected graduation date between December 2017 and May 2019

* Gain technical insight, build professional and business skills, and help us deliver customer-centric solutions.

* Work along and network with engineers, developers, business analysts, and other professionals

* Complete an intern project highlighting your experiences and impact. At the conclusion of your program, present your contributions to peers, managers, and GTO leaders

* High-potential interns will be encouraged to apply for the MTU Professional Program for full-time employment following graduation

MTU Interns will work in one of the many US locations. Previous interns have worked in the following:

* Bridgewater, NJ

* Cary, NC

* Clarks Summit, PA

* Greenville, SC

* Long Island City, NY

* New York, NY

* Troy, NY

* Oriskany, NY

* Tampa, FL

* Troy, NY

MTU Interns will be placed in one of the following tracks based on skills and interests:

* Application & Software Development

* Business Systems Analysis

* Database Administration

* Data Science

* Finance

* Infrastructure & Network Engineering

* IT Security

* Operations

* Project Management & Project Lead

Job Requirements:

* Minimum GPA 3.0

* Ability to work full 10 weeks from June 5, 2017 to August 11, 2017

* Must be currently enrolled in a Bachelor's degree program at an accredited college/university with expected graduation date between December 2017 and May 2019

* Students academically involved in major that relates to their field of interest

* Relevant campus involvement, work experience, and/or community service experience

* Strong technical curiosity and entrepreneurial mindset

* Excellent problem solving and analysis skills

* Ability to work independently as well as in a team environment
