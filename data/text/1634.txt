          Function:
         Under minimal supervision, develops standard work and drives completion of tasks in support of the Datacentricity Program within the Commercial and Industrial Business Unit. May work as a liaison between Operations and IT to assure goals and objectives are met within an arranged timeframe. Defines and manages tasks associated with data migration from legacy systems to the Information Platform. Ensures these solutions meet or exceed program and business expectations.
          Responsibilities:
         1.Assists with the development and management of new data handling and migration processes and with the improvement of current processes in support of UL&#8217;s new datacentric environment.
         2.Coordinates and collaborates with Industry Leads as needed to gain agreement on approach for the forward and reverse migration of each industry&#8217;s assets (models) and associated attributes into the Information Platform.
         3.Works with IT system support and the datacentricity team to resolve questions, problems, and enhancement requests in support of data migration efforts.
         4.Consults with business and technical teams to refine necessary requirements.
         5.Supports the coordination of tasks directly related to migration activities, including pre and post-data cleansing and non-PCD migration tasks.
         6.Develops, documents and shares best practices in the form of Standard Work for all aspects of data migration workflow.
         7.Supports the Global Datacentricity Training as necessary.
         8.Supports the development of monitoring reports and validation processes for the product data and overall Datacentricity Program.
         9.Supports the development of technical enhancements including trouble shooting solutions, business user testing, and production deployment with a focus on data migration workflow.
         10.Assists with the mass migration of product data from existing sources to the Information Platform.
         11. Performs other duties as directed.
