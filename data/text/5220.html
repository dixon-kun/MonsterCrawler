Senior Product Manager, IoT Platforms

Location: Atlanta, GA

Honeywell is transforming from a traditional industrial company to a contemporary digital industrial business, harnessing the power of cloud, big data/analytics, Internet of things (IoT), and design thinking. We are leading change that brings value to our customers, partners, and shareholders through the creation of innovative software and data-driven products and services. Honeywell IT is at the forefront of this change and we are looking for a team member who thrives on challenging the status quo, embraces new technology and intelligent risk taking, has passion for innovation, is results-oriented and excels in a fast paced environment.

The IoT Platform Product Management team will be responsible for the overall coordination across various product management, marketing, engineering and IT teams across different business units, centers of excellence and IT delivery teams. The product management team will lead defining the next generation of cloud-native software services for big data, analytics and mobile apps, and collaborate with different engineering teams to build a platform of reusable building blocks and micro-services. Our goal is to provide a foundation for our businesses to innovate faster and drive our competitive advantage.

As a member of the Honeywell IoT Platform Products team, the Technical Product Manager (TPM) will be passionate about defining and driving the vision, strategy and execution for connected solutions, analytics and hyper-scale cloud-native big data solutions for IoT platforms.

This position involves close interaction with product managers, architects, development leads, and key business groups and their IT. Technical Product Managers (TPMs) work closely with the Product Managers (PMs) to develop a product backlog and participate in roadmap planning. The Technical Product Manager will interact closely with Engineering and has the strong technical competence to help Engineering understand the intent of requirements so that they can build better specifications. Once platform products are built, the TPM will enable other functions and business groups by delivering training, demos and authoring product capabilities documents/knowledge base and participating in relevant knowledge communities. The TPM evangelizes the vision and capabilities of the platform with various stakeholders.
