At Edmunds we’re driven to make car buying easier.  Ever since we began publishing printed car guides in the 60’s, the company has been in the business of trust, innovating ways to empower and support car shoppers.  When Edmunds launched the car industry’s first Internet site in 1994, we established a leadership position online and have never looked back.  Now, as one of the most trusted review sites on the Internet, millions of visitors use our research, shopping and buying tools every month to make an easy and informed decision on their next car.  For consumers, we bring peace of mind. For dealers, we make tools to help them solve their problems and sell more cars.  How do we do it,  you ask? The key ingredients are our enthusiastic employees, progressive company culture and cutting-edge technology.  Want to join the team?  Read on to find out how!

What You’re Applying For:

The Market Research and Consumer Insights Manager will work independently and in conjunction with the Director of Market Research and Customer Insights and VP of Corporate Strategy to build research capabilities and customer insights at Edmunds.  Leveraging qualitative and quantitative primary research and secondary research, the Manager will generate strategic insights to help the business identify new areas of opportunity and product, experience, and sales enhancements.  In addition, the Manager will be responsible for identifying needs, leading, analyzing, interpreting and communicating the results of research efforts, and working with teams around the organization to turn their insights into action.  Ultimately, the Manager is responsible for leading critical and highly impactful research efforts for Edmunds, covering customer, dealer and OEM needs, market trends and competitive intelligence.

What You’ll Do :

  • Conduct market research on and build knowledge of key trends in the automotive and tech industries to generate strategic insights
  • Conduct end-to-end primary research projects, including defining the project scope, developing survey/research, fielding research, managing vendors, analyzing results, and delivering/communicating key insights
  • Effectively connect primary and secondary research with internal data to uncover unique and actionable insights, partnering with Analytics and Data Science as needed
  • Analyze outputs from Edmunds' research tools (including customer and dealer panels, NPS survey, Brand Tracker, and survey creation tools) and work with the Director and executives across the company to develop action plans from these results
  • Help craft compelling executive-level stories with surprising insights
  • Act as a thought partner to the business leaders of the company to help provide a strategic research perspective on critical questions
  • Bring your experience with other companies and best practices to help Edmunds build a best-in-class research approach and focus
  • Keep abreast of new developments, methods, and technologies in research to expand Edmunds' repository of effective techniques
  • Proactively identify areas of opportunity that we could support with primary or secondary research
  • Work with teams around the company to build understanding of the uses and limitations of research and when and how to best engage the research team

Challenges :

The Market Research and Customer Insights Manager will be responsible for digging into questions such as:

  • What does the customer buying journey look like? What are the key activities, needs, emotions, pain-points at each stage of the journey?
  • Who are Edmunds' main competitors? What products do they offer, what is their share of the market, and what are their value propositions? How are we differentiated from them?
  • Who are our customers? What are the key needs for each kind of customer? What job does Edmunds do for them?
  • What creative research techniques can we use to get to the next order of insight about our consumers, dealers, and OEMs?

He or she will also be responsible for the dissemination of this information to the rest of the company in a way that is fun, engaging and memorable.

What You Need :

  • Master's degree strongly in field requiring extensive primary and secondary research preferred, BA or BS considered with strong experience
  • 6+ years' experience in customer insights, market research, analytics
  • Superior analytical ability coupled with ability to quickly formulate non-obvious insights from analysis
  • Action-oriented and focused on the implication of work, not just the output
  • Outstanding written and verbal communication skills, including comfort presenting to executives
  • Demonstrated ability to work cross-functionally with range of people with different styles, backgrounds and experiences
  • History of strong interpersonal skills and being a team player who quickly builds trust and credibility through relationships
  • Exceptional ability to read and understand people, to glean insights beyond just what is said
  • understanding of tech and website structure, with experience translating research into design or commercialization efforts
  • Demonstrated success in managing research, research vendors and clients
  • Strong project management skills
  • Experience in either tech or consumer products strongly preferred
  • Technical and software experience required: Tableau and SPSS (or other statistical software tool) required, SQL and Redshift, or similar, highly desirable
  • Research tool experience required: need to have experience with or ability to learn Qualtrics (if no direct experience, then experience with Maritz, Medallia, or similar required)
  • Able to travel as needed; only minimal-moderate travel anticipated
