           Marketing Scientist - Attribution Analytics
         You will be a key leader on the team that develops and optimizes actionable analytics solutions to help our clients address their marketing challenges such as marketing effectiveness, ROI, brand equity impact, channel effectiveness and pricing.

          (Even the ones who are terrified of math and analytics)
          What you&#8217;ll be doing:
          Build elegant, easy to understand analytic attribution models
          Enhance MarketShare&#8217;s digital analytics thought leadership in data-mining and big-data analytics applications, specifically related to the AdTech Ecosystem
          Ensure rigorous quality control over data, analyses, metrics
          Own onboarding of client data into our attribution solution by deep diving into cookie level data sets
          Lead Client R&amp;D and Implementations: Work with cross-functional teams to lead enhancing our analytic solutions for client needs
          Collaborate regional marketing analytics teams to ensure alignment on project prioritization, actionable insights and analytical/technical best practices
          Interdisciplinary work: interact with multiple teams across the firm to provide and improve our end-to-end solution and implement new analytics capabilities: Data Management, Econometric Modeling, Optimization, Strategy and Engineering
          Skills, Talents, Experience you will need:
          You will have experience specifically in Algorithmic Attribution or Last Click Attribution, Building Predictive Models, Shopping Basket Analysis, Targeting and Next Best Actions.
          Our ideal candidates posess a combination of professional work experience in digital marketing analytics, especially in AdTech
          Experience in applying Data Mining, Machine Learning, and Statistical techniques to solve large-scale information retrieval, online search, online advertising attribution, online/offline marketing optimization problems
          Well versed in using SQL to process/extract data from data processing platforms
          Experience with Hadoop, Hive, R, Matlab or similar is highly desirable
          1-5 years of work in Digital Analytics with hands on experience in Adserver/Web Analytics Logs is desirable
          (1-3 years) working in digital AdTech industry with thorough understanding of digital metrics is desirable
          Bachelor&#8217;s degree or above (preferred) with mathematical background: Statistics, Data Mining, Operations Research, Economics, Finance, Engineering or similar
          Ability to communicate well with colleagues from other technical disciplines, herding cats, juggling flaming chainsaws would also be welcome
