        QUALIFICATIONS:
        The ideal candidate for this role will have:
        * 5+ years business development experience
        * Experienced with digital media buying, selling, and measurement tools
        * A relentless work ethic and desire to roll-up his/her sleeves as necessary and go the extra mile
        * Strong integrity, always striving for excellence and leading by example
        * Effectively worked cross functionally at all levels of a partners and client's organization
        * Demonstrated success in and enjoys working in an entrepreneurial environment
        * Excellent communication (verbal and written) and presentation skills
        * Knowledge of the TV marketplace and business model
        * Strong analytical, problem solving, critical thinking skills
        * Ability to influence and manage people and outcomes outside their scope of authority

        * Ability to forge and actively maintain relationships
        * Infectious passion and a championing attitude
        * Is both a "hunter" and a "farmer" with the ability to adapt to the circumstance
        RESPONSIBILITIES:
        Director of Business Development position will be expected to:
        * Identify and manage the expansion of partner relationships, fueling the overall flow of revenue from the partner "channel" in totality
        * Be the first point of contact for clients introduced to TiVo through the partnership
        * Work collaboratively with TiVo sales team
        * Independently manage day-to-day relationships with DSPs, DMPs, SSPs and Programmatic TV companies
        * Address and support partner and partners' client needs and requests
        * Champion TiVo offerings and vision across partner organization, agencies, clients, and at industry events

        * Participate in "big picture" and product strategy and vision activities
        * Anticipate client's needs and identify additional penetration opportunities within assigned clients
        * Document and report account status on a weekly basis to management
        * Meet project deadlines and required deliverables accurately and efficiently
        * Consistently and effectively collaborate with Data Science, Product, Delivery, Operations, Research, Finance &amp; Engineering to manage partner and client expectations and ensure timely delivery of all deliverables
