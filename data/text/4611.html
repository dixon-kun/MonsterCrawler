Summary

Zipcar, the world's largest car-sharing company, is looking to add new talent to our team.

Today, Zipcar provides hundreds of thousands of people with a viable alternative to traditional car ownership, saving them money, time and headaches. Around the world—from coast to coast, across Canada, the United Kingdom, Spain, Austria, and college campuses in between—people are making the decision to live car free and enjoying the convenience and environmental benefits of having "wheels when you want them".

As we continue to grow our operations, we are looking for talented, passionate people with great ideas to join the family. We aim to create an environment that is supportive, diverse, and as fun as our brand. If you're results-oriented, dedicated, and ready to contribute to an unparalleled member experience for our community of Zipsters, we encourage you to apply.

To learn more about Zipcar, what we do, and how we do it, visit zipcar.com. For further employment opportunities, visit zipcar.com/careers.

Zipcar is the world's largest shared-mobility company. We are adding senior talent to our engineering team at our Boston office. We have a rich software stack with which we solve a wide range of challenges: embedded custom hardware and firmware, mobile-first user experience, analytics, scalable real-time scheduling and monitoring, etc.

The technology choices we're standardizing on are:

* Service-oriented architecture (SOA) using message queues for internal communication, with services mainly written for the JDK (Java/Groovy/Scala/Clojure) and Ruby

* Modern continuous delivery, using technologies including Concourse, Docker, and BOSH

* Native mobile applications on Android and iOS – mobile-first wherever it makes sense

* Big Data technologies like Hadoop, Cassandra, Apache Spark, Python, Haskell, machine learning, to make informed business decisions

We are looking for an experienced data engineer, already seasoned with Hadoop (Cloudera) tools and approaches, to play a leading technical role in moving Zipcar's reporting, analytics and data science to Hadoop and related infrastructure. The area of focus is streaming data ingest and transformation, data modeling, data flow architecting, and coding (Flume, Python, Scala, Spark) that will satisfy our business needs for analysis and reporting. You will collaborate with data engineers, data analysts, data scientists, business stakeholders and other software engineering teams. Join our team of capable, passionate people in the Zipcar family. Our work environment is as supportive, diverse, and as fun as our brand. If you're results-oriented, dedicated, and ready to contribute to an unparalleled member experience for our community of Zipsters, we encourage you to apply.

To learn more about Zipcar, what we do, and how we do it, visit zipcar.com. For further employment opportunities, visit http://www.zipcar.com/about/jobs

Responsibilities:

* Lead an effort to set us on the road toward effective use of Hadoop and other tools for reporting, analysis, and data pipeline management for our new platform reporting.

* Help us set processes and workflows that enable us to rapidly operationalize data insights and data experiments to make our business more efficient and more responsive to our members.

* Work closely with stakeholders, data analysts, engineers, and scientists, as well as operations and quality engineering to help overhaul the role of data in our organization

* Help us build data quality/data validation processes and actively work with source systems and users to improve data quality

* Identify opportunities to automate data gathering, reducing manual processes and data manipulation

* Support technology initiatives that focus on streamlining the data pipeline and enhancing data quality

* Use modern tooling for rapid development, integrated testing, and high performance

* Teach and train data engineers in best practices for Hadoop and related tooling. Help us build a team of engineers, scientists, analysts and data stewards

* Strive for continuous improvement of the Zipcar member experience

Requirements:

* BS in Computer Science or equivalent experience

* Substantial experience and skill in technologies including

* Unstructured data, especially Hadoop (Cloudera)

* Data collection, curation, preparation and transformation

* Data warehousing, reporting and business intelligence

* Software development in Python, Scala, Java; Spark

* Relational and nonrelational databases

* Data validation processes and software quality assurance

* Experience with a 24/7 production environment, managing hundreds of application instances

* Experience with data-driven business

* Clear verbal and written communication; demonstrated ability to collaborate with peers from a variety of disciplines

The Ideal Candidate Has:

* Breadth and depth of skill to build and deliver scalable data and reporting solutions for Zipcar and ABG

* Deep knowledge and experience with the Hadoop environment, software tools, and performance optimization

* An obsession with delivering outstanding customer experience and site resiliency

* Desire, initiative and resourcefulness to learn new tools, frameworks, languages

* A commitment to Zipcar's ideals of sustainable resource sharing and urban mobility
