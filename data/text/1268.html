About the role:

The Integrated Producer will have a strong conceptual understanding of the latest marketing platforms and technologies. You will work on all project elements and phases from inception to completion and have the ability to stay cool under pressure.  You will craft relevant documentation: proposals, scopes of work, production schedules, budgets.  You will be scoping, budgeting, managing the client's expectations and will be responsible for the financial monitoring of projects, ensuring that projects are scoped accurately and are profitable, delivered on time and on budget and to the highest quality.


  • The Integrated Producer is the leader/coordinator for the client and coordinates with strategists, designers, creative, tech, studio, account and executive management in determining project scope, estimating, planning, executing and monitoring assigned projects and deliverables
  • Ensures that sound implementation plans are developed and that each plan meets the project strategy while considering both budget and timing
  • Ability to anticipate project issues/risks and clearly communicate resolutions to the client and internal team
  • Maintain schedules, budgets, assets and all project details to ensure all teams are working towards a successful execution
  • Participate in/schedule brainstorms in an efficient manner
  • Assist the creative teams from concepting ideas, development and execution
  • Provide production oversight (print, video, photo, multimedia) along with studio team for all stages of content development from creative concepting, planning, pre-pro, production and post production

  • 3-5 years of agency experience managing a range of integrated projects (broadcast, print, social content, digital media)
  • Knowledge of broadcast production and post production
  • Experience developing SOWs, establishing budgets, staff plans and timelines
  • Knowledge of all agency functional areas; including creative, media, and production
  • Knowledge of asset management platforms and workflow tools
  • Comfortable being client-facing and presenting work
  • BA or BS degree
