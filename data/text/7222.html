As a Senior Product Designer for our product team, you will work closely with Product Managers and Developers to translate business goals into engaging products. Using Sketch, Adobe CC, Invision, and associated tools, you'll design journey maps, wireframe, comps and other illustrations of the user experience for our next generation data science platform. You'll work in our downtown Austin, TX office, collaborating with a set of awesome peers that cut across product, design, development, subject matter experts, and marketing among others. You'll also have the ability to take the great knowledge you have and innovate on a leading product that will be adopted by millions of users around the world.

Main Responsibilities

* Provide UX and Design leadership on projects

* Drive work through to execution

* Cooperate with cross-functional members of the team to ensure proper completion of work as it relates to the goals of the project or task at hand.

* Utilize UX design skills in the execution of projects

* Design journey maps, wireframe, comps and other illustrations of the user experience

* Explore a problem or planned feature using tools like personas, journey maps, task analysis, stakeholder interviews, and contextual inquiry

Requirements and Position Qualifications

* Bachelor's or Master's Degree in Design, Human-Computer Interaction, Computer Science, Graphics Design or related disciplines

* At least 5 years of professional experience

* 2-4 years' experience managing UX teams of at least 3 people

* Exceptional communication skills

* Advanced understanding of UX ideation and design techniques

* Ability to switch gears, juggle multiple projects and work within tight deadlines

* Ability to be an effective team member

* Collaborate with Product Managers to create and maintain a consistent user experience throughout the product

* Adept with design tools such as Sketch, Adobe CC and Invision.

* Basic understanding of web technologies (HTML5 and CSS3)

* Possess expert understanding of user-centered design methods and best practices

* Experienced: Good knowledge of design disciplines and principles (user-centered design, layout, information architecture, typography, color theory, ux patterns)

* Must be based in the Austin, TX area or willing to relocate

Desired Qualifications

* Experience with user research

* Experience designing data science, machine learning, BI, or analytics software

Our Values

* Passionate CONTRIBUTION – engaging deeply as community builders

* Spirit of CRAFTSMANSHIP – taking an ownership and pride in our work, tools and continual improvement

* CUSTOMER-Driven Action

Help Us Shape the Future of Data

Continuum is seeking people who want to play a role in shaping the future of data, analytics, and visualization. Candidates for technical roles should be knowledgeable and capable, but always eager to learn more and to teach others. Overall, we strive to create a culture that is both relaxed and focused, and we stress empathy and collaboration with our customers, open source users, and with each other. Our primary employee perk is that we are actively working on things that have a global impact, whether it's modeling risk and detecting fraud in the financial markets, or accelerating cancer research, or fighting human trafficking and terrorism. We are part of a global community on the cutting edge of open source analytics, and our employees gain exposure and participate in all that.

Based in Austin, TX, with offices in New York and employees worldwide, Continuum Analytics is the creator and driving force behind Anaconda, the world's leading Open Data Science platform. Counting more than 200 of the Fortune 500, 19 of the Fortune 25 and over 8,000 universities around the world, the Anaconda community is rapidly expanding with over 11M downloads to date. As a Continuum Analytics Senior Product Designer, you will be part of a growing company whose products connect the brightest minds with their data, while learning a great deal both from those you work with and the customers you support.

Customers include more than 200 of the Fortune 500, 19 of the Fortune 25 and 8,000 universities around the world. Boeing, Procter & Gamble, Pepsi, Schlumberger, the U.S. Department of the Treasury and the Securities and Exchange Commission are among current industry leaders who rely on Anaconda.

We have recently secured a $24 million Series A funding round led by General Catalyst Partners and BuildGroup, bringing total funding to-date to $34 million. We're growing fast and looking for A Players to take us to the next phase of our business.

To learn more about the Anaconda platform, training and consulting services, visit www.continuum.io.
