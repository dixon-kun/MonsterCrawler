Responsibilities:

Interns support and partner with the Insurer Consulting Group (ICG) team members. In order to gain skills and develop professionally, they will:

* Begin to develop an understanding of ICG deliverables and methodologies.

* Conduct basic data analysis for deliverables preparation.

* Support production of the annual Carrier Interaction Survey Report.

* Clean and prepare data for analysis.

* Undergraduate Seniors or 1st Year Masters/ MBA Students

* Pursuing Bachelor's or Master's Degree in Economics, Math, Statistics, Actuarial Science, operations research, data science, computer science, engineering

* Superior analytical and mathematic skills

* Statistical modeling knowledge desirable

* Experience with R programming desirable

* Ability to develop strong client oriented solutions

* Superior detail orientation, excellent communication and interpersonal skills

* Excellent Microsoft Office skills, particularly in Excel

* Be able to show success in multi-tasking, prioritization, and meeting deadlines.

* Strong problem solving skills

* Excellent verbal and written communication skills.

* Authorized to work for an employer in the U.S.
