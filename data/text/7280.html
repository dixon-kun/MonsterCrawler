IntelliGenesis LLC is a veteran-owned, woman-owned company established in 2007. We provide Software Development, Intelligence Analysis, Artificial Intelligence, Computer Network Operations, and Cyber related Internal Research and Development (IR&D) services that result in higher efficiency, automation, and improved analysis, protection, and defense of National Security missions.

IntelliGenesis offers an extremely competitive and generous benefits package including Medical, Dental, Vision, 20 PTO days with the option to purchase up to an additional 5 days, 10 % 401k Match, Holiday pay, Technology Budget, Education/Training Reimbursement, and paid STD, LTD, and Life Insurance.

We are currently seeking a Cloud Software Engineer! Come join our team where you will enjoy interesting and challenging work on high-end, massive scale development opportunities. Top salaries offered with a sign-on bonus!

Required Skills:

  • Must be a US Citizen
  • Active clearance/polygraph required
  • Shall have at least five (5) years experience in software development/engineering, including requirements analysis, software development, installation, integration,
  • Shall have at least four (4) years of experience developing software with high level languages such as Java, C, C++.
  • Shall have experience with the Map Reduce programming model and technologies such as Hadoop, Hive, Pig, and so forth.
  • Shall have experience with the Hadoop Distributed File System (HDFS).
  • Shall have experience with Serialization such as JSON and/or BSON.
  • Shall have at least three (3) years experience developing software for Windows (2000, 2003, XP, Vista), or UNIX/Linux (Redhat versions 3-5) operating systems.
  • Shall have experience on the design and development of at least one Object Oriented system.
  • Shall have experience developing solutions integrating and extending FOSS/COTS products.
  • Shall have at least three (3) years experience in software integration and software testing, to include developing and implementing test plans and test scripts.
  • Shall have demonstrated technical writing skills and have generated technical documents in support of a software development project.
  • Shall have demonstrated work experience with distributed scalable Big Data Store (NoSQL) such as Hbase, Cloudbase/Acumulo, Big Table, and so forth.
  • Shall have demonstrated experience, work or college level courses, in at least two (2) of the desired characteristics below.

Desired Skills:

  • Background in statistics or data sciences
  • Experience deploying applications in a cloud environment.
  • Understanding of Cloud Scalability.
  • Hadoop/Cloud Certification.
  • Experience designing and developing automated analytic software, techniques, and algorithms.
  • Experience with taxonomy construction for analytic disciplines, knowledge areas and skills.
  • Experience developing and deploying: data driven analytics; event driven analytics; sets of analytics orchestrated through rules engines.
  • Experience with linguistics (grammar, morphology, concepts).
  • Experience developing and deploying analytics that discover and exploit social networks.
  • Experience documenting ontologies, data models, schemas, formats, data element dictionaries, software application program interfaces and other technical specifications.
  • Experience developing and deploying analytics within a heterogeneous schema environment.
