        Design and implement data warehouse ETL architecture and data models.
        ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:
        1. Re-architect existing marts and define ETL architecture for new marts, by resolving architectural conflicts and ensuring consistency and alignment with industry best practices.

        5. Potentially introduce and manage unstructured, "Big Data" such as telematic, internet-of-things (IOT), drone, wearables, sensor, and social media, in a distributed ecosystem like Hadoop.
        6. Act independently under general supervision using established automated software tools.
        7. Design, supervise, plan, and coordinate highly complex projects.
        8. Consider equipment capacity, limitations, and operating time while completing assignments.
        9. Interpret requirements (written or oral) into technical specifications for projects across a broad knowledge base.
        10. Have full technical knowledge of all phases of applications systems analysis and programming, including but not limited to designing, coding, testing, debugging, documenting, installing, etc.

        13. Evaluate progress and results to recommend changes in procedures.
        14. May assist with the review and evaluation of personnel performance.
        15. Interact and communicate effectively with all business contacts, including group presentations and meeting facilitation.

        18. Update the problem management and tracking system to reflect problem status.
        19. Update the Performance Objectives/Evaluation document.
