Research Engineer, Computer Vision & Deep Learning

Position Overview

CCC information Services, Inc. is seeking anResearch Engineer, Computer Vision & Deep Learning, to work on the Data Science team to turn data insights into business recommendations and model the value of an insight. The Data Science team at CCC is currently working on the most important strategic initiatives of the company. The Research Engineer, Computer Vision & Deep Learning will be a part of the Data Science team and will contribute to a photo analytics project with the goal of developing a fully automated solution for an auto collision damage appraisal. We are seeking a candidate who will bolster existing capabilities and suggest new, exciting changes and improvements.

Duties
•    Lead implementation and scaling of vision and estimation algorithms
•    Work with other vision scientists and engineers to deliver deployable technology while seeking creative ways to streamline processing and workload
•    Achieve development concepts of computer vision algorithms that utilize deep learning


Required Skills
•    The ideal candidate will have 2 plus years of experience with research, computer vision and image processing and/or deep learning.
•    Experience programming in Python, or C++, or Matlab, will be required
•    Exceptional problem solver and communicator
•    Comfortable with OpenCV and other computer vision libraries
•    Knowledge of Linux systems in Ubuntu, CentOS
•    Experience in 3D computer vision and video analytics algorithms such as image segmentation, 3D reconstruction, object and motion detection, and classification
•    Experience in machine learning algorithms for vision problems including deep learning algorithms
•    Effective documentation practices and experience writing reports and protocols
•    Expert in algorithm design, creative problem solving, and performance optimization
•    Advanced modeling skillset
•    Skilled with creating and manipulating spreadsheets and scripted languages
•    Knowledgeable with computational geometry, linear algebra, and statistics
•    Advanced documenting and research techniques

Education

•    Must have a Ph.D. or Master’s degree in Applied Math, Electronics Engineering, Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or related engineering field

Supplemental Qualifications
•    Parallel computing and analytics skills
•    Understanding of camera calibration, bundle adjustment, and 3D reconstruction techniques
•    Familiarity with software design patterns and scalable software architectures
•    Practical and theoretical experience in at least of two of the following: image matching, 2D or 3D alignment, CNN, pattern recognition, deep learning, and/orand optimization
