Position Overview:

ADP is hiring a Director / Oracle Application Architect, who is responsible to lead the planning, designing, developing, testing and deploying efficient solutions within Oracle EBS system to satisfy strategic, financial, and operational needs.

* Will provide overall solution design and all the detailed solution elements and requirements within the respective business process: Record to Report, Order-to-Cash and Procure-to-Pay.

* Will work with technical developers and business analysts to properly engineer and configure the solution as well as work with the business SMEs to setup and execute testing and validation of the solution.

* Responsible for generating and compiling reports based on their findings, complete with probable causes and possible solutions to systems issues.

* Will apply proven leadership, communication, analytical, and problem-solving skills to maximize the benefit of IT system investments.

Qualifications:

* 10+ years related experience with 5+ years in leadership managing a team of direct report and matrixed resources.

* Savvy and persuasive Communication Skills are key to gain buy-in across multiple teams and business verticals.

* Expertise with Oracle e-Business Suite ( Oracle EBS ) modules, and Oracle AIM Methodology.

* Experience implementing Concur Travel & Expense and Ariba systems.

* Bachelor's Degree in technical or business discipline or related experience. Master's preferred.

Experience:

* 15+ years of experience with delivering global end-to-end Oracle EBS based solutions

* A Project Manager and/or Team Lead on at least 3+ full cycle Oracle EBS Financials implementations and/or large scale EBS Financials upgrades.

* Expert knowledge in Oracle EBS (R12) modules including General Ledger, Accounts Payable, Cash Management, Accounts Receivable, Advance Collections, iReceivables, eBusiness Tax, Fixed Assets, Cash Management, Order Management, Advance Pricing, etc.

* Thorough knowledge of accounting and finance, including GAAP procedures, SOX compliance, localizations

* Experience managing project scope, change requests, delivery against defined milestones; mitigate and remediate risk factors and propose solutions based on best practices

* A lead resource in defining systems strategy, developing systems requirements, designing and prototyping practical business solutions, identifying and consulting with clients on business process transformation, optimization and best-practice initiatives, testing, training, managing go-live and system cutovers, defining support procedures

* Develops and implements policies and procedures to ensure that systems support the organization's business requirements and meet the needs of end users

* Ability to lead a team, worked effectively in a multi-cultural environment and good at learning and teaching others

* Defines systems requirements based on user/client needs, cost, and required integration with existing applications, systems, or platforms

* Articulate communication with Business Users and Technical Team members and be the liaison between Executive / Business Users and Information Technology teams.

* Ability to use logic and reasoning to identify strengths and weaknesses of alternative solutions, and provide conclusions or approaches to the job

* Experience making configurations to an existing system, operating specific components, troubleshooting problems, and conducting research to develop better processes to make use of Oracle EBS Suite software more efficient

* Techno / Functional Expertise in Oracle EBS business process flow such as: Record to Report, Order to Cash and Procure to Pay

* Well versed and ability to deliver documentation based on Oracle's AIM Methodology

* Technical Expertise with tools and languages such as PL/SQL, Forms, Reports, Workflow Builder, Java, etc.

* Proficient in design process of RICE (Reports, Interfaces, Configurations and Enhancements), data conversion, data mapping and conversion strategy within various EBS Modules

* Lead solution design / coding, testing and integration for the overall business process (Record to Report, Order to Cash and Procure to Pay)

* Meet with decision makers, systems owners, and end users to define business, operations requirements and systems goals, and identify and resolve systems issues

* Lead design sessions in prototyping new systems for enhancing business processes, operations, and information process flow

* Review and analyze the effectiveness and efficiency of existing systems and develop strategies for improving or further leveraging these systems

* Prepare and deliver reports, recommendations, or alternatives that address existing and potential trouble areas in operating systems across the organization

* Collaborate in the planning, design, development, and deployment of new applications, and enhancements to existing applications

* Perform cost-benefit and return on investment analyses for proposed systems to aid management in making implementation decisions

* Create systems models, specifications, diagrams and charts to provide direction to system programmers

* Identify and establish scope and parameters of systems analysis to define outcome criteria and measure-taking actions

* Coordinate and perform in-depth tests, including end-user reviews, for modified and new systems, and other post-implementation support

* Conduct research on software and hardware products to justify recommendations and to support purchasing efforts

* Experience implementing Concur Travel & Expense and Ariba systems will be a plus

* Prefer resumes in Word or PDF Format.
