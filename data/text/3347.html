About the Job

Brambles is looking to extend market leadership and has created of an IOT division called BXB Digital .  By tapping into the Internet of Things we are looking to transform the supply chain industry by bringing digital intelligence to the physical assets the company owns globally.



Brambles is the world leader in pallet and container pooling services, serving many of the world's largest companies. Brambles issues, collects, conditions and reissues more than 500 million pallets and containers from a global network of more than 850 service centers in 80 countries, helping manufacturers and growers transport their products to distributors and retailers.



With more than 300,000 customers around the globe, including Procter & Gamble, SYSCO, Carrefour, Kellogg's, Woolworths, Kraft, Nestlé, Lion Nathan, The Home Depot, Tesco, Unilever, Hewlett Packard, Ford and GM,  Brambles for "Handling The World's Most Important Products…Everyday."



Brambles employs more than 14,000 employees in 80 countries and benefits from more than four decades of industry experience internationally.





SENIOR MARKETING MANAGER



COMPANY OVERVIEW

Established in 2016, BXB Digital provides data-driven supply chain logistics solutions that connect physical platforms with digital capabilities that enable visibility of goods as they move through the supply chain. By combining deep data science techniques, cutting-edge Internet of Things (IoT) technologies, and Software as a Service, BXB Digital enables a more connected, intelligent and efficient supply chain.



BXB Digital is based in Silicon Valley, California with additional operations in London, UK and Bangalore, India.



BXB Digital is a Brambles company, the global leader in supply chain solutions operating through the CHEP and IFCO brands. Brambles Limited is listed on the Australian Securities Exchange (ASX) and has its headquarters in Sydney, Australia, but operates in more than 60 countries, with its largest operations in North America and Western Europe. Brambles employs more than 14,500 people and owns over 550 million pallets, crates and containers through a network of approximately 850 service centres.



POSITION DESCRIPTION

As part of our Marketing team you will directly support and promote BXB Digital in the areas of reputation, relationships and revenue. This role will report into the VP of Products and be responsible for developing, executing, and measuring an integrated marketing plan that leverages internal and external channels to brand BXB Digital in the market for its unique approach, capabilities and services.



Success in this role includes collaboration with Brambles corporate programs and business units to co-develop, leverage and amplify programs and assets that will be critical in delivering a differentiated value proposition in the market.



RESPONSIBILITIES

• Develop a point of view and differentiated value proposition that serves as the basis for all go-to-market campaigns, internal and external communications, and asset development

• Deliver an integrated marketing plan that leverages multiple channels/programs - analyst, PR, digital platforms, conferences, as well as asset development

• Serve as the expert to create the digital and brand presence in the digital/web space, including launching a digital/web presence for a technical or IoT organization

• Measure effectiveness and ROI of programs focused on the reputation, relationships and revenue

• Close collaboration with teams across the organization to influence and raise the profile of BXB Digital and Brambles

• Apply appropriate use of firm branding, positioning and visual identity in all deliverables.

• Take initiative and challenge the status quo using diplomacy, when required

• Manage marketing spend related to projects/programs



KNOWLEDGE & SKILLS

• Creative, innovative and critical thinking with a strong track record of developing credible ideas for content

• Strong understanding of innovation concepts and disruptors with ability to keep up with latest developments particularly in the areas of technology and supply chain logistics

• Meticulous copy editing skills with strong attention to detail and commitment to delivering high-quality output

• Experience leading integrated brand-building, marketing and communications programs within a complex business environment, with proven results



QUALIFICATIONS

• Bachelor’s Degree, MBA preferred

• 10 years + experience in marketing management in software technology or supply chain industries with proven track record of creating and executing innovative programs

• Strong relationship-building skills with proven ability to influence and build collaborative relationships with senior-level stakeholders

• Substantial creative content and technical experience

• Excellent verbal and written communication skills as well as listening and interpretation skills

• Strong project management skills, including prioritization and ability to multi-task

• Microsoft Office suite skills
