McIntire Solutions is interested in inviting customer focused, values based talent to join our team.  We work very hard, think about our customers’ needs, and do whatever it takes to create an ethical and successful customer outcome. We do it by treating each other and customers with respect. We do it by functioning as a TEAM!



Offering a competitive benefit and compensation model, we value working with great people, solving stimulating challenges and believing in what we can accomplish as a team. We emphasize individual and collective excellence, continual innovation and customer intimacy. We encourage our people to grow and develop, stretching into new roles that strengthen skills and deliver even greater value to our customers. The missions we support are important and your role makes a difference!

  • Medical, Dental and Vision Insurance : Substantial employer contribution toward medical (PPO), dental and vision insurance
  • Life and Disability Insurance : Company paid short/long term disability and life insurance policy
  • Professional Development : Up to an annual $5K benefit for tuition, training, and certification reimbursements
  • Paid Time Off : Ample leave and holiday pay
  • Retirement : Superior 401K company benefit that’s immediately vested
  • Bonuses : Generous bonuses provided to teammates who contribute above and beyond to corporate strategic goals
  • Sponsored Social Events : Holiday parties, happy hours, picnics, and teambuilding events
