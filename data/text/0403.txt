        About Us
        Neustar, Inc. (NYSE: NSR) is a trusted, neutral provider of real-time information and analytics to the communications services, financial services, retail, and media and advertising sectors. Neustar applies its advanced, secure technologies to help its clients promote and protect their businesses. More information is available at www.neustar.biz.
        Description
        Neustar is looking for talented individuals with a passion for marketing analytics and consulting to help our clients' address their most challenging marketing effectiveness needs. The ideal candidate has a strong quantitative background, marketing experience, and is well versed in the art of client management.
        Marketers can't hide behind old-school metrics and gut feel any longer. The best ones are marrying technology, data, and strategic expertise to make the right marketing investments. That's where Neustar comes in. In the Consultant role, you'll support multi-million dollar customer engagements, with responsibility for organizing internal teams and developing enduring, trusted, and effective relationships with your customers.
        As a Consultant on the Advisory Services team, you will manage and lead the day-to-day delivery of marketing mix modeling, multi-touch attribution, segmentation, or DMP engagements. You'll build your network with top Marketing, Finance, and Analytics leadership in major companies across virtually all industries. You'll work with a team of the best business analysts, data scientists, and product specialists in the business to tackle challenging business problems while exceeding client expectations. Our work is not just about delivering a project and moving on to the next challenge. Instead, it's about delivering an evolving stream of value and enabling data-driven decision-making. At Neustar, you will be given the opportunity to learn and grow at an accelerated rate. If you get excited about the prospect of being challenged everyday, then Neustar is the place for you.
        Is this you?
        * Self-driven and motivated &#8211; a go-getter seeking ownership and responsibility
        * Resourceful &#8211; you find creative ways to make things happen
        * Intellectually curious &#8211; continually driving for self-improvement
        * Analytically minded &#8211; like to look at data to uncover patterns and insights
        * Consultative &#8211; enjoy helping others solve challenging business problems
        What you will be doing:
        * Strategy: Strategically transform findings from modeling analyses to qualitative insights to help clients understand marketing ROI, key business drivers, and opportunities to improve performance
        * Client Management: Align on objectives, expectations, and requirements. Support account lead and sales executives to strengthen and grow client relationships
        * Analytics: Oversee data collection, review, and validation to ensure accurate information is used for modeling analyses; develop a deep understanding of client data &#8211; including strengths and weaknesses
        * Project Management: Work closely with PMO to manage multiple work streams and priorities; escalate issues and risks to account lead as appropriate
        Skills, Talents, Experience you'll need:
        * Exceptional analytics and quantitative acumen with understanding of marketing mix models or close experience such as predictive analytics.
        * Strong work ethic with the ability to adapt to the situation, be flexible and nimble in terms of work planning, and the ability to think quickly on your feet
        * Demonstrated experience using analytics to solve business problems
        * Advanced proficiency with software tools such as Excel to gather and assess data and PowerPoint to build presentations
        * Skilled in developing relationships and delivering on projects that have grown into long-term relationships.
        * Specific experience in the ad-tech industry, a digital marketing agency, a programmatic buying company or client side digital advertising is a plus.
        * Bachelor's degree preferably in applied science, statistics, mathematics, business, or economics
        * 5+ years of experience; an MBA is also preferred. Big brand experience a big plus
