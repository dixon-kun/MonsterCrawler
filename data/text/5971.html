American Career College and West Coast University are industry leaders in helping individuals pursue their educational and professional dreams. We are currently seeking a Director of Predictive Analytics for our Administration Office in Irvine, California. This is an outstanding opportunity for a professional individual to join a growing organization.

Summary:

Reporting to the President of Shared Services and CFO for ACC and WCU, the Director of Predictive Analytics collates, reports on, and provides interpretation of university metrics (i.e., retention, placement, graduation rates, student learning, room utilization, bad debt reserves, etc.) for administrative decision making and strategic planning. Conducts research to determine best approach to collect required data. Supervises the collection, analysis, and preservation of data for use by the Organization. Provides data analysis, interpretation, and preparation of reports. Provides information and analytic support to the Organization’s administrative decision makers in order to facilitate strategic planning, policy-making, resource allocations, enrollment projections, and programmatic improvements.


  • Master’s degree(doctorate preferred) in social or behavioral sciences, statistics, or a closely related field, plus demonstrated working knowledge and experience navigating relational databases and writing/verifying custom SQL queries; experience with SPSS Modeler, R, Java Script and Tableau or comparable software.
  • Demonstrated professional experience and working knowledge of quantitative, modeling and data analysis skills
  • Demonstrated professional experience and working knowledge of longitudinal and relational database and database query tools
  • The position requires a minimum of 5 years of progressively responsible professional full-time experience in higher education Institutional Research or Data Science or Analytical Studies.
  • Ability to develop metrics, measurements, templates and tools for continual improvement tracking and monitoring of key indicators for areas of responsibility that facilitate the process of research within a culture of “management by evidence”, decisions based on data.
  • Ability to gather, analyze, and synthesize data from multiple resources, prepare necessary reports, evaluate and analyze program effectiveness and resource utilization.
  • Ability to manage and work effectively in a highly ethnic and culturally diverse student and associate community as well as work with individuals at all levels of the organization.
  • Ability to use excellent organizational and analytical skills to self-direct, self-pace, multi-task and function well under pressure of deadlines and conflicting priorities.
  • Ability to effectively use interpersonal communication skills, both oral and written, follow through with duties, provide attention to detail, and demonstrate the ability to motivate others.
  • Ability to exercise good judgment.
  • Ability and willingness to work a flexible schedule.
  • Ability to travel/drive locally on a weekly, daily and/or on as needed basis.
Full-time associates are eligible for an exceptional benefits package which includes:
  • Health, dental and vision plan
  • Vacation, sick and holiday schedule
  • Tuition Reimbursement
  • 401(k) Plan with employer match
  • Flexible Spending Account
  • Long/short term disability and more.
