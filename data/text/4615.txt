        ADMINISTRATIVE ASSISTANT II, Statistics and Data Science Center (SDSC), Institute for Data, Systems, and Society (IDSS), to handle a diverse set of complex administrative duties in support of the faculty director of the SDSC in his academic, research, and administrative roles.         Job Requirements
        REQUIRED: a minimum of three years' direct/related experience; attention to detail; ability to work both independently and as a team member with minimal supervision; strong organizational, multitasking and interpersonal skills; sound judgment with confidential information/issues; exceptional verbal and written communication skills; comfort interacting with a wide range of faculty, staff, and students; ability to work in a high pressure environment with changing priorities; strong technical skills including proficiency with MS Office, LaTeX, and other computer skills (updating websites using WordPress, publishing information, etc.).
