About Us. The ADP Global Enterprise Technology Solutions -- PAAS Application Management team is in need of qualified individuals to provide varying degrees of Tech and App Mgt support for hosted products which ADP uses to deliver services to our Clients. The successful candidate will provide timely and effective 2nd Level Infrastructure Support on web-products in a fast paced environment. Different from a classic "Help Desk" role, this is technical role working as a bridge between help desk Associates and the Developers & Engineers who design and build our products.

RESPONSIBILTIES:

* Monitor the Service Desk Queue for incoming cases and messages, take calls as directed by mangers and/or senior members of the team

* Monitor Synthetic Transaction alerts to confirm a "green" state for all of the application monitors

* Participate in Bridge calls as needed to help resolve system issues and restore service to our Clients

* * Utilize Incident and Problem Management standards & tools to push for continuous improvement of the Client Experience (ITIL knowledge is a plus)

* Create Knowledgebase entries to educate and help peers and field associates as directed by senior team members

* Report product defects and request product enhancements

* Perform a variety of tasks connected with improving the Client experience with ADP's products

The position calls for a normal eight hour work day beginning sometime between 8:30am and 12:30pm ET US, Monday thru Friday with the possibility of overtime, including some weekend work. After a period of training and familiarization with the product set, we may allow more flexible start times. Minimal travel may be required - up to twice per year for up to several days per visit (but probably less).

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:

* Completion of undergraduate and/or graduate degree

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:

* Excellent written and spoken English language skills with an ability to speak loudly and clearly

* Excellent client-service skills (communication, ownership, follow-up, etc.)

* A "get things done" attitude with a willingness to speak up and give opinions, answers and guidance without, necessarily, being specifically asked to do so.

* Ability to explain difficult concepts in clear, easily understood terms

* Significant experience with Microsoft Windows and associated applications

* Basic Understanding of Network Operating Systems and configurations

* Troubleshooting and problem analysis skills -- experience dong Incident and Problem Management following the standards put forth by ITIL

* Familiarity with WEB-based applications and browsers

* Basic understanding of relational DB concepts along with basic knowledge of building and executing simple SQL queries

* Familiarity with relational DB products such as Crystal Reports, ReportSmith, Business Objects is a plus

* Basic UNIX knowledge is a plus

* Experience providing support on software product infrastructure as opposed to "how to" questions on the application itself

* Comfortable with "crisis" situations - quick thinking, problem definition and diagnosis skills

* The desire to get to root cause of issues and work with appropriate Development or Engineering Associates to prevent recurrence.
