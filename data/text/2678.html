Our most successful and happy employees are those that have a passion for the work we do, the people we serve and an intellectual curiosity. That realization prompted us to 'kill' the job description. We are a technology focused company and tend to work with many of the newest flavors of tech. So, it seemed futile to try and create traditional job descriptions seeking candidates that have xx number of years’ experience for a technology that is less than a year old. So, we're changing the game.

We encourage anyone interested in an opportunity to work with us to investigate our website, get a sense for the work we do and who we do it for and the technology we primarily use. If you're passionate about working on something that has the potential to make a significant, positive impact in the world - let's talk. If you have a technical aptitude and capability - it's possible we can take what you know, introduce you to what we're working with and help you become a supporting teammate. We work on meaningful projects, get to play with the latest tech and enjoy an empowering work environment.

If that speaks to you, you're encouraged to explore our website and to reach out and connect with us to explore possible career opportunities. If there isn't a fit for you right now, that's ok, at least a relationship can be established so you can be considered when the timing is right. And if there is an appropriate opportunity available, let's explore that possibility.

IST Research is the creator and developer of the proprietary technology known as Pulse™. Pulse™ is a web-based technology platform that enables information collection from marginalized populations or difficult locations using methodologies that are most appropriate for the environment. Pulse collects, analyzes and visualizes both online data and data collected directly from populations of interest. Our campaign design, execution, and analysis methodology is anchored in cognitive psychology, social science, and data science.

We are looking to augment our TechOps team with a Senior Infrastructure Engineer.  The current TechOps team members are highly skilled and versatile, focused on critical infrastructure issues like Data Preservation (no data loss), Reliability (service to customers & teammates is top priority) and Security (including drafting & enforcing security policies). What the TechOps team does not spend their time on is things like UI work (web development), web application management (i.e. Sharepoint, Google Apps, etc) or any tech support (Windows/Mac).

The Senior Infrastructure Engineer we are looking for will share the responsibility for managing the framework of our Cloud Computing network. This is a critical aspect of our work that spans across the entire company and projects. The SIE will need to have in-depth knowledge and experience with the container/cluster orchestration such as Docker swarm, Kubernetes or Mesos. We are ardent users, supporters and contributors of open source technologies. In our environment, these technologies are used to store and analyze hundreds of millions of documents scraped and collected for efforts to effect a positive impact on societal and global issues. Your efforts in this position have the potential to help offer solutions to real problems like human trafficking, labor trafficking, violent extremists, illegal firearms sales, counterfeit pharmaceuticals, Securities and Exchange Commission fraud as well as providing a voice to those without ready access to the digital world. Other desirable attributes for those interested in this position would be experience with Linux, setting up S/W packages, server automation and development tools. Some exposure to PHP, Python, Java and Shellscripts would also be a plus. While IST Research is based in Virginia, we're a remote friendly work environment and will consider candidates from anywhere in the U.S. The work is fast paced, cutting edge, rewarding and you face new challenges regularly. If that appeals to you, we encourage you to connect with us to explore this opportunity.
