ENABLING SMARTER HEALTHCARE

At Change Healthcare, we are focused on enabling smarter healthcare. Everyday, we are re-imagining how insights can lead to smarter healthcare, and we are committed to delivering products, services, innovations, and experiences to our clients across the continuum of care.

Like the payers, providers, pharmacies and consumers we serve, we care deeply about the triple aim of healthcare: improving the patient experience, enabling better health of populations and reducing the cost of care.

We help connect the dots between payers, providers and patients, leveraging the data needed to deliver predictive insights to clients, who use this knowledge to solve some of their most pressing issues and capitalize on their most exciting opportunities.

Ours is the single largest financial and administrative healthcare network in the United States, reaching approximately 750,000 physicians, 105,000 dentists, 60,000 pharmacies, 5,000 hospitals, 600 vendors, 450 laboratories and 1,200 government and commercial payers. We have developed this network of payers and providers over 30 years in the industry, connecting virtually all private and government payers, claim-submitting providers and pharmacies in a hybrid cloud based, user-centric and secure infrastructure environment.

Change Healthcare has the depth of experience to navigate the changing healthcare landscape side by side with our clients. Responsibilities

The essential job responsibilities include but are not limited to:

* Design and oversee research initiatives to glean new insights into

healthcare purchasing behavior as it pertains to plan design and market behavior, as well as author and maintain data mining algorithms that drive the core Consumer Engagement product suite.

* Provide data-driven insight into product development and innovation.

* This is a unique position that will work closely with all aspects of the organization by providing key metrics to the sales, marketing, product development, and engagement teams while working with team member and external resources to publish research in academic and industry journals.

* This is a high impact position and a successful applicant must have a broad foundation in statistical modeling and deep genuine desire to extract new information from proprietary data sets

RequirementsQualifications

* BS in statistics, data science, healthcare/clinical informatics, public health, or similar degree

* Masters level degree in highly quantitative program is a plus

* Experience with analysis and manipulation of healthcare claims and health related data

* 3+ years experience conducting statistical analysis such as: regression models (linear, logistic, etc.), ANOVA, sensitivity and survival analysis, cluster based sampling, and attribute matching algorithms

* Experience with conditional probabilities

* Strong skills in one or more of the following: R, Python, Scala

* 2+ years of SQL programming experience

* Excellent written communications skill as demonstrated in the authorship of technical documentation, published white papers, or peer reviewed journals

* Ability to accurately document technical processes

* Strong consultative abilities to work with internal and external business stakeholders

* Ability to work with a sense of urgency in a dynamic environment

* Ability to work independently in an efficient and responsible manner
