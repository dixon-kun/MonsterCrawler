The GETS mission is to deliver GEOINT toolsets (the development of software tools and tooling that reside on the GETS platform) through an enterprise-grade, Software as a Service (SaaS) platform. The specific mission focus pertaining to this performance work statement involves daily operation of GETS IT hardware, integration activities with GEOINT processes, direct end-user support, and development of new automated technical capabilities to satisfy the supported commanders' stated requirements.
