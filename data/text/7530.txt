        ByteCubed is looking for a Chief Technology Officer (CTO) who is passionate about delivering high quality products and services to both government and large commercial clients at our headquarters in Arlington, Virginia. The CTO will be responsible for growing and nurturing a team of technologists, creatives, and product managers that love good solutions to hard problems.
        Through vision, inspiration, and coaching, the CTO will lead the team to be able to solve difficult client and technical problems, develop innovative solutions, deliver technical products and services within agreed upon schedule and cost. The CTO will engage and partner with executive leadership and client directors on vision and expectations, to develop and implement the ByteCubeds strategic goals for delivering differentiated technical offerings for continued growth within both current and new markets. Applicants selected will be subject to a US Government security investigation and must meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information. Due to the nature of work performed within our facilities, US citizenship is required.
        Responsibilities:
        * Develop and own long-term technical roadmap and budget; drive execution and enable the team to meet deliverables; make key architectural and build vs. buy decisions; ensure that resources are invested wisely.
        * Implement vision to integrate a cross-functional team of developers, data scientists, product managers, and designers into a high performing product development and design.
        * Provide thought leadership within the company; research and evaluate cutting-edge practices and enabling technologies for internal adoption.
        * Establish governance to ensure that projects and schedules are achieved while flexibility and innovation nurtured.
        * Ensure security is integrated throughout the technology lifecycle to protect the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of company and client solutions.
        * Communicate the companys technology strategy to management, staff, partners, customers, and stakeholders.
        * Integrate customer service, maintenance, and continued solution operations and support into the product team to support rapid resolution of customer issues and improve application usability.
        * Provide thought leadership outside the company in delivering innovative technology solutions to high regulated and process intensive industry including Federal Government and Fortune 1000 markets.
        * Lead and mentor the team of senior technology leaders in meeting these responsibilities
        Basic Qualifications:
        * Minimum a Bachelors degree in computer science, engineering, software development or a directly related field.
        * Experience delivering technology solutions using multiple business models including SaaS, Products, and Services.
        * Experience and past success deploying solutions with highly regulated security and compliance requirements in either Federal Government or Financial Industry.
        * 10+ years of full lifecycle of leading web based software implementations experience.
        * 10+ years leadership experience recruit, retain, develop, and promote technical professionals, including management of team lead and manager level positions.
        * 5+ years of experience responsibility with operations of secure cloud based systems.
        * Practical experience with more than one software development methodology.
        * Ability to discern executive sponsors requirements and develop specifications.
        * Knowledge of emerging trends in data science and machine learning.
        * Full stack with expertise with multiple frameworks.
        * Knowledge of multiple data store approaches and their trade-offs at scale.
        * US Citizenship.
        Preferred Qualifications:
        * Active contributing member of technology communities both locally and nationally.
        * Advanced degree and/or Masters Degree.
        * Experience in organizational theory and application to business process development, governance processes, management, budgeting, and operations.
        * 5+ years of experience with delivering data driven technology solutions.
        * 5+ years of experience in the technical consulting and services industry.
        * Department of Defense SECRET security clearance.
