Aetna s Consumer Health Services Analytics Team creates cutting edge analytic tools to drive better and faster decision making within the organization. We are currently looking for an exceptional Senior Data Scientist to play a pivotal role in the creation and deployment of information products. The position is office based in either our Wellesley, MA; Harford, CT or our New York, NY location. 
Position Summary: Leads and participates in the development, validation and delivery of algorithms, statistical models and reporting tools. Solves complex analytical problems.
Fundamental Components: 
&#183; Develops and/or uses algorithms and statistical predictive models and determines analytical approaches and modeling techniques to evaluate scenarios and potential future outcomes
&#183; Performs analyses of structured and unstructured data to solve multiple and/or complex business problems utilizing advanced statistical techniques and mathematical analyses and broad knowledge of the organization and/or industry
&#183; Collaborates with business partners to understand their problems and goals, develop predictive modeling, statistical analysis, data reports and performance metrics 
&#183; Develops and participates in presentations and consultations to existing and prospective constituents on analytics results and solutions
&#183; Interacts with internal and external peers and managers to exchange complex information related to areas of specialization
&#183; Use strong knowledge in algorithms and predictive models to investigate problems, detect patterns and recommend solutions
&#183; Use strong programming skills to explore, examine and interpret large volumes of data in various forms.
&#183; Anticipates and prevents problems and roadblocks before they occur
&#183; Demonstrates strong ability to communicate technical concepts and implications to business partners
Background/Experience: 
&#183; 5 or more years of progressively complex data analytics related experience.
&#183; Demonstrates proficiency in most areas of mathematical analysis methods, machine learning, statistical analyses, and predictive modeling and in-depth specialization in some areas.
&#183; Strong knowledge of advanced analytics tools and languages to analyze large data sets from multiple data sources.
&#183; Experience in SAS or SQL programming languages.
&#183; Experience with big data technologies such as Python, R, Hadoop, and Tableau.
&#183; Demonstrated ability to communicate technical ideas and results to non-technical clients in written and verbal form.
&#183; Strong organizational and leadership skills; excellent analytical and problem solving skills.
Education:
