          The Regeneron Genetics Center is a wholly-owned subsidiary of the Company organized to collaborate with health systems and research groups to elucidate, on a large scale, genetic factors that cause or influence a range of human diseases. Building upon Regeneron's strengths in mouse genetics and genetics-driven drug discovery and development, the Center will specialize in ultra-high-throughput exome sequencing and computational biology; discovery of genotype-phenotype associations through linkage to well-annotated de-identified patient electronic medical records; and validation of discoveries using Regeneron&#8217;s VelociGene&#174; technology. Our interests encompass a breadth of different areas such as Mendelian and family frameworks, large-scale population genetics (both common and rare variants), and gene-gene interactions. Program goals include target discovery, indication discovery, and patient-disease stratification. Objectives include advancing basic science around the world through public sharing of discoveries, providing clinically-valuable insights to physicians and patients of collaborating health-care systems, and identifying novel targets for drug development.
          Summary:
          We are looking for a Clinical Informatics Analyst to join the Regeneron Genetics Center&#8217;s Clinical Informatics team. The Clinical Informatics (CI) Analyst will focus on the management, maintenance, modeling, extraction and analysis of clinical and other phenotypic data to support research and applications. Most of this work will focus on activities such as:
          - Management, administration, quality control and analysis of clinical and research phenotypic datasets
          - Development of complex queries and algorithms to capture cohorts defined by specific features and produce analytical datasets
          Key Responsibilities:
          - Work within a team of programmers, database administrators, statisticians, clinical and data scientists to facilitate Electronic Health Record and research phenotypic data extraction, transformation, ingestion and analysis from multiple health system and research partners
          - Structure data and develop specialized queries, algorithms and views to access, curate, mine and enhance the clinical and phenotypic datasets
          - Participate in the development and maintenance of applications that utilize the clinical and phenotypic datasets

          Experience and Required Skills:
          This position requires a minimum of a BS in Computer Science, Information Technology, Biomedical Research or a life science. 3-5+ years work experience is strongly preferred. Experience should include:

          - Strong SQL skills. Familiarity with additional programming languages preferred
          - Familiarity with database environments and data warehousing concepts
          - Familiarity with Clinical/EMR data standards, coding schemes and ontologies (ICD-9/10, SNOMED, RxNorm, LOINC, CPT)
          - Familiarity with research databases such as REDcap and research cohorts
          - Experience with mining, interpretation and QA/QC of clinical and research data
          - Familiarity with statistical toolsets (such as R) preferred
