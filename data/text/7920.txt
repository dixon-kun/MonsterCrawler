        Job Description:
        We are looking for an energetic and enthusiastic candidate who is interested in joining our BI/Visualization group.         * A strong analytical background with a Masters degree in Engineering, Mathematics, Computer Science, Economics preferred
        * 2 years in strategy consulting or experience in BI/technology. Marketing analytics experience preferred.
        * Deep skills in Tableau or other visualization tools (TIBCO, QlikView, etc..)
        * SQL proficiency and have worked with cloud services technologies such as AWS Redshift.
        * Hands on UX experience (HTML, JavaScript, CSS, D3 libraries) preferred but is not a must.

        * A story teller with a strong business understanding of the solutions and problems
        * Interested in growing as a data scientist or visualization expert to help organizations extract insights in an easy and rapid manner
        * A team player that can work with internal and external clients and work with groups on-shore and off-shore
        * Familiar with visualization techniques and hands-on experience analyzing large and complex datasets to extract and represent in an easy to use, intuitive interface

        * Attention to detail

        * Demonstrable Tableau experience
        * Dashboard design experience
