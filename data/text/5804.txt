           LIFE. POWERED BY YOU.
           Are you looking to make a difference in your career? We&#8217;re working on smarter grids, cleaner energy and tools to help people manage energy more efficiently.
            Position Overview:
           This position will be in the Human Resources division within Southern California Edison. This position will report to the Principal Manager, Strategy &amp; Workforce Insights. The HR Workforce Analytics &amp; Insights Senior Manager will manage a team of 5 and is responsible for the development and delivery of strategies, processes and capabilities for HR business intelligence, workforce analytics, human capital business insights and workforce planning to the enterprise. This leader will drive the workforce planning, budget planning, data analytics/insight strategies aligned with HR organizational goals, vision and people strategy.
           The Senior HR Workforce Analytics &amp; Insights Manager will engage with HR and business leaders to understand business and people strategies, leverage enterprise data investments and translate these needs into the mission &amp; strategy of their team. He/she will lead a team of analytic professionals to deliver services on diagnostic analytics to address business identified talent issues; proactive analytics to independently identify workforce &amp; talent opportunities for the business; predictive analytics to impact attraction, development &amp; retention trends; and prescriptive analytics to build an ability to understand what is happening in our workforce, why it&#8217;s happening, what actions will improve or maintain it and the best approaches to achieve desired results.

            Management of the enterprise Employee Engagement survey and Exit Survey functions.
