BE is looking for an Solution Architect with development and consulting experience in IT services, specifically candidates with hands-on, practical experience in the Big Data and analytics fields. This position is ideal for those who like a mixture of responsibilities, including client service delivery, engineering, business development support, and solution development.  This position requires technical and thought leadership delivered in the form of briefs, demos, proof of concepts, blogs, speaking engagements, and other visible means.

Candidates must have demonstrated successful track record of developing and delivering software applications and systems as well as being able to present them orally and in writing through presentations and briefs and proposals.  Development and technical background is required.  Experience in a range of software technologies is expected, and the candidate must be prepared to provide technical expertise and leadership in one or more of the following areas: Big Data, IoT, predictive analytics and data science, and open source platforms. Candidates will be expected to practice modern approaches such as Agile software development and applying DevOps framework and techniques.

Big Data platforms and related programming skills and experience are expected, although specific programming languages are flexible. Examples include but are not limited to R, Python, Scala, Java, Hadoop, Spark, MongoDB, Cassandra, and cloud-based implementations (e.g. AWS Kinesis, Azure HDInsight).

Areas of anticipated exposure and work will be in areas where BE is building solutions and practices around such technologies as predictive analytics, Internet of Things, and other emerging and in-demand technologies. Communication skills are key ----both oral and written.  Candidate should be comfortable with speaking engagements, networking, and marketing oneself as well as BE.

The candidate will work with the Chief Technology Officer supporting business development, proposals, internal projects, demos, and expanding the company’s core capabilities. The candidate will also have strategic client service delivery projects which require a clearance .


Secret; Top Secret is preferred.
  • Develop and deliver, orally and in writing, effective demos, proof-of-concepts, and re-usable solutions
  • Draft technical proposals and provide technical support to business development efforts
  • Create solution briefs, whitepapers, videos, technical blogs, and deliver brown bags and other conferences that increase BE’s visibility and the visibility of our solutions best practices
  • Deliver internal projects which automate processes, enhance delivery, and/or otherwise reduce cost, improve quality, and increase value
  • Deliver customer-facing service delivery projects to realize their strategic and tactical value for both customer and BE

  • 7+ years’ experience with consulting, systems development, or similar IT customer services delivery experience
  • 4+ years’ experience with Big Data, analytics, data science, IoT, or other experience in an emerging technology field
  • 3+ years’ of Agile, DevOps experience and knowledge of other modern SDLC practices, techniques, and development tools
  • Excellent organizational, time management and verbal/written communication skills.

Buchanan & Edwards, Inc. (BE) is an Information Technology and Professional Services consulting firm located in Arlington, VA. BE is a diversified high-technology services company, providing government, commercial and nonprofit agencies technology solutions and organizational management services to ensure mission success. Serving the federal sector since 1998, we base our solutions on an in-depth understanding of our clients, their mission and the unique challenges they face. BE is the winner of the 2015 Microsoft U.S. Federal Solution Partner of the Year award, a 2015 Washington Post Top Workplace and has been an Inc. 500/5000 awardee for six consecutive years.
