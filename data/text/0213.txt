         About Protagonist Technology
         Position Summary
        The Protagonist Technology Analyst has a passion for using data to derive insights that leads to innovative solutions for our clients. The Analyst delivers high quality, innovative Narrative Analytics products, and helps ensure client satisfaction that leads to repeat business. The Analyst has a special combination of strong analytical skills; the ability to translate analytic findings into meaning for our clients; strong communications skills (written and graphical/presentational); and great organizational skills to work on projects with many moving parts.&#160; They are enthusiastic, creative and resourceful in using technology and data to develop new approaches to solving complex problems. Reporting to the Director of Analytics and Insights, the Analyst collaborates with colleagues across the organization.
         Primary Responsibilities
          Collaborate with cross-functional teams to analyze and synthesize data, identify and visualize insights that answer key client questions, and craft compelling, data-driven recommendations
          Creatively utilize a variety of tools, technologies, and methods to provide information and insights
          Take ownership of delivery tasks, collaborate closely with teammates to deliver the highest quality product most efficiently, proactively suggest alternate approaches to deliver projects more efficiently and effectively, and work with teammates to coordinate project activities
          Surface and articulate narratives and create visually appealing final products
          Apply new tools, technologies, and innovative methods that address client needs and create efficiencies and economies of scale
          Collaborate with Sales and Client Engagement teams on sales efforts as needed
          Develop and maintain strong relationships with colleagues to effectively and consistently deliver value to clients
          Keep abreast of industry trends, to help identify both business development and product growth opportunities
