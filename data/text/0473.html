We operate at a scale that is matched only by very few internet companies today and that brings with it great technical challenges. Our team is responsible for building our in-browser/in-app ad tag technologies that drive the entire business, so we’re looking for inventive people with a passion for building high performance products in JavaScript.

We're an agile shop and our product is constantly evolving to meet market demands that change in days and weeks rather than months or years. We practice test driven development, use continuous integration, and can push code to production daily. If working on a platform that collects tens of billions of events per day excites you, we should talk.

Integral Ad Science is seeking a Team Lead , Software Engineering to lead our video team in New York, NY.

This is a hands-on engineering team lead role for a strong technologist and high performing manager. This role requires a highly motivated individual who is comfortable with rapid changes in direction and environment. To be successful, you will need to work well in a fast paced, entrepreneurial environment. You will be responsible for leading teams through rapidly evolving complex demands, including multiple projects with dependencies on other internal organizations. You will bring excellent coding, design and architecture skills along with strong people and management acumen and experience.

If you are a customer-focused, results-oriented software leader who gets excited about starting from scratch, running hard, and solving tough technical, operational and business challenges, join us. Become part of this unique opportunity to transform online advertising.

Key Responsibilities

  • Working with product management to develop and maintain a roadmap for your team
  • Drive your team to innovate and deliver roadmap features with agility and high quality
  • Hire, develop and mentor top Engineering talent
  • Represent your team and product line in cross-functional discussions

Qualifications

  • 7+ years of progressively responsible experience in software development
  • 3+ years of experience leading other software engineers
  • Ability to work well with others, motivate other team members, and thrive in a dynamic and challenging work environment. Able to set goals and achieve scheduled deadlines.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Strong JavaScript development skills required
  • Experience with agile development methodologies
  • Deep understanding of system architecture and experience with cloud based systems
  • Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field, or equivalent work experience
Nice to haves:
    • Experience in ad tech and/or online video, a strong plus
    • Experience with Java development
    • Experience with automated testing approaches
    • Experience with build systems (maven, grunt)

About Integral Ad Science

Integral Ad Science (IAS) is a global technology and data company that builds verification, optimization, and analytics solutions to empower the advertising industry to effectively influence consumers everywhere, on every device. We solve the most pressing problems for brands, agencies, publishers, and technology companies by verifying that every impression has the opportunity to be effective, optimizing towards opportunities to consistently improve results, and analyzing digital’s impact on consumer actions. Built on data science and engineering, IAS is headquartered in New York with global operations in ten countries. Our growth and innovation have been recognized in Inc. 500, Crain’s Fast 50, Forbes America’s Most Promising Companies, and I-COM’s Smart Data Marketing Technology Company. IAS was also named to Crain’s Best Places to Work in NYC for three years running, Great Companies to Work For in NYS, and AdAge's list of Best Places to Work in the US.
