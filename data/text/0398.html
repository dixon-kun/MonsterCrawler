Position Summary

Known for its scientific and operational excellence, Regeneron is a leading science-based biopharmaceutical company that discovers, invents, develops, manufactures, and commercializes medicines for the treatment of serious medical conditions. Regeneron commercializes medicines for eye diseases, high LDL-cholesterol, and a rare inflammatory condition and has product candidates in development in other areas of high unmet medical need, including oncology, rheumatoid arthritis, asthma, atopic dermatitis, pain, and infectious diseases.


This person will be working in Talent Acquisition Operations in the Human Resources Department in Tarrytown, NY. The student will have challenging individual responsibilities and multiple work projects personally experiencing and learning how we deliver for our internal clients and external candidates. You’ll have the opportunity to network widely with our talented HR colleagues and others throughout the business.
• Work on projects relating to various TA processes, procedures and systems contributing individually and also working with different business project teams. This would include developing new ideas, building upon existing solutions, creating presentations and presenting results.
• Handle various TA Operations Customer Care programs and initiatives including internal candidate matters and online monitoring of external candidate inquires, coordination of responses, developing dashboards and reporting.
• Leverage technology and understanding of different analytical methods to identify, explore and solve TA Operations problems of varying complexities. This would include methods related to problem identification, data science, statistics, communications (written, verbal and visual) and technologies.
• Assist in coordination of different TA Operations programs including tracking, record keeping and database compliance.
• Participate on other HR related projects and ad-hoc responsibilities including reviewing job posting, screening resumes, interview scheduling, etc.
• Interact and work with the larger human resources team in support of different Talent Acquisition organizational programs and initiatives.

Start building your skills at Regeneron.
• Prefer a rising junior, rising senior or masters student
• Must be pursuing a degree in Human Resources, Business or Psychology
• Entrepreneurial, ability to problem solve, self-disciplined, resilient, versatile, Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
• Experience working in databases, reporting and dealing with customers a plus
• Involvement in campus activities a plus

Intern Program Requirements:
• Must be enrolled in, or accepted to, an academic program pursuing an Associate's, Bachelor's, Master's, or PhD
• Prefer demonstrated leadership in areas such as campus activities, clubs, sports or the community
• Minimum GPA of 3.0

Other Details:
• A free shuttle is offered from the North White Plains or Tarrytown train stations to the Tarrytown campus


This is an opportunity to join our select team that is already leading the way in the Pharmaceutical/Biotech industry. Apply today and learn more about Regeneron’s unwavering commitment to combining good science & good business.
