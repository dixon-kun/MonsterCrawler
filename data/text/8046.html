Work Schedule : Normal (M - F)
Location : Houston , TX

Responsible for providing support to the Products Performance Function Leadership Team with montlhly and quarterly reporting and analysis,  MPRs, and budgets.  The position will also assist Products Performance Leadership with special projects, which include; act as Program Manager for Data Science MTBF Devleopment, Development of Business Cases to Support Product Improvement Initiatives, Financial Account Tracking of Products Performance associated expenses, Executive presentations, strategic business plans, process improvement and standardization efforts, etc.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Montlhy & Quarterly reporting for Products Performance Function
    • MPR Reports for each Products Performance Manager and Consolidated Overall Products Performance Function
    • Cost Center & Internal Order Performance Reports
  • Montlhy / Quaterly Analysis and Summary of PONC
    • Collect regional Service PONC data associated with Products Performance
    • Follow up with regional controllers / quality managers
    • Prepare global Products Performance PONC report
    • Provide information for montly MPRs
  • Program Manage the Development of Data Analytics & Applications for Products PErformance
    • Own the overall Historical Reliability Perofrmance Program Schedule.
    • Ensure all resources are executing their tasks/activities to the schedule
    • Ensure Schedule is up-dated on a monthly basis (last day of the month)
    • Establish and monitor earned value progress of work
    • Coordinate with stakeholders to ensure data gaps are closed on a timely basis to support the delivery of historical product MTBF and reliability performance data
    • Manage the development of specifications for new Live FMECA data analytics to support the future capabilities of monitoring product performance real-time.
    • Elevate barriers to Products Performance Management
  • Annual operations budget/Forecasts
    • Provide support in annual budget preparation for the Products Performance
    • Analyze and consolidate data
    • Follow up with Product Line Products Performance leads to summarize key initiatives
    • Follow up with Product Line Products Performance leads to analyze impact on their budgets
    • Develop presentation for senior and executive management
  • Special projects to support Products Performance Management Team
  • Ensure Global Alignment
  • Comply with Common Policies, Processes, Practices and Toolsets

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Bachelor’s degree in engineering, finance, operations management, business  or related field strongly preferred with five to seven years of experience in the area.  MBA required.
  • Five or more years experience in Service or Products Management.
  • Demonstrated leadership capabilities required.
  • Strong strategic thinking with experience in development and implementation of strategies.
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills related to functional area, understanding impact on the overall global business.
  • Solid understanding of internal and external economic factors such as costs, margins, and profit impact of decisions affecting the ability to satisfy customer requirements
  • Ability to meet deadlines and manage priorities is critical.
  • Ability to lead cross-functionally.   Must demonstrate exceptional negotiation, communication, interpersonal, and team building skills in order to interact with co-workers and customers in a team-based environment
  • Strong presentation and persuasion skills in order to facilitate change within the organization preferred.  Must be able to effectively operate in a matrix environment.
  • Must have ability to handle multiple tasks and work independently with minimal direction.
  • Must have good communication skills; written, verbal and listening.
  • Must have expert knowledge of computer based analytical tools such as Excel, PowerPoint and Access and ability to utilize ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) Systems efficiently.  SAP experience in the FI and CS modules a plus.
