           The Job
          Pindrop solves tough problems and invents new ways to secure the voice channel using data and audio science in the cloud. We are looking for a Voice Interface Designer to help our customers build delightful voice interfaces using Pindrop's Authentication product. If you&#8217;re passionate about making voice the world&#8217;s primary computing interface, we&#8217;re looking for you.
           What you do
           Analyze call flows and identify opportunities to improve the customer experience using Pindrop&#8217;s Authentication capabilities
           Create call flow diagrams and voice user interface specs for speech and DTMF IVR applications
           Conduct usability testing of call flows and leverage iterative principles to constantly improve your designs
           Work with the Authentication Product and Engineering teams to maximize Pindrop&#8217;s impact within the call center
           Support Sales and Customer Success in the creation of call flow documentation and materials to showcase Pindrop&#8217;s Authentication capabilities and demonstrate value to our customers
           Who You Are
           5+ years voice interface design or relevant experience
           Deep knowledge of IVR platforms and call flow design
           Experience creating VoiceXML 2 or 2.1, including SRGS and SSML
           Experience with emerging voice platforms such as Amazon Echo ideal
           Agile methodologies knowledge helpful
           Prior experience in a start-up environment strongly desired
           Proven track record of successful and timely product delivery and of driving technical innovation
           Ability to deal with ambiguity in a fast-paced dynamic environment
           Strong communication skills, both inward and outward
           BS in CS, EE, Linguistics or relevant discipline
