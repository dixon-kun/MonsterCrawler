It’s an exciting time to be at UMass Medical School!  U.S. News & World Report ranks UMass Medical School 12th in the country for primary care, and this opportunity gives the right candidate the chance to be part of an elite academic and research institution that is among the most affordable in the country.  As a member of our Information Technology Department, you will join a dynamic team of dedicated individuals committed to delivering forward thinking solutions with a focus on continuous improvement. At UMass Medical School IT we recognize and reward independent thought and innovative ideas. We have fostered an environment where the team dynamic is embraced and collaboration and FUN are core components of our culture!

Under the general direction of the Associate Chief Information Officer or designee, the Manager Data Sciences and Technology is responsible for the planning, design, review, programming and implementation of institutional software and biomedical big data solutions at the University of Massachusetts Medical School for advancing research and translation of research findings to clinics. This includes designing, developing and implementing various solutions of capturing and integrating heterogeneous biomedical research data. In addition, he/she will serve as product owner, scientific consultant, and instructor to the Medical School faculty and staff. Performs diverse and complex duties in a manner consistent with a dynamic and active biomedical education and research community.


MAJOR RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Envision and deliver information systems solutions in alignment with the Medical School's strategic and tactical business plans.
  • Oversee existing platforms and processes to deliver efficient and effective services to meet the expectations developed with stakeholders. Identify areas of improvement for processes, technology and resources and implements these improvements
  • Manage a technical team involving both functional and matrix resources that delivers technology solutions for business users
  • Manage strategic vendor relationships including platform vendors, support and development partners.
  • Play a leadership role in consulting with business units across UMMS to define business opportunities and map potential technology solutions to enable UMMS strategic direction. Maintain strategic focus on developing new functionality to serve UMMS community and business partners.Participate long-range planning and budgeting, and ensures the achievement of organizational and operational plans
  • Leads or participates in RFP development, acquisitions, evaluations and contract negotiations. Plans and coordinates software acquisitions, conversions and implementations. Responsible for vendor relations
  • Ensure that priorities are established and evaluated on an ongoing basis for systems modifications and requests from our user community. Maintain effective organization and staffing to meet demand
  • In coordination with other departments, ensure thorough evaluation of equipment/hardware capabilities and recommend hardware and platform acquisitions. Evaluate and recommend hardware environment architectures to support ongoing customer needs
  • Identify efficient tools for business and clinical application design and development. Incorporate technology tools where appropriate
  • Effectively implement, promote and ensure compliance with professional and industry standards, UMASS policies and procedures, and IS quality standards for database administration, application development, business analysis requirement definition, technology development, software testing, reporting, project planning/management, and administration infrastructure and support capabilities
    • Manage staff staff development, and training within the Information Technology department
    • Ensure the accuracy of work estimates, improving the overall quality of deliverables/systems, and ensuring data models, database code, and enterprise reporting components developed by staff conform with identified best practices (i.e.: scalability, security, usability, etc.)
  • Participate in the oral presentation of all project findings and abstracts including participation in periodic project status meetings and presentation of final project deliverables
  • Prepare informational/educational materials for users and train clinical researchers in the use of computing resources
  • Arrange informational seminars and teach technology courses as requested as relevant to clinical research.
  • Manage staff including participate in hire, discipline, termination, performance evaluations, and employee development
  • Perform other duties as assigned

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS:

  • B.S. in Computer Science, Informatics, a related field, or equivalent experience
  • 7 years of experience in development and implementation of technologies preferably for an academic research organization
  • Proven experience with research data in a scientific research environment
  • Strong interpersonal and communication skills required
  • Demonstrated ability to provide documentation.
  • Ability to communicate effectively in writing

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Advanced degree
  • Experience with the application of programming expertise in computational chemistry and/or genomic data analysis and/or visualization
  • Exposure to various types of biomedical big data, tools, and high-throughput data analysis
  • Experience with whole genome analysis, such as assembly and mapping and/or knowledge of variant (SNP, indel, PAV, CNV, etc)
  • Experience with Computational Chemistry and drug design tools like Schrodinger
  • Experience in the use of Chemical/biological databases, particularly the NCBI tools
  • Experience with design and analysis of research projects making use of computational resources
  • Experience in small molecule screening

ADDITIONAL RESPONSIBILITES:

  • Lead development of software tools and libraries for institutional analytics applications associated with the Clinical and Research Data Repositories using tools like Perl, Python, R etc.
  • Help researchers solve unique, biomedical big data problems using the Clinical and Research Data Repositories and software systems like Schrodinger
  • Apply programming expertise for developing/implementing institutional technology solutions in computational chemistry, drug design, high-throughput genomic data analysis and visualization.
  • Function as the expert for institutional software tools for computational chemistry, drug design, high-throughput genomic data analysis and visualization
  • Work closely with other product managers, engineers, and data analysts to develop prototypes and production data and visualization systems.
  • Lead the acquisition/development of software, products, data marts, and services to Enhance clinical/research discoveries
  • Provide information technology consulting on data and analytics
  • Identify and evaluate computing research opportunities and actively engage in seeking funding under the direction of the head of the department
