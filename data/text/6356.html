Data Scientist at Bookstores.com

Columbus, OH 43212

Report this job

About the Job

General Purpose

Data Scientists play a key role in the planning and execution of projects. They work with the team to manage the backlog of questions, develop analytical plans before projects are initiated, execute the analytical plan, uncover key insights from data through statistical and mathematical modeling and assist in creating deliverables for the business partners.

Main Job Tasks and Responsibilities

  • Identifies business trends and problems through complex big data analysis
  • Excess Inventory Analysis
  • Familiarity and experience with creating analytical data sets
  • Interact with multiple business areas to gather and interpret business requirements, and deliver a data-based solution
  • Analyze numerous variables across very large samples; identifying new patterns and variables
  • Leading and participating in projects that require advanced data and business analysis skills including best in practice statistical methodologies and modeling
  • Facilitate the definition of assigned project scope, goals and deliverables
  • Interprets results from multiple sources using a variety of techniques, ranging from simple data aggregation via statistical analysis to complex data mining independently.

Experience

  • Experience in creating and implementing machine learning algorithms and advanced statistics such as: regression, clustering, decision trees, exploratory data analysis methodology, simulation, scenario analysis, modeling, and neural networks.
  • Experience in analyzing data from business line data applications and data providers such as Google Analytics, Site Catalyst, Coremetrics, Adwords, Crimson Hexagon, Facebook Insights, Nielsen, Comscore, Simmons, MRI and etc.
  • Experience in visualizing data to stakeholders in a simple and concise manner through visualization software such as ggplot, D3,Tableau Qlinkview, Periscope, Business Objects, or other similar software.
  • Ability to analyze data, draw insights, and prepare reports in a cohesive, intuitive, and simplistic manner.
  • Strong communication skills
  • Experience working in a consumer-centric company with teams of technical professionals in a cross-functional environment.
  • Excellent organization and prioritization skills
  • Must have strong understanding of data mining models, structures, theories, principles, and practices, including machine learning, statistics and text mining techniques.
  • Experience developing and applying statistical or machine learning methods in a corporate environment through applications such as SAS, R, S-PLUS, or python.
  • Fluent in programming languages such as Python, Java, or C++ in a data mining context, and experience building web-based applications using HTML, CSS, Perl, and JavaScript.
  • Direct experience with SQL programming and large database applications such as Oracle, DB2, SQL Server, Teradata, and Hadoop.

Education Requirements:

  • Master’s Degree in Statistics, Math, Economics, Engineering, Computer Science, Business Analytics, Data Science or a related field, or a Bachelor’s Degree with equivalent work experience.
  • 5-7 years of relevant work experience
  • Retail or E-Commerce experience a plus


Bookstores.com is proud to be an equal opportunity employer. Must be eligible to work in the U.S. on a permanent basis.
