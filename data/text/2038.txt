         Responsibilities:
            Noblis is building a behavioral analytics team to support a wide variety of client needs focused on detecting fraudulent electronic transactions/usage. &#160;Cyber Fraud Forensic Analysis team members will work collaboratively with the Noblis Lead Data Analytics architects and data scientists to detect fraud through analytics capability and visualization in support of Federal Agencies. The Cyber Fraud Forensic Analyst will assist the customer in strengthening their security posture against fraudulent transactions, through detailed and accurate forensic cyber analysis and reporting.&#160; Analysts will work closely with government personnel and other Noblis professionals in a team environment.
            Responsibilities include:
              Traige and interpret system logs
              Detect fraud through analytic methodologies
              Identify new trends for report development and analysis
              Collaborate with Tool and Report Developers
              Recommend improvements for the detection of fraudulent behavior through the analysis of data from many disparate sources
              Author findings summary reports to enable partner organization awareness and response
              Understand business processes and synthesize potential fraud scenarios and risk indicators
              Interact directly with client project team members and operational staff
              Summarize findings in concise and coherent manner for delivery to client senior leadership
