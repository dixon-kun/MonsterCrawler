Data Analytics Specialist (Data Science/Google Analyst) – Trinity, FL

Who is Nicopure Labs?

Nicopure Labs is the manufacturer for the industry leading HALO & eVo e-Liquid brands. Our vision is to be the thought-leader and the very best in the vaping industry by providing consistent, quality, and great-tasting American-made e-liquids. So how do we get there? By delivering premium value to every consumer, business partner, employee and maintaining the highest standards in all aspects of our business, while promoting tobacco harm reduction.

What are we looking for?

We have a tremendous opportunity for an experienced and highly analytical Data Analytics Specialist dedicated to enhancing consumer engagement experiences and digital marketing efforts and performance. This position will join a growing Marketing Department based in our Corporate HQ office in Trinity, FL (North Tampa Bay).

Our Data Analytics Specialist is responsible for a nalytics, insights, and reporting for all behavior metrics, including acquisition and retention analytics by geography, device, channel, and demographics.  They will also be working with various teams to deliver consulting services and cross-functional solutions.

What does Nicopure Labs offer?

Growth! Nicopure Labs is investing a tremendous amount of resources throughout our company, none more important than adding the right talent, that want to grow with us. You will help us continue to spearhead this brand new and exciting industry for generations to come. We also offer a competitive base salary, along with benefits (Health, Dental, Vision, Life Insurance, and 401k).


What will you be doing?

  • Analyzing and forecasting data as well as effectively auditing existing implementations of Google Analytics to present actionable insights and recommendations.
  • Develop custom performance measurement schemas, algorithms, models and analyses in support of consumer engagement and marketing efforts.
  • Implementing solutions via Google Tag Manager, JavaScript & related technologies.
  • Experience with working with Google Analytics API.
  • Produce and maintain KPI reports that monitor the performance of eCommerce Programs, preform ad-hoc deep-dive analysis.
  • Manage relationships with key marketing and analytical vendors. Also, responsible for managing the budget on an annual basis.
  • Drive, test and learn activities for marketing spend and consumer engagement activities.
  • Excellent communication skills with the ability to simplify and convey complex issues or challenges to better educate business partners.
  • Produce regular reporting and periodic research into key consumer segments and digital product usage by leveraging Google Analytics, Marketo, SAP and other tools.

Who is the ideal candidate?

  • BS/BA degree required in relevant field of study.
  • 3-5 years working experience in Web Analytics, Google Analytics preferred.
  • 1+ years eCommerce experience.
  • A good understanding of statics is a must.
  • Expert skills in data analysis in Excel and SQL.
  • Highly organized and methodical with a keen attention to detail.
  • Demonstrates a high degree of self-sufficiency and ability to work on individual projects, as well as those that require working with teams.
  • Personable, proactive team player that can handle multiple tasks and deadlines with positivity and enthusiasm.
