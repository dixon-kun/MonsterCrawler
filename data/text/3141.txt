         Undergrad Technical Software Intern
          Job Description
         Job Description: Undergraduate machine learning and Data Science Intern for New Devices Group Innovation team. NDG's smart device innovation group is hiring talented interns in the field of machine learning and data science. As a part of our diverse and multidisciplinary team, you will be exposed to the cutting-edge technologies, and have an opportunity to apply your skills for creating unique solutions in the innovation and product development space. The machine learning and data science intern will usually work in a project team that aims at finding an appropriate algorithmic solution to a given problem while using his or her machine learning knowledge and experience. He or she will be part of the project's team throughout the project's life cycle - exploration, planning, analysis, design, development, testing and implementation.
          Qualifications
         You must possess the below minimum qualifications to be initially considered for this position.
         Preferred qualifications are in addition to the minimum requirements and are considered a plus factor in identifying top candidates.
         Experience listed below would be obtained through a combination of your school work/classes/research and/or relevant previous job and/or internship experiences.
         The responsibilities will include, but are not limited to the following:
         - Ability to work with minimal supervision
         - Ability to work effectively in a multidisciplinary and matrixed team
         Minimum Qualifications:
         - Must be pursuing a B.Sc in Computer Science or Information Technology
         - 1 years of experience with machine learning, data analysis and analytics and data science.
         - 1 years of experience with Matlab/R/Python or other similar languages
         - 1 years of experience with C/C , Java along with app development.
