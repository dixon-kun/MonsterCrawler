         The Department of Evaluation &amp; Decision Science at Mercy Virtual is seeking a Senior Data Scientist to work within a team of other advanced scientists in the generation, extraction, and compilation of data to perform creative, high-quality, state-of-the-art analyses and evaluations that answer questions, make discoveries, and support decision-making within the emerging virtual healthcare segment. Mercy has an incredible amount of operational, clinical, and financial data and increasing capacity to integrate additional data from external and device sources, such as geospatial indexes and biosensors. This is a great opportunity to work on the forefront of technology and methodology to address challenging health care questions and uncover the keys to the successful delivery of virtual healthcare services. The candidate should have an affinity for learning and a growth mindset, be able to effectively develop, document, and communicate project plans, demonstrate expertise with data analytic functions and related software tools, and deliver outputs on a tight time budget. This position will report to the Director of the Department of Evaluation &amp; Decision Science.
          Responsibilities:
          Proven ability to build and manage successful relationships with internal and external stakeholders and work both independently and within a team-centric environment
          Effectively communicate with internal and external stakeholders to develop requirements, document data definitions, select appropriate data sources, extract, integrate, visualize, and analyze data using appropriate data mining and data analysis tools e.g. SQL, R, SAS, Python, etc
          Integrate decentralized data systems, devise/implement strategies for the development of data marts and data warehouses, and work with the Mercy Virtual team members on data commercialization opportunities
          Develop data commercialization and publication quality data sets and analyses utilizing data from various internal and external sources in collaboration with internal and external business partners
          Perform duties and responsibilities in a manner consistent with our Mission, Values, and Charism
