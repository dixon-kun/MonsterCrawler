Data Scientist, Mid

Key Role:

Apply technical and analytical expertise to explore and examine data from multiple disparate sources with the goal of discovering patterns and previously hidden insights, providing a competitive advantage, or addressing a pressing business problem. Demonstrate expertise in computer science, software development, and the latest technologies to design and implement analysis infrastructure and tools, analytic workflow processes, and complex data visualizations. Leverage knowledge of mathematical and statistical concepts, computer science, or a domain to bridge the gap between technologists and mathematicians, ensuring software solutions meet analytic requirements.

Basic Qualifications:

-2+ years of experience with data science

-Experience with statistical theory and application through Open Source

-Experience with statistical theory through the use of R or Python

-Ability to communicate technical results to non–technical clients

-Secret clearance

-BA or BS degree

Additional Qualifications:

-Possession of excellent oral and written communication skills

-Possession of excellent analytical and organizational skills

-MS degree in Statistics, Mathematics Operations Research, or a related field preferred

Clearance:

Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information; Secret clearance is required.
