About the role:

The Project Manager will have a strong conceptual understanding of the latest marketing platforms and technologies. You will have previous experience in the delivery of digital (email) and offline campaigns. You will have a strong client facing experience and have experience of delivering projects throughout the full project management life-cycle and managing client expectations throughout the project. You will be scoping, budgeting and managing the client's expectations. You will also be responsible for the financial monitoring of projects, ensuring that projects are scoped accurately and are profitable, making sure that all projects are delivered on time and on budget and to the highest quality.
