             POSITION SUMMARY
             The Marketing Director position within the Enterprise Marketing team will provide strategic marketing insights and consultation to corporate areas senior leaders. The Marketing Director will lead and direct marketing initiatives for these clients and across Enterprise Marketing. This position will develop and manage broad-reaching, complex projects, plans, and applicable metrics for impactful outreach targeting stakeholders and aligning with Enterprise Marketing and corporate areas goals and objectives.
             Fundamental Components:
             - Influences senior corporate and marketing leaders and other stakeholders to turn vision into action and measurable outcomes. Initiates and develops marketing strategies utilizing marketing insights, best practices, data analytics, and enterprise intelligence. Drives change and sets direction via strategic consultation . Identifies and understands key priorities and programs and positively impacts results.
             - Leads and owns all aspects of complex projects that have broad impact across and for the enterprise. Leads ideation and influences connections to engage project partners and drives and delivers results. Creates and has full accountability for projects and initiatives targeting key external and internal audiences.
             - Leads and directs high visibility, high impact projects for internal corporate clients and Enterprise Marketing. Establishes best practices and influences operating model to effectively market Marketing across the enterprise. Anticipates cross-functional needs and engages, directs and manages internal and external partners and resources outside of span of control. Drives overarching planning and execution oversight and management. Manages complex project plans and resources. Develops and tracks key success metrics utilizing data science and marketing analytics.
             - Creates and promotes compelling industry differentiation externally utilizing thought leadership, online and offline marketing content, and communications via various channels. Ensures corporate and enterprise partners are utilized effectively to drive internal engagement and drive collaboration to expand external reach, influence, and impact.
