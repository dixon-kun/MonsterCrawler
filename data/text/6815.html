Overview of Role

Windows Engineer will have proven experience in all aspects of building, imaging, and deployment to end user devices (i.e. desktops, laptops and tablets). This includes configuration, customization, implementation, testing, changes and operational support of the operating system and all deployed desktop. Candidate will serve as a subject matter expert for all end user device hardware and standard software. Provide L3 support when escalated by operation/support teams, perform root cause assessment and remediation. Candidate will develop and maintain device images, documentation on all current and newly implemented technologies.
