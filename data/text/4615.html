ADMINISTRATIVE ASSISTANT II, Statistics and Data Science Center (SDSC), Institute for Data, Systems, and Society (IDSS), to handle a diverse set of complex administrative duties in support of the faculty director of the SDSC in his academic, research, and administrative roles. Will serve as initial point of contact for all inquiries for the director from both inside and outside MIT; draft, edit, and proofread all correspondence, reports, memoranda, and presentations; assemble complex data (tables, charts, reports, memorandums, and PowerPoint presentations); collect and organize information for the director; organize and schedule complex appointments and travel arrangements; handle logistics (e.g., room reservation, food, audio visual, etc.) for meetings, weekly seminars, and other workshops; copy and distribute documents or teaching materials; maintain SDSC web pages; serve as the contact person for student advisees; support faculty member's research assistants and research staff; assist with faculty and postdoc searches; work closely with the staff in the IDSS Academic Office to support the statistics programs; process travel expense vouchers, requests for payment, and credit card purchases; generate requisitions and purchase orders; and take on special projects as assigned.

Job Requirements

REQUIRED: a minimum of three years' direct/related experience; attention to detail; ability to work both independently and as a team member with minimal supervision; strong organizational, multitasking and interpersonal skills; sound judgment with confidential information/issues; exceptional verbal and written communication skills; comfort interacting with a wide range of faculty, staff, and students; ability to work in a high pressure environment with changing priorities; strong technical skills including proficiency with MS Office, LaTeX, and other computer skills (updating websites using WordPress, publishing information, etc.).
