Investment Data Specialist
Data Management Division
SWIB is seeking an experienced Investment Data Specialist to lead, implement and manage SWIB’s data management practices. The specialist is responsible for working with different areas of the organization to ensure that implementation of data governance principles, policies and standards are incorporated within the business areas. The individual must be an advocate for data governance and data quality.
Essential activities:
  • Lead SWIB’s data management practices
  • Assist in the development of the strategic direction of data governance
  • Communicate data management concepts, strategic data initiatives, and data related issues to senior management
  • Function as central business contact for SWIB data, information flows and processes
  • Lead security master data stewardship and work with delegated data owners and data stewards to ensure data quality of assigned data domains
  • Work with Enterprise Risk and Compliance to identify data related controls, assess risks and develop additional controls where needed
  • Research and remain knowledgeable about new tools and applications that can be utilized at SWIB to enhance the value of quality data, metrics and transparent reporting
  • Provide project management of individual projects and research efforts related to data governance
The ideal candidate will have:
  • Bachelor’s degree in finance, data science, technology, business, economics, or related required
  • At least 5 years of experience in a data management, governance, and quality capacity
  • Certified Data Management Professional (CDMP) certification (preferred)
  • Investment industry experience (preferred)
  • An ability to be adaptive and thrive in a fast-paced, changing environment
  • Superb work ethic, attention to detail, team orientation, and commitment to excellence
Deadline to apply is March 3, 2017. Resumes will be accepted and reviewed after that date if the position has not been filled.
