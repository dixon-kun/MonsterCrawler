          What You&#8217;re Applying For:
         The Market Research and Consumer Insights Manager will work independently and in conjunction with the Director of Market Research and Customer Insights and VP of Corporate Strategy to build research capabilities and customer insights at Edmunds. &#160;Leveraging qualitative and quantitative primary research and secondary research, the Manager will generate strategic insights to help the business identify new areas of opportunity and product, experience, and sales enhancements. &#160;In addition, the Manager will be responsible for identifying needs, leading, analyzing, interpreting and communicating the results of research efforts, and working with teams around the organization to turn their insights into action. &#160;Ultimately, the Manager is responsible for leading critical and highly impactful research efforts for Edmunds, covering customer, dealer and OEM needs, market trends and competitive intelligence.
          What You&#8217;ll Do
         :
          Conduct market research on and build knowledge of key trends in the automotive and tech industries to generate strategic insights
          Conduct end-to-end primary research projects, including defining the project scope, developing survey/research, fielding research, managing vendors, analyzing results, and delivering/communicating key insights
          Effectively connect primary and secondary research with internal data to uncover unique and actionable insights, partnering with Analytics and Data Science as needed
          Analyze outputs from Edmunds' research tools (including customer and dealer panels, NPS survey, Brand Tracker, and survey creation tools) and work with the Director and executives across the company to develop action plans from these results
          Help craft compelling executive-level stories with surprising insights
          Act as a thought partner to the business leaders of the company to help provide a strategic research perspective on critical questions
          Bring your experience with other companies and best practices to help Edmunds build a best-in-class research approach and focus
          Keep abreast of new developments, methods, and technologies in research to expand Edmunds' repository of effective techniques
          Proactively identify areas of opportunity that we could support with primary or secondary research
          Work with teams around the company to build understanding of the uses and limitations of research and when and how to best engage the research team
          Challenges
         :
         The Market Research and Customer Insights Manager will be responsible for digging into questions such as:
          What does the customer buying journey look like? What are the key activities, needs, emotions, pain-points at each stage of the journey?
          Who are Edmunds' main competitors? What products do they offer, what is their share of the market, and what are their value propositions? How are we differentiated from them?
          Who are our customers? What are the key needs for each kind of customer? What job does Edmunds do for them?
          What creative research techniques can we use to get to the next order of insight about our consumers, dealers, and OEMs?
         He or she will also be responsible for the dissemination of this information to the rest of the company in a way that is fun, engaging and memorable.

          Superior analytical ability coupled with ability to quickly formulate non-obvious insights from analysis
          Action-oriented and focused on the implication of work, not just the output

          Demonstrated ability to work cross-functionally with range of people with different styles, backgrounds and experiences
          History of strong interpersonal skills and being a team player who quickly builds trust and credibility through relationships
          Exceptional ability to read and understand people, to glean insights beyond just what is said
