        Photo Intern
        The BuzzFeed Entertainment Group design team needs a hard-working intern for a full-time paid position in the Los Angeles office, to start as soon as possible.
        We are looking for someone with a passion for photography, an understanding of photo rights, and the ability to work in a fast-paced environment.
        Responsibilities:
        * Photo Editing:
        * Downloading images for writers as requested
        * Assist in photo retouching when necessary
        * Upload and archive photos into server
        * Contribute to brainstorms for original photography
        * Pitch photo essays for photo newsletter
        * In Studio:
        * Participate in planning meetings for upcoming shoots
        * Assist photographer with load in, setup and break down
        * Assist photographer when on set (lighting, directing)
        Requirements:
        * Degree or course of study in photography or related field
        * Proficiency in Adobe Photoshop and Bridge
        * Knowledge of basic studio lighting
        * Experience with DSLR cameras
        * Capture One experience is a plus
        * Excellent communication skills and a talent for collaboration
        * An interest in current events, pop culture, and social media
        * Positive attitude and ability to adapt to any situation
        * Please include a link to a portfolio site of your recent work, no PDFs or dropbox links please
