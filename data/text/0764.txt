        The Editorial Project Coordinator will work closely with the Managing Editor and help support the team in an administrative capacity, managing everything from calendars to budgets, along with anticipating the logistical needs of a growing office. This is a great opportunity for anyone looking to learn more about online publishing and operations at a fast-growing media company.
        Responsibilities:
        * Coordinate/manage mid to large-scale editorial projects
        * Use data to track success and provide insights for future projects
        * Provide general support to the Managing Editor and edit team with day-to-day administrative tasks, including managing calendars, planning team events and booking travel
        * Work with the Operations Associate to anticipate the needs of new staff and maintaining a fun, organized atmosphere
        Requirements:
        * Proven organizational and administrative skills, showing a precise attention to detail
        * Experience working for a print or online publishing company
        * Ability to multitask and anticipate needs
        * Confidence in a fast paced environment and with a good amount of autonomy
        * Emotional intelligence and a sense of humor
