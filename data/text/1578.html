We are looking for a data scientist with experience in marketing analytics, to join us with the title of Analyst. This person will support the Millward Brown Analytics team in applying sophisticated data science techniques to address a variety of marketing problems.  This position provides support to clients by recommending analyses most appropriate for research objectives, interpreting statistical results and drawing conclusions about findings.  In addition, you will have the opportunity to contribute to study design, solution development and ongoing innovation initiatives.

We work across a wide variety of statistical techniques such as driver analyses, segmentation, customer experience analyses and media mix modelling to name a few.  It is expected that the candidate will have familiarity in using software such as SAS, R, Matlab, Python, etc. extensively to analyze survey as well as industry data

Kantar Millward Brown Analytics is a leading strategic marketing analytics, research and management consulting firm based in the US (http://www.millwardbrownanalytics.com). We are unique in our ability to effectively combine state-of-the-art data science approaches with a custom marketing consulting approach to solve our client’s most difficult business issues. Our clients are Fortune 100 companies across a diverse mix of industries (e.g., financial services, telecom, retail, entertainment, technology, CPG, healthcare), most of whom are market leaders in their respective industries.

KEY OUTCOMES

Ability to manage multiple demands simultaneously and meet deadlines
Applied knowledge of advanced statistical techniques used in quantitative analysis; ability to select the appropriate tools and solutions to answer a client’s central business issue
Excellent problem-solving skills; strong ability to proactively identify and implement effective solutions
Ability to summarize and present information in a way that provides clarity and interest

CAPABILITIES

Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Statistics or other applied quantitative discipline.
1-2 years of experience working in a marketing science and/or advanced analytics role in related areas.
Intermediate Microsoft Office skills, including PowerPoint, Excel and Outlook required
Intermediate SAS  and/or SPSS software skills required; additionally, experience with Tableau, Sawtooth, R programming, database management, dashboard development, simulator development and data visualization tools are desirable
