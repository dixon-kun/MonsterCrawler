Job Description - Systems Engineer Senior (17637) Experian is an Equal Opportunity Employer Systems Engineer Senior - Job Number: 17637 Description As a member of the Data Science Solutions Support team, you will be responsible for the implementation and operational support of Experian DSS Customer Solutions. Employees in this role primarily support and maintain servers, and during troubleshooting also deal with networks and network devices, load balancers, firewalls, storage and storage network switching. This is a hands-on position that will require interacting with customers, performing system analysis, build deployment and automation, solution design, system configuration maintenance activities. The successful candidate is a senior level resource and will possess a solid understanding of enterprise environments including VMware virtualization and be able to troubleshoot and resolve software, hardware, configuration and operational problems across the VMware, Wintel, x86‑64 Linux platforms. Knowledge of multiple vendor technologies and platforms including VMware, Microsoft Windows, MS SQL Server, Linux variants (SLES11, RHEL6/7) Candidates should be team players with excellent communication skills and have the ability to manage assignments independently. Qualifications Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Networking, Engineering, or equivalent career experience in a related technical field. Experience in Infrastructure design and deployments or related field (minimum 5+ years) Experience in network and infrastructure support and administration (minimum 5 years) Experience working in a team-oriented and collaborative environment. Ability to demonstrate strong troubleshooting/diagnostic skills, think analytically while working in a fast paced IT environment. Proven ability to provide high customer satisfaction Strong communication and interpersonal skills, both written and verbal. Proven analytical and problem solving skills Ability to communicate highly technical information to business oriented audiences Ability to perform multiple functions and operate in dynamic environment with minimal supervision Proven background of providing great customer service. Demonstrated ability to achieve successful outcomes in handling difficult situations and work with various customers and management levels. Shows initiative and acts independently to resolve problems. Demonstrated ability to manage multiple priorities and follow through on projects to completion. Works effectively both as a team member and independently. Hands-on technical experience desired in the following areas: Advanced experience with Microsoft Operating Systems: Windows Server 2003/2008+/2012+, Windows 7/8/10 Advanced experience with Active Directory, ADFS Experience with the support and performance tuning in a distributed environment Experience with TCP/IP, routing, Layer 2 and 3 switching, WAN connections, VPN, DHCP, DNS, VLAN’s, SSL certificates, HTTP/HTTPS, etc. Strong understanding of networking protocols network layer troubleshooting Scripting/automation knowledge of Windows scripting (CMD, Powershell) Scripting/automation knowledge of knowledge of Linux shell scripting (sh/bash, perl) Experience with SQL scripting and database management to include designing and implementing maintenance schedules and profiling for performance enhancements Experience with troubleshooting web services. General knowledge of XML, SOAP, and .NET desired Knowledge of Transact and ATB highly regarded Understanding of ITIL concepts preferred. Experience with scripting and automation a strong plus. Experience with PCI concepts a strong plus. Experience with SQL Server a plus. Experience with network and server load balancing tools a plus. Experience with monitoring and infrastructure management tools a plus.
