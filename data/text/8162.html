Provide leadership and technical expertise to execute the planning, installation, configuration, operation, maintenance, management and support of network infrastructure. In particular, this position has primary responsibility for our NetScaler ADC environment.


  • Primarily responsible for design, engineering, analysis, configuration, and troubleshooting of Wunderman network Citrix NetScaler ADC environment
  • Secondary responsibilities include network infrastructure and services, including routers/switches, firewalls, wireless, and VoIP environment
  • Design, implement, and enhance network infrastructure that enables business and client objectives
  • Resolve Incidents, Problems, and Service Requests to meet business and client service objectives
  • Develop detailed documentation that consist of guidelines, checklist, and project plans
  • Multi-tasks, prioritizes according to business priorities and production availability requirements
  • Build trusted relationships with business partners and other IT teams in order to achieve results
  • Required to be available for on call duties, and must be reachable during those times

  • Be able to deliver sound technical solutions that enable WDM to achieve its business objectives in accordance with budgetary and security requirements
  • Demonstrate a high level of knowledge and experience with:
    • managing SSL certificates and various cipher suites, as well as best practice configurations for encrypted communications utilizing NetScaler
    • NetScaler responder policy configuration
    • NetScaler access gateway configuration
    • NetScaler content switching configurations
  • Quickly understand new complex technical multi-tiered environments
  • Have exceptional interpersonal skills, technical communication skills, as well as a strong work ethic, and positive attitude
  • Have strong and creative problem analysis and problem solving skills
  • Be able to manage priorities to meet broader organizational and personal project objectives, and adapt to frequently changing priorities
  • Have excellent written and verbal communication skill
  • Be process oriented to enable repeatable and comprehensive solutions
  • Be able to work independently, while knowing when and how to contribute to a team
  • Have familiarity with ITIL principles and framework. Foundation certification is a plus.
  • Be able to lift up to 20 pounds

Technology

  • Citrix Netscaler ADCs
  • Palo Alto, Cisco, and Check Point firewalls
  • Cisco Data Center and Enterprise routers and switches
  • Various packet capture & analysis tools
  • Netflow and Appflow reporting
  • Monitoring tools, e.g. Solar Winds

Education / Experience

  • CCP-N or 5+ years equivalent experience implementing and managing NetScaler Gateway Enterprise Edition, including EPA and VPN plugins
  • CCP-N or 5+ years equivalent experience implementing and managing Netscaler ADCs utilizing load balancing, content switching, SSL offloading, and AppFirewall running on VPX and SDX appliances
  • BS in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field required; Equivalent work experience may be substituted in lieu of a degree
