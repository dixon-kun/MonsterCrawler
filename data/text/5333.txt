          Position Summary
          Razorfish Health is currently seeking a Regulatory Manager will be primarily responsible for overseeing the protocols for submitting marketing materials to our pharmaceutical clients for Medical-Legal-Regulatory (MLR) review (a board of experts typically consisting of medical doctors, lawyers and experts in FDA regulations). The Manager oversees larger brands or clients as well as junior and senior associates and spearheads the creation and timely, consistent, and compliant delivery marketing materials, as well as tracking the progress of each project through the various stages of MLR review. Other responsibilities include, but are not limited to:
          &#8226; Client-facing role responsible for communication and delivery to client contacts with regards to all things related to MLR submission
          &#8226; Determine the time and effort required to complete each submission
          &#8226; Establish a timeline and assign responsibility to the appropriate team members for the delivery of the components of the submission
          &#8226; Responsible for daily support, oversight, and performance management of junior and senior associates
          &#8226; Accountable for the quality and delivery of work completed by junior and senior associates
          &#8226; Collaborate with Regulatory Affairs staff and interdisciplinary teams on the creation of MLR submissions
          &#8226; Act as the primary point of contact between the agency and the clients' MLR Review department, both to get information regarding submissions requirements and to answer procedural questions that arise during the MLR review process
          &#8226; Oversee the creation of the components and ensure they conform to client and Razorfish Health guidelines for quality and timely completion of MLR deliverables
          &#8226; Maintain a comprehensive, accurate dashboard of all current and upcoming submissions including the stage of review, job codes, and deadlines
