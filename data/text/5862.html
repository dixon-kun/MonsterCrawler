* Support and manage the design, development, implementation, and application of modeling and simulation tools which integrate other tools for assessment, experimentation, concept development, demonstrations, prototyping and alternate futures analysis and other analytical efforts

* Demonstrated hands-on analytical problem solving skills for conceiving solutions, testing hypothesis, communicating, getting buy-in and implementing decision support system(s)

* Experience with high end manufacturing and complex manufacturing systems

* Ability to analyze, dissect, translate, synthesize and abstract (as necessary) manufacturing system design requirements and build simulation models according to those requirements

* Utilize discrete event simulation software to plan and optimize new assembly lines and processes prior to execution. Work with the Manufacturing Engineers and Industrial Engineers to identify, scope, and execute projects on existing process to improve inefficiencies

* Proficiency with a discrete-event simulation software tool, such as Plant Simulate, Arena, Simio, AnyLogic, ProModel, FlexSim, Simul8, ExtendSim, SimEvents, JaamSim, etc.

* Strong domain expertise in at least one of the following areas: agent based modeling, systems dynamic modeling, discrete event modeling, applied probability and statistics, or operations research

* Experience in Object-Oriented Programming (C , C#, Java)

* Integrate simulation solutions into NGC's software and analytics architecture

* Work with NGC Teammates in delivering actionable decision support solutions to real and proof-of-concept problems

* Work with internal clients, project stakeholders and support partners to gather system design requirements and create technical specifications

* Strong written, oral and interpersonal communication skills, with particular experience in creating clear and concise presentations and communicating results and findings in meetings with clients, project stakeholders and support partners

Basic Qualifications

Bachelor's Science STEM (Science,Technology, Engineering, Math) with a minimum of 5 years experience; 3 years experience with Master's Science STEM; 1 year experience with PhD. The ideal candidate should have experience with Data Science, Statistics, Predictive Analytics, Applied Mathematics, Operations Research, manufacturing and/or related quantitative field. Ability to obtain and maintain DoD security clearance. Proficient in Microsoft Excel, Project, Power Point, discrete event simulation software experience.

Preferred Qualifications

2 years experience working with discrete event simulation

Experience in Siemens Team Center, Siemens Plant Simulate

Bachelor's Science Industrial Engineering

Must be able to maintain multiple simultaneous projects, working alone or in a team setting.

Proven group facilitation, strong oral and written communication skills, and strong interpersonal and collaboration skills.
