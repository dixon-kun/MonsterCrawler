          ADP is hiring a Product Manager focused on Analytics
         . ADP is at the forefront of using data to improve how customer's use our products, to continuously improve the user experience.          The Product Manager of Analytics will operate as an analyst supporting the needs of many product managers -- both at helping identify critical KPIs and usage measures to track and report against. They will lead data instrumentation initiatives and developing analytics strategies to help optimize customer experiences. They will take part in the entire product life-cycle from ideation to launch to help optimize the customer experience.
         Strong critical thinking skills, the ability to navigate through various data environments, and a drive to understand the product experience and business objectives is essential to succeed in this role. The successful candidate will be a thought partner to the product management team and will help lead them to formulate the right business questions need to drive proper KPIs. This individual will dig into the data (Backend DBs and Google Analytics) to investigate and unearth critical insights, and will need to transform that information into powerful visualizations (using Tableau, Excel, etc.). The ability to effectively articulate and present the story behind the data will be very important.
         The role will involve close interaction with product management, user experience design, data warehouse developers, data architects, and software development teams. Strong communication and interpersonal skills are critical.
