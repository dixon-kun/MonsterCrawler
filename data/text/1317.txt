        Here at Rush University Medical Center in Chicago, Illinois, we are committed to advancing scientific medical knowledge through research. We are revitalizing our medical centers with an emphasis on productivity, transparency and accountability. Are you looking to be a part of the exciting, always evolving and expanding new developments in research here at Rush University Medical Center? Are you well experienced in working with a variety of group, single and industry oriented studies?
        If this describes you, you may be the right candidate for the Clinical Data Analyst position in Research Core-Cofocal Micro
        Position Summary :
        The Analyst will support the clinical, business and research data analysis needs of the Rush Center for Community Health Equity (CCHE). The Analyst will assist faculty and other center team members to identify and gather necessary data from electronic medical records from SQL databases. The analyst will have a direct reporting relationship to the Director of Research and Evaluation for CCHE and will work closely with the Director of Knowledge Management, who is a subject matter expert in data access and research data requests. The individual who holds this position exemplifies the Rush mission, vision and values and acts in accordance with Rush policies and procedure.
        Position Qualifications:
        * Bachelor's degree in information science or computer science, or equivalent technical experience (at least 4 years)
        * At least three years of experience in data extraction using SQL, with data science experience preferred.
        * Experience extracting data from electronic medical records preferred.
        Familiarity with research, healthcare industry or healthcare information systems preferred.
        * Demonstrated expertise writing SQL stored procedures or queries.
        * Able to prioritize and multi-task to manage expectations of multiple stakeholders.
        * Be able to deliver high quality, timely work product with significant autonomy.
        * SSIS package development using Visual Studio
        * PL/SQL Developer
        * ArcGIS
