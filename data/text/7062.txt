This position will work closely on a daily basis with internal Marketing department stakeholders to meet their information requirements. If you enjoy working with numbers, can manage and understand significant amounts of data in order to support the generation of meaningful insights that will drive company strategy and decision making while having the opportunity to present your findings to management and other team members, you could be a great candidate for this role. Responsibilities Include: Perform in-depth product point-of-sale data analysis (data extractions from external database, monthly and weekly report compilations, etc.) Perform analysis of the data provided by syndicated market research companies (product shipments, purchase/usage behavior, etc.) to identify trends and insights that can affect company planning and decision making. Work closely with internal customers to extrapolate, analyze and disseminate market data that can be used for trend analysis and improved marketing strategies. Seek and provide information to help internal teams determine their position in the marketplace. Perform in-depth analysis of primary market research data. Assist with development of overall departmental data mining, analysis, insight capabilities and skills. Periodically review and attempt to improve and standardize reporting metrics so that management and other internal stakeholders can quickly determine the issues and/or successes of our products. Maintain day-to-day relationship with designated secondary market research agencies for user access and analyst inquiries. Assist Manager with annual subscription/program evaluations and renewals for designated secondary market research agencies. Assist Manager with the development, planning and execution of various primary research projects (i.e. surveys, focus groups).
