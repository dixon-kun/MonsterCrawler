Join CSRA’s Growth organization and help develop the technical solutions and proposals needed to win a diverse portfolio of new business and recompete opportunities. As a Solution Architect, you will be responsible for understanding our customers’ needs and priorities, helping to craft technical solutions responsive to those needs, helping to shape customer acquisitions and position CSRA to win, and supporting the delivery of our written and oral technical proposals.

Intelligent Business Process Services help our clients deliver effective, efficient, and agile business process services and enhanced customer experiences enabled by cutting edge IT and software. Intelligent Business Process services include transaction processing, shared services, service desk/contact centers, workflow and process-based systems and organizational change management.

CSRA is the largest pure-play IT services provider serving the U.S. government sector with combined FY15 revenues of approximately $5.5 billion and nearly 19,000 employees. We are a leader in providing digital services, digital platform solutions, cyber security, data services and solutions, enterprise business solutions, and additional professional services to our customers.

We offer a fast-paced, highly dynamic and collaborative environment in which self-motivated, strategic thought leaders earn executive recognition and rapid advancement. Help CSRA to stand out in this highly competitive and opportunity-rich market!

Key Responsibilities include:

Pursuit/Capture leadership:

  • Lead and support technical solution strategy throughout the Capture and Proposal lifecycle, from early Qualification through Proposal submission
  • Contribute to CSRA win strategies and lead the development of technical win themes and solution readiness plans
  • Engage current and prospective customers to understand critical needs, socialize and vet solutions, and advise on strategies to shape acquisitions that achieve desired results
  • Contribute technical solutions and approaches to meet anticipated or actual acquisition requirements
  • Support the design and the development of key artifacts including, but not limited to architectural models, process diagrams, concepts of operation, etc.
  • Help to develop unsolicited technical recommendations, collateral and Requests for Information (RFI)/Sources Sought Notification (SSN) responses to engage prospective customers and position CSRA to win customers and position CSRA to win
  • Collaborate with CSRA technical service areas and lines of business to identify and bring the best technical and business strategies and solutions to differentiate CSRA
  • Contribute to teaming strategies including identifying and vetting potential partners
  • Develop and support cost strategies, Basis of Estimates (BOEs)/Bill of Materials (BOMs), and associated rationale

Proposal leadership:

  • Ensure readiness of solution and proposal support team to prepare a compelling and compliant response to the government’s solicitation
  • Ensure integration of technical solution with all sections and volumes of the proposal
  • Review proposals and provide critical feedback needed to strengthen our solution and proposals
  • Author Technical volume introductions, key sections and orals slides including developing compelling graphics

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree; an additional 4 years of experience may be considered in lieu of degree
  • 8+ years technical experience
  • 2 or more years of experience successfully developing technical solutions and leading/authoring a minimum of 5 federal pursuits/proposals
  • Experience working in one or more IT domains that may include software development, IT infrastructure services, cloud, cyber security, data management services
  • Experience with technical leadership and project management
  • Willingness to travel (up to 30%)
  • US citizenship; must be able to obtain and maintain a clearance as required

Other Qualifications:

  • Optional – Secret, TS or higher Clearance
  • Preferred – Experience in data analytics or data sciences.
  • Optional – Red Team leadership experience
  • Optional – Orals leadership and coaching experience
  • Optional – Basis of Estimate and competitive estimation strategy
  • Ability to successfully lead or support solution development and Technical proposal authoring for small ($2M-50M) to moderate ($50M-100M) size deals
  • Ability to thrive in a highly collaborative, fast-paced, growth-focused environment
  • Ability to perform exceptionally over long stretches of time (during capture) and in challenging situations (during proposal development)
