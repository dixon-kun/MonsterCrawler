Monitoring Tools Specialist at Buchanan & Edwards

Washington, DC 20535

Report this job

About the Job

Are you looking for an opportunity to join a growing company that is innovative, cutting edge, and mission centered?  Our prestigious technical and management consultants work closely with our customers in a dynamic, solution-oriented environment, and we are inviting you to apply for our recent opening as a Monitoring Tools Specialist .

The ideal candidate will:

  • Have experience with multiple security focused tools including, but not limited to central logging & monitoring, capacity planning, and technical operations.
  • Analyzes existing systems and defines, designs and develops new monitoring system tools.
  • Assesses architecture and current hardware limitations and working parameters for hardware/software compatibility.
  • Determines system specifications, input/output processes and working parameters for hardware/software compatibility.
  • Coordinates design of subsystems and integration of total system. Identifies, analyzes and resolves program support deficiencies.


  • Responsibilities

    • Act as an escalation point for operations support for monitoring tools.
    • Perform installation, configuration management, capacity planning, license management, data integration, data transformation, field extraction, event parsing, data preview, and application management.
    • Troubleshoot indexers, search heads, heavy forwarder and forwarder issues.
    • Design core scripts to automate maintenance monitoring and alerting tasks.
    • Standardize and implement monitoring tools configuration and maintenance for Windows platforms
    • Continuously engage with IT & security management, project managers, technical staff, and enterprise peers to achieve organizational data science goals.
    • Store, manage, and ensure availability of historical data to satisfy retention policies by source.
    • Provide reports on an ongoing basis on system utilization and forecast trends.

    • Qualifications

      SECURITY CLEARANCE : Top Secret (CI Poly will need to be taken after hired)

      • Bachelor’s Degree or documented equivalent work experience of at least 4+ years.
      • Network/System monitoring toolset(s) with 2+ years experience.
      • Work experience with dashboard design and utilization.
      • Experience actively using Python and Perl scripts
      • Experience in any of the following products: Nagios, Icinga, Anturis, Monitis, OP5, Cacti, Splunk , Zenoss, OSSIM, Spiceworks, or Zabbix. *** Splunk highly desired***
      • Strong communication, written and verbal skills with the ability and comfort level to do presentations to senior government leaders.
      • Basic understanding of TCP/IP and network operation fundamentals.
