Analytic Solutions New Business Leader

Location: Morris Plains, NJ,

Do you passionately pursue the stories hidden in a large repository of data, understand the power of analytical models and the power of insight? Do you relish working with customers to identify their unsatisfied or unmet business needs and working through all available data and analytical models to search for answers? Do you enjoy telling a meaningful story with data driven insights? If your answer is yes, Honeywell has an intriguing opportunity for you an analytics solutions product manager.

You will be responsible for working within Honeywell to identify opportunities for new growth and efficiency based on data analysis. The focus will be on the Performance Materials and Technologies business unit. This product role will work closely with experts in the business unit, data scientists and solution architects to solve client unmet needs through data modeling and delivering insights by integrating results into operational platforms or creating new insight solutions.

You will be a member of a team that delivers contemporary analytics solutions for all Honeywell business groups and functions. You will be expected to actively participate in defining and governing our analytics strategy for Honeywell, as well as begin a strong leader in executing data science driven projects as we build out capabilities and our architecture. You will report to the Data Science Leader in Honeywell's Business Intelligence and Analytics organization.

Honeywell International is a $40 billion diversified Fortune 100 leader with more than 131,000 employees in 100 countries around the world. We invent and manufacture technologies that address some of the world's toughest challenges linked to global macro trends like energy efficiency, clean energy generation, safety and security, globalization and customer productivity. We are building a world that's safer and more secure, more comfortable and energy efficient, more innovative and productive. For more information, please visit Honeywell.com or follow us @HoneywellNow.

Specific Responsibilities

* Critically analyze and understand complex trends and technology relevant to Honeywell now and in the future, using the results to shape the future of Hoenywell's research and development strategy in the complex intersection of business and technology that is the IoTS.

* Lead and contribute to projects and studies together with international research teams and business units to elicit application and domain requirements, propose technical directions, and push the envelope on next generation products.

* Constantly keep abreast of the latest innovations by surveying emerging science and benchmarking new technologies for the IoTS. Drive Honeywell's engagement with research groups at top-tier universities (e.g. Carnegie Mellon University), government (e.g. National Renewable Energy Laboratory) as well as industry players both large (e.g. industrial consortia) and small (e.g. startups and incubators).

* Identify data and analytical monetization opportunities by working with the Honeywell business teams.

* Develop relationships with IoT thought leaders and 3rd party IoT companies to identify partner opportunities that can leverage Honeywell's IoT data and analytics to create new and relevant experiences making a difference in the word and the lives of others.

* Develop relationships with the Performance Materials and Technologies business team members by being proactive, displaying a thorough understanding of the business processes and by recommending innovative solutions

* Work with data scientists to provide data modeling, mining, pattern analysis, data visualization and machine learning solutions to address customer needs

* Work with solution architects to create detail requirements and the development and assurance teams to deliver on the client delivery solution.

* Communicate project output in terms of customer value, business objectives, and product opportunity

* Attend industry conferences to stay current on industry trends, challenges, and potential market opportunities

* Promote data science methods and processes across functions

* Work with our businesses and infrastructure teams to develop a strategy that will clearly outline how our enterprise platforms can enable business growth & productivity by seamlessly combining structured and unstructured data into a single, self-service analytical environment

YOU MUST HAVE:

* At least 5-10 years working in an analytics environment focused on data driven solutions with 2-3 years of experience with business partner development / mergers and acquisitions OR, 5-10 years of experience with business partner development / mergers and acquisitions and 2-3 years working in an analytics environment focused on data driven solutions

WE VALUE:

* IoT related experience within the industrial space.

* Experience working in a large, corporate enterprise environment.

* Able to communicate complex ideas and assessments to both technical and business audiences. Experience in strategy development and communication to executive-level stakeholders is a plus.

* Strong track record of research and advanced concept implementation. Candidates should have a strong familiarity with several aspects of the software development lifecycle, including architecture, design, implementation, and testing. Knowledge of modern development, integration, testing and deployment environments is a plus.

* Strong business acumen, with ability to draw clear connections between data modeling activities and business challenges/opportunities and customer experiences

* 5 years or experience in ideation to market launch of SaaS or data and analytics products

* Deep knowledge of industrial markets preferred

* 2 years experience bringing prototypes to production on Hadoop and/or NoSQL platforms

* 2 years experience with visualization software (Tableau, Spotfire, Qlikview, d3.js)

* Experience working with remote and global teams

* Strong customer focus and process discipline

* Results driven with a positive can do attitude

* Excellent collaboration and negotiation skills

* Highly team oriented

* Demonstrated success in cross business leadership roles

* Proven program management skill set

* Ability to work collaboratively with development/implementation staff through the design and build of new business processes and systems

* Background that includes both business and change management experience

* In depth understanding of best-in-class model and data configuration and development processes

* Understanding of data science trends and future state technologies

* Ability to travel – up to 10%. Higher percentage (up to 25%) preferred but not required.
