Job Description:


Organizational Overview


At DST Financial Services we believe that in order to better service our clients it’s through our use of data. In our Australia, United Kingdom, and United States offices we provide services to mutual funds, brokerage firms, retirement plans, wealth management, and other financial services. Everyday our global offices work together to master the complexity of the financial industry to transform the most challenging tasks into opportunity and competitive advantage.

Role Overview


We are currently hiring a Data Scientist to join our Applied Analytics division, product development team.  A key focus for the Applied Analytics division has been the creation of a data repository, freeing over 6 petabytes of data from DST’s traditional mainframe environment and housing it within a big data stack. This approach enables the development of industry focused products, industry research and client specific solutions within a scalable environment decreasing time to market. Supporting the new platform is a robust data governance and data management methodology, incorporating best practices in anonymization and entity level management.

Our product development team focuses on combining benchmarking, segmentation, predictive models and recommender systems to create scalable, repeatable products that allow clients to leverage our analytic capabilities.

Responsibilities


Data Scientists perform complex statistical analysis on customer data to identify, analyze, interpret, and validate trends and patterns to locate influences and business opportunities to provide robust solutions to business problems. They work on prototypes and implementing products that include designed experiments, data mining and signal detection, advanced analytic modeling, segmentation and profiling, and recommender systems. They deliver statistical models such as segmentation, predictive, decision trees, and other mathematical models.

Qualifications


Minimal:

  • Graduate Degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Physics, Computer Science or equivalent
  • Minimum of 5 years experience with real data; analytics application development, systems optimization, and behavior/predictive analysis experience
  • strong background in statistical concepts and calculations
  • proficiency with statistical analysis tools (R, SAS, SPSS)
  • Strong analytic experience and solving complex problems
  • Solid understanding of SQL, Python, other scripting languages, and statistical software packages
  • Experience in prototyping and building products at scale


Optimal:

  • Experience in data mining, signal detection, NLP, Machine Learning, predictive modeling, Latent class and multivariate analysis, Recommender systems experience a plus.
  • Possesses professional knowledge in compliance as it relates to a regulated industry (IE financial services preferred, oil/gas/utilities, insurance, healthcare, government)
  • Graduate Degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Physics, Computer Science or equivalent
