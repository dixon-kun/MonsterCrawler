        * Event Management. Manages changes in state of IT Infrastructure leveraging alert systems and tools. Triage based on Run Book. Escalates to Tier II operations support. Accountable for all hardware break fix coordinating support with vendors, data centers, and implementation engineering. Maintains operations logs/reports, shift schedule logs/reports, and scheduled reports.
        * Availability Management. Monitors technology performance 24/7/365 with accountability to business users, clients, and service managers for service availability. Understands business requirements and cycles escalating utilization, throughput, and maintenance window concerns. Measures and reports on availability. Analyzes information and identifies trends. Provides feedback to implementation engineering and service management on alert thresholds.
        * Continuity Management. Supports disaster recovery testing efforts. Supplies feedback to design engineering in post test reviews. Executes standard recovery procedures in the case of true disaster declaration.
        * Capacity Management. Monitors technology performance during business use cycles with accountability for meeting capacity demands. Triages performance and capacity issues based on Run Book prescribed instruction. Escalates to Technical &amp; Applications Management and/or Implementation Engineering as required. Provides feedback on capacity concerns and observed trends. Performs health checks.
