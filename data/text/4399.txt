         Description:
         As Senior Consumer Marketing Analyst, you provide vital KPIs and actionable insights to support Keurig&#8217;s e-Commerce consumers, specifically our Consumer Data Platform (CDP). This is a role where you will make a major impact on the short and long term success of the online company as part of our enterprise digital strategy and to support broader omni-channel marketing. CDP will support outbound digital marketing, commerce personalization, integration to digital media buying (DMP), and partner campaign integration. The CDP will be our customer master data management platform. The analytics and reporting functions of this role will be key to enabling the business to make better data-driven decisions to grow our digital share and increase revenue.
          What you will do:
          Provide Digital Marketing Analytics from the CDP, focused on assessment of performance to our current buyers and targeting and conversion of both known and unknown prospects. This can include analysis of personalized consumer journeys across omni-channels.
          Conduct cluster and segmentation analysis to build and define Keurig consumer personas.
          Develop machine learning and predictive modeling capabilities.
          Own and manage a 3

          Mentor a consumer analytics team member and provide guidance with growth opportunities.
          Collaborate as a member the Digital Marketing Analytics Team to produce and conduct the Digital Monthly Business Review with actionable insights.
          Assist the Business to develop look-a-like modeling for brewer prospects and converting drip-users to Keurig single serve.
          Analyze consumer trends from the CDP, as well as retention performance, utilizing Digital Prospect and Buyer data in SQL Server databases and Hadoop environment.
          Demonstrate agility by using multiple data sources, finding data patterns, and extracting actionable insights both at the aggregate and segmented consumer level.
          Establish strong working relationships within the digital team as well as with other internal teams such as IT, Brands, Partners, Consumer Care, and Loyalty, providing analytical support.
