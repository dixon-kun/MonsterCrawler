        Advanced Analytics Leader &#8211; Machine Learning
        Location: Morris Plains, NJ
        Do you enjoy evangelizing the power and impact of analytics for a business? Do you relish the idea of promoting consistent analytic implementation and technology, along with improving an organizations capabilities in data and analytics governance? Do you enjoy leading a team of enthusiastic and skilled data scientists? If your answer is yes, Honeywell has an intriguing opportunity for you as Machine Learning &amp; Advanced Analytics Leader.
        You will be responsible for working within Honeywell to set the strategic direction for the development of Machine Learning and Advanced Analytics. You will lead and motivate a team of data scientists to develop a consistent development methodology and playbook for Machine Learning and Advanced Analytics. The Machine Learning &amp; Advanced Analytics Leader will work closely with the business to design, develop and deliver analytic solutions that meet our internal and external business needs. Incrementally they will collaborate with application architects to integrate results into operational platforms, including Hadoop and NoSQL architectures.
        You will be the development leader on the team that delivers contemporary analytics solutions for all Honeywell business groups and functions. You will be expected to actively participate in defining and governing our analytics strategy for Honeywell, as well as leading a team in executing data science driven projects as we build out capabilities and our architecture. You be a leader in Honeywell's Business Intelligence and Analytics organization.
        Honeywell International is a $40 billion diversified Fortune 100 leader with more than 131,000 employees in 100 countries around the world. We invent and manufacture technologies that address some of the world's toughest challenges linked to global macro trends like energy efficiency, clean energy generation, safety and security, globalization and customer productivity. We are building a world that's safer and more secure, more comfortable and energy efficient, more innovative and productive. For more information, please visit Honeywell.com or follow us @HoneywellNow.
        Specific Responsibilities
        * The Leader is responsible for guiding and directing Managers of talented modeling teams designing, developing, and deploying state-of-the-art, data-driven predictive models to solve business problems using the latest technologies in neural networks, machine learning, statistical modeling, pattern recognition, and artificial intelligence.
        * Ensure high quality on time delivery of the Analytic Roadmap and projects
        * Managing highly talented analytic resources
        * Developing Managers and Scientists on the team in their career development
        * A key responsibility is the development of a repeatable analytic development methodology which is effective and easily understood by the business.
        * Developing a repeatable analytic methodology that can be leveraged to build a "Analytic Factory" or delivery capability
        * Bringing expertise with different types of algorithms and modeling processes
        * Working directly with clients and supporting models in production
        * Play a central role in the Action, Insight &amp; Analytics Leadership team providing insight and new ideas for both growth and efficiency opportunities leveraging data and analytics.

        * Minimum 3 years of experience leading a data science team
