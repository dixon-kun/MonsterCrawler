           As the Data officer you will lead the effort to identify gaps in our data landscape and lead the effort to close gaps required to provide the most comprehensive view of a patient. You will work with teams throughout HIS to identify existing data resources, establish and manage the data inventory and work to drive product innovation necessary to increase the quality of this asset and its application to solving some of the most important problems in healthcare.
            Primary Responsibilities
           include but are not limited to the following:
            Lead 3M HIS&#8217; strategic use of data to create new products delivering value to our healthcare customers.
            Continuously improve how 3M HIS collects, uses, manages, and publishes data.
            Identify data gaps and work with teams on prioritizing data acquisition activities.
            Lead efforts to track data collections, databases, physical data models, and linkages between datasets.
            Define and lead programs to decrease the cost of managing data and increasing the value of the data we have available
            Improve data quality and establish metrics for how we measure data quality
            Ensure data is available, reliable, consistent, accessible, secure, and timely to maximize its value to internal and external users.
            Manage the tradeoffs between simplifying the collection and integration of data for data science to encourage data sharing, and aligning and standardizing data models / schemas
            Working closely with security and compliance teams, minimize the risks of working with healthcare data while facilitating innovative data science and new application development
