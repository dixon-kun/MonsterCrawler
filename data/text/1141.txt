        Job Description:
        3M is seeking a Senior Data Scientist for the Safety &amp; Graphics Business Laboratory (SGBL).
        Job Summary:
        3M SGBL is seeking an experienced Data Scientist with expertise in the areas of Data Science, Data Analytics, Machine Learning and Computational Statistics. Person hired will be responsible for providing technical leadership and fostering collaboration with research teams in corporate and business units to build new capabilities, technologies and products. Central to the position, is the ability to make uncommon connections and synergies between 3M core materials and digitally connected platforms to bring differentiated value to our customers and drive significant business impact to 3M.
        Primary Responsibilities include but are not limited to the following:
        * Invent and develop data science technologies that create new opportunities, in addition to supporting existing 3M products and businesses.
        * Lead and collaborate with research teams in corporate and business units to build capabilities and applications that use scientific, mathematical, and computational skills.
        * Collect and prepare real world data for computer vision, machine learning, data science, data analytics, probabilistic inference, and/or computational statistics applications.
        * Use a variety of programming languages to quickly develop proof of concept algorithms and optimize algorithms for commercialization.
        * Publish and present knowledge within 3M and in top-tier journals and conferences.
        * Perform other duties as assigned.
        Basic Qualifications:
        * Master's degree or higher from an accredited university
        * Combined experience in data mining, data science, data analytics, machine learning, and/or computer vision
        Preferred Qualifications:
        * PhD in Cognitive Science, Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Applied Mathematics, Mathematics, or Statistics from an accredited university.
        * Three (3) years combined experience in the fields of data mining, data science, data analytics, machine learning, and/or computer vision.
        * Expertise in machine learning sub-disciplines such as: deep learning, data mining, data wrangling, analysis and reduction of high-dimensional data, manifold embedding, PCA, time series analysis techniques, markov techniques, genetic algorithms, kernel methods, natural language processing, signal processing, sensors and electronics, probabilistic inference, and/or computational statistics.
        * Programming languages: Python, MATLAB, C, C++, OpenCV, R
        * Strong familiarity and experience with Linux operating system
        * Strong scientific and technical acumen, with demonstrated interest and ability to make connections between science/technology and real-world constraints
        * Ability to deal with the ambiguity of early-stage scouting and evaluation of new opportunities &#8211; flexibility to change direction as additional information becomes available &#8211; help architect new business opportunity areas
        * Demonstrated scientific excellence as evidenced by publications and presentations
        * Leadership and networking skills
        * Self-motivated, self-directed, entrepreneurial, and business savvy
        * Strong mechanical aptitude and problem solving skills
        * Excellent written and oral communication skills
