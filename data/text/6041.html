DRINKS, a stealth-mode VC-backed startup, is a top-five US Direct to Consumer (DTC) marketing technology company that manages wine brands. The Company is led by an entrepreneurial team of marketing technology, data science, DTC, and M&A experts with multiple successful exits. DRINKS operates in a compelling market: a $33B industry virtually untouched by the Internet, though that is now changing quickly.

To help us build the next generation of online and mobile wine stores that will be used by tens of millions of people, we’re on the hunt for a superb Technical Writer, Technology with a strong background in working with technology teams who is excited to launch new products.

The ideal Technical Writer has 5+ years of experience in translating complex systems and APIs into human-readable (even, enjoyable!) verbiage.  They will be responsible for documenting and clarifying the ‘how’ of our internal technologies, systems and processes to audiences both internal and external to the company.  But, they will shine by also explaining why these are important.

Responsibilities:

·       Develop a documentation framework and standards for all technical documentation for DRINKS.

·       Use a combination of user interviews and demos to gain a complete understanding of DRINKS' platform and associated workflows.

·       Develop documentation for the following aspects of the platform:

o   System architecture.

o   Data processing flows.

o   Interactions with external services.

o   Partner API.

o   Administration interface.

Key skills and experience:

·       5+ years’ experience of technical writing specifically in high-tech, online platforms, games, or similarly complex technologies.

·       Demonstrated experience in successfully working with small engineering teams in a rapidly-evolving, startup environment.

·      Substantial direct experience documenting APIs.

·      A proven track record of successfully navigating ambiguous requirements, and interfacing with engineering teams to extract information for documentation.

·      Expertise in common documentation tools, preferably using a single-source solution, and diagramming tools. For example, Markdown, Pandoc, Confluence, JIRA, MadCap Flare, Visio, Omnigraffle.

·       Expert abilities in building and mixing text, imagery, diagrams, flowcharts, etc to succinctly communicate information.

·       Familiarity in coding in Ruby, Java, etc.  Past professional coding experience a large bonus.

·       Background in commerce of physical products (like wine) a bonus.

We cater lunch weekly, host frequent wine giveaways and tastings, and provide your choice of leading-edge technology tools.
