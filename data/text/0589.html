This is a great opportunity for a proven leader to join the award winning digital team at The Weather Company, now a part of IBM, to run our data-focused advertising products. These products run across mobile, tablet and web traffic and touch over 200MM people every month, both on-property and off. Our data products include:WEATHERfx, a sophisticated weather targeting platform that brings together client sales data and granular weather data in order to understand how weather impacts consumer spending (more here and here and here).JOURNEYfx, a location targeting platform that leverages our app users’ location data in order to deliver more relevant ads and experiences (more here and here and here).AUDIENCEfx, our user-based targeting product, combining first- and third-party data on digital consumers to deliver the best performance for our advertising clients by ensuring we are finding the right customers at the right time (more here and here). This role is responsible for managing the roadmaps of the entire data targeting product catalog, as well as the product management team that oversees them. Working closely with our engineering, data science, measurement, creative lab, and QA teams (as well as third-party vendors), this role is responsible for the success of these products, from both client performance and a consumer experience perspectives. You'll know your way around the industry and be ready to meet with vendors, our agile development teams and be able to manage the process from end to end. It sounds hard, and it will be, but you'll have a great team alongside you and we're ready to take on the world.RESPONSIBILITIES: + Manage a solid team of product managers, providing mentorship and defining clear individual goals that align with Weather’s revenue and consumer objectives. + Be a thought leader who can blend existing industry knowledge and awareness with ad product performance, ad product needs, and sales feedback to drive innovation and technical excellence. + Act as day-to-day contact for vendor relationships to ensure all data driven product needs are anticipated and supported. + Continually monitor performance of ad products and adjust our approach to development based on that feedback. + Track and report on product delivery and performance. + Offer sales support by brainstorming new product ideas, assisting with strategic packaging and product positioning, and answering any targeting ad product related questions. + Communicate new ad product capabilities, product launches or updates, and provide training (as needed) to sales and sales marketing teams. + Prioritize product pipeline effectively based on company strategies, resources, and time. + Effectively and efficiently deliver requirements to our engineering and data science teams (the data science team alone supporting these efforts is more than 15 people) - Understanding of web-based ad serving systems, creative technologies, and database marketing - Demonstrated success in team leadership, stakeholder management, process adherence, ownership/ commitment, learning agility and risk management capabilities - Recent experience in managing large projects with high complexity; multiple projects, programs in a media environment - Strong stakeholder management skills with proven ability to work with C-Level project sponsors - Previous experience managing / controlling project budgets greater than $5M- The Weather Company
