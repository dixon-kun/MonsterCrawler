We currently have an opening for an IP/Commercial Attorney in our NYC office. We are looking for a business-minded and self-motivated attorney to support our business operation with at least 3 years of experience in the commercial, intellectual property and/or Internet (preferably media) sectors; advertising, software licensing, internet marketing and regulatory law experience a plus.

This job reports to the attorneys on BuzzFeed's business team and is based in our NYC offices.

DESCRIPTION AND DUTIES:

* Support all of BuzzFeed's business units in drafting and negotiating a variety of commercial agreements, including advertising, research and marketing, events, vendor, license, business development, SaaS and other technology, and other commercial and operational contracts.

* Support BuzzFeed's creative department with intellectual property advice regarding copyright, trademark, right of publicity and commercial speech issues.

* Support BuzzFeed's enforcement of its intellectual property through cease and desist letters, DMCA takedowns, UDRP proceedings and litigation, and defense of BuzzFeed from similar allegations.

* Provide advice on all legal matters affecting the business.

QUALIFICATIONS AND SKILLS:

* Must be an admitted attorney with 3-5 years of relevant experience at a law firm or in-house legal department.

* Contract law, intellectual property and Internet and/or technology law experience required whether in a commercial contracts or transactional context. Experience with advertising and/or media industry, and regulatory experience in the advertising industry, is a plus.

* An ideal candidate will also have a practical, business-oriented approach to problem-solving and thrive in a fast-paced environment, tempered by an appreciation of legal complexity and risk.

* Strong desire to learn the business side and function smoothly as part of an interdisciplinary team.

* Must be able to prioritize and manage workload and meet deadlines within a fast-paced, rapidly evolving environment.

* Must fit the strong collegial culture in the department and the company. Bad taste in music preferred.

About BuzzFeed

BuzzFeed is the leading independent digital media and tech company delivering news and entertainment content to a global audience. We have offices in 18 cities around the world and more than 1300 employees including reporters, video producers, data scientists, engineers, brand strategists, and more. We infuse hard work and fun though a culture of experimentation, teamwork, equality, and humble confidence. As an employee, you'll enjoy perks like office events, snacks, career development courses, and no work on your birthday! But most importantly, you'll work with inspiring colleagues to build tools and create content that helps connect people all over the world.
