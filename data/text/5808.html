About the Company :

DRINKS is a top-five Direct to Consumer (DTC) marketing technology company focused on beverage alcohol. The Company is led by an entrepreneurial team of marketing technology, data science, DTC, and M&A experts with several successful exits.

DRINKS’ goal is to disrupt the wine market by changing the way wine is sold. The Company leverages big data wherever possible to provide the best possible customer experience: data scientists over wine makers; analytics over sommeliers; investments in machine learning, data management platforms, and recommendation engines over sales trips to far-off vineyards.

We are seeking a Creative Director who will be responsible for leading the creative vision to elevate our portfolio of brands. This individual will lead and inspire a team of designers and creative professionals, and will also take on the responsibilities of an Art Director. The Creative Director will have experience managing multiple projects across a variety of creative platforms within a pre-set budget, and a demonstrated ability to align creative execution with overall organizational goals.

Core Job Objectives

·  Lead the creative development for multiple projects and/or campaigns across multiple consumer touch-points

·  Evolve and maintain brand voice and tone, ensuring consistency throughout the consumer journey

·  Partner with marketing department to build and drive the messaging and channels for key company initiatives

Job Responsibilities

·  Oversee planning and development of all creative work (content creation, web and mobile design, product photography/videography, etc.)

·  Advocate and support brand design direction and guidelines at every level of the organization

·  Present and communicate objective creative solutions with clarity, effectively selling ideas to stakeholders

·  Identify and develop creative executions to successfully reach target customers and communicate key messages using direct response creative, branded ads, or a combination of both

·  Lead, inspire, and supervise a team of designers throughout the creative process while managing workflow efficiently

·  Manage a pipeline of new wine brands and design wine labels, in conjunction with our partner wineries

·  Lead recruiting efforts for additional team members or sourcing third party resources to supplement the internal team

Requirements:

·  8-10 years of industry experience

·  3 years of personnel management experience

·  BS/MS degree in design, communications, marketing or a related field

·  Portfolio of work

·  Proven experience with the design process (project scope and time planning, design steps, production/execution of design solution) as well as digital, social media, print and other marketing campaigns

·  Excellent organization skills and attention to detail

·  Ability to build relationships, understand stakeholder vision/direction and present designs and concepts

·  A passion for wine is a plus, but not required!

Complete benefit packaging including a flexible vacation plan, generous healthcare plan, 401(k) with competitive company matching, dental insurance, vision insurance, life insurance, short and long term disability insurance, health club membership, and covered garage parking. Plus, we cater lunch weekly, host frequent wine giveaways and tastings, and provide leading-edge technology tools.

To be considered, please send your resume and portfolio to: mike.reisman@drinks.com
