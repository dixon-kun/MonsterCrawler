A NBC NewCo is working on a cloud-based platform to automate industrial asset integrity and risk assessment, by fusing asset inspection data with business operations data. The platform analytics use Predictive Modeling and Machine Learning techniques. We are looking for Data Analysts interns who can work with Subject Matter Experts to create customer user-experience stories that can be implemented in interactive dashboards.
• Work with clients and Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) to analyze and develop business requirements to drive data analytics
• Work with Subject Matter Experts to create user experience stories and Dashboards across various datasets
• Work with any preferred data analysis and visualization tools such as BIRT, Pentaho, JasperSoft, Qlik, Microstrategy, or Tableau to analyze the data and generate reports and summaries
