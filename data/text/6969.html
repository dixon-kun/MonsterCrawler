Responsibilities

You will manage and lead an IT Digital Analytics team and projects to drive insights and actions to improve operational efficiencies, customer experience, revenue growth and profitability as part of the Digital Transformation. You will provide vision and leadership to a strong team of about 6 engineers, both full-time employees and consultants as well as senior level technical individuals.


You will have the opportunity to really get an understanding of the customer journey and work with a huge amount of data. You will tie digital to cross channel experience using your analytic development and insights experience. You will drive actionable insights to personalization that impacts business metrics.

• Manage schedules, be accountable and deliver projects on time in a fast paced agile environment.
• Contribute technically and own architectural oversight of system/services.
• Build a culture focuses on speed and agility, avoiding silos and focuses outward on the customer.
• Align technology to business strategy, and proactively drive a technical direction throughout the organization.
• Drive results within budget, time and quality.

Qualifications

Must have:
• Bachelor degree in Business Intelligence and Analytics, Computer Science, Mathematics or equivalent experience with extensive knowledge in the application of statistics and computer sciences
• Prior experience with Digital, ISO, PA, Marketing, Sales and other business organizations on their analytical needs
• Experience in the software development cycle.
• Experience leading or managing software projects/development teams

Ideally, you will also have:
• Master’s Degree
• Extensive experience in Digital and Cross Channel Analytics using Discovery and Data Science.
• Experience with Adobe Marketing Cloud or Marketing Cloud
• Experience with Quantitative and Qualitative data analysis.
• Familiarity with predictive and prescriptive analytics.
• Experience of Data Warehousing and Teradata SQL.
• Experience creating dashboards with Tableau
• Experience with tools such as Aster, R, Kibana, ClickFox or any other tools to visually look at the customer journey
• Working knowledge of machine learning techniques, distributive computing tools (Spark, Hive, etc) and Hadoop.
• Working knowledge of self-serve analytic tools for business users.
• Ability to package analysis in clear and concise reports and presentations.
• Experience with Adobe Workbench, Adobe Test/Target, Adobe Audience Manager and any tools for competitive intelligence reports.
• Experience with Big Data as well as R, Python and Aster Teradata.
