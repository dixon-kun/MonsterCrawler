Description
We are actively searching for qualified candidates for this potential opening. We do not have assurance at this time that the position will be opened and when. Because we have strong reason to believe the position will be approved soon, we are going ahead with identifying candidates.


The contractor shall participate in and provide recommendations for Network Synchronization Working Groups, Network Capability Reviews, and “Good Enough” Drills.


The contractor shall make recommendations on Spectrum Allocation/De-confliction for CS fielding, focused on reducing Soldier Radio Waveform spectrum consumption in current and future Army Networks based upon the overall Network Design.

The contractor shall provide analysis and engineering design of networks, tactical networks, applications, spectrum usage, network test, and network performances. The contractor shall provide network visual documentation of the designed networks. The contractor shall prepare information briefs presenting issues, addressing potential impacts, providing courses of action, and proposing recommended solutions to fill capability gaps and mitigate program risks.

Qualifications
A master's degree in the specialized field plus a minimum of 3 year of applicable experience; or, a bachelor's degree in the specialized field plus 7 years of applicable experience; or, a bachelor's degree with a minimum of 9 years of specialized experience

Standard Qualification Applies Plus

Shall have 8 years of experience in designing and developing network performance visualization products and Big data visualization products, to include writing code to produce these products from raw data and/or processed data gathered during network/tactical network lab and field events. Strong knowledge of Java, HTML5, User interfaces design principles, R and Matlab visualization libraries and various packages.

Must be able to obtain and maintain a US security clearance.
