Function/Purpose:&#13;
The Director IT Data Analytics is required to lead the advanced analytics team responsible for managing all non-Business Intelligence analytics functions at Hasbro including Social Reporting, eCommerce, Consumer Insights, Digital Marketing, and other advanced analytics and data science capabilities. This person will serve as the interface between the IT analytics team and those business partners who require analytics support to make data-driven decisions by identifying and documenting analytics capability and business requirements. This individual will also lead a team whose responsibilities include data science, architectural design of the underlying technology stack (data, compute/tools, &amp; presentation layers) and delivering a solution that will allow our business partners to use the capability in a test and learn manner in the hopes of making more successful, timely, data-driven decisions.&#13;
