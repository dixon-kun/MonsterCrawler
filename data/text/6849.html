About the Job

For highly-motivated,change champions looking for a dynamic team environment with the opportunity to learn and grow professionally, Brother offers a collaborative workplace that continues to innovate award winning products, while maintaining a focused commitment to the communities where we live and work.

The Senior Market Intelligence Analyst will be a member of our Business Machines Group Marketing Department and is responsible for all points of data extraction, manipulation, reporting and analysis. This position is responsible for improving the data mining, analysis, insight capability as well as developing proficiency with various marketing research tools (i.e. Excel, Tableau) of key stakeholders within the department. The position will be located in Bridgewater, NJ.

While involved with all types of market data to support the Business Machine Group, this position will primarily focus on Secondary Market Research data concerning product information and trends (POS data, syndicated market research reports and forecasts, voice of customer data, etc.) from vendors such as NPD and IDC that pertain to our current and potential marketplaces. This position will work closely on a daily basis with internal Marketing department stakeholders to meet their information requirements.

If you enjoy working with numbers, can manage and understand significant amounts of data in order to support the generation of meaningful insights that will drive company strategy and decision making while having the opportunity to present your findings to management and other team members, you could be a great candidate for this role.

Responsibilities Include:

  • Perform in-depth product point-of-sale data analysis (data extractions from external database, monthly and weekly report compilations, etc.)
  • Perform analysis of the data provided by syndicated market research companies (product shipments, purchase/usage behavior, etc.) to identify trends and insights that can affect company planning and decision making.
  • Prepare and present market situation and trend analysis reports/dashboards for all product categories that are delivered to key stakeholders on a periodic basis that draw upon various data sets and information sources that directly aid in the development of insights and inform strategy development and plans.
  • Work closely with internal customers to extrapolate, analyze and disseminate market data that can be used for trend analysis and improved marketing strategies.
  • Seek and provide information to help internal teams determine their position in the marketplace.
  • Perform in-depth analysis of primary market research data.
  • Assist with development of overall departmental data mining, analysis, insight capabilities and skills.
  • Periodically review and attempt to improve and standardize reporting metrics so that management and other internal stakeholders can quickly determine the issues and/or successes of our products.
  • Maintain day-to-day relationship with designated secondary market research agencies for user access and analyst inquiries.
  • Assist Manager with annual subscription/program evaluations and renewals for designated secondary market research agencies.
  • Assist Manager with the development, planning and execution of various primary research projects (i.e. surveys, focus groups).
  • Assist with performing analysis of various “voice of customer” data (customer sentiments, reviews, NPS).
  • Periodically will support special projects that impact numerous BMG-Marketing workgroups and ultimately benefit Brother International Corporation.

Required Skills and Experience:

  • Bachelor’s degree or higher, ideally in marketing, business administration or psychology with concentration in mathematics, data science and/or statistics.  MBA or equivalent advanced degree a plus.
  • A minimum of 5 years of total professional experience with 4 or more years of data analysis or market research experience.
  • Previous experience working with secondary market research agencies in analyzing secondary/syndicated market research data.
  • Advanced MS Excel skills.
  • Intermediate to advanced MS Word and PowerPoint
  • Experience with Tableau Desktop
  • Ability to manage project timelines with little direct supervision.
  • Strong communication skills – writing, data visualization and presentation abilities
  • Ability to clearly translate and articulate complex data sets into understandable and applicable insights and recommendations with all key stakeholders.
  • Strong team player with the ability to effectively work with a diverse range of stakeholders.
  • On occasion and when required, have the flexibility on short notice to work outside of and longer than normal business hours.
