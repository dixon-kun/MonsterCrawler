The Product Analyst is an instrumental member of the Products function within C&I Sales team, through developing, integrating and delivering products to customers that decrease their energy costs.

Key Duties &Responsibilities:

  • Assist in developing Product Offerings, including building Financial Models to showcase value proposition, Analytical Models for evaluating Customer Data and Reporting, as well as Settlement Models for Accounting purposes
  • Manage Demand Response and other programs, including executing on Administrative and Technical tasks
  • Work closely with other Functional Areas and Third-party vendors, including Risk and Pricing, Operations, Engineeringand Marketing
  • Communicate product functionality andtechnical features to internal teams (training) as well as customers and partners
  • Test and analyse product and technologyperformance under various market conditions and tariffs
  • Function as the operational lead for testevents, performance assessment hours, and any needed customer interventions
  • Gathering energy pricing as well as weather, production, and occupancy information. Use market and customer information to make energy management�event� recommendations, including participation in Demand Response and otherprograms
  • Assist Director, Products in identifying andevaluating new energy saving technologies and make recommendations to stakeholders on the prioritization
  • Remain current on competitor products, technologies
  • Identify new opportunities and products for AEP Energy

Knowledge &Skill Requirements

  • Familiarity with Energy Management practicessuch as Peak Load Management and Demand Response
  • Working knowledge of energy efficiency measures and technologies
  • Strong data driven mind-set with an ability tomake quick decisions
  • Process driven with meticulous attention todetail
  • Problem Solver with minimum supervision
