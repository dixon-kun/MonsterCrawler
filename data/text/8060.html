Solera Holdings, Inc. is a leading provider of risk and asset management software and services to the automotive and property marketplace, including the global P&C insurance industry.

ABOUT THE ROLE

Solera’s Global Data Sciences & Services Team is seeking a senior level MicroStrategy Project Architect with proven experience to lead the design, architecture and development of MicroStrategy BI solutions.

WHAT MAKES THIS ROLE UNCOMMON (aka AWESOME)

Unique selling points of position/team

  • Be part of a rapidly growing big data team with an opportunity to work on developing next gen analytics on a state of the art Hadoop big data platform
  • Opportunity to build analytics for our customers throughout the world.

ABOUT OUR IDEAL CANDIDATE

Solera is seeking an uncommon MicroStrategy expert who will utilize his/her skills and expertise to design and architect world-class MicroStrategy BI solutions.

Candidates must demonstrate excellent time management and the ability to excel in a fast paced, rapidly changing environment.


  • Architect and Design of Analytics as a Service for our customers – insurers, repairers and OEM’s; using the MicroStrategy Business Intelligence platform
  • Architect and Design of Solera-wide internal financial reporting solutions.
  • Incorporate product and business requirements to technical specifications for development of MicroStrategy applications by liaising with the product management and the data team.

Core Mission 20’s

  • Provide feedback to the data team on data structure design based on the requirements as well as query performance.
  • Guide the MicroStrategy BI developers in the development of the BI applications.
  • Provide support and guidance to MicroStrategy BI developers.

- Bachelors or equivalent degree in a business, technical, or related field
- Demonstrated and Proven MicroStrategy Architecture and design expertise:

  • 7+ years of experience in working on large and complex data warehouse systems (Teradata preferred) and design and architecting of MicroStrategy projects
  • Full stack knowledge of MicroStrategy BI software – MicroStrategy 10.x
  • Ability to work on multiple projects and enterprise class projects simultaneously as a Business Intelligence applications leader and subject matter expert
  • Strong Relational database and data warehousing concepts
  • Knowledge of logical and physical data modeling (relational, dimensional data modeling)
  • Strong advanced SQL and query optimization skills
  • Collaborating with the EDW team in recommending data warehouse designs that provide reusable architectural components and enable high-performance data access and distribution based on the business requirements
  • Experience with multi-tenant and distributed BI and reporting systems is a must
  • Development experience with MicroStrategy BI environments which leverages I-Cubes, Web, Mobile and Distribution Services
  • Finalizing business requirements and create high-level design documentation to demonstrate the mapping of business requirements to data requirement fulfillment
  • Leading the design, development and support of Enterprise BI applications, technologies, and architecture
  • Strong project management skills with ability to organize and drive new EDW projects working with multi-functional or cross-functional teams
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Strong team-oriented inter-personal and communication skills
  • Ability to establish and maintain a high level of customer trust and confidence in the BI/EDW team's knowledge and concern for customer's business needs
  • Hands on experience working on MicroStrategy Windows and Linux environments

PREFFERED QUALIFICATIONS / ADDED BONUS IF YOU HAVE THESE

  • MicroStrategy Certification as a project Architect (MCEP or MCE)
  • MicroStrategy SDK Development
  • Hadoop experience is a plus
  • Experience with Financial Reporting
  • Knowledge of Insurance Industry
