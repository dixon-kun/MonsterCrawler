        Job Description:
        JOB OVERVIEW
        The Research IT Support Analyst works in a Senior Analyst capacity to optimize development and support operational use of R&amp;D technology to streamline the Institutes business processes, by performing the following duties.
        ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES
        Supports critical R&amp;D IT operations processes, troubleshooting, and resumption in case of malfunction, to provide business process continuity.
        Collaborates with the IT team, Project Directors, Department Directors, and business partners to create and develop multi-year technology road maps.
        Establishes and promotes methods for sharing knowledge, tools, and processes.
        Manages R&amp;D IT related projects advancing the Institute periodically.
        Serves as an independent advisor to business units to collaborate and evaluate information .technologies that would improve security, workflow, and business processes.
        Manages the Strategic Information Technology Plan and Support for R&amp;D.
        Ensures delivery of effective technology solutions to support business needs and strategies within cost, schedule and scope.
        Maintains responsibility for Global support process design and Global R&amp;D intellectual property security.
        Creates and maintains security profiles for various R&amp;D IT processes.
        Identifies and advises on strategies for efficient technology implementation and support.
        Identifies key technologies required to keep the Institute on the leading edge.
        Consults with teams during Technical Planning process and advises on technology and process decisions.
        Monitors team progress toward technical plans and advises on corrective action when necessary.
        Works with project team and software architects to constantly improve existing technologies and processes.
        Monitors technology evaluation efforts by project teams and consults with them as necessary.
        Identifies potentially useful technologies and brings them to the attention of project teams.
        Ensures the results of evaluations are communicated to all project teams.
        Identifies R&amp;D opportunities for technologies that will benefit multiple projects.
        Maintains responsibility for Back-up, security, and Disaster Recovery Design and Planning for R&amp;D functions.
        Supports Research database operations, including conversion of scientific data file formats, generation of graph images from data, and file transfer and synchronization with remote sites.
        Participates as a member of the IT support team.
        Communicates effectively, gets along with coworkers and management, and deals with others effectively and professionally under pressure.
        TECHNICAL COMPETENCIES (Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities)
        To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required.
        Ability to analyze the pros and cons of technology that can provide gains in time and cost.
        Highly technically proficient and extensive experience in the management of technology from a global perspective.
        Excellent problem solving, communication, listening and leadership skills are vital, as is the ability to work collaboratively with all stakeholders.
        Experience in data sciences and digital image development
        Highly proficient in Linux, bash shell, shell scripting, C programming.
        Highly proficient in Information Technology methods and best practices.
        Experience in supporting complex systems in global set-up.
        Excellent analytical, problem solving, communication, listening and leadership skills.
        Familiarity with the PostScript programming language is a plus.
        Proven ability to work in a team environment in a collaborative role.
        EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE
        Master's Degree in computer science, business, engineering or related field; and seven years of related experience in Information Technology; or equivalent combination of education and experience.
        Linux Certification.
