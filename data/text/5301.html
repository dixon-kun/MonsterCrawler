Position Overview

Razorfish Health is seeking a VP, Director of Analytics that will be the right-hand woman or man, to the VP, Group Director of Analytics. The ideal candidate will not only be responsible for developing custom data and analytical solutions to help our growing list of clients but also the leader and mentor to one of the most robust analytics team our industry has to offer. The VP, Director of Analytics will work closely with our client's analytics, marketing, content, and consumer research teams. This position will be responsible for advancing our analytic engagements with key clients by bringing to light modern measurement solutions and techniques.

Note from the manager:

Hello. If you're reading his then you're likely considered a data and analytics guru. You've been working closely with clients to understand their business challenges and thinking about how the integration of data and technology can help to answer their key questions. You're ready to take the next step though – you want to have the freedom and encouragement to push your clients to move beyond the expected, to propel their business forward through data; you want to inspire and guide a strong team to continuously advance their skills and thinking. We've been waiting for you.

I can't wait to meet you!

Other responsibilities include but are not limited to:

* Developing and cultivating relationships with key team members across the agency such as the technology, strategy, creative and account management teams

* Collect, store, and process both digital and offline data for analytic purposes

* Design and implement custom analysis on a project-by-project basis

* Develop Business Intelligence presentations for clients and internal teams

* Perform hands-on analysis to proactively provide in depth insights, trending, behaviors and relationships across all channels to help support the client's needs

* Must be able to identify issues with the data and problem solve

* Serve as project and support lead on multiple projects

* Develop clear analytic strategies/actionable next steps

Qualifications

* 6-8 years of agency or analytics experience

* Pharma and/or Healthcare experience is strongly preferred

* Prescription Data experience is strongly preferred

* Successfully led large scale and complex marketing analytics engagements

* Mastery of the digital analytics space and has innovated to develop new opportunities

* Has demonstrated thought leadership within their organization by becoming a subject matter expert within an analytics environment

* Has exhibited strong communication skills and has the ability to explain complex concepts to diverse audiences

* Ability to translate findings/results into clear, actionable and executable insights

* Exceptional communication and presentation skills; strong business development skills a plus
