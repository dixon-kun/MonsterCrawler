As an IT Solution Architect, you will play a pivotal role in the business by supporting our software and cloud infrastructure activities within the value stream. You will have the opportunity to work closely with other architects, helping to ensure the consistent application of design patterns, architecture principles and frameworks across the teams. This is a chance for you to work with the architecture and value stream teams to continuously develop and content manage the architecture / technical governance frameworks.

At Rolls-Royce, we create integrated power solutions and innovative technologies that keep our customers one step ahead of an ever-changing world. To make that happen we're building on our current Digital capability, which will take our customer offering from great to extraordinary. That's why we're looking for technical problem solvers, innovative thinkers and data manipulators - wherever you are in the world.

Everything we do comes back to our vision of powering the digital revolution. Here, our engineering expertise, global reach and deep industry knowledge come together to deliver outstanding customer relationships and solutions.

For almost two decades, we have been using the power of Big Data to give our customers greater control of their operations. Over the years, our dedicated digital teams have already changed the way our engines are designed, manufactured, operated and maintained on behalf of our customers.

Key Accountabilities

* Working according to DevOps to help build the continuous delivery capability of the value stream.

* Creating solutions and support / guide design activities from others within the value stream.

* Considering security, performance, cost and other business (non-functional) requirements as part of all solutions.

* Contributing to reviews and continuous improvement of methods, tools and standards.

* Documenting all work in accordance with agreed standards.

* Providing guidance and assistance to colleagues in design, creation, testing and documentation.

* Participating in evaluations and reviews of methods, tools and standards.

* Carrying out process improvement assignments as part of a team, applying knowledge, skills and expertise.

* Following the rules of Scrum / Agile and meets your daily and sprint objectives.

* Working directly with internal and external customers to create and integrate architectural solutions.

* Working directly with suppliers and external agencies to enhance our technical capability.

* Supporting marketing and sales activities from an architecture perspective.

Rolls-Royce is a Military Friendly Employer. We evaluate qualified applicants without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, disability, veteran status, or any other protected characteristic.

Essential

* Bachelor's degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Engineering, Computer Science and 8 years of experience in an IT or Software / System Engineering role.

* Or a Master's degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Engineering, Computer Science and 6 years of experience in an IT or Software / System Engineering role.

* Or a JD / PhD.

* Must be a U.S. citizen.

Desirable

* Experience with architecture frameworks such as TOGAF.

* Experience with modeling tools and languages such as UML, SySML, BPMN.

* Hands-on experience with Microsoft Azure cloud services and technologies (preferably related to Azure AD, HDInsight, Data Factory, App Services, Storage and Power BI).

* Experience with application lifecycle tools such as VSTS (Visual Studio Team Services) or TFS (Team Foundation Server) and Git.

* Understanding of SQL and Microsoft SQL Server, in particular Azure SQL and Azure SQL Data Warehouse.

* Experience of participation in agile ceremonies such as sprint planning would be useful as would experience with techniques such as affinity sizing.

* Experience of working in an analytical engineering / data science environment would be desirable.

As the pace of technological advancement continues to quicken, and with data connecting everyone and everything around the globe, the way we are operating and serving our customers is developing further. The exponential rise in the volume and sources of data presents massive opportunities to reveal new insights that have previously remained hidden, and therewith create new value for the customers.

The level of investment in Digital is evidence of the confidence we're placing on our digital future. Ultimately, our aim is to create a series of highly collaborative digital teams across the globe, each ready to share knowledge and embrace change to improve the way we work. With a diverse network of multi-skilled cells, we're bringing innovation with integrity to deliver greater value to clients. Just one example of this culture lies in our ongoing collaboration with Microsoft, in which we're working together to bring the next generation of intelligent engines to the aviation industry.

Here you'll use the latest Big Data and IoT tools and technologies to translate our increasing volume and variety of operational data into meaningful insights. We need people from around the business with a range of skills and experiences to help shape the future of our Digital organisation and to create positive change through service, maintenance, repair and overhaul and factory enablement.

We're changing the way the world is powered by unleashing the value of data.

Trusted to deliver excellence.

We are an equal opportunities employer.
