Position Overview:

Canon U.S.A., Inc. is seeking an experienced Data Scientist (Analyst, Business Intelligence) to join our Advanced Business Analytics and Data Management team. This team is responsible for collaborating and working closely with Canon’s Marketing, Sales, and Business Development divisions to:

  1. Understand and identify new revenue growth opportunities, unseen value, and insights from business user requests or from self-identified analytics.
  2. Support the timely and effective presentation of analytics to business users and/or senior business management.
  3. Support the complete usage and/or execution of the delivered analytics.

The ideal candidate will have the unique capability and visual passion for finding new revenue growth opportunities, unseen value, and insights with ever growing data sources.

The candidate must be a strong communicator, adaptive, creative, motivated, persistent, and demonstrate a “can do” attitude.

Must be able to work in a high-performing team with other BI Analysts to:

  1. Share your analytic best practices and methods used.
  2. Share your draft output for review and input.
  3. Individually drive to an end-state goal to deliver clean, concise, accurate and ready-to-execute output

You will also use your expertise to support, expand, and champion BI understanding and usage through training and collaborating with others to maximize value and insights, actions taken, and benefits realized within the company.

Responsibilities:
  • Work in a high-performing team to identify new revenue opportunities and extract interesting insights (patterns, correlations, etc.) from large data sets using dashboards, visualizations, and complex reporting solutions utilizing SAP Business Objects, Tableau, R, Python, or other tools.
  • Ongoing collaboration with various business units (Product Marketing, Sales, Digital Marketing, Business Development) to support advanced analytics needs and to execute BI recommendations.
  • Support the effective usage of BI for existing business operations and the development of new business areas and opportunities.
  • Identify new advanced analytic opportunities and new data sources for further evaluation.
  • Proactively determine best methods to gather new and existing data to solve complex problems.
  • Build and expand knowledge of BI through training and collaborating with other BI Users and Leaders.
  • Partner with Business Analysts to compile requirements for BI Solution expansion and support user acceptance testing.
  • Document and communicate status of projects and initiatives to senior team members.
Qualifications:
  • Bachelor’s degree required. Master’s Degree is a plus.
  • Minimum of 3 years in Business Intelligence and/or Business Analytics.
  • Minimum of 2 years of experience with SQL.
  • Minimum of 1 year of experience with R, Python, and a data visualization tool (e.g., SAP Business Objects or Tableau).
  • Strong skills in problem solving with a passion for data and a curiosity to find the story.
  • Demonstrated ability to manipulate large data sets from multiple sources.
  • Ability to work independently, ensure business objectives are met, and able to drive tasks to completion.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills and the ability to interact and engage with a diverse group (executives, managers, and subject matter experts).
  • Strong working knowledge of Microsoft Excel, and building an accurate, clean presentation story and final deliverable with PowerPoint.
