RESPONSIBILITIES:

* Works on agile teams in building an innovative, modern experience for users of ADP Retirement Services.

* Create amazing and beautiful experiences, which meet business requirements, end user needs

* Work closely with Product Management, Development, UX Research and end users, in exploratory research, concept development and addressing usability issues.

* Work collaboratively with larger design team in brainstorming new ideas and ideating on existing design concepts

* Work closely with front end Software Developers in applying ADP standard "look and feel".

* Communicates with technical and non-technical staff to ensure full integration and goal attainment.

* Create designs, which surface complex data sets to end users, in an understandable and meaningful way.

* Incorporates the ADP UX Standards and Templates into work products and application UI design.

* Ensures style sheets, images and icons are applied correctly. Provide support for communication and enhancement of standards including style guides, patterns, and behavior clarifications.

QUALIFICATIONS:

* 5+ years of UX design in a corporate or agency context, assisting with a broad range of assets from wireframes to production ready designs.

* Able to take feedback constructively and iterate on designs and ideas from product owners, UX research and end users

* Experience with leveraging user research in a large-scale enterprise product

* Creative thinker who can effectively innovate big ideas and also work out all the details.

* Experience moving your designs throughout an agile development process.

* InVision and Axure tools

* Skilled in using wire framing/flow and design tools such as Adobe Creative Cloud, Sketch- Experience with responsive web and native mobile technologies, as well firm understanding of best practices

* Able to show off terrific portfolio of professional design work (UX and/or Visual Design).

* Up to 20% Travel

* Experience with design management a plus

* Experience with creating data visualizations a plus

* Experience in FinTech and/or Financial Services a plus

* Balsamiq and Omnigraffle a plus!
