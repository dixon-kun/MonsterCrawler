         Sr. Technical Consulting Engineer
          Job Description
         This is an exciting position for software engineers who want to combine their passion for Software to drive and deliver influence ant impact for Cloud Service Providers, High Performance Computing (HPC) and Enterprise customers during pre-sales and post-sales cycle of various Intel Software Development Tools. As a Sr. Technical Consulting Engineer, you will use your strong systems software knowledge and experience to provide consulting services and support to drive enablement and adoption of Intel Distribution in various market segments like IoT, HPC, Enterprise and Cloud Service Providers.Your responsibilities will include, but not be limited to:- help our customers in IoT, HPC, Enterprise and Cloud Service Providers segment improve software quality and performance by driving adoption of Intel Distribution- Offer Technical consulting and training to key customers remotely or on-site.- Creating technical marketing collateral, knowledge base articles and customer case studies.- Developing, prototyping, and documenting code samples for technical trainings, seminars and webinar like activities.- Soliciting customer feedback proactively on our software products and technologies and ensure this feedback is understood by the appropriate teams within Intel.- Leveraging customer feedback in conjunction with appropriate use of internal investigations to drive the design requirements for the next generation of hardware and software.- Collaborating with product development teams in planning next generation product features.- Determining and defining best practices for Intel developer products and highlighting these to educate customers via various venues.This is a unique and exciting position bringing your creativity and passion for software for enabling our customers in IoT, HPC, Enterprise and Cloud Service Providers segment maximize the value of IA platforms through our software development tools.
          Qualifications
         Required Qualifications:- Master's degree in an Engineering discipline e.g. Computer Science, Computer Engineering, EE, Math or Data Science. Ph. D in the aforementioned areas would be a plus - 5 years of Python software development experience - Strong communications skills, analytic and problem solving skills are mustPreferred Qualifications:- 1-2 years of experience in statistical analysis, machine learning, and data mining-1-2 years of development experience with one or more of the following: Apache Hadoop, Apache Spark,Theano, Caffe and Torch- 1-2 years development experience with data visualization and BI tools like Tableau is a plus- Customer facing/enabling experience
