        About Cazena:
        Cazena is an early stage, fast growing startup that has just launched a fully managed big data platform as a service. Cazenas mission is to accelerate a $40B market by making it easy for any enterprise to process big data in the cloud. Cazena was founded by former Netezza leaders and is backed by top investors like Andreessen Horowitz. Cazena offers secure cloud data science sandboxes, data marts, and data lakes that are simple, trusted and cost-effective. Cazena is expanding its team in a few key areas. We are looking for high energy, technically strong, team players with a passion to win and have fun in the process.
        Job Description:
        Cazena is seeking a principal level software engineer to join its Cloud Software Engineering (CSE) team in Waltham, MA. The CSE team is responsible for design and development of several Cazena Software as a Service (SaaS) features such as orchestration, provisioning, backup/restore, and upgrade as well as our cloud platform, DevOps/Site Reliability Engineering (SRE), and release engineering.
        Baseline Qualifications:
        * BS/MS in computer science/related major or equivalent work experience
        * 5+ years of software development experience in Java, with significant experience developing in Java for Linux environments
        * 2+ years of development experience with the AWS and/or Azure public clouds and their CLIs and APIs.
        * Experience developing RESTful web services
        * Comfortable working within a fast, agile development environment with continuous integration
        * Understanding of networking, storage, and virtual computing concepts, multi-threading, and parallel processing.
        * Experience with Object Oriented Programming (OOP) and/or Design (OOD)
        * Knowledge of professional software engineering practices and best practices for the full software development life cycle, including coding standards, code reviews, source control management, build processes, testing, and operations
        Desirable:
        * Experience with the Akka toolkit and the Spring framework
        * Experience with Ruby, Python, and/or Golang
        * Experience with large scale distributed systems
        * Understanding of Big Data technologies and solutions
        * Experience with system configuration management and monitoring tools such as SaltStack and Sensu.
