Responsibilities

Responsibilities

As a Senior Manager within the PNB (Product and New Business) Measures & Analytics team, you will partner with product leaders to use data-driven techniques to shape the strategy and direction of new products and services.

The Sr. Manager role will supervise 4-8 Managers on the Measures and Analytics team who will partner with product leaders to translate product vision into quantifiable success through rigorous analysis and data exploration.

The Analytics Sr. Product Manager will also:

  • Partner with business units, product owners, data scientists and development teams to translate potential data-driven ideas into fully developed product solutions.
  • Champion ideas that lead to end-to-end visibility of the performance and health of PNB initiatives.
  • Architect and create post-event and predictive analysis for PNB products from relevant rich data sources across Verizon and externally.
  • Analyzes  complex business  problems  using   data  from   internal and   external  data  sources  to  provide  strategic and   actionable  business  insights  to  decision  makers
  • Identifies   and  interprets  trends  in  patterns  to  locate  influences   and  construct  forecasts  and  recommendations  on  strategic/tactical   plans
  • Define and implement specifications  for reports  and analysis  based on business  needs  and  required/ available  data  elements

Responsibilities

  • Working with the business units and product teams, scope and quantify new data-driven product concepts
  • Create and implement  18 month rolling Roadmap of new tolls and functionality the drives values across the enterprise
  • Develop the BI &A team with the best talent and mentor then toward successful deliverable end to end
  • Define  advanced analytics and data mining to identify insights and develop product concepts
  • Demonstrate the value of different product alternatives and potential investments through analyses
  • Partner with Data Scientists to shape the development of prioritized solutions
  • Use analytics to help inform the product roadmap
  • Make and present data/findings in a relevant, consumable, meaningful and insightful   way (including reports and dashboards)
  • Provide analysis to support the development and deployment of new product solutions across the Consumer Products organization and PNB as a whole.
  • Validate solutions by devising well-constructed data experiments
  • Provide decision-support to product leaders, development teams, and business units
  • Define and develop key product metrics that drive product and business success
  • Generate decision-support tools to monitor post-deployment performance
  • Analyze and monitor key performance metrics and recommend product enhancements
  • Create visualizations to aid in understanding product data and build rich tools that enable product leads to work effectively.
  • Supports  various systems & tools operations and development
  • Product Planning/Sourcing Agreements and accounts payables Support
  • IT Prioritization Lean Business Agile lead and management

Qualifications

Must have:

  • Bachelor’s degree or six or more years of work experience.
  • Six or more years of relevant work experience.
  • Experience in reporting / business intelligence / business analytics.
  • Data analytics application development and behavior/predictive analysis experience
  • Project management experience

Ideally, you’ll also have

  • Degree in Math, Physics, Computer Science, Engineering or other quantitative discipline; MBA or Master’s Degree in quantitative discipline preferred, PhD highly desired.
  • Extensive experience in reporting / business intelligence / business analytics with increasing responsibility.
  • Strong experience in a product management or technical consulting role
  • Business Intelligence  Certification (CPBI)
  • Passionate about producing high-quality analytics deliverables and communicating results to a broad audience
  • Demonstrated success in data or business analytics, product management analytics, and proven project management experience
  • Experience in product development or analytics in the Technology sector
  • Experience with complex data analytics, data products, and Big Data technologies.  Passion for Big Data technologies and how they will change business models
  • Hands-on experience working with data analysis
  • Excellent SQL skills; comfortable using various data access tools
  • Experience working with Hadoop and Map/Reduce a plus
  • Experience with statistical tools such as R or SAS and scripting language (Python, Perl) experience a plus
  • Experience with BI platforms such as Tableau, scripting language (Python, Perl) experience a plus
  • Familiarity with the identification and resolution of  issues working with disparate sources of data and data quality issues
  • Deep Experience working with cross-functional teams to develop products and services. Ability to build interdepartmental relationships
  • Excellent analytical, written and verbal communication skills
  • High level of intellectual curiosity and comfort with ambiguity
  • Self-motivated; independent, organized and proactive; highly responsive, flexible, and adaptable when working across multiple product teams
