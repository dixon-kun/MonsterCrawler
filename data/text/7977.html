Wunderman is the Official experiential AOR for the United States Navy.  We are part of a large integrated team servicing the Navy ‘s recruiting efforts.  Other integrated aspects of the all Navy Partnership are Advertising, Media, Social, Digital, Data, Direct, PR, Diversity and Call Center

The Events AS will represent the Chicago Experiential/Sponsorship/Event practice, based in Memphis.  He/she will travel to and manage activations at events, provide on-site support, operational/management, implementation and post-event reporting. Position reports to an Account Director.


  • Assist in developing a yearly strategic plan for event and sponsorships. Work closely with the team, the lead strategist on developing a proposed go to market plan.
  • Along with the other AS, lead point on contact with the corresponding Navy counterparts on all elements of the mobile STEM display units, sponsorships and events.
    • Manage all components of major national mobile tours, conventions and title sponsorship of sporting events.
  • Expected to attend regular meetings in Millington, TN; on base with the respective client (s)
  • Work with Account Director for planning and oversight for all contractual components.
  • Manage the support team in the office and third party vendors on the road.
  • Facilitate and attend weekly status meetings, client meetings, client calls
  • Pre-event planning including, but not limited to overseeing third party vendors (staffing and mobile company), building event itineraries, managing a budget and ticket/assets fulfillment.
  • Manage day to day activities as needed which may include evaluations of proposals, manage tour route and scheduling, etc.
  • On-site management of all activities. Ability to supervise and organize vendors, interact with third party vendors, internal staff and clients throughout event weekend ensuring success.
  • Provide post event analysis and participant feedback to incorporate into future learning’s based on measurement goals set.
  • Working alongside the diversity agency (UWG) to ensure all events are being coordinated and managed per plan.
  • Work/collaborate with WPP partner agencies
  • Maintain a metrics dashboard for client review.
  • Passion for always thinking above and beyond current account activation, bringing out of the box, fresh, new thinking and ideas to the table.
  • Stay atop of key industry trends, bringing new activation ideas to the table on a regular basis
  • Maintain a calendar of responsible events and implement a system that enables and anticipates both short term and long term planning and effective management for the organization meetings, events and activities.

  • Minimum of 7 years of event experience with an agency, property or brand.
  • Management and development of staff.
  • Ability to multi-task and juggle numerous tasks for each event.
  • Extremely organized and detailed with effective time management skills.
  • Ability to travel to multiple events in a year; including weekends.
  • Knowledge or previous experience in management of mobile tours preferred.
  • Based in Memphis, TN office
  • BA/BS degree required; Marketing, Business, or Communications degree preferred
