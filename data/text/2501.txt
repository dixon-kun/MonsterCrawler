        Contact Center Operations / Process Analyst
        Manager &#8211; Community Data Science and Technology
        Non-Exempt
        9/14/2015
        JOB SUMMARY:
        The Contact Center Operations / Process Analyst assists in completing, analyzing and reporting on advanced contact center tasks. To perform this job successfully, the candidate needs to have a strong analytical/mathematical mindset, excellent communication skills, be flexible, be able to multitask, be able learn new systems and processes quickly, be internally motivated, and be able to think critically. The candidate must not only be able to gather and compile metrics, but also must be capable of reporting them in such a way that they are meaningful, useful, and widely available. This function will also work with updating and creating scripts, SQL reports and HTML/Java updates as well as any other Contact Center Operations functions, which include quality assurance, workforce management, attendance and adherence management.
        ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
        1. Gather, compile, submit and report metrics for the contact center on regular intervals.
        2. Assist in maintaining existing technology solutions (e.g. troubleshoot Agent Manager and Agent Toolbar issues, work to resolve heads-up display outages, identify reporting irregularities or inconsistencies, assist in upgrades and new implementations/projects)
        3. Assist in maintaining agent and customer tools and back end systems. This will require knowledge of MS SQL, HTML, Java, and VB
        3. Regularly takes contacts in an overflow capacity.
        4. Completes daily operational tasks such as global returns, creating contact center tickets, and distributor notifications.
        5. Serves as department subject matter expert on assigned operational tasks.
        PHYSICAL DEMANDS AND WORKING CONDITIONS:
        Ability to sit for extended periods of time.
        Ability to operate desktop computer and relevant applications.
        Ability to work in team environment.
        Ability to coordinate and communicate effectively.
        KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES:
        Microsoft Office Suite (Advanced knowledge in Excel or Access).
        Task Oriented, able to remain focused while completing long/repetitive tasks.
        Reporting experience, including report development.
        Excellent organizational, written, and verbal communication skills.
        Ability to perform comfortably in a fast-paced, deadline-oriented work environment.
        Understanding of standard operations procedures, with preferred MLM knowledge and experience.
        SQL, Visual Basic or Web Development is needed
        EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:
        1. High School Diploma or equivalent.
        2. 3+ years of relevant contact center / customer service experience
        3. Working towards a B.S. in Computer Science, Mathematics, or Statistics
