AcreValueis a farmland data and valuation platform (think Zillow for farmland). We stitch together many large geospatial data sets, analyze that data to come up with an automated estimate of value and serve the data up in a map-based web application used by farmers, land investors, real estate professionals, and anyone else active in the land market.

We are looking for a front-end engineer to join our AcreValue team.

WHAT YOU'LL BE WORKING ON

  • Work closely with other developers and data science to design and develop a high-performance, scalable web application for consumer and enterprise use.
  • Create an application that aggregates and analyzes terabytes of data about soils, climate, crop rotations, taxes, interest rates, and corn prices to calculate the estimated value of an individual field.

WHAT WE'RE LOOKING FOR

  • No farming/agriculture knowledge required!
  • 3 years of experience developing user-friendly web applications in a fast-paced, small team environment
  • Significant experience with current technology.  We use the technologies listed below, but they are not a requirement.
  • Excellent code quality and attention to detail. Must be passionate about avoiding technical debt
  • Driven and strong sense for high-quality user experience
  • Background in analysis and visualization of geospatial data a plus
  • The passion for revolutionizing the agriculture industry with your hard earned engineering skills!
  • B.S. in Computer Science or equivalent experience
  • Start-up experience a plus.

PERKS

  • We value diversity and treat every employee with a high degree of autonomy
  • Have a big impact by joining a small, fast-paced, smart team. We are having fun building a great company and helping one of the most important industries in the world
  • Competitive salary, commission program, and stock options
  • Medical, dental, vision and 401(K) benefits
  • All the perks you'd expect from a San Francisco startup … free lunch, happy hours, ping pong, and a high degree of autonomy!

TECHNOLOGIES USED

Python, Ubuntu, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, AngularJS, CSS3, HTML5, Django
