DESCRIPTION

Software Web Developer with knowledge in designing and developing new software products or major enhancements to existing software.

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Minimum 5 years of experience in data management, including data warehousing systems and concepts, as well as data transformation and integration techniques.
  • Minimum 5 years of experience in performing data extractions, data analysis and programming, to include writing advanced SQL Queries or creating programs in a statistical software package such as SAS, R, or Stata for the purpose of data analytics and decision science.
  • Minimum of a Bachelor's Degree required, preferably in a field related to mathematical statistics or biostatistics, data science, computer information systems, finance, accounting, public health, informatics or healthcare administration. Can substitute education requirement for 10 years of related experience.
  • Security Preferred

SPECIFIC TASKS

  • Provide support for the sustainment and maintenance of current and future operational requirements for intranet/internet mobile ready applications. The Contractor will be required to work with government developers and follow their lead on work, timelines, deliverables and utilize industry standard methods to enhance security, maintainability, and sustainability.
  • Provide support for troubleshooting & resolving bug and issues with applications.
  • Work within TFS Team Project Collection following a defined process and adhere to best practices.
  • Shall aide government developers in the development, sustainment and implementation of ETL processes to include data mapping, writing script, analyzing raw data for data transformation, quality assurance testing and creating SQL queries & Stored Procedures for data transformation & Ad-Hoc reporting.
  • Provide support to the government developers on the development, sustainment, integration, and modification of SQL databases, SQL Queries & Stored Procedures and Ad-Hoc Reporting as it pertains to application requirements.
  • Software Web Developer will provide support for the development, sustainment and maintenance of current and future operational requirements for intranet/internet web based applications.
  • Support and maintain the design and implementation of application modules to include data models, controllers, and views for the presentation of information
