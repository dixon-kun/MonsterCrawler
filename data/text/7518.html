Data Science Intern

Every day, the Internet enables countless opportunities in ways never before imaginable. And every day, Verisign engineers enable the full potential of the Internet through our management of the indispensable infrastructure of .com and .net. We hire managers and engineers with an exceptional ability to execute and who are looking for a fast-paced product development environment where innovation is recognized and rewarded. Every day we solve the kind of complex problems that no one else in the world is facing. The world is counting on us and we deliver.

As a Data Science intern, you will be part of the team studying how domain names are used. In this role you will build tools that help us study internet content and internet traffic patterns. On top of that, you’ll get to learn from some of the smartest engineers in the industry.

What You Do

  • Develop web based applications using latest technologies
  • Contribute to web content and traffic pattern feature extraction algorithms and study the difference that these changes make to our categorization engine
  • Learn from the most talented and motivated engineers in the industry
  • Use agile software development processes to iterate on solutions, leveraging open source tools and utilities to automate daily builds and unit testing
  • Collaborate effectively with cross-functional organizations including system architects, project managers, quality assurance, product managers and operations
Qualifications

Who You Are

  • Have basic knowledge of Java, XML, JavaScript or similar languages
  • Know computer science fundamentals
  • Have knowledge or interest in learning Web Development
  • Have Good verbal and written communication skills.

Education

  • Be a student working towards a BS/BA or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering or Information Technology
