Company Description

The E-470 Public Highway Authority is one of the most technologically advanced transportation organizations in the world – based right here in Denver, Colorado. We are one of the few fully automated toll collection systems in the nation and a showcase for the future of toll way administration. As a well-funded, public/private partnership, technology is strategic to our operation. Are you ready to put your talent to work with us?

Job Description

As our Numbers Ninja you will be the organization’s go-to for data extracts, data insights and information intelligence necessary to run our business. We’re no small cheese when it comes to databases – we track millions of transactions every week – the roads in Colorado are SUPER busy! You thrive when you are creating models, swimming in database fields and creating dashboards. You have a gift for pulling from all sources – internal and external – and communicating the results in a meaningful way to your business partners. You are passionate about working at all levels and across all functions of the organization.

How you’ll spend your time:
• Providing analytical support, data, recommendations and analysis to your business stakeholders
• Developing an in-depth understanding of underlying data, data structures, KPIs, and business uses of information in order to provide clear data visualizations and analysis
• Producing dashboards and analysis for the various departments within the company
• Driving consistency in analysis across the company
• Collaborating with cross-functional teams to provide continual improvement in business intelligence & the systems landscape at the company
• Establishing a strong working relationship with our key business leaders to support them with any data, dashboards, or analysis needs they have
• Working with your technology team to coordinate master data changes
• Responding swiftly to queries and faults; investigating and answering with clarity
• Delivering training to your users centered around how to access, consume and utilize BI analysis
• Promoting the use of BI solutions throughout the business, driving adoption and growth in user base

Qualifications

What you’ll bring to the role:
• BS/BA in computer science, math, statistics, data science or equivalent work experience.
• Minimum 5 years of experience working in a data analysis role where ETL, data modeling and data quality are a big part of your day
• Deep knowledge of Big Data analysis and manipulation tools – we are a Microsoft shop so we use SSAS and PowerBI
• A SQL Guru and Excel Master
• Experience Implementing dashboarding tools like Tableau, Lookr or PowerBI
• Confidence in your ability to decompose complex problems and identify the root cause
• Committed to quality in everything you do
• Able to apply creative methods for translating abstract information concepts into understandable visuals for even the most non-technical audience
• Your brain is focused on creating efficiencies and making improvements whenever possible
• A gifted communicator with the natural ability to build strong relationships up, down and across the organization.



Additional Information

And finally, what you’ll enjoy:
• Competitive Salary
• Outstanding package of benefits and generous time off
• Working with a fun, hard charging, small team of highly competent individuals
