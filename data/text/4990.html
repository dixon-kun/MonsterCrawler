Job Description

Berico is actively conducting SIGINT and geospatial intelligence analysis supporting an Ops Center at Fort Gordon, GA for a government customer.

We are hiring Junior Geolocation Operators with either Active and Passive or just Passive experience to produce geo-location data to support the development of various intelligence reports. The qualified candidate must possess the skills and knowledge to manage and facilitate the prosecution of collection data files. Additionally, the candidate will conduct general and directed radio frequency searches on targets of interest in the OEF Theater of Operation. This is a 24-hour operation and shift work is required.

Job Qualifications

* 2 years or more experience in geo-location within the last 5 years within NSA, a service cryptologic element of Other Government Agency (OGA)

* Experience with communications intelligence collection equipment is required.

* Experience with ground, airborne, and/or national geo-location systems is preferred.

* The successful candidate will have previous experience with processing collection data files.

* Must have the Passive or both Passive & Active Geo-location experience or certification

* Active TS/SCI and CI Poly

Desired Skills

* Previous target experience with Middle East, North Africa, and Central Asia is beneficial.

* Experience with any of the following is beneficial: GILGAMESH, AIRHANDLER, TPCS, AN/PRD-13, MEV/SS, ELCC, WITCHHUNT, HITS DRAGONFLY, WHAMI, TEMPTRESS, RAS Subsystems, and STORY CLASSIC
