The Full Motion Video (FMV) Analyst provides direct intelligence support to National and Tactical objectives. Successful candidates will join a high ops tempo team, embedded in a multi-intelligence fusion watch center environment, working a 24x7 rotating shift schedule. The analysts use desktop software applications to view the FMV, as well as other imagery, and relevant information for developing intelligence analysis products.  FMV Analysts exploit video collected by ISR platforms, and correlate information from both classified and unclassified sources to develop intelligence products.  The FMV analysts create intelligence reports and presentations for use in briefings to high-level government personnel.  FMV Analysts are required to successfully complete any positional certifications within the designated timeline prescribed by the customer.

Minimum Qualifications:

· Completion of a military or government basic Imagery intelligence course.

· Excellent written communication, research, and analytic skills.

· Excellent oral communication skills for presenting and briefing.

· Ability to manage research and coordinate projects.

· TS/SCI security clearance.

· HS diploma/GED AND with at least 3 years of current GEOINT Analysis experience.

· Must be able to work rotating shift schedule as required.

Desired Qualifications:

  • Proficiency with MAAS, Falcon View, Google Earth, RemoteView, Socet GXP, ArcGIS, mIRC, Windows Movie Maker, Super Buffer Check, IEC:
  • Background in Counter-Terrorism analysis.

· Knowledge of Intelligence Community programs and products

Headquartered in McLean, Virginia, Altamira Technologies Corporation provides multi-INT analytic and engineering services to the defense, intelligence, and homeland security communities.
