PURPOSE AND OBJECTIVES

Join our commerce insights and analytics team as part of our customer experience group of the SAP Hybris commerce division. https://www.hybris.com/en/commerce/customer-experience

Your Responsibilities

  • As ETL - Data Scientist - Hadoop Developer you’ll be primarily responsible for developing batch and real time ETL to create meaningful insights and analytics
  • Be an active member in one of our self-organized and cross-functional teams, producing software according to agile and scrum principles
  • Ensure fully automated testing by designing and writing automated unit, integration and acceptance tests
  • Actively participate in addressing non-functional requirements such as performance, security, scalability, continuous integration, migration and compatibility
  • Collaborate with members of the wider product development team across multiple distributed locations mainly in Europe and North-America
  • Take ownership from design of the feature through first lines of code to how it performs in production (You build it, you run it)

Your Qualifications

  • Bachelor/Master degree in computer science, software engineering or equivalent, or be a recognized expert in the field
  • Strong knowledge of Python, knowing Java is also helpful
  • Expert knowledge of ETL processes
  • Knowledge of big data and ETL technologies such as Druid, Flink, Kafka, Hadoop, Hive, Storm, Flume, MongoDB…
  • Experience working with cloud services (AWS, SaaS model) and microservices
  • Ability to adapt quickly to changing technologies, frameworks, language etc.
  • Being flexible and open to regular changes
  • Believer in pair programming, Test Driven Development, and continuous integration
  • Devops mindset with a passion for delivering working code frequently in production
  • Desire to continue learning and improving
  • Good knowledge of issue tracking (Jira), source code management (Git), Linux/Mac OS administration, build tools, continuous integration systems, package management and testing framework
  • Good communication skills, with the ability to contribute to a collaborative working culture
  • Good verbal and written English

Makes You Stand Out

  • Experience with the SAP Hybris Commerce Platform and or yaas.io
  • Knowledge of the enterprise ecommerce industry
  • Send us code samples via Github or others
  • Send us examples of your projects and highlight your personal contribution
