Astellas is announcing a Manager, Medical Affairs Americas Data and Analytics, Location, Northbrook, IL 
Purpose &amp; Scope of Position: 
Responsible for analytics and trending analysis across multiple systems and formats to support Voice of Customer (VOC) and Core Medical Plan (CMP) development and updates for Medical Affairs, Americas (MAA) by various data sources. Implements data solutions to address Medical Affairs, Americas business needs, working in partnership with all Medical Affairs entities, Global Development, Finance, Legal, Compliance, IT and external vendors as needed. 
Essential Job Responsibilities: 
* Manages and leads technology based projects supporting MAA objectives and provides ongoing updates or change notifications to appropriate stakeholders.
* Manages, develops, maintains and supports tools to assist in collection and anaylsis of data across multiple systems and formats to support VOC ,CMP development and other key activities associated with the overall MAA mission/vision.
* Leads implementation of new MAA IT systems from vendor selection, and procurement to implemention. 
* Ensures developed solutions meet the business needs of Medical Affairs, Americas and are vetted with appropriate stakeholders (e.g. Legal, Compliance, Astellas IT) 
* Acts as Primary system administrator for systems/repositories which support MAA. 
Quantitative Dimensions: 
* Successful management of complex data sources (structured and unstructured) and resulting datasets/outputs.
* Successful communication of change, delays and outages to all Medical Affairs stakeholders. 
Organizational Context: 
* Reports to Director Medical Affairs, Americas Operations.
* Collaborates with internal MAA stakeholders, other Astellas stakeholders (Procurement, Legal, Compliance, IT, etc.), and external vendors
Qualifications: 
Required: 
* Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or Data Science required.
* At least 5 years experience in healthcare industry, with at least 3 years in an data analytics role.
* Demonstrated collaboration and leadership in a matrix organization.
* Strong project management and leadership skills with the ability to lead non-direct reports in cross-functional areas.
* Ability to manipulate complex data sets and with an appropriate set of programming tools (e.g., Phython, R, SQL, Java, SAS, Unix and/or Hadoop).
* Recognizes problem situations and uses both formal and informal methods to find solutions. 
* Demonstrated ability in taking initiative to proactively evaluate existing strategies and processes and recommend changes, when appropriate.
* Extensive computer proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite: Excel, SharePoint, Access, Visio, Project and PowerPoint.
* Ability to translate between technical and business management to effectively achieve results.
* Self-directed, with the ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment.
* Experience with data warehouse reporting and related database management solutions.
* Excellent skills in the following areas: organization, written and verbal communication, accuracy, and personal initiative.
* Strong attention to detail.
Preferred: 
* IT certifications in appropriate area.
* Masters or equivalent level degree in data or computer sciences.
* Previous experience working in a pharmaceutical data environment.
* Administrator Certification for Veeva CRM and Veeva Vault
