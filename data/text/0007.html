JOB DESCRIPTION

DIGITAL DATA SCIENCE CONSULTANT

Data Modeler – SAS, R, Enterprise Minder

POSITION:

This is for a Data Modeler to do modeling using SAS, R, and Enterprise Minder to build predictive models | data science

RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Build forecast and other prediction models for KPIs leveraging  web analytics data as well as transactional, promotional data from disparate systems
  • Set up anomaly detection, alerts and KPI dashboards leveraging web analytics data and prediction models
  • Analyze web analytics data, perform click-stream analysis and ad-hoc analysis to support various digital initiatives

REQUIREMENT :

  • Analytical experience in web (digital) data analysis, click stream analysis, customer level analysis, methodology, trending and behavioral diagnosis with a proven track record of success and attention to detail.
  • Knowledge of Data Science techniques including:
    • Data Stitching
    • Univariate and Multivariate Analysis
    • Hypothesis testing,
    • ANOVA
    • Segmentation
    • Clustering
    • Bayesian Statistics
    • Forecasting
    • Anomaly detection
    • Boosting and Bagging
    • Bias corrections
  • Expertise with one or more Big Data technology and Relational databases

  • Experience in SAS , R, with working knowledge of extr action and manipulation of data
  • R
  • Enterprise Minder
  • Experience with tagging technology/tag managers such as Tealium and Ensighten

EDUCATION :

Bachelor's Degree in statistics, mathematics, computer science, quantitative economics or related field
