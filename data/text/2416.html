JOB SUMMARY –

H&R Block is transforming tax preparation through data to ensure that every client we serve gets the best tax outcome possible. The Director, Data Science and Analytics is a critical role in this transformation. He/She will be responsible for the process of mining our Assisted and DIY data, extracting insights and partnering with our business units to create a process to apply the insights in our tax prep process at an individual client level.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES –

The Director, Data Science leads and directs work associated with advanced analytics in the corporate analytics team, blending the skills of inventor/explorer, a business thinker and a data analyst: asking questions, turning data into knowledge and client facing outcomes that enable the company to innovate, differentiate itself from its competitors and enhance profitability.

This individual will partner with business owners, client experience and our external partner IBM Watson to be a change agent transforming our data from a reporting tool into a true competitive advantage. The Director, Data Science will also participate in the creation of and lead measurement initiatives related to the development of new business processes, tools, and products.

Key responsibilities:

* Serve as the day-to-day Analytics leader for our new client experience development

* Supporting our Assisted, DIY and New Business business units

* Partner with IBM Watson and Client Experience teams to create new data-driven client experience

* Shift data science efforts from macro business focus to productionalized application at the client level

* Direct and execute the mining of large amounts of data and perform data analysis

* Initial study design, acquisition of data, prototyping and production roll out

* Create models, simulations, and optimization routines

* Recruit, train, develop and motivate advanced analytics talent

* Build skills of the analytics team for current and future projects

* Manages, assesses performance, mentors and balance workloads of individuals assigned to manager

* Maintain an active, positive role as a representative for Scripps in the advanced analytics community

* Provide platform oversight, selection and maintenance leadership for the software and other tools used to develop analyses that identify patterns, influences, correlations, relationships, predictive factors, risk factors, and that provide better situational awareness

* Ensure modern features are available for the creation of models, simulations, and optimization routines

* Proactively identify tools, techniques, software and talent needed to support current and future products and services -- including data acquisition alternatives, identifying new data sources and evaluation (testing) plans for data

* Provide input into product and service roadmaps

* Be a positive force to cultivate a culture that is passionate about developing analytics as a business competency

* Drive accountability for value recognition and business results

* Create and contribute to a quality work environment that motivates team members to perform at their highest levels and positively affects employee and business partner relationships

* Be a publicly visible presence, including creating communications and leading training sessions, advocating for the benefits of analytics

Education / work experience:

* Masters or PhD in business analytics or related field, or equivalent experience

* 10+ years of experience in solving analytical problems using quantitative approaches (or equivalent)

* Proven success leading, managing or mentoring a team of analytic professionals

* Demonstrated leadership using statistical analysis and research to drive positive change in a business setting

* Brand, product development, marketing, media or advertising agency experience (preferred)

* Demonstrated experience using R and or SAS to craft data driven solutions to business problems.

* Familiarity with the Hadoop/Spark ecosystem

* Understanding of and experience using unstructured data to solve business problems
