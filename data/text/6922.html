Company Overview:

At Nuance, we empower people with the ability to seamlessly interact with their connected devices and the digital world around them.  We are creating a world where technology thinks and acts the way people do by designing the most human, natural, and intuitive ways of interacting with technology.

Our nimble technology uses analytics and advanced algorithms to transform the inanimate into animate and reduce complicated processes into simple ones.

Job Summary:

Summary: At Nuance Communications we are defining the future of how people will interact and communicate with intelligent machines. We are looking for a World Wide Head of Cognitive Innovation to lead Nuance’s Cognitive Innovation Group (CIG). The CIG team is focused on applying the latest techniques in machine learning and natural language processing to advance the development of artificially intelligent systems. The CIG team collaborates with leading fortune 500 enterprise customers in a fast-paced, stimulating work environment. The work done by this team drives the future of product development and has broad visibility to senior executives within Nuance as well as Nuance’s customers.

Job Responsibilities:
The selected candidate will lead Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning technical strategy for Nuance’s Enterprise business unit. This position will be responsible for defining and driving Nuance’s machine learning architecture as well as applying the latest Machine Learning techniques to Enterprise problems. The candidate will leverage open source tools along with Nuance’s proprietary technologies in order to develop new products focused on analytics, prediction, recommender systems, knowledge systems and human assisted learning. The position will also be responsible for evangelizing Nuance’s A.I. capabilities and supporting sales with Nuance’s Enterprise customers.

Duties & Responsibilities

• Serve as the head of A.I./M.L. technical strategy for the enterprise business unit.
• Work with stake holders across the organization to understand business requirements and translate into technical strategy for Nuance’s A.I. / ML platform.
• Serve as subject matter expert across the company and provide recommendations on how to leverage machine learning to developing new products and solutions.
• Work with other team members to investigate design approaches, prototype new technology and evaluate technical feasibility.
• Lead the design and production of scalable backend and client applications
• Support sales in discussing and A.I. capabilities, strategy, and vision.

Required Skills

• Experience with Machine Learning, A.I. or NLP Program
• 10+ years experience leading high visibility programs and interacting with senior executives
• Proven experience developing and driving technical strategy within an enterprise focused company
• 12+ years of machine learning, data science, statistical analysis, quantitative analytics, and/or forecasting/predicting analytics
• 5+ years of applying machine learning using distributed systems
• 10 + years of experience with R or Python
• Experience working in an Agile Software Development environment
