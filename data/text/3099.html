Job Specifics

We are currently looking to bring another committed innovator onto our team to help deliver quality analytics solutions that will serve hundreds of organizations across the globe.

* Be the point person for managing IT delivery, bringing your understanding of agile, tracking expertise of cost/status/risk, and the know-how to break barriers to get the job done right

* Develop and execute procedures for project-level financial tracking and process to roll up to organization-level tracking

* Bring order, organization, and structured procedures to a dynamic and growing team

* Work closely with technical team to ensure timely delivery for product definition, design, and implementation milestones while ensuring high-quality products and insights are brought to Cubic customers

* Manage outward expectations with customers and internally communicate priorities and schedule impact

* Instill structure and adherence to maintaining knowledgebase and documentations in an organized and thoughtful manner

* Coordinate amongst the team and get the right stakeholders together in a matrix organization

* Effectively manage software and resourcing contracts, project tracking, and agile processes

* Identify issues with sufficient lead for timely resolution and actively pursues risk reduction measures

* Performs Earned Value management tasks

* Demonstrates proven understanding of engineering processes, including the delivery of data management, reporting and analysis solutions, quality assurance, configuration management, contracts administration, project accounting and finance, and product management

If you're motivated to harness the power of our agile teams to improve the lives of millions of travelers and can check the boxes above, let's talk.

Four year college degree, in computer science, software engineering or related technical discipline, plus a minimum of six years of related technical or engineering experience, including four years of project engineering, project management, or other applicable supervisory responsibility. Advanced engineering degree or MBA is desirable.

In addition, should be able to demonstrate the following professional experience:

Proven ability to lead cross-functional project teams

Ability to effectively communicate verbally and in writing

Ability to clearly present technical information and project details to business leaders and technical teams
