Job Description

How would you like to work on data using and building some of the tools that are critical to moving & transforming this data into valuable and insightful information? What about presenting that data in a way individuals can consume visually in a quick and easy manner? As an Analytics Engineer, we have many opportunities to work with huge amounts of data each and every day. Our Analytics Engineer works very closely with all aspects of data, both internal and external. They need the mindset to use a variety of tools and platforms to suitably move data across systems and build essential tables needed to perform analysis. In this role, your work will broadly influence the company's data consumers and analysts.

The perfect applicant for this role will have a strong ability to guide others through the data and analytics infrastructure as well as the skills that it takes to participate in the design and development of creative and innovative data tools.

You accomplish this by:

  • Design, Architect and Develop data solutions to help product and business teams make data driven decisions
  • Interface closely with Data Scientists, Data Engineers, as well as other operational teams within the business to create cross-functional relationships that help with the understanding of company data needs.
  • Develop deep understanding of people data model – write queries and manage data transformation to enable analytics and reporting
  • Help analyze, visualize, and provide analytics on data to build reporting solutions to support various company initiatives
  • Build rich and dynamic dashboards using out-of-box features, customizations, and visualizations using Tableau or equivalent
  • Support end users on ad hoc data usage and be a subject matter expert on functional side of the business
  • Maintain the integrity, updates, and implementation of tools used to perform data analysis and modeling.

You’d be a great fit if your current track record looks like this:

  • Expertise with SQL and at least one programming language (PowerShell, Python, Perl etc.)
  • Strong foundation in Database Systems
  • Strong analytical skills with passion for data
  • Strong communication skills and willingness to take initiative to contribute beyond basic responsibilities
  • Experience with data warehousing architecture and data modeling best practices: Experience with Hadoop, Impala or Hive preferred
  • Demonstrable skills building responsive data visualizations using Tableau or equivalent
