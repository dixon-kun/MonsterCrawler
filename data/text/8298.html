Job Description

Working with the massive datasets, information, and knowledge being collected to help drive innovation and enhance user experience. • Making recommendations on the business direction to various stakeholders within the company. • Build scalable predictive models based on data sets • Managing our rapid growth by developing and executing data analyses. • Developing models and data-driven solutions that add material insight to our clients understanding of their business and their business environment • Engage with others in specifying improvements to our EPCI based analytical environment • Collaborate with others in Data Science and Analytics, and other adequately-prepared power users elsewhere in the company on Data Mining and Predictive Modeling as required • Plans, directs and maintains EA pilot or proof of concept projects. Reviews work requests and estimates scope of projects. Plans the use of project resources. Assists Project Managers in the implementation and control of project schedules. Follows up to ensure goals were met. • Pro-actively advising on opportunities to apply and the best approach to apply underlying empirically-developed algorithms. • Developing Proof of concepts and reporting methods to support the company’s business objectives.

Job Requirements

Bachelor’s degree in computer science, computer engineering or related field

Experience in Data Science & Machine Learning Methodologies • Experience in Data Analysis & Programming (SQL, Splunk, Hadoop, Spark, Java) • Experience in Statistical/Mathematical Programming (MATLAB, R, Scala, Python + Pandas) • Experience in Statistics and Mathematical Modeling • Experience in Data Visualization • Experience with graph theory, natural language processing, search theory, and anomaly detection algorithms • Experience with various types of static/dynamic languages : Java, Javascript, etc. • Experience with basic web-based rapid prototyping techniques (python/javascript/web/etc). • Deep Knowledge of both relational /No relational databases and Hadoop-based frameworks • Demonstrated ability to work on multiple projects with multiple teams • Deep understanding of statistical and predictive modeling concepts, machine-learning approaches, clustering and classification techniques and recommendation and optimization algorithms • Demonstrated ability to solve business problems and find patterns and insights with structured and unstructured data • Experience in IoT platforms
