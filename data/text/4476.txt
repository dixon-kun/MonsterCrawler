        Principal Analyst
        Liberty Mutual Group, Inc. is seeking a Principal Analyst to be responsible for collaborating with business partners to develop predictive analytic solutions that enable data-driven strategic decision-making.
        Duties include:
        * Utilize data science to manipulate data sets, identify patterns in raw data, &amp; develop models to predict the likelihood of a future outcome or optimize business solutions;
        * Execute predictive modeling &amp; other analytic initiatives to address business challenges utilizing SAS, R or equivalent;
        * Perform data preparation steps, including extraction of target data from multiple databases, integration of multiple datasets, &amp; creation of derived variables, application of business rules, &amp; quality control checks;
        * Collaborate with other functional areas &amp; actuarial teams to gather business requirements &amp; develop specifications for research projects &amp; activities including project design, data requests &amp; reports, project goals &amp; priorities, &amp; implementation strategy;
        * Research, recommend, &amp; implement new &amp;/or alternative statistical &amp; other mathematical methodologies appropriate for given model or analysis;
        * Prepare written reports and presentations regarding research methodology, project status, results &amp; recommendations to broad range of audiences;
        * Mentor junior team members/summer interns &amp; peer review/quality assurance checks work of other team members/departments;
        * Provide technical &amp; analytical assessments of issues facing the business;
        * Use information gathered to develop standard, repeatable &amp; documented processes &amp; reporting;
        * Innovate &amp; improve methodologies &amp; processes; communicate results in written, oral and presentation formats.
        Minimum requirements:
        Master's degree in Statistics, Economics, Mathematics, or related field plus 2 years' experience with predictive modeling.
        Experience must include:
        * 2 years' experience with problem solving &amp; research for business analysis issues;
        * 2 years' experience in business analysis for the insurance industry;
        * 2 years' experience with tools &amp; techniques to perform specific analyses to include statistical &amp; analytic software tools such as SAS or R;
        * 2 years' experience with large analysis datasets &amp; Enterprise-scale database systems;
        * 2 years' experience writing reports/proposals &amp; making presentations to communicate technical results to non-technical business audience.
