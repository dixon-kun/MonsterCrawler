        **CCB Risk**
        Chase Consumer &amp; Community Banking \(CCB\) serves consumers and small businesses with a broad range of financial services, including personal banking, small business banking and lending, mortgages, credit cards, payments, auto finance and investment advice\.Consumer &amp; Community Banking Risk Management partners with each CCB sub\-line of business to identify, assess, prioritize and remediate risk\.Types of risk that occur in consumer businesses include fraud, reputation, operational, credit, market and regulatory, among others
        The strategic analytics division in Mortgage Banking risk provides reporting and analytic solutions that are needed to manage risks in the business\. Solutions cover all businesses in Mortgage &#8211; Production, Servicing and the real estate portfolio\. We seek to continuously add value to the business through creative analytic insights that help to grow and de\-risk the portfolios in a responsible manner\. As we seek to make decisions based on facts, analysis and information, this team plays a key role in shaping the direction of the second largest mortgage business in the United States\.
        The position will drive strategic analytics through sophisticated decision science methodologies and create meaningful conclusions that can be actioned by risk management and the business\. In particular, the role will provide thoughtful insights to accelerate implementation of Robotics, Artificial Intelligence \(AI\) and Machine Learning \(ML\) capabilities within Mortgage Risk Management\.

        + Responsible for analytics in different areas of risk including but not limited to customer level risk exposure and growth management, systemic risk measurements, risk\-reward frameworks, capital implications on risk appetite etc\.

        + Leverage structured/unstructured data from internal and external data sources to deploy Machine Learning algorithms &amp; identify emerging risk trends\.
        + Lead the Robotic Process Automation \(RPA\) initiatives within Mortgage risk to improve productivity and efficiencies within the Mortgage Risk Operational processes\.
        + Understand the latest developments within AI/ML, develop new ideas and apply AI/ML to specific challenges within Mortgage Risk\.
