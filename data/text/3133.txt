                       Purpose
                      adidas Digital Sports is creating an interactive community on a foundation of data science, sport science and behavior science. At the core of this community is our strategic purpose  to enable a healthier global population.  The data we collect from the community will help us better understand our consumers, enhance their experience with the brand and keep them engaged in a healthy lifestyle.   We have identified a key set of KPIs that reflect the goals we have for this digital experience.
                       Key Responsibilities
                      Provide analytics and business intelligence for Business and Marketing KPIs.
                      Create and maintain a dashboard that the business will use to monitor performance vs. our objectives.
                      Support CRM needs with tracking, reporting and analysis of email, push and in-app notifications and blog.
                      Use analytics to identify new metrics that define success for the app.
                      Understand how our users interact inside and outside of the app.  Highlight insights and recommendations along with supporting data.
                      Identify changes in metrics and root causes for change.
                      Maintain an analytics toolbox (both internal and external tools), enabling the organization to work in a data driven process that takes action based on timely insights.
                      Assist with developing and measuring marketing stimuli (A/B and multi-variant) test results in collaboration with marketing and CRM to build robust strategies.
                      Provide a view of forecasted growth based on the marketing plan.
                      Work with analytics to evaluate impact of product, marketing and CRM on KPIs.
                      Design, specify and build custom reports and dashboards as needed.
                       Key Relationships
                      This role reports to the Product Owner.
                      The Analytics Manager will work with the CRM lead, Marketing and Product teams to provide the data needed for prioritization and optimization of tactics and programs.
                      Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
                      Strong understanding of marketing and CRM.
                      Strong analytical skills, including data querying, dash-boarding, and experimental design are required.
                      Data visualization skills required (e.g. Tableau, Looker).
                      Strong presentation skills (e.g. PowerPoint) required.
                      Working knowledge of 3rd party app analytics tools like Amplitude, Google Analytics, Flurry, MixPanel, etc.  bonus points
                       Qualifications
                      University degree from an accredited institution with a relevant quantitative or analytical degree.
                      5 years of experience in marketing analytics or business intelligence (focused in analytics) with a proven track record of delivering performance results required.
