The Data Visualization Engineer is usually considered the “face” for any data-driven project, especially as visualizations become one of the primary forms of data consumption and analysis. The Data Visualization Engineer is responsible for developing aesthetically pleasing, complex visualizations that are intuitive to end users and shows them data in ways they hadn’t considered before to drive new insights.

In addition you will :


  • Design aesthetically appealing and informative visualizations through collection of business requirements and user stories for internal or external stakeholders
  • Create standard and custom visualizations that meet customer demands while ensuring data validity, statistical integrity, and maximizing user experience
  • Work with Engineering team to determine sources of needed data
  • Work with Analytics team to incorporate advanced statistical and data science models directly into data visualizations
  • Demonstrate visualizations directly with business partners for feedback and quick iterations using a mix of tools such as Tibco Spotfire and Tableau
  • Develop data visualization strategies, standards, and best practices
  • Develop analytics code and data transformation scripts using tools to reveal hidden insights, trends, and opportunities across seemingly unrelated datasets

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science / Engineering, IT, or Data Analytics from an accredited university or college
  • Minimum of 2 years of Data Visualization experience in a modern Data Viz tool such as Spotfire, Tableau, Qlik Sense, etc.
  • Minimum of 2 years of experience writing complex SQL statements
  • Minimum of one year of experience with at least one scripting or data language (Python, JavaScript, R, etc.)
