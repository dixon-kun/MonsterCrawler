        You will be asked to show leadership and think creatively. You will be asked to collaborate with data science and information technology teams to strategize future product solutions. You will be asked to oversee others in complex analyses. You will be asked to translate client needs or requests into enhancements of existing products and to make fact-based, data-driven decisions. You will be an integral part of a talented and dedicated development team. Are you ready for some new challenges?
        Primary Responsibilities:
        * Provide pharmacy expertise, content, and support for the development of new, or enhancement of, existing healthcare analytic products for internal and external clients
        * Provide pharmacy expertise for consulting projects
        * Direct or guide clinical colleagues who support a variety of healthcare analytic solutions
        * Drive innovation for analytic solutions within the pharmacy area
        * Lead small projects from idea to solution
        * Work with the clinical informatics team to facilitate the incorporation of clinical data sources into analytical products
        * Serve as a Optum subject matter expert at conferences and sales meetings
        * Oversee or participate in analytic product testing and validation activities
        * Utilizing pharma and analytic knowledge &amp; experience to perform research, informatics, or product development related to the pharmaceutical sector (e.g., pharmacy benefits management, operations, academic research, quality measurement, etc.)
        * The successful individual would show demonstrated ability to be self-directed and work with minimal guidance
        * Utilize analysis skills to solve complex problems and/or conduct complex analyses
        * Demonstrated ability in written and verbal communication and presentation skills
        * Utilize your strong interpersonal skills to work cohesively in a team environment
        * Demonstrate strong organizational skills (e.g., prioritize and manage multiple tasks, meet deadlines; complete complex long-term projects)
        Required Qualifications:
        * PharmD or BS Pharm degree with an active, unrestricted license Masters degree or above in business, pharmacy, informatics, health services research or related fields
        * Experience as a practicing pharmacist in the United States
        * 3 years working with pharmacy clinical and/or claims data
        * 3 year experience utilizing industry-standard data analysis tools such as: (ACCESS, SQL, SAS, R, EXCEL, and such)
        * Required location: Twin Cities, MN or Boston, MA
        Preferred Qualifications:
        * Experience leading or managing a project
        * Have formal informatics training
        * Possess recent knowledge and/or experience in quality measures, e.g., STARS, HEDIS, State measures
        * Published in peer-review journals
        * Possess a certification through the Board of Pharmacy Specialties
        * Experience working within a managed care environment from either a payer or provider perspective
        * Experience integrating clinical data from electronic medical records or health records into optimizing health care solutions, e.g., clinical decision support systems, care management programs
        * Experience using medical, drug, and lab test taxonomies (i.e. RxNorm, NDC, CPT, LOINC, HCPCS, ICD-9/10)
