Job Summary:

Who are we?

Ticketmaster, a proud division of Live Nation Entertainment, is the world's leading live event high tech ticketing company selling nearly 455 million tickets each year, supporting over 145,000 events and more than 12,000 clients in 21 countries worldwide.  At Ticketmaster, we power the people who create culture through live events, connecting more passionate fans to those experiences than anyone else.  We are innovators that embrace change and work hard to help others succeed.  We believe in open communication and stress accountability, because fans and clients count on us every day.  We strive for ease of use for our clients and fans while seeking out those who have a bias for action versus a desire for directives.

Why Ticketmaster?

A career at Ticketmaster will challenge and engage you. The pace here is fast, the atmosphere is fun and a passion for live events is a common thread that ties us together. As a global and growing business, we can truly offer a world of opportunities to expand your skills and develop your career.  Visit any of our offices and you'll find a diverse mix of passionate employees, helping fans around the globe connect with the artists, teams and events they love.  It truly is a unique and rewarding environment.  See and hear more about why you should consider joining our rock-star team: recruiting video

The Role:

Are you an individual with a strong curiosity about how data can improve the live entertainment industry and ticket purchase experience? Data Sciences connects data to the products and the marketing that enables fans to discover the live events they crave and remember. You will focus on improving Ticketmaster's data infrastructure and governance.  Your work will increase the value and the quality of the data used to drive the Ticketmaster business and that of Ticketmaster’s clients.

The Product Manager, Data, will own, develop and maintain data governance across the entire Ticketmaster ecosystem. You will be responsible for the digital data layer that is required to run our business as well as overseeing its implementation. You will create standards across our various ticketing channels, while keeping pace with new features that are continuously being introduced to improve our fan experience.

The perfect candidate has a deep understanding of web based analytics and online marketing. This candidate takes nothing for granted, always challenging new models or technologies to ensure they are sound. The perfect candidate is as obsessed with how the data is collected as he or she is with the data itself.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop clear and compelling product requirements / user stories for the data layer and data governance
  • Work with product managers and business units to determine data requirements, tracking needs and specifications for all products bringing a data driven mindset to all new features.
  • Collaborate with cross-departmental teams (Product, Media, SEO, Ad Ops, Marketing, and Technology) to develop a cohesive and comprehensive understanding of the key drivers of the business and use that knowledge to shape and drive the data roadmap.
  • Plan/manage product roll-out and assist with the day-to-day operational support post roll-out.
  • Work closely with data scientists, engineers, analysts, third-party partners and internal QA teams in multiple geographic locations to insure quality, accessibility, and consistency of data.
  • Work with clients to implement marketing attribution and analytics tracking
  • Evangelize the value of data and help drive a data-informed culture. Data presentation is everything, and the person in this role should have a point of view on how to make our data intelligible and important to the rest of the company.

Qualifications:

Work Experience:

  • Ideal candidate will have a minimum of 3+ years product management experience with marketing channels, platforms and analysis
  • Proficient in requirements definition, authoring epics, user stories and acceptance criteria within the Agile/Scrum methodology.
  • Familiar with lean product management principles.
  • Prior experience in eCommerce a plus.
  • Understanding of the ticketing and/or live event industry a plus.

Management and Communication:

  • Must have excellent communication skills, including executive level communication and presentation.
  • Must be capable of managing multiple projects simultaneously, and handle shifting priorities.
  • Must be solution-oriented.
  • Must be team-oriented and a true team player.
  • Must be able to work independently and adapt to changes in a rapidly evolving industry.
  • Maintain a focus on quality.
  • Ability to maintain a broad and big-picture view, and weigh varied/multiple factors when making product decisions.

Technical Skills:

  • Education or work experience programming a plus.
  • Experience with tag management solutions and tracking pixels a plus

Education:

  • Bachelor’s degree, MBA or CS Degree is a plus but not required.
