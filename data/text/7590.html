About the Job

The TIBCO Spotfire Data Science team is offering a unique opportunity to be part of one of fastest growing groups in TIBCO responsible for creating products driven by advanced computational and visual analytics. This is a software test engineer position with an opportunity to contribute to cutting edge technology and work alongside top talent in the areas of computational statistics, business intelligence and data visualization.  If you are passionate about software quality, detail oriented, looking to work with top notch developers and testers and have excellent programming skills, we would like to talk to you.

  • Perform manual, ad-hoc and automated testing as part of various product releases
  • Maintain and enhance various internal tools  for automated testing and deployment
  • Work closely with developers to investigate and report bugs in products
  • Work closely with the documentation and support teams as part of product releases
  • Maintain a strong working relationship with developers, technical leads and project managers
  • Work with teams that are spread out geographically
