              Position Summary
             : Sevatec, Inc., a high-technology services firm specializing in Agile, data sciences, cyber engineering, and cloud solutions, has an immediate need for a multitalented, hands-on program manager to lead a collection of client-facing and internal projects, ensuring each team&#8217;s operational performance achieves all stakeholders&#8217; expectations.&#160; This position is located at our Fairfax, Virginia headquarters and reports to the Vice President of Federal Civilian Programs.
              Responsibilities:
              Lead by example and foster the company&#8217;s &#8220;Inspired to Serve&#8221; culture across all customer and company engagements.
              Manage multiple contracts, prioritizing efforts as required, whereas successful contract and subcontract performance achieves high-quality deliverables and customer satisfaction.
              Develop and maintain strong relationship through regular interactions with client personnel.
              Work collaboratively with the clients to assess needs, assists in resolving problems, provide appropriately skilled resources and implement existing technology and/or new functionality.
              Lead project staff in the day to day operations to outline work plans, duties, responsibilities and evaluate progress in achieving program objectives.
              Provide project and financial management oversight inclusive of developing or reviewing internal and external budgets, schedules and status reports.
              Provide guidance to less experienced staff and feedback on project personnel performance.
              Identify and lead continuous improvement initiatives within the company. Encourage and facilitate cross-functional cooperation and promote collaboration between organizational teams to achieve project goals and deliverables.
              Proactively prepare to transition recently bid proposals to contract performance upon award.
              Identify and communicate opportunities to provide new value-added services to better achieve the client&#8217;s mission.
              Support proposals, as required.
              Requirements:
              Proven track-record of leading technical IT teams
              Bachelor&#8217;s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Business or related fields.
              Minimum of 8 years of related experience.
              Project Management Professional (PMP) certified.
              A demonstrated ability to work effectively with multiple stakeholders in a government contracting environment and manage competing priorities and deadlines.
              Experience developing strategic plans to help execute customer mission requirements innovatively while managing risk cost-effectively.
              Self-starting, collaborative and involved team player with a demonstrated sense of urgency to responsibly and decisively solve problems.
              Excellent written and verbal communication skills, comfortable with developing and delivering presentations to customers and senior leaders.
              Desired Skills and Experience:
              Database management, COOP/FCD2, and critical infrastructure experience considered a plus.
              Experience with Agile methodologies and process improvements initiatives considered favorable.
              Recent experience with HHS or a joint venture considered a plus.
              Security Clearance:
             Must be a US Citizen with an active Top Secret clearance.
