Job Description - Software Sales Consultant, Data Sciences Solutions - Midwest region (17713) Experian is an Equal Opportunity Employer Software Sales Consultant, Data Sciences Solutions - Midwest region - Job Number: 17713 Description Experian is the leading global information services company, providing data and analytical tools to clients in more than 65 countries around the world. The company helps organizations to manage credit risk, prevent fraud, provide data security and automate decision making. Experian Decision Analytics integrates predictive data and analytics into valuable business decisions that provide greater insight into decision performance and helps companies keep pace with changing business priorities. By applying expert consulting, analytical tools, software and systems to convert data into valuable business decisions, we help businesses Manage Credit Risk, Detect and Prevent Fraud, Predict Behavior, Automate Decisioning, Enhance Credit Management Strategies, Maintain Regulatory Compliance. Our expertise spans a variety of industries: including banking, utilities, telecommunications, mortgage, leasing, insurance, automotive, mortgage, retail finance, public sector, mail order, small business and unsecured lending. We are looking for the best of the best to join a high performing team. The Decision Analytics Sales Specialist is charged with generating new software, analytics, and consulting sales within North America in rapidly growing markets including Banking, Financial Services, Alternative Lending and/or Automotive Finance. You will collaborate with sales leadership to develop a strategic territory plan and develop a robust pipeline that ensures you achieve (or exceed) annual targets. Working effectively with Senior business, product, consulting and IT talent within Experian and with clients adeptly will be critical to your success. With responsibility for lead generation independently and with Product Marketing, you will develop relationships with senior leaders and key stakeholders in our client organizations. Through thought leadership, formal sales presentations, compelling solution design and proposal, you will lead the effort to negotiate contracts and commitment which provide market-differentiating value for our clients. The primary responsibility is to sell Experian’s Decision Analytics capabilities to midsize through large regional banks and financial institutions. The ideal candidate will possess a successful track record for selling financial service products; have good negotiation and communication skills. In this role, there will be time spent selling into existing customers. This role requires the ability to build strong professional relationships. This position will be viewed not only as a salesperson, but also as a valued consultant helping customers expand their business opportunities utilizing Experian products while creating opportunities for selling additional products and services. In addition, they will spend time developing new opportunities within the targeted accounts. It is important that the candidate be a self-starter, have superior people skills and be very goal and results driven with the ability to work independently. This is a remote position based in the Midwest region of the United States. Qualifications Bachelor’s degree in general business, marketing or related major 8+ years of successful Software selling experience across vertical markets. Specific experience selling large complex analytical products and decisioning software in the financial services industry is highly preferred. Previous experience with top enterprise business software providers (i.e. SAS, Oracle, SAP, IBM) a plus. Having sold into large financial institutions is desirable, along with experience in non-bank financial (both unsecured and secured lending etc). Experience with alternate vertical markets (i.e. automotive, alternative financing, health, insurance, public sector, telecom) also considered. Understanding of customer decisioning technology and scoring applications is a must Ability to establish consultative business relationships with existing and new customers at a senior executive / C-level is crucial for ongoing success Must have complex strategic sales experience Excellent written and verbal communication skills with the ability to successfully relate to people at all levels Candidate must be available for business travel Benefits: Working for leading FTSE 50 global information Services Company is just one of the rewards of joining Experian. Our benefits package is designed to reward contribution and loyalty and to attract the kind of talented individuals who have their pick of employers. That is why we offer a highly competitive package which comprises: Competitive base salary Aggressive bonus plan Core benefits including: full medical, dental, vision, matching 401K and the opportunity to work with a global leader. Experian is listed on the London Stock Exchange (EXPN) and is a constituent of the FTSE 100 Index. Total revenue for the year ended March 31, 2016 was $4.6 billion. Experian employs approximately 17,000 people in over 40 countries. Experian is an Equal Opportunity Employer.
