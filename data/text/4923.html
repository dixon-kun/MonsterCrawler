Description

Position Title: Director,  Data Science
Location: Atlanta, GA


Reports to: Global Supply ChainTransformation Lead

The Director - Data Scientist will play an integralrole in helping the company achieve its ambition by ensuring that Newell Brandsis leveraging scientific methods and analytics to drive Supply ChainImprovements.  Reporting to the SupplyChain Transformation Office, the role will be highly visible with impact acrossall Newell Divisions.

Specific Responsibilities
  • Develop strategy and business requirements for aSupply Chain data repository to consolidate multiple ERP data sources into acomprehensive source for analytics
  • Partner with the IT organization on the developmentand implementation of algorithms required to extract and cleanse data into theSupply Chain data repository.
  • Develop and implement diagnostic tools for use bybusiness users to identify performance oppourtunities and root cause for actionplanning
  • Developand implement forecast algorithms leveraging time series and machine learningmethods to improve forecast accuracy for Demand Planning and FinancialPlanning.
  • Developand implement optimization programs to improve planning outputs formanufacturing and distribution opertations
  • Manageteam of data scientists and analyst to achieve key deliverables



Qualfications

  • MS in Statistics, Operations Research, or Engineering.  PhD preffered.
  • 10+ years proven experience successfully developing and deploying optimization programs in a business environment
  • Ability to analyze and assess organizational needs and provide solutions.
  • 5+ years proven Supply Chain Planning experience.
  • 5+ years proven experience managing a team of data science or operations research professionals
  • Expert level  SQL skills with demonstrated experience with large data sets.
  • Expert level statistical forecasting skills in a practical environment.
  • Advanced experience in open source programming languages (“R”)
  • Intermediate level experience is demand forecasting packages like APO, JDA, and SAS.
  • Advanced experince with data visualization tools like Tableau, QlikView, Qlik Sense, etc..
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills.
  • Interpersonally savvy.
  • Ability to motivate teams to produce desired outcomes within tight timeframes.
  • Ability to work with and connect with all levels of employees. Proven experience working in a matrix environment
  • Strong planning & organizing skills.
  • Initiative & follow-up, including a strong sense of autonomy.
  • Strong Character – ability to champion the right decision in difficult situations
  • Demonstrated ability to handle multiple tasks and assignments simultaneously.
