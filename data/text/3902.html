South San Francisco, California

The Position

Real World Data Science - Advanced Analytics InternLocation: South San Francisco
Genentech is seeking an Intern pursuing a PhD with expertise in machine learning to help us explore its potential applications in healthcare. The successful candidate will join our Global Real World Data Science (RWDS) team and work on an innovative project looking at applying machine learning approaches to the company’s real world (e.g. EHR, insurance claims), genomic and clinical trial data assets spanning multiple disease areas.
Responsibilities:

  • To collaborate with the RWDS team and other stakeholders to evaluate potential machine learning techniques and applications
  • To design, build and interpret machine learning algorithms to address selected research questions (including preparing the input data)
  • To proactively share learnings and knowledge to support the development of the wider RWDS team
  • To help shape the direction of machine learning and artificial intelligence for the RWDS team
