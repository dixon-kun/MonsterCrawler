        Basic Function
        As a recognized geoscience expert, candidate will undertake specific assignments that can only be handled satisfactorily by one possessing a high level of technical ability, specialized expertise or extensive background.
        Duties &amp; Responsibilities
        * Perform advanced statistical analyses of geoscience and engineering data to obtain insights into trends and opportunities
        * Create algorithms for processing and interpreting reservoir and well data
        * Develop frameworks for fast analyses of large data files compromising geoscience and petroleum engineering data
        * Provide consultation to Aramco Services Company (ASC) Upstream personnel on advanced analytics as it applies to subsurface data
        * Serve as consultant to management on data science that require an unusually high degree of skill and experience for their solution
        * Act as a technical advisor to assigned professionals and researchers as is necessary to satisfactorily perform work assignments
        * Prepare reports and oral presentations of the results of studies for review by management
        * Assist in training the less experienced professionals through discussions and work sessions
        * Attend meetings and conferences that contribute significantly to advancing the individual's expertise
        * Perform other related duties as assigned
        Education and Experience
        * Bachelor's degree required in Geophysics or related field (e.g., Geology, Physics, Mathematics, Electrical Engineering or other science and engineering fields); graduate study or extensive formal training in specialty field is desirable
        * Normally expected to have twelve (12) years of professional experience, at least five (5) years of which should be as a geophysicist or petrophysicist; candidate must be highly regarded and accepted as an expert within the Company
        * Knowledge of Big Data Analytics, Multivariate Statistics, Numerical Analysis, and Machine Learning Techniques is strongly desired
        * Experience in software and programming tools for data-mining and advanced analytics strongly preferred
        * Must be able to communicate and comprehend both orally and in writing, accurately, clearly and concisely in English at the high level required to perform the job as outlined
        * Must possess good work habits, a strong work ethic, and be able to adhere to company work hours, policies, and standard business etiquette
