DRINKS, a stealth-mode VC-backed startup, is a top-five US Direct to Consumer (DTC) marketing technology company that manages wine brands. The Company is led by an entrepreneurial team of marketing technology, data science, DTC, and M&A experts with several successful exits. DRINKS operates in a compelling market: a $33B industry virtually untouched by the Internet, though that is now changing quickly.

To help us build the next generation of online and mobile wine stores that will be used by tens of millions of people, we’re on a relentless hunt for a superb QA Engineer who shares our passion for building cutting-edge experiences to transform how people buy wine.

The Role:

QA Engineer should be a motivated and passionate person with a high attention to detail who has a professional and methodical approach to software development and testing.

You will be working within the QA / Product team and giving qualitative feedback on platform functionalities and design. In addition, you will be involved in the project lifecycle from the concept stages to final release, where your input will help shape and influence the software that we develop.

As an QA Engineer, you will be responsible for creating, maintaining and executing manual tests, as well as providing constructive feedback throughout the development lifecycle – technical knowledge, confidence and excellent communication skills are a plus.

Responsibilities

●      Design, develop and execute functional test cases that ensure the software meets the intended requirements and quality standards.

●      Work closely with the product team to understand product specifics and to create a corresponding test plan, quality standards and strategies for the project.

●      Participate in improving test processes, propose new ideas on how to improve customer satisfaction and quality of product.

●      Document and classify defects in a defect-tracking tool.

●      Assist the operations and customer service teams in their daily activities.

●      Ensure that all best QA practices and enterprise standards are followed.

●      Assist in cross browser and mobile testing.

About You:

●      1-3 years experience in QA or engineering in a software development company.

●      Experience in both functional/black-box testing and white/grey-box testing.

●      Knowledge of HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

●      Understanding of database concepts and SQL.

●      Capable multi-tasker.

Bonus:

●      Programming experience.

●      Experience with automation (Selenium, etc).

Personal Qualities

●      Very strong verbal/written communication skills & excellent attention to detail

●      Energetic, enthusiastic and self-motivated with a strong work ethic

●      Excellent judgment skills and team working abilitiesA proactive and driven attitude towards QA and ability to work under pressure

●      Demonstrable ability to self-manage and be organized.

●      Confidence to manage the needs and demands of multiple development projects simultaneously

●      Highly adaptable and capable of working in a high pressure environment
