Principal Data Scientist

Averity - Philadelphia, PA 19019

Posted: 2/25/2017

We are hiring a Principal Data Scientist to join one of the country's largest media companies that is putting a large focus and investment in data science.

What is the Job?

As a Principal Data Scientist, you will be part of our Data Science team and will be responsible for providing our internal stakeholders analytical insights into various areas of our company (marketing analytics, finance, HR, Product, etc).

You will analyze, manipulate and interpret massive amounts of data from various sources and use your big data (Spark, Python, R, Scala, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence) and analytic skills to help provide action and value to our company's most important business decisions.

What Skills Do You Need?

• Applied Experience with Big Data stack

• Experience with predictive modeling

• Proficiency with SQL

• Interesting in working with internal stakeholders and communication with others

Compensation

• $155,000- $160,000

• Mandatory Bonus

• 401K

• Full Benefits

Who Are We?

We are a Fortune 500 company and the largest global telecommunications conglomerate in the world.

What's In It For You?

This is an opportunity for an experienced Data Scientist to use your background and experience in the Big Data space to impact a Fortune 50's most important business decisions.
