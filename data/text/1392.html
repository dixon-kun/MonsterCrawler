Aisle Rocket Studios is looking for a Senior Marketing Director to lead an initiative comprising the post-purchase experience of a major high-end brand. You will lead and manage the complete lifecycle of the program from conception to launch and beyond. You will manage the relationship with the client and lead the program's business objectives with support from data science, content strategy and technology teams. This employee will work closely with both our Palatine and St. Joseph MI offices.

Responsibilities:

* Create a nationwide B2B network of professional service providers to serve the customer

* Coordinate with the Director of Product Management to deploy a technology- platform in the market

* Manage relationships with the clients and vendors

* Lead the marketing research initiative

* Mastermind the strategy and execution plan to launch and scale the program

Qualifications:

* Degree in Marketing, preferably an MBA in Marketing Management

* Proven experience in market creation and customer acquisition

* Proven experience with behavioral customer segmentation

* Experience with luxury brands and/or high-end customer segments

* Experience with marketing research methods and tools

* Familiarity with data–driven marketing

* Loyalty marketing / gamified loyalty systems is a nice to have

* SaaS / Startup marketing experience is nice to have

Company Description

At Aisle Rocket Studios (ARS), be part of a team that builds highly persuasive experiences, in-store, online and beyond. We're driven by a diverse group of talent who have a passion for customer experience. Here, game-changing marketing ideas are brought to life. Award-winning integrated multichannel campaigns are created and new ways to optimize the shopping experience are generated.

* Easy Train commute – Located across the street from the Palatine (IL) Metra Train Station

What We Bring to the Table

* Creative lounge and game/rec rooms

* Social events and impromptu happy hours

* Small-agency feel with big-agency clients

* Dry cleaner, restaurants and more within walking distance

* Flexible work schedules (subject to manager's approval)
