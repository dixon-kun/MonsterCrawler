Remarketable is a revolutionary product in the MarTech space, connecting businesses to their prospective customers in previously impossible ways. As we prepare to launch it in 2017, we need a leadership team with expertise across a range of disciplines who are experienced enough to think fast, act faster, react nimbly and collaborate closely to achieve critical goals. Key among those roles is the Senior Director of Product Design and Creative Services.

We are currently seeking a Senior Director, Product Design and Creative Services to join our Philadelphia, PA team of professionals.

Reporting directly to the Senior Vice President of Marketing, and indirectly to the Chief Product Officer, the Senior Director of Product Design and Creative Services helps drive both the product experience and the ways in which the product is marketed and sold, always with an eye toward the product’s growth, profitability and value. The primary mission of this role is to design and iterate upon the Remarketable product, as well as create and evolve the brand expression of Remarketable, in order to establish it as the world’s leading multichannel retargeting platform.

We want to be your Employer of Choice!

ALC Digital offers excellent benefits including medical, dental, vision, life, LTD, 401k w/ company match, paid time off, business casual dress, summer hours, customized training & more!  If you are one of the “best and brightest” in your field and you’re looking to position yourself for the future, this is a great opportunity.

For immediate consideration, please create a profile and make sure you provide your resume, salary requirements, and current contact information.  We look forward to learning more about you and the value you can bring to ALC Digital!

EOE/M/F/V


In addition, our "ideal candidate" has the following skills & experience:

  • You have a Bachelor’s or Master's degree from a reputable design school, and have a passion for design;
  • Your experience is strong with at least 5+ years in a Design Leadership role;
  • You know what it takes to be a successful designer due to your prior design experience and you have the DRIVE and leadership abilities to inspire others to execute your vision;
  • Your presentation skills, and a recognition for the power of great storytelling is unmatched and you can easily present at the Executive level;
  • You are comfortable managing others and being hands on when needed;
  • You are agile enough to stay cool in a fast changing environment;
  • You will bring lots of ideas to the table and are highly collaborative in helping evolve your ideas and everyone else’s;
  • You have the vision and the temerity to truly own a product, and evolve it.

Responsibilities for this role include, but are not limited to :

  • Help to ensure that Remarketable’s messaging effectively communicates our unique advantages and points of differentiation;
  • Establish a brand identity for Remarketable that sets it apart from its competition, is extensible and comprehensive, modern and contemporary, and reflective of design best practices across a wide range of mediums;
  • Apply the Remarketable brand to a variety of marketing and sales materials, and creating templates and patterns that speed the creation of new materials;
  • Apply the Remarketable brand to our website;
    • Collaborating on the structure of the website
    • Collaborating on the features and functionality of the website;
  • Retool and ongoing maintenance of Remarketable design Sharepoint, to become the go-to portal for sales, marketing, events, and other activities that require design assets;
  • Design of the Remarketable product in close partnership with our Chief Product Officer and Data Science team, to ensure that it reflects best-in-class data visualization and functionality;
    • Collaborate on strategic vision for future feature enhancements;
  • Design of the Remarketable client portal;
    • Research with key clients to ensure thorough knowledge of key client tasks and requirements;
    • Creation of ongoing customer research program and client design research panel to gain continued learnings and feedback, and to test new features and ideas;
    • Ongoing testing and optimization to ensure product continues to evolve with client needs and product enhancements;
  • Concept and design for the Remarketable book;
  • Design of trade show and event materials including booth design, swag/giveaways, and sales materials;
  • Concept and design for Remarketable video library to support sales efforts and for usage at events;
  • Consult with clients and outside vendors as needed to ensure quality control of creative produced as part of Remarketable campaigns;
  • Grow and manage a team of designers to assist with campaign creation and other creative services needs on behalf of our clients .
