   Director of Content Management - SAE International | Jobs.com
        SUMMARY:
        The Director of Content Management (DCM) will excel at putting SAE content in the service of making engineers, students and faculty, and the people who serve them, more successful at work, in school and in life.
        The DCM will do this by employing strategies, tactics and technology that render all SAE content, as well as content from other sources, authoritative, discoverable, and deliverable according to users&#8217; needs and preferences.
        The DCM will work collaboratively, across the organization, to source, review, describe, implement and analyze content. This includes, among other things, enabling aggregated technical content to be interrogated as data, and individual documents to be published many times in multiple formats.
        Analysis of how users access and utilize content to drive new products and marketing, discovery and curation of third-party content are also key responsibilities. This person will also maintain a state-of-the-art content repository that enables effective, efficient, and optimized re-use of content for products and the market.
        The successful candidate will have proven success building and leading excellent teams to pursue these goals. The DCM will understand content development and production processes, possess the ability to define and drive technologies that support SAE&#8217;s revenue growth, and work collaboratively in a matrixed organization.
        PRIMARY RESPONSIBILTIES AND DUTIES:
        * Has overall accountability for identifying and implementing content strategies, procedures and technologies that support SAE&#8217;s goals across the organization, with particular emphasis on growth measured in usage and revenue.
        * Creates and executes a strategy for sourcing, reviewing, describing, implementing and analyzing proprietary and third-party content with business owners.
        * In collaboration with Legal establishes best practices for revenue-generating content licensing and subsidiary rights granting. Oversee permissions.
        * Responsible for delivery of standards, technical publications, book and journal content in products and for events, on time and within budget.
        * Evangelize the content available with product and marketing teams to ensure efficient use and leveraging of content assets.
        EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:
        * Bachelor&#8217;s degree.
        * 10+ years of experience in a leadership role in content management; product management or content strategy also applicable.
        * MBA or other related advanced degree in information science, product management, or marketing
        * Demonstrated experience with content technologies including content analytics, machine-aided indexing, new learning technologies, information architecture, data science.
        SPECIALIZED KNOWLEDGE AND SKILLS:
        * Excellent leadership and organization capabilities.
        * Demonstrated ability to collaborate, build and strengthen working relationships, and gain alignment across business units and functional groups.
        * Demonstrated success with managing logistics of complex initiatives with multiple stakeholders.
        * Demonstrated writing and verbal presentation skills enabling her/him to prepare and present complex information and recommendations to a diverse audience.
