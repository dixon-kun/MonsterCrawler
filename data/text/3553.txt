        Position Summary
        We are looking for a passionate, experienced data scientist professional to join the customer relationship management (CRM) team @WalmartLabs. You will have the unique opportunity to work with massive business and social data generated by WalMart's unparalleled online and offline commerce. You will be responsible to use cutting-edge machine learning, data mining, and optimization techniques to design and prototype robust and scalable predictive models. You will be a part of a cross-functional team of scientists, engineers, and business professionals that sit at the intersection of data science, IT infrastructure, and business operations.
        Responsibilities
        * Perform hands-on data exploration and modeling work on massive data sets.
        * Use machine learning and data mining techniques to develop robust models in areas such as customer loyalty prediction, segmentation and profiling, retention analysis, churn assessment, and product recommendation.
        * Pull data from warehouse, perform feature engineering, back-test models, compare model performances and communicate the results.
        * Architect, develop, and maintain data analytics platforms that visualize data and drive business insights that can be translated into product strategies and marketing decisions.
        * Spend time researching state of the art modeling techniques and implement them.

Staff Data Scientists are responsible for building large data sets from multiple sources in order to build algorithms for predicting future data characteristics.
