The Business Analytics team at CA Technologies is an enterprise resource within Information Technology, tasked with promoting growth through advanced analytics. It supports executive management with business insights covering CA's operations and customers, as well as the broader enterprise software market. Business Analytics also provides internal consulting to analytical groups in other parts of the company, in both client-facing (sales, marketing, customer success) and internal (finance, human resources) functions. The team implements projects by utilizing CA's global resources in data science, business intelligence, big data operations, and master data management. IT leadership measures the success of the Business Analytics function in terms of enterprise adoption, durable business value, and business growth.

The Sr Principal Business Analyst, Data Analytics will be a cross-functional leader, interfacing with senior management, internal business clients, and technical resources globally. The central focus of the role is to generate, develop, and deliver analytical insights and projects using data and advanced methods to enable growth for CA Technologies. The successful candidate must be able to function autonomously in a fast-moving, fluid competitive environment, and to manage multiple priorities and ambiguous directives with incomplete information. As a business/technical ambassador, it is essential that the Director be able to view business challenges from multiple functional perspectives and be able to communicate effectively with specialist leaders across the enterprise.

Responsibilities:

* Identify opportunities to enhance growth using data and advanced analytics. Develop well-rounded business proposals and guide them through the internal approval and budget process.

* Provide strategic direction to the Business Analysis project management team. Structure and sequence project milestones to promote flexibility, cumulative business gains, and lasting value while managing risk and planning for future needs.

* Translate business challenges into analytical actions plans, incorporating solution and data architecture, project outlines, and process and tool recommendations. Apply engineering disciplines appropriate to the problem domain to enhance the reliability, scalability, and cost-effectiveness of project outcomes.

* Work with IT resources to develop proof of concept for data-driven products, services, and feature enhancements.

* Drive end-to-end project success by applying critical thinking to business problems up front, properly structuring and expediting workflows mid-stream, and ensuring excellence in delivery and ongoing support.

* Create systems and processes to monitor the long-term effectiveness and business value of delivered solutions.

* Integrate the global data science team into CA's mainstream business decision processes. Provide business context to the team, and advocate on its behalf in business discussions.

* Act as a networking and research resource, bringing outside experts, data sources, technology, benchmarks and best practices into CA.

* Mentor staff inside and outside of the Business Analysis team to improve the overall level of analytics literacy in the organization.

Qualifications:

* Masters degree in Computer Science, Statistics, or Data Analysis.

* MBA with combined experience of at least 15 years in at least three of the following business functions: Marketing, Sales, Client Service, IT, Finance, Operations, R&D.

* Consulting skills and experience managing internal and external clients.

* Executive presence, with the ability to clearly and intuitively state the business case for complex, technical projects to non-technical audiences.

* Excellent presentation, advocacy, and written communication skills.

* Experience with senior management decision processes, including strategic planning, staff planning, budgeting, financial metrics and business reviews.

* Proven track record of developing, delivering, operationalizing and supporting analytical projects.

* Experience in vendor selection and management.

* Cross-border experience in global, highly matrixed organizations.
