           Job Purposes
         As an integral part of the Enterprise Supply Chain framework, ESC Analytics is responsible for reporting and analysis on the end-to-end supply chain operations within Citigroup on a global basis.&#160; Within that the Data Sciences Team works with advanced technologies to develop predictive capabilities to support the ESC Functions, and various Citi Businesses that use third parties in their supply chains.
         This role will focus on data exploration, extraction, analysis and presentation to support ESC&#8217;s strategic mission. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in data modeling, coding (SQL, DAX, Power Query), and experience in complex problem solving. Additionally, have a passion for data analysis, discovery of next-gen tools and thrive in a highly complex environment.
           Primary Responsibilities
          Business Analysis:&#160; Interface with ESC business units to analyze business processes and document BI analytics requirements for rapid build of data visualizations based on global standards.
          Data Modeling:&#160; Design and build efficient, flexible and sustainable data models within Power Pivot (or other BI tool) based on analytic requirements to support decision making within the organization.
          Data Analysis:&#160; Query structured and unstructured data and perform complex data transformation using ETL tools (Power Query). Understand strengths and weakness of the data and have the ability to operate in the &#8220;grey&#8221; and make logically sound decisions to support data modeling.
          Statistical Analysis:&#160; Using statistical analysis, design algorithms that can be used as the basis of predictive trends and outlier identification.
          Visualizations: Develop comprehensive visualizations that supports and grow a data culture within the organization. Bring to the forefront process and expense opportunities to assist with strategic conversations through compelling visualizations.
          Analysis: Provide analytic insights through financial and supply chain experience.
