Axway Syncplicity is the leading hybrid enterprise-grade file sync-and-share (EFSS) solution on the market and is redefining how organizations provide digital workplace transformation that their users love, but that IT trusts. We give users access to all their files anytime, anywhere and from any device, from both inside and outside the corporate network, with the peace of mind that all the documents they have invested time and energy into are backed up and continuously accessible. With a rich and secure desktop, mobile and web experience, our customers can easily and securely share documents internally and externally, while IT departments maintain the data governance security controls and storage location choice options they need.

We are seeking a Product Manager to guide the development effort of our EFSS (Syncplicity) Desktop Clients by conveying the business vision to the engineering team, outlining the scope and prioritization of work, and assuming accountability for successful market acceptance.

As the Axway Senior Product Manager responsible for EFSS (Syncplicity) Desktop Clients, you will be responsible for guiding the development of our Windows and Mac desktop clients that are at the core of the Syncplicity sync and share solution.  We are at the forefront of the EFSS industry where our new SyncDrive capability is allowing users to access all their business content without having to sync it all locally, and you will own the growth and expansion of this new and innovative feature across a range of enterprise environments.  You will collaborate closely with development managers, project managers, developers, designers, architects, testers, analysts and documenters. This leadership role is responsible for managing the product backlog, articulating the “what” and “why” for each product release and negotiating release features with the team. You will also need to maintain direct in-depth knowledge of customer requirements and champion their needs within the EFSS Product Line team, understand the key competitive movements and innately know where the key players are headed in terms of strategy and product capabilities. You should possess strong product management skills, working with Engineering, user experience (UX) designers, and other stakeholders to ensure requirements are delivered on-time, with high quality, while maintaining a high innovation velocity. The primary focus is ensuring that releases delight our customers in terms of value and timeliness, while also differentiating the Axway Syncplicity offering in the market.


  • Articulate the “what” and “why” for the Axway Syncplicity Desktop Clients product releases.
  • Define and prioritize customer requirements and user stories based on business value.
  • Effectively communicate, verbally, visually, and in writing, with the engineering team to determine the scope of work for any given release.
  • Communicate regularly with customers to ensure a rapid and effective feedback loop into development progress and decisions.
  • Clarify acceptance criteria, articulate customer desires, and validate that the “definition of done” for each iteration or sprint in the release cycle meets the needs of the business.
  • Be committed to delivering products that not only satisfy customer requirements, but that also excite them.
  • Make data-driven decisions. Monitor active user metrics using a variety of tools.
  • Interface with Customer Support to ensure customer needs are prioritized.
  • Work with the documentation team to deliver clear and concise release notes, user training manuals and online documentation that answers common product usage and deployment questions for both end-users and IT administrators.
  • Safeguarding Axway and Axway customer data through compliance with Axway policies and the appropriate use of Axway equipment and facilities

Provide training and demos of new and existing features to all stakeholders.


  • 3+ years of product management experience owning products within software organizations, with a reputation of delighting customers, meeting tight deadlines, and fostering collaborative working relationships with engineering.
  • Product management experience with desktop client products, and SaaS based services, is a plus.
  • Strong customer and usability focus with an understanding of how to leverage the voice-of-the-customer and capable of translating business constructs into detailed requirements and instigating healthy debate and ambitious thinking within a team environment.
  • Passion for design and experience with ability to create wireframes and simple prototypes.
  • Takes an inclusive and listen-first approach to appropriately influence release contents, schedules, and technical decisions, forming a highly interactive tight-knit relationship built on trust.
  • Continues learning and adapting to change by being receptive to coaching and addressing gaps by proactively developing desired proficiencies. Seeks mentoring and feedback.
  • Thrives in a fast-paced environment – we want candidates with that entrepreneurial spark.
  • Demonstrated ability to quantify the success of decisions by analyzing data and measuring against metrics.
  • Experience with the agile methodology of software development.
  • Familiarity with the latest desktop client interfaces, web technologies, and REST APIs is a plus.

Education

  • Bachelor degree in User-Centered Design, Computer Science, Engineering, Data Science, Management Information Systems, Computer Information Systems, or closely related technical discipline.
  • Master of Business Administration (MBA) or Master of Science degree is a plus.
