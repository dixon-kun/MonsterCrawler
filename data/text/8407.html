Subsidiary:

AZZ Inc. Corporate Office - 3100 West 7th Street, Suite 500, Ft. Worth, TX 76107

General Description:

AZZ Inc. is looking for an IT Operations Intern to work with leading technology in Security Information and Event Management. In this position, you will help build and scale our monitoring and alerting infrastructure using IT operations data collected by Splunk, System Center, and related tools. You will work with the IT infrastructure team to analyze system predictability, performance, and security using data collected from core systems and applications. Your exact projects to pusue are flexible and will be tailored to candidate interests and degree program. This is a paid internship for summer 2017, specifically May 15th through August 11th.

Responsibilities:

  • Design and implement a Splunk log collection solution
  • Build Splunk dashboards with search forms, views, packaging and distribution
  • Implement Splunk integrations to external systems
  • Create Customized Splunk queries using Splunk query language

Requirements:

The IT Operations Intern must have excellent verbal and written communication skills and be able and willing to give effective presentations. The ideal candidate will have an intense curiosity about data and what treasures lie hidden within.

Majors:

A Bachelor of Science in Engineering related to the Project Field, such as Software Engineering, Data science, Computer Science, InfoSec, Mathematics, or Economics. Must be a Junior or Senior undergraduate student in the Ft. Worth area. Prefer a GPA of 3.0+.

Schedule:

Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
