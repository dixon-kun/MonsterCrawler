Located at our offices in Green Bay, WI, our co-op program is designed to provide each student with a professional work experience and exposure to the various career paths available within our Information Technology organization. The focus of the co-op is to provide a working knowledge of information technology focusing on business intelligence and will include exposure to the corporate environment.

The role is a 6-month co-op, with the opportunity to extend beyond 6 months.


A Successful Candidate Will Have the Following Attributes:

  • Effective oral and written communication skills
  • Interpersonal communication and collaborative team work with the ability to speak in front of business leaders
  • Motivated and a self-learner

A Day In The Life Typically Includes:

  • Preparing data, generating new reporting, assisting in maintaining Business Intelligence (BI) infrastructure, and operating as first line support for BI technology questions from the Data Analyst team
  • Complying with the IT team standards, while working directly with both technical and non-technical users
  • Making suggestions for process and system improvements to help streamline the organization
  • Creating value by increasing the turnaround time and volume of BI solutions

What You Will Need:


Basic Qualifications:
  • 1 semester of coursework OR experience with SQL coding
  • 1 semester of coursework OR professional experience working with a programming language other than SQL
  • Proficient in Microsoft Excel and Microsoft Access
  • Ability to work at least 15 hours/week, Monday-Friday, in Green Bay, WI
  • Pursuing Associates Degree or higher in Computer Science, Computer Information Systems, Information Technology, Computer Engineering, Management Information Systems, Data Science or related degree program

What Will Put You Ahead?


Preferred Qualifications:
  • Sophomore status at the start of the co-op
  • Experience in at least one of the following tools, techniques or languages: SSRS, SSAS, SSIS, Power BI, Tableau, Qlik, SAS, R Scripting, MDX, DAX, PowerShell, VBA, VB .Net, C#, Database Design, Statistical Math, or Data Mining
