INMAR OVERVIEW

Anyone who has redeemed a coupon, filled a prescription or returned a product, has touched Inmar. We apply technology and data science to improve outcomes for consumers and those who serve them. As a trusted intermediary for over 35 years, we have unmatched access to billions of consumer and business transactions in real time. Our analytics, platforms and services enable engagement with shoppers and patients, and optimize results.


POSITION SUMMARY

As a Software Engineering Intern, you will support Inmar’s software engineering or infrastructure efforts by working on business applications utilizing multiple platforms that might include cloud or mobile apps, or other components that interact with Inmar’s cutting edge platforms. You will be exposed to automated testing and continuous integration methodologies.  This position will also expose you to multi-discipline software development teams and agile processes, providing you with a working knowledge of software engineering development standards. The range of technologies available will include HTML5 (Ajax/Bootstrap), Python (Linux), Java (Android), Objective C (iOS), C# (.NET/Windows/Azure), node.js, MySQL, PostgreSQL, MSSQL and MongoDB. This internship program is for the 10 weeks in the summer of 2017.

MAJOR JOB RESPONSIBILITES

  • Actively participate in Agile development projects
  • Perform support activities as they relate to application development
  • Special projects that support the development teams, such as preparing and implementing environments, reports and/or documentation to support development teams.

EDUCATION / QUALIFICATIONS / EXPERIENCE

  • Strong preference for Students having completed their junior year and  on target to graduate in May 2017, with a Computer Science or MIS focus and exposure to one or more of the following development tools (.Net, C#, VB .Net, ASP.net, Python, Java, SQL Server)
  • Direct hands on coding experience either as part of project team, school project or individual effort is strong plus
  • Strong collaboration and teamwork skills
  • Extensive understanding of the Internet and social media sites
  • Ability to work independently with confidence and a detail orientation
  • S trong skills in preparing reports and research results
  • Must submit college transcript with application in order to be considered
