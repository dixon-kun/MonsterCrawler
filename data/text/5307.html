Social Media Community Leader
Division Outside Sales and Marketing
City Coopersburg
State/Territory PA
Apply Now

Lutron Electronics Co., Inc has an immediate opening for Social Media Community Leader .  In this position, you would be a key part of the marcomm team.  A successful Social Media Community Leader will ….

Responsibilities:

  • Work closely with our key business stakeholders, create and execute integrated, online marketing & engagement campaigns for Lutron’s social media channels:
    • Facebook
    • YouTube
    • Pinterest
    • Linked In
  • Blog sponsorships
  • Expand the Lutron social media program into new platforms that will enhance our brand and drive business results for end-user (residential and commercial) and professional audiences
  • Develop and manage content calendars, including assets, to drive engagement
  • Work closely with agencies to execute daily social media activities
  • Oversee daily social media activity and elevate opportunities and challenges as they arise, including working with internal resources to answer technical and customer service-related questions when posted to social media channels.
  • Manage and communicate reports in the social media and listening platform and other systems as assigned to determine success for each campaign
  • Coordinate online social media programs with off-line marketing activities including PR, tradeshows, advertising, events, etc.
  • Translate social media campaigns into clear performance metrics and continually optimize results
  • Stay informed of industry trends and best practices to understand how they can be applied to Lutron’s social media program
  • Provide competitive or industry research when necessary
  • Stay aligned to Lutron’s culture and values
  • Be passionate
  • Be flexible and adaptable to changing markets
  • Be an innovative thinker
  • Be customer focused
  • Be ethical

Requirements:

  • Bachelor’s Degree in marketing, English, communications, History, Psychology or data sciences area of study preferred
  • 3+ years’ experience in a marketing/communications role and 2+ years of experience in the social media marketing / paid search industry, preferably in an agency environment
  • Excellent communication & presentation skills; this is a client-facing and team-facing role that requires confidence and the ability to persuade and educate others
  • Creativity and a strong understanding of the customer
  • Ability to produce great work results through relentless organization, uncompromising integrity, effective communication, and proactive problem solving
  • Urgency, an attention to detail and a passion for social media
  • Capacity to interact well with outside agencies as well as internal employees
  • Resourceful, self-starter
  • Subject matter expert in mainstream social media platforms (Facebook, Twitter, Linked In, Instagram, YouTube, Pinterest, etc)
  • Working understanding of SEM best practices
  • Experience using search engine tools and bid management systems (e.g. Google Analytics, Google Trends, Google AdWords, etc.)
  • Demonstrated analytical skills and familiarity with measurement tools that monitor traffic and engagement statistics.
  • Google AdWords Certification or Google Analytics Individual Qualification is a plus
