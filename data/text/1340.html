Crowe Horwath LLP ( www.crowehorwath.com ) is one of the largest public accounting and consulting firms in the United States. Under its core purpose of “Building Value with Values®,” Crowe uses its deep industry expertise to provide audit services to public and private entities while also helping clients reach their goals with tax, advisory, risk and performance services. With a total of more than 3,000 personnel, Crowe and its subsidiaries have offices coast to coast. The firm is recognized by many organizations as one of the country's best places to work. Crowe serves clients worldwide as an independent member of Crowe Horwath International, one of the largest global accounting networks in the world, consisting of more than 150 independent accounting and advisory services firms in more than 100 countries around the world


We are seeking an experienced data analysis manager in our Audit team.

  • This professional will leverage advanced technologies (machine learning, statistics, data mining, data analytics and data visualization techniques) to Crowe’s external audit practice.
  • The professional will work with Audit Methodology, APP, audit personnel, audit engagement teams and Audit Business Unit (BU) leadership to transform our delivery of audit services and value proposition to our clients.
  • The professional will serve on the newly formed Audit BU Data Analytics and Machine Learning Committee and support the Audit BU data analytics and machine learning pilots and innovational projects.
  • This professional will serve as a liaison to Applied Technology resources on Audit BU projects in this area of specialization.
  • Handle other duties assigned by employer.
  • This role is not hands-on for actually creating the machine learning models, but then working as a liaison to the machine learning team.


Skill sets required include:

  • Professional must have business acumen and a background in math, applied statistics, decision sciences and/or data science
  • Demonstrated experience with data analysis, data integration and data validation activities
  • Demonstrated leadership skills and the ability to manage multiple projects.
  • Strong organizational, interpersonal and presentation skills.
  • Strong written communication skills and ability to provide thought leadership relevant to the interests and needs of our client base.
  • Highly effective verbal communication skills and the ability to interact with all levels of management.

Qualifications:

  • MS in Applied Statistics, Quantative Finance, Mathematics, or Accounting with course work in Management Information Systems.
  • 4 to 8 years of recent relevant experience
  • Experience with data analysis, data validations, data integration/ETL technologies, and business analysis.
  • Possesses knowledge of the challenges for a data integration implementation and data integration methodology.
  • Experience with Data Integration/Business Intelligence projects
  • Experience with complete project lifecycle in this area, including but not limited to requirements gathering, data analysis, design, development, testing and data validation.
  • Ability to work with IT and business personnel.
  • Experienced and successful professional services/consulting background would be preferred.
  • Strong analytical, attention to detail, organizational and core project management skills.
  • Strong teamwork, communication and customer service skills.
  • Experience with analytical software packages/tools/languages ((i.e. R, SAS, SQL, ACL) and visualization tools (i.e. Tableau, Telerik, and Microsoft Power BI).
  • Experience applying such tools to solve complex analytics problems in a team environment.
  • Accounting and/or auditing knowledge is preferred, but not required.
