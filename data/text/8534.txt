        Position Summary
        * The Quality Data Analyst shall be responsible for analyzing and reporting on clinical performance improvement activities including: identifying and accessing relevant data sources, aggregating and manipulating data to perform statistical analyses, preparing graphs and tables to facilitate data interpretation and ensuring the integrity and validity of data used to assess clinical performance. Working closely with other team members, the Quality Data Analyst will select and implement appropriate analytic techniques for evaluating the impact of quality improvement interventions, in addition to supporting the data analysis needs of organization-wide strategic planning and program evaluation in areas of clinical quality and patient safety.
        * Required Minimum Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities (KSAs)
        * Bachelor's degree in health data science, healthcare administration or related field required.
        * Master's degree in health data science, healthcare administration, health services research or related field preferred.
        * At least 3 (three) years of experience in analyzing healthcare data, managing clinical datasets and creating relevant reports in a large healthcare organization required.
        * Proficient with use of statistical software packages, e.g., SAS or SPSS, to aggregate, manipulate and analyze data to report results.
        * Proficient with use of Tableau and other graphical software packages.
        * Familiarity with commonly used electronic health record systems, e.g., EPIC, as well as HIPAA privacy regulations.
        * Familiarity with commonly used clinical performance reporting software, e.g., SAS or SAP.
        * Demonstrated ability to manage and lead complex data projects.
        * Effective verbal and written communication skills.
        * Strong analytical and problem solving skills.
        * Proficient in Microsoft Office suite of applications (Word, Excel, Access, PowerPoint, SharePoint and Outlook).
