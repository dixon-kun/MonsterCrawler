Python Application Developer

Office location for this position: 14900 Charlson Rd, Eden Prairie, MN 55347 USA

What's the opportunity?

We currently have a position for a Software Developer skilled in the Python ecosystem. We are looking for an open-minded and proactive person to support our data science team with the improvement of current schemes involving various proof of concept and production ready machine learning projects. The successful applicant will be a creative, self-motivated individual, capable of working independently or in a team environment, and on multiple projects at once.

What will you be doing?

* Improve and further develop machine learning programs leveraging single machine architectures and highly distributed Hadoop/Spark clusters.

* Aid in standardizing and optimizing current Machine Learning technology stacks.

* Perform studies displaying the achieved performance among various machine learning algorithms and supporting languages.

* Translate data science logic into production worthy software.

What are we looking for?

* A minimum of 3 -5 years programming experience in Information Technology with exposure to Machine Learning.

* Bachelor/master degree preferably in mathematics, computer science or physics.

* Solid understanding of Python. Ideally you also have some experience in R, Java, Scala and C.

* Some exposure to various Python oriented machine learning algorithms and libraries such as NumPy, SciPy, Scikit-Learn, etc.

* Working experience with Windows and Linux operating systems with the ability to move freely between each.

* Provide innovative ideas and solutions in an ever-changing environment.

* Possess solid communication skills and a enjoy working in a highly collaborative environment.

* Maintain knowledge of emerging technologies and using those technologies to solve business problems.
