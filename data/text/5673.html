Description:

We are looking for someone who is passionate about understanding data, well versed in scalable data mining and machine learning techniques, and loves to build models. This role is a multidisciplinary blend of statistics, technology, and business strategy, all applied in tandem to discover key insights that lie hidden in our data sets. The ideal candidate will be able to understand business challenges, map analytical approaches and build models to derive actionable insights.


Responsibilities:

Meet with executive management to understand major business opportunities and challenges (likely areas include financial performance, customer satisfaction, demographics and local economics)

Frame relevant issues, map out analytical approaches and gather data

Design, build, and run complex analytic models that drive insights significant to the business

Use business intelligence tools to communicate insights to executive management


Qualifications:

Familiarity with relational databases and SQL, data transformation (ETL), data mining, ad-hoc analysis

Extensive experience with Python, R, Stata, SAS, SPSS, Matlab or another statistical language

Deep knowledge of standard techniques (e.g., Logistic Regression, Cluster Analysis, Random Forests, Experimental Design)

Experience with data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau)

Experience working with real data either through a current job or through projects during graduate school

Ability to learn things quickly and work independently

An analytical, creative, and innovative approach to solving difficult problems
