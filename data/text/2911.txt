              Position Summary:
             Sevatec is seeking an experienced Chief Technology Officer (CTO) to lead the firm&#8217;s strategy and execution in solutions and technical innovations in core capabilities, intellectual property development, customer mission support, and competitive organic growth. The candidate will be responsible for growing and nurturing our team of technologists, subject matter experts, and operational leaders supporting the National Security industry with Agile SW Development and DevOps, Cyber Engineering, Data Sciences, and Cloud and Next Generation IT infrastructure.
             The CTO works with Executive Management, Innovations, Growth and Operations to position the firm for future growth by continually improving, enhancing, and growing Sevatec&#8217;s capabilities and strategic vendor partnerships. Through vision, inspiration, and coaching, the CTO will lead the team in solving complex technical challenges, developing innovative solutions, and delivering technical products and services to meet market demands.
              Responsibilities:
              Lead the company&#8217;s technical vision in capabilities, develop branded technical approaches and methodologies, and build technical solutions and demonstrations that solidify and differentiate Sevatec in the market.
              Develop and own related short-term and long-term technical roadmaps and budgets; drive execution and enable the team to meet intended outcomes; make key architectural and build vs. buy decisions; ensure that resources are invested wisely.
              Directs the company&#8217;s technical strategic direction, development and capacity for future growth through core capabilities, solutions, and strategic partnerships.
              Owns technical solutions from the early stages of business development, through capture, and proposal development, including authoring and delivering technical volumes.
              Developing staffing solutions and basis of estimates for technical solutions for proposals.
              Supports operations as needed in project transitions, project execution and close out.
              Works in a consultative fashion with other department heads as an advisor of technologies that may improve their efficiency and effectiveness.
              Provide thought leadership within the company; research and evaluate cutting-edge practices and enabling technologies for internal adoption.
              Communicate the company's technology strategy to management, staff, partners, customers, and stakeholders.
              Acts as a good steward of Sevatec resources and ensures control of technology investments and achieving expected returns.
              Required Skills and Experience:
              BS in related field and at least 15 years of experience in Technology, at least 10 years of management and strategic experience in this field or MS in related filed with 10 years of experience, 7 of which must be managerial and strategic.
              Experience and past success developing and deploying solutions using Agile and DevOps, Data, Cyber and Cloud methodologies for the Federal Government
              10+ years of full lifecycle of leading web based software engineering experience.
              10+ years leadership experience &#8211; recruit, retain, develop, and promote technical professionals, including management of team lead and manager level positions.
              5+ years of experience in Developing Technical Solutions and communicating them though white papers, technical demos, and proposals for the Federal government.
              5+ years of experience in the technical consulting and services industry.
              Ability to communicate effectively (oral and written), inspire and motivate all levels of staff.
              Ability to think and act strategically and proactively.
              Strong writing and presentation skills.
