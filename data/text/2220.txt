        Position Purpose
        The Project Manager III creates and executes project work plans for the Dept. of Medical Informatics and Data Science (MIDS) including but bot exclusive to the Big Data Program, Dignity Health Insights.
        In addition to being accountable to direct line management, the Project Manager III will work very closely with IT Solution Architecture and its PMO department.
        Nature and Scope:
        This position reports to the Chief Medical Information Officer.
        Primary Responsibilities
        * Leads the development and implementation for a broad, coordinated set of plans and programs in the Dept. of Medical Informatics and Data Science (MIDS) to meet the goals and priorities of the organization
        Duty to Support the Mission and Philosophy of Dignity Health:
        This position upholds the best interests of Dignity Health by adhering to the highest standards of ethical and moral conduct and by committing to the values of Dignity Health's mission and philosophy.
        Key Responsibilities:
        * Follow a defined Project Management methodology, accountable for the detailed planning of steps and resources needed to meet objectives
        * Monitor and prepare reports to ensure work is progressing to meet the deliverables on time and on or under budget
        * Manage multiple medium or large, complex, capital and non-capital IT projects simultaneously
        * Manage day-to-day operational aspects of a project and scope
        * Apply IT's methodology and enforce Project standards
        * Ensures that in-flights reviews and quality assurance procedures take place for all projects
        * Manage Project-related risks and issues
        * Solve conflicts and issues in a timely manner, escalating to manager as appropriate
        * Ensure Project documents are complete, current, and stored appropriately
        * Manage Project budget as applicable
        * Ensure quality control for all projects
        * Effectively communicate relevant project information to leadership
        * Provide leadership to lower level Project Managers
        * Provide leadership to multi-disciplinary teams, as well as manages work of outside consultants
        * Contribute to problem solving and process design to address identified DHI data discrepancies.
        * Lead the development and implementation for a broad, coordinated set of plans and programs to meet the goals and priorities of the organization
        * Work with Business Analysts and other SMEs, directs requirements gathering processes
        * Perform business case analysis
        * Translate business requirements into process/functional design/system solutions
        * Drive the completion of all project-related documentation
        * Ensure the preparation of test plans and scripts to perform system and user acceptance testing, execution of test cases and scenarios for the system, documentation of all issues, collaboration with other team members, and follow through to problem resolution
        * Develop and maintain project and resource plans and maintains project quality and focus
        * Work with managers, finance, and other Dignity Health organizations to determine required project budget
        * Monitors, controls, and reports on project expenditure against approved budget
        * Establish communication plans and delivers project status by publishing high-quality project reports and presentations
        Qualifications:
        Academic:
        * Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration, Health Care Administration, Operations Research, Public Health Administration, or other related field
        * PMI Certification preferred
        Experience:
        * 5 years Information Systems
        * 7 years Project Management
        * 2 years in a Healthcare setting
        * Project Management experience in Healthcare preferred
        Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:
        * Knowledge of Patient Revenue Cycle operational workflows, inpatient and ambulatory healthcare management
        * Technical computer skills
        * Highly self-motivated, self-directed, and attentive to detail
        * Strong interpersonal and relationship-building-skills
        * Possess thorough understanding of project management phases, techniques and tools
        * Possess strong and tested project management skills, including risk management
        * Proven abilities in problem management, process analysis, data analysis and root cause analysis
        * Identification with and support of the Mission and Values of Dignity Health and its Sponsors
        * Excellent written and verbal communication skills
        * Ability to work in a complex and matrixed team environment
        * Ability to handle multiple tasks, set priorities, schedule and meet deadlines
        * Skilled in Microsoft Project, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Visio
        * A passionate desire to grow, learn and advance
        * General understanding in the areas of application programming, database and system design
        * Presentation skills and experience in presenting complex plans and designs to executive-level management
        * Excellent knowledge of Healthcare-related business software applications
        * Ability to quickly learn new software systems and new technologies to successfully support a dynamic and growing organization
        * Understand Internet, Intranet, Extranet and client/server architectures
