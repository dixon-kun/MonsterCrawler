Purpose of Job

This role provides executive oversight for the teams responsible for identifying opportunities through data and information exploration, research of emerging technologies or analytical techniques and long-term defined/project specific strategic assignments that are not a part of the recurring analytical work.

Job Requirements

  • Provides executive leadership of the exploration of promising new technologies, analytical methods and data sources (internal to USAA & external) that have potential to add value and recommends investments in those that are most promising.
  • Leads the performance of advanced analytical work for long-term project assignments that are not part of the recurring analytical work. Builds and leads a high performing team of advanced analytics experts.
  • Coordinates with the Analytics Community Leaders across USAA in the deployment of a USAA-wide analytic operating model and talent management framework.

Required:

  • Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Business, Economics, Statistics, Actuary or related field
  • 10 years experience in analytic experiencing leveraging technical & business responsibilities
  • 3 years proven executive leadership experience

In addition to the requirements mentioned above, candidates are also evaluated against job related competencies that are aligned with performance expectations for this role.  These competencies are referred to as USAA Leadership Imperatives and are contained in the attached addendum.
