Committed to educating the next generation of leaders and to inspiring innovation and advancing entrepreneurship, NYIT enrolls approximately 10,000 students on campuses in North America, China and the Middle East. As a forward-thinking institution NYIT has pioneered educational practices to serve student needs since 1955. NYIT offers 90 undergraduate, graduate and professional degrees in 50 fields of study and is home to students from more than 100 countries and from 50 states. Its seven schools and colleges include: School of Architecture and Design; College of Arts and Sciences, School of Engineering and Computing Sciences; School of Health Professions; School of Management; College of Osteopathic Medicine, and the newly formed School of Interdisciplinary Studies and Education. NYIT sponsors 12 NCAA Division II athletic programs and one Division I team.


  • Perform routine and ad hoc analyses of institutional and external data to support planning, assessment, institutional effectiveness, recruitment, and student success at NYIT.
  • Prepare and archive data extracts and aggregations to support predictive analytical modeling using internal and external data sources.
  • Develop predictive analytical models of application yield, student retention and other measures of student success, and work with the Director of Analytics and Business Intelligence and the Vice President for Planning, Analytics, & Decision Support to identify other such models to be developed.
  • Collaborate with other PADS staff and with ITI to develop and maintain data integrity standards and to disseminate in a secure manner data extracts and aggregations to support institutional decision making.

  • Bachelor's degree required in computer science, data science, statistics, economics, or a related field.
  • Master's degree preferred in data science, computer science, statistics, economic, or a related field.
  • 1-3 years directly applicable experience or 3-5 years related experience in data science with preferred field of expertise in database design.
  • Advanced skills in data mining, machine learning, statistics, and other data analysis.
  • Knowledge of non-institutional data sources that can aid institutional decision-making.
  • Advanced technical writing skills.
  • Knowledge of higher education institutional data sources and definitions.

EOE AA M/F/Vet/Disability
