ADP Dealer Services is now CDK Global.

CDK Global is where Stability meets Agility. CDK Global is one of the largest technology providers to the automotive industry, with more than 26,000 dealer sites in over 100 countries and nearly 9,000 employees worldwide. From data scientists to sales and operations experts, we're hiring to support your growth and ours. GreenLight Your Career!

We are looking for an innovative Manager of Software Development to enhance and expand our Used Inventory Management solution. You will own and provide overall leadership for the entire space. You will lead the transformation of an industry worth hundreds of millions of dollars, building quality software solutions that absolutely delight our customers.

If you are passionate about technology, want to work with the best business/technology leaders in the industry and are looking for an exciting career with a strong fortune 1000 company that is blazing the trail on the digital frontier, you may have found your home away from home.

At CDK Global we love creating a great fun loving work environment. We create software solutions that are used by the majority of the Automotive Dealers in the US and around the world. We have presence in over a 100 countries. We thrive on providing solutions to dealer problems that no one else can!

In this capacity, you would:

* Manage the sprints team(s) in this space and remove any impediments for them

* Develop highly scalable, dynamic, and incredibly fast technology solutions that meet the needs of the business

* Actively work with business stakeholders to define and prioritize needs

* Actively work with your peers and stakeholders for other/related products to ensure broader roadmap sync and cohesive integrated workflows for the users

* Actively mentor a team of engineers with varying skill types and levels, both locally and off-shore, to help them grow professionally

* Drive best practices for all facets of the development process with a focus on continuous integration/delivery, extreme quality and operational excellence

* Regularly keep management educated of technical/product progress, issues/impediments, mitigation plans and their impact to the business

* Proactively recommend and/or champion process, tool and development methodology improvements

* Champion organizational changes across the teams you manage

We have been growing rapidly for a while and are always hiring people who love what they do! If you like what you have heard thus far, we invite you to reach out to us. If you are not enjoying what you are doing now as much as you should, let us have a coffee. We would very much like to get to know you and understand what you really want to do. We believe life is too short to not love what you do.

CDK Global is an Equal Opportunity Employer; Minorities/Women/Veterans/Disabled. At CDK Global, we believe that diversity leads to strength.

We realize that everyone's situation is unique, however our general guidelines for requirements include:

* BS in Computer Science or equivalent

* 10+ years of experience in software development with a proven track record of management success and engineering excellence delivering SaaS and/or Enterprise class solutions

* Extensive experience building solutions that can handle large scale using .NET/C#, ASP.Net/MVC, Web Services, Entity Framework/nHibernate, SQL Server/Oracle, JavaScript and Web Technologies

* Experience with modern architectural styles & patterns such as SOA, MVC

* Experience with JavaScript MV* frameworks like Angular/Backbone

* An understanding of dependency injection, inversion of control and other such patterns

* Experience with unit, integration, and system testing

* Proven ability to define and articulate a vision, set goals, develop & execute strategies and measure results

* Experience participating in cross department efforts by communicating and negotiating with multiple teams to accomplish goals

* Experience directing software teams in an agile software development environment

* Experience directing teams composed for both Onsite and Offshore team members

* Experience working with and upgrading legacy products and platforms to new state of the art solutions

* Ability to solve problems with out of box thinking & approaches and iteratively & incrementally improving both products and processes

* Strong leadership with ability to motivate teams

* Strong communication skills and ability to translate highly complex technical issues to executive and non-technical audience

* Nice to have: experience in continuous integration/delivery
