Maana is looking for a Sr. Solutions Analyst to join our Customer Solutions team in Houston, TX.

We are bold, smart, passionate and on the mission to create a new category “The Knowledge Technology”, which goes far beyond the current data-centric technologies.

We strive to create a product that solves our customers’ most complex data challenges that no other vendor has been able to solve.  The majority of our new clients come from customer referrals, testimony to the business results Maana delivers to Fortune 500.

Responsibilities

The role will be responsible for working directly with customers to understand and analyze their problem, working with a Solutions Architect to define the optimal solution, managing the solution through development and implementation, and maintaining a relationship with the customer by providing updates and walkthroughs as needed.

Throughout the solution development process, the Sr. Solutions Analyst will need to work with multiple internal teams (engineering, data science, etc…) to ensure that the project deliverables are understood, and are completed on time, while meeting/exceeding expectations of the customer. This requires elements of both leadership and project management.

This is a role that inherently deals with ambiguity, and the Sr. Solutions Analyst must be comfortable working with a standard framework, but willing to proactively learn and help move projects forward even when dealing with uncertainty.

The Sr. Solutions Analyst is expected to own the project relationship with one of our clients with minimal supervision. This encompasses managing multiple projects simultaneously for that customer (up to 3 at the same time), while working with them to articulate and capture how these projects could be taken further, how they could connect where possible, and how to move them into production level deployments.

Specific responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Running an engagement workshop to identify a customer’s business problem and decompose it into the Maana Knowledge Model framework
  • Capturing end user experience and creating initial mockups of that experience
  • Working with Customer Solutions teams to ensure that a project deliverables are on track
  • Identification of follow-on efforts that can leverage the Knowledge Model’s developed in past customer projects
  • Provide feedback to product management about the successes and failures in the field.
  • Serving as a mentor/coach to Solutions Analysts
  • Helping develop team best practices

Qualifications

  • 7 years of experience in a business or technical analyst role for medium to large scale business software implementation projects
  • Experience working on multiple concurrent projects
  • Experience working directly with external end customers
  • Demonstrated ability to manage individual and engagement-wide scope of work
  • Demonstrated client relationship and interpersonal skills
  • Strong project management skills and proven ability to execute against a defined methodology and deliver projects within scope, budget, and timeline
  • Excellent organizational, analytical, and problem solving skills
  • Client-focused attitude -- a customer advocate
  • Strong oral and written communication skills, including presentation skills
  • Understanding of machine learning and data science concepts
  • 4-year degree in engineering, computer science, MIS, finance or equivalent

Preferred Qualifications

  • Masters degree or MBA
  • Software configuration experience.
  • Basic programming skills and data analytics e.g. HTML, SQL, Excel, Access, Business Objects, R, Python, etc.
  • Executive report and dashboard design, KPI’s, etc.
  • Experience in Big Data technologies (Hadoop, Accumulo, Spark, etc.)
  • Experience with cloud environments (Azure, AWS, etc.)

About Maana

Maana pioneered the “Knowledge Technology” for the enterprise.  The Maana Knowledge Platform accelerates knowledge discovery to increase profitability. Maana’s patented Knowledge Graph™ — a breakthrough invention — at the core of the platform combined with Maana’s algorithms, expedite extracting knowledge from data silos, to reveal the relationships in the context of an asset or process companies want to optimize.

Fortune 500 companies such as GE, Chevron and Shell are increasing profitability with Maana by operationalizing insights into line-of-business applications for thousands of employees to make faster data-driven decisions.

Investors include Chevron Technology Ventures, ConocoPhillips Technology Ventures, Frost Data Capital, GE Ventures, Intel Capital, Saudi Aramco Energy Ventures, and Shell Technology Ventures, many of which are also Maana customers. Maana is privately held with offices in Palo Alto, California, and Bellevue, Washington. Visit us at http://www.maana.io/
