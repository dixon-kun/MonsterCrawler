The Job

Our engineering team solves tough problems and invents new ways to battle fraud using big data and audio science in the cloud.  We are looking for a Director of Software Engineering - Call Analytics to help define, design, lead and manage the creation of our bleeding edge products.  This role will interface with Engineering, Product, and Research to lead expansion of machine learning capabilities across Pindrop’s products. We have technically challenging problems to be solved and must demonstrate thought leadership in the audio security space, so we need a leader who can execute our strategy on a daily basis by inspiring and growing a high caliber software engineering team.

Responsibilities

  • Responsible for evolving the architecture of the solution and developing a comprehensive technical implementation plan
  • Improve and drive Software Engineering teams and implementation of technology related products
  • Lead the team that engineers the software pipeline to enable and manage the patented audio feature extraction
  • Continuous improvement in process and implementing controls to increase efficiency and productivity of engineering practices
  • Improve the team’s technical ability, utilization, and overall efficiency by establishing best practices and creating individual development plans
  • Collaborate closely with leadership in Product Management and the Executive team to deliver expected results
  • Expand solution capabilities, implementing new features driven by technological innovation that will lead the voice security market
  • Perform one-on-one performance reviews with all software engineers on a regular basis.
  • Develop status updates, reports and metrics

Education and Experience

  • BS in CS or EE. Masters in an engineering discipline preferred
  • 5+ years as a manager of software engineering teams and experience in analytics / machine learning
  • Linux experience a must
  • Experience implementing a machine learning platform
  • Python and C++ experience preferred
  • Software architecture and patterns, including Software as a Service and API development
  • Strong collaboration skills with disciplines like QA, Product Management, and Customer Success
  • Building out engineering systems including project management, source control, and QA
  • Proven track record of successful and timely product delivery, and of driving technical innovation
  • Experience developing and presenting information to executive-level audiences.
  • Scrum/Agile methodologies knowledge helpful
  • Experience with Cloud-based solutions preferred
  • Ability to deal with ambiguity in a fast-paced dynamic environment
  • Managerial skills and ability to grow people to their true potential
  • Strong communication skills, both inward and outward

Our Bio

Founded in 2011  |  Raised $122 million

Venture backed by Andreessen Horowitz, Google Capital, IVP, Citi Ventures, Felicis Ventures, & GV

Pindrop is the pioneer in voice fraud prevention and authentication. We provide enterprise solutions to reduce fraud losses and authentication expense for some of the largest call centers in the world. Pindrop’s patented Phoneprinting™ technology can identify, locate and authenticate phone devices uniquely just from the call audio thereby detecting fraudulent calls as well as verifying legitimate callers. We have been selected by the world’s largest banks, insurers, brokerages and retailers, detecting over 80% of fraud even for attackers never seen before. Pindrop’s solutions are allowing customers to reduce call time and improve their customers’ experience even while reducing fraud losses.
