OV ERVIEW:

We’re looking for a Director of Strategic Partnerships to join our rapidly growing revenue team.

As Director of Strategic Partnerships, you will be exposed to all facets of the business and be encouraged to learn and develop skills both within and beyond your role. You’ll be working in one of the most collaborative offices in Center City Philly (or remotely), developing and working with machine learning technology, surrounded by passionate, high-performing individuals.

QUALIFICATIONS:

The Director of Strategic Partnerships candidate is an intelligent, motivated person that enjoys exploring and establishing partnership opportunities in the marketing, tech, and digital advertising space while empowering colleagues to succeed and grow. The candidate should be:

  • Experienced in leading partnership conversations, preferably in the software industry
  • An eCommerce marketer at heart -- having spent time working for or with eCommerce companies in some capacity
  • A strong communicator, with the ability to navigate the hierarchy of organizations of all sizes, and build relationships with day-to-day managers and C-Level execs alike
  • Versed at building a partnership team within a growing org, with particular strengths in building process around how partnerships are sold, managed, and grown over time
  • Equal parts hunter and strategist -- capable of uncovering new opportunities and finding unique ways in which partnerships can add value to Sidecar’s technology and client base
  • Analytical in nature (our clients and partners are all in an ROI driven industry)
  • A big picture thinker, with the ability to understand the high level opportunity and put the puzzle pieces together, while also able to get into the weeds of partner management and execution
  • Enthusiastic and highly motivated, with the ability to inspire others and mold high performers



RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Support strengthening our existing relationships with some of the largest and most exciting players in digital advertising -- think Google, Facebook, Bing, Pinterest, and more
  • Identify potential opportunities in collaboration with relevant Sidecar stakeholders in established categories of co-marketing, product partnerships, data integration partnerships, and distribution partnerships
  • Effectively manage a growing partnerships team (1 direct report)
  • Meet and exceed aggressive goals while trailblazing new strategic initiatives
  • Regularly evaluate the effectiveness of the partnership program using an evolving set of objective and subjective KPIs

WHO WE ARE:
Sidecar built a technology from the ground up that’s dedicated to one mission: Tackle the challenges retailers face in data-driven e-commerce channels. B ased on advanced machine learning algorithms and deep data science expertise, our technology digests and analyzes massive, real-time datasets, allowing it to spot trends and continually improve campaigns for Google Shopping, Facebook dynamic ads, and Bing Shopping.

These channels are changing every day—and have become too complex and competitive to manage manually or with outdated technologies. They require automated, sophisticated, and ever-evolving technology to stay ahead. That’s what Sidecar is all about. We’re proud to work with retailers of all sizes and verticals in the U.S. and abroad, including Under Armour, Crate & Barrel, and Boscov’s, to name a few.

As a company, we’re a group of energetic people who work hard to make a lasting impact in the rapidly evolving world of e-commerce marketing. And our work is paying off—we are quickly becoming a tech leader and the go-to partner for e-commerce marketing pros.

Our open-floor work environment and Slack -enabled team encourages collaboration, creativity, and growth as individuals and as a company. Many of us come from e-commerce, statistical, or start-up backgrounds, but we certainly welcome talent with outside perspective and experience. We believe in regular company social events inspire friendships and strengthen our bond.



BENEFITS:

  • Competitive salary, based on experience

  • Health and dental coverage

  • A company 401(k) plan

  • Unlimited PTO (our policy is simple, respect your team, and take time off when you need it)

  • Flexible schedule

  • Choose your flavor of device: Mac, Windows, Linux

  • Pre-tax deductions of public transportation costs for your commute

  • Discounted gym membership
  • Team and social events
