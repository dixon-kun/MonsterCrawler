Sensus, a Xylem brand, is a leading clean technology solutions company offering smart meters, communication systems, software and services for the electric, gas, and water industries. Sensus technology helps utilities drive operational efficiency and customer engagement with applications that include advanced meter reading, data acquisition, demand response, distribution automation, home area networking and outdoor lighting control big data solutions and cloud-based management. Customers worldwide trust the innovation, quality and reliability of Sensus solutions for the intelligent use and conservation of energy and water.

Imagine working for a team of industry leading professionals providing real world, proven solutions for water, gas, electric and heat utilities. Envision yourself in a startup-like environment growing its smart grid solutions portfolio; we are the only US based organization in this industry with global presence. Wouldn’t it be cool to work with the largest Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI) bases in the world, where there are no spectators allowed?  Read below on how this critical role will impact our business objectives while helping the world!

The Role: Sensus, a Xylem brand, is seeking Intern Candidates with a Software Engineering background to join our team.   The successful candidate will have demonstrated the ability to succeed in a fast paced, fluid environment, while ensuring that project initiatives are met. If you are excited and passionate to work on state-of-the art technology trends: SaaS, data aggregation and big data analytics, we want to hear from you!

We want someone who:

  • Wants to build game-changing software applications and analytics for the Utility industry
  • Takes great personal pride in building robust software
  • Has strong sense of ownership and drive
  • Is passionate about applications, analytics, storage and distributed systems
  • Enjoys working in a fast-paced agile environment using Scrum
  • Has excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Has strong customer focus

Requirements:

  • Attending an accredited University studying toward a Bachelors of Science degree in Computer Science or equivalent.
  • Minimum rising junior in standing
  • Ability to work collaboratively within a team environment of engineers to meet aggressive goals and high quality standards
  • Demonstrated experience working with cross functional teams
  • Preferred experience in the following areas:
    • Software Development
    • QA & Test
    • System Administration
    • Data Science
    • Business Intelligence
    • Big Data Architecture
    • User experience

It would be awesome if you:

  • Have experience with online distributed systems and platform infrastructure design and implementation
  • Have experience in data analytics using Hadoop, Pig or Hive
  • Have experience in databases, analytics, big data systems or business intelligence products
  • Experience with Agile (SCRUM, RUP, XP), OO modeling, web services, Linux, middleware, database and data mining systems

EOE/Females/Minorities/Protected Veterans/Disabled
