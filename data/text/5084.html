ADP is looking to hire a RPA Process Developer- Automation Solutions

We are looking for an experienced process developer who possesses a passion to push RPA and other automation technologies and will work with ADP's talented team to build the next generation of Automation that can work across various applications and devices.

Job Responsibilities:

* Work in a team environment to improve process performance

* Identify new process opportunities and accurately analyzing and estimating new processes, quickly assessing feasibility

* Communicate technical infrastructure requirements

* Design RPA process solutions

* Complete RPA process configuration and objects using workflow principles

* Complete unit-test code for usability, reliability and overall performance

* Create and maintain solutions design documentation

* Support existing implemented processes and follow a change control process for any changes

* Create and maintain RPA processes following solution standards

* Communicate with vendors on software related issues, suggest improvements and participate with other users in the RPA community

* Create and document test procedures and scenarios pre UAT.

* Communicate and support the project team throughout the rollout phases (Assessment, requirement creation, testing and operations deployment)

Desired Skills, Experience and Qualifications

* Previous experience delivering and supporting excel macros, Visual Basic scripts and other scripting type of technology

* Good communication and written skills with the ability to produce clear and concise documentation

* Understanding of workflow based logic and the ability to both understand a business process from a workflow diagram and to conceptualize it as an automated solution

* 1-2 years of Knowledge of RPA software (BluePrism preferred)

* Highly motivated and dedicated to delivering results within tight timeframes

* Strong relationship builder with people within and across the organization

* Good problem solving skills and able to logically solve configuration errors

* Able to follow a structured methodology and provide input for potential improvements

* Knowledge with Workfusion is a plus
