        The Role:
        This Director, Assistant General Counsel position will be responsible for assisting on a broad range of consumer regulatory matters related to Capital One's Auto Finance business as a member of Capital One's Financial Services Legal team in Plano, Texas. The position will serve as a legal advisor for Capital One's Auto Finance business on matters relating to sales, marketing and analysis, contract originations and consumer lending issues. This includes consumer protection regulations, dealer matters, product development, and fair lending issues, among others. This position will advise Capital One's Auto Finance line of business on creation of new products, services, fees, compliance with consumer regulations, state and federal laws and regulations including but not limited to TILA, ECOA, FCRA, SCRA, GLBA, Dodd Frank Act and related matters and will collaborate regularly with other stakeholders within Capital One.
        Responsibilities:
        * Serve as legal subject matter expert and counselor, providing advice to Capital One's Auto Finance business with a focus on sales, marketing and contract originations.
        * Provide strategic and proactive leadership on legal and regulatory issues affecting the Auto Finance business.
        * Ensure timely identification, mitigation, resolution and reporting/escalation of existing and emerging legal issues and risks.
        * Review internal policies and procedures and advise Capital One's Auto Finance business on best practices, policies and procedures.
        * Provide advice and guidance to the Auto Finance business on federal and state laws, rules and regulations relevant to consumer financing.
        * Support new business initiatives, services, and other innovations.
        * Build and maintain effective working relationships with internal stakeholder groups.
        A Successful Candidate will have:
        * Financial services industry knowledge and expertise, including experience with consumer regulatory requirements relating to auto finance.
        * A thorough knowledge of consumer finance and protection laws, including TILA, ECOA, FCRA, SCRA, GLBA, Dodd Frank Act and accompanying regulations.
        * Complete understanding of the regulatory framework applicable to a bank subject to oversight by the OCC and CFPB.
        * Excellent judgment
        * Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with minimal supervision.
        * Ability to manage large volume of work, including complex legal issues that may arise suddenly.
        * Ability to thrive in an environment primarily defined by close collaboration with the Auto Finance business and legal colleagues to solve complex legal issues and to develop guidance and advice.
        * Ability and willingness to work as a team player, with comfort interacting with colleagues across departments and job levels, both inside and outside the legal department.
        Basic Qualifications:
        * Juris Doctorate (J.D.) and membership in good standing of a state bar
        * At least 7 years of law firm, in-house legal department or government experience in an attorney role
        * At least 2 years of experience working with consumer regulatory matters in an attorney role
        Preferred Qualifications:
        * 2+ years of experience working with consumer regulatory matters in an attorney role, with a focus on sales, marketing and contract originations in the auto finance industry
        * 8+ years of law firm, in-house legal department or government experience in an attorney role
        * 5+ years of experience working with consumer regulatory matters in an attorney role, with a focus on sales, marketing and contract originations in the auto finance industry
