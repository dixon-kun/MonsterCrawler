Position Description

This isn't packaged goods. It's a bigger challenge than that. Here you're making a difference in people's lives, starting with your own. Join us and start doing your life's best work.(sm)

Primary Responsibilities:

  • Translate business objectives and product features into enterprise - level machine learning solutions through the application of your expertise in quantitative analysis, data mining, and the presentation of data
  • Work with cross-functional team members to identify and prioritize actionable, high-impact insights across a variety of core business areas
  • Research, design, implement and validate cutting-edge algorithms to analyze diverse sources of data to achieve targeted outcomes
  • Provide expertise on mathematical concepts for the broader applied analytics team and inspire the adoption of advanced analytics and data science across the entire breadth of our organization



Requirements

To be considered for this position, applicants need to meet the qualifications listed in this posting.

Required Qualifications:

  • Masters degree in Mathematics, Statistics or similar field
  • 6+ years of experience in driving data science outcomes, solving complex analytical problems, using a blend of analytical, mathematical and technical skills
  • Expert in analyzing large, complex, multi-dimensional datasets
  • Accomplished in utilizing statistical analysis tools such as SAS, R, Python
  • Expert in working in Hadoop, Hive or MapReduce environments

Preferred Qualifications:

  • PhD Mathematics, Statistics or similar field
  • Experience utilizing Oracle and DB2 Database tool
  • Experience with NoSQL databases or related query tools for relational databases (such as Marklogic, MongoDB, Cassandra, or HBase)
  • Management of small reporting and analytics teams
  • Business Intelligence tool implementation and management, such as Tableau
  • Ability to make complex concepts simple and easy to understand to business partners with little or no analytical experience
  • Ability to work autonomously
  • Healthcare industry experience
  • Very strong Communication Skills
