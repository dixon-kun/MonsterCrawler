Position Overview:
ADP is hiring a Director / Oracle Application Architect, who is responsible to lead the planning, designing, developing, testing and deploying efficient solutions within Oracle EBS system to satisfy strategic, financial, and operational needs. 

 
*  Will provide overall solution design and all the detailed solution elements and requirements within the respective business process: Record to Report, Order-to-Cash and Procure-to-Pay.  
*  Will work with technical developers and business analysts to properly engineer and configure the solution as well as work with the business SMEs to setup and execute testing and validation of the solution.  
*  Responsible for generating and compiling reports based on their findings, complete with probable causes and possible solutions to systems issues.  
*  Will apply proven leadership, communication, analytical, and problem-solving skills to maximize the benefit of IT system investments.  

Qualifications: 

 
*  10+ years related experience with 5+ years in leadership managing a team of direct report and matrixed resources.  
*  Savvy and persuasive Communication Skills are key to gain buy-in across multiple teams and business verticals. 
*  Expertise with Oracle e-Business Suite ( Oracle EBS ) modules, and Oracle AIM Methodology. 
*  Experience implementing Concur Travel & Expense and Ariba systems. 
*  Bachelor's Degree in technical or business discipline or related experience. Master's preferred.
