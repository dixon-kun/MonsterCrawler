The L'Oreal Tech Incubator group is looking for an ambitious junior graphic designer with an interest in learning about user experience and user interface design. The Incubator works across many brands to help find opportunities for connecting beauty to technology. The person will be expected to work alongside senior designers, engineers, data scientists, and project managers. In the course of this role there will be exposure to the latest technology centered around virtual reality, augmented reality, connected and or 'smart' devices.

We are looking for a talented designer to create amazing user experiences on and off the screen. The ideal candidate should produce clean and artful designs in a detail-oriented and rapidly responsive manner. Further, we are looking for someone who displays superior UI skills and is able to translate high-level requirements into interaction flows and artifacts. This person will use the adobe suite to create beautiful, intuitive, and functional user interfaces.

PRINCIPAL ACCOUNTABILITIES

* Building Mobile App Information Infrastructures

* Wireframing

* Prototyping

* User Interface conceptual Designs

* Style Guiding

* Slicing Designs for iOS & Android Developers

* Facilitate Market & Consumer Research

DESIRABLE TRAINING AND EXPERIENCE

Requirements:

* Associates or Bachelor's Degree in Design, User Experience or related field

* Portfolio of digital and/or graphic designs

* Understanding of interaction design, and designing intuitive user interfaces and navigation

* Ability to rapid prototype creative concepts is a must

* Knowledge of Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop and Illustrator required)

* Familiarity with PowerPoint
