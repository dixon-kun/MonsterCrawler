Data Scientist

Key Role:

Work as a data scientist with advanced analytics expertise for Booz Allen Hamilton. Apply expertise in advanced analytics and work with industry

-leading Cloud computing technologists and data scientists to solve large

-scale, complex, and challenging business problems across the public and private sectors. Allocate across markets and industries, address business challenges, perform analysis, explain the analysis to clients, and provide solutions to the problem with Data-driven products. Develop machine learning, data mining, and statistical and graph

-based algorithms designed to analyze massive data sets and partner with Cloud technologists to ensure proper implementation and usage of algorithms. Serve as a client interface and participate in requirements gathering and analysis, process and systems definition, data analysis, presentation of analysis to the clients, and development of algorithm

-driven products and solutions.

Basic Qualifications:

-Experience with data analytics

-Experience with programming using an object

-oriented language, including Java, C++, C#, or Python

-TS/SCI clearance

-BA or BS degree

Additional Qualifications:

-MA or MS degree preferred

Clearance:

Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information TS/SCI clearance is required.
