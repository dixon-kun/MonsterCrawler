Inventory Analyst/Product Planner/Demand Planning Analyst

DRINKS, a stealth-mode VC-backed startup, is a top-five US Direct to Consumer (DTC) marketing technology company that manages wine brands. The Company is led by an entrepreneurial team of marketing technology, data science, DTC, and M&A experts with several successful exits. DRINKS operates in a compelling market: a $33B industry virtually untouched by the Internet, though that is now changing quickly.

To help us build the next generation of online and mobile wine stores that will be used by tens of millions of people, we’re on a relentless hunt for an Inventory Analyst who shares our passion for building cutting-edge experiences to transform how people buy wine.


RESPONSIBILITIES:

* Contributing to the overall achievement of inventory targets by creating specific analysis related to sales trends, customer purchase behavior, and production capacity.
* Processing and analyzing product forecasts, procurement, and inventory control to ensure the adequacy of inventory levels,
* Providing product availability information to sales and marketing through different inventory reports, and ensuring that inventory targets are met monthly according to plan.

CORE ACCOUNTABILITIES:

Inventory Management
* Creates and submits product forecasts for assigned product line.
* Creates and maintains inventory planning report (IPR) for assigned product group.
* Procures product to ensure adequate inventory to meet sales forecasts and planned DOS.
* Collaborates with sales and marketing to develop product forecasts.
* Analyze sales forecasts, product trends, and inventory levels to optimize sales and while ensuring an adequate inventory.
* Ensures accurate new product set-up information (e.g. sku, description, weights).
* Provides vendors with timely orders adjustment requests based on current market conditions.
* Creates and prepares inventory key data point report

Inventory Analyst

Fast paced aggressive growth Consumer product company is looking for an energetic, ambitious and forward thinking individual to join the Inventory Mgmt. Dept. as an Analyst. The ideal candidate will have 5+ years inventory and planning experience preferably in an MRP environment. Duties will include SKU allocation, monitoring inventory, planning, and maintaining acceptable inventory levels of finished goods, in transit, at our warehouse and 3rd party vendors.

Qualifications:

* Must have 5+ years’ experience in Inventory Mgmt and Production Planning (MRP)
* Must have experience in project management
* Consumer product background a plus
* Excellent oral and written communication skills
* Literacy in MRP Inventory Planning systems (preferred), and strong MS Excel, Access, Word
* Must have the ability to make quick and proficient decisions

General Duties:

Maintain a professional inventory management perspective while performing daily inventory and planning activities for a consumer product company, with minimal supervision and assistance. Ability to multi-task on various projects at any one time.

Essential Duties:

1. Working system knowledge in inventory management and planning functions
2. Monitor and maintain "ownership" of FG inventory accuracy
3. Maintain Data Integrity
4. Maintain weekly/monthly reports, OOS, AtRisk, Short Dated/Expired inventories, and other Inventory Mgmt reports.
5. Maintain accurate inventory levels at our warehouse and subcontracting plants.
6. Allocation maintenance accuracy for all SKUS
7. Communicate with various departments
8. Work with marketing department to forecast demand and align with inventory purchase planning
9. Maintain Open to Buy Analysis
10. Attendance and significant participation in meetings
11. Intermediate to advanced data analytic skills
12. Well-developed communication and interpersonal skills with the ability to lead and influence functional groups.
13. Ability to engage in healthy conflict and effectively problem solve within a team environment
14. Ability to multi-task, manages priorities, and meets commitments.
15. Self-motivated and results-oriented.
