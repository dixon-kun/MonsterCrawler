Data Scientist at Hoplite Solutions LLC

Reston, VA

Report this job

About the Job

Hoplite Solutions LLC is seeking an experienced Data Scientist to join our dynamic team to support of customers in the Washington, Maryland and Virginia.

Required Skills:

* 4 - 6 yrs programming expertise with languages such as Java, Python, Hadoop.

* Experience with data manipulation and analysis using any of the following: SQL, Python, Java, Shell scripting, any data mining/machine learning libraries (via R, H2O, Python, Spark, etc), MapReduce, PIG, HIVE.

* 2 - 3 yrs experience with AWS or any other Cloud platform preferred.

* 3+ yrs experience working with big data and real time/near real time analytics and big data platforms like Hadoop, Spark.

* Strong practical knowledge of analytical techniques and methodologies such as machine learning/supervised and unsupervised techniques, data mining and optimization techniques.

* Demonstrated technical expertise in Object Oriented and database technologies / concepts which resulted in deployment of enterprise quality solutions.

* Past experience with developing enterprise quality solutions in an iterative or Agile environment.

* Knowledge of industry leading software engineering approaches including Test Automation, Build Automation and Configuration Management frameworks.

* Demonstrated ability to develop effective working relationships that improved the quality of work products.

* At least 2 years of strong programming experience in Python (skill in R will be considered a plus).

* At least 1 year of strong SQL experience (experience with NoSQL databases is a plus).

* Experience with Hadoop, Solr, and Elastic Search and serialization formats such as RDF, XML and JSON.

* Programming experience with Java, JavaScript, C++ in an agile development environment.

* Strong written and verbal communications skills; comfortable communicating with both business and technology leadership, team player.

Education:

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related discipline, e.g. Computer Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Information Science/Technology, Statistics, Mathematics, or related discipline. Master’s degree is preferred.

***Please note, this is a government project which requires U.S. Citizenship in order to obtain a clearance***

Clearance:

Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information; TS/SCI clearance with polygraph is required.

Hoplite Solutions offer a competitive benefits package for Full time and Part time employees to include: paid holidays, paid time off, medical, dental, vision, flexible spending account, long and short term disability and 401(k), referral bonuses and tuition reimbursement.

Turn your interest into action, apply today! If your background matches the requirements, you will be contacted by one of our Recruiters!
