Position Title: Director, Product - ID Graph We’re looking for a super smart, highly motivated product manager to help us deliver on bleeding edge technology and innovation for the Oracle ID Graph. The Oracle ID Graph connects disparate identifiers, both known and anonymous, offline and online, to create a unified view of the consumer across a multitude of devices in a privacy safe way. It is created through the application of data sources, technology, and data science and powers the Oracle Data Cloud (ODC) offerings. The Oracle ID Graph is not a standalone product or capability, but underlying technology that differentiates the accuracy, addressability and scale of ODC products. Position Overview: The Oracle ID Graph is positioned to be the largest and most accurate identity graph in the addressable marketing space and will cover more individuals and more linkages than Facebook or Google. We need a driven product manager to help roadmap the foundational ID graph data processes and structures to optimize them for scale and accuracy, and make them available for downstream ODC products Job Responsibilities: As a Senior Product Manager you will engage with a very smart, cross-disciplinary staff to conceive, design and implement a product strategy from day one, covering all bases from workflows, to product features and new products. You will ensure our needs are being met across our tech, analytics and external partners, with best-in-class results being generated for our internal and external customers. You are responsible for defining the priorities and optimizing your roadmap for the biggest wins. You must be responsive, flexible and able to succeed in an open collaborative peer environment. Fundamentally, you will be responsible for ensuring that the ODC ID Graph represents the best available ID graph solution in the market. Candidate Profile:   • Have built, or worked on, products in the adtech/marketing arena, landing those products successfully, driving revenue and business impact   • A stickler for making things right, details and the finer points of analytical models.   • You love working in teams – have the ability to work with designers, developers and business leaders to generate buy in for the product direction you are setting   • 8+ years’ experience in relevant, innovative product management job functions   • Bachelor’s degree from a top university, or appropriate work experience working on well-known software products   • Excellent oral, written and presentation skills.   • Understand digital/addressable identity spaces, both new and old. Preferred Skills and Qualifications: Bonus points if you have been responsible for identity and/or data cooperative products in the marketing space or others Experience in the AdTech or marketing tech space Location: Broomfield, CO Travel: up to 25% Process: Prior to any consideration for this opportunity, Oracle requires that you complete the following 3-step process:   • Logon to https://irecruitment.oracle.com and create a user profile   • Keyword search: TBD   • Apply for the role As part of Oracle's standard employment process, candidates will undergo a complete background check prior to an offer being extended.
