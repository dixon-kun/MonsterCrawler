Focused on improving profitability, efficiency and data comprehension primarily through Business Intelligence development and performance improvement, authoring reports, creating dashboards and investigating data opportunities. Advance LTF analytic capabilities through implementation of the Business Intelligence roadmap strategy.

Examples of Work Performed

* Liaison with BI users to elicit requirements for identifying and developing reports.

* Manage multiple development projects at one time and meet deadlines

* Leverage Cognos BI Report Studio to develop prompted reports with drill-through capabilities.

* Develop Cognos BI solutions using Framework Manager, Report Studio, PowerPlay Transformer, Workspace Advanced and Workspace.

* Monitoring production usage and recommend solutions to improve performance and stability of applications.

* Write and analyze complex SQL.

* Perform data analysis.

Knowledge, Skills, Abilities and Other Characteristics

* Experience developing financial reports strongly preferred

* Experience with Workday and Qlick View/Sense preferred

* Advanced use of SQL, stored procedures and relational data sources

* Ability to work with summary tables, views or multi-dimensional cubes

* Willingness to learn new technology

* Strong documentation skills to build test cases on information solutions that can be executed

* Exceptionally strong organizational skills, with the ability to prioritize and multi-task

* Proven problem solving skills to maximize data models for information output

* Ability to follow-through on deliverables and pay attention to details in the midst of multiple projects and deadlines

Minimum Qualifications

* Education: Four-year college degree in Finance, Accounting, Information Systems, Business or related field

* Experience: Three - Five years relevant experience

Physical / Mental Requirements and Working Conditions

Work is performed in an office setting. This position is required to regularly:

* Talk and hear

* Read, write, type and comprehend text

* Observe objects up close and at a distance

* Effectively communicate and interact with others

* Use reasoning and solve problems through deduction
