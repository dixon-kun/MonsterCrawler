With wide exposure to our clients' business challenges, the ASSOCIATE DIRECTOR, DATA SCIENCE will access, analyze, and interpret product assortment, pricing, and marketing data on their behalf. We build advanced predictive models leveraging statistics, mathematics, and econometrics to support business objectives, and we develop solutions and approaches to the biggest analytical problems encountered by these clients.

What you'll do

* Develop materials and participate in discussions in client acquisition/business development opportunities

* Develop advanced statistical and econometric models of pricing, assortment, and promotion/marketing

* Provide analytical consulting on best practices and approaches

* Interpret, document, and present/communicate analytical results to multiple business disciplines, providing conclusions and recommendations based on customer-centric data. Be an internal expert in advanced capabilities

* Work closely with clients and their teams to develop methodology and implement analyses and technology that enables more profitable assortment, pricing, and marketing/promotion decisions in support of client strategies

* Take analytical objectives and define data requirements

* Extract, clean, and transform customer and item-level data for purposes of analysis, modeling/segmentation, and reporting

* Identify, develop, and make recommendations for process improvements and best practices; own implementation of recommendations required

Who you are

* You have a Master's Degree (PhD preferred) in Statistics, Operations Research, Mathematics, Economics, Econometrics, Industrial Engineering, or Computer Science

* You have a minimum of 5 years of directly related work experience in statistical or econometric modeling

* Expert-level proficiency in developing original statistical and econometric models in pricing, assortment and marketing mix to support clients in B2B, retail and CPG areas

* Expert-level skillset in implementing models using SAS or R

* You possess working knowledge of SQL

* You have advanced knowledge of Microsoft Word, Excel, and PowerPoint
