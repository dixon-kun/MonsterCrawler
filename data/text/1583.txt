        DataXu's vision is to make marketing better using data science. The world's top brands and agencies partner with DataXu to better understand and engage their customers across all available media formats, devices, channels, and buying modes. Our advertising solution, ranked #1 in the industry by Forrester Research in 2015, provides marketers with unparalleled Media Activation, Marketing Analytics and Data Management capabilities.
        DataXu is looking for driven, focused and high impact Account Directors to join our Go to Market team. The Account Director is responsible for developing meaningful relationships both internally and externally, the manifestation of which is to be viewed as a trusted advisor and the DataXu "voice" within their assigned accounts. Account management, client service and digital strategy are at the foundation of the position. Each are critical components of revenue attainment practiced through thoughtful program plans, effective deployment and excellent communications skills. Complementary to the day to day account management focus, a strong sales mentality is needed to drive further success and commercial expansion on strategic accounts and nurturing newer business through activation stages of the relationship. The Account Director reports to the Director of Client Development and is part of a regional Sales team in the North American Go to Market hierarchy.
        On the Job
        * Develops DataXu product expert, both in workflows and capabilities
        * Works in conjunction with Sales Executives and Sales Planning team on deal qualification and delivery of effective proposals for customers highlighting the value propositions of the DataXu platform and Services teams to create a foundation for success
        * Owns post sale customer relationship activities across a diverse portfolio of clients while providing timely, flexible, innovative and proactive service to customers
        * Advises customers regarding digital marketing strategy, leveraging DataXu's programmatic best practices in conjunction with 3rd party ad technologies
        * Works with regional leadership to provide service scope recommendations and resource allocation plans for assigned projects
        * Manages the day-to-day program lifecycle of opportunities closed by Sales Executives from activation and kick-off activities to final deliverables including campaign analysis and insights that prove success and provide actionable recommendations to inform future efforts
        * Guides the Ad Ops teams throughout the duration of programs to ensure success by clearly communicating objectives, strategy, requirements and client feedback
        * Actively tracks on program health such as performance, pace, troubleshooting and escalations while understanding the appropriate time and path to take action
        * Sets and leads client communication cadence including, but not limited to status meetings, deliverable schedules and other SLAs for standard and custom service offerings
        * Aligns with other sales enablement organizations within DataXu such as Business Development, Marketing, the Advanced Analytics Group, Solutions Architects and Application Engineering to coordinate resources needed for successful program execution
        * Facilitates account planning and milestone reviews with customers to effectively assess and communicate the current and desired future state of the partnership
        * Helps customers understand how the critical capabilities of DataXu solutions and the general programmatic industry are aligned with their business objectives
        * Identifies growth opportunities through client expansion and product attachment to achieve revenue growth and profitability
        * Fosters continued customer learning by educating the benefits of programmatic media and key DataXu value propositions
