Sr. SW Engineer (Analytics)

Back River Search - Cambridge, MA 02138

Posted: 3/2/2017

Company:

Our client is the world’s leading travel search engine. They search other travel sites and shows travelers the information they need to find the right flights, hotels, rental cars and vacation packages. The company’s website and apps also offer tools to help travelers plan and manage their trip, including price alerts, price forecasts and free itinerary management. Each year, they process more than one billion searches for travel information and has local sites in more than 35 countries and 20 languages.


The Position:

You're someone who likes working with a lot of data and are excited by the power tools like Hive and Spark put in your hands. You want to work on a team of people who are smart and hold themselves to a high professional standard, but still care about each other, help each other, and enjoy the occasional team dinner together. You want to work for a manager that cares about you, helps you grow, and gives you the space you need to be your best.

This team is all about moving, cleansing, and processing data in a way that is reliable and repeatable. We're a hybrid team that sits between the functions of Software Engineering, Data Science, and Data Warehousing with an emphasis on engineering. Our projects are varied and we work with many different teams. Typically, our work is focused in one of four areas:

  1. Writing production quality code for processing our internal advertising data to create reports for our partners.
  2. Ensuring we have the architectural pieces in place for a successful long term Big Data strategy. This often includes POC's and helping with the adoption of new technologies.
  3. Building analytic features into the client's product (for example: real time flight deal identification).
  4. ETL (Extract, Transform, and Load) of various data sources to be used for analytics by Data Science teams.

Requirements:

In the past, we've used Java, Groovy, Hive, and Pig. In the future, we'll probably see more Scala with Spark. We, like you, recognize that the Big Data space is in flux. We stay flexible and are constantly learning new technologies that improve our effectiveness.

You're a problem solver who likes to deliver value to the business. Perhaps you have a lot of experience, or maybe you're just extremely talented. Either way, you like helping teammates grow and are comfortable being a leader by example. Bottom line, our client want us all to succeed and keep getting better.

Qualifications

  • BS in Computer Science or related field
  • 5+ years of backend programming experience in SQL and Java (or a similar Object Oriented language).
  • 3+ years of software design experience
  • Bonus: Experience with a functional programming language like Scala
  • Bonus: Experience with Spark and Hadoop
