Hi. We're TiVo. At our core, we're innovators who continuously seek to fuel the ultimate entertainment experience. We touch the lives of binge-watching, music-loving, entertainment fanatics every day by inventing and delivering beautiful user experiences, and enable the world's leading media and entertainment providers to nurture more meaningful relationships with their audiences.

We work hard, celebrate success and challenge everyone in our organization to make an impact. If you are as passionate as we are about the intersection of technology and entertainment, join us today.

The candidate for the TiVo Director of Business Development role will have a deep understanding of media planning and buying, especially around data driven marketing, and have established relationships at DMPs, DSPs, SSPs and Agencies. They will have a strong understanding of cross-platform audience research, measurement tools and analytics. A successful track record of building relationships and driving business at television networks or advertisers is a plus.

This is an opportunity to join a dynamic division of a technology pioneer, and a chance to help shape the future of how television and digital media come together into a unified multi-platform interactive experience.

QUALIFICATIONS:

The ideal candidate for this role will have:

* 5+ years business development experience

* Experienced with digital media buying, selling, and measurement tools

* A relentless work ethic and desire to roll-up his/her sleeves as necessary and go the extra mile

* Strong integrity, always striving for excellence and leading by example

* Effectively worked cross functionally at all levels of a partners and client's organization

* Demonstrated success in and enjoys working in an entrepreneurial environment

* Excellent communication (verbal and written) and presentation skills

* Knowledge of the TV marketplace and business model

* Strong analytical, problem solving, critical thinking skills

* Ability to influence and manage people and outcomes outside their scope of authority

* Ability to interact with clients, facilitate small/mid-sized groups and articulate information effectively

* Ability to forge and actively maintain relationships

* Infectious passion and a championing attitude

* Is both a "hunter" and a "farmer" with the ability to adapt to the circumstance

RESPONSIBILITIES:

Director of Business Development position will be expected to:

* Identify and manage the expansion of partner relationships, fueling the overall flow of revenue from the partner "channel" in totality

* Be the first point of contact for clients introduced to TiVo through the partnership

* Work collaboratively with TiVo sales team

* Independently manage day-to-day relationships with DSPs, DMPs, SSPs and Programmatic TV companies

* Address and support partner and partners' client needs and requests

* Champion TiVo offerings and vision across partner organization, agencies, clients, and at industry events

* Continuously educate and stay on top of new technology trends, especially as related to data driven marketing and ad tech

* Participate in "big picture" and product strategy and vision activities

* Anticipate client's needs and identify additional penetration opportunities within assigned clients

* Document and report account status on a weekly basis to management

* Meet project deadlines and required deliverables accurately and efficiently

* Consistently and effectively collaborate with Data Science, Product, Delivery, Operations, Research, Finance & Engineering to manage partner and client expectations and ensure timely delivery of all deliverables

* Respond to inquiries & concerns and resolve problems in a timely manner

Benefits & Perks

Our employees and their families are important to us and our comprehensive pay, stocks and benefits programs reflect that. TiVo supports personal well-being, builds financial security, and enables employees to share in the success of TiVo. Rewards include:

* Competitive compensation (salary, equity and bonuses) and comprehensive benefits designed to foster work-life balance, care for your health, protect your finances, and help you save and invest for the future.

* Generous paid time away from work including vacation, holidays, sick time, and 2 days of paid time off each year to serve and learn through TiVo Community Outreach.

* Great perks, which vary by location and can include: employee discounts, transportation reimbursements, subsidized cafes and fitness facilities, conveniences such as dry cleaning and car washes, and recycling programs.
