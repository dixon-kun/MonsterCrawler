Job description:

  • Responsible for providing service design solutions for ADP hosted products with a strong emphasis placed on adhering to ITIL principles and guidelines.
  • Develop application profiles and delivery of the overall solution design requirements.
  • Act as a primary liaison for hosted application infrastructure Engineering with the development organization, product management, integrated service teams and appropriate service managers.
  • Maintain the Vendor/Development relationship.
  • Deliver migration and upgrade solutions to the client implementation support and upgrade teams.
  • Provide hosted solutions as defined by the initial design specifications, on time, reliably, and with approvals from all stakeholders.
  • Provide Tier-3 Infrastructure & Operations support as the product subject matter expert.
  • Assist application management with root cause analysis and problem management.
  • Mentor team members and provide guidance as needed
  • Work closely with internal and external customers on proof of concept trials and document results.
  • Work closely with development and other service teams on performance tests and interpretation of results with a familiarity or background on performance and testing tools such as apache bench, jmeter, loadrunner, etc.
  • Undertake performance tuning and troubleshooting of application services, develop enhanced monitoring, and evaluate application TCP/IP tracing using standard tools such as tcpdump, wireshark, etc.
  • Analysis of memory heap dumps and server level memory dumps when needed utilizing windows debugging tools and symbols, visual studio, eclipse, etc.
  • Assist development and other technical teams in identifying application defects and resolving, such as memory leaks or unhandled exceptions, with a capability of interpreting code written in platforms such as C#, Java, Perl, VB.NET, etc.
  • Provide Continuous service improvement by evaluating event patterns to improving product quality and availability.
  • Automate common tasks via automation frameworks or scripting.
  • Accountable for certifying and integrating new hardware/applications in the hosted environment.
  • Maintain and develop architectural diagrams and provide all service design package components required for operation.

Job qualifications:

  • Technical Bachelors / Master's Degree and 7+ years' work experience in systems engineering.
  • Scripting and automation experience Python, PHP, Ruby, Puppet, Chef, Go, C, C++, Java. Automation is key to our success!
  • 5+ years or more experience managing customer-facing, highly available, web-based applications that use load balancing and caching
  • 3 or more year of experience building, automating and maintaining any software defined solutions .
  • Strong background with batch, Visual Basic, Perl, and shell scripting.
  • Experience with server level and infrastructure level automations using tools such as BladeLogic, puppet, etc.
  • Experience in internet based server technologies, such as J2EE, IIS, Apache, Mobile Web, etc.
  • Excellent verbal and documentation skills.
  • Experience working with load balancing and web based routing technologies such as F5.
  • Ability to work independently on projects and deliverables with the capability to work on multiple tasks simultaneously.
  • Strong troubleshooting and diagnostic skills capable of diagnosing issues at all layers of the OSI model using industry standard tools and utilities.
  • Team oriented personality, capable of working with all team members to accomplish tasks and project deliverables.
  • Full understanding of the development life cycle, agile development process, and devops philosophy.
  • Expert level knowledge of SQL Server and Oracle RDBMS platform, as well as NoSQL technologies.
  • Strong understanding of log aggregation tools such as Splunk Enterprise.
  • Ability to work independently on projects and deliverables with the capability to work on multiple tasks simultaneously.
  • Strong understanding and familiarity with log aggregation tools such as splunk enterprise, logstash, etc.
