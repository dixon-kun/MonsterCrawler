Aspire Health is the nation’s leading community-based palliative care provider.  Aspire specializes in providing an extra layer of support to patients facing a serious illness through an ambitious palliative care program focused on caring for patients in their homes and outpatient clinics.  Aspire’s clinicians are experts in providing patients with relief from the symptoms, pain, and stress of a serious illness; helping patients and caregivers navigate the healthcare system; guiding patients and caregivers through difficult and complex treatment choices; and providing emotional and spiritual support to patients and their families.

Position Overview

In this position, you will work directly for the Senior Vice President of Central Operations to align internal resources (recruiting, data science, call center) with patient needs and client requests.  You will manage the resource planning field analysts and their work product as they support the market leadership. You will set the tone for operational excellence within a mission-driven organization.  The role will be fast-paced and will require you to generate your own analyses while also managing the output of the team.

Responsibilities

  • Align productivity targets with the appropriate resource needs and constraints across all markets, including: staffing targets, referral needs, analysis of capacity constraints, and productivity goals
  • Support market leadership with staffing plans, referral campaign strategies, availability analyses, and general operations requests
  • Collaborate with care center to produce detailed productivity schedules and goals
  • Manage patient and provider outreach campaigns in collaboration with field, account management, care center, and data teams
  • Produce internal executive-level reporting dashboards and presentations on a daily/weekly basis
  • Collaborate with financial planning and analysis team to align operational targets with financial strategy
  • Manage operational improvement projects in collaboration with the business transformation team
  • Execute against implementation plans for new markets and health plan partners
  • Other duties as deemed necessary by Aspire Health

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree required (MBA is a plus)
  • 3+ years in operations, supply chain management, or lean processing (e.g. Six Sigma) is required
  • Demonstrated ability to manage a detailed plan of action balancing organizational targets and resource constraints
  • Management experience in a role that required cross-functional alignment
  • Strong analytical and Excel modeling skills combined with strategic thinking, problem solving ability, and operations mindset
  • Strong project management skills including the ability to concurrently drive multiple initiatives and keep to deadlines
  • Strong interpersonal and leadership skills
  • Articulate with excellent verbal and written communication skills, including Powerpoint
  • Ability to think creatively, highly-driven and self-motivated
  • Demonstrated ability to roll-up sleeves and work with team members, with a willingness to put in the hours necessary to achieve success
