General Responsibilities:

Identify opportunities to ensure and enhance customer satisfaction through better fleet/car model choices for the customer. Work collaboratively with division and business leaders and lead & develop a team of 1-3 full time data scientists.

  1. Identify opportunities to ensure and enhance customer satisfaction through better fleet/car model choices for the customer. Includes
    1. Develop tools to monitor customer preferences by car class, make and model – by location and season – and determine implications on desired fleet mix
    2. Refine replenishment logic based on customer preferences (e.g., what upgrade models to put in PC vs. Five Star or Gold)
    3. DTG/HLE/Hertz AP fleet segregation guidelines by make and model and mileage
    4. Identify fleet rebalancing opportunities within pools and across pools and work with RVPs, OAP leadership, and Fleet VPs to coordinate
  2. Develop car assignment logic and tool the field can use for live assignment of cars into lots; takes into account:
    1. Customer bookings for next 72 hours by hour and by car class
    2. Loyalty status and upgrade entitlement
    3. Ensuring upgrade success AND variety of car class/model choices for customers in Gold/Five Star/PC/Platinum
    4. Fleet needed for upsell (based on historical upsell performance by location and day); logic to ensure that upsell lot has multiple car classes to offer (“what’s on the shelf”)
    5. Fleet availability
  3. Work with Revenue Management to optimize caps
  4. Develop performance management reports (upgrades vs. potential given fleet, variety of choice vs. achievable given fleet and bookings, upsell, customer preferences on make/model/trim)
  5. Run daily, weekly, monthly performance reports
  6. Work with upgrade field support team and operators to field test and iterate tools and playbooks with airport leadership
