In this role, the Data Scientist will be responsible for the following:

* Transform global high volume manufacturing business using advanced analytics

* Provide thought leadership in defining, designing and delivering enterprise wide advanced analytics solutions

* Work with senior business and IT leaders in identifying, building and deploying advanced analytical capabilities

* Lead and facilitate cross functional cross regional global team to deliver advanced analytics solution to solve complex business problem

Required qualifications:

* Bachelor's degree in computer science, statistics or related quantitative degree program

* Master's degree in computer science, statistics or related quantitative program is preferred

* 8+ years of directly related experience and progressive accountability with solving complex business problems through system support of analytics and / or statistical methods

* 3+ years of experience with Big data and analytics leader

* 2+ complete project life cycle experience in deploying advanced analytics solution leveraging big data technologies

* Experience with Oracle Big Data Appliance is preferred.
