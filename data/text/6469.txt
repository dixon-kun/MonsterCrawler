        Position Description
        Surveillance Analyst in the Control Room will be part of a global team tasked with the oversight of electronic communications. A person in this role will be responsible for identifying and escalating potential compliance issues arising out of review of electronic communications analyze and report on relevant productivity and risk indicators.
        Key Roles &amp; Responsibilities
        * Review electronic communications flagged by our electronic surveillance system and validate that a further review is warranted.
        * Analyze content of electronic communications and escalate to the next level of review if communication where it is warranted.
        * Manage escalation cases and prepare required documentation
        * Prepare management information reports showing relevant data yielded as part of our surveillance activities.
        * Assist in the administration of the electronic surveillance systems, perform reconciliations against employee data.
        * Monitor and report on productivity indicators
        Functional &amp; Business Knowledge
        * Good knowledge of credit markets and understanding of financial regulation.
        * Excellent organizational skills with attention to details
        * Interest in global work and able to work effectively with individuals of diverse cultural backgrounds.
        * Good communicator with the ability to support his or her decisions.
        * A self-starter with good judgment.
        * Ability to read in at least one of the following languages is a strong plus (Spanish, French, German, Mandarin, Japanese, Portuguese)
        Minimum requirements:
        * Bachelor's degree
        * 1-2 Years in Industry: Financial Services/Operations/Controls/Data Science
