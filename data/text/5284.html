OV ERVIEW:

We’re looking for a Product Manager to join our product team.

As a Product Manager, you will be exposed to all facets of the business and be encouraged to learn and develop skills both within and beyond your role. You’ll be working in one of the most collaborative offices in Center City Philly, developing and working with machine learning technology, surrounded by passionate, high-performing individuals.



QUALIFICATIONS:

The Product Manager candidate is an intellectual, motivated person that enjoys working with all parts of the organization to build products that our customers love. The ideal candidate has:

  • 2+ years of product management experience at software companies
  • Strong organizational skills, with the ability to drive teams forward in a fast-paced environment
  • Intellectual curiosity - enjoying new ideas, the opportunity to learn, and grasping concepts quickly
  • A creative mindset with the aptitude to think outside the box and escape the status quo
  • Ability to articulate abstract concepts, and create a clear path toward execution
  • Excellent listening and clear communication skills



RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Play a key role in the strategic direction of the product, including roadmaps, feature ideation, and new product line exploration
  • Communicate with teams across the company to gather feedback that will serve as a key input for product roadmap decisions
  • Put yourself in the customer’s shoes -- through research and direct customer contact -- to represent the needs of our customers in our product development process
  • Develop business cases and author product epics that provide clear definition of work for engineers
  • Work closely with engineering and stakeholders throughout the development process to ensure features match business needs



WHO WE ARE:
Sidecar built a technology from the ground up that’s dedicated to one mission: Tackle the challenges retailers face in data-driven e-commerce channels. B ased on advanced machine learning algorithms and deep data science expertise, our technology digests and analyzes massive, real-time datasets, allowing it to spot trends and continually improve campaigns for Google Shopping, Facebook dynamic ads, and Bing Shopping.

These channels are changing every day—and have become too complex and competitive to manage manually or with outdated technologies. They require automated, sophisticated, and ever-evolving technology to stay ahead. That’s what Sidecar is all about. We’re proud to work with retailers of all sizes and verticals in the U.S. and abroad, including Under Armour, Crate & Barrel, and Boscov’s, to name a few.

As a company, we’re a group of energetic people who work hard to make a lasting impact in the rapidly evolving world of e-commerce marketing. And our work is paying off—we are quickly becoming a tech leader and the go-to partner for e-commerce marketing pros.

Our open-floor work environment and Slack -enabled team encourages collaboration, creativity, and growth as individuals and as a company. Many of us come from e-commerce, statistical, or start-up backgrounds, but we certainly welcome talent with outside perspective and experience. We believe in regular company social events inspire friendships and strengthen our bond.



BENEFITS:

  • Competitive salary, based on experience

  • Health and dental coverage

  • A company 401(k) plan

  • Unlimited PTO (our policy is simple, respect your team, and take time off when you need it)

  • Flexible schedule

  • Choose your flavor of device: Mac, Windows, Linux

  • Pre-tax deductions of public transportation costs for your commute

  • Discounted gym membership
  • Team and social events
