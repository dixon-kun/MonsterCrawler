        Senior Business Intelligence Analyst (17007073)
        Description
        The Senior Business Intelligence Analyst (BI Analyst) will be someone who is extremely passionate about leveraging technology in order to extract and share analytics insights, using creative data visualizations. He or she will be self-motivated and results-oriented and possess the ability to work independently, juggle multiple tasks, prioritize workload, and adhere to tight deadlines, within a collaborative team environment. The ideal candidate will weave together disparate data, derive insights and build a compelling story to drive action. He/she can work at a strategic level, while helping shape the team's roadmap and will not shy away from tactical tasks, which drive the best solutions and the team's mission. He/she will be responsible for the design, development, implementation, and support of mission-critical enterprise Business Intelligence solutions, using Tableau sourcing data from many desperate sources. Additionally, this role will mine and analyze data that supports key business initiatives and business insights deep dives.
        Responsibilities
        In addition to following Chipotle's policies and procedures, principal responsibilities include, but are not limited to:
        * Building prototypes for demonstration and illustration purposes for peer groups, Business partners, or senior leaders.
        * Collaborating with other BI Analysts and Engineers around data/table structures in order to optimize for Tableau Dashboards and other self-service needs.
        * Supporting peers and stakeholders as a Subject Matter Expert (SME) through troubleshooting and analysis of results.
        * Acting as mentor and Tableau SME for other Tableau users across the company.
        * Looking for opportunities to improve current processes or find efficiencies by applying industry best practices as a BI Analyst.
        * Maintaining a high degree of customer satisfaction through the implementation, investigation, analysis, training and relationship building efforts.
        * Clearly communicating and driving discussions on data and insights, context, and implications with business and technology partners.
        * Documenting and communicating technical specifications to ensure that proper and optimized techniques, queries, and final deliverables are understood and incorporated into data and analytics processes.
        The ideal candidate will:
        * Have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Math, Finance, Statistics or related discipline. (Preferred)
        * Possess extreme passion for using data to uncover insights and drive business performance.
        * 5+ years of relevant experience in a business intelligence role, including data warehousing and business intelligence tools, techniques and technology, or experience in analytics, business analysis or comparable consumer analytics solutions.
        * 5+ years of experience writing advanced SQL, performance tuning of BI queries, data modeling, and data mining from multiple sources (SQL, ETL, data warehousing).
        * 5+ years of experience using business intelligence reporting tools. Mastery of Tableau for the creation and automation of Enterprise Scale dashboards, standard reports, scorecards, and ad-hoc reports to provide key business insights for entire organization.
        * Have experience with advanced Tableau topics, such as complex calculations, table calculations, parameters, geographic mapping, multiple disparate data sources and performance optimization.
        * Experience with large enterprise Tableau deployments and gaining user adoption
        * Be familiar with Data Visualization best practices.
        * Have experience in data mining, ETL, etc. and using databases in a business environment.
        * Have experience taking large volumes of data and condensing it into a clean, insightful, and automated format.
        * Possess a strong foundation in data analytics, an understanding and foundation in data science
        * Have exposure to statistical modeling experience (R, SAS, SPSS). (Preferred)
        * A strong commitment to data governance and security.
        * Be trusted to work independently with stakeholders at all levels.
        * Have attention to detail to ensure the highest level of quality/rigor in reports and analyses.
        * Possess the ability to identify, analyze, and resolve problems logically and systematically.
        * Be highly flexible, with the ability to execute well in a fast-paced, rapidly-evolving organization.
        * Have the ability to function collaboratively as part of a fast-paced, customer oriented team, perform effectively as an independent producer under broad management direction, and demonstrated willingness to support the team on all levels to get the job done.
        * Restaurant industry experience a plus.
