Internship Description

The Advanced Analytics Intern will perform data analyses to derive insight, patterns and correlations from Hallmark's data and other external data to derive insights that can inform revenue growth opportunities, identify cost avoidance opportunities, and shape strategy. Analysis will include applying advanced statistical, machine learning, and advanced data mining techniques. The analyses will need to be translated into a form for easy consumption by decision makers leveraging a variety of visual approaches. * Currently enrolled as a fulltime rising Junior, Senior, or Graduate Student

* Experience using at least 1 of the following: Python, R, Tableau and / or SAS

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS NEEDED - ideal candidate will have:

* Strong curiosity and learning agility

* Demonstrated application of Advanced Analytical skills

* Working knowledge of Excel, Word and PowerPoint

* Must be goal oriented and results driven

* Strong analytical and problem solving skills required to analyze multiple systems to ensure data integrity

* Permanent authorization to work in the US without company sponsorship preferred
