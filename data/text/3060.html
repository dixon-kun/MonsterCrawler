Altamira has an upcoming need for a Principle WTI Analyst (PA). The PA's role:

* Coordinates all WTI related intelligence research and production activities of the team, tracking the status of production and timeliness of RFI responses, ensuring all activities are entered, and properly updated, in the CITP production tracker.

* Coordinates and synchronizes activities of deployed and CONUS team members to ensure CONUS-based analysts are supporting deployed team members.

* Conducts initial quality control of all team products and responses to RFIs.

* Oversees the preparation of team members for OCONUS deployment, ensuring the contract analyst is technically qualified, completes all pre-deployment paperwork and processing, and has adequately coordinated in advance with the forward deployed analyst he/she is replacing.

* Is responsible for the training of team members.

* Assists the WTI and Network Analysis SPAs in determining OCONUS rotations, status of analyst training and preparedness for deployment.

Contractor employees filling this position shall be considered key personnel. The PA can be deployed, if necessary, to fill a deployed SPA position. When not deployed, this is a Charlottesville, VA based position.

Role Requirements:

* This position requires individuals to be government trained intelligence analysts, Explosive Ordnance Disposal (EOD) specialists, or electronics technicians with a minimum of 10 years of experience dealing with IEDs and related triggering device technologies.

* Experienced with data processing systems such as DCGS-A, CIDNE, and SIPRNET and JWICS search engines

* Working knowledge of i2 Analyst Notebook, AXIS Pro, WebTAS, ArcGIS, and Google Earth

* Ability to train and mentor junior analysts to prepare them for deployment

* Former military occupational Specialty (MOS) 18C, 180A, 31D, 311D, 35D 35F, 350F, 35T, 35S, 89D, 94F, 94E, or DoD joint service equivalent, or civilian 0132, 1811, and a Bachelor's Degree (may substitute 4 years of experience for education requirement).

* This position requires current TS/SCI clearance.
