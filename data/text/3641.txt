          The engineers in the tool &amp; data science group are responsible for creating tools to help our internal customers analyze&#160;large sets of challenging real-world data in order to understand what types of malware are affecting our customers.
           Your day-to-day

           We use rapid, iterative development processes to create UI-based and command-line tools for internal users, both client and server side. &#160;While working on new tools, you&#8217;ll be demo&#8217;ing your work every couple of weeks to get feedback and refine requirements.
