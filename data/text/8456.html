About the Job


About Us:
Infosys is a global leader in consulting, technology and outsourcing solutions. We enable clients, in more than 30 countries, to stay a step ahead of emerging business trends and outperform the competition. We help them transform and thrive in a changing world by co-creating breakthrough solutions that combine strategic insights and execution excellence. Our consultants partner with clients to transform their business - one conversation; one idea; one insight at a time.  While we continue to challenge convention and the status quo, some things remain unchanged:  the unwavering ethics, transparency and respect behind everything we do. We will always be a company powered by intellect and driven by values.  So, if your passion is to build solutions that really make a difference to enterprises, the community and your world, Infosys may be the right place for you.


Infosys Consulting:

Infosys Consulting is a dynamic, innovative firm focused on creating competitive advantage for our clients. We employ and develop passionate thinkers - and are building an inspirational place that every individual can succeed to their fullest potential.

We aspire to be global advisors enabling organizations to reimagine their future and create sustainable value leveraging disruptive technologies.


Enterprise Insights - Infosys Consulting

The vision for Enterprise Insights Practice is to "Help clients create Business Value from Data and Insights". Our service offerings include: Business Analytics, Big Data, Data Warehousing, Visual Stories, Predictive Analytics, Master Data Management and Data Governance

Our consultants combine Enterprise Insights capabilities with industry domain knowledge to create business value for our clients


Principal - Business Consulting (Big Data)
.

Responsibilities:
* Architect, design, prototype, and implement architectures to tackle the Big Data and Data Science needs on a variety of hosted settings, such as AWS and Azure
* Work in cross-disciplinary teams with to understand client needs and ingest rich data sources such as online analytics events, social media, operational logs and a variety of transactions
* Research, experiment, and utilize leading Big Data methodologies, such as Hadoop, Spark, MPP Data Warehouses, NoSQL, In-Memory DB, Search Engines
* Experience in designing and implementing data lakes
* Experience in creating reference architecture for Information that encompasses traditional structured data and modern unstructured, streaming data and other sources
* Experienced in migrating the data warehouse and reporting landscape from on premise to cloud
* Expertise in working with IT and Business stake holders to develop their DW BI modernization strategy
* Ensuring data quality (taking ownership of issues, investigating and resolving them)
* Demonstrated ability to work with strict attention to detail.
* Excellent verbal, comprehension and written communication skills (English)

Basic Qualifications:
* Bachelor's degree or foreign equivalent required.  Will also consider three years of progressive experience in the specialty in lieu of every year of education.
* 8+ years of relevant work experience with at least 3 years of experience in consulting services.
* Ability to travel 4 days a week to multiple local, state and national client locations
* U.S. citizens and those authorized to work in the U.S. are encouraged to apply.  We are unable to sponsor at this time.

Preferred qualifications:
* Demonstrated experience designing and delivering solutions utilizing AWS tools like: Redshift, S3, RDS, DynamoDB, ElasticSearch, EMR or similar is a plus
* Efficient in data modeling across multiple database technologies and a variety of business domains like marketing, operations or compliance for analytical applications
* Thorough understanding of big data architectures including physical composition (e.g. network, storage, compute, and hardware) as well as physical modeling, distribution, partitioning, and parallelism.
* Thorough understanding of Data Warehousing architectures, techniques, and methodologies; including Star Schemas, Snowflake Schemas, Slowly Changing Dimensions, and Aggregation Techniques
* Definition of strategy, business case, roadmap to implement data management and analytics solutions
* User requirements gathering, design, modelling & implementation of leading data management and analytics solutions in an agile environment
* Ability to work with team members and clients to assess needs, provide assistance, and resolve problems, using excellent problem-solving skills, verbal/written communication, and the ability to explain technical concepts to business audiences.


This job may entail an extensive amount of travel. The job also entails sitting as well as working at a computer for extended periods of time. Should be able to communicate by telephone, email or face to face.
