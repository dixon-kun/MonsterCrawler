Do you want to use Analytics to help companies better understand their customers?

Companies understand all about their sales by store, region, product, department and season. In today’s environment, this is not enough. An understanding of their customers is essential. At Wunderman, we help companies understand their customer’s shopping patterns – How often do they shop? What channels do they use? What do they buy together? What are their trip drivers? How valuable is a Direct Mail contact compared to an email? How do they use the web and interact with social media ? Wunderman puts its expertise to work to answer these important questions for or customers.


  • Use customers’ raw data and analytical tools such as SAS, SQL, Excel and PowerPoint to help them better understand their customer base
  • Make data-based recommendations to improve customers’ bottom line
  • Develop and build a variety of reports for the client, primarily using SAS and Excel, but also with other software packages as directed
  • Build, validate and deliver specific analytic models designed to generate true customer insight
  • Experience working with clients and internal account teams on analytical issues, such as predictive modeling, segmentation, experimental design, LTV, and program evaluation
  • Develop and provide written and verbal output on reports and presentations
  • Manage and resolve the key data issues involving quality, consistency of data for analytical purposes
  • Contribute at every step from reading in the data received from the customer, rolling it up, analyzing the results, preparing the PowerPoint deck and presenting it to the customer
  • This is not the job where you are given a set list of tasks to do, but rather you must be able to go where the data tells you to go



  • Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Analytics, Statistics or a related field – Master’s degree a plus
  • 5+ Years experience with SAS, SQL, or any other analysis or database software
  • 5+ years of database marketing and analytics experience using data management and analytical methods
  • Experience with dealing directly with clients and presenting results
  • Experience with SAS and SQL to manipulate an analyze large dataset
  • Must be able to get shoulder deep in the details to understand what the information is saying but yet keep your head above water in order to see the big picture and make strategic recommendations
  • Self-starter requiring minimal supervision
  • Some knowledge of multivariate statistical and modeling techniques
  • Strong oral and written communication skills
  • Strong presentation skills

What people do well on our team?

  • SMART – Well rounded, with emotional intelligence and clear SME areas
  • ENGAGED – Small business owner mentality
  • CURIOUS – Always learning and questioning what he/she can do more
  • FUN – Good attitude under stress and pressure, pleasant to work with
  • TEAM PLAYER – Recognizes others, helps when possible, looks to contribute to a broader goal
