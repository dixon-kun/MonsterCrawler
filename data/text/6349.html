  • Develop and implement analytics and visualization tools to drive the success of the Ferguson (FEI) Strategic Account Team’s strategy and tactics to deliver topline growth goals .
  • Act as a primary Moen contact for key operational relationships with our customer.
  • Act as a primary point of contact for business performance and analytics needs specific to FEI.
  • Support the strategic decision-making processes by defining, monitoring and reporting key metrics. Continue to evolve key metrics as business strategies and objectives evolve.
  • Track, analyze and report on critical KPI’s on a regular cadence; including but not limited to financial performance, inventory, Point of Sale (POS), Input, Shipments, Backlog, Forecasting, Service, Showroom performance, New Product Performance, Returns and Allowances and Customer Incentive Program (CIP) status.
  • Mine and interpret data to yield relevant, actionable and timely insights including but not limited to trend analysis, root cause analysis, opportunity and threat identification.
  • Partner with customer service and IT in managing customer data including Customer Master Data (Electronic Data Interchange (EDI), account information, Point of Sale (POS) data loads); understand and coach others on all customer data available for Moen to leverage.
  • Partner with the eCommerce Analyst to understand Build.com performance as it relates to the overall Strategic Account.
  • Collaborate with Sales, Finance and Operations to model the account forecast based on trends and insights.
  • Partner with experienced peers or Managers to sense and identify new and emerging trends from analysis of data. Develop insights into how these trends may impact business performance. Present these insights to the Business Unit.
  • Collaborate with Sales, Marketing, Finance, and IT in problem solving sessions, particularly in defining problem statements and building the fact basis needed to create recommendations.
  • Use data visualization (i.e. create dashboards, graphics and reports) to assist critical decision-makers in effectively interpreting data to arrive at insights.
  • Support ad hoc analysis requirements based on business priority.

Bu            Business Intelligence Development

  • In collaboration with IT, participate in problem solving activity for Business Intelligence (BI) needs to deliver the highest value to the organization and identify development solutions for BI requirements .
  • Support development and testing needs of BI projects to ensure data integrity and appropriate, intuitive interpretation by users.
  • Support BI Systems and Process Analysts to support continuous improvement to analytics tools and methodologies (Wholesale BU).

  • Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Data Science, Information Systems, Mathematics, Business Intelligence or related quantitative discipline is required.
  • Minimum of 3 years of experience in a Business Intelligence-related, analytical role .
  • Demonstrated sense of urgency and ability to persevere to reach objectives for challenging problems.
  • Strong communication skills with experience presenting and translating data into decision-ready insights is preferred.
  • Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to collaborate in a team environment.
  • Demonstrated passion for learning and adoption of new technology.
  • Proficient in the use of Microsoft Office Suite. Intermediate to Advanced Excel skills are preferred.
  • Experience developing reports using reporting tools and dashboards, such as BW Query Design, Qlikview or Tableau, is preferred.
  • Ability to travel ~ 10%.

COMP  Competencies

  • Being Resilient
  • Manages Complexity
  • Persuades
  • Plans and Aligns
  • Situational Adaptability
