Job Specific Description:

The Software Engineer (SE) is responsible for providing technology solution to various business areas across Retail, Preferred and Global Wealth Investment Management technology organization. Software Engineer will work closely with Project / Product Management and Delivery teams to translate requirements into software components design and code. Also responsible to play active role and support various phases of life cycle from requirements, analysis, design, code, testing, build & deploy and implementation. Software engineer is expected to learn the business and technical components of an application to appropriately communicate risk and status to senior project management and executives. Work with their peers across the organization to build synergies with cross function teams, drive efficiencies, improve knowledge management, and enhance the software development process life cycle. Grow your career in our dynamic and fast paced environment of diverse development platforms and challenging projects that shape the future of the banking industry.

Enterprise Role Overview:

Develops, enhances, tests, supports, maintains, and debugs software applications that support business units or support functions. Competent to work under supervision on simpler applications coding from documented logic. Limited systems analysis responsibility. Limited knowledge of business or function for which application is designed. Responsible for specific assigned programs. Typically an entry level position.

Qualifications Required Skills:

Recent or upcoming Bachelor's or Master's in Computer Science or quantitative field.

Good project experience with Relational Databases

Excellent in SQL Programming

Genuine passion for data centric projects

Hands on with at least one of the following: Shell, Perl, Python

Experience on any scheduling tool such as Autosys, Tivoli, Control-M

Must be motivated, a quick learner, to work independently or on a team and drive solutions with minimal supervision

Understanding of the software development lifecycle process

Ability to work productively in a globally distributed development team

Strong problem-solving & troubleshooting skills

Excellent communication skills

Desired Skills:

Experience with ETL tools such as Datastage, Informatica, Pentaho, Talend

Working experience with any of DB2, Vertica, Oracle, Teradata, Netezza

Project experience on any BI tools such as Cognos, Microstrategy, Tableau

Strong foothold on one of the programming language - Java, .NET, C#

Knowledge on Machine Learning & Data Science

Very good datawarehouse concepts

Experience in SDLC- Waterfall / Agile

Any experience with financial domain
