        Who We Are
        Our mission is to invent the future of farming. We're transforming the oldest and most important industry in the world from "gut instinct" to data science. Our platform already exposes critical operational data insights to 1 out of 3 farms in the U.S., and our customers absolutely love us.
        Who You Are
        You're a veteran Technical Writer who has lots of experience editing and writing end-user technical documentation for both web and print. You understand comma usage like the back of your hand and can jump right into any project using an established Style Guide. You've mastered best practices for localization and spent some time in the trenches creating graphic illustrations and video tutorials. Nothing irks you more than a comma splice, and few things bring you as much as joy as a finished piece of beautifully-written, easy-to-follow technical documentation.
        About the role
        Our Customer Experience (CX) department is among the best in the business, and we're looking to add an expert Customer Education Manager to it. You will work cross-functionally with Science, Engineering, Customer Happiness, Customer Success, Design, and Product Management to research and document the installation, configuration, and use of FarmLogs products. Your deliverables will include support page articles, Quick Start Cards, educational snippets for support conversations, and internal Knowledge Base content.
        You will spend much of your time editing and mentoring other people in the CX department to write according to the FarmLogs style, enabling us to continue to scale quickly and efficiently and maintain exceptional customer experience standards.
        Tasks you'll be juggling:
        * Propose, write, and/or collaborate on customer education materials (e.g., video tutorials, support page articles, email content) to ensure that paying customers have the resources necessary to be successful with FarmLogs
        * Propose, write, and/or edit process and educational documentation in the department's various internal Knowledge Bases
        * Edit and provide feedback on team writing to ensure that customers get the most accurate, pleasantly-delivered, and helpful information possible
        * Interview subject matter experts to understand and translate content into documentation for users
        * Strive to understand customer needs and gain in-depth product knowledge
        * Understand, follow, maintain, and contribute to the CX department's Style Guide
        * Coordinate with other CX department members to share processes and identify improvements that enhance our ability to deliver content on time and simplify customers' experience with documentation
        * Create screenshots, illustrations, and videos for use in product documentation where needed
        * Accurately estimate and plan documentation tasks
        Your Creds
        * Excellent grammar and written communication skills
        * Passion for amazing customer experiences
        * Master's degree in Technical Writing
        * 5+ years of experience of writing and editing end user documentation
        * Experience mentoring and leading other writers
        * Demonstrated knowledge acquisition in technical and/or scientific subject areas
        * Demonstrated editing skills
        * Demonstrated ability to understand and translate technical concepts to customers
        * Demonstrated mastery of authoring and publishing tools
        * Demonstrated mastery of grammar principles for writing for translation
        * Experience using iOS, Android, and web apps
        * Experience in a customer service or support role
        * Proficiency in HTML
        * Experience with visual content creation tools, such as Adobe Creative Suite and Camtasia
