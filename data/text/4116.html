Industrial Engineer 4 (Manufacturing)

Lam Research is a leading supplier of wafer fabrication equipment and services to the global semiconductor industry.  Lam Research develops innovative solutions that help our customers build smaller, faster, more powerful, and more power-efficient electronic devices—the kind that are driving the proliferation of technology into our everyday lives.

Lam Research is seeking a Senior Industrial Engineer in Livermore, California.

This critical position will partner with manufacturing management and will be responsible for optimizing manufacturing processes by identifying and driving the implementation of appropriate Lean methodologies.

Job Functions/Duties:

  • Enabling continuous improvement in Lam’s Livermore factory
  • Factory designs
  • Project management
  • Data compilation and analysis
  • Executive presentations

Required Skills :

  • Expert knowledge of Toyota Production System / Lean Manufacturing concepts
    • Kanban
    • Elimination of Muda
    • Kaizen
  • Operations and material flow
  • AutoCAD
  • Facility/cleanroom designs and facilities
  • Manufacturing engineering and test
  • Database structures and queries
  • SAP/ERP
  • Capability to analyze and interpret data with statistical methods
  • Strong leadership, communication, and project management skills
  • Must have a passion for manufacturing, software and data, and finding better/faster ways of doing everything you do

Education :

Typically requires a minimum of 8 years of related experience with a Bachelor’s degree or equivalent; or 6 years and a Master’s degree; or equivalent experience
