        The Role
        The Application Architect, Digital Application Architecture works as part of Digital Business Technology and is responsible for design, technical specifications, and implementation of digital solutions.

        * Work with business and IT partners to successfully implement projects that advance our digital strategy.
        * Stay up to date on the latest industry techniques and capabilities.
        * Drive technical designs to consensus and approval by development teams and Enterprise Architecture, providing framework, interfaces and services that can be extended and reused in future strategic initiatives.
        * Ensure high-quality output including: project design documents, reference architectures and decision documents.
        * Work closely with Enterprise Architecture, Enterprise Data Management and Security Assurance.
        * Evaluate vendor software and offerings from a financial, technical and requirements viewpoint. Drive conversations with vendors and participate in proof of concepts.
        * Work with project teams to coordinate environment availability and scheduling.
        Technical
        * Assist in troubleshooting of production issues.

        * Enforce architectural and development guidelines and best practices.

        * Strategic thinking and experience with presenting to varying audience sizes a must.
        * Engage IT partners as necessary.
