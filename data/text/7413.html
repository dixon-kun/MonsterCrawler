Data Visualization and Analytics Analyst

Key Role:

Maintain responsibility for data analytics support using Tableau software. Assist with drafting or editing all metrics for a defense IT client. Determine data requirements, prioritize the work, and define necessary additional expertise when required to accomplish tasks. Apply operations research and statistical techniques across a wide range of problems, including personnel, language requirements, operations, and strategic planning for the analytics section of the annual report. Define problems, identify performance measures, and perform gap and root cause analyses to identify alternatives and recommendations to improve performance measures. Author detailed written documentation and deliver technical briefings to senior client leaders and translate complex mathematical methods clearly in comprehensible terms, including applications, results, and return on investment.

Basic Qualifications:

-Experience with Microsoft Office

-Knowledge of management consulting techniques, including client interviews, data gathering, and problem solving
-Knowledge of data analytics or statistical software, including Tableau, R, Python, MapReduce, and Redshift

-Knowledge of operations research techniques, including probability and statistical methods
-TS/SCI clearance
-BA or BS degree

Additional Qualifications:

-BA or BS degree in Operations Research, Management Science, Mathematics, Statistics, or a Data Science field

Clearance:
Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information; TS/SCI clearance is required.
