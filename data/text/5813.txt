        Tala's mission is to improve the financial lives of millions of people in developing markets by creating new, accessible financial services that are most relevant to their needs.

        Responsibilities:
        * Understand the technical problems the business faces; prioritize and productize identified needs into solutions and tools integral to our operations, and technical infrastructure to enable scale and innovation
        * Lead cross-functional teams, including engineering and data science teammates from thoughtful ideation to frequent deployment via tools of the trade: Agile scrum, A/B testing, usability studies, acceptance testing / validation
        * Collaboratively define and progress product strategy within the company: champion product decisions, prioritize projects, and allocate resources
        Qualifications:
        * Technical - you love new technology and understand the science underneath
        * Empathetic - you care deeply about users and strive to understand them
        * Detail-oriented - you have a keen eye and mind for the most minute details
        * Translator - you have experience working closely with cross-functional teams and users to translate requirements into product
        * Analytical - you can tackle new unstructured problems, bringing data to the conversation
        * Organized - you effectively manage multiple priorities while maintaining high quality
        * Comfortable with ambiguity - you can handle the ambiguities of product strategy within a startup environment and prioritize accordingly
        Requirements:
        * BA/BS in Computer Science, Engineering, Math, Statistics, or Science
        * 4+ years experience as product manager for data, infrastructure, and/or technical product
        * Experience working with engineering teams on daily basis
        * Experience with agile methodologies
        Preferred Skills &amp; Experience:
        * Experience with data science and machine learning
        * Experience in tracking mobile apps with Amplitude or other analytics products
        * Background in consumer finance, or products servicing users in different geographies and/or spanning timezones
        * Proven oral and written communication skills
        * Advanced degree
        About Tala:
