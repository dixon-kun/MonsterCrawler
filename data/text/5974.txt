        Resident Illustrator/Animator, Shorter than Short Form (STSF)
        BuzzFeed Video is looking for an Illustrator/Animator Resident to come work with our Shorter than Short Form (STSF) Team creating content for Snapchat.         This is a 3-month temporary assignment in our Los Angeles office. We offer free lunch, free snacks, and social events ranging from movie screenings to happy hours.
        Requirements:
        * Brainstorm and illustrate ideas quickly and effectively
        * Willingness to learn quickly from metrics and reiterate on what we learn
        * Experience with Photoshop, After Effects and Illustrator
        * Experience with Premier and Cinema 4D are a huge plus!
        * Uses Snapchat on a daily basis and LOVES the platform
        * Loves to work quickly and efficiently
        * NO HATERS!
        Application: Along with your resume, please provide the hyperlink to your website, blog, work, etc. We want to see what you have produced!
