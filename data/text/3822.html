Adobe Marketing Cloud (AMC) Architect

Marketing & Advertising Santa Clara, California

Apply

Description

Palo Alto Networks® is the fastest-growing security company in history. We offer the chance to be part of an important mission: ending breaches and protecting our way of digital life. If you are a motivated, intelligent, creative, and hardworking individual, then this job is for you!

The marketing operations team has a global responsibility for building pipeline. We use the latest tools (including marketing automation, sales CRM, mobile, web analytics), have interesting and challenging problems to solve, and have CEO level down support for our mission.

As the Adobe Marketing Cloud (AMC) Architect, you will play a key role by driving initiatives and projects to achieve our vision of predictive marketing. Palo Alto Networks has implemented and operationalized seven of the eight AMC products.

With your exceptional technical skills, you can architect, design, configure, operate, administer and develop, as well as integrate the AMC products to achieve the business goals of customer acquisition, conversion, and revenue growth. You can design technical solutions to drive multi-touch/multi-channel marketing, defined by the buyer’s journey, using a global/regional demand center model. With your highly developed project management skills, you can systematically and methodically identify dependencies, develop, document, and UAT the solution. You know how to step back and look at the big picture, as well as write specs for developers and “roll up your sleeves” to carry out projects from soup to nuts. Your track record in marketing will speak for itself and you have many achievements to share.

The ideal candidate is a subject matter expert in AMC as well as demand generation, lead management, campaign management, and reporting. You’ll collaborate with our data science team and help operationalize things like predictive models, predictive next-best message marketing, and whatever your imagination and creativity can conjure up.

Our culture demands that you bridge between talking to business users and build multi-channel, predictive campaigns, as well as collaborating effectively across organizations like IT, Product Marketing, and Creative Services. We push decisions down—so you’ll run mostly on autopilot. You are free to create, innovate and transform the business. This is an awesome opportunity for somebody who is proficient in AMC, and wants to take on new exciting challenges and a leadership role.

Responsibilities:

  • Configure, administer, and operate the Adobe Marketing Cloud to build multi-channel, predictive, as well as next best message campaigns.
  • Accelerate implementation and achieve ROI on the AMC investment in Palo Alto Networks.
  • Teach and mentor the Palo Alto Networks team to become self-sufficient in AMC topics.
  • Replace Adobe Professional Services (PS) and use them for only very specialized tasks that need deep expertise. Make the Palo Alto Networks team largely self-sufficient for configuring and operating AMC.
  • Candidate shall have hands-on expertise in AMC that meets or exceeds the expertise of Adobe Multi Solution Architects.
  • Candidate should have implemented several use cases from this list: http://www.adobe.com/marketing-cloud/use-cases.html .
  • Assess and audit the configuration of the AMC products used by Palo Alto Networks.
  • Present the result of the audit and implement action items to remediate gaps in configuration of the AMC products used by Palo Alto Networks.
  • For campaigns that have one or more of the following characteristics—multi-channel, predictive, next best message, the candidate shall.
  • Scope and list steps to execute such campaigns.
  • Prepare a detailed project plan that list tasks, milestones, timelines, deliverables, dependencies, and task owners.
  • List success criteria and steps to measure ROI of the AMC implementation in Palo Alto Networks.
  • Manage stakeholder expectations to ensure adoption and active participation.
  • Document the process used to set up such campaigns so that more people can execute such campaigns.
  • Configure, integrate, administer, and operate the AMC products used by Palo Alto Networks.
  • Document the steps so they can be reviewed and repeated at a later date.
  • Train one or two Palo Alto Networks personnel to configure, integrate, and operate the AMC products (knowledge transfer).
  • Troubleshoot configuration and integration issues for the AMC products used by Palo Alto Networks.
  • Engage with Adobe Support and Adobe PS as needed to troubleshoot configuration and integration issues.
  • Coach Palo Alto Networks personnel on how to troubleshoot and resolve such issues (knowledge transfer).
  • Define, collect, evangelize, and publish metrics to show the ROI of the multi-channel, predictive, and next best message campaigns. Train Palo Alto Networks personnel to generate and interpret the metrics (knowledge transfer).
  • Define and implement best in class (lightweight) governance for multi-channel, predictive, and next best message campaigns.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in business administration, marketing, computer science, management information systems, or related discipline.
  • 4 - 6 years experience in email marketing at a B2B technology company; enterprise software experience preferred.
  • Candidate must have excellent communication skills—verbal as well as written. AMC is a complex topic, candidate must be able to simplify the subject and show how the AMC components work together.
  • Excellent organizational, project management, critical thinking, and communication skills (both verbal and written) in Lean Startups. Experience in rolling out complex, cross-functional projects. Skilled at resolving conflicts by taking a positive, win-win approach. Able to develop productive and consultative working relationships across marketing, sales, IT, and data warehousing teams.
  • Candidate shall follow the Agile/Scrum method to execute projects.
  • Define tasks prior to a Sprint.
  • Participate in daily scrums to provide status.
  • Actively contribute suggestions and lessons learned in retrospective meetings.
  • Use Palo Alto Networks internal tools to publish project plans and status.
  • Fast learner, eager to learn new technologies. Roll-up-your-sleeves work ethic and willingness to take the initiative and make things happen.
  • Ability to work on multiple projects at once, prioritize, work independently, and problem solve, working comfortably under pressure.
  • Proven experience in finding creative ways to execute in a fast paced work environment and a track record of delivering results on time and on budget.
  • Be a go-getter and self-starter, and show a passion to bring order from chaos and an obsession with building smooth-running systems. Extreme attention to detail.
  • Able to thrive in fast-paced hyper growth company environment. Able to stay calm and operate at high performance levels under pressure.
