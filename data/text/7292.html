Position: Data Scientist
Location: Bethesda, MD
Employment Type: Full Time Direct Hire
Compensation: Very Competitive
Position Overview:
The ideal candidate will have experience in providing advisory and implementation expertise to leverage tools, processes and procedures to create, manipulate and manage large data sets. This may include analytics strategy and business case development for Cognitive Computing, Machine Learning, AI, and/or Natural Language Processing. The candidate must possess proven experience in delivering advanced analytics. This position requires strong oral and written communication skills, including presentation skills (MS Visio, MS PowerPoint) as well as strong problem solving and troubleshooting skills with the ability to exercise mature judgment.
The candidate will also work closely with senior management to develop, document and transfer knowledge for the development of new service offerings.
Responsibilities:
  • Leverage tools, processes and procedures to create, manipulate, and manage large data sets.
  • Apply machine learning techniques to conduct insightful research and build projects that leverage internal, third-party, and public data
  • Develop high performance computing and data science applications across multiple platforms.
  • Test, launch, troubleshoot and accurately measure performance of solutions.
  • Communicate findings with both technical and non-technical audiences.
Requirements
  • Experience with common data science toolkits, such as R, Python, SAS.
  • Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau, and experienced with utilizing visualization techniques such as standard charting, Three Dimensional graphs, HeatMaps, Trellis diagams, etc.
  • Proficiency in using query languages such as SQL, Hive
  • Experience with NoSQL databases, such as HBase, MongoDB, etc.
  • Skills such as Social Network Analysis, Text Mining, Experimental designs, etc. are a plus.
  • Experience in developing simulations using standard coding languages is a plus.
  • Strong scripting skills and coding skills in popular languages such as Python, R, or Java.
  • Bachelors or Master's degree in Mathematics, Computer Science, or related field and a minimum of 3 years of related work experience
  • Experience working in an environment utilizing current and leading edge technologies preferred.
  • Ability to work effectively in a dynamic environment while managing multiple priorities.
  • Ability to translate complex information into ideas and effectively communicate.
  • Strong cross-functional team collaboration skills.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Strong facilitation and presentation skills.
  • Strong analytical skills.
