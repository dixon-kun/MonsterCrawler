Job Description

"We make magic." That's our motto at Walt Disney Parks and Resorts. And it permeates everything we do. At Disney, you'll help inspire that magic by enabling our teams to push the limits of entertainment and create the never-before-seen!

The Principal Information Architect provides thought leadership on technology solution design across Walt Disney Attractions Technology (WDAT). The Principal Information Architect leads Technology Solutioning activities on highly complex, strategic projects or programs. The Principal Information Architect identifies, plans, and coordinates a set of related technical activities on multiple projects within a program, to manage their interdependencies in support of specific business strategies. The Principal Information Architect maintains a strategic view of future state technology over a set of projects, providing the framework for implementing business initiatives. The Principal Information Architect leads the mentoring and coaching of other technical experts and leads the development of best practices for the organization. The Principal Information Architect leads business engagement and stakeholder management activities, and ensures

the required outcomes are built, tested, and delivered for multiple large and complex projects.

Employment Type

Full Time

Job Posting Company

Disney Parks & Resorts

Additional Information

* This position is with the legal entity of Walt Disney Attractions Technology LLC, an equal opportunity employer.

Basic Qualifications

* Experience leading data, reporting, and analytic solutions with demonstrated exposure to platforms such as AWS EMR, Hadoop, Spark, or Kafka and languages such as Python, JAVA, or SQL

* Demonstrated ability to become an established and trusted advisor with business and technology audiences.

* Proven ability to leverage and integrate technology components into an overall solution architecture in support of business requirements.

* Demonstrate knowledge of Data Architecture, Data Warehousing, Advanced Analytic, or Data Science implementations.

* Demonstrated product and delivery experience across all tiers in the solutioning of information management solutions â€“ from data sourcing to end-state delivery / consumption.

* Disciplined approach to the development and delivery of technology solutions.

* Experience presenting technical concepts and solutions to business and technology stakeholders.

* Proven ability to drive and influence change through innovation & process improvement.

* Professional written and verbal communication skills.

Required Education

* Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field

Primary Location-Postal Code

32830

Responsibilities

* Acting as a senior architect, work with business and technology cast members to define information needs and architect solutions in the form of a Solution Architecture that supports desired business and technical capabilities / requirements.

* Deliver hands-on components to support strategic and project-centric prototyping, proof-of-concept (POC), and solution prescriptions across emerging platforms

* Support the Information Management Reference Architecture and ensure the alignment of solutioning and delivery activities against the Reference Architecture.

* Produce artifacts in support of reference architecture advocacy and implementation, including authoring documentation, white papers, and presentations/diagrams for dissemination to technical and business audiences.

* Proactively look for opportunities to align technology as an enabler for business needs and capabilities (e.g., identify need for enterprise data warehouse, advanced analytics, etc).

* Perform industry research for best practices in architecture and data management for current initiatives and proactively share this information as applicable with other members of the team.

* Research leading edge technologies for future projects and use cases, including Cloud and Big Data platforms, business rules/data classification engines, near-real-time reporting, integration technologies, and data virtualization.

* Conduct pre-project business problem elicitation, feasibility, and business case definition on highly complex programs and projects.

* Work with the business to determine the real issue(s) that need to be solved, or the opportunity to do things differently.

* Develop the business case, in particular ensuring benefits align to strategic and tactical business objectives, managing interdependencies on multiple projects.

* Advises and makes recommendations on emerging trends which will be useful to the business, IT, and the entire segment in the delivery of future projects.

* Support the business in conducting business, technical, and feasibility assessments.

* Ensuring quality business requirements for large and highly complex projects.

* Ensures business requirements are concise, complete, specific, and unambiguous and coordinates related business activities in support of specific business strategies.

* Combines all business requirements into a clear, managed, prioritized backlog (agile) or business requirements document (waterfall).

* Drives business requirements to a sufficient level of detail to allow Architects and Software Engineers to design and implement appropriate solutions.

* Provides refined requirements into project iterations in time to allow design/delivery to commence.

* Manages the scope and traceability of requirements throughout the project lifecycle, ensuring that they are understood by the project team and met by the delivered solution.

* Identifies interdependencies across projects in support of specific business strategies.

* Leads business change management activities.

* Ensures the benefits, impact and release of all changes are tracked and managed and communicated to relevant stakeholders.

* Ensures end users and IT support teams are fully trained to use, support and maintain the application/solution.

* Provides project management support

* Provides subject matter expertise on the business unit, associated processes and systems, and business analysis thought leadership to all relevant team members.

* Works with the Project Manager to identify and manage risks, issues and dependencies.

* Resolves questions related to requirements or process.

* Delivers the required documentation during each stage of the project.

* Provides input into the RFI and RFP for third party/hardware/software selection, and assists in the assessment of responses against requirements.

* Reviews contracts drawn up with any third party suppliers to ensure that they accurately reflect the needs of the business.
