        Title: Data Scientist - 18871
        Your Responsibilities
        As a Data Scientist, you will work in an Analytics Team within the John Deere Enterprise Analytics Accelerator (EAA) to generate business insights on transformational, high value analytics projects. You will:
        * Work with complex, diverse data sets and perform appropriate analytical methodology to provide insights and decision modelling for the specified EAA projects, sometimes under guidance from a lead Data Scientist.
        * Create and implement simple algorithms which manage the data to enable it to be analyzed in an efficient manner and to enable repeatability.
        * Work closely with the EAA Data Wrangler to identify required data sources and to implement methodologies to retrieve and use this data.
        * Works with Data Wrangler and Data Infrastructure teams to identify gaps and improvements in data sets and identifies opportunities for new processes. Communicate derived insights for assigned projects in a manner easily understandable by business partners, especially through appropriate visualization techniques.
        * Proactively stay up-to-date with the latest appropriate analytical techniques and recommend these where needed, especially from academia.
        * Align with the Data Infrastructure teams to ensure data is modeled efficiently and in such a way to facilitate the required analytics.
        * Use analytical techniques to prospect for business insights and find patterns in data which could be valuable for the business.
        * Support the Lead Data Scientist as technical consultant for IT Domain management for all matters concerning analytical techniques.
        * Support the role in assessing third party analytical offerings.
        * Supports the development of new data and analytic products through research, modeling and advanced analytics
        * Use analytical skill set to help advance the business transformation of the Enterprise through analytics, for example through discussions with the relevant businesses.
        What Skills You Need
        * 3 + years of experience applying statistical analyses and analytical methodologies on analytics projects, with the ability to demonstrate the development of algorithms to generate business insights.
        * Adequate experience with current analytical platforms to utilize these fully independently or with minimal supervision from the Lead Data Scientist.
        * Experience in proficient usage of at least two of these programming languages - SQL, SAS, R, Python
        * Working knowledge of Heavy Machinery product lines and the types of data available for each
        * Ability to communicate complex analytical insights in a manner which is clearly understandable by the business partners.
        * Ability to demonstrate a combination of theoretical knowledge and practical experience in statistical and analytical techniques together with the development of algorithms.
        What Makes You Stand Out
        Other areas of experience or interest that will help us place you on the right team include:
        * Ability and desire to learn new techniques in the field of data and analytics, potentially leading to a relevant Advanced Degree.
        * Experience in development of new analytical applications and providing expert advice and support for other practitioners
        * Experience performing projects using the Agile methodology.
        * Advanced degree or certification in Analytics
        Education:
        Ideally, you will have a degree in a relevant study area such as mathematics, statistics or business analytics. The ability to continue study to obtain an Advanced Level Degree or Advanced Certification in Analytics is desired.
