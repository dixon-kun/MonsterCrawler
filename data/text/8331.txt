          Whole Foods Market is looking for a highly motivated Manager of Macro Space/Floor Planning to join our Space Planning team. You will be part of a team responsible for delivering data-driven, actionable insights, tools and processes that contribute to the overall productivity of our stores.           The Manager of Macro Space/Floor Planning is responsible for managing a team to deliver these macro space practices, tools and techniques. This role with drive sales through providing tools for macro space knowledge and execution tools for operational efficiencies.
            Responsibilities:
            Leads the development of macro space information collection and ongoing accuracy, including, but not limited to, floorplans, fixtures, fixture components, adjacencies and set sizes. Manages storage and reporting from information collected to partners in Purchasing Operations, Purchasing and Regions.
            Leads the development of floor plans to be used for analysis, store execution and visual analytical or information share illustrations.
            Develops floor plan templates used for each audience and ensures adherence to standards.
            Work with cross functional project teams including Purchasing, Purchasing Operations, Regional Operations, Inventory Planning, Data Science, serving as the subject matter expert on floor planning.
            Creates an environment of detailed accuracy in floor plan information.
            Leads team and partners in ongoing improvement of practices and tools.
            Works collaboratively to ensure floor plans are in alignment with strategic objectives and take into account operational efficiencies in stores.
            Manages relationships and budgets with third party vendor(s) for macro space collection and vendor(s) for software.
            Manage and oversee deliverables of team members.
            Exemplify conscience leadership to mentor, coach and develop Space Planning team members.
            Consistently communicate and model Whole Foods Market core values and goals.
            Qualifications:
            Preferred Bachelor&#8217;s Degree and/or equivalent experience and at least 5-7 years of experience in space planning, field retail, merchandising, inventory management, or related role.
            Minimum of 2 years directly supervising a team.
            Proficient skill level in JDA Floor Planning (or similar tool), CAD, Microsoft Excel, Word, PowerPoint.
            Experience in creating/updating floor plans for mid- to large-scale retailer accounting for brand/classification set size, flow &amp; adjacencies and operational efficiencies.
            Demonstrated understanding of retail merchandising basics (ie, category management), and purchasing organizational structures and processes.
            Team oriented with excellent project management and organization skills.
