           The Implementation Specialist (IS) will support the Implementation Sciences Manager to ensure that the integration of our evaluation data findings and other such evidence improve our ability to strengthen, enhance and continuously improve the Evidence-Based Youth Development Division&#8217;s suite of programs and practices.
           The IS will be responsible for working with Y-USA Technical Assistance providers who consult to individual Ys and groups of Ys that are utilizing Y-USA tools and resources to put into place specific programs or practices.&#160; The IS will focus on applying analyzed data and evaluation results to improve the fidelity and quality of these programs and practices through technical assistance. The IS must identify and communicate to TAs the key features and principles of assigned programs and practices, common pitfalls in their implementation, and solutions to address fidelity, compliance and overall efficacy of implementation.
           This role will help ensure that effective implementation of identified practices and programs occurs across EBYD by bridging the gaps between collected data, science and practices.&#160; The IS will support the broader team so that our work continues to improve, refine and develop over time to produce long-term effects and sustainability.
           This position will work closely with all members of the Evidence-Based Youth Development Division as well as the Research Evaluation and Data Sciences team and other departments so that effective planning and execution take place.
            This position is grant funded, and contingent upon future funding.
            This position is located in our Chicago, Illinois office.
