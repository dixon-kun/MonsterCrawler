Systems Architect

Byte Systems - Herndon, VA 20170

Posted: 3/24/2017

MUST be a US Citizen with a U.S. Government clearance - TS/SCI with Polygraph

Description

Sponsor is seeking Systems Architects who will be involved in the development and implementation of a Sponsor's system with capabilities that include extensive data management services on a shared cloud computing environment. The project will support the current and future implementation of sponsors software development and analytic business process transformations where system architecture includes data ingestion, transformation, and summarizations via data science methods to provide the substantive information to underlie the development of the system. The selected Systems Architect-SMEs will provide technical direction that helps design, develop, and maintain an enterprise application capable of supporting a very diverse enterprise customer-base. The architects will work with developers to design a robust, dynamic workflow interfaces, insight interactives and data dissemination capabilities. The candidates will work in a mixed technical environment and design and develop software in an agile fashion.

Mandatory Skills:

1. Demonstrated on-the-job experience with AWS Cloud Solutions (to include design, development and deployment). 2. Demonstrated on-the-job experience with systems security plans and accreditation's. 3. Demonstrated on-the-job experience with agile program. 4. Demonstrated on-the-job experience working with enterprise products for high throughput data ingestion and transformation processing. 5. Demonstrated on-the-job experience with high volume data environments.

Desired Skills:

1. Demonstrated on-the-job experience or knowledge of Sponsor and Sponsor's Partners data standards. 2. Demonstrated on-the-job experience and fluency with leading technologies and utilizes best practices.
