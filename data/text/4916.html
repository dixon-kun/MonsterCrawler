The Job

Our engineering team solves tough problems and invents new ways to battle fraud using big data and audio science in the cloud. We are looking for an experienced DevOps Engineer with cloud experience to help build and expand the products that will make the world a safer place for your wallet.

Responsibilities:

  • Provide operational experience and expertise to engineering teams
  • Design and build monitoring and alerting into the product
  • Provide oversight and input our deployment and upgrade automation
  • Assess the costs of the teams application’s resource usage and provide pragmatic guidance on ways to manage it
  • Perform smart capacity planning
  • Participate with the rest of the team in periodic on-call rotations
  • Lead and participate in blameless post-mortems to improve the products reliability and the team’s response to incidents
  • Ensure tools and automation are in place to monitor application health, latency, availability and performance

Education and Experience:

  • Experience with public, private and/or hybrid cloud environments
  • DevOps experience with: Linux, Docker, Nginx, Python
  • Bonus points if you’ve got experience with any of the following: Kubernetes, Postgres, RabbitMQ, Terraform, Go, SIP/RTP, Elastic stack (Kibana, Elasticsearch, Beats, Logstash), PCI compliance
  • Strong streak of empathy with your teammates and our paying customers consuming your application
  • Proven track record of providing stable, secure systems and environments that change and improve rapidly
  • History of making smart, pragmatic choices about designs for your systems and their features
  • A perfectionist streak that’s strongly tempered by deadlines
  • History of making smart tradeoffs between security, usability and compliance
  • Easily convey complex technical solutions to executive leadership and decision makers
  • Agile methodologies knowledge helpful
  • Ability to deal with ambiguity in a fast-paced dynamic environment
  • Strong communication skills, both inward and outward

Fighting criminals is in the job description. No matter which facet of Pindrop you want to work for, we are all working towards the same goal-- making sure the bad guys don’t win.

Our Bio

Founded in 2011  |  Raised $122 million

Venture backed by Andreessen Horowitz, Google Capital, IVP, Citi Ventures, Felicis Ventures, & GV

Pindrop is the pioneer in voice fraud prevention and authentication. We provide enterprise solutions to reduce fraud losses and authentication expense for some of the largest call centers in the world. Pindrop’s patented Phoneprinting™ technology can identify, locate and authenticate phone devices uniquely just from the call audio thereby detecting fraudulent calls as well as verifying legitimate callers. We have been selected by the world’s largest banks, insurers, brokerages and retailers, detecting over 80% of fraud even for attackers never seen before. Pindrop’s solutions are allowing customers to reduce call time and improve their customers’ experience even while reducing fraud losses.
