1)    Senior Data Scientist - Rally Health | Jobs.com
        As a key member of the Consumer Insight team at Rally Health you will empower Rally to more deeply impact our users health and wellness by leveraging data to better understand the customer feedback and success story. To do this you will leverage structured and unstructured data we gather from user experience across our different channels--web, chat, voice, community, surveys, and email in order to craft impactful insights. The analysis you conduct is critical to how our Consumer Insights team empowers improvements to operational processes, marketing campaigns, and our product's feature design. You are responsible for understanding the end-to-end customer experience and use this knowledge to bring the "voice of the customer" to our business partners.
        Responsibilities:
        * Developing a data science product to bring together a complete view of our customer experience across all listening posts
        * Producing high-quality systematic approach to analyze consumer stories &amp; create meaningful insights for senior cross functional team including product leaders, client account managers, operations and engineering
        * Partnering with internal teams to better understand how new features have impacted customer experience data and work to refine and improve the data analysis they need to improve their work
        * Champion of quality; you effectively validate and vouch for quality data and insights
        Requirements:
        * 2-5 years of demonstrated experience with data science or business intelligence.
        * Experience developing actionable insights with executives and VP level business partners
        * Proven track record of driving product or process improvements based on data driven analysis and recommendations.
        * Strong working knowledge of SQL and data analysis with a scripting language such as Python or R
        * Statistical toolkit sufficient to know when a stats test is needed and when a bar graph is good enough
        Nice to have:
        * Subject matter expertise with Voice of the Customer or NPS driven product design
        * Exposure to CRM software such as Salesforce, Satmetrix, or Clarabridge
        * Experience with Hadoop, Spark, MapReduce and similar technical infrastructure
        * Health, wellness, and software development specific business experience
