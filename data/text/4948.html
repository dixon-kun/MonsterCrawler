POSITION SUMMARY

The Principal Application Developer will become part of our myADP development team, working on a large initiative to integrate multiple different user interfaces into one. This application will serve as the central entry"face" for all of Employee and Manager Self Service Applications at ADP. This is all new user interface development. Candidates must have a passion for web development and be self-starters. Candidates need to work well in a team environment as they will be required to collaborate with other team members in an effort to provide the best solution.

This candidate will be focused specifically on Android and mobile solutions for the most part.

We strive for every interaction to be driven by our CORE values: Insightful Expertise, Integrity is Everything, Service Excellence, Inspiring Innovation, Each Person Counts, Results-Driven, & Social Responsibility.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

* Understands the Able to lead a team of developers to deliver on assigned deliverables and help guide efficient and scalable architectural solutions

* Mentor, teach, train and coach other developers in addition to be able to efficiently deliver code themselves

* Contributes to and enforces code standards as well as facilitates code reviews and GIT Flow processes.

* Understand and promote Unit Testing, Code Quality and E2E testing (automation)

* Can provide high-level architectural diagrams, slides, etc. about system integration, technologies, etc.

* Work closely with UX to understand and promote consistency and reusability among components throughout the application

* Work with leadership to understand goals, vision, etc. and translate to help team(s) execute on those deliverables

* Work across multiple teams to coordinate the best solutions that are reusable and potentially have project-wide, larger-scale implications

* Promote innovation, creative thinking, communication and collaboration among other teams, product, UX, etc.

QUALIFICATIONS:

Bachelor's degree in a related area and at least 8+ years of experience in the field or in a related area. Familiar with a variety of the field's concepts, practices, and procedures.

Relies on experience and judgment to plan and accomplish goals. Performs a variety of complicated tasks. Directs and leads the work of others. A wide degree of creativity and latitude

PREFERRED/REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS: Preference will be given to candidates who have the following:

* Java Experience

* Android Ecosystem Experience

* Mobile Patterns and Ecosystem Experience

* REST APIs and JSON Experience

* Strong Javascript Skills

* Strong HTML (HTML5) skills

* Strong Communication Skills

* Must be an independent self-starter
