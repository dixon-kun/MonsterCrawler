Wunderman, with a growing portfolio of top-tier clients specializes in designing consumer-centric strategies focused on combining creative with data to build valuable relationships between consumers and brands.

We are looking for a dynamic Senior Strategist who will nurture and continue to build our strategic capabilities for clients.  This is an exciting opportunity to lead, leverage and deliver powerful  ideas and solutions across the full spectrum of data, creative, technology, and marketing services, in order to help our clients maximize the opportunities provided by our integrated offering.


  • Day-to-day client brief intake and development of actionable agency creative briefs: identify insights, communication angles and connect the dots in multi-channel messaging.  Inspire our teams to create amazing ideas.
  • Collaborate with account and creative teams to develop, crystalize and sell campaigns and communications that drive action and business results.
  • Identify and champion emerging technologies and human behaviors that make our work more impactful, engaging, or demonstrate creative prowess.
  • Author point-of-view documents on upcoming trends and consumer insights and identify opportunities to leverage new ideas, solutions and platforms with clients.
  • Conduct competitive reviews to gain insights and drive innovation.
  • Collaborate with Analytics to devise performance measurement frameworks and incorporate learnings into Creative briefs.
  • Collaborate with account and creative teams during execution of campaigns.

  • Exceptional verbal and written communication skills; strong client interaction skills.
  • A natural curiosity for people, culture, brands and how they intersect with technology.
  • Strong qualitative and quantitative analytical skills and data sources – and the ability to know when and how to use each either individually or together.
  • Appreciation for data analytics to drive actionable focused creative executions.
  • Proven leadership skills, with ability to inspire team members.
  • 5-7 years of experience working in an agency environment with a focus in strategy experience development.
  • Bachelor’s degree required.
  • Inspirational, compelling personality; the one others always look to for ideas.
  • Strong brainstorm contributor; facilitation of conceptual idea development.
  • Proven success working in cross-functional teams.
  • Ability to work independently in a fast-paced work environment.

Wunderman is Creatively Driven. Data Inspired. A leading global digital agency, Wunderman combines creativity and data into work that inspires people to take action and delivers results for brands. In 2015, industry analysts named Wunderman a leader in marketing database operations as well as a strong performer in customer engagement strategy. Headquartered in New York, the agency brings together 7,000 creatives, data scientists, strategists and technologists in 175 offices in 60 markets. Wunderman is a WPP company (NASDAQ: WPPGY). For more information, please visit www.wunderman.com and follow us @Wunderman.
