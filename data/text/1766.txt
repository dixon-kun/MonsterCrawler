        Overview:
        \n
        This position demands an experienced, self-starter capable of successfully solving difficult problems that require adaptation and modification of standard techniques, procedures and criteria. This position will be responsible for evaluating, testing and commercializing 3rd party energy related solutions/technologies. The position also requires technical development of internal solutions related to energy efficiency in buildings. This person will need to be able to document the solution as well as perform a feasibility study for each proposed solution. When a solution/technology is evaluated and determined to be a viable solutionsechnology, this person will need to be able to deliver all aspects of the solution to market. As part of those responsibilities this person will be responsible for sales documentation, workflow documentation, pilot/beta site coordination and continued support of the released solutions/technologies.

        Responsibilities

        &#167; Advanced and emerging building technology testing and validation, including patent, product spec and design documentation reviews, onsite install visits, savings calc validation, and energy modeling when applicable.

        &#167; Develop pilot testing procedures, overseeing pilot testing, results validation, recommendations for product improvement, and as-is adoption or rejection for business
        \n
        &#167; When necessary, perform on-site testing of HVAC, lighting, power, or control systems in existing building in conjunction with onsite Siemens resources, O&amp;M or contractor personnel
        \n

        \n
        &#167; Create &amp; maintain detailed and accurate pilot project records, cautions/what to watch out for, best practices
        \n
        &#167; Develop and refine methodologies/tools, and standards in product installations and make improvements in field standards for these new technologies as we integrate them into our portfolio
        \n
        &#167; Prepare technical reports and presentations on technologies for recommendations for a variety of audiences
