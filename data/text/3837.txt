        We are :
        The Search Relevance team at salesforce.com is seeking a proven technical leader to help build out Search Ranking, Relevance and Machine Learning teams to power search intelligence for our multi-tenant cloud-scale search system. As part of the Search Relevance team, you will work on relevance and ranking functions that drive the richness, quality and effectiveness of the search experience across all of Salesforce's products.
        You are :
        Ideal candidates have experience solving real-world search relevancy challenges in large scale hosted search systems, have a strong track record of leading teams focussed on building machine learning models and deploying them in production. Your role will be to develop and implement strategies for utilizing natural language processing, intent analysis, machine learning and other leading edge technologies such as learning to rank methods to help drive measurable relevancy improvements in a variety of use cases and businesses that leverage our search to deliver rich user experiences. We are looking for a talented architect super passionate about search.
        If you are excited by the opportunity to work with a world class technical team to deliver a high traffic, high performance, highly evolved search solution that satisfies millions of requests a day and delivers an experience that delights millions of users, then we want to talk with you.
        Responsibilities:
        * Set the vision for engineers and data scientists that apply Information Retrieval and Machine Learning techniques to retrieve relevant results and provide optimal ranking.
        * Help design, develop, and support new "intelligence" features on a world-class search service that serves millions of requests daily on a diverse corpus of data including structured, unstructured and social feeds.
        * Analyze and understand the different products, devices and use cases that are driven by search across salesforce and develop strategies for improving relevancy.
        * Conduct extensive analysis/study of usage metrics and patterns to identify opportunities to improve relevancy of search.
        * Evangelize the use of machine learning by deploying models at scale and subsequently sharing impact from A/B testing (including interpretation of results).
        * Develop new ranking features and techniques build upon the latest results from the research community.
        * Contribute to the analysis of functional requirements

        * Master's or PhD in a relevant field and/or experience in any of the following is highly regarded: Machine learning and modeling techniques including classification, regression and Bayesian analysis.

        * Expertise in Computer Science fundamentals in data structures, algorithm design, and complexity analysis.
        * Iteratively analyzing data, integrating new data sources, experimenting and optimizing.
        * Excellent oral and written communication skills
