        Altamira Technologies is searching for talented Requirements Manager to work with the Government Task Manager (GTM), designated Subject Matter Experts (SME), and Systems Engineering and Technical support to collect system requirements. New system requirements to be implemented in new releases will be analyzed and introduced for consideration at the Technical Review Board (TRB) meetings. Responsible to formulate a Requirements Report supporting the efficient and complete capture of technical and system requirements.
        Altamira brings a commercial mindset to solving the most complex national security problems by delivering mission application development, multi-intelligence analysis, and data science technologies and solutions to the defense, intelligence, and homeland security communities. Altamira's culture of innovation and excellence, and mid-market-size, positions us as the premier next generation leader bringing technology solutions to mission.
        Job Responsibilities
        * Identify all requirements and whether they are currently implemented in or planned for the systems
        * Collect requirements per high level features identified by government or designated representative
        * Capture and manage all requirements in an automated requirements management tool, utilizing the required fields from the Requirements Report
        * Review all NGIC Web related ticket items and capture those items that introduce new system requirements
        * During sprint planning, report on new requirements using the Requirements Report at which point the government will review and prioritize requirements for the next release
        * Provide the number of system requirements not yet analyzed, and number of requirements awaiting review to the GTM during the Monthly MSR and Invoice Meetings
        Desired Qualifications
        * Active TS/SCI
        * BS and MS degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, Physics, or Mathematics
        * Experience in lieu of MS degree also acceptable.
        Required Skills
        * 10 years' experience designing and implementing information management systems
        * Subject matter expert in computer system support to enterprise intelligence production and dissemination systems
        Required Experience
        * Experience defining project objectives and requirements, establishing system architecture, ensuring information assurance compliance, directing, coordinating and completing project efforts, interfacing with government personnel, and providing progress reports
        * Experience with web services
        * Demonstrated recent, in-depth experience in product development, to include direct control and responsibility over subordinate developers working the development of a web-based, as well as data intensive system
