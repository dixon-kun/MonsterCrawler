Qualifications

* Bachelor's degree/Master's Degree in Computer Science, Design, Information Systems, Electrical Engineering or related discipline with excellent academic record

* Expert understanding of full-stack web development and common frameworks (e.g. Django, Ruby or JavaScript runtime platforms like Node.js)

* Strong knowledge of best practices for design and experience with data-driven UX research and responsive, clean UI development

* Experience with data visualization (e.g. d3) or a willingness to learn

* Experience with web testing, analytics, and KPI optimization

* Proven record of shipping cutting-edge, intuitive, responsive web applications

* Proven record of leadership in a work setting and/or through personal project portfolio

* Excellent attention to detail

* Excellent ability to work collaboratively across all levels and functions of a multi-functional, global organization

* Excellent ability to communicate complex ideas effectively – both verbally and in writing

Who You'll Work With

You will be working with McKinsey's Banking Advanced Analytics Solution team to develop leading edge analytics software for the banking industry.

Banking Advanced Analytics is part of McKinsey Solutions and McKinsey's New Ventures. Our work encompasses overall strategies and specific solutions for consumer banking and wholesale business; advanced analytics and client segments; organizational issues; performance improvement; and mergers and alliances. We also undertake work on client issues in marketing, risk management, branding and customer relationship management.

McKinsey helps a broad range of banking and consumer finance firms navigate a dynamically changing environment, in which regulatory constraints, consumer de-leveraging and evolving channel dynamics present significant obstacles to profitability. Our approach to supporting clients is customized and collaborative, and combines deep industry expertise and experience with proprietary quantitative analytics and research. We help banks build the capabilities they need to succeed in the long term.

New Ventures fosters innovation driven by analytics, design thinking, mobile and social by developing new products/services and integrating them into our client work. It is helping to shift our model toward asset-based consulting and is a foundation for –and expands our investment in –our entrepreneurial culture. Through innovative software as a service solutions, strategic acquisitions, and a vibrant ecosystem of alliances, we are redefining what it means to work with McKinsey.

As one of the fastest-growing parts of our firm, New Ventures has more than 1,000 dedicated professionals (including more than 800 data scientists and technologists) and we're hiring more mathematicians, data scientists, designers, software engineers, designers, product managers, client development managers and general managers.

What You'll Do

You will design and build the web platform that transforms and aggregates the advanced analytics performed by our data scientists into concise, accurate, and focused visualizations, dashboards, and tools.

You will work tol enhance customer acquisition and retention, fraud detection, customer service, and more for forward-thinking financial services clients. This is a unique opportunity for you to exercise your creativity, design and computer science skills whilst transforming the financial services industry.
