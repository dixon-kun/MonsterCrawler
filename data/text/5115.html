Application Systems Architect - DevOps-49007

Description

The Development Operations Architect will work collaboratively with the Continuous Integration and Development teams to boost the overall organizational performance. You will be looked to for setting and advancing our overall DevOps technical strategy. They will help architect, streamline and automate our operations and processes through build and test automation. Partner to build and maintain tools for deployment, systems monitoring and operational support; troubleshoot/resolve issues in our automated development, test and production environments. The role will provide leadership support in all areas of Development Operations and Software Configuration Management including configuration item identification, application build management, develop scripts for automation, develop and maintain branching strategies, provide configuration status accounting, and ensure configuration management procedures are followed. The Architect will be looked to for researching, recommending and developing innovative and progressive development operations solutions and providing justification for your proposed solutions. You will be expected to mentor other team members.  This role must be team oriented and possess a continuous improvement mindset that contributes to the overall success of our development operations vision.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Establishes and communicates a broad technical vision and encourages development teams to adhere to the principles, guidelines and standards that have been established.
  • In addition to creating the vision, the role is responsible for working with the development and operation teams to create and manage a roadmap to realize the vision. Works with IT projects, programs, and operations to ensure alignment with the overall strategy and objectives.
  • Anticipates future technology needs and business requirements to drive new technological approaches and tools.
  • Works with product vendors and 3rd party providers to evaluate options and tools.
  • Interacts and communicates effectively with internal and external customers to clarify business, operational, or technical requirements. Builds consensus across stakeholder groups including management and individual contributors.
  • Author internal documentation, such as environment diagrams, installation/configuration documents and presentations.
  • Assist in establishing and implementing configuration management program and policies.
  • Acts as a DevOps evangelist

Qualifications

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, or related discipline
  • 10+ years of experience in the development of high volume, mission critical applications in heterogeneous environments/architectures for multi-user systems
  • 5+ years of technical leadership and infrastructure architecture experience
  • Proven success in defining architectures that satisfy security, scalability, high availability, and network management/monitoring requirements
  • Strong understanding of current technology approaches, techniques and tools used for emerging trends, including data science and analytics, SOA, and cloud “-as-a-service” offerings
  • Must demonstrate proficiency in architecting large scale solutions using infrastructure automation techniques on “on premise” and hybrid cloud services: Microsoft Azure, AWS, etc
  • Experience with TFS Release Management, Octopus Deploy is required
  • Strong scripting skills with PowerShell, PowerShell DSC, Perl, Python
  • Up to 30% of domestic travel will be required

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience with TFS migrations is preferred.
  • Experience with other continuous integration and deployment automation tools andvarious container technologies is preferred.
  • Experience with relational databases and the design of highly available, distributed database applications.
  • Experience with Agile/Scrum development methodologies.
  • Experience with automated testing tools is preferred.
  • Outstanding oral, written, presentation and listening communication skills.
  • Expertise in a broad set of technologies and tools: C#, .NET, Java EE, EJB, MSMQ, JMS, IIS, Python, Octopus, Jenkins, Puppet, Chef, GO, Docker, etc.

Professional Skills:

  • Ability to think and operate as a team player
  • Proven track record of project delivery results.
  • Extremely strong interpersonal and communication skills (written and verbal) with the ability to assess and clearly communicate technical and project risk.
  • Ability to act as a consultant and provide recommendations to all project teams.
  • Ability to operate as independently or as part of collaborative effort
  • Demonstrate strong project leadership and ability to develop strong working relationships.
  • The candidate must be able to learn quickly, work in a fast-paced, team-driven environment and have the ability to communicate well with technical and non-technical personnel.
  • Willing to work after hours and weekends (as needed) in order to oversee deployment, troubleshoot if needed.
  • Must be able to multi-task, manage details and keep sight of overall objectives.
