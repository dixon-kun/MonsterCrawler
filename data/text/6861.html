Position Description/Requirements

A Technical Solution Engineer is required to have broad functional understanding and working knowledge of platforms, operating systems, load balancers, databases, storage, networking, containers and clouds in addition to excellent troubleshooting and problem solving skills.

Minimum of 10 years of experience in IT infrastructure and operations architecting, designing, implementing and supporting high volume, complex enterprise applications using a mix of technologies such as Java EE, WebSphere, Tomcat/WebSphere Liberty Profile, Web Tier (F5/Apache), Coherence, Gemfire, Oracle, Data Reporting/Analytics (Crystal, Actuate IHub), Docker, Storage (EMC Isilon) in a virtualized Linux environment.

  • At least 7 years of Java Application Server WebSphere/Tomcat experience (certifications desirable)
  • Systems Engineering/Administration experience designing, building and configuring system infrastructures in support of web-based/cloud applications
  • Strong functional knowledge of and hands-on experience with operating systems and scripting
  • Must possess strong analytical, problem solving skills, excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Must possess a strong work ethic and be willing & available to work extended hours and weekends as necessary to complete assigned tasks and projects as well attend specially scheduled events such as weekend performance tests or major technology upgrades, major releases and client migrations.
  • Must be a team player who freely and openly shares work related information with co-workers.
  • Must be open to others suggestions and must be willing to provide input to others for improvement
  • Must be comfortable supporting and working closely with, in a professional manner with cross functional teams comprised of personnel from Infrastructure & Operations, Development, Quality Assurance, and End user communities
  • Must be available to provide L3 level support to troubleshoot and resolve system problems of a complex nature specifically after changes were made by the Solution Engineering Team such as modifying an existing solution, adding capacity or implementing a new solution.
  • Must possess strong knowledge of Microsoft office products, Visio, PowerPoint and effectively utilizing these tools to develop system documentation such as run books, system topology diagrams, hardware/software specifications
  • Must be capable of working effectively in a fast-paced demanding environment with conflicting and changing priorities individually as well as part of the team receiving appropriate amount of supervision
  • Must be able to prioritize and effectively manage time supporting multiple concurrent efforts
  • Must be able to visit and effectively collaborate with various groups within R&D organizations both onshore and offshore in a true DevOps spirit
