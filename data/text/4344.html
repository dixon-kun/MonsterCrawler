Altamira brings a commercial mindset to solving the most complex national security problems by delivering mission application development, multi-intelligence analysis, and data science technologies and solutions to the defense, intelligence, and homeland security communities.  Altamira’s culture of innovation and excellence, and mid-market-size, positions us as the premier next generation leader bringing technology solutions to mission.

We are seeking a candidate to work in support of the help desk supporting the AFLCMC CSS program. To perform in this position you must gather and analyze data, present analysis, propose and implement solutions; design and implement high quality Operating System components; and work closely with project leads and other engineers

The ideal candidate will have an excellent understanding of Operating Systems; familiarity with virtualization platforms; 3 or more years of in-depth experience in one or more areas, such as file systems, memory and CPU management, compression algorithms, drivers; and knowledge of one or more computer hardware architectures.

Primary Duties/Responsibilities:

  • Expert Engineering support of the AF system/networks.
  • Incident investigation, diagnosis, and resolution as a member of a team in a 24-hour per day/7-days per week/365 days per year support environment.
  • Incident management via the use of the AF Enterprise Information Technology Service Management (EITSM) system,
  • Support of both secured and unsecured technologies including classified and unclassified incident management

Skills Required:

Mandatory:

Bachelor's degree and 4-6 years relevant IT experience; or 7 to 9 years experience without a degree

  • ITIL v3, IAT II, Security+,
  • MCSA SRV 2008 and 2012

Desirable:

  • Familiarity/Experience with working in a high-security environment.
