Move currently has an opportunity for a Senior Data Scientist located in our Santa Clara, California office. At Move Inc. data and analytics is the core of our business and growth strategy. we collect and process terabytes of data every day, transforming that data into information that powers decisions for millions of real estate offices and consumers across the country. We combine real estate property data with active listing data and track consumer behavior on our sites.

The Data Scientist will serve as the lead analytics and data science partner to Realtor.coms Marketing Team. He or she will work on strategic projects that span from using data to design and optimize our website and mobile apps as well as support and improve our Marketing, Financial Planning & Analysis and Sales functions. The person will be responsible for driving end to end projects entailing opportunity identification and sizing, data discovery, performance measurement, statistical modeling, analytics, storytelling with data and helping automate KPI tracking and models. The right individual should have an ability to apply sophisticated analytical acumen to a wide range of complex business problems within a consumer internet business, a willingness to assume responsibility for a broad range of evolving priorities, a strong analytic foundation within an analytics oriented role, and a proven track record of creating value by facilitating data-driven decision making across an organization and with executive-level constituents. Ideally, this individual should be a catalyst within the marketing team to imagine and implement data driven analysis and initiatives that improve marketings overall ability to achievement established goals.

Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with business teams to identify critical business problems and areas of opportunity and translate business questions into quantitative analyses
  • Work with senior leaders to understand key business problems, define KPIs to track performance and offer recommendations to optimize business performance
  • Create highly sophisticated analytic frameworks and functional models to forecast the business and provide recommendations for solving key business problems, while growing/optimizing revenues and profits.
  • Key areas of focus will include: business line strategy, consumer audience acquisition and engagement, marketing channel ROI, page yield and inventory optimization, product line pricing and analyses, sales and sales channel analytics and decision support, customer ROI and value analyses, and analysis, etc.
  • Lead and drive end to end data initiatives including working with data engineers to automate tracking, measurements and models
  • Drive A/B, multivariate tests and design of experiments to facilitate testing of new product and marketing initiatives, with focus on improving acquisition, engagement, retention, and conversion.
  • Select, apply, and tune a diverse set of tools to coherently solve challenging business goals
  • Create automated learning systems that gracefully scale to increasing complexity and expectation
  • Develop predictive, explanatory models and machine learning algorithms
  • Generate descriptive visualizations and presentations to communicate insights
  • Be partner to business, marketing and product team in helping define and scope larger business intelligence and technology initiatives to support business goals
  • Work with a sense of ownership and urgency, advocate for experimentation based, agile culture

Education, Skills and Experience:

  • MS or PhD in statistics, mathematics, computer science, quantitative analysis, economics or related field is required.
  • 5+ years of relevant experience in data science, data analytics, or applied statistics,
  • 3+ years experience with machine learning, data mining, statistical modeling tools and underlying algorithms
  • 3+ years experience in R, Perl, Python, Spark, or other languages and frameworks appropriate for large scale analysis of numerical, textual, image, and video data
  • Working experience with relational databases and SQL
  • Experience with experiment design and A/B and multivariate tests
  • Experience and proven track record developing online data products
  • Strong creative thinking and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent oral and written communication and presentation skills
  • Experience managing, directing or interfacing with technical resources with broad, multi-disciplinary expertise.
  • Experience applying advanced analytic disciplines to financial, consumer audience/user experience, and product-level modeling, forecasting, pricing, etc.
  • Proficiency with SQL, Excel (pivot tables, lookups, data linking), and PowerPoint (advanced graphing and presentation).
  • Strong SQL skills and experience with one or more BI/analytics tools like Omniture, Microstrategy or Tableau
  • Ability to organize, direct and develop personnel, and to plan and coordinate timely accomplishments of responsibilities.
  • Extensive business partnering and influencing skills.
  • Superior presentation and communication skills; mature presence coupled with emotional intelligence
  • Results driven and ability to coordinate and prioritize multiple tasks in a fast-paced environment
