Do you have a passion for building a better community? Come join our amazingly talented and innovative team that is changing the way your local government works.   If you are interested in top notch customer service, network administration, wireless networks, desktop visualization, webpage story development or helping your community by changing raw data into useful information, we have an opportunity for you.

The Town of Cary is committed to service excellence in the delivery of all of its programs. The Town strives to meet the needs of citizens in a cost-effective and efficient manner. In fact, Cary is recognized for innovation in program and service delivery. With a vast range of program and service offerings, the Town continuously improves on the delivery of our programs and services.

As a Technology Services Intern, you will have the opportunity to be part of a team working with experienced Town staff to solve some information challenges and support technology initiatives. you will be working with experienced Town staff to solve information challenges.

Assignments will be located in Cary offices located within Wake County. This position will be appealing to those interested in applying a well-rounded approach to information technology and innovation.

Work schedules can be adjusted to meet school demands but will not exceed 20 hours per week.

Job Type: Temporary, Part-Time
