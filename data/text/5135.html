The Job

Our engineering team solves tough problems and invents new ways to battle fraud using big data and audio science in the cloud.  We are looking for a Lead Test Automation Engineer within the Engineering team to help define, design, and manage the testing strategy for our bleeding edge products. We have technically challenging problems to be solved and must demonstrate thought leadership in the voice security space, so we need a leader who can execute our strategy on a daily basis by inspiring and leading a high caliber software test engineering team.

Responsibilities

  • Lead a team of test engineers focussed on improving product quality
  • Responsible for developing a comprehensive test strategy and test implementation plan and implementing test frameworks/harnesses to facilitate rapid test creation
  • Establish close working relationships with existing technical leads and staff to understand architecture and challenges with validating it
  • Improve the team’s technical ability, utilization, and overall efficiency by establishing best practices and creating individual development plans
  • Expand solution capabilities, enabling new features driven by technological innovation that will lead the voice security market
  • Improve and drive the system testing and implementation of technology related products
  • Continuous improvement in process and implementing controls to increase efficiency and productivity of engineering practices

Skills

  • 5+ years member of a system test engineering team
  • Technical experience leading test architecture and automation required
  • Experience/exposure to VoIP and/or web application testing strongly desired
  • Prior experience in a start-up environment strongly desired
  • Experience developing or implementing a new test framework and strategy from ground up desired
  • Proven track record of successful and timely product delivery, and of driving technical innovation
  • Linux experience a must
  • Strong experience in continuous integration (Jenkins, Travis, etc.)
  • Python, Go or C++ experience preferred
  • Agile methodologies knowledge helpful
  • Ability to deal with ambiguity in a fast-paced dynamic environment.
  • Strong communication skills, both inward and outward
  • BS in CS or EE or relevant technical discipline

Our Bio

Founded in 2011  |  Raised $122 million

Venture backed by Andreessen Horowitz, Google Capital, IVP, Citi Ventures, Felicis Ventures, & GV

Pindrop is the pioneer in voice fraud prevention and authentication. We provide enterprise solutions to reduce fraud losses and authentication expense for some of the largest call centers in the world. Pindrop’s patented Phoneprinting™ technology can identify, locate and authenticate phone devices uniquely just from the call audio thereby detecting fraudulent calls as well as verifying legitimate callers. We have been selected by the world’s largest banks, insurers, brokerages and retailers, detecting over 80% of fraud even for attackers never seen before. Pindrop’s solutions are allowing customers to reduce call time and improve their customers’ experience even while reducing fraud losses.
