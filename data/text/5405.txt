Define and implement best practices and standard operating procedures for the Data Engineering team. Work collaboratively with the Director of Analytics and Managers of, Performance Consultants, Data Engineers, Analytics Governance, and Analytics Operations to develop and implement an agile analytics process by continuously improving methods and procedures.
