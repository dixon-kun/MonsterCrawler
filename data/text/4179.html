Position Title: Director of Data Science
Location: San Francisco, CA (Candidates can also be in Milpitas, CA or Reston, VA. Exceptional remote candidates can be considered.)
The Company:
FireEye is the intelligence-led security company. Working as a seamless, scalable extension of customer security operations, FireEye offers a single platform that blends innovative security technologies, nation-state grade threat intelligence, and world-renowned Mandiant® consulting. With this approach, FireEye eliminates the complexity and burden of cyber security for organizations struggling to prepare for, prevent, and respond to cyber attacks. FireEye has over 5,300 customers across 67 countries, including more than 825 of the Forbes Global 2000.
The Role:
If you dream of a job working in a field where all of the hard problems are solved and you get to re-use previous research to get your job done, you need not apply. If, however, you are someone who wants to tackle problems that truly are on the cutting edge, then we encourage you to keep reading.
Data Science is an emerging field within cyber security. FireEye, given its deep expertise and comprehensive view on the advanced threat landscape,is uniquely positioned to enable data scientists to have major impact within our industry, company, and across our customer base. Detecting security breaches using machine learning and data analytics is an unsolved problem (this isn’t hand writing recognition folks), and has huge potential. If you are someone who wants to be on the cutting edge of a high profile industry, who wants to make an impact by pushing both the field of data science and cyber security forward, you may be the perfect candidate to help us on our mission.
Responsibilities:
Asthe Director ofData Scienceat FireEye,you will be defining and executing company-wide data driven strategies.You will beleading andperforming research in areas that have the greatest impact to the company. That can range from adding new analytic capabilities to existing FireEye products and services to generating newproducts altogether.You will be expected to prove out your ideas by building proof of concepts, working with subject matter experts to refine models, working with software engineers to ensure models are implemented well, and finally following through to ensure the analytics capabilities are functioning as expected. Being able to exchange ideas, prioritize projects, allocate resources,ask questions and the ability to work with and communicate clearly to non-data scientists are all musts.
  • Manage andprovide technical leadership and research strategy for a mission-focused and high performing team of data scientists and engineers
  • Lead the continued growth and innovation of our Data Science team
  • Partner with Product, Marketing, Consulting, and other teams to ensure value and competitive leadership
  • Drive effective research of data analysis to improve our incident response capabilities
  • Manage a portfolio of technical R&D projects
  • Build proof of concepts and refined data analysis models
  • Stimulate machine learning advancements
  • Operate as a thought leader in the data sciencefor cyber securitycommunity, publishing novel research results
Requirements:
  • Experience in applying a variety of unsupervised and supervised machine learning techniques to security problems
  • Familiar with tools for processing and modeling large volumes of data, and the ability to turn big data into actionable intelligence
  • Experience with adversarial machine learning and how models can be circumvented
  • Ability to drive the research lifecycle from inception to polished deliverable
  • Ability to document and explain technical details clearly and concisely to all audiences (technical, non-technical, executive leadership)
  • B.S. inComputer Scienceor Statistics, orequivalentexperience (M.S. and Ph.D. are preferred)
  • Minimum of 8 years of experience required
  • 3+ years of experience providing technical leadership and directly managing data scientists and engineers
  • Proven experience leading development teams working with Python, Go, C, C++ and Scala
  • Strongwrittenand verbal communication skills
Additional Qualifications:
  • Previousexperience with deploying and managing machine learning solutions at scale
  • Experience with software packages for analyzing and visualizing data
  • Experience with Hadoop and Spark and the hardware to support different tasks (incl. GPUs)
