Senior Quantitative Analyst-Predictive Modeling at SNI Financial

Boston, MA

Report this job

About the Job


Senior Quantitative Analyst| Predictive Modeling |
My client is an independently owned financial technology business with operations moving to Boston, specializing in delivering data driven insights supporting pricing, valuations, lending, funding and investments. This is a dynamic and innovative data driven organization founded as a start-up over 5 years ago; my client has experienced impressive success, including year on year growth and an expanding product portfolio.

As a Senior Quantitative Analyst you will be working with real time data, using various statistical modeling techniques such as sampling, logistic regression, linear regression, decision trees, neural networks and time series analysis to develop sophisticated predictive models and to drive insights and recommendations. We need someone who can be "hands on", who will be given large data sets and then will lead the charge in figuring out what the data says that is relevant to small/mid-size banks. This is the 1 st true "data science" role and this critical position will be the one paving the path to the future and success of the company.

Key Accountabilities;

Quantitative modeling to support pricing, valuations, lending, funding and investments
Using various statistical techniques to develop models from scratch such as regression, neural networks and time series analysis
End to end model development and design from data preparation to the build, validation and calibration of predictive models
Development of risk scorecards
Segmentation analysis
Analytical research and development
Experience Required;

Proven experience ( minimum 5 years +) in working in a data rich environment with data mining techniques and predictive analytics
Understanding of statistical methodology and modeling including regression, time series analysis and neural networks
Strong programming skills in SQL preferred although consideration will also be given to candidate with strong SAS skills
Financial services experience preferred
Tertiary qualified in a relevant disciple such as Statistics, Mathematics; a Post Grad qualification in addition to this is highly desirable
The Environment

Sociable, smart, successful and capable team
Agile and dynamic environment,
An organization that values innovative and creative analytical thinking; a great opportunity to apply this in a modeling environment
