Pattern Energy Group is an independent, fully integrated energy company that develops, constructs, owns and operates renewable energy projects and transmission assets across North America, Japan, and parts of Latin America.  The company focuses primarily on wind, solar and transmission. The Pattern Energy Group team has a history as one of the top North American renewable energy and transmission providers in the industry. The team is dedicated to delivering the highest value for its customers, partners, financial supporters and the communities in which it works, while exhibiting a strong commitment to promoting environmental stewardship and corporate responsibility.

Pattern Energy Group operates in the United States, Canada, Japan, Chile, and Mexico with offices in San Francisco, Houston, San Diego, New York, Santiago, Tokyo, and Toronto.  Pattern Energy Group’s corporate headquarters is in San Francisco.


JOB PURPOSE

The purpose of the Manager of Data Analytics is to oversee development and maintenance of the reporting and analytics tools critical for assessing the technical performance and reliability of the Pattern operational fleet. This includes developing the automation of key performance indicators for the Pattern fleet, and integrating software and solutions that improve Pattern’s ability to identify and remediate technical issues. This role requires cross functional interaction across the company and oversight of contractors for multiple data systems and software platforms.

Key Accountabilities

The key responsibilities will include the following:

  • Manage a small team responsible for building reports, developing BI tools, performing ad hoc analyses, and writing programming logic tailored to operations and engineering needs. This role will also be responsible with directly participating in these tasks when needed.
  • Oversee the development, implementation, & maintenance of Pattern’s data architecture and associated software.
  • Identify new growth opportunities, and leverage contractors, service providers, and other internal resources to create sustainable and reliable solutions for Pattern’s business needs.
  • Work within cross functional teams aimed at requirements gathering for new systems and existing system improvements for an international organization that finds the best solution given business constraints.
  • Manage routine availability, energy, and performance reporting for the Pattern fleet.
  • Assist with ad hoc reports and analysis as required.
  • Manage Pattern’s Operations & Maintenance Model for long term forecasting and business projections.
  • Manage Pattern’s service provider availability calculations as per power plant contractual agreements.

Educational Experience

  • A Bachelor’s degree in engineering, computer science, or related field is required as a minimum.
  • Master’s degree preferred

Work Experience

  • 5+ years of experience in data analytics, data visualization, and/or data science
  • 2+ years of experience in wind power operations or Electric Utility operations preferred but not required
  • Experience managing a team of data analysts or developers preferred

Additional Qualifications

  • Strong technical capabilities and a creative, curious, self-driven work ethic
  • Ability to work well in and/or lead cross functional teams with little supervision or oversight
  • Ability to travel up to 10% or as needed.

Technical Skills

  • Demonstrated programming skills with applications, databases, and languages, including:
  • SQL query programming and data structure
  • Tableau or other business intelligence platforms
  • Python, R, or other data science languages and applications
  • SharePoint list organization and workflow management
  • Demonstrated excellence and ability to learn new programming skills and languages.
  • Familiarity with SCADA communication and architecture
  • Ability to occasionally perform physical requirements including standing, sitting, walking, kneeling, twisting, reaching, and climbing at project locations in order to install or inspect equipment.
