         As a member of the Fraud Management Solution Testing Team, you will perform software testing activities for the SAS Fraud Management software product. SAS Fraud Management is the most complete fraud detection system available in the market today and it enables financial institutions to protect their customers and reduce fraud losses by scoring 100% of all transactions in real-time at the point of sale.
         In this role, you will work with limited requirements or design specifications to create unique test cases in accordance with criteria reflecting real customer environments. Your duties will include scoping, planning and executing testing work for new features and reporting issues in the defect system. While some of the work will be exploratory in nature, other critical&#160;duties will involve developing and documenting usage test cases, functional testing, REST api testing, validating output,&#160;test automation, Continuous Integration and end-to-end testing.
