Description

Wyndham Destination Network is a pioneer in the sharing economy, offering travelers a place to feel at home no matter where their journey takes them.  One of three hospitality business units of Wyndham Worldwide , we have been helping travelers vacation the way they want for more than 70 years, and with a network of more than 112,000 unique properties in more than 100 countries, the options are virtually endless.
From cottages and condos to castles and villas, we offer an unmatched network of professionally managed, unique vacation accommodations. Wyndham Destination Network’s trusted brands give travelers the peace-of-mind to vacation the way they want. Over the past 70 years, we’ve built our brands around a culture of service to positively impact our communities and the environment.
Our network of affiliated timeshare properties and vacation rental properties fall under more than a dozen brands including: Wyndham Vacation Rentals, RCI, Hoseasons, cottages.com, James Villa Holidays, The Registry Collection program, Landal GreenParks, and Novasol. Last year, these brands sent more than 13 million travelers on vacation.
The WDN Analytics Department is currently looking for a Business Analyst to work in our Parsippany, NJ headquarters within our Advanced Analytics team.  The Advanced Analytics team is responsible to help drive business strategy and execution by delivering analytical decision support solutions and ad hoc analytical analyses.  Our aim is to provide ongoing recommendations and insights to decision makers and business leaders based on data, analysis and modeling.  We work with a variety of business units and functional areas, including Revenue Management, Marketing, Finance, and Business Development.  We are a fast paced team with tremendous opportunity for learning, rewards and recognition for the right candidate.
Key Responsibilities:
  • Develop strong understanding of WDN’s lines of business, business rules and processes.
  • Develop strong understanding of Advanced Analytics teams development process, technology infrastructure and data architecture.
  • Translate client business needs into technical operational requirements
  • Design business and technical solutions and process improvements across a wide variety of business applications
  • Support the delivery of our analytical solutions including gathering, understanding and tracking client requirements, documenting progress towards milestones, and executing testing procedures.
  • Communicate project status to stakeholders across the business



Qualifications

Basic
Qualifications :
  • Bachelor's degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Management Science, Business, Economics, Engineering, or related field required.
  • 0-3 years of related experience desired.
  • Educational or work experience in using technology to solve business problems is required.
  • Ability to combine technical expertise with business acumen and communication skill to design, develop and communicate solutions to complex business problems is required.
  • Excellent oral and written communication skills.
Preferred
Qualifications :
  • Business coursework is a plus.
  • Experience/knowledge of programming languages such as SAS, SQL, VBA is a plus.
  • Educational or work experience in utilizing quantitative data analysis, statistics & data mining to solve business problems is a plus.  Experience with data science models or optimization modeling is a plus.
