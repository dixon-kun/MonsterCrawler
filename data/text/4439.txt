          Senior Manager: &#160;Automation through Data Analytics
         If you're passionate about e-commerce and creating scalable processes that will drive our business and elevate customer, supplier, and employee experiences, we are looking for you to join our Product Catalog team.
         Senior Managers on our team must be able to transform data into insights and strategy to improve the throughput and quality of products in the Wayfair online catalog. They each lead a medium sized team of analysts and managers to help them improve upon the way we bring products to market.&#160; They work cross functionally with Executive Level Leadership, making appropriate project related tradeoffs across brands, and measuring success of the team&#8217;s initiatives.
          This Specific Leader Will:
          Learn the intricacies of Wayfair&#8217;s merchandising processes to become a subject matter expert.
          Drive an agenda of automation and quality improvement leveraging the expertise of our Data Science team
          Validate the results and quantifying the value add of the improvements made.
          Example Projects Include:
          Mining the supplier provided &#8220;blob&#8221; of romance copy into structured data tags that can power storefront filters
          Using image recognition to identify potential duplicate products and likely option variants (eg a new color of an existing product)
          Using image recognition to tag attributes of an image (eg is it a &#190; angle shot, is it in a holiday setting, is the lamp on or off)
          Mining product data to determine the appropriate class of product
         In all these cases this leader will be handling the project management and validation work, while the Data Science team will be developing the algorithms.

          Process-oriented with strong analytical and communication skills

          Ability to manage through ambiguity, stress, adversity and conflict to establish focus and deliver against business goals
