           &#8211; Decision Science works on problems and data sets across the Workday organization: anomaly detection, time series forecasting, clustering, classification, natural language processing, customer segmentation, just to name a few. Your work will inform critical product and operational decisions, and will also be built into the product to directly improve customer experience. In addition to projects, you will be able to learn from fellow top notch data scientists and engineers, follow and share cutting-edge machine learning and deep learning research, and publish academic papers and patents.
            Tech:
