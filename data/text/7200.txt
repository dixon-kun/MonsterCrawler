        Must Haves:
        * Education: Bachelor's Degree
        * 5+ years of professional experience in commercial software development
        * Familiarity and experience with SQL querying and database design
        * Professional experience coding quantitative or statistical algorithms in R, Python, or a related high-level language
        Even Better:
        * Professional experience at a startup or developing a new product
        * Full-stack development experience

        * Familiarity with AWS, Docker, Linux systems administration, DevOps experience
