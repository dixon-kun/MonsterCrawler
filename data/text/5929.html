Position overview:

Hawaiian Airlines Revenue Analytics is seeking a high-performing individual and team player to lead mathematical modeling and optimization projects across teams department-wide. This position provides advanced analytical products and services, specializing in creating innovative data-driven decision-support tools to optimize business decisions, through application of statistical and mathematical modeling.

Key Responsibilities:

* Provide analytical consultation, statistical/mathematical modeling, and solutions to achieve business objectives

* Research, design, develop, implement, and support decision-science models.

* Conduct research on analytical techniques and translate that research into usable and sustainable solutions for users of production systems

* Perform data collection, data cleaning, data validation, and reporting tasks for analytical systems and applications

* Perform exploratory data analysis to identify patterns and trends in customer behavior

* Lead the analysis and validation of statistical, forecasting, and optimization models

* Communicate analytical results to business partners and clients

* Serve as technical consultant in the fields of operations research, data science, decision analytics, and applied mathematics.

* Design and develops advanced analytical data mining, forecasting, estimation, simulation, and/or optimization models

* Provide science calibration support for decision-science models

* Identify and apply best practices in field of advanced analytics

Requirements

Minimum Requirements:

* Master's degree in Statistics, Operations Research, Econometrics, Applied Mathematics, Machine Learning, Decision Science, Industrial Engineering, or related field

* 5+ years of experience in applying advanced analytics to support business decisions, yielding measured outcomes improvement

* Expert‚Äêlevel fluency in applying advance multivariate statistical analysis (logistic & linear regression, decision tree, cluster analysis, ANOVA) to categorize, classify, and detect relationships in large datasets

* Proficiency in extracting and cleansing large datasets from different data sources

* Proficiency in programming with knowledge of SQL, R, SAS, Python, or other similar languages

* Ability to understand complex business requirements and define key performance metrics to measure benefits and communicate complex quantitative analysis in a clear, precise, and actionable manner

Preferred qualifications:

* Ph.D. in Statistics, Operations Research, Econometrics, Applied Mathematics, Machine Learning, Decision Science, Industrial Engineering, or related field

* Strong grasp of revenue management theory and concepts

* Experience leading diverse teams and interacting with vendors

* Demonstrated familiarity with the airline or transportation industries
