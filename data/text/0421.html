We are looking for a Presentation Specialist to help support the North American Business Development initiatives, and North American CEO with internal and external presentations.

This Presentations Specialist requires an advanced design skillset, and the ability to efficiently and accurately conceptualize, create and format documents such as pitch decks, proposals and other types of presentations, using the Microsoft Office suite of applications (PowerPoint and Word). They should also have knowledge of advanced applications, such as, Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator, and/or other specialized applications like Keynote.

This right candidate will report to the Director of Business Development and will have close working relationships with the other Business Development leads in the region and globally. This role will be based out of our New York office.


  • Accurately complete all assigned jobs within the allocated timeframe and in line with specified formatting and layout standards
  • Develop client templates and alternative creative options as required
  • Take ownership of any discrepancies in the deck and have a keen eye for fixing errors like spelling, formatting,
  • Develop understanding of Wunderman global branding and strategy and identity in order to continuously improve service provision
  • Keep New Business lead updated on progress, advising immediately of any delays or issues
  • Receive feedback on completed work and apply this to continuous improvements in quality output.
  • Develop capabilities for innovation and service excellence
  • Develop knowledge of processes and agency roles in the interest of improving understanding of document requirements and of providing output excellence
  • Understand quality and timeliness performance metrics, within the context of continuous improvement of quality and service.
  • Maintain a consistently high level of job accuracy and efficiency
  • Take ownership of your performance and seek to excel wherever possible
  • Maintain performance within established team procedures and policies

  • Has good oral and written communication skills must be able to communicate/collaborate virtually and in person with internal teams.
  • Has excellent presentation and layout skills
  • Is adaptable, flexible and enjoys working in a team environment
  • Shows ownership and initiative
  • Has the ability to multi-task
  • Accuracy, attention to detail and ability to proof own work / work with proofreading team where applicable
  • Has prior experience at an advertising agency, within new business / business development is a bonus
  • Displays enthusiasm to support the team whenever/wherever necessary – sometimes over and above normal work hours and listed tasks.

About Wunderman

Wunderman is Creatively Driven. Data Inspired . A leading global digital agency, Wunderman combines creativity and data into work that inspires people to take action and delivers results for brands. In 2015, industry analysts named Wunderman a leader in marketing database operations as well as a strong performer in customer engagement strategy.

Headquartered in New York, the agency brings together 7,000 creatives, data scientists, strategists and technologists in 175 offices in 60 markets. Wunderman is a WPP company (NASDAQ: WPPGY). For more information, please visit www.wunderman.com and follow us @Wunderman.
