U.S. Bank is seeking an Enterprise Security Architect to join our team in Atlanta, GA. The Enterprise Security Architect will provide leadership on definition of security architecture, including the development and implementation of effective security administration processes for all platforms, solutions and products. This role will ensure alignment of Information Security initiatives and services with business and technology systems and processes. They will support the Information Security team by engaging peers, the business and technical stakeholders to develop and drive strategy for the Information Security program. This includes technology and process capability maturity assessments, liaising with security teams, business units, technical groups and management to define and manage requirements for development or purchase of technology, platforms, and processes.

* Develop and recommend security designs and frameworks for technology systems by defining process and architecture for creating secure networks, systems, and applications.

* Represents the Information Security team in the Enterprise Architecture (EA) program to align security strategy and raise awareness of technological business requirements and drivers.

* Serve as an internal Information Security consultant to the organization with the ability and the finesse to influence and drive better security throughout the organization.

* Participate on projects and recommend secure solutions based on policies, standards and best practices.

* Maintain personal and management awareness of emerging industry security technologies, technology issues, regulatory issues and practices.

* Provide Information Security subject matter expertise in risk, technology, and security assessments. Establishes and maintains risk management capabilities and processes that are implemented in a eGRC tool.

* Ability to weigh business needs against security concerns and recommends necessary changes to enhance information systems security.

* Help develop templates to drive consistency with security teams at each layer (e.g. conceptual, logical, physical, etc.).

* Consult with business lines and IT Development Groups on the development of Information Security questions, design and consulting.

* Can communicate to executives and business partners. Provides subject matter expertise for customer initiated audits of business security practices.

* Has experience using common architectural reference models and tools such as TOGAF and SABSA.

* Understands and can implement information security risk management (ISO\IEC 27001:2013, Octave-Allegro, ISO\IEC 31000, FAIR).

* Has experience in and understands eGRC applications and processes. Consumes security issues and findings from multiple risk partners and sources and translates to business risks to support the security and information strategy and roadmap.

* Worked with data science technologies such as Data Mining, Predictive Analytics, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing and Data Visualization/Presentation.

* Fluency with SQL on one or more relational databases such as Microsoft SQL Server, MySQL, Postgres or Oracle.
