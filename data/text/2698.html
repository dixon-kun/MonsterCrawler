POSITION OVERVIEW

Are you passionate about solving complex puzzles and problems? Do you get excited by extreme data sets and analyzing data from the Internet of Things? A rapidly expanding, entrepreneurial technology company, Alarm.com is seeking ambitious, hard working, college graduates with strong academic qualifications, diverse interests, and proven experiences in a dynamic team environment to become Data Analysts, supporting internal and external customers. Very few small companies innovate and develop across as broad of a range of technologies as Alarm.com.

This position is ideal for the candidate who seeks a small, friendly company culture where one can work closely with smart and highly productive people across a very interesting spectrum of technologies to develop outstanding consumer products.

RESPONSIBILTIES

The Data Analyst's primary job responsibilities will include:

* Interpret data, analyze results using statistical techniques and provide ongoing reports. Execute quantitative analyses that translate data into actionable insights. Provide analytical and data-driven decision-making support for key projects.

* Develop and automate reports, solving for the Business Intelligence (BI) and analytical needs of our full range of stakeholders. Work with stakeholders to establish the right metrics for monitoring the business.

* Work with stakeholder to gather functional requirements and work with engineering to help deliver data driven solutions to stakeholders

* Communicate findings on a regular basis to all levels of business and management.

* Filter and "clean" data, and review computer reports, printouts, and performance indicators to locate and correct code problems

* Pull and model data from internal data warehouses in order to create quantitative models of business trends and, where relevant, implement process improvements.

Experienced Responsibilities

* Developing strategic initiatives to improve usage among the customer base

* Improving processes including but not limited to customer education, technical education, sales education, and mobile-web interaction

* Help train internal and external stakeholders on utilizing reporting tools and analyzing results.

* Set standards for data management and conceive projects needed to eliminate the gap between current state and future goals

* Assist in defining and driving standards for data management and deliver projects needed to eliminate the gap between current state and future goals

College Grad

* BA/BS in Computer Science, Management Information Systems, Data Science, Math, Physics, Engineering, Statistics or other technical field. Advanced degrees preferred.

* Experience in SQL and familiarity with other programming languages. Some development experience in at least one scripting language (PHP, Python, Perl, etc.)

* Familiarity with business intelligence tools (MicroStrategy, Tableau, Qlik, BOBJ, Cognos) and statistical package such as R, Python, etc.

* Demonstrated success when working cross-functionally and is able to translate technical content to a general audience.

* Understanding of Data Management and the data lifecycle.

* Excellent written/oral communication and interpersonal skills

* Strong problem solving skills

* An interest in technology

* Willingness to learn new skills and tools and flexibility to accept a wide range of responsibilities in our small company, entrepreneurial environment.

* Highly motivated, self-starter that has ability to operate under minimal supervision, Ability to work on multiple projects simultaneously in a small, fast-paced environment

Experienced

* 2+ years of post-college work in a quantitative role.

* Fluency in SQL or other programming languages. Some development experience in at least one scripting language (PHP, Python, Perl, etc.)

* Advanced understanding of statistical analysis.

* Experience with a statistical package such as R, Python, etc.

* Demonstrable experience successfully managing complex cross-functional projects.

* Strong hands-on experience with data warehousing, data modeling, dashboarding and reporting, involving very large datasets and multiple data sources, with ability to interpret data and produce meaningful insights.

* Ability to communicate with both business and technical counterparts, including the skill of expressing the tracking needs to be implemented/developed in detail.

* Previous experience in a technical or business position (Internship/Co-Op Included)

* Technical expertise regarding data models, database design development, data mining and segmentation techniques

* Familiarity with big data tools and NoSQL platforms including

Senior Experienced

* 5+ years working as a data analyst or data engineer

* Strong project management skills and ability to manage multiple and diverse stakeholders across businesses and leadership levels.

* Proven track record of turning data into meaningful analysis, presenting to business stakeholders, and driving value for the business. Ability to see the big picture and to focus on details when necessary.

* Advanced knowledge and experience in topics of data and information management

* Hands on experience with Big Data / NoSQL Platforms with experience delivering production projects at scale.

COMPANY INFORMATION

Alarm.com is the leading platform solution for the connected home and business. Millions of people depend on Alarm.com's technology to monitor and control their property from anywhere. Centered on security and remote monitoring, our platform addresses a wide range of market needs and enables application-based control for a growing variety of Internet of Things (IoT) devices. Our security, video monitoring, intelligent automation and energy management solutions are available through our network of thousands of professional service providers in North America and around the globe. Alarm.com's common stock is traded on the Nasdaq under the ticker symbol ALRM.

Alarm.com earned the Top Workplace™ award for its employee culture and the meaningful work we do every day to develop and bring to market new technology that will make millions of people safer and help them use energy more efficiently.
