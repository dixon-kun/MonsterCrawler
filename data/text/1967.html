Key Roles and Responsibilities:

• Simultaneously manage multiple, complex survey, mixed-methods, and data science projects

• Ensure the quality and timeliness of work and the completion of the project within budget with limited direction from senior staff.
• Promote business development by supporting the proposal process. Proposal management tasks may include coordination of the technical aspects and budget preparations, writing proposal sections, and participating in or leading sales presentations.
• Identify and negotiate specific client needs at all phases of the project.
• Communicate in a timely fashion with senior management regarding status/problems associated with projects and propose solutions.
• Contribute to the development and testing of questionnaires and other data collection tools and materials.
• Write and proofread study documentation such as methods reports, IRB submissions, and training manuals.
• Provide quality control and data analysis support.
• Contribute to proposal development.

Qualifications:

• Masters degree in public health, social science, statistics, survey research, or related area and 5+ years of experience directing health-related surveys plus some experience with qualitative, mixed-methods, or data science research
• Prior experience with proposal development
• Excellent organizational and time management skills
• Excellent written and verbal communications skills with proven ability to work with clients, external collaborators, and internal project teams
• Strong computer skills in MS Word, Excel and PowerPoint
• Strong quantitative and data analytic skills
• Proficiency in statistical software (e.g. SPSS, SAS, R, Stata)
