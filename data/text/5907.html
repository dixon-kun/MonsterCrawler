Ramboll ENVIRON US Corporation (Ramboll ENVIRON) is a fast-paced, detailed oriented Environmental Consulting firm looking for a senior-level Geographic Information Systems (GIS) Analyst/Advisor to join our graphics group in our Irvine, California location. A key component of our business model is utilizing cutting edge geospatial and environmental data visualization techniques to convey complex data analyses to our clients, the public, and the regulatory community. Successful candidates must demonstrate advanced knowledge of GIS software including Esri ArcGIS (Desktop and Enterprise), Computer Aided Design (CAD) and data management skills using Excel, Access, and SQL and must also apply advanced geographic, mapping, planning, and physical/computer sciences. Typical responsibilities include those for mid-level GIS Associates in addition to providing mentoring of junior staff, performing QA/QC on work products, communicating results and findings to internal and external clients, and assisting with business development activities.

Job Responsibilities:

  • Mentor junior analysts on activities related to the application of geospatial analytical techniques and methodologies
  • Perform QA/QC on maps and analyses
  • Integrate data from multiple disparate data sets and assess the significance and reliability of incoming client data
  • Advise internal and external clients and assist in resolving complex problems
  • Professionally communicate findings as written, visual, and/or oral intelligence products to senior staff and clients
  • Collaborate with the Ramboll Environ Data Science and Integration Advisory Board
  • Participate in 1 st and 2 nd Tier technical support request resolution
  • Provide technical demonstrations and knowledge transfer/dissemination
  • Provide/coordinate training to staff as requested
  • Assist with business development initiatives (demonstrations, client meetings, presentations, bids and proposal support, etc.)
  • Provide graphics support as needed
