Uses clear understanding of economic theory and data science to develop and work with databases in order to develop models, learning algorithms and tools to evaluate and forecast commercial real estate (CRE) markets. Produces written analysis on CRE markets while meeting tight deadlines and engages directly and regularly with clients to provide insight and to answer questions.

* Recognize, monitor, research and analyze economic issues.

* Undertake quantitative analysts with time series learning algorithms and convert findings to transparent and defensible market forecasts.

* Conducts presentations internally and externally as needed to support business development efforts.

* May prepare senior executives for speaking engagements.

* Other duties may be assigned.

* No formal supervisory responsibilities in this position.

* Provides informal assistance such as technical guidance, and/or training to co-workers.

* May lead project teams and/or plan, and supervise assignments of lower level employees.

Qualifications

* Bachelor's degree from a four-year university in economics, finance, or computer science, plus a minimum of two years related experience or training.

* Master's degree or Ph.D. in economics, finance, or computer science is highly preferable.

* Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

* Strong organizational and analytical skills.

* Ability to provide efficient, timely, reliable, and courteous service to customers.

* Ability to effectively present information to both internal and external clients.

* Requires knowledge of financial terms and principles with an understanding of mathematical concepts and an ability to apply them in the pursuit of practical situations.

* Conducts financial and statistical analysis to be included the preparation of reports.

* Ability to solve advanced problems and deal with a variety of options in complicated situations.

* Requires expert-level analytical and quantitative skills with proven experience in developing strategic solutions for a growing matrix-based multi-industry sales environment.

* Draws upon the analysis of others and makes recommendations that have a direct impact on the company.

* Good knowledge of statistical learning techniques as evidenced by publications or projects.

* Good knowledge of computer software, Stata and SQL in particular.

* Decisions made with thorough understanding of procedures, company policies, and business practices to achieve general results and deadlines.

* Responsible for setting work unit and/or project deadlines.

* Errors in judgment may cause short-term impact to department.
