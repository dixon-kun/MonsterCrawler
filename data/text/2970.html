Market Engagement Designer, Senior

Key Role:
Work closely with data science and machine intelligence teams and leverage market and competitive intelligence to develop targeted and measurable engagement plans. Collaborate and coordinate with marketing channel partners on the development and execution of advertising campaigns, Web site enhancements, e-mail and social media activity, and industry and client-focused events aligned to engagement calendars. Comprehend, analyze, and improve on engagement plans based on targeted performance metrics tied to business value and help develop and manage client engagement journey maps to tie solutions, cross-channel marketing activity, and customer voice into targeted business metrics. Act as a collaborator and relationship builder, including working in tandem with marketing and communications to ensure engagement activities are executed on time, building relationships with key stakeholders across teams to prioritize campaigns, and serving as an advisor in the space. Create content across social media channels and support the data science and machine intelligence social media contributor process. Identify success metrics for each engagement activity or initiative for data science and machine intelligence. Share findings across functional teams and be comfortable with sharing recommendations for improvements and next steps.

Basic Qualifications:
-3+ years of experience in a marketing or communications role
-Experience with digital marketing performance and analytics
-Experience with writing, copyediting, storytelling and public speaking
-Experience in working with Microsoft Office, including Word, PowerPoint, and Excel
-Experience with digital marketing automation tools and technology
-BA or BS degree required

Additional Qualifications:
-Experience with management consulting and product marketing

Integrating a full range of consulting capabilities, Booz Allen is the one firm that helps clients solve their toughest problems. by their side to help them achieve their missions.  Booz Allen is committed to delivering results that endure.
