Advanced Analytics – Washignton, DC



The group has been challenged to fundamentally change the way we use data and subject matter expertise to answer key industry questions. To realize this vision, we are expanding our industry leading group with experts possessing the skills to turn data into commercial products and solutions. The group is looking for smart, driven, and creative individuals to help in the development of products, and lead our efforts by managing global teams of data scientists, and consultants in the development and delivery of novel analytic solutions for clients around the world.

Help us drive analysis:
  • Apply your machine learning and advanced analytics skills (agent based modeling, random forest, support vector machines, neural networks, survival analysis, Bayesian statistics, etc.) to our client’s unique, industry leading data to surface new insights and develop new products.
  • Be part of a unique team working across business lines with cutting edge analytics and technology to provide actionable results, support new and existing products and platforms, and to influence business decisions
  • Grow your knowledge of the latest data and analytical methodologies and technologies – Redshift, EC2, machine learning and more
  • Cooperatively work across the analytics team to share knowledge and educate one another
In order to make that happen, we’re looking for you to have these skills:
  • Minimum education requirement of four-year degree in statistics, computer science, mathematics, economics, or a similarly quantitative discipline. Additional education is preferred.
  • Five (5) or more years of relevant advanced analytics experience – strong proficiency in advanced statistical, predictive and machine learning techniques (agent based modeling, random forest, support vector machines, neural networks, survival analysis, Bayesian statistics, etc).
  • Demonstrated creative problem solving & critical reasoning abilities.
  • Ability to understand business context and translate to develop appropriate analytics solutions
  • Established ability to communicate effectively and proactively.
  • Strong proficiency in R required.
  • Experience writing code and using data science tools such as SQL, Python, Tableau, ArcGIS, and/or Hadoop highly desirable.

Primary Location:
Washington, DC

  • Health care consulting resources and services (including ICD-10)
  • Customer Relationship Management (CRM)
  • Creative (Interactive Project Manager/Art Director, Information Architect, UI/UX Designer, Web/Graphic Design)
