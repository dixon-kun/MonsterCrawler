        Overview of the Roles:
        We are currently recruiting for high performing analytics and data science professionals at various levels to join our rapidly growing Population Health Analytics team. The team is developing a purpose-driven analytics platform to maximize the health of our patients and the performance of our physicians using data science principles and proprietary applications. Successful candidates will be well rounded hands-on data lovers.
        * Collaborate closely with product &amp; technology leadership, data engineers and 3rd parties to enhance our data warehouse platform, semantic layers, and BI tool (MicroStrategy) enabling scalable, multi-market analytics and reporting
        * Spearhead data governance, developing structure and process
        * Negotiate with payers during value-based contracting phase to secure required data
        * Inform inbound data transformation rules, data architecture, and quality assurance scenarios to integrate disparate data into common formats, identify issues and define requirements for workarounds
        * Develop and test SQL scripts
        * Facilitate implementation, monitoring, and evaluation of automated clinical standing orders into the rules engine and Privia App
        * Become the data SME on data from payers, EMRs, ADTs, and other vendors
        * Design, build and calculate key metrics for each value-based payer contract
        * Support configuration of episode grouper technology, attribution algorithms, bundled payments
        In addition to these qualifications, successful candidates should also have the following attributes:
        * Exceptionally strong critical thinking and problem solving abilities with a track record of identifying insights from quantitative and qualitative data
        * Ability to create compelling visual presentations that tell a story
        * Ability to translate unstructured business, clinical, and contractual concepts into a structured process to query data, with keen sense of how to balance accuracy and efficiency
        * Works collaboratively in a fast-paced, unstructured environment, managing competing deadlines, and adapts to evolving responsibilities
        * Ability to build relationships, consensus, and trust with key business stakeholders across markets and Brighton holding companies
        * Inquisitive, driven, self-starter who takes initiative and knows when to seek guidance
        * An entrepreneurial spirit, comfortable working within startup culture
        Minimum Qualifications:
        * Bachelor's degree, with a quantitative major (e.g. actuarial, statistics, economics, engineering, computer science, operations research, applied math) or healthcare (health administration, public health, medicine), Advanced degrees preferred
        * Experience with one of the following: SQL, SAS, R, Python
        * Experience integrating, manipulating, transforming data (claims, EMR, ADT, Rx, labs, etc.)
        * Experience working at a consulting firm, health plan, health system, provider organization, or technology start-up
