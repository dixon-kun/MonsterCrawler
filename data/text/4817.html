The Schneider organization has an immediate need for an Advanced Analytics Systems II associate who will be responsible for the creation of tools and applications that enable advanced analytics solutions within the enterprise. This position creates conceptual prototypes to test new concepts, deploys the prototype into the business and monitors its effectiveness and usefulness. The Advanced Analytics Systems II associate will also be responsible to research and recommend the use of purchased and open-source technologies and concepts (“big data” ecosystems, machine-learning, natural language processing libraries, visual analytics platforms, speech recognition) that support the creation of tools and systems for use by technical and non-technical users.

Schneider has a well-deserved reputation for being a technical environment and an industry leader.  One instance of this is our co-development and implementation of a first-of-its kind ERP system with Oracle.  In recognition of that accomplishment, our former CIO was named as Oracle’s North America CIO of the Year.   In this position, you will be challenged as a professional and you will have the opportunity to design and implement leading edge technology that is vital to the success of Schneider.

Duties and responsibilities to include but not limited to:

  • Gathers requirements, designs, develops, tests, implements and supports applications developed across various development environments and in various languages.
  • Works with the Engineering team to develop and apply heuristics and mathematical concepts, such as natural language processing (NLP), simulation, optimization, machine learning, and statistical models to business problems.
  • Follows appropriate development methodologies while developing applications.
  • Researches purchased and open-source languages, techniques, and concepts applicable to Schneider.  Shares these new concepts with the larger department and creates proof-of-concept applications to showcase value.  Creates approved tools and applications that allow others to make use of the concepts for solving problems.
  • Creates Statements of Benefit and Statements of Work for new concepts before they are approved.  Communicates the value for projects to non-technical stakeholders.
  • Self-manages multiple projects, priorities, and timelines.  Communicates progress and conflicts appropriately to management and stakeholders.

Our preferred candidate will have:

  • Bachelor degree in Computer Science, Applied Mathematics or a related field or equivalent experience.
  • Minimum of 2 years of relevant business and technical experience in data analytics, application development, or operations research.
  • Proficient with one or more of the following programming languages: Java, Python, JavaScript, C++, Ruby, C#, Objective-C or R.
  • Experience with Python and/or Java required.
  • Experience with any data science tools such as R, SAS, or SPSS a plus.  Experience with the HDFS/YARN/Spark and related tools a plus.
  • Experience with mathematical modeling languages (DOcplex/CPLEX, Matlab, GAMS) a plus.
  • Experience developing applications in a Linux server environment preferred.
  • Demonstrated ability to self-manage multiple projects, priorities, and timelines.
  • Demonstrated ability to appropriately communicate issues, changes, and progress to stakeholders and interested parties.
  • Transportation and Logistics experience preferred.
