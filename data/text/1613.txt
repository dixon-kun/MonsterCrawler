            The Junior Consultant, Analytics is responsible for delivery of productivity solutions for GE&#8217;s healthcare clients. Analytics Consulting is an exciting discipline combining traditional consulting with analytics disciplines such as data science, statistics, simulation and predictive modeling. Engagements follow a problem back approach, leveraging interviews and data mining to align clients around high-value problem statements, then designing targeted solutions to improve performance.  Project types include designing efficient healthcare facilities, reconfiguring existing capacity for patient throughput and experience, improving health system utilization and implementing real-time analytics in command centers to dynamically optimize coordination of patient care.
            The Junior Consultant, Analytics will work with clients and multi-disciplinary consulting teams and will possess strong analytical skills, a structured approach to problem solving and experience with process improvement and statistical analysis. In addition, the Junior Consultant, Analytics must be a self-starter with potential to lead. The career path for Analytics Consultants grows from delivering analytics to leading projects and eventually managing clients.  The ability to effectively communicate complex concepts and lead change is a must.
