Systems Administrator

Sabre is currently recruiting for a Systems Administrator to work at Ft. Dix,, New Jersey.   The Systems Administrator is responsible for ensuring the security, availability, and optimal performance of a wide variety of systems to support the DOD  mission in the Central NJ area..  This position will work closely with other network and system engineers to implement, document, and facilitate system capabilities for DoD Research and Development (R&D) environments. A key component of this position will be to perform day-to-day maintenance, patching and ensuring STIG compliance by monitoring, testing performance, troubleshooting OS, resolving hardware and software problems, scheduling upgrades, and collaborating with other engineers on network / system optimization.

The successful applicant will have:

  • Ability to quickly learn new concepts and software.
  • Experience presenting work to both technical and non-technical audiences.
  • Excellent teamwork and interpersonal skills.
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills.
  • Ability to quickly adapt to a changing environment.
  • Ability to handle multiple clients and multiple team members with confidence.
  • Ability to multi-task & support multiple priorities.
  • Self-motivated, independent, detail oriented, and responsible.

Description of Work:

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Managing systems and data within a domain by use of group policy (Active Directory).
  • Securing systems through the use of EPO server (McAfee HBSS).
  • Patching system OS and applications via Microsoft System Center (SCCM).
  • Scanning network segments with ACAS server and reporting non-compliance of vendor systems.
  • Deploying, patching, STIG, and managing operating systems (Microsoft 8.1, Microsoft Server 2012 R2, ESXi, Red Hat, and Cent OS).
  • Providing troubleshooting, support, and resolving system failures.
  • Configuring and maintaining PKI infrastructure for secured host communication, authentication, and authorization.
  • Configuring, maintaining, and troubleshooting domain services such DHCP, DNS, and WSUS.
  • Performing hardware and software maintenance and pushing updates to maintain IAVM compliance.
  • Maintaining system hardware, including servers, storage arrays, workstations, laptops, and their components (ram, hard drives, mice, keyboards).
  • Documenting supported systems and ensuring that such documentation is kept current.
  • Performing other functional area duties as required to meet business needs.




Job Requirements:

Education:

  • Bachelor of Science degree, preferably in Computer Science, Engineering (computer, electrical, financial), Mathematics, Statistics, or any other data science related field.

Experience:

  • Must have a minimum of 3 years’ experience in system administration.
  • Must have extensive experience configuring and managing McAfee ePolicy Orchestrator (McAfee HBSS for Antivirus, Firewall, and Data Management
  • Must have extensive hands-on experience deploying and managing domain services (GPO, DHCP, DNS, WSUS) via Microsoft Server.
  • Must have extensive experience deploying operating systems (WDS).
  • Must have extensive experience patching systems and software (WSUS, System Center).
  • Scanning network segments with ACAS server

Certifications:

  • Must have at least 1 of the following Industry Certifications: MCSA or MCSE.
  • Must have at least 1 of the following DoD IAT Level II Certifications: GSEC, CCNA Security, Security+ CE, or SSCP.

Candidate must have an active DOD Secret Clearance or be a US Citizen with the ability to obtain and maintain a Secret Clearance.  Candidates will be required to pass a background check for this position.
