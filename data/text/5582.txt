        Responsibilities
        * Be an integral part of the business teams' efforts to establish and evaluate existing and potential business relationships, with significant responsibilities for negotiating, preparing, and reviewing partner contracts.
        * Provide management team with day-to-day legal perspective and guidance, as well as risk assessment and mitigation.
        * Serve as legal advisor and active participant in establishing partnerships and facilitating merger, acquisition and divestiture transactions, including due diligence, negotiation and preparation of term sheets and definitive agreements, and assistance with financing documentation and closing process.
        * Assist with a variety of corporate matters including organizational documents, company structure, and management of corporate entities.
        * Assist with management of intellectual property portfolio.
        * Work closely with Chief Compliance Officer on risk assessment and on compliance-related processes and procedures to support the company's compliance environment and ever-present focus on quality.
        * Address employment related legal issues in conjunction with Human Resources department, including EEOC and worker's compensation claims.
        * Coordinate with outside counsel on litigation and specialized issues as required.
