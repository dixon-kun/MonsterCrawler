Tastemade is building a state of the art Content Intelligence system, that understands video, stories, and their impact on human emotion. Our goal is to revolutionize the way people interact with lifestyle content, through VR, video, and everything else. We have the top content creators in the WORLD, and a rapidly expanding library.

Tastemade is curating a SMALL core of engineers to solve BIG world problems; you will have a lot of responsibility and tons of freedom. We are looking to lay the foundations for massive-scale data generation, storage and processing.

As a data engineer, you will build and architect:

* SQL and NoSQL databases, from determining business requirements to planning and deploying production systems.

* Crawlers against structured and unstructured data sources to grow our ever-expanding data repositories.

* Scalable, high availability data generation at web-scale.

* Interface with engineers, analysts and product to translate needs to systems and data representations.

* Build out structured data representations to enable cutting-edge algorithm development.

* Own data quality and build data enterprise.

* Design build and launch ETLs in production.

You will join Tastemade as one of your kind - a data genius who is passionate about transforming the org through data. You will build out a system from the ground up and take it to scale.

What you'll bring:

* MS/BS/BA in CS/Math/EE or related disciplines.

* 3+ years experience with multi-terabyte data systems.

* Experience with NoSQL or RDBMS platforms - Cassandra, PostgreSQL, MySQL or Oracle.

* Experience with custom ETL design, implementation and maintenance.

* Experience working with either an MPP or MapReduce system - Hadoop, Spark or Elastic Search.

* Strong understanding of Cloud technologies, architecting, developing or maintaining cloud solutions in cloud environments (e.g Google Cloud Platform or Amazon Web Services).

* 3+ years programming experience, Python, R, Spark preferred.

* Deep, hands-on experience with schema design and dimensional data modeling.

* Ability to analyze data to identify deliverables, gaps and inconsistencies.

Why Tastemade:

* Get in at the ground floor. As one of the first members of the data science team, you will DEFINE Data and Content Intelligence here

* Apply your Data chops to some of the hottest problems in the space

* Tastemade community parties

* Work in a movie studio. Literally. Our Santa Monica offices are the old MTV studios

* Plugged into a community of food and travel lovers

* Be truly global, we have offices in Santa Monica, New York, Austin, London, Paris, Brazil, Argentina and Japan.

* Stock Options

* Work with a cool mix of artists, actors, editors, writers and engineers

* Data is the biggest focus area at Tastemade right now. We want you.

Perks:

* Competitive salary and benefits package

* Stock options

* Flexible vacation days

* Apple iMac, MacBook or equivalent hardware

* Colleagues who are fellow food & travel lovers!
