Specific Responsibilities

* Identify data and analytical monetization product opportunities by working with the Honeywell business teams.

* Build relationships within the business unit, across Honeywell and with potential clients. Evangelize and promote the value of data and analytics inside and outside the organization.

* Create compelling messaging and branding that captures the unique advantages of Honeywell's data.

* Build and execute a metrics-driven marketing plan combining traditional marketing techniques (events, PR, direct, sales enablement) combined with digital expertise (search, social, inbound content marketing) to drive awareness and leads.

* Promote Honeywell's thought leadership by building using our own data to illustrate trends, stories and possibilities. Examples include webinars, infographics, white papers, etc.

* Define go-to-market path (sales, partnerships) and build marketing programs to support these channels.

* Attend and arrange presence at industry conferences to stay current on industry trends, challenges, and potential market opportunities

* Energize and excite our internal and external customers to clearly outline how our enterprise platforms can enable business growth & productivity by seamlessly combining structured and unstructured data into a single, self-service analytical environment
