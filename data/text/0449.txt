           About You:
          You recognize that the recruiting process demands creativity, hustle, and precise coordination. You&#8217;re hyper-focused, and an excellent communicator who thrives in a fast-paced environment managing multiple priorities at once.

           Responsibilities:
          Your recruiting focus will be aimed at supporting the hiring of the software engineering, product, &amp; data science talent that fuels our ability to create beautiful data-driven products. Our environment will provide you with the chance to utilize your strengths, stretch your skills, and work with a group of intellectually curious, generous and fun colleagues.

           You are a strong and personable communicator who can provide a unique and impactful recruiting experience while developing lasting relationships with both candidates and hiring managers alike.
           A diligent work ethic and an entrepreneurial mindset, with the ability to prioritize and succeed in an environment with competing and changing demands.

           Capable of juggling different priorities: scheduling interviews, internal meetings, travel arrangements for candidates, conference rooms booking.

           Work on other side projects related to recruiting.
           You embody our company values of generosity, curiosity, &amp; hustle.
           Our Story:
          Founded in 2011, Enigma grew out of an effort to retrieve, normalize, and host public data. Fun fact, our big coming out party was winning TechCrunch Disrupt's 2013 Battlefield.
          Our vision is to empower people to interpret, and improve, the world around them. We place data in the context of the real world and make it connected, open and actionable. Enigma has one of the world&#8217;s largest repositories of public data and a powerful infrastructure for operationalizing data in the enterprise. We help global-scale companies tackle some of their hardest problems by driving public data and enterprise ontologies in mission-critical workflows.
