Company Description

The only place to preorder designer collections straight from the runway, Moda Operandi has revolutionized luxury retail with a high-tech meets high-touch shopping experience. This distinctive concept is complemented by an expertly curated in-season selection that offers a unique point of view on fashion. Moda Operandi's unrivaled customer care anticipates the needs of its sophisticated clientele worldwide. Through engaging editorial content, spearheaded by the industry's leading talent, M'O defines what customers want to buy both this season and next.

Job Description

Position Summary

The Customer Insights Analyst is a data analyst and insights professional who is passionate about disseminating valuable insights from large and complex data sets. Data-driven, with an ability to understand the big picture, the analyst is responsible for providing actionable insights into the Moda Operandi customer, and is comfortable presenting those insights and suggestions to upper management.

Role Priorities

  • Provide a detailed and complete understanding of the Moda Operandi customer, maintaining a close view of customer KPI’s and performance metrics on customer acquisition, engagement and retention.
  • Partner with Stylist & Customer Experience teams to apply data-driven approaches to improving customer lifetime value.
  • Drive segmentation & personalization of both online & offline customer touchpoints through detailed analysis of customer data.

Performance Requirements

  • Own the delivery of analytics and insights across the business units, making recommendations and implementing improvements to current business practice.
  • Perform ad hoc analysis and reports using SQL and other tools to support marketing & other business areas.
  • Apply statistical methodologies to provide insights into the customer/marketing data


Qualifications

Essential Skills, Experience and Educational Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in math, statistics, finance, or related analytical field
  • 2-3 years of experience in an analytics or data science role
  • Strong Microsoft Excel skills
  • Working knowledge of SQL
  • Self-starter & able to manage projects from ideation through completion
  • Exceptional communication skills
  • Excellent attention to detail
  • Strong data presentation and visualization skills. Tableau a plus.
  • Experience with Google Analytics, Omniture or other web analytics tools a plus
  • Working knowledge of HTML/CSS/Javascript a plus

Required Competencies

Communication and Influencing

  • Demonstrate ability to build and foster productive working relationships with cross-functional teams
  • Creative thinker with the confidence to share ideas with the team

Impact – the ability to:

  • Lead and support cross-functional team improvement initiatives
  • Execute appropriate processes and decisions across the organization

Industry Knowledge

  • Comprehensive understanding of the fashion and luxury goods industry, consumer products and personal technology industries, and trends affecting them

Resiliency

  • Thrive in a fast-paced environment, welcoming challenges
  • Adapts to change with ease and composure

The job description is to be used merely as a guide of expectations rather than an exhaustive list of all duties and competencies. All requirements and skills are subject to change as business needs evolve.
