Job Summary:

The PNO Report Analyst supports business and initiatives undertaken by Corporate Provider Network Operations by liaising with applicable line of business contacts to monitor, acquire, extract, manipulate and evaluate/analyze data. Coordinates with intra-departmental and corporate resources to facilitate timely completion and communication of data gathering, data design and reporting activities:

Major Accountabilities:

  • Utilizes MS Access and Excel to manipulate and analyze data.
  • Utilizes Provider Data Systems and Back-End data tables to evaluate data.
  • Understands provider data and deliverables related contract or regulatory procedures associated with assigned lines of business. with current knowledge of changes and upgrades that affect the quality and accuracy of the products.
  • Monitors updates and changes related to data requirements and reporting for internal and external customers.
  • Collaborates and executes processes & procedures and process improvement initiatives/documentation.
  • Supports PDM upgrades to effectively and efficiently deliver results to all LOBs and new markets.
  • Collaborates and oversees directory work with print and online processes, on-demand and data pulls.
  • Supports new business initiatives and implementation work for network intelligence and RFPs.

Job Related Requirements Qualifications/Knowledge/Skills/Abilities:

  • Required Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent experience
    • 3 years managed care experience with Bachelor’s in math, data science, statistics  computer science or related field or
    • Minimum of 5 years with equivalent experience without  Bachelor’s
  • Required 3-5 years of experience with Microsoft Access and Excel
  • Required coding experience or certifications highly beneficial.
  • Required working knowledge and understanding of Enterprise systems  (ability to navigate claims, provider, benefits and enrollment data/information)
  • Required proven work experience and competency with data collection, data structure and data analysis
  • Required excellent written and verbal communication
  • Recent college graduate degreed in Math or IT will be given solid consideration.

Telecommuter considered with 25% travel to corporate office or plan office
