Security Architect

Pindrop has an exciting opportunity for a Security Architect to lead the security technology strategy and help secure the advancement of our business. This role will be responsible for championing the security of Pindrop products and systems and protecting customer data in both physical and cloud environments. Yes, this is a lot of responsibility; we are a small company that is targeting the security program maturity of a Fortune 100 company. We run lean and wear a lot of hats.

We are looking for someone with broad and deep experience in security technologies, cloud security, and advanced threats to design secure internal and external products and solutions. The new Pindropper must also have experience with application security best practices and tools and building security into an Agile CI/CD, as well as strong communication skills and interpersonal skills to drive and influence security with engineering and operations teams.

Responsibilities

  • Act as security expert that defines, analyzes and ensures proper implementation of appropriate security controls, processes, and principles
  • Creation and maintenance of enterprise and product security principles requirements
  • Collaborating with Pindrop teams to ensure the security of our products, services, and corporate environment
  • Raising security awareness across the enterprise
  • Architects and coordinates the choice of security technologies and processes to ensure a highly secure business enabled environment
  • Collaborates with procurement, contracting and business units to perform security assessments of our critical partners
  • Build strong cross-organizational relationships and effectively influence teams across the organization
  • Stay current with threats and security technologies and make risk based recommendations
  • Assists in the audit and compliance process

Who You Are

  • Accountable and empowered professional
  • Curious and always looking to learn
  • Highly interested in how things work
  • Gets excited by control frameworks and risk models
  • Strong communication skills whether talking to executives or software engineers

Tech Experience

  • At least 8 years of professional information technology experience including 3+ years security architecture or security engineering in a high security environment
  • Broad technical expertise with application development, cloud (IaaS, SaaS) security architecture and technologies
  • Experience with secure coding practices, threat modeling, and application security tools and processes
  • Knowledge and expertise in data-protection mechanisms including an understanding of cryptographic algorithms and key management protocols and techniques
  • Experience in creating and delivering security architecture specifications and requirements for complex systems
  • Networking experience, including TCP/IP, routing, switching
  • Working knowledge of Linux and security procedures and controls for Linux
  • Experience in a DevOps/Agile environment preferred
  • Experience with control frameworks such as ISO 2700x, PCI DSS, NIST, and privacy law
  • Demonstrated ability to develop and manage security policies, standards, guidelines and procedures
  • Demonstrated capability to learn and adapt to new situations and requirements in a dynamic environment
  • Demonstrated experience driving risk based decisions supporting business owner expectations and needs
  • Experience working across business lines to engage team members
  • At least one certification such as CISSP, CISM, and/or CISA
  • Bachelor’s degree in cyber security, computer science or engineering, or related field

Our Bio

Founded in 2011  |  Raised $122 million

Venture backed by Andreessen Horowitz, Google Capital, IVP, Citi Ventures, Felicis Ventures, & GV

Pindrop is the pioneer in voice fraud prevention and authentication. We provide enterprise solutions to reduce fraud losses and authentication expense for some of the largest call centers in the world. Pindrop’s patented Phoneprinting™ technology can identify, locate and authenticate phone devices uniquely just from the call audio thereby detecting fraudulent calls as well as verifying legitimate callers. We have been selected by the world’s largest banks, insurers, brokerages and retailers, detecting over 80% of fraud even for attackers never seen before. Pindrop’s solutions are allowing customers to reduce call time and improve their customers’ experience even while reducing fraud losses.
