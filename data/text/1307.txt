        We are seeking a Marketing Coordinator in our Minnetonka, MN office.
        The Marketing Coordinator will play a critical role for our sales, marketing, and operations teams. This role crosses functions between three teams in a fast-paced environment!
        What You'll Do:
        Researching and operationalizing prospect companies' qualification and prioritization, leveraging tools, technologies and 3rd parties to determine ideal customer profile fit into our well-defined segmentation requirements.
        * Leverage existing process and technology solutions to automate data enrichment for our current databases and acquisition targets.
        * Participate in researching and testing new processes, technologies, and 3rd parties to improve data accuracy and acquisition.
        * Support the responsibility of documenting, maintain documentation and share data enrichment process with the cross-functional team
        * Light project management support for the cross functional by documenting and sharing tasks, task status and reminders, meeting agendas and scheduling.
        * Work with operations teams to analyze gaps in our data, contact acquisition strategy, and support practices that ensure data quality and process to measure database health
        * Support the of our ideal client profile segment including analysis of firmographic, technographic and persona data to help determine qualification and prioritization
        * Support database management and segmentation by helping with lead scoring and segmentation programs, and participating in defining criteria for targets for marketing programs
        * Assist with database hygiene for target segments &#8211; that is correct, captured and categorized appropriately.
        * Data append, entry, cleanse, capture &amp; upload
        * Document, monitor and ICP nomination and removal process.
