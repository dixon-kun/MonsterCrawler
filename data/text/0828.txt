        Academy Data Science Program Manager - New York
        The Point72 Academy is the Firm's primary training ground for its junior long/short equities Analysts. The Academy is a comprehensive development program where undergraduates and young professionals become highly-skilled Analysts on an accelerated timeline. The Academy curriculum currently includes intense modeling and research skill-building, of which data science is an essential skill. From the day they start, Academy members have substantial responsibility and opportunities to contribute in a small team setting. Today, more than three-quarters of Point72's current long/short equities Portfolio Managers started as Analysts at the Firm. The Point72 Academy will support the Firm's strategic initiative to grow that number over time.
        Job Description
        As big data increasingly becomes a significant source to identify trends and patterns within financial markets, Point72 is focused on integrating data science into The Academy and training our future academy members to be armed in data analytics in addition to financial valuation and modeling. The Academy Data Science Program Manager will play an integral role in integrating data science into the program, thereby shaping the vision of the Academy, and will lead data science and analytics training. The Manager will play a unique role that is part data science business advisor to the Academy Director and Chief Educator of Big Data within the Academy. This is an exciting opportunity for a high-achieving candidate to help develop and shape Point72's future Research Analysts and will also provide significant exposure to the firm's Senior Management. While teaching is an important part of the role, leadership opportunities within the group will be introduced as The Academy program continues to grow.
        The role will be based primarily in New York with limited time in Point 72's Stamford, Connecticut office.
        Job Responsibilities
        * Lead the execution of the Academy's data science curriculum from beginning to end, lead internal and external activities, including the development and preparation of program materials.
        * Coordinate recruiting efforts with members of the Academy's Talent Development team.
        * Execute, oversee and advise on special projects.
        * Ensure timely follow through of The Academy's agendas and assignments.
        * Work on special projects and collaborate with our internal Market Intelligence team.
        * Serve as a data science point of contact for internal program participants and external vendors.
        * Handle meeting coverage for the team when needed.
        Desirable Candidates
        * Be a seasoned, decisive and creative professional with promise to provide leadership within a complex entity.
        * Be a consensus builder who promotes understanding, collaborations, and solutions.
        * Possess solid business and financial acumen (specifically strong data science and business analytics), problem solving and analytical abilities, and strong communication skills (written and verbal).
        * Have a proven record of working in complex and dynamic environments, and who is able to consistently deliver outstanding results.
        Other requirements include:
        * Track record of experience developing/adapting/improving statistical and machine learning models on real world, noisy datasets. Experience with NLP and Deep Learning frameworks (Tensor Flow, Theano) is a plus.
        * MS or PhD degree in a quantitative/ technical field from top tier university.
        * Strong coding skills (R, Python).
        * Strong understanding of the entire life cycle of data science including data ingestion (ETL), cleaning, statistical modeling, and visualization.
        * Strong Knowledge of SQL and big data frameworks (Spark, Hadoop)
        * A passion for teaching and mentoring junior talent.
        * Proven ability to get things done &#8211; leadership, project management skills and resourcefulness, including the ability to persuade others.
        * Strategic and creative thinker with an interest in learning. Must be able to deal with ambiguity.
        * Flexible; ability to multi-task, while managing priorities and workflow, and the ability to handle changing priorities and demands on time.
        * Polished presence and a track record of developing and managing key relationships and partners.
        * Integrity and ethics are paramount; commitment to excellence and high standards is a must.
        * Background in training and development is a plus, but not required.
