        What is Amplify?
        RBC Amplify is an intensive program designed to solve big business challenges. Our goal is to bring in the hardest working students with the freshest ideas to reimagine how we do things at RBC. We're looking for individuals who stand out in a crowd. If you're top of your class, love learning, think big and are perpetually curious, we want you! For more information on RBC's Amplify program, please visit https://connect.rbc.com/.
        Each Amplify team consists of four individuals &#8211; one with Digital Marketing experience, two with Software Development experience, and one focused on User Interface / User Experience (UI/UX). This posting is for applicants to fill the Software Development and UI/UX roles, generally referred to as the Digital Design side of the project team.
        What is the opportunity?
        RBC Amplify is an intensive summer internship where students are put on cross-functional teams and given a real life RBC business challenge. Over the course of the summer you will have access to subject matter experts, coaches and mentors, as well as technical and professional training. At the end of the summer you will have the opportunity to present your solution to a panel of judges, and our top teams will be awarded scholarships or funding for their ideas.
        What will you do?
        * Immerse yourself in a full-scale project with an intense focus on collaboration to develop an innovative solution to your business challenge.
        * Work with subject matter experts, mentors and executive sponsors to build your solution.
        * Design, troubleshoot and develop your solution, with an end goal of creating and delivering a minimum viable product (MVP) and presenting your strategy and solution to executive leadership.
        * Step outside your comfort zone and utilize your teamwork and collaboration skills to help your team succeed.
        * Demonstrate your critical thinking, time management and leadership skills within your team.
        * Engage in design thinking, agile development and other tools, technologies and methodologies.
        What do you need to succeed?
        Must-have
        * Must be entering final year of undergraduate studies or currently enrolled in a post graduate program (masters, doctorate, etc.), preferably with a focus on Computer Science, Engineering, Technology, Data Science, or User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) design
        * Experience or understanding of programming languages, databases, platforms, frameworks, design techniques and tools, code management tooling, and/or web technologies
        * Excellent interpersonal, written and verbal communication skills
        * Comfort and experience delivering presentations containing complex ideas
        * Excellent critical thinking, time management and leadership skills
        * Creative thinker who is self-driven and capable of working in a fast-paced team environment
        * Ability to challenge the status quo and think outside the box to develop solutions
        * Excellent teamwork, collaboration and problem solving skills
        * Ability and willingness to work 40 hours per week from early June to mid-August 2017 (exact dates TBD)
        Nice-to-have
        * Experience in web or mobile development
        * Experience in agile or design thinking methodologies or frameworks
        * Experience with statistics, deep-dive analytics, predictive modelling or algorithms
        * Experience with machine learning, pattern intelligence, computational mathematics, bioinformatics or artificial intelligence
        * Experience with hackathons
        What's in it for you?
        We thrive on the challenge to be our best, think progressively to keep growing, and work together to deliver trusted advice to help our clients thrive and communities prosper. We care about each other reaching our potential, making a difference in our communities, and achieving success that is mutual.

        * Work in a dynamic, collaborative, progressive environment as part of a high performing team
