Company Overview

At Proofpoint, we have a passion for protecting people, data, and brands from today’s advanced threats and compliance risks. We hire the best people in the business to:

  • Build and enhance our proven security platform
  • Blend innovation and speed in a constantly evolving cloud architecture
  • Analyze new threats and offer deep insight through data-driven intel
  • Collaborate with customers to help solve their toughest security challenges

We are singularly devoted to helping our customers protect what matters most. That’s why we’re a leader in next- generation cybersecurity—and why more than half of the Fortune 100 trust us as a security partner.

The Role

Do you love breaking things? (In order to understand how to make them bulletproof?) Do you love digging into systems in order to understand their week points, where things start to fall over, etc.? Are you looking for an opportunity where you work collaboratively with Software Engineers rather than have stuff thrown over the wall to you? Are you passionate about ensuring a product has your quality stamp of approval on it? If so we are looking for you. We need a great Quality Assurance engineer who will work on a cross-functional team to help uncover cyber threat information hidden in large amounts of data from various sources.Your primary focus will implementing automated tests the ensure that the system meets the requirements of function, performance and scale.

Your day-to-day

  • Define and maintain quality standards for your cross-functional team
  • Develop automated test to ensure quality of product function, performance and scale
  • Champion and make strategic decisions about processes, tools and platform required for QA process
  • Work collaboratively with engineers and data scientists to fix issues with the product

What you bring to the team

  • 3+ years of experience in automated testing preferably with large, distributed applications
  • Strong knowledge of development and testing methodologies
  • Commitment to excellence
  • Experience validating applications utilizing AWS services
  • SQL and NoSQL knowledge
  • Experience automating continuous delivery
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills

Why Proofpoint

As a customer focused and driven-to-win organization with leading edge products, there are many exciting reasons to join the Proofpoint team. We believe in hiring the best the brightest and cultivating a culture of collaboration and appreciation. As we continue to grow and expand globally, we understand that hiring the right people and treating them well is key to our success! We are a multi-national company with locations in 10 countries, with each location contributing to Proofpoint’s amazing culture!
