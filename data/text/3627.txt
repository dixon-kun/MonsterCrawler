        Department Overview: Data Science Apps (DSA)
        Salesforce.com builds the nerve centers that allow the fortune 500 and small businesses alike to seemlessly manage their customer relationships, housing one of the worlds largest and richest B2B datasets. The Data Science Apps team is a family of multi-talented technologists with backgrounds ranging from Artificial Intelligence to Finance. What connects us all is a shared curiosity for how to drive product strategy with data, a passion for getting the job done, and a healthy disregard for constraints.
        Who You Are:
        * You know your stuff, we don't care how you learned it.
        * You are an expert (10+ years) in statistics or machine learning (and your friends glaze over because you can't stop talking about them over dinner)
        * You have 7+ years experience in writing scalable code. R, Python and Spark are our favorite languages but not your top ones? We can teach you.
        * You know how to beat data into submission using languages like pandas, SQL, Pig, and Spark
        * You have strong communication skills and prefer to chart your own course to get the job done
        * Curious and effective. (Sense a theme? The ability to recognize patterns is important)
        * Know how to prioritize because there are only so many hours in a day and your friends and family deserve some of it
        * You know how to communicate insights with graphs and pictures
        * Understand why simplicity is important and good enough is not a cop out
        * Innovative, you find that unique viewpoint to solve problems that make others run and cower
        * Collaborative, we love to brainstorm together and it would be weird to have one person who never came out of the corner.
        What You Will Do:
        The Principal Data Scientist will report to the Sr. Director, Data Science Apps, working on problems that require tremendous creativity. You will often have to invent your own techniques to get the job done. This candidate will design and deploy predictive models leveraging large data sets including data about our Product Utilization, Sales, and Marketing activities. These models will underpin applications which provide prescriptions for Salesforce Executives to effect the bottom line, primarily through driving product adoption. In detail a successful candidate will need to:
        * Thoroughly understand the company's usage data
        * Support ongoing business analyses as required to inform management decisions
        * Develop algorithms and predictive models to solve critical business problems for salesforce including the development of smarter CRM applications
        * Interface with product teams and executives, modulating communication to match the audience
        * Identify new sources of data, including free web, to enrich ongoing analyses
        * Develop tools and libraries that will help us to surface deeper more valuable insights faster
