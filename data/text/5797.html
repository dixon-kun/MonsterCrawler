We are seeking an individual with 0 – 2 years of experience who is eager to uphold the regulatory structure of the renewable fuels industry on behalf of Genscape clients. In this position, you will be responsible for desk auditing the physical production and transfer of biofuels and renewable energy credits that can be associated with the fuel. You will support the data and documentation collection process for Genscape's Quality Assurance Plan (QAP) and similar future services.

You will also assist in analyzing data that is collected from Genscape monitors at biofuels facilities for auditing processes and maintenance needs. This role will work closely with both the operations and field services teams.

You should view this as a position that will help develop knowledge and experience for a career in energy, compliance, and/or quality assurance. There will be a continuous external client-facing component to the role where you will have dialogue with clients to respond to regulatory questions and to perform the duties of an auditor.

If you enjoy research, are process oriented, and have a passion for the renewable energy industry, than this may be the position for you…

A typical day in the life of a QAP Analyst at Genscape will include:

* Report preparation for Genscape's quarterly desk audit procedures including client documentation review and mass & energy analysis.

* Reviewing monitored data, such as images and time series signals, and compare to data provided by clients.

* Packaging reports to be submitted to regulatory agencies.

* Actively communicating with clients in an auditing capacity.

* Verifying incoming data quality for installed monitors, including basic statistical data analysis and review of imagery.

* Performing research related to the biofuels industry including facility research, EPA regulations, and industry information.

* Coordinating with field services team on installations and maintenance work.

* Other duties as needed for successful operation of the Ag & Biofuels business.

This job might be for you if:

* You are obsessed with detail and data quality.

* You have past experience with research and financial analysis.

* You are an excellent communicator, both with clients and with your team.

* Also, if you are comfortable handling a fluid and demanding work environment, which often coincides with a startup environment, than this will be a great position for you.

* As a team, we value creativity and a strong work ethic. You must have the ability to work well in a team, as well as autonomously.

To land this position, you:

* Must have a Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Data Science, Statistics, Law, or related.

* Need to have a strong attention to detail with ability to follow established processes and procedures carefully.

* Will need an auditing and quantitative mindset.

* Should possess excellent organizational and communication skills.

* Need to have an ability to analyze data from a variety of sources quickly and efficiently.

* Will need the ability to execute in a deadline driven environment.

* Love research.

* Multi-task on a daily basis, are process oriented, and highly analytical.

It would also be great if you have:

* Knowledge of the mechanics and participants of agricultural and biofuels industry.

* Past experience and or familiarity with government compliance and regulations pertaining to the energy industry.

* Past experience with credit analysis, bookkeeping, tax preparation, law, or similar.

Once you land this position, you'll enjoy:

* A fun, fast paced, entrepreneurial environment

* Competitive salary and annual bonus potential

* An awesome office location, with plenty of perks!

* A company that offers a strong focus on work life balance

* Plenty of paid time off

* 401k and a full benefits package that begins on your first day

* The ability to customize your own career path
