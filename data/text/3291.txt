        HR Data Science &amp; Modeling Consultant
        Overview
        * Partner in the research and development of data models to produce workforce insights around employee attitudes, preferences and experiences
        * Design, build and validate workforce models aimed at predicting talent outcomes supporting attraction, workforce development, engagement and retention
        * Work with vendors, IT and business customers to help develop requirements for employee / workforce data
        * Perform data analysis and rigorous testing to ensure high quality assets
        * Analyze new data sources for availability and quality, and integrate with internal sources to support research or analytics
        * Build both ad hoc and reusable data access code and maintain that code to support analytics
        * Provide training and support to analytics team and advance users
        * Partner with IT on data, reporting &amp; analytics projects as needed
        * Create &amp; maintain documentation for models and outcomes
        Qualifications
        * Degree in statistics, actuarial science, economics, computer science, or other quantitative field required; advanced degree preferred.
        * 3 - 5 years' experience in advanced data analytics
        * Moderate programming skills in tools such as R, Python, SAS, SQL, SQR, Tableau
        * Ability to partner with other analysts, business leaders and HR business partners to define modeling requirements and translating requirements into viable, production models
        * Knowledge of HR processes, systems and data, a plus
        * Knowledge of data warehouses, HR, Hiring and Talent Management Systems
