        The Data Operations team is a key department within the company's growing Global Data, Insights and Analytics division. Data Ops is responsible for the discovery, profiling and governance of new and existing data sources. As trusted advisors within GDIA, we help identify untapped opportunities in data, which are then converted into profitable growth.         -Define Data Transformations while retaining the associated lineage and information flows
        -Implement Data Shepherding practices including: creation of Data Standards, Business Glossary, Master Data Management (MDM) and the enforcement Security policies to support data sharing
        -Provide visibility of any Data Quality issues and work with the business owners to resolve
