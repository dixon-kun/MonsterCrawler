        We're looking for a capital markets consultant to assist with debt financing strategy and deal structuring. Our ideal candidate will have some combination of experience in corporate debt, venture debt, and/or asset-backed financing backed by unsecured consumer loans. This is a part-time role, with the potential to develop into a full-time role. Open to candidates working remotely.
        You will primarily work with the Leadership Team, namely the CEO, Chief of Staff, Director of Credit Risk and VP of Operations.
        Responsibilities:
        * Weighing various financing options and recommending the best fit for Tala, based on all-in cost of capital, scalability, scenario analysis, and FX moves, among other considerations
        * Working with the Tala team and deal counsel on deal structure of whichever financing option Tala decides to pursue
        * Periodically interfacing with financing counterparties to discuss deal details
        Requirements:
        * 10+ years of debt financing experience at a bank, fund or other financial institution in a structuring or risk management capacity
        * Comfort modeling various deal structures under different scenarios, in an intuitive manner
        * Strong understanding of deal documentation
        * Experience making strategic financing recommendations
        Preferred requirements:
        * Understanding of asset-backed or corporate debt in emerging markets
        * Experience working with financing counterparties
        * Knowledge of the fintech space
        * CFA holder
