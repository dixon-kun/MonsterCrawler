Senior Associate in Business Modeling &#8211; Data Analytics
Across industries, our clients are focused on improving business performance, responding quickly and effectively to crisis situations, and extracting value from transactions. As part of EY's Transaction Advisory Services practice, our growing Business Modeling team supports and enhances our clients&#8217; financial decision-making processes. We work with corporate and private equity clients on complex decisions, often involving multiple stakeholders and high degrees of uncertainty, where such decisions require issue assessment/definition, solution design and rigorous financial analysis, robust modeling and advanced data analytics. We work in all industries and operate across a broad range of disciplines including:
+ Decision support and analysis
+ Transaction evaluation and management
+ Benefits estimation, management and realization 
+ Business case development
+ Budgeting and forecasting and management planning
+ Simulation and optimization
+ Model risk assessment and remediation
+ Descriptive data analytics as part of the transaction lifecycle
+ Predictive data analytics focused on the capital allocation process as well as the broader corporate finance function
Specifically, our data analytics service offerings leverage the power of data to enable our clients to execute their transactions in an efficient and accelerated manner to maximize the deal value and make more efficient and effective financial and operational decisions.  Our analytics solutions extract fact-based, data driven insights to inform deal makers on a variety of high impact topics from the divested business' financial statements to optimization of the combined entity&#8217;s pricing and customer structure post transaction. Our Business Modeling team brings a unique perspective through the convergence of deal acumen, finance and accounting, statistical analytics and deep technical capabilities.  We are currently looking for a Senior Associate specializing in Data Analytics to join our high performing team.
