POSITION SUMMARY

Work Remotely: This position can be located anywhere in the U.S.

Reporting to the Senior Director, Promotions Account Executives, the Sr. Retail Account Exeutive, will be responsible for driving Inmar’s digital business efforts with one or more of Inmar’s strategic retail partners. He/she will be a strategic, creative thinker that will devise and frame approaches to meet revenue targets.

The Sr. Account Executive will be responsible for relationship management with existing strategic business partners. This position also requires the ability to work across all aspects of the business spectrum from strategic planning to project management to detailed execution. A successful Sr. Account Executive will continually analyze programs and make specific recommendations for optimal performance. He/she will effortlessly communicate across all levels of Inmar and partner organizations to sell key constituents on new programs/services and to ensure all parties are meeting program goals

This position will also develop and execute business plans to improve con sumer engagement and across assigned IDPN retailer sites. The ideal candidate is someone with business and account management experience, experience working with or in Federal government is preferred.

The successful candidate will flourish in a team environment and will exude positive energy and demonstrate a strong work ethic. If you have a desire to grow with a dynamic company, make a major difference within the organization, have great advancement opportunities and thrive on working in a fast paced, exciting environment, then this is the opportunity for you.

MAJOR JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:

Manage Inmar’s digital & loyalty programs for assigned Retail Partners

  • Garner holistic knowledge of the long term strategies and short term needs of each of the assigned Retail Partners for use in devising business plans and programs that tie into those strategies and enable Inmar and each partner to meet or exceed goals.
  • Leverage Retail expertise to develop innovative marketing and promotional plans for acquisition & engagement programs to exceed revenue goals.
  • Respond to Client's requests and inquiries in a timely manner and develop proactive communications to establish a strong collaborative relationship with each Partner.
  • Works with Inmar and Partner departments (e.g., Operations, IT, Implementation Teams, Marketing, Reporting, etc.) to understand trends of the business, identify and seize new opportunities to grow revenue, business opportunities with each partner and engagement and solve any potential issues.
  • Think outside the box; bring new programs that are relevant to each Retailer by working directly with Category Management or the MFR community.
  • Develop merchant programs; work with Retail Merchants as advocates to the vendor community.

Manage existing Retail Partner relationship(s) to enhance client satisfaction and drive results

  • Develop overall knowledge of clients business including in-depth knowledge of marketing plans, objectives and strategies
  • Build relationships with Category Management & Marketing to design cohesive plan for CPG sales and promotions.
  • Develop strategic business plans to identify opportunities for Inmar to further penetrate client’s business with additional products and services to maximize revenue within assigned clients.
  • Execute business/marketing plan to meet clients digital marketing objectives, drive consumer awareness, customer acquisition, and continued growth of digital within client

Development and sales of brand sponsored digital programs

  • Play a key role in the development of a Client-branded structured program that blend the goals and objectives of the retailer client with the needs of the brand participants
  • Present pricing and program overview to interested brands and close sales independently or in conjunction with Inmar’s sales team.
  • Coordinate programs with CPG’s, category managers, merchandisers, and marketing to ensure seamless execution.

Analyze and make recommendations regarding Program performance

  • Deliver insights on program outcomes and develop recommendations for future programs and opportunities.
  • Develop metrics and measures to help quickly access the relative success or failure of programs, and the overall success of the program to the client.

EDUCATION / QUALIFICATIONS / EXPERIENCE

  • Assertive, entrepreneurial business person with 4-6 years of experience in marketing/sales, partner management, business management, business development.
  • 5 or more years of experience in the Retail and/or CPG industry strongly preferred
  • Knowledge of digital marketing strongly preferred
  • Proven skill in developing strategic plans to meet short term business objectives and evolve partner relationships to ensure future growth and gaining buy in on those plans from partners.
  • Strong leadership and negotiation skills mandatory.
  • Ability to communicate, cultivate relationships and work with various internal customers and external partners.
  • Ability to analyze internal, coupon-related business operations and make appropriate recommendations.
  • Ability to learn and understand common retailer metrics at all levels of retailer business hierarchy.
  • Proficiency in adapting to rapidly changing business needs and ensuing opportunities.
  • Ability to prioritize and manage multiple tasks requiring extensive follow up and timely completion.
  • Expertise in delivering solutions and value to retail and/or CPG partners.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Positive attitude and ability to operate within and foster a team environment.
  • Must thrive in a fast-paced, high pressure, dynamic atmosphere.
  • Solid presentation, strong project management skills and acute attention to detail.
  • BA/BS Degree required, MBA preferred.
  • Ability to adapt to rapidly changing business needs and ensuing opportunities
