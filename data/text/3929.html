RESEARCH HEALTH SCIENCE SPECIALIST

Palo Alto, CA

Duties:

This position serves as a Research Health Science Specialist in the Program Evaluation Resource Center (PERC) within the VA Palo Alto Health Care System in Palo Alto, CA. Duties include, but may not be limited to the following:

* Develops or contributes to data products (dashboards, databases, data tables, and files) which can be shared and accessed by a variety of customers.

* Creates and maintains systems and databases designed to ensure that projects comply with relevant policies.

* Completes projects independently that are both methodologically sound and meet requested timeframes.

* Plans the acquisition of data resources to meet project needs, for example, by interacting with database administrators for different data systems.

* Uses big data to understand areas including mental health and substance use disorder treatment, health system functioning, and staffing.

* Performs Extract, Transform, Load (ETL) activities with a deep understanding of relational databases.

* Trains data analysts on best practices for data analysis, database management, and data security and privacy issues.

* Mentors and provides technical and scientific supervision to a wide range of personnel to advance their work with big data.

* Documents existing processes and events that occur during query design and development to ensure operational continuity.

* Generates clear and thorough responses to ad hoc queries to ensure stakeholders receive results in their requested timeframe.

* Qualifications & Evaluations

* BASIC EDUCATION REQUIREMENT Applicants must have a bachelor's degree (or higher) with major study in an academic field related to the health sciences or allied sciences appropriate to the work of this position. (TRANSCRIPT REQUIRED)

This position includes a Selective Placement Factor (skill, knowledge, ability or other worker characteristic basic to - and essential for - satisfactory performance of the job). Selective Placement Factors are a prerequisite to appointment and represent minimum requirements for a position. Applicants who do not meet it are ineligible for further consideration.

The Selective Placement Factor for this position is: Experience using Structured Querying Language (SQL), SQL Server Integration Services (SSIS), and database design for SQL Server Reporting Services (SSRS) to analyze extremely large datasets?

AND

SPECIALIZED EXPERIENCE GS-13: In addition to meeting the basic education requirement above, applicants must have at least one (1) year of specialized experience that equipped them with the knowledge, skill and ability to successfully perform the duties of this position, and that is typically in or related to the work of this position. To be creditable, this specialized experience must have been equivalent to at least the next lower grade level in the Federal service. Qualifying specialized experience includes: Understanding large and complex health care programs and datasets, including health care coding systems; Developing or contributing to data products (dashboards, databases, data tables, and files) which can be shared and accessed by a variety of customers; Using big data to understand areas including mental health and substance use disorder treatment, health system functioning, and staffing; Planning the acquisition of data resources to meet project needs, for example, by interacting with database administrators for different data systems; Performing Extract, Transform, Load (ETL) activities with a deep understanding of relational databases; Mentoring and providing technical and scientific supervision to a wide range of personnel to advance their work with big data; Generating clear and thorough responses to ad hoc queries to ensure stakeholders receive results in their requested timeframe; Consulting with colleagues to analyze, interpret, and anticipate technical trends in data science and working with big data.

You will be rated on the following Knowledge, Skills, Abilities, and Other characteristics (KSAOs) as part of the assessment questionnaire for this position:

* Skill planning and conducting complex, high-profile projects involving big data.

* Ability to apply appropriate research and statistical methods to extract value from data sources.

* Skill collaborating on program evaluation projects to improve mental health treatment.

* Ability to communicate information in writing and in person in a clear and concise manner to diverse stakeholders.

PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS: The work is primarily sedentary.
