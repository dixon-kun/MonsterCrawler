        Position Summary:
        The Product Manager, Digital Conversations will primarily be responsible for developing and managing the life cycle of products and tools including product strategy, requirements gathering, cost/benefits analysis, scoping, development, roll-out, reporting, optimization &amp; continuous innovation. Specific product focus areas may include:
        * Digital engagement applications
        * Sales and customer service conversations
        * AI backed bot models assisting customers, &amp; customer service teams
        The Product Manager will utilize available data, insights, and voice of the customer/associates to make informed decisions about product roadmaps and their prioritization. He/she will keep abreast of evolving industry tools and technologies that enhance the website, inside sales &amp; field sales experience to better serve Staples customers. Given the highly strategic and cross-channel nature of this role, the successful individual must possess a unique combination of structured strategic thinking, passion, and curiosity for technology, as well as strong leadership and collaboration skills.
        Responsibilities:
        * Own product strategy, roadmap, and delivery
        * Analysis of internal &amp; external customer needs
        * Interpret sales &amp; customer performance metrics and the financial implication
        * Develop the near, mid and long-term holistic roadmap of initiatives aligned to business objectives
        * Assessment of build vs. partner options with 3rd parties including the industry trends
        * Execution of the in-house or 3rd party vendor solutions including running of various tests/pilots through A/B testing
        * Closely work with Staples Global Technology, Sales Operations and various cross-functional teams to deliver, and manage the product lifecycle
        * Strong knowledge of CRM tools and ability to merge online/offline sales and service efforts for the benefit of the customer and efficiencies. Salesforce.com knowledge is a plus
        * Unlock the power of data
        * Develop various frameworks and point of views to ground internal teams and stakeholders on data-driven product approach
        * Ability to work with technical teams to understand existing data systems and retrieval of information as an input to the sales enablement product &amp; tools
        * Understand requirements of sales &amp; marketing teams and work closely with data science and analytic teams for testing and deployment
        * Focus on product performance, data insights, optimization and continuous innovation
        * Help define key performance indicators for each product/tool/capability
        * Develop business case to justify the prioritization and benefits
        * Track and report on the benefits/impact after go-live
        * Drive broad organizational engagement to deliver products on-time and on budget
        * Energetic, passionate, and inspired leadership
        * An active networker partnering with cross-functional teams across Staples
        * Regularly, impactful communication of progress and impact
        * Develop processes to listen, learn and incorporate customer feedback in products
