Senior Analytics Consultant

DataXu's vision is to make marketing better using data science. The world's top brands and agencies partner with DataXu to better understand and engage their customers across all available media formats, devices, channels, and buying modes. Our advertising solution, ranked #1 in the industry by Forrester Research in 2015, provides marketers with unparalleled Media Activation, Marketing Analytics and Data Management capabilities.

https://www.dataxu.com/programmatic-marketing/

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5lBeQxdSEAs

On the Job

* Reinforce the value of the DataXu platform through advanced analytical insights and strategic recommendations for our clients and their advertising campaigns

* Derive analysis and insights for advertisers using consumer data acquired from their datasets including online ad campaigns

* Present insights to advertisers creating a narrative that is easy to follow, typically in PowerPoint

* Create innovative analytics, define requirements for analysis automation, and propose platform improvements

* Support internal client and sales teams with sales proposals to highlight our advanced analytics offerings

About You…

* Experience working with clients to understand their business needs and translating their questions into insightful and actionable analysis

* Comfort presenting complex but clear analysis to non-technical audiences

* Great verbal and written communicator; experience and comfort in client-facing environments

* Ability to prepare analysis on their own, leveraging their understanding of datasets, excel, and basic statistics

* Willingness to 'roll up sleeves' and work in a start-up environment

* Experience in advertising, marketing analytics, or be a quick learner of the marketing environment

* Good command of Excel, including pivot tables, and PowerPoint slide creation

* Understanding of Data mining and predictive modeling techniques

* Understanding of databases (SQL query language)

* Preferred – strong SQL or other familiarity with other programming languages
