Responsibilities:

Under the general supervision of the Director of Business Intelligence, responsibilities will include analysis, design and development activities related to automated testing and data load for operations processing. As a Quality Assurance Technical Analyst, this resource will participate in the data analysis, script design and development. Working closely with the Director of Business Intelligence and Data Science team this resource will be required to understand the Operations Processing and execution across all lines of business.

* Ownership of system functionality from an end-to-end automation of Operation processes, in particular the processing of claims.

* Write and manage design documentation and program specifications for development.

* Performing Q/A for programs developed and code changes made.

* Monitor Operations Activities (Claims, Provider, Enrollment, UM)

* Write Program Specifications for Development

* Utilize SQL and SAS to analyze data prior to the execution of automate processes to validate data is eligible for the project.

* Utilize SQL and MS Excel post execution of the automated process to analyze results and provide data for the requestor of the project.

Education/Experience:

* Bachelor's Degree in Healthcare Informatics, Computer Science or related field required

* Three to five years developing and testing programs with SAS and SQL to verify desired results are achieved.

* Developing and executing both manual and automated test scripts / cases for multiple products, also provide critical analysis and refinement of Business Requirements.

* Experience in HealthCare claims, clinical and financials area is important.

* QA/Testing experience is a plus.

* Experience working in the Hadoop environment is a plus.

* High level of organizational, written and oral communication skills

* Strong background in healthcare operations (claims and/or system configuration)

* Strong abilities to analyze requirements, conduct research, think analytically and work in a diverse group situations

* Proficient in Microsoft Office applications (Access, Excel, Word, PowerPoint)
