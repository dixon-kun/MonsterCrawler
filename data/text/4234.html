Description

We deliver our customers peace of mind every day by helping them protect what they value most. Our passion for placing the customer at the center of everything we do is driving a transformational shift at Liberty Mutual. Operating as a tech startup within a Fortune 100 company, we are leading a digital disruption that will redefine how people experience insurance.



Standard job introduction:


Global Specialty Information Technology team provides support and operations for all Global Specialty lines including Liberty International Underwriters (LIU), Liberty Specialty Markets (LSM), and Liberty Mutual Surety (LMS) across 19 countries, through one unified Technology team. We are looking to add a Senior Software Developer on our team. The successful candidate will work closely with the Global Specialty Business and Analytics teams in order to deliver Advanced Analytics to the End User in a consistent, reliable and scalable way. Additionally, the candidate will participate in a new technology evaluations aimed at increasing Global Specialty’s ability and capacity to deliver models rapidly around the world.



Responsibilities:

  • Collaborates with Global Specialty’s Technology architecture team and Global Specialty’s IM technologists to understand and incorporate design patterns and reference architectures and best practices in place for Global Specialty’s IM team.

  • Contributes to solution design, documenting a high level and detailed design document, in collaboration with the Global Specialty’s IM Business Intelligence and data architects and incorporating the patterns, models and standards previously identified.

  • Supports Global Specialty’s Analytics team in SAS Best Practices and Model Deployments.

  • Understands large scale data models of existing Business Intelligence and source systems.



Desired Skills:

  • Bachelors or Master's degree in technical or business discipline or equivalent experience.

  • Minimum 5+ years of development experience.

  • Minimum 2+ in MS BI technologies (SSAS, SSRS, SSIS)

  • Familiarity in Modeling/Data Science Languages (SAS, R)

  • Strong oral and written communication skills; presentation skills.

  • Proficient in negotiation, facilitation and consensus building skills.

  • Proficient in new and emerging technologies.

  • Thorough knowledge of the following: IT concepts, strategies and methodologies. Business function(s) and of business operations.

  • Design and development tools. Architectures and technical standards. Thorough knowledge of layered systems architectures and layered solutions and designs; understanding of shared software concepts. Proficiency in multiple programming languages and tools. Understanding of agile software development concepts and processes

  • Must be proactive, and demonstrate initiative and be a logical thinker.

  • Consultative skills, including the ability to understand and apply customer requirements, including drawing out unforeseen implications and making recommendations for design, the ability to define design reasoning, understanding potential impacts of design requirements.

  • Thorough understanding of backlog tracking, burn down metrics, and incremental delivery. Collaboration, prioritization, and adaptability skills required.
