Marketing Summer Internship

We’re looking for a Marketing Intern to join the Business Support Marketing Team for Atlas Copco Rental in La Porte, Texas.

Current undergraduate student of junior or senior status majoring in marketing, management, or data science. Minimum 3.0 GPA

  • Exceptional research and analytical abilities
  • Assist with administrative and project management tasks

  • Working on marketing projects including:
    • Contract accounts data management and analytics
    • Optimized pricing initiatives
    • R-PSM (CRM) – Dashboards usage study/implementation
    • Profitable Growth Accelerator (PGA) project team support
