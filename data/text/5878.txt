        What You're Applying For:
        The Analytics Research and Development team at Edmunds is seeking an R&amp;D Data Scientist who will play a crucial role in designing and implementing data-driven research initiatives. These initiatives will involve in-depth studies of Edmunds' users and clients, as well as the automotive marketplace, to directly improve revenue-producing products and processes at Edmunds and inspire new product development. Each R&amp;D project will involve sound statistical models, carefully planned and executed experiments, creative uses of Edmunds' vast data resources, thoughtful analysis and beautiful visualization of results.
        We are looking for a person that is smart, creative, ambitious and able to work and communicate well with a variety of people from varying technical backgrounds. We especially want someone who is excited about engaging in data-driven research and using this research to strategically optimize Edmunds' product offerings for our customers and clients. Edmunds is very much about helping its people grow - meaningfully, effectively and prosperously. We care about what we do and we care about our people. A demonstrated passion for research and analytics will provide incredible growth opportunities and will make this the best job you've ever had.
        What You'll Do:
        * Design and implement in-depth studies of Edmunds' users and clients, using machine learning and a wide array of cutting-edge data science techniques
        * Build predictive models to optimize the Edmunds experience
        * Have a hand in the end-to-end implementation of machine learning models
        * Design and implement A/B tests
        * Prepare visualizations of data in order to explain and persuade
        * Present results to key business decision-makers throughout the company
        * Identify key areas for future research
        Challenges:
        Key challenges include generating significant results that lead to actionable insights that are consistent with key business goals and communicating those results effectively to business decision makers.
        What You Need:
        * Bachelor's Degree in a quantitative field preferred AND

        * 3-4 years' work experience in an analytics-related position
        * Strong R and/or Python skills
        * Strong statistical analysis/modeling/machine learning skills
        * Ability to extract data using SQL
        * Ability to take findings and make simple/beautiful/easy-to-interpret data visualizations
        * Excellent presentation and communication skills
        * Enjoyable person to work with
