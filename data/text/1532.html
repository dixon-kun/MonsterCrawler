AbbVie Data Science is a global organization consisting of more than 200 individuals dispersed across 8 business centers. Approximately, 50% of these individuals are provided by a functional service provider (FSP) in India. The Total Quality Management Lead will be required to work with the FSP leadership to maintain a sustainable business model including robust processes, statistical control and cost of quality methods, while also managing general Data Science business interest in India. While this is a Lake County, IL-based role, 75% travel is expected.

Primary Job Function:

Identifies, develops and implements required policies and procedures.

Manages quality and process control.

Liaises with Data Sciences leadership and various functional groups by giving progress reports.

Coordinates all activities of functional groups to ensure that tasks undertaken do not overlap, repeat or conflict.

Creates an environment of continuous improvement and learning.

Ensures adequate communications at all levels and among all organization members.

Utilizes appropriate quality tools to promote TQM culture and ensure process robustness.

Promotes and implements statistical techniques throughout the organization to better view and control processes.

Creates and manages a system and processes to ensure continuous inspection readiness.

Utilizes cost of quality techniques to control and effectively improve process quality.

Promotes six sigma techniques to improve quality of processes.

Utilizes quality by design as a core guiding principle

Maps processes and utilizes quality tools to improve them.

Develops, monitors, measures and analyzes processes within the organization.

Develops means of data analysis to identify problematic process elements and to improve on process design.

Aligns operations, globally, including operations shared across internal and external resources

Ensures that providers meet quality and agility levels equal to those of internal resources and operations for all deliverables

Core Job Functions:

Responsible for compliance with applicable corporate and divisional policies and procedures.

Responsible for the creation of a framework that ensures robust process design and control, and continuous inspection readiness.

Establish a quality orientation within DSS and with key within key provider operations.

Create and utilize process analysis tools.

Participate in the definition and creation of global processes.

Manage relationships with outsourced clinical support teams with respect to quality, process control, and remediation efforts.

Responsible for timely and effective communication with regard to state of quality and processes across Data Sciences and providers, both internal and external.

Communicate requirements for inspection readiness to Data Sciences teams and quality stakeholders.

Manage inspection readiness activities.

Will be accountable for the reporting of metrics that accurately reflect the state of the business

Position Accountability & Scope:

Accountable for establishing a quality focused culture within DSS and across affiliated providers.

Accountable for creating and utilizing process analysis tools to design, monitor and refine processes.

Accountable to assist in the definition of processes with focus on quality and process robustness.

Accountable for directing outsourced clinical support teams in regard to process and quality control.

Accountable for effective communication with Data Sciences leadership team.

Accountable for establishment of continuous inspection readiness within Data Sciences.

The TQM Lead will travel approximately 75% with majority of travel within India.

Position will be hired based on level of experience

Must have 6 years of total quality management, general operations or clinical development experience.

Must have knowledge of data analytics as applied to process control and experience in using analytical tools.

Must have strong understanding of regulatory guidelines (e.g., FDA, EMEA, PMDA, MHRA)

Must have technical expertise in using analytical tools.

Must have deep understanding of the clinical development process.

Must have demonstrated effective communication skills and ability to influence without authority.

Global experience is required

Key Leadership Competencies:

Builds strong relationships with peers and cross functionally with partners outside of team to enable higher performance

Learns fast, grasps the 'essence' and can change the course quickly where indicated

Raises the bar and is never satisfied with the status quo

Creates a learning environment, open to suggestions and experimentation for improvement

Embraces the ideas of others, nurtures innovation and manages innovation to reality
