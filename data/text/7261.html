Econometric Modeler with R programming at Experis

Washington, DC 20431

Report this job

About the Job


Position: Econometric Modeler
Location: Washington, DC (metro accessible)
Duration: Long Term (multi year) Contract
Required: PhD with superior R programming experience/knowledge

Econometric Modeler with R programming
PhD ideally in Applied Math, Statistics, Data Science, Physics, Computer Science, or Engineering (Mechanical or Electrical)
Excellent R programming expertise with demonstrated experience (from Master, PhD, research or work experience)
High-performance computing
Optimizing computational solutions

The successful candidate will serve as a member of the Econometric and Modeling Support Team, and work on quantitative finance modeling, advanced econometric methods and numerical algorithms using several programming languages. In addition, the candidate will participate in the design and implementation of high-performance computing solutions for large-scale computationally intensive models. The successful candidate will also be expected to provide support and training for the models being developed.

This position designs, develops, and optimizes computational solutions to address the specific scientific computing needs of financial and economic models, ensuring that the appropriate technology resources are identified and utilized optimally. This position utilizes applicable software packages and algorithms, and assists in achieving high scalability and parallelization as needed.

Knowledge
Expert knowledge of R.
Python experience is a plus.
Knowledge of high-performance computing architectures and systems, including parallel programming models (e.g., OpenMP, MPI, CUDA).
Advanced knowledge of Data Science related tools.
Advanced knowledge of Mathematica is a plus.

Skills
Making complex technical design decisions involving numerical algorithm implementation and testing.
Following up current academic research on quantitative finance, computational economics, statistics, and econometrics.
Training or teaching others on highly specialized topics.
Finding innovative solutions to unusual problems.
Writing and communicating effectively with peers, users and management.
