Machine Learning + Cloud Analytics for Enterprise Marketers Our team is changing the way marketing decision makers understand their cross channel portfolio and with our advanced attribution platform (XC Logic) delivering actionable insights in real-time across all devices. By using our proprietary Ensemble Method and state-of-the-art machine learning, which enables us to break free of the one size fits all algorithm and create a truly unique dynamic predictive solution for our customers, marketers can identify clear understandable ways to achieve efficiency gains of 30-50%. And as an independent lean startup, Conversion Logic is untethered from any media entity influence. Changing an industry is never a small task and requires team of exceptional people. Our Client Services Team is seeking a Sales Engineer who will leverage their experience in Ad Tech and industry insights to ensure the integrity of ‘big data’ flowing in and out of our platform. Someone who can work closely with our Sales, Client Services, Engineering and Data Science teams, our wonderful big-brand customers and leading industry technology vendors, and must be able to communicate effectively both in technical and non-technical settings. The Solutions Engineer must be able to quickly understand complex distributed systems and architect scalable and robust systems for collecting data. Strong tech chops are a must...along with a broad knowledge of ad serving and tracking technologies and systems. HOW YOU WILL HAVE AN IMPACT... • Support and assist Sales by providing technical overviews of the Conversion Logic platform for prospective clients, and by identifying complexities and optimal configurations during (and after) the Sales cycle. • Understand platform limitations and opportunities for extensibility. • Provide recommendations for scalable and robust integrations of client and partner data into the Conversion Logic platform Ensure alignment across internal teams and external clients and partners regarding technical integrations through communication and documentation. WHAT YOU WILL NEED... • B.S. or Minor in an analytical field such as Computer Science, Math, Statistics, Engineering, Economics or equivalent experience. • 3-5 years work experience with data and Internet technologies. • The ability to read code (Python), API documentation and write technical specifications for the engineering teams. • Analytical thinker with ability to juggle multiple projects and context switch throughout the day. • Problem Solver with ability to articulate trade-offs and solutions to technical and non-technical audiences. • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment with tight deadlines. • Working knowledge and experience with several areas of technology (relational or NoSQL databases, networking protocols, big data / cloud computing services, etc). • Team-player mentality with a GREAT attitude. • Travel to clients as needed (about 30% of time). • Experience with Digital Advertising and Data Management Platforms is a huge plus! So if you like solving data, product or engineering puzzles. Are excited by great data visualization. If happy clients make your day. And want to work in a lean startup with brilliant people. Then Conversion Logic is your next company. Our culture is defined by smart people who like to solve difficult problems.
