        About the company
        Boxed is the easiest and most modern way to shop for bulk goods. As a mobile-first company, customers can shop directly from Boxed's app on their iOS or Android mobile device, or online at www.boxed.com. With Boxed, customers can have their household, personal care, and pantry favorites delivered conveniently to their home or office.
        Our office is a dynamic, collaborative family of individuals. A technology company at core, we are a vibrant, fun and close group of engineers, designers, marketers, and operations specialists with a passion for driving superior value and experience to our customers.
        Our core value is transparency. We believe the company's performance is everyone's business, and we share the results of our work with all stakeholders. We're transparent about what we believe in and what we don't. In the end, we're all working together to make this company a success.
        Business Operations Team
        Business operations is a new team that will unlock scale to support our rapid growth. It is a tremendous opportunity to be a founding member of this critical team, both in terms of contribution to the company's success and the potential for personal and professional growth.
        We believe in using data to inform decisions, and we work hard to ensure that every dollar invested can create maximum value for the company. Business operations work cross-functionally to improve business processes, develop standardized analyses and tools, and support strategic initiatives through project-based and ad-hoc analyses. Our contributions empower, support, and evaluate business decisions and performance. We will play a crucial role in revealing and capitalizing on revenue growth, margin expansion, cash preservation, and other strategic opportunities. This role will report directly to the VP of Finance &amp; Strategy.
        You will:
        * Partner with various teams at Boxed including marketing, planning, merchandising, B2B, partnerships, data science, product management, and more; here are example initiatives:
        * Develop spend management and reporting tools for marketing acquisition team by channel and partner (e.g., CAC, ROAS, ROI) and for cohort based insights into customer behavior (e.g., LTV/CLV, recency, frequency)
        * Develop tools to empower merchandise buying teams to develop and manage unit planning and reporting across key metrics (e.g., Sales/GMV, sell-through, product margins, inventory turns)
        * Empower inventory replenishment teams to efficiently maximize sales by developing analytical tools to analyze inventory availability, out-of-stocks, inventory turns, supply chain management, and other key drivers
        * Develop and manage various projects in support of operations monitoring and performance management; develop evaluation tools to empower teams to evaluate and prioritize new business opportunities, including needs assessments, costs and benefits estimates, requirements definitions/documentation, and analysis
        * Synthesize and distribute reports with actionable insights to varied stakeholders including executive team, operations teams, and others
        * Support RFP processes and evaluations of new tools and upgrades to existing tools for operations
        * Support F&amp;S team in their forecasting efforts and the annual planning processes
        Requirements
        * Target 2-4+ years of experience in analytically focused role, ideally management consulting, banking, private equity and/or start-ups
        * Experience in consumer products, retail, and/or tech-enabled services a plus
        * Familiar with P&amp;L and metrics-based reporting; experience with financial planning applications and/or processes preferred; strong level of proficiency in Excel and Powerpoint
        * Experience applying advanced analytics to drive sales and profit: data analysis, segmentation, regression modeling, forecasting, predictive modeling
        * Experience with business intelligence tools (e.g., Periscope, DOMO) and comfort working with and manipulating large amounts of data (preferably SQL); experience with google analytics a plus
        * Strong interpersonal and communication skills, including the ability to present information and interact effectively across all levels of the organization
        * Strong business judgment and communication skills; high level of organization with ability to manage multiple projects; track record of taking ownership and executing on opportunities to drive results in a fast-paced, unstructured environment
