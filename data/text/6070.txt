        The University of Southern California (USC), founded in 1880, is located in the heart of downtown L.A. and is the largest private employer in the City of Los Angeles. As an employee of USC, you will be a part of a world-class research university and a member of the "Trojan Family," which is comprised of the faculty, students and staff that make the university what it is.
        University Advancement is seeking an experienced data scientist to join its team and create analytical solutions for business problems in fundraising. This position is responsible for delivering accurate business information, high-quality analytical datasets, data visualization and reports.
        Responsibilities
        * Develop and support reports from Salesforce using Cognos, Tableau, and/or other reporting software
        * Assist in standardizing database design and data transformation
        * Extract data from various data sources using SQL
        * Monitor quality of data and assist in fixing issues once discovered
        * Ensure the validity and accuracy of data and reports on a regular basis
        * Collaborate and brainstorm on problems with advanced users and experts
        * Document processes and data sources
        * Conduct other duties as assigned
        Who you are
        * You have strong critical thinking and project management skills
        * You understand the importance of data validation and data quality improvement
        * You strive to provide excellent customer service
        * You have a passion to learn and create new things
        What you will bring
        * Bachelor's Degree in Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science, Engineering or any other quantitative majors
        * Work experience in this field: 2 or more years
        * Expert knowledge of SQL
        * Database management skills using Microsoft SQL Server, Oracle, Salesforce or other cloud databases
        * Reporting skills using software including but not limited to Cognos and Tableau
        * Strong problem solving and analytical skills
        * Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
        Note to applicant
        * Attached cover letter and resume required
        Minimum Education: Master's degree, Combined experience/education as substitute for minimum education
        Minimum Experience: 2 years, Combined education/experience as substitute for minimum experience
        Minimum Field of Expertise: Biometry, Biostatistics or Statistics; mainframe computer and PC experience; experience with SAS, Epilog, BMDP, GLIM or SPSS.
