Sr. Staff SQA Engineer (Big Data/Cloud)

Engineering Santa Clara, California

Apply

Description

Palo Alto Networks® is the fastest-growing security company in history.  We offer the chance to be part of an important mission: ending breaches and protecting our way of digital life. If you are a motivated, intelligent, creative, and hardworking individual, then this job is for you!

Palo Alto Networks provides threat intelligence cloud services to our customers. As a Senior Software QA Engineer for our cloud services, you will be responsible for testing different components of the system for gathering/ingesting data from multiple heterogeneous sources and processing data for reporting/analytics. This work would involve working with our research teams including data scientists to derive intelligence from our data.


Requirements:

  • 6+ years of experience in a QA position.
  • Experience with different tools for massive data set analysis.
  • Experience with testing analytics/reporting on processed data.
  • Experience with testing data ingestion from multiple sources.
  • Drive different testing strategies for different aspect of a big data system, from data ingestion to system level features, functionality and scalability.
  • Experience with bug tracking tools such as Bugzilla or Jira.
  • Experience in setting up environment with large data processing tools such as Hadoop, HBase, ElasticSearch, RabbitMQ, and Kafka.
  • Linux expertise.
  • Experience with scripting and/or programming on test automation is a must.
  • User experience with databases, such as MySQL, MongoDB, or similar.
  • Experience with Python, Java, Tomcat, JS, AWS, Docker, VMWare ESX, and SaltStack is a plus.
  • Excellent communication and collaborative skills.

Qualifications:

  • BS or advanced degree in computer engineering or related technical field.
