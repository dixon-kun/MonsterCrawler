        Job Summary: As a Data Architect, you will work with the business and technical teams to define the BI or analytics solutions needed to meet the short term and long term business objective. You will be responsible for architecting the data flow, data integration patterns, logical and physical data models, define new data standards, and define and design integrations to and from our analytical environments (Hadoop, Reporting Cubes, Complex Event Processing, EDW, and specialized data marts).
        HEAD FOR BUSINESS - Ability to leverage your technical expertise and business process knowledge to help define, design and implement great solutions across many lines of business (Supply Chain, Transportation, Rx, Store Merchandising, Purchasing, Inventory Management, e-Commerce, Customer Analytics, Accounting, Loss Prevention, and much more)?
        HEART FOR PEOPLE - Willingness to share your experience and knowledge while working on many different teams?
        PASSION FOR RESULTS - Adaptability and innovation, with a strong eye for the possible and an ear for the business problem?
        You should have:
        * 5-8 Years of deep experience with designing and scaling data
        * Experience in OLTP, OLAP, and unstructured data across numerous data platforms
        * Track record for listening to many points of view, ask clarifying questions, and can lead a group to design the best solutions
        * A proven ability to pivot as business clarity is defined
        * Extremely strong analytical and problem solving skills
        * CS degree, Bachelors in Information Systems, or proven experience
        What is the work? (other duties may be assigned as necessary)
        Data Design:
        * Follow the data: Design data flows and define integration and transformation patterns from data creation into end points.
        * Understand the business: Work with the IT and business teams to define the business opportunity and associated business processes and systems.
        * Balance the long-term and short-term: Design and anticipate for the long term while delivering value back to the business as quickly as possible.
        * Design for scalability: For our DW environment, able to define and design proper usage columnar datasets vs standard relational datasets.
        * Enterprise data design: Proven experience in advanced data modeling and data modeling within an enterprise data warehouse.
        * Analytical modeling: Able to work with data science and data analytic teams to understand the required inputs and outputs into various statistical models.
        * Technology selection: Select technologies that meet the business and organizational objectives to fulfill the business objectives.
        * Physical environments: Has a strong understanding of the physical environment, constraints, and available options. Is able to make advanced recommendations when expansions may be needed.
        Teamwork and Mentorship:
        * Business process understanding: Able to work with business teams to learn and refine the business opportunities. This individual should be a person who seeks clarity in order to make a good business recommendation, and then refine that recommendation with more input.
        * Technical thought leadership: As a technical thought leader, you will be responsible for recommending and vetting new data technologies.
        * Training data engineering staff: Work with data development teams to improve usage of tools and data processing techniques. Able to get into the details in order to find technical design gaps, tool gaps, or implementation opportunities.
        * Implementation Verification: An energetic person to follow their recommendations into production. You should be willing to verify and validate the application teams implemented your collective solutions effectively and accurately.
        * Broad architectural consideration: Able to gather and implement feedback from other application, security, and data architects.
        Communication and Learning:
        * Concise written communication: Able to remove excess noise from written technical communication to effectively give development code ready architectural design models and documents
        * Be the question: Able to humbly ask questions when tasked with business challenges that you have limited familiarity.
        * Head for the situation: Able to decipher various business situations to know when to go deep with a technological recommendation or when to focus on the process of the solution.
        * Constant learner: Proven track record of being a student of the business and an eye to understand technology. Able to develop an opinion on the forces in technology today and which should be considered as best of breed in their space, and which are too early to jump into.
        * Constant learner: Able to keep an eye on emerging trends to identify opportunities to prove out viability, timeliness and overall effectiveness of the numerous trends.
        * Standards definition: Able to define and evolve data standards as technologies are implemented or retired.
