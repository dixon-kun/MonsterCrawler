        Job Responsibilities
        This Lead Infrastructure Engineer position is responsible for the design, deployment, maintenance, and monitoring of production systems running within the company's cloud infrastructure. The successful applicant will work within the Cloud Infrastructure Team and with external support groups during all phases of the deployment and maintenance of the system environments. This is a hands-on IT infrastructure position in a dynamic and fast paced environment.
        Essential Duties and Responsibilities
        * Lead a team of infrastructure engineers on technical architecture and project planning
        * Handle escalations from within the team and from other departments at the company
        * Mentor, guide, and cross-train team members
        * Lead architecture discussions with the team, other departments, and directly with external customers of the company
        * Design, deploy, and maintain system environments and applications within the cloud infrastructure
        * Follow and lead change control by reviewing changes for technical accuracy and risk analysis
        * Work with external vendors on solution architecture and procurement
        * Provide inputs and review of infrastructure budgeting
        * Create and maintain policies, procedures, and technical documentation
        * Monitor the operation of the applications and hardware resources
        * Generate periodic reports of system availability and capacity management to management and external teams
        * Create runbooks and documentation for level 1 engineers
        * Identify and work with other groups to coordinate application interdependencies and resolve issues
        * Evaluate, recommend, and implement strategies for growth and scalability
        * Provides leadership to identify, manage risks and resolve issues in a team environment
        * Work closely with application performance and sizing teams to improve products
        * Work independently with little supervision on day to day tasks, receiving general instructions on new assignments
        * Commitment to quality customer service
        * Support systems in a 24x7 data center
        * Participate in 24x7 on-call rotation
        * Other duties as assigned
        Qualifications
        * 10+ years in an enterprise IT environment with previous leadership or architecture experience
        * Experience with designing, deploying, administering, and monitoring several of the following systems:
        * Cisco UCS
        * Cisco networking
        * Load balancers
        * Linux/UNIX
        * Microsoft Active Directory
        * Microsoft Windows Server 2012
        * Microsoft Azure IaaS
        * PowerShell scripting
        * SAN
        * VMware
        * High-availability and clustering
        * Disaster recovery
        * Project management experience creating and maintaining project plans
        * Develop and follow security best practices in a Cloud environment
        * Experience working under the compliance of industry standards, such as SOX, SOC2, and ISO 27001
        * Experience with maintaining a Cloud data center infrastructure to meet SLAs
        * Excellent time management, organizational skills, crisis management and problem-solving skills
        * Excellent verbal and written communication skills
        Experience/knowledge in the following is a plus:
        * SQL/Oracle database administration
        * Applicable IT certifications
