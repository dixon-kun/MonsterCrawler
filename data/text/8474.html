We are looking for a Software Test Engineer for our current work in Richardson, TX.

This role offers a highly skilled and motivated Test Engineer an opportunity to join the Intelligence, Information, and Services (IIS) engineering team. The successful candidate will support the integration and test of custom application software onto the supporting infrastructure. The candidate must be have 2+ years of experience working on highly complex systems comprised of a diverse suite of COTS products and state of the art technologies. He/she will be responsible for studying technical documentation and system capability rollout plans to define integration and validation checklists to comprehensively test new capabilities as they are incrementally delivered to the test team in software releases every 8 to 10 weeks. After developing the checklists, he/she will then execute the checklists and assess whether the system is functioning properly; documenting any discrepancies encountered, helping establish relative priorities of outstanding discrepancies, and retesting fixes as they are provided. After each software release, testing will focus on ensuring the new capabilities are functioning as designed, that previously delivered capabilities remain fully functional, that the overall system is stable, and that operator interfaces are functioning properly and provide accurate information and sufficient feedback and controls to enable operations personnel to operate the system. He/she must be a quick learner that functions well in a dynamic, collaborative environment.

Required Skills:

* Experience in provisioning and implementing plans to migrate systems to common mission environments.

* Knowledge of at least two of the following systems engineering functions: requirements development and management, architecture, system design, interface definition and control, integration and test

* Knowledge of software development lifecycle models (waterfall and Agile) and program lifecycle modes.

* Proven written and oral communication skills

* Experience with Microsoft Office suite of tools, particularly Excel, Project, and PowerPoint.

* 4+ years engineering experience or an engineering support role

* Must be able to interface with design engineers, customers, and end users

* Must be willing to work overtime and weekends

* Active TS/SCI Clearance
