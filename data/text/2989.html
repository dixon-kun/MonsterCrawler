Description:

Leidos is currently seeking an Arabic Strategic Communication Analyst. The analyst will perform a full range of activities in processing materials or participating in situations requiring a general professional capability as follows:

* Develop and apply advanced methodological approaches to measures of effectiveness analysis of strategic communications for Intelligence Community customers.

* Provide research and analysis to support measures of effectiveness.

* Assist in the development of collection plans and evaluate effectiveness.

* Assist in developing and recommending measures of effectiveness objectives for mission support.

* Frequently interact with customers and/or functional peer group managers.

* Candidates must possess strong written and oral English, and possess professional proficiency in reading, listening and translating Arabic-language skills.

* Candidates must demonstrate strong communication skills, analytical ability and solid interpersonal skills.

* Candidates must be able to work under tight deadlines.

* Candidates must be able to perform job requirements independently and in team environments and must have ability to interface with all levels of management.

* Candidates must demonstrate a familiarity data sciences, marketing techniques, statistics, and experience with standard intelligence databases, systems and procedures.

* Candidate must have ability to conduct online media research, retrieval and integration of all-source data from various sources including classified sites, unclassified sites, and finished intelligence.

Qualifications:

* Candidates must possess strong written and oral English, and possess native or near native reading, listening and translating Arabic-language skills.
