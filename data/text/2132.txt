        As a Business Intelligence Analyst in the Sales Department at Micron Technology, Inc., you will work with cross functional teams to create analytic capabilities, providing decision makers with actionable business intelligence. You will partner closely with Sales and IT functional teams to understand business processes, learn the data, and craft solutions to solve problems. You will also define best practices for your area of focus which include determining baseline metrics and to-be-goals, as well as recommending process improvements.
        This position requires cross functional coordination, outstanding communication skills, creative problem solving and a highly organized, process-focused aptitude to manage a variety of responsibilities and deliverables. In this role, you have the opportunity to utilize your passion for technology and business process excellence.
        Responsibilities and Tasks:
        * Work with business leaders and users to define business intelligence needs and translate them to user stories and acceptance criteria
        * Lead the BI Solution lifecycle through requirement gathering, analysis, design, data-modeling, testing and deployment
        * Architect, design, and prototype solutions to support business strategies and deliver business value
        * Analyze impacts to existing system functionality and business processes
        * Develop reporting frameworks, deliverables, and in some cases help develop reporting solutions or dashboards
        * Guide development of BI solutions to align with the requirements of our business partners
        * Analyze and test data to confirm accuracy and value
        * Communicate with users to ensure BI deliverables are meeting or exceeding expectations
        * Help business users leverage the appropriate BI techniques and tools
        * Work with end users and IT to resolve operational issues and mitigate risks as applicable
        * Serve as a BI thought leader
        Qualifications and Experience:
        Required Qualifications and Experience:
        * 3+ years of relevant work experience in Business Intelligence
        * Excellent analytic and problem solving skills
        * Exceptional knowledge of data analysis, data architecture, data warehousing, dimensional modeling, SQL and reporting tools
        * Strong analytical, problem solving and organizational skills
        * Proficiency in reading and writing SQL queries
        * The ability to work in a dynamic, fast-paced, 24x7 work environment
        * The ability to manage multiple projects and tasks concurrently
        * Self-motivated with the ability to work with minimal supervision
        * Excellent interpersonal and communication skills
        * Teamwork and consensus building skills
        * Command skills to tackle tough challenges and face adversity head on
        Desired Qualifiations and Experience:
        * Experience in specific database platforms (SQL Server [preferred] and Oracle [secondary]) including stored procedures, functions, triggers, partitioning, etc.
        * 1+ year experience in any of the following Tableau, Microsoft Integration Services, Analysis Services or Reporting Services, Data Science
        * Experience using several of the following: Agile; Data Warehousing; Microsoft BI Suite; Tableau; SAP/BW/Business Objects; SQL; Data Modeling; Data flow mapping; Source to target mapping; Requirements gathering and data analysis; Testing and data validation techniques
        * Experience in SAP ECC, APO, BW and/or Hana
        * Working knowledge of Sales processes and key performance indicators
        * Experience in Sales
        Education:
        Bachelor's Degree in Information Systems or Information Technology, Computer Science, or related field of study
