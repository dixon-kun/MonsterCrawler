Qualifications

* 2+ years of industry experience in sales operations, deal desk or contracts management within a technology firm

* 7+ years of relevant professional work experience with reputed analytics/consulting firms

* Master's Degree in Business with high rankings and from reputed institution(s)

* Experience with drafting, redlining and negotiating contractual agreements

* Experience with diverse sales methodologies and best practices

* Strong working knowledge of commercial Customer Relationship Manager (CRM) and Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems

* Familiarity with SaaS pricing models and legal terms and conditions (both standard and non-standard)

* Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, written and verbal

* Skilled in leading and influencing at all levels of an organization

* Strong analytical and problem solving experience, combined with strong business judgment, process orientation, and an ability to present analysis in a clear and compelling manner (strong command of MS Excel; VBA, SQL are a plus)

* Ability to act autonomously, bringing structure and organization to work

* High level of ownership and independent thinking

* Ability to work under pressure and deliver on tight deadlines

* Ability to work well within teams across continents/time zones

* Ability to interact in a fluid, demanding and unstructured environment where priorities evolve constantly

* Ability to travel abroad for working sessions and meetings with the global Periscope team

Who You'll Work With

You'll work with our Periscope team in Jersey City, New Jersey.

Founded in 2007, Periscope™ by McKinsey enables better commercial decisions by uncovering actionable insights. The Periscope platform combines world leading intellectual property, prescriptive analytics, and cloud based tools to enable25 solutions which focus on insights and marketing,expert support and training. It is a unique combination that drives revenue growth both now and in the future. Customer experience, performance, pricing, category, and sales optimization are powered by the Periscope platform. Periscope has a presence in 26 locations across 16 countries with a team of 350+ IT and business professionals and a network of 300+ experts. To learn more about how Periscope's solutions and experts are helping businesses continually drive better performance, visit http://www.periscope-solutions.com/.

McKinsey New Ventures fosters innovation driven by analytics, design thinking, mobile and social by developing new products/services and integrating them into our client work. It is helping to shift our model toward asset-based consulting and is a foundation for –and expands our investment in –our entrepreneurial culture. Through innovative software as a service solutions, strategic acquisitions, and a vibrant ecosystem of alliances, we are redefining what it means to work with McKinsey.

As one of the fastest-growing parts of our firm, New Ventures has more than 1,500 dedicated professionals (including more than 800 analysts and data scientists) and we're hiring more mathematicians, data scientists, designers, software engineers, product managers, client development managers and general managers

What You'll Do

As a Deal Desk Operations Specialist, you will be part of the growing Client Development Excellence team which is at the core of bridging client business challenges with the capabilities of our solutions.

Client Development Excellence is a global team that aims to provide structured support to firm Partners and Solution leaders, to enable them to accelerate client development lifecycle across both standard and complex deals. You will serve as a trusted advisor to multiple internal stakeholders – Client Developers, Solution Management & Product Development leaders, along with McKinsey Client Service Teams and other relevant parties on pricing, product configuration, and business terms to drive agreement closure for multiple complex deals.

You'll design the process to build and operate a best in class deal management hub supporting globally spread client development needs. You'll align with Periscope leadership on key metrics and the reporting protocol to measure, monitor, and report select performance metrics. You'll act as an escalation point to Periscope leadership for quote-to-cash operations, and you'll ensure compliance with published sales and business approval policies.

Additionally, you'll build productive relationships and feedback loops with Legal, Finance, Sales and Product teams to drive operational and process efficiencies, interpret and negotiate contractual language, and manage approvals for all non-standard agreement (terms & conditions) language.
