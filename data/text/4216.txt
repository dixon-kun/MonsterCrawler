        Position Title - Senior Accountant
        Position Description/Responsibilities:
        * Month End Close &#8211; Preparation of journal entries, review of general ledger activity and preparation of account reconciliations
        * Review AP, corporate credit cards, and expense reports (Concur) for proper coding
        * Bank reconciliations &#8211; prepares monthly bank to book reconciliation for all cash accounts
        * Fixed assets &#8211; maintains fixed asset subledger; tracks and records in-transit fixed assets and capital in process; understands capitalization rules, runs fixed asset reports, records depreciation, and prepares fixed asset roll-forward. Responsible for periodic physical fixed asset count
        * Prepaid assets &#8211; maintains prepaid asset reconciliation; records prepaid assets and related amortization; ensures proper classification between LT and ST
        * Liabilities &#8211; responsible for ensuring accrued liabilities are recorded properly; works with outside vendors to track costs; and liability account reconciliation
        * Assists with consolidation and intercompany reconciliation process
        * Coordinate with subsidiaries in APAC and EMEA and assist with reviewing of international management reports
        * Assist in preparation of the month over flux analysis for management review
        * Own the publisher payout process. Run reports from internal databases to compile a monthly list of payments to our publishers, and coordinate with business owners for approvals and entering the payments into our payment processor (Tipalti).
        * Partner with Engineering Team and Account Managers to reconcile and resolve issues related to publisher payout process
        * Miscellaneous &#8211; assists with preparing and gathering information for external audits, assists with ad hoc projects
