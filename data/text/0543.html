Founded in 2000, Portware, LLC is the financial industry's leading developer of broker-neutral, automated trading solutions for global equities, futures, options and FX. With offices in New York, London, Hong Kong, and Hyderabad, Portware works in partnership with its clients to create highly integrated solutions to streamline workflow and increase operational efficiencies on trading desks worldwide. Portware's flagship product, Portware Enterprise, is a fully customizable trade management and execution system for single stock, portfolio, basket, automated and algorithmic trading. Role/Department Description:
Design, build and deploy mathematical models for the Portware Brain with applications in trading optimization.

Responsibilities:
· Combine data science and an understanding of underlying processes to build predictive models in areas ranging from market microstructure dynamics to portfolio risk factors.
· Work with Portware’s New York-based Alpha Vision machine learning and analytics teams in order to expand the predictive modeling services, and work with state-of-the art stack of data modeling paradigms.
· Get responsibility right from the first day and the unique chance to enrich the environment of key metrics Portware's trade automation services rely on.
· Write reusable research code and train and deploy predictive models
· Take part in all aspects of the model deployment life cycle, including specification, analysis, design, development, statistical testing and publications in white paper or peer-reviewed formats.
· Search, read, understand, and communicate relevant academic papers related to your projects.
· Participate in brainstorming sessions for new ideas

Job Requirements:
·  MSc/PhD in a quantitative in applied mathematics, computer science, engineering, physics, operations research, Econometrics, Stochastic finance, or another quantitative discipline. The candidate should be a creative scientist with experience in the financial markets.
·  Excellent analytical skills
·  Proven hands-on design and implementation of predictive modeling solutions
·  Experience in financial data in various asset classes
·  Sound knowledge and application of advanced statistical methods and time series analysis.
·  Proficiency with at least one of R, Python, Matlab
·  Excellent communication skills

Highly Desired:
·  Experience in risk, statistical arbitrage or asset pricing
·  NLP applications in news, blogs or social media
·  Proficiency with SQL and database technologies
·  Self- Starter, Ability to work under minimum supervision, Creativity, Multi-tasking
