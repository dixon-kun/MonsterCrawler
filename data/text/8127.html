This is a position within the Wunderman Data Management Product Development team. This individual works in an architect role, as part of a product development team designing software products and services that will operate as part of the Consumer Engagement Platform (CEP), which supports clients’ digital marketing activities. Software solutions include creating and supporting big data, real time services, data warehouses and data marts of client’s customers and prospects; creating extract, transform and load (ETL) data movement processes; and creating data extraction processes.


Working under general supervision or independently, this individual performs the following functions:

  • Interacts with all levels of team members and clients
  • Selects and integrates big data tools and frameworks
  • Designs ETL or workflow processes
  • Designs web services using REST or SOAP transactions
  • Conducts reviews of developed code
  • Mentors developers on established guidelines and best practices
  • Creates design and other system documentation in accordance with established processes and policies
  • Interacts frequently with Account Managers, Project Managers, and clients.
  • Diagnoses and corrects system problems

  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Management Information Systems, Mathematics, Engineering, or equivalent
  • Eight years relevant job experience
  • Interest in learning the concepts and practices, acquiring the skills, and developing the expertise needed to succeed in this position
  • Must possess good interpersonal skills
  • Must possess good communication skills in both verbal and written English
  • Must have demonstrated expertise in core competencies of data modeling, and SQL (preferably using Oracle PL/SQL or SYBASE IQ)
  • The ideal candidate will have some experience designing and implementing big data management solutions:
    • Experience with “big data” platforms and tools including data acquisition, storage and transformation (such as Hadoop, YARN, Storm, and Kafka)
    • Experience with highly available, scalable database technologies (such as IBM Big SQL, Pivotal HAWQ, or Cloudera Impala)
    • Experience with real-time data ingestion, transformation, and delivery in a Hadoop environment
  • Experience with iterative and/or hybrid software development lifecycle methodologies is desired
  • Experience working in a software product development setting is desired (especially delivering Software as a Service)
  • Experience with JSON and XML
  • Nice to have Java or other development language background
  • Nice to have dimensional data modeling to design data warehouses, data marts and metadata
  • Nice to have experience with Rally software

Wunderman is Creatively Driven. Data Inspired. A leading global digital agency, Wunderman combines creativity and data into work that inspires people to take action and delivers results for brands. In 2015, industry analysts named Wunderman a leader in marketing database operations as well as a strong performer in customer engagement strategy. Headquartered in New York, the agency brings together 7,000 creatives, data scientists, strategists and technologists in 175 offices in 60 markets. Wunderman is a WPP company (NASDAQ: WPPGY).
