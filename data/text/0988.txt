        About Us
        SmartAsset is a venture backed, Y Combinator Company with over 30 million users and growing fast. We use our patent pending financial modeling technology to provide accurate, interactive answers to important personal finance questions. We are a consumer advocate, recommending specific strategies so people can make smarter decisions and save money. We have been covered by over 50 publications, including the New York Times, Techcrunch, Fast Company and the US News and World Report.
        Job Description
        SmartAsset is looking for an experienced and energetic Associate Marketing Manager to drive profitable lead acquisition and revenue growth across a variety of financial products. The ideal candidate will have a deep understanding of direct response digital marketing, be fluent with DR KPIs and optimization strategies, and have superior quantitative and analytical skills. This person will manage ad campaigns across several traffic sources, including Outbrain, Taboola, Facebook and AdWords. This person will help define, execute and report on a testing and optimization roadmap for products within the retirement, mortgage and credit cards verticals.
        Responsibilities
        * Set-up and manage ad campaigns across a range of paid traffic sources, especially Native and PPC
        * Monitor and report on performance of ad copy, images and landing pages
        * Make ongoing recommendations for testing based on quantitative analysis
        * Manage ad spend to hit growth targets in revenue, CPL and ROI
        * Collaborate with Product and Editorial teams to drive improvements to landing pages and lead funnels
        * Prioritize opportunities based on quantitative modeling
        * Work with tech and data science teams to improve / automate reporting
        * Conduct competitive intelligence and market research on a regular basis
        * Liaise with account reps at traffic sources for ideas and optimization guidance
        Requirements
        * Bachelor's degree
        * 2-3 years' experience with paid digital marketing, especially PPC and Native ads
        * Advanced understanding of direct response concepts and the associated math
        * Expert at calculating and talking about CPA/CPL, eCPC, CPM, ROI, CTR, etc.
        * Fluent with attribution models and conversion tracking
        * Great Excel skills and a passion for data, able to create marketing forecast models
        * Must be a self-starter; able to work with minimal supervision to initiate and complete tasks
        * Eager to take ownership of a major revenue growth opportunity and dive into all aspects of campaign optimization
        * Must be able to manage multiple projects with conflicting priorities in a deadline-driven environment and work well under pressure
        * Retargeting experience a plus
        Perks
        * Work to solve problems that bring real positive impact to people's lives
        * Help shape the future of the consumer finance industry
        * Fun work environment with passionate colleagues
        * Meritocracy! We reward the awesome
        * Medical, Dental, 401K, and take-as-you-need vacation policy
