The Business Reporting & Analytics team offers an exciting opportunity to work with highly complex data sets representing the interactions, outcomes and profitability of over 8 million customers. The data discovery, data-derived insights and big data analysis performed by this team arm leaders with outcome and trending facts that drive business management, strategy and transformation. Reporting to the CFO of Empower Retirement, this team has a unique ability to leverage a broad perspective of sales, service, operational and financial data science to perform work.

In this role you will play a leadership role in developing and monitoring key performance measures as well as providing trending and forecasting analytics across multiple business areas. This includes developing new insights and understanding of business performance based on data and statistical methods. You will analyze external market dynamics and other data sources to assess trends and develop actionable insights and recommendations to management and internal partners, via understanding of the business model and the information available for analysis.


  • Design and deliver reports, dashboards and analytics for end-users in the business and shared services (finance, project, technology, etc)
  • Explore and extract data from primary and secondary sources using SQL/related language to be used in reports and statistical analysis
  • Gather questions and requirements from end users (managers, leaders) in the business to inform analyses; present resultant findings to applicable functions and organizational layers, as necessary
  • Develop and drive cross-functional partnerships with business analysts/SMEs, technology and shared services associates to perform work and increase organizational awareness, curiosity and leveragability of our data
  • Develop data visualizations and presentations using Tableau and/or PowerPoint

  • Degree in Systems or Mathematics (or Analytics, Decision Sciences, Statistics) or equivalent combination of education and experience
  • 4+ years of experience in data analytics and reporting role or Master’s degree in Data Science or like discipline.
  • Advanced knowledge of SQL for facilitating reporting across various data sources
  • Knowledge of SAS, Python, R
  • Working knowledge of using an integrated toolset to leverage big data
  • Working knowledge of BI tools (Tableau, Salesforce Wave, Qlik, Domo, SAP Business Objects or similar tools)
  • Proficiency with Microsoft Excel
  • Knowledge of Hadoop, SAP HANA or other Big Data environments
  • Ability to work with both structured and unstructured data types
  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Develop and drive strong partnership with the business (sales, service and operations) and shared services (technology, product development, marketing)
  • Ability to present data-driven insights to business management and leadership to influence decisions and strategy
  • Ability to balance short-term priorities with long-term objectives
  • Ability to leverage nebulous and disparate data sources to tell the story of the business
  • Entrepreneurial spirit, eager to learn, endlessly curious
  • Demonstrated ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment and are seeking a high-growth opportunity
  • Financial services experience preferred
  • HTML knowledge
