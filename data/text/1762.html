Azure Technical Evangelist

Cloud Computing is the next step toward ubiquitous and pervasive computing and Microsoft Azure is Microsoft's world-class cloud computing platform. It can be used to build, host, and scale a variety of cloud-based applications and workloads and is an open platform enabling hosting of Windows and Linux virtual machines while also supporting modern programming languages such as .NET, Java, Node.js, and PHP. Join the US Developer Evangelism & Experience (DX) technical evangelism team as an Azure Technical Evangelist and be front and center in the transformation to cloud-based architectures while at the same time playing an influential role in transforming Microsoft to a mobile first, cloud first company.

As an Azure Technical Evangelist (TE), your primary responsibility will be to ensure our ISV partners are successful with Azure and to help them move customer workloads to Azure. The TE is a technical partner-facing role accountable for the end-to-end cloud technical architecture and solution deployment experience. In this role, you will own technical partner engagement leading Envisioning Sessions, Architecture Design Sessions (ADS), implementation projects and Proofs of Concept (POC).

The ideal candidate will have experience in customer- and partner-facing roles and have demonstrated success leading technical and economic value discussions with senior executives to drive key decisions and implementation. Candidates need to possess the ability to impact and influence customers and partners with a high degree of autonomy, energy, flexibility and the drive to create real and measurable business results as evidenced by deploying qualified Cloud ISV partner solutions in Microsoft Azure.

The Azure Technical Evangelist is responsible for:

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Serving as a trusted advisor to top ISV partners

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Executing rapid technical feasibility studies for moving identified workloads to Azure

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Building technical and security architectures in Azure for identified workloads

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Selecting a migration approach to move workloads to Azure or architecting a greenfield development or production platform for new applications

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Executing Architecture Design Sessions (ADS) or Proofs of Concept (POC) Workshops

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Driving onboarding plan in conjunction with integration partners

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Documenting and sharing technical best practices and insights with Microsoft product groups and the USDX Technical Evangelist community

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Ensuring partner plan execution and Azure consumption targets are met

Qualifications include:

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Strong understanding of cloud computing technologies, business drivers, and emerging trends

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Depth and Breadth understanding of both big data and advanced analytics paradigms including: Big Data Processing & Storage, Machine Learning, Data Mining, Data Warehousing, Business Intelligence, Data Governance, Large Scale Storage, Enterprise Data & Information Management

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Working knowledge of the full spectrum of analytics from descriptive, diagnostic, predictive and prescriptive analytics in a production environment

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Ability to build technical and business credibility with senior executives and grow mind share with highly strategic customers and partners

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Track record of driving decisions collaboratively and resolving conflicts with consistent and reliable follow through

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Exceptional verbal and written communication

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Ability to connect technology with measurable business value

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Demonstrate technical thought leadership in customer-facing situations

Requirements:

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Bachelor of Science in Computer Science, Mathematics, or related degree preferred

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp 5+ year's design, implementation and/or support of highly distributed applications demonstrating strong architectural skills to ensure availability, reliability, etc.

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Development experience leveraging Agile with SCRUM and Application Lifecycle Management (ALM) with one or more of the following environments: R, Python, MATLAB, SAS, .NET, SQL, C#, C++, Java, Pig/Hive

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Big Data and Advanced Analytics experience including one or more of: Data Mining (I.e. Hadoop, Spark), NoSQL Databases (i.e. Redis), Enterprise Search (I.e. Lucene, ElasticSearch), Complex Event Processing (I.e. Storm, StreamInsight), in-memory analytics data structures (i.e. Columnar storage), Data Visualizations (i.e. Power BI, d3.js)

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Data science experience including experience with training/operationalizing machine learning projects with supervised & unsupervised learning techniques, knowledge of R/Python programming languages, and understanding & applying fundamental statistics and probability

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Passion for data, analytics & technology, especially for Microsoft Azure and modern application practices

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Presentation skills with a high degree of comfort with both small and large audiences

* &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Ability to adapt to a dynamic changing environment
