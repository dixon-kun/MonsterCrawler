Responsibilities: As a member of our Publisher Marketplace Data Science and Analytics Team, you will be based in our Boston office and will report directly to the Sr. Manager Data Science & Analytics who is based in London. You will be responsible for: Working with the Publisher marketplace team in Americas as main point of contact to drive business intelligence resulting in revenue growth and strengthened relationship with our direct Publishers.Partnering with the other PuMP data analysts (in Barcelona and Tokyo) to build an understanding of our key KPIs and processes and suggest operational improvements.Leveraging your technical skills to analyze complex data from multiple sources, detect problems with the performance of our partners and suggest revenue generating strategies.Streamlining the reporting suite by automating business metrics and reports that will enable you to present actionable insights to teams across the business.Working on ad hoc projects that will inform roadmap for the development of our Publisher products, and run real world tests on new developments.
