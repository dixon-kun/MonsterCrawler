About the Job




Data Analyst needed for a Temp to Perm opportunity with our client in the Newton, MA area. The ideal candidate would have a BS in Environmental Science, Engineering, Chemistry or Biology and at least 2 years of relevant data analyst/data coordinating experience in an Environmental field.

Responsibilities for the Scientist

  • Collect and configure large datasets to meet project specifications.
  • Provide reporting support by generating and formatting tables, charts, or graphs.
  • Interpret data, analyze results using statistical techniques and provide ongoing reports.
  • Develop and implement data collection systems and other strategies that optimize statistical efficiency and data quality.
  • Identify, analyze, and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets.

Requirements for the Scientist

  • BS degree in Environmental Science, Chemistry, Biology, Engineering, or Applied Computer Science.
  • 2 - 4 years of experience conducting data analysis in an environmental field.
  • Knowledge of statistics and experience using statistical packages for analyzing large datasets (R, SAS, Python, etc.)
  • Strong analytical skills with the ability to collect, organize, analyze, and disseminate significant amounts of information with attention to detail and accuracy.
