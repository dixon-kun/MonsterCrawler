As a member of the Fraud Management Solution Testing Team, you will perform software testing activities for the SAS Fraud Management software product. SAS Fraud Management is the most complete fraud detection system available in the market today and it enables financial institutions to protect their customers and reduce fraud losses by scoring 100% of all transactions in real-time at the point of sale.


In this role, you will work with limited requirements or design specifications to create unique test cases in accordance with criteria reflecting real customer environments. Your duties will include scoping, planning and executing testing work for new features and reporting issues in the defect system. While some of the work will be exploratory in nature, other critical duties will involve developing and documenting usage test cases, functional testing, REST api testing, validating output, test automation, Continuous Integration and end-to-end testing.

Qualifications

Essential Requirements of this role:

  • Bachelor's degree in computer science, mathematics, engineering, or related field.
  • At least 5 years of experience in software development testing on complex multi-tier software solutions.
  • Experience testing in a UNIX/Mid-range environment.
  • Experience testing in an enviornment with complex analytical models and experiecne working closely with Data Scientist and other analytical staff including development and consulting.

Additional Aptitude and Knowledge:

  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • Excellent interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to remain flexible in a rapidly changing environment.
  • Ability to work independently to prioritize and craft test cases.
  • Ability to work as part of a team.
  • Ability to work with global teams

Preferred Skills and Experience, the ideal Candidate will have some of the below:

  • Experience with standard testing tools for coverage analysis and automation.
  • Experience with Financial or Card Authorization systems.
  • Experience installing complex n-tier software, such as WebLogic or WebSphere.
  • Project leadership skills, which include scoping, documenting, and planning.
  • Experience testing in close development partnerships.
  • Experience with Selenium
  • Experience with REST APIs
  • Experience with SOAP UI
  • Familiarity with MQ Series.
  • Familiarity with Mainframe
  • Log reading and debugging.
  • Programming skills, for example, SQL (DB2 or Oracle), SAS (Macro).
  • Experience with the SAS Business Intelligence platform.
  • Experience scripting or coding in Shell, bat files, or Java.
  • Experience with test automation, both programmatic and non-UI.
