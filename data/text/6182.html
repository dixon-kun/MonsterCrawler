Position Overview:

The Climate Corporation is seeking bright, creative, scientific computing experts to join the Fertility Science team.  We leverage big data and cloud computing to tackle real-world problems in agriculture, including crop nutrient and risk management.  We work with weather data, environmental sensors, soil measurements, agricultural statistics, geological surveys, satellite imagery, and more.  We are looking for scientific engineers who are passionate about working with domain experts to design, build and evaluate models to support farmers’ decisions on nutrient management.

What You Will Do:

  • Design, build, and test new algorithms and models in a fast-paced, iterative development environment.
  • Work closely with agronomists, crop modelers, statisticians and data scientists to implement scientific algorithms in research and production environments.
  • Engage in model calibration and performance testing using multiple sources of ground truth data.
  • Develop and apply numerical solution to fluid flow problems and other systems of partial differential equations.
  • Work with engineers to implement models in a production environment.


Basic Qualifications:

  • Ph.D. or MS/MA + 2 years of relevant experience in an engineering field with emphasis in scientific computing, including applied mathematics, chemical engineering, civil/environmental engineering, agricultural engineering, or physics
  • Two years of experience writing code in a team environment (industry or academic)
  • Deep understanding and fluency of at least one compiled language (preferably Java) and one scripting language
  • Experience with data processing and statistical analysis tools using R, Matlab, Python, or similar

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Mastering best engineering practices and able to write well-structured, maintainable, performant, and well-documented code, with an emphasis on scientific code (correct naming of variables, fluency in units) Experience translating scientific models into code
  • Experience in agricultural ecosystem science (including hydrology, nutrient cycling, soil science, agronomy)
  • Experience working with large data sets

What We Offer:

Our teams are composed of industry experts, top scientists, and talented engineers. The environment is extremely engaging and fast-paced, with dozens of specialties coming together to provide the best possible products and experiences for our customers.

We provide competitive salaries and some of the best perks in the industry, including:

  • Superb medical, dental, vision, life, disability benefits, and a 401k matching program
  • A stocked kitchen with a large assortment of snacks & drinks to get you through the day
  • Encouragement to get out of the office and into the field with agents and farmers to see first-hand how our products are being used
  • We take part and offer various workshops, conferences, meet-up groups, tech-talks, and hackathons to encourage participation and growth in both community involvement and career development

We also hinge our cultural DNA on these five values:

  • Inspire one another
  • Innovate in all we do
  • Leave a mark on the world
  • Find the possible in the impossible
  • Be direct and transparent
