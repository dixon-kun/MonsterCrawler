Computer/Data Research Scientists (Las Vegas, NV)

Research computer science problems and design innovative uses for Zappos' software, such as our digital marketplace, databases, and reports. May perform additional duties that are similar and consistent with stated position requirements.

Qualified candidates must possess a Master’s degree in Computer Science or closely related field, including a master’s course in each of the following subjects:

  • High performance computing (HPC) and parallel processing
  • Theory and application of algorithms used for numerical approximation
  • C programming and systems level architecture

Must pass standard background check.
