About the Job

Sr. Director of Digital Operations & Infrastructure / OpenStack SME


Atlanta - ITP


Perm





eHire’s client, a global, digital industrial company, transforming industries and software-defined machines and solutions that are connected, responsive and predictive, has engaged eHire in a search for a Senior Director of Digital Operations.


The Impact


The selected individual will be an integral part of building out our client’s new innovation center, in support of creating a new ecosystem for digital industrial transformation.


The Challenge


Our global client is investing heavily in the IoT space.  The effort will be a key player its success, providing not only leadership, but also technical subject matter expertise. The Senior Director of Digital Operations will build, and lead a talented team of engineers in structuring, designing, and executing this solution.


The Opportunity


This is one of those unique opportunities to be part of the digital revolution.  Transformation is all around us, and with the global footprint of our client, coupled with the push to create software defined machines and solutions, you will be in a position to lead, learn, and executive on some of the most technically advanced products in the industry through responsive and predictive data solutions.


Background and Scope


Our client is building operations teams focused on performance and availability of Cloud infrastructure and tools consumed by all of their business segments.  The successful candidate will lead the operations team supporting these cloud services through utilization of OpenStack.  The ideal individual will have deep leadership skills, with a relentless passion for technology, and automation with customer experience driving all activities.

  • Provides leadership and hands-on technical direction to deliver problem, solution, tactical, and break-fix capability
  • Oversees and provides leadership direction during operational incidents, ensuring necessary resourcing and availability of subject matter expertise as well as proactive, business communications.
  • Delivery of solutions for compute and network monitoring and automation utilizing OpenStack technology; particular focus on improved customer experience
  • Define strategic direction for delivering contemporary monitoring and automation solutions in alignment with organizational priorities, collaborating closely with other technology organizations
  • Develop complex implementations, ensuring all standards, upgrades are adhered to and are managed within budget and aligned to technology platforms appropriately
  • Defines the operating structure, processes, procedures and talent profiles required to build a world class team
  • Develop automated solutions to address potential problems before they result in a service interruption
  • Builds a customer obsessed culture that focuses on customer success as its primary goal
  • Partners with the CTO organization to design for security, reliability and operational excellence across the technologies suite of tools and resources
  • Manages performance, coaches team leaders and members, and resources team appropriately to deliver on objectives while driving a culture of excellence
  • Anticipates obstacles and develops plans to resolve
  • Creates actionable strategies and operational plans
  • Investigates and develops new best practices and methodologies


Requirements
  • Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Information Management or other related technical domain or equivalent work experience
    • Advanced Degree preferred



  • 5 to 10+ years of experience developing monitoring and automation implementations in a large production environment, supporting bi-modal IT environments
  • 5 to 10+ years' experience transforming large programs with demonstrated leadership ability
  • Operational expertise in not only building cloud infrastructure solutions, but also maintaining that solution in a large enterprise environment.
  • OpenStack technology subject matter expertise is a MUST
  • Preferred Technical skills:
    • Experience using an automated configuration management system (Ansible, Chef, Puppet, etc.)
    • Strong experience with private and public cloud technologies (AWS, Azure, etc.)
    • Hands-on experience Developing Solutions with Cloud Orchestration Tools (Terraform, Chef, Puppet, Salt, etc.)
    • Demonstrated experience delivering monitoring and automation capabilities
    • Provide operations leadership of monitoring and automation solutions
    • Planning, evaluation, design, development and implementation of cloud based solutions
    • DevOps experience supporting applications in cloud environments

  • Must thrive in a highly collaborative environment
  • Partner with stakeholders in technologies, service management, operations to evaluate, strategize and improve operational processes / function, focusing on customer experience and operational excellence
  • Thought leadership - Must keep in step with new and emerging technologies to make technology, process, and application recommendations that improve productivity and enable efficiency
  • Leadership Attributes
    • Supports and drives change, and confronts difficult circumstances in creative ways
    • Ability to build / lead cross-functional teams, engage and influence individuals at multiple levels within and external to the organization, and influence direction with Senior IT Leaders
    • Able to lead current and evolving initiatives across large, matrixed organizations, leveraging global resources across verticals, business partners, and vendor resources as needed
    • Ability to set and drive strategy in a complex business environment where technical requirements are continuously evolving and where technical and business solutions are undefined
    • Ability to identify and capitalize on resources (technical, process, etc.) across the organization
    • Demonstrated competency in strategic thinking and leadership with strong relationship management ability
    • Excellent communication, negotiating and influencing skills at all levels of the organization as well as with external organizations.






About Our Client


Our client is bringing the Industrial Internet to life by connecting minds and machines. They combine decades of experience building innovative industrial machines with cutting-edge data science and analytics expertise.
