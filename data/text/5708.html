JOB DUTIES:
1. Provides leadership and direction to drive evolution of Predictive Analytics at JHACH.
2. Engages with key stakeholders and other business/technology leaders to frame the vision and long term objectives that will drive measurable advancement of machine learning and predictive analytic objectives.
3. Guides predictive analytics team in devising appropriate measurements and metrics, designing appropriate studies/experiments, building visualizations, and tackling complex, open-ended problems.
4. Provides insight into aspects of project prioritization and request tracking system to facilitate efficient processing of stakeholder requests through JHACH Health Informatics.
5. Engages with JHACH leadership to establish scalable infrastructure to support data and analytic solutions.
6. Drives recruitment of talented individuals and implements strategies to ensure retention.
7. Remains informed and educated on industry trends, research findings, and evaluation methods.
8. Contributes to a motivated work environment by encouraging collaboration to achieve JHACH strategic goals.
9. Resolves employee performance challenges and assigns tasks to team members matching project complexity with staff strengths and abilities.

SCHEDULED HOURS:
8:00   - 5:00


LOCATION:
Main Campus - OCC


MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS:
• Minimum 5 years of experience in leadership role relevant to data science and/or analytics.
• Experience with core analytic methodologies (e.g. regression, classification, clustering, matrix factorization, natural language processing, decision trees, support vector machines, neural networks) and knowledge of R, SQL or other interpreted developmental languages (e.g. Python).
• Experience with development of machine learning algorithms and predictive modeling.

EDUCATION:
• PhD in data science, analytics, informatics, statistics, mathematics, computer science, or related field.
