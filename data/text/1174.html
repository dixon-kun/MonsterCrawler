Job Description:

3M is seeking a Data Scientist for the corporate Financial Planning & Analysis organization located in Maplewood, MN.

Job Summary:

The person hired for the position of Data Scientist will have an excellent opportunity to shape the future of data analytics at 3M and in 3M Finance. The purpose of this position will be to enhance 3M Finance’s use of data, statistical analysis, and predictive analytics in financial planning while providing counsel to the corporate finance FP&A team and finance leadership.  This position will work closely with FP&A, Business Group finance teams, Treasury, Tax, Audit and Internal reporting teams to complete high impact projects.

Primary Responsibilities include but are not limited to the following:

  • Develops algorithms to improve effectiveness, accuracy, and productivity of critical business and financial processes through the use of using statistics, machine learning, and/or optimization
  • Develops analysis and opportunities for driving growth and performance improvements in 3M’s portfolio
  • Teaches core data analytics principles, practices, and skills to our organization as we embed this skill across finance
  • Implements new practices and methodologies using statistical analysis or data science techniques for financial planning and financial performance measurement

Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree or higher from an accredited institution
  • Minimum of four (4) years of combined experience developing statistical analyses, building predictive models, and/or utilizing data science techniques to provide business insights

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Master's degree or higher in a quantitative discipline such as Mathematics, Engineering, Computer Science, Finance, Statistics, and/or Economics
  • Proficient in a programming language such as R, Python, Java, and/or Scala
  • Experience working in big data environments such as Hadoop
  • Experience developing algorithms to translate data into meaningful business insights
  • Thinks broadly of intersections between business, financial, and economic data
  • Ability to be a change agent for driving new methods of analysis
  • Solid communication skills, ability to interact with all levels within organization
  • Self-starter willing to develop new ideas and thoughts in white space area
  • Strong financial acumen
  • SAP experience

Location: Maplewood, MN

Travel: May include up to 10%

Relocation Benefits: is authorized for this position

Must be legally authorized to work in country of employment without a sponsorship for employment visa status (e.e. H1B status).
