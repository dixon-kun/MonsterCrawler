POSITION SUMMARY

The Product Marketing Manager’s primary responsibility is to partner with sales and marketing leaders to ensure all internal and external communications properly position Inmar for the target audience and motivates and activates the target against the desired behavior.  The Product Marketing Manager will develop and execute marketing plans to achieve the financial and strategic objectives of the government channel. The government product suite is a combination of existing B2B products in three business divisions modified for the government audience as well as net new products developed specifically for the government needs.

The Product Marketing Manager is expected to be a self-starting, driven resource that is analytical and creative; must be a strategic thinker; can effectively lead and manage projects delivering flawless execution; can effectively communicate; and can identify, develop and execute marketing strategies and tactics that advance Inmar’s products and services in a B2B environment.

The Product Marketing Manager must be able to work independently while also working in a collaborative manner to engage and align key stakeholders (product, sales, IT, client service, and leadership) toward a partnership that drives growth.

PRIMARY JOB RESPONSIBILITIES include (with other duties as assigned):

  • Research and define the total addressable market, target audiences, buyer personas and competitive landscape
  • Develop and apply ideation discipline to enable product and sales teams to facilitate product development, product positioning and the commercial B2B buying process
  • Develop and execute the go-to-market product strategy, including both external and external audiences.
  • Support the development and implementation of the annual marketing plan for the government channel.
  • Generate thought leadership and lead generation strategy using various tactics such as email campaigns, webinars, search engine marketing, trade shows, advertising, social media, etc.
  • Identify, monitor and report on the key metrics for all marketing activities to maximize return on investment and resource allocation.
  • Work with Creative Design, Marketing Operations and Senior Content Manager to produce collateral and event support.
  • Support marketing leadership as needed on other initiatives.
  • Develop strong relationships with sales counterparts, product and marketing leadership.
  • Other duties as assigned.

Preferred Requirements/Skills:

  • Bachelor’s Degree required
  • 5+ years’ experience in business-to-business marketing, working with product development and sales teams and/or marketing experience in the government channel

Desired Talents:

  • Is positive and self-motivated to meet goals with an entrepreneurial spirit
  • Identify client business needs and match to existing services, including creation of business cases, value propositions and sales stories
  • Works well under pressure and at a fast pace, yet with acute attention to detail
  • Prioritizes and manages multiple tasks requiring extensive follow-up and timely completion of all projects
  • Is accountable, dependable performer and can hold others accountable as well in cross-functional settings
  • Effectively communicates in any setting and medium; has excellent presentation and strong writing skills and is a strong influencer
  • Confidently facilitates internal meetings by understanding objectives and deliverables
  • Is independent thinker and comfortable with risk-taking as necessary
  • Learns quickly, embraces new processes, retains and applies to future work
  • Is a proven team player that can work across boundaries and maintain strong working relationships
  • Approaches work with creativity and innovation
  • Works with composure, professionalism and ability to interact with external stakeholders
