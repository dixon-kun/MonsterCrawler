        As a Senior Product Designer for our product team, you will work closely with Product Managers and Developers to translate business goals into engaging products. Using Sketch, Adobe CC, Invision, and associated tools, you'll design journey maps, wireframe, comps and other illustrations of the user experience for our next generation data science platform. You'll work in our downtown Austin, TX office, collaborating with a set of awesome peers that cut across product, design, development, subject matter experts, and marketing among others. You'll also have the ability to take the great knowledge you have and innovate on a leading product that will be adopted by millions of users around the world.
        Main Responsibilities
        * Provide UX and Design leadership on projects
        * Drive work through to execution
        * Cooperate with cross-functional members of the team to ensure proper completion of work as it relates to the goals of the project or task at hand.
        * Utilize UX design skills in the execution of projects
        * Design journey maps, wireframe, comps and other illustrations of the user experience
        * Explore a problem or planned feature using tools like personas, journey maps, task analysis, stakeholder interviews, and contextual inquiry
        Requirements and Position Qualifications
        * Bachelor's or Master's Degree in Design, Human-Computer Interaction, Computer Science, Graphics Design or related disciplines
        * At least 5 years of professional experience
        * 2-4 years' experience managing UX teams of at least 3 people
        * Exceptional communication skills
        * Advanced understanding of UX ideation and design techniques
        * Ability to switch gears, juggle multiple projects and work within tight deadlines
        * Ability to be an effective team member
        * Collaborate with Product Managers to create and maintain a consistent user experience throughout the product
        * Adept with design tools such as Sketch, Adobe CC and Invision.
        * Basic understanding of web technologies (HTML5 and CSS3)
        * Possess expert understanding of user-centered design methods and best practices
        * Experienced: Good knowledge of design disciplines and principles (user-centered design, layout, information architecture, typography, color theory, ux patterns)
        * Must be based in the Austin, TX area or willing to relocate
        Desired Qualifications
        * Experience with user research
        * Experience designing data science, machine learning, BI, or analytics software
