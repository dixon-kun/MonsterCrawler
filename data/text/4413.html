Position is a home-based assignment with primary responsibility for the North East and Central Regions. Location must be within 50 miles of a Major US Domestic/International Airport and in the Boston, MA area. Domestic travel is predominantly driving within 300 mile radius and flying; travel is estimated to be up to 80% on a recurring basis. This position is expected to be 40 hours per week with some periodic overtime. Job responsibilities include: Monitoring assigned suppliers performance, analyze gaps to achieve100% quality, collaborate with programs and suppliers to develop / execute performance improvement plans, perform final verification of product (source inspection) at suppliers in accordance with applicable drawing and purchase order requirements, witness / verify product qualification, verify first article inspection in accordance with AS9102, conduct technical data package reviews, participate in supplier design reviews (system verification/validation, hardware, and software), participate in problem resolution during program design and execution phases. When requested, conduct process and product verification assessments, in-process assessments, pre/post award assessments and develop process improvement plans in collaboration with supplier.
