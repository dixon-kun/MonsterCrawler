Data Management Architect

Key Role:
Plan, conduct, and coordinate major scientific computer programming applications of considerable complexity that require thorough comprehension of all parameters that affect and interface with the application. Maintain responsibility for projects that are normally on the forefront of new technology and require the use and development of state-of-the-art methods. Provide technical advice and consultation on complex scientific applications and organize and direct the execution of broad programming projects. Provide analytical support in the conceptualization, development, and implementation of multiple, inter-linked programs. Recommend and develop scientific programming methods and documentation standards. Prepare cost analyses and justification for programming projects. Act as a technical project leader or provide work leadership to junior employees, as required.

Basic Qualifications:
-7 years of experience with providing technical support for data warehousing, data management, or data science projects
-Experience with developing analytical reporting and analysis using relational and dimensional modeling techniques
-Experience with one or more Extract, Transformation, and Load (ETL) tools
-Experience with two or more analytical and reporting tools
-Experience with developing reporting and analytical models
-Ability to obtain a security clearance
-BS degree in CS or Engineering

Additional Qualifications:
-Public Trust clearance

Clearance:
Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information.
