About the Job

We have a 9 month contract opportunity for a Software Technician in Hillsboro, OR



Job Description



New Business Initiative is looking for a talented and detailed oriented SW technician to join our expanding data science/SW team. Candidate must have a background in setting up wearable devices such as flashing FW, sensor configuration, collecting data for the machine learning applications in the past 2 years. He will play an important role in the enthusiastic and energetic ML/algorithm team and will contribute to develop its algorithms for embedded devices as well as cloud platform.



Responsibilities:



- Help define and own creation of data collection protocols and document, support, and train users of the protocol



- Collection and scrubbing of data collected on a wireless wearable device for delivery to engineering teams. Includes validation that data was collected correctly.



- Configure HW and SW used for data collection. Provide first level support to HW and SW used in data collection with internal engineering team and external test data suppliers



- Participate in the data forensic, debugging of tools, SW, validation of fixes



- Equipment management for lab apparatus (inventory, shipping, documentation, ordering, troubleshooting)



Skills/Requirements:



- General computer skills: install SW packages, check performance, and HW



- General programming skills: 2 years of scripting experience in high-level languages such as Python, debug scripts in Python, programs in C, Windows and UNIX



- General wireless networking skills: configure BLE, WiFi, etc.



- Solid PC client OS management skills (file manipulation)



- Demonstrated attention to detail and can take direction and follow-through on detailed task assignments



- Experience with data acquisition methodology, test engineering, and/or sensor data collection and processing



- Good written and verbal communication skills



- embedded system configuration and integration experience (equipment I/O, peripheral device connectivity)



- Experience with web/cloud tools and services and developer collaboration tools (GoogleDrive, Confluence)



- Knowledge of video playback and recording formats and PC based image capture devices a plus



HS diploma or GED equivalent is required
