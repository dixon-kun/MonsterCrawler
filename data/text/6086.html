The Director of Data Science and Analytics will be responsible for converting digital and web data into business value. As the analytics leader you will guide a team to help the business better understand sales performance and optimize marketing effectiveness.

Here's what you'll do:


* Lead internal analytics and data science team in the production of cyclical and ad hoc reporting analysis
* Forecast sales performance, product mix expectations, and promotional program impact
* Report findings and recommendations across business functions and senior management
* Work closely with program managers to measure digital media effectiveness through digital attribution data monitoring and CLV analysis
* Aid in the design of new promotions by providing predictive forecasting of performance
* Lead team in developing effective customer segments
* Represent findings and recommendations to key internal and external stakeholders
* Partner with IT to ensure appropriate data infrastructure and data integrity
* Manage 3rd party vendors

Here's what you'll bring to the table:


* BA/BS in Mathematics, Statistics, Finance, Marketing or Economics (MBA is a plus)
* 8-10 years' experience in business/marketing analytics function
* Strong hands-on quantitative modeling skills and competency in SQL, SAS, BI segmentation, predictive and descriptive modeling
* Understanding of data management and warehousing, OBIEE/OBIFS preferred
* Data visualization expertise
* Experience working with web analytics tools (Omniture is a plus)
* Experience working with tag management tools
* Expertise and knowledge in testing methodologies such as A/B, multivariate, etc
* Must possess strong organizational skills and pay close attention to detail
