POSITION SUMMARY

MassMutual's Data Science team is looking for a Head of Data Engineering. In this role reporting to the VP of Data Science, the incumbent will act as a strategist, innovator and leader. You will define the strategic goals and assume responsibility for developing a cutting-edge enterprise data environment that enables and scales what has been widely recognized as the leading data science and analytics capability in the life insurance industry. More specifically, you will manage a growing group of 25 and define the strategic goals of 5 small teams owning the company's data continuum: Data ingestion, Data platforms (Big Data, SQL and Streaming), Data Integration, Data Management and governance, and DevOps.

Roles and Responsibilities

* Create and implement a comprehensive data engineering strategy to enhance the practice across the enterprise.

* Provide strategic support to internal constituencies such as data science, digital marketing, mobile and digital development.

* Lead the design, build, and operations of data ingestion and integration pipelines. Examples include database extracts, application server logs, scanned images, voice recordings, Twitter streams, websites, and health sensor data.

* Provide strategy and guidance for the design, building and measurement of complex ETL jobs to process disparate, dirty data sources and form a high integrity, high quality, clean data asset.

* Establish framework to support and scale big data infrastructure (Cloudera CDH 5, MongoDB, and HP Vertica)

* Monitor and track data quality and flow dynamics

* Guide the design and build of web based APIs to facilitate easy access to data

* Coordinate data governance and management work through the establishment of a working group composed of multiple stakeholder constituencies.

* Define and execute goals

* Promote a high performing, collaborative, and agile culture

* Lead the growth of the function via active recruitment and employee development.

Skills, Experience, Education

* Degree in Computer Science or Engineering (M.S. or Ph.D. preferred)

* 5-7+ years experience growing and leading a data engineering function in a fast paced environment.

* A proven strategist with a track record of transformational execution

* An inspiring leader and talent developer

* A passion for open source software

* Prior experience developing and administering large data systems

* Prior experience coding and scripting (Python/Java/Scala/Go/Bash/Git)

* Prior experience in data modeling and administration of NoSQL and SQL databases.

* Prior hands-on experience with at least one these: Hadoop, MapReduce, HDFS, HBase, Hive, Kafka, Spark, Vertica, SQL

* Prior experience with cloud infrastructure and devops workflows

* Outstanding written and verbal communication skills

* Comfortable working in ambiguity, capable of establishing and growing functions and systems from the ground up

* An action-oriented, driven, entrepreneurial mindset with prior start-up experience.
