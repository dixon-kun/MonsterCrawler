        Title: Data Scientist
        Location: Skokie, IL
        Job Summary: The Data Scientist is responsible for the design and development of a core set of analytic data models and tools to support our industry leading healthcare analytic platform. This position also supports and maintains existing analytic data models and tools within Sg2.
        Responsibilities:
        * Data exploration (supervised and unsupervised), hypothesis creation (from business and product goals), testing algorithms, scaling to large data-sets and validating results
        * Collect, cleanse, standardize and analyze healthcare utilization from a variety of third party sources
        * Create baseline and benchmarked data to support new and existing products
        * Research new product development initiatives
        * Support internal ad hoc research
        * Maintain a current knowledge of emerging tools and techniques in machine learning, statistical modelling and analytics
        * Continually seek and drive opportunities to increase product quality and customer satisfaction
        Qualifications: We are seeking highly motivated individuals with a consistent record of professional and personal achievement. Creativity, initiative, resourcefulness and an entrepreneurial spirit are a must, in addition to the following:
        * BA/BS in Mathematics, statistics, computer Science or related concentration or equivalent work experience
        * 5+ years' experience in data development, preferably in an Agile development environment
        * Experience with large health care databases &#8211; both clinical and financial. (example: Commercial claims data, Hospital State Data, SAF, and other CMS databases)
        * Experience with clinical coding systems: ICD-9/10, CPT-4, HCPCS, etc.
        * Familiarity with clinical grouping and severity adjustment tools: DRGs, APR-DRGs, etc.
        * Strong statistical analysis and data modeling skills
        * Experience with technical documentation of code and processes
        * Experience with programming languages and data analysis tools (SQL,SAS)
        * Experience with ETL (Extract, Transform and Load) tools
        * Strong analytical and problem solving skills
        * Strong communication skills &#8211; ability to discuss technical issues with a variety of audiences
