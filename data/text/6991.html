Unlock Your Career Potential: Technology at ADP. Do you enjoy exploring, identifying and inspiring the future of the workplace and the lives of millions of people? At ADP, the world's largest B2B cloud company, our Technology team is comprised of brilliant engineers, architects, data scientists, infrastructure experts, and more. We were first in our industry to offer a SaaS solution and continue to push the envelope utilizing the latest operating platforms to deliver the highly automated, intelligent and predictive solutions that are redefining what is possible. Named one of Forbes' "Most Innovative Companies" and one of Computerworld's "100 Best Places to Work in IT", we are committed to leading the way in product development and research, empowering you to bring to life the latest innovations that will forever change the way businesses manage their most vital asset, employees. 

The Insight Advisor is responsible to provide consulting advice and support to clients, prospects, ADP Associates on the optimal usage of the ADP DataCloud. This billable role enables the implementation, adoption, usage and proper interpretation of the ADP DataCloud features. The feedback provided by clients will be brought back to the ADP Insight in Action, ADP DataCloud product management and development teams for synthesis into future product enhancements and future advice to other clients. This role will also be seen as a thought leader providing high quality and impactful presentations during sales approaches, client meetings, and at internal and external conferences representing ADP, ADP Insight In Action and the ADP DataCloud. 

We strive for every interaction to be driven by our CORE values: Insightful Expertise, Integrity is Everything, Service Excellence, Inspiring Innovation, Each Person Counts, Results-Driven, & Social Responsibility.

ESSENTIAL RESPONSIBILITIES 

 
*  Develops and delivers consulting process and materials that help clients in the adoption of the DataCloud features. 
*  Works in pre-sales situations with Sales teams to help amplify the Big Data messages and differentiated nature of the ADP DataCloud and ADP Insight in Action. 
*  Manages a relationship with clients after commencement of relationship to provide advice on adoption, process and change management of the ADP DataCloud. 
*  Trains clients on how to use Reporting, Analytics, Benchmarks and other ADP DataCloud features as part of the ADP Insight in Action Boot Camps or in billable situations. 
*  Produces the materials and content for/and leads the ADP Insight in Action Boot Camps.  
*  Provides thought leadership in ADP DataCloud product features and other Big Data topics to internal and external audiences. 
*  Educates ADP Associates on the value, usage and purpose of the ADP DataCloud and underlying Big Data concepts. 
*  Gathers big data practices for use in further client consulting situations and maintains a database of potential client problem solutions with outcomes (Lumin). 
*  Gathers product feedback at conferences, client meetings, analyst discussions, service and implementation discussions and distills that feedback into stories for future product development or enhancements. 
*  Develops product materials in conjunction with Product Marketing for use in describing the ADP DataCloud and ADP Insight in Action. 
*   Provides assistance to the product owners when required to clarify requirements based on market need and best practices.  

EDUCATION: 

 
*  Bachelor's Degree required or equivalent in education and experience. 
*  MBA and/or advanced Technical Degree preferred.  

EXPERIENCE: 

 
*  10+ years of experience in a market-facing HCM product role. 
*  Consulting or HR Technology Practitioner background is required. 
*  Demonstrated acumen in Big Data, Analytics, Reporting and/or Benchmarking is required. 
*  Experience working with Agile methodology strongly preferred. 
*  Significant knowledge of the HCM market preferred. 
*  HCM implementation experience is preferred.  

  

CRITICAL SKILLS: 

 
*  Excellent oral and written communication and presentation skills required, including representing ADP externally to our clients and the market. 
*  Product demonstration skills are key. 
*  Strong executive presence and ability to influence senior leaders without having a formal reporting relationship. 
*  Ability to conceptualize, negotiate and sell ideas internally and externally. 
*  Ability to implement concepts, ideas and plans while functioning in a flexible, constantly changing environment necessary. 
*  Must be a strong collaborator, team player as well as an individual contributor. 
*  Ability to travel regularly
