Lead Data Engineer Needed for Disruptive Tech Firm

Averity - New York City, NY 10001

Posted: 3/24/2017

Want to work in a fun, work hard/play hard environment for one of the most powerful tech companies to disrupt the space? We're looking for a Lead Data Engineer to forge our data endeavors forward.

What Is the Job?

• As a Data Engineer reporting directly to our Chief Data Officer, you will be tasked with creating our large scale databases, integrating and querying our transactional big data flows to be analyzed by our Data Science and Data Intelligence teams. You'll play an integral role in the architecture and strategy as we continue to grow.

• The team acts like a startup in that we are nimble, collaborative, and encourage all members to express their opinions.

• You'll play an important role as an original contributer as well as project manager, making sure data engineering projects are completed

• In charge of ETL processes, data quality, pipelining and data base architecture

Who Are We?

We are a data-focused tech firm that uses our analytic ability to provide our clients with an automated platform to optimize pricing and marketing for ticket resale.

Compensation

• $120,000 - $150,000

• Full Benefits (medical, dental and vision)

• Retirement Plan

• Generous Wellness Programs

What Skills Do You Need?

• Strong coding with object orientated background with C++, Python, Java

• Scripting in UNIX

• Experience with Database building, ETL process and data quality

• Knowledge of Scala, Hive, Spark and HBASE is a plus

• Experience with transactional data is a plus

What's In It For You?

This is a great opportunity for a Data Engineer who wants to move up as a Lead and looking to work for a strong, disruptive company where they will be given ample responsibility as they learn and grow.
