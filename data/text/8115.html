External Position Title

Media Strategy & Insights Manager

Job Description

  • Want to take your digital marketing experience to the next level and lead the most advanced Media Strategy team in CPG?  The Media Strategy & Analytics Manager will play a critical role in shaping the future of digital for PepsiCo.
  • In this role, you will be the thought leader for PepsiCo on everything ranging from media investment strategies, media planning, media analytics, and optimization.  This is a rare opportunity to set the agenda and drive impact on over $300MM of media investments annually.
  • You will lead day to day oversight of Media Agency and in-house activation plans across all brand media.  This role will also oversee prioritization, delivery, and implementation of the learning agenda.
  • Accountabilities Include:
    • Drive integrated Media Strategy for FLNA focused on increased efficiency and effectiveness across all consumer touchpoints.
    • Manage brand media investment forecast, analytics, and reporting.
    • Manage day-to-day relationship with agency and analytics partners.
    • Consults and Liaison for Brand Teams Media Planning, Activation, and Insights.
    • Work across teams and agencies to define, track, and report on key media KPIs and benchmarking.
    • Own learning agenda development, execution, communication, and updates for all media.
    • Partner with Business Analytics and Data Sciences teams from across the organization to gather insights and implement strategies to drive portfolio volume growth.
    • Lead frequent share-outs to Brand Teams and Broader Marketing Team on digital learnings, best practices, and innovation.
    • Partner with In-house media team on planning, briefing, media analytics, and data strategies.
    • Effectively manage media insights budget with support from Media Strategy Sr. Analyst.

Qualifications/Requirements

  • Undergraduate degree in Marketing, Communications, or Emerging Media.  Focus on digital and analytics preferred.  MBA / Graduate Degree preferred.
  • Candidate must be passionate about digital media and data driven marketing.
  • Minimum of 5 years experience in marketing and advertising with significant exposure to digital media and analytics.
  • Candidate must be comfortable leading in a highly dynamic, data driven environment.
  • Excellent communication, project management, and analytical skills.
  • Must be able to communicate complex concepts to non-technical audiences.
