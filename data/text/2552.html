McIntire Solutions seeks a Data Analyst to support Human Capital workforce and resource analysis within the Office of the Director of National Intelligence or Intelligence Agency to join our team in Northern Virginia.


  • Design qualitative and quantitative methodologies and repeatable processes to capture, synthesize, and analyze IC wide workforce data
  • Conduct data acquisition, programmatic collection and data organization in preparation for analysis, working with representatives across IC agencies
  • Design data collection strategies including database selection, dataset scoping, and parameterization
  • Conduct objective and data-driven analyses to inform major decisions regarding future investment or allocation, priorities, or policy guidance
  • Assess effectiveness of process, programs and major decisions to gather insights and provide corrective actions and lessons learned
  • Review, identify, and evaluate trends and patterns as it relates to human capital data
  • Work with IC tools databases, develop database management strategies, and identify and evaluate analytic software, methods and processes
  • Develop analytical tools to process large and/or unstructured datasets

  • Bachelor’s degree from an accredited college or university, preferably in Operations Research, Mathematics, Economics, Data Science, Cost Analysis; Master’s degree preferred
  • Minimum of five (5) years of experience in data analytics or operations research and analysis and have a minimum of three (3) years of experience within the Intelligence Community
  • Advanced proficiency using Microsoft Excel and pivot tables, PowerPoint, and Word
  • Experience in data collection, normalization, and summarizing results in graphical or tabular form
  • Experience working in a dynamic, fast-paced environment with short and rigid deadlines
  • Excellent writing and editing skills, including clear organization and concise presentation
  • Outstanding interpersonal and customer service skills, including the ability to work effectively independently and in a team environment
  • Be adaptable and flexible to perform a range of tasks and work with many different offices and organizations across the ODNI and the IC
  • Active TS/SCI clearance with polygraph
