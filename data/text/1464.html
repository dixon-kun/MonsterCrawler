Cars.com is seeking talented interns to join the Data team. We are building the next generation of data platforms and analytic capabilities. The Data Analyst intern will work with data engineering teams and assist in the day to day development activities.

RESPONSIBILITIES INCLUDE:

  • Understand and elicit data needs using interviews, data analysis, business process descriptions, stories, scenarios, business analysis, and workflow analysis.
  • Create and execute SQL queries to aid in the troubleshooting process of potential data anomalies. This role will aid in the testing and validation of the delivered data products.
  • Collaborate with technical experts and business units to determine the best possible data and reporting solutions.
  • Assist with regular agile process activities, including daily standups, sprint and release planning, sprint open, close and retrospective; lead those activities when necessary


Required Skills

REQUIRED SKILLS

  • Solid understanding of database structures and fundamentals
  • Excellent interpersonal skills, ability to work with diverse personality types
  • Must have solid verbal and written communication, organization, and time management skills
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills with a high attention to detail
  • Working experience in Agile/Scrum environment is preferred
  • Passion to proactively seek ways to improve processes
  • Self-confidence to be assertive when taking a position and can constructively resolve problems
  • Creative, resourceful and innovative at tackling complex challenges.
  • The ability to interact professionally with a diverse group, including executives, managers, and subject matter experts


Required Experience

REQUIRED EXPERIENCE

  • Advanced SQL development skills
  • Experience working with a Data Warehouse
  • Experience working with a Big Data (Hadoop, Hive, Hue, NoSQL) environment.
  • Previous development experience considered a plus.
  • Bachelor’s or Master’s degree candidates: Computer Science, Data Science, Engineering
