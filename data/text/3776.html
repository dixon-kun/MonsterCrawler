Description

Role: Director of Interoperability (Data Science)
Assignment: Transcend Insights
Location: San Diego or Campbell, CA
Our Search:  Director of Interoperability
We are looking for a Director of Interoperability with strong analytical and technical capabilities who will be responsible for establishing and developing Transcend Insights data engineering & analytics team, a critical function for product success.  The selected leader will develop strategies to analyze and transform healthcare data according to standards for our population health platform. This individual will also represent our company when interfacing with customers and will be responsible for growing established customer relationships.
Key Role Objectives/Responsibilities
The Director of Interoperability will become an integral part of the company, and own, direct and manage the receipt, collection, and transformation of data for our population health platform.  In addition, the incumbent will:
  • Develop strategies to analyze and transform healthcare data according to standards and evaluate baseline performance and utilization measures
  • Ensure data validity and mapping so that data run through the Platform is accurate for the patient population at any point time
  • Conduct interviews with customers to determine information and data needs and identify gaps and make recommendations for addressing gaps
  • Create a talent acquisition plan to meet the needs of the business
  • Evolve the capabilities of the team as required to support the business

Qualifications

Our Ideal Candidate

A passion/commitment to improving data quality and outcomes as well as building scalable solutions is fundamental to this role.  A firm understanding of the changing health market, particularly the wellness segment, is imperative for both enterprise and personal success.

Requirements

·      Minimum 6 years’ experience in healthcare information systems

·      Minimum 3  years’ experience building and leading high performing teams

·      Deep knowledge of healthcare message standards, such as HL7 v2, v3, CCDA, FHIR, and EDI 837/5

·      Strong business acumen, and superior written, verbal and presentation skills

·      Passionate about data quality as well as building scalable solutions through rapid prototyping and iteration

·      Bachelor’s degree (preferably in a quantitative field such as math, statistics, engineering, economics, computer science, operations research)

Role Desirables

·      Master’s degree highly desired, but not required

What is Population Wellness? At Transcend Insights, we define population wellness as the health outcomes—including prevention of negative outcomes—of a community of individuals. Population wellness emphasizes maintaining, restoring or supporting the best health and promoting the best life of a given population. Population health and wellness requires an active relationship among individuals, their physicians and care teams, and their communities. A proactive—not merely reactive—mindset and approach are required.

Our parent, Humana

Humana, a 50+ year old health and wellness company, has a long history and reputation of successful reinvention, transforming itself from the largest U.S. nursing home company in the 70’s into the largest U.S. hospital corporation in the 80’s and into a highly respected health benefits company in the mid 90’s.  After 2000, the organization became a recognized leader in consumer-focused health solutions , and in recent history, we’ve emerged as a Fortune 73 company with the most trusted brand in our space.
