Job Description:

3M is seeking a Research Scientist – Data Science for the Corporate Research Analytical Laboratory (CRAL) located in Maplewood, MN.

Job Summary :

The person hired for this Research Scientist position will join a team of research chemists, physicists and engineers in the Corporate Research Lab of 3M and apply their Computer Vision/Machine Learning/Data Analytics expertise and experience to invent, develop, and create new business opportunities and support existing 3M products and businesses.  This position offers a chance to work in exciting technologies at the heart of tomorrow's most creative products.

Primary Responsibilities include but are not limited to the following:

  • Lead projects in machine learning, data mining, natural language processing, signal processing, sensors and electronics, probabilistic inference, and/or computational statistics
  • Develop and implement relevant and valuable computational algorithms that support 3M businesses
  • Collaborate with a team of chemists, physicists and engineers on both theoretical and practical projects that will use his/her scientific, mathematical and computational skills to drive new-to-the-world technology development and introduction

Basic Qualifications:

  • Master’s degree or higher in a science, engineering or mathematics discipline from an accredited university
  • Academic or working combined experience in any of the following: computer vision, machine learning, and/or data science technologies

Preferred Qualifications:

  • MS/PhD in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Applied Mathematics, or Mathematics from an accredited university augmented with an undergraduate degree or minor in chemistry, physics, mechanical or chemical engineering.
  • Minimum of one (1) year of computer vision and image processing and/or data analytics experience in academia, industry or national lab environment
  • Expertise in machine learning, data mining, natural language processing, signal processing, sensors and electronics, probabilistic inference, and/or computational statistics, 3D computer vision (acquisition, segmentation, registration, classification), deep learning, analysis and reduction of high-dimensional data from
  • Programming languages: Python, MATLAB, C, C++, OpenCV, R
  • Strong scientific and technical acumen, with demonstrated interest and ability to make connections between science/technology and real-world concerns
  • Demonstrated scientific excellence as evidenced by publications and presentations
  • Leadership and networking skills
  • Self-motivated and self-directed
  • Excellent written and oral communication skills

Location: Maplewood, MN

Travel: May include up to 2% domestic/international

Relocation Benefits: Are authorized for this position
