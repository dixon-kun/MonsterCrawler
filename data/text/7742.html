Am Law Top 20 with a deep commitment to advanced legal analytics in discovery, data sciences, and creative problem solving seeks a seasoned law firm experienced project manager for their DC office. There is a local team of five and a large national e-discovery team. The PM is responsible for planning, managing, executing and finalizing the day-to-day e-discovery activities on a matter. Candidate will also be responsible for coordinating with the firm’s attorneys on matter status, deadline management, e-discovery budget, logistics and documentation of efforts for defensibility or process. Bachelor’s degree, law firm, and large-scale litigation experience required. RCA and Relativity analytics certification highly desired. Send resumes to tammy@trustaffingpartners.com.The e-Discovery Project Manager plans, manages coordinates, executes and finalizes the day to day e-discovery activities on a matter. In this capacity the Project Manager for e-Discovery: Plans, coordinates and manages all of the day to day e-discovery project activities on a matter including vendor management, communication and monitoring. Coordinates with the firm’s attorneys on matter status, deadline management, e-discovery budget, logistics and documentation of efforts for defensibility or process; May act as a subject matter expert for e-discovery, and provides guidance and general oversight for internal clients during the e-discovery life-cycle; Gathers, documents and coordinates project specification from internal and external clients and works with internal operational teams to ensure requirements and timelines are understood and met; Identifies and manages issues and risks;  Performs quality control and assurance of project data prior to client delivery;  Work overtime as required, in particular at month end and year end; and Handle additional related projects as assigned. Qualifications: We are seeking candidates who have: Bachelor’s degree with major in computer science, electrical engineering or related field (required); Excellent communication skills both oral and written with strong analytical and presentation skills specifically working with attorneys in litigation; Ability to communicate directly with clients, both internal and external; Ability to work effectively with both the legal and technical teams; Advanced proficiency in organization, coordinating tasks on a project(s) simultaneously; Advanced knowledge of project management best practices; Advanced knowledge of the concepts, principles, management and processing of electronically stored information as they relate to legal matters; Knowledge of litigation domain.
