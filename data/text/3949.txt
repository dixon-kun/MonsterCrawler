        Senior Solution Specialist - SAP Innovation Center Network
        PURPOSE AND OBJECTIVES
        The SAP Innovation Center Network is a strategic development entity within SAP, combining software engineering excellence with thought leadership and entrepreneurial spirit to create new markets for SAP. Across various focus clusters and by building on strong teams, we pioneer game-changing solutions in close collaboration with startups, customers &amp; partners in industry and research as well as with other SAP units. To ensure a successful go-to-market, we follow an end-to-end responsibility approach. By exploring unconventional ideas and developing inspiring proofs of concepts, we push the state-of-the-art in technology and its applied fields to provide SAP with foresight into future trends.
        As a Solution Specialist, you will work in a team of experience experienced developer and data scientists taking on challenges posed by the SAP customers and product units. You will have the chance to work with SAP customers and product units directly to understand domain specific challenges and propose solution leveraging big data, machine learning techniques in an innovative way. Your primary goal will be accompanying SAP customers in discovering the right technology and right solutions specific for their needs, translate them into development component for engineering team, using both technical and business skills to drive the project forward with best efficiency.
        EXPECTATIONS AND TASKS
        * Take responsibility for leading the technical project, including such activities as technology advocacy, discovery/design thinking workshop, supporting bid responses, product and solution briefings, proof-of-concept work, and the coordination of supporting technical resources.
        * Work hands-on with SAP Clea Platform to demonstrate and prototype integrations in customer/partner environments.
        * Onsite Customer location frequently for business meetings, technical reviews and onsite delivery activities.
        * Prepare and deliver product messaging to highlight SAP Clea Platform value proposition using techniques to include whiteboard and slide presentations, product/solution demonstrations, white papers, trial management and RFI response documents.
        * Make recommendations on enterprise architectures, platforms and application infrastructure required to successfully adopt solution providing best practice advice to customers to optimize SAP Clea Platform effectiveness.
        EDUCATION AND QUALIFICATIONS
        * BA/BS degree in Computer Science or a related software engineering field, or equivalent practical experience.
        * 5 years' experience in all aspects of machine learning, big data, cloud computing (infrastructure, storage, platforms and data).
        * 5 year's customer facing experience with both technical and business exposure.
        * Ability to speak and write in English fluently and idiomatically.
        * Excellent communication, relationship skills and a strong team player
        * Able to work onsite in Palo Alto, CA and travel to customer site frequently if needed.
        PREFFERED QUALIFICATIONS
        * Master's degree in Computer Science or a related technical field.
        * Extensive experience serving as technical sales engineer in a cloud computing environment or equivalent experience in a customer facing role (including working as a member of a professional services or systems engineering team).
        * Experience writing machine learning code, handling big data, perform statistics analysis of real problem.
        * Ability to explain and demystify relevant techniques to those who are new to machine learning. Ability to advise customers on making real world tradeoffs regarding speed vs accuracy vs cost.
        * Ability to quickly learn, understand, and work with new emerging technologies, methodologies, and solutions in the Machine Learning technology space.
