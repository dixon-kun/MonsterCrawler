Your role: The Manager Commercial Development, Chemoinformatics Technology will build market position by locating, developing, defining, negotiating, and closing business relationships. He/She will:

* Build and lead a commercial team to drive customer adoption for our Cheminformatics products and Services

* Lead a team consisting of highly technical sales specialists, Service specialists and support specialists

* Network with Key Opinion Leaders in the Industry to gain insights into current trends and build trust around our technology

* Lead the technical sales process and provide customer demos and training in collaboration with the R&D team

* Close new business deals by coordinating requirements; developing and negotiating contracts; integrating contract requirements with business operations.

* Ensure training for internal stake holders in general sales, technical support and sales support.

* Update job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities; reading professional publications; maintaining personal networks; participating in professional organizations.

Who you are:

* Strong interpersonal and leadership skills

* Strong Technical Sales, Negotiating skills

* Graduate degree in Chemistry (or equivalent) that provides knowledge of theories, principles and concepts within a discipline. Ph.D. and MBA a plus. Further education/experience in Data Science, Cheminformatics a plus

* Depth and/ or breadth of expertise in Organic Synthesis and/or Data Science combined with knowledge of the Chemistry market

* Self-motivated and energetic

* Results-oriented, with a proven track record of success

* Ability to travel domestically and/or internationally. Travel may be approximately 40-50%

* Experience:

* Three to five years of experience in life sciences or chemistry commercial development or equivalent, involving sophisticated negotiations.

* Successful track record in driving positive commercial outcomes in Life Sciences / Chemistry

* Leadership Experience
