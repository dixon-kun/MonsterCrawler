        Position Overview
        The Director of the Technology Business Management (TBM) Office within Freddie Mac's Information Technology Chief Financial Officer's (IT CFO) organization is seeking a Reporting Senior. In this newly created role, the chosen candidate will be responsible for defining, developing and deploying new reports to support dissemination of granular, actionable IT cost analytics data to Finance, IT and the IT service consuming Lines of Business (LOBs). Activities led by the Reporting Senior will often be time sensitive, highly visible within the Freddie Mac organization and will require collaboration within the Finance organization and with IT and LOB partners.
        Responsibilities include:
        * Define enterprise reporting portfolio for the TBM Office; document current state and future state of reporting; identify and assess alternative functional and technical solutions to bridge reporting gaps
        * Research, review, and analyze the effectiveness and efficiency of existing reports within the Apptio IT Cost Transparency module, and develop strategies for enhancing and improving the performance of these reports
        * Implement companion reporting tool(s) to resolve any gaps in Apptio reporting functionality
        * Gather non-technical functional requirements from Stakeholders in Finance, IT and the LOBs and translate into technical reporting deliverables; process will include requirements documentation, analysis, iterative design and development, testing, implementation and continuous improvement activities
        * Synchronize inputs from trending data, root cause analyses and discussions with IT and Finance management (Manager, Director and VP level) to produce meaningful and timely monthly reporting packages of actual versus expected results related to IT consumption and associated costs, accompanied by relevant interpretive analysis
        * Deliver TBM Office performance reporting (monthly), TBM submissions to IT Monthly Management Reporting (MMR) and Quarterly Business Performance Review (BPR) process, and ad hoc reports as required
        * Serve as TBM champion and educator for stakeholders unfamiliar with associated practices and technologies
        * Provide IT and business partners with information to define, demand and manage their consumption of IT services
        Qualifications
        * 5-7 years of related experience
        * Bachelor's Degree in a finance (Finance, Economics, Business Administration) or technology discipline (Engineering, Computer Science, Management Information Systems)
        * 5+ years of experience working with business intelligence, data management, ad hoc query, and/or data analysis tools such as Tableau, Cognos, MicroStrategy, Crystal Reports, Informatica, SQL Server Reporting Services (SSRS), etc.
        * 5+ years of experience in data analysis, data visualization, data science, or data mining
        * 5+ years of experience in relational database management system (RDBMS) technologies such as: SQL Server, Sybase, Oracle, SQLlite, MySQL, PostgreSQL, etc.
        * Strong financial aptitude and robust analytical skills - experience with financial modeling, budgeting, cost allocations, Activity Based Costing, capital and operating expense handling, program portfolio management and application / infrastructure support within an IT organization (internal or client organization)
        * Understanding of IT infrastructure domains including data centers, network/communications, server utilization, virtual environments, storage, and application development, and how those items drive total cost of ownership of an application, platform, or solution
        * Experience working with IT Cost Management or Cost Transparency processes and tools (Apptio)
        * Proficiency with enterprise business management software (PeopleSoft, Hyperion, ServiceNow, etc.)
        * Personality type who thrives in a team oriented, start-up type environment; can easily shift focus between high priority tasks as required
        * Intellectually curious; seeks out opportunities to expand professional knowledge and network; funnels found knowledge into continuous process improvement activity
        * Proven track record of navigating across organizational boundaries to successfully influence others and build trust-based relationships; maintains poise and professionalism during challenging conversations
        * Strong to expert level skills in data transformation and ETL using MS Excel, Access or similar tools. Strong skills in formulaic logic such as MS Excel pivot tables, macros, VLOOKUP, "if" statements, etc.
        * Strong report presentation development skills (MS PowerPoint, Tableau) to facilitate messaging for executive management audiences
        Preferred Skills
        * Master's degree in Computer Science/Engineering, Data Analytics or Data Science, or Finance related field
        * Certified Data Management Professional (CDMP) certification
        * IT Asset Management, IT Information Library (ITIL Foundation) and Service Management (ITSM) knowledge
        * Experience with the Agile software development methodology
        * Minimum of 2+ years custom ETL using T-SQL, PL/SQL, Python, VBA, or other scripting language
