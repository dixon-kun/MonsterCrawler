**5245BR** **Job Description:** We are building a team that is changing the perceptions of one of the most recognized brands in the world. This new team will be comprised of creative, innovative and disciplined storytellers that are committed to telling smart, succinct and sustained stories on behalf of McDonald’s. McDonald’s is changing and is committed to doing the right thing, committed to using its size and scale for good and committed to consistently delivering feel good moments for all of its customers and employees. If you are looking to join an organization as an analyst and financial supervisor that acts as a change agent, that influences the future of a leadership brand and helps shapes new processes and modern public relations plans, then this position is for you. As the Supervisor of Brand Trust Analytics for McDonald's Corporate Communications team, you will help create and change the perceptions of one of the most recognized brands in the world. **Requisition Number:** 5245BR **Country:** United States **EOE Statement:** McDonald’s Corporation is an equal opportunity employer committed to a diverse and inclusive workforce. **Job Type:** full-time **Minimum Requirements:** **Basic Qualifications:** + BA/BS in Data Science, Business/Marketing, Communications/PR, or other related field. + 2+ years of experience working with consumer brand(s) to develop reputational and content performance models. **Preferred Qualifications:** + Agency/media experience preferred. + Passion for project management, processes and tools that drive efficiencies. + Familiarity with traditional and social media monitoring tools and related metrics reporting (third / first-party resources and technologies). + Passion for measurement and insights gathering. + Must enjoy working in a fast-paced, multi-tasking, deadline-oriented dynamic environment with constant change. + Detail-oriented, self-starter and a team player. + Solution-oriented problem solver. + High level of passion for details, planning, and data. IndeedMCD *LI-NT **State:** Illinois **Responsibilities:** As part of this communications campaign effort, McDonald’s is seeking an **Analytics & Systems Supervisor.** In this role, you will be part of a cross-functional team, helping to create, manage and measure ROI against the perceptions of one of the world’s most recognized brands, dedicated to this initiative. Reporting to the Strategy and Planning Sr. Director, the Analytics Supervisor will be responsible for leading KPI Dashboard development with agency partners and ongoing tracking and reporting, management of the data sources and integrating those sources so that the team can use the dashboard for planning purposes. The results from the Analytics Supervisors work will deliver data-driven results to inform strategic decision making, and support an integrated reporting model. Additional project management, newsroom and asset management systems will be the focus in this role. Supporting the Strategy & Planning team by identifying and normalizing project management tools, master editorial calendar management, newsroom maintenance and identify new process needs for the team. The Insights Supervisor will have a direct connection to the Campaign brand hub, which has the following accountabilities: + Leverage data and intelligence to inform strategy and tactics + Manage campaign dashboard and real time trends and insights + Support the editorial strategy, campaign cadence and proactive and reactive efforts across owned and earned channels + Develop and maintain tools needed for team The Analytics Supervisor will work closely with traditional media and social media functions and proactively counsel business partners to effectively reach and engage defined audiences (e.g., media, consumers, bloggers, third party organizations, etc.) with McDonald's brand story and messages, drive awareness and sales and protect/enhance overall brand image. Principal accountabilities include leading the following workstreams: + Monitor all data sources for on-going dashboard development. + Manage relationships with the data sources used for dashboard development + Create real time reports as needed + Create ongoing deep dive reports identified by needs of the strategy and planning team + Work with the third party agency responsible for all third party measurement of communications efforts + Manage all planning and project management tools + Drive efficiencies across team by identify and ultimately helping solve barriers **Relationship Building** + Develops and maintains relationships with key stakeholders across the U.S. Business, especially with those departments and individuals that the Communications team partners with including insights, digital/social, public affairs and marketing as well as agency partners. + Demonstrates teamwork within the Communications team as a whole i.e., supporting team members, providing coaching/mentoring, identifying ongoing opportunities to contribute to cross-functional initiatives. + Represents Communications both internally within the McDonald's system and externally at industry and business functions, events, etc. Brings back knowledge and new information to the team and department in order to further collective skills development, leadership. **Resource Management/Administration** + Supports the development and management of tools/technology to support insights and intelligence needs, aligning to communications and campaign objectives (e.g., budget, agency support, team support, etc). + Must have knowledge of project management tools, with a keen passion for organization and process. **Job Title:** Analytics & Systems Supervisor - Brand Communications and Corporate Relations **City:** Oak Brook
