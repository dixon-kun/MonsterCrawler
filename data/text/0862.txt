        About Moat
        We help brands and media companies push the envelope on privacy-friendly models of advertising, help them make sound product decisions that involve running fewer ads, help make their ads load faster, fight botnets, and strive to de-fund fake news. We are a New York City-headquartered startup with a mission to help the world's top publishers and marketers make smarter decisions through real-time data. Our analytics and intelligence software is used by The New York Times, Cond&#233; Nast, Kellogg's, P&amp;G, Spotify, and hundreds of other major platforms and advertisers, and we have unique measurement integrations into Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Snapchat, and YouTube.

        * Design and implement systems to process and aggregate terabytes of streaming data in real time.
        * Build micro-services and APIs that facilitate data processing and expose Moat data to our clients
