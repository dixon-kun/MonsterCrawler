        Global Security Business Data Analyst
        Are you passionate about supporting critical business decision making by identifying business impacts, risks and threats through the use of data analysis and business intelligence?Do you enjoy driving business change with data analysis, measures and the use of analytical tools?If the answer is yes, Raytheon Missile Systems (RMS) has an exciting opportunity for you in the Global Security Business Data Analyst position.
        Applicants may be hired as a Global Business Data Analyst I or a Global Business Data Analyst II based on the candidates experience, skills &amp; education as they relate to the position.
        Job Description:
        This position is part of the RMS Global Security Services (GSS) Business Analysis &amp; Emergency Preparedness organization charged with providing functional data science support to all global security disciplines. The Global Security Business Data Analyst is a key member of a team that delivers strategic data and predictive analytics to business leaders to help identify and convey (i.e. visualize) global security risks and threats to our business.This will require working with security data analysts in expanding and maintaining the framework for overall global security data analytics and business intelligence.
        Responsibilities will include but are not limited to:
        * Identifying, monitoring, analyzing, and evaluating information
        * Notifying decision makers and global security disciplines about events and incidents from various business tools and sources that could impact Raytheon
        * Conduct advanced analysis utilizing visualization tools, network theory and data science analysis of security data to predict threats and vulnerabilities
        * Develop processes, procedures, and notifications to mitigate security risk
        * Manages various analytical tools and information systems to identify and follow trends, and provides actionable intelligence to key business leaders
        * Study historical data, researches trends, and analyzes effects of decisions or events to evaluate security performance
        Required Experience, Skills &amp; Education:
        * A minimum of 1-2 years of data modeling and analysis experience (network/data visualization model)
        * Experience with reporting and visualization software (Tableau, R, Minitab, Gephi)
        * Proficiency across multiple devices and platforms to include database user interfaces and query software
        * Knowledge of Global Security, Business Continuity, and Business Risk Management
        * Experience with database management systems, data warehousing, query language, reporting generators
        * The ability to obtain a U.S. DoD SECRET security clearance
        * Bachelor's in business analytics, data sciences, statistics, math or similar discipline
        Desired Experience, Skills &amp; Education:
        * 2-3 years of Data Analysis, Business Intelligence or Global/Corporate Security experience
        * Strong experience in Tableau
        * Experience in building data analytic procedures and processes
        * Understanding of IT, business processes, and auditing
        * The ability to work effectively with people in various functions, programs, and business concentrations
        * Presentation and leadership briefing skills
        * Graduate degree in business analytics, data sciences, statistics, math or similar discipline
        * U.S. Citizenship
        Security Clearance Requirements:
        This position requires the successful issuance, transfer or maintenance of a Secret security Clearance. Non-US citizens may not be eligible to obtain a security clearance. The Defense Industrial Security Clearance Office (DISCO), an agency of the Department of Defense, handles and adjudicates the security clearance process. Security clearance factors include, but are not limited to, allegiance to the US, foreign influence, foreign preference, criminal conduct, security violations and drug involvement.
