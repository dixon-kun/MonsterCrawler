This position is responsible for maintaining stellar day-to-day processes and procedures to complete Commercial Print jobs.

Overall duties include, but are not limited to:

* Tactical execution and analysis of the processes involved to fulfill Commercial Print (including Direct Mail), as well as Preprint jobs; enhancing for constant improvement and superior quality assurance

* Quality assurance in commercial work to reduce or eliminate credits or make goods, and maintain a high level of customer service

Position is ultimately responsible for these tasks across the group:

Commercial Printing

* Quoting process, and profitability analysis of all Direct Mail/Commercial print jobs in support of Direct Mail sales and that staff; includes quote development, list requests and purchases, custom quote preparation with vendors, working with outsource partners (such as ValPak), and communication with and reps and direct mail specialists as needed

* Process supervision for commercial print, including coordination with press, bindery, data, outsourcing partners and vendors; delivery and billing as needed.

* Being a well-versed postal mail design requirement resource; and having knowledge of postal requirements for mailing specifications and types of postage, to assist reps/specialists as needed

* Understanding data lists and data analysis to prepare basic direct mail and commercial print quotes; coordinating the work with internal and external data science sources for fulfillment

* Analyzing and assessing jobs for profitability and expense coverage; reporting on our profitability and processing as necessary

* Supervise print scheduling, verification of postal charges and incoming/outgoing processing for direct mail, commercial print, and preprints

* May be involved in production system upgrades, planning or regionalization as necessary

Communication

* Ensures CMGO departments have a resource available to them regarding inserting, printing, mailing and delivery questions; in charge of contact for production department for all work related to insert planning and zoning;

* Includes: Art files delivery, prepress/layout, delivery schedules / bills of lading, outside vendors and partners, bindery/mail, press room, advertising dept., circulation dept., and data.

* Reporting - prepare profitability reports as needed.

Skills

* Strong analytical skills

* Working knowledge of postal processes and procedures

* Great communication skills to work with multiple departments in demanding situations

* Works well under pressure

* Extremely detail-oriented

* Understanding of prepress processes and bindery processes

Experience

* Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience

* Proven process and project management experience

* Strong computer skills; skilled in Excel and the suite of Microsoft Office

* Work with Access a plus

* USPS Mail Design certification a plus

* Knowledge of Mail or press production systems a plus

* Knowledge of PACE and/or DTI a plus
