The IBM US Sales & Distribution team is seeking highly talented graduate and undergraduate level candidates to fill Technical Solution Specialist roles. Our clients are game changers, be part of the team that makes it happen.At IBM, our clients have the future in mind and your job is to help them get there. As part of IBM’s sales team, you will work with clients as they transform organizations and entire industries. We’re driven by more than just selling solutions. We want our clients to succeed. IBMers can access our entire organization to develop original ideas that help clients create the world they imagine.As an IBM Technical Solution Specialist you will be guided by IBM's purpose – to be essential to its clients - actively engaging clients to develop the trust and credibility to help them grow their businesses by solving strategic and tactical business problems with IBM’s proven cognitive and cloud technology solutions. You must be self-motivated, ready to interact with a variety of other smart and highly motivated people daily, and work in a changing environment where no two days are ever the same. IBM Technical Solution Specialists deliver industry-centric business solutions to clients of all sizes and markets. This position requires a passion for client success, sales achievement, and daily collaboration with clients and other IBM teammates. You will work with any of the following business units: Analytics, Cognitive Solutions, Cloud, Commerce, Internet of Things, Security, Social, Systems Hardware, or Systems Software.During the Summit Program’s nine month development program you will be required to travel 25% of the time. You will receive exceptional technology, industry solution training, shadowing opportunities, and consultative sales training. Training will be delivered through a combination of classroom and online resources, hands-on field experiences, and structured mentorship that will equip you with the knowledge and skills needed to match the technical capabilities of IBM offerings to client requirements. Training takes place in 8 metro regions. Ideal candidates would reside within a reasonable commuting distance (generally 50 miles or less) of one of the following cities: Atlanta GA, Boston MA, Chicago IL, Columbus OH, Dallas TX, New York NY, San Francisco CA, and Washington DC.Following the training period, you will join a team partnering with clients and leverage your technical expertise to assist your sales counterparts and maintain client satisfaction. As a Technical Solution Specialist, you will work with the client account team and drive revenue through in-depth, complex technical demonstrations, designing solutions, technical evaluations, and/or proof-of-concepts. Please note: this is not a coding, development or extensive hands-on role. This role will involve technical consulting with clients and helping clients design technical solutions. If you would like additional information about the Summit program please visit:http://www-03.ibm.com/employment/summit/Please Note: Candidate must have Legal authorization to work in US. Sponsorship for employment visa status will not be provided + Completed Bachelor or Masters' degree in Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Data Science, Electrical Engineering, Information Technology or similar disciplines by start date of employment + English: Fluent S&D Non-brand
