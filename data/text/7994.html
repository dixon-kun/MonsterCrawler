At Wunderman, we are creatively driven and data inspired— it’s what sets us apart from other agencies. We are seeking savvy digital experts with vision who focus on results. Our Technical SEO Specialists create paths for each client by evaluating stated goals/objectives and providing performance-based solutions for paid, owned and earned media. To do this effectively, Technical SEO Specialists must keep an active pulse on digital trends and tactics to create both short-term and long-term strategies that encourage client retention alongside immediate goal accomplishments.

As a Technical SEO Specialist, you will serve as an SEO expert seamlessly uniting our content and development practices to drive search engine rank performance, traffic, engagement and conversions for our clients.


  • Be the SEO expert, particularly as it relates to the technical aspects (e.g. able to implement structured markup, provide detailed instructions for handling server errors and poor page speed, etc.)
  • Perform full SEO site audits and analysis (technical, analytics, content, social, etc.)
  • Perform full keyword research and develop related strategies
  • Bring paid, owned, and earned media together to create cohesive interactions within the spaces
  • Communicate effectively across an organization, internally and externally – from developers to executives
  • Research and create POVs on SEO trends and new implications (e.g. mobile, apps, and voice recognition); educate internal team members and clients
  • Determine KPIs for each recommendation, forecast impact, and create case studies

  • Minimum 3 years as an SEO specialist
  • Must have a proven track record of driving results
  • Technical/development inclined
  • Have a working knowledge in HTML, Javascript, and other programming languages
  • Analytics Certified (e.g. Google, Adobe, Coremetrics, etc.) | Expertise with Google and Bing Search Console
  • Social Media savvy
  • Dedicated to research and learning
  • Clear communication skills in multiple forms (e.g. verbal, e-mail, PowerPoint and in-person presentations)
  • Ability to write web copy is a nice-to-have
  • Prior agency or consulting experience is a plus
