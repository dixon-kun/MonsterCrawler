          Position:
         Analyst, Marketing Intelligence/Marketing Solutions
          Location(s):
         Columbia, MD; Little Rock, AR; Denver, CO
          Purpose
         TheAnalyst, Marketing Intelligence/Marketing Solutionswill use in-depth knowledge in quantitative database marketing and an understanding of business processes to develop technical solutions for business issues. This person will analyze data through a business, industry, and marketing lens to solve client problems.
          Key Responsibilities
          Provide customer profiling, segmentation analysis, survey design and analysis
          Conduct data analysis and mining, external data enhancement
          Assist in determining quantitative methods for solving client issues
          Help to deliver practical and actionable recommendations based data analysis
          Work cross-functionally throughout the organization to implement client solution recommendations
          Develop effective reporting and dashboards with an objective to evaluate marketing performance and provide recommendations for enhancement
          Anticipate project risk and constraints and proactively work on solutions to address the risk/constraints
          Under guidance, responsible to understand client business problems, solution approach, and execute basic analysis and research projects, package and assist in presenting insights to stake holders
          Key Skills and Experience
          Bachelor or Master degree in statistics, math, economics or related quantitative field with strong grades both in their major and overall; also including rising seniors graduating in August 2017 or earlier
          Minimum of 0-1 years of experience in quantitative/analytic arena, preferably in database marketing
          Demonstrated, hands-on experience with analytics tools preferred SAS or R
          Working knowledge of database systems (relational/RDBMS, "Big data"/Hadoop) required
          Experience with data query languages (SQL, HIVE [data sci]) required
          Ability to execute advanced analytic tasks (including but not limited to modeling, segmentation, DOE, forecasting, etc.) and create meaningful analytical outputs
          Ability to discover hidden insights or complex patterns through advanced multi-variate data mining techniques on un-structured business problems
          Understanding of digital data is preferred, including the systems and processes used to generate and manipulate it by media type: web, ad, email, mobile, etc.
          Well-versed in the core MS Office suite programs; Access, Visio, One Note are also a plus
          Ability to explain and present complicated/advanced analytical methodology and results to non-technical audiences
          Must be able to communicate effectively by phone; write clear emails and memos; be friendly, cheerful, and patient; and enjoy explaining complicated concepts to others
          Hard-working and eager to learn
          Ability to handle multiple projects, prioritize responsibilities and work efficiently in a fast-pace environment
          High level of accuracy and attention to detail
          Keen problem-solving and trouble-shooting abilities
