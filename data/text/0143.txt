        Vox Media builds smart media brands people love, empowering trusted digital voices with proprietary technology to create and distribute stories to an audience of hundreds of millions of young adults worldwide. Vox Media is eight distinct brands: The Verge (Technology and Culture), Vox (General News), SB Nation (Sports), Polygon (Gaming), Racked (Shopping), Eater (Food and Nightlife), Curbed (Real Estate and Home), and Recode (Technology and Business).
        As Head of Data Science, you will report directly to the Chief Product Officer and be the center of gravity for our data efforts in an applied way. You will lead efforts to extract mission critical insights from troves of data, working with product and business partners to research, develop, and operationalize our data strategies toward company growth and ultimately drive value to all departments. In an executive management role, you'll build, lead and directly manage a team of Data Scientists, analysts, and directors as well as work with a cross functional group of Engineers, Designers, and Product Managers, collaborating closely with leaders across the company including Product, Growth &amp; Development, Analytics, Editorial, Revenue, and Corporate.
        Leading this group, you'll help establish and evolve the company's key metrics, experimental design practices, and provide guidance around measurable goals and forecasting; help to build intelligent tools for our editorial and video teams, product developers, business operations and revenue organization; and put data to work through clear insights and machine learning. Your goal will be to help us understand the nuances of our audience and our content, across all platforms, better than any other organization, ultimately to help us grow our premium media brands and revenue everywhere. You will ultimately drive a shift in deepening and broadening our data informed culture enabling data as a true currency.
        Core responsibilities:
        * Foster a data-informed culture at Vox Media through clear direction and results
        * Design data processes and best practices with the rest of the company in a way that drives value into every group and aligns to all strategic business priorities
        * Drive the coordination and execution of ongoing audience and revenue data governance in support of analysts across the company
        * Work with the growth and analytics organization to establish strategies across Vox Media brands and business units for using internal and external data to better inform decision-making and investments and identifying opportunities for data-driven growth and monetization across all platforms;
        * Work with our Data teams to deliver data-powered, intelligent tools, products, and automated services for editorial, video and revenue teams as well as consumers;
        * Support formulation of KPIs and goals measurement, modeling key factors to performance outcomes, and working to optimize key metrics with executives, editors, developers, product managers, and designers through informed experimentation; partner to design and deploy machine learning features;
        * Work closely with the Engineering organization to develop infrastructure and capabilities for data capture, collection, processing, reporting and usage;
        * Distill complex data sets into clear, actionable insight;
        * Head the management of internal data initiatives, and communicate these priorities to stakeholders and company leadership;
        * Collaborate on the management of vendor solutions;
        * Keep up to date with emerging digital technologies, and assess their relevance to our products, developers, and internal clients.
        Basic Qualifications
        * 8+ years of experience successfully delivering actionable data insights and building operations that accelerate crucial business outcomes, preferably in a digital media environment;
        * A degree in a mathematical or technical discipline (mathematics, engineering, computer science, economics, statistics, sciences);
        * Demonstrated knowledge of multivariate statistics and modeling techniques;
        * Demonstrated expertise in using SQL for data extraction, manipulation and insights analysis
        * Familiarity with Amazon Web Services / Google Cloud Platform a plus
        * Demonstrated understanding of social media platforms, video platforms, and user behavior;
        * Outstanding communication skills and a track record of being able to distill highly technical information into palatable, meaningful statements for both technical and non-technical audiences;
        * Hands-on experience and expertise in Python, R, SAS, SPSS, SQL, Spark, and other related analytical/programming tools and software strongly preferred;
        * Experience managing, attracting, and mentoring high-performing teams, ideally having worked closely with data science practitioners.
