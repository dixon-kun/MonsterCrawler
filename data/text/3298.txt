             POSITION SUMMARY
             This position resides within Aetna's Special Investigation Unit (SIU) leading teams responsible for Fraud, Waste and Abuse data mining activities, application development and support, SIU case intake, and regulatory reporting. The position will also focus on managing the implementation of key SIU projects and initiatives.
             Fundamental Components:
             The Special Investigations Unit is looking for an established leader to oversee all Development and Data Mining initiatives. The ideal candidate would have a proven record of leading change based on credible process improvement measures. The selected candidate will have the opportunity to step in and lead the SIU partnership with Data Sciences in the development and implementation of new fraud detection rules and algorithms as well as all related software tools. Candidates should have extensive SAS &amp; SQL coding experience in either the PC and/or the UNIX environment as well as expertise in the utilization of medical and pharmaceutical claim data to identify outlier behavior.
             Job responsibilities include:
             Leading all aspects of data analyses (i.e., data acquisition, data management, SAS programming and documentation) including development of reports and analyses for both internal and external customers as well as ongoing maintenance of these solutions
             Implementation of improved reporting and metrics supporting both case work and claims handling operations
             Responsible for leading the production and distribution of state and federal reporting
             Managing both internal &amp; external projects and implementations
             Candidates will also be expected to build relationships with state and federal agencies such as the FBI, The Office of Personnel Management (OPM), and State Departments of Insurance.
             BACKGROUND/EXPERIENCE desired:
             -8 or more years of healthcare claims analytic experience using SAS and SQL coding techniques
             -Extensive knowledge of Aetnas data warehouse and other internal data sources required
             -Experience implementing process improvement measures. Exposure to Aetna Business Excellence (ABX) methodologies preferred.
