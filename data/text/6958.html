Hadoop Application Architect – Life Sciences - NJ

Location – NJ

Required Skills

  • Currently hands on development of in Big Data Hadoop ecosystem & Analytics space
  • MS/BS degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Engineering or related field.
  • 12+ years of experience as a technology leader designing and developing data architecture solutions with more than 2+ years specializing in big data architecture or data analytics.
  • Minimum of 10+ years of hands-on experience including architecture, design of enterprise applications in Java .
  • Experience of implementing solutions on Big data technologies - Hadoop, MapReduce, Pig, Hive, Spark, Storm, Impala, Oozie, Mahout, Flume, ZooKeeper and/or Sqoop
  • Solid understanding of NoSQL and prior experience working with NoSQL databases Hbase, MongoDB, Cassandra.
  • Come up with solutions based on open source Big Data Analytics framework.
  • Closely follow the changes in the open source community specifically regarding Big Data, Data Science and Analytics
  • Our technology stack currently includes: Java, Spring, Hibernate, Restful Web Services, HTML5, AngularJS, Hadoop, Hive, Kafka, Sqoop, Spark, Python, AWS/Google compute/Azure
  • Plus to have prior experience with Analytics platforms (e.g. Pentaho, Business Objects or similar), OLAP technologies and ETL tools such as Informatica, Talend and/or Pentaho
  • Experience working with Cloud Storage solutions in AWS, Azure etc. is a plus.
  • Enterprise Business Intelligence and Analytics (e.g. SAP, Oracle, Teradata, Vertica)
  • Exposure to technologies not included in our stack is a plus

Responsibilities

  • It’s essential for this role to take a system view of architecture rather than just designing code modules. This will involve adopting and integrating new technologies constantly.
  • Be technical mentor to the engineering team that owns the development of Analytics Framework
  • Review design and code of the engineering team
  • Evaluate new technologies and be able to give recommendations
  • Lead technical solutions effort for Big Data Analytics Framework deal pre-sales
  • Support customer conversations (remotely or in person)
  • Actively participate in roadmap discussions
  • Hands on technology evaluation to implement POCs or research.
