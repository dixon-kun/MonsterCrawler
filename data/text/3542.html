About the Job

* FT, Software Engineer - SOFIA Data Processing System at NASA, in Mountain View, CA. *

ABOUT US:

LOGYX™ provides intelligent solutions to assist our government and private industry clients. We have a proven history delivering cost-effective, scalable, and sustainable solutions to a diverse customer base, achieving customer goals and objectives with unparalleled commitment and dedication. http://www.logyx.com/

RECIPIENT OF THE 2011 & 2013 NASA AMES RESEARCH CENTER SMALL BUSINESS PRIME CONTRACTOR OF THE YEAR AWARD

POSITION OVERVIEW:

Logyx LLC has a full time, regular Software Engineer, working at NASA, in Mountain View, CA. We have excellent benefits, which includes a 401k match. Join a team working on this exciting project!

Provide software systems engineering support to the Stratospheric Observatory for Infrared Astronomy (SOFIA) program, particularly the SOFIA Data Processing System (DPS).  Support the development of SOFIA Data Handling Tools. Support requirements definition and I&T activities for SOFIA science instrument data pipeline processing systems.

REQUIREMENTS:

Required Education/Experience:

  • Bachelor's degree in software engineering or related scientific/engineering discipline.
  • Minimum 5 years of experience in software development for NASA flight missions and/or ground systems.
  • Knowledge of NASA software development policies and procedures (NPR 7150.2).

Required Skills/Training:

  • Knowledge of Python, IDL, Bash, and/or Perl programming languages.
  • Familiarity with Linux, Unix , and Mac operating systems.

Desired Education, Experience, Skills or Training (if applicable):

  • Advanced degree in software engineering or related scientific/engineering discipline.
  • Image processing experience.
  • Experience in performing astronomical observations and/or performing data reduction processing of astronomical observation data.
  • Familiarity with JAVA and/or C programming languages

RESPONSIBILITES:

Provide software systems engineering support for the following software: the Data Processing System (DPS), including the science data reduction pipelines; and the Data Handling Tools. This support includes:

  • Provide technical insight and review of the software engineering activities from a NASA perspective, including advice for requirement specification, design, development, verification and validation (V&V), and acceptance activities in according with the SOFIA Software Management Plan
  • Prepare, coordinate, and/or participate in reviews, and review software documentation, including requirements and test results
  • Update or generate software documentation, including software requirements documents, software ICDs,software design documents, test / V&V plans and procedures, test reports, and version description documents
  • Develop and execute test / V&V procedures
  • Lead and/or coordinate software configuration control boards
  • Submit and sponsor Discrepancy Reports (DRs), Configuration Change Requests (CCRs) and/or Requests for Deviations or Waivers (RDWs) through the Observatory Configuration Control Board (OCCB)
  • Lead design, development, and test of the Data Handling Tools. Serve as liaison with Mission Operations on needs and usage of the tools. Train SOFIA staff on use of the tools.
  • Support completion of the SOFIA Engineering Data Management Plan including analysis of user needs, data volumes, and current problems.
  • Provide recommendations for work process, software, hardware, and policy improvements.
  • Review / monitor progress versus plan for the implementation of the improvements across SOFIA.
  • Provide support for the Program’s Metrics Working Group for metrics related to processing of science data and science productivity as defined in the SOFIA Metrics Document.
  • Provide input to weekly/monthly/quarterly task technical reports.
  • Travel for this task is estimated at 2 trips of 3 days duration each per year to Building 703 in Palmdale, CA where the SOFIA aircraft and Armstrong Flight Research Center (AFRC) SOFIA staff are located.
