ISO Solutions group of Verisk Insurance Solutions, is seeking an experienced data scientist to serve as the lead technical resource in the development of new insurance products and supports the enhancement of existing products.

Title: Director Analytics / Principal II - Research and Development

Organizational Unit: ISO Solutions

Supervisory Responsibility: Yes

Requirements:

* Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience. CPCU or comparable professional designation desired.

* Minimum 10 years data science experience required.

* Minimum 10 years product development experience desired.

* Effectively communicates (i.e., interpersonal, written, oral) with colleagues, staff, and external customers.

* Ability to function effectively in a project leadership capacity. Coordinate and lead interdepartmental project / research teams.

* Demonstrated ability to research complex insurance-related issues and develop and present concise/comprehensive recommendations for management consideration.

* Knowledgeable of insurance industry trends and emerging issues.

Responsibilities:

* Controls the conceptual and technical development of new and revised insurance coverages, as assigned, in response to changing business environment. Reviews industry news and events for their impact on ISO insurance programs.

* Controls the research, preparation and presentation of agenda items to provide insurer panels with all the background, staff analysis, alternatives, and recommendations needed to assess issues and provide advice. Controls the preparation of related panel minutes.

* Performs and may direct the conceptual and technical development of new and revised insurance products in response to changing insurer needs.

* Participate in a collaborative process with all affected disciplines in finalizing product revisions and filings.

* May review the analysis of company programs to determine independent status and impact on statistical plans.

* Controls the preparation of special projects, as assigned.

* Responsible for the performance of assigned staff on particular projects. Serve as mentor to line staff and assist in training and development of staff.

* Monitor and communicate developments in project status to AVPs and senior management as needed.

* Perform and direct consulting projects (e.g., policy comparisons and PHNs for companies looking to switch from a competitor to an ISO program, specialized 3rd party policy form development, etc.).
