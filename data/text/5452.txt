        With a focus on speed and execution, we handle the full cycle of data science problems: mining data and working with business groups to identify new opportunities, rapidly prototyping solutions using cutting edge techniques from machine learning and statistical modeling, deploying those solutions as products in real time settings, and monitoring and improving them over time.
        We're a tight-knit, fast-paced team working with a large amount of complete and powerful data that are committed to staying at the cutting edge of data science and having a huge impact in a multi-billion dollar business.
        We are looking for:
        * 1 &#8211; 2 years experience as a data scientist or equivalent educational training
        * Proficient at collecting and mining data from disparate data sources
        * Extensive hands on experience working with large data sets, including statistical analyses, hypothesis testing, and data cleansing/transformation.
        * Ability to create visualizations to help tell stories about the data (d3, ggplot2, Seaborn, googleVis, etc.)
        * Experience building predictive models inside a statistical computing environment (e.g., R, Python, Sklearn, SPSS, Matlab, Octave)
        * Experimental design and controls, interpretation of results and statistical rigor.
        * Analytical and detail oriented with the ability to prioritize, execute and deliver projects on time
        * Must be comfortable with unstructured, fast moving and constantly evolving high growth environment
        Nice to have but not required:
        * Experience with a high level programming language (e.g., Java, Python, C/C++)
        * Experience working with Big Data Solutions such as Pig, Cassandra, HDFS, Spark and HBase
        * Experience with deploying data science solutions in a production environment, especially in near real-time settings
