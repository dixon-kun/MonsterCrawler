           Data Scientist Summary:
          Signature Consultants has an opportunity for a Data Scientist.&#160; A Data Scientist is an expert in statistical sampling and analytic methods, creates measurements and analytic models, and develops reports and insights on map quality. This role is responsible for developing analytics. The candidate will perform a variety of complex tasks requiring discretion and expert judgment. They will suggest improvements and seek opportunities for innovation. The candidate will share knowledge with less experienced peers and operate with a minimal degree of supervision and coaching. He/she will be responsible for quality and accuracy metrics and analytics. The ideal candidate demonstrates initiative and contributes to problem solving, makes decisions affecting own work within set parameters and elevates others.\
           Responsibilities:
           Expected to be the Subject Matter Expert (SME) for product test design methodologies and statistical estimators and analytics
           Develop sampling plans for data collection, quality evaluation, and the production of Training Data
           Design and create technical estimators for the evaluation of map quality along various internal and customer use-cases
           Design and analyze A/B experiments that validate different optimization and solution approaches, and to calibrate model parameter.
           Build and test analytic and statistical models to support improvement of a wide variety of data-driven processes for map-making data evaluation and decisions
           Work with map experts, engineering teams, and other analytic teams
           Provide support to the statistical and analytical needs of various departments within the company
           Train other teams within the organization on statistical thinking and methods
           Reviews priorities with Principal Data Scientist
           Liaison responsibilities across various organizations within the company
           Qualifications:
           Master&#8217;s degree or PhD required. Degree in Statistics, Mathematics, Econometrics, or related fields
           2+ years of related work experience
           Knowledge of sampling methods, and data mining and analytic methods such as regression, classifiers, clustering, association rules, decision trees, etc.
           Proficiency with analysis and programming in R, or any similar package (Python, Matlab, SAS, etc.)
           Knowledge and experience with using GIS tools for spatial data analysis
           Knowledge of tools such as Pig, Hive, etc. for working with big data in Hadoop or Spark for data extraction/prep for analysis
           Application of statistics in quality area or Six Sigma will be a plus
           Experience with understanding, specifying and explaining measurement and analytic systems with other teams of data scientists and engineers to execute projects delivering those solutions
           Excellent oral and written communication skills
           Ability to explain statistics and analytic concepts to non-SME
