Company Description

customedialabs is a digital transformation partner that helps businesses thrive in a digital world. We are a team of scientists, adventurers, artists, and revolutionaries. We are here because our natures compel us to challenge, disrupt, and reinvent. Job Description

Act as a trusted key account advisor to strategically guide clients from a state of analog business to a state of digital business, especially focusing in the sales & marketing business areas .

Own the end to end digital transformation engagements from a business development, relationship, account and delivery management, and business architecture standpoint.

Articulate client’s digital business model, digital organization governance, operating and org models, and change management plans to achieve digital success.

Educate clients to understand and align to digital opportunities that drive business growth.  Meet and exceed customer experience expectations in constantly increasing and changing digital channels and touch points.

Execute near and long-term strategies, actionable plans, business rationale, roadmap and enterprise-wide digital transformation guidance for people, process and technology.

A successful candidate will possess many of the following characteristics:

- Ability to be a holistic thinker and problem solver

- Ability to be practical and technical as well as creative.

- Ability to consultatively sell and grow customer relationships with all levels and types of client stakeholders.  Ability to maintain excellent relationships with all levels and types of internal stakeholders.

- Endlessly curious in growing as an innovative digital solutions architect.

- Passionate advocate of using customer-centered value to build and activate brands.

- Willing to roll up sleeves to get work done on time and on budget.

Qualifications

- Bachelor of Science Degree in Business or Engineering.

- Master's Degree preferred.

- Work experience or Research experience in user-centered technology

- Excellent verbal & written communication.
