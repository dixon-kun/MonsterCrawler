The VP of Data Science and Analytics will provide oversight and leadership to a team of data analysts and scientists in the Direct channel. This role has ownership and guides the vision for advanced analytics to drive marketing strategy in direct response channels. This position is based in San Mateo, CA and there may be occasional travel to Netspend's headquarters in Austin, Texas.

Responsibilities:

* Champion a data-driven culture and provide analytics thought leadership to the organization

* Manage and develop an analytics team of 3 team members; capability to inspire and motivate a high performing team

* Advanced Analytics, Measurement, and Campaign Guidance: Oversee the development of test design, forward looking learning agendas, measurement framework, consumer insights, predictive analytics, and media mix modeling.

* Data Strategy: Finding key insights in disparate datasets including leads, consumer transactions, website engagement, search patterns, customer segmentations.

* Modeling: Advance existing proprietary models and identify additional modeling opportunities to propel the company forward in acquisition, retention, and attribution

* Provide guidance on data infrastructure and logic to ensure platform is scalable for continued growth

* Work cross-functionally with marketing and account management team to provide the data and analytics needed to drive acquisition growth, campaign efficiency and program effectiveness

Skills/Education/Experience:

* 10+ years in marketing analytics with a proven track record; experience in financial services industry preferred

* Bachelors Degree in Quantitative Analytics, Statistics, Mathematics, Marketing Science, or similar discipline; Masters/PHD preferred

* Practical working knowledge of multi-channel consumer marketing strategies with a deep familiarity with media analytics

* Technical Skills: Statistical Modeling, Advanced Analytics methods, Machine Learning, familiarity with "Big Data" frameworks, hands-on experience and expertise with SQL/PLSQL, R, QlikView/Tableau

* Forward-thinking approach and successful history in driving their company's use of advanced analytics and automated processes leading to continuous business improvement

* Proven ability to establish productive working relationships at all levels and influence change with internal and external business partners

* Ability to maintain good balance of strategic vision and tactical implementation

* A strong communicator with the ability to explain complex technical subject matter to non-technical people

* Strong professional ethics and impeccable personal integrity

* As provided in Netspend's Background Check policy, eligible candidates may be subject to drug, criminal history, and credit checks, which will be conducted and used in accordance with applicable local, state, and federal laws
