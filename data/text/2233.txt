Analyst - Data Science Informatics - DuPont | Jobs.com
        DuPont has a rich history of scientific discovery that has enabled countless innovations and today, we're looking for more people, in more places, to collaborate with us to make life the best that it can be.
        This position is a project leadership role focused on delivering software solutions in the area of molecular breeding and trial execution. The research analyst will work as part of a larger molecular and applied plant breeding and research information management support team to assist in the development of a data management, software and statistical/genetic analysis strategy to meet the needs of the research clients.
        Duties/Responsibilities:
        Molecular Breeding Science-related job responsibilities:
        * Take a leadership role within a larger Molecular Breeding, Genetic Data Management and Trial, providing support for the deployment of novel statistical and molecular technologies to facilitate marker enhanced breeding with special emphasis on the areas of:
        * Experimental design and analysis
        * Analysis of molecular information
        * Marker enhanced selection and prediction
        * Mining and sharing QTL information
        * Collaborate with in-house research efforts to identify and monitor germplasm diversity using molecular tools, and work with the breeding community to develop strategies to identify the underlying genetic factors responsible for traits important to the breeding program, this includes enhanced field experiment analysis techniques and trait introgression processes.
        General IM-related job responsibilities:
        * Lead the software design work for proprietary information management software including database queries and transactions, algorithms, and user interface design.
        * Prepare specifications from which information systems are developed. Ensure that software information requirements are met through the development of software applications.
        * Help with strategic data management and data transfers between core repositories where necessary for process flow.
        * Work in partnership with Research and Information Management staff to define the scope and objectives of a project. Facilitate communications to assure understanding between the project team and all stakeholders of the system requirements.
        Education and Experience:
        Master's degree in an agriculture or scientific related field and 2-4 years of industry related experience.
        Ph.D. in plant breeding, agronomy, applied statistics, systems modeling, or related field is preferred.
        Competencies:
        An ideal candidate would possess most of the following competencies and experience:
        General Competencies:
        * Excellent written and oral communication skills and demonstrated ability to communicate complex information in a clear and concise manner.
        * Ability to work both independently and collaboratively in a team environment. Strong leadership, communication, project management and time management skills.
        * Demonstrated ability to effectively manage priorities in a multi-task work environment.
        * Demonstrated ability to work cooperatively in a team environment.
        * Strong interpersonal, communication, problem solving, and leadership skills.
        * Ability to communicate, read, and interpret instructions in English and able to carry out tasks in the absence of supervision
        Science Competencies:
        * Demonstrated skills in creating and using statistical models and in software development projects.
        * Demonstrated knowledge of plant biological research for the development of commercial seed products.
        * Experience using information management/software tools for plant breeding is required.
        * Demonstrated knowledge and skill in DNA marker technologies and their application to applied plant breeding programs is required.
        * Demonstrated knowledge on Applied Statistics: experimental design and analysis, with special emphasis on application of mixed models to agriculture.
        * Demonstrated knowledge of QTL mapping and on the applications of marker/trait associations to marker enhanced breeding.
        * Demonstrated knowledge of plant breeding field research, including breeding strategies and experimental design.
        IM Competencies:
        * Experience working on Agile software development projects, and understanding of databases and data visualization. Knowledge of software development processes as they apply to a research environment.
        * Understanding of information management needs as they relate to statistical analysis of molecular markers, genotypic, and phenotypic information.
        * Ability to work collaboratively on teams and communicate complex technical information to a variety of audiences in a clear and concise manner.
        * Knowledgeable in working with and analyzing large quantities of complex data.
        * Demonstrated project and people management skills in working with a variety of research groups and managing multiple project deadlines.
        * Proficiency with advanced applications of Microsoft Excel and is required.
        * Familiarity with personal computer software, client-server technology, internet technology and their application to plant breeding and gene discovery projects.
        * Demonstrated understanding of relational databases and ability to extract, summarize, and report large amounts of data is required.
