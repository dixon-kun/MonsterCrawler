NJVC, LLC.

Company Job Title: Junior Cloud Analytics Developer
Clearance: TS/SCI
Location: Alexandria, VA
Reports To: Director, Engineering
FLSA Status: Exempt, Full Time, Regular


Summary: The Junior Cloud Analytics Developer will provide support for enterprise application software. The Junior Cloud Analytics Developer will need to be an individual who wants an opportunity to use their skills and knowledge in technologies for Cloud Analytics. We are looking for someone who thrives in a team with strong engineers to learn from, and where all members of the engineering team, including management, are close to the code and making technical contributions.

Essential Duties and Responsibilities: (Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions of this position)

· Map reduce programming model and technologies, such as Hadoop, Hive, Pig, and HDFS.

· Perform requirements analysis, installation, integration, evaluation, design, development, enhancement, maintenance, testing, and diagnosis or resolution of distributed algorithms and analytics.

Non-Essential Duties: Other duties as assigned

Supervisory Responsibilities: None

Minimum Qualifications: (To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily.)

· Bachelor's Degree required

· 4+ years of experience required.

· Experience as a data scientist with practical implementation experience.

· TS/SCI Security Clearance Required.

· DoD 8570 Level II compliant within 60 days of hire.

Knowledge, Skills and Abilities:

· Ability to travel as required.

· Knowledge of distributed scalable Big Data store, including

o Accumulo

o HBase big table

· Ability to obtain DoD 8570 Level II compliant within 60 days of hire.
