Responsibilities:

Noblis is seeking a highly qualified Division Leader, Advanced Analytics and Agile IT for their Reston, VA location.

The Advanced Analytics and Agile IT Division is one of three divisions in Noblis’ National Security and Intelligence (NSI) mission area. The mission is to employ innovative analytics, applied agile IT, and advanced cybersecurity methods to exploit, enable and protect data across national security missions. Focus areas include intelligence analytics, data science, bioinformatics, law enforcement systems and cloud security.  In addition, the division executes multiple Noblis sponsored research projects in fields that include big data analytics, genomics, cryptanalysis, and high performance computing applications.  This research applies across Noblis’ other mission areas and subsidiaries.

Current Clients include: DHS NPPD Division, FBI; U.S. Government Classified, Virginia Contracting Authority and DTRA.  This position will be a direct report to the SVP for National Security.  Overall span of responsibility will be about $25-30M in annual revenue and roughly 90 personnel.

Job responsibilities include:

Provides full “P&L” leadership of the division portfolio of programs with a focus on sustained business growth for high consequence missions with financial viability.

Leads the division management team to develop and execute the division strategy and business plan within the mission area’s and larger company’s priorities.

Responsible for leading the division’s organic business growth and supporting strategic business development opportunities as part of the relevant account strategies in cooperation with business development account leaders.

Initiates new business lines and offerings within the division focus areas and leverages deep client relationships and cross-company technology.

Leads cross-mission area and cross-company strategic initiatives that utilize multiple disciplines and the larger capabilities beyond those in the division.

Responsible for overseeing the quality, timeliness and relevance of client deliverables across the division portfolio

Responsible for the division’s human capital planning, workforce management and professional development within the Noblis career progression roadmap.  Attracts, retains and develops and a highly qualified, diverse staff across relevant experience levels and disciplines.

Provides sound financial stewardship of client resources and company allocated resources with fiscal discipline, efficiency and cost savings.

Works with COE function to identify, develop and transfer technology innovation projects that enable future business growth, client impact and unique Noblis differentiators.

Qualifications:

The ideal candidate will have:

  • 10-15 years of leadership experience with progressively larger and more complex tasks, projects, programs and a portfolio of programs. Direct experience working in or directly for government national security missions is a plus.
  • Proven record of sustained business growth and client diversification underpinned with substantive and broad client relationships. Tangible successes in developing new business lines and offerings from incubation to mature execution.
  • Deep domain experience in the core missions, functions and agencies where the division’s current and planned portfolio of work resides.
  • Advanced degree in a relevant discipline. PhD a plus but not required.
  • Tangible experience leading a diverse, highly qualified workforce across a wide range of disciplines, skills, experience levels and educational backgrounds.
  • Recognized expert in a key technology, mission or discipline related to the division’s primary areas of work.
  • Extraordinary communications skills. Adept at communicating complex subjects to senior executives and communicating broad issues to the workforce with clarity.
  • Results driven leader with tangible record of successes in business growth, mission execution, technology innovation and workforce management.
  • Substantive and successful experience leading new business development initiatives from concept exploration, capture management, proposal development, and post award transition.
  • Top Secret clearance with SCI access is required. A valid full scope polygraph is a plus.
Overview:

Noblis and our wholly owned subsidiaries, Noblis ESI and Noblis NSP, are solving difficult problems that help our government and our country. We bring the best of scientific thought, management, and engineering expertise with a reputation for independence and objectivity. We support a wide range of government and industry clients in the areas of national security, intelligence, transportation, healthcare, environmental sustainability, and enterprise engineering. Learn more at noblis.org/about.

Why work at a Noblis company?

Our employees find greater meaning in their work and balance the other things in life that matter to them. Our people are our greatest asset. They are exceptionally skilled, knowledgeable, team-oriented, and mission-driven individuals who want to do work that matters and benefits the public.

Noblis has won numerous workplace awards. Noblis maintains a drug-free workplace and is an equal opportunity employer. All qualified applicants will receive consideration for employment without regard to race.
