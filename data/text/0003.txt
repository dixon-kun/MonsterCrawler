        Director IT Data Analytics
        Hasbro is looking for a Director, IT Data Analytics who will be the glue between advanced analytics, data science capabilities and all non-business intelligence analytic functions including: Social Reporting, eCommerce, Consumer Insights and Digital Marketing.
        The goal is to lead an IT analytics team who provides digestible information for business partners that requires analytic support to make strategic data driven decisions. This position will oversee and manage the process for identifying and documenting analytics capability and business requirements with data science and architectural design of the underlying technology stack (data, compute/tools and presentation layers).
        Primary Duties and Responsibilities::
        * Serve as the analytics interface to the global business partners, identifying all data sources and KPTs in need of tracking to measure success as well as those functions required to create analytics insights (i.e. A/B testing)
        * Working with the Enterprise Architecture and Advanced Analytics teams to establish a technology platform roadmap for Hasbro
        * Lead a team to increase and enhance the use of advanced modeling and analytic methods and produce results that fully leverage available data and analytics.
        * Implement "test and learn" analytic methodologies to ensure Hasbro improves upon our ability to implement a variety of methodologies which improve conversation rates and increase sales
        * Analyze outcomes from the sales team engagements to continue to understand drivers and learnings to increase sales.
        * Craft and maintain budget necessary to fulfill ongoing projects and support future analytics projects
        * Manage vendor relationships with key analytics vendors and partners
        Qualifications: The ideal candidate will have a mix of the following:
        Experience
        * MBA or advanced degree in quantitative discipline preferred
        * BS equivalent required
        * 10+ years professional experience, preferably in data management, analytics platforms, and business interaction.
        * Experience and knowledge in advanced analytics data structures, data lake, compute layer and visualization tools.
        * Proven experience as an integral member of a high performing work team
        Values / What drives you
        * Data driven decision making
        * Comfortable working in a cross-functional role
        * Passion and desire to recognize opportunities and convey information to the business through analytics.
        * Possess a 'think for yourself' attitude that yields creative solutions, enables your ability to be a Self-starter, fast learner and adapt to a fast-paced working environment
        * Desire to lead others
        * Ability to uncover and resolve communication gaps between departments
        Skills
        * Strong analytical and strategic thinking skills required
        * Strong communication skills, both written and verbal, with the ability and confidence to succinctly convey complex information to senior management
        * Advanced MS Excel skills necessary
