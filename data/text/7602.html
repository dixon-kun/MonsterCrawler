Talent Analytics Human Capital Analyst

Key Role:

Apply knowledge of human capital, talent management, leadership development, organizational development, workforce planning, and analytics to government organizations. Demonstrate expertise in designing, developing, and implementing strategic human capital programs and solutions for the technical workforce, including data science and Cybersecurity. Leverage knowledge of human capital strategy, business development, and data analytic techniques to conduct and evaluate trends and analyses aimed at driving strategic and tactical organizational decision–making, influencing organizational change and improving productivity. Support marketing activities, including whitepapers, proposals, and capability briefings specifically focused on strategic human capital management. Serve as a task lead for projects by assisting with managing delivery and client relationships. Serve as a mentor to junior-level staff.

Basic Qualifications:

-5+ years of experience with statistical analysis

-Experience with working in a team–based environment

-Knowledge of key human capital principles

-Knowledge of Microsoft PowerPoint and Excel

-Ability to obtain a security clearance

-BS degree in Business Administration, Industrial or Organizational Psychology, Organization Development, or Human Resource Development

Additional Qualifications:

-Experience with delivering management consulting services to the federal government

-Knowledge of analytical or technical client environments, including data science, Cybersecurity, and predictive intelligence

-Knowledge of federal human resources policies and regulations

-Possession of excellent critical thinking skills

-Possession of excellent oral and written communication skills

-Possession of excellent leadership, analysis, and data gathering skills

-MA or MS degree in Business Administration, Industrial or Organizational Psychology, Organization Development, or Human Resource Development

Clearance:

Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information.
