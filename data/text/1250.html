Description

YOU MUST HAVE:
  • Master’s degree in Engineering, Computer Science or related scientific and technical disciplines
  • 3 years’ experience using knowledge of system physics to develop software or IoT solutions
  • 1 year data science experience applying data modeling techniques to solving customer problems, using tools such as R, Pandas, Scikit learn, Mlib or Matlab
    .
WE VALUE:
  • Ph.D in Engineering or Science
  • Coursework in signal processing, systems theory and statistics
  • Understanding of basic principles of physics governing the solutions enabled by IoT (e.g. environmental control in buildings), and those that enable IoT (embedded systems, wireless communications)
  • Knowledge of controls in industrial or building systems, first principles based modeling and simulation (e.g. thermodynamics)
  • Experience in the design and development of innovative control or data solutions in connected buildings or homes
  • Cross-disciplinary studies or experience
  • Knowledge and experience with open source software frameworks
  • Familiarity with end-to-end system architecture and testing in the full IoT system – device, connectivity, cloud platform and analytics services.
  • Understanding of technology trends and passion for keeping current evolving technology trends
  • Experience and fluency in programming or scripting language (e.g. Python, Java)
  • Experience in software development, cloud technologies, and web application development experience for quick prototyping
  • Entrepreneurial, results driven with a positive can do attitude
  • Have a passion for R&D and innovation and a flexible, creative approach to problem solving.
  • Highly team oriented
  • Being comfortable working in a dynamic, research-oriented group with several ongoing concurrent projects.
  • Keen instinct to discern the ideas likely to have the most measurable benefit to our products from the many possible ideas and work closely with others to execute them.
  • Excellent communications skills including the ability to present hypotheses and analysis results to a wide audience in a clear and concise manner.
  • Ability to persuade stakeholders and champion effective techniques during new product development.
  • Experience in turning research ideas into actionable designs.
  • A demonstrated passionate commitment to the customer and product quality.
INCLUDES
• 10% travel with the opportunity for worldwide travel
• Continuous personal development

Golden Valley
