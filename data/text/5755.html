Job Title: Senior CRM Analyst

Senior Analyst, CRM

You have a relentless intellectual curiosity. You love it when your friends and co-workers call you a data nerd. You're a manic with numbers: organizing and analyzing just about anything puts you in a true flow. You love how machine works – you may even have built one. You are fascinated about how systems and data can be used. You never stop learning. You speak SAS when others speak sports. You are passionate about analytics, CRM and Email Marketing. You thrive in a fast-paced, open-minded, energetic and diverse environment. You have a "Glass Half Awesome" attitude.

Our Story

Carnival Cruise Line was founded in 1972 with a single ship – today we have 25 ships and over 10 million passengers annually, making us the largest cruise line in the world. Right now is a great time to be a part of Carnival Cruise Line's Marketing Department! We are growing our Digital Media and CRM Analytics Function within our Marketing Department and are looking for an analytical and technically savvy Senior CRM Analyst. If you are passionate about leveraging your analytical mindset, use data to drive insights and a thorough understanding of consumer online behavior to make our CRM marketing strategy more targeted, effective, personalized and persuasive we would love to speak to you.

"We think about the customer journey from prebooking, to precruise, to during cruise, and to postcruise. It becomes our responsibility to enhance the experience and the service at, and across, each step via digital."

* Kathy Mayor, Chief Marketing Officer

Role Purpose

Our Marketing Department is being enhanced to strengthen our Analytics team and make it faster, more efficient and nimbler. We are in the process of bringing more SAS based reporting and analytics capabilities in-house which means that we are developing scripts, procedures and processes, often from scratch. The successful Senior Analyst, CRM candidate will be an integral part of this process and will have excellent opportunities for growth and development. This also means that the candidate we are looking for is a natural self-starter and must be very comfortable with dealing with ambiguity. By bringing more Analytics capabilities in-house we are creating the environment for deeper insights, root cause analysis and continuous improvement through the Marketing Department. The Senior Analyst, CRM, will support all customer marketing (retention and acquisition) programs for non-casino campaigns. This includes collaborating with key internal and external stakeholders and thought leaders within CRM Analytics as well as supporting the management of all annual strategic marketing initiatives.

Essential Functions:

* CONTACT STRATEGY: Collaborate on the definition of a detailed guest centric CRM, Digital, Email and Social contact strategy to maximize engagement and conversion (booking) for all prospects, past and booked guests. Build data visualization and performance tool required to support and analyze the 360 view of the customer across the customer journey, and marketing-related business intelligence and data science.

* DATA STRATEGY: Define and own the propensity models to optimize the campaign results for traffic and bookings, all done in collaboration with the agency. Propose and execute initiatives to increase lead generation and contact of existing guests. Become the subject matter expert in CRM contact strategy and performance review for the entire marketing and operations team (e.g. booked guests). The predictive models should include likelihood to respond, next best offer and customer lifetime value, and should allow teams to be targeted/personalized and more effective/efficient.

* BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE: Analyze email and CRM campaigns, develop real-time dashboards to manage marketing performance across campaigns and channels and provide the platform for real-time optimization. Lead the performance review meetings with all senior leadership on the marketing team, providing specific root cause analysis and improvement recommendations for email/direct mail/mobile and social. Support the execution of the recommendations

* EMAIL/CRM/DIRECT MAIL CAMPAIGNS GROW AND OPTIMIZATION: Define the right strategy to grow pool of contactable customers, including managing partnerships for data exchange /barter and its execution. Develop and execute campaigns with targeted, data-based and agile, A/B testing approach to drive incremental and profitable repeat visitation in pursuit of bookings goals. Collaborate with revenue management on offer and with social and paid media on overall contact strategy to maximize reach and response.

* BOOKED GUEST CAMPAIGNS: Grow pool of customers contactable by email. Develop and execute email campaigns to drive pre cruise onboard spend in support of operations.

* SOCIAL CRM: Support social marketing team with analytics-based insight to (a) develop machine learning-based chatbot that would support online bookings and in-between sailings social engagement, and (b) social campaigns that incentivize and reward social advocacy and in-between sailing social engagement.

Qualifications

Education: Bachelor's degree preferable in Marketing, Business, Engineering, Computer Science or similar quantitative area of study

Experience: Minimum 4 years of work experience. Experience with travel industry preferred. Experience working in a corporate environment that required planning and collaboration among cross-functional business units.

Knowledge, Skills & Abilities:

* Strong analytical skills and logic / problem solving skills

* Intellectual curiosity

* Excellent communication and presentation skills

* Ability to analyze results to help make program enhancements over time

* Passion for, and understanding of CRM Systems and Best Practices

* Preference to be a team player—versus an individual performer

* Expert on SAS / SAS CI based relational databases

* Expert Excel and DW querying skills

* Working knowledge of Oracle, SQL Server, Hyperion
