        Job Description:
        Work in the Raytheon Security SAS Counterintelligence and Investigations Organization in accordance with the requirements set forth in Company Policies and US Government Regulations and US Law.Specific duties will include, but are not limited to investigating and documenting a wide variety of incidents by working with other Raytheon investigation team members in Raytheon Security, Raytheon Legal, Raytheon Ethics and Raytheon Human Resources.Will be responsible for conducting witness and subject interviews and also properly documenting those interviews in a timely manner.Will likely interact with local, state, federal, and international law enforcement and investigative agencies regarding Raytheon related issues.A working relationship with various law enforcement agencies will also need to be established.A very close working relationship with Human Resources, Ethics, Legal, Export Control, and IT Security is expected in this job function to ensure proper case facts are investigated, documented and communicated clearly and discretely with Customers and employees at all levels within confidential need-to-know parameters.Must have the ability to exercise discretion, superior judgment and problem solving skills when dealing with extremely sensitive investigations.This position will also be responsible for handling suspicious emails and insider threat investigations SAS-wide, as well as inputting and maintaining Adverse information reports in the appropriate databases and metrics reporting.An excellent working-knowledge of computers and office applications is also needed.Work must be able to be performed without appreciable direction. Exercises considerable latitude in determining objectives and approaches to assignment.
