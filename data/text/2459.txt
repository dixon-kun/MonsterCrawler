        Senior Analysis Manager Digital Insights
        Position Summary:
        Digital Insights Senior Manager.This role will be a key contributor to Adobe's
        Digital Insights team and will involve managing analysis and working with large data sets to identify important trends in online consumer behavior. See (adobe.ly/digitalinsights)
        Responsibilities
        * We're looking for someone who is comfortable working in a fast-paced environment
        and can adapt to shifting conditions, priorities, or deadlines, is meticulous and detail oriented, and is a self-starter.
        * This person will be responsible for analyzing large sets of data to identify and
        benchmark internet trends and for developing new ways to visualize and
        communicate that data.
        * This person will work with technical resources within Adobe to develop and automate
        the processes by which additional data can be brought into Digital Index analyses.
        * This person will also be responsible to organize and improve taxonomy and metadata
        regarding websites in the database and develop processes to crowd source website
        categorization.
        * This person will also need to contribute to organizing content releases and will manage other analysts with focus on developing greater international data findings.
        Requirements
        * BS/BA in marketing, economics, information
        systems, statistics or other quantitative degree.
        * 5+ years in an analyst or data science type
        role working in a highly matrixed organization effectively
        * Experience with using data to tell stories
        * Great communication skills
        * Love for data analysis and knowledge of R, SQL,
        and predictive statistical analysis
        * Comfortable with database and scripting languages for
        accessing
        * Able to spot news-worthy trends and interesting anomalies&#8226; Motivated to dream up new technology tools to improve data insights
