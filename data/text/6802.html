ADP is looking for a Senior Director of Design & Implementation Engineering to lead Directory Services, Identity Management and Associate Platform Security within the End User Computing department.

Under the general direction of executive management, the Senior Director will work closely with key members of the Global Enterprise Technology and Solutions departments and the ADP Global Security Organization to design and implement an identity and access management program (IAM) across the enterprise. The services to be provided by the IAM program will include the following:

Global Identity Management & Active Directory standards and services System integration for identity lifecycle management and access management.

User and system account provisioning/deprovisioning

Privileged identity management Governance and operation of IAM technologies

In addition, the Senior Director will provide oversight to the associate platform security team in direct alignment with the goals and objectives of the Global Security Organization.

Key Responsibilities:

Development of the strategic global identity and access management program for all ADP associates

Partner with the Global Security Organization to establish a secure computing framework for identity management and directory services.

Chair the Identity Management and Directory Services working group and foster working relationships with the Global Security Office and Enterprise Engineering team to drive innovation and continuous improvement within the IAM program.

Define and develop an IAM reporting framework to ensure transparent communications on the programs progress.

Oversee the design and implementation engineering teams for the global IAM technology solutions.

Development of a comprehensive endpoint security framework to protect the ADP associate platforms

Assess the current associate platform security capabilities, strengths and weaknesses to define the future state information security roadmap as it pertains to associate platforms.

Oversite and management of the design and implementation engineering for the Associate Platform Security team.

Qualifications Required:

10+ years of management experience with a proven track record of hiring, coaching and retaining cross-functional technical teams

8+ years of relevant industry experience and a proven track record of expertise with Active Directory, Identity & Access Management (IAM) and Information Technology security with demonstrated progression of responsibilities.

Proven leadership experience within a global organization

Ability to set strategic and tactical departmental level goals that align with overall IT direction.
