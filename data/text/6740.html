Job Description

The Senior Data Engineer will serve as a key resource in building and executing on Chubb’s Advanced Analytics Strategy.  This role is in direct alignment with the Chubb goal of continuing to act as an Information-Driven organization through the use of advanced analytics to provide business insights.

This role directly supports the goal of becoming an Information-Driven organization by aligning analytics with business information needs.  By answering core business questions through advanced analytics techniques such as data mining, predictive modelling, pattern detection, graphical or statistical analysis, as well as by utilizing innovative solutions and capabilities for rapid delivery of business insights.

Essential Skills

·Excellent understanding of design, coding, and implementation of data systems with cloud, big data and relational technologies

·Full stack engineering experience across AWS, Azure and/or Google Cloud technologies

·Experience working with management partners and internal data center, network and storage engineers to maintain stability of Cloud environments

· Experienced in testing and validation of data systems

·Ability to quickly assimilate to new software packages (both open source and off the shelf) in the data and analytics domain

·Expert at compiling data flows and reducing/simplifying data pipelines

·Experienced in working in a project-based and agile methodology

· Experienced leading a team of geographically distributed or offshore resources

·Experienced in working with vendor partners and DevOps

·Experienced in implementing open source software in an enterprise environment

Technical Skills

·Experienced working with and design and deployment of applications on to AWS, Azure and/or Google Cloud platforms

· Experience in working in highly secure and encrypted environments in Cloud

·Experienced in parallel, distributed systems with technologies such as Hadoop, MapReduce, or Spark

·Experienced in data storage technologies such as HDFS, HBase, or Hive

·Experienced in high volume relational database technologies such as Netezza, Teradata, Oracle, Redshift, etc.

· Experienced in high-volume data systems and performance tuning concepts such as distribution, indexing, compression, clustering, or sharding

·Experienced in programming languages such as Python, SQL, Pig, Scala, Java, Clojure or Groovy

·Experienced in operating system command languages such as Bash or Ksh

·Experienced in application and systems development in a Linux environment

· Experienced in development tools such as Git version control and Integrated development environments or programmers text editor

·Familiarity with big data processing in Amazon, Azure, or other cloud platforms

·Familiarity with data science and analytic tool sets such as Python NLTK, SAS, Matlab, or Ipython Notebook/Jupyter

·Familiarity with search platforms such as Apache Solr

· Familiarity with JVM technologies such as Jboss, Jetty, Tomcat, Maven, etc

·Familiarity with web technologies and frameworks such as Node.js, .NET, Django, or Ruby on Rails

·Familiarity with application configuration both off the shelf and open source

Responsibilities

·Lead project-based teams with the aim of design, implementation, and deployment high volume data systems

· Build efficient, scalable and resilient data pipelines for the ingestion of many/all types of source data both internal and external

·Design, develop, and implement data systems ingesting data involving multiple geographies, languages, time zones, and currencies

·Ensure team members retain organization of ingested data in ingestion layers, staging, relational databases, and distributed file systems

·Act as a technical lead mentoring team members in all aspects of data system design and implementation

· Lead performance tuning for new and existing data systems

·Reason and communicate the trade-offs of various data system designs and options

·Responsible for the data delivery to consumers and systems in accordance with specified security procedures

·Works directly with and supplements the data science team through the application of techniques such as statistical analysis, predictive modeling, machine learning, and others

· Works in an agile fashion altering priorities as directed by business customers and stakeholders

·Documents systems, code, issues, wiki, project milestones/etc. in enterprise repository

·Leads code reviews and design reviews to ensure team members and vendor partners provide high quality code and systems

·Ensures solutions are in alignment with Chubb Information Strategy

· Educate the organization both from IT and the business perspectives on new approaches for extracting business value from the data

·Lead proof of concept and prototyping initiatives to identify the appropriate tools, technologies, and techniques to enable business capabilities

·Utilize proof of concept findings to support the business case for governance approval and funding

Desired experience

·Big Data Technologies (Hadoop, Spark, Map Reduce)

· Data Warehousing (modeling, design, integration, replication, processing, SQL, etc.)

·Expert programming skills (e.g. Python, Scala, JVM, Java, C/C++, .NET), as well as Hadoop MapReduce or other big data frameworks

·Understanding of the insurance industry (sales & marketing, rating, registration, reinsurance, claim handling, etc)

·Experience in business needs assessments, prioritization techniques, and business case development

· Strong communication and interpersonal skills. The ability to communicate with the business to understand requirements and present back findings and recommendations by demonstrating the potential value to the organization.

·Self-starter. The ability to come up with solutions to loosely defined business problems by leveraging pattern detection over potentially large datasets.  Innovative, with the ability to set direction and effectively obtain results from others; Ability to multi-task & adapt within a constantly changing environment.
