        Job Description
        The IT Business Partner, Business Intelligence Solutions Delivery is responsible and accountable for identifying and shaping demand for Business Intelligence and Information Management solutions across Mallinckrodt's Hospital Acute Care business unit. In this capacity, the incumbent will engage directly with commercial business leadership teams to assess needs for analytics, define the full portfolio of business intelligence and analytics technology solutions, manage the solution delivery and communicate the value realization. Working in conjunction with the IT Director, Business Intelligence &amp; Information Management, the incumbent will participate in defining the Information Management and Business Intelligence technology roadmap and will facilitate its adoption through focused awareness and change management initiatives. The incumbent will liaise and collaborate with the IS &#8211; Commercial Excellence team and other related IS service providers (such as IT Infrastructure, Digital Strategy, Security, BI Managed Services) to insure consistent delivery of IT capabilities to all business clients.
        This role will be a trusted advisor and key member of the Business Intelligence &amp; Information Management leadership team and will be a major contributor to the transformation of Mallinckrodt's strategic analytics capabilities.
        ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:
        * Serve as the business relationship/engagement manager to the business unit or function assigned to define and deliver the portfolio of data and analytics solutions and to insure alignment to business outcomes.
        * Participate in the design, implementation and evolution of the Mallinckrodt Business Intelligence &amp; Information Management services catalog and operating model.
        * Serve as a project manager to deliver on all BI and Data Warehouse related initiatives.
        * Interact with the business partners to gather requirements, prioritize work, document solutions, develop enhancements to the existing system, coordinate testing, and perform change management/training for reporting and applications.
        * Oversee and approve functional and technical specifications for reports and dashboards, using creativity and experience to design the best solution for the business.
        * Manage and govern the development of data warehouse content, operational reports, performance dashboards, and executive scorecards related to the business unit or function supported.
        * Partner with the associated business unit or function to identify and deliver key performance indicators.
        * Manage and collaborate with the Mallinckrodt Business Intelligence Managed Services offshore team to monitor daily data ingest processing, communicate and troubleshoot issues with data integrity, data design, and functional and technical software issues.
        * Manage vendor relationships and partner with external BI service providers in the support and delivery of projects and ensure adherence to all service level agreements.
        * Proactively educate business users on specifics of report usage, limitations, and contextual understanding of the data, supporting the onboarding and training of Super Users as identified.
        * Serve as an advisor to the Mallinckrodt BI and Data Governance councils as needed.
        * Serve as a mentor to junior team members to develop and mature core competencies in Business Intelligence, Advanced Analytics, Data Warehousing, and Big Data.
        MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS:
        * Bachelor's degree required (concentration in Computer Science, MIS, Information Science, Data Science or related field). Master's degree preferred.
        * 7 or more years of experience in Business Intelligence, Data Warehousing, and Analytics required, preferably within a life sciences, pharmaceuticals, or CPG industry. Delivery of at least one full lifecycle BI/DW project highly desirable.
        * 5 years of customer facing business engagement experience.
        * 5 years of project management experience.
        * Previous experience developing and delivering business intelligence solutions using mainstream and emerging analytics tools such as Qlik, Cognos, Business Objects
        PREFERRED SKILLS/QUALIFICATIONS:
        * 5 years of experience developing and delivering executive level performance dashboards and scorecards
        * 5 years of vendor management experience in the IT industry, preferably within a managed services environment.
        * Experience in managing the design, development and delivery of large enterprise data warehouses (experience with cloud-based solutions such as Amazon Web Services (AWS) is a plus)
        * Experience with delivery of reporting and analytic solutions on an enterprise BI platform (QlikView &amp; QlikSense analytics applications is preferred but not required).
        * Experience with advanced data warehousing DBMS platforms such as Amazon RedShift, SAP HANA, or similar in memory/columnar data warehouse technologies.
        * Experience with development and delivery of Data Science/statistical analysis solutions using RStudio.
        * Experience with data integration/ETL technologies including Informatica and Microsoft Sql Server Integration Services
        * Deep familiarity with relational modeling, dimensional modeling and standard data warehouse ecosystems
        * Strong vendor management skills to work with third party data providers and technology partners.
        * Strong business knowledge and mindset to provide guidance on how data can be applied to answer business questions.
        * Ability to communicate and forge lasting relationships with business leaders at all levels of the organization.
        * Proven analytical, problem solving, technical and writing skills are a must with a professional demeanor and good business acumen.
        * Excellent interpersonal and teamwork skills.
        * Self-starter and can operate independently as needed.
        RELATIONSHIP WITH OTHERS/SCOPE OF POSITION:
        This position is a senior level individual contributor role reporting to the IT Director, Business Intelligence. This position will work with business and IT leaders at all levels of the organization to shape demand for Business Intelligence solutions and to define and execute business unit specific Data &amp; Analytics strategy aligned to business outcomes.
