Job Summary

At Urban Insights we’re changing the way people travel – to work, to school, to wherever they want or need to go, and home again. Come join our team of data experts as we build innovative technologies and leverage the latest data science techniques to tackle transportation’s most pressing challenges. As a global company our work impacts the lives of millions of people around the world in cities like London, New York, Sydney, and many more. Urban Insights is the analytics center of excellence for Cubic Transportation Systems, and we are using data to optimize transit networks, reduce road congestion, improve the travel experience, and minimize each city’s carbon footprint.

You will collaborate with a diverse group of talented professionals who are as passionate about their work as you are, and are committed to delivering only the highest quality solutions. We are an agile team and this means you’re empowered to deliver the best results, have the opportunity to influence all aspects of the business, and know that each contribution you make is significant. In an industry where Cubic is a world leader, we’re driving a paradigm shift in how transportation services are planned, delivered, and consumed to better meet the needs of an increasing and ever-more concentrated urban population. Don’t take a backseat. Join Urban Insights and be instrumental in driving the future of travel.

Perks

  • A culture that respects work-life balance and individual work styles
  • Medical, dental, vision, disability and life insurance options, and 401K with company match
  • Generous tuition reimbursement program, Paid Time Off and 9/80 work schedule

Job Specifics

We are currently looking to bring another committed innovator onto our team to help deliver a highly scalable analytics solution that will serve hundreds of organizations across the globe.

  • Apply strong analytical and problem solving skills, attention to details, critical thinking ability, and creativity
  • Use data requirements to design methods and workflows applying structure to raw data, define new variables, conduct analysis, draw inferences, and produce summaries of results
  • Design algorithms and developing software to automate analytic workloads
  • Apply machine-learning and data-mining techniques to discover and characterize patterns in the development of predictive models
  • Develop customer analytics for segmentation and behavior analysis, to drive the improvement of products and customer experience
  • Design and develop solutions using Hadoop, NoSQL databases, and other distributed data management systems
  • Create innovative solutions to problems by employing a strong knowledge of statistics and broad understanding of predictive analytics tools and techniques
  • Design models, measures, scoring and classification algorithms that are suited to describing particular phenomenon

Technologies we use:

  • Greenplum
  • Various Relational Databases
  • Hadoop, MongoDB
  • R, Python
  • Tableau
  • Pentaho
  • Angular 2
  • Spring Framework
  • Cloud: Azure Services, AWS
  • Javascript

Minimum Requirements:

Bachelor’s degree in computer science, statistics, economics, mathematics, operations research, informatics, or related field plus a minimum of 5 years of experience. Master’s degree in a related discipline is a plus. Strong analytical and problem solving skills, attention to detail, critical thinking ability, and creativity required. Must have excellent written and verbal communication skills used to effectively communicate and clearly present complex information in a manner appropriate to the audience. Prior experience working in the transportation or logistics industries is a plus.

If you’re motivated to harness the power of data to improve the lives of millions of travelers and can check many of the boxes above, let's talk.
