We are currently looking to bring another committed innovator onto our team to help deliver a highly scalable analytics solution that will serve hundreds of organizations across the globe.

* Be the point person for this technical delivery team, bringing your deep understanding of IT systems and technologies, direction-setting, and providing expert guidance to developers, systems administrators, and others.

* Work closely with project managers and lead the Urban Insights technical team in the design, planning, and implementation of integrated systems that deliver high-value insights to Cubic customers

* Research emerging design patterns, methods and technologies to formulate technical recommendations that drive greater value into our systems and solutions
