Northrop Grumman is a leading global security company providing innovative systems, products and solutions in unmanned systems, cyber, C4ISR, and logistics and modernization to government and commercial customers worldwide\. Our mission is to be at the forefront of technology and innovation, deliver superior capability in tandem with maximized cost efficiencies\. The security solutions we provide help secure freedoms for our nation as well as those of our allies\.
Northrop Grumman Enterprise Services and Corporate Strategy Offices \(ES&CSO\) is seeking candidates for a Data Science intern position\. Individuals in this role are expected to have a basic familiarity with a variety of programming languages\.
The ideal candidate will possess a passion for learning, must have strong problem solving capabilities, and a keen interest in the tools and processes around big data, performance metrics, and data visualizations\. Candidates should also possess strong communications and team collaboration skills\. This role will support senior data scientists in identifying and answering questions that help us deliver the best service\.
Basic Qualifications:
+ Minimum GPA of 3\.25\.
+ Currently enrolled in an accredited college or university degree program pursing a Bachelor’s or Master’s in STEM or a quantitative field \- Analytics, Computer Science, Data Operations Research, Economics, Engineering, Management Science, Mathematics, Physics, Science, Statistics, or similar discipline\.
Preferred Qualifications:
+ Familiarity with any of the following languages: ASP\.Net, C, C\#, C , CSS, HTML, Java, JavaScript, jQuery, JSP, OQL, PowerShell, Python, Servlets, SQL, VB\.NET, VB6, VBScript, XML, XPath, XQuery, XSLT\.
+ Excellent critical thinking, analytical thinking, and problem solving skills\.
+ Strong attention to detail\.
+ Strong orientation to quantitative analysis within stats\-based tools, such as R, Python, SPSS, SAS, MATLAB, etc\.
+ Ability to code in SQL, R, & Python\.
+ Understanding of Oracle and SQL Server Relational databases\.
+ Familiarity with the Hortonworks Big Data Platform\.
+ Familiarity with Machine Learning Algorithms, such as Boosting, Decision Trees, Random Forests, Bayes Classifiers, Ensembles, etc\.
+ Familiarity of Natural Language Processing and Natural Language Generation techniques\.
