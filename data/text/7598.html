Service Designer

Key Role:

Leverage experience with communicating and envisioning service concepts across a variety of platforms on client–facing projects. Maintain responsibility for the delivery of projects in industries ranging from healthcare, finance, and a variety of public sector clients. Create journey maps to identify points of improvement or designing a system to measure client experience. Comprehend the generative and evaluative user research to easily weave multiple stakeholders into the project plan. Define project scope and work with the program manager to proactively respond to rapidly changing environments. Apply a mix of left and right–brain thinking to guide the production of high–quality deliverables, including progress reports, journey maps, service blueprints, and interactive prototypes. Leverage expertise from the Booz Allen digital team, including data scientists, UX/UI designers, and illustrators to enable the team to look at the problem from different points of view, translating human–centered insights into solutions to better peoples’ lives.

Basic Qualifications:

-3+ years of experience with user experience, service design, design thinking, design research, business design, or client experience

-3+ years of experience in working with digital technologies, including mobile, Web, and applications and prototyping tools

-Experience with client negotiation and setting expectations against real world limitations

-Ability to be inspiring, open–minded, hardworking, collaborative, and thoughtful

-Ability to obtain a security clearance

-BA or BS degree

Additional Qualifications:

-Experience in consulting, facilitation, or training environments a plus

-Experience with communicating visually using tools, including AutoCAD, Adobe InDesign, Photoshop, or Illustrator a plus

-Experience with business modeling, research and analytics, and development a plus

-BA or BS degree in Design, Anthropology, Sociology, Business, or a related field preferred

Clearance:

Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information.
