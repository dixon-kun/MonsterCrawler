Hipmunk, part of Concur, is a small, passionate, and diverse team making travel planning simple and delightful for millions of people. We have lots of data and lots of challenging problems to solve in our business, from complicated industry dynamics to changing customer behavior. We have a lot of fun, too: happy hours, tech talks, offsites, and more. We share a beautiful office with another Concur brand, TripIt, in San Francisco.

Concur, part of SAP, imagines the way the world should work, offering cloud-based services that make it simple to manage travel and expenses. By connecting data, applications and people, Concur delivers an effortless experience and total transparency into spend wherever and whenever it happens. Concur services adapt to individual employee preferences and scale to meet the needs of companies from small to large, so they can focus on what matters most. The company serves more than 30,000 clients representing more than 27 million users in over 150 countries.

We hire Data Science Interns that are passionate about solving Big Data problems and impacting millions of end-users. We recognize that Intern contributions fuel some of our greatest innovations. That’s why we assign students to our toughest challenges and most forward-thinking projects. This summer, we are looking for an intern to:

  • Develop machine learning models to predict user characteristics based on their site usage and search criteria, so we can better personalize their site experience.
  • Identify patterns in site usage and search criteria that indicate which users are the most (and least) likely to book their travel from Hipmunk.

Our continued growth is dependent upon talented people with a variety of skills, knowledge and backgrounds. We’re looking for a Data Science intern with experience in the following areas:

  • Experience building supervised machine learning models
  • Conceptual understanding of what causes ML models to make good (or poor) predictions
  • Experience with Python
  • Experience with statistics, including a solid understanding of statistical significance
  • An intellectual curiosity that drives you to not only figure out the problem at-hand, but to dig deeper to discover new insights

Value Competencies

  • Displays passion for & responsibility to the customer
  • Displays leadership through innovation in everything you do
  • Displays a passion for what you do and a drive to improve
  • Displays a relentless commitment to win
  • Displays personal & corporate integrity
