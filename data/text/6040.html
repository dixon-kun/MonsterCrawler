SUMMARY

Hulu’s Data Science team is seeking a Data Analyst who will be an exceptional addition to our Data Science team.  As a Data Analyst at Hulu, you will be responsible for providing support and analysis for business teams who will be using your data insights to make business decisions.  The right person for this role thrives in a fast-pace environment, loves problem-solving and is a strong communicator.  If you are someone who gains personal satisfaction when transforming data into knowledge for business insights, then this is a great role for you.


WHAT YOU’LL DO

  • Apply machine learning, statistics or data mining to translate ad-hoc business questions into data analyses with meaningful business insights. Determine which requests can/should be automated into a long-term data science tool.
  • Be a strong representative of both data science and business teams – understanding what is feasible within data science resources while understanding the need and urgency for data analysis in business. Fully communicate deliverables/tools and how to use/interpret findings.
  • Collect requirements for long-term data science solutions that support business decisions or provide recommendations for business decisions.
  • Create insightful presentations that summarize analysis and present them to groups of business team members and executives to influence decision-making.
  • Align business units by finding and communicating data-driven insights that are generalizable.
  • Be a thought leader in new types of analytics we can do on behalf of our stakeholders.

WHAT TO BRING

  • Bachelor’s degree in Statistics or related quantitative field (e.g. Computer Science, Physics, Mathematics, Operations Research).
  • Mastery of SQL and/or Hive with 3+ years related experience
  • Familiar with at least one statistical programming language (e.g. R, Python)
  • Experience working at Media/Digital Streaming companies
  • Exceptional interpersonal and communication skills, including the ability to convey insights effectively in high-level forums
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced environment, executing high quality deliverables that help inform business decisions

NICE-TO-HAVES

  • Experience in Machine Learning, Optimization, Data Visualization & Communication
  • Master’s degree (MS) in Statistics or related quantitative field (e.g. Computer Science, Econometrics, Mathematics, Physics, Operations Research)
  • Demonstrated self-direction
  • Real world analytical solution building experience
