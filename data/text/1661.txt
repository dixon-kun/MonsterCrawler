        Job Summary:
        The SG Universe team is looking for an insightful, driven Analytics Engineer to join our growing family to focus specifically on cultivating tools to aid in the evaluation of product and marketing performance for our Play4Fun Network social casino game.
        Responsible for maintaining existing analytics tools using R and Python, constructing similar tools for future development, and performing data analysis for fast-paced social and mobile applications. This will include requirements gathering, code instrumentation, creating and analyzing reports, and providing insights to optimize high volume web and native mobile applications. Through this work, you will help make recommendations to optimize product engagement.
        As part of the Business Intelligence arm of the SG Universe Play4Fun team, the selected candidate will be integral in providing terrestrial casinos the capabilities they need to provide their players fantastic casino experiences on web and mobile devices.
        Essential Job Functions:
        * Maintain existing data analytics systems constructed with R and Python
        * Assist in the design of custom analytics reports and database queries
        * Collaborate on and lead the construction of new tools to handle future analytics needs
        * Identify trends in user behavior using integrated analytics systems
        * Coordinate with other team members to provide recommendations for changes and improvements to increase player retention and monetization
        * Provide ad hoc Customized Reports and Analysis to Project Lead
        * Assist in the implementation of additional Analytics API calls for custom in-game events in the codebase
        Position Requirements
        Qualifications:

        * Experience performing statistical analysis, particularly predictive analysis
        * Understanding of database models
        * MS Excel and other statistical reporting tools
