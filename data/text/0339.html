Company Description

The only place to preorder designer collections straight from the runway, Moda Operandi has revolutionized luxury retail with a high-tech meets high-touch shopping experience. This distinctive concept is complemented by an expertly curated in-season selection that offers a unique point of view on fashion. Moda Operandi's unrivaled customer care anticipates the needs of its sophisticated clientele worldwide. Through engaging editorial content, spearheaded by the industry's leading talent, M'O defines what customers want to buy both this season and next.

Job Description

Position Summary

The Director of Marketing Analytics is responsible for defining, building, and managing the analytics capability and reporting processes. This role will work with a broad group of cross-functional teams to be a thought-leader for the company, shape Moda Operandi’s analytics capabilities, as well as support the optimization of all customer interactions across the organization. This position is also responsible for setting the analytic agenda, designing best practices, and applying innovative techniques and tools to create a competitive advantage for Moda Operandi.

Role Priorities

  • Primarily responsible for developing KPI’s and reporting structure around all customer interactions, ranging from awareness, through to acquisition & retention
  • Drive optimization of marketing channels through test & learn programs to achieve company goals
  • Develop key insights into customer purchasing behavior, using analytical frameworks and methods to identify opportunities for LTV growth
  • Responsible for analysing and reporting on key business metrics around Marketing efficiency, in addition to maintaining a forward-looking projection based on current trends

Performance Requirements

  • Understands the dynamics that are globally shaping both the fashion and omnichannel landscapes
  • Leads the data synthesis process to assess risk, address issues, and identify costs and opportunities
  • Translates attributes into key priorities with the greatest impact on performance
  • Increases client acquisition, retention, and enhance the experience at every opportunity
  • Demonstrates exceptional strategic agility and hands-on ownership of action based project plans
  • Communicates analytic direction, marshals alignment to vision and strategy, and applies specific timelines for achievement
  • Facilitates processes/programs cross-functionally and serves as a strategic business partner across the organization
  • Effectively influences and maintains collaborative partnerships
  • Leads, develops, and motivates teams in the development of comprehensive project plans and throughout execution
  • Sets culture of strategic and innovative thinking by teaching and mentoring associates to see the big picture
  • Ensures the highest standards for the Moda Operandi brand and client experience
  • Possesses excellent presentation and communication skills (verbal and written)

Qualifications

Essential Skills, Experience and Educational Requirements

Minimum Experience:

  • 5 years of directly related experience in large-scale data analysis
    • 3 years of experience leading an analytics team within a global ecommerce on omnichannel environment

Industry Experience:

  • Direct ecommerce/omnichannel experience

Specific Skill Requirements:

  • Ability to extract, transform and clean data sets from multiple sources
  • Experience designing and analyzing experiments to measure the impact of marketing campaigns. This includes sampling techniques and testing for statistical significance
  • Advanced knowledge of SQL
  • Strong working knowledge of Google Analytics
  • Strong working knowledge of Tableau
  • Strong understanding of website technologies, including site tagging, optimization & personalization

Educational Requirements:

  • MBA, or other advanced quantitative degree in statistics, data science, or economics

Required Competencies

Impact – the ability to:

  • Drive change and tangible business impact, in a matrix organization, even under challenging circumstances
  • Develop strategies and translate them into business plans that create measurable business results and outcomes

Communication and Influencing

  • Communicates effectively and persuasively, with broad ranges of stakeholders--including investors, senior executives & staff
  • Ability to gain the respect, collaboration and support of cross-functional team-members without line-authority
  • Team-player, with strong communication, motivational, and diplomatic skills
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills; ability to effectively interact with all levels, clients, and customers

Analytical Ability

  • Demonstrated ability to dig deep and deliver causal insights
  • Selects from a range of analytic methods to design and conducts analysis appropriate to each project
  • Self-motivated and passionate about creating analytical solutions for diverse and challenging problems

· Strong working knowledge of visualization tools such as Tableau and SAS Visual Analytics

· Demonstrated ability to integrate multiple data sources (including sales data, customer data, primary and secondary research, social media) into clear actionable insights and business strategies

· Demonstrated ability in creating customer segmentation frameworks

Strategic & Innovative Thinking

  • Thinks strategically; quickly discerns patterns and trends, and understands their implications
  • Creative thinker who proactively seeks new and innovative approaches

Project Leadership

  • Independently and simultaneously manages several complex, high-profile projects under tight timelines
