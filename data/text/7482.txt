         JOB DESCRIPTION:
         The Leidos Global Services Group (GSG) is seeking a Senior Data Scientist to support our National Security customer in Springfield, VA. The successful candidate will work as a member of a government-contractor team providing data research and analysis to solve complex intelligence problems.
         The Senior Data Scientist will determine and design the most applicable research projects, geospatial data collection and methodologies; generate and prepare thorough, specific tactical and/or strategic data science products (e.g. technical articles, visualizations, graphics, and intelligence reports) to encapsulate the methods and results of data science solutions.&#160; The successful candidate will identify, retrieve, manipulate, and/or exploit multiple structured data sets from a wide range of geospatial and related sources; identify geospatial and related problems where data science can be applied and initiate appropriate solutions; interpret and evaluate results of data science methods, models and/or algorithms to better understand the meaning, limitations and scope of the results; summarize and convey precise output for data science and/or missions and identify additional applications for use.&#160; Initiate the efficient implementation of methods, tools, and algorithms, including preliminary data exploration, visualization and preparation, model calibration, specification or estimation: and algorithm validation and verification using a comprehensive range of technologies (e.g. software packages, programming languages, computational environments).
         Qualifications:
         TYPICAL EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE:
           11 years or more of relevant experience in the Intelligence Community and/or DoD.
           BA/BS degree in a related field (or equivalent experience).
           Highly proficient designing and conducting data science research, geospatial data collection and methodologies and algorithms.
           Identify, retrieve, manipulate, relate and/or exploit multiple structured data sets from various sources.
           Highly knowledgeable and experienced with geospatial products.
           Highly experienced using intelligence community software.
           Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite.
           Proactively lead colleagues and customer(s) in prioritizing and solving complex data science challenges and problems.
           Utilize strong communication skills to create and present briefing and presentations.
           Ability to work in a very dynamic and demanding work environment.
           Work cooperatively as a team member and provide guidance to analysts to build effective customer relationships.
