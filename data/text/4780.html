Judge Group Job Order ID: 519892

We seek an experienced Senior Data Engineer to join our team and enhance our ability to create, deploy and operate a world-class data engineering system. You will be responsible for developing solutions for an end-to-end ""pipeline"" that turns data from our SaaS Application Security Platform into actionable information for our internal and external customers. You will be part of a team consisting of highly skilled data engineers, analysts, and BI developers supporting our ambitious data goals.

Duties and Responsibilities:

• Build, enhance, and operate an Internet-scale data lake, analytical data store and embedded BI applications

• Support existing and new BI production solutions

• Ability to translate customer needs into clear and concise analytics that drive value

• Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Information Science or Engineering. Master's degree preferred.• 5 - 10 years of experience with data warehouse systems, data modeling, ETL and BI design and development

• Experience with MPP and columnar database like AWS Redshift a plus

• Experience with SQL (Oracle, Aurora, or MySQL) and NoSQL (DynamoDB)

. Experience with database design; query tuning and optimization preferred.

• Hands on experience with multi-tenant and embedded analytics approaches

• Experience with the following BI solutions is preferred: Looker, Tableau and Birst

• Experience with Big Data methods, topologies and tools, such as Map/Reduce (e.g. AWS EMR), Apache Spark, etc., preferred

Ability to thrive in a fast paced and agile engineering environment

** Due to the nature of this project, US Citizenship is required**

Company Description:

Working at the crossroads of people and transformative technologies, The Judge Group delivers innovative business solutions – powered by top talent – to help organizations reach their strategic goals and realize opportunities now and in the future.

The Judge Group is a leading professional services firm specializing in technology, talent, and learning solutions. Our services are successfully delivered through a network of more than 35 offices in the United States, Canada, China, and India.
