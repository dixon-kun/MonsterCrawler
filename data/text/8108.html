Description:

Sotera Defense Solutions, recognized as a Top 50 Government Technology Contractor by the Washington Business Journal, has an immediate funded opportunity for a Cyber Operations Specialist / Splunk Administrator to join our prime contract in San Antonio, TX

Candidate will be performing transition work of newly developed analytics to an existing SOC framework.  Must know the customer and be able to integrate effectively in the customer workspace.  Successful candidate will be able to actively work with teams remotely and be able to travel, 25%.

Candidate will work with various network and host-based security applications and tools, such as network and host assessment/scanning tools, network and host based intrusion detection systems, and other security software packages.

Requirements:

Active Top Secret clearance with SCI Eligibility

Must have at least 5 years of security operations center (SOC) experience to include the following:

  • Log analytics, hunting, and incident response;
  • Splunk
  • Some programming experience with C, C++, C#, Python, HTML, JavaScript, .NET
  • IDS monitoring and analysis, analyze network traffic, log analysis, prioritize and differentiate between potential intrusion attempts and false alarms
  • Experience on a Computer Incident Response Team (CIRT), Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT)

Preferred:

  • Ability to work with data Scientists on brand new analytics and implementation of these analytics into an existing framework to enhance existing SOC operations.
  • Host-based forensics and malware analysis experience.  Candidate must possess excellent written communication skills and the proven ability to present complex, technical information to both technical and nontechnical audiences.
  • The candidate must stay up to date with current vulnerabilities, attacks, and countermeasures. Engages with other internal and external parties to get and share information to improve processes and security posture

About The Organization:

Sotera Defense Solutions, Inc. (Sotera) is an agile, mid-size national security technology company that delivers innovative systems, solutions and services in support of the critical missions of the Intelligence Community, Department of Defense, Department of Homeland Security and federal law enforcement agencies charged with ensuring the safety and security of our nation. Our 1,200 employees are focused on delivering essential counterterrorism, cyber operations, intelligence, data analytics, C4ISR and force mobility solutions to our customers throughout the national security community.
