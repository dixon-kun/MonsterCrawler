        CVP is seeking a highly talented Data BI Specialist to engage in full Agile lifecycle software development, including requirements analysis, design, development, testing and maintenance of CVP's Quality Compass business intelligence platform. As a thought leader at CVP, the Data BI Specialist will make a significant impact on a variety of projects across the firm.
        The Data BI Specialist position is located at our Columbia, Maryland office.
        Major Areas of Responsibility
        -    Perform business requirement analysis and resulting solution design
        -    Design and develop database objects
        -    Support database administration Microsoft SQL Server, MySQL
        -    Develop reports, dashboard, and visualizations within business intelligence tools
        -    Perform unit testing, integration testing, system testing, and regression testing
        -    Document user guides
        Requirements
        -    3-5 years of progressive experience in at least two of the following BI Tools: SAP Business Objects, MicroStrategy, Tableau, Power BI, QlikView or similar
        -    Data analysis experience utilizing business intelligence tools (both open source and premium)
        -    Familiarity with metadata layer design principles
        -    Expert level skills with SQL
        -    Exposure to Python and/or R for data science
        -    Strong ability to provide thought leadership as a subject matter expert and highly relied upon client partner
        -    Expert analytical skills; excellent communication, including writing and presentation skills
        -    Ability to triage, troubleshoot and resolve multiple issues concurrently
        -    Bachelor's Degree or 5-7 years of professional experience in Data Analytics, Reporting and Data Visualization
