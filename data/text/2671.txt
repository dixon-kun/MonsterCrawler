          Data Visualization Designer(30825)
            POSITION SUMMARY:
           The position will involve development and deployment of novel visualizations as well as overall data product design with our team of data science and healthcare experts. Successful candidates will blend technical skills with creative vision.
            ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
           1.Develop and deploy data visualizations
            a.Create charts, graphs, infographics, dashboards, etc. to support knowledge acquisition and decision making throughout the UVA Health System
            b.Deploy visualizations using a variety of technologies suitable to the end users (e.g. web, mobile, print)
            c.Create interactive visualization applications to allow users to ask and answer questions.
           2.Distill qualitative and quantitative insight to communicate compelling stories
            a.Use visualization to help determine the next best action
            b.Develop materials to present to the Analytics team, clinical staff, and strategic planners
            c.Visually engage end-users with tools to facilitate their day-to-day work
           3.Be an active member of the data product design team
            a.Engage in user-centered design
            b.Collaborate with data science and reporting teams
            c.Creatively solve problems to drive better operations of the Health System
           4.Interact with and understand data customers in the Health System
            a.Discover needs of patients, health care providers and administration
            b.Iterate with them on developing a data-driven experience that meets their needs
            MINIMUM REQUIREMENTS:
            Requires a Master's Degree or foreign equivalent in mathematics, statistics, engineering, computer science and one (1) year of experience in a data mining and or analytics related field. Or, Bachelor's degree in mathematics, statistics, engineering, computer science or related field with three (3) years of experience in a data mining and or analytics related field.
            Other Requirements: Requires proficiency with the following programming languages, web technologies, databases and tools (may be acquired through coursework or employment experience): 1. Data visualization, data mining and/or analytics. 2. JavaScript and JavaScript libraries including as extJS, jQuery, AngularJS. 3. Development of interactive dashboards or other visualizations. 4. HTML5 and CSS. 5. D3
            License/Certification: None required.
