Altamira has an upcoming need for a Security Officer. The Security Officer's role:

* Provides personnel and document security support to CITP.

* Works closely with the NGIC S2 and Special Security Officer (SSO) to manage CITP Contractor clearances and access.

* In-processes and out-processes CITP personnel and arranges security in-briefs and debriefs for appropriate levels of access, including SI, TK, Gamma, HUMINT Control System (HCS), NATO, and programs requiring access information controlled under Alternative or Compensatory Control Measures (ACCM), or Special Category (SPECAT) programs.

* Ensures Contractor personnel are properly maintained in the Joint Personnel Adjudication System (JPAS), and as necessary in the Army Clearance Access Verification System (ACAVS).

* Monitor Contractor compliance with annual security training requirements including Intelligence Oversight and other NGIC, INSCOM, DA, DoD and Theater security requirements

This position shall be considered key personnel.

Role Requirements:

* This position requires individuals with 5 years of personnel and document security experience, or equivalent amount of experience as a Special Security Officer.

* Be experienced with JWICS , SIPRNET and NIPRNET tools, including JPAS and ACAVS and databases used to support security activities.

* Have a working knowledge of Microsoft® Office® tools, including SharePoint®.

* A high school diploma or GED is required, Bachelor's Degree preferred.

* This position requires current TS/SCI clearance.
