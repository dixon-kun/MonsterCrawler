As the world's leading Software Company, Microsoft has pioneered many of the technology innovations that paved the way for the digital information revolution. With products ranging from the Windows operating systems to the Office productivity suite, the Visual Studio suite of developer's tools, Xbox, and a broad range of new offerings in high-growth areas including Web-based services, unified communications, search, advertising and much more, Microsoft continues to be the worldwide leader in helping people and businesses realize their full potential. Microsoft's Universal Store BizOps team is looking for a data scientist who is passionate about online commerce and is looking for a unique opportunity to contribute enabling ecommerce capabilities for all of Microsoft's consumer and business online services.

We are currently looking for a high-caliber individual to join our team and to help create the most sophisticated analytic solutions. &nbsp The data scientist would analyze, optimize and implement models for a diverse range of problems in the Universal Store. We are looking for a customer-focused, detail-oriented, results-driven analytic professional with great communication skills and a strong knowledge of predictive modeling and statistical techniques in the payments and fraud prevention space.

Responsibilities:

1. &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Understand complex payment and risk mitigation work flow in an ecommerce transaction system.

2. &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Manipulate large volumes of data, create new and improved techniques and/or solutions for data collection, management and usage.

3. &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Use advanced statistical methods to unravel deep insights from large amounts of transaction and usage data.

4. &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Build predictive models and conduct experiments to gain insights into the business performance.

5. &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Identify key metrics to follow that determine the health of systems.

6. &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Closely work with business owners and engineering teams to understand customer pain points and translate them to tools and/or features. &nbsp

7. &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Visualize and report on key findings. &nbsp

Minimum Requirements: &nbsp

1. &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Graduate degree in Statistics, Operations Research, Machine Learning or other related fields. OR Minimum of 3 years related work experience.

Preferred Qualifications:

1. &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Proficiency in statistical, predictive modeling, machine learning with big data using tools like R, Python.

2. &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Experience in database record manipulation (mainly SQL) and big data solutions.

3. &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Proven ability and experience in using data science, statistical computing, and modeling to improve business KPIs.

4. &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Strong understanding of ecommerce and payments systems. &nbsp

5. &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Ability to be flexible enough to jump in and act as a team-member, taking immediate ownership of unanticipated scenarios, and responding to ad hoc requests requiring database skill to solve. To that end, the candidate must be comfortable writing SQL queries, stored procedures and/or trigger jobs. &nbsp

6. &nbsp&nbsp&nbsp&nbsp Additionally, the candidate must demonstrate high energy/creativity, a passion for analyzing highly complex data sets, strong communication and project management skills, strong debugging skills, an entrepreneurial spirit, a relentless customer-focus, a practical understanding of quantitative methods, ability to collaborate well with others, and superb attention to detail. &nbsp
