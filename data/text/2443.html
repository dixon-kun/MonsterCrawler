We are looking for a Business Intelligence Analyst to join our Koch Audit and Advisory team. This position will provide support across Koch Audit and Advisory and Compliance and Ethics.

Attributes of a successful candidate:

  • Technical Skills: use programs or software to mine data sources and look for trends and insight

  • Analytical Skills: attention to detail and able to determine what data trends mean including being able to translate data into knowledge

  • Communication Skills: able to communicate insights to auditors or management

  • Problem-Solving: demonstrated problem solving skills and critical thinking skills

  • Time Management: work on projects independently or on teams and manage multiple deadlines


A Day In The Life Typically Includes:


  • Proactively identifying and improving data management, governance, and analysis, where it can improve audit effectiveness and/or reduce costs
  • Leveraging audit findings and events in Enablon to identify risks and trends that may help scope and plan audits more effectively
  • Internalizing learnings or insights from historical data multiple audit groups share
  • Developing and maintain key management reporting and metrics including ad-hoc reporting and research projects
  • Helping to facilitate peer to peer reviews on audit engagements
  • Reviewing expense trends for multiple cost centers to identify trends. Identify and remediate outliers and errors. Improved projection accuracy.
  • Coordinate with multiple cost center owners to perform project billings and submit for processing
  • Managing and maintain audit plans and SharePoint site for audit and advisory activity
  • Monitoring emerging technology that could benefit the team

What You Will Need:


Basic Qualifications:

  • Bachelors’ degree in Business, Statistics, Information Systems OR a Bachelor’s degree with 1 years’ experience as a Data Analyst, Business Analyst or comparable analyst position handling large, complex data
  • Advanced Microsoft® Excel skills, such as Visual Basic / macros / advanced formula
  • Not eligible for Visa Sponsorship


What Will Put You Ahead?


Preferred Qualifications:

  • Experience administering SharePoint 2013 sites, including permissions
  • Experience working with data and analysis
  • Data science degree or experience
  • 1 years of data analysis and/or process improvement experience

Want to learn more about KII?

Based in Wichita, Kan., Koch Industries, Inc. is one of the largest private companies in America with estimated annual revenues as high as $115 billion, according to Forbes. It owns a diverse group of companies involved in refining, chemicals, grain processing and biofuels; forest and consumer products; fertilizers; polymers and fibers; process and pollution control equipment and electronic components; commodity trading; minerals; energy; ranching; glass; and investments. Since 2003, Koch companies have invested approximately $65 billion in acquisitions and other capital expenditures. With a presence in about 60 countries, Koch companies employ more than 100,000 people worldwide, with about 60,000 of those in the United States. From January 2009 to present, Koch companies have earned more than 930 awards for safety, environmental excellence, community stewardship, innovation, and customer service. For more news and stories, visit www.kochnews.com.

The businesses' popular consumer brands include Brawny® paper towels, Quilted Northern® and Angel Soft® bath tissue, Dixie® tabletop products, LYCRA® fiber, STAINMASTER® carpet and more.
