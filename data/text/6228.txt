          Consulting Director - Technical Solutions - St Louis, MO-171841
            Description
          The Consulting Director leads the consulting activities for targeted accounts/customers. He or she works as a member of multiple (typically 3-4) account teams. As a key part of the account team, the Consulting Director works to identify and pursue opportunities for Teradata&#8217;s solutions and services with the goal of providing value and high-impact business outcomes for the customer. To support this objective, the Consulting Director must maintain a strong awareness of Teradata&#8217;s solutions and service and their value proposition, and also have relationships with the internal Teradata teams who own, advocate for, and implement these solutions.
          The Consulting Director is responsible for building and maintaining a growing set of contacts/relationships within their assigned customer accounts, within both the Business and IT areas of the customer. These activities, as well as the opportunity pursuits, will be accomplished as a part of an overall account strategy and closely coordinated with, and often partnered with, the Account Executive.
          In addition to business development and relationship development responsibilities, the Consulting Director has overall responsibility for the successful delivery of services engagements within their accounts, both from the perspective of client satisfaction, and as it relates to the successful financial management of the engagement. Additional responsibilities within this area include leading the scoping, pricing, contracting of services, staffing, and collaborating with Teradata Centers of Expertise and product teams within Teradata. This position will report to the Managing Consulting Director within the region.
          Key Responsibilities:
          &#8226; Creates demand for consulting services in new areas of client organization through business development efforts, focused on business outcomes and realized value for the client from their investments in Teradata&#8217;s platform solutions.
          &#8226; Maintains aware of Teradata&#8217;s various non-platform, solution and service offerings including key aspects of opportunity identification/qualification and value proposition, as well as typical titles or department areas who would be most likely to benefit from and/or purchase the offering.
          &#8226; Works closely with the Teradata Centers of Expertise or product groups that own these solutions.
          &#8226; Brings deep industry and/or functional expertise to the account team in order to sell Teradata consulting services
          &#8226; Understands client business problems and develops POVs which articulate problems along with Teradata solutions
          &#8226; Builds relationships in new parts of the client business organization to generate demand for solutions in new areas
          &#8226; Identifies opportunities which would require other specialized expertise and coordinates with internal resources to bring experts in as needed.
          &#8226; Coaches and develops Project Managers and Consultants
          &#8226; Manages process of scoping, planning, costing, and pricing new projects
          &#8226; Develops Statements of Work for new projects; defines goals and objectives, client requirements, resources needed, budget, risks, and deliverables
          &#8226; Serves on consulting engagements as a thought partner and subject matter expert for the consulting delivery team and as a key relationship map for project sponsors
          &#8226; Engages with other account teams and consulting leadership as needed to use expertise to help sell solutions to other clients
          Skills &amp; Attributes:
          &#8226; Robust history of success in consultative selling, particularly in services to business buyers
          &#8226; Strong functional/technical expertise in Data and Analytics
          &#8226; Industry-specific knowledge
          &#8226; Customer relationship management experience
