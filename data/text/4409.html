About the Opportunity

As an Applied Analytics Sales Executive at Pitney Bowes, you thrive on impacting business results and you deliver with precision. You see opportunities where others see challenges.

Build. Deploy. Bridge. Grow.
In this role, you’ll join our Americas Services Sales team covering the Northeast region of the U.S. and report to the Director of Services Sales for Software. You will be responsible for
  • Closing Applied Analytic sales opportunities
  • Developing relationships with our data scientists and consulting teams to identify solution opportunities
  • Establishing the best possible long-term relationship with each prospect and client so as to maximize future revenue opportunities. Included are meaningful business relationships with "C" level executives.
  • Building and maintaining an active pipeline of qualified leads through selective cold calling, attending industry trade shows, providing solution demonstrations, working leads from direct mail campaigns and other marketing campaigns
  • Managing the sales process including Bids, RFPs, MSAs and SOWs.
  • Obtaining the maximum revenue from each account and sales opportunity through recognition of broader needs that PB Software can address and through the strongest possible adherence to approved pricing

Qualifications
  • Minimum of 5 years’ experience selling location intelligence analytics or data-driven consulting services in a matrixed sales organization
  • Demonstrated experience selling solutions that meet client business objectives
  • Clear understanding of creating demand and delivering high quality effective customer engagement with full life cycle customer experience
  • Possess an in-depth understanding of technology solutions, software, and analytics as well as knowledge of applying domain expertise on behalf of corporate clients
  • Excellent communicator who practices direct, candid and open dialogue
The Team
Our Digital Commerce team powers global commerce, processing billions of transactions every year. Our technology helps businesses operate faster and more efficiently. How? By identifying trusted data and insights to create targeted, effective messaging, by providing accurate geographic data to make smarter business decisions, and by delivering relevant and engaging interactions across the customer lifecycle to drive increased satisfaction.
