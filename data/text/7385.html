Position: Data Engineer
Location: Arlington, VA
Employment Type: Contact to Hire

Position Overview:
The Data Engineer will be someone who has an appreciation for the data diversity in scientific analysis, stays on the cutting edge of analysis methods and new data science approaches in addition to their existing database skills. The Data Engineer will be part of a dynamic, accomplished informatics team that will support multiple areas in experimentation, discovery and development of innovative products and services. In this position, youâ€™ll engage with large amounts of information every day and spot trends as well as uncover unseen correlations via data mining expertise. As a member of the EIM team you will apply a diverse set of technical skills to assist ARC with making data driven decisions through the use of advanced analytical techniques and rigorous statistical approaches.

Job Duties:
  • Identify and create new derivatives and/or aggregations of data to be used across multiple ARC products and markets
  • Processing complex, large scale data sets used for modeling, data mining, and research
  • Be a subject matter expert on ARC data and related industry data sources including common uses, scope and anomalies in the data.
  • Create advanced models in support of advanced analytical applications for customers and products
  • Provide consulting and investigative expertise to the product team, account managers, and the EIM team on data problems or questions about the scope or potential uses of data
  • Aid decision making by building easy to deploy tools that present both insightful data summaries and actionable modeling results
  • Design and implement statistical data quality procedures for new data sources
  • Perform statistical analyses in tools such as SAS, Tableau, or R
  • Visualize and report data findings creatively using best practice visualization techniques to provide insights to the organization
  • Responsible for promoting ARC data expertise and socializing new and creative derivatives and aggregations of data within ARC as a basis for new product concepts and more efficient use of ARC data resources.

Requirements
  • Experience with advanced statistical modeling techniques, tools, and best practices, Tableau, Cognos, Teradata SQL, SAS and/or R experience is preferred
  • Statistical or Numerical analysis, working with large volumes of data, creating reports and performing data-based research
  • Execute qualitative and quantitative assessments of data and communicate to multiple levels effectively.Experience with relational databases and SQL
  • Ability to correlate and compare non-ARC data sources to ARC data
  • Ability to provide expert level consulting to all [technical] levels of internal and external customers
  • Good communication ability to translate technical information and statistical models into business terms used by Product, Account Management, and IS teams within ARC, at all levels.
  • General knowledge of airline distribution, ticketing, and settlement data and processes
  • Understanding of business rules associated with data sourcing, usage, or creation
  • Ability to independently determine potential customer uses of new derived and aggregated data items and views
  • Ability to craft hypotheses and experiments to determine both statistical and business value of data derivatives
  • Ability to determine exceptions in data that should be excluded from analysis or explained as outliers
  • Able to build constructive and effective cross functional relationships in support of job function
