Algorithm Developer, Senior

Key Role:

Solve the latest problems with our Cyber Applied Research Team focusing in the application of the latest scientific research and development to our clients Cyber challenges. Apply your expertise as one of our research engineers and algorithm developers in a highly technical team. Develop advanced algorithms for the situational assessment, classification, information fusion, and automated exploitations of the heterogeneous information gathered from the Cyberspace domain.

Basic Qualifications:

-6+ years of experience with creating new data association methods to confirm known correlations between different data streams

-6+ years of experience with analyzing structured and unstructured data streams and developing models for situational assessment and data fusion

-5+ years of experience with data mining, data science, and programming and working with computational algorithms

-5+ years of experience with information retrieval, including organizing and structuring data

-5+ years of experience with software engineering and systems design and analysis

-Knowledge of evidence foundational skills in the Cyber domain and how to document results of research activities and write technical reports

-Ability to review existing algorithms and assess their applicability to sponsor tasks

-Ability to perform statistical analysis

-TS/SCI clearance with a polygraph

-BA or BS degree

Additional Qualifications:

-Experience with MATLAB

-Experience in research with developing algorithms for protocol analysis or correlating diverse sensor data with RF or protocol data

-Knowledge of Object Oriented Programming (OOP), C++, or Java

-Ability to build algorithms, mathematical models, or similar applications

-Ability to be a creative thinker with a track record of generating innovative ideas

Clearance:

Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information; TS/SCI clearance with polygraph is required.
