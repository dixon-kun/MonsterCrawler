        General Description
        In today's fast evolving technology world, one aspect remains common &#8211; reliance on data to drive the next wave of innovation. Strategic Analytics is Samsung's Center-of-Excellence for driving the adoption of data driven decision making and product development across the company. The team's core focus is developing best-in-class solutions that provide Samsung's marketing and service organizations with a 360 degree view of the Samsung customers. Strategic Analytics is powering a paradigm shift at Samsung and the global industry. We are looking for highly technical team members who are passionate about data, have the rigor needed to solve billion dollar problems, and possess an innate entrepreneurial spirit to explore the uncharted. Strategic Analytics combines the engineering backbone of a best-in-class Big Data Platform with the analytic expertise of advanced mining and predictive modeling. If you want to work among the very best talent in the industry, working on the most innovative products in the world, Samsung is the place to be.We are seeking an outstanding analytic leader experienced in designing, developing, and distributing data insights and solutions to Samsung's marketing and service organizations that have a measurable impact on the business. This person will work within and across teams to help identify viable opportunities to maximize the use of customer analytics in daily operations. This is a leadership role accountable for driving the data strategy, business requirements, design, development, deployment, and ongoing enhancements of the US Customer Profile in support of the SEA business lines. This person will be responsible for managing the complex integration of business needs and partnerships in the execution of the required solutions. The jobholder will need to have the leadership skills to drive to closure on key functional and non-functional requirements while managing the relationships with new and existing business partners, and the other teams within SEA. He or she will craft and manage descriptive analyses and predictive models, profile delivery standards, and organizational reporting strategies as well as being able to understand how to integrate different data sources within the organization.
