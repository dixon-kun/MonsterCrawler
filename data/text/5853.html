We're looking for a Software Architect Engineer to architect and implement core components of our platform and scale it to serve millions of users across the world. The technical scope of the project is broad and calls for technical expertise across a wide range of problem domains.

Responsibilities:

* You will architect and implement substantial portions of the full-stack infrastructure (both frontend and backend).

* You will tackle scalability challenges as the infrastructure grows to support increasing user base.

* You will mentor and grow junior engineers on best practices and code efficiency.

* You will have the opportunity to code using cutting-edge platforms and interacing with our data science team.

Qualifications:

* Technical Leader - you love new technology and always strive for the most fundamentally sound architecture and solution.

* Detailed-oriented- you are a proven developer who values clean and efficient code, and always goes into the weeds to find the root cause of problems.

* Translator- you have experience working closely with product managers, cross-functional teams, and customers to translate requirements into technical design.

* Solutions-driven- you have experience thinking out loud to create the best solutions to complex, tricky problems, using the latest technical methodologies/processes.

* Craftsmanship - you treat software development as a professional craft and constantly practice your skills.

Requirements:

* 6+ years experience working on both frontend and backend software using modern languages and frameworks (GO, Scala, Python, Play, Django, AngularJS, React).

* Advanced degree in Computer Science and/or Math.

* Solid experience in building prototypes to help validate business assumptions.

* Expert proficiency in Agile development process.

* Expert knowledge in API development for mobile/web use.

* Strong relational database experience (MySQL, PostgreSQL, Oracle, or MS SQL).

Preferred skills & experience:

* Highly motivated and self disciplined.

* Architected and built large scale platforms from scratch.

* Strong Computer Science fundamentals.

* Excellent ability to prioritize and communicate in a fast-paced environment.

* Bonus:Experience in predictive analysis and machine learning.

About Tala

Tala is a mobile technology and data science company that is changing the way credit scoring and financial services work around the world. Tala's smartphone app instantly evaluates customers for credit using only the data on their devices and delivers customized loans in minutes.

Tala, formerly known as InVenture, is backed by IVP, Ribbit Capital, Lowercase Capital, Data Collective, Collaborative Fund and other leading venture and impact investors. In 2016, Fast Company ranked Tala, formerly known as InVenture, as one of its top 10 companies in Money, and Forbes named Tala as one of the top 50 FinTech companies in the world. Tala is headquartered in Santa Monica with additional offices in Nairobi and Manila.
