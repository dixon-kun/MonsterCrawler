Oracle Data Cloud (ODC) - Advanced TV Business Development Director

Preferred Qualifications

The Oracle Data Cloud (ODC) is looking for a highly motivated, results-driven sales and partnershipprofessional to join the TV Solutions team.



This strategic role isresponsible for helping grow Oracle’s TV audience and analytics business tosolve for needs across our large diverse base of advertiser, media and platformclients. Ideal candidates must have experience in television and data and possessa combination of critical thinking, business development and strategic salesskills. Candidates must also possess outstanding communication skills and beable to successfully work in a fast-paced, entrepreneurial environment within alarger organization.



The ideal candidate’sapproach is disciplined, thoughtful and consultative, having a unique abilityto navigate their way through even the most complex organizations to win oversenior-level decision makers.



This is an opportunityto join and contribute to a dynamic fast-growing entrepreneurial team at awell-respected Fortune 100 organization, to pioneer new capability within anexciting and rapidly disrupting industry.



Key Responsibilities


Identify, qualify and close newrevenue-generating opportunities with partners and clients (cable and satelliteoperators, data partners, advanced TV platforms, connected TVs, broadcasters,cable networks, advertisers, agencies and others..) that contribute to ODC’srapidly expanding TV capability.


Lead the negotiation of expectations,terms, conditions, pricing and licensing of data and analytics products and serviceswith these new partners and clients.


Contribute to the strategic andoperational planning, direction and positioning of our team and products.


Champion ODC TV’s leadership andevangelize the sales effort for all types of Advanced TV throughout the ODCorganization and its advertiser and agency clients to build and scale this newand critical business.


Develop marketing initiatives and  participate as a senior-level leader atindustry events and function as an expert on all ODC data products and services


Develop presentations as needed tosupport new business and opportunity reviews with internal teams


Build effective consultativerelationships and deliver on high customer service with clients and partnersthroughout the deal-making process.


Work across multiple business unitswithin Oracle, and work closely with cross-functional teams and executives rangingfrom sales to data science to product to engineering and support.



Skills &Qualifications


8+ years track record of winningnew business/sales and partnerships within TV, online video and digital media.


Strong relationships with keystakeholders throughout the media and tech ecosystem.


Deep knowledge of Advanced TVadvertising, audience targeting, measurement, digital video, display, mobile, social,DMPs, DSPs, SSPs, key players and key trends in our space.


Understanding of how to workwith TV planning and buying platforms, data partners, media publishers,  ad networks, and agencies.


Proven track record ofdeveloping strategic business relationships, structuring creative andsuccessful partnerships and taking them through to close.


Demonstrated resultsorientation: consistent delivery of revenue targets and business growth


Strong energy and drive:self-motivated, focused on the bottom line, persistent.


Organizational agility:communicating effectively, culturally savvy, managing up, down, laterally,entrepreneurial mindset that drives and is adaptive to change while workingwithin the framework of an established larger business.


Proven ability to work bothindependently and collaboratively.


Strong analytical,problem-solving, technical and strategic thinking skills


Experienced engaging withexecutive decision makers. Strong executive presence, verbal, interpersonal,presentation and written communication skills


Bachelor’s degree required;graduate degree preferred


NY location strongly preferred


Ability to travel up to40%


Detailed Description and Job Requirements
Builds long term, strategic relationships with named and vertical business alliance partners.

Facilitates the development of strategic marketing and new business plans for all assigned partners to meet or exceed assigned business goals. Works with Partners to identify opportunities and create demand through lead generation activities and target account selling strategies. Actively track joint sales pipeline and meet or exceed quarterly and annual revenue targets. Develop and maintain relationships with global counterparts to leverage corporate initiatives and to ensure adherence to Alliances and Channel standards. Monitor partners business results, making recommendations for improvements to increase penetration for the strategic partners. Establish rules of engagement and operational escalation procedures to quickly identify and resolve issues.

Leading contributor individually and as a team member, providing direction and mentoring to others. Work is non-routine and very complex, involving the application of advanced technical/business skills in area of specialization. Demonstrated track record developing and managing Global Partnerships and driving Partner account activity. Prefer strong analytical, sales channel and marketing skills. Prefer 8 years of related experience with a secondary education in Marketing or a related field.
