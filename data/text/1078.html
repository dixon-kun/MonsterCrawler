Job Description

At GORE, our global teams bring together extensive knowledge and collaborate closely to create and deliver products that solve complex problems and perform in the most demanding environments. Our corporate functions help ensure that the company is well-positioned for long-term success, with an environment where associates work together effectively and pursue the highest impact opportunities. We are looking for an Enterprise Sales Business Intelligence Champion to join our team. The position is based in Newark, DE with flexibility to work remotely from a home office, along with an expectation for travel as required.

If you are searching for a company where you can make a difference, we're looking for you. In this role, you will be a part of the newly formed Analytics and Insight Center of Expertise (COE) for Enterprise Sales & Marketing. This COE will define and implement best practices, KPIs, and standards for data analysis and reporting that provide insights for our sales and marketing activities and deliver improved customer experiences. You will guide reporting strategy for the Enterprise's Salesforce® solution, assess the effectiveness of sales processes, and seek insights into customer and sales data.

The ideal candidate will:

* Be passionate about providing Sales analysis and insight for a world-class manufacturer

* Be energized by collaborating with cross-functional and cross-regional teams to bring value to our customers and our internal partners

* Recognize the importance of building and maintaining strong interpersonal relationships

Responsibilities include:

* Establishing and leading best practices and governance for Salesforce® Sales Cloud reporting by coordinating with the application support team, other members of the COE, internal business partners, and outside consultants

* Maintaining a deep understanding of:

* Gore's sales processes, data, and technologies

* Key performance metrics for Gore's businesses

* The evolving capabilities of Salesforce® products and related technologies.

* Working with business and Enterprise sales leaders to establish and measure key metrics for the effectiveness and efficiency of sales processes across several go-to-market models (data sources for these metrics may include Salesforce®, ERP, web, and non-structured data accessed in an Enterprise data lake environment)

* Seeking new insights into sales data that will improve the outcomes of our sales processes and positively impact the customer experience including new strategies for increasing customer retention and growing new business

* Collaborating as needed on projects with business partners, data scientists, and statisticians

Required Job Qualifications:

* Bachelor's degree required (degree in Finance, Management Information Systems, or Economics preferred)

* 10 years' experience working in a sales and/or marketing environment with at least 3 years of experience in a sales analysis role

* Experience partnering with sales and marketing leaders on a global basis

* Proven track record of thought leadership in applying data driven insights that have a tangible impact on sales performance

* Experience with advanced data/reporting structures, BI, and data visualization tools

* Working knowledge of CRM systems, preferably Salesforce® Sales Cloud

* Strong problem solving skills, with ability to synthesize data into useful formats and bring actionable insights through effective communication

* Ability to:

* Apply business judgment to prioritize business requests

* Prioritize

* Manage multiple commitments

* Work in fast paced environment

* Challenge and influence

* Travel as required (maximum 25%)

Desired Job Qualifications:

* Advanced degree in financial analysis, data science, or marketing

* Knowledge of R, SAS, or COGNOS Analytics
