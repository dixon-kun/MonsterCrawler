Inmar Overview:

Anyone who has redeemed a coupon, filled a prescription or returned a product, has touched Inmar. We apply technology and data science to improve outcomes for consumers and those who serve them. As a trusted intermediary for over 35 years, we have unmatched access to billions of consumer and business transactions in real time. Our analytics, platforms and services enable engagement with shoppers and patients, and optimize results.


Major Responsibilities/Essential Functions:

Overall Responsibilities:

  • Prepares purchase orders by verifying specifications and price; obtaining recommendations from suppliers for substitute items; obtaining approval from requisitioning department.
  • Verifies purchase requisitions by comparing items requested to master list; clarifying unclear items; recommending alternatives.
  • Obtains purchased items by forwarding orders to suppliers; monitoring and expediting orders; resolves shipments in error with suppliers.
  • Communicate with suppliers, receiving locations and Disbursements Accounting personnel to resolve invoice discrepancies to ensure timely payment of invoices in order to capture available discounts and avoid late payment fees and interest charges. Perform other activities, as required, to reconcile purchase orders.
  • Manages the facilitation of new software requests
  • Assists with ordering new maintenance
  • Provides purchasing planning and control information by collecting, analyzing, and summarizing data and trends.
  • Tracks savings based on first quote to last quote.
  • Makes sure items and services purchased are received in a timely manner.
  • Responsible for spend categories that could include Auto Fleet, Real-estate, Travel and Expenses and Telecommunications.
  • Assists various departments with any customer service needs .


Minimum Requirements:

  • 4 Year Business/Finance Degree.
  • Proficient in the use of MS Office Suite.
  • Prior experience utilizing an electronic purchasing tool highly desired.
  • Provide timely and excellent customer service to internal clients.
