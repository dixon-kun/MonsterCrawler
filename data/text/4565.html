Imprivata is the leader in secure access and collaboration for healthcare. Founded in 2002 with the vision of making it easy for clinicians to securely access health information, Imprivata boasts a growing population of over 4 million clinical users worldwide. As we take on new and exciting challenges in healthcare, we’re looking for skilled professionals that can help propel us into new areas of growth.

Take a walk around our offices and you’ll see a dedicated group of professionals invested in our mission. We’re a diverse group that thrives on collaboration and sharing ideas, whether in a cross-functional meeting or at one of our office events. Most of all, you’ll find a group that shares a passion to improve the healthcare experience for providers and patients.


Job Summary
The Portfolio Operations team provides the analytics, infrastructure, and processes that facilitate the success of the product portfolio. We are looking for a business intelligent analytics professional to help Imprivata product management create successful products that delight the market.


Essential responsibilities

  • Play a strategic role in driving product growth by creating the actionable analyses that impact adoption, usability, engagement, retention, cross-sell and upsell. Locate the trends and business drivers.
  • Support the growth of our product pipeline by suggesting and creating the analyses that measure the value of early-stage product efforts
  • Make the analysis sing – understand the numbers, but explain the impact in business terms and in ways that are actionable. An ability to create meaningful and efficient visuals is a must.
  • Manage operational dashboards to monitor performance at the product and portfolio level
  • Work with finance, marketing, engineering, sales operations, user experience, and product management to identify, locate, and combine data from different sources to support needed analyses
  • Conduct ad-hoc analyses upon request
  • Work with internal stakeholders to create automated and easily accessed dashboards and reports
  • Become an expert in our product and business space to allow you to suggest impactful analyses.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s Degree in science, computer science, statistics, economics, mathematics, or similar quantitative discipline.
  • 5+ years of experience in analytics, reporting and data collection and interpretation with large data sets. Solid experience in presenting findings to diverse groups, including at the executive level.
  • Data science and business background are a distinct plus.
  • Sound understanding of SQL or ability to learn based on prior experience. Scripting or programming experience desired.
  • Excellent communication and organizational skills. You will need to interact well with many Imprivata stakeholders, at all levels of the organization.
  • Experience in a software product development environment is a plus.

At Imprivata, we have a top notch work environment, developmental opportunities, a competitive total rewards package, and the desire to have fun. If you have the skills and qualifications as we have described above, we want to hear from you!

Imprivata provides equal employment opportunities to minorities, females, veterans, and disabled individuals, as well as other protected groups.
