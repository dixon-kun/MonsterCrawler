Born out of President Obama’s re-election campaign in 2012, Civis Analytics is a rapidly-growing data science technology and services company based in Chicago, IL.  We empower our customers to use data to drive their organizational decisions, and we are proud to work on some of the world’s most meaningful data science challenges.  Over the past three years, we have transformed our services into a comprehensive data science SaaS platform and suite of applications, making cutting-edge analytics techniques available to a broad audience.

Civis is on the cutting edge of the information age, and we tackle novel legal, ethical, and compliance issues at the intersection of technology and data science.  We are seeking a smart, energetic, and highly-motivated corporate paralegal to join our legal team. This position reports to our Legal Counsel and is located in our Chicago headquarters.  You will be a driving force in scaling our legal department to support the company’s high rate of growth, and you'll be trusted with a wide range of responsibilities related to our legal, commercial, and compliance initiatives.

Responsibilities

  • Create and improve processes for legal operations to help things get done better and faster.  For example, these may relate to commercial contracting, facilitating cross-departmental communications, development of legal resources, or the storage, retrieval, and sharing of corporate and commercial documents
  • Collaborate with legal, engineering, and business teams in designing and managing compliance programs -- e.g. in security, privacy, corporate
  • Conduct legal research and draft and edit contracts, NDAs, amendments, statements of work, and other documents that underpin the exciting work we do
  • Create aggregate summaries of relevant contracts and business terms
  • Assist in responding to other legal requests
  • Assist in the development and maintenance of the company’s intellectual property portfolio

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s Degree from an accredited university
  • At least 3 years of relevant experience, preferably in a technology company
  • Passion - We love what we do, and we want our colleagues to be as excited as we are about Civis’ mission and coming into work each day
  • High integrity, highly-organized, and detail-oriented with a strong work ethic and excellent all-around judgment
  • Self-starter who thrives when trusted to identify areas in which they can add value and take initiative
  • Proven ability to manage numerous projects simultaneously under time-pressure in a fast-paced environment
  • Growth-minded, welcomes mentorship and constructive feedback, and a constant desire to improve personally and professionally
  • Excellent people skills, a good sense of humor, and someone who doesn’t take themselves too seriously
We want to know more about you than what's on your resume, so please include a cover letter to discuss what interests you about Civis, our work, and the analytics field in general.
