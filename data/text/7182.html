Job Duties and Responsibilities Become an integral part of a diverse team that leads the world in the Mission, Cyber, and Intelligence Solutions group. At ManTech International Corporation, you ll help protect our national security while working on innovative projects that offer opportunities for advancement.

Currently, ManTech is seeking a motivated, career and customer oriented Research Analyst to join our team in the Springfield, VA area to provide unparalleled support to our customer and to begin an exciting and rewarding career within ManTech. Â The selected individual will provide network analysis support as part of an integrated agile team that will be responsible for using, developing, maintaining, and enhancing models, tools and software for Intelligence Analysis-Counter-terrorism-Network Analysis, Predictive Modeling, Threat Research Support, Visualization and Query Support.

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:
  • Deliver analysis for the identification and analysis of transnational criminal organizations, their future actions, and their methods
  • Search and analyze intelligence data, synthesize intelligence information into relationship or threat model data, and infer the confidence level and completeness of the information
  • Research additional sources such as trade, travel, criminal activity, and money movement to isolate actors, activities, and networks that represent threats to the US security or legal trade and travel
  • Provide analytical support for the development of analytic's models to identify networks, predict future threats, and isolate and evaluate threat scenarios
  • Work in data science teams that include Intel analysts, data and visualization experts, and software developers
  • Construct of dossiers for specific threats
  • Prepare documentation validating reasoning, certainty, and completeness in synthesized information

Position Requirements:
  • Bachelor's degree and four or more years of experience are required
  • Demonstrated expertise in understanding large, complex transaction data, with the ability to synthesize information from large amounts of data
  • Experience with Intelligence Analysis
  • Experience in community and threat Identification
  • Experience presenting work to both technical and non-technical audiences
  • Experience evaluating uncertainty in relationship data
  • Experience working in law enforcement or classified intelligence analysis environment

U.S Citizenship with the ability to obtain DHS CBP Suitability is required
