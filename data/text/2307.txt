        Who We Are
        Deepfield is a Nokia owned company that builds the analytic and security solutions that monitor and defend over 90% of internet backbone networks. We produce cutting-edge research at the intersection of data science, visualization, and network analytics, and we create, deploy, and maintain the largest global network analytics platform. Come join us and help advance state-of-the-art network intelligence.
        What We Do
        At Deepfield, a Nokia owned company, our customers are the internet - from content providers to global network operators. There's no shortage of data in these environments; the challenge is to turn that data into actionable information for everything from strategic business decisions to automated network operation. The biggest players in the industry are turning to Deepfield for answers.
        As the Scrum Master, you will be embedded on multiple software development teams to help keep teams focused on delivering high quality, excellent products and features. Your facilitation skills are required to support planning exercises with the team to create and then execute a feature backlog. While guiding the team through our release cycle, you'll challenge the team to focus on delivering a minimum viable product to deliver features on time and within budget.
        The Scrum Master will be collaborating with Product Owners, UX Designers, Architects, and development teams to define and cost the breath of projects, and track their progress. During your day to day activities, you will be balancing competing priorities and demands from our customers, sales, technical operations, and R&amp;D to guide teams to their fullest potential.
        Job Duties
        * Facilitate Agile ceremonies (Standup/Scrum, Sprint Planning, Retrospectives, Release Planning, etc&#8230;) and championing Agile development best practices
        * Communicate project progress, obstacles, and project risks to leadership
        * Hold a deep understanding of project details, dependencies, and use cases
        * Proactively lead and support development teams to refine their processes to become highly effective teams
        * Collaborate with team leads to create, maintain, and organize backlogs
        * Continuous learning about networking and the evolving problems we're trying to solve

        * Superb written and verbal communication skills are a must and have the ability to scale communications to technical and non-technical team members
        * Previous work in a web-based application development environment
