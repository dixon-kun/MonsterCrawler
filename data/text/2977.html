Responsibilities

Join our Information Technology team where you will work on new technologies and find ways to meet our customers’ needs and make it easy for them to do business with us. You will regularly lead multiple small to medium-size projects or one large project and perform project management activities such as planning, sizing, configuring and scheduling. You will develop resolutions to complex problems creatively and make decisions that impact projects & staff members.

We are looking for a hands-on, tech savy Cloud Architect to help suport a suite of applications within various groups such as Sales, Provisioning, Enterprise and Marketing. We work with tons of data and on the latest and greatest technology.

The candidate will take multiple projects from the business and see it through to implemention and also provide innovative solutions. In addition, he or she will lead a dynamic team in a very fast paced environment to educate the team on cloud technologies.  The candidate will also perform end-to-end systems analysis and design and evaluate solution's alignment with the end user experience.

Software/ Application Development & Architecture

  • Build highly scalable and high performance architecture to help support Verizon  sales and services.
  • Design and develop front-end and back-end services and REST Api’s to help support high volume web  transactions.
  • Cloud Native and Api First Technology expert in the area of   Access Services Provider using open source products across all business units to help advance and drive internal and external innovation.
  • Identify potential emerging technologies that help run Digital platforms, in a highly scalable manner for a high traffic volume web setup.
  • Lead and coordinate the Innovation Review forums for the Digital Platforms group at Verizon Sales and Services teams.
  • Apply Machine Learning and Data Science to find opportunities to provide business solutions
  • Partnering with architects and engineers to build strategies for execution, drive and/or facilitate key decisions, and influence others and lead change where appropriate.

Qualifications

Must have:

  • Bachelor’s degree or six or more years of work experience.
  • Six or more years of relevant work experience.
  • Experience developing highly scalable Restful services for customer facing front end and backend systems.

Ideally, you’ll also have:

  • Degree in Computer Science.
  • Capability to refactor code for a “green field” setup using cloud based technologies for both private and public.
  • Familiarity with a broad mix of technologies including a strong subset of: Microservices, AngularJS, React, Java, JSP, Pivotal Cloud Foundry, Spring Boot/Netflix OSS , Oracle 12 c, MySQL, NoSQL (Cassandra), RabbitMQ, AWS Web Services, Apigee, Play/Akka, Linux, and a range of open source libraries.  Also important to have familiarity with distributed architecture, SOA, and platform concepts.
  • Well versed with industry leading practices in the cloud and microservices development areas. Also, well aware of tools and techniques at all layers of the stack.
  • Data Analysis , Big Data  & Machine Learning Experience.
  • Experience with Chatbot is a plus.

Not to boast, but a little bit about us

Verizon powers America’s fastest and most reliable network. We’re also leading the way in cloud and security solutions, Internet of Things and video entertainment. Technology moves fast and so do we. We believe that bringing great ideas and customer experiences to life should be recognized and rewarded.  Whether you think in code, words, pictures or numbers, find your future at Verizon.
