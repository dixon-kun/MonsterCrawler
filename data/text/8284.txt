        We are currently seeking talented Associate Business Information Architect for our San Antonio, TX or Remote facility.
        Incumbents are responsible for the development and implementation of the information architecture to support the measuring and monitoring of enterprise business needs. Maintains the design around the full set of Enterprise information capabilities that support existing and strategic business capabilities.
        Job Requirements
        * Under minimal supervision, applies advanced knowledge to perform regular assessments of the health and maturity of information capabilities for a given domain.
        * Applies advanced knowledge to identify new information capabilities needed to support business capabilities of a given business domain. Maintains the information capability repository with these identified needs.
        * Acts as a key contributor in efforts to define the mission, goals, critical success factors, principles, and policies for information strategy and information architecture.
        * Partners with the Business Information Architects and other technical and business architects to enable the development of the USAA Integrated Information Capability Roadmap. Guides other team members to goal achievement and team success.
        * Partners with key stakeholders to build the business cases required to secure approval and funding for the Data and Analytical efforts
        needed to support the Information Capability Roadmap.
        * Acts as a key contributor in the creation and maintenance of a conceptual model for information capabilities for a given domain.
        Understands end to end change impact by managing linkages from information capabilities to technical assets (operational + analytical).
        * Acts as a key contributor in the development and maintenance of an integrated logical data model to understand data and its
        Interdependencies regardless of its usage pattern. Provides consulting and guidance regarding the usage of the enterprise integrated logical model
        Minimum Requirements
        * Bachelor's Degree in Information Architecture, Information Management, Information and Data Science, Information Studies or a related technical field. Four years work experience in information management or related experience can be substituted in lieu of a degree in addition to the minimum years of work experience required OR A Master's Degree in Information Architecture, Information Management, Information and Data Science, Information Studies or a related technical field OR A PhD in Information Architecture, Information Management, Information and Data Science, Information Studies or a related technical field.
        * If Bachelor's degree, 6+ years experience in information and/or data architecture to include strategy, planning, and delivery oversight OR If Master's degree, 4+ years experience in information and/or data architecture to include strategy, planning, and delivery oversight OR If PhD, 3+ years experience in information and/or data architecture to include strategy, planning, and delivery oversight.
        * Qualifications may warrant placement in a different job level*
