We are seeking a Digital Marketing Specialist to join our growing Global Business Intelligence organization. Our team is highly specialized in digital marketing, data visualization and data sciences that can yield valuable market insights and craft effective strategies to capitalize on market opportunities at the right time. The right candidate will complement the team by bringing a versatile skill set, using innovative tactics and strategies to increase effectiveness of all digital marketing initiatives.

The Digital Marketing Specialist will be a valuable member of our Programs Team, which facilitates global demand generation and marketing automation projects for multiple countries around the world. The Programs team in the Global Business Intelligence Business unit oversees all scoping, implementation, reporting, work-flow development and program execution. The Programs team will work hand in hand with our Programs Operations Team (based in Chennai, India).

The Digital Marketing Specialist will support the operational functions of our data driven programs and will oversee the elements of the marketing mix, ensuring strong integration with offline elements, setting and monitoring strategic KPI’s, and fostering strong alignment across other departments to ensure that the implementation of integrated marketing plans are coordinated, aligned, and consistently meeting business objectives.

The Digital Marketing Specialist is required to have a wide breadth of basic knowledge across many overlapping disciplines, including email, PPC, display, video, HTML, user experience, SEO, social, community, blogging, and content.

  1. ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS:

  • Plan and scope projects
    • Define the scope of the project in collaboration with our Consultant team
    • Create a detailed work plan which identifies and sequences the activities needed to successfully complete the project
    • Develop a schedule for project completion that effectively allocates the resources to the activities
    • Review the project schedule with BI staff to ensure all resources are aware and aligned in preparation for execution
    • Determine the objectives and measures upon which the project will be evaluated at its completion
    • Scope project timeline and map to the appropriate resources across the BI Operations team(s)
  • Oversee project/program execution
    • Oversee the execution against the project plan
    • Monitor the progress of the project and make adjustments as necessary to ensure the successful completion of the project – support project workflow through JIRA
    • Establish a communication schedule to update stakeholders including appropriate staff in the organization on the progress of the project
    • Review the quality of the work completed with the project team on a regular basis to ensure that it meets the project standards
    • Communicate with project sponsors
    • Monitor and approve all budgeted project expenditures
    • Ensure that all financial records for the project are up to date
    • Ensure that the project deliverables are on time, within budget and at the required level of quality
    • Evaluate the outcomes of the project as established during the planning phase
  • Post Project Completion
    • For all projects that now will turn into Programs, ensure that there is proper process documentation
    • If not, then build it
    • Work with key BI associates to coordinate training of the appropriate individuals to be able to executive this program systematically
    • Work with key BI associates to implement a QA process for the program
  • Explore alternate Marketing Channels
    • Make recommendations on new ways to reach our target audience
    • Explore new digital delivery vehicles to replace email based marketing (Apps, SEO, Landing pages, etc.)

III. Requirements:

  1. Education: Bachelor's degree or relevant additional experience in related field.
  2. Experience: 1 to 3 years related industry experience in program management, project management or digital marketing experience.
    1. Experience with marketing automation platform (e.g. Eloqua, Marketo) strongly preferred.
    2. Advanced skills in Microsoft Excel and SQL strongly preferred.
    3. Experience with workflow tools desired (JIRA, PODIO)
    4. Experience working within the digital marketing domain, including Email Marketing, Paid Search, SEO, Web Analytics and Social Media
    5. Possess the ability to think strategically, examine, and re-engineer operations and procedures and implement new strategies and procedures.
    6. Possess the ability to effectively communicate in written and verbal format, as well as manage details, and represent IM in a professional manner. Ability to gather data, compile information, and prepare reports.
    7. Strong interpersonal skills including the ability to work well with others and foster a cooperative work environment.
    8. Must possess project and time management skills.
    9. Skilled in handling projects and good follow up skills. Possess the ability to maintain rapport with customer contacts and vendor representatives. Must be able to prioritize assignments.
    10. Program planning and implementation skills; including strong organization skills and conflict resolution.
