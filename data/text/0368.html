DESCRIPTION:

The Sr. Manager, Media supports the media team with the development of off-network media strategy and plans that drive customer acquisition and retention for Showtime-over-the-Internet and traditional distribution platforms.

PRINCIPAL RESPONSIBILITIES:

Showtime-Over-the-Internet - Work with media team on day-to-day operations pertaining to media for the Showtime app and third party OTT streaming services.

* Continuously refine the media strategy for Showtime-over-the-Internet to achieve customer acquisition and retention goals.

* Serve as a regular point-of-contact for the customer engagement agency, providing guidance on targeting, plan development, optimizations, and connection to larger series-specific advertising strategy.

* Ensure plans are consistent with media strategy, in line with budget, flighted properly, and executable based on production schedules.

* Attend and be an active participant in all weekly status meetings with the customer engagement agency and various internal marketing teams.

* Communicate premiere dates, product updates, and other pertinent information as it becomes available.

* Connect both media agencies to make sure plan details are shared in order to ensure plans are best complementing each other.

* Work closely with the Subscription Marketing team in the stewardship of SOI media plans to closely sync up media tactics and agency deliverables with specific marketing needs.

* Review targeting parameters for media partners from customer engagement agency

* Oversee the management of the media budget for Showtime-over-the-internet.

* Contribute to the development and authoring of presentations that articulate media strategies and tactics to secure sign-off to activate plans. Opportunity to present this work to senior management and executives in collaborating departments.

* Communicate media approach, targeting, strategy, tactics, and reporting to Program Promotion Media team in an effort to identify areas of overlap or to register transferrable learnings.

* Analyze campaign results with an emphasis on digital media KPIs (provider leads, app downloads, etc.), and connect to business KPIs (conversion, churn, consumer lifetime value) to ensure cumulative refinement for future initiatives.

* Aim to apply transferrable learnings across both series tune-in and SOI media campaigns to optimize fully comprehensive and complementary advertising initiatives.

* Read, analyze and utilize media research—and support team with deep analysis of emerging digital media tactics with an emphasis on digital innovation, programmatic buying, connected TV landscape, and advances in measurement tech.

* Serve as a day-to-day point for colleagues within the subscription marketing, program marketing, digital marketing, product, and key account groups.

* Represent the Media team alongside peers in cross-functional meetings with same teams mentioned above and participate as a creative and vocal contributor in brainstorming sessions.

* Communicate regularly with Product team to make sure measurement capabilities are up-to-date and working properly, especially around new product launches.

* Advocate and make recommendations for where SOI support should be infused into Program Promotion plans; this could live as working media, unified measurement, creative messaging, etc.

* Work with Promotions and Partnerships team to identify smart ways to incorporate an SOI component into new and existing activations or partnerships.

* Stay abreast of television and media landscape developments by monitoring trade publications and meeting with industry and product experts.

Streaming Key Accounts + Traditional Distributors

* Work closely with Showtime Key Account Marketing and Sales teams to achieve subscriber and revenue goals through highly targeted and cost-efficient media campaigns. Counsel them to maximize value in the management of digital and offline media opportunities.

* Identify opportunities within programming and distributor-specific windows in order to effectively plan quarters at a time well in advance.

* In addition to planning media, this position will need to negotiate acceptable rates, set up internal billing and tracking, monitor pacing and delivery, and obtain and evaluate performance.

* Consult and assist in plan activation details with the creative members of the Key Account team

* Serve as a client-facing partner to distributor contacts to discuss PP and SOI media plans constructed to support mutual marketing goals.

* Recommend network and daypart mix and flighting for traditional distributor co-marketing TV campaigns based on the available featured spots.

Leadership & Training:

* Manage and develop the Media Supervisor:

* Train, manage and mentor the Supervisor to deliver excellent work for all assigned tasks.

* Continue to educate the Supervisor on media planning and buying.

* Offer ongoing performance feedback and broader business insights, thereby inspiring the best work possible from direct reports and the AOR.

* Assign projects that will foster professional development and round out the skills and experience of the Media Supervisor.

* Direct Media Supervisor to collect and maintain vendor information and competitive data as well as issuing new plans and updates to Senior Management.

* Identify and share professional reading material, educational seminars and industry sessions to foster perpetual career enrichment.

* Work with the Media Supervisor on budget management process; review invoice coding and distribution of agency invoices to various internal teams.

Smithsonian Earth - Smithsonian Earth is a digital streaming service with original documentaries that bring you closer to nature's most fascinating animals and wildest locations

* Media consultant for Smithsonian Earth colleagues. Responsibilities include:

* Working with agency to develop media target, strategies, and plan

* Tracking and managing campaign summary reports

* Presenting plans and metrics to Smithsonian Team

* Manage invoice distribution and financial processes as needed

QUALIFICATIONS:

* 5+ years of digital media planning and/or customer acquisition marketing and/or entertainment branding experience

* Strong technical understanding of the streaming landscape and emerging media opportunities to support acquisition and viewership initiatives for Showtime-Over-the-Internet.

* Highly developed communication skills, including: written, verbal, listening and presentation skills

* Extensive knowledge of marketing, advertising and media planning principles, with an expert knowledge of interactive TV applications, connected TVs, and programmatic buying methods

* Constantly stays abreast of digital trends, emerging technologies, entertainment streaming platforms, social media community developments, and first-to-market advertising opportunities

* Passion for the entertainment space and Showtime’s programming. Strong knowledge and understanding of the cable television industry, particularly the premium cable business, including the programming, marketing, and distribution aspects of the competitive category

* Supervisory and management skills, including the ability to train and motivate a report. * Collaborative, strategic, resourceful, articulate, analytical, and well poised. Must be able to represent Showtime exceptionally on a daily basis to external partners and media colleagues

* Exceptional critical thinking skills and the ability to craft a story using data science

* Highly developed communication skills, including: written, verbal, listening and presentation skills

* Proactive, diligent, and unwaveringly solution-oriented. Collaborative, strategic, pragmatic, cheerful, and perennially displays a "can-do" attitude

* Comfort analyzing data and using it to illustrate a marketing story
