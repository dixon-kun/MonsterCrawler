Job Title

People Analytics Specialist

Job Description

The People Analytics Specialist will support the HR team and other people-related functions at Kaplan to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of an array of HR capabilities through data, analytics, and insights. Primary areas of focus will be in compensation and recruitment and may evolve to include training, development, engagement, talent mobility, and retention.

In this hands-on dynamic role, you will produce one-time and ongoing reporting and analytics that support day-to-day decisions and processes in addition to longer range planning.


Ideal candidates are strong systems-thinkers, problem-solvers, and highly detail-oriented.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Understand business goals and how HR processes support said goals

  • Define and iterate reporting requirements and output in accordance with stakeholder needs

  • Prepare and build reports, validating data and associated insights throughout the process

  • Deliver reports and analysis in a fast-paced environment and effectively share and summarize findings with stakeholders

  • Identify and solution data integrity issues (from quick-fixes to systemic issues)

  • Identify opportunities to streamline and improve the accuracy, breadth, and depth of data and insights

  • Provide administrative support for HR, primarily in facilitating compensation and recruitment processes

  • Assist with proactive and reactive compensation benchmarking requests

Requirements:

  • BA / BS

  • 1+ year/s experience in field with emphasis on data, analysis, and reporting (e.g. finance, accounting, compensation, research, sales operations, organizational development/effectiveness, etc…)

  • Ability to effectively manage and work with sensitive / confidential HR data

  • Strong analytical skills with passion for details and “digging in”

  • Proficiency with Microsoft Office including intermediate Excel and/or Google Sheets skills

  • Ability to organize and work with large amounts of complex information, managing multiple priorities

  • Exceptional attention to detail, deadline-oriented and flexible to meet changing requirements of the role

  • Positive, tenacious outlook and ability to establish rapport and work across a matrixed organization

  • Experience analyzing compensation/recruitment data and highlighting trends a plus!
