        Advance your Career at Solaria Labs, a Liberty Mutual Endeavor
        The insurance industry is being transformed: self-driving cars, the Internet of Things, the Sharing Economy, and an ever-changing set of start-up competitors are all major contributors to the disruption of current business models. In response, Liberty Mutual has established Solaria Labs, an innovation center dedicated to testing new ideas, turning those ideas into products, and launching new companies.The Labs is also exploring emerging technologies like blockchain and machine learning: evaluating their current state and building products for the insurance industry and beyond. Liberty Mutual's innovation efforts are gathering quite the buzz in Boston, having been named one of BostInno's 50 on Fire and having been featured in the Boston Globe and TechCrunch. Solaria Labs is part of a community of innovation, partnering and co-developing with startups, several universities like MIT, and Smart Cities like Chicago and Singapore. We are looking for innovators who are passionate about making an impact, are entrepreneurial, and are willing to test and learn. Does building a new future for a Fortune 100 company like Liberty Mutual excite you? If so, join our team!
        Many of the concepts developed at Solaria Labs require the application of advanced technology, such as data science and blockchain platforms.As Applied Science Lead for Solaria Labs U.S., you will be responsible for leading the application of these advanced technologies to solve complex customer problems.This role will work closely with our product managers to flesh out concepts by identifying the most promising technical approach followed by building working prototypes.A key element of this role will be staying abreast of current and emerging technology frameworks to ensure that Solaria Labs is efficient and effective as an innovation center.The role includes maintaining collaborations and co-develop efforts with the larger technology community, including startup, academic, or consortium partners, including several Smart Cities who will be providing data.Finally, the role will include interfacing with other Solaria Labs global locations to coordinate Applied Science efforts.
        Job Responsibilities
        * Manage and develop a multi-disciplinary team of applied scientists.Lead the application of applied science to build new disruptive and transformational products and services
        * Develop and demonstrate technology prototypes as part of an agile development team.In parallel, advise 3-5 other Solaria Labs teams on how to use technology within their products
        * Ensure the highest standard of technical excellence for all efforts in the lab, adopting best practices from other organizations and vetting new technologies or frameworks as appropriate
        * Maintain the Applied Science infrastructure of Solaria Labs, including open source frameworks, to maximize reuse and sharing across projects
        * Work with other Liberty Mutual teams rotating through Solaria labs, helping them identify and apply technology to help their customers
        * Manage meetings with business partners and other Solaria Labs leaders to coordinate Applied Science efforts and share learnings
        * Mentor junior staff from Solaria Labs and other Liberty Mutual rotational teams
        * Achieve higher rate and lower cost technology adoption by leveraging Design Thinking and Agile methodologies
        Qualifications
        * Bachelor's degree in engineering, computer science, mathematics, or related field plus a minimum of 10 years of proven progressive experience and a minimum of five years of proven team management and leadership experience.Masters or Ph.D. level degree is preferred
        * Leadership experience within a corporate or startup innovation environment is critical
        * Expertise with R, Python, Java, C++, or similar language, along with standard scientific computing libraries.
        * Successful candidates must have a proven track record of rapid prototyping using open source or commercial frameworks (e.g., TensorFlow, Domino, Bluemix, Ethereum, etc), and a constant drive to learn new technologies to build better products.
        * Computer science background, comfortable working with both technical and non-technical staff to translate concepts and algorithms into working prototypes
        * Past participation in technical conferences and professional societies.Technical publications of any sort are desirable.
        * Ability to thrive in a fast paced environment with significant uncertainty, applying test and learn with rapid iterations to converge on a solution
        * Ability to manage unstructured information and communicate/translate it to others in a more structure way
        * Must possess strong analytical, strategic, project management, decision-making and problem-solving skills.
