We empower small businesses with a suite of powerful and easy-to-use email, marketing automation, and analytics tools that integrate with hundreds of popular applications and services.

The team is looking for a Systems Engineer to join their ranks. You will work closely with the Development, Marketing, and Data Science teams to support the infrastructure and monitoring for large-scale projects and products that reach millions of users around the world. Responsibilities

* Provisioning, configuring, and maintaining of servers and services

* Primary support for production outages

* Provide support to all environments (prod, stage, qa)

* Ensure monitoring is in line with current needs

* Be part of an after-hours on-call rotation

* Work collaboratively with devs and engineers to solve complex system issues

Requirements

* At least one year of experience (3+ is ideal) working within large-scale linux environments (100+ servers, 1000+ a bonus)

* Previous experience monitoring, configuring, and provisioning the servers and services (apache/nginx, mysql, named, etc.)

* Experience with system-level troubleshooting: understanding logs, strace, netstat, top/htop, iostat, etc.

* Skill in Linux (intermediate to advanced), Apache/Nginx (intermediate), Zabbix/Graphite/Similar (intermediate), Bash/Python/Ruby (intermediate), MySQL/PostgreSQL (beginner to intermediate), Puppet
