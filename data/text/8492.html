Pre-Sales Engineer

Job Summary

If you desire a fast-paced, dynamic, challenging role, consider joining the Pre-Sales Engineering team at PROS. Your responsibilities will include understanding the common issues facing global sales organizations and to create proof of value solutions using your technical and business skills.

If you are an analytical person with business acumen, and you are familiar with pricing concepts, sales workflows, and economics, consider joining PROS. We will provide you with the opportunity to utilize your talents and cultivate your career in a fast growing market.

Position Responsibilities:

* Collaborate with our sales, strategic consulting, and product teams to create compelling technical solutions that demonstrate real value

* Fulfill all the technical requirements for the solutions used in a typical sales cycle

* Advise the sales team with winning solution strategies and the demo use cases needed to address specific business problems

* Identify and mitigate project risks. Manage and control project scope and timelines

* Lead and participate on projects with tight deadlines in a collaborative team environment where communication is vital

Requirements:

* BA/BS degree in a technical or business discipline

* High level of energy, persistence, and strong desire to do whatever it takes to succeed

* Excellent communication skills. Must be comfortable in customer-facing environments

* Proven ability to thrive in a team environment

* Exceptional analytical and problem solving skills

* Experience writing SQL, data manipulation and/or strong Excel expertise

Preferred Requirements:

* Sales engineering/technical pre-sales support

* Database experience (Microsoft SQL Server, MySQL, Oracle)

* BI and reporting tools

* Experience with pricing and revenue optimization, ERP, CRM or CPQ

Work Environment:

Most work activities are performed in an office environment and require little to moderate physical exertion. Work activities may require periods of extended hours, critical deadlines and stressful situations. To successfully complete the tasks of this position, individuals must be able to communicate clearly (in writing and orally), comprehend business terminology, interpret numerical data, hear effectively, use hands and fingers to type, write, grasp/hold, and similar tasks. Specific positions may require frequent travel domestically and abroad.

About PROS

PROS Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: PRO) is a big data software company that helps customers outperform in their markets by using big data to sell more effectively. We apply 29 years of data science experience to unlock buying patterns and preferences within transaction data to reveal which opportunities are most likely to close, which offers are most likely to sell and which prices are most likely to win. PROS offers big data solutions to optimize sales, pricing, quoting, rebates and revenue management across more than 40 industries. PROS has completed over 700 implementations of its solutions in more than 55 countries. The PROS team comprises approximately 1,000 professionals around the world. To learn more, visit www.pros.com.
