        Role in Brief:
        Education Advisory Board (EAB) is looking for a multi-talented Data Scientist with programming, machine learning, and data processing skills to help create and improve modern analytics services for applications used by students and administrators at over 200 colleges and universities. These tools impact student success by helping people make better choices about courses, majors, and other activities, enabling more students to graduate and get the most out of their education. Your primary role on the Data Science team will be to build things &#8212; systems and frameworks for large-scale data analytics, workflow tools for machine learning, web apps for data visualization, and more.
        This position will be located in Washington, DC.
        What You'll Be Doing:
        * Collaborate with quantitative researchers and statistical programmers to integrate research-driven statistical models into production software systems
        * Build internal tools for data science/analytics workflows
        * Manage data processing pipelines for analyzing gigabyte to terabyte-scale data from multiple sources
        * Write Python and R code using current best practices
        * Communicate and coordinate effectively with product managers, software engineers, and customer-facing teams
        Must Haves:
        * Education: Bachelor's degree in a quantitative field, or equivalent education and experience
        * 2+ years of experience in commercial software development
        * Experience building applications in Python, R, or another high-level language used in statistical computing
        * Database design and SQL-based data cleansing, transformation, summarization, and analysis
        * Experience with machine learning techniques and approaches, such as generalized linear models and random forests
        * Experience working on a diverse team
        * Ability to present to, communicate with, and collaborate well with non-technical people
        * Demonstrated interest in learning a wide range of new skills
        Even Better:
        * Masters degree or PhD in a quantitative field, or equivalent experience
        * Experience with data warehousing, data pipeline management, and/or Hadoop development
        * Linux systems administration or DevOps experience
        * Experience with statistics and scientific methods
        * Conscientious, dedicated, and quality-focused
        * Experience in EdTech or Higher Education effectiveness
        * Active in your professional community
