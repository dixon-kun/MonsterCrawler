Associate, Aperio Data Science - New York

Job Description:

Point72 Asset Management, L.P., seeks an Associate, Aperio Data Science in New York, NY to work with modeling team to develop and productionize quantitative models for equities and other asset classes. Please e-mail your resume to: Recruiting2014@Point72.com and reference job code ZD082016. NO CALLS PLEASE. EOE/M/F/D/V

Requirements:

Master's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Machine Learning, or related quantitative field or equivalent and 1 year of experience productionizing quantitative models for Equities and other asset classes. Prior experience must include 1 year of building effective and maintainable data solutions utilizing datastructures, algorithms, and computer programming languages including Java and Python; processing data with SQL; working with a large number of big data sources; analyzing large datasets using Hadoop and Spark; building performance monitoring tools; performing software testing and prototyping; designing and formulating model validation rules and production procedures for trading and research departments.
