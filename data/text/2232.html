Associate QA Engineer

Responsible for creating, maintaining, and executing test plans for clients, vendors and other ADP product areas. Interfaces with internal clients to test resolved defects and new configuration.

Responsibilities:

* Reviews and provides feedback on requirements and design specifications. Key participant in requirements, design, and code reviews. Participates in plan/specification peer reviews to ensure technical completeness and accuracy.

* Develops test plans, test specifications, test cases and test scripts.

* Inputs all documented data conditions to execute test cases. Processes tests. Generates test results.

* Analyzes test results to ensure quality standards are met and the software meets/exceeds documented business requirements. Submits potential deficiencies into the Defect Tracking System. Interfaces with configuration analysts, application developers and service to resolve possible deficiencies to ensure exit/entry criteria is met and conforms to design and business requirements. Provides stakeholders with timely status updates and applicable QA metrics throughout the SDLC.

* Setting and adherence to QA standards, full participation in development lifecycle.

* Ensure QA Entrance and Exit criteria are met.

* Develops and manages work plans for specified client base. Manages self to the completion of those tasks and informs others when problems occur or when commitments cannot be met as planned. Responds and reacts to a variety of critical issues simultaneously.

* Writes data base queries and reports as appropriate. Creates, updates and maintains functional documentation to support client processing.

* Demonstrates thorough knowledge of applicable platforms/applications. Develops solutions and recommendations to client needs through the use of the application; understands impact on scope and timelines. Identifies and reports potential scope changes and opportunities to appropriate management.

* Uses effective communication and facilitation skills to advise and influence clients at the user and line management levels. Prepare written communications such as status reports, project/internal correspondence, and functional specifications.

Education:

* Bachelor's Degree - Or equivalent in education & experience

* 1 - 3 Years - Proven working experience in software quality assurance or equivalent desired.
