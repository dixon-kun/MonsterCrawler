Position Description

Sr. Digital Marketing Analyst

JOB PURPOSE

Under the general guidance and supervision of the Analytics Manager, this position provides strategic development and analysis to support planning, execution and optimization of the Independent Beauty Consultants’ experience across all sales force digital and offline communication channels.

The role requires a unique blend of strategic, analytical, and innovative thinking as well as strong project management and influence skills. The Assistant Manager is an expert resource in tracking, reporting and analyzing web site and digital related channels, as well as offline, where information is available.

The person in this role understands business goals and maps them against metrics and measurement capabilities. The Assistant Manager is instrumental in the development and delivery of business intelligence reporting with actionable data and analysis easily accessible and understandable to business decision makers.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

1. Leads the effort to define, measure, and analyze our sales force digital programs, InTouch website, email, and sales force communications to strategically link collected data back to business objectives of web campaigns, offering insights and recommendations for best practices. Utilizes web analytics reporting tools such as Adobe Analytics, Salesforce Marketing Cloud (ExactTarget), Sitecore Analytics, and Tableau for use in data analysis reports and presentations. Develops data into meaningful analytics to help business owners evaluate sales force contests, challenges, and the launch of new product marketing campaigns. Leads the development and execution of digital marketing and measurement frameworks in collaboration with internal partners.

2. Leads discussions with business owners to identify business objectives and expected outcomes of web marketing campaigns that can be measured. Provides guidance to internal partners to create processes for gathering data that can be turned into critical information to facilitate business decisions before, during and after completion of campaigns. Collaborates across teams (IT, Creative, Marketing, Merchandising teams) to continuously improve the User Experience, collecting and analyzing data to present findings and recommendations. Leads email analytics.

3. Leads efforts with stakeholders to access analytic needs so a tagging and reporting plan can be created and implemented to track important KPIs for digital campaigns. Supports test setup and analysis control of marketing testing.

4. Leads the effort to define, measure, and tell the story of our digital communication efforts to the sales force. Communicates results both written and verbally to make recommendations for future campaigns based on analysis of data. Develops and presents customized, data driven presentations for leadership and business owners with actionable insights and recommendations to help drive revenue and other key outcomes based on the analysis of the collected data. Produces campaign dashboards and KPI reporting.

5. Drives the capabilities of the analytic reporting tools by staying current on the latest data science, advanced analytics, and big data trends. Maintains and increases functional knowledge of new features, products and initiatives across our analytic tools.

6. Provides various ad-hoc analyzes, data mining, and reporting as needed. Trains business users in Adobe Analytics and consumption of reports/dashboards. Leads and educates discussions on KPIs and web metrics with internal partners to ensure alignment.

KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS AND ABILITIES

Education

Bachelor’s degree or equivalent in statistics, computer science, business administration, or marketing. Other training may be acceptable by demonstrating substantial experience working in a related field, such as statistical analysis, web analytics or research.

Experience

5 + years of experience working with Adobe Analytics. Knowledge of Tableau, Sitecore Analytics and Salesforce Marketing Cloud (ExactTarget). Previous experience in data mining or any statistical function in a retail or direct selling environment preferred.

1. Requires basic knowledge of principles and techniques of website development and working knowledge of web servers and components that use web servers such as, SQL databases or content management software.

2. Supervisory and strong leadership experience required in order to drive change and provide strategic direction.

3. Must be able to build strong relationships in order to influence internal partners and various department stakeholders.

4. Work cross fuctionally and interacts with business partners at all levels.

5. Requires the use of analytical logic and reasoning to identify the strengths and weaknesses of alternative solutions, conclusions or approaches to problems. Must be able to identify problems and review related information to develop and evaluate options and implement solutions and bring to supervisors attention.

6. Experience in ecommerce and email marketing.

7. Excellent communication, discretion, administrative and interpersonal skills required to work with various levels of Company employees. Strong written and verbal communication skills with the ability to interpret data providing simple, well thought out conclusions.

8. Strong verbal, written, and presentation communication skills.

9. Strong attention to detail, commitment to accuracy and timelessness, and exceptional organizational skills with the ability to manage multiple priorities at once.

10. Basic understanding of statistics, customer profiling and segmentation.

11. Omniture tagging and auditing expertise is a plus.

12. Responsible for meeting all production/quality standards as defined by the department for this job.

13. This position requires strong analytical background and vast familiarity with online analytic tools.
