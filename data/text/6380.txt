        Summary
        SafeAuto's Data team is at the forefront of enhancing SafeAuto's data science infrastructure and capabilities. The focus of this team is enabling better business decisions through the wide usage of robust data solutions. This includes maintaining our existing data environments while migrating us to new environments and capabilities. The team is responsible for the entire data analysis tool stack, including ETL development, warehouse modeling and development, and structured &amp; unstructured data analysis toolsets. This position will be part of a development data team, not an operational team.
        In this role, the candidate will assist other SQL Developers and Business Intelligence developers to Create SQL Server Data Tools deployment packages (DACPAC), create and deploy SSIS packages to support staging mart and cube databases, improve monitoring capability error logging and error recovery capability of ETL jobs. Knowledge and experience as or working with an operational DBA team will be helpful.
