Be a key part of our new team devoted to improving UnitedHealthcare.

Join our team and you will use your outstanding analytic, big data, and statistical skills to deliver studies, analyses, forecasts, and predictive models.  You will develop and implement strategic solutions through your research and analysis of data and business processes.

Primary Responsibilities:

Big Data and Analytics Skills

  • Use your analytic skills to make a more efficient UnitedHealthcare – reducing claims rework, improving processes, and driving higher quality – all at a massive scale.
  • Initiate and execute enterprise-wide big data analyses.
  • Exercise your technical expertise – especially SQL, R/SAS, Tableau, and potentially big data analytics solutions such as Hadoop, Hive, Pig, Spark, etc.
  • Propose and deliver innovative ways to look at our business problems using analytics, data mining, data science, and other techniques.
  • Demonstrate your statistical acumen by building analytic tests to improve our processes.
  • Deliver and “production-alize” predictive models leading to improved decision making and reduced overhead.
  • Discover cross-functional, enterprise wide, end-to-end information hidden in vast amounts of data, and help the company make smarter decisions to reduce rework and improve issue resolution.

Communication and Team-Working Skills

  • Demonstrate outstanding communication skills when presenting your findings, describing your work both orally and visually in compelling, actionable ways.
  • Document your findings in a clear, structured manner.
  • Use your strong business and problem-solving skills to quickly develop hypotheses, test, validate, and drive business process improvements.
  • Work closely with other business and technology leaders.
  • Manage subject matter expertise and internal consulting resources to drive analytic, data science, and other related projects.
  • Engage constructively with project teams to support our objectives with your sound, advanced analytical skills.
  • Develop productive working relationships, mentor teammates, and actively use your skills to improve the team’s performance

Key Characteristics of a Successful Candidate

  • Performance : Demonstrates urgency in pursing goals. Puts in extra effort to achieve outcomes required.  Has a strong commitment to quality and accuracy and getting thing done correctly the first time, as well as managing time effectively.
  • Innovative : Quickly adapts to change. Refocuses efforts as needed. Maintains a positive attitude when dealing with change and ambiguity.
  • Customer Focused : Consistently meets or exceeds customer expectations. Identifies and develops new processes to enhance customer outcomes.
  • Communication : Excellent oral and written communication skills, with advanced skills in Microsoft office suite (i.e. PowerPoint, Excel, Word, Visio) and the ability to develop your own presentations.
  • Influence : Presents a logical case when offering opinions and ideas. Tactfully handles disagreements and opposing viewpoints.
  • Logical Thinking and Decision Making : Makes logical, fact-based decisions.  Understands root cause issues.  Considers the impact of the decision on other parts of the business.
  • Change Agent : Challenges the status quo and creates a vision for why change is needed.
  • Business Focus : Applies the company's vision, mission and strategy to best achieve long and short-term goals.

Required Qualifications:

  • Degree in scientific or quantitative field, including computer science, statistics, mathematics, bioinformatics, epidemiology or similar degree
  • 3+ years of statistical analysis, quantitative analytics, and / or forecasting / predicting analytics
  • 2+ years of SQL, Python, or other similar, programming experience
  • 2+ years of experience with statistical software such as R, SAS, or other similar programs
  • Exceptional communication skills and the ability to make complex concepts simple and easy to understand

Preferred Qualifications:

  • 2+ years of developing machine learning solutions
  • Knowledge of big data systems such as Hadoop, Hive, Spark or similar libraries
  • Experience working in an Agile Software Development environment
