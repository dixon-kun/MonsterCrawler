About the Job

Senior Strategy and Performance Advisor

Salary: up to $80,000 Commensurate with Experience
Excellent Benefits Package

Brooklyn, NY

Reporting to the Director of Strategy and Performance, the Senior Strategy and Performance Advisor will be responsible for supporting a management system for driving performance related to the execution of agency strategy and operations. As the lead analyst for the team, the Senior Strategy and Performance Advisor will be responsible for executing three performance-related objectives, among other priorities: Evaluate and regularly report agency performance through key performance indicators; conduct ad hoc analyses and draw data-driven insights to help improve performance; and produce analytic content of value for all agency staff to inform decision-making at every level.

Responsibilities will include:

· Assist in the development of DoITT’s overall strategy, as well as the translation of that strategy to the agency’s divisions;

· Develop the appropriate set of metrics to track and evaluate title performance of divisions and functions against their contribution to the overall strategy;

· Conduct deep-dive analyses using appropriate methods of data collection and integration, statistical analysis, data mining, and computer programming while guiding junior analysts on such techniques;

· Present findings through data visualization and written and verbal communication;

· Oversee the importing, transforming, validating, and modelling of DolTT's performance data;

· Support the performance management process with the Mayor's Office of Operations, resulting in monthly agency reports and the Mayoral Management Reports (MMR) and Preliminary MMRs;

· Support special projects and the development of strategy and policy as needed.

Preferred Skills:

The successful candidate should possess the following:

· A bachelor's or master's degree from an accredited college in computer science, data science, statistics, mathematics, economics, public policy, information technology management, business administration or other related field, or equivalent work experience;

· 5-7 years of experience;

· Strong analytical skills, including data preprocessing, statistical analysis, and data visualization;

· Experience working in an organization responsible for delivering enterprise information technology;

· Experience working with a large public entity or government agency;

· An understanding of programming logic is required and experience is preferred with languages and platforms related to statistical analysis, such as Python (most preferred), R, MATLAB, Excel;

· Familiarity with SQL and system databases, data elements, and application software solutions for gathering and integrating data, including business intelligence platforms such as Oracle OBIEE;

· Strong presentation and writing skills, including experience giving executive level presentations or public reports;

· Ability to motivate and mentor a team of junior analysts;

· Ability to take the initiatives and work independently to deliver against objectives;

· Ability to manage multiple tasks under tight deadlines.

Qualification Requirements:

A bachelor’s degree and 2 years’ experience related to the projects and policies to be studied in the particular position.
