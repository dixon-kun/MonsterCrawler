        Sr. Information Security Engineer / Architect
        Cloud Infrastructure
        Overview:
        This role will lead and actively manage "Cloud" infrastructure security (IaaS and PaaS); network integrity for all company networks (internal corporate and external SaaS); and drive continuous improvement to defensive posture, visibility, response, and situational awareness. The role will also oversee and improve operational security activity within domain of expertise; Investigate and solve problems of technology, process, and organization; Seek out, identify, and drive security capability via root cause attribution; and identify and evaluate solutions addressing root cause and capability gap. We seek an established security practitioner able to think on the tactical level who will recommend solutions aligned with strategic vision.
        The PROS Information Security Engineer / Architect will:
        * Protect cloud infrastructure and sensitive data on public infrastructure (IaaS) and platforms (PaaS)
        * Provide practical solutions to ensure secure principles are achieved.
        * Lead cloud and network security implementations and administration.
        * Research, review, and recommend new technology, process, or policy to support mission.
        * Participate in training and education to maintain skill relevancy.
        * Develop deep understanding of deployed technology infrastructure and relevant business drivers
        * Support the Application Security team with cloud security initiatives.
        * Support the DevOps initiative with focus on cloud security and automation.
        * Partner internally with IT, SaaS, Product Development, Customer Support, and Legal functions to discover, track, and promote security.
        Education:
        * Bachelor's degree in Comp. Sci, MIS or other related field. Equivalent work experience will be considered if non-degreed
        * Master's considered a plus
        Experience:
        * 1+ years of experience with securing "Cloud" environments, preferably in Microsoft Azure.
        * Hands on experience with network security architecture and best practices.
        * Experience with configuring and managing Network security tools and technologies.
        * Experience troubleshooting and investigating security issues in cloud infrastructures.
        * Experience with network protocols, data flow analysis, and network design and troubleshooting.
        * Experience with scripting languages &#8211; for example, powershell, shell, python, perl, etc.
        Knowledge:
        * Design and implementation of Infrastructure and cloud architecture
        * Network based forensic investigations, methods, and tools such as Firewalls, IPS, WAF etc.
        * Defense in Depth principles and technology including access/control, authorization, Identification and authentication, public key infrastructure, network, and end point protection.
        * System and network exploitation, attack pathologies and intrusion techniques (such as denial of service, sync attacks, malicious code, password cracking, etc.)
        * Techniques to automate common security and infrastructure tasks in the cloud.
        * Industry standard DevOps tools (Chef, git, VMWare, Puppet).
