Mgr/Sr Mgr - Amex Advance Platform & Data Strategy-17003990
Description

The Amex Advance Platform team within Global Information Management is focused on bringing to life an industry-leading platform to enable the company’s new strategic initiative on Performance Marketing & Data Products, in the realm of digital and mobile marketing.  We are accelerating the use of Big Data and information-based assets to create best-in-class capabilities to promote commerce . . . keeping customer privacy at the forefront of everything we do.


The team partners closely with multiple business partners such as technology, business development, and product development, to harness the full power of our data assets while deploying the latest technologies in Big Data and APIs. A measure of success for the platform is its ability to enable new partner use-cases and business development opportunities with minimal customization.


The ideal candidate for a role on the Amex Advance Platform team is a results-oriented individual who has demonstrated sound leadership skills, innovative thinking, and the ability to design, manage, and execute strategic initiatives. The candidate should be able to build and leverage relationships with teams across AXP and external partners in order to achieve results.  Another critical factor of success is the candidate’s ability to work in a fast paced start-up environment that has ambiguity and white-space . . . we are looking for a team member who will roll up their sleeves, be part of the team, with a can-do positive attitude.


Key Responsibilities:


  • Architecting use cases / data flows to solve business challenges with partners.

  • Cultivate business development and partnerships that enable monetization of performance marketing and data product capabilities.

  • Managing the end-to-end project delivery.  Influence platform strategy, including data & matching, data science, and APIs.

  • Identifying critical data and infrastructure gaps in our platform and develop architectural solutions/ enhancements.

  • Collaborate closely with business partners to assess new ideas, services, and leading edge capabilities to inform the product roadmap.

  • Being the subject matter expert for 3 rd party data to internal partners, educating and evolving Amex Advance

  • Liaison with Amex Technology and Data Science teams
Qualifications
Qualifications:
  • Experience using data and analytics to drive successful marketing programs, directly and via ad networks

  • Knowledge of digital and mobile advertising
  • Ability to manage complex, high-profile projects end-to-end

  • Familiarity with working with Advanced Data Capabilities and platforms, including Teradata, is a plus.

  • Can see the big picture of every project and is able to get into the details when needed

  • Strong project, portfolio management, and organizational skills (ability to multi-task and work under strict and fast timelines)

  • Strong communication skills, both verbal and written to all levels of the organization

  • Superb Microsoft office suite skill, particularly Powerpoint

  • Experience must include strong problem solving and analytical skills (data analysis, architecture analysis, reporting) and exceptional project management (drive multiple priorities concurrently)

    ·Familiarity with large unstructured data, Cloud Computing skills (AWS/Azure), Big Data analytical tools (MapReduce, Hive, Pig)

  • Bachelor's degree required

  • Master’s Degree or equivalent in Information Management or a related field plus one year of experience in developing Big Data analytical infrastructure and statistical capabilities preferred
