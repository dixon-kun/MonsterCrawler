                Position Highlights:

Using your technical skills, business acumen and creativity, you will build tools to automate reporting and generate insight that will allow internal clients to quickly and accurately see how our key business products and processes are performing.&#160; Previous experience working with business partners to frame operational problems, analyze information and design and develop reporting and metrics that properly evaluate outcomes.&#160; Previous experience with Oracle and Microsoft databases with emphasis on providing business intelligence and data driven insight through reporting, will have equipped you well for this role.
