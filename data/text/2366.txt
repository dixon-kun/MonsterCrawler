        The Dow Chemical Company has internship opportunities during the summer of 2017 within its Advanced Analytics organization for individuals who are within one or two years of graduation and are currently enrolled in a university working towards a Master's or PhD degree in a related quantitative data science technical field. The internships will be located at Dow's global headquarters in Midland, MI. The duration of these internships is typically 12 weeks.
        Interns will assist Advanced Analytics Data Scientists in driving bottom-line value, enabling Dow's mission to address the world's most challenging problems. You will collaborate with a world-class multidisciplinary team to translate data into profit using analytics to address a wide range of business problems. Your challenges may come from Commercial, tackling pricing or customer insights, or Supply Chain, analyzing and improving cost to serve, or Procurement, where you can ensure Dow acquires material and services optimally, among other opportunities. You will engage with internal customers on selected projects to ensure analytic solutions meet their needs and drive results.
        Interns will potentially be involved in one or more of the following:
        * Listening to business problems and collaborating with subject matter experts to fully understand the domain.
        * Translating real business challenges into analytical terms, and using rigorous techniques to solve problems in the areas of commercial, marketing, pricing, supply chain, demand planning and strategy.
        * Working with cross-functional teams to acquire, clean and harmonize data needed, project managers to successfully complete projects, and end-users to ensure results become embedded in business processes.
        * Using a potential broad set of tools, such as R, Python, SAS, SQL, Excel, Hadoop, Tableau and others.
        The incumbent may work with personnel from the United States; Sao Paulo, Brazil; Mumbai, India; and Shanghai, China, as well as business clients from any other Dow global location.
        Candidates must have a strong interest in the application of data science to meet rapidly changing business needs. They must have good judgment, solid quantitative technical skills, and the ability to work with diverse, global teams in an empowered environment. Strong human relations skills, teamwork, and flexibility are essential for success.
        Must be within one or two years of graduation and currently enrolled in a university working towards a Master's or PhD degree in a related quantitative data science technical field. Prefer degrees in such areas as Applied Statistics, Industrial Engineering, Computational Analysis, or Business Analytics.
        A minimum 2.700 cumulative GPA on a 4.000 scale at your current university is required, but we are seeking GPAs of 3.000 or higher.
        A minimum requirement for this US-based position is the ability to work legally in the United States on a permanent basis.
