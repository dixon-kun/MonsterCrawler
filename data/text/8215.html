Analytics Manager - eClerx Digital

As a result of our rapid growth in the analytics and insights practice, we are currently looking for an Analytics Manager to further our growth and maturity in this key domain. This role is to manage, mentor, and train our onshore consultants who directly engage clients adding onsite presence to our traditional offshore delivery model. This leadership role requires strong problem solving skills and business acumen, as the manager is responsible for consistent and timely delivery of quality analyses by the team.

The successful candidate will have an extensive knowledge in the advanced analytics and database marketing operations of large brands, particularly in the use of data and analytics contributing to marketing decisions and day-to-day operations. He/she will also require an enthusiasm in working with our clients’ marketing stakeholders, acting as a subject matter expert for our onshore analysts and delivery teams. Experience in managing analytical professionals is a must.

This position will report to the Analytics & Insights Practice Lead based in New York City.   The role will collaborate across many areas of our business, including service delivery, client engagement, business development, and practice development. The Analytics Manager will be working with our analysts primarily, but will be required to visit our clients in their offices whenever necessary, helping to design the most efficient data and analytics roadmap for marketing decision support and activities through all conceivable marketing channels. On the delivery side, the manager will be acting as a project manager, supervising all aspects of analytical projects.


In this position, the candidate will:

  • Consult with clients to identify marketing objectives and needs ; develop data and analytics roadmaps to meet long and short-term marketing goals in the most efficient ways
  • Supervise analysts in defining project scope and deliverables that support business goals, and translating them into project specifications for the delivery teams.
  • Collaborate with client engagement managers to develop and finalize SOWs.
  • Guide analysts in terms of prescribing suitable solutions with an understanding in limitations of toolsets and available data.
  • Help defining targets and analytical universe for statistical modeling; define goals and parameters for segmentation efforts.
  • Act as a project managers, supervising analysts during the course of project development and execution; set major milestones for the projects and manage client expectations at a high level during the critical delivery phase.
  • Standardize repeatable processes; create templates for major KPI’s, reports and visual presentations.
  • Guide analysts interpreting analytical results and providing insights; take a leadership role in recommending next steps to clients.
  • Continue to identify domain and industry best practices and incorporate them into our practice.
  • Audit deliverables ensuring accuracy by critically examining the data and reports against requirements.
  • Collaborate with Client Engagement Managers and delivery teams to identify expansion opportunities within assigned customer accounts.
  • Onboard new analysts and train them not only for technical skills, but also business aspects of client-facing analytics.
  • Set goals for individual analysts and provide consistent feedback; lead employee review process for analysts.
  • Traveling is required depending on client expectations.

A successful candidate will have:

  • 7-10 years of experience in an advanced analytics and/or data scientist role
  • Previous experience in managing statistical analysts in a consulting company or a large company
  • Strong critical thinking, problem solving and creative analytical skills
  • Hands-on experience in digital marketing and/or 1:1 marketing in any channel; expert level knowledge in database marketing and CRM
  • Strong analytical and storytelling skills; ability to derive relevant insights from large reports and piles of disparate data
  • Working knowledge of analytical/statistical techniques and their applications; experience in SAS and/or R is a definite plus
  • Understanding of data manipulation technologies and data platforms (based on either prepackaged ETL tools or custom programming)
  • Excellent communications skills, both written and spoken; ability to explain complex technical concepts in plain English
  • Strong interpersonal skills, interacting with clients from the C-suite to marketing managers to technical specialists
  • Working knowledge in data visualization tools (Tableau, QlikView, etc.) is a plus
  • Ability to manage multiple priorities and projects at a supervisory level, aligning teams to project timelines and ensuring quality of deliverables
  • Passion for data and analytics for marketing and eagerness to evangelize good practices
  • Strong business acumen, comfortable working autonomously with broad guidelines
  • Ability to streamline complex processes and enforce rules to analysts whenever necessary
