        Janssen Research &amp; Development LLC, a Johnson &amp; Johnson company, is recruiting for an IT Lead, R&amp;D Emerging Technologies Product Line Offering.         At the Janssen Pharmaceutical Companies of Johnson &amp; Johnson, what matters most is helping people live full and healthy lives. We focus on treating, curing and preventing some of the most devastating and complex diseases of our time. And we pursue the most promising science, wherever it might be found.
        Janssen Research &amp; Development, LLC discovers and develops innovative medical solutions to address important unmet medical needs in oncology, immunology, neuroscience, infectious diseases and vaccines, and cardiovascular and metabolic diseases. Please visit http://www.JanssenRnD.com for more information.
        We are Janssen. Our mission drives us. Our patients inspire us. We collaborate with the world for the health of everyone in it.
        The R&amp;D Emerging Technologies Product Line Offering (PLO) is part of a broader R&amp;D Innovation Enablement organization that works on leading-edge technology solutions to drive the Pharma R&amp;D business. R&amp;D Emerging Technologies is made up of a group of technical engineers and solution architects engaged in the hands-on delivery of business solutions. Team members typically work directly with business partners in delivering solutions. The team prides itself on being closely involved in all aspects of deployments, including assisting with defining business value propositions and user story collections, designing robust solution architectures and implementing the technical solutions. Our mission is to enable our business partners to capitalize on opportunities that could not have been done before through the technologies we deliver. Real World Evidence -- the analysis of electronic medical records, hospital and insurance data, as well as patient reported outcome and biosensor data in the future -- is a major focus for Janssen.
        The IT Lead, R&amp;D Emerging Technologies Product Line Offering will be a part of this team, leading projects, assessing technologies and delivering solutions in Research Cloud Engineering.
        The IT Lead, R&amp;D Emerging Technologies Product Line Offering will:
        * Lead and implement technology deployments to business partners in the areas of Real World Evidence (RWE) and Digital Health. This includes partners in the Therapeutic Areas, Quantitative Sciences, Epidemiology, Health Economics and Outcomes Research, and others.
        * Partner with Data Sciences as well as other groups across the IT organization.
        * Serve as a key technical contributor in this space and help drive the high performant and scalable cloud computing technology stack necessary for business partners to perform advanced analytics.
        * Play a key role in deploying the technology platform and solutions for business partners to perform deep analytics that integrate complex data types for Digital Health, the integration of digital patient reported outcomes and biosensor/wearable data into clinical trial and healthcare settings.
        * Work with numerous and varied technologies in implementing solutions with our business partners. These technologies include cloud services such as Amazon Web Services' Redshift, Simple Storage Service (S3), Elastic Map Reduce (EMR) / Hadoop, Elastic Compute Cloud (EC2), Lambda and Kinesis; analytics &amp; visualization packages such as R, MATLAB, SAS, Qlik, and Tableau; and configuration management tools such as Chef and Puppet.
        * Mentor junior employees on these and other technologies, as well as manage vendor partners.
