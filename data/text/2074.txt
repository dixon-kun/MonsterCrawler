        PROGRAM DESCRIPTION
        The mission of the Data Science and Information Technology Program (DSITP) is to develop an enterprise-level, consolidated information technology infrastructure that provides exceptional IT capabilities to the Frederick National Labs for Cancer Research (NCI-Frederick/FNLCR) in support of basic, translational, and clinical cancer and AIDS research. The Biomedical Informatics Applications Development (BIAD)Group is part of the DSITP within Leidos Biomedical Research. The BIAD team is primarily involved in the development of the projects sponsored by NCI. Currently BIAD is developing the following high visibility projects NCI MATCH https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/clinical-trials/nci-supported/nci-match , Pediatric MATCH https://www.cancer.gov/about-cancer/treatment/clinical-trials/nci-supported/pediatric-match and NCI CTRP https://www.cancer.gov/about-nci/organization/ccct/ctrp
        JOB DESCRIPTION/BASIC FUNCTION
        BIAD Group is currently looking for a talented technical lead for a key role in CTRP project.
        Responsibilities of a Technical Lead:
        * Lead a team of software engineers in a dynamic and agile environment
        * Write high-quality, efficient, and testable code in ruby, python, java and JavaScript
        * Ensure all engineers follow a test-driven development mindset and code are tested at the unit and functional level
        * Drive technical projects and provide leadership in an innovative and fast-paced environment
        * Take responsibility for the overall planning, execution, and success of complex technical projects
        * Work closely with management, business analysts, and customer to ensure the best system is built
        Qualifications:
        BASIC QUALIFICATIONS
        To be considered for this position, you must minimally meet the knowledge, skills, and abilities listed below:
        * Possession of a Master's degree from an accredited college/university according to the Council for Higher Education Accreditation (CHEA) in Computer Science or closely related degree or six (6) years equivalent experience in lieu of degree
        * Foreign degrees must be evaluated for U.S. equivalency
        * A minimum of six (6) years of progressively responsible job related experience including two (2) years of experience in a leadership capacity
        * Hands on experience with microservice architecture
        * Hands-on programming capabilities in Python, Java and JavaScript
        * 5-8+ years professional experience in software development
        * Experience with Agile development methodologies (Scrum, Kanban, etc.)
        * 3+ years of hands-on technical leadership and people management experience
        * Must be able to obtain and maintain a Security Clearance
        PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
        Candidates with these desired skills will be given preferential consideration:
        * Proficiency in programming in Python, Ruby or Java
        * Ability to effectively articulate technical challenges and solutions
        * Experience with distributed systems, algorithms, and RDMS/NoSQL databases
        * Experience working in an Agile software development environment
        * Experience with continuous integration and continuous deployment
        * Experience with design patterns and modern frameworks, Java Spring, Ruby Rails, Python Flask, AngularJS
        * Experience with designing and developing RESTful service architecture.
        * Experience with distributed system performance analysis and optimization
        * Ability to deal with ambiguous and loosely defined requirements
        * Proficiency in writing unit tests
