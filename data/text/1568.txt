        POSITION SUMMARY
        The Medical Director plays an essential role in developing, implementing and measuring the success of Healthagens mission and strategies. This requires interaction with executives, clinicians and analysts across Aetnas who serve population health, healthcare delivery systems, pharmacy and sales, as well as direct interactions with prospective and established clients. As well, the Medical Director will work with the Outcomes team to provide clinical and informatics guidance across all phases of a projectsales, definition, proposal, study design, protocol, execution and presentation.
        Fundamental Components:
        PRINICIPAL SPECIFIC ROLES
        The Medical Director will report to Healthagen Outcomes president and work closely with the program director. Daily interaction with the program director, sales/marketing, biostatisticians and analysts is essential
        Based on an understanding of Aetna and Healthagens short- and long-term strategies and priorities, and the evolving clinical and healthcare environment: Bring insight and help set direction for Outcomes overall, and for specific potential or actual projects
        External: In sales and implementation, liaise with prospective and established clients to understand their strategies and needs; translate specific analytic questions into a Statement of Work that can be addressed with available data; and meet with clients as projects evolve. Manage relationships with clients health economists and outcomes researchers (HEOR)
        Internal: For population health and therapeutics projects: help set design, methodology, metrics; write or review study protocols for Institutional Research Boards and internal use; work with data scientists, analysts and biostatisticians to develop, implement and review analyses; help prepare and deliver interim and final presentations in sales and client situations
        BACKGROUND/EXPERIENCE desired:
        Required:
