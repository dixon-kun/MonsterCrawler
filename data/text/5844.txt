POSITION SUMMARY 
The Principal Scrum Master will have a primary focus to provide collaborative leadership to one or more teams by applying Agile practices such as Scrum, Kanban and help team members embrace and practice the values and principles of Agile. The Scrum Master will work closely with Development, QA and Product and Program to facilitate Agile Scrum Methodology. The Agile Scrum Master will work with development teams responsible for delivering Enterprise Application assignments in quick 2 week coding cycles. The ideal candidate will be expected to help support the Product Owner in their efforts to facilitate a healthy intra-team dynamic with respect to priorities and delivering business as well as aid in coaching team members. Must exhibit superior communication skills and is comfortable with communication to Senior Leadership. 
We develop using current technologies such as Java, J2EE, JSF, JSON/REST, JAX-WS, WSDL, OOAD. Our applications are multi-tiered and utilize Oracle DB, PL/SQL. 
We strive for every interaction to be driven by our CORE values: Insightful Expertise, Integrity is Everything, Service Excellence, Inspiring Innovation, Each Person Counts, Results-Driven, &amp; Social Responsibility.

*  Execute as a team player and leader; must be equally comfortable orchestrating team success or yielding control to the Product Owner and Team.

*  Identify and track key dependencies for the Scrum teams
