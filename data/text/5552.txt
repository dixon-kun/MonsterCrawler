**Job summary**
The Red Hat Customer Experience and Engagement (CEE) Analytics team is looking for a Website Data Analyst to join us in Raleigh, NC. In this role, you will provide essential analysis of traffic patterns and user behavior in the Red Hat Customer Portal to improve site usability and content relevance. You&#8217;ll also assist with strategic initiatives, and provide actionable, data-based insights to stakeholders. As a Website Data Analyst, you will work with business leads, product management, web and content development teams, and other stakeholders to understand and achieve business priorities through data analysis and reporting. We&#8217;ll need you to be able to translate complex findings into meaningful stories and visualizations for business stakeholders.
**Primary job responsibilities**
+ Analyze web analytics data and develop reports and dashboards to optimize outcomes and influence business decisions
+ Lead tagging and tracking implementation, maintenance, and improvement initiatives for the Customer Portal
+ Translate web analytics data and advise teams that support the Customer Portal
+ Monitor multiple data streams, including search and user data, to report on specific trends and assist with Customer Portal optimization decisions
+ Perform ad hoc analysis and provide analytical consultation and support to key stakeholders
+ Provide content gap and user behavior analysis, and work with content and web development teams on potential solutions
+ Assist in defining and tracking key performance indicators (KPIs) for the Customer Portal
+ Provide measurement support and consultation on click tracking, A/B testing, and other web optimization initiatives
**Required skills**
+ Proficiency in collecting and analyzing data from web reporting tools; experience managing Adobe Analytics or Google Analytics
+ Advanced data visualization and executive-level reporting skills
+ Solid organizational, problem-solving, and analytical skills
The following are considered a plus:
+ Bachelor's degree in IT, computer science, statistics, or data science
+ Experience with tag management tools like Adobe Dynamic Tag Management or Google Tag Manager
+ Experience with tools like Tableau or Qlik (Sense, View)
