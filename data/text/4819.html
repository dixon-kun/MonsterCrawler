Be a part of a new team leading the way to leverage Big Data at Alliant Energy.

We have created a new team that will lead a transformation in how we leverage data to achieve our strategic objectives. As a Data Scientist you will be responsible for discovering actionable insights hidden in vast amounts of data through use of data integration, data modeling, statistical and visualization techniques to enable better decision making.

In this role, you will be part of developing the analytic program from defining new process to support analytics to establishing the infrastructure needed to enable analytics.

This role offers:

* Creativity

* Innovation

* Leadership

* Challenging work

* Exploring multiple data facets/solutions

Help us unlock the value of our data by considering this opportunity at Alliant Energy.

Applications will be accepted until a qualified pool of candidates is received.

Job Responsibilities

* Designs experiments, tests hypotheses and builds models to solve analytics problems and communicates results and methodologies to the customer.

* Works with the key stakeholders to define use case requirements to ensure that results are meaningful and enable improved decision making.

* Works with the business to assess the validity of the data and achieve minimum levels of quality.

* Combines multiple data sources (both internal and external)

* Leads change and applies change management principles to drive cultural transformation and improve the adoption of analytics.

* Collaborates with others responsible for analytics.

* Assures the creation and maintenance of measurement systems for analytics including the tracking of results.

* Works with technology teams to develop and implement analytical solutions in a production environment

* Performs all other duties as assigned.

Job Qualifications Education and Experience Requirements:

* This position requires a Bachelor's degree from an accredited college with a degree in math, statistics, analytics, operations research, computer science or other applicable fields and six years of direct experience in a related field (statistics, performance management, analytics, research).

Preferred Experience:

* Master's degree preferred.

* 2+ years of experience working as a data scientist preferred

Required Skills:

* Demonstrated Proficiency in programming languages such as Python and SQL.

* Proficiency in Visualization Tools such as Tableau, Power BI.

* Experience working with Hadoop.

* Demonstrated knowledge with pattern recognition, machine learning, predictive modeling, and descriptive inferential statistics preferred.

* Demonstrated effective leadership skills.

* Ability to work effectively and lead change in a diverse work environment.

* NOTE: All candidates selected for employment with Alliant Energy will be required to successfully complete a drug screen and background check.
