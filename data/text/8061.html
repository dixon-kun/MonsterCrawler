Who You Are:

* Data driven science analyst passionate about leading and executing customer insights such as Data Validation, Key Value Identification, Value Measurement and Price Strategy analyses

* Thrive in a customer / project facing role

* Willing and able to translate intricate science insights, superb written and oral communication

* Excited and energized by working with data

* Smart yet humble

* Able to travel to Central and South America (25-40%)

What's in Your Tool Box:

* Past experience managing retail analytics data

* Spanish fluency to work with our LATAM clients and counterparts

* Prowess using analytic scripting tools such as Python, R, Alteryx, Matlab

* Strong SQL skills and experience with relational databases (MS SQL Server, Teradata, Oracle)

* Hands on experience with data validation / cleansing and outlier identification methodologies

* Masters or PhD in a relevant degree program
