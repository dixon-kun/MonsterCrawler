           Senior Data Engineer - Advanced Analytics
           We are looking for a passionate technologist with a knack for delivery to join a team of engineers to build automated, cloud based environments to support our Data Science and Quantitative Analyst communities. Our goal is to re-imagine how predictive models are delivered at Capital One, and we will not rest until Capital One is on the leading edge of how financial institutions rapidly build and progress predictive models from an idea to production execution.
            On any given day you will:
            Design and code full stack data products within our AWS environments
            Work directly with the Product Manager and end-users to constantly deliver products in a highly collaborative and agile environment
            Help grow and mentor team memberson the fine art of data engineering and application delivery
            Be an evangelist at hackathons and other external events for the FinTech industry
            Who You Are:
            Curious. You ask why, you explore, you're not afraid of challenging the status quo
            Passionate. You care about growing others and bringing them together around what&#8217;s possible. You&#8217;ve started Meetup groups or taught courses because you love enabling others.
            Communicator. You can communicate complex ideas clearly to both technical and non-technical audiences, your team knows their priorities and so does your manager.
            Leader. You create high performing teams everywhere you go, with a focus on enabling an inclusive, creative, nurturing, and fun team environment.
            Do-er. You have a bias toward action, you try things, and you wear your failures as a badge of honor
            Fearless. Big, undefined problems don't frighten you.
            Basic Qualifications:
            Bachelors Degree or military experience
            At least 3 years of experience in an object oriented or functional languages(Java, Scala)
            At least 2 years of experience delivering Cloud or Hadoop based solutions within an agile delivery environment
            At least 2 years of experience with Unix/Linux systems with scripting experience in Shell, Perl or Python
            At least 2 years of experience working with distributed computed (Spark)
            At least 1 year of experience working with Amazon Web Services (AWS)
            At least 1 year of experience working with automated build and continuous integration systems such as Ansible, Chef or Puppet, and Jenkins
            At least 1 year of experience working with container solutions (Docker)
