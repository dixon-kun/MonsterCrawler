        PURPOSE AND OBJECTIVES
        SAP is the world's leading business software company with more than 70,000 employees all over the world and more than 296000 customers in 190 countries, helping businesses of all sizes, in all
        industries run better.
        SAP Custom Development, a division within SAP, is a global organization dedicated to delivering innovative custom solutions to meet our customers unique business process needs. We deliver a full lifecycle of custom development services from strategic planning, risk assessment, development and quality assurance to maintenance. With development centers in US, Canada, Germany, Brazil, India, China &amp; Japan, we are able to leverage a global delivery model that utilizes the specific strengths of each location. To ensure excellence in execution, all development is delivered by following our solidly defined methodology. SAP Custom Development helps customers maximize the value of our customers' technology investments and can ultimately help to enhance their competitive advantage. SAP Custom Development provides development services in the area of advanced analytics to customers, helping them to improve their business performance based on scientific analysis of large amounts of business data, predictive modeling and optimization algorithms.
        The application of data science techniques in SAP customer projects is extremely diverse and ranges from supply chain optimization solutions over point-of-sales data analytics in retail to applications in machine sensor analytics and predictive maintenance, to name just a few examples.
        EXPECTATIONS AND TASKS
        SAP offers experienced data scientists the opportunity to boost their career, working in a high-performance development/consulting team that is focused on innovation in the intersection of business optimization, mathematics and software technology.
        A Data Scientist is expected to lead small project teams in the execution of our customer projects. These teams execute the mathematical modeling of our customers' business problems, develop algorithms and applications to solve these problems, and help our customers directly to understand and implement the results of the analysis. In this role, the Data Scientist works directly with industry experts and technology experts to translate our customers' business needs into mathematical models and present and deliver a solution to the client. The Data Scientist is also a supporter and expert advisor to SAP's sales team (Go-To-Market) in order to generate new customer business in the area of data science. This position requires the ability to work and communicate as a part of a high performance team in an international environment.
