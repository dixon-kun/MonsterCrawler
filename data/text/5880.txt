        What You're Applying For:
        The Corporate Strategy Manager will work in conjunction with the VP and Director of Corporate Strategy to develop and drive the long-term strategic vision of Edmunds, launch cross-functional strategic initiatives, and evaluate new opportunities for Edmunds to determine size, attractiveness and fit with the company's goals. In addition, the Manager will partner with the organization to identify and address needs within the business. Ultimately, the Manager is responsible for identifying areas of opportunity within the business, evaluating and analyzing these opportunities, and working with the strategy team and the rest of the organization to develop recommendations and execution plans.
        What You'll Do:
        * In concert with the VP and Director, Corporate Strategy, the manager will work with executives and top business leaders to develop the long-term strategy for Edmunds and translate this into effective and actionable strategies for the business
        * Partner with the business to support the ongoing corporate strategy process and help drive the implementation of key strategic initiatives, including development of roadmaps and support processes
        * Be an emissary for the strategy team to help provide a strategic perspective on critical questions with business leaders of the company
        * Identify new areas of opportunity for Edmunds, either internally or externally, and develop plans and analyses for evaluating these areas of opportunity
        * Evaluate and size ad-hoc opportunities either identified by business partners or external parties
        * Support the VP, Partnerships and the Finance team to evaluate potential acquisition opportunities
        * In concert with Analytics and Data Science, conduct analyses around strategic options or initiatives
        * Build and maintain knowledge of key trends in the automotive and tech industries, as well as competitive positioning, to inform and shape Edmunds' positioning in the market and strategic initiatives
        * Help craft critical corporate or partner-facing communication and presentations to deliver clear, compelling stories
        What You Need:
        * 2-4 years of strategy experience, in either management consulting or a top-tier strategy department
        * Bachelor's degree from a top university
        * Superior analytics skills and attention to detail
        * Ability to translate analyses into implications and the "so-what" for the business
        * Entrepreneurial and proactive approach, with desire to join and help shape a newly formed team
        * Interest and demonstrated ability in identifying areas of opportunity within businesses and developing clear, executable solutions
        * Experience in developing and testing hypotheses through structured inquiry and analysis
        * Strong executive presence and communication skills, both written and oral
        * Courage to stand up for own perspective, even when it may not be the most common opinion
        * Demonstrated ability to work cross-functionally with range of people with different styles, backgrounds, and experiences and to drive these teams to action, even without formal authority
        * History of being a team player who quickly builds trust and credibility through relationships
        * Tech or automotive experience preferred
