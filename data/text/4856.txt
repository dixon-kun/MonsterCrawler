        Our Teams:
        Dealer.com's teams are made up of tech-savvy and adaptable thinkers with diverse skill sets who help drive our success. Our professionals are committed to changing the way people shop for and purchase vehicles through our top-of-the-line, innovative digital marketing software solutions.
        Job Summary:
        The Senior Operational Insights Analyst is an experienced member of the Media Insights team, providing custom analysis and insights to our internal teams. The Senior Operational Insights Analyst will leverage skills in business requirement gathering, project management, data acquisition, data analysis and data visualization skills to provide clear reporting, insight and recommendations to internal business units. This position will report to the Manager of Operational Insights.
        The Senior Operational Insights Analyst is expected to be comfortable working somewhat autonomously, identifying stakeholders, project dependencies, facilitating communications and managing towards the successful completion of analysis and reporting projects. This position should have experience developing custom reports and tailored insights with visualizations that help tell the story within the data. This position will need to leverage a working knowledge of various source systems, the ability to extract data from these sources, and then provide actionable recommendations to drive operational efficiencies and ultimately improve client experience. This position is expected to become a knowledge expert with to the data that is unique to the business unit to which they are aligned. They will work with other teams and departments to ensure data context, accuracy and use cases are understood so data is not misinterpreted when analyzed. Finally, as a senior member of the team, you are expected to provide some level of mentoring and knowledge share to other team members, pulling from several years of related working experience.
        Your Role:
        * Provide comprehensive data analysis, reporting, and recommendations across to support the business unit to which are you assigned
        * Collaborate with stakeholders to develop and refine reporting dashboards for internal business units and teams dependent on that BU's data
        * Provide insights using data visualization and story telling techniques to clearly address the needs and questions of stakeholders
        * Facilitate cross training and mentoring fellow team members on internal reporting tools, data analysis techniques, internal systems/data, processes and best practices
        * Ability to think creatively to incorporate new types of data into custom insights that provide direction guidance towards business questions
        * Create and maintain documentation supporting the data context, reporting and information sharing tools
        * Oversee and monitor data portfolio to ensure that analysis efforts are not duplicated
        * Develop complex MySQL queries and data extraction methodologies
        * Fulfill ad hoc reporting requests as needed
        * Bachelor's degree required. Concentration in Business Analytics, Business Administration or Data Science preferred
        * 4 years of analysis and data visualization experience, preferably in the digital media fields
        * Experience working with large sets of data and pulling directly from various data sources
        * Experience in using Microsoft Excel, Tableau, Business Objects and/or other dashboard development tools in order to create actionable insights
        * Experience accessing and querying SQL and MySQL based data storage
        * Excellent written, oral, and project management skills to see insight projects through from initial request to delivery
        * High level understanding of digital advertising, the primary channels (Search, Display, Social, Video) and their uses for influencing consumer behavior
        * High level understanding of website usability and standard metrics used to define user website interactions
        * High level understanding of client experience metrics and their uses within front line teams (Hold times, ticket volumes, portfolio management, etc.)
        * Must be self-motivated and passionate about data while promoting learning and assisting others in using or understanding reporting systems.
