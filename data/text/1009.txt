            About the Group:
           The Commercial Strategy team is a group of approximately 15 resourceful and highly motivated professionals within the broader 50+-person Commercial Strategy &amp; Innovation organization dedicated to setting the segment&#8217;s strategic direction and exploring the potential to create disruptive, digital products. The team selectively engages on the highest-impact issues that drive the direction of the Commercial Bank, collaborating closely with Company leaders and commercial banking lines-of-business.
           Capital One&#8217;s unique business advantage derives from its dedication to rigorous analysis and strategy, inherited from Founder and CEO Rich Fairbank. Commercial Strategy &amp; Innovation is a critical component of this advantage, and is helping to chart the Company&#8217;s path forward by working closely with technologists, data scientists, and designers to upend old models of banking and transform the industry.
            About the Role:
           Commercial Strategy &amp; Innovation is seeking high-performing self-starters eager to disrupt traditional modes of banking and define the future of Capital One. Senior Managers will lead and contribute to the development of credible, data-driven recommendations within collaborative strategy case teams. Hires are expected to be inventive problem-solvers both comfortable addressing ambiguous questions and executing sophisticated analyses. They will manage workstreams and present their findings and recommendations to senior leaders, with whom they will be thought-partners. Successful Managers will have the opportunity to work closely with the innovation teams located in our Digital Lab to identify the ways in which we can deliver revolutionary new products to customers and harness the explosion data and digital tools to change our business.
            Core Responsibilities:
            Work with cross-functional teams and leverage data and analysis to solve complex business problems

            Build working relationships with Commercial Bank lines-of-business and other partner teams
            Develop casework findings and recommendations for senior leaders, including Company senior management
            Assume leadership role on project and people management
            Promote the culture and values of Capital One and the Commercial Strategy &amp; Innovation group
