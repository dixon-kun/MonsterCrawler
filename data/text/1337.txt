        Title: Data Wrangler - 18930
        Your Responsibilities
        As a Data Wrangler you will work in an Analytics Team within the Enterprise Analytics Accelerator (EAA) to generate business insights on transformational, high value analytics projects together with a Data Catalyst and Data Scientist. Using analytical programming languages, you will create automated processes for data gathering, cleansing &amp; improve efficiency and accuracy of data collection, entry &amp; verification. In addition, you will:
        * Work with the business and IT domains to design and integrate the data required for Data Scientists to generate insights &amp; provide data access to Data Scientists for modeling or predictive analysis.
        * Validate data quality in conjunction with respective data stewards by developing reports and tools to monitor and visualize data quality.
        * Perform activities to support all aspects of bringing new data sources into the EAA.
        * Collaborate with IT domains to implement data ingestion, data models and automated operational metrics for consistent high quality data loading to EAA.
        * Locate and acquire data for designated projects within the Enterprise Analytics Accelerator (EAA).
        * Combine data sources under supervision from Lead Data Wrangler and makes data available to be used to create analytics models.
        * Organize and clean the acquired data to remove quality issues.
        * Support the effort in closing systemic data quality gaps by working with business and across IT domains to identify process or data management changes, integration opportunities with other data groups, and/or data training needs within the business.
        * Align with other IT domains to ensure the architecture of the data meets the needs of the EAA analytics teams and aligns with overall IT data management strategies.
        * Participate in creating analytics models as required by the EAA Data Scientists to ensure the data is valid, accurate, and applicable for approved EAA projects.
        What Skills You Need
        * 3 + years of experience managing databases.
        * 2 + years of experience with using and application of statistical methodologies and analytics.
        * Experience with data base design, administration and performance and can apply these skills in complex situations leading to the acquisition of appropriate data sets.
        * Experience with advanced data gathering and analysis techniques, including statistical analyses.
        What Makes You Stand Out
        Other areas of experience or interest that will help us place you on the right team include:
        * Programming language skills such as SQL, Python, R, PYSPARK and other analytics tools.
        * Knowledge of Data Lake/Data Cloud technologies
        * Master's degree in programming or similar field or equivalent experience
        Education:
        Ideally, you will have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Economics, or equivalent quantitative field.
