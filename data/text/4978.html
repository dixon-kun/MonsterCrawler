ADP is hiring a Senior Director Technology. In this position you will be responsible for the strategy, design, administration, and integrity of ADP Omni-Channel technology within the National Accounts business unit.

Automatic Data Processing, Inc. (ADP) is one of the largest providers of business processing and cloud-based solutions -- including payroll, talent management, human resource management, benefits administration and time and attendance -- to employers around the world. ADP builds configurable solutions that can fit your organization's unique goals and increase your productivity. For more information about ADP, visit the company's Web site at http://www.ADP.com.

Responsibilities

Provide strategic direction and planning on all areas related to Omni-Channel technology, including telephony, email, chat, mobile, and self-help, to start.
Drive strategy to align technology to support business needs in a consultative service environment
* Lead efforts to modernize global service models
* Partner with business leaders to drive process improvements throughout the business
* Partner with global technology to accelerate the pace of technological change
* Responsibility for multiple teams driving technology initiatives

Management:

* Oversee multiple managers/project leads on a variety of diverse projects
* Ensure project objectives, schedules, and quality standards are met
* Allocate personnel in accord with priorities
* Organization Development and Leadership
* Develop, hire, and lead teams focused on leading aspects of enterprise technology
* Be an agent for change
* Management of year end process including monitoring of all applications and products
* Responsible for managing several critical business initiative efforts.
* Fully analyzes cost/benefit impact of business initiatives
* Prepare annual expense budget for multiple departments
* Prepare salary planning
* Monitor actual budget performance throughout the year
* Participates in key staff meetings, project reviews and organizational activities
* Facilitates the generation and implementation of procedures and standards to ensure configuration management compliance with development effectiveness objectives.

Omni-Channel Technology:
* Oversee all major project activities within assigned territory/BU's
* Serve as an escalation point for local management; assist in resolving chronic service problems, system malfunctions, vendor issues, and other service-related problems.
* Track and report activities across all relevant channels.
* Provides consulting and management expertise to the implementation of major system installations within the division
* Evaluate and recommend hardware, software, and network services as appropriate to meet national capacity requirements, address special applications, and achieve cost/service benefits.

QUALIFICATIONS REQUIRED:
Thorough background (10-15 years) in omni-channel, IVR, and telephony systems in a large scale enterprise environment

B.S. or M.S. degree in Computer Science or related field (or equivalent in education and experience)
Experience driving omni-channel strategy and implementation in a $2B or larger enterprise
Experience with the Genesys, NICE, and AriA CIMplicity technologies
