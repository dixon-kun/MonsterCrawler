        We're looking for an Industry Relations Manager to join our Sales team in NYC!
        The Industry Relations Manager is responsible for building strong and mutually beneficial relationships within advertising industry associations. You will own the IAS relationship with important trade organizations with the end goal of communicating needs and opportunities back to IAS. This is a critical position that will ensure that IAS participates in key industry projects that drive the industry forward. Superior communication and persuasiveness skills are an absolute must to be successful in this position.
        Objectives:
        * Be the primary point of contact for advertising industry associations including the AAAA's, ANA, ARF, IAB, MRC and others.
        * Evangelize IAS products, services, thought leadership and education within these organizations
        Responsibilities:
        * Seeks to create meaningful and mutually beneficial relationships within industry associations
        * Manage industry outreach and work with partner associations to promote IAS products, services and thought leadership
        * Provide support to partner associations and be the communications conduit back into our organization.
        * Provide regular updates to IAS staff of the priority projects happening within each association
        * Alerts internal teams to opportunities to participate in relevant industry projects
        * Co-ordinates IAS participation within these organizations across the cross-functional teams including product management, executive leadership, marketing etc.
        * Include IAS information where relevant in educational programs
        * Shares trends, insights, case studies and IAS product updates with partners
        Requirements:
        * 10 years experience in digital media, ad tech or digital research solutions
        * Deep working knowledge of digital media landscape
        * Senior relationships within industry associations a plus
        * Highly articulate and persuasive
