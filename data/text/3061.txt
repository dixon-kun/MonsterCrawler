        Data Science Intern Job Description
        As a Data Science Engineer Intern at Micron Technology Inc., you will research and employ techniques and theories drawn from areas of mathematics, statistics, and information technology to uncover patterns in data from which predictive models, actionable insights, and solutions can be developed. This will involve one or more of the following methods or subjects: signal processing, probability models, machine learning, statistical learning, computer programming, data engineering, pattern recognition and learning, visualization, uncertainty modeling, Big Data, and high performance computing. You will interact with Data Scientists and engineering teams to identify questions and issues for data analysis projects. In this position, you will help develop and code software programs, algorithms and/or automated processes to cleanse, integrate and evaluate large datasets from multiple disparate sources.
        Qualifications
        * Desire to work on data analysis projects that will improve yield, quality, reduce costs and cycle time, and/or optimize business processes.
        * Ability to extract data from different databases via SQL and other query languages and applying data cleansing, outlier identification, and missing data techniques.
        * Ability to work with small, big, structured, semi-structured, and unstructured data.
        * Experience with or desire to learn:
        * R, JMP, and/or other statistical software including scripting capability for automating analyses,
        * Machine learning and other advanced analytical methods,
        * Hadoop and/or Teradata, and
        * Python, HIVE, PIG, Spark, etc.
        Education Requirements:
        A B.Sc. degree in statistics, physics, computer science, engineering, applied mathematics, etc. with the intentions of completing a MS or Ph.D. in one of these fields.
