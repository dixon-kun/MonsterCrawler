Description

As a not-for-profit organization, Partners HealthCare is committed to supporting patient care, research, teaching, and service to the community by leading innovation across our system. Several of our hospitals are teaching affiliates of Harvard Medical School, and our system is a national leader in biomedical research.

We’re focused on a people-first culture for our system’s patients and our professional family. That’s why we provide our employees with more ways to achieve their potential. Partners HealthCare is committed to aligning our employees’ personal aspirations with projects that match their capabilities and creating a culture that empowers our managers to become trusted mentors. We support each member of our team to own their personal development—and we recognize success at every step.

General Summary/Overview

The Center for Clinical Data Science (CCDS) is an exciting new undertaking by MGH, in conjunction with Partners HealthCare, that aims to transform healthcare through data science. We are seeking an individual to interface between the IT infrastructure and data science environments in order to optimize our operations for healthcare discovery. In this crucial role, the candidate will have broad responsibilities in supporting research and development of machine learning, including programming, documenting, implementing open source (and commercial) tools/applications, developing imaging pipelines, cohort management tools for accessing various data medical repositories including multiple PACS.

To be successful, this individual must be eager to learn new platforms and approaches, extrapolate on current methodologies and thrive in a team environment solving complex problems.

Principal Duties and Responsibilities

• Support CCDS infrastructure including data interfaces, processing pipelines and general programming tasks.
• Work directly with business owners and application/informatics development groups as a member of their team in design, testing, implementation, debugging, problem analysis, recovery and documentation pipelines—owning the pipeline from idea to support. In conjunction, the project owner provides project management of active projects. Participate in architecting and designing pipeline systems and services that are sustainable and extensible.
• Analyze and resolve customer and technical problems in developed/implemented systems.
• Analyze results and implement changes to improve performance, processing and utilization of computing resources. Provide break/fix support, setup/installation support, escalation support, and solutions support.
• Develop and maintain system documentation as well as training and reference materials for the community using wiki and knowledge management tools.
• Administer Windows, Linux, UNIX, and web applications as appropriate.
• Use the Partners HealthCare values to govern decisions, actions and behaviors. These values guide how we get our work done: Patients, Affordability, Accountability & Service Commitment, Decisiveness, Innovation & Thoughtful Risk; and how we treat each other: Diversity & Inclusion, Integrity & Respect, Learning, Continuous Improvement & Personal Growth, Teamwork & Collaboration.
• Perform other duties as assigned.

Qualifications

• Bachelor's degree in Computer Science required or equivalent combination of education and experience. Master's degree a plus.
• 5+ years of IT experience
• Working knowledge of at least two scripting or object oriented languages: .NET, JAVA, PHP, Python, Perl, R, UNIX shell scripting, with proven ability to quickly learn others.
• 4+ years of experience with development and management of web applications and servers (JBOSS, Tomcat, Eclipse) in a database environment on Windows and Unix/Linux systems
• 2+ years of experience supporting informatics systems in a biological, medical, or basic science research and development environments.
• Knowledge of Relational Database Systems--Oracle / Microsoft SQL Server / Postgres / MySQL—and developing/understanding relational database structures and query language (SQL).
• Experience supporting related components of 3rd-party and open-source applications.

Skills/Abilities/Competencies Required



• Excellent project management skills (ability to multitask and prioritize work requirements) with a strong commitment to customer service
• Must be capable of contributing within a team, exhibit a high level of initiative, and have an eagerness to learn new technologies
• Ability to effectively translate business requirements to functional requirements
• Strong problem solving and negotiation skills
• Excellent interpersonal skills to effectively communicate with diverse, cross functional teams including staff at all levels of the organization including both technical and non-technical personnel.
• Requires minimal direction from leadership and possesses the ability to learn quickly
• Ability to successfully negotiate and collaborate with others of different skill sets, backgrounds and levels within and external to the organization
