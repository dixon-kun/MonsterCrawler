UL is a global independent safety science company with more than a century of expertise innovating safety solutions from the public adoption of electricity to new breakthroughs in areas such as sustainability, renewable energy, mobile payment security and nanotechnology. Dedicated to promoting safe living and working environments, UL helps safeguard people, products and places in important ways, facilitates trade and provides peace of mind.


Function:

Under minimal supervision, develops standard work and drives completion of tasks in support of the Datacentricity Program within the Commercial and Industrial Business Unit. May work as a liaison between Operations and IT to assure goals and objectives are met within an arranged timeframe. Defines and manages tasks associated with data migration from legacy systems to the Information Platform. Ensures these solutions meet or exceed program and business expectations.

Responsibilities:

1.Assists with the development and management of new data handling and migration processes and with the improvement of current processes in support of UL’s new datacentric environment.

2.Coordinates and collaborates with Industry Leads as needed to gain agreement on approach for the forward and reverse migration of each industry’s assets (models) and associated attributes into the Information Platform.

3.Works with IT system support and the datacentricity team to resolve questions, problems, and enhancement requests in support of data migration efforts.

4.Consults with business and technical teams to refine necessary requirements.

5.Supports the coordination of tasks directly related to migration activities, including pre and post-data cleansing and non-PCD migration tasks.

6.Develops, documents and shares best practices in the form of Standard Work for all aspects of data migration workflow.

7.Supports the Global Datacentricity Training as necessary.

8.Supports the development of monitoring reports and validation processes for the product data and overall Datacentricity Program.

9.Supports the development of technical enhancements including trouble shooting solutions, business user testing, and production deployment with a focus on data migration workflow.

10.Assists with the mass migration of product data from existing sources to the Information Platform.

11. Performs other duties as directed.

Qualifications:

  • General data/engineering competency to understand engineering terminology and systems/tools, manage and maintain information around products, data, governance, SOPs, etc. (B.S. in Data Science or Engineering is preferred but not required)
  • Strong project management and organizational skills, with an emphasis on managing completion of tasks by non-direct reports.
  • Strong analytical and problem solving skills and excellent verbal and written communication skills.
  • Excellent communication skills working with people of various levels and units in the company.
  • Demonstrated ability to manage multiple projects with a high attention to detail, while maintaining excellent quality and superior customer service.
  • Experience working in a matrix organization with teams that may be virtual in nature.
  • Comfort and flexibility working in a dynamic environment.
  • Knowledge related to computer technology, network infrastructure, systems and applications, security, and storage.
  • Intermediate knowledge and experience with Microsoft Office Suite with proficiency in Excel.
  • Intermediate knowledge and experience of UL systems, such as PCD, LIS, ODS, IQ, etc.
  • Ability to work effectively with internal and external IT support, senior leadership, project teams and individuals
  • Ability to perform in a dynamic project management role requiring pro-active task, resource and time management.
  • Ability to troubleshoot issues and assist with support.
  • Ability to collaborate, engage and manage resources outside of the Datacentricity Team.
  • Ability to influence resources towards on-time task completion.
  • Ability to work remotely (self-directed; minimal supervision).
  • Ability to identify when and how to escalate issues
