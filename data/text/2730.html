McIntire Solutions is seeking a Technology Insertion Engineer with 10 years of experience innovating the application of emerging, disruptive, and advanced technologies to Intelligence Community missions.


  • Assesses emerging technologies and their contribution to advance mission objectives, enhance analytic capabilities and services to meet user needs, and complies with enterprise technical architecture & standards
  • Identify tactical and strategic insertion of emerging technology as it aligns to the customer’s Technology Roadmap and Strategic Plan
  • Lead the integration of traditional, non-traditional, and emerging technology solutions related to cloud technologies, data analytics, social media analytics, security, data extraction, machine learning, and language translation into the 24/7 mission operational baseline
  • Serve as an advisor to government staff and end users on advanced technical research studies and applications
  • Lead and coordinate specific technology transition events, ensuring all required mission capabilities & services are delivered into operations in an efficient and coordinated manner, with a minimum of disruption to the operational baseline.
  • Provide technical direction for development, engineering, interfacing, integration and testing of components of new systems
  • Develop advanced technological ideas and guides their development throughout the systems engineering lifecycle

  • Bachelor’s degree required from an accredited college or university, in Engineering, Information Technology, Information Services, Computer Science, Data Science, Neuroscience or related discipline; Master’s degree preferred
  • 10+ years of systems engineering experience with a t least 4 years’ experience applying emerging technology into operational use
  • Experience with one or more of the following: cloud technologies, data analytics, social media analytics, security, data extraction, machine learning, or language translation
  • Experience transitioning applications, software, hardware and data from legacy systems
  • Knowledge of IC ITE future architecture and services
  • Ability to produce timely, logical, and concise work products requiring little or no editing
  • Ability to identify, research, and produce documentation and briefings under tight deadlines
  • Superior oral and written communication skills; outstanding interpersonal and customer service skills, including the ability to work effectively independently and in a team environment
  • Ability to work with senior officials, to build and sustain professional networks in a diverse, fast-paced, multi-task environment, and to manage competing priorities
  • Adaptable and flexible to perform a range of tasks and work with various NRO directorates
  • Proactive in developing innovative ideas and in communicating them effectively to Government staff
  • Active TS/SCI clearance with polygraph

Desired Qualifications

  • Knowledge of C2S, IC Gov Cloud, and Amazon Web Services
  • Knowledge of Intelligence Community advanced research topics
  • Experience in IARPA, DARPA, In-Q-Tel, or other Science & Technology organizations within the IC or DoD
