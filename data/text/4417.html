Director of Engineering

On the Job….

* As the Senior /Director of Engineering, you will provide strong technical leadership to major projects as well as manage teams of engineers.

* You will build and scale our infrastructure; you will construct algorithms and new distributed protocols.

* You will oversee the work of talented, cross-functional teams to strategically plan, design and develop infrastructure solutions to help drive our continued growth all vital to the success of our business.

* Contribute substantively to our technical architecture decisions for site projects and lead efforts to build and maintain an extremely large scale software services platform

* Recruit and retain top tier engineers, hire and mentor tech leaders, and provide career development and professional growth for our talented engineers and managers

* Continue to add to our company culture; maintain a happy and productive engineering organization

About you…..

* At least 10 years of experience and knowledge of commercial software development and relevant experience in leadership roles within engineering organizations and recruiting senior engineers.

* Good understanding of algorithms, data structures, performance optimization techniques, and object-oriented programming

* Structured thinker, effective communicator, with excellent programming and analytic skills

* Experience working on advertising systems

* Java

* Algorithms

* Data Structures

* Performance Optimization

* Object-Oriented Design

* Advertising Systems

* Master's Degree or PhD in Computer Science or related field

* Strategic mind with strong operational, project management and technical architecture skills

* Demonstrated ability to deliver products on time

* Ability to excel within a fast paced startup environment with a flat organizational structure, and provide hands-on leadership to our top-notch engineers

* A track record of highly influential technical and leadership achievements; strong and current technical contributions

* Experience in RTB (real-time bidding) a PLUS!-

Desired:

* Knowledge of markets, auctions and/or advertising

* Experience with Deep Learning

Values

Innovation, Trust, Excellence, Collaboration and Customer Obsession. These are our corporate values and we look for people who align with them as much as we do.
