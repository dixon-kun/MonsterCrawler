Big Data Analytics Solution Architect / Lead Engineer

Do you have the basic Big Data skills and want to take your skills to the next level? We can help you become a complete Data expert. Join this startup-like group within a large organization to get the best of both worlds.

Work with us to solve some of the most difficult Big Data problems in the world. Using the most innovative Analytics tools (from Hadoop/Spark to Elasticsearch, Machine Learning with R, Hive/Pig, Tableau and much more), you will be a key contributor to teams developing sophisticated algorithms, working closely with end-customers to solve real world problems.

From IoT to 5G, our team successfully addresses challenges in the Big Data domain daily. You will have a chance to work on myriad technologies in this space including WiFi, VoLTE and other Internet technologies, Customer Experience, Finance/Billing etc.

As a Big Data and Analytics Architect for Ericsson, you will be designing, developing, and deploying analytics methods for large data sets. You will be working with Big Data tools and frameworks across data storage, extraction, normalization, validation, statistical modeling, machine learning, and visualization. Your role will vary depending on the project needs at client sites.

Ericsson’s Big Data and Analytics practice architects provide services across the entire value chain from Big Data Infrastructure, Data Science, and Analytics. We apply data science methods in combination with our domain and industry expertise to develop actionable insights which cannot be derived using conventional techniques. We have applied this approach to use cases across marketing, network operations, and customer experience management. We are growing our team as we continue to address more of these opportunities.

Technical Qualifications:

  • Ability to architect large scale big data analytic systems.
  • Thorough understanding and hands on experience on Hadoop/Spark based distributed storage and computing frameworks.
  • Good understanding of machine learning techniques and statistical methods such as predictive modeling, pattern recognition, text mining, sentiment analysis, index building, clustering.
  • Hands on experience on AI related technologies and Data Science.
  • Proficiency in several technologies in the Big Data ecosystem, such as SQL, NoSQL, database design, ETL, Talend, Hadoop, MapReduce, Hive, Pig, Impala, Spark, HBase, Flume, Mahout, Scoop, Kafka, H2O
  • Experience in programming scripting languages such as Python, Java, Ruby, Perl, Scala
  • Experience in analytics tools such as R, SAS, SPSS, RapidMiner, Knime
  • Real-Time analytics and batch processing
  • Strong experience in architecting analytical applications in cloud environment such as Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure and Openstack

General Qualifications:

  • 2+ years of experience with the Big Data ecosystem. Prefer 7 years of experience in Analytics/BI.
  • Excellent customer interaction skills to present proposals, articulate analytics value to customers and lead customer engagements with a matrix structure
  • Desirable to have strong knowledge in Wireless domain and consumer data analytics
  • 5-10 years of consulting/solution delivery/engineering experience in Wireless/ Telecommunication/Cable or Enterprise market.
  • At least 5 years of experience in Wireless/Telecommunication/Cable
  • Strong technical team leadership, mentorship and ability collaborate with peer teams.
  • Ability to develop technical relationships with partner companies and vendors.
  • Graduate degree in Computer Science, Statistics or an equivalent Engineering degree required. Master’s/PhD is a big plus

Travel Requirements: 25% or more
