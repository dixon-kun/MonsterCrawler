        POSITION DESCRIPTION:
        The quest for better health and high quality care at more affordable costs is the driving force behind Medtronic's commitment to be a leader in the transformation of healthcare, partnering closely with payers, providers, regulators, patients or private organizations. The Data Science Program Manager will be a key member of the Corporate Data Science organization, and the Provider Data Networks team in particular, that is driven by this quest.
        The Data Science Program Manager will help architect new strategies and methodologies to source, process and use real-world data from provider data networks. We are driven to shape the future of data-driven healthcare decisions and to provide reliable real-world data for various evidentiary needs &#8211; from clinical research, to post-market product surveillance, or value-based healthcare.
        The candidate will lead, direct and organize Data Science pilots in real-world clinical settings, partnering with internal and external stakeholders to inform valuable, transformative business strategies for Medtronic and the industry as a whole. You will connect teams on advanced quantitative tools and modeling techniques in order to derive business insights, solve complex business problems and drive systematic, data-driven decision-making. You will also develop new knowledge and methodologies for this fast-paced group, and support its positioning and deployment across the enterprise through consistent dissemination of our activities and learnings.
        You will be counted upon to partner closely with various internal and external stakeholders to develop and execute a meaningful roadmap for a greater understanding of the feasibility and usability of real-world data and standard work processes that enable Data Science to build capabilities, drive collaboration, and create synergies through high-quality work.
        This position will be responsible for the successful execution of selected projects and the implementation of new solutions. The Data Science Program Manager will also be the central point of contact in the team for managing or coordinating all technical activities required to define, design, develop and deliver novel data science projects.
        The position operates in a cross-business unit and cross-functional matrix environment, collaborating with colleagues from the fields of Clinical Research, IT, Advanced Analytics, Quality, Research &amp; Development, Reimbursement, Connected Care, Biostatistics, and Regulatory.
        POSITION RESPONSIBILITIES:
        * Be the go-to person and consultant in the team for all data or systems- related questions; manage all related communication with key partner organizations throughout various levels of the organization, ranging from individual project contributors to leadership.
        * Interface with internal and external customers and stakeholders to understand data science needs and solutions, and collaborate effectively, working toward a shared and aligned goal, executing predefined project plans. Manage project risks and ensure customer engagement and satisfaction.
        * Contribute a strong technical voice when participating in the creation of deliverables, milestones and processes attributed to the projects; be a quick, tactical decision-maker that is capable and comfortable with handling unforeseen issues independently.
        * Lead and oversee projects that can range in scope and will include pre-concept/early development projects that will require a hands-on mentality and nimble ability to work.
        * Continuously build and leverage a broad network across the organization to ensure best team structure and members are identified and engaged; successfully navigate and influence across the matrix; identify key stakeholders, understand and manage their expectations, and establish and follow effective communication plans
        * Direct project teams comprised of a broad range of diverse cross-functional members, including external stakeholders such as providers or academic institutions.
        * Foster a high-performing, fast-paced, collaborative team culture characterized by trust, empowerment, accountability and recognition in an ambiguous environment.
        * Maintain a sense of urgency and be execution driven; proactively identify challenges and facilitate problem solving activities or discussion to address obstacles and find the best solutions
        * Perform all work according to Medtronic compliance, privacy, security or regulatory standard processes
        * Perform additional duties as assigned.
