The Strategic Intelligence and Analytics team focuses on qualitative and quantitative analysis related to economic, client and competitive ecosystems in which CME Group operates. Through data science, generating insight, developing tools, and report automation, the team creates competitive advantage to drive better and faster decision making by the firm’s senior leaders. We serve the broader units to identify and promote significant market and client opportunities.

Minimum Job Qualifications:
• Currently enrolled at an accredited university with a finance, business, economics or data analytics similar focus
• MS Office proficiency, with Excel and Power Point
• Excellent written and verbal communication skills
• Strong attention to detail

Preferred Job Qualifications:
• Currently enrolled at an accredited university with a Finance, Business, Economics, Data Analytics, or similar focus
• Interest in pursuing a career in the financial industry and markets
• Academic excellence with the ability to navigate ambiguity
• Analytical skills and the ability to decipher complex business problems

Key areas of exposure for the internship duration include:
• Compiling metrics on efforts utilizing internal and external data sources.
• Assist with creation and clean-up of enterprise data mapping, “internal and external sources”.
• Assist in the definition of a complete customer 360 view. Mapping the data flow life cycle through the enterprise.
• Identify usage of all of our data sources – as well inventorying all the analytics tools that are available and capabilities.
• Help establish Data Quality standards and policies
• Assist in developing business line data driven performance reports

Job location: Chicago, IL
