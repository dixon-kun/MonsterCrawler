Senior Manager – Data Science (B2C)


Westfield Data and Westfield Labs are undertaking a digital transformation strategy to build a direct relationship with customers (B2C).  We are investing in innovative but proven way to deepen customer engagement and profitability through the use of digital channels and position Westfield as the premier retail shopping experience.  Our goal is to provide a highly personalized, engaging experience that represents the quality and value we bring to retail shopping.


Westfield Data Science and Westfield Labs are undertaking a digital transformation strategy to build digital capabilities to enable a seamless shopping experience between physical and digital shopping.  Our strategy is to provide a highly personalized, engaging experience that represents the quality and value we bring to retail shopping.


The Senior Manager – Data Science within the Westfield Data team will be part of a greenfield opportunity, responsible for developing digital and data products and drawing actionable insights through data mining to help guide business decisions. We are looking for candidates who are passionate about analytics, with great teamwork and collaboration skills, and who are excited by the opportunity to be part of a team that is building and shaping Westfield’s analytic capabilities.


Key Responsibilities:

  • Execution and delivery of high quality data-driven business analyses, empirically derived analytic models, and strategies that address business issues and provide key insights leading to actionable recommendations.
  • Design and develop data products that address internal and external business needs using analytic tools/languages such as R, python, and SAS, as well as BI tools such as Tableau.
  • Use cutting edge BI, big data processing tools, and analytic / machine learning techniques, including parametric and non-parametric modeling.
  • Explore multiple data sources, internal and external, for analyses.
  • Translate business problems into analytical framework; develop and deliver on analytic design.
  • Develop and execute project plans in a timely manner, considering agile-like, iterative delivery and adaptation.
  • Ability to manage multiple analytics requests and priorities at the same time.
  • Manage and execute projects independently; guide and mentor junior team members as needed. Work with cross-functional and global teams to deliver on these projects.
  • Champion the use of data-driven decision-making.

Qualifications :

  • Graduate degree in a quantitative field such as Mathematics, Operations Research, Statistics, Economics, Computer Science or Engineering
  • 5+ years’ experience in an analytical environment with advanced analytic rigor including modeling, performing business analyses, generating data-driven insights, and presenting them to stakeholders.
  • Subject matter expertise in several of following analytic areas: marketing analytics, predictive modeling, next best offer recommendations, web/app analytics, A/B testing, customer lifetime value, customer profiling and segmentation, search algorithms, and data visualization
  • Knowledge of varied machine learning techniques and empirical modeling using parametric and non-parametric modeling
  • Expertise in Big data processing and manipulation in at least one of the following; R, Python, Hadoop, BigQuery, SQL, or SAS
  • Proven knowledge in using visualization data in tools such as PowerPoint, Tableau
  • Experience with structuring data and models, and drawing insights from data
  • Strong problem solving skills with demonstrated rigor in analyzing data
  • Innovative thinker with intellectual curiosity, with an eye to developing new solutions and tools
  • Strong collaboration skills, clear team player, can-do attitude
  • Strong excel and PowerPoint skills
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills, as required to deliver an analysis end to end
  • Detail-oriented, deliver with the highest quality
  • Strong multi-tasking skill

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Knowledge of multiple varied machine learning techniques and empirical modeling using parametric and non-parametric modeling
  • Expertise in Big data processing and manipulation in several of the following; R, Python, Hadoop, BigQuery, SQL, or SAS
  • Prior experience in Advertising Technology

If you’ve ever wanted to be part of an innovative business, on the cutting edge of developing and evolving the shopping experience for customers, this is your chance!
