Job Description:

Responsible for developing, modifying and/or maintaining applications in the Global Markets environment. Software developers design, code, test, debug and document programs as well as maintain corporate systems architecture. Employees work closely with business partners in defining requirements for system applications. Employees typically have in-depth knowledge of development tools and languages. Employees are expected to have in-depth global markets product knowledge, and manage a high level of risk. Is clearly recognized as a content expert by peers. Coordinates resources across various applications, operations or projects. Manages employees and makes all decisions on personnel actions including staffing, performance management and comp decision-making. Monitors progress and adjusts resources and priorities accordingly. Typically requires 10+ years of applicable experience. This job code is only to be used for employees supporting Global Markets.

Organization Overview:

The Global Risk Analytics team is building a world class quantitative function to develop the models and framework to manage Risk and Capital across Bank of America, with the objectives to:

* Develop a consistent and coherent set of models and analytical tools that facilitate effective Risk and Capital measurement, management and reporting

* Pro-actively identify areas of problems that would benefit from additional quantitative focus as well as responding to internal and regulatory demands

* Think expansively and strategically to anticipate future needs and to deliver a truly flexible and robust Risk and Capital Infrastructure

* Drive forward the technology infrastructure to support the needs of Global Risk Management and the wider Enterprise

* Strive to exceed regulatory requirements and expectations in supporting our business partners, this role provides a great opportunity to make a material impact in the fast changing arena of Global Analytics Technology.

Role Overview:

The Global Risk Analytics Technology Team have a requirement for a senior-level technology Manager with a primary focus to build and lead the team of technologists responsible for delivery of the data architecture and technical solutions. A detailed understanding of Risk Analytics is preferred. Also important is detailed knowledge of Data Science, including:

* Strong technical knowledge / experience of Big Data ecosystems.

* Experience and working knowledge of business intelligence, data mining, machine learning and data warehousing technologies and solutions.

* Experience of data governance, lineage techniques and stewardship

Responsibilities:

Within this areas, the successful candidate will have responsibilities including:

* Owning the GRA Data processing strategy with responsibility for taking the current infrastructures into their strategic target

* Engaging with D & MD level stakeholders (and below) across multiple GRA Business groups to understand and capture data deficiencies, and ascertain approaches for integration of missing data into source systems

* Working with the delivery teams in Charlotte, Dublin, and near-shore and off-shore teams, to drive delivery to support the global data eco-system

* Liaising with various non-Risk Technology teams to ingest "Golden Source" data into GRA environment

* Understanding Enterprise data strategies to influence key players to help drive direction for long term data requirements within GRA

* Building a team (initially 6 – 12 staff) to drive the technology solutions required.

* Engaging with relevant stakeholders to drive forward discussion and resolution around key issues

* In conjunction with peers, help to drive the overall GRA technology strategy by joining the GRA Technology Senior Management team

Required Skills:

* Strong technical skills in Python, Scala, C++, Java.

* Strong design and architectural skills

* Experience with Big Data technologies (Hadoop, and Spark) and modelling technologies (R, SAS, Matlab)

* Track record of successful delivery of data-centric multi-tiered technology solutions using Open Source grid technologies.

* Experience of management, customization and execution of 3rd party cloud-based processing systems such as Google, AWS etc.

* Strong communication skills to enable managing up and down the technology chain, as well as working with business partners from multiple disciplines with differing priorities across multiple, global locations.

* Proven ability to operate within the global environment operating from multiple locations and time zones.

* Building a team (initially 6 – 12 staff) to drive the technology solutions required.
