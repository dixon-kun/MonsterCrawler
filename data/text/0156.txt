          Overview:
         Based in Northern, VA, Axiologic Solutions LLC has opportunities for you to become part of our high-quality team that delivers innovative solutions to key federal clients. We are currently seeking a candidate to provide analytic support to our DoD client. This  position will be located in either Reston, VA or Washington, DC.
          Responsibilities:
          Establish as needed, APIs and connections to those APIs.
          Develop Data flow diagrams depicting movement of data through the data centric architecture.
          Provide recommendation to simplify existing user processes and automate where appropriate to achieve greater efficiency within mission and business operations.
          Obtain government acceptance of recommendations, time permitting.
          Identify a mechanism to capture data use requirements, to include but not limited to volume, velocity, variety.
          Deliver DoDIIS and IC ITE data services which increase data enrichment, improve access to DIA data sources and support data analytics and data science.
          Provide capability to ingest and extract data.
          Provide an accredited IOC data repository and enterprise data warehouses as representative components of Data Centric Strategy.
          Dynamic: intent to create repeatable, reusable profiles/queries/stored procedures, the contractor shall deliver a dynamic data visualization product that changes as the data against it changes. (geographical, graphical, textual, object or link analysis, relationship mapping).
          Qualification:
          Must have an active/current TS/SCI and CI Poly.
          A bachelor's degree or equivalent training.
          At least 7 years or more of specialized experience in process improvement.
          Manage fact finding, analysis and development of final reports and delivery of presentations related to process improvement.
          Knowledge of organization operations and business objectives.
          Collects, analyzes and interprets process data in one or more areas.
          Support activity and data modeling, development of modern business methods, and identification of best practices.
          Develops or modifies processes to solve complex problems for computer systems and business and electronic interfaces to achieve desired results through the use of a variety of process improvement methodologies.
          Apply process improvement, reengineering methodologies, and internet-related methodologies and principles to conduct process modernization projects.
          Develops and apply advanced process engineering and design methods, theories and research techniques in the investigation and solution to a variety of complex and advanced requirements and problems.
