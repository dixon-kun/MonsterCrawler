        Title: Principal Research Software / Data Scientist
        Group: Center for Advanced Machine Learning (CAML)
        Location: Atlanta, GA
        Position: Full Time
        Job Description
        Our ideal candidate is a data scientist / developer with 5-10 years of experience. You have worked on data science use cases, are proficient in Python and other languages such as C++, Java, Scala, or Javascript. You have plenty of experience in software development, including the software development lifecycle, agile and version control. You're intellectually curious and tenacious, and eager to learn new technologies like advanced machine learning (from the application point-of-view). You can hit the ground running.
        You will work closely with our advanced machine learning research team to build cutting edge machine learning applications and microservices on AWS, as well as contributing to existing applied research and data science on security use cases.
        A background in statistics, math, machine learning, or data science is a plus, but not required.
        Responsibilities
        * Work with senior machine learning researchers to develop and deliver proofs-of-concepts for machine learning / data science applications using state-of-the-art technologies in machine learning.
        * Wrangle new data sources and set up queries and connectors to get data from Symantec's cloud-hosted telemetry sources.
        * Architect, develop and work closely with research and engineering team members on scalable cloud-based machine learning workflow systems, microservices, and applications.
        * Participate in machine learning and data science projects to improve our company and products' security postures, enhance the organization's ability to detect and analyze security threats, and develop new and advanced security features.
        * Collaborate with diverse functional engineering and product teams across Symantec.
        Requirements
        * B.S. or M.S. in computer science, engineering, or other technical domain.
        * 5+ years of experience in software engineering.
        * Expert programming skills in Python.
        * Comfortable and productive working with Linux systems and tools, e.g., bash scripting, curl, make, etc.
        * Savvy and precise at data wrangling, including SQL &amp; NoSQL data stores, text and log files, etc.
        * Exceptional communication skills, both verbal and written.
        * Personal characteristics:
        o Excellent interpersonal skills with an ability to work well in dynamic teams, with individuals across all departments, levels and skills
        o Excellent troubleshooting and problem solving skills
        o Ability to work independently and productively without supervision
        o Demonstrated proactiveness and resourcefulness
        o Strong attention to detail &amp; precision
        o Effective multi-tasking
        Preferred Qualifications (One or more):
        * Experience using Docker for containerizing applications, especially in multi-container architectures.
        * Experience with core big data technologies like Hadoop, Hive, Spark, etc.
        * Experience working with cross-functional, highly-technical teams.
        * Familiarity with fundamentals of statistics and machine learning.
        About CAML:
        Symantec's Center for Advanced Machine Learning (CAML) is a group of PhDs and software engineers, saving the world from the bad guys by using cutting edge machine learning. We do translational and advanced research and development to improve Symantec's products, technology, and human capital efficiency. We focus our efforts in four key areas:
        * Automation &#8211; scaling our people, processes and products
        * Personalization &#8211; customizing our technology and products for specific tasks, customers and contexts
        * Innovation &#8211; developing new solutions and advancing the state-of-the-art in machine learning and cybersecurity protection
        * Education &#8211; teaching our engineering, product and research communities
        We collaborate closely with groups across Symantec to capitalize on Symantec's unique data and resources, and we relentlessly strive to solve current and future challenges in security and information management.
        About Symantec:
        Symantec is a global leader in security, backup and availability solutions. Our innovative products and services protect people and information in any digital environment &#8211; from the smallest mobile device, to the enterprise data center, to cloud-based systems. Our software and services protect against advanced threats independent of the device and environment in which information is used or stored.
        Since its inception in 1982, Symantec has grown into a Fortune 500 company through a combination of internal development, strategic acquisition and partnering with industry leaders. Our award-winning solutions enable our customers to trust that their information and identities are secure independent of device or application.
