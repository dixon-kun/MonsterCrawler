          POSITION SUMMARY:
         We are currently seeking a Statistical/Database Marketing Analyst for our Database Marketing division. The Statistical Marketing Analyst contributes to the company&#8217;s business strategy through the use of innovative analytic approaches and predictive models. This role is an integral part of the analytics team that influences decisions made within multiple internal groups at FULLBEAUTY Brands.
         The Statistical Marketing Analyst will develop, implement and maintain statistical models using modeling techniques including (but not limited to) regression, cluster analysis, and multi-stage models. This position will apply innovative analytic approaches to optimize marketing efforts and further support business decisions in a fast-paced retail environment. This person will also conduct statistical analysis using SAS, and will leverage SQL-based relational database programming to extract data for summary and analysis.
          PRINCIPAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:
          Build predictive models and perform descriptive analytics using statistical methods such as clustering and segmentation techniques, regression, and propensity modeling to support the marketing efforts and business needs of the internal clients.
          Ability to pull data using SAS, SQL, and other technologies from the analytics database and other sources.
          Assess business needs and select optimal analytic/statistical technique(s) to apply for best results ranging from descriptive analyses to robust predictive models.
          Create performance metrics and tracking processes to measure the effectiveness of the predictive modeling efforts. Responsible for the integrity of all models in use including reporting of model performance.
          Work in a collaborative manner with team members to gather requirements, analyze data and provide workable solutions.
          REQUIREMENTS:
          MA/MS degree in Statistics, Data Science or related analytical field of study.
          Minimum of 1-2 years of experience with hands-on consumer data research and marketing databases; serving preferably in a data-intense retail environment.
          Completion of one or more programming courses like C++, Java etc required.
          Capability to execute the complete modeling process from data extraction/manipulation through model development and presentation.
          Familiarity with a variety of statistical models including regression, PCA, cluster analysis, propensity models, and other modeling techniques
          Proven command of SAS, SQL, MS Excel/PowerPoint and ability to build predictive models. Experience with other BI tools a plus.
          Ability to summarize/present analyses to both technical and non-technical audiences
          Strong oral and written communication skills.
          Capable of working in a dynamic, fast-paced environment, handling multiple projects, meeting deadlines, able to prioritize appropriately and respond to issues quickly and creatively with an open and positive attitude.
