        Who We Are
        Our mission is to invent the future of farming. We're transforming the oldest and most important industry in the world from "gut instinct" to data science. Our platform already exposes critical operational data insights to 1 out of every 3 farms in the U.S. and our users absolutely love us.
        Who You Are
        You are committed to building a winning sales culture that people want to be a part of. You have a passion for mentoring, coaching and training junior sales team members. In fact, you have a proven history of helping junior talent grow in their careers. In addition, you have a track record of meeting and exceeding quotas and you are willing to dig in and roll up your sleeves in order to help your team do so. You find fulfillment in influencing processes and best practices to help shape an entire department.
        About the Role
        We are looking for a Sales Manager to grow new business and sell FarmLogs' services to farmers throughout the country. You will be the first Sales Manager located in our Iowa office. We are looking for an expert to lead all business development efforts, cultivate a sales strategy and engineer a sales platform.
        Tasks You'll Be Juggling
        * Develop new relationships with farmers and drive significant new profitable revenue to FarmLogs and the client
        * Prioritize potential opportunities and focus resources on the clients that will have the greatest impact on our business
        * Develop a sales strategy, tactics, quotas, and plans to meet sales targets
        * Identify areas to improve effectiveness and efficiencies with processes, tools and product offerings
        * Deliver compelling product presentations and demonstrations in person and through online meetings
        * Lead negotiations and serve as a contact for solution, price, and proposals
        * Understand the client's business goals and recommend solution(s)
        * Be able to easily speak to and sell all aspects of the FarmLogs' product
        * Utilize rapport building skills to create trust and credibility with the customer.
        * Use probing questions to discover and evaluate customer's needs
        * Demonstrate/present FarmLogs' value proposition to answer the implied questions: why me, why FarmLogs, and why now
        * Provide field intelligence concerning regional market conditions, emerging industry trends, competitive product activities and strategies
        * Coach, mentor and train a junior sales team with the goal of reaching organizational quotas on a monthly, quarterly and annual basis
        * Assist Director of Sales in developing individual and group training within the sales organization
        * Help close business
        * Manage day to day business operations including CRM management, forecasting, sales process adherence and improvement
        * Manage prospecting of all net new customers
        * Manage a team of quota carrying sales reps as well as dedicated schedulers
        * Quickly develop a working knowledge of our product and customer base
        Your Creds
        Required:
        * 5+ years of sales experience
        * At least one year of direct management experience in a sales driven organization
        * Proven ability and track record of meeting and exceeding quotas
        * Expertise in selling strategies/methodologies, strategic planning and execution
        * Strong verbal &amp; written communication skills
        * Enjoys working in fast-paced, roll up your sleeves, chaotic start up environment. Must be able to multitask.
