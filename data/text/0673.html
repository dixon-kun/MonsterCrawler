Data Visualization Expert - PERM - New York, NY Data Visualization Expert Skills & Requirements: Minimum 5 years industry experience with a focus on interactive data visualization, information visualization, information graphics, or related fields. Ability to rapidly iterate through conceptual ideas or designs and effectively communicate these using low- to medium-fidelity prototypes, such as sketches, paper prototypes, wireframes, storyboards, interactive click-throughs etc. Strategic project leadership: experience leading projects through concept, pitch, iterative design and development, delivery, and ongoing support Prior experience working in an interdisciplinary team of developers, designers, and/or subject-matter experts. Ability to communicate with and present to stakeholders at all levels of the organization. Undergraduate/Graduate Degree or equivalent experience in any of the following fields: Information Design, Interaction Design, User Experience, Human-Computer Interaction, Graphic design, Statistics, Quantitative Finance, Data science and Analytics. Desirable Skills and Experience: Familiarity with visual analysis software e.g. Tableau Spotfire, Qlik, VisIt. Familiarity with high-fidelity visualization prototyping tools such as d3.js, Processing, three.js, ggplot2/R, Python or similar. Financial background or experience. Experience designing for desktop & mobile and 2D & 3D data visualizations. Portfolio Data Visualization Expert Job Summary: The role will encompass the research activities necessary to form an understanding of user needs and tasks, as well as the presentation and communication of concepts and solutions through sketches, wireframes, user workflows, and simple data-driven prototypes. Succeeding in this role will require an ability to understand and communicate the complex relationships between markets, governments, businesses, and people hidden in huge datasets. Work with an interdisciplinary team of designers, developers, data scientists, subject-matter experts, and product managers to implement and deploy data visualization tools and should be prepared to pitch concepts to senior stakeholders.
