Job Description

Position Overview

The internship position is primarily responsible for assisting the development team to perform data science tasks and implement software components for the purpose of data science. The position requires strong math and problem-solving skills. This position will work with the architecture team and developers to deliver new and enhanced functionality.

Responsibilities

* Evaluate the performance and accuracy of different data mining and machine learning algorithms for solving defined problems

* Design solutions to enable non-technical users to benefit from advanced analytics, and work with development team for implementation

* Architecture, design, and implementation of multi-tenant scalable data analytics platform and analytical applications

* Assist technical team members during development cycles

* Work closely with the Omneo architecture team to create and enhance

Omneo platform components.

* Work with product owners, QA team and technical publications team as part of agile scrum.

* Work as a data scientist for design, implementation & troubleshooting

Required Knowledge/Skills, Education, and Experience

* B.S and working towards Master's or PhD degree in Engineering,

Computer Science, Math or related field

* Experience designing, developing and deploying high-volume, multi-tiered, distributed applications

* Experience with Hadoop stack (MapReduce, HDFS, Hive, Pig, Oozie) and search technologies (Solr, Lucene)

* Proficiency and hands-on experience in Java, and Spring, REST/SOA frameworks 2 years or more of experience using eclipse, maven, git, Jenkins, nexus or equivalent technologies.

* Experience assessing performance characteristics of software components for optimization

* A strong background in math, statistics and advanced analytics

* Experience in multi-variant analysis techniques such as ANOVA,

Regression Analysis, PCA

* Experience with clustering and classification techniques such as k-mean, Bayesian networks, and decision trees

* Strong analytical and quantitative problem solving ability.

* Hands-on programming experience: Java, C , D3JS

* Good communication skills

* Self-directed with ability to learn new technologies quickly

* Ability to work with tough deadlines and challenging problems

* Passion for working with big data and scalable solutions to help customer make data-driven decisions

Remove if VISA sponsorship is available

Qualified Applicants must be legally authorized for employment in the Unites States. Qualified Applicants will not require employer sponsored work authorization now or in the future for employment in the United States.

Preferred Knowledge/Skills, Education, and Experience

* Experience with high volume data ingestion.

* Experience with Big Data systems in a cloud environment (Ex AWS)

* Understanding of design principles for multi-tenancy, performance, security, high availability, & scalability in a cloud environment

* Knowledge of real-time data processing technologies such as Kafka,

Spark or Storm is a plus

* Industry expertise in electronics, manufacturing, supply chain, and/or product quality data is a plus
