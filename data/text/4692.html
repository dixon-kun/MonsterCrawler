Position Summary

Job ID: 261728

Contract Length: 3 Month - Contract to Hire

Our client has created technology solutions to keep drivers safe on the road. They are looking for a Project Manager to join their team in a contract to hire role. In this role, you will develop and manage a motivated, effective technical team in area of core competency. You will implement planning activities that ensure goals will be met. This person must develop an outstanding organization through good organizational structure, development of employee's capabilities, creative motivational programs and building positive working relationships. Additionally, the person in this role will direct and coordinate development and production activities of technology department by performing the following duties.

What You'll Do:

  • Oversee a team of Data Scientists - the Data Scientists are responsible for gathering the data, this data is then used for tracking purposes and implemented for use in the Driver 360 Platform
  • The Driver 360 Platform is similar to the Product Verizon has developed and can sense crashes, call the police, track vehicle performance, communicate driver behavior to the insurance companies, parents can use the GPS services/functions when their children are using the cars
  • Should have overseen technical, data centric projects
  • Should have experience with the Front end, Middle tier and Back end
  • The project involves Hadoop and Python

Who You Are:

  • Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience
  • 8+ years relevant experience, 4 years of increasingly responsible management or project management experience in Data/cloud/big data Architecture
  • Able to interact with customers (internal and external) at all levels of organization
  • Proactively partners with other departments to provide information and feedback, analyze reports for continuous improvement and resolve issues
